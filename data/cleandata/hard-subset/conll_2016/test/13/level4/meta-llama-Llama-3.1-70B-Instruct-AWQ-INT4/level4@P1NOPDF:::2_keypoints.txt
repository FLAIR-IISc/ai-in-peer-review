- Novelty is unclear: using sentential CNN features for classification is not new; paper needs explicit comparisons to existing continuous-space/event linking methods and a more thorough set of baselines than Table 3.

- Evaluate on additional datasets (e.g., EventCorefBank) and consider joint entity-event coreference to demonstrate robustness beyond the small ACE corpus.
