- Personalized PageRank to prioritize meaningful words over stop words.  
- Small set of head-dependent rules to ensure function words are leaves.  
- Estimation of adposition direction directly from test data.  
- Competitive performance on most UD languages (all but two).  
- Robust to domain changes, with lower across-domain standard deviation than MSD.  
- Outperforms the MSD transfer system on some domains (e.g., Italian).  
- Limited novelty, largely building on Søgaard (2012).  
- Missing POS-tagging scores in prediction mode, preventing analysis of tag–parse correlation.  
- Evaluation and analysis need improvement (e.g., add charts for clearer insights).  
- How will authors address error propagation from POS tagging to dependency parsing?  
- Can authors provide more detailed performance analysis by part of speech?  
- How will the parser be extended to handle multiword expressions and coordination?  
- Overall: a solid unsupervised parsing contribution with potential to be more robust and competitive after improvements.
