- Paper proposes UDP: an unsupervised, POS-only UD parser using UD-motivated head rules, a modified PageRank that personalizes the main predicate and downweights function words, and a two-pass decoding (content then function words).
- Evaluated across 32 languages and multiple domains; competitive with a multi-source delexicalized transfer baseline.
- Main contribution: a strong, UD-compliant, training-free baseline using POS-only PageRank plus two-pass decoding and simple directionality estimation.
- Main contribution: a pragmatic PageRank personalization that boosts the main predicate and implicitly deprioritizes stop words.
- Main contribution: a simple data-driven method to infer adposition (ADP) direction from test bigrams.
- Strength: clear, simple, and broadly applicable method requiring no treebank training and only POS tags and a compact rule set.
- Strength: competitive performance with gold POS and more robust degradation with predicted POS than the supervised baseline.
- Strength: linguistically motivated UD-aware design (function-word leafness, language-agnostic ADP direction) that improves effectiveness.
- Strength: transparent analysis with ablations showing PageRank's contribution and informative POS-wise breakdowns.
- Weakness: limited novelty versus Søgaard (2012); core idea of ranking-before-decoding is very similar and lacks a direct controlled comparison.
- Weakness: personalization is under-explored—only the main predicate is boosted and alternatives (verbs, content words, multi-root) are not systematically tested.
- Weakness: personalization weight (5×) is arbitrary and lacks sensitivity analysis or theoretical justification.
- Weakness: results presentation relies heavily on tables; lacks visualizations that would clarify trends and robustness claims.
- Weakness: insufficient reporting of per-language POS tagging accuracies and lack of correlation analysis between POS errors and UAS degradation.
- Question: include a direct replication of Søgaard (2012) and ablations isolating personalization and two-pass UD-specific decoding to quantify delta over Søgaard?
- Question: why choose 5× for personalization; was a weight sweep or alternative personalization (verbs, nouns, content words, downweighted stop words, multiple root candidates) tried?
- Question: were function words excluded from PR nodes or reweighted, and how sensitive are results to graph construction choices like teleport probability (0.05)?
- Question: please report per-language POS accuracies in prediction mode and provide scatter plots correlating POS accuracy with UAS drop to analyze error propagation.
- Question: clarify “no training” claim given English-dev tuning—what choices were made using English gold trees and what is the impact if removed?
- Suggestion: add visualizations (POS vs UAS drop per language; domain-wise variance plots; ablation curves for personalization weight and head rules).
- Suggestion: report statistical significance or confidence intervals for language-level comparisons to MSD.
- Suggestion: consider extending personalization beyond the root (verbs/nouns or reified edge-level PR) and add a small experiment now if possible.
- Minor: fix typo l.207 “The the main idea” → “The main idea.”
