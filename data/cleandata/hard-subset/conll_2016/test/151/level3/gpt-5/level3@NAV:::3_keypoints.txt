- Proposal: an unsupervised UD parser using only POS tags and PageRank on a rule-licensed graph, with two-step decoding to downweight function words, personalization to boost the main predicate, and an adposition (ADP) directionality heuristic.

Contributions:
- Reimplementation and small extension of Søgaard (2012)-style PageRank parsing for UD, POS-only input, and function-word handling.
- Broad multilingual and cross-domain evaluation (32 languages) showing competitive UAS and robustness to domain shift.
- Simple heuristic to estimate adposition attachment direction from test data.

Strengths:
- Strong cross-lingual scope and robustness across languages and domains, making it a valuable training-free baseline for low-resource settings.
- Simple, transparent, and reproducible method (POS-only, explicit rules, deterministic decoding) that aids error analysis and deployment.
- Effective use of UD constraints (function words as leaves, UD-compliant head rules) that narrows the gap to delexicalized transfer.
- Personalization of the main predicate improves root identification and speeds convergence.

Weaknesses:
- Limited novelty: largely mirrors Søgaard (2012) with incremental UD-specific extensions.
- Personalization is under-explored (only applied to main predicate; no ablations or broader strategies tested).
- Arbitrary hyperparameter choice (personalization weight = 5) with no sensitivity analysis or justification.
- Presentation relies heavily on large tables; key takeaways would benefit from visualizations (scatter plots, bar charts with error bars).
- Missing details on predicted-POS mode: per-language POS tagger accuracies and correlation with UAS degradation are not reported.

Questions / Requests for Authors:
- Precisely delineate novelty over Søgaard (2012) and ablate each addition (UD rules, two-step decoding, stop-word downweighting, personalization).
- How sensitive are results to the personalization weight and to which token is boosted (e.g., last verb vs finite verb vs morphologically marked predicate)?
- Please report per-language POS accuracies (prediction mode) and provide UAS vs POS-accuracy correlation plots.
- Did you try personalizing other content words or edge-level PageRank (reified graph)? Any improvements?
- Include charts summarizing cross-domain variance and ablations; also fix minor typo (l.207 “The the main idea”).
