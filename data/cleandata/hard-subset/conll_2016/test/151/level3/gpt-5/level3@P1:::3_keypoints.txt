- Proposes UDP: a training-free UD parser using POS tags, UD-inspired head rules, personalized PageRank for root/content ranking, two-step decoding enforcing function-word leafness, and on-the-fly adposition direction estimation.
- Claims main contributions: a simple robust unsupervised UD parser competitive with delexicalized transfer, lightweight adposition-direction heuristic, and cross-domain evaluation with error-propagation analysis.

Strengths
- Strong cross-lingual and cross-domain robustness: UAS close to MSD with gold POS (avg gap 6.4%) and lower standard deviation across domains.
- Linguistically grounded, simple design: two-step decoding enforces UD function-word leafness and produces well-formed trees.
- Effective ADP-direction heuristic: data-free estimation achieves ~75% ADP UAS on average.
- Favorable error propagation under predicted POS: smaller UAS drop and lower estimated propagation than MSD.
- Ablation shows PR importance: PR contributes ~9 UAS points; decoding constraints add ~4 points over rules.

Weaknesses
- Limited methodological novelty relative to Søgaard (2012); contributions are modest extensions (UD constraints, ADP heuristic, personalization).
- Personalization choice (boosting first verb with weight=5) is ad hoc and insufficiently justified or analyzed.
- Missing/sparse analyses: per-language POS impacts, visualizations for trends, and deeper study of PR graph construction choices.
- Coverage and structural biases: system omits some phenomena (coordination, relative clauses via excluding NOUN→VERB heads), likely capping upper-bound performance.
- Minor presentation issues and unexplained hyperparameters (typo at l.207 and teleport/personalization choices lack principled justification).

Questions for authors
- Why personalization weight=5? Provide sensitivity analysis across weights, languages, and alternative schemes (distribute mass across verbs, data-driven priors).
- How often is the first verb not the root, and can personalization use morpho-syntactic cues (AUX vs VERB) to improve root selection?
- Are function words included as PR nodes, and what is their impact? Did you try excluding them or reifying/weighting edges by distance?
- Please report per-language POS accuracies and correlation between POS accuracy and UAS drop (gold→predicted) for UDP and MSD.
- How sensitive are results to teleport probability (0.05) and what are runtime/convergence stats by sentence length?
- Can you quantify errors specifically due to coordination and relative clauses to prioritize future work?

Additional comments
- Add summary plots (per-language UAS deltas vs MSD, domain variance bars) to improve interpretability.
- Fix the typo at l.207 and tighten some phrasing.
- Release per-language, hyperparameter-free scripts and domain split metadata to aid reproducibility.
