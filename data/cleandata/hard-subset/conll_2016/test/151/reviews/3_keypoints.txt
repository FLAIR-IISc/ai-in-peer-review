- Proposes parsing universal dependency treebanks using only POS tags and a modified PageRank that downweights stop words.
- Idea is interesting but closely mirrors Søgaard (2012), with limited novelty.
- Personalization boosting the main predicate is useful but under-explored and applied only to the main predicate.
- The chosen personalization weight of 5 appears arbitrary and unjustified.
- Evaluation would benefit from visualizations (charts), since tables alone make results hard to interpret.
- Report POS tagging scores in prediction mode to check whether parsing degradation correlates with tagging degradation.
- Overall contribution over Søgaard (2012) is small.
- Minor issue: typo at l.207 ("The the main idea" → "The main idea").
