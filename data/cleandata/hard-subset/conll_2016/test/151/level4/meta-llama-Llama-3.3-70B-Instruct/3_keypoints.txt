- Proposes parsing universal dependency treebanks using only POS tags and a modified PageRank that prioritizes content words over stop words.  
- Concept is intriguing but strongly resembles Søgaard (2012).  
- Adds a personalization factor to weight the main predicate — a notable idea that is under-explored.  
- Personalization appears limited to the main predicate and the chosen weight of 5 seems arbitrary.  
- Evaluation clarity suffers from lack of charts; tables are sometimes hard to interpret.  
- Missing POS tagging scores in prediction mode; these are needed to evaluate correlation between tagging and parsing degradation.  
- Overall contribution beyond Søgaard (2012) is relatively modest.  
- Minor issue: typo on Line 207 ("The the main idea" → "The main idea").
