This paper introduces a novel, training-free unsupervised algorithm for Universal Dependencies parsing, leveraging PageRank for word-level tagging in conjunction with a limited set of defined rules. 

The manuscript is exceptionally well-written and provides a thorough explanation of the methodology, with clear rationale presented for the incorporation of each component.

I assess the contribution to be significant within the area of unsupervised parsing. Furthermore, the authors’ discussion of potential future research directions is particularly insightful and suggests promising avenues for continued investigation.
