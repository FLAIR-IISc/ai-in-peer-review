The authors have proposed an unsupervised algorithm for universal dependencies that does not require training. The tagging is based on PageRank for words and a small amount of hard-coded rules.

The article is well-written, very detailed, and clearly explains the insights behind all the prior knowledge being added to the model.

I think the contribution in the field of unsupervised parsing is significant, and the possibilities of future work presented by the authors lead to additional research.
