- Introduces a POS-only tree parsing method using a modified PageRank to emphasize meaningful words over stop words.
- Strong resemblance to Søgaard (2012); overall contribution beyond that work is relatively modest.
- Personalization factor to weight the main predicate is a nice idea but underdeveloped.
- Personalization appears applied only to the main predicate and the chosen weight of 5 seems arbitrary.
- Evaluation lacks charts/visualizations, making it hard to grasp key insights from tables alone.
- Should report POS tagging scores in prediction mode to determine if parsing degradation is due to tagging decline.
- Overall interesting paper but limited novelty compared to prior work.
- Minor typo: l. 207 "The the main idea" should be "The main idea."
