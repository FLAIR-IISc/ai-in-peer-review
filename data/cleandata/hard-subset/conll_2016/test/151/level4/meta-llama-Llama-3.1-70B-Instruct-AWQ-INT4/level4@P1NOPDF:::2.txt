The authors propose an unsupervised algorithm for Universal Dependencies that eliminates the need for training. The tagging process relies on PageRank for word ranking and a small set of hard-coded rules.

The article is well-written, providing a high level of detail, and effectively explains the intuition behind incorporating prior information into the model.

In my opinion, the contribution is substantial to the field of unsupervised parsing, and the potential avenues for future research presented by the authors are likely to inspire additional investigation.
