The authors propose an unsupervised algorithm for Universal Dependencies that operates without the need for training data. The approach leverages PageRank to assign part-of-speech tags to words, supplemented by a minimal set of hard-coded rules. 

The article is well-written and provides a high level of detail, effectively conveying the intuition behind the incorporation of prior information into the model. The clarity of the explanation facilitates a thorough understanding of the methodology.

The contribution of this work is substantial, particularly in the realm of unsupervised parsing. Furthermore, the authors present several avenues for future research, which have the potential to inspire additional studies and advancements in the field.
