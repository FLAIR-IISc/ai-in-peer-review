The authors have presented a novel unsupervised algorithm for Universal Dependencies, eliminating the need for training data. This approach leverages PageRank for word tagging and incorporates a limited set of predetermined rules. 

The manuscript is well-crafted, providing meticulous details and clearly articulating the rationale behind the incorporation of prior knowledge into the model.

In my opinion, the contribution of this work is significant to the field of unsupervised parsing, and the avenues for future research proposed by the authors offer a fertile ground for further investigation.
