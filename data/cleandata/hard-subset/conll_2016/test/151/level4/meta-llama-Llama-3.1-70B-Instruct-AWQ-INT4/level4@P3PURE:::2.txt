The authors propose an unsupervised algorithm for Universal Dependencies that does not require training, relying on PageRank for word tagging and a small set of hard-coded rules. 

The article is well-written, highly detailed, and effectively explains the intuition behind incorporating prior information into the model. 

I believe the contribution is substantial to the field of unsupervised parsing, and the possibilities for future work presented by the authors are likely to inspire additional research.
