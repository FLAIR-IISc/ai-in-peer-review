- Proposes an unsupervised UD parsing method using a modified PageRank to prioritize meaningful words over function words.  
- Approach is similar to Søgaard (2012); overall contribution over that prior work is modest.  
- Adds a personalization factor to emphasize the main predicate—useful but applied narrowly and the chosen weight (5) seems arbitrary.  
- Evaluation and analysis are thorough, but presenting results only in tables makes trends hard to discern; charts/visualizations would help.  
- Should report POS tagging scores in prediction mode to evaluate correlation between tagging degradation and parsing performance loss.  
- Minor issue: typo at l.207 (“The the main idea” → “The main idea”).
