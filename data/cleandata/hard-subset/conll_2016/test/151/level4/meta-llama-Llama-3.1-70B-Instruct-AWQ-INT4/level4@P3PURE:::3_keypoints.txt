- Proposes parsing UD treebanks using only POS tags with a modified PageRank that weights meaningful words over stop words.
- Idea is interesting but closely resembles Søgaard (2012), so the contribution may be incremental.
- Introduces a personalization factor prioritizing the main predicate, which is a welcome addition.
- Personalization appears applied only to the main predicate and the chosen weight (5) seems arbitrary and unexplored.
- Evaluation lacks plots; charts would make results clearer since tables are hard to interpret.
- Should report POS tagging scores in prediction mode to assess whether parsing decline correlates with tagging degradation.
- Overall contribution is modestly incremental over prior work.
- Minor typo: line 207 "The the main idea" should be "The main idea".
