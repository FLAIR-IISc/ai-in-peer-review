- Proposes parsing universal dependency treebanks using only POS tags and a modified PageRank to emphasize meaningful words over stop words.  
- The idea strongly resembles Søgaard (2012), limiting the paper’s novelty.  
- Adds a personalization factor to give greater weight to the main predicate, which is a useful extension.  
- The personalization factor is underutilized and the chosen weight of 5 for the main predicate is arbitrary and should be optimized.  
- Evaluation would benefit from charts to complement tables, which can be hard to interpret.  
- Should report POS tagging scores in prediction mode to examine correlation between tagging and parsing degradation.  
- Overall contribution is interesting but incremental compared to Søgaard (2012).  
- Minor issue: typo at l.207 ("The the main idea" → "The main idea").
