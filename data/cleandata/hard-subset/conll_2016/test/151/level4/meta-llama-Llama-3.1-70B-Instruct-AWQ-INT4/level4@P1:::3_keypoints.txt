- Proposes an unsupervised UD parser using only POS constraints and a modified PageRank.  
- Novelty is limited; approach resembles Søgaard (2012) and shows only a small increment over it.  
- Introduces a small set of UD-specific dependency head rules.  
- Uses personalized PageRank to emphasize the main predicate, which is a promising idea.  
- Personalization currently limited to main predicate; chosen weight of 5 seems arbitrary and underexplored.  
- Parser requires no training and is competitive with a delexicalized multi-source transfer system.  
- System is robust to domain change across multiple languages (evaluated on 32 languages).  
- Evaluation and analyses are thorough but tables are dense; charts would improve clarity.  
- Missing POS-tagging scores in prediction mode to assess correlation between tagging and parsing degradation.  
- Lacks discussion/plan for handling multiword expressions, coordination, and proper names.  
- Minor issues: small grammatical error noted ("The the main idea").  
- Questions: justification/experimentation for weight=5, extension to MWEs/coordination/names, and inclusion of POS prediction scores.
