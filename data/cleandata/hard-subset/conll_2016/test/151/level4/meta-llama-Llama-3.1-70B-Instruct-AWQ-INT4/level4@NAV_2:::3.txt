This paper presents an unsupervised dependency parsing algorithm for Universal Dependencies (UD) that leverages a modified version of PageRank to prioritize meaningful words over function words. The approach is intriguing, but its similarity to Søgaard (2012)'s work is notable. The introduction of a personalization factor to emphasize the main predicate is a welcome addition, although its application seems limited and the chosen weight of 5 appears arbitrary.

The evaluation and analysis sections are thorough, but the use of tables to present results makes it challenging to discern key trends and insights. Incorporating charts or visualizations would have enhanced the clarity and impact of the findings.

Furthermore, it would have been beneficial to include the scores of the POS tagging in prediction mode to assess the correlation between the degradation in parsing performance and the degradation in tagging performance.

Overall, the paper is interesting, but the incremental contribution over Søgaard (2012)'s work is modest.
