The authors propose an unsupervised algorithm for Universal Dependencies that eliminates the need for training data, instead relying on PageRank for word tagging and a limited set of hard-coded rules. 

The article is well-written, providing a high level of detail, and effectively conveys the intuition behind the incorporation of prior information into the model.

In my opinion, the contribution of this work is substantial to the field of unsupervised parsing, and the potential avenues for future research presented by the authors are likely to inspire further investigation.
