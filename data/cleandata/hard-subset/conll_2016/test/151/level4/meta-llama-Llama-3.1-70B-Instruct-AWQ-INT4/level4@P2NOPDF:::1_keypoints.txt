- Proposes a novel deterministic dependency parser using POS-based head rules, biased PageRank ranking, and greedy linking under tree, head-rule, and directionality constraints.
- Paper is well-structured and engaging but the contribution seems modest compared to Sogaard (2012).
- Head-rule table needs deeper corpus analysis: report how often rules produce disconnected graphs and typical component sizes.
- Questionable choice to determine head direction from test data rather than training/dev data, despite quick convergence.
- Clarify tie-breaking for identical PageRank scores and whether ties materially affect performance.
- Recommend reporting oracle/upper-bound results for each constraint (head rules, directionality, distance) to show their impact on maximum achievable score.
- Minor wording fixes: rephrase "we obtain [the] rank" (Section 4.1) and "isolate the [contribution] of both" (Section 6.1).
- Inconsistent column ordering between Table 5 and Table 7; prefer the ordering used in Table 7.
