The authors propose an unsupervised algorithm for Universal Dependencies that does not require training, relying on PageRank for word tagging and a small set of hard-coded rules. 

The article is well-written, providing a high level of detail that effectively conveys the intuition behind the incorporation of prior information into the model. The clarity of the explanations is commendable, making the content accessible to a broad audience.

In my opinion, the contribution of this work is substantial to the field of unsupervised parsing. The authors present several possibilities for future research, which will likely give rise to additional studies and advancements in this area.
