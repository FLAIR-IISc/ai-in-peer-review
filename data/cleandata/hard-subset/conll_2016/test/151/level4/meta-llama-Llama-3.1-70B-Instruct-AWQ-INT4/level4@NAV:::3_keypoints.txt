- Proposes a novel POS-only parsing method for universal dependency treebanks using a modified PageRank.
- Method emphasizes meaningful words over stop words, which is an interesting idea.
- Approach resembles Søgaard (2012); incremental contribution over that work appears minor.
- Adds a personalization factor to emphasize the primary predicate, but its potential is underexplored.
- Personalization seems limited to the main predicate and uses an arbitrary weight of 5.
- Evaluation lacks visual aids (charts) that would clarify table data.
- Should report POS tagging scores in prediction mode to check correlation between tagging degradation and parsing performance drop.
- Minor typo: l.207 "The the main idea" → "The main idea".
