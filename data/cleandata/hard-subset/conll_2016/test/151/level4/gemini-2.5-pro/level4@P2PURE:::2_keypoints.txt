- Proposes a novel unsupervised Universal Dependency parsing algorithm that requires no training.  
- Generates dependency tags using a PageRank-based method applied to words, supplemented by a small set of hard-coded rules.  
- Paper is clearly written and detailed.  
- Authors provide strong explanations of the intuition behind each piece of prior information used.  
- Represents a substantial contribution to unsupervised parsing.  
- Suggested future work is compelling and likely to stimulate further research.
