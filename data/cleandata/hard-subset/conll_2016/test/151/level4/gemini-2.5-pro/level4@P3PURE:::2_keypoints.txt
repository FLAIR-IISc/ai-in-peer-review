- Proposes an unsupervised algorithm for Universal Dependencies that requires no training.
- Tagging method uses PageRank over words combined with a small set of hard-coded rules.
- Paper is well-written and provides a detailed account of the method.
- Authors clearly explain the intuition behind each piece of prior information integrated into the model.
- Represents a substantial contribution to unsupervised parsing.
- Presented avenues for future work are promising and likely to stimulate further research.
