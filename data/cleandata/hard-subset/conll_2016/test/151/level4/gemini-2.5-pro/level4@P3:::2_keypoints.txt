- Paper introduces UDP, an unsupervised dependency parser for UD that needs no training and uses POS-based head-selection rules.  
- Core algorithm: personalized PageRank to rank content words (biasing main predicate to root) plus a two-step decoder attaching content then function words, with adposition direction estimated from test data.  
- Evaluated on 32 languages from UD v1.2 against a multi-source delexicalized transfer parser, including robustness checks to POS errors and domain shift.  

- Contribution: a simple, effective unsupervised parser combining PageRank with a small set of UD-derived rules that matches competitive supervised delexicalized baselines without training.  
- Contribution: extensive cross-lingual and cross-domain evaluation demonstrating robustness and stability across genres and POS settings.  

- Strength: Strong performance for a training-free method, within 6.4% UAS of a multi-source delexicalized supervised system on gold POS, and better on some languages (e.g., Hindi).  
- Strength: High robustness to domain shift, showing lower performance variance across genres compared to the supervised baseline.  
- Strength: Thorough analyses and ablations, showing POS-tag robustness and a significant PageRank contribution (~+9 UAS), plus error breakdowns by POS and constructions.  

- Weakness: Key design choices (head rules, PageRank teleport prob, fixed function-word directions) were tuned on English dev data, risking English bias and undermining full language-agnostic claims.  
- Weakness: Limited syntactic coverage due to a small rule set (e.g., excludes NOUN->VERB so cannot handle relative clauses), restricting handling of common constructions.  
- Weakness: Deterministic mapping from POS sequences to trees — identical POS sequences always yield identical parses, preventing modeling of ambiguity or uncertainty.  

- Question: Have you considered dynamically estimating attachment direction for other function words (AUX, DET, CONJ) as you do for adpositions to better handle non-English word orders?  
- Question: Please quantify the dev-set performance drop that motivated excluding the NOUN->VERB rule and clarify its impact across languages.  
- Question: How sensitive is performance to the PageRank personalization weight (5x for main predicate), and did you try alternative weights or personalization heuristics?
