- Introduces a deterministic unsupervised dependency parser using predefined head rules, biased PageRank for word ranking, and greedy attachment to nearest valid heads while preserving tree structure.  
- Paper is well-written and presents an interesting approach but seems incremental relative to Sogaard (2012); authors should clearly state novel contributions versus that prior work.  
- Recommend deeper analysis of head-rule constraints (Table 1), including how often rules yield disconnected graphs and typical component sizes across corpora.  
- Suggest an oracle analysis to quantify the performance ceiling imposed by each constraint (head rules, directionality, distance) to identify biggest improvement opportunities.  
- Question the choice to determine head directionality on the test set rather than a development set; request rationale.  
- Ask for clarification on tie-breaking for identical PageRank scores and whether ties affect parsing accuracy.  
- Minor corrections: change "we obtain rank" to "we obtain the rank" (Section 4.1) and "isolate the of both" to "isolate the contribution of both" (Section 6.1).  
- Suggest standardizing column ordering/presentation across tables (e.g., make Table 5 match the more intuitive ordering in Table 7).
