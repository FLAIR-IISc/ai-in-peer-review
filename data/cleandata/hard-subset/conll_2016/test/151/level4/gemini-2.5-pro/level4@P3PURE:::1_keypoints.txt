- Paper proposes a deterministic dependency parser using POS-based head rules, biased PageRank to rank words, and greedy linking to the nearest permitted head under tree and directionality constraints.
- Overall interesting and clearly presented, but method appears only slightly different from Søgaard (2012) "Unsupervised Dependency Parsing without Training".
- Head-rule analysis needed: quantify how often the rule-induced graph is disconnected and report typical component sizes.
- Clarify why head directionality is determined using test data instead of a training/dev set, despite reported quick convergence.
- Specify the PageRank tie-breaking mechanism and report whether ties materially affect performance.
- Provide oracle analyses for each constraint (head rules, directionality, distance) to determine their individual upper-bound impact on performance.
- Minor: fix Section 4.1 phrasing from "we obtain rank" to "we obtain the rank".
- Minor: make the column order in Tables 5 and 7 consistent (prefer the clearer Table 7 ordering).
- Minor: fix Section 6.1 phrasing from "isolate the of both" to "isolate the contribution of both".
