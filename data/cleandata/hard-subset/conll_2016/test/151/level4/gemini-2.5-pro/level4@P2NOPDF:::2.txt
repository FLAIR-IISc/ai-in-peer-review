This paper proposes an unsupervised, training-free algorithm for Universal Dependency parsing. The core of the approach is an innovative use of the PageRank algorithm to score word prominence, which is then supplemented by a small set of handcrafted linguistic rules.

The article is exceptionally well-written and detailed. The authors provide clear and compelling intuition for the incorporation of each piece of prior knowledge, making the model's design easy to follow.

The work represents a substantial contribution to the field of unsupervised parsing. Furthermore, the possibilities for future work presented by the authors are promising and open up valuable avenues for additional research.
