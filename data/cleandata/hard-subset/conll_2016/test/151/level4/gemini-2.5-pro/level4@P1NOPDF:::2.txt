This paper presents a training-free, unsupervised algorithm for Universal Dependency parsing. The core of the method relies on a PageRank-based word scoring mechanism, combined with a small set of hand-crafted rules.

The paper is well-written and detailed. The authors provide clear and compelling intuitions for the prior information incorporated into their model.

This work represents a substantial contribution to the field of unsupervised parsing. Furthermore, the avenues for future work outlined by the authors are promising and likely to inspire additional research.
