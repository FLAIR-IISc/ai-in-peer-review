- Proposes a UD treebank parser that relies solely on POS tags.
- Uses a modified PageRank to give higher weight to content words over stop words.
- Core idea is interesting but closely resembles Søgaard (2012), limiting novelty.
- Introduces a personalization factor to boost the main predicate, which is a novel element.
- Personalization appears applied only to the main predicate and the chosen weight (5) is arbitrary and unjustified.
- Evaluation presentation lacks charts/visualizations, making trends hard to interpret from tables alone.
- Authors should report POS tagging accuracy with predicted tags to assess how tag errors affect parsing performance.
- Overall contribution is incremental over Søgaard (2012).
- Minor typo: l.207 "The the main idea" → "The main idea".
