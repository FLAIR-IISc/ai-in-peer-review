The authors present an unsupervised approach for Universal Dependencies that operates without a training phase. The core of the tagging mechanism is founded on applying PageRank to words, augmented by a minimal set of hard-coded rules.

The paper is well-articulated and detailed, providing a clear justification for the prior information incorporated into the model. I believe this work is a significant contribution to the field of unsupervised parsing, and the avenues for future work suggested by the authors are poised to inspire additional investigation.
