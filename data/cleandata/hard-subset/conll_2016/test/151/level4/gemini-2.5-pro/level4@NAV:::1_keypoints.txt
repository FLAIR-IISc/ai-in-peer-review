- Introduces a deterministic dependency parsing algorithm using POS-based head rules, PageRank-based word ranking, and nearest-available attachment under tree and directionality constraints.  
- Paper is interesting and well-presented but novelty seems incremental compared to Søgaard (2012).  
- Request a deeper, corpus-based analysis of the head rules (Table 1), including frequency of disconnected graphs and sizes of resulting components.  
- Question the unusual choice to determine head direction on test data rather than a train/dev split, even if the decision converges quickly.  
- Ask for clarification of the tie-breaking mechanism for identical PageRank scores and an analysis of how often ties occur and their impact on performance.  
- Note that the model constraints (head rules, directionality, distance) impose a performance ceiling and request oracle experiments isolating each constraint’s cost.  
- Minor wording correction: change “we obtain rank” to “we obtain the rank” in Section 4.1.  
- Minor formatting: make column ordering consistent across Tables 5 and 7, preferably using Table 7’s layout.  
- Typo in Section 6.1: “isolate the of both” should likely be “isolate the contribution of both.”
