- Presents a deterministic POS-based dependency parsing algorithm using biased PageRank and nearest permissible-head attachment.
- Paper is clearly presented but appears incremental relative to Søgaard (2012).
- Head-rule analysis is insufficient: no quantification of how often rules produce disconnected graphs or component sizes.
- Choosing head directionality based on the test set is unusual, even if it converges quickly.
- Tie-breaking for equal PageRank scores is not described; impact on performance is unclear.
- Recommend oracle analyses for each constraint (head rules, directionality, distance) to show upper-bound effects and guide future work.
- Minor editorial issues: "we obtain rank" → "we obtain the rank".
- Minor formatting: inconsistent column order between Table 5 and Table 7 (Table 7 clearer).
- Minor wording: Section 6.1 likely missing "contribution" in "isolate the of both".
