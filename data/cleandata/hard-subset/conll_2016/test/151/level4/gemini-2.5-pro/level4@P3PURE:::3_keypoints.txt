- Method parses Universal Dependencies using only POS tags and a modified PageRank to weight meaningful words over stopwords.
- Uses a personalization factor to boost main predicates, but personalization seems applied only to the main predicate.
- Choice of personalization weight (5) is arbitrary and lacks justification or analysis.
- Core idea is interesting but largely similar to Søgaard (2012), so contribution appears incremental.
- Personalization concept could be explored more thoroughly (e.g., varying targets, weights, ablation).
- Evaluation presentation is weak—tables alone make trends hard to see; include charts/graphs for clarity.
- Authors should report POS tagging accuracy in prediction mode to analyze how tag degradation affects parsing.
- Overall conclusion: paper is interesting but offers a small incremental advance over prior work.
- Minor issue: typo on line 207 ("The the main idea" → "The main idea").
