The authors propose an unsupervised algorithm for Universal Dependencies that requires no training. The tagging method is based on a PageRank algorithm applied to words, in conjunction with a small set of hard-coded rules.

The article is well-written and provides a detailed account of the proposed method. The authors offer clear explanations for the intuition behind each piece of prior information integrated into the model.

This work represents a substantial contribution to the field of unsupervised parsing. Furthermore, the avenues for future work presented by the authors are promising and likely to stimulate additional research in the area.
