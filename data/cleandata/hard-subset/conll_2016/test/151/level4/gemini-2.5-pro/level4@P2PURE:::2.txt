The authors propose a novel unsupervised algorithm for Universal Dependency parsing that requires no training. The approach generates dependency tags using a PageRank-based method applied to words, supplemented by a small set of hard-coded rules.

The paper is clearly written and detailed. The authors do an excellent job of explaining the intuition behind each piece of prior information integrated into their model.

This work represents a substantial contribution to the field of unsupervised parsing. Furthermore, the avenues for future work suggested by the authors are compelling and likely to stimulate further research.
