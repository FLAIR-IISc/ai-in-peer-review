- Proposes a POS-only UD parsing method using a modified PageRank to up-weight content words.  
- Concept is compelling but strongly resembles Søgaard (2012), making the contribution appear incremental.  
- Adds a personalization factor to up-weight the main predicate, which is useful but underdeveloped.  
- Personalization seems applied only to the main predicate and the chosen weight (5) is arbitrary and unjustified.  
- Evaluation presentation relies on tables; adding charts/visualizations would improve interpretability.  
- Missing POS tagging performance with predicted tags — needed to analyze correlation between tagging degradation and parsing performance.  
- Overall contribution beyond Søgaard (2012) is modest.  
- Minor issue: typo on l.207 ("The the main idea" → "The main idea").
