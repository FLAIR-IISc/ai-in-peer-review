- Proposes a training-free, unsupervised algorithm for Universal Dependency parsing.  
- Core method uses PageRank-based word scoring combined with a small set of hand-crafted rules.  
- Paper is well-written and provides detailed exposition.  
- Authors give clear and compelling intuitions for the prior information used.  
- Represents a substantial contribution to unsupervised parsing.  
- Outlined future work avenues are promising and likely to inspire further research.
