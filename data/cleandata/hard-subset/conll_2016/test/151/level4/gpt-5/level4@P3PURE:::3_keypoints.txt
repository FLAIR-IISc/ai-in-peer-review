- Proposes a POS-only dependency parsing method using a modified PageRank to weight meaningful words over stop words.  
- The idea is interesting but highly similar to Søgaard (2012).  
- Adds a personalization factor to boost the main predicate, which is a useful extension.  
- Personalization appears applied only to the main predicate and the chosen weight of 5 seems arbitrary and underexplored.  
- Evaluation lacks visualizations (charts) and detailed analyses, making key takeaways hard to extract from tables alone.  
- Should report POS-tagging scores in prediction mode to check whether parsing degradation correlates with tagging degradation.  
- Overall contribution is modest — an interesting incremental improvement over Søgaard (2012).  
- Minor issue: typo “The the main idea” → “The main idea” (l. 207).
