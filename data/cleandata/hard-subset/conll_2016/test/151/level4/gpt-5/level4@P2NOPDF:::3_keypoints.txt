- Proposes a POS-only parsing method using a modified PageRank to upweight informative words vs stop words.  
- Idea is appealing but closely resembles Søgaard (2012), limiting novelty.  
- Personalization to increase the main predicate’s weight is sensible but underdeveloped and only applied to the main predicate.  
- The fixed personalization weight of 5 appears arbitrary; needs justification or ablation (vary weight, personalize more content words, or learn weights).  
- Evaluation presentation is dense; plots would help make trends and effect sizes easier to grasp.  
- Recommend visualizing performance by language, POS ambiguity, or sentence length to clarify analysis.  
- Report POS tagging accuracy in the prediction setting and analyze its relationship with parsing performance to assess impact of POS errors.  
- Overall: interesting but a small incremental advance; stronger empirical support for personalization and clearer results would improve impact.  
- Minor issue: typo l.207 “The the main idea” → “The main idea.”
