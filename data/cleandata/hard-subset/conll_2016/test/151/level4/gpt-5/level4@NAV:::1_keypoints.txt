- Introduces a deterministic dependency parser using POS-based head rules and a biased PageRank token ranking; evaluated across several languages.  
- Paper is interesting and clearly written but seems to differ only modestly from Søgaard (2012).  
- Request to analyze head rules against the corpus: report how often the allowed-dependency graph is disconnected and typical component sizes.  
- Concern that head directionality was chosen using test data rather than training/dev, though the decision converges quickly (10–15 sentences).  
- Ask how ties in PageRank scores are resolved and whether ties materially affect performance.  
- Recommend reporting oracle results for each constraint (head rules, directionality, distance) to quantify their impact on maximum achievable performance.  
- Minor wording fixes: 4.1 “we obtain [the] rank” and 6.1 “isolate the [contribution] of both.”  
- Minor formatting: Table 5 and Table 7 use different column orders; Table 7’s layout is clearer.
