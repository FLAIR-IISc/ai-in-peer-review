- Proposes parsing Universal Dependencies using only POS tags plus a modified PageRank that upweights meaningful words versus stop words.
- Idea is interesting but very close to Søgaard (2012); incremental contribution is small.
- Personalization to upweight the main predicate is a nice touch but seems applied only to the main predicate and the chosen weight of 5 appears arbitrary — needs further development.
- Evaluation/analysis lacks visualizations (charts); key takeaways are hard to extract from tables alone.
- Should report POS tagging accuracy in prediction mode to assess whether parsing drops are driven by tagging drops.
- Minor typo: l.207 “The the main idea” → “The main idea.”
