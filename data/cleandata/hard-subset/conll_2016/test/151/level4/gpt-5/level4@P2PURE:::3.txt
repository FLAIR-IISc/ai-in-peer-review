This paper proposes a method to parse trees—specifically the Universal Dependencies treebanks—using only POS tags and a modified PageRank that assigns greater weight to meaningful words (as opposed to stop words). The idea is interesting, but it is very close to Søgaard (2012). The personalization factor that increases the weight of the main predicate is a nice touch, but it should be developed further. As far as I can tell, personalization is applied only to the main predicate, and the chosen weight of 5 appears arbitrary.

Regarding the evaluation and detailed analyses, including some charts would have been helpful, as it is sometimes hard to extract the main message from the tables. Finally, it would have been useful to report POS tagging scores in prediction mode, to assess whether the drop in parsing performance is strongly correlated with the drop in tagging performance (as expected).

All in all, the paper is interesting, but the increment over Søgaard (2012) is small.
