The paper proposes an unsupervised parser for Universal Dependencies that requires no training. The approach relies on PageRank to rank words and a small set of hard-coded head rules.

The article is clearly written and detailed, and the intuition behind each piece of prior knowledge added to the model is explained well.

Overall, I consider the contribution substantial for unsupervised parsing, and the directions for future work outlined by the authors suggest promising avenues for further research.
