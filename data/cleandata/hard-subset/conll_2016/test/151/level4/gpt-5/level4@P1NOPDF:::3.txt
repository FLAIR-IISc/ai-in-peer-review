This paper proposes a method for parsing Universal Dependencies treebanks using only POS tags, along with a modified PageRank algorithm that increases the weight of meaningful words (as opposed to stop words). The idea is interesting, but it is very close to what was done in Søgaard (2012). The use of personalization to upweight the main predicate is a nice touch, but it would be better to develop this further. As far as I can tell, personalization is applied only to the main predicate, and the chosen weight of 5 seems arbitrary.

Regarding evaluation and analysis, some charts would have been helpful, as the key takeaways are sometimes hard to extract from the tables.

It would also be useful to report POS tagging accuracy in prediction mode to determine whether the drop in parsing performance is strongly correlated with a drop in tagging performance (as would be expected).

Overall, the paper is interesting, but the incremental contribution over Søgaard (2012) is small.
