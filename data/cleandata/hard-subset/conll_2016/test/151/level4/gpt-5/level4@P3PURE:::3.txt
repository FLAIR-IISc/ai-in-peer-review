This paper presents a method for parsing trees in the Universal Dependencies treebanks using only POS tags, leveraging a modified PageRank to assign greater weight to more meaningful words (as opposed to stop words). The idea is interesting, though very close to what was done in Søgaard (2012).

The personalization factor that increases the weight of the main predicate is a nice addition, but it would have been better to take it further. As far as I can tell, personalization is applied solely to the main predicate, and the chosen weight of 5 appears arbitrary.

Regarding the evaluation and detailed analyses, charts would have been beneficial, as the key takeaways are sometimes hard to extract from the tables. It would also be helpful to report POS-tagging scores in prediction mode to assess whether the degradation in parsing performance is strongly correlated with the degradation in tagging performance (as expected).

Overall, the paper is interesting, but the increment over the work of Søgaard (2012) is small.
