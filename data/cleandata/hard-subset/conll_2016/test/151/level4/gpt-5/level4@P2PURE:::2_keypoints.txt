- Presents an unsupervised Universal Dependencies algorithm that requires no training.
- Tagging is based on word-level PageRank plus a small set of hard-coded rules.
- Paper is well written, detailed, and clearly explains methods.
- Clearly explains the intuition for incorporating prior information into the model.
- Contribution is substantial to the field of unsupervised parsing.
- Proposed avenues for future work are promising for further research.
