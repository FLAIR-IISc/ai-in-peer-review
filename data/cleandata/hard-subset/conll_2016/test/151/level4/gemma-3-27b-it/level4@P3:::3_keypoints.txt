- Paper proposes UDP, an unsupervised UD parser using POS constraints and a personalized PageRank algorithm.  
- Core idea is building a parser that relies only on POS tags and a modified PageRank; similar to prior work (Søgaard 2012).  
- Personalizing PageRank to prioritize likely predicates improves performance, but the 5x weight on main predicate lacks strong justification.  

Main contributions:
- Presents a competitive unsupervised UD parser achieving strong UAS across many languages without training data.  
- Adapts personalized PageRank to dependency parsing by weighting likely predicates.  
- Shows UDP is more robust to domain shifts than a supervised multi-source delexicalized baseline.  

Strengths:
- Evaluated on 32 languages, demonstrating broad coverage and generality.  
- Achieves UAS close to a strong supervised baseline and outperforms it on some languages (e.g., Hindi).  
- Provides quantitative robustness analysis across domains (Tables 6 & 7).  
- Methodology and decoding algorithm are clearly presented and illustrated.  
- Parser and annotated evaluation datasets are made available to the community.  

Weaknesses:
- Incremental novelty over prior work; personalization choice feels empirical rather than theoretically justified.  
- Error analysis is limited; deeper investigation into error types is needed beyond PUNCT and CONJ.  
- POS tagging accuracy for the predicted-POS experiments is not reported.  
- Some tables (e.g., Table 5) are dense and hard to interpret quickly.  
- Head-rule selection appears ad-hoc, chosen by development performance rather than systematic or linguistic criteria.  

Questions for authors:
- Please provide POS tagging accuracy for the predicted POS experiments.  
- Why was 5x chosen for the main-predicate personalization weight; what range was tested?  
- Can you supply a more detailed error analysis identifying common parsing errors?  
- How sensitive is performance to the PageRank teleport probability?  

Additional comments:
- Minor typo: "The the main idea" → "The main idea" (Line 207).  
- Recommend comparing to more recent unsupervised parsing methods beyond Søgaard (2012).  
- Suggest discussing limitations of the UD formalism and their impact on parser performance.
