- Paper introduces a novel deterministic dependency parsing algorithm based on rule-specified allowable dependencies and a slightly weighted PageRank ranking.  
- Algorithm links each ranked word to its nearest compatible head iteratively, enforcing tree structure, head rules, and directionality constraints.  
- Manuscript is well-written and approach is intriguing but appears incremental relative to prior work (e.g., Sogaard 2012).  
- Recommend corpus-based evaluation of the head rules: quantify how often rules produce disconnected graphs and typical component sizes.  
- Selection of head direction using test data is unexpected; authors should explain rationale despite rapid convergence.  
- Clarify tie-breaking for equal PageRank scores and analyze whether ties affect performance.  
- Constraints (head rules, directionality, distance) likely limit performance; provide oracle results per constraint to show performance loss.  
- Minor wording edit: change “we obtain [the] rank” in Section 4.1 for clarity.  
- Minor formatting: Tables 5 and 7 use different column orders—consider making them consistent (Table 7 order preferred).  
- Minor wording: revise Section 6.1 to “isolate the [contribution] of both.”
