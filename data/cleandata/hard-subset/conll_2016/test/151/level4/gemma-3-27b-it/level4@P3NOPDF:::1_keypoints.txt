- Introduces a deterministic dependency parsing algorithm using POS-based head rules, a slightly biased PageRank to rank words, and iterative nearest-head linking that preserves tree structure and directionality.

- Paper is well-written but the contribution seems incremental and similar to Søgaard (2012); explicitly state differences/improvements over Søgaard to strengthen impact.

- Analyze head-rule interaction with corpora: quantify how often the rules yield disconnected graphs and the typical sizes of disconnected components.

- Explain the choice to determine head direction using test data (despite rapid convergence in 10–15 sentences) and justify why training/dev data were not used.

- Specify how ties in PageRank scores are broken and assess whether tie-breaking affects performance.

- Report oracle/upper-bound results by relaxing each constraint (head rules, directionality, distance) individually to measure how each limits performance and guide future work.

- Minor edits: add “the” in Section 4.1; make column ordering consistent between Tables 5 and 7 (use Table 7’s order); replace “both” with “individual” (or similar) in Section 6.1.
