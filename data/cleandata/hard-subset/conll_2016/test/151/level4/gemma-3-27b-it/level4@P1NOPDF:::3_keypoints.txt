- Proposes parsing Universal Dependency treebanks using only POS tags with a modified PageRank algorithm.  
- Approach is closely related to Søgaard (2012); the advancement appears incremental.  
- Introduces a personalization factor to upweight the main predicate, which is a useful idea but underdeveloped.  
- Personalization is applied only to the main predicate and the chosen weight of 5 appears arbitrary.  
- Evaluation lacks charts/visualizations, making it hard to extract insights from the tables.  
- Missing POS tagging scores in prediction mode; needed to assess whether parsing performance drops due to tagging errors.  
- Overall interesting work but offers only incremental improvement over prior art.  
- Minor issue: Line 207 typo “The the main idea” → “The main idea.”
