- Introduces UDP, a deterministic, unsupervised dependency parser using linguistically motivated head rules and personalized PageRank, operating without training data.  
- Main contribution: a competitive unsupervised parser adapted to UD that estimates adposition attachment direction on test data and needs no language-specific parameters.  
- Secondary contribution: comprehensive empirical evaluation across 32 languages and multiple domains, with comparisons to a strong delexicalized transfer baseline.  
- Method is clearly and thoroughly described, facilitating reproducibility (rules, PageRank setup, decoding).  

Strengths:
- Competitive performance: UDP approaches or surpasses delexicalized transfer (MSDG) on many languages despite being unsupervised.  
- Robustness to domain shift: shows lower variance and better stability across domains than MSD.  
- Comprehensive evaluation: includes gold and predicted POS settings, POS-quality analysis, and detailed breakdowns.  
- Clear methodology: detailed algorithm description, head rules, and figures aid reproducibility.  

Weaknesses:
- Limited novelty: components (PageRank, rule-based parsing, UD constraints) are not individually novel and need clearer differentiation from prior work (e.g., Søgaard 2012).  
- Insufficient analysis of head rules: origins, justification, application frequency, error impact, and coverage (e.g., disconnected graphs) are under-analyzed.  
- Undescribed tie-breaking: tie-breaking for equal PageRank scores is not specified and its effect on performance is unclear.  
- Missing oracle bounds: no oracle experiments quantifying performance limits imposed by rules, directionality, or distance constraints.  

Questions for authors:
- How were the head rules in Table 1 selected and conflicts resolved; what criteria guided inclusion?  
- What tie-breaking mechanism is used for identical PageRank scores and how much does it affect results?  
- Can you provide oracle results for each constraint (head rules, directionality, distance) to quantify their impact?  
- What details and findings motivate the 5x personalization weight for the estimated main predicate in PageRank?
