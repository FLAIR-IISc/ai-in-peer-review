This paper presents a method for parsing dependency trees – specifically, Universal Dependency Treebanks – by relying solely on part-of-speech (POS) tags and employing a modified PageRank algorithm. This approach is interesting, although quite closely related to the work presented in Søgaard (2012). The personalization factor, which assigns greater weight to the main predicate, is a valuable addition, but could have been further developed. 

As I understand it, personalization is applied only to the main predicate, and the assigned weight of 5 appears arbitrary. 

Concerning the evaluation and detailed analyses, the inclusion of some charts would have been helpful, as extracting key insights from the tables can be challenging at times. Furthermore, it would have been informative to see the POS tagging scores in prediction mode. This would allow an assessment of whether the decrease in parsing performance is strongly correlated with a decrease in tagging performance, as one might expect.

Overall, the paper is interesting, but the advancement over Søgaard (2012) is incremental.
