This paper introduces a novel, training-free unsupervised algorithm for Universal Dependencies parsing. The approach leverages PageRank applied to words, supplemented by a limited set of manually defined rules, to achieve dependency tagging. 

The manuscript is exceptionally well-written and provides a thorough explanation of the methodology, with clear justification for the integration of each component.

I assess the contribution to be significant within the area of unsupervised parsing. Furthermore, the authors identify promising avenues for future research, potentially stimulating further investigation in this domain.
