This paper presents an innovative unsupervised algorithm for Universal Dependency parsing that operates without requiring any training data. The core of the approach leverages PageRank to determine word-level tags, supplemented by a limited set of manually defined rules.

The paper is exceptionally well-written and provides a comprehensive explanation of the methodology. The authors clearly articulate the rationale behind incorporating prior linguistic knowledge into the model, enhancing understanding and reproducibility. The level of detail is commendable.

The contribution of this work is significant to the field of unsupervised parsing. By demonstrating effective parsing capabilities without reliance on annotated corpora, the authors address a critical limitation of existing techniques. Furthermore, the authors thoughtfully outline promising avenues for future research, stimulating potential extensions and investigations within the community. The presented work opens up interesting possibilities and will likely inspire further exploration in this area.
