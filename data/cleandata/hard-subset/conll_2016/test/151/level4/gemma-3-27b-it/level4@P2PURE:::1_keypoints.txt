- Introduces a deterministic, rule-based dependency parser using POS-based head rules and a biased PageRank ranking to iteratively attach words to nearest compatible heads while enforcing tree structure and directionality constraints.  
- Paper is well-written and interesting but represents an incremental advance over Sogaard (2012).  
- Head rules (Table 1) need analysis on the corpora: how often they produce disconnected graphs and typical sizes of resulting components.  
- Unexpected choice to determine head directionality using the test data instead of dedicated training/dev data, even if convergence is rapid (10–15 sentences).  
- Clarify tie-breaking for identical PageRank scores and report whether ties affect overall parsing performance.  
- The imposed constraints (head rules, directionality, distance) inherently cap performance; provide oracle results per constraint to quantify their individual performance penalties.  
- Minor editorial/finality fixes: rephrase “we obtain [the] rank” in §4.1; unify column ordering between Tables 5 and 7 (prefer Table 7 order); reword “isolate the [contribution] of both” in §6.1.
