The authors present an unsupervised algorithm for Universal Dependencies that eliminates the need for training data. Their approach leverages PageRank for word tagging, supplemented by a limited set of manually defined rules. 

The article is exceptionally well-written and detailed, and the rationale for incorporating each element of prior knowledge into the model is clearly articulated.

I believe the contribution to be significant within the area of unsupervised parsing, and the authors’ suggestions for future research offer promising avenues for further investigation.
