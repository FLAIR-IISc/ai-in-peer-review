The authors propose an unsupervised algorithm for Universal Dependencies that requires no training data. The approach leverages PageRank for word tagging, supplemented by a small set of hard-coded rules.

This article is well written and highly detailed; the intuition behind incorporating prior linguistic information into the model is explained clearly. 

I believe the contribution to be substantial within the field of unsupervised parsing, and the authors’ suggestions for future work offer promising avenues for further research.
