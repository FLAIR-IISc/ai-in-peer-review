- Introduces a deterministic dependency parser that uses POS-based head rules, a biased PageRank to rank words, and a greedy linking strategy to build trees.
- Paper is interesting and clearly presented but seems only marginally distinct from Søgaard (2012).
- Request quantitative analysis of head rules: how often they produce a disconnected graph and typical sizes of disconnected components.
- Question the use of test data to determine head directionality — this unexpected choice needs justification despite rapid convergence.
- Clarify how PageRank ties are broken and whether tie-breaking materially affects performance.
- Recommend constraint analysis: provide oracle results per constraint (head rules, directionality, distance) to show upper bounds and identify optimization targets.
- Minor editorial fixes: remove brackets in Section 4.1 and 6.1, and standardize column ordering between Tables 5 and 7 (Table 7’s order is clearer).
