- Introduces a deterministic dependency parsing algorithm based on POS-tag head rules.  
- Ranks words using a subtly biased PageRank on a graph whose edges are permitted dependencies.  
- Builds trees by linking each word to the nearest preceding word that preserves tree structure, head rules, and directionality.  
- Paper is well-presented but appears only marginally different from Sogaard (2012).  
- Requests analysis tying head rules to the corpus: how often graphs are disconnected and typical component sizes.  
- Notes surprising design choice of selecting head direction on test data, though the decision converges quickly.  
- Asks how ties in identical PageRank scores are resolved and whether tie-breaking impacts performance.  
- Suggests reporting oracle results per constraint (head rules, directionality, distance) to quantify upper bounds.  
- Minor fixes: wording in Section 4.1, inconsistent column ordering in Tables 5 and 7, and wording in Section 6.1.
