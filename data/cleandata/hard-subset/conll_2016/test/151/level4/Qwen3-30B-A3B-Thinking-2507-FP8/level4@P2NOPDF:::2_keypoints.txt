- Proposes an unsupervised dependency parsing algorithm under Universal Dependencies that requires no training data.  
- Method combines PageRank-based word-level ranking with a small set of hand-crafted linguistic rules.  
- Paper is exceptionally well-written, detailed, and clear in presenting the method.  
- All incorporated linguistic knowledge and design choices are thoroughly justified.  
- Offers a novel, principled approach that achieves meaningful unsupervised parsing results.  
- Authors provide concrete future research directions and extensions, including cross-lingual applications.  
- Framework shows clear potential to advance unsupervised parsing in resource-constrained settings.
