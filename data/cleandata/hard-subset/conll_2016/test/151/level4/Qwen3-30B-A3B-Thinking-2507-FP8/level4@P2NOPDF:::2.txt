

The authors present an unsupervised algorithm for dependency parsing under the Universal Dependencies framework, requiring no training data. The approach leverages PageRank for word-level ranking alongside a minimal set of hand-crafted linguistic rules.  

The paper is exceptionally well-written and meticulously detailed, with clear explanations for all incorporated linguistic knowledge. The rationale behind each design choice is thoroughly justified, enhancing the accessibility of the method for readers.  

This work constitutes a significant contribution to unsupervised parsing research, offering a novel and principled approach that achieves meaningful results without supervision. The authors’ discussion of future research directions further strengthens the paper’s impact, highlighting concrete pathways for extending the methodology to broader linguistic phenomena and cross-lingual settings. The proposed framework demonstrates clear potential to advance unsupervised parsing beyond current limitations, particularly in resource-constrained scenarios.
