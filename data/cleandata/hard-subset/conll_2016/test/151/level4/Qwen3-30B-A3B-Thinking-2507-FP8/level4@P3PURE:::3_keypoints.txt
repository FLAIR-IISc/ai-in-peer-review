- Proposes POS-only dependency parsing using a modified PageRank that upweights meaningful words vs. stop words.
- Idea is interesting but very close to Søgaard (2012); overall contribution is modest.
- Personalization that prioritizes the main predicate is a thoughtful addition but underexplored.
- Personalization appears applied only to the main predicate with a fixed weight of 5, which seems arbitrary.
- Evaluation lacks supplementary charts; tables alone make key insights hard to discern.
- Should report POS tagging scores in prediction mode to assess correlation between tagging and parsing degradation.
- Minor issue: typo at l.207 "The the main idea" → "The main idea".
