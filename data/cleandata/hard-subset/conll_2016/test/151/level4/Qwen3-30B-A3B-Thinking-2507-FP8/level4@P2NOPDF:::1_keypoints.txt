- Introduces a deterministic dependency parser using POS-based head rules, biased PageRank, and sequential linking; presentation is clear but novelty is incremental and overlaps Sogaard (2012).

- Lacks corpus analysis of head rules (Table 1): needs frequency of disconnected graphs and sizes of disconnected components across languages.

- Unusual methodological choice to determine head directionality from test data; requires justification despite quick convergence.

- Does not specify tie-breaking for PageRank scores; must clarify mechanism and its impact on accuracy and reproducibility.

- Should include oracle analyses showing maximum achievable score under each constraint (head rules, directionality, distance) to quantify performance ceilings.

- Minor corrections: remove "[the]" in Section 4.1, align column ordering between Table 5 and Table 7, and fix phrasing in Section 6.1 ("isolate the contribution of both").

- Overall assessment: solid algorithmic core but limited novelty and insufficient analysis of constraint impacts and rule behavior; deeper evaluation would strengthen the paper.
