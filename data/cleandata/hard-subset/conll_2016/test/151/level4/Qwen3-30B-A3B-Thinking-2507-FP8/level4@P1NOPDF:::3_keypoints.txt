- Proposes a POS-only dependency parsing method using a modified PageRank that weights content words over stop words.  
- Core idea closely resembles Søgaard (2012), making the contribution feel incremental.  
- Personalization factor that prioritizes the main predicate is a thoughtful addition but is applied narrowly.  
- The personalization weight (set to 5) is arbitrary and unexplored; no justification or sensitivity analysis is provided.  
- Extending personalization beyond the main predicate would strengthen the method.  
- Evaluation tables are clear but need visualizations (e.g., error breakdowns, category-wise comparisons) for easier insight.  
- Missing POS tagging accuracy under the prediction setup; reporting it is necessary to assess pipeline dependency and parsing drop.  
- Overall feasible approach but does not substantially advance state of the art given limited novelty and missing analyses.  
- Minor issue: typo at l.207 ("The the main idea" → "The main idea").
