- Proposes parsing universal dependency treebanks using only POS tags with a modified PageRank that weights meaningful words over stop words.
- Idea is interesting but very close to Søgaard (2012), so the contribution is incremental.
- Personalization that prioritizes the main predicate is a positive addition but appears applied only to the main predicate.
- The personalization weight (value 5) appears arbitrary and unjustified.
- The approach should be extended further beyond just main-predicate personalization.
- Evaluation and analysis are weak—supplementary charts and more detailed analysis are needed because tables alone are hard to interpret.
- Should report POS tagging scores in prediction mode to verify whether parsing decline correlates with tagging decline.
- Minor issue: typo at l. 207 ("The the main idea" → "The main idea").
