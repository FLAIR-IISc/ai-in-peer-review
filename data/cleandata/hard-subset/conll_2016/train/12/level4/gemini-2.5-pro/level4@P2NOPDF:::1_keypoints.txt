- Recommends acceptance; views the paper as a valuable contribution.  
- Previous experimental flaw (noted in ACL 2016 short submission) has been thoroughly addressed.  
- Core methodology is sound, but framing of the contribution could be improved.  
- Experimental setup is somewhat idealized and doesn’t account for practical overheads of discovering, recruiting, and training annotators in truly low-resource languages.  
- Strongly suggests adding a discussion of this limitation to avoid overstating applicability in the hardest low-resource scenarios.  
- Related work section should better connect to annotation projection literature, specifically Johannsen et al. (ACL 2016) and Agic et al. (ACL 2015).  
- Final version should state whether data, models, and code will be publicly released, as the reviewer’s positive evaluation assumes their availability.
