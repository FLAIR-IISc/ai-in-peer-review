- Authors fixed a major experimental flaw from an earlier version, making the evaluation methodologically sound.  
- The paper presents a novel, well-motivated approach that shows solid empirical evidence of effectiveness under the paper's assumptions.  
- Main limitation: the experimental setup assumes cost is primarily annotator payment, which may be unrealistic for truly low-resource languages where finding qualified speakers is the bottleneck.  
- Recommend the authors explicitly discuss this limitation to avoid overselling practical applicability.  
- Related work section should include and discuss recent cross-lingual/annotation-projection studies (e.g., Johannsen et al. 2016, Agic et al. 2015).  
- Authors should clarify whether data, resources, and code will be publicly released to ensure reproducibility.  
- Overall recommendation: accept, pending minor revisions addressing framing, related work, and data/code availability.
