- Strong recommendation to accept the paper for CONLL 2016; previous experimental flaw has been corrected.  
- The corrected setup resolves a prior issue flagged in the ACL 2016 short-paper review.  
- Concern that the cost model (cost of finding annotators vs paying them) may not reflect real constraints in truly low-resource languages.  
- Paper should acknowledge this limitation and avoid overclaiming applicability to all low-resource scenarios.  
- Related work section is incomplete; should discuss annotation projection studies (e.g., Johannsen et al. 2016, Agic et al. 2015) for better context.  
- Overall assessment: well-executed, insightful study with quality research warranting acceptance.  
- Request explicit statement on whether the datasets will be publicly released for reproducibility.
