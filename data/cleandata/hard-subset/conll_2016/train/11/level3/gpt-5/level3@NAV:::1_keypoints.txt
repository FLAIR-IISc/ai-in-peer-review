Summary: Revisits “main concept” resolution in Wikipedia, annotates 30 articles (WCR), trains a binary classifier to detect MC mentions, and shows integrating it into Dcoref improves Wikipedia coreference.

Main contributions:
- Operationalization and public release of a WCR testbed and protocol for MC resolution.
- Empirical finding that a simple binary classifier using Wikipedia markup/Freebase significantly improves MC detection and boosts end-to-end CR when integrated into Dcoref.
- Practical framing of MC as a discourse anchor that can bridge coreference resolution and entity linking.

Strengths:
- Targets mentions most relevant to IE (biographies, dossiers), offering a principled discourse anchor where generic CR struggles.
- Strong empirical gains: ~89 F1 on MC detection, +13–15 F1 over baselines, and ~+4 CoNLL F1 when added to Dcoref.
- Effective, high-quality signals (links, redirects, Freebase) with informative ablation studies.
- Resource contribution: WCR annotations provide a needed evaluation bed for Wikipedia CR research.

Weaknesses:
- Limited methodological novelty: primarily a binary classifier with standard features; model should be stated earlier.
- Small dataset (30 documents) risks overfitting/topic leakage; no cross-genre or larger-scale validation.
- Baselines/comparability issues: only two CR systems analyzed; need clarity on tuning and possible asymmetric heuristics (e.g., pronoun expansion).
- Clarity/organization problems: dataset description too late, missing explicit citations/definitions, and unclear propagation of hyperlink attributes and mention unions across sections.

Questions to authors:
- Precisely define the “candidate list” and the rule/filtering by which hyperlink targets assign attributes to unmatched surface mentions.
- What is the exact composition of the final mention set in Section 6.1—how many mentions per fold come from WCR vs Raghunathan et al.’s detector?
- Do MC title/alias features risk trivial matches or train/test leakage, and how do you prevent contamination when building MC variants from markup across CV folds?
