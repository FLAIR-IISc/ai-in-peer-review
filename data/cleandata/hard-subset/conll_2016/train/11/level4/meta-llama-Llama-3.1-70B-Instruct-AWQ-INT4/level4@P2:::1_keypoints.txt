- New task: Main Concept Resolution for Wikipedia — identify the coreference chain for the article’s primary entity.  
- Data: 30 Wikipedia articles annotated with all coreference chains; ~25% of mentions refer to the main concept.  
- Baselines and model: Simple baselines plus a basic classifier that outperforms them.  
- Integration: Classifier integrated into Stanford rule-based coreference system, yielding substantial improvements over prior state-of-the-art on Wikipedia.  
- Contribution: Proposes a motivating, information-extraction–oriented variant of coreference resolution.  
- Contribution: Develops a classifier for main concept resolution that complements existing coreference systems.
