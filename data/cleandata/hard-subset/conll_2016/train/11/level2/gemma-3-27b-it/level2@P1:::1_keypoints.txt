- Contribution — Dataset/Testbed: Introduces the WCR dataset (30 docs) with OntoNotes-style annotations and promises public release.  
- Contribution — Feature Engineering: Demonstrates effective feature design combining Wikipedia markup, Freebase, and traditional coreference features with clear ablation studies.  
- Contribution — Improved Performance: Significantly outperforms baselines on main concept resolution and modestly improves a rule-based system (Dcoref++).  
- Strength: Clear Problem Definition: Precisely defines and motivates the main concept resolution task for Wikipedia.  
- Strength: Thorough Experimental Evaluation: Comprehensive comparisons and ablation studies support the method’s effectiveness.  
- Strength: Good Use of External Knowledge: Clever integration of Wikipedia links and Freebase information.  
- Strength: Practical Application: Integration into Dcoref++ shows real-world impact potential.  
- Strength: Detailed Feature Description: Features are well-documented and organized.  
- Weakness: Dataset Size: WCR’s small size limits generalizability; larger, more diverse data needed.  
- Weakness: Baseline Selection: Needs comparisons to more recent or specialized coreference systems beyond news-trained models.  
- Weakness: Limited Error Analysis: Error analysis is sparse; deeper investigation (e.g., failure cases like Figure 3(g)) is needed.  
- Weakness: Over-reliance on Freebase: Performance depends heavily on Freebase quality/coverage; mitigation strategies are missing.  
- Weakness: Presentation of Dcoref++ Results: Table 5 is hard to interpret without separate Dcoref and Dcoref++ scores.  
- Question: Annotation Quality: What procedures ensured annotation quality and consistency in WCR?  
- Question: Freebase Sensitivity: How sensitive is classifier performance to Freebase quality, and how handle missing/inaccurate data?  
- Question: Error Analysis Request: Can you provide a more detailed error analysis and taxonomy of failure modes?  
- Question: Robustness to Wikipedia Variation: How robust is the method to variations in Wikipedia formatting or structure?  
- Question: Data Release Details: When and where will the dataset and MC mention dump be made available?  
- Additional: Writing Quality: The paper is generally clear and well-organized.  
- Additional: Related Work: Expand related work to include recent knowledge-graph embedding advances and their relevance.  
- Additional: Citation Style: Check citation style for consistency.
