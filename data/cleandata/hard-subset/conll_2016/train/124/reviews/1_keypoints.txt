- Proposes using eye-movement (cognitive) features for sentiment analysis and sarcasm detection, claiming first use in NLP.
- Paper is well written and easy to follow.
- Idea is interesting and could inspire work on other NLP tasks.
- Motivation for why cognitive features should help detect sentiment polarity is weak and unclear beyond reading ease.
- Improvement from adding cognitive features is marginal (Sn+Sr+Gz vs Sn+Sr).
- Feasibility discussion (especially example in Section 7.2) is unconvincing and does not demonstrate clear benefit.
