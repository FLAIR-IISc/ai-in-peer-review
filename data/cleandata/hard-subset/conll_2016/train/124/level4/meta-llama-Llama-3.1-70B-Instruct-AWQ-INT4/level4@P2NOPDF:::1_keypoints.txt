- Paper is well-written and accessible to a broad audience.  
- Utilizing cognitive (eye-movement) features is an innovative idea with cross-task potential.  
- Motivation is weak: unclear why cognitive features should specifically help detect sentiment polarities.  
- Empirical gains are marginal (e.g., Sn+Sr+Gz vs. Sn+Sr), raising questions about significance.  
- Feasibility/example in Section 7.2 is unconvincing and needs clearer justification.  
- Overall: interesting concept but requires stronger justification and more convincing results.
