- Integrating eye-tracking features into sentiment analysis as a cognitive feature is a promising idea.  
- The authors' discussion and comparative analysis of feature sets is clear and informative.  
- The feasibility assessment in Section 7 is a valuable contribution.  
- Evaluation should use domain-specific datasets instead of mixing domains (e.g., movie reviews and tweets) to improve predictive power and interpretability.  
- Results in Table 2 are lower than state-of-the-art for the Pang & Lee dataset but more comparable to Twitter data results.  
- Section 3.3 assumes no overlap between training and testing snippets for datasets 1 and 2 despite originating from the same sources (Pang & Lee, Sentiment140).  
- Minor suggestion: reconsider using bold formatting as it can be distracting.
