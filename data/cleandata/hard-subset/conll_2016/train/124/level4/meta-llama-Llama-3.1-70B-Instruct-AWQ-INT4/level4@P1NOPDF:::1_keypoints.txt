- Paper is well-written and easy to follow, making it accessible to a broad audience.
- Using cognitive (eye-movement) features is an interesting idea that could inspire new approaches in other NLP tasks.
- Motivation is weak: unclear why cognitive features that reflect reading ease should help detect sentiment polarity.
- Empirical gains are marginal (Sn+Sr+Gz vs Sn+Sr), raising doubts about the practical benefit of cognitive features.
- Feasibility/example in Section 7.2 is unconvincing and needs clearer justification of practical applicability.
- Overall, the idea is innovative but needs stronger justification and more convincing results to demonstrate effectiveness.
