Paper proposes using cognitive features (annotator eye‑movement patterns) for sentiment analysis and sarcasm detection, claiming first incorporation into NLP.

Paper is well written and easy to follow.

Idea is interesting and could inspire research in other NLP tasks.

Motivation for using cognitive features is weak; unclear why they help detect sentiment polarity beyond indicating reading ease.

Reported performance gains from cognitive features are marginal (Sn+Sr+Gz vs Sn+Sr).

Feasibility/explanation in Section 7 is unconvincing, especially the example in Section 7.2 which does not clarify why the technique is helpful.
