- Introducing eye-tracking features as cognitive signals for sentiment analysis is an intriguing and innovative idea.  
- The feature discussion and comparison of feature sets are clear and informative.  
- Addressing feasibility in Section 7 strengthens the paper’s credibility.  
- Evaluation is weakened by conflating domains (movie reviews and tweets); datasets should be separated for clearer, more effective results.  
- Results in Table 2 look low compared to Pang & Lee state-of-the-art but comparatively better for Twitter data.  
- Clarification needed: are there overlapping snippets between training and test data in datasets 1 and 2 given shared sources?  
- Minor formatting issue: excessive use of bold font is distracting and unnecessary.  
- Overall: promising contribution with clear writing that needs improved evaluation methodology and minor formatting revisions.
