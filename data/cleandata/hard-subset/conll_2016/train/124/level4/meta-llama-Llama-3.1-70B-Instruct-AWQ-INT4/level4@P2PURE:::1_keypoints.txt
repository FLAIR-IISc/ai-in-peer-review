- Paper is well-written and easy to follow.
- Innovative idea: using cognitive (eye-movement) features with potential to inspire other NLP tasks.
- Motivation for using cognitive features for sentiment analysis is not adequately justified; unclear connection to sentiment polarity.
- Empirical improvement from adding cognitive features is marginal (Sn+Sr+Gz vs Sn+Sr).
- Feasibility/example in Section 7.2 is unconvincing and unclear on practical benefits.
