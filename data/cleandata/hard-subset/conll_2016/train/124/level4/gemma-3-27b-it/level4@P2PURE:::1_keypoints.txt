- Novel approach: integrates cognitive features from annotators’ eye movements for sentiment and sarcasm detection; authors claim it's the first such NLP work.
- Paper is generally well-written and clear.
- Core idea is interesting and has potential to spur further NLP research.
- Motivation weak: connection between cognitive/readability cues and sentiment polarity is not well justified.
- Performance gain is marginal (Sn+Sr+Gz vs Sn+Sr shows limited improvement).
- Feasibility concerns persist despite Section 7; the example in Section 7.2 fails to convincingly demonstrate benefits.
