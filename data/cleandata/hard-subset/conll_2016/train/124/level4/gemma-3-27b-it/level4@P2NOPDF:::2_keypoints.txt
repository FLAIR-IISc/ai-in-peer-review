- Paper proposes using eye-tracking as cognitive features for sentiment analysis — novel and promising idea.
- Presentation of features and comparative analysis of feature sets is well-executed and informative.
- Feasibility discussion (Section 7) is realistic and appreciated.
- Dataset composition concern: combining movie reviews and tweets may introduce confounding factors; recommend training/testing within the same domain and separating datasets.
- Results comparison issue: accuracy in Table 2 seems low vs. Pang & Lee but more competitive for Twitter — disentangle dataset-specific comparisons.
- Clarification requested: confirm no overlap between training and testing sets for datasets 1 and 2 given shared sources (e.g., Pang & Lee, Sentiment140).
- Minor stylistic note: reduce frequent use of bold text to improve readability.
