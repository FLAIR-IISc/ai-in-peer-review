- Proposes leveraging cognitive features (eye-movement patterns) for sentiment analysis and sarcasm detection, claiming first integration of cognitive features into the NLP pipeline.
- Paper is generally well-written and easy to follow.
- Core idea is highly interesting and could inspire work on other NLP tasks.
- Motivation for using cognitive features in sentiment analysis is not fully justified; link to sentiment polarity beyond reading ease is unclear.
- Performance improvement from adding cognitive features appears marginal (comparison of Sn+Sr+Gz vs Sn+Sr).
- Feasibility argument is unconvincing; the Section 7.2 example does not clearly demonstrate a tangible benefit or practical usefulness.
