- Novel approach: integrates cognitive features from annotators’ eye-movement for sentiment and sarcasm detection, positioned as a pioneering effort in NLP.

- Paper is clearly written and easy to understand.

- Central idea is highly innovative and could stimulate further research across NLP applications.

- Rationale for using cognitive features in sentiment analysis is underdeveloped; the link to sentiment polarity is unclear.

- Performance gains from cognitive features are modest (small difference between Sn+Sr+Gz and Sn+Sr).

- Feasibility discussion (Section 7) is inadequate; the example in Section 7.2 does not convincingly demonstrate practical utility.
