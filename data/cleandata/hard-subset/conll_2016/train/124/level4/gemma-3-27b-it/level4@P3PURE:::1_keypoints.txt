- Paper is well-written and easy to follow.
- Core idea is novel and could inspire work in other NLP tasks.
- Motivation for using cognitive (eye-movement) features for sentiment detection is weak; link to polarity is unclear.
- Performance improvement from adding cognitive features is marginal (Sn+Sr+Gz vs Sn+Sr).
- Feasibility arguments (Section 7), especially the example in 7.2, are unconvincing and lack persuasive rationale.
