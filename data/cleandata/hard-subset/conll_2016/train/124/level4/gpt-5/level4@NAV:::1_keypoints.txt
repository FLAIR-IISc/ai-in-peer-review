- Leverages cognitive features (eye-movement patterns) for sentiment analysis and sarcasm detection.
- Claims to be the first work to introduce cognitive features into the NLP community.
- Strength: Paper is well written and easy to follow.
- Strength: Idea is intriguing and may inspire research across other NLP tasks.
- Weakness: Motivation for using cognitive features is weak; unclear why reading patterns indicate sentiment polarity.
- Weakness: Performance gain from adding cognitive features is marginal (Sn+Sr+Gz vs Sn+Sr).
- Weakness: Feasibility discussion is unconvincing; Section 7.2 example does not clearly show practical benefit.
