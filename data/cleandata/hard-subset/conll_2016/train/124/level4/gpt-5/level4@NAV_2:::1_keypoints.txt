- Introduces approach of augmenting sentiment analysis and sarcasm detection with annotators’ eye-movement (gaze) features.
- Authors claim this is the first NLP work to incorporate such cognitive signals.
- Manuscript is clearly written and easy to follow.
- Central idea is novel and could stimulate research across other NLP tasks.
- Motivation is weak: link between gaze signals and reliably inferring sentiment polarity is unclear.
- Incremental performance gains from adding gaze features are small.
- Application scenario and feasibility example are unconvincing and lack concrete usefulness.
