- Proposes integrating cognitive features (human eye-movement) into sentiment analysis and sarcasm detection; claims to be first NLP work using such signals.
- Paper is well written and easy to follow.
- Idea is novel and could inspire further NLP research.
- Motivation is weak—connection between cognitive features and sentiment polarity detection is unclear and not convincingly argued.
- Performance gains from adding cognitive features are marginal (comparison of Sn+Sr+Gz vs Sn+Sr).
- Feasibility argument in Section 7 is unconvincing; the example in Section 7.2 does not clearly show practical benefit.
