- Paper introduces cognitive features from annotators' eye-movements for sentiment and sarcasm detection, claiming first use in NLP.
- Strength: The paper is well-written and accessible.
- Strength: The core idea is highly innovative and could inspire further NLP research.
- Weakness: Motivation for using cognitive features is weak; unclear why they specifically aid sentiment polarity detection beyond reflecting reading ease.
- Weakness: Performance gains from adding cognitive features (Sn+Sr+Gz vs Sn+Sr) are marginal.
- Weakness: Feasibility arguments in Section 7 are unconvincing, particularly the example in Section 7.2, so the practical utility remains unclear.
