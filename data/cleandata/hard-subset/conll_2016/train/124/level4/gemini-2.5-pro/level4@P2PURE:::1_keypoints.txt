- Paper is clearly written, well-structured, and easy to follow.
- Leveraging cognitive signals (eye-movement) is an innovative idea that could inspire new NLP research.
- Motivation for using cognitive features is weak; the paper does not clearly link eye-movement patterns to sentiment polarity.
- Empirical gains from adding cognitive features are marginal (comparison of Sn+Sr vs Sn+Sr+Gz).
- Practical feasibility discussion (Section 7), particularly the example in Section 7.2, is unconvincing and fails to demonstrate utility.
