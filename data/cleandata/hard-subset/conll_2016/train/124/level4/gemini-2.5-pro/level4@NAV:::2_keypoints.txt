- Introduces eye-tracking data as a cognitive feature for sentiment analysis; idea is interesting and novel.
- Discussion of features and comparative analysis of feature sets is clear and insightful.
- Inclusion of Section 7 on practical feasibility is a commendable strength.
- Evaluation mixes disparate domains (movie reviews and Twitter), which may hurt performance and interpretability.
- Recommend training and testing on domain-specific data to improve predictive performance for each domain.
- Results are low relative to Pang & Lee state-of-the-art but competitive on Twitter, suggesting a domain-mixing effect.
- Clarification needed: confirm no overlapping instances between training and testing for datasets 1 and 2 (same sources).
- Minor suggestion: frequent use of bold formatting is distracting.
