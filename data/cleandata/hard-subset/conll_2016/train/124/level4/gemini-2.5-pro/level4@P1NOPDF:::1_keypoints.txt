- Paper is well-written, clearly structured, and easy to follow.
- Central idea of using eye-movement cognitive features for sentiment and sarcasm detection is novel and potentially inspiring for other NLP tasks.
- Motivation is weak: the paper fails to establish a clear theoretical link between eye-movement patterns and sentiment polarity detection.
- Reported performance gains from adding cognitive features are marginal, questioning practical value given the high data-collection overhead.
- Real-world feasibility discussion (Section 7) is unconvincing, and the example in Section 7.2 does not demonstrate practical utility.
