- Introduces eye-tracking features as a proxy for cognitive load in sentiment analysis; novel and well-motivated.  
- Clear and insightful discussion of proposed features and comparative analysis of feature sets.  
- Section 7 on practical feasibility is a notable strength.  
- Evaluation weakness: datasets conflate domains (movie reviews and tweets); separating domains/in-domain training could improve performance and interpretability.  
- Reported performance is hard to interpret: appears low vs. Pang & Lee movie-review SOTA but more competitive for Twitter—needs in-domain evaluation.  
- Clarification needed on whether training and test splits for datasets 1 and 2 contain overlapping text snippets from the same original sources.  
- Minor issue: excessive use of bold formatting is distracting.
