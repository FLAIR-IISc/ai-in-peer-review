- Introduces using eye‑movement–based cognitive features for sentiment analysis and sarcasm detection.  
- Claims to be the first study incorporating cognitive features into NLP.  
- Strength: well‑structured and easy to follow.  
- Strength: highly innovative idea with potential to inspire further NLP work.  
- Weakness: insufficient justification of why cognitive features should help identify sentiment polarities.  
- Weakness: only minimal performance gain from adding cognitive features (Sn+Sr+Gz vs Sn+Sr).  
- Weakness: feasibility discussion (Section 7) is unconvincing and the example in Section 7.2 fails to demonstrate effectiveness.
