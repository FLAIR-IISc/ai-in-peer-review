- Paper proposes a novel word-embedding method (PoP) using a random projection matrix with positive expected value to enable PPMI and efficient computation.  
- Unclear distinction from prior random projection techniques, making claimed advantages hard to evaluate.  
- Overall arguments and exposition (starting from the introduction) are difficult to follow.  
- Specific statements lack evidence or justification (lines 114, 137, 154 require more support).  
- Paper is not self-contained or accessible to readers familiar with vector space models but not random projections.  
- Numerous English language errors throughout the manuscript.  
- Evaluation is insufficiently comprehensive—method tested only on the MEN dataset instead of multiple standard benchmarks.  
- Recommendation: substantially improve presentation, address concerns, and resubmit to another venue.
