- Proposes PoP: a random projection matrix with positive expected value to enable PPMI and claims computational efficiency.
- Reviewer finds the core differences between PoP and existing random projection techniques unclear, obscuring claimed advantages.
- The introduction and overall argumentation are difficult to follow and lack clarity.
- Several arguments are inadequately supported; transitional sentences (lines 114, 137, 154) lack clear connections to preceding text.
- Paper is not self-contained or accessible to readers with only basic knowledge of vector space modeling/random projections.
- Manuscript contains English errors (e.g., "To a large extend", "such PPMI") that reduce quality.
- Evaluation is limited to the MEN dataset; reviewer recommends including standard benchmarks (WordSim, SimLex) or justifying single-dataset use.
- Recommendation: substantially revise presentation, improve clarity and coherence, fix language and evaluation issues, then resubmit.
