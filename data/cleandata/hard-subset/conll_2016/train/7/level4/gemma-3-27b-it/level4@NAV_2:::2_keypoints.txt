- Introduces PoP: a positive-only random projection matrix with positive expected value that enables post-projection PPMI while keeping random-projection efficiency.  
- Primary contribution: proposes a non-traditional random projection that permits PPMI weighting, bridging efficiency and weighted-model benefits.  
- Secondary contribution: empirical evidence of competitive performance on the MEN relatedness benchmark.  

- Strength — Novelty: combining positive-valued random projection with post-projection PPMI addresses a limitation of standard random projections.  
- Strength — Efficiency: allows scalable, low-dimensional PPMI application versus expensive high-dimensional count-based weighting.  
- Strength — Empirical results: achieves results comparable to state-of-the-art on MEN, showing practical viability.  
- Strength — Clear problem framing: clearly states limitations of existing methods and motivates PoP as a solution.
