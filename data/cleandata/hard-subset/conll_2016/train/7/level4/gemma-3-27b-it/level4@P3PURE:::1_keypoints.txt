- Method is compelling: significantly faster training than neural networks and preserves distributional properties during dimensionality reduction.  
- Rationale for requiring vectors to be transformable with PPMI is not fully convincing.  
- Most critical: paper lacks direct empirical comparison to related work (only descriptive discussion).  
- Authors’ definition of Kendall’s tau is unconventional and does not match the standard formula.  
- Question why Spearman correlation wasn’t used, given its prevalence in semantic tasks and later use by the authors.  
- Evaluation datasets deviate from standard semantic relatedness benchmarks; recommend including established datasets.  
- Figure 1 is incorrect/incomplete: the described third line is missing (only two lines shown).  
- Minor typo: “large extend” should be “large extent.”
