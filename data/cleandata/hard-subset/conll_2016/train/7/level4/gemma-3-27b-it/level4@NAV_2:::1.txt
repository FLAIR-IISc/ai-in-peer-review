I am buying some of the motivation: the proposed method is much faster to train than it is to train a neural network. Also, it preserves some properties of the distribution when reducing dimensionality. 
However, I am not fully convinced of the importance of enabling PPMI transformation; the benefits are not clearly demonstrated.
Most importantly, the paper lacks a direct comparative analysis with related work.
