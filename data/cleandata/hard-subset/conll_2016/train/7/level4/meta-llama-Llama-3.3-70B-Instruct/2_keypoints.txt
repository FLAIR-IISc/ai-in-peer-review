- Paper proposes Positive-only Projection (PoP), a random-projection-based word embedding claiming positive expected value enables PPMI and efficiency.  
- Unclear distinction from existing random projection methods and insufficient justification of claimed advantages.  
- Overall argumentation and paper structure (starting from the introduction) are hard to follow.  
- Several specific claims lack supporting evidence (lines cited: ~114, ~137, ~154).  
- Paper is not self-contained for readers familiar with VSMs but not expert in random projections.  
- Numerous English language errors reduce clarity (examples given).  
- Evaluation is insufficiently narrow—only tested on the MEN dataset despite standard benchmarks like WordSim and SimLex.  
- Recommendation: substantially improve presentation and clarity and resubmit after broader evaluation.
