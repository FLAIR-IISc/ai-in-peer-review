I find some of the motivation persuasive: the proposed method trains much faster than neural networks and preserves certain distributional properties under dimensionality reduction. However, I am not convinced that it is important for the vectors to be transformable via PPMI. Most importantly, there is no direct comparison to related work.

Detailed comments:
- p.3: The definition of Kendall’s tau used here is unusual. It is not the standard/original formula; I am unsure what variant this is and where it comes from.
- p.3: Why not use Spearman’s rank correlation, which is standard in semantic tasks (and which the authors use at evaluation time)?
- The evaluation datasets are not the standard benchmarks for semantic relatedness commonly used in the NLP community. It is fine to include additional datasets, but please also report results on the standard ones.
- I can only see two lines in Figure 1. Where is the third line?
- There is no direct comparison to related work; the manuscript only provides a statement without empirical comparison.
