- Proposal: Introduces a positive-only projection (PoP) random projection for word embeddings with a random matrix having positive expectation, claiming it enables PPMI use and is computationally efficient.  
- Main concern: Overall clarity of the paper is poor.  
- Unclear novelty: Core distinction from prior random projection methods is not explained, preventing assessment of claimed benefits.  
- Weak presentation: Argumentation is hard to follow starting from the introduction.  
- Unsupported claims: Several assertions lack adequate support (flags at lines 114, 137, 154).  
- Accessibility: Paper is not self-contained for readers unfamiliar with random projections; rationale for the direction isn’t made clear.  
- Language issues: Numerous English errors throughout (examples at lines 86 and 142).  
- Evaluation limited: Experiments only use the MEN benchmark; broader standard evaluation or justification is required.  
- Recommendation: Significantly improve presentation and resubmit to another conference.
