- Positive: Method appears much faster to train than neural networks and seems to preserve certain distributional properties under dimensionality reduction.
- Concern: Unclear why requiring vectors to be transformable with PPMI is important or justified.
- Major flaw: Paper lacks a direct, quantitative comparison to related work—no empirical evidence against baselines.
- Methodology: The paper uses an unusual definition of Kendall’s tau; the origin and justification must be clarified.
- Metric choice: Why not use Spearman correlation, a standard semantic-evaluation metric (and one the authors use elsewhere)?
- Evaluation: Datasets are non-standard for semantic relatedness; include standard NLP benchmarks in results.
- Figure issue: Figure 1 only shows two lines while a third line is expected—please explain or fix.
- Typo: “large extend” should be “large extent.”
