- Positive: Method trains much faster than neural networks while preserving key distributional properties in dimensionality reduction.  
- Unclear why PPMI vector transformation is necessary; authors do not convincingly justify its critical role.  
- Missing direct comparisons to relevant prior methods; claiming "related work is less effective" without benchmarks weakens novelty/advantage claims.  
- Kendall’s tau definition is unconventional and diverges from standard formulation; origin and rationale need clarification.  
- Choice of Kendall over Spearman is unjustified; Spearman is the conventional metric for semantic relatedness and is used elsewhere in the paper.  
- Evaluation datasets are nonstandard; results on established benchmarks (e.g., WordSim-353, SimLex-999) are needed for meaningful comparison.  
- Figure 1 is incorrect: only two lines are visible though a third is referenced in the caption.  
- Typo: "large extend" should be "large extent" (page 3).
