- Proposes Positive-only Projection (PoP), a random projection with positive expected value intended to enable PPMI and retain random-projection efficiency.  
- Core novelty unclear: paper does not clearly differentiate PoP from prior random projection methods, so claimed advantages are hard to assess.  
- Poor logical flow starting from the introduction, hindering comprehension.  
- Several key claims lack supporting evidence or justification (e.g., problematic uses of "in addition", "Since", and "thus" at Lines 114, 137, 154).  
- Fails to provide a self-contained explanation of the research rationale for readers familiar with vector space modeling.  
- Contains multiple English language errors that reduce readability (examples at Lines 86 and 142).  
- Evaluation is inadequate: only the MEN benchmark is used; standard practice requires multiple datasets (e.g., WordSim, SimLex) and omission undermines validity.  
- Recommendation: major revisions to clarity, justification, and presentation before resubmission to a different venue.
