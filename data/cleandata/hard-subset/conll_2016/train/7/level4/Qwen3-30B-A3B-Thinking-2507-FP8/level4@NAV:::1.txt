

The proposed method demonstrates notable advantages in training speed compared to neural network approaches and effectively preserves certain distributional properties during dimensionality reduction. However, the necessity of transforming vectors via PPMI is not convincingly justified. Crucially, the manuscript lacks direct comparisons against relevant prior work.  

Detailed comments:  
- p.3: The definition of Kendall's tau employed deviates from the standard formulation; its origin and rationale require clarification.  
- p.3: Spearman correlation is conventionally used for semantic tasks (and employed in evaluation), making its absence in the definition rationale unclear.  
- The evaluation datasets do not align with standard benchmarks for semantic relatedness in NLP; inclusion of established benchmarks is recommended alongside the chosen sets.  
- Figure 1 displays only two lines; the third line referenced in the text is not visible.  
- No direct comparison to related work is provided, despite the claim that the method outperforms existing approaches.
