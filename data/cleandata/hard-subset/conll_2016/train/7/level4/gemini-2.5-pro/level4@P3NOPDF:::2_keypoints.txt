- Proposes Positive-only Projection (PoP), a random-projection-based word embedding with a non-negative expected projection matrix to enable PPMI and keep computational efficiency.
- Novelty and motivation are unclear: the paper does not clearly differentiate PoP from existing random projections or explain why non-negative expectation justifies PPMI.
- Manuscript is hard to follow and not self-contained, assuming specialist knowledge and lacking clear rationale and explanations.
- Experimental evaluation is insufficient: only evaluated on MEN, missing standard benchmarks (e.g., WordSim-353, SimLex-999, SimVerb-3500) and comparisons to relevant baselines/SOTA.
- Contains unsupported arguments and logical gaps where conclusions/transitions are not justified by prior text.
- Writing and presentation suffer from numerous grammatical errors and awkward phrasing, hurting readability.
- Recommendation: reject in current form; requires major revision to improve clarity, provide rigorous theoretical justification, and perform comprehensive empirical evaluation.
