- Proposal: Introduces Positive-only Projection (PoP), a random projection for word embeddings using a positive-expected-value projection matrix to enable PPMI while claiming retained computational efficiency.

- Main reservation: Poor clarity of the paper prevents proper assessment of its contributions.

- Novelty unclear: Fails to clearly distinguish PoP from prior random projection methods and to explain how positive expectation enables PPMI.

- Presentation inaccessible: Assumes specialized knowledge and is not reasonably self-contained for general vector-space researchers.

- Unsupported claims: Several conclusions are stated without sufficient justification or logical support.

- Weak evaluation: Experiments are limited to the MEN dataset; lacks evaluation on standard benchmarks (e.g., WordSim-353, SimLex-999) to show robustness.

- Language issues: Numerous grammar and awkward phrasing errors reduce readability and professionalism.

- Recommendation: Reject; authors should substantially revise clarity, motivation, and experimental rigor before resubmission.
