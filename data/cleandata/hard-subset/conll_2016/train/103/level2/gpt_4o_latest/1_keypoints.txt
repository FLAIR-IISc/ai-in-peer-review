- Introduces TBuckets: group topic words into thematic "buckets" where a single large bucket indicates high topic coherence.  
- Proposes three bucket-creation techniques: TBuckets-Clustering, TBuckets-SVD, and TBuckets-SVD-Reorg.  
- TBuckets-SVD and TBuckets-SVD-Reorg use SVD to identify sub-themes; Reorg further improves bucket coherence.  
- Evaluated on three public datasets and shows superior correlation with human-annotated coherence scores.  
- Demonstrates TBuckets improves weakly supervised text classification by selecting high-quality topics.  
- Contribution: parameter-free SVD-based coherence measures (TBuckets-SVD and -Reorg) that outperform state-of-the-art on two datasets.  
- Contribution: practical utility in weakly supervised classification by boosting accuracy via topic selection.  
- Contribution: detailed analysis of limitations of existing coherence measures and emphasis on TBuckets' interpretability and efficiency.  
- Strength: Novel thematic grouping and SVD-based approach, with an innovative reorganization step.  
- Strength: Thorough empirical validation across datasets and advantage of being parameter-free.  
- Strength: Practical relevance shown through improved classification accuracy using high-quality topics.  
- Strength: Clear methodological and mathematical exposition that aids reproducibility.  
- Weakness: TBuckets-Clustering offers limited novelty, relying on agglomerative clustering with a single parameter.  
- Weakness: Domain-specific limitations (e.g., Genomics) noted but no proposed solutions or experiments.  
- Weakness: Evaluation focuses mainly on Pearson correlation; lacks qualitative bucket analysis and comparisons like NPMI.  
- Weakness: Scalability of SVD-based methods for large or high-dimensional datasets is not addressed.  
- Question: How does the computational cost of TBuckets-SVD and TBuckets-SVD-Reorg compare to state-of-the-art, especially on large datasets?  
- Question: Have alternative word similarity measures (e.g., contextual embeddings like BERT) been considered for domain-specific datasets?  
- Question: Can TBuckets be extended to multilingual or cross-lingual topic models, and what challenges are expected?  
- Recommendation: Recommend acceptance, with a suggestion to address scalability and domain-specific adaptations to strengthen the work.
