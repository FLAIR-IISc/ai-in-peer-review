1. Proposes TBuckets: grouping topic words into thematic clusters using embeddings and dimensionality reduction to evaluate topic quality.  
2. Evaluates TBuckets across 20NG, NYT, and Genomics datasets to assess generalizability and compare to state-of-the-art.  
3. Demonstrates TBuckets’ utility for topic selection in weakly supervised text classification.  
4. Novel methodology of forming word “buckets” from embeddings to assess thematic coherence.  
5. TBuckets-SVD-Reorg outperforms prior methods on NYT and 20NG datasets.  
6. SVD-based variants are presented as parameter-free, reducing tuning effort.  
7. Shows practical downstream benefit by improving weakly supervised classification.  
8. Experimental setup is thorough and compares to relevant baselines for reproducibility.  
9. Performance is inconsistent across datasets, raising robustness and generalizability concerns.  
10. Poor results on Genomics dataset highlight a major weakness and lack of robustness.  
11. Authors fail to analyze the impact of high out-of-vocabulary (OOV) terms on Genomics results.  
12. No guidance on when to choose clustering vs. SVD vs. SVD-Reorg variants under different conditions.  
13. Treatment of word embeddings is oversimplified; normalization and cosine similarity issues need clarification.  
14. Claim of “no parameters” for SVD methods is misleading because choice of pre-trained embeddings is effectively a parameter.  
15. Relevant prior work on lexical chaining is missing and should be cited/discussed.  
16. Presentation issues: Figure 1 is too small to read and assess Max Dist effects for TBuckets-Clustering.  
17. Question: How are OOV terms handled with pre-trained embeddings, and how does that affect results (esp. Genomics)?  
18. Question: Why does TBuckets perform poorly on Genomics—are OOV terms the primary cause and how to mitigate?  
19. Question: Please clarify embedding normalization—are embeddings unit length and how does that affect cosine similarity?  
20. Question: How does TBuckets relate to and differ from lexical chaining approaches?  
21. Question: What are the computational costs of each TBuckets variant (clustering, SVD, SVD-Reorg)?  
22. Suggestion: Provide clearer notation/explanation for coherence measure formulas (CUMASS, CUSheffield).  
23. Suggestion: Include analysis of topic types where TBuckets succeeds versus where it struggles.
