- Paper proposes TBuckets: grouping topic words into semantic "buckets" using word embeddings to measure topic coherence (coherent topics → one large bucket; incoherent → many small buckets).  
- Explores three bucket-creation methods: clustering-based, SVD-based, and SVD-based with a reorganization step.  
- Evaluated on three public datasets and shown effective for weakly supervised text classification, matching or exceeding prior methods (e.g., Röder et al. 2015).  
- Main contribution: TBuckets as a new, intuitive semantic coherence metric that leverages modern embeddings rather than co-occurrence or knowledge resources.  
- Secondary contribution: SVD-based TBuckets variants (especially with reorganization) provide a parameter-free alternative useful in practice.  
- Empirical validation across datasets and a downstream task supports TBuckets' practical effectiveness.  
- Strength: Novel shift from statistical co-occurrence to embedding-based semantic grouping for topic evaluation.  
- Strength: Strong empirical performance, often competitive with or better than state-of-the-art metrics.  
- Strength: Parameter-free SVD methods reduce hyperparameter tuning and ease deployment.  
- Strength: Demonstrated usefulness in a downstream weakly supervised classification task, not just intrinsic evaluation.  
- Strength: Clear motivation and framing around topic quality and interpretability.  
- Weakness: Lacks theoretical justification for why embedding-based word grouping reliably reflects topic coherence.  
- Weakness: Potential contextual mismatch—pretrained sequential-context embeddings may not capture document-level topic relationships.  
- Weakness: Section 3 explaining methods is convoluted and poorly structured; needs clearer subsections and active voice.  
- Weakness: Reorganization step is described verbosely with unnecessary detail about dynamic thresholding; should be streamlined.  
- Weakness: Missing statistical significance tests for reported correlations in Table 1, making performance differences hard to assess.  
- Question: Have statistical significance tests (e.g., paired t-tests) been performed for Table 1 results?  
- Question: How sensitive is TBuckets to the choice of word embeddings (e.g., word2vec vs. FastText)?  
- Question: Can authors elaborate on limitations of using sequential-context embeddings for document-level topics and mitigation strategies?  
- Question: Please provide an intuitive explanation and justification for the dynamic threshold formula in TBuckets-SVD-Reorg.  
- Additional comment: Related work section should be more concise and focused on core contributions.  
- Additional comment: Improve writing quality with proofreading to fix grammatical/stylistic issues.  
- Additional comment: Provide more illustrative examples of coherent vs. incoherent topics to strengthen intuition.
