- Introduces TBuckets, a method that measures topic coherence by grouping top-N topic words into buckets using cosine similarity of pre-trained word embeddings.  
- Implements three variants: TBuckets-Clustering (agglomerative), TBuckets-SVD (parameter-free), and TBuckets-SVD-Reorg (SVD plus a reorganization step).  
- Evaluates methods by Pearson correlation with human judgments on three standard datasets.  
- Reports TBuckets-SVD-Reorg achieves state-of-the-art correlation on two of three datasets (NYT and 20NG).  
- Demonstrates practical utility: using TBuckets to select LDA runs improves weakly supervised text classification performance.  
- Contribution: a simple, intuitive TBuckets framework leveraging embeddings to assess topic coherence.  
- Contribution: SVD-based, parameter-free variants that reduce the need for tuning compared to prior measures.  
- Contribution: strong empirical validation and downstream-task demonstration showing real-world value.  
- Strength: strong empirical performance, competitive or superior to Röder et al. (2015) on benchmarks.  
- Strength: simplicity and lack of parameters make the approach practical and easy to use.  
- Strength: clear, intuitive motivation that coherent topics should group into a single semantic bucket.  
- Strength: inclusion of a downstream classification experiment strengthens practical relevance.  
- Weakness: limited theoretical grounding—method presented as a heuristic without connection to topic-model statistics or cognitive theory.  
- Weakness: potential mismatch between local-window embeddings (GloVe) and document-level co-occurrence used by LDA is not discussed.  
- Weakness: exposition issues—TBuckets-SVD-Reorg description and dynamic threshold rationale are hard to follow; needs pseudocode/clearer steps.  
- Weakness: no statistical significance testing reported to confirm small reported improvements in Pearson r are meaningful.  
- Questions: Have you run statistical tests (e.g., Williams test) to confirm improvements over Röder et al. (2015) are significant?  
- Questions: How sensitive is TBuckets to the choice of embeddings (word2vec, fastText), training corpora, and vector dimensionality?  
- Questions: Please clarify TBuckets-SVD-Reorg with pseudocode and justify the dynamic threshold formulation (AWES − AWPS/AWES).
