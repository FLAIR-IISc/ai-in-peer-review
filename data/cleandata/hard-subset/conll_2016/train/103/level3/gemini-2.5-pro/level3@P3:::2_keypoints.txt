- Proposes TBuckets: partition top-N topic words into embedding-based "buckets" to measure topic coherence.  
- Introduces three bucketing algorithms: greedy clustering, SVD-based, and SVD with a reorganization step (TBuckets-SVD-Reorg).  
- Evaluated by correlating scores with human judgments on three datasets; TBuckets-SVD-Reorg matches/outperforms Röder et al. (2015).  
- Demonstrates downstream utility: selecting high-coherence topic models improves weakly supervised text classification.  
- Contribution: a simple, parameter-free, empirically effective topic coherence metric using pre-trained embeddings.  
- Contribution: validation of metric usefulness via improved performance in a downstream classification task.  
- Strength: strong empirical performance, achieving state-of-the-art correlation on NYT and 20NG and competitive on Genomics.  
- Strength: conceptual simplicity and no parameter tuning make the method practical and easy to adopt.  
- Strength: intuitive core idea—coherent topics concentrate into one large semantic bucket.  
- Strength: practical demonstration that coherence filtering improves labeling and classification accuracy.  
- Weakness: lacks theoretical justification for why embedding-based word partitioning should measure topic quality.  
- Weakness: potential mismatch between embedding (local/contextual) semantics and topic model (document-level aboutness) not explored.  
- Weakness: exposition of TBuckets-SVD-Reorg (especially the dynamic threshold) is unclear and reads ad hoc.  
- Weakness: incomplete experiments—no statistical significance tests reported for Table 1 results.  
- Weakness: evaluation uses only one embedding (300d GloVe); sensitivity to embedding choice and domain-specific embeddings is untested.  
- Questions: please provide statistical significance tests for Table 1 comparisons with Röder et al. (2015).  
- Questions: how sensitive is TBuckets to embedding choice (word2vec/FastText, dimensions, domain-specific embeddings like PubMed)?  
- Questions: clarify motivation and derivation of the dynamic threshold formula in TBuckets-SVD-Reorg and whether alternatives were tried.  
- Questions: comment on the conceptual mismatch between local-context embeddings and document-level topic signals and its implications/limitations.
