- TBuckets: groups topic words into thematic “buckets” and measures topic coherence using properties of the largest bucket.
- Introduces three bucket-construction techniques: TBuckets‑Clustering, TBuckets‑SVD, and TBuckets‑SVD‑Reorg.
- Evaluated on three public datasets and reports better performance than prior state-of-the-art.
- Shows strong results on two of the three datasets.
- Highlights utility of TBuckets for weakly supervised text classification.
- Results are inconsistent across methods and datasets, raising practical applicability concerns.
- SVD-based methods perform poorly on the genomics dataset, likely due to many out‑of‑vocabulary (OOV) terms.
- The paper does not discuss the impact or handling of OOV terms.
- Claiming the method has “no parameters” is misleading because pre‑trained word embeddings carry many implicit parameters.
- Reviewer asks how the authors will address inconsistent cross‑dataset performance.
- Reviewer requests more insight into why SVD‑based methods fail on the genomics dataset.
- Reviewer asks how the authors plan to handle OOV terms.
- Reviewer asks for clarification on the role and impact of pre‑trained word embeddings on performance.
