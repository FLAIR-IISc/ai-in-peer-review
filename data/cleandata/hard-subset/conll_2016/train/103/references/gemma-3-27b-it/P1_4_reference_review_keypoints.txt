- Proposes TBuckets: grouping topic words into thematic buckets to measure coherence, with coherent topics yielding one large bucket and few small ones.  
- Evaluates three bucket-creation methods: clustering-based, SVD-based, and SVD with reorganization (SVD-Reorg).  
- Finds SVD-Reorg achieves the best correlation with human judgments and is parameter-free.  
- Demonstrates TBuckets can improve weakly supervised text classification by guiding topic labeling.

- Contribution: Introduces an intuitive, human-interpretable bucket-based topic coherence metric.  
- Contribution: Provides an effective, parameter-free SVD-based implementation (TBuckets-SVD-Reorg).  
- Contribution: Shows practical utility by improving downstream text classification using topic labeling.

- Strength: Strong empirical results with significant improvements and high correlation with human judgments across datasets.  
- Strength: Clear, well-explained methodology with helpful illustrative examples of the bucket procedure.  
- Strength: Practical advantage of not requiring parameter tuning for the SVD-based methods.  
- Strength: Addresses a relevant problem in topic modeling and interpretability.  
- Strength: Includes a downstream application (text classification) to demonstrate usefulness beyond evaluation.

- Weakness: Individual techniques (embeddings, SVD, clustering) are standard; novelty is mainly in their combination and the bucket concept.  
- Weakness: Reorganization step lacks rigorous justification; thresholding appears ad-hoc and needs analysis.  
- Weakness: Reliance on pre-trained GloVe embeddings may limit generalizability; embedding sensitivity not explored.  
- Weakness: Limited error analysis; paper lacks in-depth examples explaining why TBuckets outperforms alternatives.  
- Weakness: Dense presentation of results (e.g., Table 1) reduces readability.

- Question: How are AWPS and AWES calculated for the adaptive reorganization threshold, and how sensitive are results to the formula?  
- Question: Have you evaluated TBuckets with other embeddings (Word2Vec, FastText) or dataset-specific fine-tuning?  
- Question: Can you provide concrete topic examples where SVD-Reorg markedly improves coherence and show how reorganization changes groupings?  
- Question: In the classification experiments, how were topics labeled by annotators and was TBuckets used to filter/prioritize topics shown?  
- Question: How does TBuckets fit into Roder et al. (2015)’s unifying framework and what new dimensions does it add?

- Additional: Improve structure in some sections and add computational complexity discussion for the TBuckets variants.  
- Additional: Include visualizations of bucket structures for example topics to aid understanding.
