- Proposal: TBuckets groups topic words into thematic "buckets" to measure LDA topic coherence, evaluated with clustering, SVD, and SVD-Reorg variants.
- Empirical result: TBuckets, especially TBuckets-SVD-Reorg, shows improved correlation with human judgments on 20NG and NYT (and competitive on Genomics).
- Practical impact: TBuckets can improve weakly supervised text classification by selecting higher-quality topics.
- Contribution 1: Intuitive, human-aligned metric formulation that improves interpretability over purely mathematical coherence scores.
- Contribution 2: Effective, parameter-free SVD-based bucket creation (notably SVD-Reorg) that outperforms prior methods without tuning.
- Contribution 3: Demonstration of downstream utility beyond evaluation, via a text classification experiment.
- Strength: Strong empirical performance on two datasets, with claims of statistical significance needing explicit reporting.
- Strength: Parameter-free SVD variants increase ease of application and reduce need for hyperparameter tuning.
- Strength: Clear, detailed method explanation and helpful example dry runs that aid reproducibility and understanding.
- Strength: Well-organized paper structure and inclusion of a practical application experiment.
- Weakness: Individual components (embeddings, SVD, clustering) are established techniques; novelty is mainly in their combination and reorganization step.
- Weakness: Limited dataset variety (only three datasets); broader-domain evaluation would strengthen claims of generality.
- Weakness: Reorganization threshold (AWES − AWPS / AWES) lacks sensitivity analysis and clearer rationale for the subtraction/ratio formulation.
- Weakness: Comparisons to prior work (e.g., Röder et al. 2015) lack deeper analysis explaining why TBuckets outperforms alternative configurations.
- Question: Provide statistical significance tests (e.g., p-values) for reported improvements in Table 1.
- Question: How sensitive is TBuckets-SVD-Reorg to the dynamic threshold; provide a sensitivity/ablation analysis.
- Question: What is the computational cost/time complexity of TBuckets-SVD-Reorg compared to other coherence measures, given SVD?
- Question: Explain the intuition and justification for the AWES − AWPS / AWES reorganization threshold formula.
- Question: How well does TBuckets generalize across different embedding spaces (fastText, BERT, etc.); any experiments with alternative embeddings?
- Additional comment: Include a visual example of the bucket creation process for a sample topic to aid clarity.
- Additional comment: Add a discussion of TBuckets' limitations and failure modes.
- Additional comment: Related work section is comprehensive but should more explicitly contrast TBuckets with existing methods and detail improvements.
