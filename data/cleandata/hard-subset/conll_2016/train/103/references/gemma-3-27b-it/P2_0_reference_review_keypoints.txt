- Proposes TBuckets: grouping topic words into thematic "buckets" with three creation methods (clustering, SVD, SVD+reorg); TBuckets-SVD-Reorg best correlates with human judgments.  
- SVD-based TBuckets (especially with reorganization) performs strongly and is parameter-free, easing practical use.  
- Demonstrates practical utility: filtering low-quality topics with TBuckets improves weakly supervised text classification.  
- Strong empirical results: outperforms state-of-the-art on two of three datasets and includes intuitive illustrative examples.  
- Provides thorough related work and clear motivation for the bucket metaphor.  
- Reorganization step (SVD-Reorg) adds complexity and needs clearer explanation or pseudocode; dynamic thresholding may be sensitive.  
- Limited dataset diversity; generalizability beyond textual human-judged datasets (e.g., images, code) not evaluated.  
- Relies on pre-trained word embeddings (GloVe); impact of alternative embeddings not explored.  
- Lacks detailed error analysis explaining when and why TBuckets-SVD-Reorg succeeds or fails.  
- Requests from reviewer: provide pseudocode for SVD-Reorg, report experiments with different embeddings, detail computational cost, show failure-case examples, and analyze sensitivity to similarity metric.  
- Minor presentation issues: SVD explanation could be expanded and Table 1 formatting improved.  
- Overall recommendation: accept with minor revisions.
