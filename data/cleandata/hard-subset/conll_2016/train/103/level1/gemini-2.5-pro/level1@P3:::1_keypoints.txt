- Proposes TBuckets, a coherence metric that groups topic words into semantic "buckets" using pre-trained embeddings and scores coherence from the largest bucket.
- Implements three bucketing techniques: agglomerative clustering (TBuckets-Clustering), SVD-based orthogonal sub-theme extraction (TBuckets-SVD), and an SVD-based reorganization heuristic (TBuckets-SVD-Reorg).
- Represents topic words with GloVe embeddings and derives coherence from bucket size and composition.
- Evaluates methods by correlating scores with human judgments on three standard datasets; TBuckets-SVD-Reorg outperforms Röder et al. (2015) on two datasets.
- Shows practical utility by using the metric to select topic models that improve weakly supervised text classification performance.
- Contribution: a novel, parameter-free SVD-based coherence method using right singular vectors to identify orthogonal sub-themes.
- Contribution: an extrinsic evaluation demonstrating that the coherence metric improves real-world task performance (text classification).
- Strength: strong empirical performance and state-of-the-art correlations on standard datasets achieved without parameter tuning.
- Strength: method is novel, intuitive (bucketing), and provides a global semantic view unlike pairwise PMI/NPMI measures.
- Strength: practical utility demonstrated by improved classification F1 when selecting high-coherence topic models.
- Strength: clear, well-written presentation with illustrative examples and a helpful step-by-step dry run.
- Weakness: TBuckets-SVD-Reorg introduces complex, ad-hoc reorganization heuristics (four similarity pulls and a dynamic threshold) that reduce interpretability.
- Weakness: comparison to Röder et al. (2015) may be unfair because TBuckets uses 300-d GloVe embeddings while baselines rely on corpus co-occurrence statistics.
- Weakness: ambiguity about how Max Dist parameter for TBuckets-Clustering was chosen; potential for post-hoc tuning on test data.
- Question: please justify or ablate the dynamic threshold formulation in SVD-Reorg and report sensitivity (e.g., simpler rules or fixed thresholds).
- Question: how were optimal Max Dist values for TBuckets-Clustering selected—via a development set or peak test-set performance?
- Question: can you disentangle gains from TBuckets vs. GloVe by running baseline coherence measures (e.g., C_V or C_NPMI) using GloVe-based similarities?
