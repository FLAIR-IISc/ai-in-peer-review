- Aim: show distributional information in word vectors encodes POS labels.
- Data: uses a lemmatized BNC annotated with UD POS tags.
- Method: train word embeddings on this corpus and a logistic classifier to predict POS.
- Evaluation: cross-validation on the same corpus and tests on other corpora; results clearly presented and analyzed.
- Writing: paper is clear and well-written.
- Main issue: lacks novelty—straightforward experiments with no new NLP/ML methods.
- Contribution: provides empirical grounding for the notion of POS and is suitable for a quantitative/empirical linguistics venue.
- Literature: should cite more POS tagging/induction work using embeddings (e.g., Lin et al. 2015; Ling et al. 2015; Plank et al. 2016).
