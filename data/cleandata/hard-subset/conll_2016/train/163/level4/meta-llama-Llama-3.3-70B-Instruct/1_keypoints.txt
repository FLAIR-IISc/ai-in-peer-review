- Studies whether POS information is encoded in word embeddings trained on a lemma-replaced UD-annotated British National Corpus.  
- Trains embeddings on the modified BNC and uses them to train a logistic classifier to predict POS tags.  
- Evaluates via cross-validation on the same corpus and also tests on additional corpora.  
- Results are presented clearly, thoroughly, and accompanied by extensive discussion and analysis.  
- Paper is well-structured and clearly written.  
- Major concern: lacks novelty in NLP/ML—experiments are straightforward and introduce no new methods.  
- Despite limited novelty, results are interesting as empirical evidence for POS and suitable for quantitative/empirical linguistics venues.  
- Paper would benefit from citing more related work on POS tagging/induction with embeddings (e.g., Lin/Ammar/Duer/Levin 2015, Ling et al. 2015, Plank/Søgaard/Goldberg 2016).
