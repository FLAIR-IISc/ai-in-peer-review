- Goal: Demonstrates that word embeddings trained on a lemmatized BNC with UD POS tags encode POS information by training a logistic regression to predict POS and evaluating via cross-validation and on other corpora.  
- Presentation: Results are clearly presented and thoroughly analyzed; paper is well written.  
- Novelty: Lacks substantial methodological novelty—experiments are straightforward and introduce no new ideas in NLP/ML.  
- Contribution: Empirical evidence that distributional semantics robustly captures POS distinctions; contribution is primarily empirical and may suit a quantitative/empirical linguistics venue.  
- Related work: Literature review is incomplete and should cite more POS-tagging/induction with embeddings (e.g., Lin et al. 2015; Ling et al. 2015; Plank et al. 2016).
