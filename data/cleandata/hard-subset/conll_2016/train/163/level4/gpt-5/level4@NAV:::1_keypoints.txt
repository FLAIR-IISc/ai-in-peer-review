- Goal: Demonstrate that distributional information in word vectors contains cues about POS labels.
- Data/method: Use BNC annotated with UD POS tags (lemmas), train word embeddings, then a logistic classifier to predict POS.
- Evaluation: Cross-validation on the same corpus and testing on additional corpora; results clearly presented with thorough analysis.
- Writing: The paper is clear and well written.
- Limitation: Lacks novelty in NLP/ML—straightforward experiments without new methods or ideas.
- Contribution: Empirically supports the notion of POS and is a good fit for quantitative/empirical linguistics venues.
- Literature gap: Needs broader citation of related work on POS tagging/induction with embeddings (e.g., Lin et al. 2015; Ling et al. 2015; Plank et al. 2016).
