- Goal: Show that distributional information in word vectors encodes POS information.  
- Data: Uses BNC with UD POS tags where tokens are replaced by lemmas.  
- Method: Train word embeddings on the corpus, then train a logistic-regression classifier to predict POS from embeddings.  
- Evaluation: Assessed via cross-validation on the same corpus and on additional corpora.  
- Results: Clearly presented and extensively analyzed.  
- Writing: Paper is clear and well written.  
- Main concern: Lacks novelty in NLP/ML — experiments are straightforward and introduce no new methods.  
- Contribution: Findings still provide useful empirical grounding for the concept of POS and suit a quantitative/empirical linguistics venue.  
- Literature: Should cite POS tagging/induction work using embeddings more comprehensively (e.g., Lin et al. 2015; Ling et al. 2015; Plank et al. 2016).
