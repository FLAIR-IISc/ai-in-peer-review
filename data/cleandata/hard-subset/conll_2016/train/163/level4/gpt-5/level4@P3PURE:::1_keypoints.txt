- Demonstrates that distributional information in word embeddings encodes POS labels using a lemmatized BNC annotated with UD POS tags.
- Method: train embeddings on the BNC and use logistic regression to predict POS; evaluated via cross-validation on the corpus and on additional corpora.
- Results are clearly presented and analyzed in depth.
- Paper is clear and well written.
- Main issue: limited novelty for NLP/ML—experiments are straightforward and introduce no new methods.
- Findings are interesting and provide empirical grounding for POS; better suited to a quantitative/empirical linguistics venue.
- Related work on POS tagging/induction with embeddings is under-cited (e.g., Lin et al. 2015; Ling et al. 2015; Plank et al. 2016).
