- Objective: show that distributional information in word embeddings carries signal about POS categories.
- Data/method: use a lemmatized BNC annotated with Universal Dependencies POS, train word embeddings, and fit a multinomial logistic classifier.
- Evaluation: report cross-validation on the same corpus and evaluation on external corpora; results are clearly reported with substantial analysis.
- Writing: manuscript is clear and well written.
- Main concern: lack of novelty in NLP/ML—straightforward experiments without new methodological contributions.
- Contribution fit: findings are interesting and empirically substantiate the embedding–POS link, better suited to a quantitative/empirical linguistics venue.
- Related work: coverage of prior POS tagging/induction work with embeddings is incomplete (e.g., Lin et al. 2015; Ling et al. 2015; Plank et al. 2016).
