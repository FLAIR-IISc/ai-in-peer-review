- Paper studies whether distributional information in word vectors encodes POS categories using a lemmatized British National Corpus with UD POS tags.  
- Method: train word embeddings on the corpus and fit a logistic regression classifier to predict POS from vectors.  
- Evaluation: cross-validation on the same corpus plus tests on additional corpora to assess generalization.  
- Experiments, results, and analyses are clearly presented and discussed in depth.  
- Main limitation: limited methodological novelty—pipeline is straightforward and offers empirical rather than technical contribution.  
- Recommendation: work may be more appropriate for an empirical/quantitative linguistics venue than a methods-focused NLP/ML conference.  
- Suggestion: expand related work to better cover POS tagging and induction using embeddings (e.g., Lin/Ammar/Duer/Levin 2015; Ling et al. 2015; Plank/Søgaard/Goldberg 2016).
