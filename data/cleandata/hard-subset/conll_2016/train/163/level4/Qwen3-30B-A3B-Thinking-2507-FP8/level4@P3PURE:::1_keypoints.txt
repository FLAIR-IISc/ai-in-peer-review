- Investigates whether distributional information in word vectors encodes POS label information.  
- Uses a lemmatized British National Corpus annotated with Universal Dependencies POS tags to train embeddings.  
- Trains a logistic regression classifier on the embeddings for POS prediction and evaluates via cross-validation and on external corpora.  
- Results are clearly presented, comprehensive, and empirically validate that word vectors capture POS-related information.  
- Paper is well-written and technically sound.  
- Primary limitation: lacks novel methodological or theoretical contributions to NLP or ML.  
- Recommendation: more appropriate for a quantitative/empirical linguistics venue rather than a computational-linguistics conference like CONLL.  
- Literature review is insufficient; key prior work (e.g., Lin et al. 2015; Ling et al. 2015; Plank et al. 2016) should be cited and discussed.
