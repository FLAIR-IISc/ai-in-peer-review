- Studies whether distributional word vectors encode POS information.  
- Uses a lemmatized BNC annotated with Universal Dependencies POS tags.  
- Trains word embeddings on this corpus and a logistic regression classifier for POS prediction.  
- Evaluates via cross-validation on the corpus and on external corpora; results are clearly presented and thoroughly analyzed.  
- Paper is well-written and technically sound.  
- Major limitation: lacks substantive novelty in NLP/ML—experiments are straightforward with no new methods or theory.  
- Results are empirically interesting but do not advance the field methodologically or conceptually.  
- More suitable for a quantitative/empirical linguistics venue than an NLP conference.  
- Related work section is insufficient and needs significant expansion.  
- Must cite prior studies on POS tagging/induction with embeddings (e.g., Lin et al. 2015; Ling et al. 2015 EMNLP; Plank et al. 2016).
