- Goal: Show that distributional information in word vectors encodes POS label information.
- Data: Uses a lemma-replaced version of the British National Corpus annotated with Universal Dependencies POS tags.
- Method: Train word embeddings on the corpus and use a logistic classifier to predict word POS tags.
- Evaluation: Results evaluated via cross-validation on the BNC and on additional corpora; results are clearly presented and thoroughly analyzed.
- Clarity: Paper is well-written and clear.
- Limitation: No novel NLP or ML contribution—experiments are straightforward with no methodological or theoretical innovation.
- Recommendation: Worthy of publication in a quantitative/empirical linguistics venue.
- Literature gap: Needs more citations to POS tagging/induction work (e.g., Lin et al. 2015; Ling et al. 2015; Plank, Søgaard, and Goldberg 2016).
