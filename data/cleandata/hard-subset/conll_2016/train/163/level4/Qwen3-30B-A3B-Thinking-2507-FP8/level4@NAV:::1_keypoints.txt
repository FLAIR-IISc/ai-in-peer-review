- Investigates whether distributional information in word embeddings encodes POS label information.  
- Uses a lemmatized British National Corpus annotated with Universal Dependencies POS tags to train embeddings.  
- Trains a logistic regression classifier on the word vectors to predict POS.  
- Evaluates via cross-validation on the same corpus and on additional corpora.  
- Results are clearly presented, thoroughly discussed, and deeply analyzed.  
- Paper is well-written and clear.  
- Main limitation: no novel NLP/ML methods or conceptual insights—experiments are straightforward.  
- Empirically interesting findings but do not advance methodology.  
- Recommendation: suitable for publication in a quantitative/empirical linguistics venue.  
- Related work coverage is incomplete; should cite Lin et al. (2015), Ling et al. (2015 EMNLP), and Plank, Søgaard, and Goldberg (2016).
