- Aim: Demonstrates that distributional information in word vectors contains POS label information.  
- Data: Uses a lemmatized version of the British National Corpus annotated with Universal Dependencies POS tags.  
- Method: Trains word embeddings on the corpus and uses a logistic classifier to predict POS.  
- Evaluation: Assesses performance via cross-validation on the BNC and additional corpora; results are clearly presented and thoroughly analyzed.  
- Writing: Paper is clear and well-written.  
- Limitation: Lacks novelty—experiments are straightforward and introduce no new NLP or ML methods.  
- Venue fit: Contribution is more appropriate for a quantitative/empirical linguistics venue than a core NLP/ML conference.  
- Related work: Needs more comprehensive citation of POS tagging/induction literature using embeddings (e.g., Lin et al. 2015; Ling et al. 2015; Plank et al. 2016).
