- Paper studies how much POS information is encoded in word embeddings by training embeddings on a lemmatized UD-annotated BNC and using a logistic regression classifier to predict POS tags.
- Methodology includes evaluation on the original corpus with cross-validation and tests of generalization to other datasets.
- Results are clearly presented with thorough discussion and analysis.
- The paper is well-written and easy to understand.
- Main weakness: limited novelty—primarily a straightforward experimental evaluation rather than a new NLP/ML method.
- Contribution appears more appropriate for quantitative/empirical linguistics than for advancing NLP/ML techniques.
- Major omission: insufficient literature review on POS tagging/induction with embeddings.
- Suggested relevant prior work to cite: Lin et al. (2015); Ling et al. (2015); Plank, Søgaard, and Goldberg (2016).
