- Investigates whether distributional word vectors encode POS information.  
- Trains embeddings on a lemmatized British National Corpus annotated with Universal Dependencies POS tags.  
- Uses those embeddings to train a logistic classifier for POS prediction.  
- Evaluates on the training corpus (cross-validation) and on external datasets; results are clearly presented and thoroughly analyzed.  
- Paper is well-written and easy to understand.  
- Major limitation: lacks novelty in NLP/ML methods—direct experiments without new techniques or concepts.  
- Contribution is empirical validation of a link between distributional semantics and POS tags, not a methodological breakthrough; more suitable for quantitative/empirical linguistics venues.  
- Recommendation to expand literature review on POS tagging/induction with embeddings, citing works like Lin et al. (2015), Ling et al. (2015), and Plank et al. (2016).
