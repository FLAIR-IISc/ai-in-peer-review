- Goal: Show that distributional information in word vectors encodes POS labels using a lemmatized UD-annotated version of the BNC.
- Method: Train word embeddings on the corpus and use a logistic regression classifier to predict word POS tags.
- Evaluation: Perform cross-validation on the BNC and test on external corpora; results are clearly presented and analyzed.
- Writing: Paper is well-written and clearly articulated.
- Limitation: Lacks novelty in NLP/ML techniques; experiments are straightforward with no new methods.
- Contribution: Empirically supports POS as a distributional phenomenon and would suit a quantitative/empirical linguistics venue.
- Related work: Needs more comprehensive citations of prior POS tagging/induction work with embeddings (e.g., Lin et al. 2015; Ling et al. 2015; Plank et al. 2016).
