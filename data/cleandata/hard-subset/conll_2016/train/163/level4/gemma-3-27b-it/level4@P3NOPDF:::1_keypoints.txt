- Investigates how distributional word vectors encode POS information using a lemmatized BNC annotated with UD POS tags.  
- Trains embeddings and a logistic classifier, with evaluation via cross-validation and external corpora; results are clearly presented and analyzed.  
- Paper is well-written and easy to follow.  
- Main weakness: limited novelty—methodology is standard (train embeddings + classifier) and introduces no new NLP/ML techniques.  
- Results are empirically interesting but incremental; better suited to a quantitative or empirical linguistics venue than core NLP/ML.  
- Major omission: insufficient review of related work; should discuss prior POS tagging/induction with embeddings (e.g., Lin et al. 2015; Ling et al. 2015; Plank et al. 2016).  
- Paper should explicitly state how it differs from prior work—clarify the impact of lemmatization and the choice of the BNC corpus.  
- Overall: technically sound but limited impact; addressing related work and framing would strengthen the contribution.
