- Aim: Demonstrates that distributional information in word vectors encodes POS labels using a lemmatized UD-annotated BNC.
- Method: Trains word embeddings on this corpus and uses the vectors to train a logistic classifier for POS prediction.
- Evaluation: Assesses performance with cross-validation on the original corpus and tests on additional corpora; results are clearly presented and thoroughly analyzed.
- Clarity: Paper is clear and well-written.
- Weakness: Lacks novelty in NLP/ML; experiments are straightforward and introduce no new methods.
- Contribution: Provides empirical support for POS distinctions and is suitable for a quantitative/empirical linguistics venue.
- Literature gap: Needs more citations on POS tagging/induction with embeddings (e.g., Lin et al. 2015; Ling et al. 2015; Plank et al. 2016).
