- Investigates whether word embeddings capture POS information using a lemmatized BNC annotated with Universal Dependencies POS tags.
- Trains embeddings and a logistic classifier for POS prediction; evaluates via cross-validation on the corpus and tests on external datasets.
- Results are presented transparently and subjected to thorough analysis.
- Paper is well-written and easy to follow.
- Major concern: lack of novelty—uses established techniques without introducing new methods or concepts.
- Finding that distributional semantics relates to POS is interesting but not novel; more suitable for a quantitative/empirical linguistics venue.
- Literature review is incomplete; should discuss related work such as Lin et al. (2015), Ling et al. (2015), and Plank et al. (2016).
