- Studies whether distributional information in word embeddings predicts POS tags.
- Trains embeddings on a lemmatized British National Corpus annotated with UD POS tags.
- Uses those embeddings as features for a logistic regression POS classifier.
- Evaluates via cross-validation on the training corpus and on additional corpora.
- Results are clearly presented and analyzed in detail.
- Paper is clear and well-written.
- Main weakness: little methodological novelty; experiments are straightforward.
- Findings are interesting and provide empirical support for a distributional basis of POS categories, possibly better suited to quantitative/empirical linguistics venues.
- Related work coverage is insufficient; should cite and discuss works such as Lin et al. (2015), Ling et al. (2015), and Plank et al. (2016).
