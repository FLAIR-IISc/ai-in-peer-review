- Investigates whether distributional information in word vectors predicts POS labels.
- Trains embeddings on a lemmatized British National Corpus annotated with UD POS tags.
- Uses those word vectors as features for a logistic regression POS classifier.
- Evaluates via cross-validation on the training corpus and on additional corpora, with clear results and extensive analysis.
- Paper is clear and well-written with interesting empirical support for the distributional nature of POS categories.
- Primary weakness: lacks methodological novelty—straightforward experiments without new techniques or theoretical insights.
- Suitable for publication in a quantitative/empirical linguistics venue.
- Secondary concern: literature review is incomplete and should discuss related work on POS tagging/induction with embeddings (e.g., Lin et al., 2015; Ling et al., 2015; Plank et al., 2016).
