Paper investigates whether POS information is encoded in word vectors by training embeddings on a lemmatized BNC annotated with UD POS tags.  
Authors train word embeddings on this corpus and use them to train a logistic classifier to predict word POS.  
Evaluation is done via cross-validation on the BNC and additionally tested on other corpora, with detailed results and analysis.  
Paper is well-structured and clearly written.  
Main limitation: limited novelty — experiments are straightforward and add no new NLP/ML methods.  
Findings are valuable empirical evidence supporting the presence of POS information in distributional vectors.  
Recommendation: expand literature review on POS tagging/induction with word embeddings (e.g., Lin et al. 2015, Ling et al. 2015, Plank et al. 2016).  
Overall suitability: appropriate for publication in a quantitative or empirical linguistics venue.
