- Investigates how word embeddings relate to POS labels using a BNC+UD corpus and a logistic classifier, evaluated within and across corpora.
- Shows embeddings contain POS information that can be used to predict word POS.
- Classifier error analysis highlights POS boundary issues and annotation guideline inconsistencies.
- Method generalizes across corpora, demonstrating robustness.
- Paper is clearly written and well organized.
- Provides interesting insights into the relationship between embeddings and POS.
- Robust evaluation across multiple corpora supports the findings.
- Lacks novelty; approach is straightforward and similar to prior work.
- Literature review is limited and could be expanded on POS tagging/induction with embeddings.
- No comparison to state-of-the-art POS tagging methods is provided.
- Requests more details on preprocessing used to create the BNC version.
- Asks how the approach could be extended to other languages and corpora.
- Seeks deeper insight into classifier errors and their relation to annotation inconsistencies.
