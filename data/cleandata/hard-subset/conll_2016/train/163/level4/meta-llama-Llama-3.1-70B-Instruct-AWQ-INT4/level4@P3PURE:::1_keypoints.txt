- Paper demonstrates that distributional information in word embeddings encodes POS labels.  
- Uses a lemmatized BNC annotated with UD POS tags to train embeddings, then a logistic classifier to predict POS.  
- Evaluations include cross-validation on the same corpus and testing on other corpora; results are clearly presented and thoroughly analyzed.  
- Manuscript is well-written and clear.  
- Main drawback: no novel NLP/ML methods or innovative experimental techniques—experiments are straightforward.  
- Contribution: provides empirical evidence supporting the notion of POS and is suitable for quantitative/empirical linguistic venues.  
- Revision needed: expand literature review on POS tagging/induction with embeddings; cite works such as Lin et al. (2015), Ling et al. (2015), and Plank et al. (2016).
