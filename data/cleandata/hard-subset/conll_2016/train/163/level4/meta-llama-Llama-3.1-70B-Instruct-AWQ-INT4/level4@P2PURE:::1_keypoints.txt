- Goal: Show that distributional information in word vectors encodes POS labels.  
- Data: Uses a lemmas-only version of the BNC annotated with UD POS tags.  
- Method: Train word embeddings on this corpus and a logistic classifier to predict POS.  
- Evaluation: Results reported via cross-validation on the BNC and tested on other corpora; analyses are detailed and clear.  
- Writing: The paper is well-written and easy to follow.  
- Novelty: Lacks new methods or conceptual contributions—experiments are straightforward despite being well-executed.  
- Contribution: Provides interesting empirical evidence for POS information in embeddings and fits an empirical/quantitative linguistics venue.  
- Related work: Fails to cite relevant literature on POS tagging/induction with embeddings (e.g., Lin et al. 2015; Ling et al. 2015; Plank et al. 2016).
