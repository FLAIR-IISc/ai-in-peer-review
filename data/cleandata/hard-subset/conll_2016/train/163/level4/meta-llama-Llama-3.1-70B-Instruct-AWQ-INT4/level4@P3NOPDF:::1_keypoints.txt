- Paper demonstrates that distributional information in word embeddings trained on a lemmatized, UD-annotated BNC can predict POS labels using a logistic classifier, evaluated with cross-validation and on other corpora.
- The paper is clear, well-written, and presents results and analyses transparently.
- Main weakness: the work lacks novelty and introduces no new NLP/ML methods; experiments are straightforward.
- Findings are interesting empirically and support the notion of POS, making the paper appropriate for quantitative/empirical linguistics venues.
- Important omission: insufficient engagement with prior work on POS tagging/induction using embeddings (e.g., Lin et al. 2015; Ling et al. 2015; Plank et al. 2016); adding these citations would strengthen the paper.
- Overall: well-executed and insightful but limited by lack of novelty and limited literature integration.
