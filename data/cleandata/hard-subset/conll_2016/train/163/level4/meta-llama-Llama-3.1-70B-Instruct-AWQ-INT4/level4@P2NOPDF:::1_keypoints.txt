- Paper demonstrates that word embeddings trained on a lemmatized BNC with UD tags can predict POS labels using a logistic classifier.
- Evaluations use cross-validation on the BNC and tests on other corpora.
- Results are clearly presented, thoroughly discussed, and provide empirical evidence for distributional POS information in embeddings.
- Main limitation: no novel NLP/ML methods or innovative experimental design.
- Experiments are straightforward and lack methodological innovation.
- Paper would be more suitable for a quantitative/empirical linguistics venue than a top-tier NLP/ML conference.
- Notable omission: insufficient citations to prior POS-tagging/induction work using embeddings (e.g., Lin et al. 2015; Ling et al. 2015; Plank et al. 2016).
