- Objective: demonstrate that distributional information in word vectors encodes POS labels.
- Data/method: use BNC annotated with UD POS (lemmas only), train embeddings, then a logistic classifier to predict POS.
- Evaluation: tested via cross-validation on the same corpus and on additional corpora; results are clearly presented and thoroughly analyzed.
- Writing: paper is well-written and easy to follow.
- Limitation: limited novelty in NLP/ML—experiments are straightforward and introduce no new methods.
- Contribution: results are interesting and empirically support the concept of POS; suitable for a quantitative/empirical linguistics venue.
- Literature: needs a more comprehensive review of POS tagging/induction with embeddings (e.g., Lin et al. 2015; Ling et al. 2015; Plank et al. 2016).
