- Finding that PoS-related information is distributed across many embedding dimensions (≥100 components).
- Rigorous experimental setup using BNC, lemmatization, UPOS conversion, and robust evaluation metrics.
- Clear presentation with helpful visualizations illustrating prototypical vectors.
- Practical suggestion: use distributional models for preliminary tagging in resource-poor languages.
- Limited novelty: core claim about embeddings and PoS is already established in prior work.
