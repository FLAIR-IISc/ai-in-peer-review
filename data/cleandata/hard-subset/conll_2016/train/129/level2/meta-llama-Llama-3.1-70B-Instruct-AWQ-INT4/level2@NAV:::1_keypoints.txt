- Trains the CNN to score general-domain sentence pairs and selects top-scoring pairs to augment SMT training.  
- Contribution: introduces a semi-supervised CNN approach that leverages labeled and unlabeled data for domain adaptation.  
- Contribution: shows the method is effective with only tiny amounts of in-domain data, improving SMT performance.  
- Contribution: reports better results than several state-of-the-art language model–based data selection methods.  
- Strength: novel application of semi-supervised CNNs for domain adaptation in SMT.
