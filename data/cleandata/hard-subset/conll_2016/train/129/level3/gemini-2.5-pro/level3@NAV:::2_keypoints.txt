- Primary claim: method is highly effective with very tiny in‑domain seeds (e.g., 100 sentences), outperforming LM‑based selection in low‑resource scenarios.
- Secondary claim: yields modest but consistent gains (~+0.5 BLEU) over strong LM baselines in standard settings across multiple language pairs.
