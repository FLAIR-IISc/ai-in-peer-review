- Paper applies Gaussian processes to regress post-editing time/rates for MT quality estimation and is well-structured with a clear introduction.  
- Good description of asymmetric risk scenarios and corresponding estimators.  
- Sufficient explanation of Gaussian processes, kernel choice, and warping for readers unfamiliar with GPs.  
- Research questions are interesting and well-phrased.  
- Needs explicit justification for choosing post-editing rates over HTER/TER as the target variable.  
- Section 3.2 first paragraph is unclear; trends for fr-en and en-de vs. en-es are not well-defined.  
- Claim that NLL and NLPD “drastically” decrease with warped GPs for all datasets is not fully accurate.  
- Should include a baseline point-estimate model from prior work in result tables for better comparison.  
- Interpretation of NLL/NLPD values is challenging; include qualitative examples to illustrate the practical significance of reductions.  
- Section 4 is well-written and explains results intuitively.  
- Overall: valuable contribution highlighting issues with point estimates in QE, but not state-of-the-art or novel in modeling.  
- Minor: change “over and underestimates” to “over- and underestimates.”  
- Minor: Figure 1 caption incorrectly states line colors (blue/red vs. blue/green).  
- Minor: reference the GP toolkit used, if any.
