- Paper argues prediction uncertainty is critical for practical QE decisions and proposes GPs with Matèrn kernels and Warped GPs plus NLPD as an evaluation metric.
- Contribution: Shows uncertainty modeling matters for decision-making and that standard point-estimate metrics miss uncertainty differences.
- Contribution: Proposes Warped GPs (tanh warping) and Matèrn kernels to improve uncertainty estimation in QE.
- Contribution: Demonstrates using predictive distributions for asymmetric risk decisions without retraining.
- Strength: Convincing practical motivation showing point estimates are insufficient for workflows like post-editing and gisting.
- Strength: Strong empirical evidence that NLPD detects uncertainty modeling differences missed by MAE/Pearson’s r across three language pairs.
- Strength: Practical demonstration that Warped GPs help in optimistic/pessimistic decision scenarios, enabling better asymmetric-risk handling.
- Strength: Clear, accessible theoretical exposition of GPs, Warped GPs, and Matèrn kernels.
- Weakness: Limited justification for focusing solely on GPs and insufficient discussion of probabilistic alternatives (e.g., Bayesian neural nets).
- Weakness: Lacks comparison to state-of-the-art QE models (e.g., deep learning), so unclear impact on overall QE performance.
- Weakness: Does not address computational costs of Warped GPs versus standard GPs or other QE models, affecting deployment considerations.
- Weakness: Inconsistent results on the small en-es dataset are not thoroughly investigated, problematic for low-data QE regimes.
- Question: Why are other probabilistic models (e.g., Bayesian neural networks) considered unsuitable for QE—please provide evidence or discussion?
- Question: How do Warped GPs compare to state-of-the-art deep QE models in point estimates and overall utility when accounting for uncertainty?
- Question: Can you analyze the en-es dataset inconsistency—are results due to a fundamental limitation or dataset-specific factors?
- Question: What are the training and inference computational costs of Warped GPs relative to standard GPs and other QE models for production use?
- Overall assessment: Valuable contribution highlighting uncertainty in QE and promoting NLPD; recommend acceptance with minor revisions to address comparisons and computational aspects.
