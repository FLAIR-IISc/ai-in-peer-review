- Proposes a new multilingual NER feature that maps target-language phrases to English Wikipedia articles via a cross-lingual Wikifier.  
- Uses Wikipedia-derived features and shows they improve performance in monolingual and direct-transfer (English→target) settings.  
- Experiments are thorough, with valuable analysis on low-resource and non-Latin languages.  
- Reviewer's overall impression is positive—paper is liked and contributions are clear.  
- Concern: how are entities not present on Wikipedia affected by the method?  
- Concern: method strongly depends on the cross-lingual Wikifier—how often do Wikifier errors lead to NER prediction errors?  
- Suggestion: consider regularizing feature classes differently to prevent over-reliance on lexical features, especially given poor direct-transfer performance on Tamil and Bengali when lexical features are added.
