- Uses cross-lingual wikification for direct-transfer NER; contribution is incremental rather than technically novel but built on a coherent story with convincing experiments.  
- Related-work section needs deeper comparison to Kazama & Torisawa (2007) and clearer distinctions from other Wikipedia-based NER systems.  
- Run and report experiments that include POS-tag features (e.g., Tackström et al.) to empirically assess their usefulness across languages.  
- Section 3.3 (the edited model from Tsai and Roth) is unclear and would benefit from a running example.  
- Since approaches from Tackström et al. and Nothman et al. are claimed orthogonal, show preliminary combined-model results for some languages to support that claim.  
- Add a comparison with a strong projection baseline to better contextualize the strengths and limitations of the wikification-based approach.  
- Explain surprising language-pair results (e.g., Dutch best for Spanish, Spanish best for Yoruba) with a paragraph or two of analysis.  
- Table 4 improvements are small and inconsistent; report statistical significance tests to support the claims.  
- Note that projection can be done without parallel data (e.g., Peirsman & Pado 2009; Vulić & Moens 2013) using bilingual semantic spaces — mention and position these methods.  
- Paper contains several typos and awkward sentences; perform careful proofreading and fix the cited examples.
