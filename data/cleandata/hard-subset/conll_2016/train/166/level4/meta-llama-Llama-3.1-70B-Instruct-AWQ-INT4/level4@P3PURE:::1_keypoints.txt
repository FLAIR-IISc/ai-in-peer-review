- Proposes multilingual NER using Wikipedia-derived features via a cross-lingual Wikifier mapping target-language phrases to English articles.  
- Extracts and uses features from the mapped Wikipedia entries in the NER model.  
- Experimental results show the new feature improves performance in both monolingual and direct-transfer (English→target) settings.  
- Thorough empirical evaluation with notable analysis on low-resource and non-Latin languages.  
- Missing analysis of how the method affects named entities that lack Wikipedia entries.  
- Method success is heavily dependent on cross-lingual Wikifier accuracy; need to quantify error propagation from Wikifier mistakes.  
- Direct-transfer performance degrades for Tamil and Bengali when lexical features are added; consider differentially regularizing feature classes to prevent over-reliance on lexical features.
