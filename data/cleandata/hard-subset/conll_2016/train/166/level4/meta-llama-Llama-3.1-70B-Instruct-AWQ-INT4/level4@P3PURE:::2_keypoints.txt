- Explores cross-lingual direct transfer of NER using a recent cross-lingual wikification model.  
- Contribution is incremental but valuable, combining direct transfer for downstream NLP tasks with recent wikification advances.  
- Paper is well-structured, coherent, and supported by sufficient experiments yielding convincing results.  
- Related work should more clearly explain relation to Kazama & Torisawa (2007) and emphasize differences from other Wikipedia-based NER systems.  
- Excluding POS tags is justified, but experiments adding POS features (e.g., following Täckström et al.) across languages with universal POS tags are recommended.  
- Section 3.3 is unclear; include a running example to clarify the edited Tsai and Roth model.  
- Authors should try combining their approach with Täckström et al. and Nothman et al., report preliminary combined results, and compare to a strong projection baseline.  
- Unexpected language-pair findings (Dutch best for Spanish; Spanish best for Yoruba) need deeper analysis to determine if coincidental or meaningful.  
- Improvements in Table 4 are small and inconsistent; perform statistical significance testing to support claims.  
- Note that projection can be done without parallel data (e.g., Peirsman & Pado 2009; Vulić & Moens 2013), which should be mentioned.  
- Paper needs careful proofreading to fix several typos and wording errors.
