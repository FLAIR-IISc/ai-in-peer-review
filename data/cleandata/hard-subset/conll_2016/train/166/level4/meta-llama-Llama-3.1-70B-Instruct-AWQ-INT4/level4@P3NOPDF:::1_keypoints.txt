- Proposes a novel multilingual NER method using a cross-lingual Wikifier to map target-language phrases to English Wikipedia and extract features from those articles.  
- Shows experimental improvements in both monolingual and direct transfer settings (English model tested on target languages).  
- Contribution is notable with thorough experiments and insightful analysis of low-resource languages and non-Latin scripts.  
- Raises concern about reliance on Wikipedia and lack of analysis for named entities missing from Wikipedia.  
- Depends heavily on the cross-lingual Wikifier, introducing potential error propagation; needs analysis of Wikifier-induced errors and mitigation strategies.  
- Observes that adding lexical features hurts direct transfer for Tamil and Bengali, suggesting over-reliance on lexical features and a need to explore separate regularization for feature classes.  
- Overall positive assessment: compelling approach and solid foundation, but addressing the above limitations would strengthen the paper.
