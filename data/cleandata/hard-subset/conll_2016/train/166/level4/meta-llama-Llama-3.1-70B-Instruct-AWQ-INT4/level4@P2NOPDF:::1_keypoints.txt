- Proposes a novel multilingual NER feature derived from Wikipedia using a cross-lingual Wikifier to map target-language phrases to English Wikipedia entries.
- Demonstrates effectiveness of the Wikipedia-derived feature in both monolingual and direct-transfer (English→target) settings.
- Presents comprehensive experiments and analysis, with notable focus on low-resource languages and non-Latin scripts.
- Reviewer finds the paper compelling and methodologically thorough.
- Needs evaluation of method performance on named entities that are not present in Wikipedia to understand coverage limitations.
- The approach’s success depends on the accuracy of the cross-lingual Wikifier; quantify how Wikifier errors affect NER predictions.
- Poor direct-transfer performance for Tamil and Bengali when lexical features are added suggests investigating regularization or balancing of feature classes to avoid overreliance on lexical signals.
