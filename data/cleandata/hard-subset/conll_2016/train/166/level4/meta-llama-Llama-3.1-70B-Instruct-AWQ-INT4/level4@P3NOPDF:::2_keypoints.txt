- Explores cross-lingual direct transfer for NER using a cross-lingual wikification model; incremental idea but a meaningful combination of two research paths.  
- Paper is well-structured with a clear story and sufficient experiments showing convincing results.  
- Related work section needs expansion, especially to distinguish this work from Kazama & Torisawa (2007) and other Wikipedia-based NER systems.  
- Authors’ exclusion of POS features is justified, but experiments with POS (e.g., Tackström et al. style) should be reported to evaluate usefulness across languages.  
- Section 3.3 is unclear; add a running example to illustrate how the edited model from Tsai & Roth operates.  
- Recommend reporting preliminary results combining this approach with Tackström et al. (2012) and Nothman et al. (2012) and comparing to a strong projection baseline.  
- Notable findings (Dutch best for Spanish; Spanish best for Yoruba) warrant deeper analysis to determine if coincidental or due to underlying factors.  
- Results in Table 4 show only small improvements; include statistical significance tests to support claims.  
- Minor: Expand Section 2.1 to mention projection methods that do not require parallel data (e.g., Peirsman & Pado 2009; Vulić & Moens 2013).  
- Minor: Paper needs careful proofreading to fix typos and unclear phrasing (examples: Section 3 first sentence, “They avoid the traditional pipeline…”, “to disambiguate every n-grams”).
