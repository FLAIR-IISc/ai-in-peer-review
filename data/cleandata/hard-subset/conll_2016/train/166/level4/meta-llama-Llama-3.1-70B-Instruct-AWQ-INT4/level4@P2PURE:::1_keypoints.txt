- Proposes a novel multilingual NER method that leverages features from English Wikipedia via a cross-lingual Wikifier mapping target-language phrases to English articles.  
- Uses Wikipedia-derived features and shows effectiveness in both monolingual and direct-transfer (English→target) settings.  
- Strong experimental evaluation, with notable analysis on low-resource languages and non-Latin scripts.  
- Lacks analysis of the method’s impact on named entities that are not present in Wikipedia.  
- Method’s success is highly dependent on the accuracy of the cross-lingual Wikifier; error frequency and downstream effects need examination.  
- Poor direct-transfer performance on Tamil and Bengali when adding lexical features suggests exploring separate regularization for different feature classes to avoid over-reliance on lexical cues.
