- Introduces a novel multilingual NER method using Wikipedia-derived features via a cross-lingual Wikifier mapping target phrases to English articles.  
- Incorporates features from corresponding English Wikipedia entries and shows effectiveness in monolingual and direct-transfer (English→target) settings.  
- Reviewer finds the paper compelling, with an innovative feature and comprehensive experiments; analysis of low-resource languages and non-Latin scripts is notable.  
- Recommend evaluating the method’s performance on named entities that are not present in Wikipedia.  
- Recommend assessing and quantifying error propagation from the cross-lingual Wikifier into the prediction pipeline.  
- Notes poor direct-transfer performance on Tamil and Bengali when lexical features are used and suggests exploring regularization across feature classes to reduce dependence on lexical features.
