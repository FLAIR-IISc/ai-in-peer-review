- Proposes a language-independent cross-lingual NER method using a wikifier that maps words/phrases to English Wikipedia, with features from Wikipedia categories and Freebase types.
- Introduces cross-lingual wikifier-based feature extraction as a novel approach for NER.
- Performs extensive direct transfer experiments testing an English-trained model on target languages.
- Provides analysis of performance on low-resource languages (Turkish, Tagalog, Yoruba, Bengali, Tamil).
- Novel and effective feature extraction method via cross-lingual wikification.
- Thorough experimental evaluation across monolingual and direct transfer settings.
- Valuable insights into behavior on low-resource languages.
- Strong dependence on the accuracy of the cross-lingual wikifier, risking error propagation.
- No clear strategy for handling named entities absent from Wikipedia.
- Potential over-reliance on lexical features, hurting direct transfer for Tamil and Bengali.
- How will you handle entities not present on Wikipedia?
- Can you detail error analysis of the cross-lingual wikifier and its effect on NER performance?
- Have you considered different regularization for feature classes to avoid over-reliance on lexical features?
