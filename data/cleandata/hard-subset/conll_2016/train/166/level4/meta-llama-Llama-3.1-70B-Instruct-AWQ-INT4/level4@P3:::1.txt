**Summary of the Paper**

This paper proposes a language-independent approach for cross-lingual named entity recognition (NER) using a cross-lingual wikifier to disambiguate words and phrases to English Wikipedia entries. The proposed model uses features based on Wikipedia categories and FreeBase types as language-independent features for NER. Experiments show that this new feature helps not only in the monolingual case but also in the more interesting direct transfer setting, where the English model is tested on a target language.

**Main Contributions**

1. **Cross-lingual wikifier features**: The paper introduces a novel feature extraction method that uses a cross-lingual wikifier to disambiguate words and phrases to English Wikipedia entries, providing language-independent features for NER.
2. **Direct transfer experiments**: The paper conducts extensive experiments to evaluate the proposed model in the direct transfer setting, where the English model is tested on a target language.
3. **Analysis on low-resource languages**: The paper provides an analysis of the proposed model's performance on low-resource languages, including Turkish, Tagalog, Yoruba, Bengali, and Tamil.

**Strengths**

1. **Novel feature extraction method**: The proposed cross-lingual wikifier features provide a new and effective way to extract language-independent features for NER.
2. **Extensive experiments**: The paper conducts a thorough set of experiments to evaluate the proposed model in both monolingual and direct transfer settings.
3. **Analysis on low-resource languages**: The paper provides valuable insights into the performance of the proposed model on low-resource languages.

**Weaknesses**

1. **Dependency on cross-lingual wikifier**: The proposed method is strongly dependent on the success of the cross-lingual wikifier, which may introduce errors in the prediction.
2. **Handling of named entities not on Wikipedia**: The paper does not provide a clear solution for handling named entities that are not present on Wikipedia.
3. **Over-reliance on lexical features**: The poor performance of direct transfer on Tamil and Bengali when lexical features are added suggests that the model may become over-reliant on lexical features.

**Questions to Authors**

1. How do you plan to handle named entities that are not present on Wikipedia?
2. Can you provide more details on the error analysis of the cross-lingual wikifier and its impact on the proposed method?
3. Have you considered regularizing the various feature classes differently to prevent the model from becoming over-reliant on lexical features?
