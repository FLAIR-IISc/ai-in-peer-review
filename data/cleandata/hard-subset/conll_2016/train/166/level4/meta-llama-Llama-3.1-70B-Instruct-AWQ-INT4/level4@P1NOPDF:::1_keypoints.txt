- Proposes multilingual NER using English Wikipedia features via a cross-lingual Wikifier mapping target-language phrases to English articles.  
- Demonstrates the Wikipedia-derived feature improves performance in monolingual and direct-transfer (English→target) settings.  
- Paper is compelling with a novel feature and thorough experiments, notably analyzing low-resource languages and non-Latin scripts.  
- Limitation: method cannot handle named entities that lack a Wikipedia entry.  
- Suggestion: include analysis of how entities without Wikipedia pages are affected by the approach.  
- Concern: method success depends heavily on cross-lingual Wikifier accuracy; need to study error propagation from Wikifier to NER.  
- Observation: adding lexical features degrades direct-transfer performance on Tamil and Bengali; propose exploring differential regularization of feature classes to avoid overreliance on lexical features.
