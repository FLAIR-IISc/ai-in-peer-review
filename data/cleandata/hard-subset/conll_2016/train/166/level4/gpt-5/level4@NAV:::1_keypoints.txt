- Proposes a multilingual NER method that uses a cross-lingual Wikifier to link target-language phrases to English Wikipedia and extract features.  
- Reports improvements from the Wikipedia-derived feature in both monolingual and direct-transfer (English→target) settings.  
- Paper judged strong: introduces a novel feature and provides a fairly comprehensive experimental study.  
- Analysis of low-resource and non-Latin languages is a particularly valuable contribution.  
- Raises concern about handling named entities that are absent from Wikipedia and requests analysis of their treatment.  
- Notes the method’s heavy dependence on cross-lingual Wikifier accuracy and asks how often errors propagate from the Wikifier to predictions.  
- Observes poor direct-transfer performance on Tamil and Bengali when lexical features are included.  
- Suggests separately regularizing different feature classes to avoid overreliance on lexical features.
