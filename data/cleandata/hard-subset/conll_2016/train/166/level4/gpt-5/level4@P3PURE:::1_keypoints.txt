- Proposes a multilingual NER method that uses a cross-lingual Wikifier to link target-language phrases to English Wikipedia and derive features from those pages.
- Reports improved NER performance both in monolingual settings and in direct transfer (English→target) scenarios.
- Reviewer finds the paper strong and compelling, noting the feature is novel and the experimental evaluation is fairly thorough.
- The analysis of low-resource languages and non-Latin scripts is highlighted as particularly interesting.
- Question: How does the method handle entities absent from Wikipedia — report isolated performance on out-of-Wikipedia entities.
- Question: How much do Wikifier errors drive prediction failures — request quantitative error attribution (breakdown of failures due to Wikifier mistakes).
- Suggestion: Explore differential regularization (or similar techniques) for feature classes to reduce over-reliance on lexical features and improve direct-transfer performance for Tamil and Bengali.
