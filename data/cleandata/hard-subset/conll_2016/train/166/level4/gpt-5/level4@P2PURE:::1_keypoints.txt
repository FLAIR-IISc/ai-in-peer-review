- Proposes a multilingual NER method that leverages Wikipedia-derived features via cross-lingual wikification.
- Links target-language phrases to English Wikipedia pages and extracts features from those entries.
- Shows performance improvements in monolingual settings and in direct transfer (English→target language).
- Provides a fairly comprehensive experimental evaluation with compelling analysis for low-resource languages and non-Latin scripts.
- Raises concern about handling named entities that are absent from Wikipedia and requests analysis of their treatment.
- Notes heavy dependence on the cross-lingual wikifier and asks for evaluation of how wikification errors propagate to NER errors.
- Observes degraded direct-transfer performance on Tamil and Bengali when lexical features are added and suggests exploring separate regularization of different feature classes.
