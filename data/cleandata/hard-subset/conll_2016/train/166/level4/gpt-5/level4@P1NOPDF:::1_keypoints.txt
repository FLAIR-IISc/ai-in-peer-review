- Proposes multilingual NER using Wikipedia-derived features via a cross-lingual wikifier linking target-language phrases to English Wikipedia.
- Extracted features improve NER performance in both monolingual and direct-transfer (English→target) scenarios.
- Presents thorough experimental evaluation with engaging analyses for low-resource and non-Latin-script languages.
- Coverage gaps: lacks analysis of mentions not present in Wikipedia and potential bias toward entities with Wikipedia entries.
- Error propagation: needs breakdown of NER errors attributable to wikifier mistakes and performance conditioned on correct vs. incorrect links.
- Feature regularization: lexical features hurt transfer to Tamil and Bengali; suggest feature-group-specific regularization or tuned penalties to prevent over-reliance.
- Overall: solid contribution; addressing Wikipedia coverage, wikifier robustness, and lexical feature sensitivity would strengthen the paper.
