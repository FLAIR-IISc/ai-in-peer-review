- Introduces a multilingual NER approach that uses a cross-lingual Wikifier to map target-language phrases to English Wikipedia and extract features.  
- Experimental results show the Wikipedia-derived feature improves performance in monolingual and direct transfer (English→target) settings.  
- Presents a novel feature and comprehensive experimental evaluation, with especially compelling analysis for low-resource and non-Latin languages.  
- Does not analyze how the method handles named entities missing from Wikipedia; impact on such entities is unassessed.  
- Method’s effectiveness depends on cross-lingual Wikifier accuracy; no evaluation of how Wikifier errors propagate to NER predictions.  
- Direct transfer performance drops for Tamil and Bengali when lexical features are included, indicating possible over-reliance on lexical cues.  
- Suggests investigating different regularization strategies per feature class to mitigate over-reliance on lexical features.
