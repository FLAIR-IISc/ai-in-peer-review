- Proposes a novel multilingual NER approach leveraging Wikipedia-derived features via a cross-lingual Wikifier mapping to English articles.  
- The Wikipedia-based feature set improves performance in both monolingual and direct-transfer (English→target) scenarios.  
- Strong empirical contribution, especially for low-resource and non-Latin script languages, supported by comprehensive experiments.  
- Assumes relevant entities exist in Wikipedia; needs analysis of how missing entities are handled and their impact on performance.  
- Relies on the cross-lingual Wikifier, introducing a potential error source; must assess error propagation from Wikifier mistakes to final NER outputs.  
- Observed degradation in direct transfer for Tamil and Bengali when lexical features are included suggests model over-reliance on those features.  
- Recommend exploring differential regularization across feature classes to reduce over-reliance on lexical features.
