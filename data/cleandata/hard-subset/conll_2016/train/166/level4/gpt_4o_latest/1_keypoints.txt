- Proposes multilingual NER using Wikipedia-derived features via a cross-lingual Wikifier that maps target-language phrases to English articles.  
- Shows the Wikipedia-based features improve both monolingual and challenging direct-transfer (English→target) settings.  
- Experiments are comprehensive and well-executed, with particularly compelling analysis for low-resource and non-Latin-script languages.  
- Raises concern about how the method handles named entities that are absent from Wikipedia and requests evaluation of that case.  
- Notes dependence on the cross-lingual Wikifier and asks for analysis of how often Wikifier inaccuracies cause prediction errors.  
- Suggests exploring separate regularization of different feature classes to avoid over-reliance on lexical features, which degrades direct-transfer performance for Tamil and Bengali.
