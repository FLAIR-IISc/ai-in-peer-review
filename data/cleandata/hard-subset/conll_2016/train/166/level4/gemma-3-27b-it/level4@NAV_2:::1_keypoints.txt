- Proposal: use a cross-lingual Wikifier to map target-language phrases to English Wikipedia and derive features for multilingual NER, improving monolingual and English→target direct transfer performance.
- Overall assessment: strong contribution with a novel feature set and comprehensive experiments; analysis on low-resource languages and non-Latin scripts is insightful.
- Concern: reliance on Wikipedia—unclear how the method performs on named entities not present in Wikipedia; recommend reporting results for such entities.
- Concern: added cross-lingual wikifier step—need to quantify how often wikifier inaccuracies cause prediction errors.
- Observation/suggestion: lexical features hurt direct transfer on Tamil and Bengali; consider differential regularization of feature classes to avoid over-reliance on lexical information.
