- Proposes a novel multilingual NER method that links target-language mentions to English Wikipedia via a cross-lingual Wikifier and uses features from the linked articles.  
- Experiments show Wikipedia-derived features improve NER in monolingual settings and in English→target direct transfer.  
- Paper is a valuable contribution with comprehensive evaluation; analyses on low-resource languages and non-Latin scripts are especially insightful.  
- Limitation: approach may struggle with named entities missing from Wikipedia; the impact of missing-entity coverage needs evaluation.  
- Reliance on the cross-lingual Wikifier risks error propagation; the frequency with which Wikifier errors cause NER mistakes should be quantified.  
- Adding lexical features worsened performance for Tamil and Bengali in direct transfer, indicating possible feature imbalance.  
- Suggestion: explore differential regularization or similar techniques to reduce over-reliance on potentially misleading lexical features for certain languages.
