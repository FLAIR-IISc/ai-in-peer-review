- Introduces a novel multilingual NER approach using features from English Wikipedia via a cross-lingual Wikifier.  
- Method yields improved performance in monolingual and direct-transfer (English→other languages) settings.  
- Strong, well-motivated paper with comprehensive experimental evaluation.  
- Analysis of low-resource languages and non-Latin scripts is particularly insightful.  
- Needs investigation and reporting on performance for named entities not present in Wikipedia.  
- Reliance on the cross-lingual Wikifier is a potential error source; the impact of Wikifier errors should be quantified.  
- Addition of lexical features degrades direct-transfer performance for Tamil and Bengali; differential regularization for feature classes should be explored.
