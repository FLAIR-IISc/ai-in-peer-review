- Proposes multilingual NER using features derived from English Wikipedia by mapping target-language phrases to English articles via a cross-lingual wikifier.  
- The Wikipedia-derived feature improves NER performance in monolingual settings and especially in direct-transfer (English→target) scenarios.  
- The paper is strong and well-executed, with a comprehensive experimental evaluation.  
- The analysis of low-resource languages and languages with non-Latin scripts is particularly insightful.  
- Concern: the method’s reliance on Wikipedia—how are entities missing from Wikipedia handled, and what is the impact on performance?  
- Concern: the added cross-lingual wikifier step may introduce errors—need analysis of error propagation and how often prediction errors stem from wikifier inaccuracies.  
- Observation: direct-transfer performance degrades for Tamil and Bengali when lexical features are included; consider differential regularization to avoid overreliance on lexical features.
