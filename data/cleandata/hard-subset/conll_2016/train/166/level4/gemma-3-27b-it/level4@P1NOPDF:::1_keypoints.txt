- Proposes multilingual NER using features extracted from English Wikipedia via a cross-lingual Wikifier mapping target-language phrases to English articles.
- Demonstrates the Wikipedia-derived features improve performance in monolingual settings and more so in direct transfer (English→target language).
- Introduces a novel feature and provides comprehensive experiments validating its effectiveness.
- Provides insightful analysis focused on low-resource languages and non-Latin scripts.
- Does not evaluate how the method handles named entities missing from Wikipedia, leaving impact of coverage gaps unclear.
- Relies on the cross-lingual Wikifier; paper lacks quantification of how Wikifier errors propagate to final NER errors.
- Reports performance degradation when adding lexical features in direct transfer for Tamil and Bengali, suggesting possible over-reliance on lexical cues.
- Recommends investigating differential regularization across feature classes to mitigate over-dependence on lexical information in challenging languages.
