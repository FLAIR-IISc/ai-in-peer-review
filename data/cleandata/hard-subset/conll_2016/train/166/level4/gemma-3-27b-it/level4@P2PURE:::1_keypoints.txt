- Introduces a novel multilingual NER approach that incorporates features derived from English Wikipedia via a cross-lingual Wikifier.  
- Uses the Wikifier to map target-language phrases to English Wikipedia articles and extracts features from those entries.  
- Demonstrates improved NER performance in monolingual settings and notably in direct-transfer (English→target) scenarios.  
- Provides comprehensive experiments and a strong empirical case, with valuable analysis for low-resource languages and non-Latin scripts.  
- Raises concern about reliance on Wikipedia coverage—unclear performance on entities lacking corresponding Wikipedia pages.  
- Calls for reporting results specifically measuring the method’s impact on entities without Wikipedia pages.  
- Notes dependence on Wikifier accuracy and requests analysis of how Wikifier errors propagate to prediction errors.  
- Observes degraded direct-transfer performance for Tamil and Bengali when lexical features are added and suggests exploring differential regularization of feature classes to reduce lexical over-reliance.
