- Proposes multilingual NER using features from English Wikipedia via a cross-lingual Wikifier mapping target-language phrases to English articles.
- Introduces a novel Wikipedia-derived feature that improves monolingual performance and notably improves direct transfer from English to other languages.
- Provides a thorough experimental evaluation demonstrating the feature’s utility.
- Offers insightful analysis focused on low-resource languages and non-Latin scripts.
- Raises concern about reliance on Wikipedia: performance on named entities missing from Wikipedia is not evaluated and needs assessment.
- Notes critical dependence on cross-lingual Wikifier accuracy and requests quantification of how Wikifier errors propagate to NER errors.
- Observes degraded direct-transfer performance for Tamil and Bengali when lexical features are added and suggests exploring differential regularization to avoid overreliance on lexical features.
