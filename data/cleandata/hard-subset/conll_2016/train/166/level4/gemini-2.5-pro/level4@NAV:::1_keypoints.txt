- Proposes a multilingual NER method that extracts features from English Wikipedia via a cross-lingual Wikifier linking target-language phrases to English articles.  
- The novel Wikipedia-derived feature improves NER performance in monolingual and direct-transfer (English→target language) settings.  
- Thorough experiments and analysis, especially on low-resource and non-Latin-script languages, strengthen the paper’s contributions.  
- Lacks analysis of entities that do not have Wikipedia entries; effects on out-of-Wikipedia entities are not evaluated.  
- Method depends on cross-lingual Wikifier accuracy; missing error analysis quantifying how Wikifier mistakes propagate to final predictions.  
- Observed performance drop for Tamil and Bengali in direct transfer when lexical features are used suggests potential overfitting.  
- Recommends investigating different regularization strengths for feature classes to reduce over-reliance on non-generalizing lexical features.
