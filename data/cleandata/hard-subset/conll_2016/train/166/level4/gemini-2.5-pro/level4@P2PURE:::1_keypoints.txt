- Proposes a multilingual NER method that generates features by mapping target-language phrases to English Wikipedia articles via a cross-lingual Wikifier.
- Shows that the Wikipedia-derived feature improves NER performance in monolingual settings and in direct-transfer (English→target) experiments.
- Main strength: a novel, effective Wikipedia-based feature backed by thorough and convincing experiments.
- Analysis highlights notable benefits for low-resource and non-Latin languages.
- Missing analysis of model performance on named entities that lack corresponding Wikipedia entries (out-of-Wikipedia mentions).
- Method depends on the cross-lingual Wikifier; needs error-propagation analysis to quantify how Wikifier errors cause incorrect NER predictions.
- Adding lexical features worsens transfer for Tamil and Bengali, suggesting the model may over-rely on source-language lexical cues and could benefit from differential regularization across feature classes.
