- Introduces a novel multilingual NER approach that extracts features via a cross-lingual Wikifier mapping target-language phrases to English Wikipedia.  
- Features from Wikipedia improve NER performance in monolingual and especially direct transfer settings.  
- Main contribution: a new, effective Wikipedia-derived feature set for NER.  
- Experimental evaluation is comprehensive and convincingly demonstrates utility across settings.  
- Analysis of impact on low-resource languages and non-Latin scripts is insightful and addresses important multilingual challenges.  
- Concern: reliance on Wikipedia—no analysis of performance on entities absent from Wikipedia (“out-of-Wikipedia” entities).  
- Concern: upstream Wikifier errors could propagate; need error analysis quantifying how Wikification failures affect NER.  
- Observation: including lexical features degrades direct transfer to Tamil and Bengali, suggesting source-language overfitting.  
- Suggestion: consider differential regularization (stronger penalty on lexical features) to encourage reliance on cross-lingually robust Wikipedia features.
