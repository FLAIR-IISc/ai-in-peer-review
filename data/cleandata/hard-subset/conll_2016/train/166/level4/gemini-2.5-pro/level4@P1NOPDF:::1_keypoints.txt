- Proposes multilingual NER method that uses cross-lingual Wikifier to map target-language phrases to English Wikipedia and extract features for NER.
- New Wikipedia-derived feature improves performance in monolingual and direct transfer (English→target) settings.
- Experiments are thorough and validate the utility of the proposed feature across languages.
- Analysis of feature impact in low-resource settings and on non-Latin scripts is insightful.
- Missing analysis of entities that lack Wikipedia entries; need evaluation on out-of-Wikipedia entities to show limitations.
- Method depends heavily on the accuracy of the upstream Wikifier; requires error analysis to quantify propagation of Wikifier mistakes to NER errors.
- Direct-transfer performance drops for Tamil and Bengali when lexical features are added, suggesting possible overfitting to source-language lexical items and motivating per-feature-class regularization.
