- Novel method for multilingual NER that extracts features from Wikipedia via a cross-lingual Wikifier mapping target-language phrases to English articles.
- Uses features derived from mapped English Wikipedia pages to inform NER models.
- Experimental results show the new Wikipedia-based feature improves performance in monolingual and direct-transfer (English→target) settings.
- Paper includes comprehensive experiments and a notable analysis of low-resource languages and non-Latin scripts.
- Suggestion to evaluate the method’s impact on named entities that do not appear in Wikipedia.
- Concern that overall success depends on the cross-lingual Wikifier’s accuracy; recommend quantifying errors attributable to Wikifier mistakes.
- Observation of poor direct-transfer performance on Tamil and Bengali when lexical features are added.
- Recommendation to investigate regularization or feature-specific techniques to prevent over-reliance on lexical features.
