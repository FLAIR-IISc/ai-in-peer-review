- Studies cross-lingual NER transfer using cross-lingual wikification; coherent narrative and solid experiments but limited novelty (incremental rather than groundbreaking).  
- Main contribution is an incremental combination of direct transfer for downstream NLP and recent cross-lingual wikification advances.  
- Related work section needs deeper comparison to prior studies (e.g., Kazama & Torisawa 2007) and other Wikipedia-based NER systems to clarify distinctions.

- Add a running example in Section 3.3 to clarify the modified Tsai & Roth model description.  
- Try preliminary experiments combining orthogonal approaches (Täckström et al., Nothman et al.) and compare against a strong projection baseline for broader context.
