**Summary**

This paper proposes a novel approach to cross-lingual Named Entity Recognition (NER) using a cross-lingual wikifier to disambiguate words and phrases to English Wikipedia entries. The model is trained on a source language and can be applied to any target language with a Wikipedia presence. The authors demonstrate the effectiveness of their approach through experiments on a wide range of languages, including low-resource languages.

**Main Contributions**

1. **Novel Cross-Lingual Wikifier Features**: The authors propose a new set of features that map target-language phrases to English Wikipedia articles via a cross-lingual wikifier. These features are truly delexicalized and can be used to transfer an NER model directly.
2. **Improved Performance on Low-Resource Languages**: The authors demonstrate significant improvements over strong baselines on low-resource languages, including Turkish, Tagalog, Yoruba, Bengali, and Tamil.
3. **Analysis of Wikipedia Size and Quality of Wikifier Features**: The authors analyze the impact of Wikipedia size on the quality of wikifier features and show that smaller Wikipedia sizes result in worse features.

**Strengths**

1. **Effective Use of Wikipedia**: The authors leverage Wikipedia as a source of information for each language, which allows their model to generalize to new languages.
2. **Robust Performance on Low-Resource Languages**: The authors demonstrate that their approach can perform well on low-resource languages, which is a challenging task in NER.
3. **Extensive Experiments**: The authors conduct thorough experiments on a wide range of languages, including monolingual and cross-lingual settings.

**Weaknesses**

1. **Dependence on Cross-Lingual Wikifier**: The authors' method strongly depends on the cross-lingual wikifier, which may not always be accurate.
2. **Entities Not Present on Wikipedia**: The authors do not address how entities not present on Wikipedia are affected by their method.
3. **Over-Reliance on Lexical Features**: The authors' model may over-rely on lexical features, especially in the direct-transfer setting.

**Questions to Authors**

1. How do you plan to address the issue of entities not present on Wikipedia?
2. Can you provide more analysis on the impact of cross-lingual wikifier errors on NER prediction errors?
3. How do you plan to regularize feature classes to prevent over-reliance on lexical features?
