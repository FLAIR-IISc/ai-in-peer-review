**Summary of the Paper**

This paper proposes a language-independent method for cross-lingual Named Entity Recognition (NER) using a cross-lingual wikifier to disambiguate words and phrases to the English Wikipedia. The method is based on the idea that the typing information from Wikipedia categories and FreeBase types can be used as language-independent features for NER. The authors evaluate their method on a wide range of languages in both monolingual and cross-lingual settings and show significant improvements over strong baselines.

**Main Contributions**

1. **Novel Multilingual NER Feature**: The paper proposes a new multilingual NER feature that maps target-language phrases to English Wikipedia articles via a cross-lingual Wikifier.
2. **Improved Performance**: The authors show that their method improves performance in both monolingual and direct-transfer (English→target) settings.
3. **Thorough Experiments**: The paper presents thorough experiments on a wide range of languages, including low-resource and non-Latin languages.

**Strengths**

1. **Innovative Approach**: The paper proposes a novel approach to cross-lingual NER that leverages the power of Wikipedia-derived features.
2. **Strong Experimental Results**: The authors present strong experimental results that demonstrate the effectiveness of their method.
3. **Thorough Analysis**: The paper provides a thorough analysis of the results, including an examination of the impact of Wikipedia size on the quality of wikifier features.

**Weaknesses**

1. **Dependence on Cross-Lingual Wikifier**: The method strongly depends on the cross-lingual Wikifier, which may be prone to errors.
2. **Handling of Entities Not Present on Wikipedia**: The paper does not provide a clear explanation of how entities not present on Wikipedia are affected by the method.
3. **Over-Reliance on Lexical Features**: The method may be over-reliant on lexical features, which can lead to poor performance in certain languages (e.g., Tamil and Bengali).

**Questions to Authors**

1. How do you plan to address the issue of entities not present on Wikipedia?
2. Can you provide more information on the error analysis of the cross-lingual Wikifier and how it affects the NER prediction errors?
3. Have you considered regularizing feature classes differently to prevent over-reliance on lexical features?

**Additional Comments**

The paper is well-written, and the contributions are clear. However, the authors could benefit from addressing the concerns mentioned above to strengthen their method and provide a more comprehensive analysis of their results.
