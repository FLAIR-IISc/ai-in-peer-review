- Proposes a cross-lingual wikifier to map target-language phrases to English Wikipedia entries for cross-lingual NER transfer.
- Trains on a source language and applies to any target language with a Wikipedia presence, showing effectiveness on many languages including low-resource ones.

- Introduces novel delexicalized features from target phrases mapped to English Wikipedia for direct NER model transfer.
- Shows significant performance gains over strong baselines on low-resource languages (e.g., Turkish, Tagalog, Yoruba, Bengali, Tamil).
- Analyzes how Wikipedia size affects the quality of wikifier features, finding smaller Wikipedias yield worse features.

- Effectively leverages Wikipedia to generalize NER models across languages.
- Demonstrates robust performance on challenging low-resource languages.
- Provides extensive experiments across monolingual and cross-lingual settings.
