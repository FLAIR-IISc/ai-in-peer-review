- Key contribution: proposes a language‑independent NER model using a cross‑lingual wikifier to generate features that allow direct transfer without parallel corpora or target annotations.  
- Empirical result: shows improvements on high‑resource (CoNLL) and multiple low‑resource languages, including benefits from multi‑source training.  
- Analysis insight: demonstrates correlation between Wikipedia size/interlanguage links and wikifier feature quality.  
- Strength: clear, coherent presentation with well‑structured motivation, methodology, and experimental details.  
- Strength: thorough evaluation across nine languages (five low‑resource) with strong baselines, showing wikifier utility when annotations are absent.  
- Strength: practical, simple, and scalable approach requiring only a Wikipedia dump, suitable for real‑world low‑resource scenarios.  
- Weakness: primarily incremental—builds on existing cross‑lingual wikification (Tsai & Roth, 2016) with limited methodological novelty.  
- Weakness: missing comparison to a strong projection‑based baseline, limiting completeness of evaluation.  
- Weakness: no statistical significance testing reported for reported improvements (e.g., Table 4).  
- Weakness: related work discussion is insufficient, lacking detailed comparison to Kazama & Torisawa (2007) and other Wikipedia‑based NER systems.  
- Weakness: unexplored additional features (e.g., POS tags) and no running example to clarify wikifier modifications in Section 3.3.  
- Question: why was a strong projection‑based baseline omitted and how would the method compare?  
- Question: why do language‑pair results favor Dutch→Spanish and Spanish→Yoruba—are linguistic/cultural factors explaining this?  
- Question: would adding POS tag features improve performance, particularly for low‑resource languages?  
- Additional comments: paper needs proofreading to fix typos/unclear sentences, should include a running example for Section 3.3, and should mention alternative projection methods (e.g., Peirsman & Pado 2009; Vulic & Moens 2013).
