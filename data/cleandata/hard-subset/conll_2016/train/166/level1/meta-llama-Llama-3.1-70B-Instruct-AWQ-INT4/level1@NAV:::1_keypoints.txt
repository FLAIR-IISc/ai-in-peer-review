- Evaluation: tested on many languages including low-resource ones and shows significant improvements over strong baselines.
- Contribution: introduces cross-lingual wikifier features effective in both monolingual and cross-lingual settings.
- Contribution: proposes a language-independent NER model applicable to all languages in Wikipedia using only a Wikipedia dump.
- Contribution: evaluates on low-resource languages (Turkish, Tagalog, Yoruba, Bengali, Tamil) and outperforms existing approaches.

- Strength: method is robust to low-resource languages with limited annotated data.
- Strength: achieves significant gains over strong baselines.
