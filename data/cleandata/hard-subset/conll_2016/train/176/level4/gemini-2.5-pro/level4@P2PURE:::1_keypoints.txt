- Proposes unifying multiple sentence-pair scoring tasks under a single semantic-relation framework and training one general neural model.  
- Reviewer finds the idea compelling, timely, and likely to stimulate discussion and future work.  
- Recommends conditional acceptance contingent on addressing major concerns and completing the manuscript.  
- Major: first results table is incomplete and must be finalized.  
- Major: experimental results are numerous but under-discussed—need much more interpretation and analysis.  
- Major: abstract promises a new challenging dataset that is not presented in the paper; resolve this discrepancy.  
- Major: model performance varies widely across tasks; paper needs analysis of why some tasks succeed and others fail and whether variance reflects modeling limits or fundamental task differences.  
- Major: include illustrative dataset examples, including challenging instances the model misclassifies.  
- Major: clarify recommendation of the Ubu.RNN transfer model—whether it’s due to dataset nature or simply larger size, and how to disentangle these factors.  
- Minor language/typo issues: misuse of "weigh/weighed" vs. "weight/weighted", "sentene" misspelling, "interpunction" vs. "punctuation", and "world overlap" should be "word overlap".
