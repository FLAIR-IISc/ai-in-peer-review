- Proposes unifying multiple sentence-pair tasks (Answer Sentence Selection, RTE, Paraphrasing) as a single "semantic relations between sentences" problem.  
- Main contribution: a unified framework and standardized evaluation methodology, including MRR for next-utterance ranking.  
- Introduces two new datasets (yodaqa/large2470 and wqmprop) to address limitations of the wang dataset.  
- Provides initial evidence of positive cross-task transfer learning (Ubuntu Dialogue → other tasks).  
- Offers a comprehensive overview and benchmarking of neural architectures (RNNs, CNNs, attention models) against non-neural baselines.  
- Strength: rigorous benchmarking and dataset analysis that could standardize comparative evaluation in the field.  
- Strength: dataset contributions that improve robustness and reproducibility over the wang benchmark.  
- Strength: transfer learning results that support feasibility of general semantic representations.  
- Weakness: experimental results and discussion are incomplete and under-analyzed, with missing table entries.  
- Weakness: the claimed "more challenging" dataset promise is not fully substantiated; difficulty/novelty unclear.  
- Weakness: task heterogeneity raises doubts about the viability of a single unified modeling approach and needs deeper discussion.  
- Weakness: lacks error analysis and illustrative examples of problematic sentence pairs.  
- Weakness: minor language and terminology errors detract from manuscript polish.  
- Questions for authors: analyze factors causing task-specific performance differences and clarify whether variations stem from models or inherent task differences.  
- Questions for authors: explain how the new datasets achieve greater difficulty and provide prototypical/challenging example sentence pairs.  
- Questions for authors: perform further experiments on source dataset size effects for transfer learning (e.g., Ubuntu Dialogue scale vs target gains).  
- Additional comments: ensure consistent terminology, proofread for language issues, and include the dataset-sts framework URL.  
- Recommendation: accept pending revisions addressing the above weaknesses and questions.
