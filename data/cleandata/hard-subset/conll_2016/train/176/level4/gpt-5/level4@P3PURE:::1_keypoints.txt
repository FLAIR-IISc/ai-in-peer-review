- Novel contribution: reframes many sentence-pair scoring tasks as modeling semantic relations between two sentences and explores learning generally applicable neural models across them.

- Conditional recommendation: support acceptance only if the authors address the following major issues.

1) Complete and present the missing entries in the first results table.

2) Provide substantially more interpretation and analysis of the many individual results.

3) Resolve the abstract’s promise of a new, more challenging dataset—either include it or remove the claim.

4) Investigate why performance varies across tasks and discuss whether failures reflect modeling limitations or genuine task dissimilarities; justify grouping tasks into a unified framework.

5) Add example instances from the datasets, including prototypical pairs and difficult-to-classify cases.

6) Explain why Ubu.RNN is recommended for new tasks—due to model generality or dataset size—and describe how to empirically distinguish these causes.
