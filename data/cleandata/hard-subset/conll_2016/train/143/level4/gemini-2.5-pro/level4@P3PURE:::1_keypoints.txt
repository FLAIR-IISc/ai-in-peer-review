- Paper introduces four multilingual embedding methods (multiCluster, multiCCA, multiSkip, Translation Invariance) and a modified QVEC evaluation metric using CCA to be rotation-/linear-invariant.
- multiCluster: maps words to multilingual clusters via a dictionary and learns cluster embeddings used as word embeddings.
- multiCCA: extends bilingual CCA to multilingual by projecting monolingual spaces into an English anchor space using bilingual dictionaries.
- multiSkip: generalizes bilingual skip-based models to jointly use all parallel corpora in a single objective.
- Translation Invariance: low-rank PMI decomposition with bilingual alignment frequencies; novelty relative to Gardner et al. is unclear and may be limited to bilingual use.
- Modified QVEC: uses CCA to align embeddings with linguistic feature dimensions and is extended to the multilingual setting.
- Positive: paper is well-written and clearly presented.
- Major issue 1: unclear novelty of the translation invariance approach compared to Gardner et al.; need explicit explanation if the contribution is merely multilingual extension.
- Major issue 2: concern about super-sense annotation for cross-lingual evaluation—feature intersection across languages may be very small; footnote 9’s brief mention is insufficient.
- Major issue 3: relationship between corpus coverage and downstream performance (Table 2) is unclear—e.g., multiCluster vs multiCCA have very different coverage but similar parsing scores, complicating fair comparison.
- Major issue 4: inconsistent/inconclusive intrinsic results (Table 3)—multilingual methods often not best; surprising that bilingual invariance dominates word translation and multiCluster excels on extrinsic tasks despite losing fine-grained semantics.
- Q&A 1: What is the effect of keeping embeddings fixed during dependency parser training, and how much do pre-trained embeddings improve over random initialization in the LSTM parser?
- Q&A 2: Are the results in Table 1 averaged over all 17 embeddings from Section 5.1?
- Q&A 3: What are the conceptual or empirical advantages of joint multi-Skip versus a pipeline of bilingual embedding training followed by multi-CCA projection?
- Q&A 4: Given dictionaries are extracted from parallel corpora or Google Translate, did the authors test with noisier, real-world multilingual lexicons to assess robustness?
