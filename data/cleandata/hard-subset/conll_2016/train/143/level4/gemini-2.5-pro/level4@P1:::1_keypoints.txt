- Proposes two estimation methods (multiCluster, multiCCA) that use monolingual corpora + bilingual dictionaries to learn multilingual embeddings and compares them to multiSkip and translation-invariance.
- Introduces multiQVEC-CCA, an intrinsic evaluation metric using CCA that is rotation-invariant, normalized, and better correlated with downstream tasks.
- Releases an evaluation web portal plus data and trained models to enable standardized, reproducible multilingual embedding evaluation.
- Addresses a practical problem by scaling methods to 59 languages using resources (dictionaries, monolingual data) available for many languages.
- multiQVEC-CCA is principled and empirically validated, correcting theoretical flaws of QVEC and showing stronger correlations with downstream performance.
- Open-source portal and released resources lower barriers to entry and improve reproducibility and fair benchmarking.
- Presents a comprehensive empirical comparison across four methods, nine metrics, and experiments on 12 and 59 language sets.
- Experimental results are inconsistent and puzzling: translation-invariance often outperforms the proposed methods, notably on word translation.
- Extrinsic task results are counterintuitive (simple multiCluster performs best), suggesting the extrinsic evaluation setup may be insensitive to semantic nuance.
- Unclear whether the strong translation-invariance baseline is a novel multilingual extension or a direct application of the prior bilingual method.
- multiQVEC-CCA’s reliance on supersense annotations raises scalability/applicability concerns for many languages lacking such resources.
- Question: quantify impact of freezing embeddings in dependency parsing — loss vs fine-tuning and gain over random initialization.
- Question: how sensitive are method rankings to differing vocabulary coverage; do conclusions change when evaluating on vocabulary intersections?
- Question: did you try curated multilingual lexicons (Wiktionary, PanLex) instead of automatically extracted or Google-translated dictionaries?
- Question: clarify how correlation coefficients in Table 1 were computed — across the 17 embedding models per intrinsic/extrinsic task?
- Question: does anchoring all languages to English in multiCCA create a bottleneck/bias for typologically distant languages; have multi-anchor or inter-language transform alternatives been considered?
