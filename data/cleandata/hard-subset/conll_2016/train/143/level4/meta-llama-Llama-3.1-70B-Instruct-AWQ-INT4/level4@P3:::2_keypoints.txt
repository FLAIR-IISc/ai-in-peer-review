- Proposes two dictionary-based multilingual embedding methods: MultiCluster (clustering-motivated) and MultiCCA (CCA-motivated).
- Introduces multiQVEC-CCA, an improved intrinsic evaluation replacing QVEC's correlation with CCA and adding multilingual evaluation.
- Develops a web portal for uploading and evaluating multilingual embeddings across many tasks.
- Trains and evaluates embeddings for 59 languages and shows the proposed methods sometimes outperform baselines.
- Paper is well-written, well-organized, and meticulously documented.
- Work is timely and practically valuable to the NLP community.
- Main concern: breadth over depth—insufficient in-depth analysis of results and methodological choices.
- Requests deeper discussion on conflicting MultiCluster results between 59- and 12-language setups.
- Requests analysis of the effect of estimation parameters and design decisions in MultiCluster and MultiCCA.
- Criticizes limited comparison to other state-of-the-art methods and overall limited result analysis.
- Notes minor errors (equations overflowing margins, inconsistent citation of related work).
- Suggests adding more related work, implementation details (software/hardware), and real-world application examples.
