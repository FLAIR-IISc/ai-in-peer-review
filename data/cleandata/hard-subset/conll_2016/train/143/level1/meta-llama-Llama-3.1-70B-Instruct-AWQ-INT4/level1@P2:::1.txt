**Summary of the Paper**

The paper introduces two novel methods, multiCluster and multiCCA, for estimating word embeddings in multiple languages using dictionaries and monolingual data. These methods do not require parallel data. The authors also propose a new evaluation method, multiQVECCCA, which correlates better with two downstream tasks (text categorization and parsing) compared to previous evaluation methods. Additionally, they describe a web portal for evaluating multilingual word embeddings, which facilitates further research in this area.

**Main Contributions**

1. **Dictionary-based estimation methods for multilingual word embeddings**: The authors propose two novel methods, multiCluster and multiCCA, which use dictionaries and monolingual data to estimate word embeddings in multiple languages. These methods do not require parallel data.
2. **Improved evaluation metric for multilingual word embeddings**: The authors propose a new evaluation method, multiQVECCCA, which addresses the limitations of previous evaluation methods and correlates better with downstream tasks.
3. **Web portal for evaluating multilingual word embeddings**: The authors describe a web portal that facilitates the evaluation of multilingual word embeddings using a suite of intrinsic and extrinsic metrics.
