- Proposes multiCluster and multiCCA dictionary-based methods to learn multilingual embeddings without parallel data.  
- Introduces multiQVEC-CCA, an intrinsic evaluation metric that corrects sensitivity to linear transforms and unnormalized scores.  
- Provides a web portal for evaluating multilingual embeddings with a suite of intrinsic and extrinsic metrics.  
- Demonstrates methods and metrics via experiments on 59 languages.  
- multiCluster lacks detailed analysis and performs poorly in some experiments.  
- multiCCA depends on the English vector space, which may not be optimal for all languages.  
- Paper does not thoroughly evaluate the web portal’s usability or effectiveness.  
- Requests more details on multiCluster’s strengths and weaknesses.  
- Asks how the authors will address multiCCA’s reliance on English as the shared space.  
- Requests additional information on the web portal’s design, implementation, and evaluation.  
- Paper is well-written and easy to follow.  
- Experiments are comprehensive and well-designed.  
- Web portal is a valuable resource for the research community.
