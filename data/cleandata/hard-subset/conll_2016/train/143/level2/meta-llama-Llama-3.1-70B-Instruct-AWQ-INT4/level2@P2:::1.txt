This paper introduces new methods for estimating and evaluating word embeddings in multiple languages. The authors propose two dictionary-based methods, multiCluster and multiCCA, for estimating multilingual embeddings, and a new evaluation method, multiQVEC-CCA, which addresses some limitations of previous intrinsic evaluation metrics. The paper also describes a web portal for evaluating multilingual embeddings.

The main contributions of this paper are:

1. The proposal of two novel dictionary-based methods, multiCluster and multiCCA, for estimating multilingual embeddings.
2. The introduction of a new evaluation method, multiQVEC-CCA, which improves upon previous intrinsic evaluation metrics.
3. The development of a web portal for evaluating multilingual embeddings.

Strengths:

1. The authors provide a thorough evaluation of their proposed methods, including a comparison with existing methods and an analysis of the correlations between intrinsic and extrinsic evaluation metrics.
2. The paper introduces a new evaluation method, multiQVEC-CCA, which addresses some limitations of previous intrinsic evaluation metrics.
3. The web portal for evaluating multilingual embeddings is a valuable resource for the research community.

Weaknesses:

1. The authors do not provide a detailed analysis of the computational complexity of their proposed methods.
2. The paper assumes that the bilingual dictionaries used for training the multilingual embeddings are of high quality, but the authors do not provide any analysis of the impact of dictionary quality on the performance of the methods.
3. The authors do not provide a detailed comparison of their proposed methods with other existing methods for estimating multilingual embeddings.

Questions to Authors:

1. Can you provide a more detailed analysis of the computational complexity of your proposed methods?
2. How do you plan to address the issue of dictionary quality in future work?
3. Can you provide a more detailed comparison of your proposed methods with other existing methods for estimating multilingual embeddings?
