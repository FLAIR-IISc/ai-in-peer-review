- Introduces dictionary-based methods (multiCluster, multiCCA) for estimating multilingual embeddings in a shared space without parallel data.  
- Proposes multiQVEC-CCA, a new intrinsic evaluation metric that better correlates with downstream tasks.  
- Provides a web portal for evaluating multilingual embeddings with intrinsic and extrinsic metrics.  
- Novel methods that avoid needing parallel data, addressing a notable limitation in prior work.  
- Thorough empirical evaluation across multiple intrinsic and extrinsic metrics demonstrating effectiveness.  
- Web portal is a useful community resource for benchmarking and comparison.  
- Assumes high-quality bilingual dictionaries, which may not hold in practice.  
- Lacks detailed analysis of computational complexity and scalability for large-scale applications.  
- Relies heavily on supersense tag annotations, which are unavailable for many languages.  
- How will the authors handle low-quality or noisy bilingual dictionaries?  
- Can the authors provide detailed computational complexity analysis and possible optimizations?  
- How can the approach be extended to languages lacking supersense tag annotations?  
- Overall, the paper makes a significant contribution but should address dictionary quality and computational concerns.
