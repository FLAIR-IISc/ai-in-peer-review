- Proposes two dictionary-based methods (multiCluster, multiCCA) for multilingual word embeddings without parallel data.  
- Introduces multiQVEC-CCA, a multilingual extension of QVEC as a new evaluation metric.  
- Demonstrates effectiveness across 59 languages, claiming improvements over existing methods.  
- Provides a web portal offering intrinsic and extrinsic evaluation tools for multilingual embeddings.  
- multiCluster and multiCCA are reported to outperform prior methods on various evaluation tasks.  
- multiQVEC-CCA shows better correlation with downstream tasks than existing intrinsic metrics.  
- The web portal standardizes and simplifies evaluation for researchers.  
- Evaluation on downstream tasks is limited to document classification and dependency parsing.  
- Methods depend on bilingual dictionaries, which may be unavailable for some language pairs.  
- Potential computational expense, especially for large-scale datasets.  
- Asks how methods perform on other downstream tasks (e.g., MT, QA).  
- Asks whether methods can use other resources (monolingual corpora, multilingual dictionaries) and how to reduce computational complexity.
