- Proposes two dictionary-based methods, multiCluster and multiCCA, for estimating multilingual embeddings.  
- Introduces multiQVEC-CCA, a new intrinsic evaluation metric that improves on previous metrics.  
- Develops a web portal for evaluating multilingual embeddings.  
- Thorough evaluation comparing proposed methods with existing approaches and analyzing intrinsic–extrinsic metric correlations.  
- multiQVEC-CCA addresses limitations of prior intrinsic evaluation methods.  
- The web portal is a useful resource for the research community.  
- Lacks detailed analysis of the computational complexity of the proposed methods.  
- Assumes high-quality bilingual dictionaries without analyzing how dictionary quality affects performance.  
- Does not provide a detailed comparison with other existing multilingual embedding estimation methods.  
- Requests a more detailed computational complexity analysis.  
- Asks how the authors will address dictionary quality in future work.  
- Requests a more thorough comparison with other existing methods.  
- Paper is well-written and easy to follow.
