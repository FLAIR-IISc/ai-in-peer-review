- Proposes two dictionary-based multilingual embedding methods: multiCluster and multiCCA.  
- Introduces multiQVEC-CCA, a multilingual extension of QVEC for evaluation.  
- Provides a web portal for evaluating multilingual embeddings across tasks.  
- Novel methods that could improve multilingual NLP performance.  
- Extends QVEC to offer a more robust multilingual evaluation metric.  
- Web portal facilitates research by providing a suite of evaluation tasks.  
- Evaluation is limited to a small set of languages and tasks, needing broader testing.  
- Lacks comprehensive comparisons to existing baseline methods.  
- No theoretical analysis explaining why the methods work or how to improve them.  
- Request for details on construction of the bilingual dictionaries used.  
- Ask how the methods will be extended to more languages and tasks.  
- Request a more comprehensive empirical comparison to existing baselines.
