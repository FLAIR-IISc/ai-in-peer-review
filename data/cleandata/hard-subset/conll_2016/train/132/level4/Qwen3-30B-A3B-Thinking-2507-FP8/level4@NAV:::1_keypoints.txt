- The phrase “separating words from a marginal distribution” is undefined and unclear.
- The term “co-adaptation” is introduced without definition or explanation.
- The phrase “if we only included structure up to this point” refers to unspecified structural elements and is vague.
- Typographical error: “It’s similarity” should be “its similarity.”
- Footnote 1 breaches anonymity and should be removed or anonymized.
- No rigorous evaluation is provided; presenting example clusters is insufficient for the current state of the field.
- Figure 2 looks quantitative but is only described via an overly detailed caption rather than proper analysis.
