- Major lack of evaluation: relying on example clusters is insufficient; Figure 2 appears quantitative but its description is buried in an overly long caption.
- Conclusion overreaches on word analogy performance, showing only a few examples rather than a comprehensive evaluation.
- Citation issue: Chang's work is cited vaguely ("Advances in ...") and needs the full, specific venue information.
