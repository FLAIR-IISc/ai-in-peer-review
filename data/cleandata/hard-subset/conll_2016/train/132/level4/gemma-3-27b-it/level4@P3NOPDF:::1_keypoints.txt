- Technical presentation is unclear; Section 2 needs a major rewrite for readers familiar with word2vec and LDA.  
- High-level, intuitive explanation of the proposed model and integration of word2vec/LDA is missing.  
- Intro paragraph 3 is vague: “deriving a new approximation” lacks explanation of what is being approximated.  
- No justification for claimed development time or why feature evaluation is easier.  
- Key design choices are not motivated, e.g., using the same word vectors for pivot and target vs. standard word2vec.  
- Phrase “separate words from a marginal distribution” is unclear about which distribution and what “separation” means.  
- The concept of “co-adaptation” is introduced without definition or explanation.  
- Ambiguous phrase “If we only included structure up to this point” lacks context about which structure is meant.  
- Minor grammatical error: “it’s similarity” should be “its similarity.”  
- Violation of review anonymity in Footnote 1 must be removed.  
- Evaluation is insufficient: only example clusters shown and no proper experimental setup or established metrics.  
- Figure 2 appears to contain quantitative results but these are only in the caption and not discussed in the text.  
- Claim that the model “solves word analogies” is overblown given a few selective examples.  
- Citation for Chang et al. is incomplete; full bibliographic details are required.  
- Overall: promising idea but presentation and evaluation are inadequate; thorough revision and robust experiments are necessary.
