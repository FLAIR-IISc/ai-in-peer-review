- Main weakness: technical presentation is unclear, especially how the models are integrated.
- Section 2 needs substantial revision to clearly convey integration at a high level.
- Explanation fails to provide necessary understanding even for readers familiar with word2vec and LDA.
- Third paragraph of the introduction is unintelligible.
- Several concepts are undefined (e.g., "approximation of what?", "time consuming to develop prototypes?", "easier to evaluate features?", "separate words from a marginal distribution?", "co-adaptation?").
- Numerous ambiguous phrases (e.g., "If we only included structure up to this point…", "it's similarity") and a grammatical error ("its").
- Footnote 1 compromises reviewer anonymity.
- Evaluation is not rigorous; presenting example clusters is insufficient.
- Figure 2 seems to show quantitative results but these are only in the caption and not discussed in the text.
- Claim that the model "solves word analogies" is an overstatement supported only by a limited set of curated examples.
- Citation for Chang is incomplete, missing the full conference or journal name.
