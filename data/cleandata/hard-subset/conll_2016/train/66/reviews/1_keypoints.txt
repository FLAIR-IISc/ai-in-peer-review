- Paper presents a Stack LSTM parser combining Henderson et al.'s joint syntactic/semantic transition system with Dyer et al.'s stack LSTM, evaluated on CoNLL 2008/2009.
- The combined approach yields interesting/competitive results compared to existing joint systems on those shared tasks.
- The paper is well-written, clearly explained, and has a strong related-work section.
- Methodology is sound and well-justified, with clear system description and justified hyperparameters.
- Minor concern about the Chinese embeddings — very good embeddings may drive performance more than model innovations.
- Lacks methodological originality (builds on prior Henderson and Dyer work) though the integration was awaited and is valuable.
