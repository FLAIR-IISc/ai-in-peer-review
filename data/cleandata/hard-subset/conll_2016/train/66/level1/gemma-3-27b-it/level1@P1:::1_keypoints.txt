- Competitive empirical performance with clear comparisons to joint and pipeline baselines.
- Efficiency: linear-time greedy decoding with reported fast runtime (177.6s for CoNLL 2009 English).
- Clear and concise presentation, aided by helpful figures and tables.
- Open-source implementation promised to support reproducibility.
