- Proposes a Stack LSTM parser combining Henderson et al.'s joint syntactic/semantic transition system with Dyer et al.'s stack LSTM architecture.  
- Achieves notable results on CoNLL 2008 and 2009 shared tasks compared to prior joint systems.  
- Paper is well-written, clearly explained, and provides a thorough related work overview.  
- Methodology is sound; system description is clear and hyperparameters are well-justified.  
- Discussion is engaging and results are noteworthy, making the contribution compelling.  
- Minor concern: high-quality Chinese embeddings may be more informative than model sophistication.  
- Primary criticism: limited originality — mainly a combination/implementation of existing ideas from prior work.  
- Nevertheless valuable and timely implementation that was overdue for the community.
