Presents a Stack LSTM parser combining Henderson et al.'s joint syntactic/semantic transition system with Dyer et al.'s stack LSTM, achieving strong results on CoNLL-2008/2009.

Paper is well-written, clearly explained, and thoroughly grounded in related work.

Results are noteworthy and the methodology is sound.

Minor concern about the quality of Chinese embeddings, implying high-quality embeddings can matter more than sophisticated models.

System description is clear, hyperparameters are well-justified, and the discussion is engaging.

Main criticism: the work lacks originality, drawing heavily on established prior work, though the contribution is overdue and welcome.
