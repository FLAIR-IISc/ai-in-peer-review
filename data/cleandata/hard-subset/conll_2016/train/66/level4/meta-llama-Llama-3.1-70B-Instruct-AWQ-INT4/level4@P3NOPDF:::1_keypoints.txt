- Proposes a Stack LSTM parser combining Henderson et al.'s joint syntactic/semantic transition parsing with Dyer et al.'s stack LSTM approach.  
- Integration yields strong empirical results, especially compared to joint systems on CoNLL 2008 and 2009.  
- Paper is well-written, clearly explained, and situates itself well in related work.  
- Related work discussion is thorough and provides a solid foundation for the method.  
- Methodology and experimental design are sound, with clear system description and justified hyperparameters.  
- Discussion section is engaging and offers useful implications of the results.  
- Minor concern: Chinese embeddings may indicate that high-quality embeddings can outperform model complexity.  
- Originality is limited since core ideas build on prior work, but the implementation is a valuable and needed contribution.
