- Proposes a Stack LSTM parser combining Henderson et al.'s joint syntactic/semantic transition system with Dyer et al.'s stack LSTM architecture.
- Shows intriguing, competitive results versus joint systems on the CoNLL 2008 and 2009 shared tasks.
- Paper is well-written, clearly explained, and thoroughly grounded in related work.
- Methodology is robust; minor concern that Chinese results indicate high-quality embeddings can outweigh model sophistication.
- System description is clear, hyperparameters are well-justified, and the discussion is engaging.
- Main criticism: limited originality since it builds on Henderson and Dyer, though the implementation/combination is timely and valuable.
