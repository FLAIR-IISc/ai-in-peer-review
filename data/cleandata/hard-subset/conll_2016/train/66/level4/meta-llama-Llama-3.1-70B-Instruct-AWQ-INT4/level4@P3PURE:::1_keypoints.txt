- Proposes a Stack LSTM parser combining Henderson et al.’s joint syntactic/semantic transition system with Dyer et al.’s stack LSTM architecture.  
- Shows competitive/interesting results on CoNLL 2008 and 2009 shared tasks.  
- Paper is well-written, clearly explained, and thoroughly researched with a comprehensive related-work overview.  
- Methodology is sound and experimentally well justified.  
- System description is clear and hyperparameters are well-motivated.  
- Results are noteworthy and the discussion is engaging.  
- Minor concern: Chinese embeddings may be overly influential, implying high-quality embeddings can outweigh model sophistication.  
- Primary criticism: the approach lacks originality, primarily combining existing ideas from prior work.  
- Despite limited novelty, the implementation and application of this combination are valuable and necessary.
