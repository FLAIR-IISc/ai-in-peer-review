- Proposes a Stack LSTM parser combining Henderson et al.'s joint syntactic/semantic transition-based parsing with Dyer et al.'s stack LSTM syntactic parser.  
- Integration yields interesting, noteworthy results, particularly on CoNLL 2008 and 2009 shared tasks.  
- Paper is well-written, clearly explained, and thoroughly grounded in related work.  
- Methodology is sound; system description is clear and hyperparameters are well-justified.  
- Minor concern about Chinese embeddings suggests high-quality embeddings can outweigh model sophistication.  
- Discussion is engaging and provides valuable insights.  
- Lacks originality, as it builds on previously established frameworks (Henderson and Dyer).  
- Nonetheless, the parser's creation was anticipated and is a welcome contribution.  
- Overall strengths outweigh limitations; the paper is a valuable addition to the field.
