Paper introduces a Stack LSTM parser combining Henderson et al.'s joint syntactic/semantic transition system with Dyer et al.'s stack LSTM architecture.  
Evaluated on CoNLL 2008 and 2009, showing promising results and outperforming existing joint systems.  
Paper quality: clear writing, thorough explanations, comprehensive related work, and genuinely interesting results.  
Methodology is robust; system description and hyperparameter choices are clear and well-justified.  
Minor concern: Chinese results imply high-quality embeddings may matter more than model sophistication.  
Primary limitation: limited novelty since Henderson et al. and Dyer et al. laid much of the groundwork.  
Nonetheless, the development is a natural, valuable step and the paper successfully achieves its goals.
