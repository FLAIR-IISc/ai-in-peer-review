- Proposes a Stack LSTM parser that combines Henderson et al.'s joint syntactic/semantic transition system with Dyer et al.'s stack LSTM architecture.  
- Shows promising performance versus joint systems on the CoNLL 2008 and 2009 shared tasks.  
- Overall assessment: strong paper—well-written, clearly explained, and demonstrates solid understanding of related work.  
- Methodology is sound; system description is clear, hyperparameter choices are justified, and discussion is insightful.  
- Minor concern: Chinese embeddings quality could be a confounding factor; better embeddings might explain some gains.  
- Limited originality: largely builds on prior work (Henderson, Dyer) rather than proposing a fundamentally new method, but is a natural and valuable next step.
