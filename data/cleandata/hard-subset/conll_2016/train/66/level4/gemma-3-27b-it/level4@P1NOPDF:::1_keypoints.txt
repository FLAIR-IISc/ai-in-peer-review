- Proposes a Stack LSTM parser combining Henderson et al.'s joint syntactic/semantic transition system with Dyer et al.'s stack LSTM.  
- Achieves promising results compared to joint systems on CoNLL 2008 and 2009 shared tasks.  
- Paper is strong: well-written, clearly explained, with a thorough related work section and interesting results.  
- Methodology is sound, though there is a minor concern that Chinese embeddings quality might affect gains more than the model itself.  
- System description is clear, hyperparameters are well-justified, and the discussion is engaging.  
- Not fundamentally novel—builds directly on prior work by Henderson and Dyer—but represents a natural, well-implemented progression.
