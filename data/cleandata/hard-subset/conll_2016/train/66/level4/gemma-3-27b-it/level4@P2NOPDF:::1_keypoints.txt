- Introduces a Stack LSTM parser that combines Henderson et al.'s joint syntactic/semantic transition system with Dyer et al.'s Stack LSTM architecture.  
- Demonstrates competitive results on the CoNLL 2008 and 2009 shared tasks.  
- Strong contribution: paper is exceptionally well-written with clear motivation and implementation details.  
- Related work section is comprehensive and effectively contextualizes the approach.  
- Results are compelling and warrant attention.  
- Methodology is robust; system description provides sufficient detail for reproducibility.  
- Hyperparameter choices are well-justified and show thoughtful experimental design.  
- Discussion of results is insightful and offers valuable analysis.  
- Minor reservation about the potential impact of the Chinese embeddings used—better embeddings might yield larger gains.  
- Not fundamentally novel (builds on Henderson and Dyer), but valuable for effectively integrating existing components.  
- The design is a logical and necessary next step; authors successfully realize and demonstrate its efficacy.
