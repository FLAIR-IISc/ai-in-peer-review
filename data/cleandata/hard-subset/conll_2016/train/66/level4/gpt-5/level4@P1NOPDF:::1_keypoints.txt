- Proposes a Stack-LSTM parser combining Henderson et al.'s joint transition system with Dyer et al.'s stack-LSTM architecture.  
- Reports strong performance compared to previous joint syntax–semantics systems on CoNLL-2008 and CoNLL-2009.  
- The paper is clearly written, well explained, and grounded in solid related work.  
- Methodology appears sound; system description, hyperparameter choices, and discussion are thoughtful and informative.  
- Minor concern that very strong Chinese embeddings may account for improvements more than architectural innovation.  
- Main reservation: limited originality—primarily an effective integration of existing methods rather than a novel technique.  
- Despite that, the implementation and evaluation of this combination are valuable and timely for the community.
