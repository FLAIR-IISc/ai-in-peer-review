- Proposes a stack-LSTM parser that integrates Henderson et al.’s joint syntactic–semantic transition system with Dyer et al.’s stack-LSTM architecture.  
- Achieves strong empirical results compared to prior joint systems on CoNLL 2008 and 2009 shared tasks.  
- Paper is well written, clearly explained, and the related work is thorough.  
- Methodology is sound and described in sufficient detail.  
- Hyperparameters are well justified and the discussion is thoughtful.  
- Minor concern: Chinese improvements may depend heavily on choice of embeddings, so embeddings might drive gains there.  
- Main critique: limited originality—the work primarily integrates existing ideas rather than introducing a new paradigm.  
- Despite limited novelty, the integration is overdue and executed convincingly and carefully.
