- Introduces a Stack LSTM parser that jointly predicts syntactic and semantic dependencies by combining Henderson et al.'s transition system with Dyer et al.'s stack LSTMs.  
- The combined approach yields compelling results on the CoNLL 2008 and 2009 shared tasks.  
- Paper is very strong: clearly written, well motivated, with thorough related work and interesting results.  
- Methodology appears solid; system description is easy to follow, hyperparameter choices are justified, and the discussion is thoughtful.  
- Minor caveat: performance on Chinese suggests high-quality embeddings may sometimes drive gains more than architectural changes.  
- Main reservation: limited novelty because key foundations come from Henderson et al. and Dyer et al.  
- Nonetheless, unifying these ideas was overdue and the realized parser is effective.
