- Paper introduces a Stack LSTM parser that combines Henderson et al.'s joint syntactic/semantic transition system with Dyer et al.'s stack LSTM, applied to CoNLL 2008/2009.
- Results are interesting and competitive compared to existing joint systems on the CoNLL shared tasks.
- The paper is well written, clearly explained, and situates itself solidly in related work.
- Methodology is sound; system description is clear, hyperparameters are justified, and the discussion is engaging.
- Minor concern: unusually strong Chinese embeddings suggest embeddings might drive performance more than model innovations.
- Main reservation is limited originality—work combines existing methods rather than introducing a fundamentally new approach.
- Positive overall judgment: community interest in such a parser makes this realized combination valuable.
