- Combines Henderson et al.'s joint syntactic/semantic transition system with Dyer et al.'s stack LSTM, producing a stack LSTM parser.  
- Achieves promising, competitive results on CoNLL 2008 and 2009 shared tasks.  
- Clearly written and well-explained paper.  
- Thorough related work coverage.  
- Methodology is sound; system description is clear and hyperparameters well justified.  
- Minor concern that very strong Chinese embeddings may overshadow model sophistication.  
- Discussion is engaging.  
- Limited originality: builds directly on prior Henderson and Dyer work rather than introducing a novel method, but successfully realizes a wanted parser.
