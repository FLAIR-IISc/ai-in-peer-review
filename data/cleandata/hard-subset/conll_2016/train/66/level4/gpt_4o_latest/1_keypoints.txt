- Introduces a Stack LSTM parser that combines Henderson et al.’s joint syntactic/semantic transition system with Dyer et al.’s stack LSTM architecture.
- Shows compelling empirical results compared to joint systems on the CoNLL 2008 and 2009 shared tasks.
- Paper is clear, well-explained, and situates itself strongly within related work.
- Methodology is robust, though Chinese embedding quality raises concern that embeddings can overshadow model innovations.
- System description is detailed, hyperparameters are well-justified, and the discussion is insightful.
- Main critique: limited novelty since it builds directly on prior Henderson and Dyer work, but the progression is natural and valuable.
