- Proposes a Stack LSTM parser that integrates Henderson et al.'s joint syntactic-semantic transition system with Dyer et al.'s stack LSTM architecture.  
- Achieves strong empirical results on the CoNLL 2008 and 2009 shared tasks.  
- Paper is well-written, methodology is sound, and the system is clearly explained.  
- Provides a thorough related-work review, justifies hyperparameter choices, and discusses results insightfully.  
- Minor concern: Chinese word embeddings have a large impact, suggesting embeddings may drive performance as much as the architecture.  
- Main weakness: limited novelty since the work directly combines two existing methods, though this is considered a minor criticism.  
- Overall contribution: a logical, anticipated, and well-implemented step that delivers strong empirical gains.
