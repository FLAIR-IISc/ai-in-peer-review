- Proposes a Stack LSTM parser that adapts Henderson et al.’s joint syntactic/semantic transition system to Dyer et al.’s stack LSTM architecture and reports strong results on CoNLL 2008/2009.  
- Paper is exceptionally well-written with clear explanations of concepts.  
- Literature review is thorough and situates the work effectively.  
- Experimental results are significant and thought-provoking.  
- Methodology is robust and the system is described in sufficient detail.  
- Choice of hyperparameters is well-reasoned and the discussion offers valuable insights.  
- Notes a minor caveat that high-quality Chinese embeddings can be as influential as model architecture.  
- Main weakness is limited novelty—an effective synthesis of prior techniques rather than a fundamentally new method.  
- Overall contribution is timely, welcome, and well-designed with strong evaluation.
