- Combines Henderson et al.'s joint syntactic/semantic transition-based parsing with Dyer et al.'s stack LSTM architecture.  
- Produces promising results on CoNLL 2008 and 2009 shared tasks compared to prior joint parsers.  
- Paper is well-written and clearly explained with a thorough related work discussion.  
- Methodology is sound; system description is precise with well-justified hyperparameters.  
- Provides an insightful discussion and compelling empirical results.  
- Minor concern: Chinese results may be driven by quality of embeddings, suggesting embeddings can outweigh model novelty.  
- Main limitation: limited methodological originality since it integrates existing approaches rather than introducing fundamentally new techniques.  
- Nonetheless, the implemented parser fills a gap the field was awaiting and is a valuable contribution.
