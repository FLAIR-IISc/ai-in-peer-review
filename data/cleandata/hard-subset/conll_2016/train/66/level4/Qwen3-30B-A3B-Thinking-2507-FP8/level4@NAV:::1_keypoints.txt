- Introduces a Stack LSTM parser that integrates Henderson et al.'s joint syntactic/semantic transition framework with Dyer et al.'s Stack LSTM architecture.  
- The combined method yields compelling results on the CoNLL 2008 and 2009 shared tasks compared to prior joint systems.  
- Paper is exceptionally well-written and clearly explained with thorough related work coverage.  
- Methodology is sound, system description is lucid, hyperparameters are well-justified, and the discussion is insightful.  
- Minor concern: unusually high-quality Chinese embeddings may be driving performance more than model sophistication.  
- Main reservation: the approach lacks significant novelty, largely building on Henderson et al. and Dyer et al.'s prior contributions.  
- Despite limited novelty, the implementation fills a valuable gap and is a worthwhile realization.
