- Paper presents a Stack LSTM parser combining Henderson et al.'s joint syntactic/semantic transition framework with Dyer et al.'s stack LSTM architecture.  
- The combined approach yields strong results on the CoNLL 2008 and 2009 shared tasks compared to prior joint systems.  
- Writing is clear, explanations are thorough, related work coverage is strong, and results are insightful.  
- Methodology is sound; minor concern that exceptionally high-quality Chinese embeddings may contribute more than model sophistication.  
- System description is precise, hyperparameters are well-justified, and the discussion is engaging.  
- Main limitation: limited methodological novelty since key components were established previously.  
- Reviewer is pleased the long-awaited parser has been implemented despite the lack of fundamental novelty.
