- Proposes a communication-efficient local differential privacy mechanism: 1-bit for mean estimation and d-bit for histogram estimation, aimed at telemetry collection.
- Main advance is improved communication efficiency, important for large-scale deployment.
- Authors report a large-scale industrial deployment, which supports practicality but doesn't by itself increase scientific novelty.
- Supplementary material contains proofs but also extended figures and main text, creating an unclear separation between paper and supplement.
- Reviewer had to search for proofs; the main paper should be self-contained and the supplement limited to additional material.
- Overall presentation is clear, partly due to outsourcing content to the supplementary material.
- Alpha-rounding component remains partially unclear; authors should clarify and justify the claimed guarantees.
- Experiments are convincing and support the main claims.
- Reproducibility concern: experiments use real/company data; authors should provide simulated or open-source datasets so follow-up work can compare.
