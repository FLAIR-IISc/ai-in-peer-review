It was not clear to me what kind of confidentiality is ensured after alpha-point rounding. If a data collector reports his data repeatedly (say N times), the composition theorem should be followed to determine the confidentiality budget. Adding another random source in response to alpha-point rounding and this certainly improves confidentiality to some extent, but the composition theorem is still applied as long as he does not respond to the data collector multiple times.
