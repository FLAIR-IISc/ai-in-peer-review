- Proposes 1-bit mean and d-bit histogram local differential privacy mechanisms that greatly improve communication efficiency for telemetry data.  
- Claims a large-scale industrial deployment, potentially the largest differential privacy deployment, supporting practicality and effectiveness.  
- Supplementary material contains proofs and extended text, blurring main vs. supplement and forcing readers to search for essential content.  
- Overall presentation is clear, but the alpha-rounding explanation and its formal guarantees remain unclear and need clarification.  
- Experiments are convincing but raise reproducibility concerns due to use of real user/company data; recommend providing simulated/open-source alternatives for comparability.
