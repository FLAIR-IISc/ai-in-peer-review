Section 3.1
It was not clear to me what privacy is guaranteed after alpha-point rounding. If a data collector reports their data repeatedly (say N times), the privacy budget should be determined according to the composition theorem. Alpha-point rounding introduces another random source in the responses and it certainly improves privacy to some extent, but the composition theorem still applies as long as it answers the data collector multiple times.
