- Proposes 1-bit LDP for mean estimation and d-bit LDP for histogram estimation, aimed at telemetry collection.
- Main contribution is communication efficiency, important for large-scale deployment.
- Claims an industrial-scale implementation (potentially the largest DP deployment), demonstrating practicality though not adding scientific novelty.
- Supplementary material contains proofs and extended content, but the main paper should be self-contained.
- Redundancy and dispersion of key content into the supplement makes reviewer navigation inconvenient; clearer separation is needed.
- Overall presentation is clear, but clarity partly results from offloading material to the supplement.
- The alpha-rounding section is unclear and requires further clarification about its guarantees.
- Experiments are compelling and support the claims, but rely on proprietary real-world data, raising reproducibility concerns.
- Authors should provide simulated or public-data alternatives so future work can compare against their results.
