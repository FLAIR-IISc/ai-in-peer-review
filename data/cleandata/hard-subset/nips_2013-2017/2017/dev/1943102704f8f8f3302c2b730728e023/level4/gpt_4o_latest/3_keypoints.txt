- Correctly emphasizes key algorithmic attributes: robustness, scalability, modularity, and use of prior information.  
- Authors demonstrate strong familiarity with relevant methods and domain knowledge.  
- Algorithm 1 adds little value: unclear and redundant with the textual description.  
- Overview section is rushed and poorly written, covering many topics too quickly and lacking clarity.  
- Hard to assess validity of proposed algorithms from the presentation.

- Reliance on training data raises generalizability concerns across different laboratories.  
- Promising simulation results are provided, but real-world performance remains unclear.  
- Method is complex and somewhat ad hoc, with much technical justification missing from the main text and buried in supplementary materials.
