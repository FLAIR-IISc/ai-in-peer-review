- Correctly emphasizes key algorithmic attributes: robustness, scalability, modularity, and use of prior knowledge.  
- Demonstrates comprehensive knowledge of methods and strong domain expertise.  
- Algorithm 1 is unclear and unreadable, offering little beyond the textual description.  
- The overview section covers many topics too rapidly, reducing clarity.  
- Hard to assess validity and efficacy; the neural-network training phase is poorly explained for practical implementation.  
- Dependence on training data that varies across labs raises concerns about generalizability despite simulation results.  
- The approach is potentially interesting but hampered by complexity, an ad hoc feel, and opaque presentation.  
- Key technical details and justifications are moved to the supplementary material rather than fully explained in the main manuscript.
