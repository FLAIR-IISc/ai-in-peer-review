- Introduces a novel nearest-neighbor bootstrap procedure to generate samples approximating the conditionally independent distribution.  
- Provides theoretical guarantees on the bootstrap, including bounds on total variation distance to the true conditionally independent distribution.  
- Derives risk generalization bounds for classification under non-i.i.d. near-independent samples.  
- Empirically evaluates the method on synthetic and real-world datasets, showing performance gains over prior CI tests.
