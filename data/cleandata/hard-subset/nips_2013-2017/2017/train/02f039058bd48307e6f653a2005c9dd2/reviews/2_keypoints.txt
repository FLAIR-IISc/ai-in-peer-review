- Novel assumption on smoothness of the conditional density and a bound on the total-variation distance between the null density and the bootstrap density.  
- Uses the smoothness assumption to bound the error of the optimal classifier on the training set (section 1.1 (iii)).  
- Theorem 1, which combines smoothness, achievability of hat R with error η, and the gap between optimal and trained classifier errors, is novel, insightful, and non-trivial.  
- Paper is hard to read and offers little interpretation; unclear how small error probability in 1.1(iii) can occur when the classifier class G is too simple to distinguish f and f^CI (example with r0=0.1 raises intuition/clarity concerns).
