- Proposes a novel smoothness assumption on the conditional density and gives a bound on the total-variation distance between the null density and the bootstrap density.
- Uses the smoothness assumption to derive a bound on the error of the optimal classifier on the training set (section 1.1(iii)).
- Theorem 1 combines the smoothness assumption, an estimated-risk error bound (η), and a bound on the gap between optimal and trained classifier errors; this result is novel and non-trivial.
- Paper is difficult to read and provides insufficient interpretation of results, which limits understanding of the technical contributions.
