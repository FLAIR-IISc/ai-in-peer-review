- Strength 1: Correctly identifies and formalizes a needed smoothness assumption on the conditional density and provides a novel bound on the total variation distance between the null and bootstrap densities.

- Strength 2: Uses the smoothness assumption to derive a bound on the error of the optimal classifier on the training set.
