1. Proposes a novel conditional independence test, a significant contribution.

2. Recognizes and formalizes a necessary smoothness assumption on the conditional density and derives a total-variation bound between the null and bootstrap densities under this assumption.

3. Leverages the smoothness assumption to bound the error of the optimal classifier on the training set (eq. 1.1(iii)).

4. Theorem 1 non-trivially combines the smoothness assumption, an achievable optimal risk η, and the error gap between optimal and trained classifiers into a novel result.

5. Paper is difficult to read and lacks intuitive interpretation—unclear how a small empirical error (e.g., R̂ < r0 + o(1) with r0 = 0.1) can be achieved with a limited function class (e.g., linear), needing clearer explanation.
