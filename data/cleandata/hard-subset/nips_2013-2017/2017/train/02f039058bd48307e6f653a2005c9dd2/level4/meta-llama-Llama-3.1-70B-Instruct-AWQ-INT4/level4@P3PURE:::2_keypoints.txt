- Identifies a crucial smoothness assumption on the conditional density and derives a novel bound on the total-variation distance between the null density and the bootstrap density under that assumption.

- Leverages the smoothness assumption to bound the error of the optimal classifier on the training set (see equation 1.1(iii)).

- Presents Theorem 1, which non-trivially combines the smoothness assumption, an achievable error rate η, and the gap between optimal and trained classifier errors — a novel and insightful result.

- Paper is hard to read and lacks interpretation; it’s unclear how key conditions (e.g., ĤR < 0.1 + o1 < 0.2) can be met in practice when the function class G is small or underexpressive, so more intuition and explanation are needed.
