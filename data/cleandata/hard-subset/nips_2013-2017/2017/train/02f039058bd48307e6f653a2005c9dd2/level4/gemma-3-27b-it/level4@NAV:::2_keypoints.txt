- Novel contribution: identifies the need for smoothness assumptions on the conditional density, proposes a specific assumption, and proves a new total-variation bound between the null density and the bootstrap-estimated density.

- Leverages the smoothness assumption to derive a bound on the error rate of the optimal classifier trained on the data (section 1.1(iii)).

- Theorem 1 is a non-trivial, insightful result that integrates the smoothness assumption, an estimator error bound (η for ĤR), and a bound on the gap between optimal and trained classifier errors.
