- Strength: Novel observation that assuming smoothness of the conditional density lets the authors bound the total variation distance between the null density and the bootstrap density.  
- Strength: The paper leverages the smoothness assumption to bound the error of the optimal classifier on the training set (section 1.1 (iii)).  
- Strength: Theorem 1, which combines smoothness, an achievable error η for ĤR, and a gap assumption between optimal and trained classifiers’ errors, is novel, insightful, and non-trivial.  
- Weakness: The paper is difficult to read and provides little interpretation of the results, leaving the reviewer unsure they understand the claims.  
- Weakness (example): Reviewer questions intuitively how a small error probability (e.g., ĤR < 0.1 + o1 < 0.2) can be achieved when the alternative holds but the function class G is small/unexpressive and cannot distinguish f from f^CI.
