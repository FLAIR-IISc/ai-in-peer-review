- Recognizes need for smoothness of the conditional density and proposes such an assumption, using it to bound the total-variation distance between the null density and the bootstrap density (novel).
- Uses the smoothness assumption to bound the error of the optimal classifier on the training set (Section 1.1(iii)).
- Theorem 1 combines the smoothness assumption, achievability of hat R with error η, and an assumption on the difference between optimal and trained classifier errors; the result is novel and non-trivial.
- Paper is hard to read and gives little interpretation; unclear how a small hat R can be achieved when the classifier class G is insufficiently expressive (e.g., linear G with r0 = 0.1) as raised about Section 1.1(iii).
