- Novel conditional independence test built on a new smoothness assumption and a bound on total variation between the null density and the bootstrap-obtained density — an innovative theoretical contribution.
- The smoothness assumption is used to derive a bound on the error of the optimal classifier on the training set (Eq. 1.1 (iii)).
- Theorem 1 coherently combines the smoothness assumption, achievability of hat R with error η, and the gap between optimal and trained classifier errors — a non-trivial and insightful result.
- Paper readability and interpretation are lacking; explanations are ambiguous, e.g., it’s unclear how a small hat R is achievable under the alternative when the classifier class G is limited (needs more intuitive clarification).
