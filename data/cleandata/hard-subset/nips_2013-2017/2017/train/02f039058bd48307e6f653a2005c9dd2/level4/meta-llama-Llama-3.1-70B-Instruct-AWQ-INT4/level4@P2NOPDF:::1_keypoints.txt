- Proposes nearest-neighbor bootstrap to generate f_CI-like samples and a classifier-based test, with TV-distance and empirical risk bounds; problem is important and presentation is clear.

- Unclear if the two-step approach (sample generation + classifier) improves over existing methods (e.g., permutation-based tests) or combinations like NN bootstrap + kernel two-sample tests; authors should compare alternatives.

- Unclear how the method performs in finite samples when X and Y are weakly dependent given Z.

- Method appears asymmetric in X and Y; unclear which variable should be sampled and how this affects causal interpretations (X→Y vs Y→X).

- Choice of parameter τ in Algorithms 2 and 3 is not justified or discussed; selection procedure and impact on performance are unknown.

- Minor: empirical risk in Algorithms 2 and 3 should be divided by the sample size to obtain an unbiased estimate.
