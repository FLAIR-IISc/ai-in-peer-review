

The authors present a parallelization algorithm for aggregating weak learners using Radon partitioning, supported by theoretical analysis and empirical results. The theoretical motivation is compelling, and the empirical evaluation demonstrates meaningful improvements in training time and/or AUC over multiple baselines across diverse datasets. The authors also convincingly preempt and address key concerns in the Evaluation and Discussion sections. I recommend acceptance.
