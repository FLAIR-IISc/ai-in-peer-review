

The authors present a parallelization algorithm for aggregating weak learners using Radon partitioning, supported by compelling theoretical analysis and empirical validation. The theoretical foundation is well-motivated, and the empirical results demonstrate consistent reductions in training time and improvements in AUC across multiple datasets and baselines. The authors also thoughtfully address potential concerns in the Evaluation and Discussion sections, strengthening the paper's overall contribution. I recommend acceptance.
