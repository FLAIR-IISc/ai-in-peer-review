

The authors present a parallelization algorithm for aggregating weak learners using Radon partitioning, supported by both theoretical analysis and empirical validation. The theoretical foundation is compelling, and the empirical results demonstrate consistent training time reductions and AUC improvements over multiple baselines across diverse datasets. The Evaluation and Discussion sections effectively preempt and address key concerns. I recommend acceptance.
