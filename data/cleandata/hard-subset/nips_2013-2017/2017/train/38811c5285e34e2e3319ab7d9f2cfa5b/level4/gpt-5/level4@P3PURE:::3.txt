The authors present a parallelization algorithm for aggregating weak learners based on Radon partitioning. They provide theoretical analysis to motivate the approach and empirical results that support the theory. The analysis is interesting, and the experiments show training-time and/or AUC improvements over multiple baseline algorithms across several datasets. The Evaluation and Discussion sections proactively and convincingly address several likely questions and concerns. I recommend acceptance.
