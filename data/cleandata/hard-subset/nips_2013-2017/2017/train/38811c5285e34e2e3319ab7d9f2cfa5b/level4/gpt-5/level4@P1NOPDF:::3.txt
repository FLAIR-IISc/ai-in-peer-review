The authors present a parallelization algorithm for aggregating weak learners based on Radon partitioning. They provide theoretical analysis that motivates the approach, along with empirical results that support it. The theory is interesting, and the experiments show reduced training time and/or improved AUC relative to multiple baselines across several datasets. The authors also anticipate and convincingly address several questions and concerns in the Evaluation and Discussion sections. I recommend acceptance.
