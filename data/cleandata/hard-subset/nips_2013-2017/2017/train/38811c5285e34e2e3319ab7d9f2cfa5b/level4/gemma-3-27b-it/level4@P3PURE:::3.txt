The authors present a parallelization algorithm for aggregating weak learners based on Radon partitioning. They support the algorithm with theoretical analysis and empirical results that validate the theory. The theoretical analysis is interesting, and the empirical results demonstrate training time and/or AUC improvements over multiple baseline algorithms across several datasets. The authors also proactively and convincingly address several potential questions and concerns in the Evaluation and Discussion sections. I recommend acceptance.
