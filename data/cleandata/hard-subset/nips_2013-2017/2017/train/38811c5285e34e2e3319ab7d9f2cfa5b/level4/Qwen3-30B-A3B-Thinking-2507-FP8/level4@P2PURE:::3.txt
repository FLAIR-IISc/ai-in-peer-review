

The authors present a parallelization algorithm for aggregating weak learners using Radon partitioning, supported by theoretical analysis and empirical results. The theoretical contributions are compelling, and the empirical evaluation demonstrates training time and/or AUC improvements over multiple baselines across diverse datasets. The authors effectively preempt and address key concerns in the Evaluation and Discussion sections. I recommend acceptance.
