The authors present a parallelization algorithm for aggregating weak learners based on Radon partitioning, accompanied by a theoretical analysis that motivates the algorithm and empirical results that support the theory. The theoretical analysis is interesting, and the empirical results demonstrate improvements in training time and/or AUC over multiple baseline algorithms across several datasets. Furthermore, the authors address several potential questions and concerns in the Evaluation and Discussion sections in a convincing and preemptive manner. Based on the strengths of the paper, I recommend acceptance.
