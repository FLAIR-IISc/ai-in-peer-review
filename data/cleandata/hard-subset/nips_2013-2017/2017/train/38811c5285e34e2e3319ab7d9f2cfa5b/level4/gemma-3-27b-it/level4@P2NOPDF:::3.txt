This paper introduces a parallelization algorithm for aggregating weak learners, leveraging Radon partitioning. The authors support their approach with both theoretical analysis and empirical validation, demonstrating a compelling link between the two. The theoretical work is insightful, and the experimental results show improvements in both training time and AUC performance when compared to several baseline algorithms across a variety of datasets. A particularly strong aspect of this submission is the authors’ proactive discussion of potential concerns and limitations in the Evaluation and Discussion sections, which is handled convincingly. Overall, I recommend this paper for acceptance.
