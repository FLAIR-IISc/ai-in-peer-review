- Proposes a parallelization method using Radon points to combine outputs of parallel learners.
- Primary contribution is using Radon points as an alternative to model averaging (model averaging is included as a baseline).
- Empirical comparisons include a sequential learner, model averaging, and a Spark-based algorithm.
- Experiments show reduced learning time and accuracy comparable to or better than baselines.
- Major concern: theoretical novelty is unclear and appears to overlap substantially with prior work "Parallelizing Randomized Convex Optimization."
- Prior related work is not cited; authors must address the omission and clearly delineate the unique theoretical contributions of this paper.
