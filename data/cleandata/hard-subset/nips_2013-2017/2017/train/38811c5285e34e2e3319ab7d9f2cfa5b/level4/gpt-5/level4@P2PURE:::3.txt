The authors present a parallel algorithm for aggregating weak learners using Radon partitioning. They provide theoretical analysis to motivate the method and empirical results that support the theory. The analysis is interesting, and the experiments show improvements in training time and/or AUC over multiple baseline algorithms across several datasets. The Evaluation and Discussion sections also proactively and convincingly address several likely questions and concerns. I recommend acceptance.
