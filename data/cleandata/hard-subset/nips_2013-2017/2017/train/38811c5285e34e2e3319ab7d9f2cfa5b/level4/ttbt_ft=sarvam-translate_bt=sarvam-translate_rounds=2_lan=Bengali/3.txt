The authors present a synchronization algorithm for aggregating weak learners based on Radon partitioning. They present theoretical analysis to motivate the algorithm and empirical results to support the theory. The theoretical analysis is compelling, and the empirical results show improvements in training time and/or AUC compared to multiple baseline algorithms across multiple datasets. The authors also actively and convincingly address several questions/concerns in the evaluation and discussion sections. I recommend accepting it.
