- Proposes a parallelization method that uses Radon points instead of parameter averaging as the main contribution.  
- Includes parameter averaging, a non-parallel baseline, and existing Spark-based algorithms as empirical baselines.  
- Experiments show reduced training time with accuracy comparable to or better than the baselines.  
- Significant concern that the theoretical basis appears to overlap with a prior paper, "Parallelizing Randomized Convex Optimization," possibly by the same authors.  
- Missing citation of that prior work and unclear what novel theoretical contributions this submission makes beyond it.
