Review- The paper proposes a parallelization method for aggregating weak learners using Radon partitioning. It offers theoretical analysis that motivates the approach, along with empirical results that substantiate the theory. The analysis is compelling, and the experiments show improvements in training time and/or AUC over several baseline algorithms across multiple datasets. The Evaluation and Discussion sections also proactively and convincingly address many anticipated questions and concerns. I recommend acceptance.
