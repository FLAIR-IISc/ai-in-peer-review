- Proposal: method to parallelize learners under specific assumptions using Radon points as a central technique.  
- Main contribution: replacing baseline averaging with Radon points for aggregating local learners.  
- Evaluation: compared against no-parallelization, averaging schema, and Spark-based algorithms.  
- Results: shows reduced learning times and comparable or superior accuracy to competitors.  
- Major concern: prior theoretical work ("Parallelizing Randomized Convex Optimization") appears to cover similar foundations, is uncited, and the authors must clarify what new theoretical insights or advancements they provide.
