The authors have presented a parallelization algorithm for aggregating weak learners based on the Radon decomposition. They provide theoretical analysis to motivate the algorithm with empirical results to support the theory. The theoretical analysis is interesting, and the empirical results demonstrate improvements in training time and/or AUC on several datasets, across several baseline algorithms. The authors have also proactively and concretely addressed several questions/concerns in the evaluation and discussion sections. I recommend acceptance.
