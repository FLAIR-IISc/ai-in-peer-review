This paper introduces a parallelization scheme that aggregates weak hypotheses using Radon points. The authors provide theoretical analysis that motivates the approach and empirical evidence that aligns with the theory. The analysis is engaging, and the experiments show substantial training-time speedups and/or AUC improvements over multiple baselines across several datasets. The Evaluation and Discussion sections also proactively and convincingly address common questions and concerns. I recommend acceptance.
