- Proposes a method for parallelizing learners under certain assumptions using Radon points.  
- Main claimed contribution is replacing model averaging with Radon points.  
- Assumptions and applicability for the parallelization approach are not fully detailed in the review.  
- Experiments compare against no parallelization, an averaging scheme baseline, and Spark-based algorithms.  
- Empirical results show reduced training time and comparable or better accuracy than alternatives.  
- Theoretical component appears to overlap with a prior paper “Parallelizing Randomized Convex Optimization,” likely by the same authors.  
- The prior work is not cited; the manuscript should clarify what theoretical contributions are novel to this paper.
