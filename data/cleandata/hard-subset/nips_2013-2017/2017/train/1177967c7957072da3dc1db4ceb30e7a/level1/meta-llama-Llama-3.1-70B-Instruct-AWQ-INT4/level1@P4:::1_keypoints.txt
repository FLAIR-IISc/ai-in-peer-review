- Strength 1: Clear, well-motivated introduction to adaptive importance sampling for CD and SGD.

- Strength 2: Novel and efficient algorithm to compute safe adaptive sampling distributions.

- Strength 3: Thorough theoretical analysis with bounds on expected progress and competitive ratios.

- Strength 4: Extensive and convincing numerical experiments on real datasets.

- Weakness 3: Focuses on CD and SGD only; unclear extensibility to other optimization algorithms.

- Clarity: Well-written, concise notation, and helpful figures/tables.

- Originality: Novel convex formulation and solver for sampling using safe gradient bounds, despite prior use of gradient bounds.

- Significance: Addresses an important ML problem and shows potential for meaningful performance improvements.
