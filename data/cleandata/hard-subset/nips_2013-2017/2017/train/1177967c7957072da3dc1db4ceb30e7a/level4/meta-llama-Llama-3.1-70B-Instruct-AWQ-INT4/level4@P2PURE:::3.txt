This paper focuses on safe adaptive importance sampling, a topic of current interest in the community. Key features of this work include the generality of the proposed method, which can be readily integrated with various existing algorithms, as well as the provision of both theoretical results and practical experiments to support the proposed strategy.

One issue that requires attention is the poor visibility of the figures in Section 5. To improve readability, it is recommended that these figures be enlarged, with correspondingly larger labels, to facilitate clear viewing by the reader.
