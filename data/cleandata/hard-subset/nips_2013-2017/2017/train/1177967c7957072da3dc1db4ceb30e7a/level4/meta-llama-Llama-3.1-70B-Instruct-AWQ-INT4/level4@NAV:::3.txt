This manuscript explores the concept of safe adaptive importance sampling, a highly relevant and timely topic in the current research landscape. Notably, the proposed methodology exhibits versatility, allowing seamless integration with a wide range of existing algorithms. Furthermore, the authors provide a comprehensive validation of their approach through both theoretical analyses and empirical experiments.

To enhance the overall quality of the manuscript, attention should be directed towards revising the visual aids presented in Section 5. Specifically, the figures in this section are challenging to decipher due to their size and label clarity. Enlarging these figures and increasing the font size of their corresponding labels would significantly improve their readability, thereby facilitating a more effective communication of the research findings.
