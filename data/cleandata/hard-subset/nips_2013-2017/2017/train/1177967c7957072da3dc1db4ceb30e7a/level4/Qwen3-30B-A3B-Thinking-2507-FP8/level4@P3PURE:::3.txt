

This paper addresses safe adaptive importance sampling, an active area of research with significant community interest. Key contributions include a generic method that integrates seamlessly with existing algorithms, supported by rigorous theoretical guarantees and comprehensive empirical validation. The proposed approach demonstrates clear practical utility and theoretical soundness.
