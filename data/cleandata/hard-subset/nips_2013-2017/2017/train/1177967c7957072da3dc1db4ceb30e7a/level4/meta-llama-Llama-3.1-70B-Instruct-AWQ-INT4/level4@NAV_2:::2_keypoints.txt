- Proposes an efficient approximation of gradient-based sampling for coordinate descent and SGD using safe lower/upper gradient bounds.  
- Formulates picking the optimal sampling distribution as a convex optimization problem and provides an efficient solver.  
- Sampling scheme is generic and easily integrable into existing algorithms.  
- Theoretical analysis is thorough and experimental results are convincing.  
- Provides practical guidance on tracking gradient information in generalized linear models.  
- Method is provably better than any fixed importance sampling.  
- Per-iteration computational cost is O(n log n), comparable to evaluating one gradient component.  
- Includes analysis of competitive ratio and total algorithmic complexity.
