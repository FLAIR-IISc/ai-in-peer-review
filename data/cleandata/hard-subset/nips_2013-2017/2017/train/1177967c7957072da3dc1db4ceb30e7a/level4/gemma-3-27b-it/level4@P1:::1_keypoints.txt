- Method: Efficient approximation of gradient-based sampling computed with negligible extra cost.  
- Optimality: Proven best sampling strategy w.r.t. the given bounds, outperforming uniform or fixed importance sampling.  
- Novelty: Recasts optimal sampling distribution selection as a convex optimization problem with an efficient solver.  
- Writing and clarity: Paper is well-written and easy to follow.  
- Theoretical soundness: Rigorous theoretical analysis that appears correct.
