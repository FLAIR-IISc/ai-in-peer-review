The authors have presented a "safe" adaptive importance sampling technique for coordinate descent and stochastic gradient methods. Based on the lower and upper limits of the gradient values, an efficient approximate method of gradient-based sampling is proposed. The method is proven to be the best technique in terms of limits, which can always be computed efficiently at a negligible additional cost, better than uniform or fixed importance sampling. Although adaptive importance sampling techniques have been proposed before, the authors have presented a new formula for selecting the optimal sampling distribution as a convex optimization problem and have presented an efficient algorithm to solve it.

This paper is well-written and an excellent contribution to the study of importance sampling techniques.
