This paper presents a method for safe adaptive importance sampling, a timely and significant topic within the community. Key strengths of the work include the generality of the proposed method, which allows for straightforward integration with a wide range of existing algorithms. Furthermore, the proposed strategy is well-supported by both theoretical analysis and comprehensive empirical validation.

One area for improvement is the presentation quality of the figures in Section 5. The current figures and their corresponding labels are too small, which hinders the reader's ability to properly interpret the experimental results. To improve the paper's clarity, the authors are strongly encouraged to revise these figures by increasing their overall size and the font size of the labels and legends to ensure legibility.
