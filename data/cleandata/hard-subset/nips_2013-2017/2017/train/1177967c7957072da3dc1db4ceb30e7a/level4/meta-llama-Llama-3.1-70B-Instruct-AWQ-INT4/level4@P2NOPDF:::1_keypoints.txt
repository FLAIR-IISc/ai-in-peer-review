- Introduces a "safe" adaptive importance sampling for coordinate descent and SGD using lower/upper gradient bounds; proven optimal w.r.t. these bounds and computable at negligible extra cost.

- Novel formulation: casting optimal sampling distribution selection as a convex optimization problem and providing an efficient solver.

- Paper is well-written and a valuable contribution to importance sampling techniques.

- Lemma 2.1: possible missing factor of 2 in alpha^*; authors should verify and correct.

- Example 3.1: unclear—setting c to upper/lower bounds may outperform uniform, apparently contradicting the text; needs clarification.

- Algorithm 4: Line 4 condition (with m = max(l^{sort})) may never be satisfied; verify correctness.

- Algorithm 4: Line 7 likely should read u^{sort} instead of c^{sort}; confirm and fix.

- Experiments: add comparisons to other adaptive sampling methods (e.g., refs [2],[5],[21]) and to fixed non-uniform importance sampling for fuller evaluation.

- Experiments: include SGD timing results to better demonstrate efficiency.
