This manuscript investigates safe adaptive importance sampling, a timely and widely discussed topic in the community. Key strengths include the method’s generality and its ability to integrate with many existing algorithms, as well as the presence of both theoretical results and empirical evaluations supporting the proposed approach.

One concern that should be addressed is the readability of the figures in Section 5, which are currently difficult to interpret. The figures and their labels should be enlarged to ensure that readers can view them clearly.
