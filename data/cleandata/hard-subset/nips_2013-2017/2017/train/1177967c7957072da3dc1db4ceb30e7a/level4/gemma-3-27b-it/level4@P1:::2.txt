This paper presents an interesting and insightful contribution to the optimization of large-scale machine learning algorithms, specifically focusing on coordinate descent (CD) and stochastic gradient descent (SGD). Conclusion 2 in Theorem 3.2 is particularly noteworthy, as it demonstrates the superiority of the proposed sampling method over commonly used L<sub>i</sub>-based sampling.

4.
