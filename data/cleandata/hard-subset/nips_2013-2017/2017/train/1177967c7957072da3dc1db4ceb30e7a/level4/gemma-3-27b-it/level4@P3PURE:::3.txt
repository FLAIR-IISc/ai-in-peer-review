This paper addresses safe adaptive importance sampling, a currently active and important area of research within the community. A key strength of this work lies in the generality of the proposed method, which allows for seamless integration with a variety of existing algorithms. Furthermore, the authors support their strategy with both rigorous theoretical results and compelling practical experiments. 

One notable issue concerns the figures presented in Section 5. These figures are currently difficult to interpret due to their small size and the correspondingly small font used for labels. To improve readability and clarity, the figures should be enlarged, and the labels made more prominent.
