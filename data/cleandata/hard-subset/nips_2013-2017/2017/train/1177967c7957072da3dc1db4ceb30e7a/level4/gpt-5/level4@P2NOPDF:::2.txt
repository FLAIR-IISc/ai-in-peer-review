This paper is interesting and provides useful insights into RCD and SGD. Conclusion 2 of Theorem 3.2 is particularly valuable, as it suggests the proposed sampling scheme outperforms the commonly used L_i-based sampling. I have the following comments for the authors:

1. Line 85: In the denominator of p_i^*, it should be x_k rather than x.

2. Line 135: For consistency, consider removing the subscript k from \hat p and \hat c.

3. The figures are too small; ticks, labels, numbers, and captions are unreadable. Please move some figures to the appendix to free space so the remaining figures can be larger and legible.

4. If possible, provide a result analogous to Conclusion 2 of Theorem 3.2 that compares the proposed sampling with uniform sampling (p = 1/n), not only with the L_i-based method.

5. Please discuss how the new sampling affects the overall complexity of RCD and SGD. For example, what is the total complexity for RCD to obtain an epsilon-optimal solution when including the O(n) time needed to update l_k and u_k?
