This paper addresses the problem of safe adaptive importance sampling, a topic of significant current interest. The proposed method has several key strengths: it is generic and can be readily integrated with many existing algorithms, and the approach is well-supported by both theoretical analysis and empirical validation.

One issue that should be addressed is the legibility of the figures in Section 5. Both the figures and their corresponding labels are too small to be read clearly. I would strongly recommend that the authors enlarge these figures to improve the paper's overall readability.
