This paper focuses on safe adaptive importance sampling, a topic of significant current interest. The key strengths of this work are the generality of the proposed method, which can be integrated with many existing algorithms, and the fact that the proposed strategy is supported by both theoretical results and practical experiments.

One issue that should be addressed is the legibility of the figures in Section 5. Both the figures and their corresponding labels are too small to be interpreted clearly. For the final version, these figures should be enlarged to ensure they are readable.
