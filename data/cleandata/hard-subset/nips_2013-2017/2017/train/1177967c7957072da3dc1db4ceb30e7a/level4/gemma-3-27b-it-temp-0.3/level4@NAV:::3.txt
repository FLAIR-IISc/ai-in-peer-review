This paper presents work on safe adaptive importance sampling, a currently active and important area of research. A significant strength of this contribution lies in the generality of the proposed method, which appears readily integrable with a variety of existing algorithms. This is further supported by a combination of rigorous theoretical analysis and compelling practical experimentation demonstrating the efficacy of the approach. 
However, the visual clarity of the figures presented in Section 5 requires improvement. Specifically, the figures are currently too small and the associated labels are difficult to read. Increasing the size of both the figures and their labels would substantially enhance readability and improve the reader’s understanding of the presented results.
