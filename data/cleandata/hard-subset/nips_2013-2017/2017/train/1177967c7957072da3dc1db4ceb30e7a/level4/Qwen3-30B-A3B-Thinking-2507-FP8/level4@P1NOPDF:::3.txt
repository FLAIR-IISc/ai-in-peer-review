

This paper addresses safe adaptive importance sampling, a currently active area of research. The work presents a general method that can be incorporated into various existing algorithms, supported by both theoretical guarantees and empirical validation.  

A significant concern is the poor visibility of figures in Section 5, which are visually indistinct due to insufficient size and label clarity. These figures require enhancement—specifically, increasing their dimensions and refining label readability—to ensure readers can discern the details effectively.
