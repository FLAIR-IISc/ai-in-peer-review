This paper discusses sampling of safe adaptive importance, which is currently a popular topic in the community. Some key features of this work are that the proposed method is general and can be combined with many existing algorithms, and there are both theoretical results and practical tests to support the proposed technique.

One problem that this paper should address is the 5th-order diagrams, which are very difficult to view correctly. These diagrams should be enlarged, and the labels should also be enlarged, so that the reader can view them correctly.
