This paper focuses on safe adaptive importance sampling, a popular topic in the community at present. Key features of this work include the generality of the proposed method, which can be integrated with numerous existing algorithms, and the provision of both theoretical results and practical experiments to support the proposed strategy.

One issue with the paper that warrants attention is the figures presented in Section 5, which are difficult to decipher due to their small size. To enhance readability, it is recommended that these figures be enlarged, and their corresponding labels be increased in size, thereby facilitating a clearer view for the reader.
