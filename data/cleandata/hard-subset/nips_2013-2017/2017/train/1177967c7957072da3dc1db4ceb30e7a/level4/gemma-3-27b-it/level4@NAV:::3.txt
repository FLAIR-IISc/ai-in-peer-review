This paper presents work on safe adaptive importance sampling, a currently active and important area of research. A significant strength of this contribution lies in the generality of the proposed method, which appears readily integrable with a variety of existing algorithms. Furthermore, the authors bolster their approach with both rigorous theoretical analysis and compelling practical experiments. 

However, a key concern is the legibility of the figures presented in Section 5. These figures are currently difficult to interpret due to their small size and font. Increasing the figure size and label font sizes would substantially improve clarity and accessibility for the reader.
