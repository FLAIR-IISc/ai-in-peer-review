

This paper addresses safe adaptive importance sampling, a currently active topic in the community. Key strengths include the generality of the proposed method, which integrates with various existing algorithms, and the support provided by both theoretical results and empirical experiments. A critical issue requiring attention is the figures in Section 5, which are difficult to discern due to their small size and insufficient label legibility. These figures should be enlarged, with labels increased in size, to ensure proper readability for readers.
