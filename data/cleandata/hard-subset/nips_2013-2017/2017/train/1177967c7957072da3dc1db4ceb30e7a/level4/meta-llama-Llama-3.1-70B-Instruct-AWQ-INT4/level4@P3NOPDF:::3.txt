This paper explores the concept of safe adaptive importance sampling, a topic of significant interest in the current research landscape. Notable features of this work include the proposed method's generic nature, allowing seamless integration with various existing algorithms, as well as the provision of both theoretical results and practical experiments to substantiate the proposed strategy.

One area requiring attention is the presentation of figures in Section 5, which are challenging to decipher due to their small size. To enhance readability, it is recommended that these figures be enlarged, with corresponding increases in label size, to facilitate clearer visualization for the reader.
