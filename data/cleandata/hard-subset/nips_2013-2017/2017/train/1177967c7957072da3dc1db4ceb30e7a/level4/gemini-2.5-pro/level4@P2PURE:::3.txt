This paper addresses safe adaptive importance sampling, a topic of significant current interest. The primary strengths of this work are the generality of the proposed method, which allows for integration with many existing algorithms, and the comprehensive support from both theoretical analysis and practical experiments.

One issue that requires attention is the legibility of the figures in Section 5. They are currently too small to be interpreted clearly. To improve the paper, the authors must enlarge these figures and their corresponding labels to ensure readability.
