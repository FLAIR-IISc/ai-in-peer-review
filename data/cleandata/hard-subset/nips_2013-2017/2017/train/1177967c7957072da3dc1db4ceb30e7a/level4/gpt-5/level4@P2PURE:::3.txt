This paper investigates safe adaptive importance sampling, a timely and popular topic in the community. The proposed method is notably generic, can be integrated with many existing algorithms, and is supported by both theoretical results and practical experiments that validate the proposed strategy.

One issue that should be addressed is the readability of the figures in Section 5. They are difficult to see, and the labels are too small. The figures and their labels should be made larger so that readers can view them clearly.
