This work addresses the timely and relevant problem of safe adaptive importance sampling. The primary strengths of this contribution include the generality of the proposed method, which allows for its integration with a wide range of existing algorithms. Furthermore, the claims are well-supported by a combination of theoretical analysis and empirical validation.

One area for improvement concerns the presentation of results in Section 5. The figures in this section are difficult to read due to their small size. The authors are strongly encouraged to enlarge these plots and their associated labels to ensure the content is clearly legible for the reader.
