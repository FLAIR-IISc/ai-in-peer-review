This paper focuses on safe adaptive importance sampling, a topic of significant current interest in the community. Key features of this work include its generality, allowing integration with numerous existing algorithms, as well as the provision of both theoretical results and practical experiments to support the proposed strategy.

One issue with the paper that warrants attention is the figures presented in Section 5, which are difficult to discern due to their small size. To enhance readability, it is recommended that these figures be enlarged, with corresponding increases in label size, to facilitate clearer viewing by the reader.
