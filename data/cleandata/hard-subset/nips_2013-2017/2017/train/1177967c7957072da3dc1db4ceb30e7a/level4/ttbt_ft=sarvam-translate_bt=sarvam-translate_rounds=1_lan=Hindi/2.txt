I think this paper is interesting and provides some good information for the RCD and SGD algorithms. Conclusion 2 in Theorem 3.2 is very useful because it states that the new sampling method is better than L-based sampling, which is a commonly used strategy. I have some comments that I would like the authors to consider.

1. In line 85, in the denominator of p^*i, there should be xk, not x.
2. In line 135, you might want to remove the subscript k for \hat p and \hat c, so that it remains consistent.
3. I don't see any ticks, labels, numbers, or words in all the figures, which are too small. Please move some of them to the appendix to make room and allow you to show larger figures.
4. If possible, please provide a similar result to Conclusion 2 in Theorem 3.2, but compare it with uniform sampling (p=1/n) instead of the L-based method.
5. Please comment on how this new sampling method will affect the total complexity of RCD or SGD. For example, what would be the total complexity of RCD to find an epsilon-optimal solution (including O(n) time to update lk and uk)?
