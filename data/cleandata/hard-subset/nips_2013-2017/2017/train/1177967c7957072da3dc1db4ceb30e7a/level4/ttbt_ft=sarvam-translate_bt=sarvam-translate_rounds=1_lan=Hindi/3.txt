This paper focuses on the safe adaptation significance sample, which is a popular topic in the community at the moment. Some of the key features of this work are that the proposed method is general and can be integrated with many existing algorithms, and there are both theoretical results and practical experiments to support the proposed strategy.

One issue that should be addressed with the paper is the figures in Section 5, which are very difficult to see properly. These figures should be enlarged, and the labels should also be enlarged, so that the reader can see them properly.
