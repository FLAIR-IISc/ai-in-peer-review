This paper studies safe adaptive importance sampling, a timely and active topic. The proposed approach is generic and can be incorporated into many existing algorithms, and the paper provides both theoretical results and empirical evidence to support the strategy.

Strengths:
- Generality and ease of integration: The method is presented as broadly applicable and compatible with standard optimization routines, which increases its potential impact.
- Combination of theory and experiments: The paper supports its claims with theoretical analysis and practical experiments, which helps substantiate the proposed approach.

Weaknesses and suggestions:
- Figure readability: The figures in Section 5 are very hard to read. Please enlarge the plots and significantly increase the font sizes of axes, legends, and labels so readers can interpret the results without straining.
