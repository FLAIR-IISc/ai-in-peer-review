This paper focuses on safe adaptive importance sampling, a topic of significant interest in the community at present. Key features of this work include the proposed method's generality, allowing it to be integrated with numerous existing algorithms, as well as the provision of both theoretical results and practical experiments to support the proposed strategy.

One issue with the paper that warrants attention is the figures in Section 5, which are challenging to decipher due to their small size. To enhance readability, it would be beneficial to enlarge these figures and increase the font size of the labels, enabling readers to view them more clearly.
