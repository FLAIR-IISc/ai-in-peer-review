This paper addresses safe adaptive importance sampling, a currently popular topic within the machine learning community. A key strength of this work is its generality; the proposed method can be integrated with a wide range of existing algorithms. Furthermore, the authors support their strategy with both rigorous theoretical results and practical experiments. 

However, a significant issue detracts from the paper’s presentation: the figures in Section 5 are difficult to interpret due to their small size and font. To improve readability, these figures should be enlarged, and the labels within them should also be made larger and clearer. This would significantly aid the reader in understanding the experimental results.
