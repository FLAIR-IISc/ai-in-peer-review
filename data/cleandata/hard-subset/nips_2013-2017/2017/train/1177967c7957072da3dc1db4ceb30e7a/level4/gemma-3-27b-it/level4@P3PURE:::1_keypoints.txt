- Contribution: Proposes a “safe” adaptive importance sampling method for coordinate descent and SGD using gradient lower/upper bounds, an efficient approximation of gradient-based sampling, a convex formulation, and an efficient solver; proven optimal w.r.t. the bounds and outperforms uniform/fixed sampling at negligible extra cost.
- Overall assessment: Well-written paper and valuable contribution; reviewer recommends accept after rebuttal.

- Experiments: Add comparisons to other adaptive sampling methods (e.g., refs [2], [5], [21]) and to fixed importance sampling with a non-uniform distribution.
