- Presents a “safe” adaptive importance sampling strategy for coordinate descent and SGD using lower/upper bounds on gradient magnitudes.
- Theoretical analysis shows the strategy is optimal with respect to the bounds and always outperforms uniform or fixed importance sampling.
- Novel convex formulation for selecting the optimal sampling distribution with an efficient solver that adds negligible computational overhead.
- Paper is well-written and offers a solid contribution to importance sampling techniques.
