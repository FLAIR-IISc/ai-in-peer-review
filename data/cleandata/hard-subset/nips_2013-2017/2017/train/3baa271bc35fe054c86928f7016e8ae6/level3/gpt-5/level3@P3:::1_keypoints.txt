- Problem: robust streaming submodular maximization under cardinality constraint — one-pass summary T allowing post-hoc deletions of up to m elements while approximating OPT(k, V \ E).  
- Algorithm: STAR-T with exponentially sized partitioned buckets and exponentially decreasing thresholds; STAR-T-GREEDY runs greedy on T \ E at query time.  
- Main theorem: constant-factor approximation c ≈ 0.149 with memory O((k + m log k) log k) when a good τ is known.  
- Unknown OPT: guessing τ via geometric instances adds an O(log k) factor, giving O((k + m log k) log^2 k) memory and same approximation up to (1+ε).  
- Empirics: matches or slightly outperforms SIEVE-STREAMING on two tasks (social-network dominating set, MovieLens), even when SIEVE-STREAMING is given deletions.  
- Relation to prior work: extends greedy and SIEVE-STREAMING ideas, connects to robust non-streaming methods, and trades memory vs. approximation compared to concurrent work [18].  
- Technical quality: analysis is careful and sound, with transparent derivation of the 0.149 constant via case splits.  
- Novelty: partitioned thresholding and the balancing analysis approach are original for robust streaming.  
- Clarity issues: formal sections are dense; need more intuition, clearer presentation of τ estimation (Section 4.1) and practical choice/impact of w.  
- Limitations: restricted to monotone submodular, cardinality constraints, fixed m, and insertion-only single-pass streams; modest approximation constant.  
- Experimental gaps: limited datasets, underdeveloped application stories, and missing empirical comparison to concurrent deletion-robust streaming [18].  
- Suggested improvements: unify and foreground the two memory regimes in abstract/conclusion, add empirical comparison to [18], include ablations on w/thresholds, runtime and oracle counts, and discuss extensions to other constraints or streams.  
- Recommendation: Borderline reject — technically strong and novel but narrow scope, weak empirical motivation, and questionable fit for NIPS without strengthened experiments and clarifications.
