- Introduces STAR-T, a novel one-pass streaming algorithm for robust submodular maximization that outputs a set robust to removal of up to m elements.
- After any removal of m elements, running a simple greedy on the returned set yields a constant-factor approximation for robust submodular maximization.
- Problem is well-motivated for large-scale ML applications but would benefit from more concrete application examples and motivation.
