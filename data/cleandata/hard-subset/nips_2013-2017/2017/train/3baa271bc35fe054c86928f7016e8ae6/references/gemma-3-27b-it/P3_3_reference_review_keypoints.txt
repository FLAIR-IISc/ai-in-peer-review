- Adapts SIEVE-STREAMING with a partitioning and decreasing-threshold scheme to achieve robustness and provides a constant-factor approximation guarantee.  
- Builds on prior work in submodular maximization, streaming algorithms, and robust subset selection, and positions itself relative to PRO and SIEVE-STREAMING.  
- Strength: addresses an important, under-explored robustness problem relevant to real-world streaming applications.  
- Strength: provides a rigorous theoretical analysis with a provable constant-factor approximation.  
- Strength: introduces a novel partitioned thresholding algorithm design that is well-motivated.  
- Strength: handles unknown optimal value by proposing a practical approximation scheme.  
- Strength: empirical validation on dominating set and personalized movie recommendation shows competitive performance versus baselines.  
- Strength: supports fast re-computation after removals, making it suitable for interactive use.
