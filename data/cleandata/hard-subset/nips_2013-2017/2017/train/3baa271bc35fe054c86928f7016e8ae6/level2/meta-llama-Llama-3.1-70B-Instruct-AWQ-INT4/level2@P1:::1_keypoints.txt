- Originality: presents a novel algorithmic combination of streaming and robustness but lacks a major new technical breakthrough.
- Significance: addresses an important practical problem (e.g., data summarization, influence maximization) and advances robust optimization techniques.

- Cons: limited methodological novelty, reliance on prior ideas, and presentation/notation weaknesses argue against acceptance.
