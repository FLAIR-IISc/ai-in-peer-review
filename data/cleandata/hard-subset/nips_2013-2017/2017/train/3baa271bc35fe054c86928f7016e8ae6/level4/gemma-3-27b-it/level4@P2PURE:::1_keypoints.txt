- Problem: Maintain a set T from a ground set E in a streaming setting so that after removing any m elements from T, one can still find a size-k subset S among the remainder that maximizes a submodular function f.

- Robustness objective: Find T that guarantees a high-quality k-subset remains even after arbitrary removal of up to m elements.

- Setting & motivation: Streaming submodular maximization with robustness to later element removal (e.g., adjusting recommendations when users lose interest).

- Main result: A constant-factor approximation algorithm is given.

- Output size guarantee: The algorithm returns T of size (m log k + k) log^2 k.

- Technical contribution: Solution and analysis are non-trivial and technically challenging; results appear correct.

- Concern: Conference relevance is questionable due to weakly motivated applications and limited generality of the problem.
