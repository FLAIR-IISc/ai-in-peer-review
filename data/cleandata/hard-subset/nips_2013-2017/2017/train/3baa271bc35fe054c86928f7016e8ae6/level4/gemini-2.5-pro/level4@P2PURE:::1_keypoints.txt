- Tackles robust submodular maximization in a streaming setting where an adversary can delete m elements from the chosen summary.
- Objective: pick summary T so that after any m deletions, a k-element subset S from the remainder approximates the optimum monotone submodular value.
- Motivated by scenarios (e.g., recommendation systems) where initial solutions may be partially invalidated by users or external events.
- Main contribution: a streaming algorithm achieving a constant-factor approximation.
- Algorithm returns summary T of size O((m log k + k) log^2 k).
- Technical contributions are sound, non-trivial, and accompanied by a technically challenging analysis.
- Primary concern: relevance and impact for the conference audience is unclear.
- Motivation and example applications are underdeveloped and not strongly compelling; stronger justification of the robustness model’s broad applicability is needed.
