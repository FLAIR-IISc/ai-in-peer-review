- Problem: streaming selection of T ⊆ E so that after any removal of m elements, one can pick S of size k maximizing a submodular function f — a robust extension of submodular maximization.  
- Applications: submodular maximization is widely applicable, but the paper’s motivation for a robust variant is limited and lacks concrete, compelling examples.  
- Contribution: presents a constant-factor approximation algorithm that returns T of size (m log k + k) log^2 k.  
- Correctness/Technical quality: results appear correct; the algorithm and analysis are non-trivial and technically challenging.  
- Main concern: weak motivation/applications and limited generality raise doubts about relevance to the broader ML community and fit for the conference.
