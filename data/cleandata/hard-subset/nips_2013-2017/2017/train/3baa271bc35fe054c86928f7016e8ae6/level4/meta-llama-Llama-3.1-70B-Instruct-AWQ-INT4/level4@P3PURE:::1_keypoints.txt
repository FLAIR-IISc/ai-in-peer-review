- Studies streaming robust submodular maximization: find T ⊆ E so that after any m deletions from T, a k-element S maximizing submodular f can still be found.  
- Problem motivated by post-selection deletions in practice (e.g., recommendations), but application discussion is weak and underexplored.  
- Main contribution: constant-factor approximation algorithm returning T of size (m log k + k) log^2 k.  
- Results appear correct and the solution is non-trivial.  
- Algorithm and analysis are technically challenging and show strong technical mastery.  
- Main concern: unclear relevance/general importance for NIPS — problem may lack sufficient generality or impact.  
- Paper fails to provide a compelling case or thorough exploration of applications to justify publication.
