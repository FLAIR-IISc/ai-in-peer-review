- Studies streaming selection of T from ground set E so that after any m deletions, a subset S of size k still maximizes a submodular function f.  
- Submodular maximization has many applications; streaming variants exist, but this paper tackles a robust (post-deletion) version.  
- Robust version motivated by real scenarios (e.g., recommendations where some items are later removed).  
- Presents a constant-factor approximation algorithm that returns T of size (m log k + k) log^2 k.  
- Results appear correct; the algorithm and analysis are non-trivial and technically challenging.  
- Main concern: relevance to the conference—applications/motivation seem weak and the problem may not be general enough.
