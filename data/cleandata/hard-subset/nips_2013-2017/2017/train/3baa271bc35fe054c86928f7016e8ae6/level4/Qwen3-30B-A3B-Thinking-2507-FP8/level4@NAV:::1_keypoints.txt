- Problem: Streaming robust submodular maximization—select T from ground set E so that after deleting any m elements, the remainder still contains a size-k subset S that maximizes submodular f.  
- Motivation/Application: Motivated by post-hoc removals in practical settings (e.g., recommender systems), but the stated applications and motivation are weak.  
- Relation to prior work: Extends standard streaming submodular maximization to a robust variant; existing streaming algorithms handle the non-robust case.  
- Main result: Gives a constant-factor approximation while outputting T of size (m log k + k) log^2 k.  
- Technical assessment: Algorithm and analysis are non-trivial and appear correct.  
- Primary concern: Relevance to the conference is questionable because the problem scope is not sufficiently general.
