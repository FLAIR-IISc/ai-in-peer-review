This paper proposes a two-stage procedure for robust submodular maximization with a cardinality constraint k. The authors develop a streaming algorithm, STAR-T, which is based on a novel partitioning structure and an exponentially decreasing thresholding rule. The algorithm is designed to handle the problem of maximizing a monotone submodular function subject to a cardinality constraint, with the additional requirement of robustness against the removal of up to m elements.

The paper is well-motivated, and the problem of robust submodular maximization is well-defined. The authors provide a thorough discussion of the related work and clearly explain the contributions of their paper. The proposed algorithm, STAR-T, is well-described, and the theoretical analysis is sound. The authors provide a proof of the constant-factor approximation guarantee of the algorithm, which is a significant contribution to the field.

The experimental results are promising, and the authors demonstrate the effectiveness of their algorithm on two different data summarization tasks.
