- Problem studied: Streaming robust submodular maximization—select T from ground set E so that after any deletion of m elements, one can still extract a size-k set S that maximizes submodular function f.
- Applications: Motivated by scenarios where produced solutions may later lose elements (e.g., recommender systems), but overall application motivation is weak.
- Results: Achieves a constant-factor approximation while returning a set T of size (m log k + k) log^2 k.
- Technical assessment: Results appear correct; the solution and analysis are nontrivial and technically challenging.
- Main concern: Limited relevance to the conference due to weak application motivation and lack of sufficient generality.
