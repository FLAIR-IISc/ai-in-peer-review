- Problem: Robust streaming submodular maximization—choose T ⊆ E so that after any m removals from T, a size-k subset S remains that approximately maximizes submodular f.  
- Applications: Relevant where selected items may be removed later (e.g., personalized movie recommendations), but overall motivations presented are weak.  
- Results: A constant-factor approximation algorithm that outputs |T| = (m log k + k) log^2 k.  
- Technical assessment: Algorithm and analysis appear correct, technically sound, and non-trivial.  
- Main concern: Limited relevance to the conference due to weak applications and possibly insufficiently broad problem scope.
