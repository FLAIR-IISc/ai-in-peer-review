- Proposes PRUNE, a novel network embedding method that preserves both global node ranking and community-aware proximity.  
- Introduces a multi-task Siamese neural network to jointly learn embeddings for proximity and ranking preservation.  
- Trains the model with a combined objective designed to balance proximity preservation and global node ranking.  
- Paper is well-written with clear problem motivation, related work, and accessible technical exposition.  
- Provides a concise theoretical interpretation of the proposed objective function.  
- Thorough experiments across learning-to-rank, classification, regression, and link prediction demonstrate effectiveness.  
- Strength: novel integration of proximity preservation and global ranking in network embeddings.  
- Strength: multi-task Siamese architecture enables joint optimization of multiple objectives.  
- Strength: comprehensive empirical evaluation showing superiority over selected baselines.  
- Weakness: lacks detailed analysis of computational complexity.  
- Weakness: limited discussion of hyperparameter tuning process and model sensitivity.  
- Weakness: missing comparison with other state-of-the-art methods that explicitly preserve global node ranking.  
- Recommendation: Accept with minor revisions to address complexity analysis, hyperparameter sensitivity, and missing comparisons.  
- Rating: 8/10.
