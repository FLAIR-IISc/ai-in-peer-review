- Introduces a double sampling strategy for multiclass classification, supported by theory and experiments on text datasets with many classes.  
- Thorough experimental comparison against various baselines showing competitive accuracy and reduced training time.  
- Tackles an important problem (extreme multiclass classification) with practical relevance.  
- Heavy reliance on prior work [16], making the overall novelty somewhat limited despite two claimed contributions.  
- Prediction time remains comparatively long, which is a concern for practical deployment.  
- Practical meaning and interpretability of the novel generalization bound are unclear and need comparison to existing bounds.  
- Experimental comparisons mix batch and online algorithms without accounting for their very different memory usage characteristics.
