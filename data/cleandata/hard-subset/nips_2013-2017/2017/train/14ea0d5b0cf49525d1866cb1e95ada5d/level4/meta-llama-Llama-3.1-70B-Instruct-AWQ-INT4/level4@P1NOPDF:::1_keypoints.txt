- Proposes a double sampling strategy building on [16], with theoretical and empirical analyses for multiclass problems with many classes.
- Experiments on text datasets show the method can handle extreme multiclass classification effectively.
- Comprehensive experimental comparisons with several baselines, showing high performance and shorter training times on several datasets.
- Highlights extreme classification as an important and practically relevant research direction.
- Novelty is limited: the paper mainly extends [16] via double sampling and new local Rademacher complexity generalization bounds.
- Despite faster training, the proposed method has longer prediction times, which matters more in practice.
- The interpretation and comparative context of the new generalization bound are unclear.
- Experimental mix of batch and online algorithms overlooks their substantially different memory usage.
