This paper presents a neural network designed to predict image regions crucial for object categorization performance in a separate neural network. The paper is well-written and the experimental results demonstrating strong performance gains over prior work are compelling. The evaluation is performed on challenging datasets using state-of-the-art neural networks. 

To further strengthen the motivation, a more detailed discussion of application scenarios requiring real-time efficiency would be beneficial. Additionally, an analysis of the learned salient regions – for example, identifying which object categories leverage background biases for recognition – would provide valuable insight into the model’s behavior.
