This paper presents a neural network (NN) designed to predict the regions of an image that are crucial for another NN to perform object categorization. The paper is well-written and the experimental results are impressive, demonstrating the proposed approach's effectiveness compared to previous works, particularly on challenging datasets using state-of-the-art NNs.

One potential improvement could be to provide a clearer motivation for the need for real-time efficiency in specific applications. Additionally, it would be beneficial to include an analysis of the learned salient regions, offering insights into which object categories may be exploiting biases in the background to recognize objects, thereby providing a deeper understanding of the proposed approach's strengths and limitations.
