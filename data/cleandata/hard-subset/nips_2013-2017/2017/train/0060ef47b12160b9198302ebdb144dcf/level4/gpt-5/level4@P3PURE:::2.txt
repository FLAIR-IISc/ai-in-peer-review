This paper proposes a neural network that predicts the image regions most important to another neural network for object categorization. The paper is well written, and the empirical results are compelling: the method performs strongly compared to prior work, with evaluations on challenging datasets and using state-of-the-art networks.

I have two suggestions for improvement:
- Better motivate the need for real-time efficiency by clarifying which applications specifically require it.
- Provide more insight into the learned salient regions. For example, analyze which object categories rely on background biases to support recognition.

Overall, this is an interesting and well-executed study with strong experimental support. The additional motivation and analysis suggested above would further strengthen the contribution.
