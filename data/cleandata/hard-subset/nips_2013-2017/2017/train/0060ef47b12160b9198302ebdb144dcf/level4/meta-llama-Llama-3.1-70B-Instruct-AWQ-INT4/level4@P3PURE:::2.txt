This paper proposes a neural network (NN) that predicts the regions of an image crucial for another NN to perform object categorization. The paper is well-written and the experimental results are impressive, demonstrating its effectiveness compared to previous works, particularly on challenging datasets with state-of-the-art NNs.

One potential improvement could be to provide more motivation for applications where real-time efficiency is necessary. Additionally, it would be beneficial to gain insight from an analysis of the learned salient regions, such as identifying which object categories exploit biases in the background to recognize the object. This could further enhance the understanding of the proposed method's capabilities and limitations.
