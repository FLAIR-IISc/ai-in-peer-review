This paper introduces a neural network (NN) that can identify regions in an image that are important for another neural network's object classification. The paper is well-written and it's interesting that the experiments show it works much better than previous work. The results were obtained on challenging datasets with state-of-the-art neural networks.

I could probably have been more enthusiastic about which applications might require real-time efficiency. Also, I would have liked to see some insights from the analysis of important regions learned (e.g., which object categories use background bias to recognize objects).
