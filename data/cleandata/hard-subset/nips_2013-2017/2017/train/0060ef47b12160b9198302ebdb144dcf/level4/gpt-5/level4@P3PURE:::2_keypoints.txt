- Proposes a neural network that predicts image regions most important to another network for object categorization.  
- Paper is well written.  
- Empirical results are strong, outperforming prior work on challenging datasets using state-of-the-art networks.  
- Suggestion: Better motivate the need for real-time efficiency by specifying which applications require it.  
- Suggestion: Provide more insight into learned salient regions, e.g., analyze which object categories rely on background biases.  
- Overall: Interesting, well-executed study with strong experimental support; added motivation and analysis would strengthen the contribution.
