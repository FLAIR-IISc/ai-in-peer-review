This paper presents a neural network (NN) designed to identify the regions of an image that are crucial for another NN to accurately categorize objects. The manuscript is well-written and engaging, with experiments demonstrating its efficacy in comparison to existing works. Notably, the results are achieved using challenging datasets and state-of-the-art NNs.

A potential area for improvement could be providing a more detailed motivation for the need for real-time efficiency in specific applications. Furthermore, it would be beneficial to include an analysis of the learned salient regions, offering insights into how certain object categories may exploit background biases to facilitate object recognition (e.g., which categories exhibit this behavior).
