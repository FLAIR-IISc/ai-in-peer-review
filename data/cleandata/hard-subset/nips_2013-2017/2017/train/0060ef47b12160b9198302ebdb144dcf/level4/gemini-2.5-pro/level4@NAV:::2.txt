The authors propose a neural network to predict image regions that are salient for a separate object categorization network. The manuscript is clearly written, and the experimental results are compelling. The method is shown to significantly outperform prior work on challenging benchmarks using state-of-the-art recognition models.

A potential area for improvement is to better motivate the need for real-time efficiency by discussing specific target applications. Furthermore, the paper would be enhanced by a deeper analysis of the learned salient regions. For example, such an analysis could offer insights into cases where the object classifier relies on background context or other dataset biases for recognition.
