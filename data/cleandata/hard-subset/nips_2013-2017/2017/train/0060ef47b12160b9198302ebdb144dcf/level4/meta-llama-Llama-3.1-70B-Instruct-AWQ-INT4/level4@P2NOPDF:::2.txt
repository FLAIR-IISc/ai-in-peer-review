This paper proposes a neural network (NN) that predicts the regions of an image crucial for another NN to categorize objects effectively. The manuscript is well-written, and the experimental results are compelling, demonstrating superior performance compared to previous works, particularly on challenging datasets with state-of-the-art NNs.

One potential improvement could be to provide stronger motivation for the need for real-time efficiency in specific applications. Additionally, it would be beneficial to gain insights from an analysis of the learned salient regions, such as identifying object categories that exploit biases in the background to facilitate object recognition.
