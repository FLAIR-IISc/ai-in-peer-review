- Proposes a neural network that predicts image regions salient for a separate object categorization network.  
- Paper is well-written.  
- Experimental results are impressive and significantly outperform prior work.  
- Validation on challenging datasets with state-of-the-art networks convincingly demonstrates effectiveness.  
- Suggests stronger motivation for the need for real-time efficiency, with concrete application examples.  
- Recommends a deeper analysis of learned salient regions to identify categories that exploit background biases.
