This paper presents a novel neural network designed to identify image regions crucial for the performance of a separate neural network tasked with object categorization. The manuscript is clearly written and the experimental results demonstrating strong performance gains over existing methods are compelling. Evaluation on challenging datasets utilizing state-of-the-art neural networks further strengthens these findings. 

To enhance the work, a more detailed discussion of application scenarios demanding real-time efficiency would be beneficial. Additionally, incorporating an analysis of the learned salient regions – for example, exploring whether specific object categories leverage background biases for recognition – would provide valuable insight into the model’s behavior and potentially reveal interesting learned strategies.
