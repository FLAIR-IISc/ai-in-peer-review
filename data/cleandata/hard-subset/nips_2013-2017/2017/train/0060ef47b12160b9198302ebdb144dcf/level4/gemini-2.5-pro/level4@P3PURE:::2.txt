This paper introduces a neural network to predict image regions that are salient for a separate object categorization network. The paper is well-written, and the experimental results are impressive, demonstrating that the proposed method significantly outperforms previous work. The validation on challenging datasets using state-of-the-art neural networks convincingly demonstrates the technique's effectiveness.

For improvement, I would suggest the authors provide a stronger motivation for the necessity of real-time efficiency, including more concrete examples of applications where this capability is a critical advantage. Furthermore, the paper would benefit from a more in-depth analysis of the learned salient regions. Such an analysis could yield valuable insights, for example, by revealing which object categories cause the categorization network to exploit biases in the background for its predictions.
