This paper presents a neural network that predicts, in a single pass, which image regions are most informative to another neural network for object classification. The manuscript is clear and well organized, and the experiments indicate strong performance compared to prior approaches. The results are demonstrated on challenging benchmarks with state-of-the-art classifiers.

I suggest better motivating the need for real-time efficiency by outlining concrete application scenarios. In addition, it would be valuable to include an analysis of the learned salient regions—for example, identifying which object categories rely on background biases to achieve recognition.
