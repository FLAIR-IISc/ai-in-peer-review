

This paper presents a neural network designed to predict salient image regions critical for another network's object categorization performance. The manuscript is well-written, and the experiments demonstrate compelling results that outperform prior approaches on challenging benchmarks using state-of-the-art networks. I recommend strengthening the motivation for real-time efficiency applications. Additionally, a deeper analysis of the learned salient regions—such as how specific object categories exploit background biases for recognition—would provide valuable insights.
