This paper introduces a neural network designed to predict the image regions most salient to a separate object categorization network. The paper is well-written and easy to follow. The experimental results are a key strength, convincingly demonstrating that the proposed method significantly outperforms previous approaches. The evaluation is robust, conducted on challenging datasets with state-of-the-art classification models.

While the contributions are strong, the paper could be improved in two areas. First, the motivation for real-time efficiency could be strengthened. I suggest the authors provide more concrete examples of applications where this capability is critical. Second, the paper would benefit from a more in-depth qualitative analysis of the learned salient regions. For instance, such an analysis could provide valuable insights into the behavior of the underlying classifier, such as identifying which object categories it recognizes by exploiting contextual cues or biases in the background.
