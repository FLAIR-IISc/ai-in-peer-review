- Introduces a neural network that predicts image regions important for another NN's object categorization.
- Paper is well-written and engaging.
- Experimental results are impressive, outperforming prior work even on challenging datasets with state-of-the-art NNs.
- Need clearer motivation and concrete application scenarios for the claimed real-time efficiency requirement.
- Desires deeper analysis of learned salient regions, e.g., which object categories rely on background/context and how the method handles those cases.
