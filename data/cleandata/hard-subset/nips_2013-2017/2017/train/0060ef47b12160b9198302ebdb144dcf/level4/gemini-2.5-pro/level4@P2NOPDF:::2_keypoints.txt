- Proposes a novel network to predict saliency maps that identify image regions most influential for another model's classification.  
- Paper is well-written and the method is clearly presented.  
- Strong empirical validation on challenging, large-scale datasets using state-of-the-art recognition architectures.  
- Demonstrates significant performance improvement over existing saliency prediction methods.  
- Technical contribution and quantitative results are solid.  
- Motivation for real-time efficiency is weakly justified; needs clearer discussion of specific applications where speed is critical.  
- Lacks deeper qualitative analysis of saliency maps; should include case studies and category-level patterns to explain why the method works and reveal background/spurious correlations.  
- Addressing the motivation and qualitative analysis would further enhance the paper's impact.
