- Introduces a neural network to predict image regions most salient to a separate object categorization network.  
- Paper is well-written and easy to follow.  
- Experimental results convincingly show the proposed method significantly outperforms previous approaches.  
- Evaluation is robust, conducted on challenging datasets with state-of-the-art classification models.  
- Weakness: motivation for real-time efficiency needs strengthening—provide concrete applications where this is critical.  
- Weakness: lacks deeper qualitative analysis of learned salient regions—such analysis could reveal classifier behavior, use of contextual cues, or background biases.
