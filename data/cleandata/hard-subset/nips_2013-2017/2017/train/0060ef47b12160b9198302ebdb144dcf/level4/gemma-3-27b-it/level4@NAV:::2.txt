This paper presents a novel neural network designed to identify image regions crucial for the performance of a separate neural network tasked with object categorization. The manuscript is clearly written and the experimental results demonstrating significant improvement over existing methods are compelling. Evaluation on challenging datasets using state-of-the-art neural networks further strengthens these findings. 

To enhance the paper’s impact, a more detailed discussion regarding specific application scenarios demanding real-time efficiency would be beneficial. Furthermore, analysis of the learned salient regions – for example, exploring whether certain object categories leverage background biases for recognition – could provide valuable insight into the model’s behavior and contribute to a deeper understanding of its functionality.
