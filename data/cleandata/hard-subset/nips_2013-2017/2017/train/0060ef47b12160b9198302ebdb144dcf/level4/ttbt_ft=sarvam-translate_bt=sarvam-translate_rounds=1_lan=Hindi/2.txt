This paper introduces an NN that estimates regions of an image that are important for another NN for object classification. The paper is well-read and it is interesting that experiments show it works much better than previous works. The results are in challenging datasets with state-of-the-art NN.

I would suggest motivating a little better which applications might require real-time efficiency. Also, I would like to see some insights from the analysis of the key areas learned (for example, which object categories take advantage of biases in the background to recognize an object).
