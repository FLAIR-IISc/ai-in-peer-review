This paper introduces a neural network to predict image regions that are most important for the object categorization decisions of another neural network. The paper is well-written and the experimental results are compelling, demonstrating strong performance compared to previous work. The evaluation is performed on challenging datasets using state-of-the-art neural networks. 

To further strengthen the motivation, it would be beneficial to elaborate on specific applications where real-time efficiency in identifying these salient regions is crucial. Additionally, gaining further insight into the learned salient regions – for example, by analyzing whether certain object categories exploit biases in the background for recognition – would be valuable.
