- Proposes a neural network to predict salient image regions that most influence object categorization in another network.  
- Paper is well-written with compelling experimental results that outperform existing methods.  
- Evaluation uses challenging datasets and state-of-the-art object recognition networks, strengthening the findings.  
- Motivation for prioritizing real-time efficiency is underexplained and should be expanded.  
- Paper should specify application areas where speed is critical to boost impact.  
- Needs deeper analysis of learned salient regions, e.g., whether and for which categories the model exploits background biases.  
- Deserves insight into how the model determines importance, not just evidence that it does.
