Review- This paper proposes a neural network (NN) to predict which image regions are most informative for another NN performing object categorization. The manuscript is clearly written, and it is notable that the experiments show strong performance compared to prior work. The evaluation uses challenging datasets with state-of-the-art NNs. I suggest better motivating the application scenarios that demand real-time efficiency. Additionally, I would have appreciated insights from an analysis of the learned salient regions (e.g., which object categories exploit background biases to recognize the object).
