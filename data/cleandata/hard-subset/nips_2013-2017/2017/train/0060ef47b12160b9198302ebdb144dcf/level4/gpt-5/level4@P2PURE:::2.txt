This paper proposes a neural network that predicts which regions of an image are most informative for another neural network performing object categorization. The manuscript is clearly written, and the experiments show notably strong performance compared with prior work. The evaluation is carried out on challenging datasets using state-of-the-art neural network architectures.

I suggest better motivating the need for real-time efficiency by clarifying application scenarios where it is essential. In addition, I would have appreciated insight from an analysis of the learned salient regions—for example, identifying which object categories exploit background biases to achieve recognition.
