This paper introduces a neural network that predicts which image regions are important for a separate object categorization network. The paper is well-written, and the experiments demonstrate compelling performance that significantly outperforms previous work. These results are validated on challenging datasets using state-of-the-art neural networks.

I would suggest the authors better motivate the need for real-time efficiency by providing specific examples of applications where this is a critical requirement. Furthermore, the paper would be strengthened by an analysis of the learned salient regions. For instance, such an analysis could offer insight into which object categories are recognized by exploiting biases in the background context.
