- Proposes a neural model to predict salient image regions that are critical for object categorization by another network.  
- Well-motivated and clearly presented work.  
- Strong empirical results outperform prior methods on challenging benchmarks with state-of-the-art architectures.  
- Experiments effectively validate the approach’s ability to identify task-relevant visual features.  
- Recommend strengthening practical motivation by detailing real-time deployment scenarios (e.g., robotic vision, mobile applications).  
- Recommend deeper analysis of learned saliency maps to check for background biases or category-specific saliency patterns (e.g., “zebra” background cues).  
- Such analysis could reveal dataset artifacts and guide robustness improvements.  
- Overall, a significant and well-executed technical contribution; expand discussion of applications and interpretability to increase impact.
