- Proposes a fast image saliency mask generator (~100 224x224 images/sec on a standard GPU) that finds the tightest rectangular crop containing a requested class using black-box classifiers (AlexNet, GoogLeNet, ResNet).  
- Model architecture uses multi-scale feature maps (e.g., ResNet-50); the final scale passes through a feature filter for initial localization and upsampling blocks to refine masks.  
- Experiments claim the method outperforms other weakly supervised techniques on ImageNet localization.
