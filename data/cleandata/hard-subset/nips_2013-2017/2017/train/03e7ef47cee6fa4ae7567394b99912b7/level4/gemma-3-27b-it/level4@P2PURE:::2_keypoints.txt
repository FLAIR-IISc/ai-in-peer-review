- Contributions appear valuable if correct, but writing quality is poor and warrants substantial revision; recommendation was borderline pending fixes.  
- Several relevant prior works are missing from citations, notably labeled/weighted stochastic block model literature.

- Authors should remove/soften absolute novelty claims like “first work”, “initiate a rigorous study”, or “no systematic theoretical study”.  
- Model and problem statement need clarification: is the focus minimax bounds, and are arbitrary cluster size configurations (e.g., k-1 singletons) allowed?

- Numerical experiments are weak, comparing only to trivial baselines rather than meaningful existing methods.  
- Paper contains unclear/confusing sentences and specific phrasing errors that obscure meaning.  
- Numerous grammatical errors, typos, punctuation and stylistic issues throughout; a thorough proofread is strongly recommended.  
- Abstract is overly long and core contributions are delayed; shorten and prioritize main results.
