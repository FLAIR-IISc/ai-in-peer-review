- Verification: Reviewer could not fully check dense/lengthy upper-bound proofs due to time and paper density.

- Missing prior work (active queries): Gadde et al. (ISIT 2016) on active learning for SBM should be discussed; their model can be essentially equivalent for fixed k and linear communities.
- Novelty claims: Remove or soften absolute novelty statements (e.g., “first work”, “significantly generalizes”, “no systematic theoretical study”).
- Model clarity: State minimax perspective explicitly and specify constraints on cluster sizes (e.g., allow small/singleton clusters?).

- Presentation issues: Several sentences are confusing/incomplete (examples cited) and many grammatical errors, awkward phrasing, punctuation, and capitalization problems throughout.
