- PMD likely requires large minibatch sizes (N) to accurately estimate Wasserstein between significantly different, multimodal distributions.
- PMD may struggle on simple multimodal tasks (e.g., mixtures of Gaussians), especially when variances are also learned.
- With insufficient N, optimization can converge to suboptimal, lower-entropy solutions (e.g., SVHN samples lack diversity; digit 8 overrepresented).
