- Generalizes f-GAN to f-divergences and uses the chi-logarithm to extend the KL–exponential family connection.  
- Proves the variational information geometric f-GAN (vig-f-GAN) identity, giving an interpretation of the f-GAN objective.  
- Provides a sufficient condition on activation functions to obtain factored generative distributions.  
- Main theoretical result (vig-f-GAN identity) is interesting and has promising applicability.  
- Main theorems are hard to follow because they are presented in very general forms.  
- Section 6: experimental observations are difficult to relate back to the theoretical results.  
- Typographical/grammar issue: p.7, l.258 (“For eq.(7), is means when C …”) has wrong clause structure.
