- Proposes an information-geometric framework for f-GANs, extending the formulation to deformed exponential families.  
- Establishes a variational information-geometric identity linking the f-GAN game to a geometric optimization problem.  
- Provides a new characterization of generator convergence via information-geometric analysis.  
- Paper is well-written, clearly organized, and situates contributions relative to prior work.  
- Strong technical soundness, originality, and rigorous theoretical derivations.  
- Experimental comparisons of activation and link functions show improved performance in some cases.  
- Main weakness: emphasis on theory with limited and narrow experimental evaluation.  
- Paper assumes familiarity with information geometry and f-GANs, making it less accessible to non-experts.  
- Recommendation: accept with minor revisions—add more extensive experiments and clarify concepts/terminology.  
- Reviewer rating: 8/10.
