- Introduces an information-geometric (IG) perspective on f-GANs, deriving a variational IG identity linking f-divergences to Bregman divergences and viewing f-GAN optimization as geometric optimization on deformed exponential-family manifolds.  
- Theorem 8 is the paper’s central, elegant technical contribution; most proofs were checked and found likely correct (except full verification of Theorem 8).  
- Theorem 9’s factorization result linking deep network representations to deformed exponential families is valuable and novel.  
- Experimental section is underdeveloped: activation-function results don’t require the IG view and WGAN experiments distract from the f-GAN focus.  
- Section 5 (representational power) is unclear in how it connects to f-GAN design or guides selection of f.  
- The claim that φ_l are “deep sufficient statistics” (Theorem 6) needs clarification; Eq. (13) may not match a deformed exponential-family form.  
- Paper is dense and hard to follow for readers unfamiliar with information geometry; IG perspective is introduced too late.  
- Move Figure 1 (IT–IG connections) to the main text and shift the utility-theory paragraph to the appendix to improve clarity and focus.  
- Organization and narrative need improvement to better connect theoretical results to practical implications.  
- Originality: significant generalization of KL/Bregman relationships to f-divergences and novel factorization result, but not fully distinguished from prior IG-in-GAN work.  
- Significance: provides theoretical insights on generator convergence and practical conditions (Lemma 11) for activations, but impact limited by weak experimental connection.  
- Strengths: strong theoretical contributions (especially Theorem 8), clear foundation for parameter-space convergence, meaningful link between architectures and IG, and actionable implementation guidance.  
- Weaknesses: dense presentation, insufficient experiments demonstrating IG value, unclear Section 5 linkage, unsupported “deep sufficient statistics” claims, and distracting utility-theory content.  
- Recommendation: clear accept pending substantial reorganization and clarification—state IG usefulness early, move Figure 1 to main text, reorganize narrative, and substantially revise experiments to demonstrate the IG perspective’s value.
