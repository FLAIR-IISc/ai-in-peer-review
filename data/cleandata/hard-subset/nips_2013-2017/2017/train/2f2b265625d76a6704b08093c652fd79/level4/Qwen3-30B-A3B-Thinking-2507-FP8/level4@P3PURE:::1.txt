

This paper generalizes the f-GAN framework to f-divergences. By extending the connection between KL divergence and exponential families using the chi-logarithmic function, the authors establish the variational information geometric f-GAN (VIG-f-GAN) identity, which provides a theoretical interpretation of the f-GAN objective function. Additionally, the authors derive a sufficient condition on activation functions to achieve factored generative distributions.

The main theoretical contribution—the VIG-f-GAN identity—is interesting and its potential applicability is promising. However, the meaning of the main theorems is difficult to follow due to their overly general presentation.
