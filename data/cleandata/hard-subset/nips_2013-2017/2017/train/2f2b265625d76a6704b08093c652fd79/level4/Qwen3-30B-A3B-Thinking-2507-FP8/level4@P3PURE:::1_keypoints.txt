- Generalizes f-GAN to f-divergences via a chi-logarithmic extension of the KL–exponential family connection and introduces the variational information geometric f-GAN (VIG-f-GAN) identity.  
- Derives a sufficient condition on activation functions to obtain factored generative distributions.  
- Main theoretical contribution (VIG-f-GAN identity) is interesting and promising in potential applicability.  
- Theorems are presented overly generally, making their meaning difficult to follow.  
- Section 6: unclear how the experimental observations relate to the theoretical results.  
- Typographical/grammatical error on p.7, line 258: "is means" should read "it means".
