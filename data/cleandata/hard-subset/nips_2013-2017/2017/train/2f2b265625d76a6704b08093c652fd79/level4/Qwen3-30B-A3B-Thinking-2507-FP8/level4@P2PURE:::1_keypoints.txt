- Paper generalizes f-GAN to f-divergences via the chi-logarithm, extending the KL–exponential family connection.  
- Introduces the variational information geometric f-GAN (vig-f-GAN) identity offering a theoretical interpretation of the f-GAN objective.  
- Derives a sufficient condition on activation functions to achieve factored generative distributions.  
- Main theoretical contribution is interesting and potentially widely applicable.  
- Main theorems are overly general and hard to follow; their core meaning is unclear.  
- Section 6: unclear how experimental observations relate to the theoretical results.  
- Typo: p.7, l.258 should read "it means when C …" instead of "is means when C …".
