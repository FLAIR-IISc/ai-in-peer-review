This paper explores the f-GAN principle generalized to f-divergences, establishing a connection between the KL divergence and regular exponential families using the chi-logarithm. The authors derive the variational information geometric f-GAN (vig-f-GAN) identity, providing an interpretation of the f-GAN objective function. Additionally, they prove a sufficient condition for activation functions to obtain factored generative distributions.

The main theoretical result, the vig-f-GAN identity, is intriguing and shows promise for potential applications. However, the primary theorems are presented in a highly general form, making their meanings challenging to follow. To enhance clarity, it would be beneficial to provide more specific examples or illustrations to support the theoretical derivations.

Section 6 lacks a clear connection between the experimental observations and the theoretical results. To strengthen the paper, the authors should elaborate on how the empirical findings relate to the proposed theoretical framework.

On page 7, line 258, the sentence "For eq.(7), is means when C …" contains a grammatical error and should be revised for clarity.
