- Extends f-GANs using f-divergences and the chi-logarithm to generalize the KL–exponential family connection.  
- Introduces the variational information-geometric f-GAN (vig-f-GAN) identity to interpret the f-GAN objective.  
- Gives a sufficient condition on activation functions that yields factored generative distributions.  
- Main theoretical result is interesting and promising, but theorems are hard to follow because they are presented in very general form.  
- Section 6: unclear how the experimental observations relate to the theoretical results.
