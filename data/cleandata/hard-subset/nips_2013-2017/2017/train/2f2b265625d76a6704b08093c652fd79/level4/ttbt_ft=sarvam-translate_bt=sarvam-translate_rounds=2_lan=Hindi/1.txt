This paper considers the generalized f-GAN theory for f-divergences. By generalizing the relationship between KL divergence and regular exponential families using log-log logarithms, the authors prove the variational information geometric f-GAN (vig-f-GAN) identity, which provides an explanation of the objective function of f-GAN. The authors also prove a sufficient condition on activation functions to obtain a factorized generative distribution.

The main theoretical result, the vig-f-GAN identity, is interesting and its applicability is promising. However, it is difficult to follow the meanings of the main theorems because they are written in quite general forms.
