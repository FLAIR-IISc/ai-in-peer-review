This paper studies the f-GAN principle through the lens of f-divergences. By generalizing the connection between the KL divergence and regular exponential families via the chi-logarithm, the authors establish the variational information geometric f-GAN (vig-f-GAN) identity, which provides an interpretation of the f-GAN objective. The paper also gives a sufficient condition on activation functions to obtain factored generative distributions.

The main theoretical result—the vig-f-GAN identity—is interesting, and its potential applicability is promising. However, the statements of the main theorems are presented in very general form, which makes their meaning and implications hard to follow.

Section 6: It is difficult to see how the experimental observations support or illustrate the theoretical results. Clearer connections or explanations would help.
