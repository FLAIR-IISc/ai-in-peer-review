This paper extends the f-GAN principle using f-divergences. By employing the chi-logarithm to generalize the known connection between the KL divergence and regular exponential families, the authors establish the variational information-geometric f-GAN (vig-f-GAN) identity, which offers an interpretation of the f-GAN objective. They also provide a sufficient condition on activation functions that yields factored generative distributions.

The main theoretical result—the vig-f-GAN identity—is interesting, and its potential applicability is promising. However, the statements of the main theorems are difficult to follow because they are presented in very general form.
