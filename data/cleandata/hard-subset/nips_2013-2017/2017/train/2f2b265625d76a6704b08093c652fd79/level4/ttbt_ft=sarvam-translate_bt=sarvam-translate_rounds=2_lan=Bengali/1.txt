This paper considers the generalized f-GAN principle for f-divergence. By generalizing the connection between KL divergence and the regular exponential family using log-log logarithms, the authors prove the variational information geometric f-GAN (vig-f-GAN) identity, which provides an explanation of the purpose function of f-GAN. The authors also prove a sufficient condition on the activation function to obtain a factored generative distribution.

The main theoretical result, the vig-f-GAN identity, is interesting and its application is promising. However, it is difficult to follow the meaning of the main theorems because they are written in a rather general form.
