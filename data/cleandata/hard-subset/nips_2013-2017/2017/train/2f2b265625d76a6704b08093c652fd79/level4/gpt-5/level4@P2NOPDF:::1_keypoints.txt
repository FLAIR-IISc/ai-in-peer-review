- Develops f-GAN principle for f-divergences and extends KL–exponential-family connection via the chi-logarithm.  
- Establishes the variational information-geometric f-GAN (vig-f-GAN) identity giving an interpretation of the f-GAN objective.  
- Provides a sufficient condition on activation functions to obtain factored generative distributions.  
- Main theoretical contribution (vig-f-GAN identity) is interesting and potentially broadly applicable.  
- Central theorems are presented in an overly general form, making their meaning and implications hard to follow.  
- Section 6: relationship between experiments and the theory is unclear; experiments do not evidently illustrate or validate the theoretical results.  
- Minor issue: p.7, l.258 contains an ungrammatical clause (“For eq. (7), is means when C …”) that should be rewritten.
