- Extends f-GAN principle to f-divergences using the chi-logarithm and connection to KL and regular exponential families.  
- Establishes the variational information geometric f-GAN (vig-f-GAN) identity as a novel interpretation of the f-GAN objective.  
- Derives a sufficient condition on activation functions to obtain factored generative distributions.  
- Main theoretical result is intriguing and potentially useful but presented too generally, making its meaning hard to grasp.  
- Section 6 fails to clearly connect experimental observations to the theoretical results.
