- Extends f-GAN principle to f-divergences using the chi-logarithm to generalize the KL–exponential family relationship.
- Proposes the variational information geometric f-GAN (vig-f-GAN) identity, giving a novel interpretation of the f-GAN objective.
- Derives a sufficient condition on activation functions that yield factored generative distributions.
- Main theoretical contribution is promising, but theorems are presented in overly general forms and are hard to comprehend.
- Section 6 experiments do not clearly connect to the theoretical results, leaving their relevance unclear.
- Typographical/grammatical error on page 7, line 258 ("For eq.(7), is means when C …") needs revision.
