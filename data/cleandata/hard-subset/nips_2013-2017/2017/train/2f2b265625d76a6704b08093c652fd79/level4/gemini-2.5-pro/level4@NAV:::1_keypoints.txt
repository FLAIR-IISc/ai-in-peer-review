- Extends f-GAN principle to a broader class of f-divergences.  
- Uses the chi-logarithm to generalize the KL–exponential family connection.  
- Derives the variational information geometric f-GAN (vig-f-GAN) identity.  
- Provides a novel interpretation of the f-GAN objective.  
- Gives a sufficient condition on activation functions for producing factored generative distributions.  
- The vig-f-GAN identity is an insightful theoretical contribution with promising applications.  
- Main theorems are presented very abstractly and are hard to interpret concretely.  
- Section 6: experimental observations are not clearly connected to the theoretical results.
