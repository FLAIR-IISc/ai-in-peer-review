Extends the f‑GAN principle to f‑divergences and links the KL divergence with regular exponential families via the chi‑logarithm.

Introduces the variational information geometric f‑GAN (vig‑f‑GAN) identity, giving an interpretation of the f‑GAN objective.

Derives a sufficient condition on activation functions that permits deriving factored generative distributions.

Main theoretical contribution is intriguing and has potential practical relevance.

Presentation of main theorems is overly general and difficult to interpret.

Paper needs more concrete examples or illustrations to clarify the significance of the results.

Experimental section does not clearly connect empirical observations to the theoretical framework and needs more analysis/discussion.

Contains minor textual/grammatical errors (e.g., page 7, line 258).

Overall interesting and original but requires clearer explanations and examples to improve accessibility and impact.
