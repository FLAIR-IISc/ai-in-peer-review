- Extends f-GAN to general f-divergences via the chi-logarithm and establishes the variational information geometric f-GAN (vig-f-GAN) identity giving an interpretation of the f-GAN objective.  
- Derives a sufficient condition on activation functions that permits construction of factored generative distributions.  
- The vig-f-GAN identity is an intriguing main theoretical result with promising potential applications.  
- Theorems are presented in very general forms, making their meanings difficult to grasp.  
- Section 6 is hard to follow and does not clearly link experimental observations to the theoretical results.  
- Minor grammatical error on page 7, line 258 ("is means when C ...") that should be revised.  
- Overall, an interesting contribution that would benefit from more accessible presentation of theory and clearer explanation of empirical findings.
