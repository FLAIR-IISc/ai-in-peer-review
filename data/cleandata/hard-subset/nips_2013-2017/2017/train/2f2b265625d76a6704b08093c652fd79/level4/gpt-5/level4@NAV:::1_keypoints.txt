- Paper generalizes f-GANs via f-divergences and, using the chi-logarithm, establishes the variational information-geometric f-GAN (vig-f-GAN) identity that interprets the f-GAN objective.  
- Provides a sufficient condition on activation functions to obtain factored generative distributions.  
- The vig-f-GAN identity is an interesting and potentially useful theoretical contribution, but the main theorems are hard to follow because they are stated very generally.  
- Section 6 experiments do not clearly show how the empirical observations connect to the theoretical results.  
- Minor issue: grammatical error on p.7, l.258 (“For eq.(7), is means when C …”).
