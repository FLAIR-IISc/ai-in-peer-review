- Studies group-additive nonparametric regression and proposes learning unknown group structure via a covering-number-based penalty added to kernel ridge regression, optimizing jointly over group structure and within-group regressors; two approximation algorithms and theory (risk rates, group-consistency) plus experiments provided.
- Key innovation: quantifying group-structure complexity with covering numbers of the direct-sum RKHS, yielding a novel, well-motivated method with solid theoretical and empirical support.
- Main limitation: computationally difficult to optimize over group structures when the number of variables is large.
- Writing is clear overall but the paper contains numerous typos.
- Minor question: is there a simple characterization of interaction between two variables that avoids specifying the full model in Eq. (1)?
- Minor question: does “almost surely” on Line 169 refer to asymptotic behavior as n → ∞?
- Minor error: Equation (3) is missing a summation over i.
- Terminology suggestion: use “translation-invariant kernel” rather than “convolutional kernel.”
- Typos/examples to fix: “on RHS” → “on the RHS”; remove “can” in “bias of \hat f_{\lambda,G} reduces”; “G^ exists and unique” → “G^ exists and is unique”; “turning parameters” → “tuning parameters”; Algorithm 2 Line 1 “State with” → “Start with”; Line 251 “by compare” → “by comparing.”
