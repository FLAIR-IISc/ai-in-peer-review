- Proposes method to identify intrinsic group additive structure for nonparametric regression.
- Rigorously defines the intrinsic group additive structure as the minimal correct group partition.
- Introduces a structure-penalized kernel estimator to simultaneously identify groups and estimate functions.
- Complexity penalty is based on the covering number of the RKHS for each group structure.
- Proves consistency of the proposed method in identifying the intrinsic group additive structure.
- Strength: rigorous formal definition of the intrinsic group additive structure.
- Strength: novel complexity penalty based on RKHS covering numbers.
- Strength: provides a theoretical consistency result for the estimator.
- Weakness: exhaustive search algorithm has high computational cost for large models.
- Weakness: method performance is sensitive to choice of tuning parameters.
- Weakness: empirical evaluation is limited to a few simulations and one real dataset.
- Originality/significance: novel approach with a meaningful theoretical contribution.
- Clarity/organization: paper is well-written and easy to follow.
- Recommendation: accept with minor revisions to address computational, tuning, and empirical evaluation limitations.
