**Summary of the Paper**

The paper proposes a novel approach to identify the intrinsic group additive structure for high-dimensional nonparametric regression. The authors define the intrinsic group additive structure as the minimal group additive structure among all correct group additive structures. They develop a structure-penalized kernel method to simultaneously identify the intrinsic group additive structure and estimate the nonparametric functions. The method utilizes a novel complexity measure for group additive structures, which is based on the covering number of Reproducing Kernel Hilbert Spaces (RKHSs). The authors provide theoretical guarantees for the consistency of the proposed method and demonstrate its effectiveness through simulation studies and real data applications.
