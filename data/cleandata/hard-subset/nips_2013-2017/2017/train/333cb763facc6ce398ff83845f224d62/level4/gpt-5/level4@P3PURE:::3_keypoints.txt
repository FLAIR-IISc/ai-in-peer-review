- Introduces a new covariance structure for multi-output GPs, generalizing the spectral (Bochner) approach.
- Parameters are interpretable (e.g., inter-output delays and phase differences).
- Defining covariance for multi-output GPs is challenging and few methods exist.
- Method is theoretically sound and effective on several datasets.
- Paper is clearly written and illustrations are appropriate.
- Paper gives little information on parameter learning; this could affect usability.
- If parameter learning is manageable, the method could become a standard approach.
- Question: How does the method behave with few training points?
- Question: How does the model represent low correlation between channels with very different length-scales?
