- Introduces a multi-output Gaussian process covariance function formulated in the spectral domain.  
- Highlights spectral-domain covariance design as a valuable contribution to GP methodology.  
- Extends spectral approaches to multi-output settings, tackling the challenge of cross-covariances that need not be positive-definite.  
- Proposes a method that produces a spectral mixture covariance suitable for multi-output Gaussian processes.  
- Manuscript is clearly written with a concise problem introduction and solid GP overview.  
- Explanation of the spectral mixture kernel is lucid and the proposed extensions appear novel.  
- Paper is well-situated within existing literature and the novelty is sufficient for publication.  
- Experimental results are adequate for a theoretical paper but lack extensive intuitive insight; the Jura dataset is hard to interpret.
