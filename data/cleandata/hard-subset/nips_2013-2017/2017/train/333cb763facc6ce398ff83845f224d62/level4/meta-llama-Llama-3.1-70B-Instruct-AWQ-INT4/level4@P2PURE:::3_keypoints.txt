- Introduces a novel covariance structure for multi-output GPs, extending the spectral (Bochner) approach.
- Parameters are interpretable (e.g., delay between outputs, phase difference).
- Defining covariance structures for multi-output GPs is challenging and current methods are limited.
- The proposed method is theoretically sound.
- Effectiveness is demonstrated on several datasets.
- Paper is clearly written and well-illustrated.
- Could become a standard for multi-output GPs if parameter learning is not problematic.
