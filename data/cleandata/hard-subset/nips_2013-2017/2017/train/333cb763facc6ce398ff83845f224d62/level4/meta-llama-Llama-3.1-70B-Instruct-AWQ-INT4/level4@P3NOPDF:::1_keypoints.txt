- Novel contribution: introduces a multi-output Gaussian process covariance function defined in the spectral domain.
- Builds on prior work: extends the authors' earlier spectral mixture approach to the multi-output setting.
- Technical challenge addressed: proposes a solution for cross-covariances that need not be positive-definite, yielding a spectral mixture covariance for multi-output GPs.
- Clarity: paper is well-written with a concise introduction and lucid explanation of the spectral mixture kernel.
- Novelty vs. prior work: thorough relation to previous work and sufficient novelty to justify publication.
- Experiments: evaluation is satisfactory for a theory paper but provides limited intuition, notably on the Jura dataset.
- Impact: extension to multi-output spectral-domain GPs has strong potential to influence GP and ML research.
- Recommendation: accept for publication.
