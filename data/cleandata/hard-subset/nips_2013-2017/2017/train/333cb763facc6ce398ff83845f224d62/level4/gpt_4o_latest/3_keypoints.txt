- Proposes a novel covariance structure for multi-output GPs extending the spectral (Bochner) kernel construction of A. G. Adams and R. P. Adams.  
- Model parameters are interpretable, allowing insights like inter-output delays and phase differences.  
- Defining multi-output GP covariances is challenging with few methods; this contribution addresses that gap.  
- Method is grounded in solid theoretical foundations.  
- Empirical efficacy demonstrated across multiple datasets.  
- Manuscript is well written and illustrations are appropriate and relevant.  
- Limited information on parameter learning; if learning is tractable, the method could become a standard approach.  
- Question: How does the method perform with sparse training datasets?  
- Question: How does the model represent cases where channels have very different length-scales and thus cannot be highly correlated?  
- Comment: Specify the number of training points used in the synthetic example (Section 4.1).  
- Minor remarks: A word appears to be missing on lines 74 and 140.
