- Proposes a novel covariance structure for multi-output GPs, extending the spectral approach based on Bochner’s theorem.  
- Parameters are interpretable (e.g., delays between outputs and phase differences).  
- Developing covariance structures for multi-output GPs is challenging and few methods exist—this fills an important gap.  
- Method is theoretically sound.  
- Efficacy demonstrated on multiple datasets through experiments.  
- Paper is well-written and illustrations effectively convey ideas.  
- Concern about parameter learning: paper lacks details on optimization/stability and this could affect practicality.  
- Question: How does the method perform with only a few training points?  
- Question: How does the model capture low correlation between channels with very different length-scales?
