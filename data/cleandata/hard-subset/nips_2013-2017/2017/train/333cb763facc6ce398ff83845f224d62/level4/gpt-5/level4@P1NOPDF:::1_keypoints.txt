- Proposes a multi-output Gaussian process covariance defined in the spectral domain, extending the spectral mixture approach.  
- Provides a construction for cross-covariances in multi-output GPs that yields valid spectral mixture cross-covariances.  
- Builds on and extends ideas from Wilson & Adams (2013).  
- Claims novel extension to the multi-output setting with sufficient novelty for publication.  
- Paper is well written with a concise, clear introduction to the problem and GP fundamentals.  
- Spectral mixture kernel and the proposed extensions are clearly explained.  
- Experimental evaluation is adequate for a methodological paper but offers limited intuition.  
- Jura dataset used in experiments is difficult to interpret.
