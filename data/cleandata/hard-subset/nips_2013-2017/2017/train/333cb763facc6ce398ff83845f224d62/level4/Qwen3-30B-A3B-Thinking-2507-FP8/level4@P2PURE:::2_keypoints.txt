- Introduces a novel multi-output GP covariance by specifying cross-power spectral densities and deriving cross-covariances via inverse Fourier transform.  
- Extends the mixture-of-Gaussians PSD approach from [6] (single-output) to multi-output GPs.  
- Evaluated on a synthetic dataset plus weather and Swiss Jura real-world datasets.  
- Fails to demonstrate whether the proposed kernel inherits the extrapolation capability emphasized in [6].  
- Experiments use standard benchmarks; lacking datasets where existing models perform poorly to show clear impact.  
- Statistical significance is unclear—reported standard deviations suggest performance differences across models may be negligible.  
- Conceptually sound, but impact would be strengthened by extrapolation tests and more challenging/targeted experiments.
