- Introduces a novel multi-output Gaussian process covariance function defined in the spectral domain, a valuable contribution worth further exploration.
- Extends prior single-output spectral methods to multi-output, addressing cross-covariance modeling without requiring positive-definiteness constraints.
- Results in a spectral mixture covariance formulation for multi-output Gaussian processes.
- Paper is well-structured with a concise, clear introduction and lucid explanation of the spectral mixture kernel.
- Proposed extensions appear original and the relationship to previous work is thoroughly explained.
- Experimental evaluation is satisfactory but not particularly insightful, with the Jura dataset results being especially weak.
- Overall, a well-written, coherent manuscript that meaningfully contributes to the field.
