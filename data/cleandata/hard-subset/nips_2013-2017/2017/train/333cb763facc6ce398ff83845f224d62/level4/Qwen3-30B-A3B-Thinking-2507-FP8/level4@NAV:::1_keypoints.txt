- Introduces a multi-output Gaussian process covariance defined in the spectral domain, filling an underexplored area.  
- Extends existing spectral-domain GP frameworks to the multi-output setting.  
- Addresses the key challenge of modeling cross-covariances that need not be positive-definite.  
- Proposes a method that yields a spectral mixture covariance for multi-output GPs.  
- Paper is well-written with a clear introduction and thorough explanation of the spectral mixture kernel.  
- Relationship to prior work is well-articulated and the novelty is sufficient for publication.  
- Experimental evaluation is adequate to validate the method, though results offer limited intuitive insight (notably for the Jura dataset).
