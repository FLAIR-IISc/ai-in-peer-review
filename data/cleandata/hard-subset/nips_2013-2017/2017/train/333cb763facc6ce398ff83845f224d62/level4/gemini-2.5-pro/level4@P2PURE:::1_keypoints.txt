- Introduces a multi-output Gaussian process covariance function designed in the spectral domain, extending spectral methods to MOGPs.  
- Extends Wilson & Adams (2013) to the multi-output setting.  
- Main challenge: modeling cross-covariance functions, which are not constrained to be positive definite.  
- Authors propose a principled solution yielding a novel spectral mixture covariance for MOGPs.  
- Paper is well-written with a clear introduction and lucid explanation of the spectral mixture kernel.  
- Proposed extensions are novel and well-situated within existing literature.  
- Experiments are adequate to validate the method but provide limited intuition; Jura dataset results are hard to interpret.
