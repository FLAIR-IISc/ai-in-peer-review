- Introduces a novel covariance structure for multi-output GPs that generalizes the spectral (Bochner) approach.  
- Builds on prior work by A. G. Adams and R. P. Adams.  
- Parameters are interpretable (e.g., delay between outputs, phase difference).  
- Defining covariance structures for multi-output GPs is challenging and few methods exist.  
- Method is theoretically sound and shown effective on several datasets.  
- Paper is well-written with relevant illustrations.  
- Lacks detail on learning/estimating parameters; parameter learning could be problematic.  
- Concern about model behavior with few training points (limited data scenarios).  
- Question about handling channels with significantly different length-scales and resulting correlation limitations.  
- Suggest specifying the number of training points in the synthetic example (Section 4.1).  
- Minor typos/missing words at lines 74 and 140.  
- Overall: valuable contribution likely to become a standard with minor revisions.
