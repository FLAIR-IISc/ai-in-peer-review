- Introduces a novel multi-output GP covariance by specifying cross-power spectral densities and using inverse Fourier transform.  
- Extends the single-output PSD-mixture idea from [6], which produced powerful kernels with extrapolation capabilities.  
- The extension is clever, but it's unclear if the multi-output covariance can also enable extrapolation like [6].  
- Experiments are limited to common datasets (synthetic, weather, Swiss Jura) and show similar performance to existing multi-output kernels.  
- Recommend evaluating on datasets where existing models perform poorly to better demonstrate strengths and limitations.  
- Unclear whether statistical significance tests were done for Tables 1 and 2; reported performances appear similar within standard deviations.  
- Overall: interesting contribution that requires additional experiments and analysis to increase its impact.
