- Introduces a novel covariance structure for multi-output GPs, generalizing the spectral (Bochner) approach.
- Parameters are interpretable (e.g., delay between outputs, phase difference).
- Addresses a challenging problem with few existing methods for multi-output GP covariances.
- Method is theoretically sound and shown effective on several datasets.
- Paper is clearly written with relevant illustrations.
- Limited information on parameter learning; scalability/optimization behavior is unclear.
- Reviewer expects the method could become a standard if parameter learning is not problematic.
- Question: How does the method perform with very few training points?
- Question: How does the model represent channels with very different length-scales and thus low correlation?
- Comment: Synthetic example (Section 4.1) should report the number of training points.
- Minor remark: A word appears to be missing in lines 74 and 140.
