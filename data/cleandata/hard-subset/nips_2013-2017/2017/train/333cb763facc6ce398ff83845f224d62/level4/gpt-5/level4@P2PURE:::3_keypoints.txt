- Introduces a new covariance structure for multi-output GPs using a spectral (Bochner-based) extension of Adams & Adams kernels.  
- Key advantage: parameters are interpretable (e.g., inter-output delays, phase differences).  
- Addresses a challenging, under-explored problem where few methods exist for multi-output GP covariance design.  
- Method is theoretically sound and shown effective on several datasets.  
- Paper is well written, method clearly described, and illustrations appropriate.  
- Lacks detail on parameter learning; effectiveness may depend on how hard parameter learning is.  
- Question: how does the method perform with only a few training points?  
- Question/curiosity: how does the model reflect that a large length-scale channel cannot be highly correlated with a small length-scale channel?  
- Comment: synthetic example (Section 4.1) should specify the number of training points.  
- Minor remarks: a word appears to be missing on lines 74 and 140.
