- Proposes the MOSM kernel, a new covariance structure generalizing the spectral (Bochner) approach to model delays and phase differences in MOGPs.  
- Paper is well-written, clearly organized, and uses helpful illustrations and examples.  
- MOSM kernel is theoretically sound and effective on synthetic and real-world datasets.  
- Authors compare to existing MOGP methods and show competitive performance.  
- Model parameters are clearly interpretable, improving over previous methods.  
- Related work and limitations are discussed thoroughly.  
- Paper lacks detail on parameter learning, posing potential optimization and training concerns.  
- Unclear how the method performs with few training points; behavior in low-data regimes is not explained.  
- Could use a more thorough discussion of the method’s limitations.  
- Recommendation: Accept with minor revisions to address parameter learning details and few-shot behavior.  
- Rating: 8/10.
