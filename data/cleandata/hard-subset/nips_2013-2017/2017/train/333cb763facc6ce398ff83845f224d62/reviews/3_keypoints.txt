- Introduces a new covariance structure for multioutput GPs that generalizes the spectral (Bochner) approach.  
- Model parameters are interpretable (e.g., delays between outputs, phase differences).  
- Defining covariance structures for multioutput GPs is challenging and few methods currently exist.  
- Proposed method is theoretically sound and shown effective on several datasets.  
- Paper is clearly written and illustrations/choices are relevant.  
- Paper lacks detail on parameter learning; potential concern about optimisation/training difficulty.  
- If parameter learning is not problematic, the method could become a standard for multioutput GPs.  
- Question: How does the method behave with few training points?  
- Question/curiosity: How does the model handle correlations between channels with very different length-scales?  
- Comment: Synthetic example (Section 4.1) should state the number of training points.
