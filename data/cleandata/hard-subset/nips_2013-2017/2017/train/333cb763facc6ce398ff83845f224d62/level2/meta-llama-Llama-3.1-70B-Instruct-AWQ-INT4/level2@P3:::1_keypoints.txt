- Constructs a positive-definite matrix of complex-valued spectral densities and transforms via inverse Fourier transform using Cramér's Theorem.  
- Provides a thorough theoretical foundation with detailed derivation and discussion of relationships to existing MOGP methods.  
- Presents experiments on synthetic and real-world datasets showing the MOSM kernel can model complex inter-channel relationships.
