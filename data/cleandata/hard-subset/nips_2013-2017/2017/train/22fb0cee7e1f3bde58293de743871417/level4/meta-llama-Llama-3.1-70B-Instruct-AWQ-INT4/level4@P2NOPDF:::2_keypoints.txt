- Goal: compute optimal patient screening policy using a finite-state stochastic disease model to minimize screening costs and delays.  
- Contribution: proposes a computationally efficient approximate solution for the screening policy.  
- Empirical result: simulated breast cancer experiments show reduced delays with similar costs compared to an annual-screening baseline.
