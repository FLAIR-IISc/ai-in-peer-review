- Introduces DPSCREEN, a method for identifying optimal screening policies across broad disease classes.  
- DPSCREEN incorporates both a patient’s screening history and patient-specific features into the disease model.  
- Personalization enables more efficient screening policies while maintaining timely disease detection.  
- Paper is clearly written and technically rigorous.  
- Approach is well-motivated and situates itself with relevant related work.  
- Provides a concise, thorough mathematical treatment including computational complexity.  
- Validated on a large real-world dataset (45k patients) from the Athena Health Network.  
- Empirical results demonstrate the benefits of personalization over existing policies.  
- Model validation covers multiple disease model types.  
- Overall delivers a clear technical contribution with strong potential for practical impact.
