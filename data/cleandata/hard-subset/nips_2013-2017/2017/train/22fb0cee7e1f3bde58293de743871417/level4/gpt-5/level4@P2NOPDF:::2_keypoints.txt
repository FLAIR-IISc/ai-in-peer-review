- Personalized screening is an important and timely research direction.
- The proposed algorithm is reasonable and well developed.
- Empirical results on simulated breast cancer data are promising.
- The paper is clearly written.
- The method unrealistically assumes the patient’s disease model is known, limiting practical applicability.
- Methodological novelty appears limited.
- Experiments only compare against a simple annual-screening baseline.
- The paper lacks evaluation of robustness to model misspecification and sensitivity to model uncertainty.
- Overall: topic and algorithm are sensible, but contribution is modest and requires stronger evidence of practical relevance (robustness and broader comparisons).
