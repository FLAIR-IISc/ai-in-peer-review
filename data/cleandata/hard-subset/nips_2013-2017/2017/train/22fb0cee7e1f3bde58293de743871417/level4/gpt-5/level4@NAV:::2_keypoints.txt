- Personalized screening is an important and relevant research area.  
- The proposed algorithm is sound, well-developed, and computationally efficient.  
- Experiments show encouraging results: reduced delays while maintaining cost versus a simple baseline.  
- The paper is clearly written.  
- The method assumes the patient’s disease model is known, which is unrealistic; inferring the model from data is the main practical challenge.  
- Methodological novelty is limited.  
- Experiments only compare against an annual screening baseline.  
- The paper lacks robustness analysis to model inaccuracies; sensitivity to disease-model uncertainty is not evaluated.  
- Overall: interesting topic and reasonable algorithm, but modest novelty and insufficient evidence of practical relevance.
