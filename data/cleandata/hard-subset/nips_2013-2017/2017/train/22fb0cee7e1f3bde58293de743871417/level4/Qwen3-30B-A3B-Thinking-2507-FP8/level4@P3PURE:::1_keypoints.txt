- Introduces DPSCREEN, a method for determining optimal screening policies for large classes of diseases.  
- Novelty: incorporates patients' clinical screening history and disease-specific patient features for personalization.  
- Personalization yields efficiency gains over existing policies without compromising detection timeliness.  
- Paper is well-written and technically sound.  
- Thorough related-work coverage and concise, rigorous discussion of algorithmic foundations and computational complexity.  
- Validated on a large-scale 45,000-patient Athena Health Network dataset.  
- Results generalize across multiple disease model types and show clear practical impact.
