- Introduces DPSCREEN, a method to determine optimal screening policies for many diseases.
- DPSCREEN personalizes screening by using both patients' clinical screening histories and disease-relevant patient features.
- Personalization yields efficiency gains over existing screening policies without delaying disease detection.
- Paper is well-written and technically sound with clear motivation.
- Authors discuss related work and provide a concise, thorough mathematical description of the algorithm and its computational complexity.
- Validated on a large real-world dataset of 45,000 patients from the Athena Health Network.
- Demonstrates improvements across multiple disease model types, highlighting versatility.
- Makes a clear technical contribution with potential for practical impact.
