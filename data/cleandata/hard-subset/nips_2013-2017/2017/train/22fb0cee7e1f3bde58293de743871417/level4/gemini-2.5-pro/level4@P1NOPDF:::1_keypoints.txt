- Proposes DPSCREEN, a method for computing optimal screening policies for a broad class of diseases.
- Integrates a patient’s clinical (screening) history with disease-specific features to personalize schedules.
- Personalization produces large efficiency gains over existing policies without harming detection timeliness.
- Paper is well-written, technically sound, and well-motivated within the related-work context.
- Provides concise mathematical foundations and analyzes computational complexity.
- Validated on a large real-world dataset of 45,000 Athena Health patients, showing clear improvement.
- Demonstrates robustness across different types of disease models.
- Represents a clear technical contribution with substantial potential practical impact.
