- Paper reproduces user questions in Battleship using an artificial language with compositional (Montagovian) semantics.
- Uses a log-linear model over four simple question features: expected information gain, answer type (Boolean vs numerical), and whether the question references board features.
- Motivation in terms of active learning is reasonable.
- Core ideas are obvious; the task is very simple, highly constrained, and logically unambiguous.
- Reviewer does not believe the paper will interest the NIPS community.
