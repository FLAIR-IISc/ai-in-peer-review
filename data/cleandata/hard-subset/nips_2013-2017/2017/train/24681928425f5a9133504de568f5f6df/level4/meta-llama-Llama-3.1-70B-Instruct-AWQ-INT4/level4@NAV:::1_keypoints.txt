- Paper reproduces user queries in a Battleship scenario by reformulating square inquiries as compositional Montagovian semantic questions.
- Authors use a log-linear model leveraging four straightforward question features (e.g., expected information gain, answer type—Boolean or numerical—and board feature involvement).
- Active learning motivation is sound.
- Ideas are straightforward and lack novelty.
- Task is overly simplistic, highly constrained, and lacks logical ambiguity.
- Relevance and appeal to the NIPS community are questionable.
