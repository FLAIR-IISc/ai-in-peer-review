- Models human question-asking in a modified Battleship using an artificial compositional (Montagovian) question language.
- Uses a log-linear model to score candidate questions based on four simple features.
- Features include expected information gain (informativeness), answer type (Boolean vs numerical), and whether the question concerns board features.
- High-level motivation framed as active learning is reasonable.
- Experimental task is overly simple, highly constrained, and logically unambiguous.
- Lacks technical novelty and a sufficiently sophisticated problem formulation for the NIPS audience.
