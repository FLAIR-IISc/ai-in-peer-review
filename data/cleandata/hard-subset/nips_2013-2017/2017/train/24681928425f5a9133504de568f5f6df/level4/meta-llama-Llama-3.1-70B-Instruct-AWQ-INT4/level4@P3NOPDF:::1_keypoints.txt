- Paper studies reproducing user questions in Battleship by replacing square queries with an artificial compositional language having Montagovian semantics.  
- Uses a log-linear model with four simple features: expected information gain, answer type (Boolean vs numerical), and two board-feature indicators.  
- Motivation tied to active learning, which is reasonable.  
- Methods and ideas are straightforward and lack novelty.  
- Task is simple, highly constrained, and logically unambiguous.  
- Reviewer doubts the paper’s relevance and impact for the NIPS community.
