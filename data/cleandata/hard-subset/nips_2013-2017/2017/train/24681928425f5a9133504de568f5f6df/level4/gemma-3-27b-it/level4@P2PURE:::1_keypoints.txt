- Goal: Reproduce user questions in a Battleship game by reformulating square queries as an artificial language with compositional Montagovian semantics.  
- Method: Log-linear model using four basic features (including expected information gain/informativeness, answer type—Boolean vs numerical, and whether the question concerns the game board).  
- Framing: Motivation is sensible and cast within active learning.  
- Criticism: Ideas are largely straightforward; the task is very simple, heavily constrained, and logically unambiguous.  
- Conclusion: Unlikely to be of significant interest to the NIPS community.
