- Proposes generating Battleship queries using an artificial compositional (Montagovian) language instead of direct square inquiries.
- Employs a log-linear model over four simple features: expected information gain, answer type (Boolean vs numeric), and whether the question references board features.
- Motivation in the context of active learning is reasonable.
- Core ideas are largely straightforward and lacking novelty.
- The task is highly constrained, simple, and logically unambiguous.
- Reviewer doubts the paper will interest the NIPS community.
