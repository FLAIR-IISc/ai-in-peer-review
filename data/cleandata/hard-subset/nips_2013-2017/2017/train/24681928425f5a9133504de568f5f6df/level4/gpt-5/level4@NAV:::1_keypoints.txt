- Paper recreates user queries in a Battleship game by replacing cell-specific questions with queries in an artificial language with compositional Montagovian semantics.  
- A log-linear model is trained using four simple features, including expected information gain (informativeness), answer type (Boolean vs numerical), and whether the question references board features.  
- The active learning motivation is reasonable.  
- The ideas are fairly obvious.  
- The task setup is very simple, highly constrained, and logically unambiguous.  
- Reviewer doubts the work will interest the NIPS community.
