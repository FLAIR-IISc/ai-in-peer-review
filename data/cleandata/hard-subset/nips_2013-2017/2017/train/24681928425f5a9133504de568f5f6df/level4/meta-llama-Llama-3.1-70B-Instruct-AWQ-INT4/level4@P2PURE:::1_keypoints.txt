Paper models battleship queries as compositional logical questions using Montagovian semantics.  
It replaces individual square inquiries with compositional questions and uses a log-linear model.  
Model uses four simple features, including expected information gain (informativeness), answer type (Boolean vs numerical), and board-feature involvement.  
Motivation from an active learning perspective is reasonable.  
Methods and ideas are intuitive; the task is overly simplistic, highly constrained, and lacks logical ambiguity.  
Reviewer doubts the paper will resonate with the NIPS community.
