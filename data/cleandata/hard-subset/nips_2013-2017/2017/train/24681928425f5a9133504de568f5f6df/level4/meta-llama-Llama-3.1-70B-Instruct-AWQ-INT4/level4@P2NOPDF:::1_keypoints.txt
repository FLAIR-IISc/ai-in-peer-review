- Recreates user queries in Battleship using an artificial compositional language with Montagovian semantics.  
- Uses a log-linear model based on four simple question features (including expected information gain/informativeness, answer type—Boolean or numerical, and whether the question concerns board features).  
- Motivation is reasonable, particularly for active learning.  
- The ideas are somewhat self-evident.  
- The task is extremely simple, highly constrained, and logically unambiguous.  
- Reviewer doubts the paper will resonate with or meaningfully contribute to the NIPS community.
