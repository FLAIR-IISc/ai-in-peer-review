- Reproduces user questions in Battleship by using an artificial compositional (Montagovian) language instead of individual-square queries.  
- Employs a log-linear model over four simple features (expected information gain, answer type: Boolean/numerical, and whether the question references board features).  
- Motivation from an active learning perspective is reasonable.  
- The approach and task are straightforward, very simple, highly constrained, and logically unambiguous.  
- Reviewer doubts the work’s significance and interest for the NIPS community.
