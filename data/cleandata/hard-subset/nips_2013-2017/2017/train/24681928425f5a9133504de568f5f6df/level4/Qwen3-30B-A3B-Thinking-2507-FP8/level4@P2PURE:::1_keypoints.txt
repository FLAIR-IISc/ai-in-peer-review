- Models user queries in a Battleship variant using an artificial compositional (Montagovian) language.
- Uses a log-linear model with four simple features: expected information gain, answer type (Boolean/numerical), and whether the query references board features.
- High-level motivation (active learning) is reasonable.
- Main weaknesses: ideas are largely obvious; the task is overly simple, highly constrained, and logically unambiguous.
- Overall conclusion: insufficient novelty and technical depth for the NIPS audience.
