- Studies replicating user questioning in Battleship by recasting square queries as questions in an artificial compositional (Montagovian) language.
- Uses a log-linear model with four features, including expected information gain, answer type (Boolean vs numerical), and board-relevance.
- Active learning motivation is sound in principle.
- Concepts presented are largely intuitive and not particularly novel.
- Experimental task is simple, heavily constrained, and lacks logical ambiguity.
- Reviewer does not expect this work to attract significant interest from the NeurIPS audience.
