- Core idea is potentially interesting, but the paper’s execution needs significant improvement before NeurIPS standard.  
- Proposed “RNN” formulation is nonstandard: h_t only depends on top-layer memory vectors and not on h_{t-1}; rationale for this simplification is not provided.  
- Model specification is incomplete: key distributional details (e.g., Gamma parameterization and prior for θ_t) are missing.  
- Claim of “implicit distributions” is incorrect: Equations 9–10 define explicit Gamma conditionals that can be computed in closed form.  
- The sentence about limitations on “fanatical data and disaster data” is unclear and needs elaboration.  
- Subsection around lines 133–155 is hard to follow; it’s unclear what the “loss function” in Equation 15 specifically is.  
- Evaluation metrics (MSE/PMSE) are inappropriate for count/PF models; predictive log-likelihood should be reported.  
- Latent dimensionality K used in experiments is too small; authors should evaluate larger K (e.g., K≥100).  
- Text around lines 224–232 does not clearly connect to Figure 3; the relation should be clarified.  
- Detailed variational update equations (Eqs. 11–14) are standard and belong in the supplement to improve readability.  
- Figure 4 caption is illegible and must be fixed.  
- The manuscript overuses passive voice (notably in the abstract); revise for active voice and clearer scientific writing.  
- Overall writing quality is low with many typos, grammatical errors, missing articles, and awkward phrases; thorough proofreading required (several line-specific examples provided).
