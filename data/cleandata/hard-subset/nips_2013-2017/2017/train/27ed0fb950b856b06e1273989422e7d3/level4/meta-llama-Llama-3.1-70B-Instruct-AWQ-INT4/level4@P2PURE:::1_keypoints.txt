- Paper proposes deep dynamic Poisson factorization with a simplified RNN-like structure and variational inference, but needs stronger execution for NIPS acceptance.  
- Eq. 4 does not follow a standard RNN: h_t^{(n)} depends only on top-layer memory vectors and omits same-layer temporal dependence—author justification requested.  
- Section 2 omits or leaves unclear key model equations (Gamma parameterization in Eq. 4 and the distribution generating theta_t in Eq. 3).  
- Claim that Eqs. 9–10 define “implicit distributions” is misleading; they are explicit Gamma distributions computable in closed form.  
- The sentence about weaknesses on “fanatical data and disaster data” (lines 30–31) is unclear and needs concrete elaboration/examples.  
- Subsection lines 133–155 is unclear, especially the definition and role of the “loss function” in Eq. 15—needs clearer explanation.  
- MSE and PMSE are not ideal metrics for PF-like models; add metrics such as predictive log-likelihood for a more comprehensive evaluation.  
- The chosen latent dimension K is too small in experiments; test larger values (e.g., K=100) to show robustness.  
- Discussion in lines 224–232 does not clearly connect to Fig. 3 and should be revised for clarity and alignment with the figure.  
- Variational update equations (Eqs. 11–14) are not essential to the main text and can be moved to the supplement.  
- Caption in Fig. 4 is hard to read and should be rewritten for clarity.  
- Use active voice (especially in the abstract) and improve overall writing style.  
- Paper contains many typos, grammatical errors, and poor phrasing throughout (multiple line-specific examples provided); thorough proofreading and editing required.
