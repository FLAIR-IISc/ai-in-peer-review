- Paper proposes adding long-range temporal dependence to deep Poisson factorization using an RNN-like architecture and variational inference to learn network parameters.  
- The claim about modeling nonnegative numbers (lines 86–87) is incorrect — the Poisson Randomized Gamma distribution is used for that.  
- Table 2 should have the best results bolded for clarity.  
- Held-out perplexity would be a stronger evaluation metric than (or in addition to) MSE/PMSE.  
- While prior limitations for long-range dependency are addressed, the paper should compare to a single-level deep dynamic PF; the LSTM comparison is useful but learning-algorithm differences mean it doesn’t fully justify the proposed multi-level hierarchy.  
- The manuscript contains numerous grammatical errors and typos (many examples listed) that hinder readability and need correction.
