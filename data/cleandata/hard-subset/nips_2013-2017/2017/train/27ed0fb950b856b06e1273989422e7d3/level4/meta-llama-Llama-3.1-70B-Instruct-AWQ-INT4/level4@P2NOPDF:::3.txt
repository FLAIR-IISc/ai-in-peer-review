This paper proposes a novel approach to incorporating long-range temporal dependence in a Poisson factorization model with a deep structure. Unlike previous works on dynamic Poisson factorization (PF), this paper utilizes a recurrent neural network (RNN) like structure to model long-term dependencies. The inference process is carried out using variational inference, accompanied by optimization routines to determine the neural network parameters.

The paper demonstrates good technical content, but it is marred by several grammatical and factual errors. These issues are outlined below:

1. The statement on lines 86-87 regarding the modeling of nonnegative numbers is incorrect. The Poisson Randomized Gamma distribution is actually employed for modeling nonnegative numbers.

2. To improve the clarity of the results, it would be beneficial to bold the best results in Table 2.

3. In the experiments presented in Section 4.2, held-out perplexity would have been a stronger indicator of performance, although the use of MSE/PMSE is acceptable.

4. The paper adequately addresses the limitations of existing works in modeling long-range dependencies (references 1 and 2). However, to better illustrate the utility of the proposed method with multiple levels of hierarchy, a comparison with dynamic PF using a one-level deep architecture would have been useful. While the comparison with LSTM is informative, the differing learning algorithms make it difficult to justify the need for multiple levels of hierarchy in the proposed method.

5. There are several grammatical errors and typos throughout the paper that detract from its readability. These include:
- "unit the data" should be "until the data"
- "Noticing the lack of capturing" is an incomplete sentence
- The tense of "PFA factorized a count matrix" is inconsistent with the surrounding sentences
- "other data with different pattern long-time dependence" is unclear
- "Examples for this including" is an incomplete sentence
- "shows excellent results on predict and" is unclear
- "However, it is rough for them" is unclear
- "V-dimension sequentially" contains a typo
- "K-dimension latent variable" contains a typo
- "due to the including of feature-wise" is unclear
- "recommend systems" should be "recommendation systems"

Addressing these errors and suggestions would significantly improve the paper's clarity and overall quality.
