This paper discusses the inclusion of long-term temporal dependence in a Poisson factorization model with deep structure. Unlike previous work on dynamic PF, this paper uses a structure similar to a recurrent neural network for modeling long-term dependence. Finally, the parameters of the neural network are estimated, and variational inference is used with some optimization routines to find them. This paper has good technical content, but also some grammatical and factual errors, which are listed below:

1. The statement made in lines 86-87 about non-negative number modeling is incorrect. The Poisson randomized gamma distribution is used for non-negative number modeling.
2. Please bold the best results in Table 2.
3. There should have been a stronger indicator in the 4.2 section of the held-out perplexity test, although MSE/PMSE is fine.
4. The limitations of existing work on long-term dependence modeling (reference 1,2) are adequately discussed in this paper. However, a comparison with dynamic PF with a deep architecture can be made to illustrate the applicability of the proposed method with multiple levels of classification. Comparison with LSTM is useful, but the learning algorithms are different. Therefore, this comparison does not support the need for multiple levels of classification in the proposed method.
