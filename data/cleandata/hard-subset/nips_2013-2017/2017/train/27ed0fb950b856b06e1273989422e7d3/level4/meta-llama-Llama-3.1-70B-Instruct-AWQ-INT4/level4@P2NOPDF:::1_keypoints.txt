- Overall: idea of deep dynamic Poisson factorization is interesting but the paper needs significant improvements in execution and clarity before acceptance.
- Model/RNN structure: Eq. 4 does not follow a standard RNN — h_t^{(n)} depends only on top-layer memory vectors, not previous time steps for the same layer; reasoning for this simplification is not given.
- Missing model details: several equations and parameterizations are omitted (e.g., Gamma parameterization in Eq. 4 and the distribution generating theta_t in Eq. 3), harming clarity.
- Incorrect claim about implicit distributions: Eqs. 9–10 are explicit Gamma distributions and theta_tk can be computed in closed form conditioned on h^{(0)}, so calling them “implicit” is incorrect.
- Unclear sentence/claim: the statement about the method having “weak points” for other long-term dependence patterns (lines 30–31) is vague and needs elaboration.
- Loss function explanation unclear: subsection lines 133–155 and the definition of the “loss function” in Eq. 15 are not clearly explained.
- Evaluation metrics: MSE and PMSE are inappropriate for PF-like models; predictive log-likelihood and other suitable metrics should be included.
- Latent dimensionality K too small: experimental K (line 207) seems low; larger values (e.g., K=100) should be tried to better capture patterns.
- Text–figure mismatch: discussion in lines 224–232 is hard to reconcile with Fig. 3; more context and clearer linkage are needed.
- Variational updates placement: Eqs. 11–14 (update equations) could be moved to the supplement to improve paper flow.
- Figure caption/readability: caption in Fig. 4 is hard to read and should be revised for clarity.
- Writing style: avoid passive voice in places (notably the abstract) and follow general writing advice.
- Writing quality and typos: overall poor writing with many typos, missing articles, incomplete sentences, and specific errors noted (several line and figure examples provided).
