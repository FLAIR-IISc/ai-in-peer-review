- Proposes deep dynamic Poisson factorization (DDPF) extending Poisson factorization with temporal dependencies using a simplified RNN and variational inference; evaluated on five real datasets.  
- Overall contribution is potentially valuable but requires significant revisions before NIPS-quality acceptance.  
- Eq. 4 does not match a standard RNN: h_t appears to depend only on top-layer memory and not on previous time steps within the same layer—clarification needed.  
- Section 2 has incomplete model definitions: Gamma parameterization in Eq. 4 and the distribution of θ_t in Eq. 3 are unspecified.  
- The paper incorrectly labels some distributions as “implicit”: Eqs. 9–10 give explicit Gamma distributions, and θ_tk conditioned on h^(0) has a closed-form distribution.  
- The claim about limitations for data with “different pattern long-time dependence” (e.g., fanatical or disaster data) is unclear and needs detailed explanation.  
- Subsection lines 133–155 is unclear, especially the definition and explanation of the “loss function” in Eq. 15.  
- Evaluation metrics are inadequate: MSE and PMSE are not well-suited for PF models; predictive log-likelihood (at minimum) should be reported.  
- The chosen latent dimension K is likely too small; experiments should consider much larger values (e.g., K ≥ 100).  
- The relationship between discussion in lines 224–232 and Fig. 3 is not clear and needs to be elucidated.  
- Update equations for variational inference (Eqs. 11–14) could be moved to supplementary material.  
- Fig. 4’s caption is illegible and must be rewritten for clarity.  
- Improve writing style by reducing passive voice (particularly in the abstract).  
- Paper contains numerous writing and typographical errors and missing articles; numerous specific examples listed by line (needs comprehensive proofreading).
