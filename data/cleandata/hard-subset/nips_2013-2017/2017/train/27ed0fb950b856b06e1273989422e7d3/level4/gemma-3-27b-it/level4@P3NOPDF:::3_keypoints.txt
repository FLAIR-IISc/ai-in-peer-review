- Proposes a novel recurrent neural network augmentation to Poisson Factorization to capture long-range temporal dependencies.  
- Technical content is strong, showing solid understanding of PF and recurrent networks.  
- Empirical results are encouraging but could be made clearer (e.g., bold best results in Table 2).  
- Contains a factual inaccuracy: the claim about modeling nonnegative numbers is wrong; Poisson Randomized Gamma is typically used.  
- Evaluation metrics are limited to MSE and PMSE; held-out perplexity should be added to better assess modeling of the data distribution.  
- Comparison to LSTM is useful but insufficient due to differing learning algorithms (variational inference vs. backpropagation).  
- Needs a more direct baseline: compare against a single-layer dynamic PF model to justify the multi-level hierarchy.  
- Numerous grammatical errors and typos throughout the manuscript hinder readability and require thorough proofreading.  
- Recommendation: accept conditionally, pending correction of factual errors, stronger experimental comparisons/metrics, and language cleanup.
