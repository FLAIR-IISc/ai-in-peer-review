1) Contribution: Proposes adding long-range temporal dependencies to Poisson factorization via a deep RNN-like recurrent structure and performs inference with variational methods — technically sound.

2) Technical correction: The paper incorrectly states Poisson models nonnegative numbers; the Poisson Randomized Gamma distribution (not plain Poisson) is used for nonnegative data and this must be fixed (Lines 86–87).

3) Presentation: Bold the best-performing results in Table 2 for clarity.

4) Evaluation: Recommend using held-out perplexity (instead of or in addition to MSE/PMSE) as a stronger metric for temporal modeling.

5) Hierarchy justification: To justify the multi-level hierarchy, compare against a single-level dynamic PF baseline; the LSTM comparison alone is insufficient due to different optimization procedures.

6) Readability: Manuscript contains numerous grammatical and typographical errors that impede readability; reviewer provides many specific phrasing corrections (e.g., “unit the data” → “unite the data”, tense fixes, “recommend systems” → “recommendation systems”).
