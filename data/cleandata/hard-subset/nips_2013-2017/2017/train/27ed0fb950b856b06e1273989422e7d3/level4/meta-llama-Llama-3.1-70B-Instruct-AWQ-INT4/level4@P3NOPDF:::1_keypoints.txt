- Introduces deep dynamic Poisson factorization (PF) with a simplified RNN-like structure and variational inference plus an extra maximization over neural network parameters; experiments on five datasets.
- The proposed "RNN" in Eq. 4 is not a traditional RNN since h_t^{(n)} lacks dependence on previous time steps for the same layer; authors must justify this design.
- Several model equations are missing or unclear: Gamma parameterization in Eq. 4 and the distribution generating theta_t in Eq. 3 are not specified.
- The paper incorrectly labels Eqs. 9 and 10 as "implicit distributions"—they are explicit Gamma distributions conditional on h^{(0)}.
- The sentence about poor performance on "fanatical data and disaster data" (lines 30–31) is unclear and needs concrete explanation or examples.
- Subsection lines 133–155 is unclear, especially the definition and role of the "loss function" in Eq. 15; provide a clear definition and explanation.
- MSE/PMSE is an inappropriate sole metric for PF-like models; include predictive log-likelihood for more meaningful evaluation.
- The latent dimension K used in experiments (line 207) is too small; authors should test much larger K (e.g., K ≥ 100) to show scalability and robustness.
- The text in lines 224–232 does not clearly connect to Fig. 3; revise the discussion to explain the figure’s relevance.
- Update equations for variational inference (Eqs. 11–14) are not essential to the main paper and can be moved to the supplement.
- Fig. 4 caption is hard to read and should be rewritten to be clearer and more concise.
- Writing quality is poor with many typos, passive voice overuse, missing articles, and incomplete sentences; overall editing is required.
- Provides specific examples of problematic wording/typos (e.g., "high-dimensional", "factorize", "Dirichlet", "visual representation", "transmission") that need correction.
- Overall: idea is interesting but the paper requires substantial clarification, corrections to equations/claims, better evaluation, larger-scale experiments, clearer figures, and major writing improvements before acceptance.
