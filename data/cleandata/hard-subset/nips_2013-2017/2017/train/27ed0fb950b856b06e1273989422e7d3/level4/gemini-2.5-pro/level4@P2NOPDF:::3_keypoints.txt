- Novel integration of a recurrent architecture into deep Poisson factorization to capture long-range temporal dependencies in count data.
- Effectively addresses limitations of prior dynamic Poisson factorization models that rely on first-order Markov assumptions.
- Technical inaccuracy: the manuscript incorrectly describes Poisson as modeling non-negative reals; it likely uses a compound Poisson/Rand­omized Gamma and should be corrected.
- Evaluation metric concern: held-out perplexity is recommended over MSE/PMSE for better assessment of probabilistic model fit.
- Baseline comparisons concern: need an ablation study (single-level vs deep hierarchy) and more controlled comparisons (same training algorithms) to justify the multi-level architecture.
- Experimental validation is currently incomplete and does not fully substantiate claims about the hierarchical model’s benefits.
- Clarity and presentation issues: numerous grammatical errors, typos, inconsistent tenses, and awkward phrasing hinder readability; tables should highlight best results.
- Overall recommendation: promising and technically sound work but requires correction of technical claims, stronger/controlled experiments, and thorough proofreading before publication.
