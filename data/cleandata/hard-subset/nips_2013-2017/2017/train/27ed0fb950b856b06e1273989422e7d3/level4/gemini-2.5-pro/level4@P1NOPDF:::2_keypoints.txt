1) Novel, promising method: RNN models time-varying shape parameters of latent gamma distributions and updates network parameters via expectations of latents to reduce sampling variance.

2) Manuscript contains multiple typos; critical error in Equation 10 must be fixed.

3) Authors fix the gamma scale parameter and update only shape—this major modeling choice needs detailed justification and discussion of effects on expressiveness and performance.

4) Experimental evaluation is insufficient: only two-layer results presented; need comparisons with single-layer and deeper models and ablation studies on hyperparameters (window size, number of factors).

5) Add a detailed graphical illustration of the model (e.g., similar to Chung et al. 2015 Fig.1) to clarify data flow and dependencies.

6) Update introduction and related work to discuss recent deep Poisson factor analysis literature (e.g., Zhou et al. 2016; Cong et al. 2017) and situate the contribution.
