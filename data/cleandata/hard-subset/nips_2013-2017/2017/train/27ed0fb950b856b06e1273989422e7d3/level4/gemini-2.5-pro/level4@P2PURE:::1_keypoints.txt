- Core idea is interesting but execution needs significant improvement for a top-tier venue.  
- Model formulation deviates from standard RNN: hidden state h_t^(n) depends only on layer above, not on h_{t-1}^(n); motivation for this simplification is unclear.

- Mislabeling of distributions as "implicit": θ_{tk} (Eq.9) and h (Eq.10) are explicit Gamma conditionals computable in closed form.

- Unclear loss/optimization description: the "loss function" in Eq.15 and its role in the inference scheme are not precisely defined.  
- Inappropriate evaluation metric: MSE/PMSE is unsuitable for Poisson models; recommend using perplexity or predictive log-likelihood.  
- Experimental setup concern: number of latent components K is small; try larger K (e.g., K=100) to assess capacity.
