- Novelty: Introduces an RNN-like mechanism within deep Poisson factorization to capture long-range temporal dependencies.  
- Technical soundness: Generative model and variational inference are well-developed; integration of deep and recurrent structures is non-trivial and coherent.  
- Experimental gap: Lacks an apples-to-apples comparison to a single-level (non-deep) version to justify the multi-level hierarchy.  
- Baseline issue: Comparison to an LSTM is useful but not sufficient due to differing probabilistic assumptions and learning algorithms.  
- Evaluation metrics: Using MSE/PMSE is acceptable but held-out perplexity should be reported for count-data probabilistic models.  
- Factual inaccuracy: Incorrect claim that Poisson models non-negative real numbers—Poisson is for non-negative integers; the Gamma models real-valued rates.  
- Table readability: Table 2 should highlight best-performing results (e.g., bold) to improve clarity.  
- Grammar and typos: Manuscript contains numerous grammatical errors and awkward phrases and requires thorough proofreading.  
- Overall: Promising, technically interesting contribution that needs stronger experiments and careful revision for clarity and correctness.
