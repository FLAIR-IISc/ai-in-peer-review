- Proposes a novel deep Poisson factorization using an RNN-like structure to model long-range temporal dependencies with variational inference for learning.
- Paper has solid technical content but is undermined by multiple grammatical and factual errors.
- Factual correction: the model uses the Poisson Randomized Gamma distribution to model nonnegative numbers (lines 86–87 statement is incorrect).
- Presentation suggestion: bold the best results in Table 2 to improve clarity.
- Evaluation suggestion: held-out perplexity would be a stronger performance metric than (or complementary to) MSE/PMSE.
- Comparative analysis concern: include a comparison to dynamic PF with a one-level deep architecture to justify the need for multiple hierarchy levels, since the LSTM comparison alone is confounded by different learning algorithms.
- Numerous grammatical errors and typos throughout the paper (many incomplete/unclear sentences and phrasing issues) that need correction to improve readability and quality.
