1. Proposes modeling long-term dependencies in count vectors using RNNs to parameterize gamma-distributed latent shape parameters, combining prior and likelihood information and optimizing via expectations to reduce sampling variance.  
2. The approach is compelling but the manuscript seems hastily prepared.  
3. Contains critical typographical errors (e.g., Equation 10).  
4. Treats the gamma scale parameter as fixed and only updates the shape parameter—this design choice needs more discussion and analysis.  
5. Experiments are limited to a two-layer model with no comparisons to single- or multi-layer variants, nor sufficient analysis of layer count, window size, or number of factors.  
6. Recommends adding detailed graphical illustrations of the full model for clarity (e.g., similar to Chung et al. 2015 Fig. 1).  
7. Advises discussing recent related work on deep Poisson factor analysis (e.g., Zhou et al. 2016; Cong et al. 2017) in the Introduction.
