- Novel contribution: uses RNNs to parametrize time-dependent gamma shape parameters for count vectors, integrating prior and likelihood and updating network parameters via expected latents to reduce sampling variance and improve efficiency.

1. Manuscript contains typographical errors (e.g., equation 10) that need correction.  
2. The choice to fix the gamma scale parameter while updating only the shape parameter is unexplained and requires justification and analysis.  
3. Experiments are limited to a two-layer model; comparisons to single-layer and deeper models and sensitivity analyses (window size, number of factors) are missing.  
4. The paper lacks detailed graphical illustrations of the full model—adding a comprehensive diagram (e.g., similar to Chung et al. 2015 Fig.1) would aid understanding.  
5. The introduction should discuss recent deep Poisson factor analysis work (e.g., Zhou et al. 2016; Cong et al. 2017) to better situate the contribution.
