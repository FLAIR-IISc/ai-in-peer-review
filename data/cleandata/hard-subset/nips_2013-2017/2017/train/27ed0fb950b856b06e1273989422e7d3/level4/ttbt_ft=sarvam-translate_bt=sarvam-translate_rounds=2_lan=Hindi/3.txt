This research paper deals with incorporating long-term temporal dependence into a deep structure Poisson factorization model. Unlike previous work on dynamic PF, this paper uses a structure similar to a recurrent neural network to model long-term dependence. Finally, the parameters of the neural network are estimated using variance estimation with some optimization routines. This paper has good technical content but also contains some grammatical and factual errors. These are listed below:

1. The statement on lines 86-87 about modeling non-negative numbers is incorrect. The Poisson-randomized gamma distribution is used to model non-negative numbers.
2. Please bold the best results in Table 2.
3. Held-out perplexity should have been a stronger indicator in the experiments in section 4.2, although MSE/PMSE are fine.
4. The limitations (reference 1,2) in the existing work for modeling long-term dependence are not adequately addressed in this paper. However, a comparison with dynamic PF with a single-level deep architecture might be useful to demonstrate the utility of the proposed method with multiple levels of hierarchy. A comparison with LSTM is useful, but the learning algorithms are different. Therefore, this comparison does not justify the need for multiple levels of hierarchy in the proposed method.
