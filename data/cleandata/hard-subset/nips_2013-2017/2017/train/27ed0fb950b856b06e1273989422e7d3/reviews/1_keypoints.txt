- Paper proposes deep dynamic Poisson factorization using a simplified RNN-like structure, with variational inference and extra optimization for NN parameters; evaluated on 5 real datasets.  
- Eq. 4 does not implement a true RNN: h_t^(n) depends only on top-layer memory vectors and not on previous same-layer time steps—please justify the simplified structure.  
- Section 2 omits key model equations/parameterizations (e.g., Gamma in Eq.4 and the distribution generating θ_t in Eq.3 are unclear).  
- Claim that certain distributions are "implicit" is incorrect: Eqs.9–10 are explicit Gamma distributions with closed-form conditionals.  
- The sentence about potential weaknesses on "fanatical data and disaster data" (lines 30–31) is unclear and needs elaboration.  
- The subsection around lines 133–155 is unclear—specifically, what exactly is the "loss function" in Eq.15?  
- Evaluation uses MSE/PMSE, which is inappropriate for PF models; report predictive log-likelihood (at least) for proper evaluation.  
- The number of latent factors K in experiments is too small; authors should try larger values (e.g., K ≈ 100).  
- The discussion in lines 224–232 is not clearly connected to Fig.3 and needs clarification.  
- The variational update equations (Eqs.11–14) can be moved to the supplement to improve flow.  
- Fig.4 caption is unreadable and must be fixed.  
- Writing quality is poor: avoid passive voice (especially in abstract) and correct many typos and grammatical errors.  
- Numerous specific typos/examples throughout the paper (e.g., "high-dimensional", "factorize", missing articles, misused "Dirichlet", Fig.1 labels, incomplete sentences).  
- Overall: idea is potentially interesting but the paper requires substantial clarification, corrections, and stronger experiments before being conference-ready.
