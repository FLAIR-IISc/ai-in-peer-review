- Paper proposes a recurrent neural-network-like deep Poisson factorization model to capture long-range temporal dependency, with variational inference for learning neural network parameters.  
- Technical content is strong and the paper adequately addresses limitations of prior dynamic PF works for long-range dependency.  
- The manuscript contains a factual error: the Poisson Randomized Gamma distribution, not the stated model, is used for modeling nonnegative numbers (lines 86–87).  
- Experimental reporting: held-out perplexity would be a stronger evaluation metric and should be emphasized alongside MSE/PMSE.  
- Table 2 needs formatting improvement: mark the best results in bold.  
- Method comparison: include a baseline of dynamic PF with a single-level deep architecture to justify the benefit of multiple hierarchy levels.  
- Comparison with LSTM is informative but not definitive, since learning algorithms differ and do not fully justify hierarchical depth.  
- Numerous grammatical errors and typos make the paper harder to read and must be fixed.
