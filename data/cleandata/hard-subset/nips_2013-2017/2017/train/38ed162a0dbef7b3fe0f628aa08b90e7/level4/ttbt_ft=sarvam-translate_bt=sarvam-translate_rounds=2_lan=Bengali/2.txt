The paper is well-organized, clearly written, technically sound, and combines concepts from two exciting fields (deep networks and kernels). The stability results may be of interest to the ML community.

- Further inspiration is needed on stability analysis in the submission. Currently, there is only one short sentence (lines 56-57: 'Finally, we note that deep predictive models have proven important for achieving robustness for adversarial examples [7]') that inspires the main contribution of this paper.
- The excessive use of the \kappa symbol in (A3) [line 193] can be misleading; it refers to a function defined in kernel K in Eq. (10).
- In the equation shown between lines 384 and 385, the second part ('and \forall v...') is unnecessary; considering the symmetry of kernel k, it is identical to the first constraint ('\forall u ...').
