- Analyzes convolutional kernel networks (CKNs), studying stability of their representations to C^1 diffeomorphisms (Eq. 4).  
- Proves stability under norm-preserving and non-expansive kernels (assumptions A1–A2) when patch sizes satisfy A3.  
- Outlines an extension of the framework from (R^d, +) to locally compact groups (Section 4).  
- Paper is nicely organized, clearly written, and technically sound.  
- Stability result is of interest to the ML community.  
- Suggestion: expand motivation for studying stability (beyond a single sentence about adversarial robustness).  
- Notation issue: κ is overloaded in (A3) and in Eq. (10), which may confuse readers.  
- Redundant constraint: the second part of the displayed equation between lines 384–385 is superfluous due to symmetry of k.  
- Missing definition: φ is not defined and should be introduced in Eq. (10) as ⟨φ(z), φ(z′)⟩_{H(K)}.  
- Typographical/logic issue: the inequality in lines 427–428 appears to be an equality, and | ||z|| − ||z|| |^2 should read | ||z|| − ||z′|| |^2.  
- Bibliography: page information is missing for [3, 6, 8, 13, 14, 18, 25–27, 30, 31].  
- Reference corrections/details: full citations or appearances provided for [9], [17], [19], and acceptance note for [32].
