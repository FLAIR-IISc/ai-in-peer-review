- Paper analyzes desirable properties of deep convolutional kernel networks (CKNs): near invariance to transformation groups and stability under diffeomorphic actions, tied to patch extraction, kernel mapping, and pooling design.
- Authors show these CKN properties can, under certain conditions, be transferred to convolutional neural networks (CNNs).
- Manuscript is well-structured and technically sound.
- Major limitation: lack of empirical experiments to validate the theoretical findings; experiments demonstrating invariance and stability are recommended.
- Suggestion to explore alternative kernel approximation methods (e.g., random projection or finite Fourier series) and assess their effect on stability.
