- Shows CKNs can achieve near-invariance to transformation groups and stability to diffeomorphisms.
- These properties hinge on careful design of patch extraction, kernel mapping, and pooling operators.
- Under certain assumptions, the theoretical guarantees extend from CKNs to standard CNNs.
- Paper is well-written and the technical analysis appears sound.
- Major limitation: no empirical validation — analysis is purely theoretical.
- Recommendation: include experiments demonstrating configured CKNs’ invariance and stability to strengthen claims.
- Question/request: clarify whether alternative kernel approximations (random projections, finite Fourier features) are applicable and how they affect stability/invariance.
