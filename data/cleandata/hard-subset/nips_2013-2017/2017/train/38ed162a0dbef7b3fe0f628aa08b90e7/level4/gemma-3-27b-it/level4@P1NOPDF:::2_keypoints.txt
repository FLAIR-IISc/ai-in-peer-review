- Paper studies Convolutional Kernel Networks (CKNs) and analyzes stability of learned representations to C¹ diffeomorphisms (e.g., translations) as formalized in Eq. (4).  
- Shows stability holds under conditions A1–A2 (norm-preserving, non-expansive kernels) and A3 (appropriate patch sizes).  
- Sketches an extension of the stability results to locally compact groups in Section 4.  
- Paper is well-organized, clearly written, and technically sound, effectively combining deep networks and kernel methods.  
- Stability result is likely to be of interest to the machine learning community.  
- The manuscript needs stronger motivation for the stability analysis beyond a single sentence linking Lipschitz stability to adversarial robustness.  
- Notation conflict: kappa in condition A3 is confusing because kappa also denotes the function defining kernel K in Eq. (10).  
- Redundant constraint: the second part of the displayed equation between lines 384–385 (“and ∀v …”) is redundant given kernel symmetry.  
- Missing definition: φ is not defined at line 416 and should be introduced in Eq. (10) as ⟨φ(z),φ(z')⟩_{H(K)}.  
- Typo/inequality error on lines 427–428: the expression under '=' should be | ||z|| - ||z'|| |² (not | ||z|| - ||z|| |²).  
- Several references are missing page numbers: [3, 6, 8, 13, 14, 18, 25–27, 30, 31].  
- Specific reference updates requested for [9], [17], [19], and [32] with corrected citation details provided.
