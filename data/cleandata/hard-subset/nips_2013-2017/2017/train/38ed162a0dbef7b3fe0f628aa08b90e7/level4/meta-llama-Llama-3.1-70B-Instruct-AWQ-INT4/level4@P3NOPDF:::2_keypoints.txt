- Paper analyzes Convolutional Kernel Networks (CKNs) and proves stability to C^1 diffeomorphisms (e.g., translations) as stated in Equation (4).  
- Stability is shown to hold for norm-preserving and non-expansive kernels (assumptions A1–A2) when patch sizes satisfy (A3).  
- The manuscript outlines an extension of the framework from (R^d,+) to locally compact groups (Section 4).  
- The paper is well-organized, clearly written, and technically sound, combining deep network and kernel ideas; the stability result is potentially of interest to the community.  
- The motivation for studying stability is weak: only a brief link to adversarial robustness is given and more context/explanation is needed.  
- Notation issue: the symbol κ is overloaded in (A3) and also used for the function defining kernel K in Equation (10), which can cause confusion.  
- A displayed equation between lines 384–385 contains a redundant constraint (“and ∀v…”); symmetry of kernel k makes it identical to the first constraint and it should be removed.  
- Missing definition: the feature map φ is not introduced in Equation (10) [⟨φ(z),φ(z')⟩_{H(K)}] and should be defined.  
- Line 427–428: the inequality under ‘=’ appears to actually be an equality, and there is a typo — | ||z|| − ||z|| |^2 should be | ||z|| − ||z'|| |^2.  
- Several references [3,6,8,13,14,18,25–27,30,31] are missing page information and should be completed.  
- Corrected/complete citations are provided for [9], [17], [19], and [32] and should replace the current entries.
