- Paper analyzes stability of Convolutional Kernel Network (CKN) representations to C^1 diffeomorphisms (e.g., translations) as defined in Eq. (4).  
- Shows stability holds for norm-preserving, non-expansive kernels (A1–A2) when patch sizes are chosen per A3.  
- Sketches an extension of the analysis from (R^d,+) to locally compact groups in Section 4.  
- Manuscript is well-organized, clearly written, technically sound, and connects deep networks with kernel methods.  
- Result is potentially of significant interest to the ML community.  
- Suggest expanding motivation for this specific form of stability (beyond a single sentence linking to adversarial robustness).  
- Notation issue: \kappa is overloaded (used in A3 and to define kernel K in Eq. (10)); recommend renaming one instance.  
- Redundant constraint: the second constraint between lines 384–385 is superfluous due to kernel symmetry.  
- Missing definition: the feature map \phi is used on line 416 but never formally defined; introduce it with Eq. (10).  
- Typo: | ||z|| - ||z|| |^2 should likely be | ||z|| - ||z'|| |^2 between lines 427–428; equality remark also noted.  
- Bibliography fixes: many references lack page information; update entries for [9], [17], [19], and [32] with full citation details.
