- Shows that with properly designed patch extraction, kernel mapping, and pooling, CKNs are nearly invariant to transformation groups and stable under diffeomorphisms.
- Argues that, under certain assumptions, these favorable CKN properties extend to CNNs.
- Manuscript is clearly written and technically sound.
- Primary weakness: no empirical experiments to substantiate the theoretical analysis; reviewer requests results confirming invariance and stability.
- Asks whether random projections or finite Fourier-feature kernel approximations could be used and what effect they'd have on stability.
