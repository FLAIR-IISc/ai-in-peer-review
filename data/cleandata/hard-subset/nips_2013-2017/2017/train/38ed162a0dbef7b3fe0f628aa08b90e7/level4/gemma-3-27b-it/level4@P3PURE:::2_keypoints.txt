- Paper analyzes stability of Convolutional Kernel Networks (CKNs) to C¹ diffeomorphisms (e.g., translations) as formalized in Eq. (4).
- Shows stability holds for norm-preserving, non-expansive kernels (assumptions A1–A2) with appropriately chosen patch sizes (A3).
- Sketches an extension of the stability result from (Rᵈ,+) to locally compact groups (Section 4).
- Paper is well-organized, clearly written, and technically sound; combines deep networks and kernel ideas and may interest the ML community.
- Main weakness: insufficient motivation for why the stability analysis matters (single short sentence linking to adversarial robustness is inadequate).
- Notation clash: κ used in (A3) conflicts with κ used to define kernel K in Eq. (10) and may confuse readers.
- Redundant constraint in the displayed equation between lines 384–385: the "∀v..." part is superfluous given kernel symmetry.
- Missing definition: φ is not introduced/defined where used (should be defined in Eq. (10) as the RKHS feature map).
- Potential error/typo in lines 427–428: the displayed inequality may actually be equality, and | ||z|| - ||z|| |² is incorrect — should be | ||z|| - ||z'|| |².
- Bibliography issues: page information missing for refs [3,6,8,13,14,18,25–27,30,31]; several entries need completion or updated publication details (examples provided for [9],[17],[19],[32]).
