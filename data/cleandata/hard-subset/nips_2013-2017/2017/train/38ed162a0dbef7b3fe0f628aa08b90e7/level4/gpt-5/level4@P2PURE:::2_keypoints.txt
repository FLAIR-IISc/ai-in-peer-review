- Paper studies convolutional kernel networks (CKN) and analyzes stability of their representations to C^1 diffeomorphisms (including translations), proving stability under assumptions A1–A2 for norm-preserving/non-expansive kernels when patch sizes satisfy A3; Section 4 outlines an extension to locally compact groups.  
- Manuscript is well organized, clearly written, and technically sound; the stability result is likely of interest to the ML community.  
- Motivation is weak: the paper provides only a brief sentence linking Lipschitz stability to adversarial robustness and would benefit from stronger motivation.  
- Notation issue: kappa is overloaded in (A3) (line 193) and conflicts with kappa that defines kernel K in Eq. (10).  
- Redundant constraint: the second part of the displayed equation between lines 384–385 is redundant by symmetry of the kernel k.  
- Missing definition: phi is not defined at line 416 and should be introduced in Eq. (10) via ⟨phi(z), phi(z')⟩_{H(K)}.  
- Typo/inequality error: lines 427–428 the inequality appears to hold with equality, and | ||z|| − ||z|| |^2 should read | ||z|| − ||z'|| |^2.  
- Several references ([3, 6, 8, 13, 14, 18, 25–27, 30, 31]) lack page information.  
- Reference [9] should be updated to Amit Daniely, Roy Frostig, Yoram Singer, NeurIPS 2016 (pages 2253–2261).  
- Reference [17] should be updated to Krikamol Muandet et al., Foundations and Trends in Machine Learning, 10(1–2):1–141.  
- Reference [19] should be updated to Anant Raj et al., AISTATS 2017, PMLR 54:1225–1235.  
- Reference [32] status should be updated to “accepted” (Yuchen Zhang, Percy Liang, Martin Wainwright, ICML 2017, accepted).
