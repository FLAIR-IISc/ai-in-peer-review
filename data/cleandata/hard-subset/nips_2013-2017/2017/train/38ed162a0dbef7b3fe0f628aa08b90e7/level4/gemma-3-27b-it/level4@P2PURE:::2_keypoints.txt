- Paper analyzes stability of Convolutional Kernel Networks (CKNs) to C^1 diffeomorphisms (e.g., translations) and shows stability under suitable patch sizes and norm-preserving/non-expansive kernels; outlines extension to locally compact groups.

- Manuscript is well-organized, clearly written, and technically sound; stability result is of likely interest to the ML community.

- Motivation for studying stability is weak — currently limited to a single sentence linking Lipschitz stability to adversarial robustness.

- Notation conflict: κ in (A3) (line 193) is confusing because κ also denotes the kernel-defining function in Eq. (10).

- Redundant constraint in the equation between lines 384–385: the second part is unnecessary due to kernel symmetry.

- Missing definition of φ in Eq. (10) (line 416); φ should be defined in ⟨φ(z),φ(z')⟩_{H(K)}.

- Typo/inequality error at lines 427–428: the term should be | ||z|| − ||z'|| |^2 (and the inequality appears to be an equality).

- Several references ([3,6,8,13,14,18,25–27,30,31]) lack page numbers.
