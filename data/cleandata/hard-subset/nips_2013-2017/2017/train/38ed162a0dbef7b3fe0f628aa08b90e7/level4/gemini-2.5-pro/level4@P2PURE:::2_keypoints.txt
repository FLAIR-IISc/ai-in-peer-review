- Studies stability of Convolutional Kernel Networks (CKNs) under C^1 diffeomorphisms and proves stability for norm-preserving, non-expansive kernels (A1–A2) with suitable patch sizes (A3); sketches extension to locally compact groups (Sec. 4).
- Paper is well-organized, clearly written, and technically sound; the main stability theorem is a valuable contribution for ML.
- Motivation is weak: the connection between Lipschitz stability and adversarial robustness is only one sentence and should be expanded.
- Notation overload: κ is reused in (A3) while also denoting the kernel function K in Eq. (10), which is confusing.
- The second constraint in the displayed equation between lines 384–385 is redundant due to kernel symmetry.
- Missing definition of the feature map φ (should be introduced in Eq. (10) as K(z,z') = ⟨φ(z),φ(z')⟩_{H(K)}).
- Typo in the inequality at lines 427–428: it should read |‖z‖ − ‖z'‖|^2 (and the inequality appears to be an equality).
- Several references lack page numbers or full publication details: [3, 6, 8, 13, 14, 18, 25–27, 30, 31].
- Specific citation updates requested with full bibliographic details for [9], [17], [19], and [32].
