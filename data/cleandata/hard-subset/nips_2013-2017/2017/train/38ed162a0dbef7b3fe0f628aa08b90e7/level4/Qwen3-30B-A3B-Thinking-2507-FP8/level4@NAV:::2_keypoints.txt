- Main contribution: analyzes stability of Convolutional Kernel Network (CKN) representations to C^1 diffeomorphisms (e.g., translations) and proves stability under certain conditions.  
- Conditions for stability: requires appropriately chosen patch sizes (A3) and kernels that are norm-preserving and non-expansive (A1–A2).  
- Extension: outlines how results extend to locally compact groups (Section 4).  
- Strength: paper is well-organized, clearly written, and technically rigorous, bridging deep networks and kernel methods.  
- Impact: stability results potentially significant for the ML community.  
- Motivation weakness: manuscript provides insufficient motivation for stability analysis (only a single sentence linking to adversarial robustness).  
- Notation issue: \kappa is overloaded in (A3) and also used as the kernel-defining function in Eq. (10).  
- Redundant constraint: the second constraint between lines 384–385 is redundant due to kernel symmetry and duplicates the first.  
- Missing definition: \phi is not defined at line 416; it should be introduced in Eq. (10) via the RKHS inner product.  
- Typo/error: lines 427–428 use |\|z\| - \|z\||^2 but should be |\|z\| - \|z'\||^2.  
- References formatting: page numbers missing for [3,6,8,13,14,18,25–27,30,31].  
- Reference corrections: updated/corrected citations provided for [9], [17], [19], and [32].
