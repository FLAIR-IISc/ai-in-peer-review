- Paper analyzes stability of Convolutional Kernel Network (CKN) representations to C^1 diffeomorphisms (e.g., translations), proving stability for norm-preserving and non-expansive kernels (A1–A2) with appropriate patch sizes (A3).  
- Sketches extension of the stability results from (R^d, +) to locally compact groups (Section 4).  
- Paper is well-organized, clearly written, technically sound, and combines deep networks and kernel ideas; the stability result is potentially interesting to the community.  
- Needs stronger motivation for the stability analysis beyond a brief note linking Lipschitz stability to adversarial robustness.  
- Notation in (A3) is unclear: the symbol κ is overloaded and also used for the kernel-defining function in Eq. (10).  
- Displayed equation between lines 384–385 contains a redundant constraint (the second part duplicates the first due to kernel symmetry).  
- Definition of φ is missing at line 416 and should be introduced in Eq. (10) as ⟨φ(z),φ(z')⟩_{H(K)}.  
- Line 427–428: the presented inequality seems to hold with equality; the expression should be |‖z‖ − ‖z'‖|^2 instead of |‖z‖ − ‖z‖|^2.  
- Several references ([3,6,8,13,14,18,25–27,30,31]) lack page information; corrected/full citations provided for [9], [17], [19], and [32].
