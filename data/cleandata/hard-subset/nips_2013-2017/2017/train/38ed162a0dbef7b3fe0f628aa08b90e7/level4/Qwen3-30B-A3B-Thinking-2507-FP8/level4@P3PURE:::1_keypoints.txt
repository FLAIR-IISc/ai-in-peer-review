- Paper shows CKNs can achieve near invariance to group transformations and stability to diffeomorphisms with proper patch extraction, kernel mapping, and pooling designs.  
- Authors prove these invariance and stability properties can extend to CNNs under certain assumptions.  
- Manuscript is well written and technically sound.  
- Major limitation: no empirical validation of the theoretical claims.  
- Authors should add experiments demonstrating claimed invariance and stability for CKNs with proposed operator designs.  
- Unclear whether random projections or finite-term Fourier-series kernel approximations are viable alternatives for kernel mapping.  
- If such approximations are used, their effect on stability should be analyzed and reported.
