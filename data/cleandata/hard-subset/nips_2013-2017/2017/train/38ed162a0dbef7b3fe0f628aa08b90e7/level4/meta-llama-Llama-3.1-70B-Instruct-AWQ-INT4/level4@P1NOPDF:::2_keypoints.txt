- Paper studies Convolutional Kernel Networks (CKNs) and proves stability of their representations to C^1 diffeomorphisms (e.g., translations) under norm-preserving, non‑expansive kernels and appropriate patch sizes; Section 4 outlines extension to locally compact groups.
- Well-organized, clearly written, and technically sound; effectively connects deep networks and kernels; stability result is of interest to the community.
- Motivation for the stability analysis is weak—current justification is a single sentence linking Lipschitz stability to adversarial robustness and should be expanded.
- Notation clash: κ used in (A3) conflicts with κ as the kernel-defining function in Eq. (10); use distinct symbols to avoid confusion.
- Redundant constraint in the displayed equation between lines 384–385 (“and ∀v …”) should be removed because kernel symmetry makes it identical to the first constraint.
- Definition of φ is missing and must be introduced in Eq. (10) (〈φ(z),φ(z′)〉_{H(K)}).
- Typo/error in lines 427–428: expression | ||z|| - ||z|| |^2 should be | ||z|| - ||z′|| |^2 (inequality appears to hold with equality).
- Many references lack page information; several citations need full/updated entries (examples and corrections are listed by the reviewer).
