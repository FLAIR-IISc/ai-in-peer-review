- Studies stability of convolutional kernel network (CKN) representations to C^1 diffeomorphisms, formalized in Eq. (4).
- Establishes stability under norm-preserving and non-expansive kernels (Assumptions A1–A2) for suitable patch sizes (A3); outlines extension to locally compact groups (Sec. 4).
- Paper is well-organized, clearly written, and technically sound; bridges deep networks and kernel methods and has potential interest.
- Motivation is weak: the single brief connection to adversarial robustness is insufficient to justify the main contribution.
