- Paper analyzes stability of CKN representations to C^1 diffeomorphisms (e.g., translation) and proves stability under assumptions A1–A3; outlines extension to locally compact groups (Sec. 4).  
- Manuscript is well-organized, clearly written, technically sound, and successfully combines deep networks and kernel methods; the stability result is a valuable contribution.  
- Improve motivation: expand beyond a single sentence linking Lipschitz stability to adversarial robustness to better frame the importance of the analysis.  
- Notation conflict: using κ in (A3) is confusing because κ also denotes the kernel function in Eq. (10); use a different symbol.  
- Redundant constraint: the second constraint in the displayed equation between lines 384–385 appears redundant due to kernel symmetry.  
- Missing definition: the feature map φ is not defined and should be introduced (e.g., K(z,z') = ⟨φ(z),φ(z')⟩_{H(K)}) in Eq. (10).  
- Typo in inequality (lines 427–428): `| ||z|| - ||z|| |^2` should be `| ||z|| - ||z'|| |^2`; the inequality may actually be an equality.  
- Reference issues: page information missing for [3,6,8,13,14,18,25–27,30,31].
