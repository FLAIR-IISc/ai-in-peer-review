- Paper analyzes stability of convolutional kernel networks (CKNs) to C^1 diffeomorphisms and proves stability under assumptions A1–A3, with an extension sketched to locally compact groups.

- Well organized, clearly written, technically sound; bridges deep learning and kernel methods and offers stability guarantees of interest.

- Motivation: strengthen motivation for studying stability—expand practical/theoretical implications (invariance vs. robustness, sample complexity, generalization).

- Notation: κ in (A3) is overloaded (also used for kernel function in Eq. (10)); disambiguate.

- Redundant constraint: the second condition in the displayed equation between lines 384–385 is unnecessary by kernel symmetry.

- Missing definition: φ is not introduced when defining K via an inner product in Eq. (10); explicitly define φ and H(K).

- Typo/equality: lines 427–428 label a step “=” that seems to be an inequality; fix and correct | ||z|| − ||z|| |^2 to | ||z|| − ||z′|| |^2.

- References: add page ranges for [3,6,8,13,14,18,25–27,30,31] and update entries for [9], [17], [19], and [32] as specified.

- Overall: contribution is clear and solid; addressing the motivation and the listed minor corrections will significantly improve the paper.
