- Paper analyzes Convolutional Kernel Networks (CKNs) and proves stability of their representations to C^1 diffeomorphisms (e.g., translations) under norm-preserving, non‑expansive kernels and appropriate patch sizes, with an extension to locally compact groups.

- Manuscript is well-structured, clear, and technically rigorous; the theoretical stability result is a valuable contribution to ML.

- Motivation is weak: the connection between Lipschitz stability and adversarial robustness is stated briefly and should be expanded to better frame the contribution.

- Notation clash: the symbol κ in assumption (A3) conflicts with κ used for the kernel function in Eq. (10) and may confuse readers.

- Redundant constraint: the second part of the constraint between lines 384–385 appears redundant due to kernel symmetry.

- Missing definition: the feature map φ used in line 416 is not formally introduced and should be defined alongside Eq. (10) (K(z,z') = ⟨φ(z),φ(z')⟩_{H(K)}).

- Line 427–428 issue: the displayed inequality likely holds with equality, and there is a typo | ||z|| - ||z|| |^2 which should be | ||z|| - ||z'|| |^2.

- Bibliography errors: references [3,6,8,13,14,18,25–27,30,31] lack page numbers and several entries need full citation updates.

- Specific reference corrections provided: full citations are required for [9] (Daniely et al., NIPS 2016), [17] (Muandet et al., Foundations and Trends in ML), [19] (Raj et al., AISTATS 2017), and [32] (Zhang et al., ICML 2017).
