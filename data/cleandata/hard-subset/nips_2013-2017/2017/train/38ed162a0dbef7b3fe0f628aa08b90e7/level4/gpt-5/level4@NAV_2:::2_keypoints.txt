- Paper studies convolutional kernel networks (CKNs) and analyzes stability of the induced multilayer representation to C^1 diffeomorphisms (including translations) as per Eq. (4).
- Main result: the representation is stable when kernels satisfy norm preservation and non-expansiveness (A1–A2) and patch sizes are chosen appropriately (A3).
- Section 4 sketches an extension of the analysis from (R^d, +) to general locally compact groups.
- Manuscript is well structured, clearly presented, and technically sound; stability guarantees are potentially valuable to the ML community.
- Needs stronger motivation for the stability analysis and a clearer connection to robustness/adversarial examples beyond a brief remark.
- Notation issue: symbol κ in (A3) is overloaded with the kernel-defining function κ in Eq. (10); disambiguation needed.
- Redundant constraint in the displayed equation between lines 384–385: the second “∀ v …” constraint is equivalent to the first by kernel symmetry.
- Missing definition of φ at line 416; φ should be introduced in Eq. (10) via ⟨φ(z), φ(z′)⟩_{H(K)}.
- Typo in lines 427–428: the inequality appears to hold with equality, and | ||z|| − ||z|| |^2 should read | ||z|| − ||z′|| |^2.
- Several references ([3,6,8,13,14,18,25–27,30,31]) are missing page ranges.
