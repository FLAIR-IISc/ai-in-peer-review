- Analyzes deep CKNs' near invariance to transformation groups and stability to diffeomorphisms when patch extraction, kernel mapping, and pooling are properly designed.
- Shows that, under certain assumptions, these invariance and stability properties extend to CNNs.
- Paper is well-written and technically robust.
- Major limitation: lacks empirical experiments to validate the theoretical findings; experiments confirming CKN invariance/stability are recommended.
- Asks whether random projections or finite Fourier-series explicit kernel mappings could approximate the kernel and how these approximations impact stability.
