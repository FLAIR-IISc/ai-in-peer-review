- Central contribution: proves CKN representations are stable to C^1 diffeomorphisms (e.g., translations) under kernel assumptions A1–A2 and patch-size A3.  
- Sketches extension of the stability framework from (R^d, +) to more general locally compact groups (Section 4).  
- Paper is well-written, clearly organized, technically sound, and connects deep networks with kernel methods; main stability result is significant.  
- Suggestion: expand motivation for studying stability—explain why C^1-diffeomorphism stability matters and how it relates to generalization, invariance, and adversarial robustness.

- Redundant constraint: the second constraint between lines 384–385 is superfluous given kernel symmetry.
