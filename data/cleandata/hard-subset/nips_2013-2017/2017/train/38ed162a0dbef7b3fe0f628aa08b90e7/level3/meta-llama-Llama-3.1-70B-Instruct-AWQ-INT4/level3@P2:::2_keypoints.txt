- Paper analyzes stability and invariance properties of deep convolutional kernel networks (CKNs), combining deep learning and kernel methods.  
- Main technical contribution: proof of stability of CKNs to C^1 diffeomorphisms.  
- Additional contribution: extension of the stability analysis to locally compact groups.  
- Claims relevance to adversarial robustness, suggesting implications for CKN robustness.  
- Paper is well-organized and clearly written for readers familiar with ML and kernel methods.  
- Comprehensive related-work review, including scattering transform and other kernel-based methods.  
- Technical soundness: detailed proofs provided in the appendix.  
- Reviewer notes motivation for the stability analysis could be stronger.  
- Link between stability results and adversarial robustness is only briefly discussed.  
- Minor issues: overloaded symbol κ and missing definition of φ.  
- References: some entries missing page information or containing outdated publication details.  
- Recommendation: accept with minor revisions to fix notation/formatting, strengthen motivation, and update references.
