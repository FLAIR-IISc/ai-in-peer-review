- Proves CKN representations are stable under norm-preserving, non-expansive kernels with suitable patch sizes.  
- Shows how stability properties transfer to predictive models via RKHS norms.  
- Builds on Mairal’s CKN framework and provides deeper theoretical insights into the geometry of convolutional representations.
