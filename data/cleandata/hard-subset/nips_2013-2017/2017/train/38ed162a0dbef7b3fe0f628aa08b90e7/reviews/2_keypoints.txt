- Paper studies stability of convolutional kernel networks (CKNs) to C^1 diffeomorphisms (e.g., translations) and proves stability under norm-preserving, non-expansive kernels (A1–A2) with appropriate patch sizes (A3).
- Extension of the stability analysis from (R^d,+) to locally compact groups is sketched in Section 4.
- Manuscript is well organized, clearly written, technically sound, and combines deep networks and kernel methods; the stability result is of interest to the ML community.
- Reviewer requests stronger motivation for the stability analysis beyond a single sentence linking Lipschitz stability to adversarial robustness.
- Notation issue: \kappa is overloaded in (A3) and also used to denote the kernel function in Eq. (10), which may confuse readers.
- Math simplification: the second constraint in the displayed equation between lines 384–385 is redundant due to kernel symmetry.
- Missing definition: \phi is not defined at line 416 and should be introduced via Eq. (10) as the feature map for K.
- Typo/correction: the inequality around lines 427–428 appears to hold with equality, and ||z|| - ||z|| should be ||z|| - ||z'||.
- Bibliography issues: page information is missing for references [3,6,8,13,14,18,25–27,30,31]; several references ([9],[17],[19],[32]) need updated publication/acceptance details.
