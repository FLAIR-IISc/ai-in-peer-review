- The paper studies deep signal representations that are near-invariant to transformation groups and stable to diffeomorphisms.  
- It generalizes the multilayer kernel from convolutional kernel networks and analyzes the geometry of the corresponding RKHS.  
- The authors prove the representation is stable and show models in this functional space (including many CNNs) can inherit that stability.  
- The manuscript is well-written with a clear overview of ideas and contributions.  
- The work is related to prior research, notably the scattering transform and convolutional neural networks.  
- Technical content is sound and proofs are provided in the appendix for clarity.
