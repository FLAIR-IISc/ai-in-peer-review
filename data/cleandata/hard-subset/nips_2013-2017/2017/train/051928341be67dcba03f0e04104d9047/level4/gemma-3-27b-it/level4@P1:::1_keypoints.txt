- Related work: Provides a comprehensive overview and positions the work as addressing limitations in capturing multi-modal interactions.

- Method summary: Defines unary/pairwise/ternary potentials, uses mean-field inference for marginals, and employs tensor sketching for efficient high-dimensional decision computation.

- Experiments summary: Demonstrates SOTA performance on VQA with attention visualizations showing focused regions and question words, and comparisons that highlight benefits of higher-order correlations.

- Argument for acceptance: Addresses an important multi-modal learning problem with a novel approach, strong results, informative visualizations, and a detailed presentation.

- Argument against acceptance: Paper needs clearer writing, the contribution may be incremental, and the probabilistic basis is underdeveloped.
