- Proposes a bottom-up visual saliency model with dynamical systems to capture scanpath dynamics.  
- Positive: enjoyable read with intriguing ideas like "affordances" (curiosity, brightness invariance) and the Least Action Principle.  
- Affordances are somewhat ad-hoc and lack sufficient motivation.  
- The Least Action Principle derivation should be related to existing eye-movement research (e.g., Wolpert lab).  
- Lack of biological motivation may be a minor issue given strong deep-learning models of static salience.  
- Concern: model shows mixed performance compared to high-performing deep-learning salience models.  
- Suggestion: include main-paper figures of generated scanpaths, especially their temporal dynamics, not just overall statistics.
