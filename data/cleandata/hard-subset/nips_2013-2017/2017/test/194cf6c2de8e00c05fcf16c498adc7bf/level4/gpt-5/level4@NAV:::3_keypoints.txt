- Presents a novel approach (EYMOL) to modeling eye movements.
- Authors provide an accurate review of the current state of the art (reviewer not a specialist).
- Contrasts with existing saliency-map-based methods that select fixations via optimization.
- EYMOL is formulated directly on gaze trajectories using a Least Action Principle.
- Some mathematical details, especially in the appendix, were beyond the reviewer's physics background to assess.
- Reviewer understood the central elements and how the method departs from prior models.
- Model evaluated on real eye-movement datasets and compared to multiple alternative models.
- Results do not beat competitors on every metric but are generally favorable and support validity.
- Likely of interest to the vision science community (e.g., VSS attendees).
- Reviewer less convinced of strong contribution for NIPS but acknowledges novelty and potential to spur empirical research.
