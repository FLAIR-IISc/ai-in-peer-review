- Proposes a novel two-stage coarse-to-fine architecture for person image generation combining source appearance with target pose keypoint heat maps.
- Task is novel and interesting with promising qualitative results despite some artifacts.
- Paper is well-written, easy to follow, and includes extensive evaluation of design choices.
- Quantitative evaluation is somewhat weak due to lack of established metrics for the task.
- Reviewer recommends discussing recent iterative refinement works (e.g., iterative error feedback, stacked hourglass) in related work.
- Authors should clarify how using residual blocks specifically improved generation performance.
- Missing ablation: test Stage 2 trained adversarially with the same inputs as Stage 1 to isolate cause of blurring.
- User study is preliminary and unconvincing; a more substantive human evaluation is strongly encouraged.
- Overall: strong paper addressing a novel, challenging problem with an interesting approach; recommendation for acceptance.
