- Proposes a GAN-based system for novel-view person image synthesis conditioned on target skeleton keypoints.  
- Architecture mainly reuses existing components (DCGANs, U-Nets); contribution is application-focused rather than methodological.  
- Pros: core idea is interesting, paper is clearly written, and experiments show some degree of realism.  
- Major con: no quantitative evaluation—missing metrics or user studies to assess image quality.  
- Major con: qualitative results are unconvincing—images are not photorealistic, figures too small, and visual errors persist across main and supplementary results.  
- Overall assessment: work appears premature for publication in its current state.  
- After rebuttal: reviewers’ core concerns remain unresolved; rebuttal did not satisfactorily address quantitative or qualitative issues, so it’s not ready for NIPS.
