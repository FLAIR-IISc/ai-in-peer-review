- Introduces a novel two-stage coarse-to-fine architecture for generating person images by combining appearance and pose heatmaps.  
- Task is original and contributes meaningfully to the field.  
- Proposed two-stage architecture is unique and shows promise.  
- Paper is well-written and easy to follow.  
- Results are encouraging despite noticeable artifacts.  
- Design decision evaluations are thorough and insightful.  
- Quantitative evaluation is limited due to lack of suitable metrics.  
- Suggest adding related work on iterative refinement (e.g., Carreira et al. 2016; Newell et al. 2016).  
- Ask for elaboration on how residual blocks improve generation performance.  
- Propose testing Stage 2 with Stage 1 inputs trained adversarially and investigating whether Stage 1+GAN blurring is caused by the intermediate fully connected layer.  
- Overall recommendation: strongly recommend accepting the paper.  
- Rebuttal note: user study is too preliminary and should be strengthened, but this does not change the positive recommendation.
