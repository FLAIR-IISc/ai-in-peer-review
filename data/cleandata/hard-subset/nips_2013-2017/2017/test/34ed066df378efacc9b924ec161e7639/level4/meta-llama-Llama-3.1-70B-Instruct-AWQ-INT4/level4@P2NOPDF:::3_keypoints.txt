- Proposal: GAN-based system for novel-view person image synthesis guided by target skeleton keypoints, mainly applying established techniques (DCGANs, U-Nets) to a specific CV application.

- Positive: Idea is intriguing, writing is clear, and experiments show some ability to synthesize realistic-looking images.

- Major flaw: Lack of quantitative evaluation (e.g., no user studies or metrics to assess how often generated images deceive humans).

- Visual quality issue: Empirical results are not visually appealing; figures are too small and show noticeable visual errors compared to targets, including in supplementary material.

- Rebuttal ineffective: Author response did not adequately resolve quantitative or qualitative concerns raised by multiple reviewers.

- Conclusion: Work is promising but premature and not ready for presentation at a venue like NIPS.
