- Proposes a GAN-based system to synthesize novel-view person images guided by a target skeleton (keypoints).
- Largely leverages existing components (DCGANs, U-Nets); contribution is mainly application-focused.
- Pros: idea is interesting, writing is clear, and experiments show some degree of realism.
- Major concern: lack of quantitative evaluation (e.g., no user studies or metrics to assess realism/deceptiveness).
- Visual-quality issues: generated images are small, contain noticeable artifacts, and differ visibly from target images.
- Quality problems persist across main and supplementary figures, so results do not convincingly approach real-image quality.
- Rebuttal did not adequately address quantitative or qualitative concerns; reviewer remains unconvinced.
- Overall judgment: work is promising but not yet ready for presentation at a top venue like NIPS.
