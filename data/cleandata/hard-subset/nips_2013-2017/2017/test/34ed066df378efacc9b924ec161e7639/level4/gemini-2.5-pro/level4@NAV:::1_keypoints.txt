- Framework for synthesizing images of an individual conditioned on source appearance and target pose heatmaps.
- Novel coarse-to-fine, two-stage architecture with iterative refinement.
- Manuscript is well-structured and clearly presented.
- Generated results are promising despite visible artifacts.
- Thorough ablation study supporting architectural choices.
- Quantitative evaluation is limited, partly due to lack of established metrics for the task.
- Related work should include iterative refinement cascaded networks (Carreira et al. 2016; Newell et al. 2016).
- Authors should clarify how residual blocks improve performance (e.g., visual quality, training stability).
- Recommend an additional experiment: train Stage 2 directly on Stage 1 inputs adversarially and investigate whether Stage 1+GAN blurring stems from the intermediate fully-connected layer.
- Reviewer strongly recommends acceptance based on task novelty and method contribution.
- Rebuttal: user study is preliminary and unconvincing; a more rigorous human evaluation is recommended for the final version.
