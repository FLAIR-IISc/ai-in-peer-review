- Proposes a GAN-based system for novel-view person image generation guided by target skeleton keypoints; mainly an application of DCGANs and U-nets.  
- Contribution is primarily the specific application rather than new techniques.  
- Strength: interesting idea with clear, easy-to-follow writing.  
- Strength: empirical experiments show some capability to synthesize realistic-looking images.  
- Major weakness: lacks quantitative evaluation (e.g., no user studies to measure human deception).  
- Major weakness: qualitative results are unappealing—figures are too small and generated images show noticeable visual errors compared to targets.  
- Issues with visual quality persist across main and supplementary results.  
- Rebuttal did not adequately address the quantitative and qualitative concerns raised by multiple reviewers.  
- Conclusion: promising but premature; not ready for presentation at a venue like NIPS.
