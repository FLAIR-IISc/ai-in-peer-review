- Proposes a GAN-based system for novel-view person image generation guided by keypoint poses, mainly applying established techniques (DCGANs, U-Nets).  
- Pros: idea is interesting, writing is clear, and experiments show the model can synthesize images that appear reasonably realistic to some extent.  
- Major con: lacks quantitative evaluation (e.g., no user studies to assess human deception/fidelity).  
- Major con: qualitative results are not compelling—figures too small and generated images show noticeable visual errors compared to targets.  
- Issues with visual quality persist across supplementary materials, making strong claims premature.  
- Rebuttal failed to satisfactorily address quantitative and qualitative concerns; reviewer remains unconvinced.  
- Recommendation: paper may not be ready for presentation at a venue like NIPS.  
- Minor: reviewer suggested several wording and phrasing edits to improve clarity and tone.
