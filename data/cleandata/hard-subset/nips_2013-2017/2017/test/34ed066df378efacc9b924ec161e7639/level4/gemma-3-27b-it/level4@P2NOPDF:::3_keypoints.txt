- Architecture uses established techniques (DCGANs, U-nets); main contribution is an application-specific combination rather than a novel method.
- Core idea is compelling and the paper is clearly and well-written.
- Empirical results suggest potential to synthesize plausible images, but only to a limited degree.
- Major weakness: complete lack of quantitative evaluation (e.g., no user study or metrics to assess realism).
- Visual quality is underwhelming; presented images are too small for inspection and show noticeable artifacts relative to target skeletons.
- Claims of “good quality” or near-realistic results are unsupported by the evidence.
- Overall: promising but requires significant refinement and stronger evaluation before suitability for a top conference.
- Post-rebuttal: reviewer remains unconvinced—author responses did not adequately address quantitative and qualitative deficiencies.
