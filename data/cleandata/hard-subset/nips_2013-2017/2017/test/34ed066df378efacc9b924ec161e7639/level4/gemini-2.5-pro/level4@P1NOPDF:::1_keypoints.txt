- Proposal: two-stage coarse-to-fine architecture for person image generation combining source appearance with target pose heatmaps.  
- Strength: addresses a novel and interesting task.  
- Strength: two-stage coarse-to-fine design is novel and effective.  
- Strength: paper is well-written and easy to follow.  
- Strength: qualitative results are promising despite some artifacts.  
- Strength: extensive evaluation via ablation studies.  
- Weakness: quantitative evaluation is somewhat weak due to lack of established metrics.  
- Comment: related work should discuss cascaded/iterative networks (e.g., iterative error feedback, stacked hourglass).  
- Comment: clarify how residual blocks improve generation performance.  
- Comment: suggest ablation training Stage 2 adversarially on Stage 1 inputs to test source of blurring.  
- Overall: confident recommendation for acceptance.  
- Post-rebuttal: user study is preliminary and unconvincing; authors should include a more substantive human evaluation for the camera-ready version, but acceptance recommendation stands.
