- Proposes a GAN-based system for novel-view person synthesis guided by target skeleton keypoints.  
- Core idea is novel, well-motivated, and clearly presented.  
- Empirical results show the system can produce visually plausible images under certain conditions.  
- Lacks quantitative evaluation (e.g., user studies or metrics) to support realism claims.  
- Visual results are low-resolution and contain visible artifacts, undermining perceived quality.  
- Supplementary figures exhibit similar shortcomings, so results are not convincingly realistic.  
- Authors’ rebuttal did not adequately address the quantitative and qualitative concerns.  
- Reviewer concludes the work is not yet strong enough for publication at a venue like NeurIPS.
