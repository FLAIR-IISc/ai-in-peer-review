- Proposes a novel task and architecture for pose-guided person image generation.
- Introduces a unique two-stage coarse-to-fine generation architecture.
- Paper is well-written and clearly explained.
- Results are promising but exhibit visual artifacts.
- Quantitative evaluation is weak due to lack of suitable metrics.
- Reviewer does not consider weak metrics a major flaw.
- Suggests adding related work on iterative refinement and cascaded networks.
- Requests experiments evaluating Stage 2 using Stage 1 inputs and adversarial training.
- Recommends acceptance based on novelty, approach, and potential.
- Advises adding a substantive human evaluation in the revision.
