- Derives improved lower bounds on number of queries needed for exact and partial reconstruction (better than naive nh(p)).
- Presents a specific lower-bound formula involving nh(p) and a max over ρ (see review for expression).
- Proposes an AND-queries algorithm applicable to arbitrary k-cluster settings.

- Evaluates algorithm performance via a real-data simulation.
