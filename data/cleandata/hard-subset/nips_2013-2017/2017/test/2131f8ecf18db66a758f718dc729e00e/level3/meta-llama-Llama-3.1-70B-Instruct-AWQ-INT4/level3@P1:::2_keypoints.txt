- Derives lower bounds on the number of queries required to recover correct labels.  
- Introduces a pairwise AND querying scheme that outperforms alternatives in many settings.  
- Validates the algorithm with a real crowdsourcing experiment.  
- Builds on prior work in semisupervised clustering, crowdsourcing, and information theory.

- Strength: practical effectiveness demonstrated by experiments.  
- Weakness: presentation and clarity need improvement.

- Weakness: insufficient comparisons to state-of-the-art methods in experiments.  
- Weakness: novelty of contributions not always clearly highlighted.
