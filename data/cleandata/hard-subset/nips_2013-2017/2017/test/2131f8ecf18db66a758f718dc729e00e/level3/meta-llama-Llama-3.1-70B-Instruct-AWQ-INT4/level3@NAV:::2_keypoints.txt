- Proposes a novel semisupervised clustering method using AND-queries and locally encodable source coding.  
- Provides comprehensive theoretical analysis including lower bounds and an efficient algorithm for approximate recovery.  
- Technical contributions are sound and thoroughly analyzed.  
- Experimental results on real crowdsourced data are strong and demonstrate effectiveness.  
- Writing is sometimes dense and hard to follow; needs more paragraphing and headings for clarity.  
- Several proofs and technical details are placed in the appendix, which hinders following main arguments.  
- Comparison to prior work is limited and should be expanded.  
- Must address discrepancy between this paper’s results and those of Ahn–Lee–Suh and clarify differences from other querying schemes.  
- Algorithm assumes known cluster sizes or prior distribution; practical implications and estimation guidance are needed.  
- Overall strong and significant contribution; recommendation: accept with minor revisions to improve clarity, comparisons, and practical guidance.
