- Paper studies distributed consensus using an accelerated min-sum (min-sum splitting) algorithm.  
- Manuscript is exceptionally well-written and places the work within the current state-of-the-art.  
- Results appear novel and represent a significant improvement (reviewer not a specialist but impressed).  
- Key contribution: establishes a new connection between min-sum algorithms and lifted Markov chain methods.  
- Major clarification needed: description on page 4 of how terms are "split" (φ_vw into Γ_vw terms and φ_v into δ terms) is ambiguous because δ and Γ_vw are defined as real numbers, not integer counts.
