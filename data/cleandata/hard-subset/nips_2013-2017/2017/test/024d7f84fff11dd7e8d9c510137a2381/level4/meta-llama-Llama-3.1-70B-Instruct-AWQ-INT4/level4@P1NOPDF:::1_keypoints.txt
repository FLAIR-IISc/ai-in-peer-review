- Applies an accelerated variant of the min-sum algorithm (min-sum splitting) to the distributed consensus problem.  
- Paper is very well written and clearly places its contribution in the context of prior work.  
- Results appear novel and represent a qualitative advance (reviewer notes not being an expert).  
- Presents a novel connection between min-sum algorithms and lifted Markov chain techniques.  
- Presentation unclear on page 4: statement about "splitting each term φ_vw into Γ_vw terms and φ_v into δ terms" is confusing because Γ_vw and δ were introduced as real numbers.
