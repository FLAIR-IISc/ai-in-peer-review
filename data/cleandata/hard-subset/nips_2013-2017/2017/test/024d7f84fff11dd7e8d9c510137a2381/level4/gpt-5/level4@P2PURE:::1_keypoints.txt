- Applies an accelerated variant of min-sum (min-sum splitting) to the distributed consensus problem.  
- Manuscript is very well written and clearly positions its contributions within prior work.  
- Results appear novel and represent a qualitative advance (reviewer notes limited expertise but sees novelty).

- Exposition unclear: on page 4 the description “splitting each term phi_vw into Gamma_vw terms, and each term phi_v into delta terms” is confusing because Gamma_vw and delta were introduced as real numbers, not counts.
