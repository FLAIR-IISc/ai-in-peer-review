- Applies an accelerated min-sum variant (min-sum splitting) to the distributed consensus problem.  
- Paper is very well written and situates its contribution clearly within the state of the art.  
- Reviewer is not an expert but finds the results novel and a qualitative advance.  
- Presents a novel connection between min-sum algorithms and lifted Markov chain techniques.
