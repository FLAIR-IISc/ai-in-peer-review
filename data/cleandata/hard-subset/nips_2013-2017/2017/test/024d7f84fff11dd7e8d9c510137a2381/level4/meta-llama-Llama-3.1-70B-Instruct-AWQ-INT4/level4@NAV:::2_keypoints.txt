2. Missing citation: Olshevsky's "Linear Time Average Consensus…" should be included and its (competitive or better) convergence rate accurately reported.  
3. Missing consensus optimization references (Shi et al. on ADMM; Scaman et al. on optimal algorithms); state-of-the-art algorithms and linear rates should be reported.  
4. Overlooked related work on lifted graphs/Markov chain lifting: Franca and Bento.  
5. Last paragraph on p.5 is a known result and should be attributed to Rabbat, Nowak, and Bucklew; connections to Heavy ball/Nesterov/Polyak are established.  
6. Important consensus optimization refs omitted: "Extra" (Shi et al.) and "Fast distributed gradient methods" (Jakovetic et al.).  
7. Proposition 3 appears trivial and only a supplementary contribution.  
8. The claimed D log(D/ε) rate offers little improvement over D log(1/ε) from Olshevsky; in worst-case (D ~ n) it is worse.  
9. The auxiliary message-passing scheme may essentially be Nesterov acceleration applied to min-sum; analysis reduces to a linear system, so the novelty is unclear.  
10. Any minor gain likely comes from spectral-gap handling, but worst-case complexity remains O(n)=O(D) for general graphs.
