- Applies an accelerated min-sum splitting variant to the distributed consensus problem.  
- Paper is very well written and clearly situates its contribution within the state of the art.  
- Results appear novel and constitute a qualitative advance (reviewer caveats non-expertise).  
- Novel and noteworthy connection drawn between min-sum algorithms and lifted Markov chain techniques.  
- Presentation issue: unclear statement on page 4 about "splitting each term φ_vw into Γ_vw terms, and each φ_v into δ terms"—Γ_vw and δ were introduced as real numbers, not integers, so the meaning needs clarification.
