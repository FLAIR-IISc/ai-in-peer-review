- Introduces an accelerated min-sum splitting variant and applies it to the distributed consensus problem.  
- Paper is well-written and places the contribution clearly within the state of the art.  
- Results appear novel and form a qualitative advance, notably linking min-sum algorithms to lifted Markov chain techniques.  
- Clarification needed: the explanation on page 4 about splitting terms (φ_vw into Γ_vw terms and φ_v into δ terms) is unclear since Γ_vw and δ are introduced as real numbers.  
- Overall assessment: significant contribution; with minor revisions to clarify the splitting step, it could be a strong conference paper.
