- Paper applies an accelerated min-sum variant (min-sum splitting) to distributed consensus.  
- Manuscript is very well written and clearly situates contributions within prior work.  
- Results appear novel and represent a qualitative advance (reviewer not an expert but sees value).  
- Interesting new connection drawn between min-sum algorithms and lifted Markov chain techniques.  
- Presentation unclear about the phrase “splitting each term φ_vw into Γ_vw terms, and each term φ_v into δ terms” (page 4).  
- Need clarification whether Γ_vw and δ are intended as integer counts, scaling factors, or reweightings, and how “splitting” is defined for non-integer values.  
- Request explicit mathematical definition of the decomposition, conditions on Γ and δ for well-definedness, and how this leads to the stated update equations.
