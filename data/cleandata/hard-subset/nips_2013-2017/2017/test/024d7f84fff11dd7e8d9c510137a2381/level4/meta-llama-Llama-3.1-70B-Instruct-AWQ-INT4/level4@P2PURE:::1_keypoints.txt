- Applies an accelerated variant of the min-sum algorithm (min-sum splitting) to the distributed consensus problem.  
- Paper is very well written and situates its contribution clearly within the state of the art.  
- Results appear novel and represent a qualitative advance (reviewer notes limited domain expertise but sees novelty).  
- Presents a novel connection between min-sum algorithms and lifted Markov chain techniques.  
- A presentation/detail issue: unclear statement on page 4 about splitting each term φ_vw into Γ_vw terms and each φ_v into δ terms.  
- Ambiguity arises because Γ_vw and δ are introduced as real numbers, making their role in "splitting" the terms unclear and needing clarification.
