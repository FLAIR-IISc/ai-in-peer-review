- Paper applies an accelerated min-sum variant (min-sum splitting) to distributed consensus.  
- Exceptionally well written and clearly situates its contributions within prior work.  
- Results appear novel and represent a qualitative improvement (reviewer not an expert but finds novelty plausible).  
- Establishes a novel connection between min-sum algorithms and lifted Markov chain techniques.  
- Critical ambiguity: authors describe splitting terms into Γ_vw and δ parts, but Γ_vw and δ are introduced as real-valued parameters while splitting implies integer counts — this needs clarification.
