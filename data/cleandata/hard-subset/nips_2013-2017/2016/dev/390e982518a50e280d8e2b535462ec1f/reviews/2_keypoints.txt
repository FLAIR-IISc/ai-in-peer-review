- Proposes a neural network model integrating local node vicinity for node, edge, and graph classification.  
- Strong empirical results on node classification but poor performance on graph classification.  
- Baseline comparisons may be unfair because baselines might not have access to the same information as the proposed method.  
- Related neural-network-for-graphs work is mentioned but not empirically compared against—missing direct comparisons.  
- Edge-classification models are defined in the paper but no experimental results are reported.  
- Reviewer finds it hard to judge the novelty and contribution relative to existing literature.  
- Minor issue: Figure 1 is too small.
