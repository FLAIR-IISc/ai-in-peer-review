- Proposes combining multiple graph kernels with a diffusion-convolutional neural net by weighting the j-th hop transition for feature k in the first layer, producing activations per node, hop, feature, and graph so weights can be learned from data.  
- Addresses an important and underexplored problem: learning graph kernels from labeled graphs.  
- Clarity issues: definitions of the power spectrum P* and the notion of a “hop” are unclear and need better explanation for average readers.  
- Experiments are informative and comprehensive.
