- Integrates multiple graph kernels into a diffusion-convolutional neural network (DCNN).
- Proposes weighting the jth-hop transition for feature k in the first layer and generating activations per node, hop, feature, and graph.
- Enables learning of those hop/feature weights directly from data.
- Addresses a significant and underexplored problem: learning kernels from graph labels.
- Paper lacks clarity: definitions of the power spectrum P* and the notion of a “hop” are imprecise.
- Ambiguities may confuse the average reader despite the author's apparent understanding.
- Experiments are comprehensive and highly informative.
