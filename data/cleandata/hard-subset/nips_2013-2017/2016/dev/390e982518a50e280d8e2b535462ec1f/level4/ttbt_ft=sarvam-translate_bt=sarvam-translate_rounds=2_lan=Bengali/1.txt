This paper combines multiple graph kernels with a diffusion-convolutional neural network. Specifically, they propose weighting the jth hop transition for the first-level feature k, and then output an activation for each node, hop, feature, and graph. In this way, the weights can be learned from the data. This paper is very important because the problem of learning kernels from graph labels has not been fully discussed in the literature. The paper could have been clearer as the power spectrum P star and hop are not well-defined. I think I know what's going on, but a general reader might not. The experiments are very informative and comprehensive.
