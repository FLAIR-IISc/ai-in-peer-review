- Integrates multiple graph kernels into a diffusion-convolutional neural network architecture.  
- Introduces a weighting mechanism for the j-th hop transition of feature k, yielding activations per node/hop/feature/graph and enabling data-driven weights.  
- Tackles the important, underexplored problem of learning kernels from graph labels.  
- Clarity concerns: definition/explanation of the power spectrum P* and the notion of "hop" are unclear for non-expert readers.  
- Experimental section is informative and comprehensive.
