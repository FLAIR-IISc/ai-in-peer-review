This paper combines several graph kernels with a diffusion-convolutional neural net. Specifically, they propose weighting the jth hop transition for feature k in the first layer and then output an activation for each node, hop, feature, and graph. In this way, weights can be learned from the data. This paper is very important because the problem of learning kernels from graph labels has not been widely addressed in the literature. The paper could have been clearer, as the power spectrum P star and hops are not well-defined. I think I know what's going on, but the average reader probably doesn't. The experiments are very informative and comprehensive.
