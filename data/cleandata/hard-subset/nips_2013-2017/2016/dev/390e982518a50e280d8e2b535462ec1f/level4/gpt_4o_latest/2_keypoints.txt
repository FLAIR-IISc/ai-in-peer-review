- Introduces a neural network for node, edge, and graph classification using local neighborhood; performs well on node classification but poorly on graph classification.  
- Evaluation is confounded because many baselines lack access to the same information as the proposed method, so gains may reflect extra input rather than model quality.  
- Although related neural graph methods are acknowledged, the paper provides no direct empirical comparisons to those approaches.  
- Edge-classification models are defined but no empirical results for edge tasks are reported.  
- As a non-expert reviewer, it’s hard to judge the significance of the contribution relative to existing work.  
- Minor: Figure 1 is too small.
