- No clear motivation for using tensor networks as the feature map; choice not justified.  
- Tensor-network formulation leads to optimization difficulties; reviewer suggests investigating the kernel trick to reduce computational complexity.  
- Label handling is unclear: label index placement/movement in the tensor appears arbitrary and effectively ties the label to a single pixel; model (4)–(5) seems inappropriate for multiclass classification.  
- Authors use quadratic loss (chosen for best test results) but should report and compare other loss functions; quadratic loss is computationally easier due to differentiability.  
- Experimental details missing: number of samples (N) and number of classes (L) not reported.  
- Images are subsampled from 28x28 to 14x14 to reduce training time, but algorithmic computational complexity is not analyzed.  
- Unclear how training and test errors are estimated.  
- No comparison to state-of-the-art classification methods in terms of error rates and computational complexity.
