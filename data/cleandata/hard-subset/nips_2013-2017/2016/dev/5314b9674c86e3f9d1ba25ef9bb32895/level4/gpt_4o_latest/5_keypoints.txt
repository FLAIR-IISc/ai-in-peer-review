- Lack of motivation for using tensor networks to define the feature map.  
- Tensor networks introduce significant optimization challenges not addressed.  
- Suggestion to explore the kernel trick to reduce computational complexity.  
- Unclear and arbitrary handling of the label index in the tensor-based classification model.  
- Label treatment may effectively attach the class to a single pixel, not the whole image.  
- Model in eqs. (4)–(5) appears unsuitable for multiclass classification.  
- Choice of quadratic loss justified only by empirical results; other loss functions and comparisons are not reported.  
- Missing experimental details such as number of data points (N) and number of classes (L).  
- Downsampling from 28x28 to 14x14 is used to reduce training time, but overall computational complexity is not analyzed.  
- Method for estimating training and test errors is not clearly explained.  
- No benchmarking against state-of-the-art classification methods in terms of error rates or computational cost.
