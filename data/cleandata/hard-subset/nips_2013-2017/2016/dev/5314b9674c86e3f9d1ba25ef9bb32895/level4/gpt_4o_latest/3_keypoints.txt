- Strength: effectively bridges multiple fields by applying MPS/tensor-train methods to image classification.  
- Section 5 unclear: need clarification on how the weight tensor is approximated as an MPS and in what sense (e.g., role of taking singular values in eq. (11)).  
- Regularization connection: eqs. (11) and (14) appear to perform rank control/SVD truncation akin to spectral/nuclear-norm regularization (Signoretto et al. 2014); make this explicit instead of treating regularization as an open problem.  
- Relation to [15] (Cichocki): since eqs. (6)-(7) are linear in the unknown tensor, explain why the MPS linear-system method of [15] cannot be applied directly or what extra complications arise.  
- \tilde{\Phi}n construction: the procedure for obtaining \tilde{\Phi}n from the data is insufficiently detailed and should be explained step-by-step.  
- Equations (7)–(8): the term “leading-order update” is vague—define precisely what is meant and how it connects to the proposed update scheme.  
- Algorithmic clarity: include a concrete step-by-step algorithm showing how to get the solution B from training data to improve reproducibility.  
- Equation (3) / features: clarify whether the features are (random) Fourier features and explicitly connect to the random Fourier features literature if so.  
- Overall: paper is interesting and interdisciplinary but needs the above clarifications to improve presentation and reader comprehension.
