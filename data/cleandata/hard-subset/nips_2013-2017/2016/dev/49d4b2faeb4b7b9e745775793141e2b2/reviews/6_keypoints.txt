- Proposes Matryoshka Networks (MatNets): combines DRAW-like models and Ladder Networks to learn hierarchically deep generative models with jointly-trained inference and generation.
- Demonstrates quantitative performance on MNIST, Omniglot, and CIFAR-10.
- Shows strong results for imputing missing regions of images.
- Presents an interesting, promising framework for learning deep generative models.
- Lacks classification experiments (supervised/semi-supervised/unsupervised) on benchmarks like MNIST and CIFAR-10; adding these would strengthen the paper.
- Paper is well-written with clear claims, explanations, and derivations.
