- Paper proposes Matryoshka network (top-down, bottom-up, merge modules) for improved information flow in deep generative models, resembling LapGANs and ResNets.  
- Mathematical details are generally sufficient, but overall clarity and flow across sections need improvement.  
- Section 2.1–2.2 do not clearly connect sampling/inference/distributions to network components; the role of x is poorly defined until late.  
- Variable x is ambiguously defined and its relation to the network should be clarified.  
- The initialization procedure for latent variables z is not described and its impact is unexplored.  
- Possible typo on line 71: conv(h, w) description omits h and incorrectly references x as input.  
- Qualitative results are impressive.  
- Quantitative results outperform previous methods but the model is substantially more complex.  
- Provide an upfront mapping of inputs/outputs for the whole network and for each component (top-down, bottom-up, merge) before component details.  
- More analysis of qualitative results is needed alongside quantitative explanations.  
- Add additional quantitative measures (e.g., reconstruction error) beyond NLL.  
- Include qualitative comparisons with prior methods.
