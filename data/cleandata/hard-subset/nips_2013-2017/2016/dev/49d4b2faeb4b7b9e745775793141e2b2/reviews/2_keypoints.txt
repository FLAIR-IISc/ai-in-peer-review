- Proposal: introduces MatNet, a hierarchical generative model combining ideas from DRAW-type models, Ladder Networks, and residual nets.
- Quantitative results: reports state-of-the-art negative log-likelihood (NLL) on MNIST, Omniglot, and CIFAR-10.
- Qualitative results: shows good inpainting on CelebA and LSUN, but lacks side-by-side qualitative comparisons with other methods.
- Technical quality: experimental section is clear and reproducible, with comparisons to recent state-of-the-art (e.g., Pixel RNN).
- Missing comparisons: reviewer wanted direct side-by-side qualitative comparisons, not present in main paper or supplementary.
- Novelty: advances include using a Gaussian mixture model (GMM) prior over z_0 instead of a single Gaussian.
- Clarity issues: Figure 1 is low quality/unclear, and differences between the supplement and the main paper are not clearly specified (except extra images).
