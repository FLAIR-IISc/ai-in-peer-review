- Introduces MatNet, a hierarchical generative model combining ideas from DRAW, Ladder Networks, and residual nets.  
- Achieves state-of-the-art negative log-likelihood (NLL) on MNIST, Omniglot, and CIFAR-10.  
- Demonstrates strong qualitative inpainting results on CelebA and LSUN.  
- Experimental section is clear, straightforward, and reproducible.  
- Thorough quantitative comparisons with current state-of-the-art methods, including Pixel RNN.  
- Lacks side-by-side qualitative comparisons with other methods (not provided, even in supplementary).  
- Novelty includes using a Gaussian Mixture Model prior over z0 instead of a single Gaussian.  
- Presentation issues: Figure 1 is low quality and unclear.  
- Unclear separation between content presented in the main paper and the supplementary material.
