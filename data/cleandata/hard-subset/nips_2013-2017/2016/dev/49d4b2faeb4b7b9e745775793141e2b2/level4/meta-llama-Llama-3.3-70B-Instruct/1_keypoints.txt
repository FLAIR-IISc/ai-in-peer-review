- Paper attributes DRAW-like success to incremental generation and deterministic latent–observation relationships.  
- Introduces Matryoshka Networks (MatNets): bottom-up, top-down, and merge modules to regulate information flow.  
- Architecture resembles Probabilistic Ladder Networks (PLNs) but adds deterministic connections between latents and observations.  
- MatNets achieve competitive or state-of-the-art density modeling on MNIST, Omniglot, and CIFAR.  
- Main contribution: hierarchical VAE architecture with deterministic connections enabling incremental observation generation.  
- Novelty is limited: similar ideas in DRAW and PLNs, though deterministic generative connections are a new twist.  
- Experiments do not directly isolate or demonstrate the importance of the deterministic connections.  
- Presentation lacks a high-level probabilistic description before detailed computational specification, harming clarity.  
- Missing citations (e.g., convolutional GRUs) risk implying unwarranted novelty.  
- Related work section omits important prior art such as DRAW and PLNs.  
- It is unclear whether the inference regularization method from Section 2.3 was used in experiments.  
- Equations 11 and 12 contain typos: they should include a log term for p(x|z).
