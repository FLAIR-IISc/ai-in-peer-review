- Introduces MatNet, a novel hierarchical generative model that integrates DRAW-type models, Ladder Networks, and residual networks.  
- Achieves state-of-the-art negative log-likelihood (NLL) on MNIST, Omniglot, and CIFAR-10.  
- Produces impressive qualitative inpainting results on CelebA and LSUN.  
- Experimental protocol is well-executed, clear, and reproducible.  
- Provides comparisons to recent state-of-the-art methods, including Pixel RNN.  
- Missing side-by-side comparisons for qualitative results in both main paper and supplementary material.  
- Novel contribution: uses a Gaussian Mixture Model (GMM) prior over z₀ instead of a conventional single Gaussian.  
- Clarity issues: Figure 1 quality needs improvement and the distinction between main paper and supplementary content is unclear.
