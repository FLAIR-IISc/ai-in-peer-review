- Introduces Matryoshka Networks (MatNets), combining DRAW-like models and Ladder Networks for hierarchically deep generative models with jointly-trained inference and generation.
- Demonstrates quantitative performance on MNIST, Omniglot, and CIFAR10.
- Presents an intriguing framework for learning deep generative models.
- Shows impressive image imputation results for missing regions.
- Recommends adding classification results (supervised/semi-supervised/unsupervised) on benchmarks like MNIST and CIFAR10 to strengthen contributions.
- Well-written paper with clear claims, explanations, and derivations.
