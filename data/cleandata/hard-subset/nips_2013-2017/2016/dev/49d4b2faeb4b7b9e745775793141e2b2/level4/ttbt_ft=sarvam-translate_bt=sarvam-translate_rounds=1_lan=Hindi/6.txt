This paper proposes a new class of architecture called Matryoshka Networks (MatNets), which combines the advantages of models like DRAW and ladder networks to learn hierarchical deep generative models with jointly trained inference/generation. The paper demonstrates the quantitative performance of MatNets on MNIST, Omniglot, and CIFAR10. This paper proposes an interesting framework for learning deep generative models. The authors have shown good results when inputting missing regions of images. However, it would be more reliable if the paper included classification results (supervised/semi-supervised/unsupervised) on benchmarks like MNIST and CIFAR10. The paper is well-written. The claims, explanations, and derivations in the paper are clear and easy to understand.
