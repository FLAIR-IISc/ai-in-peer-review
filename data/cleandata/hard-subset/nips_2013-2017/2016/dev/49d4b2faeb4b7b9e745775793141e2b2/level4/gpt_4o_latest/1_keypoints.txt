- Proposal: Matryoshka Networks (MatNets) — a hierarchical VAE with bottom-up, top-down, and merge modules and deterministic connections between latents and the observation.
- Motivation: Extends DRAW-like incremental generation to hierarchical latent-variable models.
- Relation to prior work: Similar to Probabilistic Ladder Networks (PLNs) but differs by adding deterministic connections in the generative model.
- Empirical results: Achieves competitive or state-of-the-art density modeling on MNIST, Omniglot, and CIFAR.
- Main critique: No experimental analysis isolating or demonstrating the importance of the deterministic connections that are claimed as the central novelty.
- Writing/structure: Presentation could be improved — provide a high-level probabilistic model description before procedural computations (Section 2.1).
- Missing citations: Convolutional GRU usage should cite prior generative-model work (e.g., [24]); related work omitted PLNs despite discussion in the introduction.
- Experimental clarity: Unclear whether the inference regularization from Section 2.3 was applied in the reported experiments.
- Typos: Equations 11 and 12 should include a log in front of p(x|z).
