- Proposes a method to simultaneously segment and transcribe handwritten paragraphs into text.  
- Uses MDLSTMs to learn both input encoding and an attention mechanism for line segmentation.  
- Trains a Bidirectional-LSTM decoder over concatenated encoded sequences with a paragraph-level CTC loss.  
- Builds on prior work by removing the requirement for pre-segmented lines, increasing practical applicability.  
- Suggests adding a baseline using simpler Convolutional or Affine layers as a less complex alternative to MDLSTMs.  
- Recommends exploring an additional MDLSTM layer with CTCs to explicitly detect paragraph boundaries, at the cost of complexity.  
- Reports improved results over the authors’ baseline and strong performance on the Rimes benchmark.  
- Notes that performance on the IAM benchmark did not surpass prior work, indicating room for further optimization.
