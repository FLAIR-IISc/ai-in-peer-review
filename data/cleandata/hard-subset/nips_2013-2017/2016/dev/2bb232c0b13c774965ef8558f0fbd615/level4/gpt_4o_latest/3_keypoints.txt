- Proposes a unified end-to-end paragraph-level handwriting recognition model that integrates line segmentation and recognition using attention.  
- Demonstrates the proposed method outperforms traditional two-step approaches, even when they use ground-truth line segmentation.  
- Achieves competitive performance with state-of-the-art line-level methods on Rimes and IAM without complex pre-processing.  
- Attention mechanism implicitly performs line segmentation, providing a natural and intuitive formulation.  
- Aligns with deep learning trends and is claimed to be the first successful implementation of such an end-to-end system.  
- Paper is well-written and easy to follow.  
- Reviewer requests more explanation for why the attention-based model outperforms ground-truth line-based methods.  
- Reviewer suggests reporting computational time for additional insight.
