- Evaluated on Rimes and IAM: comparable to state-of-the-art on Rimes, substantially worse on IAM.  
- Nice application of region/guided attention over images, building on Xu et al. and related work.  
- Exposition is not self-contained; section 3 needs a clearer explanation of the MD-LSTM model (as in Graves et al. [18]).  
- Unexplained result: method outperforms approaches using ground-truth line labels—need clarification if decoder re-initialization or Graves implementation differences cause this.  
- Request more implementation details of the Graves et al. baseline to explain comparative behavior.  
- Claim of being 20–30× faster than Bluche et al. [6] lacks actual timing numbers for verification.  
- Suggest experiment to pretrain on the other dataset (train+test) then finetune on IAM to test if data scarcity explains poor IAM performance.
