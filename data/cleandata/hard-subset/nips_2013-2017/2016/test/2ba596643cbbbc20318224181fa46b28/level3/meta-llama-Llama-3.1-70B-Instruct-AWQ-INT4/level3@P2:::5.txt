This paper introduces a novel variant of the stochastic K-armed bandit problem, where cheap approximations to each arm are available. The paper provides theoretical results, including regret bounds for MF-UCB, and offers a lower-bound analysis showing the theoretical improvement of the approach. The authors also present simulations that demonstrate the effect and benefits of the MF bandit method.
