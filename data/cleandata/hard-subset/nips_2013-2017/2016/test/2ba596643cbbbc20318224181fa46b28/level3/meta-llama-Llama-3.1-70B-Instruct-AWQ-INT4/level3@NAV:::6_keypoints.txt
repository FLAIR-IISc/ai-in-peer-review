- Contribution: Proposes MF-UCB, an upper-confidence-bound algorithm adapted to leverage multiple fidelities.
- Theory: Proves regret bounds for MF-UCB and derives a lower bound, showing near-optimality under certain conditions.
- Empirics: Demonstrates MF-UCB’s effectiveness on synthetic simulations.
