- Introduces a new multi-armed bandit setting with variable-fidelity pulls where pull cost increases with accuracy and regret is cost × pseudo-regret.  
- Proposes MF-UCB algorithm and provides upper and lower bounds on cumulative pseudo-regret.  
- Main paper is clear and well written.  
- Supplementary materials (proofs) contain many typos and small errors that can confuse readers; fixing them would greatly improve readability.  
- Using "up-to-a-multiplicative-constant" notation is misleading because hidden constants (e.g., ρ) are algorithm parameters; presenting exact inequalities would improve clarity.  
- Practical applicability is unclear; MF-UCB may suffer from inaccurate estimation of fidelity ζ and needs discussion of practical limitations and mitigations.  
- Theoretical contribution is interesting with solid proofs; overall a significant contribution.
