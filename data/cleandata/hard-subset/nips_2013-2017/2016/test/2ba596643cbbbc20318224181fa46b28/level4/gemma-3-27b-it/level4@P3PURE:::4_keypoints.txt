- Proposes a Multi-fidelity MAB framework with an adaptive algorithm, regret analysis, lower bound, and simulations showing improvement over UCB — interesting but not ready for publication.

- Notation is confusing and scattered (e.g., variable s is used before being defined).

- Advertising as a motivating example is misleading and introduces non-stationarity outside the paper’s scope; a different illustrative example is needed.

- Regret analysis rationale is unclear: the formulation always favors the highest mean arm irrespective of cost, hiding the cost impact of low-fidelity sampling.

- A sample/cost-complexity analysis is needed (e.g., motivated by prototype testing and real-world constraints like regulation and facilities).

- Several sections are unwieldy and should be streamlined to improve readability.
