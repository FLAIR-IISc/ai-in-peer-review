- Novel multi-armed bandit framework where each arm can be pulled at varying fidelity, with cost increasing with accuracy and regret defined as cost times pseudo-regret.  
- Introduces MF-UCB algorithm and provides upper and lower bounds on cumulative pseudo-regret.  
- Main manuscript is clear and well-structured.  
- Supplementary materials (proofs) are poorly polished, containing numerous typos and minor errors that could confuse readers.  
- Use of "up-to-a-multiplicative-constant" notation is misleading because hidden constants depend on parameters (e.g., rho); recommend showing exact inequalities.  
- Practical applicability is unclear; MF-UCB may be sensitive to inaccuracies in estimating zeta, so a discussion of practical limitations and mitigations is needed.  
- Theoretical contributions are valuable, with interesting theorems and rigorous proofs; overall a meaningful contribution.
