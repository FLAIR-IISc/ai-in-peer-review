- Introduces a novel multi-fidelity multi-armed bandit setting where pulls have varying fidelity-dependent costs and defines a new cost-weighted pseudo-regret.  
- Proposes the MF-UCB algorithm and provides theoretical upper and lower bounds on cumulative pseudo-regret.  
- Strength: well-motivated, practically relevant formulation with solid theoretical analysis and clear main text.  
- Weakness: presentation of theoretical results is unclear—O-notation hides dependence on algorithm parameters (e.g., ρ); recommend showing the precise inequalities from the supplement in the main paper.  
- Weakness: practical applicability concerns—MF-UCB appears sensitive to fidelity-accuracy parameters (ζ_m) that may be unknown; paper should discuss robustness to misspecification and adaptive estimation strategies.  
- Weakness: supplementary material contains numerous typos, notational inconsistencies, and minor errors that impede verification and require thorough proofreading.  
- Conclusion: valuable contribution likely suitable for publication after clarifying main theoretical bounds and thoroughly revising the appendix.
