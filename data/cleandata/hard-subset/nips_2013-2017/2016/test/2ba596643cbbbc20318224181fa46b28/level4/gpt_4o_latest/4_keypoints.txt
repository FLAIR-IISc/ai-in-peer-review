- Paper proposes a novel Multi-fidelity Multi-armed Bandit framework, an adaptive algorithm with regret analysis and lower bound, and simulations showing improvement over UCB, but is judged not ready for publication.

1. Notation is confusing and scattered—definitions are hard to find (e.g., variable s appears before it’s defined and is rarely used).  
2. Advertising example is misleading; linking to non-stationarity is inappropriate for this work and a different motivating example is recommended.  
3. Regret-analysis justification is unclear: optimal policy ignores costs (chooses highest mean), so cost implications of low-fidelity sampling are obscured.  
4. A sample/cost-complexity analysis is more appropriate and relevant (e.g., prototype testing scenarios requiring low-fidelity pretests).  
5. Portions of the text are difficult to follow and need a thorough revision to improve readability and clarity.
