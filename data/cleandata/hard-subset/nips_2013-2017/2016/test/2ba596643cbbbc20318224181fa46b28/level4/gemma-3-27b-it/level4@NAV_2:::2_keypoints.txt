- Introduces a new multi-armed bandit setting where each arm can be evaluated at multiple fidelities with costs proportional to accuracy.
- Defines a cost-aware regret measure and proposes the MF-UCB algorithm.
- Provides upper and lower bounds on cumulative pseudo-regret for MF-UCB.
- Paper is generally clear and well-written, but the supplementary material contains numerous minor errors that hurt readability.
- Use of “up-to-a-constant” notation is potentially misleading because hidden constants depend on parameters (e.g., ρ); include the precise inequalities from the supplement.
- Practical applicability is questionable due to reliance on accurate estimation of ζ (fidelity accuracy); discuss limitations and mitigation strategies.
- Theoretical contributions are valuable, with interesting theorems and solid proofs.
- Overall assessment: significant and worthwhile contribution.
