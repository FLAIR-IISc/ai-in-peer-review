Review- This paper studies a variant of the classical stochastic K-armed bandit problem in which inexpensive approximations for each arm are available. The authors formalize this as a multi-fidelity bandit problem. Theoretical results on regret bounds are provided. Simulations are conducted to demonstrate the effect of multi-fidelity. The presentation is clear and easy to follow. The experiments showcase the effect of the multi-fidelity bandit method. There is sufficient theoretical analysis of the proposed method’s lower bound, which indicates theoretical improvement.
