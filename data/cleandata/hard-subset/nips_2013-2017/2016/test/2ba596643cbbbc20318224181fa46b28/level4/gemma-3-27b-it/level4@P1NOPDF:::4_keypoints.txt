- Proposal of a Multi-fidelity Multi-armed bandit framework with an adaptive algorithm, regret analysis, lower bound, and simulations showing better performance than UCB, but not yet ready for publication.

1. Notation is confusing and scattered (e.g., variable s is used before definition and rarely used afterward).  
2. Advertising example is misleading and poorly matched to the framework; a different motivating example is recommended.  
3. Regret analysis justification is questionable since the framework favors the highest-mean arm regardless of cost, undermining the role of low-fidelity sampling.  
4. A sample/cost-complexity analysis would be valuable and better motivated by realistic constraints (e.g., prototype testing with required low-fidelity stages).  
5. Several sections are unwieldy and need a clarity-focused revision to improve readability.
