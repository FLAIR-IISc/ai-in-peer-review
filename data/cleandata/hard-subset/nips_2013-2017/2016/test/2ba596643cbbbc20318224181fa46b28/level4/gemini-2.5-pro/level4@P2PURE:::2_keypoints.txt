- Proposes a novel multi-armed bandit model with fidelity-dependent pull costs and regret defined as cost × pseudo-regret; introduces MF-UCB and gives upper and lower bounds on cumulative pseudo-regret.  
- Main paper is clear and well-written.  
- Supplementary material (proofs) contains numerous typos and minor errors that reduce readability and must be corrected.  
- Reviewer provides a non-exhaustive, detailed list of specific typos/errors in the appendix.  
- Using "up-to-a-multiplicative-constant" notation is misleading because hidden constants depend on key parameters (e.g., ρ); recommend presenting full exact inequalities in the main paper.  
- Practical applicability is unclear: MF-UCB’s strategy of using low-fidelity pulls to discard arms may be sensitive to accurate estimation of the fidelity parameter ζ and needs discussion of limitations and mitigation strategies.  
- Despite presentation issues, the paper makes a strong theoretical contribution with solid proofs and interesting theorems.  
- Overall: significant and interesting contribution but requires fixing the supplement, clarifying hidden constants, and addressing practical concerns.
