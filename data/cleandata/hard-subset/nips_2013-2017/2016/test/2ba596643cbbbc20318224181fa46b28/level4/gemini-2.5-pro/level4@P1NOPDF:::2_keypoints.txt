- Introduces a novel multi-armed bandit setting with variable-fidelity pulls where pull cost increases with fidelity and regret is cost-weighted pseudo-regret.  
- Proposes the MF-UCB algorithm and provides upper and lower bounds on cumulative pseudo-regret.  
- Main paper is clear and well-written.  
- Supplementary materials (proofs) contain numerous typos and minor errors that hinder readability; correcting them would significantly improve the paper.  
- Reviewer provides a non-exhaustive list of specific typographical and mathematical errors in the appendix.  
- Use of asymptotic notation hides multiplicative constants (e.g., a factor of ρ in Theorem 2); exact inequalities from the supplement should be stated in the main paper for clarity.  
- Practical applicability is unclear: MF-UCB’s low-fidelity elimination strategy is sensitive to accurate estimation of the fidelity-accuracy parameter ζ.  
- Recommends adding discussion of practical limitations and potential strategies to mitigate sensitivity to ζ.  
- Theoretical contributions are valuable and interesting, with solid proofs supporting the results.
