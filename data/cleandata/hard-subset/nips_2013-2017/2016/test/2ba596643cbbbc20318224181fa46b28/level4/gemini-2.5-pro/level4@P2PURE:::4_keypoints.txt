- Proposes a Multi-fidelity MAB framework with fidelity-dependent costs, an adaptive algorithm, regret analysis, lower bound, and simulations showing improvement over UCB.  
- Notation and definitions are scattered and confusing (e.g., variable s used before definition and then hardly used).  
- Online-advertising motivation is misleading because it introduces non-stationarity, which is outside the paper’s formal setting.  
- Objective formulation is unclear: with explicit costs, optimizing reward-to-cost or accounting for sampling expense seems more natural than simply identifying the highest-mean arm.  
- The multi-fidelity problem appears better framed as cost-complexity / PAC / best-arm identification (e.g., prototype testing) than regret minimization.  
- Several sections are unwieldy and hard to follow; the paper needs substantial rewriting to improve clarity and readability.
