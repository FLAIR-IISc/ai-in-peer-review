This paper aims to provide a different form of the classical stochastic K-armed bandit problem, where cheap approximations are obtained for each arm. The authors have formalized this problem as a multi-fidelity bandit problem. Theoretical results on the regret bound are provided. Simulations are performed to demonstrate the effect of multi-fidelity. The presentation is easy to understand. The simulations demonstrate the effect of the MF bandit method. There is sufficient theoretical analysis on the lower bound of the proposed method, which shows theoretical improvement.
