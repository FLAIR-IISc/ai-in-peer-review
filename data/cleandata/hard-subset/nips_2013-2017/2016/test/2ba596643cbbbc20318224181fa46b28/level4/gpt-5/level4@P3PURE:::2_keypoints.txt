- Introduces a new multi-armed bandit setting where each arm can be pulled at different fidelities with pull cost increasing with accuracy; regret = cost × pseudo-regret.  
- Proposes MF-UCB and gives both upper and lower bounds on cumulative pseudo-regret.  
- Main paper is clear and well written.  
- Supplementary material (where most proofs appear) contains numerous typos and small errors that can confuse readers; a non-exhaustive list is provided.  
- Use of “up to a multiplicative constant” is misleading because it hides important parameter dependencies (e.g., ρ); exact inequalities would improve clarity.  
- Practical applicability is questionable: MF-UCB may be sensitive to inaccurate estimation of fidelity parameter ζ and needs discussion of limitations and mitigation strategies.  
- Theoretical contribution is significant and interesting, with solid proofs.  
- Appendix errors include incorrect symbols, missing parentheses, wrong inequalities, swapped indices, missing/additional terms and tildes, mislabels (theorem/lemma), and simple typos (e.g., “distribtuions”).
