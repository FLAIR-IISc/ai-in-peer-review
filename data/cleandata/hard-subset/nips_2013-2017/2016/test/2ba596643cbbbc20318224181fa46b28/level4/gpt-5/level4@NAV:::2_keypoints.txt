- Paper introduces MF-UCB for multi-armed bandits with multi-fidelity pulls, defines cost-weighted regret, and provides upper and lower cumulative pseudo-regret bounds.  
- Main manuscript is clear and well written.  
- Supplementary material (proofs) contains numerous typos/minor errors that hinder readability and should be corrected (author supplied a non-exhaustive list).  
- Frequent use of “up to a multiplicative constant” is misleading because some absorbed constants depend on algorithmic parameters (e.g., factor ρ in Theorem 2); exact inequalities would improve clarity.  
- Practical deployment concerns: MF-UCB’s reliance on low-fidelity pulls for early elimination may be brittle if fidelity calibration ζ is misspecified; paper should discuss limitations and mitigations.  
- Theoretical contributions are solid with interesting theorems and convincing proofs.  
- Overall assessment: significant and compelling contribution.  
- Appendix-specific errors noted (examples include incorrect subscripts/superscripts, wrong inequalities, missing parentheses/tildes, swapped indices, and typos like “distribtuions” → “distributions”); these need systematic correction.
