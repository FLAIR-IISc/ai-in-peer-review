- Introduces a novel multi-armed bandit setting with M fidelities of increasing accuracy and cost, motivated by applications like online advertising.  
- Proposes a new regret definition, develops an algorithm, and provides theoretical analysis including a regret lower bound.  
- Empirical simulations are provided and support the theoretical claims.  
- Strength: the analysis and theoretical contribution are novel and meaningful.  
- Major concern: the paper assumes known fidelity deviations ε^(m) a priori; it is unclear how these values are obtained in practice and relaxing this assumption is needed.
