- Addresses a variant of the stochastic K-armed bandit problem with cheap approximations per arm (multi-fidelity setting).  
- Formalizes the problem as a multi-fidelity bandit framework.  
- Provides theoretical regret bounds for the proposed method.  
- Supplies a thorough lower-bound analysis showing theoretical improvement over existing approaches.  
- Simulations effectively demonstrate the empirical benefits of the multi-fidelity method.  
- Paper is well-written with clear and easy-to-understand presentation.
