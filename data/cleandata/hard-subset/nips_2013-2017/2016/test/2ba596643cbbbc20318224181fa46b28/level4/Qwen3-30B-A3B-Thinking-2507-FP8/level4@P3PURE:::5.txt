

This paper proposes a variant of the classical stochastic K-armed bandit problem where cheap approximations to each arm's reward are available. The authors formalize this as a multi-fidelity bandit problem and provide theoretical regret bounds. The presentation is clear and well-structured. Simulations demonstrate the effectiveness of the proposed multi-fidelity bandit approach. Theoretical analysis includes a lower bound that establishes the optimality of the proposed method, confirming its theoretical improvement over baseline approaches.
