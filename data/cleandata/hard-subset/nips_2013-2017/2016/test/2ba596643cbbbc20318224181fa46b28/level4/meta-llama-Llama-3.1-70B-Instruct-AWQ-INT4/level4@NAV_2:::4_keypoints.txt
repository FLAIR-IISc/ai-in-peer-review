- Overall: Proposes a multi-fidelity MAB framework with algorithm, regret analysis, lower bound, and simulations, but reviewer finds it not ready for publication.

1) Notation is confusing and dispersed; key symbols (e.g., s) are defined late and barely used.

2) Advertising example is misleading—references to display-time correlations and non-stationarity are inappropriate for this paper.

3) Regret analysis is hard to justify: optimal policy focuses on highest mean regardless of cost, and forcing low-fidelity sampling can hide true sampling costs.

4) Recommend a sample-/cost-complexity formulation motivated by prototype testing scenarios where low-fidelity constraints are realistic.

5) Writing is unwieldy and the paper needs editing to be more streamlined.
