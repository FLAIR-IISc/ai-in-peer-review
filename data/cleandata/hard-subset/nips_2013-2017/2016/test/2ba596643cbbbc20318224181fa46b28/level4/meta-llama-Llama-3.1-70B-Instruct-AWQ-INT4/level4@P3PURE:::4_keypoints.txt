- Contribution: Proposes Multi-fidelity Multi-armed Bandit framework, an algorithm adapting to available fidelity approximations and costs, with regret analysis, a lower bound, and simulations showing better performance than UCB — potentially impactful but not yet publishable.

- Notation issues: Notations are scattered and confusing; key symbol s is undefined until well after its first use and then barely used.

- Example choice: Advertising example is misleading by invoking non-stationarity (irrelevant here); a prototype-testing example would be more appropriate.

- Regret analysis concerns: Analysis is hard to justify because the optimal policy reduces to the arm with highest mean regardless of cost, forcing low-fidelity sampling of the best arm and potentially hiding low-fidelity sampling costs.

- Missing analysis: A sample/cost-complexity (best-arm identification with cost) analysis is recommended and well-motivated by practical testing constraints.

- Presentation: Some parts are unwieldy and the manuscript needs streamlining and clearer exposition.
