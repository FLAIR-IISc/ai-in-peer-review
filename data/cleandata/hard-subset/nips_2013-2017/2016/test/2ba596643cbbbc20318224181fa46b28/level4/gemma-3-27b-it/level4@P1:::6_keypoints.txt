- Introduces a novel multi-fidelity bandit setting where cheaper, lower-fidelity approximations help eliminate suboptimal arms and focus high-fidelity evaluations.  
- Proposes MF-UCB algorithm with formal problem setup and a regret lower bound.  
- Provides rigorous theoretical analysis demonstrating near-optimality.  
- Validates MF-UCB via simulations; online advertising example (short-term displays → long-term CTR) is compelling.  
- Paper is well-structured, clearly motivated, and situates itself appropriately with related work.  
- Major concern: analysis assumes known fidelity biases; practical estimation or relaxing this assumption is needed.  
- Recommendation: accept—novelty, theory, and potential impact outweigh the limitation, but authors should address unknown-bias issues in future work.
