- Studies a variant of stochastic K-armed bandits where cheap approximations for arm evaluations are available (multi-fidelity setting).  
- Formalizes the problem as a multi-fidelity bandit framework.  
- Proposes a method and derives theoretical regret bounds for it.  
- Provides a lower bound that demonstrates the theoretical improvements of the approach.  
- Presents simulations that effectively illustrate performance gains from incorporating multi-fidelity information.  
- Paper is clearly written and easy to follow.
