- Introduces a new multi-armed bandit setting with variable fidelities/costs and defines regret as cost times pseudo-regret; proposes MF-UCB and gives upper and lower bounds on cumulative pseudo-regret.  
- Main paper is clear and well written, but the supplementary materials contain many typos and small errors that reduce readability and should be corrected.  
- Use of "up-to-a-multiplicative-constant" notation is misleading because constants hide algorithm parameters (e.g., ρ in Theorem 2); exact inequalities would improve clarity.  
- Practical applicability is unclear: MF-UCB’s reliance on low-accuracy pulls to eliminate arms could fail if fidelity ζ is inaccurately estimated; discussion of limitations and remedies is needed.  
- Theoretical contribution is strong: the paper offers useful insights, interesting theorems, and solid proofs.  
- Appendix contains numerous specific errors (wrong symbols, missing parentheses, incorrect variable names, swapped indices, incorrect inequalities, missing terms, wrong references to lemmas/theorems); the reviewer provides a non-exhaustive list that should be corrected.
