- Novel framework: proposes Multi-fidelity Multi-armed Bandit with fidelity-dependent costs, an adaptive algorithm, regret analysis, lower bound, and simulations showing improvement over UCB, but not yet ready for publication.

- Notation issues: definitions are introduced disorganizedly (e.g., s defined after use) and some symbols are barely used thereafter.

- Example choice: using advertising as the motivating example is misleading (mentions irrelevant non-stationarity and correlations); a different example would be more appropriate.

- Regret formulation concern: standard regret analysis is hard to justify here because the optimal policy always picks the arm with the highest mean regardless of sampling cost, potentially masking the cost of unnecessary low-fidelity sampling.

- Suggested alternative analysis: a sample-complexity or cost-complexity perspective would be more appropriate (e.g., identifying the best product with high probability while minimizing testing costs in prototype development).

- Writing/organization: several sections are unwieldy and need revision to improve flow and clarity.
