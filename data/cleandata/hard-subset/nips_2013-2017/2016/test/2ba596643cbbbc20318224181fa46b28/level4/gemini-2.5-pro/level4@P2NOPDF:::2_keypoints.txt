- Introduces a novel multi-fidelity multi-armed bandit setting where pull cost increases with fidelity and regret is cost-weighted pseudo-regret.  
- Proposes the MF-UCB algorithm and provides upper and lower bounds on cumulative pseudo-regret.  
- Main paper is clear, well-written, and presents a significant theoretical contribution with interesting theorems.  
- The appendix (proofs) contains numerous typos and minor errors that hinder readability and verification of proofs.  
- Errors in the appendix include wrong indices/superscripts, missing parentheses/symbols, incorrect inequalities, swapped indices, and mislabeled lemmas/theorems.  
- Recommends careful proofreading and correction of the supplementary material to improve quality and impact.  
- Criticizes use of "up-to-a-multiplicative-constant" notation that hides important parameter dependencies (e.g., ρ); suggests presenting exact inequalities from the appendix in the main paper.  
- Raises practical concerns about applying MF-UCB: sensitivity to misestimation of the fidelity-accuracy relation (ζ) and lack of discussion on real-world limitations and mitigation strategies.  
- Overall assessment: valuable theoretical work that would be excellent after fixing appendix errors and adding practical discussion.
