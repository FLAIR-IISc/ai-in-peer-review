- Introduces a novel multi-armed bandit framework with M fidelities where higher fidelities yield more accurate but costlier reward approximations.  
- Motivates the setting with online advertising (short displays as low-cost approximations of long-term clicks).  
- Proposes a new regret definition tailored to multi-fidelity costs.  
- Presents a novel algorithm with theoretical guarantees, including a regret lower bound.  
- Validates the approach via simulations; analysis is technically sound and novel.
