This paper is a type of classical stochastic K-armed bandit problem, where cheap estimates are available for each arm. The authors have formalized this problem as a multi-fidelty bandit problem. Theoretical results on the regret bound are provided. Simulations have been performed to demonstrate the effect of multi-fidelty. The presentation is easy to understand. The simulations demonstrate the effect of the MF bandit method. There are sufficient theoretical analyses on the lower bound of the proposed method, which show theoretical improvements.
