- Introduces a novel multi-fidelity multi-armed bandit setting where pulls have fidelity-dependent cost and defines a new cost-weighted pseudo-regret metric.  
- Proposes the MF-UCB algorithm and provides theoretical analysis with upper and lower bounds on cumulative pseudo-regret.  
- Main paper is clear and well-written, but most proofs are in the supplement, which contains many typographical and minor mathematical errors that hinder understanding.  
- Use of "up-to-a-multiplicative-constant" notation is misleading because hidden constants depend on key parameters (e.g., ρ in Theorem 2); recommend showing full exact inequalities in the main text.  
- Practical applicability is unclear—MF-UCB’s reliance on low-fidelity pulls to discard arms may be sensitive to misestimation of the fidelity-accuracy parameter ζ; paper should discuss limitations and mitigation strategies.  
- Despite the issues, the theoretical contributions are strong and represent a significant and compelling advance.  
- Reviewer supplies a non-exhaustive list of specific typos and corrections in the appendix that should be fixed to improve readability.
