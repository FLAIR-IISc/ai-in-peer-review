This paper studies a variant of the classical stochastic K-armed bandit problem in which a cheap approximation for each arm is available. The authors formalize this as a multi-fidelity bandit problem. Theoretical results on regret bounds are provided. Simulations are conducted to demonstrate the effect of the multi-fidelity (MF) bandit method. The presentation is clear and easy to understand. There is sufficient theoretical analysis of the lower bound for the proposed method, which shows theoretical improvement.
