- Provides a matching lower bound that proves the regret bound is asymptotically tight and accounts for fidelity cost structure.

- Empirical simulations (synthetic and semi-realistic) support theory, showing substantially lower regret—often orders of magnitude.  
- Ablation studies isolate effects of fidelity cost and number of arms; figures are clear and illustrative.
