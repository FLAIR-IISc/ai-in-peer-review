Paper introduces a multi-fidelity MAB setting (costs increase with accuracy), defines regret as cost × pseudo-regret, and proposes MF-UCB with matching upper and lower bounds.

Main paper is clear and well-written; theoretical contributions are significant with interesting theorems and solid proofs.

Clarity issue: using "up-to-a-multiplicative-constant" notation hides important algorithm parameters (e.g., ρ in Theorem 2); recommend stating exact inequalities from the supplement in the main text.

Practical applicability concern: MF-UCB’s strategy of using low-accuracy pulls to eliminate arms may be sensitive to misestimation of ζ; paper should discuss real-world applicability and mitigation strategies.

Supplementary materials quality: the appendix contains numerous typos and small errors that reduce readability and can confuse proofs.

Appendix contains a non-exhaustive list of specific mistakes (index errors, wrong symbols/exponents, missing parentheses, swapped indices, incorrect inequalities, and typographical errors) that must be corrected.
