- Introduces a variant of the stochastic K-armed bandit where cheap approximations of arm rewards are available.  
- Formalizes this as a multi-fidelity bandit model.  
- Derives theoretical regret bounds for the proposed approach.  
- Establishes a lower bound showing the theoretical advantage of the multi-fidelity setting.  
- Presents simulations that effectively demonstrate performance gains from the method.  
- Paper is clearly written and easy to follow.
