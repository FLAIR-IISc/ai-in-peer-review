- Introduces a new multi-armed bandit setting with per-arm evaluation fidelities and fidelity-dependent pull costs; regret combines cost and pseudo-regret.  
- Proposes MF-UCB algorithm and provides upper and lower bounds on its cumulative pseudo-regret.  
- Main paper is clear and well-written; supplementary materials (proofs) contain many typos and small errors that hinder readability.  
- “Up-to-a-multiplicative-constant” notation is misleading because hidden constants depend on algorithm parameters (e.g., ρ); presenting exact inequalities would improve clarity.  
- Practical applicability is unclear; MF-UCB’s low-accuracy elimination strategy may be sensitive to misestimation of ζ and requires discussion of limitations and remedies.  
- Theoretical contribution is valuable with interesting theorems and solid proofs despite presentation issues.  
- Overall assessment: significant and interesting contribution.
