- Problem addressed: finding an optimal Bayesian network configuration (structure learning).
- Main contribution: incorporating non-decomposable constraints, particularly ancestral constraints, into the Chen et al. [2015, 2016] structure learning approach.
- Technical approach: rephrasing the problem as a MaxSAT formulation with specific constraints.
- Empirical finding: orders-of-magnitude runtime improvements over ILP-based alternatives.
- Reviewer caveat: reviewer has limited expertise with this approach but finds the runtime comparisons impressive.
