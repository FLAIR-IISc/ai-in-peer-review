This paper presents a novel approach for learning Bayesian networks with ancestral constraints, which are non-decomposable and thus challenging for traditional score-based learning methods. The authors propose a framework that leverages a recently developed search space, called the EC tree, and empowers an oracle that optimizes decomposable scores by passing it decomposable constraints inferred from the non-decomposable ancestral constraints.

The paper is well-written, and the authors provide a clear and concise introduction to the problem, related work, and their approach. The technical sections are well-organized, and the authors provide a thorough explanation of their method, including theorems and proofs. The experimental evaluation is comprehensive, and the authors provide a detailed comparison with an ILP-based system.
