- Tackles optimal Bayesian network structure learning by integrating non-decomposable (ancestral) constraints.  
- Extends the Chen et al. [2015, 2016] structure-learning approach to handle ancestral constraints.  
- Reformulates the constrained learning problem as a MaxSAT instance with tailored constraints.  
- Empirical evaluation shows substantial runtime reductions compared to ILP-based methods.  
- Demonstrates orders-of-magnitude runtime improvements over alternative methods in runtime comparisons.
