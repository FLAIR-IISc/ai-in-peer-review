- Problem addressed: determining an optimal Bayesian network structure.  
- Main contribution: integrates non-decomposable constraints—particularly ancestral constraints—into the Chen et al. [2015, 2016] structure learning framework.  
- Methodology: reformulates the problem as a MaxSAT instance with tailored constraints.  
- Empirical result: shows orders of magnitude speedups over ILP-based methods.  
- Reviewer caveat: limited familiarity with the methodology, but finds the runtime results highly compelling.
