- Proposes a novel method for optimal Bayesian network learning under ancestral constraints.  
- Introduces the EC tree search space to handle non-decomposable scores.  
- Uses an oracle that optimizes decomposable scores to navigate the EC tree.  
- Presents a sound and complete pruning method for the EC tree based on ancestral constraints.  
- Shows how to empower the oracle by deriving decomposable constraints from non-decomposable ancestral constraints.  
- Paper is well-written, clearly explained, and includes thorough literature review.  
- Experimental evaluation is comprehensive and shows large speedups over GOBNILP and good scalability across variables, data size, and constraint counts.  
- Comparison to GOBNILP may be unfair because the ILP encoding of ancestral constraints used is suboptimal.  
- Lacks detailed comparison to other methods (e.g., van Beek and Hoffmann) that could be adapted for ancestral constraints.  
- Some technical details may be hard for non-experts to follow.  
- Quality 8/10, Clarity 9/10, Originality 8/10; recommend accept with minor revisions.
