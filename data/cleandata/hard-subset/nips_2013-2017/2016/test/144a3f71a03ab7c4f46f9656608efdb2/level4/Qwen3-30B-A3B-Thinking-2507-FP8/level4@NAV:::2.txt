

The authors propose a method that utilizes ancestral constraints to guide the admissible heuristic within an exhaustive search algorithm. However, the experimental evaluation requires clarification. Table 1 compares (EC + new work) against (GOB + new work'), while Table 3 appears to compare EC directly against GOB—this relationship is unclear. A more meaningful comparison would explicitly evaluate (EC + new work) against EC alone. Although the comparison between (GOB + new work') and GOB is interesting, EC demonstrates significantly stronger baseline performance than GOB. Consequently, even if the new work yielded substantially greater relative gains for GOB compared to EC, the approach (EC + new work) would remain preferable.
