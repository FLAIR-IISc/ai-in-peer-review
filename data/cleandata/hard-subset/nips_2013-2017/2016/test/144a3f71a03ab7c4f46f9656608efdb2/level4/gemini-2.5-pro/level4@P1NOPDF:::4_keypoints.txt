- Novelty: Proposes projecting non-decomposable ancestral constraints into decomposable edge and topological constraints (Sec. 5); paper is well-written and contributions are clear.

- Concern 1: Table 3 shows improvements for EC trees but not ILP, raising questions about the projection's generalizability to other learning algorithms; need evidence it helps methods that originally cannot handle non-decomposable constraints.

- Concern 2: Table 2 shows an "easy-hard-easy" pattern for EC trees as ancestral-constraint proportion increases; authors should explain this and whether EC tree performance is highly sensitive to the amount of domain knowledge.

- Concern 3: Table 2 reports SHD only for EC trees; corresponding ILP SHD results are missing and should be included for a complete comparison.

- Suggestion: Remove broad BN search background in Sec. 4.1 and instead expand the explanation of EC trees and the A* search algorithm, since experiments focus on EC tree search.
