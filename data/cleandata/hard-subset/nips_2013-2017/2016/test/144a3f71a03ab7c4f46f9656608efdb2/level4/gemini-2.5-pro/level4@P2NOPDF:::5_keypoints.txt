- Problem: learning optimal Bayesian network structures under challenging, non-decomposable constraints.  
- Contribution: novel method to incorporate non-decomposable (ancestral) constraints into the Chen et al. score-based structure learning framework.  
- Approach: reformulates the structure learning problem as a Maximum Satisfiability (MaxSAT) problem.  
- Empirical evaluation: provides strong evidence that the approach is effective.  
- Comparison: MaxSAT-based solver outperforms state-of-the-art ILP methods, yielding runtime reductions of several orders of magnitude.  
- Reviewer note: despite limited MaxSAT expertise, the results are impressive and represent a substantial practical contribution.
