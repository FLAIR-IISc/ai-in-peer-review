The authors present a method that leverages a given set of ancestral constraints to inform the admissible heuristic for an exhaustive search algorithm. I found the experimental setup somewhat perplexing. The existing approaches of EC and GOB are used as baselines, and Table 1 compares the performance of EC combined with the proposed method to GOB combined with a variant of the proposed method. However, it is unclear whether Table 3 is intended to compare EC directly to GOB. A more straightforward comparison would be to evaluate the proposed method's impact on EC by comparing the performance of EC with and without the new method. Although the comparison between GOB with and without the new method is also of interest, the significant performance difference between EC and GOB suggests that EC would likely remain the preferred choice even if GOB benefits more substantially from the new method.
