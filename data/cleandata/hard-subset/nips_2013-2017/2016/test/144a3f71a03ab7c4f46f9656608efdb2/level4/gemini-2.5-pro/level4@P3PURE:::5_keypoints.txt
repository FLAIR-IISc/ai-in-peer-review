- Addresses learning optimal Bayesian network structures under non-decomposable constraints.  
- Main contribution: a method to incorporate ancestral (and similar) constraints into the Chen et al. [2015, 2016] structure-learning framework.  
- Technical approach: reformulates the constrained structure-learning problem as a Maximum Satisfiability (MaxSAT) problem.  
- Empirical finding: the MaxSAT approach achieves orders-of-magnitude runtime improvements over ILP-based methods.  
- Reviewer note: despite limited MaxSAT expertise, the reported several-orders-of-magnitude performance gains are impressive.
