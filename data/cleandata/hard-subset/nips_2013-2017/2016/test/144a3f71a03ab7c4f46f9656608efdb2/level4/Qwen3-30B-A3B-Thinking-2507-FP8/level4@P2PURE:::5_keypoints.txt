- Addresses optimal Bayesian network structure learning with non-decomposable (ancestral) constraints.
- Integrates ancestral constraints into the structure learning framework of Chen et al. (2015, 2016).
- Reformulates the constrained learning problem as a MaxSAT instance with tailored constraints.
- Empirical results show orders-of-magnitude runtime reductions compared to ILP-based methods.
- Reviewer has limited expertise in the approach but finds the reported runtime improvements highly impressive.
