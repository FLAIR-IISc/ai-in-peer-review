- Paper proposes using ancestral constraints to guide the heuristic of an exhaustive search algorithm.
- Reviewer finds the experimental results confusing.
- Table 1 appears to compare augmented methods ((EC + new) vs (GOB + new')), not the base algorithms.
- Table 3 compares the base methods EC and GOB, but an explicit comparison of (EC + new) vs EC is missing.
- Since EC already outperforms GOB, the reviewer suggests showing (EC + new) vs EC because GOB benefiting more may not change EC’s superiority.
