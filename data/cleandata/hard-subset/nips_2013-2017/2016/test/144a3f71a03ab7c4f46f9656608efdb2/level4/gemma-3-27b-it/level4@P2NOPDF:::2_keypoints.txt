- Paper proposes a novel method using ancestral constraints to refine the heuristic for exhaustive search.
- Main concern: experimental results are hard to interpret.
- Table 1 appears to compare (EC + proposed method) against (GOB + a modified proposed method called “new work’”).
- Table 3 seems to attempt a direct comparison between EC and GOB.
- Missing explicit comparison of EC with the proposed method versus EC alone.
- Because EC is a much stronger baseline than GOB, improvements on GOB may be less convincing than demonstrating gains on EC.
- It’s unclear why “new work’” differs from the proposed method and whether it’s specially tailored to GOB, raising fairness concerns.
- Recommendation: directly evaluate the proposed method applied to both EC and GOB to assess effectiveness and generalizability.
