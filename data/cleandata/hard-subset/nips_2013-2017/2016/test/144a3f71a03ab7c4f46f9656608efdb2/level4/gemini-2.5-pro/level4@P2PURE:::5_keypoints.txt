- Presents a method for learning the optimal structure of Bayesian networks.  
- Extends Chen et al.'s structure learning framework to handle non-decomposable constraints, particularly ancestral constraints.  
- Formulates the structure learning problem as a Maximum Satisfiability (MaxSAT) problem.  
- Empirical results show the MaxSAT approach is far more efficient than ILP-based methods, achieving runtime reductions of several orders of magnitude.  
- Reviewer notes limited expertise with MaxSAT-based formulations but finds the reported performance gains very impressive.
