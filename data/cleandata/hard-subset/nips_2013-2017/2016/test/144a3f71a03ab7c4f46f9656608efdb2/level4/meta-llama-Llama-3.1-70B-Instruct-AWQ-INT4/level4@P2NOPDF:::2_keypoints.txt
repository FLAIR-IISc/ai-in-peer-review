- Paper proposes using ancestral constraints to build an admissible heuristic for exhaustive search.
- Experimental setup is confusing and lacks clarity.
- Baselines EC and GOB are used, but Table 1 compares EC+proposed to GOB+a variant, creating ambiguity.
- It is unclear whether Table 3 compares EC directly to GOB.
- A clearer evaluation would compare EC with and without the new method to measure the method's impact on EC.
- Comparing GOB with and without the new method is also relevant.
- Given the large performance gap between EC and GOB, EC would likely remain preferred even if GOB gains more from the proposed method.
