- Proposes a novel method for learning Bayesian networks with non-decomposable ancestral constraints.  
- Extends a recent framework for optimal structure learning with non-decomposable scores to incorporate ancestral constraints.  
- Shows how to infer decomposable constraints from non-decomposable ancestral constraints to empower an oracle for structure search.  
- Paper is well-written and explanations of problem, approach, and experiments are clear.  
- Experiments compare to a state-of-the-art ILP-based system and show significant efficiency improvements.  
- Experimental evaluation is limited and could be more comprehensive.  
- Lacks comparisons to other existing methods for constrained Bayesian network learning.  
- Needs more insight into problem types where the approach is particularly well-suited.
