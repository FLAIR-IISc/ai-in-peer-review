- Presents a method to project non-decomposable ancestral constraints into decomposable constraints, enabling decomposable-score-based structure learning to use such domain knowledge.  
- Main technical contribution (Section 5): technique to transform ancestral constraints into edge and topological ordering constraints.  
- Paper is well-written and communicates theoretical and experimental contributions clearly.  
- Concern: Table 3 shows improvement for EC trees but not ILP, raising questions about the generalizability of the projection method to other learning approaches.  
- Concern: Table 2 exhibits an easy–hard–easy performance pattern for EC trees as ancestral-constraint proportion changes; the cause and sensitivity to constraint quantity need explanation.  
- Concern: Table 2 reports structural Hamming distance only for EC trees; results should be compared with ILP for a more complete evaluation.  
- Suggestion: Omit Section 4.1 and instead expand the experimental explanation of EC trees and the A* algorithm within the EC tree setting.
