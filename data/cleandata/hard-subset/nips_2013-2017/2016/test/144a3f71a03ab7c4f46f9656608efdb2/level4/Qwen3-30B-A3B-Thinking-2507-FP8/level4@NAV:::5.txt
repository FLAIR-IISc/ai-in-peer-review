

This paper addresses the problem of determining an optimal Bayesian network structure. Specifically, the authors demonstrate how to integrate non-decomposable constraints—particularly ancestral constraints—into the structure learning framework of Chen et al. [2015, 2016]. Empirical evaluation against ILP-based methods reveals orders of magnitude speedups, achieved by reformulating the problem as a MaxSAT instance with tailored constraints. Although my familiarity with this methodology is limited, the runtime results are highly compelling, showing execution times that are orders of magnitude faster than competing approaches.
