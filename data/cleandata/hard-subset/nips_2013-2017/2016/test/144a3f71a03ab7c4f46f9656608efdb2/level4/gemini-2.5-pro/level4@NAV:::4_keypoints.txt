- Proposes a method to project non-decomposable ancestral constraints into decomposable edge and topological ordering constraints to enable decomposable score-based structure learning.  
- Core novelty is the projection technique described in Section 5.  
- Manuscript is well-written with clear theoretical and experimental sections.  
- Concern: Table 3 shows improvement for EC trees but not ILP, raising questions about the method’s generalizability and whether benefits are limited to specific algorithms.  
- Observation: Table 2 shows an "easy-hard-easy" performance pattern for EC trees as constraint proportion increases, needing explanation and suggesting sensitivity to the amount of domain knowledge.  
- Issue: Table 2 is incomplete—corresponding Structural Hamming Distance results for the ILP approach should be reported for direct comparison.  
- Suggestion: Remove or shorten Section 4.1 on BN graphs and instead expand explanation of EC trees and the A* algorithm used in experiments.
