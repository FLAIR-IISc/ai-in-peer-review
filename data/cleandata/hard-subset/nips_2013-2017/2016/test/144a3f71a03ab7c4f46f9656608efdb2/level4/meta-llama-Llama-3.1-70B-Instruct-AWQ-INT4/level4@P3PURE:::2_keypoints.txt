- Paper proposes using ancestral constraints to shape an admissible heuristic for exhaustive search.
- Experimental design and presentation are confusing.
- Table 1 compares EC combined with the new method to GOB combined with a modified new method, which is hard to interpret.
- Table 3 appears to directly compare EC to GOB, creating inconsistency with Table 1.
- The paper should include an explicit comparison of EC+new method versus EC alone to quantify the method's benefit.
- A similar comparison of GOB+new method versus GOB alone would also be informative.
- Results indicate EC significantly outperforms GOB.
- Given EC’s superior baseline, EC+new method would likely be preferred even if GOB gained more from the new method.
