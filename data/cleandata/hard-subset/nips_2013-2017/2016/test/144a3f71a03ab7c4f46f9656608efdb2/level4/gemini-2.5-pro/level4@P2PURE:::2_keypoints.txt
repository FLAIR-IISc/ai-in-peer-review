- Proposes using ancestral constraints to build an admissible heuristic for exhaustive search.

- Experimental setup is unclear: Table 1 seems to compare EC and GOB after augmentation, while Table 3 appears to show the original baselines — this needs clarification.

- The paper lacks a direct ablation comparing EC+proposed method against the original EC baseline.

- EC is a stronger baseline than GOB, so absolute performance of EC+method is more important than relative gains on GOB.
