- Extends Chen et al. (2015, 2016) to handle non-decomposable constraints, including ancestral constraints, in Bayesian network structure learning.  
- Main contribution: reformulates constrained structure learning as a MaxSAT instance with tailored constraints.  
- Empirical evaluation shows substantial performance gains over ILP-based methods.  
- Reported runtime improvements are several orders of magnitude.  
- Reviewer unfamiliarity with MaxSAT noted, but strong runtime comparisons support the authors' claims.  
- MaxSAT reformulation appears crucial for efficiently handling complex constraints that are hard to express in ILP.  
- The observed speedup could make structure learning feasible for larger and more complex datasets.  
- Request for more detail on the specific MaxSAT encoding and constraint-satisfaction strategies for less familiar readers.  
- Overall, robust empirical results provide strong evidence of the method’s effectiveness.
