- Addresses optimal Bayesian network configuration with non-decomposable (ancestral) constraints, building on Chen et al. [2015, 2016].
- Reformulates the problem as a MaxSAT problem with specific constraints to encode ancestry and other non-decomposable requirements.
- Empirical evaluation shows orders-of-magnitude runtime improvements over ILP-based methods.
- Reports a significant reduction in runtime compared to alternative methods.
- Reviewer notes limited familiarity with the approach but finds the runtime comparisons compelling.
