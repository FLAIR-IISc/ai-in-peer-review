- Paper proposes improving exhaustive search by using ancestral constraints to guide an admissible heuristic.
- Experimental comparisons between baselines (EC and GOB) are unclear and need clarification.
- Clarify whether Table 1 shows the proposed method applied to each baseline (EC+proposal vs GOB+proposal) and Table 3 shows original baselines (EC vs GOB).
- A direct ablation comparing EC+proposal against the original EC baseline is essential to isolate the contribution of the ancestral constraints.
- EC appears to be a substantially stronger baseline, so absolute performance of EC+proposal matters more than relative gains on GOB.
- Recommend including the direct EC+proposal vs EC comparison in a revised manuscript to strengthen the paper’s claims.
