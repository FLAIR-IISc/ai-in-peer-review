This paper addresses the problem of finding an optimal Bayesian network configuration. Specifically, the authors demonstrate how to incorporate non-divisive constraints (and particularly ancestral constraints) into the structure learning approach of Chen et al. [2015, 2016]. An empirical evaluation of the proposed approach against ILP-based approaches shows a sequence of magnitude improvements. The authors redefine the problem as a MaxSAT problem with specific constraints. Although my expertise in this approach is limited, the results (in terms of runtime comparison) appear quite impressive. In particular, the authors show a reduction in runtime in the order of magnitude relative to alternative methods.
