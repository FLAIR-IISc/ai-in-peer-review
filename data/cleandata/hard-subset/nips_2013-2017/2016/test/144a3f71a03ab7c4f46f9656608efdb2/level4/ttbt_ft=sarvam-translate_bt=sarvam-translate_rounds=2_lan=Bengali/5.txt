This paper discusses the problem of finding an optimal Bayesian network configuration. Specifically, the authors show how to incorporate non- separable constraints (and especially ancestor constraints) into the learning method of Chen et al. [2015, 2016]. An empirical evaluation of the proposed method shows significant improvement compared to ILP-based methods. The authors re-state the problem as a MaxSAT problem with specific constraints. Although my expertise on this method is limited, the results (runtime comparison) seem quite impressive. In particular, the authors show a significant runtime reduction compared to alternative methods.
