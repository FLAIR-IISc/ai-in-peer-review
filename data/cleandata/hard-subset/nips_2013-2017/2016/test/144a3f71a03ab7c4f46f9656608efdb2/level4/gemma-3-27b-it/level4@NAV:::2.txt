The authors introduce a technique utilizing ancestral constraints to guide the heuristic search within an exhaustive search framework. The experimental setup, however, lacked clarity. Specifically, Table 1 contrasts the performance of EC combined with the proposed method against GOB paired with a modified version of the method. This leads to a question regarding Table 3 – does it present a direct comparison between EC and GOB alone? A more informative analysis would include an explicit comparison of EC with and without the proposed enhancement. While the comparison between GOB with and without the modified method is noteworthy, the inherent advantage of EC over GOB suggests that even substantial improvements to GOB may not outweigh the benefits of applying the proposed method to EC.
