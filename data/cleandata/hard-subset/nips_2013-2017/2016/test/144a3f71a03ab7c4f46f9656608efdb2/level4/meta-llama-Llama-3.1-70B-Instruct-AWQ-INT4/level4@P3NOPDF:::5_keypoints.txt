- Addresses optimal Bayesian network configuration with incorporation of non-decomposable (ancestral) constraints.  
- Reformulates the problem as a MaxSAT encoding tailored to these constraints.  
- MaxSAT formulation enables efficient handling of complex non-decomposable constraints.  
- Empirical evaluation compares the proposed method against ILP-based approaches.  
- Results show orders-of-magnitude runtime reductions over ILP baselines.  
- Reviewer acknowledges limited expertise but finds the runtime improvements impressive.  
- Main contribution: a novel, effective approach to incorporate non-decomposable constraints into structure learning.  
- Results are well-presented and suggest clear practical advantages over ILP methods.
