- Introduces a method to project non-decomposable ancestral constraints into decomposable edge and topological ordering constraints for decomposable-score-based structure learning.  
- Core innovation formalized in Section 5; paper is well-written with clear theory and experiments.  
- Generalizability concern: Table 3 shows gains only for EC trees, not ILP, calling into question applicability beyond EC trees.  
- Sensitivity concern: Table 2’s "easy-hard-easy" pattern suggests performance depends strongly on the amount of constraints; need analysis of conflicts or over‑constraining and whether ILP shows similar sensitivity.  
- Comparative metrics missing: Table 2 reports SHD only for EC trees; must include ILP SHD under the same constraints to judge relative benefit.  
- Presentation issue: Section 4.1’s BN background feels redundant given experiments focus on EC trees; recommend integrating or omitting to streamline.  
- Overall: Promising contribution but evaluation needs broader cross-method comparisons, sensitivity analysis, and tighter exposition to demonstrate wider impact.
