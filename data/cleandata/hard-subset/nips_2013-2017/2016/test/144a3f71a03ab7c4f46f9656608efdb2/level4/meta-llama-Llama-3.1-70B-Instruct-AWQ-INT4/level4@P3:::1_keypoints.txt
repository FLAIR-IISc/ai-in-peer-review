- Proposes a novel framework for learning Bayesian networks with ancestral constraints using the EC tree search space.  
- Translates non-decomposable ancestral constraints into decomposable constraints to empower an oracle that optimizes decomposable scores.  
- Provides a sound and complete method for pruning the EC tree based on ancestral constraints.  
- Uses MaxSAT to infer ordering constraints, enabling efficient incorporation of ancestral constraints.  
- Empirical evaluations show orders-of-magnitude efficiency gains over existing ILP-based methods.  
- Paper is well-written, technically sound, and demonstrates strong understanding of related work.  
- Comparison with GOBNILP is limited due to a suboptimal ILP formulation; a tighter formulation (e.g., Cussens 2010) should be used.  
- Lacks comparison with other relevant methods (e.g., van Beek & Hoffmann 2015 constraint programming), reducing completeness of evaluation.  
- Recommendation: accept with revisions to improve ILP comparisons and include additional relevant baselines.
