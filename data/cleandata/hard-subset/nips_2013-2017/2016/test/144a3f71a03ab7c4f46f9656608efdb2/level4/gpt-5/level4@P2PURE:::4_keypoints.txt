- Main novelty: Section 5 projects non-decomposable ancestral constraints into decomposable edge and topological-ordering constraints to enable decomposable-score-based structure learning.  
- Paper quality: Well written with clear theoretical development and experiments.  
- Concern 1: Table 3 shows improvements for EC trees but not ILP, raising doubts about whether the projection generalizes beyond EC trees or benefits learners that cannot natively handle non-decomposable constraints.  
- Concern 2: Table 2 shows an easy–hard–easy pattern for EC-tree performance as ancestral-constraint proportion increases, suggesting sensitivity to the amount of domain knowledge and possible need to tune constraint quantity.  
- Concern 3: Structural Hamming distance is reported only for EC trees in Table 2; ILP comparison for SHD is missing.  
- Minor suggestion: Remove Section 4.1 on BN graphs and instead expand the explanation of EC trees and the A* algorithm in the EC-tree setting.
