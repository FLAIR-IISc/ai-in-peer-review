The authors propose a method that utilizes ancestral constraints to guide the heuristic function within an exhaustive search algorithm. I found the experimental presentation somewhat unclear. Specifically, Table 1 compares the performance of EC combined with the proposed method against GOB combined with a modified version of the proposed method (“new work'”). My understanding is that Table 3 then presents a comparison between EC and GOB directly—though I would appreciate clarification on this point. A more informative comparison would be a direct assessment of EC with the proposed method versus EC alone. While the contrast between GOB with the modified method and GOB is interesting, the significant performance advantage of EC over GOB suggests that even substantial improvements to GOB through the proposed method would likely not outweigh the benefits of applying it to EC.
