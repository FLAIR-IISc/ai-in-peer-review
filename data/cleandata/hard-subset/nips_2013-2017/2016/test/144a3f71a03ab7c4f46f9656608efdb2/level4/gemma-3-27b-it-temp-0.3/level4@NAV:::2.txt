The authors introduce a technique utilizing ancestral constraints to refine the heuristic guiding an exhaustive search. The experimental presentation, however, requires clarification. Specifically, Table 1 appears to contrast the performance of EC combined with the proposed method against GOB combined with a modified version of the proposed method (new work’). Consequently, Table 3 seems to offer a direct comparison between EC and GOB. It would be beneficial to see an explicit comparison of EC with and without the proposed enhancement – while the contrast between GOB with and without modification is noteworthy, the inherent advantage of EC over GOB suggests that even substantial improvements to GOB may not outweigh the benefits of applying the new method to EC.
