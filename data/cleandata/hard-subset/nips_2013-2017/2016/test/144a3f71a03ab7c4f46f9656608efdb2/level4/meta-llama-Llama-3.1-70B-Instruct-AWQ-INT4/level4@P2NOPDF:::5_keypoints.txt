- Addresses optimal Bayesian network configuration with emphasis on handling non-decomposable constraints (especially ancestral constraints).  
- Builds on Chen et al.’s structure learning approach to incorporate these constraints.  
- Proposes a novel reformulation of the problem as a MaxSAT instance with tailored constraints.  
- Empirically compares the MaxSAT method against ILP-based approaches.

- Reviewer notes limited familiarity with MaxSAT but finds the runtime results impressive and promising for efficient BN configuration.
