This paper addresses the problem of optimal Bayesian network configuration, extending the structure learning approach of Chen et al. [2015, 2016] to incorporate non-decomposable constraints, including ancestral constraints. The authors demonstrate significant improvements – on the order of magnitudes – in empirical evaluations compared to Integer Linear Programming (ILP) based methods. They formulate the problem as a MaxSAT instance with specialized constraints. Although my familiarity with this particular approach is limited, the reported runtime results are compelling, showing runtime reductions of several orders of magnitude over existing alternatives.
