

This paper addresses the problem of finding an optimal Bayesian network configuration by incorporating non-decomposable constraints, specifically ancestral constraints, into the structure learning framework of Chen et al. [2015, 2016]. The authors reformulate the problem as a MaxSAT instance with tailored constraints. Empirical evaluation demonstrates orders-of-magnitude runtime improvements relative to alternative ILP-based approaches. While my expertise in MaxSAT-based methods is limited, the reported runtime comparisons are compelling and represent a significant practical advancement.
