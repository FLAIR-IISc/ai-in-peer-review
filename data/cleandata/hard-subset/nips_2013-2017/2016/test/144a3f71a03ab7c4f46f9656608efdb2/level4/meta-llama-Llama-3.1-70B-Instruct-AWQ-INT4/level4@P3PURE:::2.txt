The authors present a method that leverages a given set of ancestral constraints to inform the admissible heuristic for an exhaustive search algorithm. I found the experimental design and presentation to be somewhat confusing. Given the existing approaches of EC and GOB, Table 1 compares the performance of EC combined with the new method to GOB combined with a modified version of the new method. However, if I understand correctly, Table 3 appears to compare EC to GOB directly. 

It would be more insightful to include an explicit comparison between EC combined with the new method and EC alone, as this would allow for a clearer assessment of the benefits provided by the proposed approach. Although the comparison between GOB combined with the modified new method and GOB alone is also of interest, the results suggest that EC significantly outperforms GOB. Even if GOB were to benefit more substantially from the new method than EC, the superior performance of EC would likely make EC combined with the new method the preferred choice.
