- Addresses optimal Bayesian network structure learning and incorporation of non-decomposable constraints, especially ancestral constraints.
- Extends the structure learning framework of Chen et al. (2015, 2016) to handle these constraints.
- Reformulates the problem as a MaxSAT instance with tailored constraints.
