- Novel method for learning Bayesian network structures under non-decomposable ancestral constraints via EC tree search and constraint projection.
- Key innovation: project ancestral (non-decomposable) constraints into decomposable edge and ordering constraints usable by existing decomposable-score oracles.
- EC tree search space merges Markov-equivalent structures and enables effective pruning using ancestral constraints.
- Theoretical soundness supported by Theorem 1 for pruning; overall framework is clear and well-structured.
- Practical innovation: MaxSAT-based method to infer ordering constraints from ancestral constraints.
- Empirical results show large efficiency gains (orders of magnitude) over ILP-based approaches like GOBNILP and scalability to ~20 variables.
- Builds on and extends Chen et al.'s BN graph/EC tree work to handle ancestral constraints important for domain knowledge.
- Situates well relative to prior constraint-based and ILP work (e.g., Borboudakis & Tsamardinos; Cussens; Bartlett & Cussens).
- Concern: Comparison to GOBNILP uses a MAXSAT encoding that appears suboptimal for ILP solvers and may unfairly disadvantage GOBNILP.
- Suggested better ILP formulation (per Cussens 2010): introduce ancestor indicator variables A(X,Y) and constraints linking them to parent variables for a tighter LP relaxation.
- Omission: no comparison to van Beek & Hoffmann’s constraint programming approach, which has outperformed GOBNILP in prior work.
- Clarity issues: proof of Lemma 3 in the supplement lacks justification for a key edge-existence claim.
- Terminology needs clarification: distinction between "entailed" and "inferred" constraints (what is necessarily entailed vs. optionally added consistent orderings).
- Requested minor revisions: clarify Lemma 3 proof, clarify entailed vs inferred, compare to proper ILP formulation and van Beek & Hoffmann CP method, and detail the MAXSAT encoding for ordering constraints.
- Overall assessment: technically strong, significant practical contribution, recommend acceptance pending the above minor revisions.
