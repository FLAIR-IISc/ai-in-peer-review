The authors present a method that leverages a given set of ancestral constraints to inform the admissible heuristic for an exhaustive search algorithm. I found the experimental setup somewhat confusing. Table 1 compares EC combined with the proposed method to GOB combined with a modified version of the proposed method (new work'). If my understanding is correct, Table 3 then proceeds to compare EC and GOB directly – though it’s possible I’ve misinterpreted this. It would seem more informative to include an explicit comparison between EC augmented with the proposed method and EC alone. While the comparison of GOB augmented with the modified method versus GOB is interesting, EC consistently outperforms GOB to such an extent that even substantial improvements to GOB via the proposed work may not alter the overall preference for EC combined with the proposed method.
