The authors propose a method that utilizes ancestral constraints to guide the heuristic for an exhaustive search algorithm. I found the experimental design somewhat unclear. The comparison in Table 1 between the combination of EC with the new approach and GOB with a modified version of the new approach (new work') is presented alongside existing methods. If interpreted correctly, Table 3 then evaluates EC against GOB. However, it appears more logical to directly compare the performance of EC with and without the new method. Although the comparison between GOB with and without the new approach is interesting, given the significant superiority of EC over GOB, even substantial improvements in GOB with the new method would likely not surpass the performance of EC combined with the new method.
