- Tackles optimal Bayesian network structure learning by extending Chen et al. (2015, 2016) to handle non-decomposable constraints.  
- Emphasizes ancestral constraints and incorporates them into the learning process.  
- Reformulates the task as a MaxSAT problem with carefully designed constraint encodings.  
- Empirical evaluation against ILP-based methods shows substantial runtime gains, often by orders of magnitude.  
- Reviewer has limited MaxSAT expertise but finds the comparisons sound and performance improvements striking.  
- Overall presents a clear, effective approach to include challenging constraints while achieving significant computational benefits over existing alternatives.
