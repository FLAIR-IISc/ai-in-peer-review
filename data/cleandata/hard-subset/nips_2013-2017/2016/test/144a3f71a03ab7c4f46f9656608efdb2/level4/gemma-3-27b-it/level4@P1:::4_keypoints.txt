- Novelty: method to project non-decomposable ancestral constraints into decomposable constraints, with the main contribution in Section 5.  
- Presentation: paper is well-written and clear both theoretically and experimentally.  
- Generalization concern: Table 3 shows improvements for EC trees but not ILP, questioning whether projection benefits generalize beyond EC trees and suggesting evaluation on learners that cannot natively handle non-decomposable constraints.  
- Easy-hard-easy pattern: Table 2 shows an easy-hard-easy behavior in EC tree performance as constraint proportion increases—request explanation and whether performance is sensitive to constraint amount and requires tuning.  
- Missing comparison: Table 2 gives SHD only for EC trees—ask for structural Hamming distance comparison between EC tree and ILP.  
- Minor suggestion: consider omitting Section 4.1 and reallocating space to better explain EC trees and the A* algorithm within the EC tree setting.
