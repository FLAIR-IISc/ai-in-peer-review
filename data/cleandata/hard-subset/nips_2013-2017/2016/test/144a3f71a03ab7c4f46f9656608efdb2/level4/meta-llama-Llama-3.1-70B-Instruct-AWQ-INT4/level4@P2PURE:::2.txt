The authors present a method that leverages a given set of ancestral constraints to inform the admissible heuristic for an exhaustive search algorithm. I found the experimental setup somewhat perplexing. The existing approaches of EC and GOB are combined with the new method in Table 1, comparing (EC + new work) to (GOB + new work'). If I understand this correctly, Table 3 then compares the standalone performance of EC and GOB. It would be more informative to include an explicit comparison between (EC + new work) and EC, as the relative improvement of EC with the new method is not immediately clear. Although the comparison between (GOB + new work') and GOB is interesting, given that EC appears to outperform GOB significantly, it is likely that (EC + new work) would still be the preferred choice even if GOB benefited substantially more from the new method.
