The authors have presented a method that uses a given set of predecessor constraints to inform a heuristic (acceptably) for a complete search algorithm. I found the tests to be somewhat confusing. Table 1 (EC + New Job) is compared with Table 2 (GOB + New Job) in the context of the existing EC and GOB methods. If I understand correctly, Table 3 is comparing EC to GOB (or am I misunderstanding?). It seems that a clear comparison between (EC + New Job) and EC would be meaningful – although (GOB + New Job) vs. GOB is interesting, it seems that EC is much better than GOB, even though GOB benefits much more from the new job than EC, we would still prefer to go with (EC + New Job).
