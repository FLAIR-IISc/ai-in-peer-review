- Addresses optimal Bayesian network structure learning.  
- Incorporates non-decomposable constraints, especially ancestral constraints, into the learning process.  
- Builds on the structure learning approach of Chen et al. (2015, 2016).  
- Reformulates the problem as a MaxSAT encoding with specific constraints.  
- Empirical evaluation compares the method to ILP-based alternatives.  
- Reports orders-of-magnitude runtime improvements over ILP methods.  
- Reviewer is not fully familiar with the approach but finds the runtime results particularly impressive.
