- Paper proposes using ancestral constraints to inform an admissible heuristic for exhaustive search.  
- Experimental comparisons are unclear and possibly inconsistent across tables.  
- Table 1 seems to compare (EC + new work) vs (GOB + new work'), while Table 3 appears to compare EC vs GOB (or is misinterpreted).  
- The authors should include an explicit comparison between (EC + new work) and EC.  
- EC is significantly superior to GOB, so improvements over GOB are less informative.  
- Even if GOB shows larger relative gains from the new method, (EC + new work) would likely outperform due to EC’s stronger baseline.
