- Problem: learning an optimal Bayesian network structure.  
- Main contribution: extends Chen et al.'s structure-learning framework to support non-decomposable constraints, focusing on ancestral constraints.  
- Method: reformulates the problem as a Maximum Satisfiability (MaxSAT) instance with a specialized constraint encoding.  
- Empirical result: compared to ILP-based methods, the approach achieves runtime improvements of several orders of magnitude.  
- Reviewer note: limited familiarity with the methodology but finds the runtime comparisons highly compelling.
