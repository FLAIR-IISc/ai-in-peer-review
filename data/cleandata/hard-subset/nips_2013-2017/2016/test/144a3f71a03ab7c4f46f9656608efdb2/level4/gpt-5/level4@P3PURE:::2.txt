The authors present a method that leverages a given set of ancestral constraints to inform the (admissible) heuristic for an exhaustive search algorithm. I found the experimental section somewhat confusing. Based on my reading, Table 1 compares (EC + new work) to (GOB + new work'). If I understand correctly, Table 3 then compares EC to GOB (if not, please clarify). It would be helpful to include an explicit comparison between (EC + new work) and EC alone. Although the (GOB + new work') vs. GOB comparison is interesting, EC appears to be substantially stronger than GOB; thus, even if GOB benefited more than EC from the new component, the preferable choice would likely still be (EC + new work).
