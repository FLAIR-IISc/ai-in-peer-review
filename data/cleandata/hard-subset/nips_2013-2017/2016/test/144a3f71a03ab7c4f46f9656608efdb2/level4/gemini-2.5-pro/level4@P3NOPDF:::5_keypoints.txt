- Addresses learning optimal Bayesian network structures under complex, non-decomposable constraints, with emphasis on ancestral constraints.
- Extends the Chen et al. [2015, 2016] structure learning framework to incorporate such constraints.
- Core contribution: formulates score-based structure learning as a MaxSAT problem.
- MaxSAT formulation efficiently encodes acyclicity, parent-set, and complex non-decomposable constraints.
- Strong empirical evaluation showing the MaxSAT-based solver outperforms state-of-the-art ILP methods by several orders of magnitude.
- Practical impact: makes exact structure learning feasible for instances previously intractable with ILP.
- Novel and elegant technical reframing; empirical evidence compelling even to a reviewer not specialized in satisfiability solving.
- Overall assessment: well-motivated, technically sound, experimentally strong, and a valuable contribution to graphical model learning.
