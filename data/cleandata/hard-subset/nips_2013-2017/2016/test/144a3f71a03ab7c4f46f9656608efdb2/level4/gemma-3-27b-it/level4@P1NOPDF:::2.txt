The authors present a method that leverages ancestral constraints to inform the admissible heuristic for an exhaustive search algorithm. I found the experimental setup somewhat confusing. Specifically, Table 1 compares EC combined with the proposed method to GOB combined with a modified version of the proposed method (new work'). If I understand correctly, Table 3 then compares the performance of EC and GOB directly (though I may be misinterpreting this). It would seem more informative to include an explicit comparison between EC with the proposed method and EC alone. While the comparison of GOB with the modified method (GOB + new work') versus GOB is interesting, EC already outperforms GOB to such a degree that even substantial gains from the proposed method on GOB wouldn’t necessarily make it the preferable approach; (EC + new work) would likely still be favored.
