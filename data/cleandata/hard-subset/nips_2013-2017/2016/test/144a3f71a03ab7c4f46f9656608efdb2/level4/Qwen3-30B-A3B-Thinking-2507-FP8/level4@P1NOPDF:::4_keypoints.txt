- Proposes projecting non-decomposable ancestral constraints into decomposable edge and topological-ordering constraints (novelty in Section 5).  
- Paper is well-written with clear theoretical exposition and experimental validation.  
- Table 3: projection improves EC tree performance but not ILP, suggesting limited generalization and reduced practical impact.  
- Need to show the projection improves both performance and efficiency for methods originally unable to handle ancestral constraints.  
- Table 2: “easy–hard–easy” pattern for EC trees as constraint proportion increases needs explanation; implies EC-tree sensitivity to constraint quantity and need for careful tuning.  
- Table 2 reports SHD for EC trees but omits SHD for ILP under same settings, preventing direct comparison of relative gains.  
- Section 4.1 on Bayesian networks is largely redundant given experiments only use EC trees; recommend removing it and expanding discussion of EC trees and the A* algorithm.
