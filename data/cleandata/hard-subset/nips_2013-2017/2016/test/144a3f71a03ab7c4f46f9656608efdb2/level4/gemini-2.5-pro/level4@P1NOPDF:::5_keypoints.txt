- Addresses learning optimal Bayesian network structure subject to constraints.
- Main contribution: a novel method to incorporate non-decomposable constraints, especially ancestral constraints, into Chen et al.'s framework.
- Reformulates the constrained structure learning problem as a MaxSAT instance with specialized constraints.
- Provides empirical comparisons against established ILP-based methods.
- Shows impressive runtime reductions of several orders of magnitude relative to ILP baselines.
- Reviewer notes limited personal expertise with MaxSAT but views the reported performance gains as significant.
