This paper addresses the problem of finding an optimal Bayesian network configuration. Specifically, the authors demonstrate how to incorporate non- separable constraints (and particularly ancestor constraints) into the learning method of Chen et al. [2015, 2016]. The empirical evaluation of the proposed method shows a significant improvement over ILP-based methods. The authors re-state the problem as a MaxSAT problem with specific constraints. Although my expertise on this method is limited, the results (runtime comparisons) seem quite impressive. In particular, the authors show a significant runtime reduction compared to alternative methods.
