The paper proposes a technique that uses a set of ancestral constraints to refine an admissible heuristic within a complete search procedure. I found the experimental section somewhat unclear. With EC and GOB as the established baselines, Table 1 appears to compare (EC + the proposed method) against (GOB + the corresponding enhancements). If I’m interpreting this correctly, Table 3 then compares EC to GOB alone (or did I misunderstand?). It would be helpful to include a direct ablation contrasting (EC + the proposed method) versus EC by itself—while the (GOB + enhancements) versus GOB comparison is informative, EC seems sufficiently stronger than GOB that, even if GOB gains more from the new components, the preferred choice would likely remain (EC + the proposed method).
