The paper presents a method that leverages a specified set of ancestral constraints to define an admissible heuristic for an exhaustive search algorithm. I found the experimental section somewhat confusing. Given the existing approaches EC and GOB, Table 1 seems to compare (EC + proposed method) against (GOB + “new work’”). If I’m interpreting correctly, Table 3 then compares EC directly to GOB (or did I misunderstand?). It would be helpful to include an explicit ablation comparing (EC + proposed method) to EC alone. Although the (GOB + “new work’”) vs. GOB comparison is interesting, EC appears to outperform GOB by such a wide margin that, even if GOB benefited considerably more than EC from the proposed method, the preferable choice would still be (EC + proposed method).
