- Proposes a novel framework for learning Bayesian networks with non-decomposable ancestral constraints using an EC tree search space.  
- Introduces a method to project non-decomposable ancestral constraints onto decomposable edge and ordering constraints to empower an oracle during search.  
- Paper is well-written, clearly organized, and provides accessible explanations of technical concepts.  
- Thorough and well-positioned literature review situates the contribution within existing work.  
- Theoretical treatment is rigorous and the EC tree concept is well-founded.  
- Projection method for constraints is well-motivated and clearly explained.  
- Experimental evaluation is thorough, well-described, and demonstrates orders-of-magnitude speedups over a state-of-the-art ILP-based system in most scenarios.  
- Significant contribution to Bayesian network structure learning and handling of non-decomposable constraints.  
- Assumes reader familiarity with Bayesian networks and structure learning, which may limit accessibility for non-experts.  
- Experiments could be strengthened by including more scenarios and comparisons to additional state-of-the-art methods.  
- Overall quality, clarity, originality, and significance rated highly (9/10 each).  
- Recommendation: Accept.
