**Summary of the Main Ideas**

This paper proposes a novel approach for learning Bayesian networks with ancestral constraints. The authors introduce a framework that leverages a recently proposed search space, called the EC tree, and empowers an oracle that optimizes decomposable scores. They show how to incorporate non-decomposable ancestral constraints into the structure learning approach by pruning the EC tree and inferring decomposable constraints from ancestral constraints. The proposed approach is evaluated empirically against alternative frameworks, such as integer linear programming (ILP), and demonstrates significant improvements in efficiency.
