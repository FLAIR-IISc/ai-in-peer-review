- Proposes a novel method for optimal Bayesian network learning under ancestral constraints using a framework for non-decomposable scores.  
- Leverages decomposable-score oracles by inferring decomposable constraints from ancestral (non-decomposable) constraints.  
- Introduces a sound and complete pruning method for the EC tree search space based on ancestral constraints.

- Provides empirical evaluation showing efficiency and effectiveness in learning Bayesian networks with ancestral constraints.

- Weakness: lacks a clear, direct comparison against the existing EC tree method (a primary competitor).
