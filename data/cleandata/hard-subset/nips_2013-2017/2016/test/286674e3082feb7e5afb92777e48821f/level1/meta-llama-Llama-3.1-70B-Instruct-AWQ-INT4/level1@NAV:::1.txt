This paper proposes a novel framework for graph clustering without assuming the data comes from a specific model. The authors provide theoretical guarantees for the results of model-based clustering algorithms, using a model-free approach. The main idea is to define a framework that ties in with existing model-based frameworks, allowing the exploitation of results in model-based recovery and strengthening existing results.

The paper is well-written, and the authors provide a clear and concise introduction to the problem, background, and main theorem. The technical contributions are significant, and the authors obtain several different and model-specific stability results that bound the perturbation of a clustering by the perturbation of a model.
