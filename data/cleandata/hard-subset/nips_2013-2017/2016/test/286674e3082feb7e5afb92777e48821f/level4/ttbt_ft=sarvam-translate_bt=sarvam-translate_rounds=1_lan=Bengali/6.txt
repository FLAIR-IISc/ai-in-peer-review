In the paper, the authors attempt to create a theory that works for graph clustering in a model-free framework. However, the result is simplistic and unreliable. The authors pretend to create a new theory, but their theory is merely a simple hand wave. To give a few examples: Theorem 1 (General Theorem) ... "that also fits well with G is close to C"... Proposition 3 (Informal) ... "under standard recovery conditions"...
