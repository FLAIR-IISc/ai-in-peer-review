1) Paper addresses an important goal: a model-free template giving clustering stability guarantees in terms of data-computable quantities.

2) Unclear modeling choices: why treat PFM and SBM differently when PFM contains DC-SBM/SBM; why use Laplacian for PFM and adjacency for SBM; why weighted ME vs ME; no DC-SBM results.

3) Clarify notation in Proposition 10: definition of ⟨M', M⟩_F is missing.

4) Assumption 5 ambiguity: is the quantity ||L̂^2 − L^2||_F or ||L̂ − L||_F^2, and what is the intuition for squaring Laplacians?

5) Scope question: do the techniques extend beyond disjoint block models to other spectral-clustering settings (e.g., hierarchical clustering)?

6) Evaluation/estimators assume same K for C and C'; framework cannot handle differing numbers of clusters or splits/merges.

7) Related work omissions: missing model-free results (Kumar & Kannan 2010; Balcan et al. 2012/2013); need to relate this stability notion to prior perturbation/approximation-stability notions.

8) Practicality concerns: PFM simulations tolerate only tiny perturbations; claims about eigengap effects are unclear; SBM guarantee conditions seem hard to satisfy, limiting real-world usefulness.

9) Figure 1 caption is unclear about “For SBM, δ is always greater than δ0” — specify which subplot(s) or clarify if SBM is plotted.

10) Theory/experiment mismatch: PFM theorems give weighted ME guarantees but experiments perturb plain ME — reconcile.

Minor points:
- Figure 1 missing axis labels.
- Line 44: independence statement for A_{ij} (i>j) should be conditional on cluster assignments.
- Notation inconsistency: symbols B, C, Y reused across sections; suggest clearer, consistent notation (e.g., Y^{⊥}, C_D, λ^L, λ^B) and simplify expressions (e.g., substitute YZ).
- Section 4.4 should be placed as a subsection of Related Work.
