- Presents several graph-clustering algorithms and a method to evaluate similarity between clusterings.
- Algorithms are clearly described.
- Motivation and rationale for presenting these methods as a collection are not well articulated.
- Methods appear to be modifications of existing algorithms, with the main novelty being added constraints.
- Authors need to clarify the context, inspiration, and provide stronger justification for multiple algorithms.
- Manuscript lacks a cohesive narrative tying the algorithms together and highlighting their unique contributions.
