

The paper attempts to develop a theory for graph clustering within a model-free framework. However, the theoretical contribution is naive and unconvincing. The authors present a purportedly novel theory that amounts to little more than naive handwaving. As evidenced by specific claims: Theorem 1 (Generic Theorem) states "...which also fits G well is close to C"; Proposition 3 (Informal) asserts "...under standard recovery conditions..." — these formulations lack substantive rigor.
