1) Proposes a generic, data-dependent template for theoretical stability guarantees of clustering solutions, with applications to block models and exact recovery.  
2) Questions why PFM and SBM are treated separately instead of a unified estimator (e.g., spectral clustering), and asks for justification of Laplacian vs adjacency choices and ME vs weighted ME comparisons; DC-SBM results missing.  
3) Proposition 10’s definition of ⟨M′, M⟩_F needs clarification.  
4) Ambiguity in Assumption 5: is the norm ‖ĤL^2 − L^2‖_F or ‖ĤL − L‖_F^2, and why square the Laplacians if the former?  
5) The template’s restriction to disjoint clusters limits applicability; can it extend to non-block or hierarchical clustering settings where spectral methods succeed?  
6) Framework assumes identical cluster counts for C and C′, failing to capture splits/merges when algorithms produce different K (e.g., K vs K+1).  
7) Related Work omits key model-free clustering results (Kumar & Kannan; Balcan et al.); need to relate the paper’s stability notion to perturbation resilience/approximation stability and center-perturbation stability.  
8) Simulations suggest PFM tolerates only tiny perturbations and SBM conditions are hard to satisfy; contradictions around eigengap effects and A^k smoothing require explanation, undermining practical utility.  
9) Figure 1 caption claims δ > δ0 for SBM, yet SBM is not plotted—figure/caption mismatch needs correction.  
10) Inconsistency between theory and experiments: PFM theorems use weighted ME but experiments perturb standard ME.

Minor 3) Notation inconsistencies across PFM vs SBM for B, C, Y; recommend clearer names (e.g., Y_⊥, C_D, λ^L, λ^B) and substitutions to reduce confusion.
