- Paper proposes a model-agnostic template for proving stability of a clustering C (distance to any other good-fitting C'), instantiated for PFM and SBM, with computable guarantees from the observed graph and links to exact recovery.

- Strength: Ambitious, timely goal of model-free, interpretable, data-computable guarantees.

- Strength: Clear separation between a general template and concrete PFM/SBM instantiations.

- Strength: Useful bridge from stability bounds to exact recovery guarantees.

- Concern: Why treat PFM and SBM differently when PFM subsumes DC-SBM and SBM—why different estimators/metrics instead of a unified spectral estimator?

- Concern: Why use Laplacian for PFM but adjacency for SBM?

- Concern: PFM uses weighted misclassification error while SBM uses ME—how do these compare theoretically and empirically, and why no explicit DC-SBM results?

- Concern: Proposition 10 needs explicit definition of ⟨M',M⟩_F as the Frobenius inner product and any symmetry assumptions stated.

- Concern: Assumption 5 unclear—do you mean ∥L̂^2 − L^2∥_F or ∥L̂ − L∥_F^2? If the former, justify squaring Laplacians; if the latter, fix notation.

- Concern: Scope beyond disjoint block models—can the template extend to settings with spectral guarantees (e.g., hierarchical clustering), and what are the fit functionals/distances there?

- Concern: Theory assumes same number of clusters K for C and C'—can template handle K ≠ K' via merge/split, refinements, or information-theoretic distances like VI?

- Concern: Missing discussion of related model-free stability/robustness work (Kumar & Kannan 2010; Balcan et al. 2012/2013); clarify relations and whether conditions are implied or orthogonal.

- Concern: Empirical utility—PFM tolerates only tiny perturbations; explain why large eigengaps can increase sensitivity of the fit and reconcile with experiments (e.g., powering A improves stability).

- Concern: Quantify the perturbation range where guarantees are informative on real data and characterize when SBM guarantee conditions hold (SNR, degree regime, possible relaxations).

- Concern: Figure 1 caption ambiguous about “For SBM, δ is always greater than δ0”; clarify or add corresponding plot.

- Concern: Theory–experiment mismatch—PFM theorems use weighted ME while experiments perturb ME; align metrics or justify interchangeability.

- Clarity/notation issues: reuse of symbols (B, C, Y) across sections is confusing; correct independence statement for A_ij and consider reorganizing Section 4.4 as related work.

- Suggestions: Provide a unified PFM/SBM (and DC-SBM) instantiation with a common spectral estimator/error metric, report eigengaps/noise/fit quantities in experiments, test K misspecification, quantify small-perturbation regime, and expand related-work connections.

- Overall: Promising and broadly applicable template, but differing instantiations, unclear assumptions, limited empirical robustness, and gaps in related work reduce clarity and practical impact; addressing these would substantially improve the paper.
