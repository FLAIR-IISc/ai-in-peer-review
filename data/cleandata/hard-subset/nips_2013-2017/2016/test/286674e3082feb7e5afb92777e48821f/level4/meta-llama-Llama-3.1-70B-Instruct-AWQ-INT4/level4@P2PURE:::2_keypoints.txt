- Establishes relationships between different clustering distance measures for network models (PFM and SBM).  
- Proves a locally convex-like property: two clusterings that estimate model parameters equally well are close in misclassification distance.  
- Formally states and proves equivalence between different clustering distance measures, with theoretical and experimental support.  
- Results provide insight into clustering behavior on network data.  
- Novelty is limited by closely related recent work (Peng, Sun, Zanetti COLT 2015; Chao, Lu, Ma, Zhou arXiv 2015).  
- Theoretical contributions are interesting but are relatively straightforward extensions of known results.  
- Mentions potential applications to detecting clusterability but provides no formal results.  
- Paper is well-written and clearly presents the core ideas.
