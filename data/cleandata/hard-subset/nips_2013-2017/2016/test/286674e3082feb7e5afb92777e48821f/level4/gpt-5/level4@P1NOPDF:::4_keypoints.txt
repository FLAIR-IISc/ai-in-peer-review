1) Provides model-free, data-computable clustering stability guarantees and uses them to derive exact recovery results.

2) Unclear why PFM and SBM are treated differently despite model containment—why different estimators (Laplacian vs adjacency) and weighted vs unweighted ME, and why DC‑SBM is omitted.

3) Proposition 10: the inner-product notation ⟨M', M⟩_F needs a precise definition.

4) Assumption 5 is ambiguous: is it ||L̂^2 − L^2||_F or ||L̂ − L||_F^2, and if the former, what is the intuition for squaring Laplacians?

5) Question whether the template extends beyond block models to other settings where spectral clustering works (e.g., hierarchical clustering).

6) Framework requires C and C' to have the same K, so it cannot capture closeness when cluster counts differ (splits/merges).

7) Related work omits key model-free results (Kumar & Kannan 2010; Balcan et al. 2012/2013); need to relate the paper’s stability notion to perturbation/approximation resilience.

8) Simulations show PFM tolerates extremely small perturbations and claim large eigengap increases sensitivity—this is unexplained and suggests limited practical utility; SBM conditions also hard to satisfy.

9) Figure 1 caption is unclear about which subplot “For SBM, δ is always greater than δ0” refers to; state explicitly if SBM is not plotted.

10) Mismatch between theory and experiment: PFM theorems use weighted ME while experiments perturb unweighted ME—needs reconciliation.

Minor 2) Line 44 should clarify that for block models A_{ij} (i>j) are independent given cluster assignments.

Minor 3) Notation is reused/confusing across sections (B, C, Y); recommend consistent symbols (e.g., Y_⊥, C_D, λ^L, λ^B) and simplifying expressions.

Minor 4) Section 4.4 would be better placed as a subsection of Related Work.
