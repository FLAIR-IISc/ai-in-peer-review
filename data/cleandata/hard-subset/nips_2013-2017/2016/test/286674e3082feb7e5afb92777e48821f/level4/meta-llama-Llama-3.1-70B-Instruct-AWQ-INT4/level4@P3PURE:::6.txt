In the paper, the authors attempt to develop a theory for graph clustering in a model-free framework. However, the result is naive and unconvincing. The authors purport to construct a new theory, but their approach amounts to naive handwaving. To illustrate this point, consider Theorem 1 (Generic Theorem), which relies on the vague statement that a solution "which also fits G well is close to C." Similarly, Proposition 3 (Informal) is predicated on "standard recovery conditions," a phrase that lacks clear definition or explanation.
