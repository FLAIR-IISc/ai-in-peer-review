1. Presents a model-free template for theoretical clustering stability guarantees computable from data and algorithm outputs.  
2. Unclear why PFM and SBM are treated differently (Laplacian vs adjacency, weighted ME vs ME) and why DC-SBM results are omitted despite model containment.  
3. Proposition 10 references <M', M>_F but its definition is not provided.  
4. Assumption 5 is ambiguous: is it ||L_hat^2 - L^2||_F or ||L_hat - L||_F^2, and why square the Laplacian?  
5. Unclear if the template/techniques extend beyond block models to other spectral-clustering-friendly models (e.g., hierarchical clustering).  
6. Framework requires same number of clusters for C and C', so it cannot handle splits/merges where K != K'.  
7. Related work omits several model-free stability results (e.g., Kumar & Kannan 2010; Balcan et al. 2012/2013) and does not relate the paper's stability notion to those.  
8. Simulations indicate the tolerated perturbations are tiny for PFM; large eigengaps and smoothing effects are not well explained and make guarantees practically weak.  
9. Figure 1 caption is ambiguous about which subplot shows "For SBM, δ is always greater than δ0" and whether SBM is plotted.  
10. Theorems guarantee weighted ME for PFM, but experiments perturb using ME, creating a mismatch.
