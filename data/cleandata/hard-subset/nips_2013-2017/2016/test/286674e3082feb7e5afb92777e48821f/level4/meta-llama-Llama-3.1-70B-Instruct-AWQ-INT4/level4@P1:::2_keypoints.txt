- Establishes a relationship between clustering distances for network models (PFM, SBM) and proves a locally convex-like property linking parameter-fit quality to misclassification proximity.  
- Provides theoretical proofs and experimental validation demonstrating that equally good parameter estimates yield clusterings close in misclassification distance.  
- Contributions are novel and useful, especially for understanding cluster behavior in network data.  
- Notes potential application to detecting clusterability, but does not provide formal results for this application.  
- Similar recent work exists (Peng, Sun, Zanetti COLT 2015; Chao, Lu, Ma, Zhou arXiv 2015), so novelty is not entirely isolated.  
- The theoretical developments are relatively straightforward extensions of known results.  
- Paper is well-written and explains core ideas clearly.  
- Lacks sufficient contextual background on the network models and clustering algorithms used.  
- Experimental section needs more detail about datasets and specific clustering algorithms employed.  
- Overall recommendation: accept with minor revisions; rating 7/10.
