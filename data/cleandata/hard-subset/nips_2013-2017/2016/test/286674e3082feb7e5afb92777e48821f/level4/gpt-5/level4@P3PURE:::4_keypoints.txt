- Summary: Proposes a generic, data-computable template for clustering stability and instantiates it for PFM and SBM, but empirical results and theorem conditions suggest limited practical utility.

- Scope/importance: Targets model-free, data-driven theoretical guarantees for clustering stability, which is both theoretically and practically significant.

- Modeling choices/inconsistency: Unclear why PFM and SBM are treated differently despite nesting (PFM ⊃ DC-SBM ⊃ SBM), why Laplacian is used for PFM but adjacency for SBM, and why DC-SBM lacks explicit results.

- Error metrics mismatch: Uses weighted misclassification error for PFM and standard ME for SBM without clarifying their relationship or practical comparability.

- Proposition 10 ambiguity: The inner product ⟨M', M⟩_F is not precisely defined; needs explicit statement (Frobenius inner product) and explanation of its use in the proof.

- Assumption 5 ambiguity: Unclear if the assumption involves ||L̂^2 − L^2||_F or ||L̂ − L||_F^2; requires intuition for squaring Laplacians and justification for that choice.

- Generalizability beyond disjoint blocks: Unclear whether the template extends to other spectral-clustering settings (e.g., hierarchical clustering) and how it relates to existing results like Balakrishnan et al. (2011).

- Fixed number of clusters limitation: Framework assumes C and C' have the same K and cannot compare clusterings with different numbers of clusters (e.g., a split cluster), limiting practical applicability.

- Related work omissions: Fails to cite/compare to several model-free stability frameworks (Kumar & Kannan 2010; Balcan et al. 2012, 2013) and needs to clarify relations to perturbation/approximation stability notions.

- Empirical concerns and interpretability: PFM simulations tolerate extremely small perturbations; contradictory statements about eigengap and smoothing effects; SBM guarantee conditions are often hard to satisfy, raising concerns about real-world relevance.

- Figure 1 caption unclear: Statement “For SBM, δ is always greater than δ0” needs to identify the subplot or explicitly state if SBM is not plotted.

- Metric mismatch between theory and experiments: Theory uses weighted ME for PFM but experiments perturb standard ME—requires alignment or justification and discussion of implications.

- Minor: Figure 1 lacks axis labels.

- Minor: Clarify statement on independence of A_{ij} for i>j given cluster assignments (Line 44).

- Minor: Notation inconsistency across sections (B, C, Y used differently); recommend consistent symbols and distinct eigenvalue notation (e.g., λ^L, λ^B).

- Minor: Section 4.4 reads like related work and should be moved into the Related Work section.

- Overall assessment: Promising and computable template with exact-recovery applications, but conceptual/empirical issues (model treatment, estimator choices, missing DC-SBM, metric inconsistencies, fixed-K limitation, and impractical conditions) reduce clarity and impact; addressing these would substantially strengthen the paper.
