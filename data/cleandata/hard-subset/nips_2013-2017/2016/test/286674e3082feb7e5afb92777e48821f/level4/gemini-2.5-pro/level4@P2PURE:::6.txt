The paper attempts to develop a theory for graph clustering in a model-free framework. However, the resulting theory and its claims are unconvincing and lack rigor. The authors present what they claim is a new theory, but it amounts to little more than informal arguments and hand-waving.

This lack of formalism is evident in the paper's key theoretical statements, which rely on vague, undefined terminology. For example:
*   Theorem 1 (Generic Theorem) uses the imprecise notion that a clustering "...which also fits G well is close to C..." without formally defining what it means to "fit G well".
*   Proposition 3 (Informal) is established "...under standard recovery conditions..." without specifying what these conditions are.
