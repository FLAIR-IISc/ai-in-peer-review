- Presents algorithms for graph clustering and a method to test similarity between cluster assignments.
- Algorithms are clearly described, but the motivation for their development and for presenting them as a collection is weak.
- Many algorithms are modifications of existing methods; primary novelty appears to be the constraints added at the end.
- Manuscript needs a clearer statement of the problem, limitations of existing approaches, and how the proposed methods address them.
- Presentation as a list is disjoint; the algorithms should be organized around a cohesive narrative or central concept.
- Technical content seems sound and modifications are well described.
- The constraints (main claimed novelty) are insufficiently explored and their significance needs stronger justification.
- With clearer motivation and a more cohesive presentation, the work could have greater impact.
- Reviewer rating: 6; Confidence: 4.
