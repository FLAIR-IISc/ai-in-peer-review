- Primary contribution: proposes a model-agnostic template for clustering stability with empirically computable quantities, aiming to give theoretical guarantees and exact-recovery results.

- Inconsistent treatment of models: PFM and SBM are handled differently despite SBM being a special case of PFM, which is confusing.

- Choice of estimator inconsistency: authors use the graph Laplacian for PFM but the adjacency matrix for SBM; a unified spectral approach is not justified.

- Error metric inconsistency: theoretical results use weighted ME for PFM and standard ME for SBM without clarifying their relationship or justification.

- Missing generality: results for the intermediate DC-SBM are not provided despite its relevance.

- Practical utility concern: simulation conditions appear too restrictive, suggesting the theorems may not be useful in practice.

- PFM sensitivity: simulations show PFM tolerates only very small perturbations and the paper does not explain why a large eigengap increases fragility.

- Contradictory empirical finding: powering the adjacency matrix in the political blogs data seems to improve stability, contradicting the claimed sensitivity to eigengaps and needing explanation.

- SBM conditions impractical: authors themselves note SBM guarantee conditions are very hard to satisfy.

- Limitation to fixed k: framework compares only clusterings with the same number of clusters, failing to capture splits/merges or algorithms that produce different k.

- Limited model scope: focus is restricted to block models; extension to other spectral-friendly structures (e.g., hierarchical clustering) is not discussed.

- Weak engagement with prior stability literature: paper insufficiently compares its stability notion to existing model-free stability/perturbation frameworks (e.g., Kumar & Kannan 2010; Balcan et al. 2012/2013).

- Notation/assumption ambiguities: Proposition 10 omits definition of <M',M>_F; Assumption 5’s norm expression is unclear (||L̂^2 - L^2||_F vs ||L̂ - L||_F^2) and needs intuition.

- Experiment/figure issues: mismatch between PFM theory (weighted ME) and experiments (standard ME); Figure 1’s caption about δ vs δ0 is unclear; some axes in Figure 1 are unlabeled.

- Minor technical/clarity points: clarify conditional independence of A_ij for i>j in block model description; harmonize notation across PFM and SBM (B, C, Y, λ) to avoid confusion; integrate Section 4.4 into Related Work and simplify notation in Proposition 8.
