- Paper proposes a generic template giving theoretical stability guarantees for a clustering C based on how well it fits the data and distance to other clusterings C'.

- Template is instantiated for PFM and SBM, with all quantities claimed to be computable from data, and used to derive exact recovery guarantees.

- Simulations indicate the goodness-of-fit measure or theorem conditions may make the results practically unhelpful (perturbations tolerated are very small).

- Unclear why PFM and SBM are treated differently given PFM includes DC-SBM and SBM: why different estimators (Laplacian for PFM, adjacency for SBM) and different error metrics (weighted ME vs ME)?

- DC-SBM is a special case of PFM but results for DC-SBM are not provided; comparison across models is missing.

- Proposition 10 uses inner product notation <M', M>_F but its definition is not given.

- Assumption 5 is ambiguous: it is unclear whether the intended norm is ||Ĺ^2 - L^2||_F or ||Ĺ - L||_F^2, and intuition for squaring Laplacians is not provided.

- Paper focuses on disjoint block models; it does not clarify whether the template extends to other settings where spectral clustering works (e.g., hierarchical clustering).

- Evaluation restricts C and C' to the same number of clusters, failing to capture close clusterings that differ by splits/merges (different K vs K').

- Related work omits several model-free clustering stability results (e.g., Kumar & Kannan 2010; Balcan et al. 2012/2013) and does not relate the paper’s stability notion to perturbation/approximation stability notions.

- Simulations for PFM tolerate only tiny perturbations; statements about large eigengaps making PFM sensitive are unqualified and contradictory with empirical smoothing on political blog data.

- SBM guarantee conditions appear very hard to satisfy in practice, per the authors’ own remarks and experiments.

- Figure 1 caption claims "For SBM, δ is always greater than δ0" but it’s unclear which subplot this refers to and whether SBM is actually plotted.

- Experimental/figure issues: axis labels missing in Figure 1.

- Notation inconsistencies and overload (B, C, Y used differently across sections) make the paper hard to follow; suggestions include clearer symbol choices (e.g., Y⊥, CD, λ^L, λ^B).

- Suggestion that Section 4.4 belongs in related work rather than where it is placed.

- Minor clarity/edit corrections: line 44 statement about independence of A_ij for i>j should be rephrased; YZ usage in SBM estimator could be simplified in Proposition 8.
