The paper aims to develop a model-free theoretical framework for graph clustering, but the results are naive and unconvincing. Although the authors claim to introduce a new theory, the development is largely hand-waving and lacks rigor. For example, Theorem 1 (Generic Theorem) relies on the vague assertion that a solution “which also fits G well is close to C,” and Proposition 3 (Informal) appeals to unspecified “standard recovery conditions.” These informal formulations make the contribution unpersuasive.
