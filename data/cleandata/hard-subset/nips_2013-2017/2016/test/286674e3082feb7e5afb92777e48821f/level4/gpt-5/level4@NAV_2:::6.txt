The paper attempts to develop a theory for graph clustering in a model-free setting; however, the results come across as overly simplistic and unconvincing. The authors aim to provide a framework that works without model assumptions for graph clustering, yet the contribution reads as simplistic and not persuasive. The authors claim to be constructing a new theory, but much of the development feels largely hand-waving. Just to give a couple of examples: Theorem 1 (Generic Theorem) ... "which also fits G well is close to C" ... Proposition 3 (Informal) ... "under standard recovery conditions" ...
