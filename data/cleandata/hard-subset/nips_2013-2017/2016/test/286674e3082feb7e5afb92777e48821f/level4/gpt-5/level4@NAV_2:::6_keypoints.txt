- Paper aims to develop a model-free theory for graph clustering but the results are overly simplistic and unconvincing.
- Claimed framework that works without model assumptions lacks substantive contribution and is not persuasive.
- Authors assert they construct a new theory, but much of the development reads as hand-waving and informal.
- Theorem 1 is vague (e.g., “which also fits G well is close to C”) and lacks precise, rigorous statement.
- Proposition 3 is informal and depends on unspecified “standard recovery conditions” without clear justification.
