- Presents algorithms for graph clustering and for evaluating similarity between two cluster assignments.
- Individual algorithms are clearly described.
- Overarching motivation for the work is not well explained.
- Paper fails to justify why these methods are presented together, making it read like a collection of techniques rather than a cohesive contribution.
- Algorithms appear to be modifications of existing methods rather than wholly new approaches.
- Primary novelty is limited to the set of constraints introduced in the final section.
