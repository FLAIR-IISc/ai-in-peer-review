- Paper proves a locally convex-like property: clusterings that fit network models (PFM, SBM) equally well are close under misclassification-based distances.  
- Core theoretical result shows equivalence between different clustering distance measures for network data.  
- Authors provide rigorous proofs and clear mathematical exposition of the main claims.  
- Experimental validation supports the theoretical findings.  
- Results are novel and constitute a useful contribution to the study of network clusterings.  
- Theoretical developments build naturally on established results, representing an accessible extension of prior work.  
- Related literature (e.g., Peng, Sun, and Zanetti COLT 2015; Chao et al. arXiv 2015) should be acknowledged to place the contribution in context.  
- The paper briefly mentions detecting clusterability as an application but lacks formal results or detailed exploration of this direction.  
- Overall the paper is well-written and effectively communicates its central ideas.
