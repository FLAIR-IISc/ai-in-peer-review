- Major: The paper proposes a model-free template for proving clustering stability and instantiates it for PFM and SBM, with computable data-driven bounds and exact recovery guarantees.

- Major: Practical utility is limited — PFM guarantees hold only for tiny perturbations and SBM conditions are hard to satisfy, questioning robustness and applicability.

- Major: Unclear rationale for treating PFM and SBM differently despite model hierarchy (PFM ⊃ DC-SBM ⊃ SBM); estimator choices (Laplacian vs adjacency) need justification.

- Major: Inconsistent metrics — PFM results use weighted misclassification error while SBM uses standard ME; comparability and relation between these metrics are not clarified.

- Major: Framework scope is limited — it only compares clusterings with the same number of clusters and cannot handle splits/merges or extensions beyond disjoint block models (e.g., hierarchical clustering).

- Major: Related-work coverage is incomplete; should compare and contrast with other model-free stability notions (e.g., Kumar & Kannan 2010; Balcan et al. 2012, 2013).

- Minor: Proposition 10 omits the definition of the Frobenius inner product <M', M>_F.

- Minor: Assumption 5 is ambiguous about whether the term is ||L_hat^2 - L^2||_F or ||L_hat - L||_F^2, and the intuition for squaring Laplacians is missing.

- Minor: Political blog experiment appears to contradict sensitivity claims — raising adjacency to a power (increasing eigengaps) seems to improve stability and needs explanation.

- Minor: Figure 1 caption mentions "For SBM, delta is always greater than delta0" but it's unclear which subplot this refers to; caption should clarify if SBM plots are omitted.

- Minor: Theory/experiment mismatch for PFM — theorems guarantee weighted ME but experiments report standard ME.

- Minor: Axes in Figure 1 are missing labels.

- Minor: Line 44 incorrectly states conditional independence of A_ij for i > j given cluster assignments; should state dependence on both nodes' cluster labels.

- Minor: Notation is reused inconsistently across PFM and SBM (e.g., B, C, Y, lambda); distinct notation for different models would improve readability.

- Minor: Expression involving YZ in Proposition 8 could be expanded rather than introducing extra notation.

- Minor: Section 4.4 fits better within the related work section rather than as a standalone section.
