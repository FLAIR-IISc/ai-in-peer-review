This paper proposes a theory for graph clustering in a model-free framework. However, the results are naive and unconvincing. Although the authors present this as a new theory, much of it reads as hand-waving rather than a rigorous, well-specified framework.

For example:
- Theorem 1 (Generic Theorem) includes the assertion “which also fits G well is close to C,” but the key notions involved are left vague and undefined.
- Proposition 3 (Informal) appeals to “under standard recovery conditions” without specifying what those conditions are.

Such imprecise and informal statements make the main claims difficult to evaluate and leave the theoretical contribution unsubstantiated.
