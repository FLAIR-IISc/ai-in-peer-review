This manuscript presents a set of algorithms for graph clustering and for evaluating the similarity between two cluster assignments. While the individual algorithms are clearly described, their overarching motivation is not well-explained. The manuscript does not sufficiently justify why these particular methods are presented together, making the paper read like a collection of techniques rather than a cohesive contribution. Furthermore, the algorithms appear to be modifications of existing methods, with the primary novelty confined to the set of constraints introduced in the final section.
