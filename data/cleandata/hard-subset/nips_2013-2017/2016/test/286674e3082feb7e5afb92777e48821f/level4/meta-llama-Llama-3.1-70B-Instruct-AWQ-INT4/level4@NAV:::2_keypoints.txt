- Connects various clustering distances for network models, focusing on PFM and SBM.  
- Shows a locally convex–like property: clusterings with similar model fit are close in misclassification distance.  
- Derives an equivalence between distinct clustering distance measures for network data.  
- Supports theoretical claims with experimental evidence.  
- Results are innovative but resemble recent work (Peng et al. COLT 2015; Chao et al. arXiv 2015).  
- Theoretical development builds on established concepts in a straightforward way.  
- Mentions potential applications to detecting clusterability but provides no formal results.  
- Paper is well-structured and clearly communicates core ideas.
