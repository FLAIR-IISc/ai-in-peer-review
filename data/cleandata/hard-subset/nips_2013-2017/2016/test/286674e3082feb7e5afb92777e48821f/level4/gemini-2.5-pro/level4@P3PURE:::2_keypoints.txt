- Establishes relationship between distance metrics for clusterings on network data, focusing on PFM and SBM.  
- Proves a locally convex-like property: clusterings that explain data equally well must be close in misclassification distance.  
- Shows equivalence between model fit and clustering similarity — theoretically and experimentally validated.  
- Paper is well-written and clearly explains core ideas.  
- Results are novel but follow a recent line of related work (e.g., Peng et al. COLT 2015; Chao et al. 2015).  
- Theoretical analysis appears to be a relatively straightforward extension of known results.  
- Suggests applications for detecting clusterability but provides no formal results in that direction, weakening impact.
