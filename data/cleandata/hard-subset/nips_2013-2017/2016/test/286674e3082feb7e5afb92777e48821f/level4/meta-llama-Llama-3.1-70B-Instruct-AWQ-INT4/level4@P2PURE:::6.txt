The authors attempt to develop a theory for graph clustering in a model-free framework, but the result is unconvincing and overly simplistic. While they claim to construct a new theory, it appears to be based on naive handwaving rather than rigorous mathematical foundations. For instance, Theorem 1 (Generic Theorem) lacks precision, as it vaguely states that a solution "which also fits G well is close to C." Furthermore, Proposition 3 (Informal) relies on unspecified "standard recovery conditions," which undermines the validity of the claim.
