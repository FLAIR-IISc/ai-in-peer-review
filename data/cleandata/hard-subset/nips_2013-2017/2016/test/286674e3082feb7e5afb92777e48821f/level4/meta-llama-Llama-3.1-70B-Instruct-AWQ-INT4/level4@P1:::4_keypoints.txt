1) Proposes a model-free template for clustering stability guarantees with quantities computable from data and algorithm outputs.

2) Unclear why PFM and SBM are treated differently despite model inclusion relations; inconsistent use of Laplacian vs adjacency, weighted ME vs ME, and omission of DC-SBM results.

3) Need definition/clarification of the inner product <M', M>_F used in Proposition 10.

4) Ambiguity in Assumption 5: is the norm ||L̂^2 - L^2||_F or ||L̂ - L||_F^2, and what is the intuition for squaring Laplacians?

5) Question whether the template/techniques extend beyond block models to other settings where spectral clustering works (e.g., hierarchical clustering).

6) Framework requires C and C' to have the same number of clusters, so it cannot capture close clusterings that differ by splits/merges (different K).

7) Related work section omits several model-free stability results (Kumar & Kannan 2010; Balcan et al. 2012/2013); request comparison between stability notions.

8) Simulations show only tiny perturbations tolerated for PFM; unclear/exaggerated claims about eigengap effects and inconsistent behavior on political blog data; SBM conditions appear hard to satisfy.

9) Figure 1 caption is unclear about which subplot shows "For SBM, δ is always greater than δ0" and whether SBM is plotted.

10) Theorems give guarantees for weighted ME in PFM but experiments use ME — a mismatch between theory and evaluation.
