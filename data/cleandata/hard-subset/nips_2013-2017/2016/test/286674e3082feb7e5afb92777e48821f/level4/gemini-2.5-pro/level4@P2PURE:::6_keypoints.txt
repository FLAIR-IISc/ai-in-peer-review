- Paper aims to develop a model-free theory for graph clustering.
- Resulting theory and claims are unconvincing and lack rigor.
- Claimed "new theory" largely consists of informal arguments and hand-waving.
- Key theoretical statements rely on vague, undefined terminology.
- Theorem 1 uses the imprecise notion that a clustering "fits G well" without defining it.
- Proposition 3 invokes "standard recovery conditions" but does not specify them.
