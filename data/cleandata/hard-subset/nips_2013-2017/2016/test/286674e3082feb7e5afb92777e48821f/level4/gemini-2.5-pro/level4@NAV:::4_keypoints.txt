1) Presents a valuable model-agnostic framework for clustering stability with data-computable bounds.  
2) Unclear why PFM and SBM are treated separately; justify Laplacian vs adjacency choices, weighted ME vs standard ME, and absence of DC-SBM results.  
3) Proposition 10 should explicitly define the Frobenius inner product <M', M>_F.  
4) Clarify Assumption 5: is it ||L̂^2 - L^2||_F or ||L̂ - L||_F^2, and why consider squared Laplacians if the former.  
5) Analysis is limited to disjoint block models; unclear if the template extends to other spectral-clustering settings (e.g., hierarchical clustering).  
6) Framework requires C and C' to have the same number of clusters, so it cannot capture splits/merges or differing K between algorithms.  
7) Related work omits key model-free results (Kumar & Kannan 2010; Balcan et al. 2012, 2013); need to relate the paper’s stability notion to these.  
8) Simulations show extreme sensitivity for PFM and unclear role of eigengaps (contradictory behavior on blog data); practical usefulness and SBM conditions are questionable.  
9) Figure 1 caption is ambiguous about which subplot “For SBM, δ is always greater than δ0” refers to; clarify whether SBM is plotted.  
10) Theory/experiment mismatch for PFM: theorems guarantee weighted ME but experiments report standard ME.

Minor points:  
1) Figure 1 axes lack labels.  
2) Line 44 should state that A_ij for i>j are conditionally independent given node cluster assignments.  
3) Notation is inconsistent (B, C, Y reused across PFM/SBM); recommend clearer distinct symbols and explicit eigenvalue notation (e.g., λ^L, λ^A).  
4) Section 4.4 belongs in the related work section.
