1) Proposes a model-free template for clustering stability with guarantees based on data-computable quantities — useful for theory and practice.  
2) Unclear why PFM and SBM are treated separately given model hierarchy; inconsistent use of Laplacian vs adjacency, weighted ME vs standard ME, and missing DC-SBM comparisons.  
3) Proposition 10: the definition/meaning of the Frobenius inner product <M', M>_F needs clarification.  
4) Assumption 5 ambiguous: is it ||L̂² - L²||_F or ||L̂ - L||_F²? If the former, explain why Laplacians are squared.  
5) Paper focuses on disjoint clusters — unclear whether the framework extends to other spectral-effective models (e.g., hierarchical clustering).  
6) Analysis restricts to clusterings with the same number of clusters and does not handle small refinements or cases where algorithms output different K.  
7) Related work omits key model-free results (Kumar & Kannan 2010; Balcan et al. 2012,2013); need to relate this stability notion to prior center-perturbation-based stability.  
8) Simulations show small perturbations can break PFM stability and SBM conditions are hard to meet; explanations are lacking and practical relevance is questioned.  
9) Figure 1 caption is ambiguous about “For SBM, δ is always greater than δ0” — specify which subplot or state if SBM is not plotted.  
10) Theoretical PFM results use weighted ME while experiments use standard ME — inconsistency between theory and evaluation.  

Minor points:  
11) Figure 1 lacks axis labels.  
12) Clarify (Line 44) that A_ij for i>j are independent given cluster assignments.  
13) Notation inconsistent (B, C, Y differ between PFM and SBM); recommend consistent symbols and clearer eigenvalue notation (e.g., λL, λB).  
14) Section 4.4 belongs in the related work section.
