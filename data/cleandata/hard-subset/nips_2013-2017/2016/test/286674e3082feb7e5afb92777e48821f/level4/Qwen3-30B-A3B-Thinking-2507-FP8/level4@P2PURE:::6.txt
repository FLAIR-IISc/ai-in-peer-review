

The paper proposes a theory for model-free graph clustering, but the result is naive and unconvincing. The authors present what they claim is a novel theory, yet their approach amounts to little more than naive handwaving. For instance: Theorem 1 (Generic Theorem) states "...which also fits G well is close to C"; Proposition 3 (Informal) asserts "...under standard recovery conditions" — these formulations lack substantive theoretical grounding.
