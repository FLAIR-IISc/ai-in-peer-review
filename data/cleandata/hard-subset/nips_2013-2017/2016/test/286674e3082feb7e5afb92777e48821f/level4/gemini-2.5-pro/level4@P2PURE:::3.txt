This manuscript proposes several algorithms for graph clustering, as well as a method for testing the similarity between two cluster assignments. Although the mechanics of the algorithms are clear, their motivation is not well-articulated. The paper lacks a unifying narrative to explain why these specific algorithms are presented together, making it read like a disconnected collection of methods. Furthermore, the algorithms appear to be incremental modifications of existing work, with the primary novel contribution being the set of constraints introduced at the end.
