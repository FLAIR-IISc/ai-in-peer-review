- Proposes several graph clustering algorithms plus a method to test similarity between two cluster assignments.  
- Algorithm mechanics are clear, but the paper fails to articulate their motivation.  
- Lacks a unifying narrative tying the algorithms together; reads like a disconnected collection of methods.  
- Algorithms appear to be incremental modifications of existing work rather than major advances.  
- The main novel contribution seems to be the set of constraints introduced at the end.
