- Establishes connections between various clustering distances for network models (PFM and SBM).  
- Proves a locally convex-like property for clustering: clusterings that equally well estimate model parameters are close in misclassification distance.  
- Demonstrates equivalence between different clustering distance measures for network data.  
- Provides both theoretical proofs and experimental validation supporting the claims.  
- Results are novel but related to recent network literature (Peng, Sun, Zanetti COLT 2015; Chao, Lu, Ma, Zhou arXiv 2015).  
- Theoretical contributions are largely straightforward extensions of existing results.  
- Mentions potential applications to detecting clusterability but does not include formal results.  
- Paper is well-structured and clearly written.
