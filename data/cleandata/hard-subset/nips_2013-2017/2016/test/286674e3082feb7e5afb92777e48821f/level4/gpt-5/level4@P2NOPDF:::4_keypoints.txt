- Scope & significance: Proposes a valuable model-agnostic, data-computable stability template for clustering with potential theoretical and practical impact.

- Choice of estimators & model hierarchy: Unclear why PFM and SBM are treated differently (no unified estimator), why Laplacian is used for PFM vs adjacency for SBM, how weighted ME vs ME compare, and why DC-SBM is omitted.

- Proposition 10: Needs an explicit definition of ⟨M′,M⟩_F (presumably the Frobenius inner product ∑_ij M′_ij M_ij).

- Assumption 5: Ambiguous whether the quantity is ∥L̂^2 − L^2∥_F or ∥L̂ − L∥_F^2; if the former, justify squaring the Laplacians and its role in stability analysis.

- Beyond disjoint block models: Ask whether the template extends to other spectral-clustering settings (e.g., hierarchical clustering) and what the computable certificate would be there.

- Mismatched numbers of clusters: Framework assumes same K for C and C′; request extension to handle merges/splits or refinement/quotient mappings so stability is meaningful across different K.

- Relation to prior “model-free” guarantees: Paper should better compare to Kumar & Kannan (2010), Balcan et al. (2012, 2013) and clarify how its stability notion relates to perturbation resilience and approximation stability.

- Experimental thresholds & interpretability: Simulated tolerated perturbations are extremely small; request quantification of eigengap effects, reconciliation of contrasting observations (PFM vs political blogs), and discussion whether stringent SBM conditions are proof artifacts or inherent.

- Figure 1 caption: Clarify which subplot shows “For SBM, δ is always greater than δ0” or explicitly state if SBM is not plotted.

- Metric mismatch between theory & experiments: Theorems use weighted ME but experiments perturb ME—align metrics or justify transfer of conclusions.

- Minor: Figure 1 lacks axis labels.

- Minor: Fix Line 44 to state conditional independence of A_ij for i>j given cluster labels.

- Minor: Notation is overloaded (B, C, Y); enforce consistent notation and distinguish eigenvalues by source (e.g., λ^L vs λ^B).

- Minor: Section 4.4 is more related work than main text and should be moved or reframed.

- Overall assessment: Strengths—clear model-agnostic stability template, PFM/SBM instantiations, links to exact recovery; Weaknesses—no unified estimator, missing DC-SBM results, fragile/stringent practical conditions, gaps between theory and experiments, limited related-work comparison, and inability to handle differing K.
