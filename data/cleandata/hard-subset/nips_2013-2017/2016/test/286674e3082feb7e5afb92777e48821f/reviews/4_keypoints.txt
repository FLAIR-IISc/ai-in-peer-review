1) Paper proposes a model-free template for clustering stability guarantees, with computable quantities, applied to PFM and SBM and used to derive exact recovery results, but simulations question practical usefulness.

2) Providing model-free theoretical guarantees for clustering is important and valuable for theory and practice.

3) It's unclear why PFM and SBM are treated differently given PFM includes DC-SBM/SBM; inconsistent estimator choices (Laplacian for PFM vs adjacency for SBM) and differing metrics (weighted ME vs ME) need justification and comparison; DC-SBM results are missing.

4) Clarify the definition of <M', M>_F in Proposition 10 (presumably Frobenius inner product).

5) Assumption 5 is ambiguous: is it ||Ĺ^2 - L^2||_F or ||Ĺ - L||_F^2? If the former, explain the intuition for squaring Laplacians.

6) Do the techniques extend beyond block models to other settings where spectral clustering works (e.g., hierarchical clustering / Balakrishnan et al. 2011)? Show how the template would apply.

7) The framework requires C and C' to have the same number of clusters, so it cannot handle splits/merges (K vs K+1) where clusterings are still “close.”

8) Related work omits several model-free stability results (Kumar & Kannan 2010; Balcan et al. 2012/2013); clarify how this paper’s notion of stability relates to perturbation/approximation/center-perturbation stability.

9) Simulations for PFM tolerate only tiny perturbations; the claim that large eigengap makes PFM(G,C) sensitive is unqualified and contradictory to the smoothing-by-powering-A observation; SBM conditions appear very hard to satisfy, undermining practical relevance.

10) Figure 1 caption is unclear about which subplot shows “For SBM, δ is always greater than δ0”; clarify whether SBM is plotted.

11) Theoretical guarantees for PFM are stated in weighted ME, but experiments perturb using ME — this mismatch between theory and experiments must be addressed.

12) Minor: Figure 1 axes are missing labels.

13) Minor: Correct wording at Line 44 — for block models A_{ij} for i>j are independent given cluster assignments.

14) Minor: Notation is inconsistent across sections (B, C, Y used differently for PFM vs SBM); recommend consistent symbols (e.g., Y⊥, CD, λ^L/λ^B) and avoid unnecessary YZ in SBM estimator.

15) Minor: Section 4.4 would fit better as a subsection of Related Work.
