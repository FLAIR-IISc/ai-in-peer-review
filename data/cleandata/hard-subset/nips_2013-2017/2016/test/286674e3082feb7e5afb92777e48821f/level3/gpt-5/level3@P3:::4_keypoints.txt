- Proposes a model-free certification template: fit a model M(G,C) (PFM or SBM), measure fit; if fit is good then any other well-fitting clustering C′ must be close to C (ME or wME), with instantiations and bounds for PFM and SBM.  
- Connects to spectral recovery guarantees and clustering stability, but omits several model-free stability literatures (center perturbation, approximation stability, perturbation resilience).  
- Strength: clear, general, data-dependent template with computable quantities (ε, eigengaps, δ, δ0) and straightforward proofs (Davis–Kahan + subspace geometry).  
- Strength: provides instantiations for PFM and SBM and a “bridge” to reuse model-based analyses for model-free certification.  
- Strength: exact-recovery style corollaries are clean and interpretable when conditions hold.  
- Major concern: inconsistent treatment of PFM vs SBM (estimators and metrics differ despite model nesting); request to unify spectral object and metrics and add DC-SBM instantiation.  
- Clarify Prop.10: explicitly define ⟨M̂, M⟩F as trace(M̂^T M) and state matrix dimensions to avoid ambiguity.  
- Assump.5 ambiguous: specify whether norm is ||L̂^2 − L^2||F or ||L̂ − L||_2F, give intuition and correct constants.  
- Extensibility claim weak: illustrate template on a non-block setting (e.g., hierarchical/similarity clustering with Laplacian eigenmaps) to show broader applicability.  
- Framework enforces same K for C and C′; discuss adaptability to splits/merges (K vs K+1) or provide negative result re: local-equivalence approach.  
- Missing comparison to model-free stability notions (α-approximation stability, perturbation resilience, center stability); clarify implications between notions.  
- Empirics: PFM certificates only hold for tiny perturbations; need explanation that ε grows with eigengap for fixed subspace deviation and quantify this effect.  
- Recommend discussing smoothing-by-powering-A as a preprocessing to reduce ε and give guidance on power choice.  
- SBM bounds are vacuous in experiments (δ always > δ0); suggest improving constants or regularizing adjacency (Qin–Rohe 2013) to obtain informative δ.  
- Figure 1 issues: unclear which plots show SBM, missing axis labels/units, and mismatch between theory metric (wME) and perturbation metric (ME); fix captions and report both metrics.  
- Minor corrections: fix wording about Aij independence, unify inconsistent notation (B, C, Y, YZ), and provide a symbol table.  
- Related work placement: move/merge Section 4.4 into Related Work and tighten presentation.  
- Recommendation: weak accept if authors (i) unify metrics/estimators and add DC-SBM, (ii) clarify Prop.10 and Assump.5, (iii) fix figures/experiment-theory mismatches, and (iv) expand related work and extensions; otherwise borderline due to limited practical relevance now.
