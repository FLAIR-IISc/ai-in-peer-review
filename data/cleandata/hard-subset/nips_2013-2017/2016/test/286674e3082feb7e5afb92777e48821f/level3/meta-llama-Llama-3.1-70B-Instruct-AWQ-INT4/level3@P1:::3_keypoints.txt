- Proposes a novel model-free framework for graph clustering that does not rely on parametric assumptions.
- Introduces a set of clearly presented algorithms for clustering and testing similarity between clusterings.
- Main novelty is adding constraints to existing algorithms, presented late in the paper.
- Lacks a clear motivation and narrative explaining why the work is needed.

- Paper is well written, with algorithms and results presented clearly and accessibly.

- Arguments for rejection: weak motivation, list-like presentation, limited methodological novelty, and lack of comparisons to prior work.
