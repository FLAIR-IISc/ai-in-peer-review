- Proposes an alternative to Gaussian Mixture Models using a Locally Adaptive Normal Distribution grounded in Riemannian manifolds.
- Introduces a maximum likelihood estimator for the distribution’s parameters.
- Provides experiments on both artificial and real-world datasets.
- Paper is well written, with sound theoretical development and strong empirical results.
- Insufficient demonstrated novelty relative to Simo-Serra et al. [19].
- Key distinctions from the state-of-the-art (Simo-Serra et al.) are not clearly articulated until the conclusion.
- Reference [19] is missing from the introduction; differences should be explicitly presented there.
