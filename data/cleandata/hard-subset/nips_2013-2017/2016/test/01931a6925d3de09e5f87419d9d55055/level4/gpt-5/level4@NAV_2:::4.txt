This paper introduces a Locally Adaptive Normal Distribution as an alternative to Gaussian mixture models, formulated on Riemannian manifolds. It presents a maximum-likelihood estimator for the distribution parameters and evaluates the approach on both synthetic and real-world datasets. The manuscript is clearly written, with solid theoretical grounding and strong experimental support. However, in comparison to Simo-Serra et al. [19], the novelty appears limited. The distinctions from prior state of the art are not made clear until the conclusion; these differences should be explicitly articulated earlier, ideally in the introduction (notably, [19] is not cited there).
