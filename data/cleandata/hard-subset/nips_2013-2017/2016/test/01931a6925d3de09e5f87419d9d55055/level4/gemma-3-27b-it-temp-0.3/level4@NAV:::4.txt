This paper introduces a Locally Adaptive Normal Distribution as an alternative to Gaussian Mixture Models, grounding the approach in Riemannian Manifold theory. An Maximum Likelihood Estimator is developed for the distribution’s parameters, and the method’s performance is demonstrated through experiments on both synthetic and real datasets. The manuscript is clearly written, theoretically sound, and supported by compelling experimental results. However, the novelty presented feels incremental when considered alongside related work, particularly Simo-Serra et al. [19]. The distinctions between the proposed method and existing state-of-the-art techniques are not sufficiently highlighted throughout the paper; this comparison would benefit from earlier and more explicit discussion, ideally beginning in the introduction (where Simo-Serra et al. [19] is currently not cited).
