- Proposes the Locally Adaptive Normal Distribution (LAND) as an alternative to Gaussian Mixture Models, built using Riemannian manifolds.  
- Presents a Maximum Likelihood Estimation (MLE) approach for parameter estimation.  
- Validates the method with experiments on synthetic and real-world datasets showing solid performance.  
- Manuscript is well-written with robust theoretical foundations and strong experimental results.  
- Contribution appears to lack significant novelty compared to Simo-Serra et al. [19].  
- Distinctions from the current state-of-the-art are not clearly explained until the conclusion and should be clarified earlier.  
- Reference [19] is missing from the introduction; comparative differences should be explicitly highlighted there.
