- Proposes a Locally Adaptive Normal distribution as an alternative to Gaussian Mixture Models using Riemannian manifolds.  
- Derives a Maximum Likelihood Estimator for the distribution parameters.  
- Evaluated through experiments on synthetic and real-world datasets.  
- Paper is well-written with sound theoretical foundations and well-designed experiments.  
- Lacks a significant novel contribution compared to Simo-Serra et al. [19].  
- Differences from the state-of-the-art are not clearly explained until the conclusion.  
- Reference [19] is missing from the introduction, which obscures the distinction from prior work.
