- Proposes a Locally Adaptive Normal Distribution (Riemannian-manifold-based) as an alternative to Gaussian Mixture Models.  
- Derives a maximum likelihood estimator for the distribution’s parameters.  
- Evaluates the method on both artificial and real-world datasets with sound experimental design.  
- Paper is well-written and has solid theoretical foundations.  
- Offers limited novelty compared to Simo-Serra et al. [19].  
- Differences from the state-of-the-art are not clearly explained until the conclusion, hindering reader appreciation.  
- Introduction fails to highlight key differences and omits reference [19].
