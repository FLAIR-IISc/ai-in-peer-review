This paper introduces the Locally Adaptive Normal Distribution as an alternative to Gaussian Mixture Models, leveraging Riemannian Manifolds. The authors propose a Maximum Likelihood Estimator for parameter estimation and validate their approach using experiments on both synthetic and real-world datasets. The manuscript is well-structured, with a solid theoretical foundation and comprehensive experiments. However, in comparison to the work by Simo-Serra et al. [19], the paper's novelty is not substantial. A clear distinction between the proposed method and the current state-of-the-art is not adequately established until the conclusion. Notably, reference [19] is absent from the introduction, where this comparison should have been explicitly highlighted to provide context for the reader.
