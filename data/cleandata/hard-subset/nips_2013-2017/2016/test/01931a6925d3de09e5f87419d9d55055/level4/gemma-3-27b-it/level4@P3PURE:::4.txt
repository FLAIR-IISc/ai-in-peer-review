The paper proposes a Locally Adaptive Normal Distribution as an alternative to Gaussian Mixture Models, grounded in the framework of Riemannian Manifolds. An MLE estimator for the distribution’s parameters is developed, and the method is evaluated through experiments on both synthetic and real-world datasets. The paper is well-written, presenting sound theoretical foundations and supported by compelling experimental results. However, the work lacks a strong novelty claim when considered alongside Simo-Serra et al. [19]. The distinctions between the proposed method and existing state-of-the-art approaches are not sufficiently articulated until the conclusion; these differences should have been clearly established earlier, ideally beginning in the introduction – particularly as ref [19] is not mentioned there.
