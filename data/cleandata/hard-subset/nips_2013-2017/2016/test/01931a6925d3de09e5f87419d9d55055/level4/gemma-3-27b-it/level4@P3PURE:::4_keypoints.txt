- Proposes a Locally Adaptive Normal Distribution as an alternative to Gaussian Mixture Models within a Riemannian manifold framework.
- Develops an MLE estimator for the distribution’s parameters.
- Evaluated on synthetic and real-world datasets with compelling experimental results.
- Paper is well-written and presents sound theoretical foundations.
- Lacks a strong novelty claim relative to Simo-Serra et al. [19].
- Distinctions from existing state-of-the-art are not clearly articulated until the conclusion.
- Reference [19] is omitted from the introduction; key differences should be stated earlier.
