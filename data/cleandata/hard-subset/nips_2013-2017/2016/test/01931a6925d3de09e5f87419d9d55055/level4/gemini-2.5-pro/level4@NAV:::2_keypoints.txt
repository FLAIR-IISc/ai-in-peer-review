- Proposes a generalization of the multivariate normal by replacing the Euclidean metric with a locally adaptive Riemannian metric.  
- Effectively extends the normal distribution to the manifold-learning setting.  
- Demonstrates wide applicability, supported by convincing experiments.  
- Provides a succinct yet complete maximum likelihood procedure for parameter estimation.  
- Extends the approach to a mixture model, offering a practical and compelling alternative to standard GMMs.  
- Experimental results are compelling and point to many promising directions for future work.  
- Manuscript quality is high: clear language, strong structure, comprehensive bibliography, and good conference relevance.
