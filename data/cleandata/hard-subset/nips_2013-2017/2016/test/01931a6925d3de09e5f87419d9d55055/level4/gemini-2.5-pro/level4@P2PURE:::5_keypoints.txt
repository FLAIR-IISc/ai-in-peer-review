- Proposes Locally Adaptive Normal Distribution (LAND) using a learned geodesic manifold traversal distance replacing Mahalanobis distance in Gaussian densities.
- Extends the model to a mixture of LANDs and evaluates on synthetic and EEG datasets.
- Central idea (using geodesic distance akin to Isomap) is intriguing but presentation lacks clarity.
- Definition of the inverse metric tensor (Eq. 7) appears ad hoc; authors should provide intuition for using a Gaussian kernel to weight local covariances.
- Unclear relationship between the local covariance in Eq. 7 and its MAP estimation in Eq. 11 — are they the same quantity?
- Experimental evaluation is insufficiently thorough.
- Major concern about scalability and computational efficiency; paper lacks computational complexity analysis.
- Authors should compare runtime to a standard EM implementation.
- Overall interesting theoretical contribution but practical applicability is not well established.
- Minor: In Algorithm 1, lines 4–5 the symbol 'd' should be replaced with '∇'.
