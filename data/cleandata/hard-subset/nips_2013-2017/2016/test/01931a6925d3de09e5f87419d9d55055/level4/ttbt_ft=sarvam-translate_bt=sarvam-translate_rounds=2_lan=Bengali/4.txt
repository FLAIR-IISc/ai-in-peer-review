This paper proposes an alternative to the Gaussian mixture model, specifically a locally adapted normal distribution. The proposed distribution is based on Riemannian manifolds. The paper proposes an MLE estimator for the distribution's parameters and evaluates the method through experiments on both synthetic and real data. The paper is well-written, with theory and good testing. However, compared to SIMO-Sera and others [19], this paper does not offer much novelty. The differences between the proposed method and the state-of-the-art methods were not well explained before the reader reaches the conclusion. These differences should have been clarified at the beginning of the introduction (note that the reference [19] is not in the introduction).
