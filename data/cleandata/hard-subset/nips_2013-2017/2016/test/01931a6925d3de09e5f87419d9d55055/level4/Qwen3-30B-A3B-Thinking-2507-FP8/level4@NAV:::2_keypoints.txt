- Proposes replacing the Euclidean metric in the multivariate normal with a locally adaptive Riemannian metric.  
- Generalizes the normal distribution to the manifold learning setting.  
- Demonstrates broad applicability through convincing experiments.  
- Presents a concise, complete maximum likelihood–type parameter estimation procedure.  
- Extends to a mixture of LANDS, making it a practical competitor to GMMs.  
- Experiments are compelling and suggest significant future research directions.  
- Writing, structure, bibliography, and relevance to the conference are excellent.
