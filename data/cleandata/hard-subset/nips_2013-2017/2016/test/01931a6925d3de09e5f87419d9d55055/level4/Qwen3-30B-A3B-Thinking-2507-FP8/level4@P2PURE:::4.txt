

The paper proposes an alternative to Gaussian Mixture Models based on a Locally Adaptive Normal Distribution, grounded in Riemannian manifolds. It introduces an MLE estimator for the distribution's parameters and evaluates the method using experiments on both artificial and real-world data. The paper is well written, with sound theory and strong experimental results. However, compared to Simo-Serra et al. [19], the paper does not establish sufficient novelty. The distinctions between the proposed method and the state-of-the-art are not clearly articulated before the conclusion; these differences should have been explicitly presented in the introduction (note that reference [19] is missing from the introduction).
