- Introduces the Locally Adaptive Normal (LAN) Distribution as an alternative to Gaussian Mixture Models, grounded in Riemannian manifold principles.  
- Derives a Maximum Likelihood Estimator for the LAN distribution parameters.  
- Validated approach through experiments on synthetic and real-world datasets.  
- Manuscript is well-presented with rigorous theoretical foundations.  
- Experimental evaluation is thorough.  
- Main weakness: limited novelty relative to prior work, specifically Simo-Serra et al. [19].  
- Paper fails to clearly articulate distinctions from state-of-the-art early on; reference [19] is missing from the introduction and should be discussed upfront.
