- Proposes a Locally Adaptive Normal Distribution (LAND) as an alternative to Gaussian Mixture Models using Riemannian manifolds.
- Derives a Maximum Likelihood Estimator for the distribution’s parameters.
- Evaluated on both artificial and real-world datasets with well-designed experiments.
- Paper is well-written and grounded in sound theoretical foundations.
- Novelty is questioned due to similarities with Simo-Serra et al. [19].
- Differences from Simo-Serra et al. are not clearly explained until the conclusion.
- Reference [19] is absent from the introduction, leaving background/context unclear for readers unfamiliar with it.
- Suggest reorganizing the introduction to explicitly compare with existing methods, particularly Simo-Serra et al., to clarify novelty.
- Clarifying these distinctions in the intro would improve the paper’s impact, significance, and reader understanding.
