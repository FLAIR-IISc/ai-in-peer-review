- Replaces Euclidean metric in multivariate normal with a locally adaptive, smoothly varying Riemannian metric.  
- Effectively generalizes the normal distribution to a manifold-learning framework with broad applicability.  
- Presents a compact yet complete maximum-likelihood parameter estimation procedure.  
- Extends to mixtures of LANDs, offering a practical alternative to GMMs.  
- Experiments are compelling and suggest promising future directions.  
- Writing, organization, bibliography, and fit to NIPS scope are excellent.
