- Introduces the locally adaptive normal distribution (LAND) using learned geodesic manifold distances and a mixture extension; evaluated on synthetic and EEG data.
- Novel idea: using geodesic distance akin to Isomap is interesting and a valuable contribution.
- Equation (7) (inverse metric tensor) lacks clear motivation; use of a Gaussian kernel to weight local covariances appears ad hoc.
- Relationship between the local covariance in Equation (7) and the MAP estimation in Equation (11) is unclear; need to clarify if they denote the same quantity.
- Experimental validation is underdeveloped and needs expansion.
- Scalability and computational efficiency are not analyzed; missing complexity analysis and runtime comparisons (e.g., versus standard EM).
- Practical applicability is questionable without more thorough computational and empirical evaluation.
- Minor typographical error: in Algorithm 1 lines 4–5, variable `d` should be the gradient symbol `∇`.
