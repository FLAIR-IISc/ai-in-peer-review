This paper proposes an alternative model for the Gaussian mixture model, locally adapted normal distribution. The proposed distribution is based on a Riemannian manifold. The paper proposes an MLE estimator for the distribution's parameters and evaluates this method using experiments on both synthetic and real-world data. The paper is well-written, with solid theory and good experiments. However, compared to Simo-Serra et al. [19], this paper does not offer any significant novelty. The difference between the proposed method and the state-of-the-art technology is not clearly explained before the reader reaches a conclusion. These differences should have been clarified before the introduction (note that the reference [19] is absent in the introduction).
