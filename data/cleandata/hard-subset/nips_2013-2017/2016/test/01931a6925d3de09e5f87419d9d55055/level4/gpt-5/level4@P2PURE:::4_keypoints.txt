- Introduces a Locally Adaptive Normal Distribution as an alternative to Gaussian Mixture Models.
- Formulates the distribution on Riemannian manifolds.
- Derives a maximum likelihood estimator for the distribution’s parameters.
- Evaluates the method on both synthetic and real-world datasets with strong experimental results.
- Manuscript is well written and presents solid theoretical development.
- Contribution offers limited novelty compared to Simo-Serra et al. [19].
- Differences from [19] are not clearly articulated until the conclusion and [19] is not cited in the introduction; these distinctions should be made explicit early.
