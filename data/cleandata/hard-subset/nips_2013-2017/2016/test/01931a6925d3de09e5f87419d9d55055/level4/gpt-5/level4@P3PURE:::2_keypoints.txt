- Replaces the Euclidean metric in the multivariate normal with a locally adaptive Riemannian metric.
- Generalizes the normal distribution to the manifold-learning setting with broad applicability.
- Presents a succinct, complete maximum-likelihood parameter estimation procedure.
- Extends to a mixture of LANDS, making it a practical competitor to Gaussian mixture models.
- Experiments are exciting and suggest wide avenues for future research.
- Paper is brief and well-written; language, structure, bibliography, and relevance to NIPS are excellent.
