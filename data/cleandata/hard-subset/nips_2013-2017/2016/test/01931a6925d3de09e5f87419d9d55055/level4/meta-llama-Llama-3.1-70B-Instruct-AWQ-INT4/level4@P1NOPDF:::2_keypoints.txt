- Extends multivariate normal by replacing Euclidean metric with a locally adaptive Riemannian metric.
- Generalizes the normal distribution to manifold learning settings.
- Demonstrates wide applicability through reported experiments.
- Provides a concise and complete maximum-likelihood–type parameter estimation procedure.
- Extends to a mixture model (LANDS), making it a practical competitor to GMMs.
- Experiments are exciting and open avenues for future research.
- Language, structure, bibliography, and relevance to NIPS are excellent.
