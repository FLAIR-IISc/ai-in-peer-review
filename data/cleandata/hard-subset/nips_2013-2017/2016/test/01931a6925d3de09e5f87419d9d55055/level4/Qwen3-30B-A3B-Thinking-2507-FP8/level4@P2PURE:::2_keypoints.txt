- Replaces the Euclidean metric in the multivariate normal with a locally adaptive Riemannian metric.
- Generalizes the normal distribution to manifold-learning settings with broad applicability.
- Demonstrates applicability through compelling experiments.
- Provides a succinct, complete maximum-likelihood parameter estimation procedure.
- Extends to mixtures of LANDS, making it a practical competitor to GMMs.
- Experiments are exciting and open many avenues for future research.
- Language, structure, bibliography, and relevance to NeurIPS are excellent.
