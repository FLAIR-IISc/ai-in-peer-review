- Proposes a Locally Adaptive Normal Distribution as an alternative to Gaussian Mixture Models.  
- Framework is grounded in Riemannian manifold theory.  
- Derives an MLE estimator for the distribution’s parameters.  
- Validated experimentally on synthetic and real-world datasets.  
- Manuscript is clearly written with strong theoretical grounding and well-executed experiments.  
- Lacks significant novelty relative to Simo-Serra et al. [19].  
- Differences from the state-of-the-art are not adequately clarified, especially in the introduction.  
- Reference [19] is omitted from the introduction; distinctions should be explicitly stated early.
