- Proposes Locally Adaptive Normal Distribution (LAND) as an alternative to Gaussian Mixture Models.  
- Defines the distribution on Riemannian manifolds.  
- Derives a maximum likelihood estimator for LAND parameters.  
- Evaluates the method on synthetic and real-world datasets with solid experiments.  
- Paper is well written and theoretical development appears sound.  
- Insufficient novelty relative to Simo-Serra et al. [19].  
- Distinctions from prior work are not clearly articulated until the conclusion.  
- Differences should be made explicit earlier (ideally in the introduction); [19] is not cited there.
