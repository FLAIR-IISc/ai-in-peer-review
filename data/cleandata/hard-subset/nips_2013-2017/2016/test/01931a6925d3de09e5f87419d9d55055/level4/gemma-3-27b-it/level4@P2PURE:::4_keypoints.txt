- Proposes a Locally Adaptive Normal Distribution as an alternative to Gaussian Mixture Models within a Riemannian manifold framework.  
- Derives a maximum likelihood estimator for the distribution’s parameters.  
- Validated with experiments on synthetic and real-world datasets showing solid performance.  
- Paper is technically sound and well-written.  
- Novelty appears incremental relative to prior work, especially Simo-Serra et al. [19].  
- Key distinctions from state-of-the-art are not emphasized early enough; they appear only in the conclusion.  
- Simo-Serra et al. [19] is omitted from the introduction despite its relevance and should be cited and contrasted earlier.
