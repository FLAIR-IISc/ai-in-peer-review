- Introduces the Locally Adaptive Normal Distribution (Riemannian-manifold-based) as an alternative to Gaussian Mixture Models.
- Proposes a Maximum Likelihood Estimator for the distribution parameters and evaluates on synthetic and real-world data.
- Paper is well-written with solid theoretical foundation and effective experiments.
- Novelty is limited compared to Simo-Serra et al. [19].
- Key differences from the state-of-the-art are not clearly explained until the conclusion.
- Introduction lacks reference to [19]; authors should explicitly state differences and motivation there to improve impact.
