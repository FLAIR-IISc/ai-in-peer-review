- Extends the multivariate normal by replacing the Euclidean metric with a locally adaptive Riemannian metric.
- Provides a principled generalization of the normal distribution for manifold-learning with broad applicability.
- Offers a succinct, complete maximum-likelihood parameter estimation procedure.
- Extension to a mixture of LANDs makes the method a practical competitor to GMMs.
- Experiments are exciting and open promising avenues for future research.
- Language, structure, bibliography, and relevance to the conference are excellent.
