- Replaces Euclidean metric in multivariate normal with a locally adaptive Riemannian metric, extending the normal density.
- Produces a generalized normal distribution suitable for manifold learning with broad applicability.
- Provides a concise, complete maximum-likelihood parameter estimation procedure.
- Extends to mixtures of LANDS, making it a practical competitor to Gaussian mixture models (GMMs).
- Experiments are exciting and suggest wide avenues for future research.
- Language, structure, bibliography, and relevance to the conference are excellent.
