- Introduces the Locally Adaptive Normal Distribution, a Riemannian manifold–based alternative to Gaussian Mixture Models.  
- Derives a Maximum Likelihood Estimation (MLE) procedure for the model parameters.  
- Evaluates the method on synthetic and real-world datasets with solid experimental validation.  
- Paper is generally well-written and has a sound theoretical basis.  
- Primary weakness: limited novelty relative to prior work, notably Simo-Serra et al. [19].  
- Key differences from Simo-Serra et al. are not clearly articulated until the conclusion.  
- Reference [19] is omitted from the introduction, preventing proper early contextualization of the contribution.
