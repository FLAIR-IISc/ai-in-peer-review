- Replaces the Euclidean metric in the multivariate normal with a locally adaptive Riemannian metric to extend the normal density.  
- Generalizes the normal distribution to the manifold-learning setting, increasing applicability.  
- Presents a complete and succinct maximum likelihood parameter estimation procedure.  
- Extends the model to mixtures, making it a practical competitor to Gaussian Mixture Models (GMMs).  
- Experiments are promising and suggest many avenues for future research.  
- Paper quality is high in language, structure, and bibliography.  
- Highly relevant to the NIPS conference.
