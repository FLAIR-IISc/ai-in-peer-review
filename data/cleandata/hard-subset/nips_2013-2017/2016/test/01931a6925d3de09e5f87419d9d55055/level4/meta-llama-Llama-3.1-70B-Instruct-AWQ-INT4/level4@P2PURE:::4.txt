The paper proposes an alternative to Gaussian Mixture Models, introducing a Locally Adaptive Normal Distribution based on Riemannian Manifolds. The authors develop a Maximum Likelihood Estimator (MLE) for the distribution's parameters and evaluate the method through experiments on both artificial and real-world data. The paper is well-written, with a solid theoretical foundation and effective experiments. However, in comparison to Simo-Serra et al. [19], the paper lacks a significant novel contribution. The differences between the proposed method and the state-of-the-art are not clearly explained until the conclusion, and these distinctions should have been explicitly stated in the introduction, particularly given the absence of reference [19] in the introductory section.
