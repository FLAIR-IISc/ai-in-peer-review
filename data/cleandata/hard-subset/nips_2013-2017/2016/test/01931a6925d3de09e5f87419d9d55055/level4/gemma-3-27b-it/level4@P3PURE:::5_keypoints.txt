- Proposes a locally adaptive normal distribution (LAND) using a learned geodesic manifold traversal distance, replacing Mahalanobis distance; extended to mixtures and evaluated on synthetic and EEG data.

- Core idea of using a geodesic (Isomap-like) distance is interesting.

- Definition of the inverse metric tensor in Eq. (7) appears ad hoc and needs justification.

- Need more intuition for using a Gaussian kernel for weights in the weighted sum of local covariances.

- Notation is confusing; unclear whether the local covariance in Eq. (7) and its MAP estimate in Eq. (11) refer to the same quantity.

- Experimental evaluations are unconvincing.

- Major concern about scalability and computational efficiency; paper lacks computational complexity analysis and runtime comparison to standard EM.

- Interesting theoretical contribution but practical applicability is questionable.

- Minor: Algorithm 1, lines 4–5, should replace 'd' with '∇'.
