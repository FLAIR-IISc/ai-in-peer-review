- Proposes LAND distribution using a learned geodesic manifold traversal distance instead of Mahalanobis distance; extends to mixtures and evaluates on synthetic and EEG data.

- Definition of the inverse metric tensor in Eq. (7) seems ad hoc; authors should justify using a Gaussian-kernel-weighted sum of local covariances.

- Unclear relationship between the local covariance in Eq. (7) and its MAP estimation in Eq. (11); need to clarify if they denote the same quantity.

- Experimental evaluation is limited (one synthetic, one real dataset), providing insufficient evidence of effectiveness versus existing methods.

- No analysis of scalability or computational complexity and no runtime comparison to standard methods (e.g., EM), raising practical applicability concerns.

- Minor: Algorithm 1 lines 4–5 use 'd' where the gradient symbol '∇' is likely intended.
