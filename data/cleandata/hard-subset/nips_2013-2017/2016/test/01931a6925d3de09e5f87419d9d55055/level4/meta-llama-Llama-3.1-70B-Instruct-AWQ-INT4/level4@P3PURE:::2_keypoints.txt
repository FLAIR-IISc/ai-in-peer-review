- Replaces Euclidean metric in multivariate normal with a locally adaptive Riemannian metric, generalizing the normal distribution.
- Targets manifold learning and demonstrates wide applicability through experiments.
- Presents a concise, complete maximum-likelihood–type parameter estimation procedure.
- Extends to a mixture of LANDS, offering a practical alternative to Gaussian Mixture Models (GMMs).
- Experiments are exciting and open broad avenues for future research.
- Language, structure, bibliography, and relevance to the NIPS remit are all excellent.
