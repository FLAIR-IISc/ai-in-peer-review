The paper proposes an alternative to Gaussian Mixture Models, namely a Locally Adaptive Normal Distribution, which is based on Riemannian Manifolds. The authors also derive an MLE estimator for the distribution's parameters and evaluate the method using experiments on both artificial and real-world data. The paper is well-written, with sound theoretical foundations and good experimental design. However, compared to Simo-Serra et al. [19], the paper does not offer a significant novelty. Unfortunately, the differences between the proposed method and the state-of-the-art are not well-explained until the conclusion, which makes it difficult for the reader to appreciate the contribution of the paper. These differences should have been clearly highlighted in the introduction, which notably omits reference [19].
