- Proposes the Locally Adaptive Normal Distribution, a Riemannian-manifold-based model as an alternative to Gaussian Mixture Models.
- Derives a Maximum Likelihood Estimator for the model parameters.
- Evaluates the method on synthetic and real-world datasets with strong experimental support.
- Paper is well-written and has a sound theoretical foundation.
- Main weakness: limited novelty compared to Simo-Serra et al. [19].
- Fails to clearly articulate differences from the state-of-the-art until the conclusion.
- Reference [19] is missing from the introduction and the critical comparison should be presented earlier.
