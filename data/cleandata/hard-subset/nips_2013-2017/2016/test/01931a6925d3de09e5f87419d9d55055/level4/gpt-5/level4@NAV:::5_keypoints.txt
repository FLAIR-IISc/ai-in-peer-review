- Paper proposes a locally adaptive normal distribution using a data-driven geodesic manifold traversal distance replacing Mahalanobis distance in a Gaussian model.
- Presents an extension to a mixture model composed of LAND components.
- Evaluated on a synthetic dataset and a real EEG dataset, but experiments are limited.
- Definition of the inverse metric tensor (Eq. 7) appears ad hoc and needs more intuition.
- Request explanation for using a Gaussian kernel to weight a sum of local covariance matrices in Eq. 7.
- Unclear whether the local covariance in Eq. 7 and the MAP estimate in Eq. 11 refer to the same quantity.
- Major concern about scalability and computational efficiency—ask for computational complexity and wall-clock time comparison to standard EM.
- Minor correction: in Algorithm 1, lines 4 and 5, replace d with ∇.
