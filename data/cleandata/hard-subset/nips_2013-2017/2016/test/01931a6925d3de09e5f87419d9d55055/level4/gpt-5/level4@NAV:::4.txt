The paper introduces an alternative to Gaussian Mixture Models, namely a Locally Adaptive Normal Distribution. The proposed distribution is grounded in Riemannian Manifolds. The paper derives a maximum likelihood estimator for the distribution’s parameters and evaluates the approach using experiments on both synthetic and real-world data. The paper is well written, with solid theory and strong experiments. However, compared with Simo-Serra et al. [19], the work does not present substantial novelty. The differences between the proposed method and the state of the art are not clearly articulated before the conclusion. These distinctions should have been made explicit in the introduction (note that ref. [19] is absent from the introduction).
