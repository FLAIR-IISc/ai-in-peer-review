- Introduces a Locally Adaptive Normal Distribution as an alternative to Gaussian Mixture Models, grounded in Riemannian manifolds.
- Derives a maximum likelihood estimator for the distribution’s parameters.
- Evaluates the method with solid experiments on both synthetic and real-world data.
- Paper is well written with strong theoretical development.
- Lacks substantial novelty relative to Simo-Serra et al. [19].
- Differences from the state of the art are not clearly articulated until the conclusion.
- Simo-Serra et al. [19] is omitted from the introduction; distinctions should be made explicit early.
