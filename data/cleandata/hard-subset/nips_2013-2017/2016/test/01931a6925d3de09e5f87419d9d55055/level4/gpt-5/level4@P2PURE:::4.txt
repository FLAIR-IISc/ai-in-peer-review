The paper introduces a Locally Adaptive Normal Distribution as an alternative to Gaussian Mixture Models. The distribution is formulated on Riemannian manifolds. The authors derive a maximum likelihood estimator for its parameters and evaluate the method on both synthetic and real-world datasets. The manuscript is well written, with solid theory and strong experiments. However, compared with Simo-Serra et al. [19], the contribution offers limited novelty. The differences between the proposed method and the state of the art are not clearly articulated until the conclusion. These distinctions should be made explicit from the introduction (notably, [19] is not cited there).
