- Introduces Locally Adaptive Normal Distribution (LAND) using geodesic manifold traversal distance (replacing Mahalanobis), extended to mixtures and evaluated on synthetic and EEG data.
- Core idea of employing a geodesic distance (reminiscent of Isomap) is genuinely interesting.
- Clarity issue: the inverse metric tensor in Eq. (7) appears arbitrary and needs justification.
- Authors should explain the rationale for using a Gaussian kernel to weight the local covariance sum.
- Unclear distinction between the local covariance in Eq. (7) and its MAP estimate in Eq. (11); clarify if they are the same.
- Experimental evaluation is rather limited.
- Major concern about scalability and computational efficiency; provide computational complexity analysis and runtime comparison to standard EM.
- Although theoretically compelling, practical applicability remains questionable.
- Minor correction: In Algorithm 1 (lines 4–5) replace 'd' with '\nabla'.
