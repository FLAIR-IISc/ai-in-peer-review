This paper introduces a Locally Adaptive Normal Distribution as an alternative to Gaussian Mixture Models, grounded in the framework of Riemannian Manifolds. An Maximum Likelihood Estimator is developed for the distribution’s parameters, and the method’s performance is demonstrated through experiments utilizing both synthetic and real-world datasets. The manuscript is clearly written and presents a strong theoretical foundation alongside compelling experimental results. However, the novelty presented feels incremental when considered alongside related work such as Simo-Serra et al. [19]. Critically, the distinctions between the proposed approach and current state-of-the-art techniques are not sufficiently highlighted throughout the paper; this comparison remains unclear until the conclusion. Explicitly addressing these differences, and referencing relevant work like [19], earlier in the introduction would significantly strengthen the contribution.
