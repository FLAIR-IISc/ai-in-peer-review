The paper proposes an alternative to Gaussian Mixture Models, namely a Locally Adaptive Normal Distribution, based on Riemannian Manifolds. The authors derive a Maximum Likelihood Estimator (MLE) for the distribution's parameters and evaluate the method using experiments on both artificial and real-world data. The paper is well-written, with sound theoretical foundations and well-designed experiments. However, compared to Simo-Serra et al. [19], the paper lacks a significant novel contribution. The differences between the proposed method and the state-of-the-art are not clearly explained until the conclusion, which would have been more effective if presented in the introduction. Notably, reference [19] is absent from the introduction, which further obscures the distinction between the proposed approach and existing work.
