- Replaces Euclidean metric in the multivariate normal with a locally adaptive Riemannian metric.
- Generalizes the normal distribution to the manifold-learning setting with broad applicability, supported by experiments.
- Presents a succinct, complete maximum-likelihood–type parameter estimation procedure.
- Extends to a mixture of LANDS, making it a practical competitor to Gaussian mixture models.
- Experiments are exciting and open wide avenues for future research.
- Language, structure, bibliography, and relevance to the conference remit are excellent.
