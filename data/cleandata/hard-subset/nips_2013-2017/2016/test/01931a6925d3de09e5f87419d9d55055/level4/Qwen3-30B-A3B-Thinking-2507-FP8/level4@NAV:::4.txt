

The paper introduces a Locally Adaptive Normal Distribution as an alternative to Gaussian Mixture Models, grounded in Riemannian Manifolds. It presents an MLE estimator for the distribution's parameters and validates the approach through experiments on synthetic and real-world datasets. The manuscript is clearly written, with robust theoretical foundations and well-executed experiments. However, the work lacks significant novelty when compared to Simo-Serra & al. [19], as the distinctions between the proposed method and the state-of-the-art are not adequately clarified in the introduction. These differences should have been explicitly stated early in the paper (noting that reference [19] is omitted from the introduction).
