- Proposes Locally Adaptive Normal Distribution as an alternative to Gaussian Mixture Models.
- Method grounded in Riemannian manifold theory.
- Introduces an MLE estimator for the distribution parameters.
- Evaluated with experiments on both synthetic and real-world datasets.
- Paper is well written with sound theoretical development and good experiments.
- Lacks clear, strong novelty compared to Simo‑Serra et al. [19].
- Differences from the state-of-the-art are not clearly explained early; ref. [19] is missing from the introduction.
