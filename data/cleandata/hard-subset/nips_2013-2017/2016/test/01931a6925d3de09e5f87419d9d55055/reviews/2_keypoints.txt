- Replaces the Euclidean metric in the multivariate normal with a locally adaptive Riemannian metric, extending the normal density.
- Generalizes the normal distribution to the manifold learning setting with broad applicability.
- Experiments illustrate the method’s practicality and effectiveness.
- Provides a concise, complete maximum-likelihood parameter estimation procedure.
- Extends to a mixture of LANDS, making it a practical competitor to GMMs.
- Experiments are exciting and suggest wide avenues for future research.
- Language, structure, bibliography, and relevance to the conference are excellent.
