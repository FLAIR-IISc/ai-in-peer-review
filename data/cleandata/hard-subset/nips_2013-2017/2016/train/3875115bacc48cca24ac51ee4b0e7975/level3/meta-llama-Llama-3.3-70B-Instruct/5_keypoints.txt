- Addresses unsupervised learning of similarities for many exemplars using CNNs by generating batches of compact, mutually dissimilar cliques for training.
- Novel optimization formulation to create training batches that avoids reliance on large labeled datasets.
- Well-written paper with clear motivation, approach, and experimental evaluation.
- Thorough related-work review and clear articulation of advantages over existing methods.
- Strong experimental results showing effective learning of fine-grained similarity, especially for posture analysis and object classification.
- Strength: clear and well-motivated problem statement.
- Strength: novel and well-explained unsupervised approach.
- Strength: strong empirical validation.
- Strength: comprehensive discussion of related work and benefits.
- Weakness: optimization formulation may be complex and hard to understand for some readers.
- Weakness: experimental evaluation could be more comprehensive with additional datasets and comparisons to more state-of-the-art methods.
- Weakness: insufficient discussion of potential applications and limitations.
- Recommendation: Accept with minor revisions to address the noted weaknesses.
