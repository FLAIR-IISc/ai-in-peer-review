- Shows EM and gradient EM can converge to non-optimal local maxima with exponentially high probability under common random initialization.  
- Authors review maximization schemes for GMMs (EM, gradient EM) and discuss common initialization choices.  
- Primary theorems provide the formal counterexamples and convergence results.  
- Paper balances intuition and technical detail, providing clear and detailed analysis.  
- Results have significant implications for non-convex optimization and practical use of EM/gradient EM.
