- Paper proposes a batch Knowledge Gradient (KG) implementation using Monte Carlo gradient estimation and reports empirical gains over other batch Bayesian optimization methods.

- Novelty is unclear: prior batch KG works (e.g., [1], [2]) exist, so the main contribution may be the gradient estimator and requires comparative analysis against those implementations.

- Section 5.2 (core gradient estimation) is under-explained and should be expanded, as it is central to the paper's contribution.

- The noisy-setting comparison (Section 6.2) may be unfair if standard EI (which assumes noiseless observations) is used; clarify whether a noise-aware EI was employed or discuss EI’s limitations under noise.

- Stylistic issue: the abbreviation “IPA” is redefined at line 162, which is unusual.
