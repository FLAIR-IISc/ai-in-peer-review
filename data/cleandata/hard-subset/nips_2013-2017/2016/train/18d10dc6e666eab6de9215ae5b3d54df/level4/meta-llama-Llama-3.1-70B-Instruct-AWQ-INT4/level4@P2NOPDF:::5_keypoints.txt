- Paper proposes a batch Knowledge Gradient (KG) method with a Monte-Carlo-based gradient estimator and shows empirical gains over other batch BO methods.  
- Novelty relative to prior batch KG work (e.g., [1], [2]) is unclear and should be explicitly clarified.  
- Primary claimed contribution appears to be the new gradient estimation method; authors should compare its efficiency and effectiveness to previous batch KG methods via experiments or analysis.  
- Section 5.2 is central to the contribution and needs further elaboration and clarification.  
- Comparison against Expected Improvement (EI) in noisy settings may be unfair or unclear—uncertain whether a noise-aware EI variant was used; results should be discussed in that context.  
- The abbreviation “IPA” is redefined (line 162), creating potential confusion; terminology should be made consistent.  
- Overall the approach is interesting but requires clarifications, additional comparisons, and analysis to strengthen the paper’s contribution.
