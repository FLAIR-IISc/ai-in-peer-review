This paper creates a batch version of the Knowledge Gradient (KG). A Monte-Carlo simulation-based gradient estimation method is proposed to find an optimal batch of design points. Experiments show that the proposed method performs better than other batch Bayesian optimization (BO) methods.

1. To my knowledge, the KG batch of this paper is not a new proposed method. Clarify the contribution of this paper compared to previous KG batch papers like [1] and [2]. In my opinion, its contribution is to estimate the gradient to find an optimal batch.
