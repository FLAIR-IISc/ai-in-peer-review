This paper creates a batch-version of the Knowledge Gradient (KG). A Monte-Carlo simulation-based gradient estimation method is proposed to find an optimal batch of design points. Experiments show that the proposed method performs better than other batch Bayesian optimization (BO) methods.

1. To my knowledge, the batch KG in this paper is not a new proposed method. Clarify the contribution of this paper compared to previous batch KG papers like [1] and [2]. In my opinion, its contribution is to estimate the gradient to find an optimal batch. If so, the efficiency/effectiveness of the proposed method should be compared with previous batch KG papers through testing or possibly mathematical analysis.

2. Section 5.2 should be discussed in more detail, as I believe this section is the core of this paper.

3. Essentially, EI assumes wordless observations. Therefore, it is inappropriate to compare in a word-filled environment. Is any type of word-related EI used? If general EI is used, the results of section 6.2 should be discussed on the grounds that EI does not consider words.
