- Paper proposes a batch Knowledge Gradient (KG) using a Monte Carlo gradient estimator and shows promising empirical performance but needs clarification and improvements.  
- Prior work on batch KG exists; authors must better position their contribution and compare their gradient estimator’s efficiency/effectiveness to previous methods (empirically or analytically).  
- Section 5.2 (central technical contribution) is under-detailed and should be expanded to fully explain the proposed gradient estimation method and implementation.  
- Comparison to Expected Improvement (EI) in the noisy setting may be unfair—clarify whether a noise-aware EI was used and, if not, acknowledge that standard EI is not designed for noisy observations.  
- Editorial: the abbreviation “IPA” is redefined on line 162.
