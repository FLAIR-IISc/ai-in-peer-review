- Paper proposes a batch Bayesian optimization method using Knowledge Gradient with a Monte-Carlo-based gradient estimator and shows promising empirical results.  
- Major concern: novelty unclear—batch KG is not new; authors must clearly state specific contributions and compare against prior batch KG methods.  
- Major concern: core methodology (Section 5.2) lacks sufficient detail—need full derivation, implementation details, and assumptions for the proposed gradient estimator.  
- Major concern: experimental fairness—comparison to EI in the noisy setting may be inappropriate; authors must state which EI variant was used and consider a noise-aware EI baseline.  
- Minor comment: the abbreviation "IPA" is defined twice (line 162); check abbreviation consistency.
