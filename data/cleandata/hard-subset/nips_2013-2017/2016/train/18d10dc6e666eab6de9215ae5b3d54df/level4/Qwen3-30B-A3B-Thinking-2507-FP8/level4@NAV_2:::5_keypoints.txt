- Paper proposes a batch KG (q-KG) using IPA-based gradient estimation and reports it outperforms existing batch BO methods.  
- Main novelty appears to be the efficient gradient estimator, not the batch-KG formulation; authors must clarify novelty vs prior work (Wang et al., Peng) and compare estimator efficiency/effectiveness.  
- Section 5.2 (IPA-based gradient estimation) is under-explained and needs more technical detail and a clear distinction from previous gradient estimation techniques.  
- Comparison to Expected Improvement in noisy settings is questionable—authors should state if a noise-aware EI was used or justify using plain EI for noisy evaluations.  
- The acronym "IPA" is redefined later in the paper (line 162); acronyms should be defined at first use.
