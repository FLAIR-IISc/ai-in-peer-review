- Proposes a batch-version of Knowledge Gradient (q-KG) and a novel IPA-based method to compute its gradient for optimizing batches of design points.  
- Main technical contribution is an efficient gradient estimator for q-KG that enables practical acquisition optimization.  
- Experimental results indicate the proposed method outperforms other batch Bayesian optimization methods.  
- The paper is well-organized and experiments are thorough.  
- Needs clearer comparison to prior batch KG works (e.g., [1], [2]) regarding efficiency and effectiveness.  
- Section 5.2 (gradient computation) is crucial and should include more detailed derivation and implementation details.  
- Comparison in the noisy setting is unfair because EI assumes noiseless observations; authors should use a noise-aware EI variant or discuss this limitation.  
- Minor fixes needed (e.g., unnecessary redefinition of the abbreviation "IPA").  
- Overall positive contribution with potential for wide adoption, pending minor revisions.
