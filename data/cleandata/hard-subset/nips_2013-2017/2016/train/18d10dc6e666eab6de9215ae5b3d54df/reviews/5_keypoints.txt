- Paper derives a batch-version of Knowledge Gradient (KG) and proposes a Monte Carlo gradient estimator to find optimal batches; experiments claim improved performance over other batch BO methods.
- Reviewer questions novelty: batch KG is not new—clarify contribution relative to prior batch KG work (e.g., [1], [2]).
- If main contribution is the gradient estimator, empirically or theoretically compare its efficiency/effectiveness against previous batch KG methods.
- Section 5.2 is central and needs much more detail and elaboration.
- Comparison to Expected Improvement (EI) is problematic because EI assumes noiseless observations; clarify whether a noise-aware EI variant was used and discuss how noise affects the results in Section 6.2.
- Minor issue: the abbreviation “IPA” is redefined at line 162 and should be fixed.
