This paper introduces an end-to-end differentiable memory access scheme that retains the representational power of the original Neural Turing Machine (NTM), while offering significantly improved efficiency when training with very large memory. The authors present a multitude of original concepts that demonstrate technical ingenuity. The problem tackled in this work is well-motivated and effectively addressed. Given the substantial impact of scaling memory systems for neural networks, this paper clearly and concisely presents both the problem and its solution, making for a well-explained and compelling read.
