This paper presents an end-to-end differentiable memory access scheme that retains the representational power of a Neural Turing Machine (NTM) while offering significantly greater efficiency when training with very large memories. The work is founded on several novel concepts of considerable technical merit. The problem of scaling memory systems for neural networks is important, well-motivated, and effectively addressed by the proposed solution. Furthermore, the paper introduces the problem and its solution in a clear, concise, and well-explained manner.
