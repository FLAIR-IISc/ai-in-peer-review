This paper presents an end-to-end differentiable memory access scheme, offering the representational power of the original Neural Turing Machine (NTM) while achieving significant efficiency gains during training with very large memory. The authors introduce several innovative concepts that demonstrate technical brilliance. The problem addressed in the paper is well-motivated and effectively tackled. Given the substantial impact of scaling memory systems for neural networks, the authors clearly and concisely explain the problem and their proposed solution, making the paper well-explained and easy to follow.
