- Proposes an NTM with sparse read/write to reduce large physical memory and backprop costs of standard NTMs while retaining near state-of-the-art performance.
- Paper is well structured and easy to read.
- Recommend adding a brief background on memory-augmented neural networks and clearer explanation of the external memory and its operators.
- Suggest clarifying the intended role of the write operator relative to conventional memories for non-expert readers.
- Concern about fairness of Figure 4: matching physical memory footprint is defensible, but comparing under equal logical memory capacity would better reveal intrinsic performance.
- Praises the algorithm’s impressive performance close to theoretical bounds.
