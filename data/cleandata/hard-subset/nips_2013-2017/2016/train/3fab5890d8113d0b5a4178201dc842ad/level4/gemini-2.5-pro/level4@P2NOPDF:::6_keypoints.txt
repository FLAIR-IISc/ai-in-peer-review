- Proposes a Sparse-Access Neural Turing Machine to reduce memory and computational costs of NTMs while matching state-of-the-art performance.  
- Paper is well-structured and clearly communicates core ideas.  
- Tackles a significant bottleneck in memory-augmented neural networks, making NTMs more practical for real-world use.  
- Experimental results are strong, with performance close to theoretical bounds.  
- Recommend strengthening the background section with an intro to memory-augmented neural networks and a clear explanation of the NTM write operator.  
- Recommend an additional experiment comparing models with the same number of memory locations (not just same physical memory) to separate architectural benefits from memory-size effects.  
- Overall a valuable, elegant contribution that needs minor revisions for accessibility and supplementary analysis.
