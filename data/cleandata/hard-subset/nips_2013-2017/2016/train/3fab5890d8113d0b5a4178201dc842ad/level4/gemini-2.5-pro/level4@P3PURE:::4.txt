This paper introduces an end-to-end differentiable memory access scheme that retains the representational power of the original Neural Turing Machine (NTM) while being significantly more efficient for training with very large memory. The work presents several novel concepts of considerable technical merit. The problem it addresses is well-motivated, and the proposed solution is effective. The impact of scaling memory systems for neural networks is substantial, and this paper presents both the problem and its solution in a clear, concise, and well-explained manner.
