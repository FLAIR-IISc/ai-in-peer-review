This paper introduces an end-to-end differentiable memory access scheme that retains the representational power of the original Neural Turing Machine (NTM) while offering significant efficiency gains during training with very large memory. The authors present a multitude of original concepts that demonstrate technical brilliance. The problem addressed in the paper is well-motivated and effectively tackled. The impact of scaling memory systems for neural networks is substantial, and the authors clearly, concisely, and effectively explain the problem and their proposed solution.
