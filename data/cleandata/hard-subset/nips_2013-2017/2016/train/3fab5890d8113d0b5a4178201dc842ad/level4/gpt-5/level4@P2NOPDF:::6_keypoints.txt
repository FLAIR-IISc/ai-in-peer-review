- Proposes an NTM variant with sparse read/write operations to cut memory footprint and backprop computation.  
- Claims to retain state-of-the-art performance while dramatically reducing resource demands for practical deployment.  
- Paper is well organized and generally easy to follow.  
- Suggest adding a brief background explaining the memory module semantics and how the write operator differs from conventional writes for non-experts.  
- Raises fairness concern about Figure 4: comparisons equalize physical memory usage; also report results matched on logical memory capacity (same number of slots/addresses) to isolate algorithmic vs implementation effects.  
- Overall assessment: compelling approach with strong empirical results and performance near theoretical limits.
