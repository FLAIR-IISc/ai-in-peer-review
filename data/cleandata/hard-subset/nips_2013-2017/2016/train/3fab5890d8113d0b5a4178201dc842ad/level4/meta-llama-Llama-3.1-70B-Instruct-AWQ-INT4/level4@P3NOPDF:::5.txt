The paper introduces a Sparse Access Memory architecture for neural nets augmented with memory, such as Neural Turing Machines and Memory Networks. This innovation allows for remarkable run-time and memory efficiency improvements, achieving up to three orders of magnitude compared to existing neural nets augmented with memory. Furthermore, this architecture appears to enable significant performance improvements in the setting of curriculum learning and in the ability to generalize to sequences that are an order of magnitude longer than those observed in training, from sequences of up to 10,000 in training to sequences of length 200,000 in testing. These results have the potential to be groundbreaking.

The field of neural networks augmented with memory, which began with Neural Turing Machines and Memory Networks, is actively developing. While previous research in this area has yielded interesting results, they have not yet reached a level of practical applicability. However, the drastic efficiency and performance improvements reported in this paper suggest that memory-augmented neural networks are now ready for practical use. This development is likely to have far-reaching implications.

The paper's contributions are substantial, but a discussion of the reasons behind the drastic performance improvements in curriculum learning and the ability to predict sequences with lengths far beyond the training range would strengthen the paper. While run-time and memory efficiency cannot account for these improvements, it is clear that other factors are at play. One possible explanation is that the Sparse Memory Access architecture plays a similar role to that of Sparse Auto-encoders in other situations. The authors' thoughts on this topic, even if preliminary, would be valuable to readers and would provide additional insight into the reported results.

Overall, the paper presents a significant advancement in the field of neural networks augmented with memory, demonstrating impressive efficiency and performance improvements. With some additional analysis and discussion, this paper has the potential to be truly impactful.
