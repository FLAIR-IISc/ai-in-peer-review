This paper presents a novel, end-to-end differentiable memory access scheme that retains the representational capabilities of the original Neural Turing Machine (NTM) while offering significantly improved efficiency during training with extremely large memory. The authors introduce several innovative concepts that demonstrate technical ingenuity. The problem addressed in this work is well-motivated and effectively tackled. The paper clearly articulates the importance of scaling memory systems for neural networks, providing a well-structured and concise explanation of the problem and its proposed solution.
