This paper introduces an end-to-end differentiable memory access scheme that retains the representational power of the original Neural Turing Machine (NTM), while significantly improving efficiency during training with very large memory. The authors present several innovative concepts that demonstrate technical brilliance. The problem they attempt to solve is well-motivated and effectively addressed. The impact of scaling memory systems for neural networks is substantial, and the authors clearly and concisely introduce the problem and its solution, making it easy to follow.
