This work proposes a novel, end-to-end differentiable memory access mechanism that matches the representational capacity of a traditional NTM while providing significant efficiency gains for training with large-scale memory. The paper is built upon a foundation of original and technically sophisticated concepts. The problem being addressed is highly relevant, and the proposed methodology is robust and well-conceived. Scalable memory systems for neural networks are of considerable importance, and this manuscript does an excellent job of presenting both the problem and its solution in a clear, concise, and well-articulated manner.
