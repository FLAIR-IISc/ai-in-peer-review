- Proposes an NTM variant with sparse read/write to reduce prohibitively large memory and backprop costs while retaining comparable performance to state-of-the-art models.
- Paper is well-structured and easy to follow.
- Suggest adding a brief background on memory-augmented neural networks or an explanation of the memory component to help non-expert readers.
- Recommend clarifying the intended/ideal behavior of the write operator, as its differences from conventional memories may be unclear to some readers.
- Raises concern that Figure 4’s comparison (matching physical memory across models) may be unfair; suggests also comparing models using the same memory size for clearer performance differences.
- Notes the proposed algorithm performs surprisingly well and approaches theoretical bounds.
