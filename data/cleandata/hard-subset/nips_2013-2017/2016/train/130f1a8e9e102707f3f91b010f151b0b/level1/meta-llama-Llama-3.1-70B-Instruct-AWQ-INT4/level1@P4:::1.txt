The paper "Learning brain regions via large-scale online structured sparse dictionary learning" proposes a multivariate online dictionary-learning method for obtaining decompositions of brain images with structured and sparse components. The method, dubbed Smooth Sparse Online Dictionary-Learning (Smooth-SODL), combines the standard online dictionary-learning framework with a Laplacian penalty to impose spatial structure on the dictionary atoms. The authors demonstrate the effectiveness of their approach through experiments on a large public dataset, showing improvements over traditional `1-regularized dictionary learning and other state-of-the-art methods.
