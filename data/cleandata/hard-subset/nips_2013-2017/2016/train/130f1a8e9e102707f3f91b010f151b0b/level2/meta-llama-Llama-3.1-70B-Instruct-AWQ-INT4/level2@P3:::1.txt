**Summary of the Paper**

The paper proposes a multivariate online dictionary-learning method, called Smooth Sparse Online Dictionary-Learning (Smooth-SODL), for obtaining decompositions of brain images with structured and sparse components. The method combines a sparsity-inducing `1 penalty with a Laplacian penalty to impose spatial structure on the dictionary atoms. The authors demonstrate the effectiveness of their approach on a large public dataset, showing that it extracts structured and denoised dictionaries that better capture inter-subject variability in small, medium, and large-scale regimes.
