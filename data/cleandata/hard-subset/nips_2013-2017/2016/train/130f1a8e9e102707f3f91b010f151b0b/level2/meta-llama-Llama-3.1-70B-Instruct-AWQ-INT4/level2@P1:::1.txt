**Summary of the Main Ideas**

This paper proposes a multivariate online dictionary-learning method, called Smooth Sparse Online Dictionary-Learning (Smooth-SODL), for obtaining decompositions of brain images with structured and sparse components. The method combines an `1-norm constraint for sparsity and a Sobolev (Laplacian) penalty to impose piece-wise smooth and compact structures. The authors demonstrate the effectiveness of their method on a large public dataset, showing improvements over traditional `1-regularized dictionary learning and state-of-the-art models.
