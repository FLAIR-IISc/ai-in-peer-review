- Proposal: extend dictionary learning models with a Sobolev prior — interesting idea but impact unclear.
- Hard to judge improvement of the proposed algorithm from the paper.
- Main results rely on visual comparison of identified brain “blobs,” which are not clear to an untrained eye.
- Data quality and choice matter greatly; adding smoothness/spatial-consistency penalties unsurprisingly yields smoother blobs.
- With large datasets like the Human Connectome Project, smoothing may not address a real data scarcity problem.
- Reviewer expected consolidation of brain-region differences across subjects and use of inter-subject consistency for validation.
- Presented validation is subjective and does not clearly demonstrate benefit.
