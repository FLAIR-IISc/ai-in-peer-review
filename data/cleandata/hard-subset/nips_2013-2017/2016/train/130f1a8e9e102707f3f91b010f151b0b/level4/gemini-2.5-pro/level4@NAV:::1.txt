This paper introduces a large-scale online dictionary learning framework for the identification of salient brain regions. The research topic is both timely and of significant interest. However, the submission is weakened by the absence of theoretical analysis; the authors do not provide any guarantees for the effectiveness or convergence of the proposed algorithm. Furthermore, the manuscript appears to have been prepared without sufficient care. It suffers from a lack of thorough proofreading, which detracts from the overall quality of the work. A clear example is an editorial comment ("146 XXX: I don't understand the switch between V and v.") that remains in the submitted text, suggesting the draft was not finalized before submission.
