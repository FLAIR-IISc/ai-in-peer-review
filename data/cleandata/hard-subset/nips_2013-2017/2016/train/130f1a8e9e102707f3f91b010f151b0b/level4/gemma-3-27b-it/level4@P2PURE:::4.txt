The paper introduces an under-complete dictionary learning method incorporating combined Sobolev and L1 regularization for identifying functional components in fMRI data. This approach is potentially interesting, with its primary novelty lying in the use of this combined regularization to simultaneously encourage smoothness and sparsity in the learned components. However, the reported results do not demonstrate a substantial improvement over simpler methods, and any gains achieved are not sufficiently justified or clearly articulated. Furthermore, the manuscript appears to have been submitted prematurely; it contains draft comments (e.g., line 146) and is often difficult to follow due to a tedious and occasionally confusing presentation.
