This paper introduces a new regularization technique for matrix factorization, aiming to impose sparsity and smoothness. Although the authors do not provide a proof of the existence of a solution, they present an algorithm to obtain one. Experimental results demonstrate that their approach outperforms the state-of-the-art method. A significant strength of the paper is the introduction of a novel regularizer in the optimization problem. The experimental results showcase the potential of this approach.

However, as acknowledged by the authors, the problem is non-convex, and the existence of a solution remains unproven. The algorithm is primarily validated through experiments, without theoretical guarantees. Furthermore, the writing needs improvement, as exemplified by the unclear sentence on line 146.

In response to the rebuttal, I appreciate the authors' clarification on the convergence of the algorithm. I am willing to revise my rating for Clarity and Presentation from 2 to 3, reflecting the improved understanding provided by the authors.
