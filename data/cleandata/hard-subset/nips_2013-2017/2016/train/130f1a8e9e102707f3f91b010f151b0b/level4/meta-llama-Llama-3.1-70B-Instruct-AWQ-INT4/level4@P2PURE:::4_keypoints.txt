- Proposes under-complete dictionary learning with combined Sobolev/L1 regularization to identify fMRI functional components.  
- Main contribution is applying Sobolev+L1 regularization to promote smoothness and sparsity—somewhat original idea.  
- Results do not show clear or significant improvements over simpler baseline methods.  
- Explanations for any observed improvements are insufficient and underdeveloped.  
- Manuscript quality needs work: contains draft comments (e.g., line 146).  
- Writing is often tedious and sometimes confusing, making the paper hard to follow.
