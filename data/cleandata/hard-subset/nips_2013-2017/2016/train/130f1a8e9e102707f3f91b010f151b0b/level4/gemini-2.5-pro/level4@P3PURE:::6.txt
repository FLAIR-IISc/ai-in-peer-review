This paper introduces a new regularizer for matrix factorization designed to impose both sparsity and smoothness. The introduction of this novel regularization term is a key strength of the paper. Experimentally, the proposed method is shown to be superior to a state-of-the-art approach, demonstrating the potential of this technique.

However, the work has several weaknesses on the theoretical side. As the authors acknowledge, the resulting optimization problem is non-convex. A significant concern is that the paper does not provide a proof for the existence of a solution. Consequently, the proposed algorithm is justified solely by its empirical performance and lacks theoretical backing.

Additionally, the manuscript requires improvements in its writing and clarity. For example, the sentence on line 146 is unclear.

**Update after rebuttal:** I am satisfied with the authors' response regarding the convergence of the algorithm. I have raised my score for Clarity and Presentation from 2 to 3.
