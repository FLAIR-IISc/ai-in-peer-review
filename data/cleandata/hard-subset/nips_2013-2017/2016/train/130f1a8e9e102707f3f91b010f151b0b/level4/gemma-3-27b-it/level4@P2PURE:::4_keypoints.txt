- Proposes an under-complete dictionary learning method using combined Sobolev + L1 regularization for fMRI component identification (novelty in combined regularization).
- The combined regularization is intended to simultaneously enforce smoothness and sparsity in learned components.
- Experimental results do not show substantial improvement over simpler methods, and reported gains are neither well-justified nor clearly explained.
- Manuscript appears prematurely submitted, contains draft comments (e.g., line 146), and is often tedious and confusing to follow.
