This paper proposes an under-complete dictionary learning framework to identify functional components in fMRI data. The primary contribution is the use of a combined Sobolev/L1 regularization to enforce both smoothness and sparsity in the learned components.

While the approach is interesting, the results do not demonstrate a significant improvement over simpler methods; at a minimum, any such advantages are insufficiently explained and justified. Furthermore, the manuscript appears to have been prepared in haste. This is evidenced by remaining draft comments (e.g., on line 146) and the overall quality of the writing, which is often tedious and makes the paper difficult and, at times, confusing to read.
