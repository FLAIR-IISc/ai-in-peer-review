- Augments dictionary-learning with a Sobolev/Laplacian prior on atoms — interesting problem.
- Magnitude of the advance and practical improvement are difficult to assess.
- Main results depend on visual comparisons of brain “blobs” and lack objective evaluation metrics.
- Data properties can affect outcomes as much as the method; adding smoothness/spatial-coherence tends to produce smoother, more compact maps by design.
- Method might be most useful in low-data settings, but the extensive Human Connectome Project data reduces the relevance of scarcity.
- Reviewer expected consolidation of cross-subject regional differences and validation using inter-subject variability/consistency.
- Current validation is largely subjective, so the tangible benefit is unclear.
