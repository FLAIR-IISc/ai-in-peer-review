- Presents an undercomplete dictionary learning framework with combined Sobolev and L1 regularization to identify functional components in fMRI data.  
- Approach is promising.  
- Novelty is limited: essentially dictionary learning with joint Sobolev and L1 penalties to enforce smoothness and sparsity.  
- Results show only modest gains over simpler methods, or the improvement is not clearly demonstrated.  
- Manuscript appears hastily prepared and contains residual draft comments (e.g., line 146).  
- The paper is tedious and at times confusing to read.
