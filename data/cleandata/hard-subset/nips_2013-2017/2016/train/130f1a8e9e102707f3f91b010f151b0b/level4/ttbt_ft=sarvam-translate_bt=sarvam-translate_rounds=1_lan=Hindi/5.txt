This paper focuses on dictionary learning in the application of learning across brain regions. Specifically, the authors propose a large-scale online dictionary learning framework with an additional Sobolev regularization for structure. My main comments are as follows: 1) As far as I am concerned, this paper only expands on online dictionary learning with an additional Sobolev regularization term (J. Maral, JMLR2010). Thus, I feel the novelty is quite weak and fails to align with the NIPS standard. 2) To impose a structure constraint on the learned dictionary, this paper uses Sobolev regularization. However, the detailed definition of "structure" is not very clear. If structure means rarity, then why would the rarity of the dictionary significantly improve your problems? Overall, the motivation for using Sobolev is not clear. 3) I wonder if there is a mistake in equation (1); it should be [V^1,V^2,... ,V^r]^T. 4) More statements and analysis are needed regarding the construction of equation (2). For example, why is the l2 norm applied to the code instead of the l1 norm in (J. Maral, JMLR2010)? 5) What is line 146? 6) The language of this paper needs to be refined. I found several typos in it.
