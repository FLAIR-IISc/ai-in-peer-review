- Proposes an under-complete dictionary learning framework combining Sobolev and L1 regularization to identify functional components in fMRI data.
- Contribution is somewhat original in jointly using Sobolev (smoothness) and L1 (sparsity) penalties to promote smooth, sparse components.
- Approach is interesting but the empirical gains are not shown to be substantially better than simpler methods.
- The paper fails to sufficiently explain the extent and reasons for any improvements over baselines.
- Manuscript quality is poor/rushed (e.g., leftover draft comments at line 146).
- Exposition is tedious and at times confusing, reducing clarity and readability.
