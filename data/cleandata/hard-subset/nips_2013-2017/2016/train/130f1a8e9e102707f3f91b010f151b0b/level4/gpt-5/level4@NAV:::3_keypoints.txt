- Extension of dictionary learning with a Sobolev prior is an interesting contribution.
- Difficult to evaluate the algorithm's impact or improvement.
- Main results depend on visual assessment of blob clarity/consistency, which non-experts cannot easily judge.
- Data quality/quantity matter as much as the algorithm for this class of decompositions.
- Adding smoothness/spatial-consistency penalties unsurprisingly yields smoother blobs.
- Given the large Human Connectome Project dataset, the need for such regularization seems less pressing.
- The paper lacks inter-subject difference/consistency analyses that could validate performance.
- Current validation feels subjective and the practical benefit is not clearly established.
