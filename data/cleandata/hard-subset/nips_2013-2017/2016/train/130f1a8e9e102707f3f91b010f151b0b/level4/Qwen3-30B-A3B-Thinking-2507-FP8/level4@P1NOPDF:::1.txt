

This paper presents a large-scale online dictionary learning framework aimed at identifying meaningful brain regions, a topic with significant potential. However, the work lacks theoretical justification for the proposed algorithm's effectiveness, which is a critical omission. Additionally, the writing appears careless and unproofread; the manuscript retains an unedited reviewer comment ("146 XXX: I don't understand the switch between V and v."), indicating a failure to thoroughly review the draft prior to submission. These issues substantially undermine the paper's credibility and readiness for publication.
