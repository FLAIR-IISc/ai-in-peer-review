This paper presents an online dictionary learning framework on a large scale, aiming to identify interesting brain regions. The subject matter is indeed intriguing. Nevertheless, the authors fail to provide a theoretical analysis that demonstrates the efficacy of their proposed algorithm. Furthermore, the manuscript's overall quality is compromised by apparent carelessness in its preparation. Specifically, the presence of internal comments, such as "146 XXX: I don't understand the switch between V and v," suggests that the authors did not thoroughly review the draft prior to submission.
