- Proposes under-complete dictionary learning with combined Sobolev/L1 regularization to extract fMRI functional components.
- Main contribution: joint Sobolev (smoothness) and L1 (sparsity) penalty is somewhat original in this context.
- Empirical gains over simpler alternatives are limited or not convincingly demonstrated.
- Manuscript fails to clearly explain where and why the method improves over baselines and when the improvements matter.
- Added complexity of the approach is not adequately justified; need to show the combined regularization is essential with explicit comparisons.
- Paper appears rushed with draft comments still present and writing that is often tedious or confusing.
- Requires substantially improved exposition and thorough proofreading to remove artifacts and polish presentation.
