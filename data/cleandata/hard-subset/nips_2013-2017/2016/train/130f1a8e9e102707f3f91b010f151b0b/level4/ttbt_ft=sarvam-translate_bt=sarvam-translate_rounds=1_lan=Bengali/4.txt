This paper proposes under-complete dictionary learning with combined Sobolev/L1 regularization to find functional components in fMRI data. The proposed method seems promising. The main contribution here is the use of dictionary learning with combined Sobolev/L1 regularization to encourage smoothness and sparsity of components. However, the results of the new method do not seem much better than the simple method, or at least, the reason for the improvement is not adequately explained. Furthermore, the manuscript appears to have been written in a rather hasty manner; for example, there are still draft comments (line 146), and it is quite tedious, even sometimes confusing.
