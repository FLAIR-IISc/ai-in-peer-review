This paper proposes a large-scale online dictionary learning framework to identify interesting brain regions. The topic itself is intriguing and holds significant potential for advancements in the field. However, a major limitation of the paper is the lack of theoretical analysis to demonstrate the effectiveness of the proposed algorithm. This omission raises concerns about the method's validity and generalizability.

Furthermore, the writing quality and attention to detail in the paper are subpar. It appears that the authors did not thoroughly review the draft before submission, as evidenced by the presence of internal comments, such as "146 XXX: I don't understand the switch between V and v." This lack of care in the writing process undermines the overall credibility of the work and suggests a need for more rigorous editing and proofreading.
