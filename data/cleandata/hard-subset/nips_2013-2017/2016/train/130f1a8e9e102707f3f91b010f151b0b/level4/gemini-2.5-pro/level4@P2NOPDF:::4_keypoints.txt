- Primary contribution: proposes under-complete dictionary learning for fMRI with combined Sobolev + L1 regularization to promote spatial smoothness and sparsity in atoms.  
- Motivation/novelty: well-motivated and novel use of a Sobolev norm to explicitly model spatial structure in dictionary learning for brain networks.  
- Empirical weakness: evaluation is unconvincing with only marginal gains over simpler baselines and lacks analysis justifying the added complexity.  
- Analysis recommendation: authors should provide thorough experiments/analysis showing conditions where the combined regularization provides clear advantages.  
- Presentation weakness: manuscript contains draft comments and is often hard to follow; exposition of model, algorithm, and experiments needs substantial clarification.  
- Overall judgment: core idea is promising but weak empirical support and poor writing reduce impact; refine experiments and thoroughly revise the paper.
