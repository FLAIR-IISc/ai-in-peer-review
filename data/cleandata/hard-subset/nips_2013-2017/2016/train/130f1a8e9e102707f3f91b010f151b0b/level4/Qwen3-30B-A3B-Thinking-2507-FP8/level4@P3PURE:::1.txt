

This paper introduces a large-scale online dictionary learning framework aimed at identifying meaningful brain regions. While the topic holds significant interest, the work lacks a theoretical analysis demonstrating the algorithm's effectiveness. Furthermore, the manuscript exhibits notable carelessness in preparation, suggesting the authors did not thoroughly proofread the draft prior to submission. For instance, an unremoved reviewer comment—"146 XXX: I don't understand the switch between V and v."—appears verbatim in the final version. These shortcomings substantially undermine the paper's credibility and readiness for publication.
