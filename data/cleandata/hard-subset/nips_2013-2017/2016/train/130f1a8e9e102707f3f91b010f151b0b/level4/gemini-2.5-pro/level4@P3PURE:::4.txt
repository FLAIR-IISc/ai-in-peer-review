This paper proposes an under-complete dictionary learning framework to identify functional components in fMRI data. The novelty of the approach lies in using a combined Sobolev and L1 regularization to encourage both spatial smoothness and sparsity in the learned components.

While the proposed approach is interesting, its empirical evaluation is not convincing. The results do not demonstrate a significant improvement over simpler baseline methods, and any potential advantages of the proposed method are insufficiently explained. This makes it difficult to assess the practical value of the contribution.

Furthermore, the manuscript's presentation is a significant concern. The paper appears to have been prepared in a rush, as evidenced by the presence of draft comments in the text (e.g., line 146). The writing is often difficult to follow, which makes the paper a tedious and at times confusing read.
