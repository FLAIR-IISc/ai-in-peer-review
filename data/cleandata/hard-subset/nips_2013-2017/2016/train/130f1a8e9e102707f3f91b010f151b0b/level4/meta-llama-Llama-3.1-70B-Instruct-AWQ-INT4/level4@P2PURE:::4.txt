The paper proposes an under-complete dictionary learning approach with combined Sobolev/L1 regularization for identifying functional components in fMRI data, which appears to be an interesting concept. The main contribution of this work lies in its application of dictionary learning with combined Sobolev/L1 regularization to promote smoothness and sparsity of the components, a somewhat original idea. However, the results of the proposed approach do not seem to offer significant improvements over simpler methods, and the explanations provided for any observed improvements are insufficient. Furthermore, the manuscript requires refinement, as evidenced by the presence of draft comments (line 146) and a writing style that is often tedious and sometimes confusing, making it challenging to follow.
