This paper proposes a large-scale online dictionary learning framework for identifying interesting brain regions, a topic that holds considerable interest. However, a notable limitation of the paper is the lack of theoretical analysis to demonstrate the effectiveness of the proposed algorithm. Furthermore, the writing quality and attention to detail in the paper are subpar. It appears that the authors did not thoroughly review the draft before submission, as evidenced by the presence of comments such as "146 XXX: I don't understand the switch between V and v," which suggests a need for more rigorous editing and proofreading.
