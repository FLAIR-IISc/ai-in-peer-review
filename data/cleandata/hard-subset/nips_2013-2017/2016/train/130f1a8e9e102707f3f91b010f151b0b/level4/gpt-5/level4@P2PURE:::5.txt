This paper studies dictionary learning for brain region learning, proposing a large-scale online dictionary learning framework augmented with a Sobolev regularization term to enforce structure.

Major comments:
1) The contribution appears to be a straightforward extension of online dictionary learning (Mairal, JMLR 2010) by adding a Sobolev regularization term. As a result, the novelty seems weak and does not meet the NIPS standard.
2) The notion of “structure” is not clearly defined. If “structure” is intended to mean sparsity, please explain why enforcing sparsity in the dictionary itself would yield significant improvements for your problems. Overall, the motivation for using Sobolev regularization is unclear.
3) There may be a mistake in Eq. (1); it should be [V^1, V^2, …, V^r]^T.
4) More explanation and analysis of Eq. (2) are needed. For example, why impose an L2 norm on the codes instead of the L1 norm used in Mairal (JMLR 2010)?
5) What is “line 146”?
6) The language needs substantial refinement; there are many typos.
