This paper proposes an undercomplete dictionary learning approach using a combined Sobolev and L1 regularization to identify functional components in fMRI data. The primary contribution is the novel application of this combined regularization scheme to simultaneously encourage both smoothness and sparsity in the resulting components, which is an interesting direction for this problem.

However, the results presented do not convincingly demonstrate a significant improvement over simpler baseline methods. At the very least, any advantages offered by the proposed approach are insufficiently explained and quantified in the experimental section.

Furthermore, the manuscript itself appears to have been prepared in haste. This is evidenced by the presence of leftover draft comments (e.g., on line 146). More broadly, the writing is often difficult to follow, which makes the paper a tedious and at times confusing read.
