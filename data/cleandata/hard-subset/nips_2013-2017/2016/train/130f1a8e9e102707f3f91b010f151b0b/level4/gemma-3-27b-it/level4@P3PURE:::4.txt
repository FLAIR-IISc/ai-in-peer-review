The paper proposes an under-complete dictionary learning approach with combined Sobolev and L1 regularization to identify functional components in fMRI data. This approach is potentially interesting, with the primary novelty lying in the use of combined Sobolev/L1 regularization to simultaneously encourage smoothness and sparsity of the learned components. However, the results presented do not demonstrate a substantial improvement over simpler methods, and any gains achieved are not sufficiently justified or explained. Furthermore, the manuscript appears somewhat rushed; draft comments remain present (e.g., line 146), and the writing is often tedious and, at times, confusing.
