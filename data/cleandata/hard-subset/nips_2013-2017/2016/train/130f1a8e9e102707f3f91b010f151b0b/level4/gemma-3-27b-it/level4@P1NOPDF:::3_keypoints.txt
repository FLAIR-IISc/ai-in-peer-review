- Paper extends dictionary learning with a Sobolev prior (interesting idea).
- Impact and quantitative improvement of the proposed algorithm are difficult to assess.
- Main results rely on visual comparisons of clarity/consistency of brain regions, which may not be accessible to non-experts.
- Data quality often rivals algorithm choice; smoother-looking regions are unsurprising given smoothness/spatial-consistency penalties.
- Approach might help with limited data, but large datasets like the Human Connectome Project reduce that need.
- Reviewer expected more focus on consolidating inter-subject differences and using inter-subject consistency as validation.
- Current validation is largely subjective, so concrete benefits of the method remain unclear.
