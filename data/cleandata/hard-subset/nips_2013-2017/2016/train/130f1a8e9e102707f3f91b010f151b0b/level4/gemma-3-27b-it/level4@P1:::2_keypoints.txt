- Technical quality rated 3: model and algorithms are clear and follow prior work [16], and rebuttal empirically shows Sobolev prior improves clustering and interpretability.  
- Reviewer requests explicit neuroscientific criteria and quantitative metrics tied to literature to strengthen empirical claims.  
- Novelty: meaningful original contribution by extending [16] with Sobolev priors to encourage spatially structured dictionary atoms.  
- Potential impact: provides an efficient online tool for large-scale neuroimaging; could be applied to segmentation and regional sensing to show broader utility.
