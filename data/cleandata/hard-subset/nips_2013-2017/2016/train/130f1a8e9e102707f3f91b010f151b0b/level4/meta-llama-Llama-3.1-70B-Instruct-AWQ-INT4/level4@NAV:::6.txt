This paper presents a novel regularization technique for matrix factorization, which effectively promotes sparsity and smoothness. The authors provide an algorithm for obtaining a solution, although a formal proof of the solution's existence is not provided. Experimental results demonstrate the superiority of this approach over the current state-of-the-art method. A significant strength of the paper lies in the introduction of a new regularizer in the optimization problem, showcasing the approach's potential. However, as acknowledged by the authors, the problem is non-convex, and the existence of a solution remains unproven. The algorithm's validity relies solely on experimental evidence. Furthermore, the writing requires improvement, as evident in line 146. Following the rebuttal, I am satisfied with the authors' clarification on the algorithm's convergence. As a result, I am willing to revise my rating for Clarity and Presentation from 2 to 3.
