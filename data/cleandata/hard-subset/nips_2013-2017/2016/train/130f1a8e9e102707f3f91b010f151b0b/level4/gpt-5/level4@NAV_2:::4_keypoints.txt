Presents an under-complete dictionary-learning framework for fMRI that combines Sobolev (Laplacian) and L1 regularization.  
Main, somewhat novel contribution is applying a joint Sobolev/L1 penalty to enforce both smoothness and sparsity in learned components.  
Approach is appealing in concept.  
Empirical benefits over simpler baselines are limited or not clearly substantiated.  
Manuscript seems hastily prepared (residual draft remarks) and the presentation is often laborious and confusing.
