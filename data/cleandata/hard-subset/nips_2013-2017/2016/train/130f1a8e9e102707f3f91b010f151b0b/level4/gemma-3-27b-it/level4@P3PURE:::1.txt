This paper proposes a large-scale online dictionary learning framework for identifying brain regions of interest. The topic is compelling; however, the manuscript lacks theoretical analysis to substantiate the effectiveness of the proposed algorithm. Furthermore, the writing quality raises concerns, suggesting insufficient attention to detail prior to submission. Specifically, internal comments – such as “146 XXX: I don't understand the switch between V and v.” – remain present in the final draft, indicating a lack of careful review.
