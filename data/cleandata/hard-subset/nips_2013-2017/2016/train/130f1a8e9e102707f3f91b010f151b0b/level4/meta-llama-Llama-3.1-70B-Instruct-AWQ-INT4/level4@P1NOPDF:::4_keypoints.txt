- Proposes under-complete dictionary learning with combined Sobolev/L1 regularization to identify fMRI functional components.  
- Leverages combined Sobolev/L1 regularization to promote smoothness and sparsity, which is a somewhat original contribution.  
- Experimental results do not show significant improvements over simpler baseline methods.  
- Manuscript presentation is unclear and fails to clearly quantify or explain the extent of any improvement.  
- Paper appears hastily prepared, containing draft comments and a tedious, sometimes confusing writing style.
