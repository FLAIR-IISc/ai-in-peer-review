

The paper introduces a novel regularization for matrix factorization to enforce sparsity and smoothness. A significant strength lies in the proposal of a new regularizer within the optimization framework. Empirical results demonstrate superior performance compared to state-of-the-art methods, highlighting the promise of the approach. However, the paper acknowledges the problem is non-convex and fails to establish the existence of a solution. The algorithmic approach is solely supported by experimental validation without theoretical guarantees. Additionally, the writing requires improvement (e.g., line 146). Following the authors' rebuttal, I am satisfied with their explanation regarding the algorithm's convergence properties and am willing to upgrade my clarity and presentation rating from 2 to 3.
