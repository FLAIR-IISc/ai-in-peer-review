This paper focuses on dictionary learning in the application of learning regions of the brain. Specifically, the authors propose a large-scale online dictionary learning framework with an additional Sobolev regularization for the structure. My main comments are as follows: 1) In my opinion, this paper only extends the online dictionary learning (J. Mearle, JMLR2010) with an additional Sobolev regularization term. Therefore, I think the novelty is quite weak and it fails to meet NIPS standards. 2) To impose structural constraints on the learning dictionary, this paper uses Sobolev regularization. However, the detailed definition of "structure" is not very clear. If structure means rarity, then why would the rarity of the dictionary itself significantly improve your problems? Overall, the purpose of using Sobolev is not clear. 3) I am wondering if there is any error in Eq.(1); it should be [V^1,V^2,... ,V^r]^T. 4) Further statements and analysis are needed regarding the structure of Eq.(2). For example, why is the l2 norm imposed on the codes instead of the l1 norm in (J. Mearle, JMLR2010)? 5) What is line number 146? 6) The language of this paper needs to be refined. I found many typos in it.
