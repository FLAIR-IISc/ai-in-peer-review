This paper presents a novel regularization technique for matrix factorization designed to encourage both sparsity and smoothness in the resulting factors. While the authors demonstrate a practical algorithm for finding solutions, a theoretical guarantee of solution existence is currently lacking. The experimental evaluation convincingly shows performance improvements over existing state-of-the-art methods, and the introduction of this new regularizer represents a significant contribution. The reported results suggest promising potential for this approach. However, the authors acknowledge the non-convex nature of the optimization problem, and the algorithm’s validity is currently supported primarily by empirical evidence. The manuscript would benefit from improved clarity and polish – for example, around line 146. Following the rebuttal, I am satisfied with the authors’ response regarding the algorithm’s convergence properties and will revise my score for Clarity and Presentation from 2 to 3.
