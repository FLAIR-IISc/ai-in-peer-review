- Topic is interesting and relevant to neuroimaging.  
- Proposes a multivariate online dictionary-learning framework to identify brain regions.  
- Model combines an l1 sparsity constraint with a Sobolev (Laplacian) penalty for structured components.  
- Demonstrated on a large public dataset and reported to outperform state-of-the-art in explained variance and stability.  
- No theoretical analysis provided to justify algorithm effectiveness or convergence.  
- Lacks a clear, concise introduction and motivation for why the method is needed.  
- Results analysis is shallow; implications and potential applications are not discussed.  
- Writing quality is poor, with unclear phrasing and grammatical errors.  
- Careless attention to detail (e.g., reviewer comment left in the submitted text).  
- References to figures/tables that are missing and other presentation issues.  
- Overall: method has potential but the paper requires significant revisions.
