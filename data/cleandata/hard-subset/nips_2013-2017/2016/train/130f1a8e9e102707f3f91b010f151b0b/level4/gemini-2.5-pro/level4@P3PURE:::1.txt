This paper proposes a large-scale online dictionary learning framework to identify significant brain regions. The topic is of considerable interest. However, the paper has significant weaknesses. A primary concern is the absence of any theoretical analysis to justify the effectiveness of the proposed algorithm.

Furthermore, the manuscript suffers from a lack of careful proofreading, which detracts from the quality of the submission. For instance, an internal editorial comment ("146 XXX: I don't understand the switch between V and v.") has been left in the submitted text, suggesting that the draft was not reviewed carefully before submission.
