The paper proposes an under-complete dictionary learning approach with combined Sobolev/L1 regularization for identifying functional components in fMRI data, which appears to be an interesting concept. The authors' somewhat original contribution lies in applying dictionary learning with combined Sobolev/L1 regularization to promote smoothness and sparsity of the components. However, the results obtained using this new approach do not seem to significantly outperform simpler methods, and the improvement, if any, is not adequately explained. Furthermore, the manuscript appears to be incomplete, as evidenced by the presence of draft comments (line 146), and its overall quality is compromised by tedious and sometimes confusing writing.
