This paper introduces a new regularization term for matrix factorization that promotes both sparsity and smoothness. Although the authors do not establish the existence of a solution, they do present an algorithm for obtaining one. Empirically, the method appears to outperform state-of-the-art approaches.

Strengths:
- The introduction of a new regularizer within the optimization framework is a clear contribution.
- The experimental results indicate promising performance and demonstrate the potential of the approach.

Weaknesses/Concerns:
- As the authors note, the optimization problem is non-convex. However, the paper does not provide a proof of existence of a solution or other theoretical guarantees.
- The algorithm is supported only by empirical evidence; stronger theoretical backing (e.g., existence or convergence guarantees) would significantly strengthen the contribution.
- The writing needs improvement for clarity and readability (e.g., see line 146).
