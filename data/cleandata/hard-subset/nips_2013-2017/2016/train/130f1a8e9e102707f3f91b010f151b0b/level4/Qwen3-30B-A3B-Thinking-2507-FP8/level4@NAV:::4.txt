

The paper presents an interesting approach using under-complete dictionary learning with combined Sobolev/L1 regularization to identify functional components in fMRI data. While the integration of Sobolev and L1 regularization to promote smoothness and sparsity in the components constitutes a somewhat novel contribution, the empirical results fail to demonstrate substantial improvement over simpler baseline methods. The claimed advantages of the proposed technique remain insufficiently substantiated and lack clear quantitative justification. Additionally, the manuscript exhibits several issues indicative of haste, including persistent draft comments (e.g., line 146), and suffers from readability challenges that hinder comprehension.
