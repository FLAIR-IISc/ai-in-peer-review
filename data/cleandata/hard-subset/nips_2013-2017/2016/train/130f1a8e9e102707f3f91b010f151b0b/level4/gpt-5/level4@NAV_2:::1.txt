This manuscript introduces a scalable online dictionary-learning approach to identify meaningful brain regions. It presents a large-scale online framework for discovering region-level structure in neuroimaging via structured sparse dictionary learning. The topic is indeed compelling. However, the paper does not provide theoretical analysis to substantiate the effectiveness of the proposed algorithm. Moreover, the writing suggests insufficient care in preparation. It appears the draft was not carefully proofread before submission. For example, an editorial note remains in the text: “146 XXX: I don't understand the switch between V and v.”
