This paper introduces a new regularization technique for matrix factorization, aiming to impose sparsity and smoothness. Although the authors do not provide a proof of the existence of a solution, they present an algorithm for obtaining one. Experimental results demonstrate that their approach outperforms the state-of-the-art method. A significant strength of the paper is the introduction of a novel regularizer in the optimization problem, and the experimental results showcase the potential of this approach.

However, as the authors acknowledge, the problem is non-convex, and the existence of a solution is not guaranteed. The algorithm is primarily supported by experimental results, rather than theoretical analysis. Additionally, there are some issues with the writing, such as the unclear sentence on line 146.

After considering the authors' rebuttal, I am satisfied with their clarification on the convergence of the algorithm. I am willing to revise my rating for Clarity and Presentation from 2 to 3, reflecting the improved understanding of the paper's content.
