The paper introduces an under-complete dictionary learning framework that combines Sobolev and L1 regularization to identify functional components in fMRI data. The approach is interesting. The somewhat original contribution lies in employing dictionary learning with joint Sobolev/L1 regularization to promote both smoothness and sparsity of the components. However, the results do not appear substantially better than those of simpler methods, or at least the extent and reasons for any improvements are not sufficiently explained. Moreover, the manuscript feels rushed—for instance, draft comments remain (line 146)—and the exposition is often tedious and at times confusing.
