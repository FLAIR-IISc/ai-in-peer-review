- Extends dictionary learning with a Sobolev prior — an interesting problem to explore.  
- Impact and improvement of the proposed algorithm are difficult to assess.  
- Main results depend on visual comparison of blob clarity/consistency, which may be unclear to untrained observers.  
- Data quality can be as important or more important than the algorithm for this type of decomposition.  
- Adding smoothness and spatial-consistency penalties unsurprisingly yields smoother blobs.  
- With large datasets like the Human Connectome Project, the need to isolate regions due to limited data is less compelling.  
- The paper misses using inter-subject differences/consistency as an objective validation method.  
- Validation is subjective and the overall benefits of the approach remain unclear.
