- Introduces a novel regularizer that jointly enforces sparsity and spatial smoothness on matrix-factorization components.  
- Well-motivated for neuroimaging applications where components should be localized and spatially coherent.  
- Presents an online algorithm to solve the resulting optimization problem.  
- Experimental evaluation on fMRI data shows superior results compared to state-of-the-art methods.  
- Main weakness: no theoretical analysis or formal proof of solution existence for the non-convex optimization.  
- Writing quality needs improvement; manuscript would benefit from native-English proofreading.  
- Post-rebuttal: authors’ clarification about algorithm convergence satisfactorily addressed concerns, prompting a raised clarity score.
