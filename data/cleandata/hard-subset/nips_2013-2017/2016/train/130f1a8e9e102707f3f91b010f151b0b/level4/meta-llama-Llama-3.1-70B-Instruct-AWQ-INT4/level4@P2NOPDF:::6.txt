This paper introduces a novel regularization technique for matrix factorization, aiming to impose sparsity and smoothness. Although the authors do not provide a formal proof of the solution's existence, they present an algorithm for obtaining a solution. Experimental results demonstrate the superiority of their approach over the state-of-the-art method. A significant strength of the paper lies in the introduction of a new regularizer in the optimization problem. The experimental results showcase the potential of this approach.

However, as the authors acknowledge, the problem is non-convex, and the existence of a solution remains unproven. The algorithm is primarily validated through experimental results, rather than theoretical guarantees. Furthermore, the writing requires improvement, as evident in line 146.

After considering the authors' rebuttal, I am satisfied with their explanation of the algorithm's convergence. As a result, I am willing to revise my rating for Clarity and Presentation from 2 to 3.
