The paper proposes a new regularization for matrix factorization/dictionary learning that jointly enforces sparsity and smoothness, and provides an algorithm to obtain a solution. Although the existence of a solution is not established, the computational procedure is clearly described. Experiments indicate that the method outperforms state-of-the-art baselines. The introduction of this regularizer within the optimization objective is a notable strength, and the experimental results demonstrate the approach’s promise. However, as the authors note, the problem is non-convex; the existence of a solution is not proved, and the algorithm is supported primarily by empirical evidence.
