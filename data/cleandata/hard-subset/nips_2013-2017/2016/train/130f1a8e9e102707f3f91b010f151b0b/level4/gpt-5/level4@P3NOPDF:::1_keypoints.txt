- Strength: problem relevance to ML and neuroscience.
- Strength: focus on scalability for large neuroimaging datasets.
- Strength: potential to yield interpretable structures useful for science and prediction.

- Weakness: unclear methodological formulation—objective, constraints, regularizers, and update rules not well defined.
- Weakness: vague operational definition of “interesting brain regions” and lack of validation procedure.
- Weakness: insufficient empirical validation—unclear datasets, baselines, metrics, statistical significance, and sensitivity analyses.
- Weakness: lack of systematic scalability evaluation (runtime/memory vs. sample size, dimensionality, number of atoms).

- Request: detail datasets, preprocessing, evaluation metrics, comparisons to standard baselines, and quantitative/qualitative results.
- Request: unify and clarify notation, resolve V vs v inconsistency, and remove placeholder comments.
