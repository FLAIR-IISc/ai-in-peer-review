The paper presents an undercomplete dictionary learning framework with combined Sobolev/L1 regularization to identify functional components in fMRI data. The approach is promising. The limited novelty stems from employing dictionary learning with joint Sobolev and L1 penalties to enforce smoothness and sparsity of the components. However, the results show only modest gains over simpler methods, or at least the improvement is not adequately clarified. Additionally, the manuscript appears hastily prepared, with residual draft comments (line 146), and it is a rather tedious and at times confusing read.
