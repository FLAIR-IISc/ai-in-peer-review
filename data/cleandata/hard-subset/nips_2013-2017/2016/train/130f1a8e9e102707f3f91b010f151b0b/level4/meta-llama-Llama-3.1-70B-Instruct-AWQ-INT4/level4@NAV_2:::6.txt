Summary of the paper:
The authors propose a multivariate online dictionary-learning method for obtaining decompositions of brain images with structured and sparse components. The method combines the standard online dictionary-learning framework with a Laplacian penalty to impose spatial structure. The authors demonstrate the effectiveness of their approach through experiments on brain data and show that it extracts structured and denoised dictionaries that are more interpretable and better capture inter-subject variability.
