This paper proposes a large-scale online dictionary learning framework to identify interesting brain regions. The topic itself is intriguing, and the potential applications in neuroscience are substantial. However, a major limitation of the paper is the lack of theoretical analysis to demonstrate the effectiveness of the proposed algorithm. This omission makes it challenging to assess the method's validity and generalizability.

Furthermore, the writing quality and attention to detail in the paper are subpar. It appears that the authors did not thoroughly proofread the draft before submission. For instance, a comment such as "146 XXX: I don't understand the switch between V and v" was overlooked and remains in the paper. This lack of care in the writing process detracts from the overall credibility of the work and raises concerns about the authors' diligence in ensuring the accuracy and clarity of their presentation.
