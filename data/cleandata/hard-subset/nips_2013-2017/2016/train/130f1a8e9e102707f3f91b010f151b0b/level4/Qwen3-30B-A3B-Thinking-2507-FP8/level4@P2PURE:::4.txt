

The paper proposes undercomplete dictionary learning with combined Sobolev/L1 regularization to identify functional components in fMRI data. The approach appears promising, with a somewhat original contribution in applying dictionary learning with combined Sobolev/L1 regularization to enforce smoothness and sparsity in the components. However, the results of the proposed method do not demonstrate significant improvements over simpler baseline approaches, and the marginal gains, if present, are insufficiently justified. Additionally, the manuscript exhibits signs of being rushed; for instance, draft comments remain visible (line 146), and the text is often tedious and occasionally confusing to read.
