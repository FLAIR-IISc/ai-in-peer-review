This paper proposes a large-scale online dictionary learning framework for discovering interesting regions of the brain. The subject itself is very interesting. However, no theoretical analysis has been provided to demonstrate the effectiveness of the proposed algorithm. Furthermore, the authors do not seem serious about writing the paper. It appears that the authors did not carefully read the draft before submitting it. For example, comments like "146 XXX: I don't understand the switch between V and v" are still present in the paper.
