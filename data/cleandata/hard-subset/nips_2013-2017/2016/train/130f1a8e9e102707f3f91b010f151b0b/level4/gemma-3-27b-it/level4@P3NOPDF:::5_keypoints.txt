- Paper proposes large-scale online dictionary learning with a Sobolev regularizer for brain-region analysis but reviewer questions acceptance.
- Main concern: contribution is incremental—adds Sobolev term to existing online dictionary learning (Mairal et al. 2010) with limited novelty.
- Motivation for Sobolev regularization is unclear; “structure” is vaguely defined and not tied convincingly to brain-region data.
- Unclear why sparsity on the dictionary (via Sobolev) is needed for this application or what data characteristics justify it.
- Equation (1) likely has a typographical error: matrix should be transposed ([V1, V2, …, Vr]^T).
- Equation (2) needs more derivation/justification; choice of l2-norm on codes (vs. common l1-norm) must be explained and its effects discussed.
- Line 146 reference is unclear/incomplete and needs fixing.
- Paper has many typos and language issues; requires thorough proofreading and polishing.
