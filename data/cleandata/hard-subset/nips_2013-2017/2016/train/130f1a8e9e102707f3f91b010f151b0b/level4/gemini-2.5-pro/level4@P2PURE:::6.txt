This paper introduces a new regularizer for matrix factorization designed to enforce both sparsity and smoothness. The introduction of this novel regularizer is a significant contribution, and the experimental results demonstrate the potential of the approach, showing superior performance compared to state-of-the-art methods.

However, the paper has notable theoretical weaknesses. As the authors acknowledge, the optimization problem is non-convex, yet they do not provide a proof for the existence of a solution. Consequently, the proposed algorithm is justified solely by its empirical performance and lacks theoretical backing.

Additionally, the quality of the writing needs improvement for clarity (e.g., line 146).

Update after rebuttal: I am satisfied with the authors' response regarding the convergence of the algorithm. I have raised my score for Clarity and Presentation from 2 to 3.
