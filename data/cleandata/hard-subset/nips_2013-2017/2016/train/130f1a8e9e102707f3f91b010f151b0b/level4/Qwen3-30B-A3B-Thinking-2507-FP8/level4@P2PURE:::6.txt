

A novel regularization for matrix factorization is introduced to enforce sparsity and smoothness. While the existence of a solution is not theoretically established, the authors present an algorithm for obtaining a solution. Experimental results demonstrate superior performance compared to state-of-the-art methods, highlighting the potential of the approach. The introduction of a new regularizer within the optimization problem constitutes a significant strength of the paper. However, as acknowledged by the authors, the problem is non-convex, and the existence of a solution remains unproven. The algorithm is solely supported by experimental evidence. Additionally, the writing requires improvement (e.g., line 146). Following the rebuttal, I am satisfied with the authors' explanation of the algorithm at the conference and am willing to update my clarity and presentation rating from 2 to 3.
