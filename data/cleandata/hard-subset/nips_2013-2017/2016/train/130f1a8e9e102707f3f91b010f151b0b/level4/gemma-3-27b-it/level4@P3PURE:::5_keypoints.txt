1) Novelty concern: paper mainly extends existing online dictionary learning by adding a Sobolev term, raising doubts about sufficient novelty for NIPS.  
2) Unclear motivation/definition of "structure": the exact structural property enforced by Sobolev regularization is not well-defined and its benefit (e.g., sparsity) is not compellingly justified.  
3) Possible typo/error in Eq.(1): expression likely needs transposition to [V1, V2, …, Vr]^T.  
4) Eq.(2) needs more justification: unclear why an l2 norm on codes is used instead of the l1 norm from Mairal (JMLR 2010).  
5) Line 146 is unclear and requires revision/clarification.  
6) Language and presentation issues: many typos and grammatical errors; manuscript needs substantial proofreading and editing.
