This paper presents a large-scale online dictionary learning framework aimed at identifying interesting brain regions. The topic is compelling and relevant.

However, the paper provides no theoretical analysis to demonstrate the effectiveness of the proposed algorithm. Without guarantees, analysis, or discussion of convergence and sample complexity, it is difficult to assess the method’s reliability and generality.

Additionally, the manuscript appears to have been prepared carelessly. It seems the authors did not thoroughly proofread the draft before submission. For example, an internal comment remains in the text: “Line 146: XXX: I don’t understand the switch between V and v.” Such issues detract from the paper’s professionalism and make the exposition hard to follow.

Overall, the idea is promising, but the paper requires theoretical justification and careful editing.
