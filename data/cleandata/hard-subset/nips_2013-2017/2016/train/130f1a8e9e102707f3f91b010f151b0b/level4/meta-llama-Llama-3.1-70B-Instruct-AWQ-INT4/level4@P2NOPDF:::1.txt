This paper proposes a large-scale online dictionary learning framework aimed at identifying interesting brain regions, a topic that holds considerable promise. However, the authors fail to provide a theoretical analysis that demonstrates the effectiveness of their proposed algorithm, which is a notable omission. Furthermore, the overall quality of the writing suggests a lack of rigor and attention to detail. Specifically, the presence of comments such as "146 XXX: I don't understand the switch between V and v" within the text indicates that the manuscript was not thoroughly reviewed before submission, undermining the credibility of the work.
