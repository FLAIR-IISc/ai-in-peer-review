The paper proposes under-complete dictionary learning with combined Sobolev/L1 regularization to identify functional components in fMRI data. The proposed approach appears interesting, as it leverages dictionary learning with combined Sobolev/L1 regularization to promote smoothness and sparsity of the components, a somewhat original contribution. However, the results of the new approach do not seem to offer significant improvements over simpler methods. Furthermore, the manuscript's presentation of these results is often unclear, failing to adequately explain the extent of the improvement. Additionally, the manuscript appears to have been prepared in haste, as evidenced by the presence of draft comments (line 146) and a writing style that is sometimes tedious and confusing to follow.
