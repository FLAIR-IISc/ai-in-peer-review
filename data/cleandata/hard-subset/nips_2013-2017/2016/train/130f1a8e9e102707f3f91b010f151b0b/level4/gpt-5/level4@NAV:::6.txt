The paper introduces a new regularization for matrix factorization to enforce sparsity and smoothness. Although the existence of a solution is not established, an algorithm for obtaining a solution is provided. Experiments demonstrate that the method outperforms the state-of-the-art. Introducing a new regularizer into the optimization problem is a strong aspect of the work. The experimental results indicate the approach’s potential. However, as the authors note, the problem is non-convex. The existence of a solution is not proven. The algorithm is supported solely by empirical evidence.
