The paper proposes a new regularization scheme for matrix factorization to enforce sparsity and smoothness. Although the existence of a solution is not established, the paper presents an algorithm to obtain one. Empirically, the method outperforms state-of-the-art approaches. The introduction of a new regularizer within the optimization problem is a strong aspect of the work, and the experimental results demonstrate the approach’s potential.
