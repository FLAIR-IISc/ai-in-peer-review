This paper presents a novel regularization technique for matrix factorization, designed to encourage both sparsity and smoothness in the resulting factorization. While the authors demonstrate a practical algorithm for finding solutions, a theoretical guarantee of solution existence is currently lacking. The experimental validation convincingly shows performance improvements over existing state-of-the-art methods, and the introduction of this new regularizer represents a significant contribution. The experimental results highlight the promise of this approach; however, the authors acknowledge the non-convex nature of the problem, and the algorithm's efficacy is currently supported primarily through empirical evidence. The manuscript would benefit from improved clarity in certain areas, for example, around line 146. Following the rebuttal, I am satisfied with the authors’ response regarding the algorithm’s convergence properties, and I will revise my rating for Clarity and Presentation from 2 to 3.
