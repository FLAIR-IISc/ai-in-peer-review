This paper introduces a method for identifying functional components in fMRI data using under-complete dictionary learning, guided by a joint Sobolev and L1 regularization scheme. The primary contribution lies in applying this hybrid regularization to simultaneously encourage smoothness and sparsity in the learned components, which is a conceptually sound approach.

However, the empirical results presented do not demonstrate a significant advantage over simpler baseline methods. At a minimum, the paper fails to sufficiently articulate and substantiate the benefits of the proposed technique. Furthermore, the manuscript's presentation is weak. It reads as a premature draft, evidenced by remaining author comments (e.g., line 146), and its overall lack of clarity makes it a challenging and sometimes confusing read.
