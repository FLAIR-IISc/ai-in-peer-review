This paper presents a large-scale online dictionary learning framework for identifying significant brain regions. While the topic itself is of considerable interest, the submission has several major weaknesses.

First, the paper lacks any theoretical analysis to justify the effectiveness of the proposed algorithm. Second, the manuscript suffers from a notable lack of care in its preparation, suggesting it was not carefully proofread before submission. This is evidenced by the inclusion of an unresolved internal comment in the final draft: "146 XXX: I don't understand the switch between V and v."
