This paper introduces an under-complete dictionary learning method incorporating combined Sobolev and L1 regularization, applied to the problem of identifying functional components within fMRI data. The approach is potentially valuable, with the novelty stemming from the use of this combined regularization to simultaneously promote smoothness and sparsity in the learned components. However, the reported results do not demonstrate a substantial performance gain over more established, simpler methods, and the extent of any improvement is not convincingly justified. Furthermore, the manuscript would benefit from further refinement; its presentation currently feels somewhat hurried, containing draft-level comments (e.g., line 146) and proving challenging to follow in places due to its density and occasionally unclear phrasing.
