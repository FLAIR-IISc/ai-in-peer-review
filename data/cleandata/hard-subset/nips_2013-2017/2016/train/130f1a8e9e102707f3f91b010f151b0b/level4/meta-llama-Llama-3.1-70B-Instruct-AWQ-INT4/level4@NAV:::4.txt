This paper introduces an under-complete dictionary learning approach with combined Sobolev/L1 regularization for identifying functional components in fMRI data, which presents an intriguing concept. The unique aspect of this work lies in its application of dictionary learning with combined Sobolev/L1 regularization to promote component smoothness and sparsity. However, the results obtained from this novel method do not appear to offer substantial improvements over simpler alternatives, and the explanations provided for any observed enhancements are inadequate. Furthermore, the manuscript's overall quality is compromised by its rushed preparation, as evidenced by the presence of draft comments (line 146) and a writing style that is often dry and confusing.
