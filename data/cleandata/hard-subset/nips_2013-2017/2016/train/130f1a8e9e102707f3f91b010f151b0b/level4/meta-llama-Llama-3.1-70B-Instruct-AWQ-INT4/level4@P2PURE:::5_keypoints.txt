2. The paper does not clearly define what “structure” means, nor convincingly justify why Sobolev regularization (or dictionary sparsity) is the appropriate/beneficial structural constraint.

4. Eq. (2) needs more derivation and justification, in particular why the codes use an l2 norm instead of the l1 norm used in the Mairal (2010) baseline.

5. The text at line 146 is unclear and requires clarification.

6. The manuscript’s language and writing quality need improvement — numerous typos and stylistic issues reduce readability.
