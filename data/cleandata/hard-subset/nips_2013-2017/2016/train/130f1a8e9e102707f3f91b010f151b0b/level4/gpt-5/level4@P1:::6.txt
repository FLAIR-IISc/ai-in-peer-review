This paper introduces a new regularization for matrix factorization that enforces both sparsity and smoothness in the learned components. Although the paper does not prove the existence of a solution, it presents an algorithm for obtaining one and demonstrates experimentally that it outperforms state-of-the-art approaches.

Strengths:
- The introduction of a new regularizer to jointly impose sparsity and smoothness is a clear contribution.
- The experimental results are promising and show the potential of the approach.

Weaknesses and concerns:
- As the authors note, the optimization problem is non-convex. The paper does not establish the existence of a solution, and the algorithm is supported solely by empirical results rather than theoretical guarantees.
- The writing needs improvement to enhance clarity; for example, the sentence around line 146 is unclear.
