The paper proposes under-complete dictionary learning with combined Sobolev/L1 regularization to identify functional components in fMRI data. The approach appears interesting, with a somewhat original contribution being the use of dictionary learning with combined Sobolev/L1 regularization to promote smoothness and sparsity of the components. However, the results of the proposed approach do not seem to offer significant improvements over simpler methods, and the explanation for any improvement is insufficient. Furthermore, the manuscript appears to be in a rushed state, as evidenced by the presence of draft comments (line 146) and its overall clarity, which is sometimes compromised by tedious and confusing writing.
