This paper focuses on dictionary learning in the application of learning in brain regions. Specifically, the author proposes a large-scale online dictionary learning framework with additional Sobolev regularization for structure. My main comments are as follows: 1) As far as I am concerned, this paper only expands online dictionary learning with an additional Sobolev regularization term (J. Maral, JMLR2010). Thus, I think the novelty is quite weak and fails to align with the NIPS standard. 2) To impose a structure constraint on the learned dictionary, this paper uses Sobolev regularization. However, the detailed definition of "structure" is not very clear. If structure means rarity, why would dictionary rarity significantly improve your problems? Overall, the motivation for using Sobolev is not clear. 3) I wonder if there is a mistake in equation (1); it should be [V^1,V^2,... ,V^r]^T. 4) More statements and analysis are needed regarding the construction of equation (2). For example, why is the l2 criterion applied to the code instead of the l1 criterion in (J. Maral, JMLR2010)? 5) What is line 146? 6) The language of this paper needs to be refined. I found many typos in it.
