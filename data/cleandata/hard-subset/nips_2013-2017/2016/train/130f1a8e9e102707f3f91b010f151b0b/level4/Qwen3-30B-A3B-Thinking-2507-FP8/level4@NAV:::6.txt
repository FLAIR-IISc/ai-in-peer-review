

A novel regularization technique for matrix factorization is proposed to enforce sparsity and smoothness. While the existence of a solution is not theoretically established, the authors provide a concrete algorithm for obtaining a solution. Experimental results demonstrate that the approach outperforms state-of-the-art methods. The introduction of a new regularizer within the optimization framework constitutes a significant strength of the paper, and the experimental findings highlight the method's potential. However, as acknowledged by the authors, the problem is non-convex, and the solution existence remains unproven. The algorithm's validity is primarily supported by experimental evidence. Additionally, the manuscript requires improvements in clarity, as noted at line 146. Following the rebuttal, I am satisfied with the authors' explanation regarding the algorithm's convergence and am willing to upgrade the Clarity and Presentation rating from 2 to 3.
