A novel regularization technique for matrix factorization is presented, designed to encourage both sparsity and smoothness in the learned factors. While the paper does not include a proof of solution existence, it details an algorithm for obtaining a solution. Experimental results demonstrate performance superior to state-of-the-art methods. The introduction of this new regularizer represents a strong contribution. The experiments showcase the potential of the proposed approach; however, the authors acknowledge the non-convexity of the optimization problem, and the lack of a theoretical guarantee on solution existence means the algorithm’s efficacy is currently solely supported by empirical evidence.
