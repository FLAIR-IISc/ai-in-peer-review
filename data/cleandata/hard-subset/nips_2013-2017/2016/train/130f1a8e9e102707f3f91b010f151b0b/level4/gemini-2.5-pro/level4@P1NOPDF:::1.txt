This paper proposes a large-scale online dictionary learning framework for identifying interesting brain regions. The topic is timely and of considerable interest.

However, the manuscript has two major weaknesses in its current form. The first is the complete lack of theoretical analysis. The authors do not provide any convergence guarantees or other formal results to support the effectiveness of their proposed algorithm, which is a significant omission.

Furthermore, the paper suffers from a severe lack of polish and careful proofreading. The quality of the manuscript suggests it was hastily prepared and not thoroughly reviewed by the authors prior to submission. A particularly striking example of this is an unresolved internal comment that remains in the text: "146 XXX: I don't understand the switch between V and v.". The presence of such an error significantly undermines the professionalism and credibility of the work.
