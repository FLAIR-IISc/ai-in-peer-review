This paper introduces a new regularizer for matrix factorization that promotes both sparsity and smoothness. While the paper does not establish the existence of a solution, it does present an algorithm to obtain one. Empirically, the method outperforms state-of-the-art approaches, indicating strong potential.

Strengths:
- The introduction of a new regularizer in the optimization problem is a clear and valuable contribution.
- The experimental results are promising and suggest the approach has practical merit.

Concerns:
- As acknowledged by the authors, the problem is non-convex.
- The existence of a solution is not shown, and the algorithm lacks theoretical guarantees; it is supported solely by empirical evidence.
- The writing requires improvement for clarity (e.g., line 146).
