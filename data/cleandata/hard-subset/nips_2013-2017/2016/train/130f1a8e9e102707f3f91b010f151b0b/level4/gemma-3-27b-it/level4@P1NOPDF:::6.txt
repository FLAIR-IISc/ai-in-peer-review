A novel regularization technique for matrix factorization is introduced to encourage both sparsity and smoothness. While the paper does not establish the existence of a solution, it does present an algorithm for obtaining one. Experimental results demonstrate that the proposed approach outperforms state-of-the-art methods. The introduction of a new regularizer within the optimization problem is a significant strength of this work. The experimental results suggest the potential of this approach; however, as the authors acknowledge, the problem is non-convex and the existence of a solution remains unproven. The algorithm’s validity is currently supported solely by empirical evidence. A further concern is the writing quality, which could be improved in places (e.g., line 146). 

Following the rebuttal, I am satisfied with the authors’ response regarding the algorithm’s convergence properties. I am therefore willing to revise my score for Clarity and Presentation from 2 to 3.
