This paper introduces a novel regularization technique for matrix factorization, aiming to enforce sparsity and smoothness. Although the authors do not provide a proof of the solution's existence, they present an algorithm to obtain a solution. Experimental results demonstrate that their approach outperforms the state-of-the-art method. The introduction of a new regularizer in the optimization problem is a significant strength of the paper, and the experimental results showcase the potential of this approach. However, as the authors acknowledge, the problem is non-convex, and the existence of a solution is not established. The algorithm is primarily supported by experimental results, rather than theoretical guarantees. Additionally, there are issues with the writing style, such as the sentence at line 146, which requires improvement. After considering the authors' rebuttal, I am satisfied with their explanation of the algorithm's convergence and am willing to revise my rating for Clarity and Presentation from 2 to 3.
