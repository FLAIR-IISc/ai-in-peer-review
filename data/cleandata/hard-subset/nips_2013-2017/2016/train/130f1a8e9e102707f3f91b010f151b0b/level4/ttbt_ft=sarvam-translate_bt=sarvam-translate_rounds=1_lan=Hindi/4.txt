This paper proposes incomplete word learning with joint Sobolev/L1 regularization to find functional components in fMRI data. The proposed approach seems interesting. Using word learning with joint Sobolev/L1 regularization to encourage component ease and rarity is somewhat original contribution. However, the results of the new approach do not appear much better than simple methods, or at least, the improvement is inadequately explained. Furthermore, the manuscript appears to have been written in quite a hurry, for example, there are still draft comments (line 146) and it is quite tedious, sometimes even confusing.
