

The paper proposes under-complete dictionary learning with combined Sobolev/L1 regularization to identify functional components in fMRI data. The core contribution—applying dictionary learning with Sobolev (for smoothness) and L1 (for sparsity) regularization to fMRI analysis—is conceptually interesting. However, the results demonstrate only marginal improvements over simpler baseline methods, and the significance of these gains is insufficiently justified. Furthermore, the manuscript exhibits signs of being rushed, including persistent draft comments (e.g., line 146) and a tedious, occasionally confusing presentation that detracts from clarity.
