The paper presents an under-complete dictionary-learning framework that combines Sobolev (Laplacian) and L1 regularization to identify functional components in fMRI data. The approach is appealing. The primary, somewhat novel contribution is the application of dictionary learning with joint Sobolev/L1 penalties to enforce both smoothness and sparsity in the learned components.

However, the empirical benefits over simpler baselines appear limited, or at least the gains are not clearly substantiated. In addition, the manuscript seems hastily prepared—there are residual draft remarks (e.g., line 146)—and the presentation is often laborious and at times confusing.
