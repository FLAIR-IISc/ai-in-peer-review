- Paper frames crowdsourced labeling as a coding problem.  
- Workers are modeled as noisy channels: symmetric memoryless (no feedback) and spammer/hammer.  
- Budget is mapped to code rate for analysis.  
- Paper analyzes distortion versus budget tradeoffs.  
- Proposes and investigates k-incidence coding (workers label subsets of k items).
