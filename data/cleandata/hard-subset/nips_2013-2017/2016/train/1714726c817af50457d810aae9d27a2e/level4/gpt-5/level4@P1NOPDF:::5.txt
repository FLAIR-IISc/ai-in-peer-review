The paper studies accelerated descent dynamics for constrained convex optimization. Using a Lyapunov function, the authors establish the desired convergence rate. They also propose an adaptive averaging heuristic designed to accelerate the decay of the Lyapunov function. The results indicate that adaptive averaging performs as well as, and sometimes better than, adaptive restarting. I have limited expertise in this area; however, the idea of using adaptive averaging to achieve faster convergence for constrained convex optimization seems interesting and promising.
