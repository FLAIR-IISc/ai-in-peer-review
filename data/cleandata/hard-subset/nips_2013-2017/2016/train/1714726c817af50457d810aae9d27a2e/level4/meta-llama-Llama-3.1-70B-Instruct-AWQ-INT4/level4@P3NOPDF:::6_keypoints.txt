- Presents an adaptive averaging heuristic that accelerates constrained convex optimization and preserves original convergence rate when the objective is not strongly convex.  
- Theoretical analysis and experiments support the main claim.  
- Paper is difficult to follow for non-experts; needs more background and clearer notation/abbreviation explanations.  
- Equations on lines 33–34 and 46–47 require more intuitive explanations to aid understanding.  
- Request for discussion on how the proposed method relates to/adiffers from adaptive methods in non-convex optimization (e.g., Adagrad, Adam) and implications of those differences.  
- Experiments conducted only in R^3; need evidence or discussion on scalability to higher-dimensional, real-world problems.  
- Figure 1’s solid-line reference is ambiguous; suggest using different line styles or colors to clarify the primal trajectory.  
- Overall contribution is interesting and promising but would benefit from revisions to improve clarity and accessibility.
