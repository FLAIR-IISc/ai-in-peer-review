The paper investigates accelerated descent dynamics for constrained convex optimization problems. Leveraging a Lyapunov function analysis, the authors demonstrate a convergence rate matching established bounds. A novel adaptive averaging heuristic is proposed to further accelerate the convergence exhibited by the Lyapunov analysis. Empirical results indicate that this adaptive averaging scheme performs comparably to, and in some cases surpasses, adaptive restarting techniques. While my expertise in this specific area is limited, the core idea of employing adaptive averaging to achieve an improved convergence rate for constrained convex optimization appears both interesting and promising.
