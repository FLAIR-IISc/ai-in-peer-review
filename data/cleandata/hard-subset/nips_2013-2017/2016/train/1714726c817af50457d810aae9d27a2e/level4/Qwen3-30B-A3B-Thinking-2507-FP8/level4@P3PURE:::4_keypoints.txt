- Studies a class of ODEs with a generalized averaging scheme applied to solution trajectories.  
- Derives sufficient conditions on the dual learning rate and weight parameters to guarantee a targeted convergence rate.  
- Uses a Lyapunov argument to theoretically justify convergence results.  
- Proposes an adaptive averaging heuristic designed to speed the decay of the Lyapunov function.  
- Provides rigorous theoretical guarantees alongside comprehensive numerical comparisons.  
- Builds on prior empirical work on accelerating optimization methods.  
- Main contribution: shows in an ODE framework that adaptive averaging of trajectory weights can produce similar acceleration, offering new insight into accelerated dynamics.
