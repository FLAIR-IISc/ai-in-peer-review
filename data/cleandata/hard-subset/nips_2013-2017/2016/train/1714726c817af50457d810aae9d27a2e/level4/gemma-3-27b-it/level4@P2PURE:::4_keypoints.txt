- Studies a class of ODEs with a generalized averaging scheme applied to their trajectories.
- Derives sufficient conditions on the dual learning rate and averaging weights to guarantee a specific convergence rate.
- Uses Lyapunov analysis to justify the convergence guarantees.
- Proposes an adaptive averaging heuristic aimed at accelerating the decrease of the Lyapunov function.
- Provides theoretical results alongside numerical experiments to validate claims.
- Positions the work as a novel viewpoint linking adaptive averaging along ODE trajectories to performance improvements in optimization.
- Finds the adaptive averaging approach compelling and potentially complementary to empirical acceleration methods.
