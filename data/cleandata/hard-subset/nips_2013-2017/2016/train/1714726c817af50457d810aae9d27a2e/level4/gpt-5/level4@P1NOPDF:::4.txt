This paper studies a class of ODEs with a generalized averaging scheme. Using a Lyapunov argument, it provides sufficient conditions on the dual learning rate and the weights to achieve a desired convergence rate. The authors also introduce an adaptive averaging heuristic to accelerate the decay of the Lyapunov function. Both theoretical guarantees and numerical comparisons are provided.

There is a substantial line of prior work aimed at empirically speeding up the convergence of accelerated methods. This paper makes a key ODE-based observation: adaptively averaging the weights along solution trajectories can also deliver such speedups. I found this method very interesting.
