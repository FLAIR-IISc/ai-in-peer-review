The paper investigates accelerated descent dynamics for constrained convex optimization. Using a Lyapunov function, the authors derive conditions that ensure a desired convergence rate. They then introduce an adaptive averaging heuristic designed to decrease the Lyapunov function more rapidly and thus speed up convergence. Empirically, the method performs at least as well as adaptive restarting, and in some cases better.

I have limited knowledge in this field. Nevertheless, the idea of using adaptive averaging to achieve faster convergence for constrained convex optimization appears interesting and promising.
