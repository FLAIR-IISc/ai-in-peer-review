The paper studies accelerated descent dynamics for constrained convex optimization. Using a Lyapunov function, the authors establish the desired convergence rate. They also propose an adaptive averaging heuristic to accelerate the decrease of the Lyapunov function. The results indicate that adaptive averaging performs as well as, or even better than, adaptive restarting. I have limited knowledge in this field; however, the idea of using adaptive averaging to achieve a faster convergence rate for constrained convex optimization is interesting and promising.
