- Studies a class of ODEs using a generalized averaging approach.  
- Derives sufficient conditions on dual learning rate and weights to reach a target convergence rate via a Lyapunov-based argument.  
- Introduces an adaptive averaging heuristic to accelerate the decay of the Lyapunov function and improve convergence.  
- Provides theoretical guarantees supporting the method.  
- Presents numerical comparisons that corroborate the theoretical claims.  
- Positions the work within efforts to empirically accelerate accelerated methods.  
- Key insight: adaptive averaging of weights along ODE solution trajectories yields similar speedups.  
- Methodology is novel, noteworthy, and offers a fresh perspective.
