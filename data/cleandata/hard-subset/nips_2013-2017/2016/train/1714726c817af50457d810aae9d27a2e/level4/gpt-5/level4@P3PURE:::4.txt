This paper studies a class of ODEs with a generalized averaging scheme. It provides sufficient conditions on the dual learning rate and the weights to achieve a desired convergence rate using a Lyapunov argument. The authors also introduce an adaptive averaging heuristic to accelerate the decrease of the Lyapunov function. Both theoretical guarantees and numerical comparisons are provided.

There is a long line of work aimed at empirically speeding up the convergence of accelerated methods. This paper makes a crucial observation, via an ODE perspective, that adaptively averaging the weights along solution trajectories can also achieve this speedup. I found this new method very interesting.
