- Proposes an adaptive averaging heuristic for accelerating constrained convex optimization that preserves the original convergence rate for non-strongly convex objectives, with theoretical and experimental support.  
- Reviewer found the paper hard to follow due to assumed familiarity with abbreviations/terms; recommends adding background material to improve accessibility.  
- Equations between lines 33–34 and 46–47 lack intuitive explanation and would benefit from clearer interpretation.  
- Requests discussion comparing the proposed adaptive averaging to adaptive methods in non-convex optimization (e.g., Adagrad, Adam).  
- Questions practical significance because experiments are limited to R^3; asks for evidence of scalability to higher dimensions.  
- Minor: Figure 1’s reference to the solid line is ambiguous; suggest using dotted lines or colors to clarify the primal trajectory.
