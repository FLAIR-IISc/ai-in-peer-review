- Paper studies a class of ODEs that incorporate a generalized averaging scheme.
- Authors derive sufficient conditions on the dual learning rate and weights to guarantee a desired convergence rate using a Lyapunov argument.
- They propose an adaptive averaging heuristic aimed at accelerating the decrease of the Lyapunov function.
- The work provides both theoretical guarantees and numerical comparisons validating the approach.
- There is substantial prior empirical work on accelerating accelerated methods, situating this paper within that literature.
- Key contribution: showing via ODE analysis that adaptively averaging weights along solution trajectories can produce a speedup.
- Reviewer finds the adaptive-averaging ODE approach novel and particularly intriguing.
