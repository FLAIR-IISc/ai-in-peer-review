- Studies a class of ODEs with a generalized averaging scheme.  
- Provides sufficient conditions on the dual learning rate and averaging weights to achieve a target convergence rate via a Lyapunov argument.  
- Introduces an adaptive averaging heuristic designed to accelerate the decrease of the Lyapunov function.  
- Supplies both theoretical guarantees and numerical comparisons validating the approach.  
- Key novel insight: from an ODE perspective, adaptively averaging weights along solution trajectories can produce the observed speedup; reviewer finds this idea compelling.
