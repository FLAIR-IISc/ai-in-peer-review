

The paper investigates accelerated descent dynamics for constrained convex optimization. Leveraging a Lyapunov function, the authors establish the desired convergence rate. An adaptive averaging heuristic is proposed to accelerate the convergence rate of the Lyapunov-based method. Experimental results indicate that the adaptive averaging achieves performance comparable to or superior than adaptive restarting. While my expertise in this domain is limited, the concept of employing adaptive averaging to attain a faster convergence rate for constrained convex optimization appears intriguing and promising.
