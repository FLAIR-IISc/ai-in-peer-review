- Studies convergence of ODEs under a generalized averaging scheme.  
- Proves sufficient conditions on learning rate and weighting parameters for a target convergence rate using Lyapunov analysis.  
- Proposes an adaptive averaging heuristic that enhances Lyapunov dissipation and speeds up convergence.  
- Validates theoretical results with numerical experiments.  
- Novel insight: adaptive averaging of trajectory weights can effectively produce acceleration.  
- Frames contribution as an ODE-based theoretical complement to empirical acceleration studies.
