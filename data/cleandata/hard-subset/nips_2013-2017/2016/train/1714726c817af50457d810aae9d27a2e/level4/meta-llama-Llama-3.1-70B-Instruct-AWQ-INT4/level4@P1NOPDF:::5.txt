The paper investigates accelerated descent dynamics for constrained convex optimization, leveraging the Lyapunov function to achieve a desired convergence rate. By designing an adaptive averaging heuristic, the authors successfully accelerate the convergence rate of the Lyapunov function. The results demonstrate that the adaptive averaging approach performs equally well as, or even surpasses, adaptive restarting in terms of convergence rate. Although I am not an expert in this field, I find the idea of utilizing adaptive averaging to attain a faster convergence rate for constrained convex optimization to be intriguing and promising.
