- Studies a class of ODEs within a generalized averaging framework.  
- Proves sufficient conditions on the dual learning rate and weights to guarantee a desired convergence rate.  
- Uses a Lyapunov-based analysis to derive the results.  
- Proposes an adaptive averaging heuristic designed to accelerate the Lyapunov function's decay.  
- Provides both theoretical guarantees and numerical comparisons.  
- Positions the work in the context of extensive literature on empirically improving accelerated methods.  
- Main contribution: demonstrates via ODEs that adaptively averaging trajectory weights can produce acceleration.  
- Reviewer finds the adaptive-averaging approach novel and highly intriguing.
