- Proposes a novel, intuitive heuristic to adaptively reweight iterates in Nesterov's accelerated method using the ODE interpretation.  
- Method is theoretically guaranteed to dominate the standard fixed-schedule averaging scheme.  
- Theoretical guarantee is supported by empirical evaluations showing significant speed-ups on a broad class of functions.  
- Paper is well-written; the mathematical argument is elegant, simple, and easy to follow.  
- Although a direct extension of prior ODE work, the idea has high impact potential and offers a stronger baseline for future adaptive methods.  
- Recommendation: add a discrete-time (discretized) analysis to formally show the algorithm preserves its convergence rate.  
- Recommendation: discuss potential connections to the Conjugate Gradient method, especially for strongly convex quadratic problems.
