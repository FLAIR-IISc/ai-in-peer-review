Manuscript studies accelerated descent dynamics for constrained convex optimization using a Lyapunov function to obtain the desired convergence rate.
Authors introduce an adaptive averaging heuristic that enhances the convergence rate of the Lyapunov function.
Empirical results indicate adaptive averaging performs comparably to, or surpasses, adaptive restarting in convergence speed.
Reviewer reports limited expertise but finds the adaptive averaging approach intriguing and promising.
No comments provided on Quality, Novelty, Impact, or Clarity.
