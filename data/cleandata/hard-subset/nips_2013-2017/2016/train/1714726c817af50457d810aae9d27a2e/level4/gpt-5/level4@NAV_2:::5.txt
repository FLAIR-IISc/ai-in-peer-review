The paper investigates accelerated descent dynamics for constrained convex optimization. Using a Lyapunov-based analysis, the authors establish conditions that guarantee a target convergence rate. They then introduce an adaptive averaging heuristic intended to accelerate the decay of the Lyapunov function. Empirical results indicate that adaptive averaging performs on par with, and sometimes better than, adaptive restarting. I have limited expertise in this area. Nevertheless, the idea of employing adaptive averaging to attain faster convergence for constrained convex optimization appears interesting and promising.
