- Studies continuous-time accelerated descent dynamics with generalized averaging.  
- Uses Lyapunov analysis to give sufficient conditions on the dual learning rate and averaging weights for a target convergence rate.  
- Introduces an adaptive averaging heuristic that speeds up the decrease of the Lyapunov function.  
- Provides theoretical guarantees and numerical comparisons validating the approach.  
- Positions the work within a long line of empirical acceleration methods.  
- Key observation: an ODE perspective shows adaptively averaging weights along the trajectory can yield speedups.  
- Reviewer finds the method very interesting.
