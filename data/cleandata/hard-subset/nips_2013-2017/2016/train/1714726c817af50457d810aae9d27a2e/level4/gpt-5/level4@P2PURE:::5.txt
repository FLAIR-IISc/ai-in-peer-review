The paper studies accelerated descent dynamics for constrained convex optimization. Using a Lyapunov function, the authors establish the desired convergence rate. They design an adaptive averaging heuristic to accelerate the decay of the Lyapunov function. Results show that adaptive averaging performs as well as, or even better than, adaptive restarting. I have limited knowledge in this field; however, the idea of using adaptive averaging to achieve a faster convergence rate for constrained convex optimization seems interesting and promising.
