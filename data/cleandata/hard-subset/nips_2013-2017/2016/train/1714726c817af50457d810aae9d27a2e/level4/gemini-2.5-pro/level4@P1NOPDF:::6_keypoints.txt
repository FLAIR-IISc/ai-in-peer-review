- Strength: provides both theoretical analysis and experiments supporting superiority over restarting heuristics.
- Clarity issue: paper lacks background/context and is hard to follow for non-specialists; add a brief primer on notation and terminology.

- Practical significance: experiments are limited to R^3 (proof-of-concept); authors should show or argue how the method scales to high-dimensional ML problems.
