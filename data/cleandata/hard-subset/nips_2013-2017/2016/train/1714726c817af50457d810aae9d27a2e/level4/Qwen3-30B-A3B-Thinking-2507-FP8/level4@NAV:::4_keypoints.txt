- Studies a class of ODEs within a generalized averaging framework.
- Derives sufficient conditions on the dual learning rate and averaging weights to achieve a specified convergence rate via Lyapunov analysis.
- Proposes an adaptive averaging heuristic to accelerate reduction of the Lyapunov function.
- Provides theoretical guarantees and numerical comparisons.
- Builds on prior work on empirical acceleration of accelerated methods.
- Main contribution: demonstrates via ODE analysis that adaptively averaging weights along solution trajectories yields acceleration.
- Reviewer finds the ODE-based adaptive averaging approach novel and highly interesting.
