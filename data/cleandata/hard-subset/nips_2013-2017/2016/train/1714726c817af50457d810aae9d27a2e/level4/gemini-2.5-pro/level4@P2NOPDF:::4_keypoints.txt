- Analyzes a class of ODEs with a generalized averaging scheme to develop an acceleration method.
- Derives sufficient conditions on the dual learning rate and weights that guarantee a specific convergence rate.
- Uses a Lyapunov-based argument to establish the convergence analysis.
- Proposes an adaptive averaging heuristic aimed at accelerating the decrease of the Lyapunov function.
- Supports the approach with theoretical guarantees and numerical comparisons.
- Novel insight: adaptively averaging weights along solution trajectories can produce significant speedups.
- ODE-based perspective provides a fresh, elegant framing and the paper is well-executed and convincing.
