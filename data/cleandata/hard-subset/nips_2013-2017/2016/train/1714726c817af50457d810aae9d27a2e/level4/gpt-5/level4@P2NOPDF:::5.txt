The paper investigates accelerated descent dynamics for constrained convex optimization. By constructing an appropriate Lyapunov function, the authors establish the desired convergence rate. They further propose an adaptive averaging heuristic designed to accelerate convergence by improving the decay rate of the Lyapunov function. Empirical results indicate that adaptive averaging performs as well as, or better than, adaptive restarting.

I have limited expertise in this area. Nevertheless, the idea of using adaptive averaging to achieve faster convergence for constrained convex problems appears interesting and promising.
