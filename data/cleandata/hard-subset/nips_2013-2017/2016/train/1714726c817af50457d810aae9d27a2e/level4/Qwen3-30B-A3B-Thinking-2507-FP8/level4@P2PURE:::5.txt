

The paper investigates accelerated descent dynamics for constrained convex optimization. Using a Lyapunov function framework, the authors establish the desired convergence rate. They further propose an adaptive averaging heuristic to accelerate the convergence rate of the Lyapunov-based method. Experimental results demonstrate that the adaptive averaging technique achieves performance equal to or superior to adaptive restarting. While not an expert in this field, the core idea of leveraging adaptive averaging to attain a faster convergence rate for constrained convex optimization appears interesting and promising.
