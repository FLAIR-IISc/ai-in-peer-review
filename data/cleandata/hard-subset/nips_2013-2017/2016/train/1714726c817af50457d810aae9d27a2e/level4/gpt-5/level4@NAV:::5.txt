Review- The paper investigates accelerated descent dynamics for constrained convex optimization. Using a Lyapunov function, the authors establish the targeted convergence rate. An adaptive averaging heuristic is introduced to further accelerate the Lyapunov-based convergence. The results show that adaptive averaging performs as well as, or better than, adaptive restarting. I have limited expertise in this area. Nevertheless, the idea of using adaptive averaging to achieve faster convergence for constrained convex optimization is interesting and promising.
