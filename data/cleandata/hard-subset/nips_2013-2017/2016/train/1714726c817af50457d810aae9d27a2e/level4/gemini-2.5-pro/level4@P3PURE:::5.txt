This paper investigates accelerated descent dynamics for constrained convex optimization. Employing a Lyapunov function-based analysis, the authors establish a desired rate of convergence. The paper's main contribution is an adaptive averaging heuristic designed to accelerate the decrease of the Lyapunov function, which in turn improves the algorithm's convergence rate.

The empirical results demonstrate that the proposed adaptive averaging scheme performs comparably to, and in some cases better than, the adaptive restarting technique.

I have limited familiarity with this specific area of research. However, the core idea of using adaptive averaging to achieve faster convergence for constrained convex optimization problems seems interesting and promising.
