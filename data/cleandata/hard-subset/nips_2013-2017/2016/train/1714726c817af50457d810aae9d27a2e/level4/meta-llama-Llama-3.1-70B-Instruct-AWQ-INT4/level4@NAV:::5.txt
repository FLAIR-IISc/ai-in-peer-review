The paper investigates accelerated descent dynamics for constrained convex optimization, leveraging a Lyapunov function to attain the desired convergence rate. The authors propose an adaptive averaging heuristic that enhances the convergence rate of the Lyapunov function. Experimental results demonstrate that this adaptive averaging approach performs comparably to, or even surpasses, adaptive restarting methods. Although my expertise in this domain is limited, the concept of employing adaptive averaging to accelerate convergence rates in constrained convex optimization appears intriguing and holds promise.
