- Examines a class of ODEs incorporating a generalized averaging scheme.  
- Establishes sufficient conditions on dual learning rate and weights to achieve a target convergence rate.  
- Uses a Lyapunov argument to derive the theoretical convergence results.  
- Introduces an adaptive averaging heuristic to accelerate the decrease of the Lyapunov function.  
- Provides both theoretical guarantees and numerical comparisons validating the approach.  
- Positions the work within existing literature on empirically accelerating accelerated methods.  
- Key novel observation: adaptively averaging weights along solution trajectories can yield speedups; proposed method is noteworthy.
