- Studies a class of ODEs with a generalized averaging scheme for trajectory-based averaging.
- Derives sufficient conditions on the dual learning rate and weights to guarantee a target convergence rate using a Lyapunov argument.
- Proposes an adaptive averaging heuristic to accelerate the decrease of the Lyapunov function.
- Provides both theoretical guarantees and numerical comparisons validating the approach.
- Situates the work within prior efforts to empirically accelerate accelerated methods.
- Key novel observation: adaptive averaging of weights along solution trajectories yields a provable speedup.
- Reviewer finds the proposed method novel and interesting.
