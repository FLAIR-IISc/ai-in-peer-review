- Analyzes a family of optimization ODEs equipped with a generalized averaging mechanism.  
- Uses a Lyapunov-based argument to derive sufficient conditions on the dual step size and averaging weights to attain a target convergence rate.  
- Introduces an adaptive averaging heuristic that adjusts weights to accelerate the decay of the Lyapunov function.  
- Provides theoretical guarantees for the adaptive scheme and presents empirical comparisons.  
- Situates the work within existing heuristics that speed up accelerated methods, adding an ODE-based perspective.  
- Reviewer found the approach particularly interesting.
