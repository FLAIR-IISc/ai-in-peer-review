The paper investigates accelerated descent dynamics for constrained convex optimization, leveraging a Lyapunov function to attain the desired convergence rate. By incorporating an adaptive averaging heuristic, the authors successfully accelerate the convergence rate of the Lyapunov function. The experimental results demonstrate that the proposed adaptive averaging approach performs comparably to or even surpasses adaptive restarting in terms of convergence rate. Although I am not an expert in this field, the concept of utilizing adaptive averaging to achieve a faster convergence rate for constrained convex optimization appears intriguing and promising.
