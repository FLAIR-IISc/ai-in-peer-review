- Analyzes a class of ODEs incorporating a generalized averaging scheme.
- Uses a Lyapunov-based argument to derive sufficient conditions on the dual learning rate and averaging weights for a specific convergence rate.
- Proposes an adaptive averaging heuristic designed to accelerate the decrease of the Lyapunov function.
- Supports the heuristic with theoretical guarantees and numerical comparisons.
- Contributes to literature on empirically speeding up accelerated optimization methods.
- Key insight: adaptively averaging weights along solution trajectories can achieve a practical speedup; novel and interesting.
