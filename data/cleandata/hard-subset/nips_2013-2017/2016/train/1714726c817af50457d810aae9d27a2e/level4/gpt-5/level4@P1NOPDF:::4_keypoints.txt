- Studies a class of ODEs with a generalized averaging scheme for weights.  
- Uses a Lyapunov argument to give sufficient conditions on the dual learning rate and weights for a target convergence rate.  
- Proposes an adaptive averaging heuristic designed to accelerate decay of the Lyapunov function.  
- Provides theoretical guarantees supporting the method.  
- Includes numerical comparisons demonstrating empirical performance.  
- Positions the work relative to prior empirical acceleration literature and highlights the novel ODE-based observation that adaptive averaging along trajectories can speed up convergence; reviewer finds the method interesting.
