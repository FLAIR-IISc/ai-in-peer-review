- Analyzes a class of ODEs equipped with a generalized averaging scheme.  
- Uses a Lyapunov framework to give sufficient conditions on dual learning rate and averaging weights for a prescribed convergence rate.  
- Introduces an adaptive averaging heuristic aimed at accelerating the decrease of the Lyapunov function.  
- Provides both theoretical guarantees and numerical comparisons.  
- Notes substantial prior empirical work on accelerating first-order and accelerated methods.  
- Contribution: demonstrates via an ODE perspective that adaptively averaging along solution trajectories can yield speedups.  
- Reviewer finds the approach novel and compelling.
