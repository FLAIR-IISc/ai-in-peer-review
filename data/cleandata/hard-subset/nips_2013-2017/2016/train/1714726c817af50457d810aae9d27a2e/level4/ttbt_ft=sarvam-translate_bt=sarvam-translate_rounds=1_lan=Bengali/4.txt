This paper discusses a class of generalized nonlinear equations (ODE) with a common averaging method. A Lyapunov argument provides sufficient conditions for achieving the desired convergence rate through dual learning rate and weights. Furthermore, the authors used an adaptive averaging heuristic to accelerate the Lyapunov function's decay. Both theoretical guarantees and numerical comparisons are provided. This is a long-term approach aimed at accelerating the convergence of accelerated methods empirically. This paper makes an important observation through nonlinear equations that the speed can also be increased by averaging the weights along the solution path. I found this new method very interesting.
