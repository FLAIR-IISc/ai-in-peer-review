- Paper studies accelerated descent dynamics for constrained convex optimization.
- Authors use a Lyapunov function to derive conditions that guarantee a desired convergence rate.
- They propose an adaptive averaging heuristic aimed at decreasing the Lyapunov function more rapidly to speed up convergence.
- Empirical results show the method performs at least as well as adaptive restarting and sometimes better.
