- Studies accelerated descent dynamics for constrained convex optimization problems.
- Uses a Lyapunov-function-based analysis to establish a desired convergence rate.
- Main contribution is an adaptive averaging heuristic that accelerates the decrease of the Lyapunov function and improves algorithmic convergence.
- Empirical results show the adaptive averaging scheme is comparable to, and sometimes outperforms, adaptive restarting.
