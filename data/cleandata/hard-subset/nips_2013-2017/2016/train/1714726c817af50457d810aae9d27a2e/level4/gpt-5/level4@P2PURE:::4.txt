This paper analyzes a class of ODEs with a generalized averaging scheme. It derives sufficient conditions on the dual learning rate and the averaging weights that guarantee a target convergence rate, established via a Lyapunov analysis. The authors also introduce an adaptive averaging heuristic that accelerates the decrease of the Lyapunov function. The paper provides both theoretical guarantees and a numerical comparison. There is a long line of work seeking to empirically speed up the convergence of accelerated methods; this paper makes a key observation, via an ODE perspective, that adaptively averaging the weights along the solution trajectories can likewise achieve such speedups. I find this new method very interesting.
