This paper studies a class of ordinary differential equations (ODE) with a generalized average scheme. Sufficient conditions are given on the dual learning rate and weights to obtain the desired convergence rate using Lyapunov arguments. Furthermore, the authors have used an adaptive average approximation to accelerate the decay of the Lyapunov function. Both theoretical guarantees and numerical comparisons are provided. There is a long series of tasks aimed at empirically accelerating the convergence of fast methods. This paper makes an important observation through ODEs that acceleration can also be achieved by extracting the adaptive average of the weights along the solution trajectories. I found this new method very interesting.
