The paper "Adaptive Averaging in Accelerated Descent Dynamics" presents a novel approach to accelerating the convergence of optimization algorithms using a generalized averaging scheme. Secondly, it proposes an adaptive averaging heuristic that adaptively computes the weights to speed up the decrease of the Lyapunov function.
