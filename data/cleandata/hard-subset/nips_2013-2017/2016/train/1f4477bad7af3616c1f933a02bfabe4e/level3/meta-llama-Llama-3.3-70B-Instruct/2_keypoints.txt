- Provides a global analysis of EM for Gaussian mixtures, characterizing stationary points and dynamics in the large-sample limit.  
- Proves convergence of the sequence of iterates for Population EM.  
- Shows Sample-based EM fixed points converge to Population EM fixed points.  
- Connects Population EM fixed points to stationary points of the expected log-likelihood.  
- Establishes statistical consistency of EM for specific models, notably mixtures of two Gaussians.  
- Paper is well-written, clearly organized, and accessible with thorough background and related work.  
- Technical contributions include detailed proofs of main theorems and corollaries.  
- Significant advance over prior work by providing global (not just local) convergence analysis.  
- Limitation: analysis restricted to specific models (e.g., two-component Gaussian mixtures), unclear generalizability.  
- Limitation: relies on large-sample asymptotics, which may be unrealistic in practical settings.  
- Suggested improvement: add numerical experiments or simulations to illustrate and validate theoretical findings.  
- Meets conference criteria for quality, clarity, originality, and significance.
