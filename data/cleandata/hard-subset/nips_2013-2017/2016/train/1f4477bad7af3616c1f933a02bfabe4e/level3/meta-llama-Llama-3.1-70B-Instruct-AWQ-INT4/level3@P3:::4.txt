This allows the authors to leverage existing results from nonlinear Perron-Frobenius theory to establish a globally optimal algorithm for training neural networks. The authors provide a thorough analysis of their method, including a proof of convergence to the global optimum and a characterization of the Lipschitz constant of the mapping.
