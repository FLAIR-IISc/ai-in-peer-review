This paper presents a novel approach to training feedforward neural networks with a provable unique global optimizer. The authors introduce a class of generalized polynomial neural networks and propose a nonlinear spectral method to achieve global optimality. The method is based on a carefully designed objective function and a mapping that converges to the global optimum with a linear convergence rate.

The mathematical derivations are instructive, but some parts are unclear, and the reviewer had to spend some time to fully understand the proofs.
