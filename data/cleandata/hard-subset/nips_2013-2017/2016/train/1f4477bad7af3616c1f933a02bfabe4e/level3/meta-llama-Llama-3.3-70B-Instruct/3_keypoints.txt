- Presents a global analysis of EM for Gaussian mixture models, characterizing stationary points and dynamics in the large-sample limit.  
- Provides a new characterization of stationary points and dynamics for two simple yet popular GMMs.  
- Demonstrates the statistical consistency of EM in the large-sample limit.  
- Well-written with a clear introduction, organized technical sections, and proofs provided in the appendix.  
- Represents a significant improvement over prior work that focused only on local convergence.  
- Analysis is limited to Gaussian mixture models; generalization to other latent variable models is unclear.  
- Assumes large sample sizes, which may limit applicability in small-sample settings.  
- Lacks a clear comparison with other optimization algorithms (e.g., gradient ascent) for maximizing the log-likelihood.  
- Meets conference criteria for quality, clarity, originality, and significance; technically sound.  
- Reviewer recommendation: accept, as the paper makes significant contributions and provides a foundation for future work.
