This paper presents a formulation of Supervised PageRank as a constrained, non-convex optimization problem, leveraging node and edge properties. The authors then propose and explore two solution approaches: a gradient-free method utilizing an inexact zero-order oracle, and a gradient-based method employing an inexact first-order oracle. A significant concern is the omission of experimental results from the main text, rendering the presentation incomplete. Specific comments are as follows: 1. The placement of experimental results solely within the supplementary material detracts from the completeness of the main manuscript. 2. Theorem 1 assumes convexity, yet the function defined in (2.4) is demonstrably non-convex. Clarification is needed regarding the selection of the set $\Phi$ to ensure objective function convexity. 3. Theorem 3 provides a bound on $MK(xK - x{K+1})$. It is unclear whether this bound guarantees convergence of the sequence $xk$ to a stationary point. 4. Results presented in Figure 1 of the supplement indicate slow convergence for GFNM. Reporting on the computational efficiency of both algorithms would be beneficial.
