1. Paper formulates Supervised PageRank as a constrained non-convex optimization using node/edge attributes and proposes two solvers: a gradient-free inexact zero-order method and a gradient-based inexact first-order method; main concern is experiments are omitted from the main text.  
2. Experimental results placed only in supplementary material, which weakens the main paper's completeness.  
3. Theorem 1 assumes convexity, but the objective in (2.4) is non-convex; need clarification on choosing set Φ to ensure convexity.  
4. Theorem 3 bounds MK(xK - xK+1), but it's unclear whether this implies convergence of xK to a stationary point.  
5. Supplementary Figure 1 shows slow convergence for GFNM; reporting computational efficiency (runtime/complexity) for both algorithms is needed.
