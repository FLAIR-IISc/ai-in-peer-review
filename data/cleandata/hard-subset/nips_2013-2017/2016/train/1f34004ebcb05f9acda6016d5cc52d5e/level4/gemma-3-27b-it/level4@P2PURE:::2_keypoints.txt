- Formulates Supervised PageRank as a constrained non-convex optimization using node and edge features.  
- Proposes two solution methods: a gradient-free (inexact zero-order oracle) and a gradient-based (inexact first-order oracle) approach.  
- Major concern: experimental results are omitted from the main text and placed only in the supplement, making the paper feel incomplete.  
- Theorem 1 assumes convexity, but the objective in (2.4) is non-convex; no guidance on choosing Φ to ensure convexity.  
- Theorem 3 gives a bound on MK(xK − x{K+1}) but it is unclear if this implies convergence of x_k to a stationary point.  
- Supplementary Figure 1 shows the gradient-free method converges slowly; the paper should report computational efficiency for both algorithms.
