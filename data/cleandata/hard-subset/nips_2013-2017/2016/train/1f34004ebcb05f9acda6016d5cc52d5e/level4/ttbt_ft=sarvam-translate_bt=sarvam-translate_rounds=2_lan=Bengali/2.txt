This paper discusses the construction of supervisory page rank models as a bounded non-convex optimization problem, incorporating the characteristics of nodes and edges. The authors propose two methods for solving the optimization problem. The first is a gradient-free method with an implicit zero-order oracle. The second is a gradient-based method with an implicit first-order oracle. My main complaint is that none of the test results are in the main text, which makes the paper incomplete. Detailed comments: 1. The authors have included test results in the supplementary section, which makes the main text incomplete. 2. The convexities in Theorem 1 are assumed, where the function (2.4) is non-convex. How should the set $\Phi$ be chosen to ensure the convexity of the objective function? 3. Theorem 3 provides a bound for $MK(xK - x{K+1})$. Can this bound ensure that the sequence $xk$ converges to a specific point? 4. According to Figure 1 in the supplementary section, the GFNM converges slowly. The authors could also mention the algorithm's efficiency.
