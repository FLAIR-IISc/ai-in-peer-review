- Formulates Supervised PageRank as a constrained non-convex optimization incorporating node and edge properties.  
- Proposes two solution methods: a gradient-free approach using an inexact zero-order oracle and a gradient-based approach using an inexact first-order oracle.  
- Major concern: experimental results are omitted from the main text, making the paper appear incomplete.  
- Detailed comment 1: Experimental results are only provided in the supplementary material, not in the main paper.  
- Detailed comment 2: Theorem 1 assumes convexity, but the objective in (2.4) is non-convex; unclear how to choose the set Φ to ensure convexity.  
- Detailed comment 3: Theorem 3 gives a bound on M K(x_K - x_{K+1}), but it is unclear whether this implies convergence of x_k to a stationary point.  
- Detailed comment 4: Figure 1 (supplementary) shows GFNM converges slowly; authors should report and compare the efficiency of the proposed algorithms.
