This paper proposes two optimization methods to solve the supervised PageRank problem. The authors develop a gradient-based method with a theoretically guaranteed convergence rate and a gradient-free method with a guaranteed loss function decrease value. Both methods do not require the exact value of the objective function and have an estimated convergence rate, outperforming the state-of-the-art in terms of ranking quality.

The paper is well-organized and clearly written. The authors provide a thorough explanation of their methods and include all the proofs in the supplementary materials. The experimental results demonstrate the effectiveness of the proposed methods.

The main contributions of the paper are the development of two two-level optimization methods, namely gradient-based (GBN) and gradient-free (GFN), to solve the supervised PageRank problem. The lower-level and upper-level optimization are based on Nesterov and Nemirovski's work (ref:17).
