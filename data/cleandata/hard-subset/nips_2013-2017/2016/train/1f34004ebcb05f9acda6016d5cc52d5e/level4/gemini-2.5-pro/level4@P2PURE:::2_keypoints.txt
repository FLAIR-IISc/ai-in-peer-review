- Formulates Supervised PageRank as a constrained non-convex optimization incorporating node and edge properties.
- Proposes two solution approaches: a gradient-free method with an inexact zero-order oracle and a gradient-based method with an inexact first-order oracle.
- Major concern: all experimental results are in the supplementary material, leaving the main paper incomplete and hard to evaluate standalone.
- Contradiction: Theorem 1 assumes convexity while the objective in Eq. (2.4) is non-convex; clarify how Φ can be chosen to satisfy the convexity assumption.
- Convergence unclear: Theorem 3’s bound on M_K(x_K − x_{K+1}) may not suffice to show that {x_k} converges to a stationary point and requires clarification.
- Efficiency issue: GFNM appears to converge slowly (per Fig. 1 in supplement); report computational efficiency (runtime, iterations to converge, etc.).
