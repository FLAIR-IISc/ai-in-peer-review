- Paper formulates Supervised PageRank as a constrained non-convex optimization using node/edge features and proposes two solvers: an inexact zero-order (gradient-free) method and an inexact first-order (gradient-based) method.

- Major concern: the main text omits experimental results (placed in supplement), making the paper feel incomplete and needing at least a summary of key empirical findings in the main paper.

- Theorem 1 assumes convexity, but the objective in Eq. (2.4) is non-convex; it is unclear how to choose the set Φ to guarantee convexity or whether the convexity assumption can be relaxed or justified.

- Theorem 3 gives a bound for MK(xK − xK+1), but the reviewer questions whether this bound implies convergence of the sequence xk to a stationary point and requests clarification on convergence implications.

- Supplementary Figure 1 suggests the Gradient-Free Newton Method (GFNM) converges slowly; the authors should report and discuss computational efficiency (run time/complexity) alongside accuracy.
