- All experimental results are placed in the supplementary material, leaving the main text incomplete and preventing empirical evaluation of the proposed methods.  
- The paper lacks quantitative results, comparisons, and visualizations in the main text, so readers cannot assess performance, scalability, or real-world applicability.  
- Theorem 1 assumes convexity to derive a bound, but the objective in Eq. (2.4) is stated as non-convex, creating a fundamental logical inconsistency that must be resolved (clarify how Φ is chosen).  
- Theorem 3 gives a bound on ||M_K(x_K - x_{K+1})|| but does not explicitly establish convergence of {x_k} to a stationary point; the convergence implications must be clarified or additional conditions provided.  
- Supplemental Figure 1 indicates the gradient-free method converges slowly; the paper needs to report and discuss computational efficiency (wall-clock time, iterations to target accuracy) for both algorithms.  
- The authors should move essential experimental results to the main text, resolve the convexity contradiction, clarify convergence guarantees, and include efficiency analysis to support their claims.  
- In its current form—lacking empirical support and with unresolved theoretical issues—the paper is unsuitable for publication.
