Review- This paper formulates Supervised PageRank models as a constrained non-convex optimization problem by incorporating node and edge properties. The authors propose two solution approaches. The first is a gradient-free method with an inexact zero-order oracle. The second is a gradient-based method with an inexact first-order oracle. My primary concern is that none of the experimental results are included in the main text, which leaves the paper incomplete. Detailed comments. 1. Placing the experimental results in the supplement renders the main text incomplete. 2. Theorem 1 assumes convexity, while the objective in (2.4) is non-convex. How should the set Phi be selected to ensure convexity of the objective? 3. Theorem 3 provides a bound for MK(xK − x{K+1}). Does this bound guarantee that the sequence xk converges to a stationary point? 4. From Figure 1 in the supplement, GFNM appears to converge slowly. The authors should also report the algorithms’ efficiency.
