Paper formulates Supervised PageRank as a constrained non-convex optimization problem using node and edge features.

Authors propose two solvers: a gradient-free method (inexact zero-order oracle) and a gradient-based method (inexact first-order oracle).

Major weakness: no experimental results in the main text; all empirical validation is in the supplement, making the paper hard to evaluate.

Theoretical discrepancy: Theorem 1 assumes convexity but the objective in (2.4) is non-convex; need clarification on how Φ can be chosen to satisfy convexity.

Convergence guarantees are unclear: Theorem 3 bounds M_K(x_K - x_{K+1}) but it’s not shown that this suffices for convergence to a stationary point.

Practical performance concerns: GFNM appears to converge slowly (supplement Fig.1); authors should report runtimes or provide a complexity analysis.
