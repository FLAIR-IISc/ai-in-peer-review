- Paper formulates Supervised PageRank as a constrained non-convex optimization using node and edge features.  
- Two solution approaches proposed: a gradient-free method with an inexact zero-order oracle and a gradient-based method with an inexact first-order oracle.  
- Primary concern: experimental results are omitted from the main text, leaving the paper incomplete.  
- Detailed concern 1: Placing experimental results in the supplement renders the main text incomplete.  
- Detailed concern 2: Theorem 1 assumes convexity, but the objective (2.4) is non-convex—unclear how to choose Phi to ensure convexity.  
- Detailed concern 3: Theorem 3 bounds MK(xK − x{K+1}), but it’s unclear whether this guarantees convergence to a stationary point.  
- Detailed concern 4: Supplementary Figure 1 shows GFNM appears to converge slowly; the authors should report algorithmic efficiency.
