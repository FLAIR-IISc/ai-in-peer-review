- Formulates Supervised PageRank as a constrained non-convex optimization using node/edge features and proposes two solvers: a gradient-free method with an inexact zero-order oracle and a gradient-based method with an inexact first-order oracle.  
- Major structural flaw: experimental results are only in the supplementary material, making the main text non–self-contained and incomplete for a conference submission.  
- Absence of experiments in the main paper: needs at least summaries of key results, datasets, and baseline comparisons within the main text.  
- Convexity ambiguity: Theorem 1 assumes convexity but the objective (Eq. 2.4) is generally non-convex; authors must clarify conditions (e.g., choices of Φ) that make it convex.  
- Insufficient convergence guarantees: Theorem 3 bounds ||x_k - x_{k+1}|| but does not establish convergence to a stationary point; a formal proof of stationarity is required.  
- Algorithm efficiency concerns: GFNM appears to converge slowly (per supplementary Fig.1); report wall-clock time and oracle-call counts versus baselines to assess practical trade-offs.
