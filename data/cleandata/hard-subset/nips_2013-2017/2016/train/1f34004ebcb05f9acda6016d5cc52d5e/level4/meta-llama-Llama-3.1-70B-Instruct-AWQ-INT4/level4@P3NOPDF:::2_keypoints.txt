- Paper formulates Supervised PageRank as a constrained non-convex optimization and proposes two solvers: a gradient-free method (inexact zero-order oracle) and a gradient-based method (inexact first-order oracle).

- Major concern: the paper omits experiment results from the main text, making it incomplete and forcing readers to consult the supplement.

- Issue 1: Relocating experiments to the supplement disrupts flow and prevents readers from assessing method efficacy; key experimental results should be integrated into the main text.

- Issue 2: Theorem 1 assumes convexity, but the objective (Eq. 2.4) is non-convex; authors must explain how to choose the set Φ to ensure objective convexity.

- Issue 3: Theorem 3 gives a bound on M K (x_K − x_{K+1}), but it is unclear whether this suffices to guarantee convergence of the sequence x_k to a stationary point; further analysis or discussion is needed.

- Issue 4: Supplementary Figure 1 indicates slow convergence of the Gradient-Free Newton Method (GFNM); the paper should report algorithmic efficiency and practical convergence behavior.

- Recommendation: Incorporate critical experimental results and address theoretical clarity to improve the paper’s completeness, clarity, and impact.
