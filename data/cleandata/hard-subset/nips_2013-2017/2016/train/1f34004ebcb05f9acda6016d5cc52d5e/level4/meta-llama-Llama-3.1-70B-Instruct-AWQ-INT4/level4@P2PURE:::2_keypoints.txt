- Paper formulates Supervised PageRank as a constrained non-convex optimization and proposes two solvers (gradient-free with inexact zero-order oracle and gradient-based with inexact first-order oracle), but major flaw is experiments are missing from the main text.

- Relegating experimental results to the supplement makes the main text incomplete; key experimental findings should be included in the main paper.

- Theorem 1 assumes convexity, yet the objective in (2.4) is non-convex; authors must clarify how to choose the set Φ to ensure objective convexity.

- Theorem 3 bounds MK(xK − xK+1), but it is unclear whether this bound implies convergence of the sequence xk to a stationary point.

- Supplementary Figure 1 suggests GFNM converges slowly; the paper should report algorithmic efficiency (e.g., runtimes, convergence rates) for a more complete evaluation.
