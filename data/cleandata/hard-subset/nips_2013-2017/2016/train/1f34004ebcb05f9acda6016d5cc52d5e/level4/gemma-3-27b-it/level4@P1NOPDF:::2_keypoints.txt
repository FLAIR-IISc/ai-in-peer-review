- Paper formulates Supervised PageRank as a constrained, non-convex optimization using node/edge features and proposes two solvers: an inexact zero-order (gradient-free) and an inexact first-order (gradient-based) method.

- Primary concern: experimental results are omitted from the main text, making the paper feel incomplete.

- Experimental results are only in the supplementary material, which significantly detracts from the completeness and evaluation in the main paper.

- Theorem 1 assumes convexity, but the objective in (2.4) is non‑convex; need clarification on how to choose Φ to ensure convexity or correct the assumption.

- Theorem 3 gives a bound on MK(xK − xK+1) but the reviewer questions whether this implies convergence of x_k to a stationary point and asks for discussion of convergence guarantees.

- Figure 1 (supplement) shows the gradient-free method (GFNM) converges slowly; authors should report computational efficiency and runtime comparisons for both algorithms.
