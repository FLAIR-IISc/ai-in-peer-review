This paper formulates Supervised PageRank as a constrained non-convex optimization problem by incorporating node and edge features. The authors propose two solution approaches: (i) a gradient-free method with an inexact zero-order oracle, and (ii) a gradient-based method with an inexact first-order oracle. My main concern is that the main text contains no experimental results, which makes the paper feel incomplete.

Detailed comments:
1. All experimental results are placed in the supplement, leaving the main text incomplete.
2. Theorem 1 assumes convexity, yet the objective in (2.4) is non-convex. How is the set Phi chosen to ensure the objective is convex?
3. Theorem 3 provides a bound for M_K(x^K − x^{K+1}). Does this bound guarantee that the sequence x_k converges to a stationary point?
4. According to Figure 1 in the supplement, GFNM converges slowly. The authors should also report the algorithms’ efficiency.
