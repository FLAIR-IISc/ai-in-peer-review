- Paper formulates Supervised PageRank as a constrained non-convex optimization and proposes two solvers: an inexact zero-order (gradient-free) and an inexact first-order (gradient-based) method.  
- Main text omits experimental results, which are only in the supplement, making the paper feel incomplete.  
- Theorem 1 assumes convexity, but the objective in (2.4) is non-convex; authors must clarify how to choose Φ so the objective is convex.  
- Theorem 3 bounds MK(xK − xK+1) but does not clearly imply that the sequence xk converges to a stationary point.  
- Supplementary Figure 1 shows slow convergence of GFNM; authors should report algorithmic efficiency and comparative runtime to provide a full evaluation.
