- Formulates Supervised PageRank as a constrained non-convex optimization problem using node and edge features.  
- Proposes two solution approaches: (i) a gradient-free method with an inexact zero-order oracle and (ii) a gradient-based method with an inexact first-order oracle.  
- Main concern: the main text contains no experimental results, making the paper feel incomplete.  
- All experimental results are placed in the supplement, leaving the main manuscript lacking empirical validation.  
- Theorem 1 assumes convexity even though the objective in (2.4) is non-convex—unclear how the feasible set Phi is chosen to ensure convexity.  
- Theorem 3 gives a bound for M_K(x^K − x^{K+1}), but it is unclear whether this implies convergence of x_k to a stationary point.  
- Empirical critique: Figure 1 (supplement) shows GFNM converges slowly; the authors should report algorithmic efficiency and runtime comparisons.
