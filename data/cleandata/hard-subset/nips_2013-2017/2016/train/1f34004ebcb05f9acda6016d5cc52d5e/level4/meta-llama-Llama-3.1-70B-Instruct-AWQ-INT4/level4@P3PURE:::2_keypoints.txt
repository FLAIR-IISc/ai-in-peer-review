- Paper formulates Supervised PageRank as a constrained non-convex optimization using node/edge features and proposes two solvers: a gradient-free method with an inexact zero-order oracle and a gradient-based method with an inexact first-order oracle.

- Major complaint: experimental results are relegated to the supplement, making the main text incomplete and lacking empirical validation.

- Comment 1: The main paper should include at least a summary of key experimental findings rather than leaving them only in the supplement.

- Comment 2: Theorem 1 assumes convexity while the objective in (2.4) is non-convex; the authors must clarify how to choose the set Φ to guarantee objective convexity.

- Comment 3: Theorem 3 gives a bound for MK(x_K - x_{K+1}) but does not make clear whether the sequence x_k converges to a stationary point; further discussion or proof is needed.

- Comment 4: Supplementary Figure 1 indicates the Gradient-Free Newton Method (GFNM) converges slowly; the authors should report algorithmic efficiency (e.g., runtime, iteration cost) in addition to convergence properties.
