

This paper formulates Supervised PageRank models as a constrained non-convex optimization problem by incorporating node and edge properties. The authors propose two solution approaches: a gradient-free method utilizing an inexact zero-order oracle, and a gradient-based method employing an inexact first-order oracle. My major concern is that all experimental results are relegated to the supplement, preventing evaluation of the core claims in the main text. Detailed comments:  
1. The absence of experimental results in the main text renders the paper incomplete.  
2. Theorem 1 assumes convexity, yet the objective function in (2.4) is non-convex. The paper fails to specify how the set $\Phi$ should be chosen to ensure convexity of the objective.  
3. Theorem 3 provides a bound for $MK(x_K - x_{K+1})$. This bound does not clarify whether the sequence $\{x_k\}$ converges to a stationary point.  
4. Figure 1 (supplement) indicates slow convergence of the gradient-free method (GFNM). The authors should report algorithmic efficiency alongside convergence behavior.
