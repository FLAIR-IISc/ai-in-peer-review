- Paper formulates Supervised PageRank as a constrained non-convex optimization and proposes a gradient-free (inexact zero-order oracle) and a gradient-based (inexact first-order oracle) solution.  
- Main text lacks experimental results (moved to supplement), making the paper feel incomplete; include a summary of key findings in the main text.  
- Theorem 1 assumes convexity, but the objective (eq. 2.4) is non-convex; clarify how to choose Φ to ensure the required convexity.  
- Theorem 3 bounds MK(x_K − x_{K+1}) but it is unclear if this guarantees convergence of the sequence x_k to a stationary point; further analysis required.  
- Supplementary Figure 1 suggests the Gradient-Free Newton Method (GFNM) converges slowly; report computational efficiency (time, resources) for fair comparison.  
- Overall: interesting approach but needs more complete experiments, clearer theoretical justification, and better discussion of algorithmic efficiency.
