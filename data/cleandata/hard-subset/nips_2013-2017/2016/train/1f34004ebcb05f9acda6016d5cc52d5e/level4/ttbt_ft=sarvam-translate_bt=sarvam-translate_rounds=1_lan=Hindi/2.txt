This research paper focuses on formulating a supervised PageRank model as a constrained non-convex optimization problem, incorporating the properties of nodes and edges. The authors propose two approaches to solving the optimization problem. The first is a gradient-free method with an exact zero-order oracle. The second is a gradient-based method with an exact first-order oracle. My main complaint is that none of the experimental results are in the main text, making this paper incomplete. Detailed comments: 1. The authors have placed the experimental results in the appendix, making the main text incomplete. 2. Theorem 1 assumes convexity, while the function in (2.4) is non-convex. How to select the set $\Phi$ to guarantee the convexity of the objective function? 3. Theorem 3 provides a bound for $MK(xK - x{K+1})$. Can this bound ensure that the sequence $xk$ converges to a stable point? 4. According to Figure 1 in the appendix, the GFNM converges slowly. The authors may also like to provide a report on the efficiency of the algorithm.
