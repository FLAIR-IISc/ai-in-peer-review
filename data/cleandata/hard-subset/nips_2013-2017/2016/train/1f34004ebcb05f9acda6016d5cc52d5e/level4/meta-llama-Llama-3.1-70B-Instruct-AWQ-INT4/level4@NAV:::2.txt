This paper formulates the Supervised PageRank model as a constrained non-convex optimization problem, leveraging properties of nodes and edges. The authors introduce two solution approaches: a gradient-free method utilizing an inexact zero-order oracle, and a gradient-based method employing an inexact first-order oracle. However, a significant concern is that the main text lacks experiment results, rendering it incomplete.

Detailed comments:

1. The relegation of experiment results to the supplement detracts from the completeness of the main text.
2. Theorem 1 assumes convexity, yet the function in equation (2.4) is non-convex. The authors should clarify how to select the set $\Phi$ to ensure the objective function's convexity.
3. Theorem 3 provides a bound for $MK(xK - x{K+1})$. However, it is unclear whether this bound guarantees the convergence of the sequence $xk$ to a stationary point.
4. Figure 1 in the supplement indicates slow convergence of GFNM. The authors may also consider reporting the efficiency of the algorithms to provide a comprehensive evaluation.
