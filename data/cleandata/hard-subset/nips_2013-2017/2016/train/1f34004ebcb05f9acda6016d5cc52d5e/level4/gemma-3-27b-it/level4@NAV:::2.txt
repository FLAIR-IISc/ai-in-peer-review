This paper presents a formulation of Supervised PageRank as a constrained, non-convex optimization problem, leveraging node and edge attributes. To address this optimization, the authors introduce two solution approaches: a gradient-free method utilizing an inexact zero-order oracle, and a gradient-based method employing an inexact first-order oracle. A significant concern is the omission of experimental results from the main text, rendering the presentation incomplete. 

Specific comments are as follows:

1. The placement of experimental results solely within the supplementary material detracts from the completeness of the main paper.
2. Theorem 1 assumes convexity, however, the function defined in equation (2.4) is demonstrably non-convex. Clarification is needed regarding the selection of the set $\Phi$ to ensure the convexity of the objective function.
3. Theorem 3 provides a bound on $MK(xK - x{K+1})$. It would be beneficial to understand if this bound guarantees convergence of the sequence $xk$ to a stationary point.
4. The results shown in Figure 1 of the supplementary material indicate slow convergence for GFNM. Reporting on the computational efficiency of both algorithms would strengthen the evaluation.
