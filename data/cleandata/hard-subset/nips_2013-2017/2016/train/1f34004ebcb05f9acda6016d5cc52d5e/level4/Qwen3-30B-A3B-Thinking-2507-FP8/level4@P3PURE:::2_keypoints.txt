- Paper formulates Supervised PageRank as constrained non-convex optimization and proposes two solvers: a gradient-free method with an inexact zero-order oracle and a gradient-based method with an inexact first-order oracle.  
- Major concern: no experimental results in the main text, undermining the paper's completeness and evaluability.  
- Experimental results are relegated to the supplement, preventing meaningful assessment within the main paper.  
- Theorem 1 assumes convexity, but the objective (2.4) is non-convex—unclear how the choice of set Φ ensures convexity.  
- Theorem 3 gives a bound for M||x_K - x_{K+1}||, but it's unclear whether this guarantees convergence of {x_k} to a stationary point.  
- Supplementary Figure 1 shows slow convergence of GFNM; the authors should report algorithmic efficiency comparisons.
