- Introduces a Supervised PageRank model as a constrained non-convex optimization using node and edge properties.  
- Proposes two solution approaches: a gradient-free method with an inexact zero-order oracle and a gradient-based method with an inexact first-order oracle.  
- Primary concern: experimental results are omitted from the main text, making the paper incomplete and hard to assess.  
- Experimental results in the supplement should be moved into the main text to substantiate claims and improve comprehensibility.  
- Theorem 1 assumes convexity while the objective in eq. (2.4) is non-convex; the paper must explain how Φ is chosen or justify proceeding under non-convexity and discuss implications.  
- Theorem 3’s bound on M K (x_K − x_{K+1}) is unclear as to whether it guarantees convergence to a stationary point; clarify convergence conditions or practical consequences.  
- Supplementary Figure 1 suggests the Gradient-Free Newton Method converges slowly; provide runtime, scalability, complexity analysis, and comparisons to existing methods.
