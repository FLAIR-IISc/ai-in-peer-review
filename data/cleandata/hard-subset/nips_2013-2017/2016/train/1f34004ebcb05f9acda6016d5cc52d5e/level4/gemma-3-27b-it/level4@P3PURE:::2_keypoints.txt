- Paper formulates Supervised PageRank as a constrained non-convex optimization using node/edge features and proposes an inexact zero-order (gradient-free) and an inexact first-order (gradient-based) solver, but omits experiments from the main text making the paper feel incomplete.

- Placing all experimental results in the supplementary material significantly detracts from the completeness of the main manuscript.

- Theorem 1 assumes convexity while the objective in Eq. (2.4) is non-convex; the authors must explain how the set Φ is chosen to ensure convexity or clarify the theorem’s applicability.

- Theorem 3 provides a bound for M K (x_K − x_{K+1}), but it is unclear whether this bound suffices to guarantee convergence of the sequence x_k to a stationary point.

- Supplementary Figure 1 shows the Gradient-Free Newton Method (GFNM) converges slowly; the authors should report computational efficiency (runtime/complexity) for both proposed algorithms.
