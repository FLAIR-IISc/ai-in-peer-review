Paper formulates Supervised PageRank as a constrained non-convex optimization incorporating node/edge features.  
Proposes two solution approaches: a gradient-free method with inexact zero-order oracle and a gradient-based method with inexact first-order oracle.  
Major complaint: all experimental results are placed in the supplement, making the main paper incomplete.  
Detailed comment 1: Experiments in the supplement should be in the main text to make the paper self-contained.  
Detailed comment 2: Theorem 1 assumes convexity, but (2.4) is non-convex—unclear how to choose Φ to guarantee objective convexity.  
Detailed comment 3: Theorem 3 bounds MK(xK - x{K+1}) but it is unclear whether this ensures convergence of x_k to a stationary point.  
Detailed comment 4: Supplementary Figure 1 shows GFNM converges slowly; authors should report and compare algorithmic efficiency.
