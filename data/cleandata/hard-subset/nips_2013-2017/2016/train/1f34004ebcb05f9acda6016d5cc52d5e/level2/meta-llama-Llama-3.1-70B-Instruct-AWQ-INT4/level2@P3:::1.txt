**Summary of the Paper**

This paper proposes two optimization methods for learning Supervised PageRank models, which can account for features of nodes and edges in a graph. The first method is a gradient-free optimization method, and the second method is an adaptive gradient method for non-convex composite optimization problems with inexact first-order oracle. The paper also provides convergence rate analysis for both methods. The proposed methods are applied to a web-page ranking problem and compared with the state-of-the-art gradient-based method.
