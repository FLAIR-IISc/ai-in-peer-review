- Originality: Novel and well-motivated combination of inner Nesterov–Nemirovski solver with constrained inexact-oracle adaptive and gradient-free upper-level methods.
- Significance: Relevant problem and useful provable methods, but modest-scale experiments limit demonstrated impact and scalability.
- Pros: Solid theoretical advances, explicit inner–outer integration, and empirical improvement over a strong baseline.
- Cons: Lack of runtime/memory comparisons, limited evaluation scope (metrics, sensitivity analyses, larger graphs, inner-solver ablations), and some presentation issues.
- Recommendation: Weak accept, contingent on adding comprehensive runtime results and richer evaluation metrics.
