- Proposes two optimization methods (gradient-based and gradient-free) for learning Supervised PageRank with convergence guarantees and application to web page ranking.

- Strength: Introduces two novel optimization methods for Supervised PageRank.

- Strength: Provides thorough convergence rate analysis for both methods.

- Strength: Demonstrates effectiveness on a real-world web ranking problem.

- Weakness: Main paper is incomplete; all experimental results are in the supplement, hindering evaluation.

- Weakness: Theorem 1 assumes convexity though problem (2.4) is non-convex; unclear how to choose Φ to guarantee convexity.

- Weakness: Theorem 3 bounds MK(xK - x_{K+1}) but it is unclear whether this ensures convergence to a stationary point.

- Weakness: Supplementary results show GFNM converges slowly; algorithmic efficiency comparisons are missing.

- Ratings: Quality 7/10, Clarity 8/10, Originality 9/10, Significance 8/10.

- Recommendation: Accept, contingent on addressing paper incompleteness, clarifying theorem assumptions, and improving supplement organization and figure labeling.
