- Proposes replacing eigen-decomposition with Cholesky decomposition in CMA-ES to reduce time and storage complexity; manuscript is well-written and speedup results are promising.  
- Experimental speedup rates suggest the method could become a new standard CMA-ES variant.  
- Originality is limited; approach appears to be a trivial extension and essentially identical to "A More Efficient Rank-one Covariance Matrix Update for Evolution Strategies" — clarification requested.  
- Missing demonstration of equivalence to formula (2); include a brief proof or reference to prior proof.  
- Complexity claim O(μ d^2) equals CMA-ES-Ref; provide a detailed Θ-notation discussion explaining why the proposed method is faster in practice.  
- Claim that path error is small and decreases over time contradicts Hansen and lacks deterministic theoretical proof; require experiments/temporal analyses (not just final performance) to validate.  
- Notation errors/inconsistencies need fixing (e.g., wk → α_k in Algorithm 2, n → d in Table 1, F → f at line 185).  
- In current form, paper is not suitable for NIPS; must address the above concerns and provide additional clarification and supporting evidence.
