- Proposes modifying Cholesky CMA-ES by incorporating both rank-1 and rank-μ updates for the triangular Cholesky factor via a sequence of rank-1 updates.  
- Shows (under the assumption that the covariance converges) that the modified cumulative step-size adaptation does not substantially change algorithm behavior.  
- Presents experimental results evaluating the modified algorithm's performance.  
- Contribution appears limited: the method is a straightforward generalization of Krause et al. (2015) and may not warrant a standalone publication.  
- Prior theoretical work (Beyer 2014) on covariance convergence should be discussed, as it supports confidence in Lemma 1.  
- Minor technical error: denominator in Eq. (1) (and footnote 2) should be σ_t, not σ.  
- Typo: remove the extra "by" in line 94 ("which can by be motivated...").  
- Overall assessment: coherent idea but limited novelty/impact; authors should address these concerns and revise.
