- Main contribution: extends triangular Cholesky factor update to include rank-μ updates implemented as a sequence of rank-1 updates.  
- Theoretical claim: proves that, assuming covariance convergence, the modified cumulative step-size adaptation does not materially change algorithm behavior.  
- Empirical work: presents experimental results on algorithm performance.  
- Significance concern: extension appears to be a straightforward/generalization of Krause et al. (2015) and may have limited novelty.  
- Related theory: existing convergence analysis for Ct (Beyer 2014) could bolster confidence in Lemma 1.  
- Error in equations: Equation (1) denominator and Footnote 2 should use σt instead of σ.  
- Typo: line 94 contains "which can by be motivated..." — remove "by".  
- Overall assessment: interesting results but limited contribution and several errors/minor issues need correction.
