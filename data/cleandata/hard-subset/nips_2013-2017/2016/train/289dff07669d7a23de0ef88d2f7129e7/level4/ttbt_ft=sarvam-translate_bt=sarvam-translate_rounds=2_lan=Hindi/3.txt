This paper considers a Cholesky covariance matrix optimization development strategy for the triangular Cholesky factor (rank-1 update) with both rank-1 and rank-μ updates. The paper proves under the convergence assumption of the covariance that the modified cumulative step-size optimization algorithm does not make significant changes in practice. The paper presents experiments on the performance of the algorithm.

1. This paper considers the Cholesky CMA-ES. It extends the update for the triangular Cholesky factor using both rank-1 and rank-μ updates. However, I do not think its contribution is significant given the work of Krauz et al. 2015, which uses rank-1 updates for the triangular Cholesky factor. This paper presents a direct generalization of this process for rank-μ updates in a series of rank-1 updates.

2. Generally, the convergence of the covariance $Ct$ in CMA-ES is proven by theoretical analysis (Beyer, ECJ, 2014), although it is not based on the exact formulas of CMA-ES. This may increase confidence in Lemma 1.
