

This paper investigates the Cholesky CMA-ES, extending the update procedure for the triangular Cholesky factor to incorporate both rank-1 and rank-μ updates via a sequence of rank-1 updates. Under the assumption of covariance convergence, the paper proves that the modified cumulative step-size adaptation does not significantly alter the algorithm's behavior. Experimental results on algorithm performance are presented.  

1.  The paper's core contribution—generalizing Krause et al. (2015), who employed rank-1 updates for the triangular Cholesky factor, to a rank-μ update via sequential rank-1 updates—lacks sufficient novelty. This constitutes a straightforward extension rather than a significant advancement.  
2.  While the convergence of the covariance matrix $C_t$ in CMA-ES has been theoretically established (Beyer, ECJ, 2014), this analysis does not rely on the exact CMA-ES update formulae. Incorporating this result could strengthen the justification for Lemma 1.  
3.  Additional issues require correction:  
    3.1. Equation (1) uses $\sigma$ in the denominator instead of $\sigma_t$; the same error appears in Footnote 2 on page 3.  
    3.2. Line 94 contains the grammatical error "can by be motivated"; "by" should be removed.
