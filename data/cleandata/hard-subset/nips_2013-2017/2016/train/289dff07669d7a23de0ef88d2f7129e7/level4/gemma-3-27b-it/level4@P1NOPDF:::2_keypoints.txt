- Proposes using Cholesky decomposition instead of eigendecomposition to speed up and reduce storage for CMA-ES; writing is clear and experimental speedups are promising—potential to become a standard implementation.

- Originality is limited; method appears to be a straightforward extension of “A More Efficient Rank-one Covariance Matrix Update for Evolution Strategies” and may be largely identical—request clarification/correction.

- Paper omits a derivation: should explicitly demonstrate equivalence of the proposed algorithm to formula (2) for completeness.

- Computational-complexity claim O(μ d^2) matches CMA-ES-Ref; need a detailed discussion (preferably Θ-notation) explaining why the proposed method is faster in practice.

- Claim that path error is small and decreases contradicts Hansen; since analysis is not deterministic, authors should run experiments tracking error evolution over time rather than only comparing final performance.

- Minor issues: fix notation throughout (e.g., w_k in Algorithm 2 should be α_k; n in Table 1 should be d) and correct typo on Line 185 (‘F’ → ‘f’).

- Overall recommendation: in its current form the paper does not meet NIPS acceptance standards.
