- Paper extends Cholesky CMA-ES to include both rank-1 and rank-µ updates for the triangular Cholesky factor, implementing rank-µ as a sequence of rank-1 updates.  
- Authors provide theoretical analysis claiming that, assuming covariance C_t converges, the modified cumulative step-size adaptation does not significantly change algorithm behavior.  
- Experimental results are presented to evaluate the algorithm's performance.  
- Major concern: contribution significance is limited—generalizing Krause et al. (2015) to rank-µ via sequential rank-1 updates seems straightforward and needs clearer articulation of technical challenges and advantages.  
- Major concern: theoretical result depends on C_t convergence; authors should cite related convergence theory (e.g., Beyer 2014) to bolster the assumption.  
- Minor typos: Equation (1) denominator and footnote 2 should use σ_t instead of σ.  
- Minor typo: line 94 contains an extra "by" in "which can by be motivated by…".
