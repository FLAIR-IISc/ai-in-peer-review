- Uses Cholesky decomposition instead of eigen-decomposition to reduce CMA-ES computation/storage; paper is well-written, method sounds valid, and experiments show encouraging speedups with potential to become a new standard.
- Originality is limited: appears to be a straightforward extension of "A More Efficient Rank-one Covariance Matrix Update for Evolution Strategies"; authors should clarify differences.
- Lacks demonstration that the algorithm is equivalent to formula (2); this validation is essential for completeness.
- Claims computational complexity O(μ d^2) (same as CMA-ES-Ref) but needs a detailed discussion (use Θ notation) explaining why it performs better in practice.
- Theoretical claim that the path error is small and decreases contradicts Hansen; because the analysis is non-deterministic, experimental validation over time (not just final performance) is required.
- Minor notation errors: wk in Algorithm 2 should be α_k, and n in Table 1 should be d.
- Typo: line 185 uses 'F' but should be 'f.'
- Overall recommendation: in current state, the paper does not meet NIPS acceptance standards.
