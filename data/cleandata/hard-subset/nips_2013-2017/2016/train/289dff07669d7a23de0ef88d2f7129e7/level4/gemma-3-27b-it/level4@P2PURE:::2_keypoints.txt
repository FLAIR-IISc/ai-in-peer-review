- Positive: Uses Cholesky instead of eigen-decomposition to reduce CMA-ES time/storage; method is well-written, appears sound, and experimental speedups are encouraging.  
- Originality concern: Seems a straightforward extension of “A More Efficient Rank-one Covariance Matrix Update for Evolution Strategies”; clarify novelty and differences.  
- Missing proof: The paper should demonstrate equivalence of the proposed algorithm to formula (2) (crucial verification despite prior work).  
- Complexity claim unclear: Claim of O(μd²) equals CMA-ES-Ref—provide a detailed discussion (use Θ-notation) to justify observed speed improvements.  
- Path length claim questionable: Authors assert path length error diminishes over time, which contradicts Hansen; require dedicated experiments to validate this theoretical claim rather than only final performance comparisons.  
- Notation errors: In Algorithm 2, w_k should be α_k; in Table 1, n should be d.  
- Typo: Line 185 'F' should be 'f'.  
- Recommendation: As presented, the paper does not meet NIPS acceptance standards.
