- Proposes generalization of Cholesky CMA-ES update to include rank-μ by applying a sequence of rank-1 updates to the triangular Cholesky factor.

- Contribution appears limited/ incremental because Krause et al. (2015) already applied rank-1 updates to the triangular Cholesky factor; this is a direct generalization.
