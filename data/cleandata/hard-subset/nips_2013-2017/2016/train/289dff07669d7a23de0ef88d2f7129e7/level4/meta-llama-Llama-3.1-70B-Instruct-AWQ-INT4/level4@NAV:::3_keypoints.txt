- Paper extends Cholesky CMA-ES by applying rank-1 and rank-μ updates to the triangular Cholesky factor.  
- Significance is questioned because it is a straightforward extension of Krause et al. (2015), which used rank-1 updates.  
- The rank-μ update is implemented as a sequence of rank-1 updates, making the contribution incremental.  
- Covariance convergence results (Beyer, ECJ 2014) support the plausibility of Lemma 1 despite not using exact CMA-ES formulas.  
- Typo in Equation (1) and Footnote 2: denominator should be σt, not σ.  
- Grammatical error in line 94: remove the extra word "by" in "which can by be motivated by...".
