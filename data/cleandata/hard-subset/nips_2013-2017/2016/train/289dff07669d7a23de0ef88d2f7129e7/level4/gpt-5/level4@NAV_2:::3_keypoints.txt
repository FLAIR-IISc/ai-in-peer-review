- Proposes a Cholesky CMA-ES that maintains a triangular Cholesky factor and includes rank-1 and rank-µ updates (the latter implemented as a sequence of rank-1 updates), arguing a modified CSA (using the inverse triangular factor instead of C^{-1/2}) is behaviorally equivalent under a covariance-convergence assumption; includes empirical comparisons to standard CMA-ES variants.  
- Contribution is largely incremental relative to Krause & Igel (2015), which already gave a triangular Cholesky rank-1 update; this paper mainly generalizes that approach to rank-µ via composing rank-1 updates.  
- Existing theoretical analyses of covariance convergence in CMA-ES (Beyer, ECJ 2014) lend credibility to Lemma 1 despite not covering the exact CMA-ES formulas.
