- Proposes using Cholesky decomposition instead of eigendecomposition to reduce CMA-ES time and storage complexity.  
- Writing is clear and well-structured; method appears sound.  
- Experimental results show compelling speedup metrics and practical promise.  
- Limited originality: method is a straightforward extension of prior work ("A More Efficient Rank-one Covariance Matrix Update for Evolution Strategies") and may be nearly identical.  
- Must explicitly establish equivalence to Equation (2) for reproducibility despite prior proofs.  
- The claim of computational complexity O(μd²) needs deeper justification; use Θ-notation to clarify constants versus asymptotic behavior.  
- The assertion that path errors decrease over time contradicts Hansen’s analysis and requires deterministic theoretical support or additional experiments tracking path error over iterations.  
- Minor issues: inconsistent notation (Algorithm 2 uses `wk` vs `αk`; Table 1 uses `n` instead of standard `d`) and a typographical error ('F' should be 'f' on line 185).  
- Overall, despite speedups, the paper lacks sufficient novelty, rigorous complexity analysis, and resolution of theoretical contradictions to meet NIPS standards.
