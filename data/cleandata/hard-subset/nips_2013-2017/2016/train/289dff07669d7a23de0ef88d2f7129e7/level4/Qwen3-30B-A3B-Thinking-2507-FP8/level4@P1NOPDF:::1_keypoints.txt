- CMA-ES is the state-of-the-art stochastic algorithm for derivative-free optimization.  
- The paper proposes a novel covariance update using triangular Cholesky factors.  
- The new update markedly reduces computational complexity and memory for state parameters.  
- Numerical results show maintained CMA-ES performance in function evaluations to reach targets.  
- The method substantially reduces wall-clock time, especially in high-dimensional problems.  
- Eigenvalues of the covariance matrix become immediately available at no extra cost.  
- The variant is a significant contribution and is well-positioned to become the default for medium-to-large-scale problems.  
- The paper is well-written, rigorously validated, and aligns with the conference scope.  
- Minor concern: clarify implementation details for comparisons (programming language, hardware, CPU fairness).  
- Minor concern: explain the anomalous behavior for Cigar and Discus at dimension 256 in Figure 2.
