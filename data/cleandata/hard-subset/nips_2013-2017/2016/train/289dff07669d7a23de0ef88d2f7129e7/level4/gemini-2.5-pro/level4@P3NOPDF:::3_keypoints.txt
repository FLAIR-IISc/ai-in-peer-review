- Proposes extending Cholesky CMA-ES to incorporate rank-µ updates by implementing them as a sequence of rank-1 updates, with theoretical analysis and experiments.  
- Main concern: contribution appears incremental and a straightforward generalization of Krause et al. (2015), lacking demonstrated novelty.  
- Paper should clarify the non-obvious technical challenges and insights involved in moving from a single rank-1 update to sequential rank-µ updates.  
- Theoretical results hinge on the assumption that the covariance matrix C_t converges, which needs stronger justification.  
- Recommend citing and discussing prior work on covariance convergence (e.g., Beyer, ECJ 2014) to support the assumption.  
- Minor fixes: Equation (1) and Footnote 2 should use σ_t instead of σ for consistency.  
- Minor fix: remove the redundant word "by" in the sentence on line 94.
