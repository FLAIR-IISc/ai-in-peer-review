- Paper extends Cholesky CMA-ES to incorporate both rank-1 and rank-μ updates (implemented as sequential rank-1 updates), proves the modified cumulative step-size adaptation does not significantly alter behavior, and provides experimental results.

- Contribution is incremental: Krause et al. (2015) already used rank-1 updates, so generalizing to rank-μ via sequential rank-1 updates is straightforward.

- The convergence statement (Lemma 1) would be strengthened by citing broader theoretical results on CMA-ES covariance convergence (e.g., Beyer, ECJ 2014).

- Minor error: the σ in the denominator of Eq.(1) (and in footnote 2) should be σ_t.

- Minor wording typo: “which can by be motivated by…” should read “which can be motivated by…”.
