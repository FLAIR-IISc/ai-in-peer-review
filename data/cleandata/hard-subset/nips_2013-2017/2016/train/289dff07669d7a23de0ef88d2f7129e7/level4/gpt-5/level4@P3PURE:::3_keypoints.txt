- Paper extends Cholesky CMA-ES by implementing rank-μ updates on the triangular Cholesky factor as a sequence of rank-1 updates, proves modified CSA is asymptotically benign under covariance convergence, and reports experiments.

- Novelty/significance: Contribution seems limited because Krause et al. (2015) already used rank-1 updates and this is a straightforward generalization to rank-μ.

- Theoretical assumptions: Covariance convergence results (Beyer, ECJ 2014) — albeit not using exact CMA-ES formulas — support Lemma 1’s assumption.

- Other issues: Equation (1) and footnote 2 use σ in the denominator but should use σ_t.

- Other issues: Typo on line 94 — remove the extraneous word “by.”
