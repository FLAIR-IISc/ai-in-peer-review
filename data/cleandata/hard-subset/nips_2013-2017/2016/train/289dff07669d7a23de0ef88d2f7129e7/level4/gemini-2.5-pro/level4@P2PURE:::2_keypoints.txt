- Proposes replacing eigendecomposition with Cholesky in CMA-ES to reduce computation/memory; method is well-written, sound, and shows promising speedups with potential to become a standard implementation.  
- Primary weakness is limited originality: approach appears nearly identical to prior work ("A More Efficient Rank-one Covariance Matrix Update for Evolution Strategies"); authors must clarify novel contributions.  
- Missing proof of equivalence to standard CMA-ES (Equation (2)); necessary for self-containment and rigor.  
- Insufficient complexity analysis: stating O(μd²) is asymptotically identical to reference; need tighter (Θ) analysis and explanation of practical speedups and lower constant factors.  
- Lacks empirical validation of claimed small/decreasing evolution-path error; must include experiments tracking this error over optimization (claim may contradict Hansen).  
- Notational inconsistencies (e.g., wk should be αk in Algorithm 2; n in Table 1 should be d).  
- Typo: line 185 uses 'F' instead of 'f'.  
- Overall recommendation: due to originality concerns and missing analyses/validations, the paper does not meet NIPS standards in its current form.
