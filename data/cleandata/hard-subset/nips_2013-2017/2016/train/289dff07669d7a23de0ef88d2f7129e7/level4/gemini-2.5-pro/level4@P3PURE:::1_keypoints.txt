- Paper proposes a novel CMA-ES covariance update using triangular Cholesky factors.  
- Cholesky-based update reduces computational complexity and memory for storing state.  
- Method is rigorously evaluated with numerical experiments.  
- New update does not worsen number of function evaluations to reach targets.  
- Significantly reduces wall-clock time, especially in high-dimensional settings.  
- Can provide covariance eigenvalues at no extra cost.  
- Expected to become default for medium-to-large scale problems due to clear advantages.  
- Paper is well-written and the approach is carefully evaluated.
