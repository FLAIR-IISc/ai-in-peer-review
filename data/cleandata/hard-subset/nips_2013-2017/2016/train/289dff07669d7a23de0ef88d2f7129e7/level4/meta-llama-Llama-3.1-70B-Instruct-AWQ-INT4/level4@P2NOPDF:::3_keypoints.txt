- Proposes a modified Cholesky CMA-ES that incorporates both rank-1 and rank-μ updates to the triangular Cholesky factor, applied sequentially as a series of rank-1 updates.  
- Proves under the assumption of convergent covariance that the modified cumulative step-size adaptation does not significantly change algorithm behavior.  
- Presents experimental results to demonstrate the algorithm’s performance.  
- Contribution may be limited since Krause et al. (2015) already applied rank-1 updates to the triangular Cholesky factor.  
- Paper is essentially a straightforward generalization of Krause et al. to rank-μ via sequential rank-1 updates.  
- Convergence of covariance Ct has prior theoretical analysis (Beyer, ECJ 2014), which could strengthen confidence in Lemma 1 despite not using exact CMA-ES formulae.  
- Errors to fix: Equation (1) denominator should be σt (not σ), same error in Footnote 2 on page 3.  
- Typographical error in line 94: delete the extra word “by” in “which can by be motivated by principles from.”  
- Overall assessment: extension is modest given prior work and the manuscript requires the noted corrections.
