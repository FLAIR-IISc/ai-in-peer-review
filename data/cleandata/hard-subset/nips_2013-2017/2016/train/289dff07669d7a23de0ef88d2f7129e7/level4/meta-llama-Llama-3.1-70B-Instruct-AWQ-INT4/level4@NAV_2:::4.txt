This paper presents a novel approach to improve the efficiency of the Covariance Matrix Adaptation Evolution Strategy (CMA-ES) by utilizing the triangular Cholesky factorization technique. The main contribution of this work is to replace the square root of the covariance matrix with its triangular Cholesky factor, which reduces the runtime complexity of the algorithm while maintaining its performance. The authors provide a theoretical justification for this approach and demonstrate its effectiveness through empirical experiments.

The paper is well-written, and the authors have done a good job in explaining the background and the proposed method. The use of the triangular Cholesky factorization technique is a significant improvement over the standard CMA-ES, as it reduces the computational cost of calculating the inverse of matrices.
