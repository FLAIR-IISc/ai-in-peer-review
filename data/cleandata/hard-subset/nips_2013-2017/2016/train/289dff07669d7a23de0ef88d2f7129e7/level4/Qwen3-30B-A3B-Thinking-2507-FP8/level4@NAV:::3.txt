

This paper investigates the Cholesky CMA-ES, extending the update for the triangular Cholesky factor to incorporate both rank-1 and rank-μ updates (applied sequentially as rank-1 updates). Under the convergence assumption of the covariance, the paper demonstrates that the modified cumulative step-size adaptation does not substantially alter the algorithm's behavior. Experimental results on algorithm performance are presented.  
1. The paper addresses the Cholesky CMA-ES and generalizes the rank-1 update for the triangular Cholesky factor to a rank-μ update via sequential rank-1 updates. However, the contribution appears limited, as Krause et al. (2015) already established the rank-1 update approach for the triangular Cholesky factor.  
2. The convergence of the covariance $C_t$ in CMA-ES is theoretically proven (Beyer, ECJ, 2014), though not based on the exact CMA-ES update formulae. Incorporating this analysis would strengthen the confidence in Lemma 1.
