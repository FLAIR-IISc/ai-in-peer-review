- Paper is well-written and the Cholesky-based method yields promising speedups that could become a new CMA-ES variant.  
- Originality is limited: method appears to be a straightforward extension and largely identical to "A More Efficient Rank-one Covariance Matrix Update for Evolution Strategies"; authors should clarify or correct this.  
- Missing proof/derivation: need demonstration that the proposed algorithm is equivalent to formula (2) (or cite/recap the previous proof).  
- Complexity claim unclear: both methods cited as O(μ d^2); provide a detailed discussion (use Θ-notation) explaining why the proposed algorithm is actually faster.  
- Path-error claim must be validated: the assertion that path error is small and decreases (contradicting Hansen) requires experimental validation over time, not just final-performance comparisons.  
- Notation inconsistencies: fix symbols (wk → αk in Algorithm 2; n → d in Table 1; F → f at line 185).  
- Overall recommendation: in current form the paper is not suitable for NIPS.
