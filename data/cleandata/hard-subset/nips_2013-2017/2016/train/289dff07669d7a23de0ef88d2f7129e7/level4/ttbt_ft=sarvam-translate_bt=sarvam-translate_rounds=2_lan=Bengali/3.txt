This paper considers the Cholesky-Kovalevsky adaptive evolution algorithm with both rank-1 and rank-μ updates (in the order of rank-1 update) for the triangular Cholesky factor. The paper proves, under the assumption of convergence of the covariance, that the modified order-preserving step-size adaptation algorithm does not significantly change its behavior. The paper presents an experiment on the performance of the algorithm.

1. This paper considers the Cholesky CMA-ES. It extends the update using both rank-1 and rank-μ updates for the triangular Cholesky factor. However, considering Krause et al.'s 2015 work, I do not think its contribution is significant. Krause et al. used rank-1 updates for the triangular Cholesky factor. This paper presents a simplification of this method for rank-μ updates in the order of rank-1 updates.

2. In general, the convergence of the covariance in CMA-ES is proven by theoretical analysis of $Ct$ (Beyer, ECJ, 2014), although it is not based on the exact formula of CMA-ES. This may increase the confidence of Lemma 1.
