- Proposes a Cholesky-based CMA-ES variant that reduces computation and memory; writing is clear and experiments report encouraging speedups with potential for wide adoption.  
- Novelty appears incremental and closely resembles “A More Efficient Rank-one Covariance Matrix Update for Evolution Strategies”; clarification on relation to that prior work is needed.  
- Must demonstrate equivalence of the proposed algorithm to formula (2) explicitly for completeness, even if prior proofs exist.  
- Complexity claim O(μd^2) matches CMA-ES-Ref; the paper needs a more detailed justification for the observed speedup and should consider Θ-notation for precise comparison.  
- The claim of a diminishing path error contradicts Hansen’s findings and requires robust, time-evolution experiments (not just final performance) because the theoretical analysis is nondeterministic.  
- Minor corrections: Algorithm 2 uses ‘wk’ where ‘αk’ is likely intended; Table 1’s ‘n’ should be ‘d’; typo on line 185: ‘F’ → ‘f’.  
- Given these issues, the reviewer recommends rejection in its current form for NIPS.
