- Paper is well written with a reasonable approach and promising experimental speedups; could become a new CMA-ES variant.  
- Originality appears limited—method seems a straightforward extension of "A More Efficient Rank-one Covariance Matrix Update for Evolution Strategies."  
- Must demonstrate equivalence of the proposed algorithm to formula (2) (even if proven previously).  
- Complexity claim O(μ d^2) equals CMA-ES-Ref; use Θ and provide a detailed explanation why the new algorithm is actually faster.  
- Claim that path error is small and decreases over time contradicts Hansen; provide deterministic analysis or experiments to validate this claim rather than only final performance comparisons.  
- Notation errors: wk in Algorithm 2 should be αk; n in Table 1 should be d; “F” should be “f” on line 185.  
- Conclusion: paper is not ready for NIPS in its current form.
