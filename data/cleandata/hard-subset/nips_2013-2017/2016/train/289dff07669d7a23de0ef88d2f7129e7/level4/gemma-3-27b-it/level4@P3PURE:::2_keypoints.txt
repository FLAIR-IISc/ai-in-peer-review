- Paper replaces eigen-decomposition with Cholesky decomposition to reduce CMA-ES time and storage complexity.  
- Writing is clear, method is reasonable, and experimental speedup results are promising; potential to become a new standard CMA-ES implementation.  
- Originality is limited; method appears a straightforward extension of “A More Efficient Rank-one Covariance Matrix Update for Evolution Strategies.” Clarification on relation to that prior work is requested.  
- The paper omits a demonstration of equivalence to formula (2); restating this equivalence is necessary for completeness.  
- Claimed computational complexity O(μ d^2) equals CMA-ES-Ref; a detailed complexity discussion (e.g., using Θ notation) is needed to explain observed speed improvements.  
- The claim of a small and decreasing error in path length contradicts Hansen’s findings; dedicated experiments tracking error over time (not just final performance) are required.  
- Theoretical analysis is non-deterministic; more granular empirical validation of error behavior is necessary.  
- Minor notation issues: w_k in Algorithm 2 should be α_k; n in Table 1 should be d; 'F' should be 'f' on line 185.  
- Overall recommendation: paper, in current form, does not meet NIPS acceptance standards.
