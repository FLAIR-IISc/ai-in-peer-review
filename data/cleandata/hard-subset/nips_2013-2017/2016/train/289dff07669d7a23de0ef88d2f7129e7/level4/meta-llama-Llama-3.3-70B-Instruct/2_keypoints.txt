- Proposes using Cholesky decomposition instead of eigen-decomposition to reduce CMA-ES time and storage complexity; experiments show notable speedups and clear presentation.  
- Approach appears sound and could become a future standard for CMA-ES.  
- Novelty is limited; method seems a straightforward extension of "A More Efficient Rank-one Covariance Matrix Update for Evolution Strategies" and needs clarification if this assessment is incorrect.  
- Missing proof: must demonstrate equivalence of the proposed algorithm to formula (2), even if previously established.  
- Complexity claim O(μ d^2), identical to CMA-ES-Ref, requires detailed justification (use Θ notation) to substantiate the claimed advantage.  
- Claim that path error is minimal and decreases over time contradicts Hansen’s findings and needs experimental validation beyond final-performance comparisons.  
- Minor issues: inconsistent notation (wk vs α_k), Table 1 labels 'n' instead of 'd', and replace 'F' with 'f' in line 185.  
- Overall recommendation: manuscript, in its current state, does not meet NIPS acceptance standards.
