- Paper proposes using Cholesky decomposition instead of eigen-decomposition to reduce time and storage complexity of CMA-ES.  
- Writing and presentation are clear; method is reasonable and experiments show promising speedups — could become a standard variant.  
- Originality is limited: appears to be a trivial extension or basically identical to "A More Efficient Rank-one Covariance Matrix Update for Evolution Strategies."  
- Missing proof/discussion of whether the proposed algorithm is equivalent to formula (2), even if prior work covered it.  
- Complexity claim O(μ d^2) matches CMA-ES-Ref; need detailed analysis (use Θ notation) explaining why the proposed algorithm is actually faster.  
- Claim that path error is small and decreases over time contradicts Hansen; because theoretical analysis is non-deterministic, experiments should validate this claim rather than only reporting final performance.  
- Notation errors to fix: 'wk' in Algorithm 2 should be 'α_k', 'n' in Table 1 should be 'd', and 'F' should be 'f' in line 185.  
- Conclusion: paper, in current form, is not suitable for NIPS.
