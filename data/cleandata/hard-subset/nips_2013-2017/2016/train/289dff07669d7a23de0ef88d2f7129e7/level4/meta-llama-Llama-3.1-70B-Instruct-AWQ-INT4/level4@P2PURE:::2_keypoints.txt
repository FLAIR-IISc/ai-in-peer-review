- Proposes replacing eigen-decomposition with Cholesky decomposition in CMA-ES to reduce time and storage; well-written with promising speedups and potential to become a new standard.  
- Originality is limited—appears to be a straightforward extension of "A More Efficient Rank-one Covariance Matrix Update for Evolution Strategies" and methods seem essentially identical; clarification requested.  
- Paper omits a demonstration of equivalence to formula (2), which should be shown even if previously proven.  
- Claims O(μ d^2) complexity (same as CMA-ES-Ref) but lacks a detailed discussion explaining why the proposed algorithm is actually faster; suggest using Θ notation.  
- Asserts path error is small and decreases over time, contradicting Hansen; theoretical analysis is non-deterministic, so experiments are needed to validate this claim rather than only comparing final performance.  
- Minor corrections: 'wk' in Algorithm 2 should be 'αk'; 'n' in Table 1 should be 'd'; 'F' should be 'f' in line 185.  
- Overall judgment: in its current form the paper does not meet NIPS acceptance criteria.
