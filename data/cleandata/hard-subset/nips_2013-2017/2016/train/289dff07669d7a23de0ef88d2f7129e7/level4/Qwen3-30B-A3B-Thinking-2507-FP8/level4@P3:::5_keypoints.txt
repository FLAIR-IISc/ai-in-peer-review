- Introduces Cholesky-CMA-ES that replaces covariance matrix square root with Cholesky factorization to achieve O(μ d²) covariance updates (vs. O(d³)) while preserving optimization performance.  
- Maintains and adapts a lower-triangular Cholesky factor A_t via rank-1 updates (Algorithm 1) to avoid costly matrix square roots.  
- Theoretical Lemma 1 justifies replacing C_t^{-1/2}(m_{t+1}-m_t) with A_t^{-1}(m_{t+1}-m_t), showing the introduced error vanishes as an orthogonal rotation E_t→I during convergence.  
- Empirically matches state-of-the-art delayed CMA-ES in function evaluations on Sphere, Cigar, Rosenbrock, Diffpowers, while achieving up to 20× runtime speedup at d=64 and increasing gains with dimensionality.

Strengths:
- Directly addresses a fundamental computational bottleneck in CMA-ES with a novel, theoretically grounded method distinct from prior heuristic/variant-specific attempts.  
- Paper is clear and complete: algorithms and theory are concise and well-presented, and baselines are appropriate.  
- Significant for high-dimensional problems (d≫64) and applications with cheap/fast evaluations, offering optimal time/memory complexity and practical speedups.

Weaknesses / Suggestions:
- Practical relevance is somewhat niche and should be framed as enabling CMA-ES where covariance updates become non-negligible (e.g., d>64 or sub-second evaluations).  
- Empirical validation should be deepened: show E_t evolution plots, perform spectral variation/condition-number tests, and compare to non-delayed (full) CMA-ES to isolate Cholesky benefits.  
- Clarify limitations/trade-offs versus delay or diagonal strategies for small-to-moderate d (e.g., d<16–32) where alternatives may be preferable.

Pro/Con summary:
- Pro: Novel, rigorous, empirically validated speedup with no loss in solution quality and optimal complexity.  
- Con: Niche applicability, limited real-world validation, and insufficient empirical analysis of the key theoretical assumption (E_t convergence).

Recommendation:
- Recommend acceptance with minor revisions to expand empirical analysis and clarify practical scope.
