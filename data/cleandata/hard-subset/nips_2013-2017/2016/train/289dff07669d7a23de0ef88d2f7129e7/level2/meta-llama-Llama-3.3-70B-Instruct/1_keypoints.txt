- Proposes Cholesky-CMA-ES: replace covariance matrix square root with a triangular Cholesky factor to achieve O(µ d^2) covariance updates.  
- Claims maintained optimization performance comparable to original CMA-ES while improving time complexity.  
- Supports claims with theoretical analysis and empirical experiments on benchmark functions.  
- Paper is well-written with a clear, concise introduction to CMA-ES and its limitations.  
- Modification is well-motivated and theoretically justified.  
- Empirical experiments show significant speedups, especially in high-dimensional problems.  
- Comprehensive related-work review and proper citations; novelty is the covariance-update modification.  
- Provides sufficient implementation details for reproducibility and discusses limitations and risks of low-dimensional approximations.  
- Weakness: assumes reader familiarity with CMA-ES and its applications.  
- Weakness: empirical evaluation limited to a specific set of benchmark functions.  
- Weakness: lacks a detailed analysis of the computational complexity of the proposed algorithm.  
- Overall recommendation: accept — strengths and contribution outweigh weaknesses.
