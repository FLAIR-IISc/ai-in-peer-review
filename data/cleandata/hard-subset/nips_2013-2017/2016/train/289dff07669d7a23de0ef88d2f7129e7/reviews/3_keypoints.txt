- Paper studies Cholesky CMA-ES with both rank-1 and rank-μ updates for the triangular Cholesky factor (implemented as a sequence of rank-1 updates).  
- Under the covariance convergence assumption, the paper shows the modified cumulative step-size adaptation does not significantly change algorithm behavior.  
- Experimental results on algorithm performance are presented.  
- Contribution is limited: extension to rank-μ is a straightforward generalization of Krause et al. 2015, so novelty is not convincing.  
- Theoretical support for covariance convergence (e.g., Beyer, ECJ 2014) could strengthen confidence in Lemma 1.  
- Minor issues: σ in the denominator of Eq. (1) should be \tilde{σ} (same error in footnote 2 on p.3).  
- Minor typo: delete extra "by" in line 94 ("which can by be motivated ..." → remove "by").
