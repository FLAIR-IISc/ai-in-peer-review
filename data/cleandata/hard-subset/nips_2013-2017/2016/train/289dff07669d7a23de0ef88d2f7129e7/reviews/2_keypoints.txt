- Clear writing and reasonable method: replacing eigen-decomposition with Cholesky yields promising speedups and could become a new standard.  
- Limited originality: appears to be a trivial extension and basically identical to "A More Efficient Rank-one Covariance Matrix Update for Evolution Strategies."  
- Missing equivalence proof: must show the algorithm is equivalent to formula (2) (essential even if proven previously).  
- Complexity claim unclear: claiming O(μ d^2) is the same as CMA-ES-Ref; authors should use Θ-notation and explain in detail why their method is actually faster.  
- Unvalidated path-error claim: claim that path error is small and decreases (contrary to Hansen) requires experimental validation over time, not only final-performance comparison.  
- Notation and typos: wk in Algorithm 2 should be α_k; n in Table 1 should be d; 'F' should be 'f' (line 185).  
- Overall recommendation: paper is not acceptable for NIPS in its current form.
