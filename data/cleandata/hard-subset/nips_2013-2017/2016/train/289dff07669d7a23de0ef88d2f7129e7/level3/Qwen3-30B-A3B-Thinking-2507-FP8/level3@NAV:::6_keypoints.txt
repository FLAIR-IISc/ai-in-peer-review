- Misleading naming: calling it "Cholesky-CMA-ES" conflicts with Suttorp et al. (2009); rename to avoid confusion.  
- Missing prior comparison: no empirical baselines against Suttorp (2009) or Krause & Igel (2015), undermining performance claims.  
- Overstated optimality: method is not optimal for separable functions (e.g., Sphere) where diagonal covariance suffices.  
- Marginal runtime benefit: claimed ~2× runtime saving over Suttorp is minor and quadratic complexity remains, so scalability is not solved.  
- Incorrect stability claim: paper asserts Suttorp’s method is numerically unstable despite Suttorp et al. demonstrating stability.  
- Cigar function issue likely due to median-plotting artifacts and needs clarification.  
- Mischaracterization of EVD: eigenvalue decomposition is not typically done every update, contrary to the paper’s implication.  
- Trivial novelty: main change is looping rankOneUpdate (Algorithm 1), which is algorithmically simple and incremental.  
- Strength: sound theoretical justification for the step-size adaptation approximation and a well-structured algorithm description.  
- Recommendation: Reject.  
- Required fixes to salvage: rename the method; run and report experiments against Suttorp and Krause variants; temper claims to reflect marginal gains (e.g., "reduces runtime by ~2× for full covariance updates"); clarify and substantiate the stability argument.
