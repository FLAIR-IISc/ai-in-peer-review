- Proposes replacing eigen-decomposition with Cholesky factorization for CMA-ES covariance updates, claiming O(μd^2) time and optimal memory.
- Paper is well-written and the method description is reasonably clear.
- Idea is promising and could lead to significant speedups.
- Originality is limited; appears to be a trivial extension of Krause & Igel’s prior work.
- Major missing proof of equivalence between Cholesky-CMA-ES and the original CMA-ES formula (2).
- Time-complexity claim is unclear; should use Θ-notation and precisely justify why it is faster than CMA-ES-Ref.
- Claim that path error is small and decreases is unvalidated and contradicts prior findings; requires experimental evidence.
- Minor notation and typo issues (e.g., wk→α_k, n→d in Table 1, 'F'→'f' on line 185).
- Recommendation: Reject in current form but encourage revision and resubmission after addressing the above concerns.
