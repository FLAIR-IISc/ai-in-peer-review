This paper presents a novel approach to spectral analysis of nonlinear dynamical systems using the Koopman operator in reproducing kernel Hilbert spaces (RKHSs). The authors propose a modal decomposition algorithm that extends dynamic mode decomposition (DMD) to RKHSs, allowing for the approximation of Koopman eigenfunctions with richer basis functions. The algorithm is shown to be mathematically correct and is robustified using principal orthogonal directions (POD) bases.

The paper is well-written and clearly organized, making it easy to follow the authors' arguments. The introduction provides a thorough background on the Koopman operator and DMD, and the authors clearly motivate the need for a new approach. The technical sections are well-structured, and the authors provide a clear explanation of the proposed algorithm and its connection to the Koopman operator.

3. Robustification: The authors propose a robustification of the algorithm using POD bases, which is a practical and useful contribution.
