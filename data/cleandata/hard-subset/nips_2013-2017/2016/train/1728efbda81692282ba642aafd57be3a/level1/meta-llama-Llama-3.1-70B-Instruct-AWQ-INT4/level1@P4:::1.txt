This paper proposes a novel approach to spectral analysis of nonlinear dynamical systems using the Koopman operator in reproducing kernel Hilbert spaces (RKHSs). The authors extend the dynamic mode decomposition (DMD) algorithm to work with reproducing kernels, allowing for a more flexible and accurate representation of the dynamics.

The authors also propose a robustified version of the algorithm using principal orthogonal decomposition (POD) bases, which can help to mitigate numerical issues.
