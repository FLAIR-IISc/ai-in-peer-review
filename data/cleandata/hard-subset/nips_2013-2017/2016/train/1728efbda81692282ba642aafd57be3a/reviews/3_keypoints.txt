- Paper derives spectral analysis of the Koopman operator in RKHS and proposes a modal decomposition algorithm.  
- Authors include a robustifying method and present numerical experiments demonstrating the approach.  
- Framework is an extension of dynamical mode decomposition using the kernel trick.  
- Strength: paper is well written, organized, and presents theoretical concepts clearly.  
- Strength: technically solid; numerical results illustrate usefulness despite limited comparison to state-of-the-art.  
- Major concern: Section 3.1, Equation (7), and the proof of Theorem 1 are unclear.  
- Question: Equation (7) appears similar to Equation (2) — purpose and distinction are unclear.  
- Mathematical inconsistency: KH is an operator H→H but φ is a map M→H, so φ may not belong to H; this needs explanation.  
- Proof doubt: lines 144–157 — phrase “applying the operator KH to both sides” is unclear since both sides seem scalar.  
- Assumptions in Theorem 1 are strong and not explicit; additional assumptions appear in the proof and their validity is unclear.  
- Typos: Line 55 “the the”; Line 153 uses \kappaj / \lambdaj instead of \kappai / \lambda_i.
