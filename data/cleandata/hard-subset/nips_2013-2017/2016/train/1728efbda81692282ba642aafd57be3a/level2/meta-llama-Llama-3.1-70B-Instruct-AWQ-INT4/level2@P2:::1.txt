This paper proposes a novel approach to spectral analysis of nonlinear dynamical systems using the Koopman operator in reproducing kernel Hilbert spaces (RKHSs). The authors extend the dynamic mode decomposition (DMD) algorithm to work with nonlinear basis functions in RKHSs, which enables the approximation of Koopman eigenfunctions with richer basis functions. The proposed algorithm is mathematically sound and is shown to be effective in extracting meaningful information about the dynamics of nonlinear systems.

The paper is well-organized, and the authors provide a clear and concise introduction to the Koopman operator and DMD.
