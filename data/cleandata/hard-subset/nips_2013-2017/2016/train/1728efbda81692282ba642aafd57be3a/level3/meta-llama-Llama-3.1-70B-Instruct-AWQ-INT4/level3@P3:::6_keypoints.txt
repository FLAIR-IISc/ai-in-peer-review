- Proposes spectral analysis of the Koopman operator in RKHS, extending DMD with reproducing kernels.  
- Extension enables more accurate approximation of Koopman eigenfunctions using nonlinear basis functions.  
- Algorithm is mathematically sound and yields a finite modal decomposition of dynamics.  
- Paper is well-written and clearly explains theoretical background and algorithm.  
- Provides a readable introduction to Koopman operator and DMD for non-experts.  
- Use of reproducing kernels is a novel and clearly motivated contribution.  
- Empirical examples (toy system and Hénon map) demonstrate accurate eigenvalue/eigenfunction estimation.  
- Shows applications for prediction, sequential change detection, and dynamic pattern recognition.  
- Good literature review and clear differentiation from prior DMD/kernel work.  
- Paper has potential significance for nonlinear dynamical systems and various applications.  
- Limited empirical evaluation — only a few examples, unclear scalability to complex systems.  
- Lacks direct comparisons to alternative methods (e.g., standard DMD, PCA).  
- Needs a more thorough evaluation and benchmarking against other approaches.  
- Recommendation: add more empirical examples, comparisons, and detailed application guidance.  
- Overall positive assessment with a rating of 8/10.
