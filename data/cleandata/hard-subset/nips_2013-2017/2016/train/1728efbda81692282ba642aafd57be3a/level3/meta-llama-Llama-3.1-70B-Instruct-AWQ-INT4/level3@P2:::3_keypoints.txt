- Proposes spectral analysis of the Koopman operator in RKHSs and extends DMD to RKHS for nonlinear dynamical systems.
- Main contribution: derivation of a modal decomposition algorithm for the Koopman operator in RKHSs.
- Includes a robustifying method to improve algorithm stability and accuracy.
- Paper is well-written, organized, and provides numerical experiments demonstrating usefulness.
- Numerical results are illustrative but include limited comparison to state-of-the-art methods.
- Equation (7) and its relation/distinction to Equation (2) are unclear and need clarification.
- Definition of the Koopman operator KH and the map φ appears mathematically inconsistent and must be explained.
- Proof of Theorem 1 is unclear—specifically the phrase “applying the operator KH to both sides,” since both sides seem scalar.
- Assumptions in Theorem 1 are strong, not made explicit, and additional assumptions appear in the proof whose validity is unclear.
- Minor typos and inconsistent notation (e.g., "the the", kappa vs lambda) should be fixed.
- Recommendation: accept conditional on addressing the technical clarifications and expanding comparisons to related methods.
