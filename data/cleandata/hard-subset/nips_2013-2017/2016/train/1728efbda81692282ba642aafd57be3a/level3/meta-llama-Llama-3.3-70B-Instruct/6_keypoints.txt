- Proposes training uRNNs by optimizing the recurrence matrix over the Stiefel manifold of unitary matrices.  
- Provides a theoretical criterion for when a unitary parameterization has restricted capacity.  
- Shows a previously proposed unitary parameterization is capacity-restricted for hidden dimensions > 7.  
- Presents a method to directly optimize a full-capacity unitary matrix.  
- Full-capacity optimization yields significantly improved performance over restricted-capacity uRNNs.  
- Paper is well-written with clear, concise explanations and sound theoretical derivations.  
- Experimental results are strong and include thorough comparisons with LSTMs and restricted uRNNs.  
- Approach effectively addresses vanishing and exploding gradient problems via unitary recurrence matrices.  
- Optimization method is simple, effective, and empirically stable.  
- Limitations: restricted-capacity parameterizations may still be useful for some tasks; trade-offs between hidden dimension and parameterization capacity need further study.  
- Limitation: paper lacks detailed analysis of computational complexity and scalability to larger models/datasets.  
- Overall assessment: original, significant contribution with recommendation to accept.
