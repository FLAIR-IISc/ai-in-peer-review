- Proposes optimizing the recurrence matrix over the full set of unitary matrices for uRNNs, improving performance over restricted-capacity parameterizations.
- Provides a theoretical test to determine whether a unitary parameterization has restricted capacity.
- Shows a recent unitary parameterization is capacity-limited for hidden state dimension > 7.
- Proposes a stochastic gradient descent method that constrains gradients to the differentiable manifold of unitary matrices.

- Thorough analysis of limitations of restricted-capacity parameterizations.
- Demonstrates effectiveness on multiple tasks: synthetic system identification, long-term memorization, speech-frame prediction, and pixel-by-pixel handwritten digit classification.
