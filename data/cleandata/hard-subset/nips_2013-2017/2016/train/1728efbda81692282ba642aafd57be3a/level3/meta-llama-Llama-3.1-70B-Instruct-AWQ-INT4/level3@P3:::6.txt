This paper proposes a spectral analysis of the Koopman operator in reproducing kernel Hilbert spaces (RKHSs) for a nonlinear dynamical system. The authors extend the dynamic mode decomposition (DMD) with reproducing kernels, which allows for a more accurate approximation of the Koopman eigenfunctions using nonlinear basis functions. The proposed algorithm is mathematically sound and provides a modal decomposition of the dynamics into a finite number of modes.

The paper is well-written and clearly explains the theoretical background and the proposed algorithm. The authors provide a good introduction to the Koopman operator and DMD, making the paper accessible to readers unfamiliar with these concepts. The use of reproducing kernels is a novel extension of DMD, and the authors provide a clear explanation of how this approach can be used to approximate the Koopman eigenfunctions.

The empirical examples provided in the paper demonstrate the effectiveness of the proposed algorithm. The results are promising, and the authors also provide an example of how the proposed algorithm can be used for prediction, sequential change detection, and recognition of dynamic patterns.
