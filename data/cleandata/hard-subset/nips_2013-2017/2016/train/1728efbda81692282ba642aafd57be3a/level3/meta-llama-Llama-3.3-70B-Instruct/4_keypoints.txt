- Proposes optimizing the recurrence matrix over all unitary matrices (full-capacity uRNN), improving performance over restricted-capacity parameterizations.
- Provides a theoretical test for restricted-capacity parameterizations and shows a recent unitary parameterization is restricted for hidden dimension > 7.
- Introduces an SGD method that constrains gradients to the differentiable manifold of unitary matrices.
- Paper is well-written with clear background and motivation.
- Thorough analysis of limitations of restricted-capacity unitary parameterizations.
- Experiments on synthetic and natural data demonstrate full-capacity uRNNs outperform restricted uRNNs and LSTMs.
- Tasks evaluated include system identification, long-term memorization, and speech prediction.
- Provides a detailed, easy-to-follow explanation of the optimization method for full-capacity unitary matrices.
- Weakness: No comparison to non-RKHS Koopman spectral analysis in simulations.
- Weakness: Did not explore choice of kernels or its potential impact on results.
- Evaluation scores: Quality 9/10, Clarity 9/10, Originality 8/10, Significance 9/10.
- Overall recommendation: Accept — significant contribution with impressive experimental results.
