This work formulates Koopman spectral analysis within a reproducing kernel Hilbert space (RKHS) framework, validating the proposed method on both simulated and real-world datasets. The manuscript is exceptionally well-written and clear.

The contribution could be further strengthened by addressing two points. First, the experimental evaluation would benefit from a direct performance comparison against a standard non-RKHS Koopman spectral analysis baseline. Second, a discussion or empirical study on the impact of the kernel choice on the method's performance would be a valuable addition, and this could also be explored within the simulation studies.
