- Paper derives spectral analysis of the Koopman operator in RKHS and proposes a modal decomposition algorithm, extending DMD via the kernel trick.  
- Presentation is clear, well-organized, and the theoretical exposition is lucid; the approach is interesting and technically sound.  
- Lacks a detailed comparison with state-of-the-art methods, though numerical experiments sufficiently demonstrate utility.  
- Major concern with Section 3.1: Equation (7) and the proof of Theorem 1 are difficult to follow.  
- Equation (7) closely resembles Equation (2); the purpose and added value of (7) should be clarified.  
- Equation (7) appears inconsistent: applying K_H (an operator H→H) to φ (a map M→H) seems problematic and needs explanation.  
- The phrase “by applying the operator K_H to both sides” is unclear because both sides are scalars and K_H cannot act on scalars.  
- Assumptions in Theorem 1 are strong and not explicitly defined; authors should clarify their meaning and implications.  
- Additional assumptions arise within the proof and must be justified and discussed regarding how readily they hold in practice.  
- Minor typos: Line 55 “the the” → “the”; Line 153 “\kappaj / \lambdaj” → “\kappai / \lambda_i”.
