This research paper extends the Koopman spectral analysis to reproducing kernels. The method has been evaluated on simulated and real data. It is a very well-written paper. I would like to see two additional aspects in this paper: first, a comparison with non-RKHS Koopman spectral analysis in simulation. Second (and this can also be found in simulation), there would be a discussion/exploration of the choice of kernels, and how this might affect the results.
