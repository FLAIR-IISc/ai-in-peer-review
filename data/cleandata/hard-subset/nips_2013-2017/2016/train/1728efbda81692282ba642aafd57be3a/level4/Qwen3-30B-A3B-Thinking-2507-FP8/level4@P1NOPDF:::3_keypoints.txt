- Paper develops a spectral analysis framework for the Koopman composition operator in RKHSs, with a modal decomposition algorithm, robustification technique, and numerical validation.  
- Methodology extends dynamical mode decomposition via the kernel trick.  
- Paper is exceptionally well-written, logically structured, and presents theoretical concepts clearly.  
- Technical contributions appear sound.  
- Comparison with state-of-the-art methods is limited.  
- Numerical experiments effectively demonstrate the method's utility.  
- Major issue in Section 3.1: Equation (7) and the proof of Theorem 1 are problematic.  
- Equation (7) seems redundant with Equation (2), making its purpose unclear.  
- Equation (7) appears inconsistent because KH maps H→H while φ (a function M→H) is not an element of H.  
- Proof error: the phrase "by applying the operator KH to both sides" is invalid since both sides are scalars and KH acts on elements of H.  
- Theorem 1’s assumptions are strong, insufficiently detailed, and the proof introduces implicit assumptions that must be stated, justified, and shown plausible in typical settings.  
- Minor typos: Line 55 "the the" → "the"; Line 153 should read "κ_j / λ_j" (not "κ_i / λ_i").  
- Technical core is promising, but unresolved ambiguities in Section 3.1 and the theorem’s assumptions significantly affect theoretical validity and must be addressed before publication.
