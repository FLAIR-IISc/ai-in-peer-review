- Extends Koopman spectral analysis to the RKHS setting and evaluates on simulated and real datasets.  
- Manuscript is very well written and easy to follow.  
- Lacks a direct comparison with standard (non-RKHS) Koopman spectral analysis in simulations to quantify benefits and trade-offs.  
- Needs discussion and (ideally empirical) exploration of how kernel choice and hyperparameters affect results.  
- Overall a strong paper; addressing the above would make the contribution more compelling.
