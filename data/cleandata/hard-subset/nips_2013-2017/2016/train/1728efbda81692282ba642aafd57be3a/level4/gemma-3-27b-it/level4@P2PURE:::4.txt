This paper extends Koopman spectral analysis to reproducing kernel Hilbert spaces. The method is evaluated using both simulated and real-world data. The paper is very well written. I believe two additional investigations would strengthen the work. First, a comparison to Koopman spectral analysis *without* the use of reproducing kernels, performed within the simulations, would be valuable. Second, a discussion and exploration of kernel selection – and its potential impact on the results – would be beneficial, and could potentially be investigated through simulations.
