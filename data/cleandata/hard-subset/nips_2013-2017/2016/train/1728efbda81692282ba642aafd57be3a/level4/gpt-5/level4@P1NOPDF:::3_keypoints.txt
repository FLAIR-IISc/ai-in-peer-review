- Paper develops a spectral analysis of the Koopman operator in RKHSs with a modal decomposition algorithm, robustification, and illustrative numerical experiments.
- Framework is essentially a kernelized extension of dynamical mode decomposition.
- Strength: well written, well organized, and technically solid exposition.
- Strength: numerical experiments adequately demonstrate usefulness despite no head-to-head state-of-the-art comparison.
- Major concern: Equation (7) in Section 3.1 is unclear—appears essentially identical to Equation (2); its role in the derivation is not explained.
- Major concern: Type/space inconsistency in Equation (7)—KH is an operator H→H while φ is a map M→H; domains/codomains and notational conventions need to be made explicit.
- Major concern: Proof steps (lines 144–157) appear type-inconsistent—specifically the claim of “applying KH to both sides” where both sides are scalars; rigorous justification is required.
- Major concern: Theorem 1 assumptions are strong, not clearly stated in the main text, and additional unstated assumptions appear in the proof; consolidate and justify all assumptions and their practicality.
- Minor comments/typos: Line 55 “the the” → “the”; Line 153 index should be κ_j/λ_j rather than κ_i/λ_i.
- Overall summary: interesting and solid contribution, but Section 3.1 and the proof of Theorem 1 need clarification and correction regarding functional-analytic setup, operator domains, and proof validity.
