- Extends Koopman spectral analysis to reproducing kernel Hilbert spaces and demonstrates on simulated and real data.  
- Paper is exceptionally well-written and pleasurable to read.  
- Would benefit from a comparison with non-RKHS Koopman spectral analysis in simulations to show advantages.  
- Needs discussion or exploration of kernel choice and its potential impact on results.  
- Suggests examining kernel effects empirically, e.g., via additional simulations.
