- Paper proposes spectral analysis of the Koopman operator in RKHS with a modal decomposition algorithm, robustification method, and numerical experiments—an RKHS-based extension of DMD via the kernel trick.  
- Writing and organization are strong and the technical development is solid; numerical experiments effectively demonstrate usefulness despite limited comparisons to state-of-the-art.  
- Major concern: Section 3.1 (Eq. (7) and proof of Theorem 1) is unclear and possibly inconsistent—KH is an operator H→H while φ is a function M→H (not in H), and applying KH to scalar quantities is ill-defined.  
- Theorem 1’s assumptions are strong and not fully explicit; additional assumptions appear in the proof—authors should clarify why these are needed and how they can be satisfied.  
- Minor corrections: fix typo "the the" (Line 55) and notation "\kappaj / \lambdaj" (Line 153) → "\kappai / \lambda_i."
