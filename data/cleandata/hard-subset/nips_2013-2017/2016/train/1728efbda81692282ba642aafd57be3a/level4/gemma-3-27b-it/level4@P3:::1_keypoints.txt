- Introduces a Koopman operator spectral analysis method in RKHS with an eigendecomposition-based algorithm for nonlinear dynamical systems.
- Demonstrates empirical validation on synthetic and real-world datasets showing efficiency gains.
- Contribution is well-motivated with clear mathematical formulation and rigorous derivation.
- Primary concern: unclear novelty relative to existing work, especially Williams et al. [35] and extended DMD.
- Needs a thorough comparison to prior approaches (computational complexity, approximation accuracy, applicability).
- Section 4 (robustification via POD) lacks clarity on why POD projection is used and its theoretical implications.
- Literature review is incomplete; missing relevant works (Ralaivola & d’Alche-Buc, Tuia et al., Rojo-Alvarez et al., Mouattamid & Schaback, Franz & Schölkopf).
- Applicability to stochastic systems is insufficiently explored; recommend detailed analysis or toy experiments with noisy data.
- The assumed pointwise pairwise relationship between consecutive observables needs discussion for multidimensional series with weak pointwise correlations.
- Must explicitly address the causal assumption inherent in the framework.
- Experimental evaluation is limited and not ambitious enough to substantiate claims of advancement.
- Prediction via preimage is standard and not a strong experimental contribution by itself.
- “Embedding and Recognition of Dynamics” should connect to time-series clustering literature (kernel k-means, subspace kernels) and include comparative experiments.
- Sequential change-point detection experiment should be compared to state-of-the-art online kernel-based change detection methods.
- Acceptance arguments: novel kernel-based Koopman extension, empirical validation, clear math, and broad applicability.
- Rejection arguments: insufficient differentiation from prior work, limited experiments, incomplete literature review, unclear stochastic performance, and undeveloped pointwise-assumption discussion.
- Overall recommendation: weak reject pending major revisions addressing novelty, experiments, and literature gaps.
- Minor issues: fix indexing inconsistencies (τ-1 vs. τ) and replace dot with comma in kernel notation k(xi,xj).
