This paper advances Koopman spectral analysis by formulating it in a reproducing-kernel Hilbert space and introducing a corresponding modal decomposition algorithm. The approach is evaluated on both synthetic and real datasets. The manuscript is very clearly written.

I would like to see two additional aspects: first, a comparison against non-RKHS Koopman spectral analysis in the simulation studies; second (which could also be examined via simulations), a discussion and exploration of kernel choice—including kernel families and hyperparameters—and how this affects the results.
