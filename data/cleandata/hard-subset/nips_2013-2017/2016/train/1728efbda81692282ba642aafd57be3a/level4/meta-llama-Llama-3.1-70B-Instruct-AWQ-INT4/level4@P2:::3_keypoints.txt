- Paper presents a spectral analysis of the Koopman operator in RKHS and a kernelized modal decomposition algorithm extending DMD.
- Well-written and organized with clear theoretical exposition and derivation.
- Technical solidity is a main strength; derivations are thorough and easy to follow.
- Numerical experiments are well-presented and demonstrate the method’s effectiveness.
- Concern: proof of Theorem 1 (Section 3.1) needs clarification—Equation (7) appears inconsistent regarding KH and ϕ.
- Concern: assumptions in Theorem 1 are strong, not explicit, and additional assumptions are used in the proof without justification.
- Minor typos noted on Lines 55 and 153.
- Paper meets conference criteria for quality, clarity, and originality.
- Significance could be improved with more comparisons to state-of-the-art methods and discussion of potential impact.
- Request for more details and empirical evaluation of the proposed robustifying method.
- Arguments for acceptance: novel kernelized DMD extension, clear presentation, sound theory, supportive experiments.
- Arguments against acceptance: unresolved issues in Theorem 1, unclear/strong assumptions, and insufficient comparative/impact analysis; rating 7/10, recommend accept with minor revisions.
