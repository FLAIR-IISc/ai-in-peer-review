This paper presents a valuable extension of Koopman spectral analysis, applying it to the framework of reproducing kernels. The methodology is thoroughly evaluated using both simulated and real-world datasets. Overall, the manuscript is exceptionally well-written and clearly presented. To further strengthen the work, I suggest two potential additions. First, including a comparative analysis against standard, non-RKHS Koopman spectral analysis within the simulation studies would provide valuable context. Second, a discussion – potentially explored through simulations – regarding the impact of kernel selection on the observed results would be beneficial, offering insights into the practical considerations for users of this method.
