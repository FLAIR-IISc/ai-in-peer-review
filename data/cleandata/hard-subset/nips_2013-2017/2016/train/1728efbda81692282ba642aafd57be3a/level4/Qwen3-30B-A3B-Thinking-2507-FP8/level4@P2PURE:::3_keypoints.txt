- Paper develops spectral analysis of the Koopman operator in RKHS and introduces a modal decomposition algorithm with a robustifying technique and numerical validation.  
- Framework extends dynamical mode decomposition using the kernel trick.  
- Paper is exceptionally well-written, organized, and presents clear, technically sound theoretical concepts.  
- Numerical experiments illustrate method usefulness, but lack deep comparisons with state-of-the-art approaches.  
- Major concern: Equation (7) in Section 3.1 is unclear and seems redundant with Equation (2); its purpose is not explained.  
- Equation (7) appears inconsistent: K_H is an operator H→H while φ is a function M→H (not an element of H), so the equation’s types do not match.  
- The proof of Theorem 1 (Lines 144–157) is called into question because of the problematic use of Equation (7).  
- Statement "by applying the operator K_H to both sides" (Line 149) is incorrect because both sides are scalars and K_H: H→H cannot act on scalars.  
- The assumptions of Theorem 1 are strong, not made explicit enough, and additional assumptions appear in the proof without justification—authors should clarify and justify them.  
- Typos to fix: Line 55 "the the" → "the"; Line 153 " \(\kappaj / \lambdaj\)" → "\(\kappa_j / \lambda_j\)".
