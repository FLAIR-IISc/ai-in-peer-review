- Paper presents spectral analysis of the Koopman operator in RKHS and a kernelized modal decomposition algorithm with a robustifying method and numerical demos.
- Clear, well-organized exposition and sound technical development.
- Extends dynamical mode decomposition via the kernel trick; experiments adequately demonstrate usefulness despite no deep SOTA comparison.
- Major concern: Section 3.1 (Equation (7) and Theorem 1 proof) is hard to follow and needs clarification.
- Equation (7) appears redundant with Equation (2) — its purpose is unclear.
- Equation (7) seems inconsistent: KH is an operator H->H while φ is a function M->H (not an element of H).
- Line 149 claim "by applying KH to both sides" is unclear since both sides are scalar values and KH maps H->H.
- Theorem 1 assumptions seem strong, incompletely stated, and additional (unjustified) assumptions appear in the proof; need explanation why they are necessary and whether they can be satisfied.
- Minor typos: remove duplicate "the the" (Line 55) and replace "\kappaj / \lambdaj" with "\kappai / \lambda_i" (Line 153).
