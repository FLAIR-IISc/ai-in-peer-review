This paper extends Koopman spectral analysis to the setting of reproducing kernel Hilbert spaces (RKHS) and evaluates the approach on both simulated and real datasets. The manuscript is very well written and easy to follow.

To further strengthen the contribution, I would like to see: (1) a direct comparison with standard, non-RKHS Koopman spectral analysis in the simulation studies to clarify the benefits and trade-offs of the kernelized approach; and (2) a discussion and empirical exploration of kernel choice, including how different kernels and their hyperparameters affect performance and interpretation—potentially examined through additional simulations.
