- Paper proposes spectral analysis of the Koopman operator in RKHS, a kernelized modal decomposition algorithm, a robustification method, and supporting numerical experiments.  
- The framework extends dynamical mode decomposition via the kernel trick.  
- Strength: clear, well-organized presentation with a solid technical foundation.  
- Strength: numerical experiments demonstrate the method’s usefulness, though comparisons to state-of-the-art are limited.  
- Major concern: Section 3.1 (Equation (7) and proof of Theorem 1) is unclear; Eq.(7) resembles Eq.(2) and its purpose is not explained.  
- Major concern: Eq.(7) appears inconsistent because KH is an operator H→H while φ maps M→H, so applying KH as done (Line 149) is not justified.  
- Major concern: Theorem 1 uses strong/additional assumptions that are not explicitly stated or justified; authors should clarify and discuss feasibility.  
- Minor: Typos to fix — Line 55 "the the" → "the"; Line 153 "\kappaj / \lambdaj" → "\kappai / \lambda_i".
