- Well-written extension of Koopman spectral analysis to RKHS.
- Method validated on both simulated and real-world data; technical contributions clearly articulated.
- Experimental evaluation should include direct comparisons to standard (non-RKHS) Koopman spectral methods to establish a baseline.
- Paper lacks discussion and empirical exploration of kernel selection and its effect on performance.
- Recommend a sensitivity study over different kernels to assess robustness and give practical guidance.
