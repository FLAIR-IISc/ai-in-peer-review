- Paper performs spectral analysis of the Koopman operator in RKHS, introduces a modal decomposition algorithm, and uses the kernel trick to extend dynamical mode decomposition.  
- Authors propose a robustness-enhancement method and provide numerical experiments demonstrating practical utility.  
- Paper is exceptionally well-written, systematically organized, and clearly explains underlying theoretical concepts.  
- work is technically sound, and the provided experiments sufficiently illustrate utility despite lacking deep SOTA comparisons.  
- Major concern: Equation (7) in Section 3.1 appears very similar to Equation (2), making its purpose unclear.  
- Major concern: Equation (7) seems inconsistent because KH is a linear operator H→H while φ maps M→H and thus may not lie in H.  
- Major concern: The proof of Theorem 1 (Lines 144–157) is hard to follow; the phrase “by applying the operator KH to both sides” is unclear since both sides appear scalar.  
- Major concern: Assumptions of Theorem 1 seem strong and are not explicitly stated; additional assumptions arise in the proof without justification or discussion of satisfiability.  
- Typos: Line 55 has “the the”; Line 153 should replace κ_i/λ_i with κ_j/λ_j.
