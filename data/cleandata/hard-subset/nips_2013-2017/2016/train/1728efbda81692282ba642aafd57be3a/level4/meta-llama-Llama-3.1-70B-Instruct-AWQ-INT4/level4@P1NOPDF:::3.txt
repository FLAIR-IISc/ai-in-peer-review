This paper presents a spectral analysis of the Koopman operator in Reproducing Kernel Hilbert Spaces (RKHSs) and introduces a modal decomposition algorithm to perform this analysis. The authors also discuss a robustifying method and demonstrate the effectiveness of their approach through several numerical experiments. The overall framework extends the "dynamical mode decomposition" concept using the kernel trick.

A major strength of the paper is its clarity and organization. The authors provide a clear explanation of the theoretical concepts used to derive their method, which appears to be a valuable contribution. The paper is also technically sound. Although there is no in-depth comparison with state-of-the-art approaches, the numerical experiments adequately illustrate the method's usefulness, which is sufficient in my view. However, there are some concerns that need to be addressed.

The major flaw in this paper, in my opinion, lies in Section 3.1, particularly Equation (7) and the proof of Theorem 1, which I found difficult to follow. Firstly, Equation (7) bears a strong resemblance to Equation (2), so it is unclear what purpose it serves. Secondly, Equation (7) seems inconsistent, as KH is a linear operator from H to itself, whereas φ is a function from M to H, not in H. I would appreciate it if the authors could provide further clarification on this point, as it raises doubts about the proof in Lines 144-157. Additionally, the phrase "by applying the operator KH to both sides" (Line 149) is unclear, as both sides are scalar values, and the operator KH, which maps H to H, does not apply in this context. The assumptions in Theorem 1 appear to be strong and not explicitly stated. Could the authors provide a more detailed explanation of these assumptions? Furthermore, additional assumptions are introduced in the proof of the theorem. It is unclear why these assumptions are necessary and whether they can be easily satisfied.

There are also a couple of typos that need to be corrected: "the the" on Line 55 should be removed, and "\kappaj / \lambdaj" on Line 153 should be replaced with "\kappai / \lambda_i".
