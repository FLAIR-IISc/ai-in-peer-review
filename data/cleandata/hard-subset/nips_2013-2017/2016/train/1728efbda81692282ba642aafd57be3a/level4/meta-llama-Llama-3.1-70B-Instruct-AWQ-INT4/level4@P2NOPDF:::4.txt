This paper presents a novel extension of Koopman spectral analysis to reproducing kernels. The methodology is thoroughly evaluated using both simulated and real data. The writing is of exceptionally high quality, making the paper a pleasure to read. To further enhance the paper, I would suggest incorporating two additional aspects. Firstly, a comparison with non-RKHS Koopman spectral analysis in the simulations would provide valuable insights into the benefits of the proposed approach. Secondly, a discussion or exploration of the choice of kernels and their potential impact on the results would be a worthwhile addition, which could also be investigated through simulations.
