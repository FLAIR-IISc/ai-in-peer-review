- Paper presents spectral analysis of the Koopman operator in RKHS and a kernelized modal decomposition algorithm with robustification and numerical validation.  
- Writing and exposition are clear; theoretical foundations appear technically sound.  
- Numerical experiments demonstrate utility but the paper lacks comprehensive comparison to state-of-the-art methods (though omission is tolerable).  
- Major concern: Section 3.1 / Theorem 1 — Equation (7) seems redundant with Equation (2) and its role is unclear.  
- Major concern: Equation (7) is inconsistent/ill-defined since K_H : H → H while φ maps the state space to H (φ is not an element of H); authors must clarify how (7) is well-defined.  
- Major concern: Proof of Theorem 1 (Lines 144–157) incorrectly describes "applying K_H to both sides" when both sides are scalars, so applying K_H : H → H is ill-defined.  
- Major concern: Theorem 1 makes strong assumptions that are not justified, and the proof introduces additional unstated assumptions; authors should state and justify all assumptions.  
- Typos to fix: Line 55 "the the" → "the"; Line 153 "$\kappaj / \lambdaj$" → "$\kappa_j / \lambda_j$".
