- Paper presents spectral analysis of the Koopman operator in RKHS and a modal decomposition algorithm using the kernel trick.  
- Authors propose a robustification method and validate the approach with numerical experiments.  
- Work extends dynamical mode decomposition (DMD) via kernel methods.  
- Strength: clear, well-organized presentation that conveys complex theoretical concepts.  
- Strength: overall technical solidity of the paper.  
- Weakness: lacks deeper comparison with existing state-of-the-art methods.  
- Numerical experiments are adequate to demonstrate the method’s usefulness.  
- Major concern: Section 3.1 (Equation (7)) and the proof of Theorem 1 are unclear.  
- Question: purpose of Equation (7) given its similarity to Equation (2).  
- Consistency issue: Equation (7) treats KH as acting on φ, but φ is a function M→H, not necessarily an element of H.  
- Clarification needed on applying KH to scalars (Line 149), since KH: H→H does not act on scalars.  
- Assumptions in Theorem 1 are stringent, not explicit, and additional assumptions introduced during the proof lack justification; need assessment of their satisfiability.  
- Minor errors: typo "the the" (Line 55) and incorrect subscript in Line 153 (κ_j/λ_j should be κ_i/λ_i).
