- Paper proposes a spectral analysis framework for the Koopman operator in RKHSs, a modal decomposition algorithm, a robustification technique, and extends DMD via the kernel trick.  
- Paper is exceptionally well-written, logically structured, and the theoretical foundations are clearly presented and promising.  
- Technical contributions are sound and rigorous, and numerical experiments demonstrate practical utility.  
- Comparison with state-of-the-art methods is limited and should be expanded.  
- Major concern: Eq. (7) seems redundant with Eq. (2) and its purpose is unclear.  
- Major concern: Eq. (7) is mathematically inconsistent—K_H maps H→H while φ maps M→H (not an element of H), so the expression is questionable.  
- Major concern: The phrase “by applying the operator K_H to both sides” is ambiguous since both sides are scalars but K_H acts on H, not scalars.  
- Major concern: Theorem 1 uses strong, insufficiently explicit assumptions; the proof (Lines 144–157) appears to introduce additional unstated assumptions that must be clarified and justified.  
- Typos to fix: Line 55 “the the” → “the”; Line 153 “$\kappaj / \lambdaj$” → “$\kappa_j / \lambda_j$”.
