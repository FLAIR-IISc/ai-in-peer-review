This paper presents a notable extension of Koopman spectral analysis to reproducing kernels, demonstrating its efficacy through both simulated and real data experiments. The writing is of exceptionally high quality, making the content easily accessible. To further enhance the paper, two potential aspects could be considered for inclusion. Firstly, a comparative analysis with non-RKHS Koopman spectral analysis in the simulation framework would provide valuable insights. Secondly, an exploration of the impact of kernel selection on the results, potentially through simulation-based investigations, would offer a more comprehensive understanding of the method's applicability and limitations.
