- Contribution: develops spectral analysis of the Koopman operator in RKHS, introduces a modal decomposition algorithm, proposes a robustification method, and presents numerical experiments.  
- Strength: paper is clearly and organizedly presented with a technically sound derivation and useful illustrative experiments.  
- Weakness: lacks a comprehensive comparison with state-of-the-art methods.  
- Major concern: Section 3.1 (especially Equation (7) and Theorem 1 proof) is unclear and undermines confidence in the theoretical claims.  
- Specific issue: Equation (7) appears redundant with Equation (2), so its purpose is unclear.  
- Inconsistency: Equation (7) treats KH as an operator on H but uses φ which is not in H, raising validity concerns.  
- Proof ambiguity: the phrase “by applying the operator KH to both sides” is unclear because the quantities involved are scalars, not H-valued, so the operation is ill-specified.  
- Assumptions: Theorem 1 relies on strong assumptions that need clarification, and additional assumptions appear in the proof without justification or discussion of their practicality.  
- Minor errors: typo “the the” (Line 55) and incorrect notation \kappaj / \lambdaj vs \kappai / \lambda_i (Line 153).
