- Paper derives spectral analysis of the Koopman operator in RKHS and proposes a modal decomposition algorithm with a robustness method and numerical demonstrations.  
- Framework is a kernelized extension of dynamical mode decomposition.  
- Presentation is clear, well-organized, and the approach is technically sound and interesting.  
- Lacks comprehensive comparison to state-of-the-art methods, though experiments illustrate the method's value.  
- Major concern: Section 3.1 (Eq. (7)) and the proof of Theorem 1 are difficult to follow and need clarification.  
- Eq. (7) appears redundant to Eq. (2); authors should clarify its purpose.  
- Formal inconsistency: K_H is an operator H→H while phi is a function M→H (not an element of H), so applying K_H as done is problematic.  
- Claim "by applying operator K_H to both sides" (Line 149) is incorrect because both sides are scalars and K_H cannot act on scalars; this undermines validity of Lines 144–157.  
- Theorem 1 assumptions are strong, not explicitly defined, and additional unstated assumptions appear in the proof; authors should state and discuss these and their practical plausibility.  
- Minor typos: Line 55 “the the” → “the”; Line 153 “\kappaj / \lambdaj” → “\kappai / \lambda_i”.
