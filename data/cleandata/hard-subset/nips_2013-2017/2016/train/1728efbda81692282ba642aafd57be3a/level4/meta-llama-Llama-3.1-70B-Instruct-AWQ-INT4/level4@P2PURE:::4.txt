This paper extends Koopman spectral analysis to reproducing kernels and evaluates the proposed method on both simulated and real data. The paper is very well-written. To further enhance the paper, I would have liked to see two additional aspects explored. Firstly, a comparison with non-RKHS Koopman spectral analysis in the simulations would provide a more comprehensive understanding of the benefits and limitations of the proposed approach. Secondly, a discussion or exploration of the choice of kernels and their potential impact on the results would be valuable, and this could also be investigated through simulations.
