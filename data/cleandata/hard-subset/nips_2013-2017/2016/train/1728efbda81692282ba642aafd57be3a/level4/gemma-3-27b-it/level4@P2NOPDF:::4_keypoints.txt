- Extends Koopman spectral analysis to reproducing kernel Hilbert spaces (RKHS).  
- Methodology evaluated on simulated and real-world datasets; paper is clearly written and well-presented.  
- Core strength: elegant generalization that broadens applicability of Koopman analysis via kernel methods.  
- Experimental validation reasonably demonstrates the approach’s effectiveness.  
- Recommendation 1: include direct comparisons to standard (non-RKHS) Koopman spectral analysis to quantify advantages in accuracy, computational cost, and handling of system characteristics.  
- Recommendation 2: provide a detailed study of kernel selection and sensitivity, varying kernel functions and parameters and discussing theoretical guidance for kernel choice.  
- Overall: addressing these suggestions would strengthen the contribution, though the paper is already a strong and worthwhile addition.
