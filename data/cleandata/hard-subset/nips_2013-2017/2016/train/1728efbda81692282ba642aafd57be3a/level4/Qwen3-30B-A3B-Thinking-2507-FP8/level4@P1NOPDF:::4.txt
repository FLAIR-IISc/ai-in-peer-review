

This paper extends Koopman spectral analysis to reproducing kernel Hilbert spaces (RKHS). The method is evaluated on both simulated and real data, and the manuscript is well-written. To further strengthen the contribution, the authors should: (1) include a comparison with non-RKHS Koopman methods in the simulations; and (2) provide a discussion of how kernel choice impacts results, which could be explored through additional simulation experiments.
