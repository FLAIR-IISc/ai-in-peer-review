- Proposes a novel extension of Koopman spectral analysis within an RKHS framework.  
- Method validated on simulated and real-world datasets.  
- Paper is exceptionally well-written with clear, logical presentation and strong motivation.  
- Theoretical contributions and method exposition are well-structured and accessible.  
- Experimental evaluation should include direct comparisons to traditional (non-RKHS) Koopman methods to quantify empirical advantages.  
- Investigate kernel choice: provide ablation over kernels (RBF, polynomial, linear) and analyze sensitivity to kernel hyperparameters.  
- Overall a strong, well-motivated contribution whose impact would increase by strengthening empirical baselines and kernel analysis.
