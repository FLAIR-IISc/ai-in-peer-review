

This paper extends Koopman spectral analysis to reproducing kernel Hilbert spaces (RKHS). The method is evaluated on both simulated and real-world data. The paper is exceptionally well written and clearly presents its contributions. However, the authors could strengthen the evaluation by addressing two key elements: first, including a direct comparison between the RKHS-based Koopman approach and non-RKHS Koopman spectral analysis in the simulation experiments; second, providing a discussion or exploration of kernel choice and its impact on the resulting spectral decomposition, which could be addressed through additional simulations or theoretical analysis.
