This paper extends Koopman spectral analysis to the reproducing kernel Hilbert space (RKHS) framework. The proposed method is evaluated on both simulated and real-world data.

The paper is very well written and presents its contributions clearly. However, the work could be significantly strengthened by addressing two additional aspects.

First, the experimental section would benefit from a direct comparison with traditional, non-RKHS Koopman spectral analysis in the simulations. Including such a baseline would help to clearly quantify the advantages of the proposed kernel-based approach.

Second, a discussion on the choice of kernel and its impact on the results would be a valuable addition. An exploration of the method's sensitivity to different kernel functions, which could also be investigated in the simulations, would provide important practical insights for applying this technique.
