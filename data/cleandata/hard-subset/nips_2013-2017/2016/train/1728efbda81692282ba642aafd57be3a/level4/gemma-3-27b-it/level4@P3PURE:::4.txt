This paper extends Koopman spectral analysis to reproducing kernel Hilbert spaces (RKHS). The method is evaluated on both simulated and real data. This is a very well-written paper. I would appreciate seeing two additional aspects explored in future work. First, a comparison to non-RKHS Koopman spectral analysis within the simulations would be valuable. Second, a discussion and exploration of kernel choice, and its potential impact on the results, would strengthen the work – this could be readily investigated within the simulation framework.
