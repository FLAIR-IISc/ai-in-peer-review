

This paper extends Koopman spectral analysis to reproducing kernel Hilbert spaces (RKHS). The method is evaluated on both simulated and real-world datasets. The manuscript is well-written and clearly presented. I recommend including two additional elements: first, a comparison with non-RKHS Koopman spectral analysis methods in the simulation studies; second, a systematic investigation of kernel selection effects and their impact on results, which could be incorporated into the simulation analysis.
