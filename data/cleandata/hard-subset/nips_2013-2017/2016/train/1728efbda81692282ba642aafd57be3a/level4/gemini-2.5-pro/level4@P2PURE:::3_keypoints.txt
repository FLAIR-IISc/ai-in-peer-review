- Paper presents spectral analysis of the Koopman operator in RKHS, a modal decomposition algorithm, a kernelized DMD extension, a robustification method, and numerical experiments.
- Strength: Well-written and well-organized manuscript.
- Strength: Theoretical concepts are clearly presented and appear technically solid.
- Strength: Numerical experiments effectively illustrate the method’s usefulness despite limited comparison to state-of-the-art.
- Major flaw: Equation (7) appears very similar to Equation (2); authors should clarify its distinct purpose.
- Major flaw: Equation (7) seems inconsistent because K_H maps H→H while φ is a function M→H and not clearly an element of H to which K_H can be applied.
- Major flaw: The phrase “by applying the operator K_H to both sides” is confusing since both sides of the prior equation are scalars while K_H is an operator on H.
- Major flaw: Assumptions for Theorem 1 are strong, not fully explicated, and additional assumptions appear in the proof but are not stated in the theorem; authors should provide more intuition and specify conditions for practical validity.
- Typo: Line 55 has duplicated word “the the.”
- Typo: Line 153 uses `\kappai / \lambda_i` but likely should be `\kappaj / \lambdaj`.
