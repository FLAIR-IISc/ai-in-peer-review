- Introduces a spectral analysis framework for the Koopman operator in RKHS, with a modal decomposition algorithm and robustification; extends DMD via the kernel trick and shows useful numerical experiments.  
- Paper is well-written, logically structured, and the exposition of theoretical foundations is clear.  
- Theoretical contributions are rigorous and the modal decomposition derivation is well-motivated and accessible.  
- Experimental validation supports practical relevance without overclaiming.  
- Major concern: Equation (7) in Section 3.1 is unclear and appears structurally identical to Equation (2).  
- Critical inconsistency: Equation (7) seems to apply the Koopman operator K_H (which maps H→H) to φ (a map M→H), so the operator is being applied outside its domain.  
- The step “by applying K_H to both sides” is invalid because both sides are scalars and K_H cannot act on scalars.  
- Theorem 1’s assumptions are vague (“sufficiently regular”) and incomplete; additional unstated assumptions appear in the proof.  
- Authors must clarify: (1) why Eq (7) is needed given Eq (2), (2) the precise domain/role of K_H in Eq (7), and (3) exact conditions under which Theorem 1 holds.  
- Without these fixes the theoretical foundation lacks rigor and undermines confidence in the core claims; significant revision is required.  
- Minor typos to fix: Line 55 “the the” → “the”; Line 153 indexing notation should be consistent (κ_j/λ_j).
