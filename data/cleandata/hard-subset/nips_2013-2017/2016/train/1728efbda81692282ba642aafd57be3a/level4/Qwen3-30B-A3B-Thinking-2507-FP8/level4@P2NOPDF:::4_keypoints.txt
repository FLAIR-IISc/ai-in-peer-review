- Extends Koopman spectral analysis to an RKHS framework, offering a novel kernel-based approach for nonlinear dynamical systems.  
- Method evaluated rigorously on synthetic and real-world datasets, showing clear empirical benefits.  
- Manuscript is exceptionally well-written with logical flow and precise technical exposition.  
- Contributions are significant for advancing Koopman-based methods by integrating kernel techniques for complex state spaces.  
- Recommend adding direct comparisons to standard (non-RKHS) Koopman analysis with quantitative metrics (reconstruction error, spectral accuracy) and qualitative insights.  
- Recommend a systematic study of kernel selection (types, hyperparameter tuning, feature mappings), computational trade-offs, and sensitivity via ablation studies.  
- Addressing these points would clarify practical necessity, scope of improvements, and offer guidance to practitioners.  
- Overall technically sound, well-motivated work; recommend acceptance pending the suggested minor clarifications.
