This research paper expands on the Kupman spectral analysis to reproduce the kernel. This method has been evaluated on simulated and real data. It is a very well-written paper. I would like to see two additional aspects in this paper: first, a comparison with non-RKHS Kupman spectral analysis in simulation. Second (and this can also be found in simulation), there will be a discussion/exploration of the kernel's choice, and how it can affect the results.
