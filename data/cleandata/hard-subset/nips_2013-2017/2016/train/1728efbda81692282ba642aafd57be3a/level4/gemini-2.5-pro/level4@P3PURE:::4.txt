This well-written paper extends Koopman spectral analysis to reproducing kernel Hilbert spaces, evaluating the proposed method on both simulated and real-world datasets.

While the work is promising, the paper could be strengthened by addressing two aspects. First, the experimental evaluation would be more compelling with a direct comparison to standard non-RKHS Koopman spectral analysis in the simulations. This would help to clearly quantify the advantages of the proposed kernel-based approach. Second, a discussion or exploration of the choice of kernel and its effect on the results would be a valuable addition. Investigating this, perhaps within the simulations, would provide important insights into the method's sensitivity and offer practical guidance.
