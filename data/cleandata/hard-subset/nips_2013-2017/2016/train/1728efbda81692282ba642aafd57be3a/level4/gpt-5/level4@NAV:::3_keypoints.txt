- Paper: develops spectral analysis of the Koopman operator in RKHS, proposes a modal decomposition algorithm with robustness enhancement, and demonstrates utility via numerical experiments; extends DMD using the kernel trick.

- Strength: manuscript is very well written and organized.

- Strength: theoretical ingredients are clearly presented and the work is technically solid.

- Strength: numerical experiments adequately illustrate the method’s usefulness despite lacking deep comparison to state-of-the-art.

- Major flaw: Section 3.1 (Equation (7) and proof of Theorem 1) is unclear and raises serious concerns.

- Major flaw: Equation (7) appears essentially identical to Equation (2), so its purpose is unclear.

- Major flaw: Equation (7) seems inconsistent since KH is an operator H → H while φ is a map M → H (not an element of H).

- Major flaw: the phrase “applying the operator KH to both sides” is unclear—if both sides are scalars KH: H → H cannot be applied.

- Major flaw: assumptions in Theorem 1 are strong and not made precise; additional assumptions appear in the proof without explanation or verification.

- Typos: Line 55 “the the”; Line 153 κ_j / λ_j should be κ_i / λ_i.
