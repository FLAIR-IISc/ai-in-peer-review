- Introduces Koopman-operator spectral analysis in an RKHS with kernel methods for nonlinear dynamical systems; eigendecomposition yields empirical efficiency gains and is experimentally validated.

- Unclear novelty relative to Williams, Kevrekidis, and Rowley (2015); request detailed clarification of key distinctions and improved discussion of Section 4.

- Missing related-work citations in kernel-based dynamical systems (suggested: Ralaivola & d'Alche Buc; Tuia et al.; Rojo-Alvarez et al.; Mouattamid & Schaback; Franz & Schölkopf).

- Requires fuller treatment of stochastic systems: comment on performance under stochastic dynamics and ideally a simple experiment.

- Clarify implications of using independent pairs of consecutive observables (not sequences) for multidimensional time series where pointwise relations are weak, and state the causal assumptions and benefits of the kernel construction.

- Experimental evaluation limited: pre-imaging prediction is standard; embedding/recognition needs comparisons to time-series clustering and subspace-kernel methods; change-point detection should be compared to state-of-the-art online kernel methods.

- Minor typos: check indices τ−1 vs τ and replace dot with comma in k(xi.xj).
