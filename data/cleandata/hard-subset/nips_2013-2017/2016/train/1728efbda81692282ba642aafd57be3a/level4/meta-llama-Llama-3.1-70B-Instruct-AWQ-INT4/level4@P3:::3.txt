This paper proposes a novel spectral analysis method for nonlinear dynamical systems using the Koopman operator in reproducing kernel Hilbert spaces (RKHSs). The authors develop a modal decomposition algorithm to perform this analysis, which is an extension of the dynamic mode decomposition (DMD) method using the kernel trick. The paper is well-written and organized, and the authors provide a clear presentation of the theoretical concepts used in the derivation of the method.

The paper's main contribution is the development of a robust and efficient algorithm for spectral analysis of nonlinear dynamical systems. The authors demonstrate the effectiveness of their approach through several numerical experiments, including the analysis of synthetic and real-world data. The results show that the proposed method can accurately capture the underlying dynamics of the system and provide valuable insights into its behavior.

One of the strengths of the paper is its technical soundness. The authors provide a rigorous mathematical framework for their method, and the proofs of the theorems are well-presented and easy to follow. The paper also includes a discussion of related works, which provides a good context for the proposed method.

However, there are some minor issues with the paper. In Section 3.1, the authors introduce Equation (7), which seems to be similar to Equation (2). It would be helpful to clarify the purpose of Equation (7) and how it differs from Equation (2). Additionally, the proof of Theorem 1 assumes that the eigenvalues λ̃j are distinct, but it is not clear why this assumption is necessary. Furthermore, there are a few typos in the paper. For example, on Line 55, the phrase "the the" appears, and on Line 153, the notation "\kappaj / \lambdaj" is used instead of "\kappai / \lambda_i".
