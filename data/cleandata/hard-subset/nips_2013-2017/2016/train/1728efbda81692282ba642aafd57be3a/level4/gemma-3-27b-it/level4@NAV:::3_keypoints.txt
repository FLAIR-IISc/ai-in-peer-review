- Paper studies spectral analysis of the Koopman operator in RKHS and proposes a modal decomposition algorithm using the kernel trick.  
- Authors address robustness and validate the method with several numerical experiments.  
- Strengths: clear presentation, strong technical foundation, and promising overall approach.  
- Lacks comprehensive comparison to existing state-of-the-art methods.  
- Numerical results demonstrate potential and are judged adequate by the reviewer.  
- Major concern: Section 3.1 (Equation (7)) is unclear and seemingly redundant with Equation (2).  
- Major concern: Equation (7) appears inconsistent because K_H maps H→H while ϕ is a function M→H, not an element of H.  
- Major concern: Theorem 1 proof (Lines 144–157) is dubious—applying K_H to both sides is unclear since the manipulated quantities are scalars, not H-elements.  
- Major concern: Assumptions in Theorem 1 are restrictive, underspecified, and additional implicit assumptions in the proof need justification and discussion of applicability.  
- Minor issues: Line 55 has a duplicated article ("the the"); Line 153 has a typo—should be κ_i/λ_i instead of κ_j/λ_j.
