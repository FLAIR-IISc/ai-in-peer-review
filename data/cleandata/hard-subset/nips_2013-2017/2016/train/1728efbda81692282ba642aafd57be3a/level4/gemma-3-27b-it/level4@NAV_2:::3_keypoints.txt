- Paper presents spectral analysis of the Koopman operator in RKHS, a modal decomposition algorithm, robustness analysis, and numerical demonstrations—extending DMD with kernel methods.  
- Well-written and clearly organized; theoretical development is lucid and the approach appears technically sound.  
- Limited comparison with state-of-the-art methods reduces assessment of the contribution’s significance.  
- Major clarification needed on Equation (7): its purpose, why it resembles Eq. (2), and whether it is consistent.  
- Potential inconsistency: KH is an operator between Hilbert spaces while ϕ is a function M → H (not an element of H); this needs elaboration.  
- Proof of Theorem 1 (Lines 144–157) is unclear—applying KH to both sides is ill-defined if both sides are scalars; the derivation is not fully convincing.  
- Assumptions of Theorem 1 appear strong and some are implicit; they require explicit justification and verification of general validity.  
- Arguments for acceptance: novel kernel-based extension of DMD, clear theory, and convincing numerical feasibility.  
- Arguments against acceptance: insufficient SOTA comparison, unclear/mathematically questionable Section 3.1, and strong/implicit assumptions.  
- Minor edits: fix "the the" at Line 55 and correct Line 153’s “\kappaj / \lambdaj” to likely “\kappai / \lambda_i”.
