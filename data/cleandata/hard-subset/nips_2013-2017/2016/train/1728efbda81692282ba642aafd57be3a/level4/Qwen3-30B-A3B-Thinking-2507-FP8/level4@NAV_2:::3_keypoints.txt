- Introduces spectral analysis of the Koopman operator in RKHS and a modal decomposition algorithm for finite-length nonlinear system data.
- Develops a robust implementation and demonstrates utility through multiple numerical examples; extends DMD with kernel methods.
- Paper is exceptionally well-written, well-organized, and presents clear, novel, and technically sound theoretical foundations.
- Experimental validation is appropriate and effectively supports the method, though comparisons to state-of-the-art are somewhat limited.
- Major concern: Section 3.1 and Equation (7) seem to restate Equation (2), so their purpose is unclear.
- Mathematical inconsistency: KH is defined as an operator H→H while φ is a function M→H (not an element of H), raising doubts about Equation (7) and the proof of Theorem 1.
- Problematic step: “applying the operator KH to both sides” is undefined because both sides are scalar values while KH maps H→H.
- Theorem 1 relies on strong assumptions that are not made explicit; the proof introduces additional unstated assumptions that need clarification and justification.
- Typos: Line 55 'the the' → 'the'; Line 153 '\kappaj / \lambdaj' → '\kappai / \lambda_i'.
