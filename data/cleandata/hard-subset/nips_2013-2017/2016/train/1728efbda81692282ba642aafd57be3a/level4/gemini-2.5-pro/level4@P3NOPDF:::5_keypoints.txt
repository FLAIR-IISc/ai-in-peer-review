- Introduces KDMD: extends DMD to RKHS via kernel-based feature lifts for spectral analysis of nonlinear dynamical systems.  
- Core contribution is significant and elegant: principled Koopman operator approximation using richer basis functions and the kernel trick.  
- Potentially high-impact idea that could be widely cited if presented clearly.  
- Primary weakness: poor presentation—dense formalism and complex notation obscure intuition and accessibility.  
- Major concern 1: lacks clarity and pedagogical value; many concepts could be stated far more simply.  
- Major concern 2: ineffective structure—Section 4 is largely redundant; Section 5 lacks concrete examples/figures or should be condensed; Section 6 is overly compressed and needs expansion.  
- Minor issue: possible typographical error in the "NL" toy problem—dynamics appear linear, please verify.  
- Minor issue: experiments use polynomial systems; paper should detail kernel selection and link to finite-dimensional Koopman-invariant subspaces (e.g., Brunton et al., 2015).  
- Recommendation: reject due to presentation/clarity problems, but encourage major revision focused on improved exposition and restructuring because the ideas are strong.
