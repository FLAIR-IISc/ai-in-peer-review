- Paper presents spectral analysis of the Koopman operator in RKHS, a modal decomposition algorithm, a robustifying method, and numerical experiments; extends DMD via the kernel trick.  
- Manuscript is well-written, organized, with clear theoretical explanations and a solid technical presentation.  
- Lacks in-depth comparison to state-of-the-art methods, though numerical experiments sufficiently illustrate usefulness.  
- Major concern: Section 3.1 (Equation (7) and Theorem 1) is unclear—Eq. (7) resembles Eq. (2) and its purpose is ambiguous.  
- Equation (7) appears inconsistent: KH is an operator H→H while φ is a function M→H, so the notation/usage needs detailed clarification; this undermines the proof in Lines 144–157.  
- The phrase “by applying the operator KH to both sides” (Line 149) is unclear because both sides are scalar yet KH maps H→H.  
- Assumptions in Theorem 1 are strong and implicit; the authors should state them explicitly, justify them, and explain additional assumptions introduced in the proof and their validity.  
- Minor corrections: Line 55: "the the" → "the"; Line 153: "\kappaj / \lambdaj" → "\kappai / \lambda_i".
