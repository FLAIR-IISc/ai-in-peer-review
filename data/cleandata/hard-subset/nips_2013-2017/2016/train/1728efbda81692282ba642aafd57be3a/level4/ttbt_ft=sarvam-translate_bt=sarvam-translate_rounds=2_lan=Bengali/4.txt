This paper extends the Kupmann spectral analysis to kernel regeneration. The method is evaluated based on simulated and real data. It is a very well-written paper. I would like to see two more aspects in this paper: firstly, a comparison with non-RKHS Kupmann spectral analysis in simulation. Secondly (and this can also be explored in simulation), there should be a discussion/exploration of kernel choice, and how it can affect the results.
