- Paper presents spectral analysis of the Koopman operator in RKHS, proposing a kernelized modal decomposition (extending DMD) with a robustness method and supporting numerical experiments.  
- Strength: Well-written and clearly organized, making complex theory accessible.  
- Strength: Technical derivations appear solid.  
- Strength: Numerical experiments effectively demonstrate utility, though not exhaustively compared to state-of-the-art.  
- Major concern: Equation (7) is unclear and appears very similar to Equation (2); its distinct purpose needs explanation.  
- Major concern: Equation (7) shows a type mismatch—K_H: H → H is applied to φ, but φ is a function M → H, so how K_H acts on φ must be clarified.  
- Major concern: Theorem 1 proof is questionable because applying K_H to both sides (Line 149) is confusing if the sides are scalars; this step needs detailed justification.  
- Major concern: Theorem 1’s assumptions are strong, not fully detailed, and additional unstated assumptions appear in the proof; authors should clarify and justify these and discuss practical validity.  
- Typos: Line 55 "the the" → "the"; Line 153 possible index mismatch, `\kappai / \lambda_i` likely should be `\kappaj / \lambdaj`.
