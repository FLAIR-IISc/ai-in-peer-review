This paper extends Koopman spectral analysis to the reproducing-kernel setting (RKHS) and evaluates the method on both simulated and real datasets. The paper is very well written. I would, however, like to see two additional components: (1) a comparison within the simulations to standard (non-RKHS) Koopman spectral analysis; and (2) a discussion—and ideally a simulation-based exploration—of how the choice of kernel affects the results.
