This paper presents an extension of Koopman spectral analysis to reproducing kernels, evaluating the proposed method on both simulated and real data. The paper is very well-written. However, the work could be strengthened by addressing two key points. First, the simulation studies would be more compelling with a direct comparison to standard, non-RKHS Koopman spectral analysis. Second, the paper would benefit from a discussion or exploration of how the choice of kernel impacts the results, an aspect that could also be investigated within the simulations.
