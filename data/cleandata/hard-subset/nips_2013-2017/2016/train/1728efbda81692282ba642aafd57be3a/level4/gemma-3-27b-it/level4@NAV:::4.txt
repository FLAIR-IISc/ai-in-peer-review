This paper presents a valuable extension of Koopman spectral analysis through the application of reproducing kernels. The methodology is thoroughly evaluated using both simulated and real-world datasets, and the manuscript is exceptionally well-written. To further strengthen the work, I suggest two additions. First, including a comparison to standard, non-RKHS-based Koopman spectral analysis within the simulation studies would provide useful context. Second, a discussion of kernel selection – and an exploration of how different kernels might influence the results – would be beneficial, and could potentially be investigated through further simulations.
