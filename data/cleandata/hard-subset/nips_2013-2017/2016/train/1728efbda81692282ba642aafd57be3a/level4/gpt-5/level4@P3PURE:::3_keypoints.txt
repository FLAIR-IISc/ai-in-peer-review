- Paper develops spectral analysis of the Koopman operator in RKHS, proposing a modal decomposition algorithm, a robustified variant, and numerical experiments; conceptually extends DMD using the kernel trick.
- Strength: paper is very well written, well organized, and presents the theoretical concepts clearly with a technically solid approach.
- Strength: numerical experiments adequately illustrate usefulness despite lack of deep comparison to state-of-the-art.
- Major concern: Section 3.1—Equation (7) and the proof of Theorem 1—are confusing and need clarification.
- Equation (7) seems very similar to Equation (2); unclear what additional role or insight it provides beyond Eq (2).
- Type inconsistency: KH is a linear operator on H but φ is a function M→H (not in H); Eq (7) needs interpretation so the types match.
- Proof issue: phrase “applying the operator KH to both sides” is unclear/invalid if both sides are scalars, since KH maps H→H and does not act on scalars; the intended operation must be clarified.
- Theorem 1 assumptions are strong and not fully explicit; additional unstated assumptions appear in the proof and need to be stated and justified with conditions under which they hold.
- Minor typos: Line 55 “the the” → “the”; Line 153 use κ_j / λ_j instead of κ_i / λ_i.
