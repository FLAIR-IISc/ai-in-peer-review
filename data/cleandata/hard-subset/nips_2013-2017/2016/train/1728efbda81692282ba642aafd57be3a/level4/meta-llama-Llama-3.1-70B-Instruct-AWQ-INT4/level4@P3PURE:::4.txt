This paper extends Koopman spectral analysis to reproducing kernels and evaluates the method using both simulated and real data. The paper is exceptionally well-written. However, I would have appreciated the inclusion of two additional aspects to further strengthen the work. Firstly, a comparison with non-RKHS Koopman spectral analysis in the simulations would have provided valuable insights into the benefits of using reproducing kernels. Secondly, a discussion or exploration of the choice of kernels and their potential impact on the results would be beneficial, and this could also be investigated through simulations.
