- Novelty unclear relative to Williams et al. (2015); explicitly contrast the proposed kernel construction and state novel contributions (esp. Section 4).
- Literature review omits important kernel-based dynamical systems works (e.g., Ralaivola & d'Alché Buc; Tuia et al.; Rojo-Álvarez et al.; Mouatamid & Schaback; Franz & Schölkopf).
- Insufficient treatment of stochastic systems: need deeper discussion and a simple stochastic toy experiment to assess performance in realistic settings.
- Clarify the assumption requiring "pairs of consecutive observables," its implications for multidimensional time series with weak pointwise relations, and how the kernel construction helps; explicitly state causal assumptions.
- Experimental evaluation is underpowered: prediction via preimage is standard; embedding/recognition lacks comparisons to kernel k-means and subspace-kernel methods; change-point detection lacks state-of-the-art online kernel method baselines.
- Minor fixes: check indices in "tau-1 vs. tau" and correct kernel notation to k(xi, xj) (not k(xi.xj)).
- Positive: paper is well-motivated, experimentally validated, and generally well-written and clear.
