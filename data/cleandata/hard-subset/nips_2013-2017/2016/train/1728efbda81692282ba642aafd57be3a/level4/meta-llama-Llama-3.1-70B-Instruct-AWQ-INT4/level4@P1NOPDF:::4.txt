This paper extends Koopman spectral analysis to reproducing kernels, demonstrating its application on both simulated and real data. The paper is exceptionally well-written, making it a pleasure to read. However, I would have appreciated the inclusion of two additional aspects to further enhance the paper's completeness. Firstly, a comparison with non-RKHS Koopman spectral analysis in the simulations would have provided valuable insight into the benefits of the proposed approach. Secondly, a discussion or exploration of the choice of kernels and their potential impact on the results would be a worthwhile addition, potentially also examined through simulations.
