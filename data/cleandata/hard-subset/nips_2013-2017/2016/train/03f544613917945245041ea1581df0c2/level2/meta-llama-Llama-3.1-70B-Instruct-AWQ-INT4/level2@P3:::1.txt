**Summary of the Paper**

The paper proposes a novel stochastic gradient Markov Chain Monte Carlo (SG-MCMC) algorithm, called Stochastic Gradient Richardson-Romberg Langevin Dynamics (SGRRLD), which aims to reduce the bias of existing SG-MCMC methods while keeping the variance at a reasonable level. The algorithm applies the Richardson-Romberg extrapolation method to the Stochastic Gradient Langevin Dynamics (SGLD) algorithm, which is a popular SG-MCMC method. The authors provide formal theoretical analysis and show that SGRRLD is asymptotically consistent and satisfies a central limit theorem. They also derive bounds for its non-asymptotic bias and mean squared error, and demonstrate that SGRRLD attains higher rates of convergence than existing SG-MCMC methods with first-order integrators.
