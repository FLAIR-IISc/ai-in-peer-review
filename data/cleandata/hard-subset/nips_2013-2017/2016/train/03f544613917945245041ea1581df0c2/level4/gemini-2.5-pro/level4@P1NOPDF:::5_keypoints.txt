- Proposes SG-RR-MCMC to reduce bias in SG-MCMC using Richardson-Romberg extrapolation.  
- Algorithm runs two Markov chains embarrassingly parallel with no inter-chain communication.  
- Theoretical analysis shows asymptotic consistency.  
- Empirical results are satisfactory; core idea is interesting and effective.  
- Method is essentially a straightforward combination of Richardson-Romberg extrapolation and SGLD.  
- Theoretical justification lacks a convergence rate analysis to support efficiency claims.  
- Missing experimental comparison to higher-order integrators that target similar convergence gains.  
- Inconsistency in the paper's statements about the step size.  
- Discrepancy between Algorithm 1 in the supplement and Equation (5) in the main text.
