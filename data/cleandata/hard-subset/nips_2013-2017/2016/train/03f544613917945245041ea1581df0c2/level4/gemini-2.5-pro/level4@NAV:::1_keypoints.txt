- Proposes applying Richardson-Romberg extrapolation (RRE) to MCMC, introducing a novel SGLD-RRE algorithm.  
- Bridges MCMC and numerical SDE literatures, a valuable cross-community connection.  
- Provides theoretical convergence guarantees and analysis of when SGLD-RRE improves over baseline SGLD.  
- Empirically shows large gains on small synthetic problems and moderate, consistent improvements in large-scale tasks.  
- Exposition is clear, technical content appears sound, and the method is practical and computationally appealing.  
- Likely to be adopted by practitioners but viewed as an incremental rather than revolutionary improvement.  
- Minor: Figure 1a actually plots a Gaussian fit using the empirical posterior mean and variance—this should be explicitly stated.  
- Minor: Figure 2a needs a brief explanation for why bias does not necessarily decrease with reduced stepsize, as this is counterintuitive.
