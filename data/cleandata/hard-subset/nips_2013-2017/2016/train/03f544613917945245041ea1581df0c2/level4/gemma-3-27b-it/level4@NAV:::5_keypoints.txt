- Proposal of SG-RR-MCMC: a new method reducing bias in SG-MCMC by applying Richardson-Romberg extrapolation.  
- Method uses two independent, parallelizable chains and requires no inter-chain communication.  
- Theoretical analysis shows asymptotic consistency of the proposed algorithm.  
- Empirical results support the method’s effectiveness in practice.  
- The approach is an intuitive and effective combination of Richardson-Romberg extrapolation with SGLD.  
- Claim of improved convergence would be stronger with comparisons to higher-order integrators, which are missing from experiments.  
- There is a discrepancy in the discussion of step-size selection.  
- Inconsistency between Algorithm 1 in the supplement and Equation (5) in the main text.
