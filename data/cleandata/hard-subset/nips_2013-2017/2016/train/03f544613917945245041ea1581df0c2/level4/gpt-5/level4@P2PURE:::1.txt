This paper applies Richardson–Romberg extrapolation (RRE) to “big-data MCMC” methods, focusing on Stochastic Gradient Langevin Dynamics (SGLD). A key contribution is the connection it draws between the MCMC and SDE literatures, which should be valuable to both communities. The authors propose an SGLD-RRE algorithm, establish convergence results (including conditions under which SGLD-RRE outperforms vanilla SGLD), and present empirical evaluations. The experiments show large gains on toy problems and modest but consistent improvements on large-scale tasks.

Overall, the paper is clear, technically sound, and well written. The method is a relatively straightforward application of RRE to SGLD, which keeps it computationally convenient. I expect practitioners will find it useful, though it is unlikely to be transformative for current practice.
