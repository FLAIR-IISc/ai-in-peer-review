- Proposes SG-RR-MCMC: use Richardson-Romberg extrapolation by running two independent SG-MCMC chains in parallel and combining outputs to reduce bias.  
- Elegant and conceptually simple combination of Richardson-Romberg extrapolation with SGLD.  
- Provides theoretical analysis establishing asymptotic consistency of the method.  
- Empirical results show reduced bias compared to standard SG-MCMC methods.  
- Embarrassingly parallel implementation is a practical advantage.  
- Missing rigorous comparison of convergence rate with existing methods, especially higher-order integrators.  
- Inconsistency in the paper’s description of step size handling that needs clarification.  
- Discrepancy between Algorithm 1 in the supplement and Equation (5) in the main text that must be resolved for reproducibility.  
- Recommendation: accept conditional on resolving inconsistencies and adding comparative analysis.
