- Applies Richardson–Romberg extrapolation (RRE) to large-scale MCMC, focusing on SGLD.
- Bridges the MCMC and numerical SDE literatures, offering cross-disciplinary benefits.
- Introduces a new SGLD-RRE algorithm.
- Provides theoretical convergence analysis and conditions under which SGLD-RRE improves on standard SGLD.
- Empirical results: strong improvements on toy problems and modest but consistent gains in large-scale applications.
- Paper is solid, technically sound, and clearly written.
- Method is computationally convenient and straightforward; likely useful to practitioners though not transformative.
- Minor: Figure 1a may be misleading—seems to show a Gaussian fitted by empirical mean/variance rather than the empirical posterior; clarify.
- Minor: Figure 2a shows non-monotonic bias with decreasing step size; add a brief discussion explaining this.
