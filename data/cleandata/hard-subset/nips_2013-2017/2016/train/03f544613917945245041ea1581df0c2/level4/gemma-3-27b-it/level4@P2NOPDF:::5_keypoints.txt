- Proposes SG-RR-MCMC, a new method to reduce bias in SG-MCMC using Richardson-Romberg extrapolation.
- Leverages a well-established RR technique to improve sampling accuracy in stochastic gradient settings.
- Method is simple and highly parallelizable, running two independent chains without inter-chain communication.
- Provides theoretical analysis establishing asymptotic consistency of SG-RR-MCMC.
- Empirical results are encouraging and support the effectiveness of the approach.
- Conceptually a straightforward combination of Richardson-Romberg extrapolation with SGLD.
- Claimed improved convergence rate arises from using a higher-order integration scheme.
- Lacks direct empirical comparisons to other high-order integrators, weakening the uniqueness claim.
- Inconsistent discussion of step size requires clarification.
- Critical discrepancy between Algorithm 1 (supplement) and Equation (5) in the main text that must be resolved for reproducibility.
