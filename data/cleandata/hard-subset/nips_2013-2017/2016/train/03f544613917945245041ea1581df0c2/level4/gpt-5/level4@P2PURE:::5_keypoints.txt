- Proposal: Introduces SG-RR-MCMC applying Richardson–Romberg extrapolation to reduce bias in SG-MCMC.  
- Implementation: Runs two chains in embarrassingly parallel fashion with no inter-chain communication.  
- Theory: Shows asymptotic consistency and an improved convergence rate.  
- Empirics: Experimental results are satisfactory and show empirical effectiveness.  
- Novelty: Idea is interesting but essentially a straightforward combination of Richardson–Romberg and SGLD.  
- Missing comparisons: Similar gains might be achievable with higher-order integrators, yet no experimental comparison to such integrators is provided.  
- Inconsistency: There is an unresolved inconsistency regarding the step size.  
- Supplement mismatch: Algorithm 1 in the supplement is inconsistent with Equation (5).
