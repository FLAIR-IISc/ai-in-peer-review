- Proposes applying Richardson-Romberg Extrapolation (RRE) to large-scale MCMC, specifically SGLD.  
- Provides an explicit link between MCMC and numerical SDE literatures, beneficial to both communities.  
- Introduces a novel SGLD-RRE algorithm.  
- Develops theoretical convergence results for the proposed method.  
- Gives arguments about when SGLD-RRE is expected to outperform standard SGLD.  
- Numerical experiments show strong improvements on synthetic examples and modest but consistent gains on large-scale practical tasks.  
- Paper is clear, well-written, technically sound, and computationally convenient to implement.  
- Overall contribution is a useful incremental improvement likely valuable to practitioners.  
- Minor: Figure 1a caption may mislead—plot seems to show a Gaussian parameterized by empirical mean/variance rather than a direct empirical posterior visualization.  
- Minor: Figure 2a shows bias not always decreasing with step size; the paper should briefly discuss the role of discretization error versus stochastic gradient variance.
