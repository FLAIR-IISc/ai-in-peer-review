- Applies Richardson-Romberg extrapolation (RRE) to big-data MCMC, focusing on Stochastic Gradient Langevin Dynamics (SGLD).
- Bridges the MCMC and numerical SDE literatures, providing value to researchers in both areas.
- Proposes a novel SGLD-RRE algorithm as a straightforward, computationally efficient adaptation of RRE to SGLD.
- Provides theoretical convergence results showing scenarios where SGLD-RRE outperforms standard SGLD.
- Presents numerical experiments: large improvements on toy problems and consistent but modest gains in large-scale settings.
- Paper is well-executed, clearly written, and technically sound.
- Method is practical and likely to be adopted by practitioners, though not revolutionary.
- Minor critique: Fig. 1a is misleading—plots a Gaussian from empirical mean/variance rather than the empirical posterior; clarify this.
- Minor critique: Fig. 2a needs a brief explanation why bias does not necessarily decrease with smaller step sizes.
