- Applies Richardson-Romberg extrapolation (RRE) to big-data MCMC methods, focusing on SGLD.
- Provides a useful link between MCMC and SDE literature.
- Introduces the SGLD-RRE algorithm with theoretical convergence analysis.
- Analyzes conditions under which SGLD-RRE improves over vanilla SGLD.
- Empirical results show impressive gains on toy problems and modest but consistent gains on large-scale examples.
- Paper is well-organized, clearly written, and easy to follow.
- Theoretical results are solid and supported by experiments.
- Method is straightforward and computationally convenient, likely useful to practitioners.
- Originality: novel combination of existing techniques (RRE + SGLD).
- Significance: potential impact for big-data MCMC, though not revolutionary.
- Minor: Fig 1a is misleading—it's a Gaussian using empirical mean/variance, not the empirical posterior; clarify this.
- Minor: Fig 2a needs discussion on why bias may not decrease with stepsize.
- Con: improvements over vanilla SGLD are modest in large-scale examples.
- Con: paper could better discuss limitations and directions for future research.
