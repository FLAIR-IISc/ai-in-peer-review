- Applies Richardson-Romberg extrapolation (RRE) to big-data MCMC, specifically Stochastic Gradient Langevin Dynamics (SGLD).
- Leverages RRE to build a bridge between MCMC and SDE literatures, benefiting both communities.
- Proposes a novel SGLD-RRE algorithm and provides theoretical convergence results.
- Numerical experiments show large gains on toy problems and consistent but modest improvements on large-scale examples.
- Overall a solid contribution offering an interesting interdisciplinary angle and compelling theoretical insights.
- Empirical performance is satisfactory; the method is clear, technically sound, and accessible.
- RRE application here is straightforward and computationally convenient, likely to be adopted in practice though not transformative.
- Suggest clarifying Fig. 1a to indicate it shows a Gaussian approximation of the empirical posterior, not the true posterior.
- Recommend adding a brief discussion in Fig. 2a explaining why bias may not decrease with smaller step sizes.
