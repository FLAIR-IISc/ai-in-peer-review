- Applies Richardson–Romberg extrapolation (RRE) to modern “big-data MCMC”, specifically SGLD.  
- Bridges MCMC and SDE literatures, offering cross-field benefits.  
- Proposes a novel SGLD-RRE algorithm.  
- Provides theoretical convergence analysis justifying performance gains over standard SGLD.  
- Empirical results: substantial improvements on test problems and consistent but smaller gains on larger-scale applications.  
- Paper is strong, clearly written, and presents compelling theoretical and empirical findings.  
- Method is computationally efficient, readily implementable, and likely to be adopted by practitioners (though not transformative).
