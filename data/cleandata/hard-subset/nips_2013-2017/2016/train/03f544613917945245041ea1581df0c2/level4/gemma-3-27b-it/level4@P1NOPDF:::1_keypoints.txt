- Applies Richardson-Romberg extrapolation (RRE) to big-data MCMC, focusing on Stochastic Gradient Langevin Dynamics (SGLD).  
- Bridges MCMC and SDE literature, offering a useful connection for both communities.  
- Introduces a new SGLD-RRE method with theoretical convergence results and arguments for improvement over vanilla SGLD.  
- Provides supporting numerical results showing impressive gains on toy examples and modest but consistent improvements on large-scale problems.  
- Overall assessment: solid, clearly written, technically sound work with interesting connections and compelling theory.  
- Method is a relatively straightforward, computationally convenient application of RRE that practitioners will find useful but unlikely to radically change practice.  
- Minor concern: Fig. 1a is potentially misleading—plotted Gaussian uses empirical posterior mean/variance rather than the empirical posterior distribution and needs clarification.  
- Minor concern: Fig. 2a requires discussion of why bias does not necessarily decrease with smaller stepsize, as this is counterintuitive.
