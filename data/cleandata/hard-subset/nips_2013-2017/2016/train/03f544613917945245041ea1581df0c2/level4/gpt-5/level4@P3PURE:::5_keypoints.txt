- Proposes SG-RR-MCMC leveraging Richardson–Romberg extrapolation to reduce bias in SG-MCMC.  
- Runs two chains in embarrassingly parallel fashion without communication.  
- Theoretical analysis shows asymptotic consistency and suggests improved convergence rate.  
- Empirical results are satisfactory and the method appears effective.  
- Idea is essentially a simple combination of Richardson–Romberg extrapolation with SGLD and is interesting.  
- Comparable convergence gains may be obtainable via higher-order integrators; paper lacks experimental comparison to them.  
- Inconsistent statements about step size—need clarification of step-size assumptions/schedules in theory vs experiments and consistency across the paper.  
- Algorithm 1 in the supplement is inconsistent with Equation (5); this discrepancy must be resolved or explained.  
- Overall contribution is promising and well motivated but requires addressing clarity and empirical validation issues.
