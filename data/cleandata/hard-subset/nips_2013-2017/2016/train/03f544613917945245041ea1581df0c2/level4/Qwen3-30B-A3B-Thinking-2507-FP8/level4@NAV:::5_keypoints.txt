- Proposes SG-RR-MCMC to mitigate bias in SG-MCMC using Richardson-Romberg extrapolation.  
- Method runs two chains embarrassingly parallel with no inter-chain communication.  
- Theoretical analysis establishes asymptotic consistency for SG-RR-MCMC.  
- Empirical results demonstrate adequate performance.  
- Core idea is interesting but essentially a straightforward combination of Richardson-Romberg extrapolation and SGLD.  
- Claims improved convergence rate, but similar gains might be achievable with high-order integrators.  
- No comparative experiments against high-order integrators are provided.  
- Paper contains an inconsistent statement regarding step size.  
- Algorithm 1 in the supplement is inconsistent with Equation (5).
