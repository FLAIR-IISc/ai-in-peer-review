- Proposes SG-RR-MCMC to reduce SG-MCMC bias using Richardson–Romberg extrapolation.
- Implements via running two chains embarrassingly in parallel with no communication.
- Theoretical analysis shows asymptotic consistency and an improved convergence rate.
- Empirical results are satisfactory and suggest the method is effective.
- Conceptually a straightforward combination of Richardson–Romberg extrapolation and SGLD (limited novelty).
- Similar improvements may be achievable with higher-order integrators, but no experiments compare against them.
- There is an inconsistency in the paper’s discussion of the step size.
- Algorithm 1 in the supplement is inconsistent with Equation (5).
