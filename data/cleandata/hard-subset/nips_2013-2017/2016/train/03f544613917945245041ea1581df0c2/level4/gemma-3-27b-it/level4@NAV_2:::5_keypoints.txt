- Proposal: Introduces SGRRLD, applying Richardson‑Romberg extrapolation to SG‑MCMC by running two parallel chains without inter‑chain communication.
- Theoretical results: Proves asymptotic consistency, a central limit theorem, and provides bias and MSE bounds.
- Convergence: Shows faster convergence rate than standard SGLD and similar theoretical accuracy to higher‑order integrators.
- Empirical validation: Synthetic and real‑data experiments support theoretical claims and show improvement over SGLD.
- Parallelism: Algorithm is embarrassingly parallel and well‑suited for distributed/large‑scale architectures.
- Generality: Method can be applied to a variety of SG‑MCMC algorithms, increasing potential impact.
- Missing comparison: Lacks direct empirical comparison to SG‑MCMC methods with higher‑order integrators.
- Step‑size issue: Discussion of optimal step‑size selection is potentially inconsistent and needs clarification.
