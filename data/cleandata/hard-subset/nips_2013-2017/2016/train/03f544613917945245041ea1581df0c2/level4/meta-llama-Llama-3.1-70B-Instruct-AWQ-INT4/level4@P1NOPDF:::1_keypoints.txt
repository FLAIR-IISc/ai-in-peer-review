- Applies Richardson-Romberg extrapolation (RRE) to big-data MCMC, specifically Stochastic Gradient Langevin Dynamics (SGLD).  
- Bridges MCMC and SDE literatures, offering benefits to both communities.  
- Introduces a new SGLD-RRE algorithm and develops theoretical convergence results.  
- Provides arguments and conditions under which SGLD-RRE improves over vanilla SGLD.  
- Empirical results: large improvements on toy examples and modest but consistent gains on large-scale tasks.  
- Well-written, technically sound, and computationally convenient; likely adoptable by practitioners though not revolutionary.  
- Contributions (theory + empirical) are valuable additions to the field.  
- Minor: Fig.1a is misleading—plots a Gaussian with empirical mean/variance rather than the empirical posterior; clarify.  
- Minor: Fig.2a should include a brief explanation why bias may not decrease with smaller stepsize.
