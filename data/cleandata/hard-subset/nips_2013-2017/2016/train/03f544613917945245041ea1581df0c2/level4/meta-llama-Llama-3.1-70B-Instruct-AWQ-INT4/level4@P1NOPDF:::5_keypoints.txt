- Proposal of SG-RR-MCMC: a novel method using Richardson-Romberg extrapolation to reduce bias in SG-MCMC.
- SG-RR-MCMC runs two chains in parallel without communication.
- Theoretical analysis claims SG-RR-MCMC is asymptotically consistent.
- Empirical results are satisfactory and validate the method’s effectiveness.
- Combining Richardson-Romberg extrapolation with SGLD is an intriguing idea and empirically effective.
- The method’s improved convergence rate can be theoretically justified, but similar gains might be obtainable with high-order integrators.
- No experimental comparison with high-order integrators is provided.
- An inconsistent statement about the step size is present in the paper.
- Algorithm 1 in the supplement is inconsistent with Equation (5).
