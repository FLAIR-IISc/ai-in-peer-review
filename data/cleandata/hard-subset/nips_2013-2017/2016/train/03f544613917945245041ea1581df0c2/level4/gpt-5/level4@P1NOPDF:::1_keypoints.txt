- Applies Richardson–Romberg extrapolation (RRE) to big-data MCMC, specifically SGLD, bridging MCMC and SDE literatures.  
- Proposes SGLD-RRE, proves convergence results, and delineates when it outperforms vanilla SGLD.  
- Numerical results: striking improvements on toy problems; modest but consistent gains on large-scale tasks.  
- Paper is solid, clearly written, technically sound, and the method is straightforward and computationally convenient.  
- Likely to be adopted by practitioners, though not expected to revolutionize current practice.
