- Applies Richardson-Romberg extrapolation (RRE) to Stochastic Gradient Langevin Dynamics (SGLD) in the big-data MCMC context.
- Bridges MCMC and SDE literatures, offering a valuable interdisciplinary connection.
- Proposes a novel SGLD-RRE algorithm.
- Provides theoretical analysis proving convergence and conditions where SGLD-RRE outperforms standard SGLD.
- Empirical results show large gains on controlled numerical examples and modest, consistent improvements on large-scale applications.
- Paper is strong, clearly written, and presents compelling theoretical and empirical support.
- The method is computationally efficient and likely useful for practitioners, though not expected to fundamentally change workflows.
