- Adapts Richardson–Romberg extrapolation (RRE) to scalable “big-data” MCMC, focusing on SGLD.  
- Builds an explicit bridge between SG-MCMC and SDE literatures.  
- Proposes SGLD-RRE algorithm.  
- Develops convergence theory, including conditions where RRE improves over standard SGLD.  
- Empirical results: large gains on controlled toy problems; smaller but consistent gains on large-scale tasks.  
- Paper is solid, clear, and technically sound.  
- Approach is a fairly direct application of RRE, making it computationally attractive and practically useful, though not transformative.  
- Minor: Figure 1a is potentially misleading—plots a Gaussian with empirical mean/variance rather than the empirical posterior; clarify this.  
- Minor: Figure 2a should discuss why bias need not decrease monotonically as step size shrinks.
