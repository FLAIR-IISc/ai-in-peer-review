- Extends Richardson–Romberg extrapolation (RRE) to big-data MCMC, specifically SGLD.  
- Establishes a meaningful bridge between MCMC and numerical SDE literatures.  
- Proposes a novel SGLD-RRE algorithm.  
- Provides theoretical convergence guarantees for SGLD-RRE.  
- Gives conditions/arguments for when SGLD-RRE outperforms standard SGLD.  
- Empirical results: substantial gains on toy problems and consistent (but more modest) improvements at large scale.  
- Contribution is technically sound, well-motivated, and computationally efficient.  
- Writing is exceptionally clear and accessible.  
- Method is a straightforward application of RRE, so useful in practice but not a paradigm shift.  
- Minor suggestions: clarify that Figure 1a shows a Gaussian approximation (mean/variance) rather than the estimated posterior, and explain in Figure 2a why bias may not decrease as stepsize diminishes.
