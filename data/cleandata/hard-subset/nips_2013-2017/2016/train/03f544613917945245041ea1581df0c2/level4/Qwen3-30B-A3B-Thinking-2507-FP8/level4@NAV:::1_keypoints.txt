- Extends Richardson-Romberg extrapolation (RRE) to contemporary big-data MCMC, specifically SGLD.  
- Effectively bridges MCMC and SDE literatures, potentially benefiting both communities.  
- Proposes a novel SGLD-RRE algorithm.  
- Establishes theoretical convergence guarantees for the method.  
- Argues superiority over standard SGLD under specific conditions.  
- Empirical results show substantial improvements on synthetic benchmarks and consistent, moderate gains in large-scale applications.  
- Paper is well-written, technically sound, and presents a clear contribution.  
- Approach is a relatively straightforward application of RRE to SGLD and is computationally efficient.  
- Unlikely to revolutionize practice but may be adopted by practitioners for practical advantages.  
- Minor concern: Figure 1a is potentially misleading—uses a Gaussian approximation (empirical mean/variance) instead of the actual empirical posterior and should be clarified.  
- Minor concern: Figure 2a needs discussion explaining why bias does not monotonically decrease with stepsize, as this is counterintuitive.
