- Proposes SG-RR-MCMC, using Richardson–Romberg extrapolation applied to SDEs to reduce bias in stochastic gradient MCMC.  
- Runs two chains in an embarrassingly parallel way with no communication between them.  
- Theoretical analysis shows asymptotic consistency and improved convergence rates.  
- Empirical results demonstrate the method’s effectiveness.  
- Represents a straightforward integration of Richardson–Romberg extrapolation with SGLD.  
- Paper does not compare against high-order integrators, despite similar achievable convergence improvements.  
- Contains inconsistent statements about the step-size schedule.  
- Algorithm 1 (supplement) is inconsistent with Equation (5).  
- Overall, method is theoretically sound and empirically effective but technical inconsistencies must be resolved.
