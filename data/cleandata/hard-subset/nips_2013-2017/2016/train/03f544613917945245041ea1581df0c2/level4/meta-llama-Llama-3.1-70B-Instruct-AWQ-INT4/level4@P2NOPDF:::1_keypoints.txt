- Applies Richardson-Romberg extrapolation (RRE) to big-data MCMC, specifically SGLD.
- Connects MCMC and SDE literatures by leveraging RRE, potentially benefiting both communities.
- Introduces a new algorithm, SGLD-RRE.
- Provides theoretical convergence results for SGLD-RRE.
- Gives arguments and conditions under which SGLD-RRE outperforms vanilla SGLD.
- Empirical results: large improvements on toy examples and modest but consistent improvements on large-scale tasks.
- Paper is solid, well-written, clear, and technically sound.
- The RRE application is computationally convenient and straightforward.
- Not a revolutionary change but likely useful for practitioners.
- Minor: Figure 1a is potentially misleading—shows a Gaussian with empirical mean/variance instead of the empirical posterior distribution; clarify.
- Minor: Figure 2a should discuss why bias does not necessarily decrease as step size decreases.
