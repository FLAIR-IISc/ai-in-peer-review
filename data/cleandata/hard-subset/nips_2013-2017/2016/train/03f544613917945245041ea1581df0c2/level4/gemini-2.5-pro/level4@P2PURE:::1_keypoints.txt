- Proposes applying Richardson-Romberg extrapolation (RRE) to big-data MCMC methods, specifically SGLD.  
- Bridges the MCMC and numerical SDE literatures, benefiting both communities.  
- Introduces a new SGLD-RRE algorithm as a computationally convenient application of RRE.  
- Provides theoretical convergence results that show advantages over standard SGLD.  
- Empirical results: impressive gains on small illustrative examples and modest but consistent improvements on large-scale problems.  
- Paper is strong, well-written, and technically sound; contribution is valuable and likely to be adopted by practitioners.  
- Method is a straightforward (not revolutionary) application of RRE but effective in practice.  
- Minor: Figure 1a may be misinterpreted—plot shows a Gaussian parameterized by empirical posterior mean/variance, not the empirical posterior; clarify caption/text.  
- Minor: Figure 2a is counter-intuitive (bias not monotonically decreasing with step size); add a brief explanation.
