- Applies Richardson-Romberg extrapolation (RRE) to big-data MCMC, focusing on SGLD.  
- Bridges MCMC and SDE literatures, benefiting both communities.  
- Proposes a novel SGLD-RRE algorithm.  
- Provides theoretical convergence results, including regimes where SGLD-RRE outperforms standard SGLD.  
- Empirical results: large gains on toy problems and consistent but modest improvements at scale.  
- Paper is well-structured, clearly written, and offers compelling theoretical and empirical insights.  
- Method is a straightforward, computationally efficient application of RRE to SGLD.  
- Not a revolutionary change but likely to be adopted by practitioners.  
- Minor suggestions: clarify Fig. 1a to indicate it shows a Gaussian with empirical posterior mean/variance, and add brief discussion in Fig. 2a explaining why bias may not decrease with smaller stepsize.
