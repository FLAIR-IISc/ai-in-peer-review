To mitigate the biases of SG-MCMC, the authors propose a novel method called Stochastic Graded Richardson-Romberg-MCMC. This method uses Richardson-Romberg extrapolation, an off-the-shelf tool for SDE. Specifically, SG-RR-MCMC runs two series in parallel without communication. Theoretical analysis is provided, showing that SG-RR-MCMC is asymptotically consistent. Empirical results are also satisfactory. The idea is interesting and has proven effective according to empirical results. It can be seen as a simple combination of Richardson-Romberg extrapolation and SGLD. Efficiency can theoretically be validated by providing a better convergence rate, although this can be achieved using a higher-order integrator (unfortunately, the authors did not provide a comparison with an integrator in the experiment).
