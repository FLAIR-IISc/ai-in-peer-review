- Proposes SG-RR-MCMC to reduce bias in SG-MCMC using Richardson-Romberg extrapolation.
- Leverages an off-the-shelf Richardson-Romberg SDE technique and combines it with SGLD effectively.
- Method runs two independent parallel chains and is embarrassingly parallelizable.
- Theoretical analysis demonstrates asymptotic consistency.
- Empirical results are satisfactory and show the method’s effectiveness in practice.
- Efficiency claim could be strengthened by proving improved convergence rates.
- Similar convergence rates may be achieved by high-order integrators, but no experimental comparison is provided.
- Paper contains an inconsistent statement about the step size that needs clarification.
- Algorithm 1 in the supplement is inconsistent with Equation (5), causing reader confusion.
- Overall promising approach, but authors should fix inconsistencies and provide more comprehensive comparisons to existing methods.
