- Proposes SG-RR-MCMC, combining Richardson-Romberg extrapolation with SG-MCMC to reduce bias.  
- Leverages Richardson-Romberg, a known SDE technique, applied to stochastic gradient methods.  
- Implements two embarrassingly parallel chains requiring no inter-chain communication.  
- Theoretical analysis shows asymptotic consistency and suggests improved convergence rates.  
- Empirical results are satisfactory and demonstrate effectiveness.  
- Conceptually straightforward—essentially a combination of Richardson-Romberg extrapolation and SGLD.  
- Experiments lack comparison to higher-order integrators that might yield similar gains.  
- Inconsistency noted regarding the step size specification.  
- Algorithm 1 in the supplement does not match Equation (5) in the main text.
