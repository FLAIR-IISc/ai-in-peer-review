- Applies Richardson-Romberg extrapolation (RRE) to big-data MCMC methods, with a focus on SGLD.
- Bridges MCMC and SDE literatures, offering benefits to both communities.
- Introduces a new SGLD-RRE algorithm.
- Develops theoretical convergence results and conditions under which SGLD-RRE outperforms vanilla SGLD.
- Provides numerical results: impressive improvements on toy examples and modest, consistent gains in large-scale applications.
- Method is technically sound, computationally convenient, clearly written, and readily accessible.
- Anticipated to be useful to practitioners but unlikely to fundamentally change current practice.
