- Introduces stochastic gradient Richardson-Romberg MCMC (SG-RR-MCMC) to reduce SG-MCMC bias using Richardson-Romberg extrapolation.  
- SG-RR-MCMC runs two chains in parallel with no inter-chain communication required.  
- Provides theoretical analysis establishing asymptotic consistency of SG-RR-MCMC.  
- Empirical results show satisfactory performance, supporting the method’s efficacy.  
- Conceptually framed as a fusion of Richardson-Romberg extrapolation and SGLD.  
- Claims improved convergence rate comparable to using a high-order integrator.  
- Lacks experimental comparison with a true high-order integrator to validate the claimed advantage.  
- Notes inconsistencies: unclear step-size treatment and a mismatch between Algorithm 1 (supplement) and Equation (5) that need clarification.
