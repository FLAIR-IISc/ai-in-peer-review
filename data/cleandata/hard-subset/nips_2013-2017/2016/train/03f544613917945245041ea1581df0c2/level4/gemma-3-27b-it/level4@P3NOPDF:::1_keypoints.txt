- Introduces SGLD-RRE, applying Richardson-Romberg extrapolation to Stochastic Gradient Langevin Dynamics.  
- Bridges MCMC and SDE literatures, offering cross-disciplinary insights.  
- Provides rigorous theoretical convergence results and conditions when SGLD-RRE outperforms SGLD.  
- Demonstrates empirical improvements on controlled numerical examples.  
- Shows consistent but more modest gains in large-scale applications.  
- Method is computationally efficient and practically applicable.  
- Paper is clearly and concisely written.  
- Not a paradigm shift but a useful, readily adoptable contribution for practitioners.  
- Figure 1a could be misinterpreted; it depicts a Gaussian approximation using empirical mean/variance, not the empirical posterior.  
- Figure 2a shows non-monotonic bias with stepsize, which needs a brief explanatory discussion.  
- Overall recommendation: accept — technically sound, well-motivated, and impactful with minor fixes suggested.
