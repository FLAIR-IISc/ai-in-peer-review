- Proposes Richardson–Romberg extrapolation for SG-MCMC that improves bias and MSE with simple implementation and solid theoretical analysis.
- Primary theoretical concern: derivation of the claimed specific convergence rates from the main theorems (referenced as a "fatal flaw").

- High variance: the curve for the empirically optimal rate (α=0.2) fluctuates considerably, which seems inconsistent with a stable convergence rate—request explanation.
- Asymptotic error question: authors claim 1/(Kγ) dominates for small step sizes for the proposed method; why doesn’t the same dominance occur for standard SGLD under similar conditions?
