- Proposes SG-RR-MCMC, a method to reduce SG-MCMC bias using Richardson-Romberg extrapolation.  
- Leverages a readily available Richardson-Romberg tool for SDEs.  
- Can run two chains in parallel without communication, improving practical efficiency.  
- Provides theoretical analysis demonstrating asymptotic consistency.  
- Empirical results are promising and show the approach is effective with SGLD.  
- Claims efficiency can be validated via improved convergence rates.  
- Similar efficiency gains might be achieved with high-order integrators, but no experimental comparison is provided.  
- Paper has inconsistencies regarding the stated step size.  
- Algorithm 1 in the supplement does not align with Equation (5) and should be corrected.
