- Proposes SG-RR-MCMC: a novel method using Richardson-Romberg extrapolation to reduce SG-MCMC bias.
- Leverages a standard SDE technique (Richardson-Romberg) applied to stochastic-gradient MCMC.
- Uses two parallel, non-communicating chains, demonstrating computational efficiency.
- Provides theoretical analysis proving asymptotic consistency of SG-RR-MCMC.
- Empirical results are satisfactory and support the method's effectiveness.
- The idea is essentially a straightforward combination of Richardson-Romberg extrapolation and SGLD, which is interesting and empirically validated.
- Improved convergence rates could theoretically validate efficiency; similar gains might be obtained with high-order integrators.
- Experiments lack a comparative analysis against high-order integrators.
- There is an inconsistency in the manuscript regarding the step-size statement that needs correction.
- A discrepancy exists between Algorithm 1 in the supplement and Equation (5) that requires clarification.
