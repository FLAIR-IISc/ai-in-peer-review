- Applies Richardson–Romberg extrapolation (RRE) to big-data MCMC, focusing on Stochastic Gradient Langevin Dynamics (SGLD).
- Establishes an explicit connection between MCMC and SDE literatures, valuable to both communities.
- Introduces the SGLD-RRE method with a computationally convenient implementation.
- Provides theoretical convergence results and conditions under which SGLD-RRE outperforms vanilla SGLD.
- Numerical experiments: large gains on toy problems and modest but consistent improvements on large-scale tasks.
- Paper is solid, clearly written, technically sound, and offers meaningful cross-field insights.
- Likely useful in practice but unlikely to be transformative for current methods.
- Minor: Clarify Figure 1a — it shows a Gaussian using empirical mean/variance, not the empirically estimated posterior.
- Minor: Add discussion in Figure 2a about why bias may not decrease monotonically with step size.
