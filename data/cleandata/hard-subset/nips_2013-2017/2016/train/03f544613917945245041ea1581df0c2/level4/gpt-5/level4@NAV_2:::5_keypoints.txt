- Introduces SG-RR-MCMC to mitigate SG-MCMC bias using Richardson–Romberg extrapolation.  
- Leverages standard numerical acceleration for SDEs by running two chains with different step sizes in embarrassingly parallel fashion (no inter-chain communication).  
- Theoretical analysis establishes asymptotic consistency of SG-RR-MCMC.  
- Empirical results are satisfactory and indicate the idea is effective.  
- Conceptually a straightforward combination of Richardson–Romberg extrapolation with SGLD.  
- Claims improved convergence rates, but similar rates can be achieved by higher-order integrators and experiments lack comparison to those integrators.  
- Paper contains inconsistent statements about the step size.  
- Algorithm 1 (supplement) is not consistent with Equation (5).
