- Introduces SG-RR-MCMC, applying Richardson-Romberg extrapolation to reduce bias in SG-MCMC.  
- Method runs two chains in parallel without inter-chain communication.  
- Theoretical analysis establishes asymptotic consistency.  
- Empirical results support the method’s effectiveness.  
- Approach is essentially a straightforward combination of Richardson-Romberg extrapolation and SGLD.  
- Claims an improved convergence rate, theoretically validating efficiency.  
- Improved rate could also be achieved using high-order integrators.  
- No experimental comparison provided with high-order integrators.  
- Inconsistencies exist in the paper’s statements about the step size.
