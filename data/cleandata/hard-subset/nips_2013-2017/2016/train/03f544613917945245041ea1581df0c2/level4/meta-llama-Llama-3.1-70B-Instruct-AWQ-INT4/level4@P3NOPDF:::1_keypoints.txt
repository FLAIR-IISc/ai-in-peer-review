- Applies Richardson–Romberg extrapolation (RRE) to big-data MCMC, focusing on SGLD.
- Provides a useful link between the MCMC and SDE literatures.
- Introduces a new SGLD-RRE algorithm with theoretical convergence results.
- Gives arguments/conditions for when SGLD-RRE outperforms vanilla SGLD.
- Empirical results: strong gains on toy problems and modest but consistent improvements on large-scale tasks.
- Paper is clearly written, technically sound, and presents an accessible, computationally convenient method.
- Method is practically useful for practitioners, though not revolutionary.
- Presentation issue: Figure 1a is misleading—it shows a Gaussian with empirical mean/variance, not the empirical posterior; clarify this.
- Presentation issue: Figure 2a needs explanation for why bias does not necessarily decrease as step size decreases.
- Recommendation: valuable contribution that would benefit from minor revisions to the presentation.
