- Applies Richardson-Romberg extrapolation (RRE) to big-data MCMC, focusing on SGLD.  
- Builds a useful bridge between MCMC and SDE literatures.  
- Introduces the SGLD-RRE method.  
- Provides theoretical convergence results and conditions when SGLD-RRE outperforms vanilla SGLD.  
- Presents numerical experiments showing striking gains on toy problems and modest, consistent improvements on large-scale tasks.  
- Paper is solid, clearly written, and appears technically sound.  
- Method is essentially a straightforward, computationally convenient application of RRE.  
- Likely to be adopted by practitioners but not expected to transform current practice.  
- Minor concern: Fig. 1a is misleading—plotted Gaussian uses empirical mean/variance, not the empirical posterior, and should be clarified.  
- Minor concern: Fig. 2a needs discussion explaining why bias does not necessarily decrease as stepsize decreases.
