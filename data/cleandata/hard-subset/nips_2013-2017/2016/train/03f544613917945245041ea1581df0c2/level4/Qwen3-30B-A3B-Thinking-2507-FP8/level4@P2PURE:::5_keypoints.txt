- Proposal: Introduces SG-RR-MCMC, applying Richardson-Romberg extrapolation to reduce bias in stochastic-gradient MCMC.  
- Implementation: Runs two chains embarrassingly parallel with no inter-chain communication.  
- Theory: Provides theoretical analysis showing asymptotic consistency and an improved convergence rate.  
- Empirical: Experimental results are satisfactory and support the method’s effectiveness.  
- Novelty: Method is essentially a straightforward combination of Richardson-Romberg extrapolation and SGLD.  
- Missing comparison: Authors omit experimental comparison to high-order integrators, which could achieve similar efficiency gains.  
- Inconsistency: Paper contains an inconsistent statement regarding the step size.  
- Supplement mismatch: Algorithm 1 in the supplement is inconsistent with Equation (5).
