- Applies Richardson–Romberg extrapolation (RRE) to big-data MCMC, specifically SGLD, bridging MCMC and SDE literatures.
- Proposes an SGLD-RRE algorithm that is computationally convenient as a straightforward RRE application to SGLD.
- Establishes convergence results and identifies conditions where SGLD-RRE outperforms vanilla SGLD.
- Empirical evaluation shows large gains on toy problems and modest but consistent improvements on large-scale tasks.
- Paper is clear, technically sound, and well written.
- Likely useful to practitioners but unlikely to be transformative for current practice.
- Minor: Figure 1a is misleading—plots a Gaussian with empirical mean/variance, not the empirically estimated posterior; clarify.
- Minor: Figure 2a needs discussion on why bias may not decrease with smaller step size, as this can be counterintuitive.
