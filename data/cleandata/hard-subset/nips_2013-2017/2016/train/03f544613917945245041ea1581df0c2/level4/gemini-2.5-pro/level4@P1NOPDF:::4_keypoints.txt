- Proposal: apply Richardson–Romberg extrapolation to SG-MCMC to improve bias and MSE convergence rates.  
- Strength: method is simple and requires minimal changes to standard SG-MCMC implementations.  
- Theory: solid theoretical results claiming improved convergence rates.  

Major concerns:
- Theorems-to-rates gap: derivation from the general theorems to the specific final bias/MSE rates is unclear and needs a detailed step-by-step derivation or appendix.  
- Experimental fairness: matrix factorization experiments run two parallel chains, causing ~3× computational cost per epoch versus single-chain SGLD, but plots compare per-iteration rather than wall-clock, biasing the comparison. Authors should report/adjust for equal computational effort.
