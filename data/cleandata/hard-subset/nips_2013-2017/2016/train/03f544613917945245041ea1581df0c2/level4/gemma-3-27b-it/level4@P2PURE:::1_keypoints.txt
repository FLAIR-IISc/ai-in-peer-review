- Proposes applying Richardson-Romberg extrapolation (RRE) to Stochastic Gradient Langevin Dynamics (SGLD), yielding the new SGLD-RRE method.  
- Bridges MCMC and SDE literatures, offering valuable cross-disciplinary connections.  
- Provides theoretical analysis showing convergence properties and advantages over standard SGLD.  
- Empirical results: substantial improvements on toy problems and modest but consistent gains on large-scale tasks.  
- Paper is clear, technically sound, and easy to follow with insightful theoretical contributions.  
- The proposed method is computationally efficient and likely useful to practitioners, though not paradigm-shifting.  
- Minor issue: Figure 1a is potentially misleading—plots a Gaussian parameterized by empirical mean/variance rather than the empirical posterior distribution; needs clarification.  
- Minor issue: Figure 2a requires discussion of why bias does not consistently decrease with decreasing stepsize.
