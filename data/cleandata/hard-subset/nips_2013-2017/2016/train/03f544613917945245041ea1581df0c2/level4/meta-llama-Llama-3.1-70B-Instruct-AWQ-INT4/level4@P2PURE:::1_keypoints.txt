- Applies Richardson-Romberg extrapolation (RRE) to big-data MCMC, focusing on SGLD and bridging MCMC and SDE literatures.  
- Proposes a new SGLD-RRE method and derives theoretical convergence results.  
- Provides arguments identifying regimes where SGLD-RRE outperforms vanilla SGLD.  
- Empirical results: large improvements on toy problems and modest but consistent gains on large-scale examples.  
- Paper is clear, well-written, technically sound, computationally convenient, and likely useful to practitioners (though not revolutionary).  
- Minor figure issues: Fig.1a should clarify it shows a Gaussian with empirical posterior mean/variance rather than the empirical posterior distribution; Fig.2a should discuss why bias may not decrease with stepsize.
