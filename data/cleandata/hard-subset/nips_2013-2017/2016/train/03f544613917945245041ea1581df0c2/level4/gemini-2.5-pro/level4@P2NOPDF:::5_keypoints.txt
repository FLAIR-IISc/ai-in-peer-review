- Introduces SG-RR-MCMC, using Richardson-Romberg extrapolation to reduce bias in SG-MCMC.  
- Implements the method with two independent, embarrassingly parallel MCMC chains.  
- Provides theoretical analysis proving asymptotic consistency of the estimator.  
- Empirical results are compelling and demonstrate practical effectiveness.  
- Novel and interesting idea to leverage RR extrapolation for bias reduction.  
- Lacks experimental comparison with high-order SDE integrators to assess practical trade-offs.  
- Missing non-asymptotic convergence rate analysis to justify efficiency over standard SGLD.  
- Inconsistency in the manuscript’s description of the step-size schedule needs clarification.  
- Discrepancy between Equation (5) and Algorithm 1 in the supplementary material must be corrected.  
- Overall promising contribution that needs better positioning against existing techniques and fixes to clarity/inconsistency issues.
