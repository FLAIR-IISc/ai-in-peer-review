This paper proposes a novel sampling algorithm, Stochastic Gradient Richardson-Romberg Langevin Dynamics (SGRRLD), which aims to reduce the bias of Stochastic Gradient Markov Chain Monte Carlo (SG-MCMC) methods while maintaining a reasonable level of variance. The authors apply Richardson-Romberg extrapolation (RRE) to SG-MCMC, specifically to Stochastic Gradient Langevin Dynamics (SGLD), to develop SGRRLD. The paper provides a clear link between the MCMC literature and the SDE literature, which may be beneficial for both communities.

The authors present a thorough analysis of SGRRLD, including its asymptotic consistency, central limit theorem, and non-asymptotic bias and mean squared error bounds. They demonstrate that SGRRLD achieves higher rates of convergence than SGLD and other SG-MCMC methods with first-order integrators in both finite-time and asymptotic settings.

The paper is well-organized, and the writing is clear and concise. The authors provide a detailed explanation of the proposed algorithm, its theoretical analysis, and experimental results on both synthetic and real data. The experiments demonstrate the effectiveness of SGRRLD in reducing bias and improving convergence rates compared to SGLD.

The paper's strengths include its novel application of RRE to SG-MCMC, its thorough theoretical analysis, and its convincing experimental results. The authors also provide a clear discussion of the advantages and limitations of SGRRLD, including its potential for parallelization and its dependence on the choice of step size.

One minor comment is that Figure 1a could be clarified to indicate that it represents a Gaussian distribution with empirical posterior mean and variance, rather than the empirical estimated posterior distribution. Additionally, Figure 2a could benefit from a brief discussion on why the bias does not necessarily decrease as the step size decreases, as this may be counterintuitive for some readers.

Overall, this is a solid paper that makes a valuable contribution to the field of MCMC methods. The proposed algorithm, SGRRLD, has the potential to improve the efficiency and accuracy of Bayesian inference in large-scale applications.
