**Summary of Main Ideas**

The paper proposes a novel Stochastic Gradient Markov Chain Monte Carlo (SG-MCMC) algorithm, called Stochastic Gradient Richardson-Romberg Langevin Dynamics (SGRRLD). The algorithm aims to reduce the bias of existing SG-MCMC methods, such as Stochastic Gradient Langevin Dynamics (SGLD), by applying the Richardson-Romberg (RR) extrapolation method. The RR extrapolation involves running two SGLD chains with different step sizes in parallel and extrapolating their estimates to construct a new estimator. The authors provide formal theoretical analysis, including a central limit theorem and bounds for the non-asymptotic bias and mean squared error of the SGRRLD estimator. They also demonstrate the effectiveness of SGRRLD through experiments on synthetic and real data.
