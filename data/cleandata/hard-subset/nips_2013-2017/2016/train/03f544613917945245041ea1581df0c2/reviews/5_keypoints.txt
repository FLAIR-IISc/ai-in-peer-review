- Proposes SG-RR-MCMC, using Richardson-Romberg extrapolation to reduce SG-MCMC bias.  
- Method runs two chains embarrassingly parallel with no communication.  
- Theoretical analysis shows SG-RR-MCMC is asymptotically consistent.  
- Empirical results are satisfactory and show the idea is effective.  
- Conceptually a simple combination of Richardson-Romberg extrapolation and SGLD.  
- Efficiency could be validated by proving an improved convergence rate.  
- Similar improved rates could be obtained using high-order integrators, but no experimental comparison was provided.  
- There is an inconsistent statement about the step size in the paper.  
- Algorithm 1 in the supplement is inconsistent with Equation (5).
