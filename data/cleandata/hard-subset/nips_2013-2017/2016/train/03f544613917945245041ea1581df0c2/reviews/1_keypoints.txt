- Applies Richardson-Romberg extrapolation (RRE) to big-data MCMC, focusing on SGLD.
- Builds a bridge between MCMC and SDE literatures, potentially benefiting both communities.
- Proposes a new SGLD-RRE method with theoretical convergence results and conditions when it outperforms vanilla SGLD.
- Empirical results: large improvements on toy examples and modest but consistent gains on large-scale problems.
- Paper is solid, clearly written, and appears technically sound.
- Method is a straightforward, computationally convenient application of RRE to SGLD and likely useful in practice, though not transformative.
- Minor: Fig. 1a is misleading—plot shows a Gaussian with empirical mean/variance plugged in, not the empirical posterior; clarify this.
- Minor: Fig. 2a needs discussion on why bias may not decrease as stepsize decreases, which is counter-intuitive.
