- Proposes S-WMD, a supervised extension of Word Mover’s Distance that incorporates label information for document distance.
- Situates S-WMD as building on WMD/EMD and drawing inspiration from Neighborhood Components Analysis (NCA).
- Claims are technically sound and supported by theoretical analysis and experiments.
- Paper is well-written, clear, and well-organized.
- Presents a novel supervised document distance metric with demonstrated classification effectiveness.
- Potentially significant for NLP tasks like text retrieval, sentiment analysis, and topic modeling.
- Arguments for acceptance: novel method, strong empirical improvements, and solid theoretical backing.
- Arguments against acceptance: heavy reliance on prior WMD work and possible perception as incremental.
- Revision request: provide detailed computational complexity analysis and comparisons to other methods.
- Revision request: include more experimental results across diverse datasets and tasks to show robustness.
- Revision request: expand discussion of limitations and potential real-world applications.
