This paper proposes a novel approach to learning a supervised document distance metric, called Supervised Word Mover's Distance (S-WMD). The authors build upon the existing Word Mover's Distance (WMD) metric, which has shown state-of-the-art results in k-nearest neighbor (kNN) document classification. However, WMD is unsupervised and does not incorporate supervision when available. The proposed S-WMD method addresses this limitation by learning a linear transformation of the word embedding space and a word-importance weight vector to improve the WMD distance.

The paper is well-written, and the authors provide a clear and concise introduction to the background and related work. The proposed method is thoroughly explained, and the mathematical derivations are sound. The experimental evaluation is comprehensive, with comparisons to 26 competitive baselines on eight real-world text classification tasks.
