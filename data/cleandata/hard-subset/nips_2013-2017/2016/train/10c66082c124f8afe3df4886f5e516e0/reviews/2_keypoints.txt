- Proposes a supervised extension to the Word Mover's Distance that learns weights on the distance.
- Paper includes a very extensive experimental evaluation.
- Reviewer pleased someone applied discriminative training to learn a domain-specific metric from WMD.
- Method is sound and clearly explained with appropriate detail.
- Experiments cover several classification datasets and a wide range of baselines and features.
- The proposed method outperforms all baselines by a large margin.
- Reviewer considers it a solid paper suitable for presentation at NIPS.
