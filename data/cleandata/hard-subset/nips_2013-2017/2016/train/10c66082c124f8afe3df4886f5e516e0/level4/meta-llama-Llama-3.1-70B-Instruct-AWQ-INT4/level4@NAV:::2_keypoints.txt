- Novel extension of the Word Mover's Distance that uses supervision to learn weighted distances.  
- Incorporates discriminative training to develop a domain-specific metric.  
- Methodology is robust, clearly articulated, and presented with sufficient implementation detail.  
- Exhaustive and thorough experimental evaluation noted as a major strength.  
- Empirical assessment covers multiple classification datasets and a wide range of baselines and features.  
- The proposed approach achieves substantial performance gains over existing methods.  
- Overall, a well-crafted paper suitable for inclusion in the NIPS program.
