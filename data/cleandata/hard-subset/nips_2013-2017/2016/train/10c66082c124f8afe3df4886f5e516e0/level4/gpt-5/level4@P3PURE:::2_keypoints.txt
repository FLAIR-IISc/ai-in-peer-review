- Proposes a supervised extension of Word Mover’s Distance that learns weights on the distance metric.  
- Method is technically sound and clearly explained with appropriate detail.  
- Advances WMD via discriminative training to produce a domain-specific metric.  
- Experimental evaluation is extensive, covering multiple classification datasets and many baselines and feature representations.  
- Proposed method outperforms all baselines by a large margin.  
- Overall judgment: solid paper suitable for presentation at NIPS.
