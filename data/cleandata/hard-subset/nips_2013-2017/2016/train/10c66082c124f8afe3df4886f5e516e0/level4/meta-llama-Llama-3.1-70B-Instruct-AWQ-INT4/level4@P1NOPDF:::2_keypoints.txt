- Presents a supervised extension to the word mover’s distance (WMD) that learns domain-specific weights.  
- Uses discriminative training to adapt the distance metric for tasks.  
- Provides an exhaustive and notable experimental evaluation.  
- Builds appropriately on WMD; incorporating supervision is a sound methodological choice.  
- Balanced presentation with sufficient detail for understanding.  
- Experiments are comprehensive, covering multiple datasets, diverse baselines, and feature sets.  
- Results show the proposed method significantly outperforms alternatives by substantial margins.  
- Overall a solid contribution suitable for inclusion in the NIPS program.
