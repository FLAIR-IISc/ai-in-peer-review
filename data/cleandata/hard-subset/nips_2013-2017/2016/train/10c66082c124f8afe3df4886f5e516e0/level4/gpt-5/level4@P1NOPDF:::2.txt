This paper presents a supervised extension of the word mover’s distance that learns weights on the underlying distance metric. I am pleased to see the original WMD approach advanced through discriminative training to obtain a domain-specific metric.

The method appears sound and is clearly described with the right level of technical detail. The experimental evaluation is thorough: it spans multiple classification datasets, compares against a wide range of baselines and feature representations, and consistently demonstrates strong performance. Across the board, the proposed approach outperforms all competing methods by a large margin.

Overall, this is a solid contribution that advances metric learning for text representations in a principled and practical way. I would be very happy to see it presented at NIPS.
