- Proposes a supervised extension of the Word Mover's Distance using learned weights to improve the distance metric.
- Leverages discriminative training to create a domain-specific metric built on the WMD framework.
- Methodology is well-founded, clearly explained, and described with sufficient detail.
- Provides a comprehensive experimental evaluation showing the approach’s effectiveness.
- Experiments cover multiple classification datasets and a wide range of baselines and features.
- Proposed method significantly outperforms baselines; paper is robust and merits presentation at NIPS.
