- Proposes supervised refinement of Word Mover’s Distance by learning a linear transformation A and reweighting w of word distributions.  
- Paper is well-written with clear explanations and a thorough, well-structured related work section.  
- Experimental evaluation is comprehensive and includes several previously unexplored datasets.  
- Motivation for reweighting w is unclear; need justification and safeguards (constraints/regularization) to avoid altering document semantics.  
- Contribution is incremental: a reasonable extension (NCA-like objective for A and w) but lacks a novel conceptual breakthrough.  
- Unclear how learned reweighting generalizes to unseen documents; must specify inference strategy or acknowledge need for retraining.  
- The term “semantic difference” is ambiguous and should be clarified or replaced with a more precise descriptor.  
- Results are encouraging and the paper could be acceptable if authors address motivation, significance, generalization, and terminology concerns.
