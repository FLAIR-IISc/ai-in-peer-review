This paper introduces a supervised extension to the Word Mover's Distance, which learns a weighted, domain-specific distance metric. It is excellent to see the Word Mover's Distance framework being advanced with discriminative training, and the authors have executed this idea very effectively.

The proposed method is sound and exceptionally well-explained, presenting the core concepts with clarity and just the right amount of detail. A key strength of the paper is its comprehensive experimental evaluation. The authors test their method on several classification datasets against a wide variety of baseline methods and features. The results are compelling, demonstrating that the proposed approach outperforms all competitors by a significant margin.

This is a solid, well-written paper that makes a valuable contribution. I would be very pleased to see it presented at NIPS and strongly recommend it for acceptance.
