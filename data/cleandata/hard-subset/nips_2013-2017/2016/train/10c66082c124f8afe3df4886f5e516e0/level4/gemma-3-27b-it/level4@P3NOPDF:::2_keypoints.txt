- Proposes a supervised extension of Word Mover’s Distance that learns task-specific weights to refine the distance metric.  
- Addresses domain adaptation for WMD through discriminative training—a significant and useful contribution.  
- Paper is exceptionally well-written, with clear motivation and a digestible mathematical formulation.  
- Methodology balances necessary background and focus on novel components.  
- Experimental evaluation is thorough across diverse text classification datasets.  
- Demonstrates consistently strong and often substantial performance gains over baselines.  
- Compares against a comprehensive set of baselines, including sophisticated techniques.  
- Includes ablation studies that isolate the impact of different components.  
- Overall assessment: high-quality, convincing work recommended for acceptance to NIPS.
