This paper presents a novel extension of the word mover's distance, leveraging supervision to learn weighted distances. The authors have conducted an exhaustive experimental evaluation, which is a notable strength of the manuscript. It is encouraging to see the word mover's distance approach being built upon, incorporating discriminative training to develop a domain-specific metric. The methodology is robust, clearly articulated, and effectively conveyed with sufficient detail. The empirical assessment is comprehensive, spanning multiple classification datasets and a diverse range of baseline methods and features. The proposed approach demonstrates substantial performance gains over existing methods. Overall, this is a well-crafted paper that would be a valuable addition to the NIPS program.
