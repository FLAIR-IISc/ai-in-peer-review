- Proposes a supervised extension of Word Mover's Distance (WMD) that learns discriminative distance weights for domain-specific tasks.  
- Addresses a meaningful gap by moving beyond unsupervised WMD to optimize metrics for classification.  
- Conceptually sound and technically well-executed.  
- Method is clearly described with enough detail for reproducibility.  
- Experimental evaluation is thorough: multiple datasets, diverse baselines, and varied feature representations.  
- Results show the supervised WMD consistently and substantially outperforms all baselines.  
- Theoretical grounding plus extensive validation; avoids overclaiming and clearly demonstrates benefits for text classification.  
- Reviewer recommends acceptance and presentation at NIPS due to relevance and strong contribution.
