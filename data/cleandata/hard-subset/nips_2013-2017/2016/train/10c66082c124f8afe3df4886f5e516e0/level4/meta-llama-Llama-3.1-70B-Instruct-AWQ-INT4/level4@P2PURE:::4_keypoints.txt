- Proposes a supervised extension of Word Mover’s Distance using a linear transformation A and a reweighted word distribution w.  
- Paper is well-written, with a thorough related-work review and experiments on several novel datasets.  
- Motivation for the specific formulation is weak, especially the introduction of the reweighting vector w.  
- Reweighting w may alter a document’s meaning; authors must justify its benefits while preserving meaning.  
- Contribution is limited: using a linear A and reweighting w with an NCA objective is a straightforward, not novel, extension.  
- It’s unclear how to compute the reweighting w for a new, unseen document at test time.  
- The term “semantic difference” is ambiguous (seems to map to label); authors should clarify its intended meaning.  
- Reviewer would not object to acceptance if the authors adequately address these questions and concerns.
