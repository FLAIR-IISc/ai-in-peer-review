This paper presents an extension of the Word Mover’s Distance that incorporates supervision to learn weights on the distance metric. The method is technically sound and well explained, with just the right amount of detail. I am pleased to see the WMD approach advanced through discriminative training to learn a domain-specific metric. The experimental evaluation is very extensive, covering several classification datasets and a wide variety of baseline methods and feature representations. Across these comparisons, the proposed method outperforms all baselines by a large margin. Overall, this is a solid paper that I would be very happy to see presented at NIPS.
