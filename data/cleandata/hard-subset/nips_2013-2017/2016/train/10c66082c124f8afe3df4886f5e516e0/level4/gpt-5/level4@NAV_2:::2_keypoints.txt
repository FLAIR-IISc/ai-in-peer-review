- Introduces a supervised extension of Word Mover’s Distance to learn task-specific weights.
- Empirical study is comprehensive.
- Advances WMD via discriminative training to obtain a domain-tailored metric.
- Method is technically sound and clearly described with sufficient detail.
- Experiments cover multiple classification datasets and a broad array of baselines and feature representations.
- Proposed method outperforms all baselines by a large margin.
- Overall a strong paper and recommended for presentation at NIPS.
