This paper proposes a new method for metric learning for text documents, based on previous work called "Word Movers Distance." The original method is unsupervised, measuring the distance between word embeddings. This paper extends it to a supervised setting, where the supervision is of the type 'these documents belong to the same class'. A code (in MATLAB) is promised to be provided.
