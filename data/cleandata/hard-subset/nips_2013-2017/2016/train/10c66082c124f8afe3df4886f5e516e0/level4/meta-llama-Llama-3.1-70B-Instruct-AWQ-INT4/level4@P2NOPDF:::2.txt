This paper presents a supervised extension to the word mover's distance, leveraging discriminative training to learn domain-specific weights on the distance metric. The authors provide a thorough experimental evaluation, demonstrating the effectiveness of their approach. It is encouraging to see the word mover's distance method being built upon, and the incorporation of supervision is a natural and intuitive extension. The proposed method is well-motivated, clearly explained, and effectively validated through extensive experiments on multiple classification datasets and a range of baseline methods and features. The results show that the suggested approach outperforms existing methods by a significant margin, making this a solid contribution to the field. Overall, I strongly support the acceptance of this paper for presentation at NIPS.
