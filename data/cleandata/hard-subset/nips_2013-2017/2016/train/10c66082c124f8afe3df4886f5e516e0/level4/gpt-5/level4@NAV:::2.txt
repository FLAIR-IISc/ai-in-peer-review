A supervised extension of word mover’s distance that learns weights on the distance. The paper presents a very comprehensive experimental evaluation. I am pleased to see the WMD approach leveraged with discriminative training to learn a domain-specific metric. The method is sound, clearly explained, and provides just the right amount of detail. The experiments are extensive across multiple classification datasets and a wide variety of baseline methods and features. The proposed method outperforms all by a large margin. This is a strong paper that I would very much like to see presented at NIPS.
