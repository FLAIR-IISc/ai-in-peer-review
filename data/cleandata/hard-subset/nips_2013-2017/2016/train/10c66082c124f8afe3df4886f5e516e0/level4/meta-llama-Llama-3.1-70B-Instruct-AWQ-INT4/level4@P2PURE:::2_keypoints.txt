- Proposes a supervised extension to the Word Mover's Distance (WMD).  
- Uses discriminative training to learn domain-specific weights for the distance metric.  
- Provides a thorough and welcome experimental evaluation.  
- Incorporation of supervision is a natural and effective extension to WMD.  
- Methodology is well-explained, balancing detail and concision.  
- Experiments are comprehensive, covering multiple classification datasets and diverse baselines/features.  
- Proposed method yields significant performance improvements, outperforming all baselines by a substantial margin.  
- Overall, a solid contribution likely suitable for acceptance to NIPS.
