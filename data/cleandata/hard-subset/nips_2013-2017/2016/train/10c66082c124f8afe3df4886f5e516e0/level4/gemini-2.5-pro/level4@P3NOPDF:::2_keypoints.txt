- Proposes a supervised extension to Word Mover’s Distance by introducing learnable, word-specific weights to emphasize task-discriminative words.
- Method is methodologically sound, elegant, well-motivated, and clearly explained.
- Novel contribution: effectively combines semantic richness of pre-trained embeddings with task-specific discriminative training.
- Experimental evaluation is extensive and convincing across standard benchmarks, outperforming a wide range of strong baselines.
- Suggestion: include discussion of scalability and training time relative to baselines due to WMD’s computational cost.
- Suggestion: add qualitative analysis or visualizations of learned word weights to improve interpretability.
- Conclusion: solid, significant paper well-suited for NIPS; strong recommendation for acceptance.
