- Proposal: Introduces Supervised Word Mover's Distance (S-WMD), a supervised extension of Word Mover's Distance to learn a domain-specific metric.  
- Motivation & clarity: Method is well-motivated and clearly explained.  
- Experimental evaluation: Extensive experiments on multiple classification datasets with many baselines.  
- Empirical results: S-WMD significantly outperforms all compared methods, showing the benefit of discriminative training.  
- Technical soundness: Algorithm, optimization procedure, and approximation techniques are sound and well-presented.  
- Related work: Good discussion and clear distinctions from other metric learning approaches.  
- Weakness: Limited in-depth analysis of learned weights and their relation to underlying word embeddings (only some visualizations).  
- Overall judgment: Solid contribution to NLP and compelling; recommended for presentation at NIPS.  
- Strengths summarized: Motivated extension, clear exposition, extensive experiments, and sound technical methods.  
- Weakness summarized: Insufficient analysis of learned weights.  
- Recommendation: Accept.
