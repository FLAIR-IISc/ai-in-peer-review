- Proposes a supervised extension of Word Mover’s Distance that learns weights on the underlying distance metric.  
- Advances the original WMD via discriminative training to produce a domain-specific metric.  
- Method appears sound and is clearly described with appropriate technical detail.  
- Experimental evaluation is thorough, covering multiple classification datasets and a wide range of baselines and feature representations.  
- The proposed approach consistently achieves strong performance, outperforming all competing methods by a large margin.  
- Overall, a solid, principled, and practical contribution to metric learning for text representations.  
- Reviewer recommends acceptance and would be happy to see it presented at NIPS.
