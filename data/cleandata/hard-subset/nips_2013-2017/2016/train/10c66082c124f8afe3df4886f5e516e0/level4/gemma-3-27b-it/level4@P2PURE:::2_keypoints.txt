Introduces a supervised extension to Word Mover’s Distance that learns weighted distances via discriminative training.  
Provides a valuable, domain-specific metric improving on standard WMD.  
Method is technically sound and clearly explained with appropriate detail.  
Experimental evaluation is exceptionally thorough, covering multiple datasets and comprehensive baselines and feature representations.  
Proposed approach consistently and significantly outperforms the baselines.  
Strong paper; enthusiastically recommended for presentation at NIPS.
