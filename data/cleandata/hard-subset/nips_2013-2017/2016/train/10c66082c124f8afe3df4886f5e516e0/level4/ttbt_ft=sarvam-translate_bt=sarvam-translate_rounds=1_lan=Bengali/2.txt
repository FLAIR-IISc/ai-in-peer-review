There's an extension of the term "mover's distance" that uses supervised learning to learn the weight on distance. This paper contains a very detailed experimental evaluation. I'm glad to see that someone has adopted the method of "mover's distance" and used discriminative training to learn a domain-specific metric. The method is correct, well-explained, and includes accurate information. The tests are very extensive across various classification data sets and various baseline methods and features. The proposed method has surpassed all other methods by a significant margin. It's a strong paper that I would like to see presented at NIPS.
