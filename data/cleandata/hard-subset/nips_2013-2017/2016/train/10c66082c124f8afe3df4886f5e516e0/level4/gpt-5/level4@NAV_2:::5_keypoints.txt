- Extends Word Mover’s Distance for supervised text categorization by learning word-specific importance weights.
- Uses Sinkhorn-regularized optimal transport as an efficient proxy for the true Wasserstein computation.
- Empirically evaluated on eight datasets against 26 baselines, achieving the best average performance.
- Manuscript is very well written, with clear motivation and thorough mathematical analysis.
- Possible limited novelty relative to the original WMD paper.
- Discussion of the Word Centroid Distance is tangential and potentially confusing.
- Treatment of the Sinkhorn regularization parameter λ is underdeveloped; needs clarification whether chosen λ yields behavior closer to true Wasserstein or the entropic (Sinkhorn) regime.
