- Extends WMD with learned linear projection and word-specific reweighting, showing strong kNN text classification performance.
- Integrates linear metric learning and word reweighting into the transport framework in a well-motivated, readable paper.
- Introduces an efficient optimization procedure for the parameterized transport metric.
- Limited impact: earth mover’s distance with metric learning on word-centroid initialization performs nearly as well as the full learned distance.
- Table 3 omits comparisons of centroid-based document embeddings paired with standard metric-learning methods (ITML, LMNN, NCA), leaving unclear the benefit of transport vs centroid representations.
- Lacks an ablation study isolating contributions of word-specific weights vs projection matrix A (report results with only weights or only A learned).
- Originality mainly in the optimization strategy; linear metric learning within EMD is a modest incremental contribution without stronger empirical separation.
- Minor clarity issues: ambiguous reference “The authors” (which papers?), Equation (11) likely needs α and β starred, and explicitly remind reader that the procedure around Line 170 is Sinkhorn scaling.
