- Proposes an extension to Word Mover's Distance that incorporates supervision to learn a weighted distance metric.
- Builds on the WMD framework using discriminative training to learn a domain-specific metric.
- Method is technically sound and clearly and concisely explained.
- Provides a comprehensive experimental evaluation across several classification datasets.
- Compares against a wide variety of baselines and feature sets.
- Results show the proposed method outperforms all competing approaches by a significant margin.
- Strong recommendation for presentation at NIPS; overall assessment: solid paper.
