- Paper introduces a supervised adaptation of Word Mover’s Distance for document metric learning; Matlab code release is welcome.  
- Method learns a linear transformation A and word importance weights w; the intuition behind Eq. (5) (application of w) is unclear.  
- Need clarity on which component (A or w) primarily drives the reported improvements.  
- Experiments evaluate classification using the learned distance; compare to a simple classifier on the same datasets for additional insight.  
- Eq. (2) and the role of lambda require clarification; usage appears inconsistent with line 170.  
- Claim that the method tolerates syntactically different but conceptually similar words (line 316) lacks a clear rationale.  
- Reference to “average error across datasets on the right” (line 257) is missing—possibly intended for Table 3.  
- Table 2 uses “n” and “ne” without clearly distinguishing them.  
- Definition of k for kNN classification and whether k varies by dataset or method is not specified.  
- Reviewer appreciated the authors’ thorough responses to inquiries.
