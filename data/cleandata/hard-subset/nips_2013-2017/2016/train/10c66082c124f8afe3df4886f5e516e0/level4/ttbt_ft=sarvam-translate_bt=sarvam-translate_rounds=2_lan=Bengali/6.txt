This paper proposes a new method of metric learning for text documents, based on previous work known as "word movers distance." The original method is unsupervised, measuring the distance between the embedding of words. This paper extends this method to a supervised setting, where the supervision is 'these documents belong to the same class'. The code (MATLAB) is promised to be made available.
