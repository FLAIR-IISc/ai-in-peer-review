This paper presents a significant extension to the word mover's distance, leveraging supervision to learn weights on the distance. The authors have conducted a comprehensive experimental evaluation, which is a notable strength of the paper.

I am pleased to see that the approach of word mover's distance has been built upon, incorporating discriminative training to learn a domain-specific metric. The proposed method is sound, clearly explained, and effectively conveyed with the right amount of information. The experimental evaluation is extensive, covering several classification datasets and a wide range of baseline methods and features. The results demonstrate that the suggested method outperforms all others by a substantial margin.

Overall, this is a solid paper that I strongly believe deserves to be presented at NIPS. The authors' contributions to the field of natural language processing are valuable, and their work has the potential to make a meaningful impact.
