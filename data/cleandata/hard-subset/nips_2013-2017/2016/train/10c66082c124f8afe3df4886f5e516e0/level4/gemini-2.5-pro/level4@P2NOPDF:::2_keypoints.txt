- Proposes a supervised extension of Word Mover's Distance for document classification.  
- Core contribution: learns a linear transformation of the word embedding space to form a task-specific distance metric.  
- Transformation is trained discriminatively to re-weight embedding dimensions and emphasize task-relevant features.  
- Approach is well-motivated, technically sound, and builds logically on the WMD framework.  
- Methodology is presented very clearly with a good balance of theoretical detail and intuition.  
- Experimental evaluation is comprehensive and rigorous, using multiple benchmarks and many strong baselines.  
- Results are compelling: the proposed method consistently outperforms competing approaches by a large margin.  
- Strong recommendation for acceptance; significant contribution to text classification and metric learning.
