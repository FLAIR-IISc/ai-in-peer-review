- Paper proposes a supervised extension of Word Mover’s Distance for documents, learning a linear transform A and per-word importance weights w; code promised in Matlab.
- Request clearer intuition and explanation for Eq (5) that defines how w is applied.
- Ask for an analysis separating the contributions of A and w to performance gains.
- Experimental evaluation is on document classification; recommend adding a simple classifier baseline trained on the same data for comparison.
- Eq (2) is unclear and the λ symbol seems inconsistent with the λ referenced at line 170—please clarify.
- The claim that the method is “forgiving when two documents use syntactically different but conceptually similar words” (line 316) needs justification or evidence.
- Line 257 refers to “average error across datasets” on the right, but that information cannot be found—clarify if it’s in Table 3 or elsewhere.
- In Table 2, clarify the difference between the columns/labels “n” and “ne.”
- The k parameter for k-nearest neighbors classification is not defined—state how k is chosen (dataset-dependent, method-dependent, or fixed).
