This paper presents an extension to Word Mover’s Distance (WMD) that incorporates supervision to learn weights within the distance calculation. The approach of leveraging discriminative training to learn a domain-specific metric building upon WMD is a valuable contribution, and I am pleased to see this direction being explored. The method is sound and clearly explained, providing an appropriate level of detail. The experimental evaluation is particularly comprehensive, covering several classification datasets and a diverse range of baseline methods and feature representations. Notably, the proposed method consistently outperforms all baselines by a substantial margin. Overall, this is a strong paper and I would enthusiastically support its presentation at NIPS.
