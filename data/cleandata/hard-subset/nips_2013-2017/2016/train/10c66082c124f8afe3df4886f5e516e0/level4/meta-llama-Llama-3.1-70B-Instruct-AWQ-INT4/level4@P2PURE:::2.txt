This paper presents a supervised extension to the word mover's distance, leveraging discriminative training to learn domain-specific weights on the distance metric. The authors provide a thorough experimental evaluation, which is a welcome addition to the field. It is encouraging to see the word mover's distance approach being built upon, and the incorporation of supervision is a natural and effective extension. The methodology is well-explained and clearly presented, striking a good balance between detail and concision. The experimental results are comprehensive, covering multiple classification datasets and a diverse range of baseline methods and features. The proposed method demonstrates a significant performance improvement over existing approaches, outperforming all baselines by a substantial margin. Overall, this is a solid contribution that I believe would be a valuable addition to the NIPS program.
