- Uses discriminative training to obtain a domain-specific metric.  
- Conducts an extensive and comprehensive experimental evaluation.  
- Paper is well-written with clear explanations and appropriate detail.  
- Experiments cover multiple classification datasets and a wide range of baselines and features.  
- Method achieves significant performance gains, outperforming existing approaches by a substantial margin.  
- Overall assessment: solid contribution deserving presentation at NIPS.
