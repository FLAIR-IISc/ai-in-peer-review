This paper proposes an extension to the Word Mover's Distance that incorporates supervision to learn a weighted distance metric. It is commendable that the authors have built upon the Word Mover's Distance framework, using discriminative training to learn a domain-specific metric. The method is technically sound and is explained clearly and concisely, providing the right level of detail.

The paper features a comprehensive experimental evaluation across several classification datasets, comparing the proposed method against a wide variety of baselines and features. The results demonstrate that the proposed method outperforms all competing approaches by a significant margin. This is a solid paper, and I strongly recommend it for presentation at NIPS.
