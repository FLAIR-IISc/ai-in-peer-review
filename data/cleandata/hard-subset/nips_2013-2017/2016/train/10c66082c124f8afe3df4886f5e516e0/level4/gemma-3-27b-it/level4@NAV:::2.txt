This paper presents a supervised extension to Word Mover’s Distance, learning weighted distances through a training process. The work is notable for its comprehensive experimental validation. It’s encouraging to see the Word Mover’s Distance framework adopted and combined with discriminative training for domain-specific metric learning. The methodology is clearly presented and appropriately detailed. The experimental evaluation is exceptionally thorough, covering multiple classification datasets and a diverse set of baseline approaches and feature representations. The proposed method demonstrates significant performance improvements over all baselines. Overall, this is a strong contribution and I recommend it for presentation at NIPS.
