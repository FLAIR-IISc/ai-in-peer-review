- Proposes a supervised extension of Word Mover’s Distance that uses label information to learn task-specific weights for a discriminative, domain-specific metric.
- Methodology is sound and clearly described with appropriate technical detail.
- Experimental evaluation is comprehensive, covering several classification datasets.
- Comparison includes a wide range of baseline features and methods.
- The proposed method consistently outperforms baselines, often by a substantial margin.
- Results convincingly demonstrate the benefits of supervision for WMD.
- Overall a solid, well-executed contribution; recommended for acceptance (NIPS).
