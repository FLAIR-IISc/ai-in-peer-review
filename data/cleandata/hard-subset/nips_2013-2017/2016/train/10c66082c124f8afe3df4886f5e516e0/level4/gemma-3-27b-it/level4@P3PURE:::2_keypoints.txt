- Leveraging discriminative training to learn a domain-specific metric is a valuable contribution.
- Methodology is sound and clearly explained with appropriate detail.

- The proposed method consistently and substantially outperforms all baselines.
- Overall assessment: strong paper; reviewer enthusiastically supports presentation at NIPS.
