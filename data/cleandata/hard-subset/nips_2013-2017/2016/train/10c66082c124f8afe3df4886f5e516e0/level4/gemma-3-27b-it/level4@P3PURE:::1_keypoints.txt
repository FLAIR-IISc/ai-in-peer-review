- Proposes supervised metric learning extension of word-movers distance with learnable linear projection and per-word weighting, plus an efficient optimization method and thorough kNN-based experiments.  
- Technically interesting and useful extension; empirically effective on several text classification tasks.  
- Potential impact limited because EMD with metric learning initialized by the word-centroid distance performs comparably to the full proposed method (Table 3).  
- Core contributions may not substantially improve over the centroid-based initialization.  
- Missing evaluation: apply the word-centroid embedding with existing metric learning methods (ITML, LMNN, NCA) in Table 3.  
- Missing ablation: assess relative importance of word-specific weights versus projection matrix A by testing each component in isolation.  
- Originality primarily in the optimization method (Lines 156–175); linear metric learning within EMD is of limited novelty.  
- Writing is very clear and pleasant.  
- Minor clarifications requested: specify which “authors” on Line 152, superscript α and β with stars in Eq.11, and explicitly note around Line 170 that the scaling is the Sinkhorn scaling mentioned earlier.
