This paper proposes a supervised extension to the Word Mover's Distance that learns a weighted distance metric. The work is supported by a particularly thorough empirical validation. It is commendable to see the WMD framework advanced through discriminative training to learn a domain-specific metric. The proposed methodology is sound, articulated with exceptional clarity, and provides a fitting level of detail. The experiments are comprehensive, covering numerous classification datasets and a broad range of baseline methods and features. Crucially, the proposed approach outperforms all baselines by a significant margin. This is a high-quality contribution that I strongly recommend for presentation at NIPS.
