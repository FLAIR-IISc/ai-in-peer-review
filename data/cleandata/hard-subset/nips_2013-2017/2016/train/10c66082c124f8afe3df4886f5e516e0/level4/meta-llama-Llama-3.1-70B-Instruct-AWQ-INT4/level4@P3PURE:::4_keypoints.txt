- Proposes supervised extension of Word Mover's Distance by learning a linear transform A and a reweighted word distribution w.  
- Uses word2vec embeddings and optimizes A and w with a Neighborhood Components Analysis (NCA) objective.  
- Paper is well-written with a thorough related-work review and experiments on several novel datasets.  
- The reweighting w is poorly motivated and may alter document meaning; authors must justify how w improves separation without changing semantics.  
- The mathematical/formulation details need clearer explanation.  
- Contribution is incremental and not particularly novel or groundbreaking.  
- It is unclear how to obtain the reweighting w for a completely new (unseen) document.  
- The phrase “semantic difference” is ambiguous—unspecified whether it refers to labels, authorship, topics, etc.  
- Reviewer would not oppose acceptance if the authors adequately address these concerns.
