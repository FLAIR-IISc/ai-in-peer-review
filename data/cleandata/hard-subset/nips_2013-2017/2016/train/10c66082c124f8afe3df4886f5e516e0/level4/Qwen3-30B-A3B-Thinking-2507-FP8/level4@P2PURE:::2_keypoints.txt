Extends Word Mover's Distance by incorporating supervised learning to optimize weights for the distance metric.
Presents a comprehensive experimental evaluation across multiple classification datasets and a broad range of baseline methods and feature representations.
Leverages discriminative training to learn a domain-specific metric, representing a meaningful advancement over the original unsupervised WMD.
Methodology is sound, clearly articulated, and provides sufficient technical detail.
Proposed method consistently and significantly outperforms all competing baselines.
Strong contribution with thorough validation.
Reviewer strongly recommends acceptance for presentation at NIPS.
