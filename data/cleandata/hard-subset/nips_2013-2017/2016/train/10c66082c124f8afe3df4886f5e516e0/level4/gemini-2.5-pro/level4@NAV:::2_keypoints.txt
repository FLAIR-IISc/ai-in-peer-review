- Proposes a supervised extension to Word Mover's Distance that learns a weighted distance metric.  
- Advances WMD via discriminative training to obtain a domain-specific metric.  
- Methodology is sound and presented with exceptional clarity and sufficient detail.  
- Empirical validation is particularly thorough.  
- Experiments are comprehensive, covering many classification datasets and a broad range of baselines and features.  
- The proposed approach outperforms all baselines by a significant margin.  
- High-quality contribution, strongly recommended for presentation at NIPS.
