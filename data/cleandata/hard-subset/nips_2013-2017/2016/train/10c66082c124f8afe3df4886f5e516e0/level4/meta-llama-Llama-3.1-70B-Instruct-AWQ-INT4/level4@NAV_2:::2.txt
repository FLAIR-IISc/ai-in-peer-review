The main idea of the paper is to learn a supervised metric, which the authors call the Supervised Word Mover's Distance (S-WMD). This is achieved by transforming the word embedding and introducing a histogram importance vector to re-weight the word histogram values. The authors use a stochastic neighborhood relaxation to approximate the leave-one-out classification error and minimize the KL-divergence loss.
