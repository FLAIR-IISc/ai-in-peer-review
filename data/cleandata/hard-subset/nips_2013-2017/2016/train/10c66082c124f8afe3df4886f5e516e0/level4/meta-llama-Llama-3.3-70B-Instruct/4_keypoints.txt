- Proposes supervised document distance learning by modifying WMD with a linear transform A and word-reweighting w.  
- Paper is well-organized, easy to follow, and demonstrates strong grasp of related work.  
- Provides experimental results on several novel datasets.  
- Overall formulation motivation is unclear and the practical impact appears limited.  
- Reweighting w is not well-justified and may change the document's essence; needs explanation of how it improves separation without altering meaning.  
- Contribution is incremental: applying linear A and reweight w with an NCA objective is a straightforward extension lacking novelty.  
- Unclear how the method handles completely new documents—how to determine an appropriate reweight w for unseen instances.  
- The phrase "semantic difference" is ambiguous (labels vs. authorship/topic); clarify intended meaning.  
- Addressing these issues is essential to properly assess the paper’s value and could influence acceptance.
