- Proposes a supervised document distance built on WMD by learning a linear transform of embeddings (A) and per-word/document reweighting (w).
- Paper is clearly written, easy to follow, and contains a thorough related-work review.
- Experimental validation is provided across several novel datasets.
- Motivation for reweighting word distributions (w) is unclear and may distort document meaning; needs justification showing it preserves core topic while improving performance.
- Technical contribution is incremental: learning A and w with an NCA objective is a straightforward supervised extension of WMD.
- Practical application is unclear: how to determine the reweighting vector w for unseen documents is not explained.
- Terminology issue: the phrase “semantic difference” is ambiguous/confusing and appears to be used to mean class labels; needs clarification.
- Overall contribution is limited by unclear motivation, novelty concerns, and practical applicability; acceptance should require satisfactory explanations for these issues.
