Proposes a supervised extension to Word Mover’s Distance that learns weights within the distance calculation.

Method is sound, clearly explained, and provides appropriate technical detail.

Presents extensive and convincing experimental evaluation across multiple classification datasets.

Includes comprehensive baselines and features; ablation studies are well-designed and show each component’s contribution.

Proposed method consistently and significantly outperforms all baselines.

Datasets chosen cover a range of complexities and application areas.

Paper lacks discussion of computational cost and the accuracy–efficiency trade-off for learning/applying weighted WMD.

Recommends reporting training/inference times or a brief computational analysis.

Overall a substantial, well-motivated contribution with clear writing; reviewer recommends acceptance to NIPS.
