- Proposes a supervised document-distance method extending Word Mover's Distance by learning a linear embedding transform A and a document reweighting vector w to maximize class margins.
- Paper is well-written, clearly presented, and easy to follow.
- Strong, comprehensive literature survey demonstrating command of related work.
- Empirically evaluated on several novel datasets.
- Motivation for reweighting word distributions (w) is weak and may distort a document's original content; needs stronger justification and analysis.
- Contribution is incremental: applying A and w within an NCA objective is logical but not a major technical innovation.
- Unclear how the learned reweighting w generalizes to unseen documents at test time.
- The term "semantic difference" is ambiguous and appears to mean document class; the authors should clarify this usage.
- Reviewer would not oppose acceptance if the authors satisfactorily address the above concerns.
