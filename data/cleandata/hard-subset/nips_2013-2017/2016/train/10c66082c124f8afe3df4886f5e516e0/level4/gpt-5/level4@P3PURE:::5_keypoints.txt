- Extends WMD to a supervised setting using learned, word-specific weights for text classification.  
- Main technical contribution: replace exact Wasserstein computation with entropically regularized Sinkhorn distance for faster computation.  
- Empirically evaluated on eight datasets against 26 baselines and achieves the best average performance.  
- Paper is very well written with clear, in-depth mathematical treatment.  
- Novelty is limited relative to the original WMD paper.  
- Discussion of Word Centroid Distance baselines is not very relevant and comes across as confusing.  
- The role of the Sinkhorn regularization parameter λ is under-discussed and can strongly affect results.  
- Need clarification whether chosen λ values aim to emulate unregularized Wasserstein or intentionally operate in the entropic (Sinkhorn) regime, since small λ used for speed can substantially change behavior.
