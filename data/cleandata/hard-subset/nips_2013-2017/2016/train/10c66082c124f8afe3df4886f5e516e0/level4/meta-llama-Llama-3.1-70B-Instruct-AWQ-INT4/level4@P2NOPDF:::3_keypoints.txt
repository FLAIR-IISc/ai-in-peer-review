- Proposes learning WMD/EMD ground metric by parameterizing the cost matrix with a Mahalanobis distance and also learning histogram reweighting.  
- Provides gradients for NCA-type costs and presents convincing experimental evidence; paper is simple yet nontrivial to implement — reviewer supports acceptance.  
- Experimental section is a major strength.  
- Need clearer treatment of the interplay between regularized and non-regularized formulations and stricter mathematical rigor/notation.  
- Include an algorithmic box to clearly illustrate the proposed method.  
- Inconsistency in distance notation: Eq.1 uses Euclidean distance while Eq.6 uses squared Euclidean; better to state WMD as p-Wasserstein generally.  
- The reweighting idea is not novel — previously proposed by Lebanon and also present in Le & Cuturi.  
- Provide experiments analyzing the importance and practical impact of the learned weight vector w.  
- Related work must more clearly distinguish this paper’s contributions: entropy regularization for scalability, continuous cost parameterized by linear maps versus previous discrete costs.  
- Acknowledge Wang et al. but note their OT optimization is flawed (run only once and doesn’t work as proposed).
