- Proposes a supervised extension to Word Mover's Distance that learns a task-specific distance metric.  
- Method is technically sound and clearly explained with an appropriate level of detail.  
- Builds on the WMD framework by applying discriminative training to learn a domain-specific metric.  
- Provides extensive experimental evaluation across multiple classification datasets.  
- Compares against a wide range of baseline methods and feature sets.  
- Empirical results show the proposed method outperforms all baselines by a significant margin.  
- Overall a solid, valuable contribution and recommended for presentation at NIPS.
