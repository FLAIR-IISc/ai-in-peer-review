- Proposes learning WMD/EMD parameters by parameterizing the cost matrix with a Mahalanobis distance on word embeddings.  
- Also proposes learning histogram coefficient reweighting for documents (similar to Lebanon’s prior work).  
- Derives gradients for NCA-type objective functions applied to this problem.  
- Experimental section is strong and provides compelling evidence of method effectiveness.  
- Method is simple and conceptually straightforward, though implementation and testing are nontrivial.  
- Needs clearer treatment of interplay between regularized and non-regularized OT formulations.  
- Mathematical derivations and notations should be more rigorous and standardized.  
- Should include an algorithmic box to clearly illustrate the proposed method.  
- Notational inconsistency: Equation 1 uses Euclidean distance while Equation 6 uses squared Euclidean (suggest using p-Wasserstein notation).  
- The reweighting idea in Eq. 5 is not novel—similar to Guy Lebanon’s work and related histogram metric-learning papers.  
- Request experiments to evaluate the practical importance and impact of the learned weight vector w.  
- Related work section must better highlight differences from prior work (e.g., Cuturi/Avis entropy regularization and continuous vs discrete costs; clarify citation/limitations of Wang et al.).  
- Overall paper quality is high; strengths outweigh weaknesses and the reviewer supports acceptance.
