This paper presents a supervised extension to Word Mover’s Distance, learning weighted distances through discriminative training. The work is a welcome and valuable contribution, building on the Word Mover’s Distance approach by incorporating a learned, domain-specific metric. The method is technically sound and clearly explained, providing an appropriate level of detail. Notably, the experimental evaluation is exceptionally thorough, covering multiple classification datasets and a comprehensive set of baseline methods and feature representations. The proposed approach consistently and significantly outperforms these baselines. Overall, this is a strong paper and I enthusiastically recommend it for presentation at NIPS.
