This work extends the Word Mover’s Distance by introducing supervision to learn task-specific weights within the distance metric. The empirical study is very comprehensive. I’m pleased to see WMD advanced through discriminative training to obtain a domain-tailored metric. The approach is technically sound and clearly described, with the necessary level of detail. The experiments cover multiple classification datasets and a broad array of baseline methods and feature representations. The proposed method outperforms all baselines by a large margin. Overall, this is a strong paper that I would be very happy to see presented at NIPS.
