- Paper proposes supervised learning of document distance by learning a linear embedding transform A and reweighting word distributions w within an NCA objective on top of WMD.  
- Writing is clear and the literature review is comprehensive.  
- Experiments are provided on several datasets.  
- Motivation for reweighting the word distribution w is unclear and risks substantially altering document representation.  
- Authors must justify how reweighting improves discrimination while preserving document identity.  
- The contribution (linear A + reweighting w with NCA) appears incremental rather than novel or groundbreaking.  
- Practical use on unseen, unlabeled documents is unclear—how is w determined at test time?  
- The term “semantic difference” is ambiguous and should be clarified (does it mean labels, authorship, topics, etc.).  
- Reviewer is not opposed to acceptance but requests satisfactory responses to these concerns and a stronger statement of significance.
