- Proposes a supervised document distance based on WMD with a linear embedding transform (A) and a reweighted word distribution (w).
- Uses word2vec embeddings and optimizes A and w with an NCA-style objective.
- Paper is well-written, easy to follow, with a comprehensive literature review and experiments on multiple novel datasets.
- Motivation for the formulation is weak; the reweighting w is not well justified and could alter document meaning.
- Contribution appears incremental rather than groundbreaking—applying A and w with NCA is a plausible but limited extension of WMD.
- Unclear how the method handles completely new/unseen documents, specifically how to determine w for novel documents.
- The term “semantic difference” is ambiguous (seems to mean label here) and should be clarified to avoid confusion.
- Reviewer is conditionally positive and would accept if authors address the above concerns and justify their methodology.
