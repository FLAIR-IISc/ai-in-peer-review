This paper proposes a supervised extension of the Word Mover’s Distance that learns task-specific weights for the underlying distance metric. The approach is principled, clearly presented, and includes an appropriate level of technical detail. I am pleased to see WMD adapted with discriminative training to induce a domain-specific metric.

The experimental evaluation is thorough and well executed. The authors test across multiple classification datasets, compare against a wide range of baselines and feature representations, and provide comprehensive analyses. Across these settings, the proposed method consistently outperforms alternatives, often by a substantial margin.

Overall, the method is sound, well motivated, and convincingly validated. This is a strong, solid paper that I would be happy to see presented at NIPS.
