This paper introduces a supervised extension to Word Mover’s Distance, learning weighted distances through a discriminative training process. The work represents a valuable contribution, effectively building upon the established Word Mover’s Distance framework by incorporating learned, domain-specific metrics. The methodology is clearly presented and appropriately detailed. A particularly strong aspect of this submission is the comprehensive experimental evaluation, conducted across numerous classification datasets and comparing against a diverse set of baseline methods and feature representations. The proposed approach demonstrates substantial performance gains over all competitors. Overall, this is a strong and well-executed paper that would be a welcome addition to the NIPS program.
