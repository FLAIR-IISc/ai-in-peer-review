- Proposes supervised learning of document distances by extending WMD with a linear embedding transform A and a word-distribution reweighting w.
- Paper is clearly written, reviews related work thoroughly, and evaluates on many novel datasets.
- Motivation for reweighting w is weak: reweighting can substantially alter a document and needs justification and constraints (nonnegativity, normalization, sparsity, global vs. document-specific).
- Significance appears incremental: combining A and w with an NCA-style objective is reasonable but not conceptually novel.
- Unclear generalization to new documents: unclear how w is obtained or inferred at test time and whether w is global or document-specific without overfitting.
- Ambiguous use of “semantic difference”: needs clarification whether it refers to label differences, authorship, topic, or something else.
- Overall acceptable if above concerns are addressed satisfactorily, but current motivation and significance are insufficient.
