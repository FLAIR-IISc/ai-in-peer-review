This paper presents a supervised extension to the word mover's distance, leveraging discriminative training to learn domain-specific weights for the distance metric. The authors have conducted an exhaustive experimental evaluation, which is a notable strength of the paper. It is encouraging to see the word mover's distance approach being built upon, and the incorporation of supervision to learn a task-specific metric is a sound methodological choice. The paper strikes a good balance in its presentation, providing sufficient detail to facilitate understanding without overwhelming the reader. 

The experimental evaluation is comprehensive, covering multiple classification datasets and a diverse range of baseline methods and features. The results demonstrate that the proposed method significantly outperforms the alternatives, with substantial margins. Overall, this is a solid contribution that I believe would be a valuable addition to the NIPS program.
