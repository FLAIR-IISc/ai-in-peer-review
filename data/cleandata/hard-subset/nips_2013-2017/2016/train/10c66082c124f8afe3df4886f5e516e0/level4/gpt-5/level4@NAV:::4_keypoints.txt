- Proposes supervised extension of Word Mover’s Distance by learning a linear word transform A and a word-distribution reweighting w to better separate labeled documents.  
- Manuscript is clearly written with a thorough related-work review and experiments across many (including novel) datasets.  
- Motivation for reweighting w is unclear; reweighting can substantially alter a document and needs justification showing it helps without changing document meaning.  
- Contribution is modest: combining linear transform A and reweighting w with an NCA objective is reasonable but not particularly novel or sophisticated.  
- Practical question: how is distance computed for an unseen document—specifically, how is the reweight vector w determined for new documents?  
- Terminology issue: “semantic difference” is ambiguous (labels vs. authorship/topic); the paper should clarify what is meant.  
- Overall: paper is solid and could be acceptable if the above questions and clarifications are satisfactorily addressed.
