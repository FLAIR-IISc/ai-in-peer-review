

This paper extends the Word Mover's Distance by incorporating supervised learning to optimize distance weights. The authors conduct an exceptionally thorough experimental evaluation. I commend the adoption of discriminative training to develop a domain-specific metric, representing a valuable advancement of the Word Mover's Distance framework. The methodology is robust, clearly articulated with appropriate detail. Experiments are comprehensive, covering multiple classification datasets and a broad range of baseline methods and feature sets. The proposed approach significantly outperforms all competitors. This is a strong contribution deserving presentation at NIPS.
