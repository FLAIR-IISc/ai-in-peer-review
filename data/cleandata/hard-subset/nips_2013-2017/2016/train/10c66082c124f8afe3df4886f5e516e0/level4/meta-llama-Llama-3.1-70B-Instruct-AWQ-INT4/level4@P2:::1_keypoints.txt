- Proposes S-WMD: supervised metric learning on top of WMD that learns a linear projection and document-specific word weightings for transport distances based on squared Euclidean after projection.  
- Introduces an efficient optimization method to learn the proposed parameterized metric.  
- Provides thorough experimental evaluation against many baselines on document classification using a kNN classifier.  
- Paper is very well written and technically extends WMD in a useful and effective way.  
- Major concern: EMD with metric learning via a word-centroid initialization performs similarly to full S-WMD, limiting the added impact (see last two rows of Table 3).  
- Originality questioned: linear metric learning within EMD is interesting but a relatively limited contribution on its own.  
- Minor clarifications needed: identify authors at Line 152, add stars to α and β in Eq.11, and explicitly recall Sinkhorn scaling near Line 170.  
- Suggestion: include evaluation of the word-centroid embedding (weighted mean) with all tested metric learning methods (ITML, LMNN, NCA) in Table 3.  
- Suggestion: assess relative importance of word-specific weights vs. projection matrix A by reporting results when each component is used in isolation.
