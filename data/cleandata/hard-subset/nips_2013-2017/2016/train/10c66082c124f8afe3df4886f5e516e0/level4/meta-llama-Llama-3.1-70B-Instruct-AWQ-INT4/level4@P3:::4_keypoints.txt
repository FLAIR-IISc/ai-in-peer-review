- Proposes a supervised extension of WMD by learning a linear transform A and a reweighted word distribution w to separate documents by label.  
- Paper is well-written, easy to follow, with a thorough related-work review and clear literature understanding.  
- Experimental results across multiple datasets demonstrate the method's effectiveness.  
- Motivation for the formulation, particularly the reweighting w, is inadequately explained.  
- Reweighting w may change the document meaning and lacks clear justification for how it preserves semantics.  
- Contribution seems incremental—a straightforward application of linear transform A and reweighting with the NCA objective—reducing significance.  
- Unclear how to obtain the reweighting w for a completely new/unseen document, raising practical applicability concerns.  
- The term "semantic difference" is not defined (unclear if it refers to labels, topic, authorship, etc.).  
- Recommendation: Accept with minor revisions; scores: originality 6/10, quality 7/10, clarity 8/10, significance 5/10.
