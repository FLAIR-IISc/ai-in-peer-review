This paper extends the word mover’s distance by incorporating supervision to learn weights on the distance metric. The method is sound and clearly presented with an appropriate level of detail. I am pleased to see WMD leveraged with discriminative training to obtain a domain-specific metric. The experimental evaluation is very thorough: it covers several classification datasets, includes a wide range of baseline methods and feature configurations, and demonstrates that the proposed approach consistently outperforms all competitors by a large margin. Overall, this is a strong paper that I would be happy to see presented at NIPS.
