- Presents a supervised extension of Word Mover’s Distance that learns weights on the distance.  
- Leverages discriminative training to produce a domain-specific metric.  
- Method is sound, clearly explained, and provides the right amount of detail.  
- Offers a very comprehensive and extensive experimental evaluation.  
- Experiments cover multiple classification datasets and a wide variety of baselines and features.  
- Proposed method outperforms all baselines by a large margin.  
- Strong paper recommended for presentation at NIPS.
