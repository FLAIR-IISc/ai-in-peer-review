- Method learns two components: a linear transformation A and word-importance weights w.  
- Reviewer asks clearer intuition for Eq. (5) defining how w is used and requests analysis separating the contributions of A vs. w.

- Question about when Eq. (2) is used and apparent mismatch between the λ in Eq. (2) and the λ referenced at line 170.  
- Requests evidence supporting the claim that the approach is “particularly forgiving when two documents use syntactically different but conceptually similar words.”

- Table 2 clarification requested: what is the difference between “n” and “ne”?
