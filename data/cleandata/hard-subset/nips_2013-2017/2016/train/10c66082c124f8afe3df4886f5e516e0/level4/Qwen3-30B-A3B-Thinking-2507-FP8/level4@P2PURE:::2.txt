

This paper extends the Word Mover's Distance by incorporating supervised learning to optimize weights for the distance metric. The authors present a comprehensive experimental evaluation across multiple classification datasets and a broad range of baseline methods and feature representations. The approach—leveraging discriminative training to learn a domain-specific metric—represents a meaningful advancement over the original unsupervised WMD framework. The methodology is sound, clearly articulated, and provides sufficient technical detail. The proposed method consistently and significantly outperforms all competing baselines. This is a strong contribution with thorough validation, and I strongly recommend acceptance for presentation at NIPS.
