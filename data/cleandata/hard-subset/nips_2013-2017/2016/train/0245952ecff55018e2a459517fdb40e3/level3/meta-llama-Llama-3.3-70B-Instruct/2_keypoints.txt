- Proposes two novel optimization methods (gradient-free and gradient-based) for non-convex loss-minimization in Supervised PageRank.  
- Extends the inexact oracle framework to handle approximate loss and derivative computations.  
- Provides convergence rate guarantees for both proposed methods.  
- Presents detailed computational complexity analysis for the algorithms.  
- Demonstrates effectiveness via experiments on a real-world web page ranking task.  
- Paper is well-structured and clearly written.  
- Needs a more intuitive explanation of the objective formula and the algorithmic computations.  
- Should analyze the trade-off between lower-level algorithm accuracy and two-level algorithm computational cost.  
- Experimental evaluation is limited (smaller dataset, few comparisons to state-of-the-art).  
- Overall: significant contribution with potential for acceptance pending revisions addressing the weaknesses.
