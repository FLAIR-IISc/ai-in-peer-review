- Proposes Conditional Generative Moment-Matching Networks (CGMMN) extending GMMNs to conditional distributions using a Conditional Maximum Mean Discrepancy (CMMD) objective.  
- Core idea: learn p(y|x) by minimizing CMMD.  
- Motivation and contextualization (predictive modeling, contextual generation) are well articulated and the conditional extension is relevant.  
- Lacks clarity in derivation and implementation of the CMMD objective and associated algorithm.  
- Heavy mathematical formulation without intuitive explanation; Equation (2) is opaque and its derivation is missing.  
- Connection to kernel methods and Hilbert space embedding is not sufficiently explained for non-expert readers.  
- Substantial, unexplained jump from Equation (2) to Algorithm 1; supplemental material is difficult to follow.  
- Missing step-by-step computational details: how integrals are approximated and how kernel matrices are constructed/handled.  
- Mini-batch optimization is mentioned but its effect and bias on CMMD estimation are not discussed.  
- No accessible intuition for why the conditional approach should work or how it differs from training separate GMMNs per input.  
- Requests two concrete clarifications: an intuitive derivation of Equation (2) and a clear mapping from Equation (2) to the practical algorithm (kernel computations and optimization).  
- Arguments for acceptance: addresses important problem, introduces a novel CMMD objective, and shows competitive empirical performance.  
- Arguments against acceptance: insufficient clarity, missing derivation, unclear implementation, and reproducibility concerns.  
- Technical soundness is questionable due to unclear derivation and implementation; experiments look promising but are hard to evaluate.  
- Paper is poorly written and organized; heavy notation without explanation impedes understanding.  
- Originality: conditional extension is novel but its impact is reduced by lack of clarity.  
- Significance: potentially valuable contribution but currently hampered by missing details and explanation.  
- Overall recommendation: reject; require intuitive explanation of CMMD, full derivation of Equation (2), and detailed algorithmic/implementation steps for reconsideration.
