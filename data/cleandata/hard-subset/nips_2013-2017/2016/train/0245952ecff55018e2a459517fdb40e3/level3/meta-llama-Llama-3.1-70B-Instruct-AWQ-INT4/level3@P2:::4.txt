This paper proposes Conditional Generative Moment-Matching Networks (CGMMN), a novel extension of Generative Moment-Matching Networks (GMMN) to conditional generation tasks. The authors derive a conditional maximum mean discrepancy (CMMD) criterion, which enables the optimization of CGMMN using the same machinery as the unconditional GMMN. The paper demonstrates the effectiveness of CGMMN on various tasks, including predictive modeling, contextual generation, and Bayesian prediction distillation.

The main idea of CGMMN is to learn a conditional distribution given some input variables based on the CMMD criterion. The learning is performed by stochastic gradient descent with the gradient calculated by back-propagation. The authors provide a clear and well-organized presentation of the derivation of the CMMD criterion and the CGMMN architecture.

The paper is technically sound, and the authors provide a thorough discussion of the related work in the field. The empirical results demonstrate that CGMMN is competitive with state-of-the-art methods in various tasks. The paper is well-written, and the appendix contains helpful methodological and experimental details.

The novelty of the paper lies in the derivation of the empirical estimator of the CMMD criterion, which is non-obvious and useful. The framework is easy to apply, and the authors provide a simple algorithm (Algorithm 1) for optimizing CGMMN. The paper is likely to be relevant to practitioners using deep generative models or distillation.

Strengths:

* The paper proposes a novel extension of GMMN to conditional generation tasks.
* The derivation of the CMMD criterion is clear and well-presented.
* The empirical results demonstrate the effectiveness of CGMMN on various tasks.
* The paper is well-written, and the appendix contains helpful methodological and experimental details.

Weaknesses:

* The paper does not provide an exhaustive analysis of the theoretical properties of CGMMN.
* The authors do not discuss the limitations of the CMMD criterion and the CGMMN architecture.
