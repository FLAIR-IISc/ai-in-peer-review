- Proposes CMMD, extending MMD to conditional distributions, and introduces Conditional Generative Moment-Matching Networks (CGMMN).
- CGMMN is trained using stochastic gradient descent with gradients computed by back-propagation.
- Paper is well-structured and clearly written with a strong introduction and motivation.
- Provides a thorough theoretical foundation for CMMD and its relationship to MMD.
- Experimental results show effectiveness on predictive modeling, contextual generation, and Bayesian dark knowledge.
- Strength: Novel and theoretically sound approach to learning conditional generative models.
- Strength: Demonstrated effectiveness across multiple tasks.
- Strength: Clear and well-organized explanation of theoretical concepts.
- Weakness: Lacks detailed comparisons with other state-of-the-art conditional generative methods.
- Weakness: Experiments are mostly on small-scale datasets; scalability to larger datasets is untested.
- Weakness: Limited analysis of hyperparameter choices and model sensitivity.
- Overall recommendation: Recommend acceptance, contingent on addressing the above weaknesses.
