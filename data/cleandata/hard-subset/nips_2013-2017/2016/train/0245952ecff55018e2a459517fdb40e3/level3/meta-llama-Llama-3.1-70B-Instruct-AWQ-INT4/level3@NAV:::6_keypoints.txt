- Proposes Conditional GMMNs (CGMMN) using kernel conditional embeddings and a conditional MMD (CMMD) to learn conditional distributions.  
- Extends GMMNs to handle a wide range of conditional tasks while keeping training simple.  
- Claims are supported by theoretical analysis and experimental results; paper is technically sound.  
- Presentation is generally clear and well-organized, making the method easy to follow.  
- Original contribution: using CMMD for learning conditional distributions.  
- Demonstrated applicability to predictive modeling, contextual generation, and distillation of Bayesian models.  
- Thorough comparison with existing methods, showing advantages of the proposed approach.  
- Potentially significant impact on image generation, multimodal learning, and missing-data imputation.  
- Minor errors/inconsistencies: incorrect claim that the MMD estimator is unbiased.  
- Definition of the conditional embedding operator differs from cited reference [29], implying a different cross-covariance operator.  
- Sections 2.3 and 3.1 are hard to follow and required consulting referenced papers for clarity.  
- Practical drawback: requires large minibatches to avoid stochastic minibatch noise, increasing computational cost.  
- Recommendation: Accept with minor revisions to fix errors/inconsistencies; overall rating 8/10.
