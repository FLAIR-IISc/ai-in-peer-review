- Introduces Conditional Generative Moment-Matching Networks (CGMMN), extending GMMN to conditional generation via RKHS embedding and a conditional MMD (CMMD) training criterion.  
- Novel application of MMD principles to conditional generative modeling with theoretical grounding.  
- Paper is well-structured with clear motivation and technical exposition.  
- Empirical conditional operator definition omits centering terms (μ_X, μ_Y), raising concerns about gradient validity and backpropagation.  
- Authors fail to justify the centering assumption in RKHS or explain how centering was handled in experiments, creating a clarity and correctness issue.  
- Experimental results show CGMMN underperforms the Conditional Max-Margin VAE (CMMVA) on the reported datasets.  
- Evaluation lacks comparisons to other conditional generative methods (e.g., conditional GANs), limiting assessment of generative quality.  
- Manuscript contains minor typos that detract from professionalism.  
- Arguments for acceptance: novelty and theoretically motivated approach addressing an important problem.  
- Arguments against acceptance: critical theoretical omission, unconvincing experiments, limited comparisons, and typos.  
- Overall recommendation (original): reject in current form; authors must address centering, strengthen experiments, and improve clarity.  
- Update: authors added missing centering terms and justification; reviewer revised to recommend acceptance with minor revisions.
