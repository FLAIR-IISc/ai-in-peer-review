- Proposes Conditional Generative Moment-Matching Networks (CGMMN), extending GMMN to conditional distributions via a Conditional MMD (CMMD) loss.  
- CMMD is derived from Hilbert space embeddings of conditional distributions and measures discrepancy between conditional operators.  
- The generator is a deep network taking conditional input x and noise h to produce samples y.  
- Demonstrates CGMMN on three tasks: predictive classification (MNIST, SVHN), contextual generation (MNIST digits, Yale faces), and Bayesian model distillation ("dark knowledge").  
- Technically well-motivated and largely sound, grounding CMMD in operator-norm moment matching.  
- Major theoretical omission: Theorem 3 (CMMD=0 iff conditionals equal) is stated without proof or citation, weakening the paper’s theoretical foundation.  
- Experimental results are solid and show the method is functional and competitive, but do not clearly surpass state-of-the-art generative models.  
- Empirical scope is limited to relatively simple datasets; scaling to complex datasets (CIFAR-10, LSUN) is needed to assess competitiveness.  
- Bayesian distillation application is interesting and original but evaluated on only one dataset/teacher model, limiting conclusions about generality.  
- Clarity is generally good, with logical progression from MMD to CMMD and understandable architecture/training descriptions.  
- Notation issues: ambiguous expression Tr(Ld · C1) (unclear matrix vs. element-wise product) and overloaded use of symbol C cause potential confusion.  
- Original contribution is novel—first extension of moment-matching networks to conditional setting—and well-situated relative to conditional GANs/VAEs.  
- Significance lies in broadening moment-matching networks’ applicability and offering a potentially more stable alternative to adversarial training.  
- Acceptance pros: novelty, theoretical framing, versatility across tasks, clear presentation; cons: missing proof for central theorem, limited empirical scale, and minor notation/clarity problems.
