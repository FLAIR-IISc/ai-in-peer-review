1. Logical extension of GMMNs to conditional setting (CGMMN) using conditional MMD (CMMD).  
2. Solid theoretical grounding in kernel methods and Hilbert space embeddings.  
3. Practical training via backpropagation and a useful mini-batch algorithm.  
4. Theoretical novelty is limited—results are straightforward applications of existing kernel embedding theory.  
5. Experimental validation is broad (predictive and generative tasks, knowledge distillation) but not usually state-of-the-art.  
6. Lacks direct comparison to conditional generative models like C-GANs and C-VAEs.  
7. Related work section should more deeply discuss C-GANs/C-VAEs and contrast objectives (adversarial/variational vs. CMMD).  
8. Paper claims CMMD is simpler than GANs but does not quantify computational cost or hyperparameter sensitivity.  
9. Missing details on hyperparameter tuning (architectures, learning rates, regularization).  
10. No ablation studies to assess impact of components (hidden size, kernel choice, λ).  
11. Kernel selection and regularization parameter sensitivity are not discussed.  
12. Qualitative analysis of generated samples is limited; lacks metrics like Inception Score or FID.  
13. Presentation is clear but could use more intuitive explanations and discussion of limitations.  
14. Recommendations: expand related work, add comparisons to C-GAN/C-VAE, report hyperparameter tuning, run ablations, improve qualitative/quantitative evaluation, and discuss limitations.
