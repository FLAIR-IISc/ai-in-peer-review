- Paper extends GMMNs to match conditional distributions (CGMMN) by embedding conditionals in an RKHS, building on Song et al. (2009).
- CGMMN is evaluated empirically on several real datasets.
- Definition of the empirical conditional operator \hat{C}_{Y|X} omits the terms involving estimators of μ_X and μ_Y; centering in RKHS is assumed but not justified.
- Missing centering terms raise questions about gradient computation and how experiments handled centering; authors should clarify.
- Approach is novel and well-motivated.
- Experimental results are disappointing: CGMMN has higher predictive error than state-of-the-art CMMVA on both datasets.
- Generative performance is unclear because no comparisons to other conditional generative methods (e.g., conditional GANs) are provided.
- Paper contains typos (e.g., "Frobeniu norm", "an universal", "which the").
- Update: authors addressed the reviewer’s concerns and the reviewer will change their scores.
