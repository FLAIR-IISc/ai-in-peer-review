- Extends Generative Moment-Matching Networks to conditional generation via a kernel-embedding of conditional moments.  
- Derivation enables optimization using the same machinery as the unconditional GMMN.  
- Demonstrated competitive performance on predictive modeling, contextual generation, and Bayesian prediction distillation.  
- Technically sound and well presented (equations not exhaustively checked by reviewer).  
- Novelty: conditional extension is natural but the empirical estimator derivation is non-obvious and useful.  
- Framework is easy to apply (Algorithm 1) and likely relevant to practitioners using deep generative models or distillation.  
- Paper is well written and clear, with a helpful appendix containing methodological and experimental details.
