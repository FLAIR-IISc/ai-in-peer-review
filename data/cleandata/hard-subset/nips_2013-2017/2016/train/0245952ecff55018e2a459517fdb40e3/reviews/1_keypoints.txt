- Proposes a neural network method based on conditional maximum mean discrepancy (cMMD).
- Evaluated on standard small benchmark tasks for both discriminative and generative performance.
- Interesting and relevant extension in deep generative modeling.
- Paper is reasonably clear overall, though the mathematical exposition can be hard to follow at times.
- Experimental tasks are somewhat small but comparable to recent work in the area.
- Training involves computationally expensive steps; request for runtime comparison with methods like DSN.
- Reproducibility concern: reference code should be provided to strengthen the paper.
- Model description lacks sufficient detail for practitioners to exactly reproduce the evaluated models.
