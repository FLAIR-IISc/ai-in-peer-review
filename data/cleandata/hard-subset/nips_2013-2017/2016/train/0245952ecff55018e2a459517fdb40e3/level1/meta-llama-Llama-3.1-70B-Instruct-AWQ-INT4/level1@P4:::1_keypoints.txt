- Proposes a novel, well-motivated extension of GMMN to conditional distributions via a conditional MMD (CMMD) criterion.  
- Provides a thorough and sound technical derivation of the CMMD criterion.  
- Demonstrates impressive experimental performance on tasks including predictive modeling, contextual generation, and Bayesian dark knowledge.  
- Paper is well-written, clear, and well-organized with a concise introduction and related work.  
- Lacks a detailed discussion of the relationship between CMMD and other conditional modeling approaches (e.g., conditional GANs).  
- Experimental comparisons should include more state-of-the-art baselines to strengthen results.  
- Needs a more detailed analysis of the computational complexity of the proposed method.  
- Originality: CMMD is a new and significant contribution to conditional generative modeling.  
- Significance: approach appears broadly applicable and effective across multiple ML tasks.  
- Quality: technically sound and empirically convincing overall.  
- Clarity: derivations and experimental results are clearly presented.  
- Recommendation: strong paper worthy of acceptance, contingent on addressing the noted weaknesses in a revision.
