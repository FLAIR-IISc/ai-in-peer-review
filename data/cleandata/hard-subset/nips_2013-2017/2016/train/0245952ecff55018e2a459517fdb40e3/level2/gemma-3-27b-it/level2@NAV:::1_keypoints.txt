- Proposes Conditional Generative Moment-Matching Networks (CGMMN), extending GMMN to conditional generation using Conditional Maximum Mean Discrepancy (CMMD).  
- CMMD formulation is sound, with computation considerations addressed and a provided mini-batch training algorithm.  
- Evaluated on predictive modeling, contextual generation, and Bayesian dark knowledge distillation tasks.  
- Experiments on MNIST, SVHN, and Yale Face show competitive performance comparable to state-of-the-art.  
- Clear presentation and rigorous theoretical grounding connecting CMMD to RKHS/kernel embeddings.  
- Includes ablation studies and empirical comparisons that bolster the results.  
- Novelty is incremental since the core MMD approach is existing work.  
- Insufficient analysis of limitations, particularly scalability in high-dimensional conditional spaces and complex dependencies.  
- Paper leans on prior work and does not clearly emphasize specific advantages over conditional GANs or VAEs.  
- Overall recommendation: accept with minor revisions to address limitations and better justify benefits over alternatives.
