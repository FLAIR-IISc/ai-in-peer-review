- Extends Generative Moment-Matching Networks to the conditional setting via a conditional kernel embedding of moments.  
- Derives an empirical estimator enabling training with essentially the same optimization tools as the unconditional case.  
- Empirical evaluation shows solid, competitive results on predictive modeling, conditional sample generation, and distillation of Bayesian model predictions.  
- Work appears technically sound and the presentation is clear (equations not exhaustively checked).  
- Although a natural extension, the specific construction and estimator are non-obvious and provide novelty and practical value.  
- Framework is straightforward to implement (Algorithm 1 explicit) and useful for practitioners building deep conditional generative models or performing model distillation.  
- Writing is lucid and well organized; appendix provides helpful methodological and experimental details.
