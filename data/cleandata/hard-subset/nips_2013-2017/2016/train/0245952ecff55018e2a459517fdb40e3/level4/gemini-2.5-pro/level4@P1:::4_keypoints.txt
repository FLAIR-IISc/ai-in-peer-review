- Introduces Conditional Generative Moment-Matching Networks (CGMMN) extending GMMN to conditional distributions.
- Core contribution: Conditional Maximum Mean Discrepancy (CMMD) derived from kernel mean embedding of conditional distributions.
- CMMD allows efficient training using the same back-propagation machinery as the unconditional GMMN.
- Empirical evaluation shows competitive performance on predictive modeling, contextual generation, and distilling Bayesian models.
- Methods and derivations are technically sound and well-presented.
- Originality: extension is conceptually natural but the CMMD derivation and empirical estimator are non-trivial and significant.
- Framework is simple to apply in practice, with Algorithm 1 improving usability.
- Likely to be useful and relevant to researchers in deep generative modeling and model distillation.
- Paper is well-written, clear, and supplemented by a helpful appendix with experimental and methodological details.
