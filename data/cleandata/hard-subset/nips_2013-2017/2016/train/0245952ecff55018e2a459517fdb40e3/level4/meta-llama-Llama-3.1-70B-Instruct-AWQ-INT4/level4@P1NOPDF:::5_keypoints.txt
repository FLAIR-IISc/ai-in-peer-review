- Contribution: Proposes CGMMN—extending GMMNs to match conditional distributions via RKHS embeddings (based on Song et al. 2009) and evaluates it on real datasets.

- Missing term in estimator: The definition of Ċ_{Y|X} omits terms involving μ_X and μ_Y (their estimators); assuming zero means in RKHS is unusual and needs clarification.

- Gradient/experiment implications: Authors should explain how the missing mean terms affect gradient computation and experimental implementation.

- Predictive performance weak: CGMMN has higher error rates than state-of-the-art CMMVA on both datasets.

- Generative performance unclear: No comparison to other conditional generative methods (e.g., conditional GANs), so sample quality advantage is unproven.

- Minor errors/typos: Several typos noted (“Frobeniu norm”, “an universal”, “which the”).

- Rebuttal outcome: Reviewer states authors have addressed concerns and will update their scores.
