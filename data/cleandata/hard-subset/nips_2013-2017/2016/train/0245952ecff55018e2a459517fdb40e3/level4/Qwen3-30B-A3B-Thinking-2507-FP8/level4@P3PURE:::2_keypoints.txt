- Paper extends moment-matching networks to a non-trivial conditional setting where batch-wide distribution matching becomes harder because conditionals vary across samples.  
- Core technical challenge: matching distributions to inputs across large batches when per-sample conditionals produce different target distributions.  
- Main deficiency: presentation starts with heavy formalism without first giving intuitive motivation for the approach.  
- The paper does not explain the intuitive rationale behind the optimization objective (equation (2)).  
- The concrete computational steps of the algorithm translating theory to practice are not clearly described.  
- Computational efficiency and complexity of the proposed computations are not addressed.  
- Key assumptions enabling the method are not clearly stated.  
- Contrast: the non-conditional original paper offered accessible intuition, which the conditional version fails to replicate.  
- Some details are scattered in the supplement, but explanations remain fragmented and hard to follow.  
- Recommendation: include an intuitive derivation of equation (2) and a clear, step-by-step algorithmic translation for reproducibility.
