

This paper develops a neural network approach based on a conditional maximum mean discrepancy (CMMD) criterion. The proposed technique is evaluated on standard small-scale benchmark tasks for both discriminative and generative capabilities. Overall, this represents an interesting extension to the sub-area of deep learning research focused on generative modeling. The paper is reasonably clear, though the mathematical exposition can be challenging to follow at times, as is common in this field. While the benchmark tasks are modest in scale, they are reasonable when compared to recent work in this area. Given that the training algorithm involves computationally intensive steps, the authors should provide a discussion of training times relative to existing methods like DSN in the experimental section. The paper would be significantly stronger with the release of reference code for the experiments, as the model description lacks sufficient detail for reproducibility. A practitioner attempting to replicate the reported models would encounter difficulties due to insufficient implementation specifics in the model section.
