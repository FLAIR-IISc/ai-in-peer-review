- Introduces a conditional extension of moment matching networks (MMN).  
- Extension is challenging because conditioning on mini-batches with varying inputs is more nuanced than single-example updates.  
- Proposed approach feels unnecessarily complex and lacks clarity in presentation.  
- Core issue: matching distributions from mini-batches to varying conditional inputs is not well explained.  
- Paper presents formal mathematics before providing intuitive motivation, harming accessibility.  
- Optimized objective (eq. 2) is shown without sufficient explanation of why it was chosen or its underlying assumptions.  
- Algorithmic steps to implement equation (2) are inadequately detailed and relegated to a hard-to-follow supplement.  
- Missing discussion of computational complexity and scalability, especially the cost of conditioning with many inputs.  
- Authors should explicitly state assumptions and limitations to clarify when the method is applicable.  
- Overall: promising idea but presentation must improve with stronger intuition, clearer algorithm derivation, and efficiency analysis.
