- Extends Generative Moment-Matching Networks to conditional generation via a kernel embedding of conditional moments.
- Derives an empirical estimator for the conditional moment embedding that can be optimized similarly to the original unconditional model.
- Demonstrates competitive performance on predictive modeling, contextual generation, and distillation of Bayesian model predictions.
- Technically sound and well-presented, though not every equation was independently verified.
- Novelty is present: the conditional extension is natural but the specific estimator derivation is non-obvious and meaningful.
- Framework appears readily applicable in practice, with Algorithm 1 illustrating usage.
- Paper is clear and well-written, with a useful appendix providing methodological and experimental details.
