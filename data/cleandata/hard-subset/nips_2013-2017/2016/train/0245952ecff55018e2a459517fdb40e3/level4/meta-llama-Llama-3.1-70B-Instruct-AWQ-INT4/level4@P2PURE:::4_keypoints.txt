- Extends Generative Moment-Matching Networks to conditional generation using a kernel embedding of conditional moments.  
- Allows optimization with the same machinery as the unconditional GMMN.  
- Derivation of the empirical estimator is non-obvious, giving the work novelty and usefulness.  
- Shows competitive performance on predictive modeling, contextual generation, and distilling Bayesian model predictions.  
- Paper appears technically sound and well-presented, though not all equations were fully verified by the reviewer.  
- Framework is straightforward to apply (Algorithm 1) and likely useful to researchers using deep generative models.  
- Well-written, clear, and easy to read.  
- Appendix offers helpful supplementary methodological and experimental details.
