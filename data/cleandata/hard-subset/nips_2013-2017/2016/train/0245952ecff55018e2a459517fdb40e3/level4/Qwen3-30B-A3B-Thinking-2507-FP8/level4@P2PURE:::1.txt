

This paper develops a neural network approach based on a conditional maximum mean discrepancy criterion. The proposed technique is evaluated on standard small benchmark tasks for both discriminative and generative capabilities. Overall, this represents an interesting extension within the sub-area of deep learning research focused on building generative models. The paper text is reasonably clear; however, like most papers in this area, the mathematical exposition can be challenging to follow at times. The experimental tasks are modest but reasonable when compared to recent work in this domain. Given that the model training algorithm involves computationally intensive steps, the authors should provide comments in the experiment section regarding training duration relative to existing approaches such as DSN. The paper would be significantly strengthened by the availability of reference code for the experiments. Additionally, practitioners may find it difficult to reproduce the exact models evaluated, as described in the model section.
