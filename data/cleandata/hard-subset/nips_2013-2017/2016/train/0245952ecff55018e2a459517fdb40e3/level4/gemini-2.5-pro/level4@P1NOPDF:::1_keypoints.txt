- Proposes a neural network method using a conditional maximum mean discrepancy (MMD) criterion.  
- Evaluated on standard, small-scale benchmarks to test discriminative and generative performance.  
- Overall an interesting contribution to deep learning research on generative models.  
- Paper is reasonably well-written but some mathematical derivations are hard to follow.  
- Experimental tasks are somewhat small-scale, though comparable to recent work in the area.  
- Training algorithm appears computationally expensive; authors should report training time and compare to methods like DSN.  
- Reproducibility is a concern: reference code should be released and model descriptions clarified for practitioners.
