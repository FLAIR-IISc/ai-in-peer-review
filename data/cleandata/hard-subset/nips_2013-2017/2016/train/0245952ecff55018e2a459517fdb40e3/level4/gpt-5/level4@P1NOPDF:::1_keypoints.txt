- Proposes a neural network approach based on a conditional maximum mean discrepancy (MMD) criterion.  
- Evaluated on standard small-scale benchmarks for both discriminative and generative capabilities.  
- Interesting extension in generative deep learning; overall writing is reasonable but some mathematical derivations are hard to follow.  
- Experimental tasks are relatively small-scale, which is acceptable compared to recent work.  
- Training algorithm involves computationally expensive steps; paper should report training time and resource usage.  
- Needs comparisons of computational cost and scalability with existing approaches (e.g., DSN).  
- Lack of reference code and insufficient model/architecture details may hinder reproducibility.
