This paper presents a neural network approach based on a conditional maximum mean discrepancy (MMD) criterion. The proposed technique is evaluated on standard benchmark tasks to assess both its discriminative and generative capabilities. Overall, this work represents an interesting contribution to the growing body of research in deep generative models. 

The paper is generally well-written and reasonably clear; however, as is common in this field, the mathematical details can be challenging to follow. The experimental evaluation utilizes relatively small datasets, though this is not uncommon when compared to other recent publications in the area. Given the computational expense inherent in training the model, it would be beneficial to include a discussion of training time in the experiment section, particularly a comparison to existing methods such as DSN (Deep Structured Energy-Based Models). 

The paper would be significantly strengthened by the release of accompanying code. The level of detail provided in the model description may make it difficult for practitioners to precisely reproduce the reported results. Providing code would greatly enhance reproducibility and facilitate further research.
