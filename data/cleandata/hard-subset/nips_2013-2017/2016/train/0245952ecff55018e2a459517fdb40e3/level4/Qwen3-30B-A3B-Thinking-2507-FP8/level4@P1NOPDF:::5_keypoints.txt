- Contribution: Extends GMMNs to match conditional distributions by embedding conditionals in an RKHS (CGMMN), building on Song et al. (2009).  
- Critical estimator issue: Definition of \hat{C}_{Y|X} omits the terms involving \mu_X and \mu_Y (and their estimators).  
- Questionable assumption: It’s unusual to assume \mu_X and \mu_Y are zero (i.e., data centered in RKHS) without justification.  
- Required clarifications: Authors must explain (1) why those terms were omitted, (2) how that omission affects gradient computation, and (3) how it’s handled in practice; otherwise the theoretical foundation is incomplete.  
- Predictive performance: CGMMN produces higher error rates than state-of-the-art CMMVA on both datasets.  
- Generative evaluation gap: No comparisons to other conditional generative models (e.g., conditional GANs), so sample quality superiority is unsubstantiated.  
- Presentation issues: Several typos to fix ("Frobeniu norm", "an universal", "which the").  
- Update: After the rebuttal, the reviewer states the technical concerns were adequately addressed and will adjust scores accordingly.
