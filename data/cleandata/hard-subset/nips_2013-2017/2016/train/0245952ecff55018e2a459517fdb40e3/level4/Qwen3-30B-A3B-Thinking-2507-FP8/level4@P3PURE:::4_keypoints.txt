- Extends Generative Moment-Matching Networks to conditional generation.  
- Derives a kernel embedding of conditional moments for the conditional distribution.  
- Enables optimization using the same machinery as the unconditional GMMN.  
- Shows competitive performance on predictive modeling, contextual generation, and distilling Bayesian predictions.  
- Technical content is sound and core arguments were verified (not all equations scrutinized).  
- Conditional extension conceptually straightforward, but derivation and empirical estimator are non-trivial and novel.  
- Framework is straightforward to implement (Algorithm 1) and likely broadly useful.  
- Paper is well-written, clear, and accessible.  
- Supplementary appendix provides valuable methodological and experimental details.
