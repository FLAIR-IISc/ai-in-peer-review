- Proposes a neural network method based on a conditional maximum mean discrepancy (MMD) criterion.
- Evaluates the approach on standard, small-scale benchmark tasks for discriminative and generative performance.
- Considered an interesting contribution to the deep generative models subfield.
- Paper is reasonably clear overall, but some mathematical derivations are hard to follow.
- Experimental tasks are somewhat small-scale, though comparable to recent work.
- Training algorithm includes computationally expensive steps; experiments should report training time relative to methods like DSN.
- Reproducibility concern: providing reference code would strengthen the paper, as model details may be insufficient for practitioners to reproduce results.
