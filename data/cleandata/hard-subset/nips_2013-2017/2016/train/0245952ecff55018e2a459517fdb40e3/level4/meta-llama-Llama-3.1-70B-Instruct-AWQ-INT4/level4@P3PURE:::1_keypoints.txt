- Proposes a neural network method based on conditional maximum mean discrepancy, evaluated for discriminative and generative tasks.
- Interesting and relevant extension within deep generative modeling.
- Paper is reasonably clear but some mathematical derivations are difficult to follow.
- Experiments use relatively small benchmark tasks—somewhat limited but comparable to recent work.
- Training involves computationally expensive steps; report training time versus baselines (e.g., DSN) to assess practicality.
- Releasing reference code is recommended to improve reproducibility, especially for model implementation details.
