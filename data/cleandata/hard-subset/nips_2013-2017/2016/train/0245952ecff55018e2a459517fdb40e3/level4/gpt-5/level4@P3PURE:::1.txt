This paper develops a neural network approach based on a conditional maximum mean discrepancy (MMD) criterion. The proposed technique is evaluated on standard, relatively small benchmark tasks for both discriminative and generative capabilities. Overall, this is an interesting extension within the sub-area of deep learning focused on building generative models. The paper is reasonably clear, although—as in much of this literature—the mathematics can be difficult to follow at times.

The experimental tasks are somewhat small but not unreasonable compared with other recent work in this area. Because the training algorithm includes computationally expensive steps, I would like to see comments in the experiments section on how long the model takes to train, especially in comparison with existing approaches such as DSN. The paper would also be much stronger if reference code were made available for the experiments. As written, the model section may leave practitioners struggling to reproduce the exact models evaluated, so additional implementation details or open-source code would substantially improve reproducibility.
