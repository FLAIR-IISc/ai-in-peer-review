- Introduces a novel conditional loss for directed generative models using conditional kernel mean embeddings, extending GMMs' MMD criterion.  
- Evaluated on classification, digit/face generation, and Bayesian model distillation.  
- Authors addressed prior concerns about latent dimensionality, model utility versus existing methods, and clarifications.  
- Remark 1’s mapping between Equation (2) and a weighted MMD needs more detailed explanation.  
- The dot (⋅) notation is ambiguous (matrix multiplication vs. Hadamard product) and should be clarified.  
- Reusing the symbol ‘C’ for both the conditional embedding and a loss parameter is confusing and should be changed.  
- Theorems 1 and 2 properly cite prior work, but Theorem 3 lacks a citation or an included proof.  
- Empirical results are encouraging and demonstrate the method’s versatility, including supervised use as a demonstration.  
- Bayesian inference distillation is interesting but evaluated on only one dataset/model, limiting claims about generalizability.  
- Digit and face generation results are comparable to the original GMM paper, showing effectiveness on these benchmarks.  
- Recommend evaluating on more challenging datasets (e.g., CIFAR-10, LSUN) to strengthen impact and adoption.  
- Overall, a solid, logical contribution that would be improved by addressing notation, providing Theorem 3’s proof/citation, and broader experiments.
