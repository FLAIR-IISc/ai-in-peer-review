- Extends Generative Moment-Matching Networks (GMMN) to conditional generation.  
- Uses a kernel embedding of conditional moments so the model can be optimized like the unconditional GMMN.  
- The empirical estimator’s derivation/mechanism is not immediately obvious, suggesting novelty and usefulness.  
- Demonstrates competitive performance on predictive modeling, contextual generation, and distilling predictions from a Bayesian model.  
- Framework is straightforward to apply; Algorithm 1 illustrates the implementation.  
- Paper appears technically sound and well-presented, though the reviewer did not fully verify all equations.  
- Well-written and clear, with an appendix providing helpful methodological and experimental details.  
- Likely valuable to researchers working on deep generative models or distillation of complex statistical processes.
