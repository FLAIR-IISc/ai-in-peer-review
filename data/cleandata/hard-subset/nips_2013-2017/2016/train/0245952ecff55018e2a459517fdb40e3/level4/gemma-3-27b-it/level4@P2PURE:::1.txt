This paper develops a neural network approach based on a conditional maximum mean discrepancy criterion. The proposed technique is evaluated on standard benchmark tasks to assess both its discriminative and generative capabilities. Overall, this is an interesting extension to the sub-area of deep learning research focused on generative models. The paper’s text is reasonably clear, although, as is common in this field, the mathematical details can be challenging to follow. The experimental tasks are relatively small in scale, but not unreasonable compared to other recent work in the area. Given that the algorithm involves some computationally expensive steps, I would appreciate comments in the experiment section regarding training time compared to existing approaches such as DSN. The paper would be significantly strengthened by the release of reference code, as a practitioner might find it difficult to reproduce the exact models evaluated based on the current description in the model section.
