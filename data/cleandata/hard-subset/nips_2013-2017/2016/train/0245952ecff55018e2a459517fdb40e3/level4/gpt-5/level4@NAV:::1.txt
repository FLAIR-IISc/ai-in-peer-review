This paper presents a neural network method grounded in a conditional maximum mean discrepancy objective. The proposed technique is assessed on standard small-scale benchmark tasks for both discriminative and generative performance. Overall, this is a compelling extension within the segment of deep learning research devoted to generative modeling. The writing is reasonably clear, though, as with many works in this domain, the mathematical exposition is occasionally hard to follow. The experimental tasks are relatively small, but not out of line with those used in recent related work. Because the training procedure includes computationally intensive components, I would like to see discussion in the experiments section of the training time relative to existing methods such as DSN. The paper would be significantly strengthened by releasing reference code for the experiments. As written, the model section may leave practitioners struggling to reproduce the exact models evaluated.
