- Extends Generative Moment-Matching Networks to conditional generation via a kernel embedding of conditional moments.
- Derives an empirical estimator for the conditional moments that can be optimized using the same machinery as the unconditional model.
- Demonstrates competitive performance on predictive modeling, contextual generation, and distillation of Bayesian model predictions.
- Paper appears technically sound and well presented (though not all equations were independently verified).
- Novelty is present: conditional extension is natural but the empirical-estimator derivation is non-obvious and useful.
- Framework is practical and easily applicable, with Algorithm 1 illustrating implementation.
- Well-written and clear, with a detailed appendix covering methods and experimental details.
- Overall, a valuable and relevant contribution to deep generative modeling.
