This paper introduces a novel neural network method based on a conditional maximum mean discrepancy objective. The proposed approach is assessed on established benchmark tasks to demonstrate its performance in both discriminative and generative modeling. This work represents a worthwhile contribution to the field of deep learning research concerning generative models. The writing is generally lucid; however, as is common in this domain, the mathematical formulations can present challenges for readers. The experimental tasks are relatively limited in scale, though comparable to those found in recent related studies. Given the computational complexity of the training algorithm, including a discussion of training time relative to existing methods such as DSN would be valuable. The paper’s impact would be significantly enhanced by the public release of implementation code, as precise reproduction of the experimental models may be difficult based on the information provided in the model description.
