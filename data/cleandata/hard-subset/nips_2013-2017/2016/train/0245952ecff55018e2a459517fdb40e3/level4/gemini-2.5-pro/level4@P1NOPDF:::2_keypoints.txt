- Paper extends moment matching networks to the conditional setting, which is a non-trivial contribution.
- Conditional extension is challenging because each mini-batch sample is conditioned on a different input, so the model must match multiple target distributions simultaneously.
- Using the same conditional variable for an entire mini-batch is computationally inefficient, motivating the proposed approach.
- Main weakness: presentation immediately dives into RKHS and heavy math (Eq. 2) without a clear high-level, intuitive explanation.
- The paper would benefit from an intuitive derivation analogous to the original moment matching network paper.
- Practical implementation is under-specified: the concrete computational steps, efficiency, and assumptions required to implement Eq. (2) are unclear.
- Some implementation details are buried in the supplementary material and are difficult to follow; the main text should include the algorithm, its complexity, and assumptions.
