- Paper extends moment matching networks to the conditional setting and correctly notes this is non-trivial.  
- In minibatch-based moment matching, differing conditioning variables make target and generated distributions mixtures, complicating matching.  
- Main weakness is exposition: the paper jumps into formalism without providing an intuitive explanation of Equation (2).  
- The reader is left unsure why Equation (2) is the natural formulation for conditional moment matching.  
- The paper lacks clarity on concrete computational steps to implement and optimize Equation (2).  
- Important implementation details are omitted or hidden in hard-to-follow supplementary material.  
- The manuscript should provide an intuitive derivation/justification of the objective starting from the high-level goal.  
- The authors should present a clear, step-by-step description of a single training update, including assumptions, approximations, and computational-efficiency analysis.
