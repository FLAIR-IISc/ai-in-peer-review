- Extends GMMN to conditional generation by deriving a kernel embedding of conditional moments and an empirical estimator trainable like the unconditional GMMN.  
- Evaluated on predictive modeling, contextual generation, and Bayesian model distillation, achieving competitive performance.  
- Derivations appear consistent with kernel mean embedding literature and the GMMN framework; technically sound.  
- Experimental evaluation is diverse and supports claims of effectiveness without excessive complexity.  
- Manuscript is well written and clear; connection to unconditional GMMN is explicit.  
- Algorithm 1 and appendix provide practical implementation and experimental details.  
- Conditional extension is a natural direction but the specific estimator derivation is non-obvious and novel.  
- Framework is straightforward to implement and train via backprop, making it practically useful.  
- Pros: clear presentation, simple/practical estimator and training, competitive results, helpful appendices.  
- Con: reviewer did not verify all equations in full detail.  
- Overall: solid, meaningful extension of GMMN to conditional modeling likely of interest to the community.
