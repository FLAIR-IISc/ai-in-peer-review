This paper develops a neural network approach based on a conditional maximum mean discrepancy criterion. The proposed technique is evaluated on standard small benchmark tasks for both discriminative and generative capabilities. Overall, this is an interesting extension in the sub-area of deep learning research focused on building generative models.

The paper text is reasonably clear, although, like most papers in this area, the mathematical derivations can be challenging to follow at times. The tasks used in the experiments are somewhat small but not entirely unreasonable when compared to other recent work in this area.

As the algorithm for training the model involves some computationally expensive steps, it would be beneficial to include comments in the experiment section on the training time of the model compared to existing approaches like DSN. Providing this information would help assess the practicality of the proposed approach.

Furthermore, making the reference code available for the experiments would significantly strengthen the paper. Without access to the code, a practitioner may encounter difficulties reproducing the exact models evaluated in the paper, particularly when referring to the model section.
