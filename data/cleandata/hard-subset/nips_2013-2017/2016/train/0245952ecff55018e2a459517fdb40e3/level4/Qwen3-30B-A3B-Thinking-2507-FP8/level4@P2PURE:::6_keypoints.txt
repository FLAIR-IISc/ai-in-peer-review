- Introduces CGMMNs, extending GMMNs to conditional generation/prediction using kernel embeddings and a conditional MMD (CMMD) metric.
- Core technical contribution: formulation of a conditional embedding operator and CMMD for measuring conditional distribution discrepancies.
- Evaluations on classification, conditional generation, and Bayesian model distillation show promising results.
- Naive baseline (separate GMMN per conditional distribution with shared parameters) suffers severe data sparsity for continuous conditioning; it can still work for discrete conditioning, so comparing against it would strengthen the paper.
- CMMD objective is well-formulated and computationally efficient: K matrices in Equation 2 are parameter-independent, allowing standard backpropagation (Remark 1).
- Concern: CMMD may need large minibatches to reduce minibatch stochasticity, which increases computational cost and expensive matrix inverses.
- Presentation of Sections 2.3 and 3.1 is hard to follow without consulting cited papers and needs clearer exposition.
- Two minor errors: Line 77 wrongly claims the MMD estimator is unbiased; Line 90’s conditional embedding operator conflicts with reference [29], requiring a different cross-covariance operator definition.
