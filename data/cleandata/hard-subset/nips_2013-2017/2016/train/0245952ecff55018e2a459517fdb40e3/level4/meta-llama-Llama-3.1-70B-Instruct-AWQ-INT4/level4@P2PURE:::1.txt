This paper develops a neural network approach based on a conditional maximum mean discrepancy criterion. The proposed technique is evaluated on standard small benchmark tasks to assess its discriminative and generative capabilities. Overall, this is an interesting extension in the sub-area of deep learning research focused on building generative models. 

The paper text is reasonably clear, although the mathematical derivations can be challenging to follow at times, a common issue in papers within this area. The tasks used in the experiments are somewhat small, but not entirely unreasonable when compared to other recent work in this area. 

However, as the algorithm for training the model involves some computationally expensive steps, it would be beneficial to include comments in the experiment section regarding the training time of the model in comparison to existing approaches like DSN. 

Furthermore, making the reference code available for the experiments would significantly strengthen the paper. As it stands, a practitioner may encounter difficulties reproducing the exact models evaluated, based solely on the information provided in the model section.
