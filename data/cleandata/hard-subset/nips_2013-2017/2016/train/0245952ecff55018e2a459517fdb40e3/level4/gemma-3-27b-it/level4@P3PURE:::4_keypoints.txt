- Extends Generative Moment-Matching Networks to conditional generation.  
- Derives a kernel embedding of conditional moments to enable optimization with the same machinery as the unconditional model.  
- Demonstrates competitive performance on predictive modeling, contextual generation, and distillation from a Bayesian model.  
- Paper appears technically sound and clearly presented, though not all equations were fully verified by the reviewer.  
- The empirical estimator derivation is not immediately obvious, indicating novelty and practical usefulness.  
- Framework is straightforward to apply and illustrated in Algorithm 1.  
- Likely valuable and relevant to researchers in deep generative models and model distillation.  
- Well-written manuscript with a helpful appendix detailing methods and experiments.
