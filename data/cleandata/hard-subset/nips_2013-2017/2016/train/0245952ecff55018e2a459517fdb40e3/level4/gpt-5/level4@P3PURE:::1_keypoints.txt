- Proposes a neural network method using a conditional maximum mean discrepancy (MMD) criterion.
- Evaluated on standard, relatively small benchmarks for both discriminative and generative tasks.
- Interesting extension in generative deep learning; paper is reasonably clear but the math is sometimes hard to follow.
- Experimental tasks are small but comparable to recent work in the area.
- Training includes computationally expensive steps; reviewer asks for training time and comparisons to methods like DSN.
- Paper would be stronger if reference code were released.
- Model section lacks implementation details, harming reproducibility; additional details or open-source code are needed.
