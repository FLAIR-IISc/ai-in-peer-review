- Proposes a neural-network method based on conditional maximum mean discrepancy as an extension for generative models.
- Evaluated on standard small benchmarks measuring discriminative and generative performance.
- Paper text is reasonably clear but some mathematical derivations are hard to follow.
- Benchmark tasks are somewhat small, though comparable to recent work in the area.
- Should report training time and computational cost compared to existing methods (e.g., DSN).
- Releasing reference code is recommended to improve reproducibility and usability.
