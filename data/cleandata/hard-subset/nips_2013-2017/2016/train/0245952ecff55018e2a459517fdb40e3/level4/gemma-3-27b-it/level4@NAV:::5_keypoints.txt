- Paper proposes Conditional GMMN (CGMMN) extending GMMNs to match conditional distributions using RKHS embeddings.  
- Definition of the conditional operator estimator \hat{C}_{Y|X} appears incomplete—missing terms involving μ_X and μ_Y (and their estimators).  
- The authors assume zero means for μ_X and μ_Y (i.e., pre-centering in RKHS), which is atypical and needs justification.  
- Clarification required on how the missing term affects gradient computation and experimental results.  
- Method is novel and well-presented, but empirical performance is underwhelming—CGMMN has higher error rates than CMMVA on tested datasets.  
- Paper lacks comparisons to other conditional generative models (e.g., conditional GANs), limiting assessment of generative quality.  
- Manuscript contains typographical errors (examples: “Frobeniu norm”, “an universal”, “which the”).  
- Update after rebuttal: authors have satisfactorily addressed prior concerns; reviewer will revise their assessment.
