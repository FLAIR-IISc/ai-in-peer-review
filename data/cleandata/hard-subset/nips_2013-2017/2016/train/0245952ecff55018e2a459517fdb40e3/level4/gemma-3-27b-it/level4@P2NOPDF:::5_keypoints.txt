- Introduces Conditional Generative Moment Matching Networks (CGMMN), extending GMMNs to match conditional distributions.  
- Uses conditional distribution embeddings in an RKHS following Song et al. (2009).  
- Potential issue: definition of estimator hat{C}_{Y|X} appears to omit terms involving estimators of mu_X and mu_Y.  
- Unusual assumption that mu_X and mu_Y are zero (pre-centering in RKHS); needs justification.  
- Clarification required on how the missing term and centering assumption affect gradient computation and experimental results.  
- Method is novel and conceptually sound.  
- Experimental results are underwhelming: CGMMN shows higher predictive error than state-of-the-art CMMVA on tested datasets.  
- Generative evaluation incomplete: no comparison to established conditional generative models (e.g., cGANs).  
- Manuscript contains minor typographical errors (e.g., "Frobeniu norm", "an universal", "which the").  
- Update: authors addressed concerns in rebuttal; reviewer revised scores accordingly.
