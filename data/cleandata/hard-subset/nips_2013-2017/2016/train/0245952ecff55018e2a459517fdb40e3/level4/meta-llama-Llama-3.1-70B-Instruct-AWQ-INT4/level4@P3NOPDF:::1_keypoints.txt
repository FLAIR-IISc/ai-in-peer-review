Paper proposes a neural-network method based on conditional maximum mean discrepancy as an interesting extension for generative modeling.  
Evaluated on standard benchmark tasks demonstrating both discriminative and generative capabilities.  
Overall text is reasonably clear, though some mathematical derivations are hard to follow.  
Experimental tasks are somewhat small but comparable to recent work in the area.  
Paper lacks detailed information on computational efficiency and training time.  
Reviewer requests explicit comparison of training time (e.g., versus DSN) to contextualize performance.  
Reproducibility would be improved by releasing reference code for the experiments and models.  
Minor clarity and reproducibility improvements would strengthen the paper.  
Work is novel and contributes meaningfully to deep learning generative-model research with potential to inspire further work.
