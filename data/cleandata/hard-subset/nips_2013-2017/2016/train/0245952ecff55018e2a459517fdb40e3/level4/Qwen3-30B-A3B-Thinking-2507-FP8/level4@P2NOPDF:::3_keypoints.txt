- Introduces a novel conditional loss for directed generative models by extending GMMNs with conditional kernel mean embeddings and MMD.  
- Authors have addressed prior concerns about low-dimensional latent spaces, scalability, and comparative utility.  
- Remark 1 is ambiguous: the dot notation in the mapping from Eq.(2) to the weighted MMD form must be clarified (matrix product vs. Hadamard).  
- Notation conflict: symbol C is overloaded (conditional embedding vs. constant); distinct symbols should be used.  
- Theorem 3 lacks a citation or included proof and must be properly proven or referenced (Theorems 1–2 already cite prior work).  
- Empirical results are adequate for proof-of-concept but not transformative.  
- Supervised/classification application seems suboptimal and is only a demonstration of versatility, not a core contribution.  
- Bayesian knowledge distillation experiment is interesting but limited to one dataset and architecture, leaving broader applicability unclear.  
- Stronger evidence should target pure generative modeling on more challenging benchmarks (e.g., CIFAR-10, LSUN) to show advantage over SOTA.  
- Overall: technically sound with minor notation/theorem fixes needed; expand experimental scope before strong recommendation for acceptance.
