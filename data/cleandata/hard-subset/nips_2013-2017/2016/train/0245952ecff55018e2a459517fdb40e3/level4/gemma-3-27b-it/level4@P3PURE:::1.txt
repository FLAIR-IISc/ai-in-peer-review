This paper develops a neural network approach based on a conditional maximum mean discrepancy criterion. The proposed technique is evaluated on standard, small benchmark tasks for both discriminative and generative capabilities. Overall, this is an interesting extension within the sub-area of deep learning research focused on building generative models. The paper’s text is reasonably clear, although, as is common in this field, the mathematical formulations can be challenging to follow at times. The tasks used in the experiments are somewhat limited in scale, but not unreasonable when compared to other recent work. Given that the algorithm for training the model involves computationally expensive steps, I would appreciate comments in the experimental section regarding training time compared with existing approaches such as DSN. The paper would be significantly strengthened by the release of reference code for the experiments. A practitioner might find it difficult to reproduce the exact models evaluated based solely on the information provided in the model section.
