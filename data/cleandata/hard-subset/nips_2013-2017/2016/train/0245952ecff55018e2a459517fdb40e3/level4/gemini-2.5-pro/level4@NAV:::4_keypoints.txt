- Proposes a conditional extension of Generative Moment-Matching Networks by deriving a kernel embedding for conditional moments.
- Enables optimization with the same machinery as the unconditional GMMN framework.
- Demonstrates competitive performance on predictive modeling, contextual generation, and Bayesian-model distillation tasks.
- Technical contributions are solid and clearly presented, though equations were not verified line-by-line.
- Novelty is reasonable: intuitive extension but non-trivial empirical estimator derivation, offering practical value.
- Framework is straightforward to apply (illustrated in Algorithm 1) and relevant to researchers and practitioners.
- Manuscript is well-written and clear; appendix gives comprehensive methodological and experimental details.
