- Proposes a neural network approach using a conditional maximum mean discrepancy (MMD) criterion for deep generative modeling.  
- Evaluated on standard benchmarks for both discriminative and generative performance.  
- Overall an interesting and valuable contribution to deep generative models.  
- Paper is generally well-written but mathematical details are sometimes hard to follow.  
- Experiments use relatively small datasets; training is computationally expensive and the paper should report training time and compare to methods like DSN.  
- Reproducibility is limited by missing implementation details; releasing code is strongly recommended.
