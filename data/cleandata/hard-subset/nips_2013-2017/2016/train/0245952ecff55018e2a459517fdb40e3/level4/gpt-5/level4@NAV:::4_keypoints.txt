- Paper introduces a conditional variant of Generative Moment-Matching Networks (GMMNs).
- Achieves conditioning by deriving a kernel embedding of moments for the conditional distribution.
- Enables optimization with the same machinery as the unconditional GMMN.
- Evaluated with competitive results on prediction tasks, contextual generation, and distilling Bayesian model predictions.
- Work appears technically sound and clearly presented (equations not fully verified).
- Novelty: natural extension but specific mechanism and empirical estimator derivation are nonobvious and novel.
- Framework is straightforward to apply, as illustrated in Algorithm 1.
- Likely useful and relevant to researchers in deep generative modeling and model distillation.
- Paper is well written and the appendix provides comprehensive methodological and experimental details.
