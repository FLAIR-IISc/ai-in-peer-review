- Proposes a novel neural-network generative model using a conditional maximum mean discrepancy (MMD) criterion.  
- Evaluated on standard benchmarks (MNIST, SVHN, Yale Face) and shows effective discriminative and generative performance.  
- Paper is well-organized and generally clearly written, accessible to readers with basic deep-learning knowledge.  
- Mathematical derivations can be challenging to follow in places.  
- Experimental tasks/datasets are somewhat small compared to broader recent work.  
- Training involves computationally expensive steps; the paper lacks details on runtime/efficiency and comparison (e.g., vs. DSN).  
- Releasing reference code would significantly strengthen reproducibility and practical adoption.  
- Overall recommendation: accept pending minor revisions to address clarity, efficiency details, and code release.
