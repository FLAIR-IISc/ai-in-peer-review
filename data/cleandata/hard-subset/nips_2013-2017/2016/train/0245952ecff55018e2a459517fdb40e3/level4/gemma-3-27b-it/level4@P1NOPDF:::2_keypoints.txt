- Authors extend moment-matching networks to the conditional setting, which is non-trivial for single-example updates.
- The difficulty arises because distributions must be matched across large mini-batches to many different conditional inputs, so distributions are not identical per update.
- Primary weakness is presentation: the paper starts with complex formalism instead of an intuitive overview.
- The paper fails to clearly state the actual computations performed by the algorithm and to analyze computational efficiency.
- The conditional extension lacks the clear, intuitive explanation given for the original non-conditional work.
- Key derivation (eq. (2)) is not adequately explained: its optimization efficiency, required assumptions, and concrete steps are unclear.
- Important implementation details are buried in a hard-to-follow supplement.
- The paper should provide an intuitive derivation of eq. (2) and explicit steps connecting it to the practical algorithm implementation.
