- Extends Generative Moment-Matching Networks (GMMNs) to conditional generation.  
- Derives a kernel embedding of conditional moments enabling conditional distribution modeling.  
- Allows optimization using the same machinery as the unconditional GMMN variant.  
- Shows competitive empirical performance on predictive modeling, contextual generation, and distilling Bayesian model predictions.  
- Technical approach appears sound and the paper is clearly and well-structured (equations not fully verified).  
- Novelty: conditional extension is natural, but the empirical estimator derivation and its mechanism are non-obvious and meaningful.  
- Implementation is straightforward and demonstrated in Algorithm 1.  
- Likely immediately useful to practitioners applying deep generative models or distilling complex statistical processes.  
- Appendix provides valuable supplementary methodological and experimental details.  
- Overall, the work makes a solid contribution to the field.
