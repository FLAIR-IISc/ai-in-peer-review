- Extends Generative Moment Matching Networks (GMMNs) to the conditional setting using a kernel embedding of conditional moments.
- Derivation yields an empirical estimator enabling optimization with the same machinery as the unconditional GMMN.
- The model achieves reasonably competitive performance on predictive modeling, contextual generation, and distilling Bayesian model predictions.
- The paper appears technically sound and clearly presented, though not every equation was checked in detail.
- While the conditional extension is a natural direction, the specific mechanism and estimator derivation are non-obvious and provide novelty.
- Framework is straightforward to apply in practice, illustrated by Algorithm 1.
- Paper is well written, easy to follow, and the appendix contains helpful, detailed methodological and experimental information.
