- Proposes a neural network method based on conditional maximum mean discrepancy (CMMD).
- Evaluated on standard small benchmark tasks for both discriminative and generative performance.
- Represents an interesting extension within deep learning generative-model research.
- Paper text is reasonably clear, but the mathematical exposition can be difficult to follow at times.
- Experimental tasks are modest but reasonable compared to recent work in the domain.
- Training involves computationally intensive steps; authors should report training duration and compare to methods like DSN.
- Paper would be strengthened by releasing reference code, as current model descriptions may hinder reproducibility.
