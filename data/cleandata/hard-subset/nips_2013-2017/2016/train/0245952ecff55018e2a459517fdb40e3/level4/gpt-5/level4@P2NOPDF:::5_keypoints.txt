- Proposes CGMMN: extending GMMNs to conditional distributions using conditional mean embeddings in an RKHS and evaluates on real datasets.  
- Major technical concern: the estimator for the conditional operator ĈY|X appears to omit RKHS centering terms μX and μY.  
- Uncommon to assume μX = μY = 0 in RKHS; reviewer requests clarification on whether features are explicitly centered or empirical means are used.  
- Reviewer asks how the centering choice affects derivation and stability of gradients during training.  
- Reviewer requests clarification of how centering/estimator choices are implemented in the experiments.  
- Novel and technically neat approach, but predictive performance is worse than state-of-the-art CMMVA on reported datasets.  
- Generative performance evaluation is incomplete—no comparisons to other conditional generative models (e.g., conditional GANs).  
- Manuscript contains typos/minor issues to correct (e.g., “Frobeniu norm” → “Frobenius norm”, “an universal” → “a universal”, fix “which the”).  
- After the author rebuttal, the reviewer reports all concerns were addressed and will revise scores.
