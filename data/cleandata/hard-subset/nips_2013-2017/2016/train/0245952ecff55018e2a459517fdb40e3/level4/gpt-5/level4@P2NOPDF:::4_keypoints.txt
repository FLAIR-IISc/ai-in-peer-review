- Extends Generative Moment-Matching Networks (GMMNs) to the conditional setting for conditional sample generation.  
- Key idea: construct a kernel embedding of conditional distribution moments so the objective can be optimized like unconditional GMMN.  
- Evaluated on predictive modeling, contextual generation, and distillation from a Bayesian model, showing reasonably competitive performance.  
- Methodology appears technically sound and clearly presented (equations not fully verified).  
- Novelty: conditional extension is natural but the kernel-embedding construction and empirical estimator derivation are non-obvious and impactful.  
- Framework is straightforward to apply in practice (Algorithm 1) and useful for researchers/practitioners and model distillation.  
- Paper is well written with an informative appendix detailing methods and experiments.  
- Overall: a solid, meaningful, and applicable extension of a known generative approach.
