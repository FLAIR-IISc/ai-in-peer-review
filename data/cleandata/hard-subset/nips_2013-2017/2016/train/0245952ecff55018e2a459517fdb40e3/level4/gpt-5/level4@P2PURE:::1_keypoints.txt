- Proposes a neural network approach based on a conditional maximum mean discrepancy (cMMD) criterion.  
- Evaluated on standard, relatively small benchmark tasks for both discriminative and generative capabilities.  
- An interesting extension within deep generative modeling.  
- Paper is reasonably clear, though the mathematics can be difficult to follow at times.  
- Experimental tasks are somewhat small but comparable to recent work.  
- Training involves computationally expensive steps; paper should report training time and compare to approaches like DSN.  
- No reference code provided, reducing reproducibility and making it hard for practitioners to reimplement from the model section alone.
