This paper develops a neural network approach based on a conditional maximum-minimum discrepancy criterion. The proposed technique is evaluated on standard small benchmark tasks for discriminative and generative abilities. Overall, it is an interesting extension to the sub-field of deep learning research focused on generating models. The text of the paper is reasonably clear, although, like most papers in this field, following the mathematics can sometimes be a bit challenging. The tasks used in the experiment are a bit small, but not entirely unreasonable compared to other recent work in this area. Due to the algorithm involved in model training involving some computationally expensive steps, I would like to know some comments in the experiment section about how long the model takes to train compared to existing methods like DSN. The paper would be further strengthened if reference code for the experiments were made available. A practitioner evaluating the model section may find it difficult to reproduce the correct models.
