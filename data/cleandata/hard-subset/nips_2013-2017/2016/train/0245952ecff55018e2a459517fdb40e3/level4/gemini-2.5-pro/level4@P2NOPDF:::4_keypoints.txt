- Proposes a conditional extension to Generative Moment-Matching Networks (GMMN).  
- Core technical contribution: derives a kernel embedding for conditional moments enabling training with the same optimization as unconditional GMMN.  
- Demonstrates effectiveness on predictive modeling, contextual generation, and distillation from a Bayesian model with competitive results.  
- Paper appears technically sound and derivations seem correct overall (not every equation verified).  
- Novelty: extending to conditional setting is natural but the empirical estimator for conditional moments is non-trivial and insightful.  
- Framework is straightforward to implement (Algorithm 1) and practically useful for researchers and practitioners.  
- Well-written, clear, and easy to follow, with a comprehensive appendix supporting reproducibility.
