This paper presents a neural network approach grounded in a conditional maximum mean discrepancy criterion, which is assessed on standard benchmark tasks to evaluate its discriminative and generative capabilities. The work contributes an interesting extension to the deep learning research focused on generative models. The text is generally clear, although the mathematical content can be challenging to follow in certain sections, a common trait of papers in this field. The experimental tasks, while limited in scope, are comparable to those in recent studies. However, given the computationally intensive nature of the model training algorithm, it would be beneficial to include a discussion in the experimental section on the training time relative to existing approaches like DSN. Furthermore, making the reference code available for the experiments would significantly enhance the paper's impact. As it stands, practitioners may encounter difficulties in reproducing the exact models evaluated in the manuscript, highlighting the need for more detailed implementation insights.
