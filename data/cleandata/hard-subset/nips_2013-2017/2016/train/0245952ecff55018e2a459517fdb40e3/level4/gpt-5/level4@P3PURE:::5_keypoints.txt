- Paper extends GMMNs to the conditional setting via conditional mean embeddings in RKHS and evaluates the proposed CGMMN on real datasets.
- Definition of the conditional operator ˆC_{Y|X} is missing terms involving mean embeddings μ_X and μ_Y; assuming zero means is unusual and the paper should clarify how this is handled in gradient computation and experiments.
- Empirical results are disappointing: CGMMN shows higher predictive error than state-of-the-art CMMVA, and generative evaluation lacks comparisons to other conditional generative methods (e.g., conditional GANs).
- Minor typos: “Frobeniu norm” → “Frobenius norm”; “an universal” → “a universal”; fix grammatical error “which the.”
- After the author rebuttal, the reviewer reports that all concerns were addressed and will change their scores.
