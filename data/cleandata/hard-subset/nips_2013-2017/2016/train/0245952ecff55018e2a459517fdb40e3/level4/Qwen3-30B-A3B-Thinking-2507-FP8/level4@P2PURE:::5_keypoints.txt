- Proposed conditional GMMN (CGMMN) extends GMMNs by matching conditional distributions via RKHS embeddings (builds on Song et al. 2009).  
- CGMMN is evaluated empirically on multiple real datasets.  
- Definition of estimator Ć_{Y|X} (Line 90) omits terms involving estimators of μ_X and μ_Y; assuming they are zero is unusual and must be clarified.  
- Authors must explain how the missing μ terms are handled in gradient computations and in practical experiments.  
- The approach is novel and well-motivated.  
- Predictive performance is disappointing: CGMMN has higher error rates than state-of-the-art CMMVA on both datasets.  
- Generative performance is unclear because there are no comparisons to other conditional generative models (e.g., conditional GANs).  
- Paper contains typographical errors (e.g., "Frobenius norm" Line 48; "a universal" Line 65; "which the" Line 238).  
- After the authors’ rebuttal, the reviewer states all concerns were addressed and will adjust scores.
