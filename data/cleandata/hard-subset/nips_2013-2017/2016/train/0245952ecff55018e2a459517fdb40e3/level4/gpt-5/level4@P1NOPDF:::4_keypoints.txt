- Proposes a conditional extension of GMMNs via a kernel embedding of conditional moments, enabling training with similar machinery as the unconditional case.
- Derives an empirical estimator for the conditional moment embedding that is non-obvious and contributes genuine novelty.
- Demonstrates competitive empirical performance on predictive modeling, contextual generation, and distilling Bayesian model predictions.
- Method appears technically sound and well presented (reviewer did not verify every equation in detail).
- Framework is straightforward to apply in practice; Algorithm 1 clearly describes the procedure.
- Useful to researchers and practitioners interested in deep generative models or distilling complex statistical processes.
- Paper is clearly written and easy to follow; appendix provides helpful methodological and experimental details.
