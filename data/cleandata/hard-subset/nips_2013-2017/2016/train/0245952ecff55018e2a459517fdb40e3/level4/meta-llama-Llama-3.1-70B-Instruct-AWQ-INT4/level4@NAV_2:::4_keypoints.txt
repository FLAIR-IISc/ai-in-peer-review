- Extends Generative Moment-Matching Networks (GMMN) to conditional generation, proposing Conditional GMMN (CGMMN).
- Introduces Conditional Maximum Mean Discrepancy (CMMD) via kernel embedding of conditional distributions.
- Shows CMMD is a sufficient condition for two conditional distributions to be equal.
- Derives an empirical estimator and provides Algorithm 1; estimator derivation was non-obvious but implementation is straightforward.
- Paper is technically sound, well-organized, and clearly explains background and method.
- Experimental results show CGMMN is competitive on predictive modeling, contextual generation, and Bayesian dark knowledge distillation.
- Strengths: technical soundness, clarity of presentation, novelty, and practical applicability.
- Weaknesses: limited experimental scope and insufficient comparisons with other deep generative models.
- Paper would benefit from additional experiments and more discussion of limitations and future directions.
- Reviewer recommends acceptance, citing soundness, presentation, novelty, and demonstrated effectiveness.
- Arguments for acceptance: sound proposals, clear presentation, and promising empirical results.
- Arguments against acceptance: need for broader experiments/comparisons and more discussion of limitations.
