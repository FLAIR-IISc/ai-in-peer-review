

This paper introduces a neural network framework built upon a conditional maximum mean discrepancy criterion. The proposed method is assessed on standard, small-scale benchmarks for both discriminative and generative performance. Overall, this represents a meaningful contribution to the subfield of deep learning dedicated to generative model development. The manuscript is reasonably well-written, though, as is common in this domain, the mathematical exposition occasionally presents comprehension challenges. The experimental tasks are modest in scale but remain justifiable when contextualized against comparable recent studies. Given the computationally intensive training procedure, the authors should include a comparative analysis of training duration relative to established methods such as DSN in the experimental section. Availability of reference code for the experiments would significantly strengthen the paper's reproducibility. Additionally, the model section may pose implementation difficulties for practitioners seeking to replicate the exact architectures evaluated.
