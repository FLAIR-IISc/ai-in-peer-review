- Proposes a neural network method using a conditional maximum mean discrepancy (CMMD) criterion.  
- Evaluated on standard small-scale benchmarks for both discriminative and generative performance.  
- Represents an interesting extension to deep generative modeling research.  
- Paper is reasonably clear, though the mathematical exposition can be hard to follow at times.  
- Benchmark tasks are modest in scale but reasonable compared to recent work.  
- Training algorithm appears computationally intensive; authors should report training times and compare to methods like DSN.  
- Releasing reference code would significantly strengthen the paper.  
- Model description lacks implementation detail, making reproducibility and replication difficult for practitioners.
