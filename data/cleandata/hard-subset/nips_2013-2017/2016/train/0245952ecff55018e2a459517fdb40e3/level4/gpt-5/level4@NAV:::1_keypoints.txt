- Proposes a neural network method based on a conditional maximum mean discrepancy (MMD) objective.  
- Evaluated on standard small-scale benchmarks for both discriminative and generative performance.  
- Represents a compelling extension in deep generative modeling.  
- Writing is reasonably clear, but mathematical exposition can be hard to follow.  
- Experimental tasks are relatively small but comparable to recent related work.  
- Training includes computationally intensive components; paper should report training time vs. methods like DSN.  
- Paper would be stronger with released reference code; model section may hinder reproducibility.
