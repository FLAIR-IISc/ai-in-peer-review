- Paper proposes a non-trivial conditional extension of moment matching networks, which is not straightforward like single-example update models.  
- Main technical challenge: matching distributions of many samples to multiple, differing conditionals in mini-batches.  
- Using the same conditional for every update (to simplify matching) is inefficient and impractical.  
- The manuscript relies heavily on advanced math (e.g., RKHS) without providing an intuitive explanation of core ideas.  
- Equation (2) is inadequately explained: its derivation, underlying assumptions, and computational efficiency are unclear.  
- Practical implementation details and computations are buried in the supplement and difficult to follow.  
- The paper needs an intuitive derivation of equation (2) and a clear, step-by-step mapping from the formula to the algorithm.
