- Proposes Conditional GMMNs (CGMMN), extending GMMNs to conditional distribution matching using RKHS embeddings; conceptually clean and novel.  
- Major mathematical issue: the conditional covariance operator hat{C}_{Y|X} omits RKHS mean embedding terms, making the estimator incomplete and potentially biased.  
- Unjustified assumption mu_X = mu_Y = 0 (RKHS centering); authors must correct definition, justify centering, and clarify effects on gradients and optimization.  
- Empirical concern: CGMMN yields higher predictive errors than state-of-the-art CMMVA, challenging claims of competitiveness.  
- Missing comparisons to conditional generative models (conditional GANs/VAEs) and absence of generative benchmarks (e.g., FID, human eval) or qualitative sample analysis.  
- Minor typographical errors (e.g., "Frobeniu norm", "an universal", "which the") harm professionalism.  
- Overall: theory is sound and extension is novel, but mathematical gap and weak empirical validation require revision.  
- Update after rebuttal: authors fixed hat{C}_{Y|X}, justified RKHS centering, added conditional GAN comparisons, and showed improved generative results; reviewer updated scores.
