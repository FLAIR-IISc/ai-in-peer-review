Proposes a neural network approach based on a conditional maximum mean discrepancy (cMMD) criterion.

Evaluated on standard, small-scale benchmark tasks to assess discriminative and generative capabilities.

An interesting extension to deep generative modeling research.

Paper is reasonably clear but mathematical details can be difficult to follow.

Experimental tasks are somewhat small but comparable to recent work.

Training algorithm involves computationally expensive steps; experiments should report training time vs. methods like DSN.

Paper would be stronger if reference code were released.

Model description may lack sufficient detail for practitioners to exactly reproduce the experiments.
