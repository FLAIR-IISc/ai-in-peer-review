- Proposes a neural network method based on conditional maximum mean discrepancy, evaluated for discriminative and generative tasks.
- Offers an interesting extension to deep generative model research.
- Writing is generally clear, though some mathematical sections are difficult to follow.
- Experiments are limited in scope but comparable to recent work.
- Training is computationally intensive; the paper should discuss training time relative to methods like DSN.
- Releasing reference code would significantly increase the paper’s impact.
- Reproducibility is a concern; more detailed implementation and experimental details are needed.
