- Extends Generative Moment-Matching Networks to the conditional setting using a kernel embedding of conditional moments, allowing similar optimization as the unconditional case.
- Derives an empirical estimator for the conditional moment embedding; the mechanism and derivation are non-obvious and provide novelty and practical value.
- Evaluated on predictive modeling, contextual generation, and Bayesian model distillation, showing reasonably competitive performance.
- Paper appears technically sound and well presented, though not all equations were independently verified.
- Framework is easy to apply in practice (Algorithm 1 is clear) and relevant to researchers in deep generative models and model distillation.
- Well written and clear, with a helpful appendix that supplies detailed methods and experimental information.
