This paper develops a neural network approach based on a conditional maximum mean discrepancy criterion. The proposed technique is evaluated on standard, relatively small benchmark tasks for both discriminative and generative capabilities. Overall, this is an interesting extension within the deep learning sub-area focused on building generative models. The paper is reasonably clear, although—as in much of this area—the mathematics can be difficult to follow at times. The tasks used in the experiments are somewhat small but not unreasonable compared to other recent work.

Since the training algorithm includes some computationally expensive steps, I would like to see comments in the experimental section on how long the model takes to train, particularly in comparison with existing approaches such as DSN. The paper would be much stronger if reference code were provided for the experiments. As written, a practitioner may have difficulty reproducing the exact models evaluated based on the model section alone.
