- The paper extends moment matching networks to the conditional setting, which is a non-trivial and valuable contribution.
- The main difficulty is matching distributions across large mini-batches when inputs/conditionals differ, making naive updates inefficient.
- The reviewer finds the paper lacking an intuitive explanation of the core formula (eq. (2)) and the underlying assumptions used to optimize it.
- The computational efficiency and actual steps of the proposed algorithm are not clearly explained or justified.
- The manuscript relies heavily on advanced math (e.g., RKHS) without providing an accessible intuition comparable to the non-conditional version.
- Transition steps from eq. (2) to the practical algorithm are buried in the supplement and remain hard to follow.
- The reviewer requests an intuitive derivation of eq. (2) and a clear, step-by-step explanation of how it yields the implemented algorithm.
