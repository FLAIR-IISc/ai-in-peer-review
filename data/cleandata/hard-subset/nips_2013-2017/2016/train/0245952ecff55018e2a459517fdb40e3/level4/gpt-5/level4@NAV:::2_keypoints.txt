- Paper proposes a conditional extension of moment-matching networks.  
- Extension is non-trivial because matching distributions across many inputs in a mini-batch yields non-identical target distributions.  
- Reusing the same conditioning for updates would make the method inefficient.  
- Main criticism: the paper launches into heavy formalism instead of building intuition for the objective.  
- The final objective (eq. (2)) is not clearly explained or intuitively derived.  
- The concrete computations the algorithm performs and its computational efficiency are not described.  
- Key assumptions that make the objective workable are not stated.  
- The mapping from formula (2) to a practical training algorithm is unclear.  
- Some details are in the supplement but are hard to follow and insufficiently clear.
