- Proposes Conditional Generative Moment-Matching Network (CGMMN): extends GMMNs to conditional settings by embedding conditional distributions in an RKHS (building on Song et al., 2009).  
- Major technical concern: empirical conditional embedding operator \hat{C}_{Y|X} (Line 90) appears to omit mean-embedding terms, implying an implicit RKHS centering assumption that must be justified.  
- Needs clarification of how the omitted mean terms affect gradient computation and the practical implementation used in experiments.  
- Experiments: CGMMN shows higher predictive errors than the state-of-the-art CMMVA on the presented datasets, indicating weaker predictive performance.  
- Evaluation gap: lacks comparisons to other conditional generative models (e.g., cGANs), making it hard to judge generative sample quality and advantages.  
- Minor typos to fix: "Frobeniu norm" → "Frobenius norm" (Line 48); "an universal" → "a universal" (Line 65); remove or correct "which the" (Line 238).  
- Post-rebuttal: reviewer states authors have satisfactorily addressed concerns and will update scores accordingly.
