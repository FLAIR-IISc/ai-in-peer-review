This paper develops a neural network approach based on a conditional maximum mean discrepancy criterion. The proposed technique is evaluated on standard small benchmark tasks for discriminative and generative capabilities. Overall, this is an interesting extension to the sub-area of deep learning research focused on building generative models.

The paper text is reasonably clear, although, like most papers in this area, the mathematical derivations can be a bit difficult to follow at times. The tasks used in the experiments are somewhat small but not entirely unreasonable when compared to other recent work in this area. However, as the algorithm for training the model involves some computationally expensive steps, I would appreciate some comments in the experiment section on the training time of the model compared to existing approaches like DSN.

To further strengthen the paper, I suggest making the reference code available for the experiments. As it stands, a practitioner may encounter difficulty reproducing the exact models evaluated, particularly based on the information provided in the model section.
