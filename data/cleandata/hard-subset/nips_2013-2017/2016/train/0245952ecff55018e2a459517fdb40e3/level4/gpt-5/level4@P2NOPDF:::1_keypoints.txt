- Proposes a neural network method grounded in a conditional maximum mean discrepancy (MMD) criterion.  
- Evaluated on standard, small-scale benchmarks for both discriminative and generative performance.  
- Overall an interesting contribution to deep generative modeling.  
- Paper is generally clear and readable, but some mathematical derivations are difficult to follow.  
- Training appears computationally intensive; paper should report hardware, wall-clock time, and number of epochs and compare resource costs to methods like DSN.  
- Reproducibility is a concern; provide reference code and exact implementation details (architectures, hyperparameters, optimization settings, preprocessing).  
- Experimental scale is relatively small; justify the choice or evaluate on larger benchmarks for stronger validation.
