- The paper extends moment-matching networks to a conditional variant, which is non-trivial compared to single-example updates.  
- Key technical challenge: matching distributions of many samples to their corresponding conditional inputs across mini-batches.  
- Using identical conditions for all updates would be computationally inefficient.  
- Major weakness: heavy reliance on formal mathematical notation without intuitive motivation.  
- Authors do not clearly describe the practical computational steps executed by the algorithm.  
- The paper fails to explain the algorithm's efficiency characteristics.  
- Underlying assumptions enabling the method are not clearly stated.  
- Equation (2), central to the conditional approach, lacks an accessible intuitive derivation.  
- Supplement contains details but remains difficult to follow; concrete algorithmic translation of Equation (2) is missing.  
- A clear, intuitive explanation for the conditional case comparable to the non-conditional original would substantially strengthen the paper.
