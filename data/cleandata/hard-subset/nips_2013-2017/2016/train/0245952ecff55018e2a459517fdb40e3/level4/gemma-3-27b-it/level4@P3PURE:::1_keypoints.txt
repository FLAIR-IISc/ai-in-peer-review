- Proposes a neural network method based on conditional maximum mean discrepancy (MMD).  
- Evaluated on standard, small benchmark tasks for both discriminative and generative performance.  
- Represents an interesting extension in the sub-area of deep generative models.  
- Paper text is reasonably clear, though some mathematical formulations are hard to follow.  
- Experimental tasks are somewhat limited in scale but comparable to recent work.  
- Training involves computationally expensive steps; experiments should report training time and compare to methods like DSN.  
- Paper would be significantly strengthened by releasing reference code for experiments.  
- Current model description may be insufficient for practitioners to exactly reproduce the reported models.
