- Extends GMMNs to the conditional setting by matching conditional distributions via RKHS conditional embeddings (building on Song et al., 2009).  
- Proposes Conditional GMMN (CGMMN) and evaluates it on several real-world datasets.  
- Primary technical concern: empirical conditional mean embedding operator definition (hat C_{Y|X}) appears to omit mean-embedding terms (mu_X, mu_Y), implying implicit centering—authors should clarify handling and impact on gradients/experiments.  
- Predictive performance is worse than state-of-the-art CMMVA on both datasets.  
- Generative evaluation is incomplete—no comparison to other conditional generative methods (e.g., conditional GANs), so sample quality is hard to assess.  
- Minor typos noted: "Frobeniu norm" (L48), "an universal" (L65), "which the" (L238).  
- Update after rebuttal: reviewer reports authors satisfactorily addressed concerns and raised scores.
