- Proposes Conditional MMD (CMMD) by extending GMMN to conditional settings using conditional kernel mean embeddings, optimized by backprop and evaluated on supervised prediction, conditional digit/face generation, and Bayesian distillation.

- Reviewer’s prior concerns (use of low-dimensional latent space, scalability to higher-dimensional latents, comparative utility versus alternatives, and various clarifications) appear to be addressed in the revision.

- Remark 1 is unclear: the mapping to a “weighted MMD” form needs an explicit derivation and clarification of the “dot” notation (standard matrix multiplication vs. Hadamard product).

- Symbol choice: using C is confusing because it conflicts with the prior use of C for the conditional embedding operator; a different symbol is recommended.

- Theorems 1 and 2 are properly attributed, but Theorem 3 lacks a citation — the reviewer asks for a reference with a proof or to include a complete proof.

- Empirical results are solid; supervised learning application is acceptable as a demonstration of versatility though not the most compelling use case.

- Bayesian distillation experiment is interesting but limited to a single dataset and teacher model, so its broader utility remains uncertain.

- The work’s strongest potential impact is in pure generative modeling.

- Generation experiments are comparable to the original GMMN paper and demonstrate feasibility on digit and face datasets.

- For wider adoption, experiments on more challenging benchmarks (e.g., CIFAR-10 or LSUN) would be more persuasive.
