- Extends generative moment-matching networks to match conditional distributions via RKHS embeddings (based on Song et al. 2009).  
- Proposes Conditional Generative Moment-Matching Networks (CGMMN) and evaluates them on several real datasets.  
- Definition of the conditional operator estimator \hat{C}_{Y|X} is missing terms related to μ_X and μ_Y and needs clarification.  
- It is unusual and unexplained to assume μ_X and μ_Y are zero (i.e., data centered in RKHS).  
- Authors should clarify how the missing term affects gradient computation and the conducted experiments.  
- Experimental results are disappointing: CGMMN has higher error rates than state-of-the-art CMMVA on both datasets.  
- Lack of comparisons to other conditional generative methods (e.g., conditional GANs) leaves generative quality unclear.  
- Paper contains typos (e.g., "Frobeniu norm", "an universal", "which the").  
- Update: authors addressed the concerns and the reviewer updated their scores.
