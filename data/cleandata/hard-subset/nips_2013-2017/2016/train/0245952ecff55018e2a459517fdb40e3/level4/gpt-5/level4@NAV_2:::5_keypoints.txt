- Extends GMMNs to the conditional setting using RKHS conditional mean embeddings (Song et al., 2009).  
- Proposes CGMMN trained with stochastic gradient descent and back-propagation, evaluated on several real-world datasets.  
- Major technical concern: empirical conditional embedding operator Ć_{Y|X} (Line 90) omits empirical μ_X and μ_Y terms.  
- Needs clarification on whether μ_X and μ_Y are assumed zero (i.e., RKHS centering) and how they are treated in gradients/implementation.  
- Empirical results underwhelming: CGMMN yields higher prediction error than state-of-the-art CMMVA.  
- Generation results lack comparison to other conditional generative models (e.g., conditional GANs), so sample quality advantage is unclear.  
- Manuscript contains typos (e.g., “Frobeniu norm”, “an universal”, “which the”).  
- Update: After rebuttal, authors addressed concerns and reviewer will revise scores.
