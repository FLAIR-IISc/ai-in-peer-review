- Extends GMMNs to conditional setting by matching conditional distributions using conditional mean embeddings in an RKHS (following Song et al. 2009).
- Introduces the CGMMN algorithm and evaluates it on several real datasets.
- Major technical issue: empirical conditional operator C_{Y|X} definition (Line 90) omits mean-embedding terms μ_X and μ_Y.
- Questionable assumption that μ_X = 0 and μ_Y = 0 (i.e., data centered in RKHS); need clarification on handling these terms in gradients and experiments.
- Empirical results are underwhelming: CGMMN has higher predictive error than state-of-the-art CMMVA on both datasets.
- Generative performance lacks comparison to other conditional generative models (e.g., conditional GANs), so sample quality is unclear.
- Minor issues: several typos noted (“Frobeniu norm”, “an universal”, “which the”).
- Update: authors addressed concerns in rebuttal; reviewer will change scores.
