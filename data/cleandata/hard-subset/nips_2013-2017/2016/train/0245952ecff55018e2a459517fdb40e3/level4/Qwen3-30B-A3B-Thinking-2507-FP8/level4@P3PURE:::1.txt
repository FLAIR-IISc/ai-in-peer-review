

This paper develops a neural network approach based on a conditional maximum mean discrepancy criterion. The proposed technique is evaluated on standard small benchmark tasks for both discriminative and generative capabilities. Overall, this represents an interesting extension to the sub-area of deep learning research focused on building generative models. The paper text is reasonably clear; however, like most papers in this area, the mathematical exposition can be somewhat difficult to follow at times. The benchmark tasks used in the experiments are modest but reasonable when compared to other recent work in this domain. Given that the model training algorithm involves computationally expensive steps, the authors should provide additional comments in the experiment section regarding training duration relative to existing approaches such as DSN. The paper would be significantly strengthened by the availability of reference code for the experiments. Furthermore, practitioners may encounter difficulties reproducing the exact models described in the model section.
