- Introduces a loss for directed conditional generative models extending MMD via conditional kernel mean embeddings.  
- Demonstrates method on classification, handwritten digit and face generation, and Bayesian model distillation.  
- Previous concerns (low-dimensional embedding scalability, relative utility, clarifications) appear addressed in the revision.  
- The link between Equation (2) and the weighted MMD in Remark 1 is unclear; dot notation (matrix product vs. Hadamard) needs explicit clarification and derivation.  
- The variable name ‘C’ is potentially confusing since it is already used for the conditional embedding and should be renamed or clarified.  
- Theorems 1 and 2 are properly cited, but Theorem 3 lacks a citation or included proof and needs a reference or proof in the paper.  
- Empirical results are promising and competitive with the original GMM for generative modeling.  
- Applying the model to supervised learning is acceptable as a versatility demonstration, despite earlier doubts.  
- The distilled Bayesian inference application is interesting but evaluated on only one dataset/model, so its generality is uncertain.  
- Recommend stronger empirical validation on more challenging datasets (e.g., CIFAR-10, LSUN) to encourage wider adoption.
