- Proposes a neural network method using a conditional maximum mean discrepancy (MMD) criterion.
- Evaluated on standard benchmark tasks for both discriminative and generative performance.
- Represents an interesting extension in deep generative modeling.
- Paper text is reasonably clear, though the mathematical notation can be hard to follow at times.
- Experimental tasks are relatively small-scale but comparable to recent work.
- Training involves computationally expensive steps; the reviewer requests comparisons of training time to baselines (e.g., DSN).
- Paper would be significantly strengthened by releasing reference code.
- Current model description may not be sufficient for practitioners to exactly reproduce the experiments.
