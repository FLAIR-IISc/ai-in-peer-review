- Proposes a neural network method based on a conditional maximum mean discrepancy (cMMD) criterion.  
- Evaluated on standard small benchmark tasks for both discriminative and generative performance.  
- Represents an interesting extension in deep generative model research.  
- Paper text is reasonably clear overall.  
- Mathematical exposition can be difficult to follow in places.  
- Benchmark tasks are modest but reasonable compared to recent work.  
- Training algorithm involves computationally expensive steps; authors should report training duration relative to methods like DSN.  
- Availability of reference code would significantly strengthen the paper.  
- Model section lacks detail that may hinder exact reproduction by practitioners.
