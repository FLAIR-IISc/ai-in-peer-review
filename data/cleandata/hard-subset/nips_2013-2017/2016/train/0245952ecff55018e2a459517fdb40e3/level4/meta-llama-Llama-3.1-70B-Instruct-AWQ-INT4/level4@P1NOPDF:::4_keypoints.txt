- Extends Generative Moment-Matching Networks to conditional generation via a kernel embedding of conditional moments.
- Derives an empirical estimator for the conditional moment matching objective; the derivation is non-obvious and contributes novelty.
- Demonstrates competitive performance on predictive modeling, contextual generation, and distillation from a Bayesian model.
- Technically sound and convincing overall, though the reviewer did not verify every equation in detail.
- Framework is straightforward to apply (illustrated by Algorithm 1) and likely useful to researchers for deep generative modeling and distillation.
- Paper is well written, clear, and supplemented by a helpful appendix.
