- Proposes a neural network approach for generative models based on a conditional maximum mean discrepancy criterion.  
- Evaluated on standard small benchmark tasks for discriminative and generative capabilities.  
- Paper is reasonably clear and concise, but the math can be difficult to follow at times.  
- Experimental tasks are somewhat small compared to recent work.  
- Training includes computationally expensive steps; authors should report training time and compare to methods like DSN.  
- Recommend releasing reference code to improve reproducibility and help practitioners reproduce models.  
- Connection between the proposed approach and existing generative-model literature is not clearly explained.  
- Experimental results require more thorough discussion and detailed analysis of strengths and weaknesses.  
- Overall interesting and relevant; recommend acceptance with revisions to address the above issues.
