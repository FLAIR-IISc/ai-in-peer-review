- Proposes a neural network approach using a conditional maximum mean discrepancy (MMD) criterion.  
- Evaluated on standard small benchmark tasks for both discriminative and generative performance.  
- Overall an interesting and relevant extension in deep generative modeling.  
- Paper is reasonably clear, though some mathematical derivations are difficult to follow.  
- Experimental tasks are somewhat small but comparable to recent work in the area.  
- Training involves computationally expensive steps; paper should report training time and compare to methods like DSN.  
- Recommend releasing reference code because current model details may be insufficient for exact reproduction.
