- Introduces a neural network model using a conditional maximum mean discrepancy (MMD) criterion for generative/discriminative tasks.
- Evaluated on standard, small-scale benchmark tasks with reasonable choices compared to recent work.
- Overall an interesting and worthwhile contribution to deep generative models.
- Paper is generally well-written but contains dense, hard-to-follow mathematical derivations.
- Experiments are small-scale, limiting assessment of scalability despite being reasonable benchmarks.
- Need to report computational cost: include training time analysis and direct comparison (e.g., vs DSN).
- Reproducibility concern: release source code or provide more implementation details to enable exact replication.
