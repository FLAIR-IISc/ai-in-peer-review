- Introduces a novel neural network method using a conditional maximum mean discrepancy (MMD) objective.  
- Evaluated on established benchmarks showing performance in both discriminative and generative tasks.  
- Represents a worthwhile contribution to deep learning research on generative models.  
- Writing is generally lucid, but the mathematical formulations may be challenging for some readers.  
- Experimental tasks are limited in scale, though comparable to recent related work.  
- Training algorithm appears computationally complex; discussion of training time relative to methods like DSN is needed.  
- Public release of implementation code is recommended to improve reproducibility and impact.
