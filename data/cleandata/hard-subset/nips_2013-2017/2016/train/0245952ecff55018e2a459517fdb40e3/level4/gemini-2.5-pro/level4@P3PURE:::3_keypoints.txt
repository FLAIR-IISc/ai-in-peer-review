- Introduces a conditional loss for directed generative models extending GMMNs via MMD and conditional kernel mean embeddings; demonstrated on classification, digit/face generation, and Bayesian knowledge distillation.  
- Authors have addressed prior minor concerns about latent-space dimensionality, scalability, comparative utility, and clarifications.  
- Remark 1: The derivation mapping Eq. (2) to the weighted MMD is unclear; the dot notation is ambiguous (matrix multiply vs. Hadamard) and needs a more detailed derivation.  
- Notation: Using 'C' for a constant conflicts with 'C' as the conditional embedding—use a different symbol to avoid ambiguity.  
- Theorem 3: No reference or proof provided; must cite a source or include the proof in the appendix.  
- Empirical results are decent and the supervised application is an acceptable demonstration of versatility.  
- Bayesian distillation result is interesting but shown on only one dataset/model, limiting assessment of general utility.  
- Method is most promising for pure generative modeling; digit/face results match GMMN but wider adoption requires stronger results on harder datasets (e.g., CIFAR-10, LSUN).
