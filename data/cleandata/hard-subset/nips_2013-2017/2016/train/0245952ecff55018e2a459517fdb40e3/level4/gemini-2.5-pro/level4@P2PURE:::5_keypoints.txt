Paper extends GMMNs to the conditional setting using RKHS embeddings (Song et al. 2009) and proposes CGMMN, evaluated on real datasets.

Major technical concern: the empirical conditional covariance operator \hat{C}_{Y|X} appears to omit mean-embedding terms (\mu_X,\mu_Y), implying RKHS zero-centering; authors should clarify formulation and gradient/implementation handling.

Experimental weakness: CGMMN shows higher predictive error than state-of-the-art CMMVA on both datasets.

Generative-quality unclear: no comparisons to other conditional generative models (e.g., conditional GANs), making sample-quality claims unconvincing.

Paper is novel and elegant in approach.

Manuscript contains minor typos ("Frobeniu norm", "an universal", "which the").

Update: authors addressed concerns satisfactorily in rebuttal and reviewer raised their score.
