- Proposes CGMMN: extends GMMN to model conditional distributions using a conditional maximum mean discrepancy (CMMD) based on conditional kernel mean embeddings.
- Novel and effective approach for learning conditional distributions.
- Provides a detailed, sound derivation of the CMMD loss and solid technical presentation.
- Thorough introduction and clear motivation, explaining limitations of GANs and MMD.
- Experimental results show competitive performance on predictive modeling, contextual generation, and Bayesian dark knowledge distillation.
- Compares favorably against GMMN, GANs, and VAEs.
- Paper is well-organized and clearly written.
- Notation (using dot for matrix multiplication) is confusing and should be clarified.
- Related work discussion is limited and needs to be expanded to cover strengths/limitations of existing approaches.
- Experimental evaluation is limited to a few datasets/models; more extensive and challenging experiments are needed.
- Scores: Quality 8/10, Clarity 9/10, Originality 8/10, Significance 8/10.
- Recommendation: Accept with minor revisions.
