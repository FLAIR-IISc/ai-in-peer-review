- Proposes a neural network approach based on a conditional maximum mean discrepancy criterion.  
- Evaluated for discriminative and generative performance on standard, small-scale benchmark tasks.  
- Represents an interesting contribution to deep generative modeling and is written with reasonable clarity.  
- Mathematical derivations are sometimes difficult to follow.  
- Experimental tasks are small in scale, though comparable to recent work in the area.  
- Training appears computationally expensive; reviewer requests reporting of training duration and comparison to methods like DSN.  
- Reproducibility concerns: reference code should be released and the model section is currently insufficient for exact replication.
