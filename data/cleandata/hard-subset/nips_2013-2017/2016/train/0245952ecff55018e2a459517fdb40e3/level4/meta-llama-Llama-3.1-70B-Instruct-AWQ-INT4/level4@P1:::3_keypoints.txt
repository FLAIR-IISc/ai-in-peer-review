- Proposes a new loss for directed conditional generative models, extending generative moment matching networks using conditional kernel mean embeddings.  
- Demonstrates method on classification, digit/face generation, and Bayesian model distillation with decent empirical results.  
- Paper gives a good related-work overview and clearly explains the approach; well-written and well-organized.  
- Authors addressed previous-review concerns (low-dimensional space choice and model comparisons).  
- Remark 1’s notation is unclear: mapping Equation (2) to a weighted MMD form needs more detail and clarification whether the dot means matrix multiplication or Hadamard product.  
- Reusing symbol "C" for the conditional embedding operator is confusing; recommend a different symbol.  
- Theorem 3 is unreferenced; provide a citation to a proof or include the proof.  
- Using the model for supervised learning may be suboptimal; authors acknowledge this limitation.  
- Distilled Bayesian inference application is interesting but requires more experiments across datasets and models to establish effectiveness.  
- Figures and tables are helpful and results are well-explained.  
- Overall a useful contribution to generative modeling that merits acceptance pending minor revisions.  
- Recommended fixes: clarify Eq.(2)→weighted MMD mapping, change conditional-operator notation, cite/prove Theorem 3, and add broader empirical evaluation.
