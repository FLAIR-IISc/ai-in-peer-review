- Proposes a neural network method based on a conditional maximum mean discrepancy (MMD) criterion.  
- Evaluated on standard small benchmark tasks to test discriminative and generative performance.  
- Overall an interesting and relevant extension in deep generative modeling.  
- Paper is reasonably clear, but some mathematical derivations are difficult to follow.  
- Experimental tasks are somewhat small but comparable to recent related work.  
- Training includes computationally expensive steps; the paper should report training time and compare to methods like DSN.  
- Reproducibility is limited; releasing reference code and more implementation details is strongly recommended.
