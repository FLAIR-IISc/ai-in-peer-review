- Proposes a neural network method grounded in a conditional maximum mean discrepancy (MMD) objective.  
- Evaluated on standard, relatively small benchmark tasks for both discriminative and generative performance.  
- Considered a noteworthy extension within deep generative modeling.  
- Writing is reasonably clear, but the mathematical exposition is sometimes hard to follow.  
- Experimental tasks are on the small side but reasonable compared to recent work.  
- Training algorithm includes computationally intensive steps; needs explicit wall-clock timing and efficiency comparison (e.g., vs DSN).  
- Paper would be stronger if reference code for experiments were provided.  
- Model section may lack sufficient detail for practitioners to faithfully reproduce the evaluated models.
