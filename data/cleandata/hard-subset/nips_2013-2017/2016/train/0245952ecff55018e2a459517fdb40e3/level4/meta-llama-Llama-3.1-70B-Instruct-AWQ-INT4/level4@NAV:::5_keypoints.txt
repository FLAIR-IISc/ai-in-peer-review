- Proposes CGMMN: extending GMMNs to match conditional distributions using RKHS embeddings (based on Song et al. 2009).
- Empirical evaluation conducted on multiple real-world datasets.
- Definition of \hat{C}_{Y|X} (Line 90) appears incomplete—missing terms involving μ_X and μ_Y (or their estimators).
- Assuming μ_X and μ_Y are zero (centering in RKHS) is unconventional and needs justification.
- Authors should clarify the missing term and its treatment in gradient computation and experiments.
- Method is innovative and elegant but experimental results are underwhelming.
- CGMMN yields higher error rates than state-of-the-art CMMVA on both datasets.
- No comparison to other conditional generative methods (e.g., conditional GANs), so sample-generation quality advantage is unclear.
- Manuscript contains minor typos: "Frobeniu norm", "an universal", "which the".
- Update: After the rebuttal, the authors addressed concerns and the reviewer revised scores.
