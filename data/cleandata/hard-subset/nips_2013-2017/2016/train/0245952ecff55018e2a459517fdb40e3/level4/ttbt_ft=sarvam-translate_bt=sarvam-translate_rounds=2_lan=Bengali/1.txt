This paper develops a neural network approach based on a conditional maximum-minimum discrepancy criterion. The proposed technique is evaluated on standard small benchmark tasks for discriminative and generative abilities. Overall, it is an interesting extension of the sub-field of deep learning research focused on model building. Like most papers in this field, following the mathematical derivation can sometimes be a bit difficult, but the text of the paper is reasonably clear. The tasks used in the experiment are somewhat small, but not entirely unreasonable compared to other recent work in this area. There are some computationally expensive steps involved in the algorithm involved in training the model, so I would like some comments in the experiment section on how long it takes to train the model, compared to existing methods like DSN. The paper would be further strengthened if reference code for the experiments were made available. A practitioner evaluating the model section may find it difficult to reproduce the exact models.
