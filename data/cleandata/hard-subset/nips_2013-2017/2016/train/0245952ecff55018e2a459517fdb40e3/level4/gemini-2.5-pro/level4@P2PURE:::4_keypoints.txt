- Introduces a conditional extension to Generative Moment-Matching Networks (GMMN).  
- Derives a kernel embedding for moments of the conditional distribution to enable optimization with the same machinery as the unconditional setting.  
- Demonstrates reasonably competitive performance on predictive modeling, contextual generation, and distilling Bayesian model predictions.  
- Paper appears technically sound, though not every equation was fully verified by the reviewer.  
- Novelty: extension is natural but the specific mechanism and empirical estimator derivation are non-trivial and constitute a useful contribution.  
- Algorithm 1 suggests the framework is straightforward to apply, making it relevant to researchers using deep generative models or distilling complex statistical processes.  
- Writing and presentation are clear and easy to follow; appendix provides comprehensive methodological and experimental details.
