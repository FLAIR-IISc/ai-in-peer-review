This paper develops a neural network approach based on a conditional maximum mean discrepancy (MMD) criterion. The proposed technique is evaluated on standard benchmark tasks to assess its discriminative and generative capabilities. Overall, this is an interesting extension within the sub-area of deep learning research focused on building generative models. The paper’s text is reasonably clear, although, as is common in this field, the mathematical notation can be challenging to follow at times. The tasks used in the experiments are relatively small in scale, but not unreasonable when compared to other recent work in the area. Given that the algorithm for training the model involves computationally expensive steps, I would appreciate comments in the experimental section regarding training time compared to existing approaches such as DSN. The paper would be significantly strengthened by the release of reference code for the experiments. Currently, a practitioner may have difficulty reproducing the exact models evaluated based on the information provided in the model section.
