- Proposes Conditional Generative Moment-Matching Networks (CGMMN) using a conditional MMD (CMMD) criterion and conditional kernel mean embeddings.  
- Extends the GMMN framework to model conditional distributions effectively.  
- Evaluated on classification (MNIST, SVHN), image generation (MNIST, Yale Faces), and Bayesian model distillation (Boston housing).  
- Experimental results are competitive for classification and produce visually appealing image samples.  
- Distillation from Bayesian models is an interesting and encouraging application.  
- Technical presentation is generally sound with a clear explanation of CMMD and CGMMN architecture.  
- Notation issues: Remark 1 is unclear and mapping to the weighted MMD form needs more detail.  
- Notation conflict: choice for the conditional embedding operator clashes with CMMD notation.  
- Theorem 3 is not properly referenced and lacks proof or a citation to an existing proof.  
- Evaluation is limited to relatively simple datasets; broader, more challenging benchmarks are missing.  
- Unclear how widely the approach will be adopted by the community.  
- Review scores: Originality 7/10, Quality 8/10, Clarity 8/10, Significance 8/10; recommendation: accept with minor revisions.
