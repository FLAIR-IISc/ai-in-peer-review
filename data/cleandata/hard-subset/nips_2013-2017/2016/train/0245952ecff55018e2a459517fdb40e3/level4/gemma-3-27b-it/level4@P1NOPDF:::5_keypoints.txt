- Paper extends GMMNs to match conditional distributions (CGMMN) by embedding conditionals in an RKHS using Song et al. (2009).
- Definition of empirical conditional operator ĤC_{Y|X} (Line 90) appears to omit terms involving estimated μ_X and μ_Y; the zero-mean RKHS assumption is unusual and needs clarification.
- Authors should explain how the omitted terms and centering assumption affect gradient computation and experimental setup.
- Approach is novel and clearly presented.
- Experimental results are disappointing: CGMMN has higher predictive error than state-of-the-art CMMVA on both datasets.
- Generative evaluation is incomplete—no comparisons to other conditional generative methods (e.g., cGANs) to judge sample quality.
- Contains minor typos (“Frobeniu norm”, “an universal”, “which the”).
- After rebuttal, the reviewer states all concerns were addressed and will revise scores.
