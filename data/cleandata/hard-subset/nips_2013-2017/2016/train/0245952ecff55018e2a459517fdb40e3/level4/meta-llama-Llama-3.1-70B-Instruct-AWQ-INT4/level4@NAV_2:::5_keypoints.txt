- Paper extends GMMNs to match conditional distributions using RKHS conditional embeddings (Song et al. 2009) and proposes CGMMN.  
- CGMMN architecture and conditional MMD (CMMD) are clearly explained; algorithm evaluated on several real datasets.  
- Authors provide a thorough overview of related work (GMMNs, MMD, kernel conditional embedding).  
- Missing term in estimator ĤC_{Y|X}: the component involving μ_X and μ_Y (their estimators) is omitted in the definition (Line 90).  
- Unusual and unclear assumption that μ_X and μ_Y are zero (data centered in RKHS); needs clarification for gradient computation and experiments.  
- Predictive performance is disappointing: CGMMN has higher error rates than state-of-the-art CMMVA on both datasets.  
- Generative performance is unclear: no comparisons to other conditional generative methods (e.g., conditional GANs), so sample quality advantage is unproven.  
- Paper contains typos (e.g., "Frobeniu norm", "an universal", "which the") that should be fixed.  
- Arguments for acceptance: novel/neat approach, thorough related work, experiments somewhat competitive.  
- Arguments against acceptance: underwhelming experimental results, minor technical issues to address, and typographical errors.  
- Overall reviewer rating: 7.
