- Extends Generative Moment-Matching Networks to conditional generation via a derived kernel embedding for conditional moments.  
- Enables optimization of conditional moment embedding using the same framework as the unconditional version.  
- Empirical estimator derivation is non-trivial despite the naturalness of the extension, offering novel contribution.  
- Demonstrates reasonably competitive performance on predictive modeling, contextual generation, and Bayesian prediction distillation.  
- Algorithm 1 shows the framework is straightforward to apply in practice.  
- Paper appears technically sound, though not all equations were checked in detail.  
- Likely useful to researchers in deep generative models and for distilling complex statistical processes.  
- Well-written, clear presentation with a helpful appendix detailing methods and experiments.
