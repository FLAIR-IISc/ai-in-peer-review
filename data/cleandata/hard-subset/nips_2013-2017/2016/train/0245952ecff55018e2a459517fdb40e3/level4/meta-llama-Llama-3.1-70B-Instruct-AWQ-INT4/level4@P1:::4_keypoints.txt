- Extends Generative Moment-Matching Networks to conditional generation via a kernel embedding of conditional moments optimized with the same machinery as the unconditional version.
- Derives an empirical estimator for the conditional moment embedding; the mechanism and derivation were not obvious, giving the work novelty.
- Demonstrates competitive performance on predictive modeling, contextual generation, and distillation of Bayesian model predictions.
- Paper is technically sound and the methodology is clearly and thoroughly explained (equations not fully checked by reviewer).
- Framework is easy to apply in practice (Algorithm 1) and likely useful to practitioners.
- Well written and easy to read, with a helpful appendix providing method and experiment details.
- Thorough discussion of related work and clear explanation of differences from existing methods.
