- Paper extends moment matching networks to conditional case, which is non-trivial compared to feeding conditionals into single-example-update models.
- Complexity stems from matching distributions of large mini-batches to many different inputs, where per-input distributions differ.
- Main weakness: paper jumps into advanced math without providing an intuitive explanation of the conditional formulation.
- Authors fail to clearly explain the algorithmic computations, efficiency, and assumptions underlying the optimized formula (Equation 2).
- The conditional objective lacks the intuitive, low-level explanation available for the non-conditional version.
- Reviewer requests an intuitive derivation of Equation 2.
- Reviewer requests a clear, step-by-step translation of Equation 2 into a practical algorithm.
- Some relevant details are in the supplement but are hard to follow and should be better integrated into the main paper for clarity.
