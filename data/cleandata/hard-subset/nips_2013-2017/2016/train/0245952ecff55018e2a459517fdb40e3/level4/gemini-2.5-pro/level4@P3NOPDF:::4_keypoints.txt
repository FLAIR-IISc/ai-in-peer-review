- Extends GMMN to the conditional setting, a significant and practical contribution.  
- Core technical innovation: derivation of a kernel embedding for conditional moments.  
- Elegant formulation lets the conditional model be trained with the same optimization machinery as unconditional GMMN.  
- Technical contributions are sound, well-motivated, and clearly presented; theoretical arguments are convincing.  
- Novel and non-trivial mechanism for conditional generation; empirical estimator via kernel embeddings is original and insightful.  
- Empirical evaluation is diverse (predictive modeling, contextual generation, Bayesian model distillation) and shows competitive, compelling results.  
- Procedure is practical and accessible—Algorithm 1 is straightforward to implement and useful for practitioners.  
- Paper is exceptionally well-written and clear; comprehensive appendix enhances reproducibility.
