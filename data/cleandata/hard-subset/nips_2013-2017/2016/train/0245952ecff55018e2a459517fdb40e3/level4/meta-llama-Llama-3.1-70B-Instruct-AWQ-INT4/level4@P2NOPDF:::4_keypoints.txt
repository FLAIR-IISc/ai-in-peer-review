- Extends Generative Moment-Matching Networks to conditional generation via kernel embedding of conditional moments.
- Derives an empirical estimator for the conditional moment embedding enabling optimization like the unconditional version.
- Shows competitive performance on predictive modeling, contextual generation, and distilling Bayesian model predictions.
- Technically sound and convincing overall, though not all equations were exhaustively checked.
- Novelty is reasonable: extension is natural but the estimator derivation is non-obvious and contributes new ideas.
- Framework is useful and relevant for researchers using deep generative models or distilling complex statistical procedures.
- Algorithm 1 demonstrates the method’s practical applicability and ease of use.
- Paper is well-written, clear, and accessible; appendix provides helpful additional methodological and experimental details.
- Overall a valuable contribution to deep generative modeling.
