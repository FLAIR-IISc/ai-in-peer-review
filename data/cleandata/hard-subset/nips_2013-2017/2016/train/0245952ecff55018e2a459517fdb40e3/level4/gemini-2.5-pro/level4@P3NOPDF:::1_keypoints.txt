- Proposes a neural network method based on conditional MMD, evaluated for discriminative and generative capabilities on benchmarks.
- Interesting contribution to deep generative modeling.
- Presentation is reasonably clear but some mathematical derivations are hard to follow.
- Experiments are on small-scale datasets, which limits scope though is comparable to related work.
- Computational cost is a concern—training appears expensive and the paper should report training time and compare to methods like DSN.
- Reproducibility is weak—reference code should be released and the model section needs more detail to enable exact replication.
