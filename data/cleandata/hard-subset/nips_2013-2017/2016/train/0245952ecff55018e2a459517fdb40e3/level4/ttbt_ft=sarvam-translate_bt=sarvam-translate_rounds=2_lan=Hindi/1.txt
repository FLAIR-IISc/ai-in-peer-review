This paper develops a neural network approach based on a conditional maximum mean discrepancy criterion. The proposed technique is evaluated on standard small benchmark tasks for differential and generative capabilities. Overall, this is an interesting extension of the sub-field of deep learning research that focuses on building generative models. Like most papers in this area, the text of this paper is quite clear, although the mathematics can sometimes be a little difficult to understand. The tasks used in the experiments are somewhat small, but not entirely unreasonable compared to other recent work in this area. Since the algorithm includes some computationally expensive steps to train the model, I would like some comments in the experiment section on how long it takes to train the model, compared to existing approaches such as DSN. The paper would be even better if reference code for the experiments were provided. From the model section, a practitioner may have difficulty reproducing the evaluated exact model.
