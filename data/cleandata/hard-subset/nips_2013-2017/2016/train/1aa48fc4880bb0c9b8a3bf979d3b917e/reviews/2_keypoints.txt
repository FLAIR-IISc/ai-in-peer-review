- Tackles minimizing convex–concave saddle problems and extends recent variance-reduction methods to this setting.
- Provides convergence analysis for the proposed methods.
- Shows the methods can be accelerated via an extension of the Catalyst framework.
- Extends the approach to monotone operators.
- Includes numerical results.
- Criticism: assuming strong convexity on both primal and dual problems is overly strong.
- Recommendation: strong convexity should be required on only the primal or only the dual.
- Recommendation: include more numerical experiments.
