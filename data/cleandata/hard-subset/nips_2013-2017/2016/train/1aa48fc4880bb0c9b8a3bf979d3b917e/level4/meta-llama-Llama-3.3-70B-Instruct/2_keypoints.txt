- Addresses convex-concave saddle point problems using recent variance reduction techniques.  
- Provides convergence analysis for the proposed methods.  
- Shows these methods can be efficiently accelerated via an extended catalyst framework.  
- Extends the approach to monotone operators.  
- Includes numerical results to validate the methods.  
- Criticism: assuming strong convexity in both primal and dual is overly restrictive.  
- Suggestion: more realistic assumption is strong convexity in either primal or dual only.  
- Recommendation: include additional numerical examples to strengthen empirical support.
