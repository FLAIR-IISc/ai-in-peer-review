Addresses minimizing convex-concave saddle point problems and extends recent variance reduction methods to this setting.

Provides convergence analysis and shows the proposed methods can be accelerated via an extension of the Catalyst framework.

Explores extensions to monotone operators and presents numerical results.

Criticizes the assumption of strong convexity on both primal and dual as overly restrictive.

Recommends assuming strong convexity on either the primal or the dual (but not necessarily both).

Suggests adding more numerical examples to better substantiate the methods' empirical performance.
