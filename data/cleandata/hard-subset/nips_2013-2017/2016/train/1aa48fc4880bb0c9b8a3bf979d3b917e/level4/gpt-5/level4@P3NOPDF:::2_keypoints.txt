- Strength: Claimed extension to monotone operators could broaden applicability beyond standard saddle problems.

- Weakness: Unclear novelty — hard to distinguish fundamental contributions from a synthesis of known variance-reduction and primal–dual methods.

- Weakness: Monotone-operator extension is underdeveloped; oracle/model assumptions and comparisons to operator-splitting baselines are unclear.
