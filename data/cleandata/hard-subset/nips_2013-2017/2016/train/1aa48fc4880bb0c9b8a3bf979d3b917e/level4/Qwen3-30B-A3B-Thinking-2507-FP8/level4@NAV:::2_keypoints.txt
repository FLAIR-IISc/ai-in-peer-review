- Addresses minimization of convex–concave saddle-point problems.  
- Extends recent variance-reduction techniques to this setting.  
- Provides convergence analysis for the proposed methods.  
- Shows efficient acceleration via an extension of the catalyst framework.  
- Includes extensions to monotone operators and presents supporting numerical results.  
- Criticism: assumes strong convexity on both primal and dual — too strong; should require strong convexity on either primal or dual only.  
- Suggestion: include additional numerical experiments.
