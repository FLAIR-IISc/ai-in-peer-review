- Extends variance-reduction methods to convex–concave saddle-point minimization.  
- Provides convergence analysis and demonstrates acceleration via a Catalyst extension.  
- Extends results to monotone operators and presents supporting numerical experiments.  
- Major methodological concern: assumes strong convexity on both primal and dual problems, which is overly restrictive; it should suffice on only one side.  
- Empirical concern: more and diverse numerical examples are needed to strengthen validation.
