- Tackles convex–concave saddle-point optimization and adapts stochastic variance-reduction methods (SVRG/SAGA) to this setting.
- Provides convergence analyses for the proposed variance-reduced saddle-point algorithms.
- Shows these methods can be accelerated via an extension of the Catalyst framework.
- Presents extensions to general monotone operators.
- Includes empirical results demonstrating the methods.
- Criticism: Assumes strong convexity–concavity on both primal and dual, which is quite restrictive.
- Suggestion: Prefer requiring strong curvature on only one side (either primal or dual).
- Suggestion: Additional experimental examples would strengthen the paper.
