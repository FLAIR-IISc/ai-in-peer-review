- Extends variance-reduction methods to convex–concave saddle-point problems.
- Provides convergence analyses for the proposed methods.
- Shows methods can be accelerated via an extended Catalyst framework.
- Includes extensions to monotone operator settings and presents numerical results.
- Critique: assuming strong convexity on both primal and dual is overly strong; one side should suffice.
- Suggestion: add more numerical experiments.
