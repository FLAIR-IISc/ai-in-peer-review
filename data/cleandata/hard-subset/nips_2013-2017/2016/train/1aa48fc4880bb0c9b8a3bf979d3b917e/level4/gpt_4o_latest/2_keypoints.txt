- Tackles convex-concave saddle point minimization and extends recent variance-reduction techniques.  
- Provides convergence analysis for the proposed methods.  
- Demonstrates acceleration via an extension of the catalyst framework.  
- Extends the approach to monotone operators.  
- Presents numerical results.  
- Critique: assumes strong convexity on both primal and dual, which is overly restrictive.  
- Suggestion: relax assumption to strong convexity on either primal or dual (not both) and include more numerical examples.
