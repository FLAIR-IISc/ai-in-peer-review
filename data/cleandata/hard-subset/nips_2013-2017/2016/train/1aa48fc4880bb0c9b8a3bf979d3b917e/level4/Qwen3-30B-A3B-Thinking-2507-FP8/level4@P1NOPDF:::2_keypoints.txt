- Extends recent variance reduction techniques to minimize convex-concave saddle problems.
- Provides convergence analysis for the proposed methods.
- Demonstrates acceleration via a straightforward extension of the Catalyst framework.
- Extends the approach to monotone operators.
- Includes numerical results validating the methods.
- Critique: Assumes strong convexity for both primal and dual problems—overly strong; only one side needs strong convexity.
- Suggestion: Numerical experiments should include more examples to better support the theoretical claims.
