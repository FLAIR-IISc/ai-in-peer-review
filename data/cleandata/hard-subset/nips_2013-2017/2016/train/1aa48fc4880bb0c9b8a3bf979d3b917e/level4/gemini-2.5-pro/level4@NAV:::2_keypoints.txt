- Extends variance reduction methods to the convex-concave saddle point problem.  
- Provides theoretical convergence analysis for the proposed algorithms.  
- Demonstrates acceleration via an extension of the Catalyst framework.  
- Generalizes the approach to monotone operators.

- Notes that standard literature typically assumes strong convexity in only the primal or the dual.  
- Recommendation: include a more comprehensive set of numerical experiments to better validate the methods.
