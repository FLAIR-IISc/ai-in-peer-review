- Main technical contribution: convergence analysis via monotone operator theory, allowing analyses not covered by standard convex minimization.
- Results extend to variational inequalities and broader problem classes.
- Method enables handling of non-separable loss functions and regularizers through saddle-point formulation.
- Novelty/significance: important advance for models with non-separable components.
