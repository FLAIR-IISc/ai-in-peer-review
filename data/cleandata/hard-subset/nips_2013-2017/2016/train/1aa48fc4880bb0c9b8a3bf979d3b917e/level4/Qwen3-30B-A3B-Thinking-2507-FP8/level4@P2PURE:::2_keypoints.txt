- Proposes variance-reduction methods for minimizing convex–concave saddle problems.  
- Provides convergence analysis for the proposed algorithms.  
- Demonstrates acceleration via an extension of the Catalyst framework.  
- Includes extensions to monotone operators.  
- Presents numerical results validating the methods.  
- Criticism: assumes both primal and dual problems are strongly convex, which is unnecessarily strong.  
- Suggestion: only one of primal or dual needs strong convexity; add more numerical examples to support claims.
