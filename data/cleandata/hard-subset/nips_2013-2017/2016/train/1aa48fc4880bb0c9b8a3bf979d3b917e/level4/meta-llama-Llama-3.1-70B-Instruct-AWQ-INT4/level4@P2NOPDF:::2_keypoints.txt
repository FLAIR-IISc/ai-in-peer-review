- Addresses convex-concave saddle point minimization and extends variance reduction methods to this setting.
- Provides convergence analysis for the proposed algorithms.
- Demonstrates that the methods can be accelerated using an extension of the Catalyst framework.
- Extends the approach to monotone operators.
- Presents numerical results illustrating the methods.
- Criticism: Assumes strong convexity on both primal and dual problems, which is overly restrictive.
- Suggestion: Relax assumption to strong convexity on either the primal or the dual (but not necessarily both).
- Suggestion: Include more numerical examples to better evaluate and illustrate performance.
