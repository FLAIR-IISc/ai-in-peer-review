- Addresses convex–concave saddle-point problems using recent variance-reduction techniques.  
- Provides convergence analysis for the proposed methods.  
- Shows how methods can be accelerated via an extension of the Catalyst framework.  
- Extends the approach to general monotone operators.  
- Includes numerical results validating the methods.  
- Criticism: assumes strong convexity on both primal and dual sides, which is overly restrictive.  
- Suggestion: relax assumption to require strong convexity on only the primal or only the dual.  
- Suggestion: include additional numerical examples to strengthen empirical validation.
