- Extends variance-reduction methods to convex–concave saddle-point problems, with convergence analysis.  
- Shows acceleration via a Catalyst-like framework and provides an extension to monotone operators.  
- Presents supporting numerical results demonstrating the methods.  
- Major limitation: assumes strong convexity in both primal and dual problems, which is overly restrictive compared to the more common one-sided strong convexity assumption.  
- Recommendation: include additional numerical examples to better validate and illustrate the proposed methods.
