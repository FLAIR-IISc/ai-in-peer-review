- Studies convex–concave saddle-point optimization and extends recent variance-reduction techniques to this setting.
- Presents convergence analyses for the proposed methods and shows they can be accelerated via an extension of the Catalyst framework.
- Extends the approach to monotone operators and includes numerical results.
- Major concern: requires strong convexity on both primal and dual sides, which is overly restrictive.
- More appropriate assumption would be strong convexity on only one side (either primal or dual).
- Suggests adding more numerical examples to strengthen empirical validation.
