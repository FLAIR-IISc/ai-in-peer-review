- Extends variance reduction methods to convex-concave saddle point minimization problems.
- Provides convergence analysis demonstrating the methods' effectiveness.
- Demonstrates acceleration via an extension of the Catalyst framework.
- Includes extensions to monotone operators and numerical results supporting the theory.
- Imposes strong convexity on both primal and dual problems, which is overly restrictive.
- Recommends relaxing assumptions to strong convexity on either primal or dual (not necessarily both) to increase applicability.
- Numerical experiments are limited; more diverse examples are needed to show robustness and efficiency.
- Overall a valuable contribution that could have greater impact after addressing these concerns.
