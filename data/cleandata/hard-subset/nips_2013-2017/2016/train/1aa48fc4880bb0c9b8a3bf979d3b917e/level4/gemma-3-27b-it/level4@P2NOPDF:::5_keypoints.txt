- Adapts analysis of variance-reduced methods to the non-convex landscape of saddle point problems.
- Reframes saddle point problems as finding zeros of monotone operators, extending the approach to variational inequalities.
- Significance: addresses common ML settings with non-separable losses/regularizers where existing methods converge slowly.
- Achieves linear convergence, offering substantial practical improvement over prior methods.
