- Extends variance-reduction methods to convex–concave saddle-point problems with convergence analysis.
- Shows acceleration via an extension of the Catalyst framework.
- Extends results to general monotone operators and presents supporting numerical results.
- Major concern: assumes strong convexity in the primal and strong concavity in the dual simultaneously, which is very restrictive.
- Suggested improvement: extend analysis to the more practical one-sided strong-convexity setting (only primal OR dual).
- Experimental limitation: numerical evaluation is limited and should include broader, more diverse benchmarks to convincingly demonstrate practical advantages.
