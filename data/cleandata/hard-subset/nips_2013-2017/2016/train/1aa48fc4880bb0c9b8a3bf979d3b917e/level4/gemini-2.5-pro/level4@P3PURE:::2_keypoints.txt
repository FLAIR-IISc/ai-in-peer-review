- Extends variance reduction methods to convex–concave saddle-point problems.
- Provides convergence analysis for the proposed algorithms.
- Proposes an accelerated variant via an extension of the catalyst framework.
- Extends the approach to monotone operators.
- Presents numerical results.
- Major limitation: assumes strong convexity in both primal and dual, which is a strong and uncommon requirement.
- Empirical evaluation is limited and would benefit from more comprehensive numerical examples.
