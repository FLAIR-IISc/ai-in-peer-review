- Tackles minimizing convex-concave saddle point problems and extends recent variance-reduction methods to that setting.  
- Provides convergence analysis for the proposed algorithms.  
- Shows the methods can be accelerated via an extension of the Catalyst framework.  
- Discusses extensions to monotone operators.  
- Includes numerical results demonstrating the proposed methods.  
- Criticism: assuming strong convexity on both primal and dual is overly restrictive.  
- Suggestion: a more realistic assumption is strong convexity on either the primal or the dual (but not necessarily both).  
- Recommendation: strengthen the paper with additional numerical examples.
