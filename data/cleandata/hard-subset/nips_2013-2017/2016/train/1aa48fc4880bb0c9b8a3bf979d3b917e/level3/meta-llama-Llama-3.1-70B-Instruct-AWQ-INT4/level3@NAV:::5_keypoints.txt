- Extends SVRG/SAGA to handle non-separable loss functions and regularizers via a saddle-point formulation.  
- Proves convergence using a monotone-operator framework that generalizes to variational inequality problems.  
- Proposes an acceleration technique via a simple "catalyst" extension that outperforms accelerated batch algorithms.  
- First-linearly-convergent claim and algorithmic extensions represent a novel contribution to the field.
