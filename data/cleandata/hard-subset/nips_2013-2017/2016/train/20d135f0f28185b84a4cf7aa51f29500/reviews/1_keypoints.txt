- Proposes a new stochastic gradient descent algorithm to train ensemble models.
- Builds on Guzman-Rivera et al. [8], using ensemble loss equal to the best single-classifier output to encourage diversity.
- Main contribution: using SGD enables the method to scale to deep neural networks.
- Paper is interesting and clearly written.
- Major concern: the algorithmic novelty may be only incremental compared to [8].
- Reviewer is convinced the small change makes the algorithm more useful and favors acceptance.
- Suggests comparing with the MCL algorithm when using batches (rather than the entire dataset).
- Requests more analysis of the algorithm’s "diversity" effect.
