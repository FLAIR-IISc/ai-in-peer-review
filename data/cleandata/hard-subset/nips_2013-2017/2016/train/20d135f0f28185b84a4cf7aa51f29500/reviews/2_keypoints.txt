- Proposes training ensembles by using an oracle per instance to select the optimal member and backprop only on that network.  
- Encourages specialization, yielding ensembles that cover diverse outputs.  
- Strong empirical results across multiple domains; well-presented and experimentally thorough.  
- Major omission: does not address how to exploit or use the trained ensemble at inference without the oracle.  
- Practical usability limited because exploitation strategies are problem-specific and not explored.  
- Presentation issue: Fig. 2 pseudocode is embedded as a raster image instead of real text or a vector graphic.
