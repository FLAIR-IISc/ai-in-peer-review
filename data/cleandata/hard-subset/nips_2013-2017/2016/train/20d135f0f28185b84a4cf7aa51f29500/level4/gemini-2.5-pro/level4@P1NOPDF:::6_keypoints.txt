1. Proposal: novel ensemble training that updates only the single ensemble member with lowest loss per example; promising results and weak-accept recommendation pending clarifications.

2. Testing-phase clarity: unclear how model selection/aggregation is done at inference without ground-truth; authors must clarify test-time prediction procedure.

3. Undefined baseline: the "independent/regular ensembles" baseline (Indp.) is not defined; its construction must be explicitly described.

4. Specialization claim unjustified: per-example updates do not necessarily imply per-class specialization; need analysis of per-class update distributions or an explicit specialization objective.

5. Missing related work: paper omits discussion and comparison to diversity-regularization methods that promote ensemble member differences.
