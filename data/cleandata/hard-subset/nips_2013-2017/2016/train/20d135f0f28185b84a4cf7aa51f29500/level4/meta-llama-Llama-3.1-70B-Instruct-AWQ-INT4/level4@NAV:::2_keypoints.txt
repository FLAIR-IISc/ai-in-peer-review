- Introduces a novel oracle-guided backpropagation method to train DNN ensembles, letting the oracle select which member to update per instance.  
- Oracle-guided training promotes specialization of ensemble members and broader coverage of outputs of interest.  
- Authors demonstrate strong empirical performance across multiple domains.  
- Presentation is clear and experiments are comprehensive; the work is a strong contribution that merits publication.  
- Major missing discussion: how to exploit or apply the trained ensembles at inference time—beyond producing diverse outputs or matching top-k metrics.  
- Omission of practical application/use strategies hinders the practical usability of the approach, though this may be problem-specific.  
- Minor suggestion: replace the raster image of pseudo-code in Fig. 2 with typeset code or a vector image for better readability.
