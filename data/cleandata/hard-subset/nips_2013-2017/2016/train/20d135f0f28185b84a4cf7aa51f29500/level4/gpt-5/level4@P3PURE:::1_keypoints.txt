- Proposes an SGD algorithm for training ensembles based on Guzman-Rivera et al., using ensemble loss equal to the best classifier’s loss to encourage diversity and enable deep nets.
- Paper is interesting and clearly written.
- Main concern: the algorithmic novelty may be incremental relative to Guzman-Rivera et al. [8].
- Reviewer accepts the authors’ argument that the change substantially improves practicality and usefulness, and is in favor of acceptance.
- Requests an additional comparison evaluating the MCL algorithm with mini-batches rather than the entire dataset.
- Requests more analysis of the resulting “diversity” effect.
