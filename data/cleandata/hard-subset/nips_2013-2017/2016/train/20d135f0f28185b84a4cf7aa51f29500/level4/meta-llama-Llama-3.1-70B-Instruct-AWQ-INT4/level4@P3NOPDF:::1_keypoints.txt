- Proposes a novel SGD algorithm for training ensemble models, building on Guzman-Rivera et al. [8].  
- Key insight: treat the ensemble loss as the loss of the best output of a single classifier to encourage diverse classifiers.  
- Primary contribution: applying SGD makes the method applicable to deep neural networks.  
- Paper is well-written and presents an interesting approach.  
- Main concern: algorithmic novelty may appear incremental relative to [8].  
- Reviewer conclusion: modifications sufficiently improve usefulness, inclining them to support acceptance.  
- Suggestion: evaluate the MCL algorithm with mini-batches rather than using the entire dataset.  
- Suggestion: provide deeper analysis of the "diversity" effect, as it is crucial to the approach.  
- Overall: promising contribution that would benefit from additional experiments and analysis to increase impact.
