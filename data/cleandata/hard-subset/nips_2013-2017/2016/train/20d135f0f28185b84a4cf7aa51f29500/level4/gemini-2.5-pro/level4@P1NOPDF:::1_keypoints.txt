- Introduces an SGD algorithm for training ensembles based on Guzman-Rivera et al.’s ensemble loss (loss of the best individual) to encourage diversity.  
- Main contribution: adapting that objective to an SGD framework, enabling application to large-scale models like deep neural networks.  
- Paper is interesting and clearly written.  
- Primary reservation: algorithmic novelty may be incremental relative to Guzman-Rivera et al. [8].  
- Authors convincingly argue the adaptation substantially expands applicability and practical utility, especially for deep nets.  
- Reviewer recommends acceptance.  
- Suggestion 1: compare to the original MCL algorithm trained with mini-batches rather than the full dataset.  
- Suggestion 2: provide a more in-depth analysis and discussion of the diversity effect induced by the training strategy.
