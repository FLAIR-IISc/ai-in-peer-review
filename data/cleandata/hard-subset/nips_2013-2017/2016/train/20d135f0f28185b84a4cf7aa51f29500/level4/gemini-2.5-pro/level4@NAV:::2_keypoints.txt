- Proposes oracle-guided training for ensembles: an oracle selects the optimal member for each training instance.  
- Applies backpropagation only to the single selected network per instance.  
- Method encourages specialization, yielding ensembles with broad coverage of the output space.  
- Evaluated on multiple domains, demonstrating strong overall performance.  
- Paper is well-written with comprehensive experiments and is a valuable contribution warranting publication.  
- Major limitation: no developed strategy for inference-time use when the oracle is unavailable.  
- Notes compatibility with top-k metrics but lacks practical guidance for exploiting the ensemble at test time.  
- Minor suggestion: Figure 2 pseudo-code is a raster image; recommend typesetting or using a vector graphic.
