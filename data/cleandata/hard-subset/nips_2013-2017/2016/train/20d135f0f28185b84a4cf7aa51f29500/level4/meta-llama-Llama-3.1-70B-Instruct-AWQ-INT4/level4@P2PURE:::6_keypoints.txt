- Proposes a simple ensemble training method where for each training example only the member with lowest loss is updated; shows surprisingly strong experimental results and addresses some deficiencies of existing methods (weak accept).

- Testing procedure is unclear and appears to use ground-truth labels to select the best member (line 159, p.5); this needs clarification.

- The baseline called "independent ensembles" or "regular ensembles" (denoted "Indp." in figures and line 181, p.5) is not explained.

- The paper claims ensemble member specialization to different classes, but per-example reassignment during training does not fully justify this claim.

- Recommend incorporating explicit specialization into the loss function to support the specialization claim.

- Overlooks related work on diversity regularization in neural networks (e.g., Chen et al.), which should be discussed and evaluated.
