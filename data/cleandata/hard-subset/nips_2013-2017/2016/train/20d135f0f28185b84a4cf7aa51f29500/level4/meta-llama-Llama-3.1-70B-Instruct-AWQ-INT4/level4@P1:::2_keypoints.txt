- Proposes a novel oracle-guided training method that routes backprop updates to the network with the best output for a given true label.
- Oracle-guided updates enforce specialization among ensemble members so the ensemble covers diverse outputs of interest.
- Demonstrates strong empirical performance across multiple domains with comprehensive experiments.
- Paper is clearly written and well-organized.
- Main weakness: paper does not develop how to exploit or use the trained ensemble at inference beyond noting compatibility with top-k metrics.
- Minor issue: pseudo-code in Figure 2 should be replaced with actual code or embedded as a vector graphic.
- Recommendation: accept with minor revisions.
- Required minor revisions: provide guidance/strategies for exploiting the trained ensemble in practice; replace pseudo-code with code or vectorial figure.
