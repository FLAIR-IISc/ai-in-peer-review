- Proposes an ensemble training algorithm where only the single member with lowest loss for each training example is active and updated.  
- Despite its simplicity, the method yields strong empirical results; reviewer recommends weak acceptance.  
- Testing procedure is unclear; reviewer infers ground-truth labels may be used to select the best member at test time (line 159).  
- The baseline “independent ensembles” (denoted “Indp.”) is referenced but not defined or explained.  
- The claim of member specialization by output class is questionable given per-example reassignment; consider enforcing specialization via a modified loss.  
- Paper fails to discuss relevant diversity-regularization literature in neural networks (e.g., Chen et al.).
