This paper introduces a new stochastic gradient descent (SGD) algorithm for training ensemble models. The authors extend the approach of Guzman-Rivera et al. [8], which minimizes the loss of the best-performing classifier within an ensemble to encourage diversity. Their key contribution lies in adapting this loss function for training with SGD, thereby enabling its application to deep neural networks. The paper is well-written and presents an interesting approach. My primary concern revolves around the incremental nature of the algorithm’s novelty compared to that of [8]. However, the authors successfully demonstrate that this modification significantly enhances the algorithm's practical utility, leading me to recommend acceptance. A compelling addition would be a comparison of the proposed method to the MCL algorithm when utilizing mini-batches instead of the full dataset. Furthermore, I would appreciate a more in-depth analysis of the observed “diversity” effect.
