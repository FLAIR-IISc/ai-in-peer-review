- Introduces oracle-guided training: for each example, the oracle selects the ensemble member with lowest loss and backpropagates only through that member.  
- Method induces automatic specialization among ensemble members, producing diverse hypotheses that collectively cover plausible outputs.  
- Demonstrated across multiple domains with strong empirical performance, especially effective in classification.  
- Presentation is clear and experiments are comprehensive; overall assessment: a solid submission meriting acceptance.  
- Major missing piece: paper does not develop practical test-time selection methods when an oracle is unavailable, limiting deployment guidance beyond noting top-k alignment.  
- Minor: Figure 2 pseudocode is rasterized—should be typeset or provided as vector graphics.
