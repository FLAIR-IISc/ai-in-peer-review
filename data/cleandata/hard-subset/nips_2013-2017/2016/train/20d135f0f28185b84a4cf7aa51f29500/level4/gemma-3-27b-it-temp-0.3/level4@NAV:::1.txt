This paper introduces a novel stochastic gradient descent (SGD) algorithm for training ensemble models. The work extends the approach of Guzman-Rivera et al. [8], which focuses on minimizing the loss of the best-performing classifier within an ensemble to promote diversity. The key contribution here is adapting this loss function for use with SGD, thereby enabling its application to deep neural networks. The paper is well-written and presents a compelling idea. My primary reservation concerns the degree of novelty relative to the foundational work in [8]. However, the authors successfully demonstrate the practical benefit of their incremental modification, leading me to recommend acceptance. To further strengthen the work, I suggest a comparative analysis of the proposed method against the MCL algorithm when employing mini-batch training. Additionally, a more in-depth investigation into the observed “diversity” effect would be valuable.
