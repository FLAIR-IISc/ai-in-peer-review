- Novelty: updates only the single ensemble member with lowest loss per training example; simple method with strong empirical results — reviewer recommends weak accept.

- Test-time inference unclear: paper implies best-member selection using ground-truth, which is impossible at test time and must be clarified.

- Baseline definition missing: "independent ensembles" (Indp.) is referenced but not defined or cited, making comparisons hard to interpret.

- Specialization claim questionable: per-example member selection does not obviously produce stable, class-specific experts; needs justification or an explicit specialization term in the loss.

- Missing related work: no discussion/comparison with diversity-regularization methods (e.g., Chen et al.), which are directly relevant and would strengthen the paper.
