- Novelty is incremental: using MCL is straightforward and the method largely resembles a neural-network implementation of prior work [8] with minor modifications.  
- Presentation and technical detail are insufficient: Section 3 is overly condensed and the paper fails to clearly distinguish the framework from established multi-task training methods.  
- Experiments are weak: evaluation across tasks shows only modest gains and lacks strong baselines; testing on PASCAL VOC 2012 and comparisons to stronger baselines are recommended.
