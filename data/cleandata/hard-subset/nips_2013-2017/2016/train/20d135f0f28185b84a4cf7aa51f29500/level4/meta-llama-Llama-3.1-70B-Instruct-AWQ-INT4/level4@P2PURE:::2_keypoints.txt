- Proposes an oracle-guided training method that selects the optimal network per instance and backpropagates only to that network, encouraging ensemble member specialization.  
- Demonstrates impressive results across multiple domains.  
- Method appears to enforce specialization effectively in classification tasks.  
- Paper presentation is clear and experiments are broad.  
- Lacks a thorough discussion of how to exploit/use the trained ensemble at inference time beyond brief mention of top-k relevance.  
- The missing deployment/exploitation discussion is important for practical impact, though likely problem-specific.  
- Figure 2 embeds pseudo-code as a raster image; recommend including pseudo-code as text or a vector image for readability.  
- Overall contribution is significant in its current form.
