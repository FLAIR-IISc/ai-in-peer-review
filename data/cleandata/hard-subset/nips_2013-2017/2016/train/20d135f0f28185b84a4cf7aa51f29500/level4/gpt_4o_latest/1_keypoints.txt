- Introduces a novel stochastic gradient descent algorithm for training ensemble models.  
- Builds on Guzman-Rivera et al. [8], using ensemble loss equal to the best-performing single classifier to encourage diversity.  
- Main contribution: applying SGD to extend this ensemble approach to deep neural networks.  
- Paper is well-written and engaging.  
- Concern that the algorithm’s novelty may be only an incremental improvement over [8].  
- Authors convincingly show the modification substantially increases the algorithm’s practical utility, supporting acceptance.  
- Recommend comparing MCL performance with batch-based training versus using the entire dataset.  
- Request a more thorough analysis of the algorithm’s effect on classifier “diversity.”
