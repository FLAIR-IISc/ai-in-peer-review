This paper introduces a stochastic gradient descent (SGD) training procedure for ensemble models. It builds on Guzman-Rivera et al. [8], where the ensemble loss is defined as the loss of the best-performing classifier within the ensemble, which induces diversity among members. The main contribution is to train this objective with SGD, enabling practical use with deep neural networks. The paper is interesting and clearly written.

My primary concern is that the algorithmic novelty may be only incremental relative to [8]. However, the authors convincingly argue that this seemingly small change makes the approach much more practical and useful, so I am inclined to recommend acceptance.

I believe an informative comparison would be to evaluate the MCL algorithm with mini-batch training (rather than over the entire dataset). I would also like to see more analysis of the resulting “diversity” effect.
