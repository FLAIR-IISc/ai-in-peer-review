- sMCL offers only incremental novelty, effectively a neural-network implementation of prior MCL ([8]) with minor modifications.  
- Presentation is unclear and compressed (Section 3), and the paper fails to clearly distinguish the proposed framework from existing multi-task training methods.  
- Experimental evaluation uses weak baselines and shows marginal gains; needs stronger baselines (e.g., PASCAL VOC 2012) to validate efficacy.
