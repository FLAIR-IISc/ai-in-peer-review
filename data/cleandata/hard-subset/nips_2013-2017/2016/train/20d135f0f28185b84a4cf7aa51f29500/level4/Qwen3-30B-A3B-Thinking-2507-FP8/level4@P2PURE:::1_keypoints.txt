- Introduces a stochastic gradient descent (SGD) algorithm for training ensemble models.  
- Extends Guzman-Rivera et al. [8], where ensemble loss is defined by the best-performing single classifier to encourage diversity.  
- Key contribution: adapts that framework to SGD, enabling application to deep neural networks.  
- Paper is well-motivated, clearly written, and presents an interesting method.  
- Primary concern: algorithmic novelty may be incremental relative to [8].  
- Authors convincingly show that the modification significantly improves practicality, leading to support for acceptance.  
- Recommendation: include a comparison of the MCL algorithm under mini-batch versus full-batch training to assess scalability.  
- Recommendation: provide a more thorough quantitative analysis of the diversity effect to strengthen claims.
