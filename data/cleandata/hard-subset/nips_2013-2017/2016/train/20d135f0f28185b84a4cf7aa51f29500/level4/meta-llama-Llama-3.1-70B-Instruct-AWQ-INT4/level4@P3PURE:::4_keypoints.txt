- Paper proposes a stochastic Multiple Choice Learning (sMCL) SGD method applied to classification, segmentation, and captioning.
- Novelty is limited and incremental; essentially a neural-network adaptation of prior work [8] with minor adjustments.
- Motivation for MCL is clear but does not constitute a significant new contribution.
- Manuscript is poorly written; Section 3 (technical details) is only one page, raising concerns about explanatory thoroughness.
- The paper fails to clearly distinguish the proposed framework from common alternative multi-task training methods.
- Experiments use multiple vision tasks but lack strong baselines and show only small performance gains.
- Authors should run comparisons on PASCAL VOC 2012 to benchmark against more robust baselines.
