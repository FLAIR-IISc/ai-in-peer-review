- Introduces a novel training paradigm for ensembles using an instance-specific oracle to select the optimal member per training sample.  
- Backpropagation is applied only to the selected network, promoting specialization and full coverage of the target output space.  
- Demonstrates strong performance across diverse domains with clear presentation and thorough experimental validation.  
- Makes a significant contribution to ensemble learning, particularly for classification tasks where specialization is important.  
- Lacks discussion of practical deployment and usage of the trained ensemble beyond theoretical alignment with top-k metrics (e.g., ImageNet).  
- Figure 2 pseudocode is a raster image; embedding the pseudocode or using a vector format would improve reproducibility.  
- Overall a strong paper deserving publication, with minor revisions to address deployment considerations and figure formatting.
