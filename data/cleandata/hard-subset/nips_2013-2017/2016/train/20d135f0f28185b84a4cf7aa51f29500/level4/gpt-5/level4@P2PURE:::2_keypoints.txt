- Proposes training ensembles with an oracle that selects the best member per instance and applies backprop only to that member.
- Encourages specialization of ensemble members so the ensemble collectively covers outputs of interest.
- Demonstrated strong empirical performance across multiple domains.
- Well-presented, interesting idea that the reviewer views as a strong contribution meriting publication.
- Major omission: lacks discussion of how to exploit/use the trained ensemble at inference time, limiting practical applicability.
- Minor presentation issue: Figure 2 pseudo-code is rasterized — should be typeset or provided as a vector graphic.
