- Proposes a novel training method where an oracle selects the single best ensemble member per instance and only that network is backpropagated, encouraging specialization.  
- Method evaluated across multiple domains and shows impressive overall performance.  
- Review praises the idea as interesting and effective, with excellent presentation and comprehensive experiments; recommends publication.  
- Main reservation: paper lacks discussion of how to utilize the specialized ensemble at inference time (no strategy for selecting/combining models), limiting practical applicability.  
- Authors note top-k consistency but do not explore it; a test-time selection/combination strategy is a crucial missing piece for an end-to-end approach.  
- Suggests deployment/selection at inference could be future work, separate from the training contribution.  
- Minor presentation issue: pseudo-code in Figure 2 is rasterized and should be typeset or provided as a vector graphic for clarity.
