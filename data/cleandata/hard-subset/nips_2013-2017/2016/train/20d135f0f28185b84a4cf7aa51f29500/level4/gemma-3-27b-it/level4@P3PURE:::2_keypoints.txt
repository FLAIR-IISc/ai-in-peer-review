- Proposes a novel oracle-guided training method where an oracle selects the best network per instance and backpropagation updates only that network.  
- The approach encourages specialization among ensemble members, enabling broad coverage of outputs.  
- Demonstrates strong empirical performance across diverse domains, especially effective in classification tasks.  
- Presentation is excellent and experiments are comprehensive.  
- Overall contribution is substantial and the paper is deserving of publication.  
- Major omission: the paper does not address how to exploit or use the trained ensemble in practice beyond noting potential for diverse/top-k outputs.  
- The lack of practical deployment/exploitation strategies is a significant, problem-specific gap.  
- Minor issue: Fig. 2’s pseudo-code is embedded as raster images; it should be included as text or a vector image for clarity.
