- Proposes adapting Guzman-Rivera et al.'s MCL (ensemble loss = loss of best classifier) to stochastic gradient descent, making it scalable to deep neural networks.  
- Primary contribution is the scalable SGD adaptation of the original MCL approach.  
- Paper is interesting and clearly written.  
- Main concern: algorithmic novelty may be incremental over [8], though authors argue the modification significantly increases utility and applicability.  
- Reviewer recommends acceptance.  
- Suggested experiment: compare the original MCL trained on mini-batches (rather than the full dataset) to the proposed SGD variant.  
- Suggested analysis: provide a more in-depth, quantitative analysis of diversity among ensemble classifiers.
