- Proposes an ensemble training method that activates and updates only the single lowest-loss member per training example, yielding strong empirical results and meriting weak acceptance.  
- Testing/inference procedure is ambiguous and may improperly use true labels to select ensemble members.  
- The baseline labeled "independent"/"regular" ensembles (Indp.) is not defined or justified.  
- The paper's claim that members "specialize" to different classes is unsupported by the per-example reassignment mechanism and lacks explicit loss-based encouragement.  
- Related work on diversity regularization in neural networks (e.g., Chen et al.) is overlooked and should be discussed for theoretical grounding.
