- Proposes oracle-guided training where an oracle selects the best ensemble member per instance and backpropagates only to that network.  
- Method induces specialization among ensemble members, enabling broader coverage of outputs.  
- Demonstrates strong empirical results across multiple benchmark datasets.  
- Paper is well-written, clear, and conceptually interesting.  
- Limited discussion and experimentation on inference strategies for combining specialized networks' predictions.  
- Lacks analysis of how to leverage specialization during prediction (e.g., weighted averaging, stacking).  
- Missing investigation of specialization’s effects on ensemble diversity and robustness.  
- Would benefit from scenarios showing complementary strengths where the ensemble outperforms any single model.  
- Presentation issue: Figure 2 pseudo-code is rasterized, harming readability and copy-paste.  
- Overall assessment: strong accept (8/10), potentially outstanding with more work on ensemble utilization.
