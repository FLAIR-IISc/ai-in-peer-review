- Proposes an oracle-guided training method where an oracle selects the best network per instance and only that network is updated via backprop.
- Oracle-guided updates encourage specialization among ensemble members, yielding diverse coverage of possible outputs.
- Empirical results show strong, cross-domain performance and effective ensembles.
- Paper is clearly presented and experiments are well-conducted; contribution is significant and publication-worthy.
- Major omission: the paper does not sufficiently explore how to exploit/use the trained ensemble at inference/deployment time beyond a top-k metric.
- This exploitation gap is important for practical adoption but may be problem-specific.
- Minor suggestion: improve Figure 2 readability by embedding a vector image or including pseudo-code directly.
