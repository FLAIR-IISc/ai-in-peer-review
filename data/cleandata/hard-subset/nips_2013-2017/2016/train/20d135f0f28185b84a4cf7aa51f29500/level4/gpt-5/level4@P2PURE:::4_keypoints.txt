- Novelty is incremental: sMCL is essentially a neural-network instantiation of prior work ([8]) with minor adjustments, so MCL motivation isn’t a new main contribution.
- Paper is poorly written and under-specified: Section 3 is very short and it’s unclear how sMCL differs from common alternative joint-training strategies.
- Experiments are weak: lack strong baselines and show small gains; authors should evaluate on PASCAL VOC 2012 and compare to stronger baselines.
