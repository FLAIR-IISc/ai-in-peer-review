This paper proposes a stochastic gradient descent (SGD) algorithm for training ensemble models, building on Guzman-Rivera et al. [8]. In that framework, the ensemble loss is defined as the loss of the best-performing classifier in the ensemble, which yields a diverse set of classifiers. The contribution here is to train this objective with SGD, enabling application to deep neural networks. The paper is interesting and clearly written.

My main concern is that the algorithmic novelty may be only incremental relative to [8]. However, the authors make a convincing case that this seemingly small change substantially improves practicality and usefulness, so I am in favor of acceptance.

I would encourage an additional comparison evaluating the MCL algorithm when mini-batches are used (rather than the entire dataset). I would also like to see more analysis of the resulting “diversity” effect.
