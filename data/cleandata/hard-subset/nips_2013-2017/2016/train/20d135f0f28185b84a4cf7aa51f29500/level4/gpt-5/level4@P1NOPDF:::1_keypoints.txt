- Proposes an SGD-based training procedure for ensemble models using the Guzman-Rivera et al. objective that uses per-example best-classifier loss to encourage diversity.  
- Main contribution: demonstrates this objective can be effectively optimized with SGD, enabling use with deep neural networks.  
- Paper is interesting and clearly written.  
- Primary concern: algorithmic novelty may be incremental relative to Guzman-Rivera et al.  
- Reviewer is persuaded that the modification makes the approach much more practical and recommends acceptance.  
- Requests additional experiments: evaluate MCL under mini-batch training rather than full-dataset updates.  
- Requests more analysis of the resulting “diversity” effect among ensemble members.
