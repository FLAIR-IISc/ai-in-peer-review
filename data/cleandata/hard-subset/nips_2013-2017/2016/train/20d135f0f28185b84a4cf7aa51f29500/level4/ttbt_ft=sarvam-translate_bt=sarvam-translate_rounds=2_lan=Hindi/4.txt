The author has devised a Stochastic gradient descent method with multiple-choice learning. The author claims that the proposed framework has advantages in several tasks such as classification, segmentation, and captioning. 1. This paper shows that the sMCL method is somewhat incremental in its novelty. The motivation behind MCL is clear, but it cannot be considered a contribution to this work. This work is an NN version of [8] with some adjustments. 2. The paper is not well-written. The technical part of this work (section 3) is only 1 page long. What is the difference between the proposed framework and alternative training, which has been widely adopted when multiple tasks are trained simultaneously? 3. The author experiments on several computer vision tasks, such as classification, image segmentation, and captioning. However, the experiments lack strong baselines and the performance improvement is low. To prove the efficiency of his framework, the author should experiment on PASCAL VOC 2012 so that it can be compared to strong baselines.
