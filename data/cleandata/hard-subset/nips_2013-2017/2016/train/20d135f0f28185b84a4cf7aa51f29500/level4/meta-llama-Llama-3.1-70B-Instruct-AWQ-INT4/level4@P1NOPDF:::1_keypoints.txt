- Proposes a new stochastic gradient descent algorithm for training ensemble models, building on Guzman-Rivera et al. [8].  
- Defines ensemble loss as the loss of the best output of a single classifier, encouraging a diverse set of classifiers.  
- Primary contribution: using SGD enables application of the method to deep neural networks.  
- Paper is well-written and engaging.  
- Main concern: algorithmic novelty may be incremental relative to [8].  
- Authors convincingly show small changes substantially increase the algorithm's practicality, making acceptance reasonable.  
- Suggests evaluating MCL with mini-batches (rather than the full dataset) as an intriguing comparison.  
- Requests deeper analysis of the "diversity" effect, as it is key to understanding the method's behavior and benefits.
