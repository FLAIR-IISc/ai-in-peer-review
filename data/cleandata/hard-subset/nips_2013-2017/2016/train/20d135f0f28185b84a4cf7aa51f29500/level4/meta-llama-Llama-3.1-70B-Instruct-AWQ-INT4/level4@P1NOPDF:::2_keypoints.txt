- Proposes oracle-guided ensemble training: backpropagate only to the ensemble member the oracle selects for each training instance, driving specialization.  
- Method applies to DNNs and any gradient-based models and effectively enforces specialization in classification.  
- Authors report impressive, comprehensive experimental results across multiple domains.  
- Presentation is clear and the paper is well written.  
- Overall assessment: solid work that deserves publication.  
- Major missing component: paper does not develop how to exploit/use the resulting ensemble at inference (beyond noting top-k relevance), limiting practical usability.  
- The missing exploitation of ensembles is important but relatively problem-specific and does not negate the contribution.  
- Minor suggestion: improve Fig. 2 by typesetting the pseudo-code or embedding it as a vector image for better readability.
