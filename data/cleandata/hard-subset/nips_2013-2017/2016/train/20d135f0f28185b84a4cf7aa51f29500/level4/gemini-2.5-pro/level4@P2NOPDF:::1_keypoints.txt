- Introduces an SGD algorithm for training ensembles based on Guzman-Rivera et al.'s loss (loss of the single best classifier per example).  
- Core contribution: adapting the framework to SGD/mini-batches, enabling scalability to large datasets and deep networks.  
- Paper is interesting, clearly written, and has a well-established motivation.  
- Concern about incremental novelty (batch → stochastic), but the authors convincingly argue practical significance; reviewer recommends acceptance.  
- Suggestion: include an ablation comparing original MCL trained with mini-batches to isolate benefits of the proposed stochastic scheme.  
- Suggestion: provide deeper analysis of ensemble diversity using quantitative metrics (disagreement rates, Q-statistics) or qualitative visualizations.
