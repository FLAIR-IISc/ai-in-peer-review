- Introduces a novel SGD method for training ensemble models, extending Guzman-Rivera et al.'s framework.  
- Defines ensemble loss as the loss of the single classifier producing the best output to encourage classifier diversity.  
- Main contribution: adapts this loss formulation to stochastic gradient descent, enabling use with deep neural networks.  
- Manuscript is well-motivated and clearly presented.  
- Primary concern: novelty—may be only an incremental refinement over [8] rather than a major conceptual advance.  
- Recommendation: accept pending authors address a comparative analysis of MCL using mini-batches instead of full datasets.  
- Recommendation: authors should provide a deeper empirical investigation of the claimed "diversity" effect.
