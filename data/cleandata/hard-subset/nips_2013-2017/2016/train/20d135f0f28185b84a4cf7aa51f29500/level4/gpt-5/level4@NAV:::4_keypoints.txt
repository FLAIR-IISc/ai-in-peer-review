- Novelty is incremental: sMCL is essentially a neural-network adaptation of prior work ([8]) and the MCL motivation itself isn’t a new contribution.
- Paper is poorly written and technically thin (Sec. 3 only one page); it fails to clarify how the proposed framework differs from common joint-training strategies.
- Experiments use weak baselines and show only small improvements; stronger comparisons (e.g., on PASCAL VOC 2012) are needed to demonstrate effectiveness.
