- Proposes training an ensemble using an oracle that, per training instance, selects the best member and backprops only to that network, encouraging specialization.
- Applicable to any gradient-based model; experiments across multiple domains show strong overall performance.
- Idea is clear, compelling, and effectively enforces specialization in classification settings.
- Major omission: paper does not address how to use the trained ensemble at inference time without the oracle.
- While acknowledging inference strategies may be problem-specific, the paper needs concrete guidance or experiments on inference-time usage to strengthen practical impact.
- Minor presentation issue: Figure 2’s pseudo-code is a raster image—provide it as text or a vector graphic for readability.
- Overall assessment: strong work that deserves to be published.
