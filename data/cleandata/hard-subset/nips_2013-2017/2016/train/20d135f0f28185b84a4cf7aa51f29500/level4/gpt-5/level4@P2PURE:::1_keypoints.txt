- Introduces an SGD training procedure for ensemble models using the “best-performer” ensemble loss from Guzman-Rivera et al. [8].
- Main contribution: making that objective practical for deep neural networks by training it with SGD.
- Paper is interesting and clearly written.
- Primary concern: algorithmic novelty may be incremental compared to Guzman-Rivera et al. [8].
- Authors convincingly argue that the change substantially improves practicality, so reviewer leans toward acceptance.
- Suggestion: compare against MCL trained with mini-batches rather than over the entire dataset.
- Suggestion: provide more analysis of the resulting ensemble “diversity” effect.
