- Proposes a stochastic gradient descent algorithm for training ensembles, building on Guzman-Rivera et al. [8].  
- Uses the loss of the best single classifier output as the ensemble loss to encourage classifier diversity.  
- Main contribution: adapting the method to SGD, enabling application to deep neural networks.  
- Paper is well-written and engaging.  
- Major concern: algorithmic novelty may be incremental relative to [8].  
- Authors argue their small modification substantially increases practical utility; reviewer leans toward acceptance.  
- Suggested additional experiment: compare MCL performance using batches versus the entire dataset.  
- Requested deeper analysis of the "diversity" effect to better explain algorithm behavior.
