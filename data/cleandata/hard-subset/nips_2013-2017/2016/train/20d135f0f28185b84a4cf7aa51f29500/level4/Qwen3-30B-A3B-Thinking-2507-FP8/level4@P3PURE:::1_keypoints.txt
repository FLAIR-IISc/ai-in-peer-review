- Proposes a new SGD algorithm for training ensemble models, extending Guzman-Rivera et al. [8].  
- Builds on an ensemble loss defined as the loss of the best-performing single classifier, which promotes classifier diversity.  
- Main contribution: adapt this framework to use SGD, enabling application to deep neural networks.  
- Paper is well-written and presents an interesting approach.  
- Primary concern: algorithmic novelty may be incremental beyond the prior work [8].  
- Authors convincingly show the minor modification substantially improves practical utility for deep learning.  
- Reviewer recommends acceptance.  
- Suggested additions: (1) compare MCL under mini-batch training versus full-batch, (2) provide a more rigorous analysis of the diversity effect induced by the method.
