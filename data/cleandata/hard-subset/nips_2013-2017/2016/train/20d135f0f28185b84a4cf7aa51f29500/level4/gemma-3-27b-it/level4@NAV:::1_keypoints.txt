- Proposes an SGD-based algorithm for training ensemble models, adapting Guzman-Rivera et al.’s loss that minimizes the best classifier’s loss to encourage diversity.  
- Main contribution is adapting the prior loss formulation to work with SGD, enabling application to deep neural networks.  
- Paper is well-written and presents an interesting and useful extension of prior work.  
- Primary reservation: limited algorithmic novelty beyond the foundation laid in Guzman-Rivera et al. [8].  
- Empirically demonstrates practical benefits and increased utility from the modification, leading to a recommendation to accept.  
- Recommendation to compare the proposed method against MCL using batched updates (rather than full-dataset updates).  
- Suggests more detailed analysis to quantify and characterize the observed “diversity” effect.
