- Proposes oracle-guided training: an oracle selects the optimal network per instance and only that network receives backprop updates.  
- Method promotes specialization, producing ensembles that collectively cover a wide range of outputs.  
- Empirically effective across multiple domains, showing impressive performance and alignment with top-k metrics (e.g., ImageNet).  
- Presentation is clear and experiments are comprehensive.  
- Reviewer recommends publication.  
- Major omission: paper does not explore how to exploit or use the trained ensembles in practical applications.  
- Practical usability is limited by the lack of strategies for leveraging ensemble outputs (this is problem-specific but important).  
- Minor presentation issue: Figure 2 includes pseudo-code as a raster image; should be integrated as text or a vector image for readability.
