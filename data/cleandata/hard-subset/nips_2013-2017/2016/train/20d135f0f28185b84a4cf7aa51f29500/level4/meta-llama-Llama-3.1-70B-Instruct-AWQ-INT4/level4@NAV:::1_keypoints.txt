- Proposes a novel SGD algorithm for training ensemble models, extending Guzman-Rivera et al. [8].  
- Uses the ensemble loss defined by the best single-classifier output, encouraging classifier diversity.  
- Main contribution: applying stochastic gradient descent enables use with deep neural networks.  
- Paper is well-organized and clearly written.  
- Primary concern: limited algorithmic novelty—may be an incremental extension of [8].  
- Empirical results demonstrate the modification substantially increases the algorithm’s practical utility, supporting acceptance.  
- Suggested future work: evaluate the MCL algorithm with mini-batches instead of the full dataset.  
- Suggested future work: provide a deeper analysis of the “diversity” effect among ensemble members.
