- sMCL extends Multiple Choice Learning to deep networks by introducing a stochastic SGD-based training method that trains ensemble members concurrently.  
- The method removes the need for costly iterative retraining in prior MCL approaches, making MCL practical for modern deep architectures.  
- sMCL is the first practical method for training diverse deep ensembles, agnostic to architecture and loss function.  
- Empirical results show sMCL outperforms classical ensembles, MCL [8], and Dey et al. [5] by 6–10% on oracle metrics across classification, segmentation, and captioning.  
- sMCL achieves a 5× speedup over prior MCL due to concurrent training without repeated model retraining.  
- The training algorithm is rigorously derived from the oracle loss (Algorithm 2) and validated with ablation studies.  
- Ablation studies (e.g., varying k) demonstrate that ensemble specialization emerges naturally from the training procedure.  
- Experiments are thorough and well-designed, using CIFAR-10, Pascal VOC, and MSCOCO with clear metrics like oracle accuracy and IoU.  
- The paper is exceptionally clear and well-organized, with accessible problem formulation, algorithmic details, and experimental analysis.  
- Qualitative examples effectively illustrate ensemble specialization and interpretability (class-specific specialists).  
- The contribution is novel and significant, solving a major scalability gap in prior MCL and ensemble literature.  
- Practical significance is high for systems needing multiple hypotheses (e.g., autonomous vehicles, assistive tools) due to lower oracle error and interpretable ambiguity handling.  
- Strengths: solves MCL scalability for deep learning; consistent gains across tasks; emergent specialization; simple implementation with no extra hyperparameters.  
- Weaknesses: lacks quantitative diversity measurements (e.g., pairwise disagreement or mutual information); would benefit from direct comparison to MCL with mini-batch updates.  
- Overall recommendation: Accept — novel, impactful, technically rigorous, with minor addressable weaknesses.
