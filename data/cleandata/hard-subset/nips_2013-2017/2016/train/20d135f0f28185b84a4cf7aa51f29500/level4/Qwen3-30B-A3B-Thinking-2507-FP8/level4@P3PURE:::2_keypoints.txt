- Novel method: train ensembles by using an oracle to pick the optimal member per instance and backpropagate only to that selected network.
- Encourages specialization within the ensemble, promoting comprehensive coverage of the target output space.
- Strong empirical performance reported across multiple domains and classification tasks.
- Core idea is well-motivated and effectively enforces specialization.
- Presentation is clear and experimental evaluation is thorough and diverse.
- Major omission: lacks development of how the trained ensemble is deployed or used in practice, limiting real-world applicability.
- Paper notes consistency with top-k metrics (e.g., ImageNet) but does not provide a concrete post-training utilization strategy.
- Minor issue: Figure 2 pseudo-code is embedded as raster images — should be included as text or vector graphics for readability.
- Overall: substantial contribution that deserves publication, but should address the deployment/usage gap.
