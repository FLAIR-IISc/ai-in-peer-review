- Proposes an SGD method with Multiple Choice Learning (sMCL) and claims benefits across classification, segmentation, and captioning.
- Novelty is incremental: sMCL extends existing ideas and MCL motivation is not a new contribution.
- Work is essentially a neural-network adaptation of prior method [8] with limited original innovation.
- Writing and technical presentation are weak; Section 3 is only one page and lacks sufficient detail.
- Missing detailed comparisons to alternative/multi-task training methods to clarify unique aspects.
- Experiments cover multiple tasks but use weak baselines and show only modest improvements.
- Reviewer recommends evaluating on PASCAL VOC 2012 and comparing to stronger baselines for a more convincing demonstration.
