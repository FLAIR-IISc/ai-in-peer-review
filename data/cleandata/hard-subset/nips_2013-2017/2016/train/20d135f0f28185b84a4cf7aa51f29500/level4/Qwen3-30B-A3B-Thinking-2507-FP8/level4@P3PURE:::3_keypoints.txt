- Paper tackles the important problem of multiple choice learning (MCL) for deep networks.  
- Proposed solution is a simple, elegant modification to backpropagation (winner-takes-the-gradient layer).  
- Strong empirical results across image classification, segmentation, and captioning, supporting generality.  
- Over-emphasizes "diversity" despite no explicit diversity-promoting mechanism, making the term descriptive rather than prescriptive.  
- Method is a minor incremental advance over Guzman et al. (2015) relaxation framework, with limited theoretical/methodological novelty.  
- Revise the first sentence of the abstract for clarity.
