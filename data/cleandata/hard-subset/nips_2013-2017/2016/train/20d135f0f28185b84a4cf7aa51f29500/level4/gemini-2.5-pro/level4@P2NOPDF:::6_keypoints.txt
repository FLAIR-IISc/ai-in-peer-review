- Proposes a novel "winner-takes-all" ensemble training: update only the ensemble member with lowest loss per training example.
- Method is conceptually simple and shows surprisingly strong empirical performance.
- Reviewer leans toward acceptance but requests revisions to strengthen the paper.
- Inference procedure at test time is unclear — need to specify how final predictions are produced without ground truth (e.g., averaging, voting, or selecting a member).
- The "independent/regular ensemble" baseline ("Indp.") is undefined — provide precise construction and training details for reproducibility.
- Claim that members "specialize" is insufficiently justified; provide analysis showing stable class-level specialization or add mechanisms (e.g., diversity/specialization loss) to encourage it.
- Related work on diversity regularization in ensembles (e.g., methods adding diversity-promoting terms) is missing and should be discussed for context.
