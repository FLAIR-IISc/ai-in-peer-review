- Proposes a new SGD algorithm for training ensemble models.  
- Builds on Guzman-Rivera et al. [8], which minimizes the loss of the best output from a single classifier in an ensemble to encourage diversity.  
- Main contribution: demonstrating how to effectively apply SGD to this “best-output” loss, enabling its use with deep neural networks.  
- Paper is interesting and clearly written.  
- Primary concern: degree of novelty feels incremental relative to [8].  
- Reviewer persuaded that the practical utility is significantly enhanced and is inclined to accept.  
- Suggested experiments: compare performance to MCL when using mini-batches rather than the full dataset.  
- Suggested analysis: quantify and more deeply analyze the observed “diversity” effect and how the algorithm promotes ensemble diversity.
