- Proposes an ensemble training algorithm where only the lowest-loss member for each training example is updated; yields strong empirical results and the reviewer recommends weak acceptance.  
- Testing phase description is unclear; reviewer suspects authors may use ground truth to pick the best member at test time and requests a detailed explanation.  
- The terms “independent ensembles” / “regular ensembles” (Indp.) are used without definition; a brief description of these baselines is needed.  
- The claim that ensemble members “specialize” to different classes is questionable given per-example reassignment during training; consider adding specialization to the loss to justify this.  
- Related work on diversity regularization in neural networks (e.g., Chen et al.) is not discussed and should be included for completeness.  
- Addressing these clarifications and related-work omissions would strengthen the manuscript and its arguments.
