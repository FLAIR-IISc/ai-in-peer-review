- Proposes a novel training method where an oracle selects, per instance, the best ensemble member and only that network receives the backprop update.  
- Encourages specialization of individual networks, yielding ensembles with broad coverage of the output space.  
- Strong empirical results across multiple domains with thorough experimental validation.  
- Paper is well-written and presents a compelling core idea; contribution merits publication.  
- Main reservation: the paper does not adequately address how to perform inference without access to the oracle, limiting practical deployment.  
- Authors only suggest suitability for top-k metrics (e.g., ImageNet) but do not provide general or concrete inference strategies.  
- Minor issue: pseudo-code in Figure 2 is a raster image; rendering it as text or vector would improve readability.
