- Proposes a novel SGD algorithm for training ensembles, building on Guzman-Rivera et al. [8].
- Key insight: leverage the loss of the best output of a single classifier to encourage diverse classifiers.
- Primary contribution: adapt this approach for stochastic gradient descent, enabling use with deep neural networks.
- Paper is well-written and engaging.
- Main concern: algorithmic novelty might be an incremental improvement over [8].
- Reviewer believes the modification meaningfully enhances usefulness and leans toward acceptance.
- Suggestion: compare the MCL algorithm with batch processing instead of using the entire dataset.
- Request more in-depth analysis of the "diversity" effect achieved by the method.
