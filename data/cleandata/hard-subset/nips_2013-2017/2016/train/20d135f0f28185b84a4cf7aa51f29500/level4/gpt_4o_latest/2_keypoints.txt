- Proposes a novel ensemble training method where an oracle picks the best member per instance and only that network is backpropagated.  
- Method encourages specialization so the ensemble collectively covers most desired outputs.  
- Demonstrates strong performance across multiple domains.  
- Clear presentation and robust experimental evaluation.  
- Overall a solid contribution that merits publication.  
- Major omission: paper does not explore practical ways to exploit/use the trained ensembles in applications beyond noting top-k relevance.  
- Minor issue: Fig. 2 pseudocode is embedded as raster images; should be inline text or a vector graphic for readability.
