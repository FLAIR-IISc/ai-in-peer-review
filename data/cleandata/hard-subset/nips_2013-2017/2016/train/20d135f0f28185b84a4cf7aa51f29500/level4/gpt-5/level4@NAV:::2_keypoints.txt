- Introduces an oracle-guided training method where, per-instance, only the ensemble member with the best output is backpropagated, promoting specialization.  
- Method applies to DNNs and other gradient-based models.  
- Experiments across multiple domains show consistently strong performance.  
- Reviewer rates the paper as solid, clearly presented, and a strong contribution that merits publication.  
- Major missing piece: lacks concrete guidance for inference/use of the trained ensemble (training relies on an oracle; deployment strategies beyond top-k candidates are underdeveloped).  
- Presentation issue: Figure 2 pseudocode is embedded as raster images; should be inline text or vector graphics.
