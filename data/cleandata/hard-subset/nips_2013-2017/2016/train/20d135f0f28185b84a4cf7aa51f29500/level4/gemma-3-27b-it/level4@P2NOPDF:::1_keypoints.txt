- Introduces a novel SGD algorithm for training ensemble models that builds on Guzman-Rivera et al. [8].
- Extends the prior “minimize the loss of the best prediction” approach to be efficiently applied with SGD and deep neural networks.
- Main contribution is demonstrating practical, efficient SGD-based optimization for that loss in deep-learning settings.
- Paper is well-written with a clear explanation of the proposed algorithm.
- Primary concern about novelty: core idea builds directly on [8], though SGD application expands practical applicability.
- Reviewer’s overall recommendation: lean towards acceptance.
- Suggested comparison: evaluate the proposed algorithm vs. MCL when both are trained with mini-batches (not just full-dataset MCL).
- Suggested analysis: more thorough, quantitative investigation of the “diversity” effect and metrics beyond overall performance to characterize learned ensemble members.
