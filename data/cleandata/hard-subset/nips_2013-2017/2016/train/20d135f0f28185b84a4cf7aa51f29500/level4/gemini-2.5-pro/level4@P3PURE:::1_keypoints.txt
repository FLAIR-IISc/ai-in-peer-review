- Proposes a new SGD algorithm for training ensemble models.  
- Builds on Guzman-Rivera et al. by using ensemble loss defined as the loss of the single best-performing classifier per example to encourage diversity.  
- Main contribution: adapts this objective to an SGD framework, enabling application to deep neural networks.  
- Paper is interesting and clearly written.  
- Concern that algorithmic novelty may be incremental versus prior work [8], but authors argue the change makes the method substantially more useful and scalable.  
- Reviewer is in favor of acceptance.  
- Requested revisions: evaluate the MCL algorithm with mini-batches instead of the full dataset.  
- Requested revisions: provide a more thorough analysis of the ensemble members' "diversity" effect.
