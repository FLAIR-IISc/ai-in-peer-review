- Proposes oracle-guided training for DNN ensembles: oracle selects optimal network per instance and backpropagates only to that network.  
- Enables specialization of networks, yielding ensembles that cover diverse outputs; reported strong performance across domains.  
- Reviewer rates paper as strong, well-organized, with thorough cross-domain experiments and publication-worthy.  
- Major shortcoming: paper does not develop how to exploit/use the trained ensemble at inference beyond suggesting a broad result pool and top-k evaluation.  
- Omission of practical utilization is important but somewhat problem-specific and does not negate the contribution.  
- Minor suggestion: replace raster pseudo-code in Figure 2 with embedded text or a vector image for better readability.
