- Proposes a stochastic gradient descent algorithm for training ensemble models, building on Guzman-Rivera et al. [8].  
- Adopts ensemble loss as the loss of the best single classifier output, which promotes classifier diversity.  
- Key contribution: using SGD enables applying the method to deep neural networks.  
- Paper is well-written and engaging.  
- Primary concern: novelty may be an incremental extension of prior work [8].  
- Despite that, the small modification appears to significantly increase the algorithm's usefulness; reviewer leans toward acceptance.  
- Suggested experiment: compare MCL performance using mini-batches instead of the entire dataset.  
- Requested deeper analysis: provide more in-depth quantification and interpretation of the "diversity" effect.
