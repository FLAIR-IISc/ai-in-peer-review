- Proposes an SGD-based method for training ensemble models.
- Builds on Guzman-Rivera et al. [8], using ensemble loss defined by the best-performing classifier to encourage diversity.
- Main contribution: enabling the approach to scale to deep neural networks via stochastic gradient updates.
- Paper is interesting and clearly written.
- Concern that the algorithmic novelty may be only incremental relative to [8].
- Reviewer persuaded that the practical improvement is meaningful and is inclined to accept.
- Suggests an informative comparison: evaluate the MCL algorithm with mini-batches instead of using the full dataset.
- Requests more analysis of the induced “diversity” among ensemble members.
