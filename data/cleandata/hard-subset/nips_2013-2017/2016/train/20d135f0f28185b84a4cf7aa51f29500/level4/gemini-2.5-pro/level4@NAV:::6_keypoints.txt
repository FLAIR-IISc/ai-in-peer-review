- Introduces a novel training method where only the single ensemble member with minimum loss for each training instance is activated and updated.  
- Method is simple, yields impressive empirical results; reviewer recommends weak accept.  
- Inference procedure is under-described and may incorrectly use ground-truth to select members at test time (possible evaluation leakage).  
- The term “independent ensembles” / “regular ensembles” (Indp.) is used but not clearly defined as a baseline.  
- Claim that ensemble members “specialize” to specific classes is inadequately supported since selection/updating is per-instance; would benefit from explicit class-based encouragement.  
- Paper omits relevant prior work on diversity regularization (e.g., Chen et al.), which should be discussed for proper context.
