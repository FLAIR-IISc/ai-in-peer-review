- sMCL offers only incremental novelty, adapting prior MCL work ([8]) to neural networks with minor modifications rather than introducing a fundamentally new idea.
- Paper writing is poor and Section 3 is overly brief; it omits a detailed comparison with alternative training methods commonly used for concurrent/multi-task training.
- Experiments cover classification, segmentation, and captioning but use weak baselines and show marginal gains; additional evaluations (e.g., on PASCAL VOC 2012) are needed for stronger validation.
