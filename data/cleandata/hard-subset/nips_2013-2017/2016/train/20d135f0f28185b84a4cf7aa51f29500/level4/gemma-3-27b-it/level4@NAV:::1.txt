This paper introduces a stochastic gradient descent (SGD) algorithm for training ensemble models, extending the loss function approach originally presented by Guzman-Rivera et al [8] – which focuses on minimizing the loss of the best-performing classifier within the ensemble to promote diversity. The key contribution lies in adapting this approach for use with SGD, thereby enabling application to deep neural networks. The paper is well-written and presents an interesting line of work. My primary reservation concerns the extent to which the algorithmic novelty represents an advancement beyond the foundation laid in [8]. However, the authors successfully demonstrate the practical benefit and increased utility stemming from their modification, leading me to recommend acceptance. To further strengthen the work, a comparative analysis of the proposed method against the MCL algorithm using batched updates (instead of the full dataset) would be valuable. Additionally, more detailed analysis quantifying and characterizing the observed “diversity” effect would be beneficial.
