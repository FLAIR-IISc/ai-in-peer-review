- Proposes a new SGD algorithm for training ensembles, building on Guzman-Rivera et al. [8] that minimizes the loss of the best single classifier to encourage diversity.  
- Main contribution is showing SGD can be applied to this training scheme, making it usable with deep neural networks.  
- Paper is interesting and clearly written.  
- Primary concern: algorithmic novelty is incremental relative to [8].  
- Reviewer is convinced the change meaningfully improves practicality and recommends acceptance.  
- Suggests evaluating the MCL algorithm with mini-batches (not full-dataset updates) to reflect common training practice.  
- Requests more detailed analysis of the observed “diversity” effect and its impact on overall performance.
