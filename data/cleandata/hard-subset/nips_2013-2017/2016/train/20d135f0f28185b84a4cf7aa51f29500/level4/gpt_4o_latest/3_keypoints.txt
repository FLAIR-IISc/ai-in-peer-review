- Paper proposes a simple winner-takes-the-gradient layer for MCL in deep networks, enabling joint training and assignment; reports strong results on image classification, segmentation, and captioning.  
- Strength: Addresses a relevant MCL problem for the NIPS community.  
- Strength: The proposed method is simple and appealing.  
- Strength: Experiments cover three distinct tasks.  
- Weakness: Heavy emphasis on "diversity" is misleading because the model does not explicitly enforce diversity.  
- Weakness: The contribution appears incremental, especially given the relaxation by Guzman et al.  
- Minor: Revise the first sentence of the abstract for clarity.  
- Minor: Reduce the paper’s emphasis on diversity.  
- Minor: Fix typo on line 108 (replace first "f" with "g").
