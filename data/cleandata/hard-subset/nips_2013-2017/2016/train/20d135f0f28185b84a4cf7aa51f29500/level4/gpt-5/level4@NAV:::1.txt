This paper presents a new stochastic gradient descent approach for training ensemble models. The authors build on Guzman-Rivera et al. [8], where the ensemble loss is defined as the loss of the best-performing classifier within the ensemble, which promotes diversity among models. Their contribution is to propose training with stochastic gradient descent, thereby enabling application to deep neural networks. The paper is interesting and clearly written. My main concern is whether the algorithmic novelty is only incremental relative to [8]. Since the authors have persuaded me that the small change they introduce substantially improves practicality, I am inclined toward acceptance. An informative comparison would be to evaluate the MCL algorithm using mini-batches (rather than the full dataset). I would also like to see more analysis of the resulting “diversity” effect.
