- Introduces an SGD-based algorithm for training ensemble models.
- Extends Guzman-Rivera et al. [8]’s ensemble loss (loss of best-performing classifier) to promote diversity.
- Core contribution: integrating stochastic gradient descent to enable use with deep neural networks.
- Paper is engaging and well-written.
- Primary concern: algorithmic novelty may be incremental relative to [8], though authors argue it substantially increases utility.
- Reviewer recommends acceptance.
- Suggested future work: empirical comparison with MCL trained using batches rather than the entire dataset.
- Suggested future work: more in-depth analysis of the “diversity” effect produced by the method.
