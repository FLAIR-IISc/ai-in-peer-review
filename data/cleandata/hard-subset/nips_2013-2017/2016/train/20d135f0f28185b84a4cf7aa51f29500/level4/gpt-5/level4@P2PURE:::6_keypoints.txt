- Proposal: train ensembles where for each training example only the single lowest-loss member is activated and updated, yielding strong empirical results; reviewer recommends weak accept.

- Major concern 1: Test-time procedure is unclear — appears the paper may be using ground-truth labels to select the best member; clarify how inference is done without ground truth.

- Major concern 2: Baseline "independent/regular ensembles" (Indp.) is not explicitly defined in text or figures; provide a clear definition.

- Major concern 3: Claim that members “specialize” to different classes is insufficiently justified because per-example reassignment does not directly enforce class-level specialization; suggest incorporating explicit class-based assignment or regularization.

- Major concern 4: Missing discussion and comparison to diversity-regularization literature (e.g., Chen et al.), which formalizes diversity and should be contrasted with the proposed approach.
