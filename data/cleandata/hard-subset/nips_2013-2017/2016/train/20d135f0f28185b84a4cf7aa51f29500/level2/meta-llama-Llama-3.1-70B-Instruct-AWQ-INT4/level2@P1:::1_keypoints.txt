- Proposes Stochastic Multiple Choice Learning (sMCL) for training diverse deep ensembles.  
- Introduces an SGD-based algorithm that directly minimizes oracle loss — key contribution.  
- sMCL is simple to implement, architecture- and loss-agnostic, and parameter-free.  
- Addresses limitations of prior Multiple Choice Learning (MCL) methods with a novel approach.  
- Demonstrates broad applicability across varied problem domains and network architectures.  
- Experimental results are strong, showing sMCL significantly outperforms classical ensembles and a slower MCL baseline.  
- Provides qualitative evidence of solution diversity produced by sMCL ensembles.  
- Offers a thorough analysis of training behavior and ensemble outputs.
