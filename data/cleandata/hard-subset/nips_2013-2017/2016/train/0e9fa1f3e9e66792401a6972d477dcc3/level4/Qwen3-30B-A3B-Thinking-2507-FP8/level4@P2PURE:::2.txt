

This paper employs bidirectional Monte Carlo to evaluate MCMC-based posterior inference methods. The authors derive lower bounds on the KL-divergence between the true posterior and the approximate distribution. The proposed method is implemented in WebPPL and Stan. Overall, this is a strong paper. The authors leverage a recent advance—bidirectional Monte Carlo—to analyze MCMC-based posterior inference, addressing an important problem with an interesting approach. I recommend acceptance.
