This paper employs the recently proposed bidirectional Monte Carlo to evaluate MCMC-based posterior inference methods. The authors analyze lower bounds on the KL divergence between the true posterior and the approximate distribution. The approach is implemented in WebPPL and Stan. Overall, this is a strong paper: it leverages a state-of-the-art technique—bidirectional Monte Carlo—to analyze MCMC-based posterior inference. The problem is timely, and the idea is interesting. I recommend acceptance.
