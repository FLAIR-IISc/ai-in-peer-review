This paper applies the recent bidirectional Monte Carlo (BDMC) technique to evaluate MCMC-based posterior inference methods. The authors analyze lower bounds on the KL divergence between the true posterior and the approximate distribution, and they integrate the method into WebPPL and Stan.

Overall, this is a strong paper. It leverages a state-of-the-art approach—BDMC—to analyze MCMC-based posterior inference on a timely and important problem, and the idea is interesting. I recommend acceptance.
