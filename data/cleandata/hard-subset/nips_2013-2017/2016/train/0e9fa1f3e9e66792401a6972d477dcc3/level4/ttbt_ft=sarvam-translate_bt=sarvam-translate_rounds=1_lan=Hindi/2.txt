This paper uses a recently developed bidirectional Monte Carlo to evaluate MCMC-based backward estimation methods. The authors analyze the lower bounds of the KL-divergence between the true backward and the estimated distribution. This method has been integrated into WebPPL and Stan. Overall, this is a good paper. The authors use a technique, namely a bidirectional Monte Carlo, recently developed to analyze MCMC-based backward estimation. It is a vibrant problem, and the idea is interesting. I recommend accepting this paper.
