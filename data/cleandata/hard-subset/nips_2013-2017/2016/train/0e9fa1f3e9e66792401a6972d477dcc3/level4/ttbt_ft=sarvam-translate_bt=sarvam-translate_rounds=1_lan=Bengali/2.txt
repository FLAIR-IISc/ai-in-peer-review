This paper uses a recent two-way Monte Carlo method to evaluate MCMC-based backward estimation techniques. The authors analyze the lower bound of the KL-divergence between the true backward and the approximate distribution. The method is combined with WebPPL and Stan. Overall, it is a good paper. The authors use a recent technique, namely the two-way Monte Carlo method, to analyze MCMC-based backward estimation. It is a well-discussed problem and the concept is interesting. I recommend accepting this paper.
