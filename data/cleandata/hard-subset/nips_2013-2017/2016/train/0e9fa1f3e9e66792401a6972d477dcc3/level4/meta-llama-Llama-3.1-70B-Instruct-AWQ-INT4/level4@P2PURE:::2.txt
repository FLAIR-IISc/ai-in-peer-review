This paper leverages the recent bidirectional Monte Carlo technique to evaluate MCMC-based posterior inference methods, providing a valuable analysis of the lower bounds of KL-divergence between the true posterior and the approximate distribution. The authors successfully integrate their method into WebPPL and Stan, demonstrating its practical applicability. Overall, this is a well-crafted paper that effectively utilizes a recent advancement in the field, bidirectional Monte Carlo, to tackle a pressing problem in MCMC-based posterior inference. The idea is intriguing and timely, making it a compelling contribution. I strongly recommend accepting this paper.
