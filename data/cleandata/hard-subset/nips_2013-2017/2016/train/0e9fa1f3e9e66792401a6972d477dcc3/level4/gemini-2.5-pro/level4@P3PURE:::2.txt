This paper leverages the recently developed bidirectional Monte Carlo method to evaluate the quality of MCMC-based posterior inference. The authors' approach analyzes lower bounds on the KL-divergence between the true posterior and the approximate distribution. A key practical contribution is the integration of this method into the WebPPL and Stan frameworks.

Overall, this is a well-executed paper on an important topic. The application of bidirectional Monte Carlo to analyze posterior inference from MCMC methods is a novel and interesting idea. Given the significance of the problem and the strength of the proposed approach, I recommend this paper for acceptance.
