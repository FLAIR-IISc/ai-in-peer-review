This paper applies the recently introduced bidirectional Monte Carlo to assess MCMC-based posterior inference procedures. The authors study lower bounds on the KL divergence between the true posterior and the approximate sampling distribution. The method is implemented in WebPPL and Stan. Overall, this is a solid contribution. The authors leverage a recent state-of-the-art technique—bidirectional Monte Carlo—to analyze MCMC-based posterior inference. The problem is timely and the idea is compelling. I recommend acceptance.
