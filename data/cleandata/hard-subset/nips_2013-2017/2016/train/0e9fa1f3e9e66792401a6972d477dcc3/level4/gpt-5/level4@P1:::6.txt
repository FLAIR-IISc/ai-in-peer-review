This paper summarizes previously published results on bidirectional Monte Carlo (BDMC) and reports their application within two probabilistic programming languages, WebPPL and Stan. I do not see substantive original contributions: the work primarily implements known techniques and demonstrates them empirically. While the integrations may be practically useful, the lack of novel methodology or theory limits the paper’s contribution for the NIPS main conference. An MCMC-focused workshop would likely be a more appropriate venue for this work.
