This paper leverages bidirectional Monte Carlo (BMC) to evaluate Markov Chain Monte Carlo (MCMC)-based posterior inference methods. The authors analyze lower bounds on the Kullback-Leibler (KL) divergence between the true posterior and the approximate distribution produced by MCMC. The method has been integrated into both WebPPL and Stan, demonstrating practical applicability. Overall, this is a strong paper. The authors effectively apply a recent advance – bidirectional Monte Carlo – to analyze a critical problem in Bayesian inference: assessing the accuracy of MCMC-based posteriors. This is a highly relevant and interesting line of work, and I recommend acceptance.
