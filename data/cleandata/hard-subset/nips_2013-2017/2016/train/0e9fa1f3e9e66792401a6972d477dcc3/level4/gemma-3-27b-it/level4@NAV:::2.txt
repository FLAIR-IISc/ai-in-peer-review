This paper presents a valuable analysis of MCMC-based posterior inference methods using bidirectional Monte Carlo, a recently developed technique. The authors rigorously investigate lower bounds on the Kullback-Leibler (KL) divergence between the true posterior and the approximate distribution produced by these methods. Demonstrating integration with established platforms like WebPPL and Stan further strengthens the work. Overall, this is a well-executed and insightful contribution to an important and active area of research. I recommend acceptance.
