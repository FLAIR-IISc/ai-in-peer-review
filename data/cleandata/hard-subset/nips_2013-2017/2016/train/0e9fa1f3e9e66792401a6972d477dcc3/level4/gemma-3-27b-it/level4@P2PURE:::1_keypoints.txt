- Introduces a method to estimate the Jensen–Shannon divergence between an MCMC sampler’s output and the target distribution for debugging/evaluating MCMC in PPLs.
- Core idea of using bidirectional Monte Carlo (BDMC) to diagnose sampler convergence is appealing given unreliable existing MCMC diagnostics.
- Work is related to prior validation approaches (Geweke 2004; Cook et al. 2006/2012) that evaluate samplers on data drawn from the prior.
- Section 3.1 derivation is difficult to follow: q(.) is used before being defined and should be explicitly stated (likely the AIS marginal after T steps).
- Main text is not fully self-contained; key explanations are scattered and clarified only in the supplementary material.
- Recommend adding a clearer, step-by-step explanation of how the method is applied in practice.
- Notation could be simplified: splitting unobserved variables into z and θ appears unnecessary since they always appear together.
