This paper leverages the bidirectional Monte Carlo method to assess the performance of MCMC-based posterior inference techniques. The authors provide a comprehensive analysis of the lower bounds of KL-divergence between the true posterior distribution and its approximate counterpart. Notably, the proposed approach has been successfully integrated into the WebPPL and Stan frameworks. Overall, this is a well-crafted paper that effectively utilizes state-of-the-art techniques to address a pressing issue in the field. The authors' application of bidirectional Monte Carlo to analyze MCMC-based posterior inference is particularly noteworthy, given the current interest in this topic. Based on its merits, I strongly recommend accepting this paper for publication.
