

This paper employs the recent bidirectional Monte Carlo technique to evaluate MCMC-based posterior inference methods. The authors derive lower bounds for the KL-divergence between the true posterior and the approximate distribution. The approach is incorporated into WebPPL and Stan. Overall, this is a strong paper. The authors leverage a recent technique—bidirectional Monte Carlo—to analyze MCMC-based posterior inference, addressing a significant problem with an interesting and novel idea. I recommend acceptance.
