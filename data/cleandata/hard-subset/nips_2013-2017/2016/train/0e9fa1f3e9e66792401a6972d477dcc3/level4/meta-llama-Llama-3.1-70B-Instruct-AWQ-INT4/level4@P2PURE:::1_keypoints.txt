- Proposes using bidirectional Monte Carlo to estimate Jensen–Shannon divergence between an MCMC sampler and the target, aimed at debugging/evaluating MCMC in PPLs.  
- Idea is promising and useful given limits of existing MCMC diagnostics.  
- Two relevant prior works testing samplers on data from the prior should be cited: Geweke (2004) and Cook et al. (2006/2012).  
- Section 3.1 derivation is hard to follow because q(.) is used before being defined; q(.) seems to be the AIS marginal after T steps but must be stated explicitly.  
- Main text relies on the supplement for clarification; core concepts should be self-contained in the main paper.  
- The paper needs a clear, step-by-step procedure for applying the method from start to finish.  
- Suggest eliminating the distinction between unobserved variables z and θ (they always appear together) to reduce clutter and improve readability.  
- Minor typographical error: missing closing parenthesis on Line 165.
