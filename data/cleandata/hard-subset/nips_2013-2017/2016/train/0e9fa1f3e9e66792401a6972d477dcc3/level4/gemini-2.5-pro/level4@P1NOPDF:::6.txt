This paper summarizes previously published results on bidirectional Monte Carlo and reports on its implementation in two probabilistic programming languages, WebPPL and Stan. The primary contribution of this work appears to be the application of these known techniques, rather than the introduction of novel methodology. As the paper focuses on the implementation of existing methods, it seems to lack a significant original contribution. Consequently, this work might be better suited for a more specialized venue, such as a workshop on MCMC methods or probabilistic programming.
