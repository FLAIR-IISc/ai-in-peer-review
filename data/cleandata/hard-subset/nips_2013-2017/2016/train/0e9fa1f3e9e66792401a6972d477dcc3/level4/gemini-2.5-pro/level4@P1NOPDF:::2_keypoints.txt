- Presents a method for evaluating MCMC-based posterior inference using bidirectional Monte Carlo.
- Enables analysis of lower bounds on the KL divergence between the true posterior and MCMC approximations.
- Method has been integrated into the probabilistic programming frameworks WebPPL and Stan.
- Overall assessment: strong and well-executed paper.
- Applying state-of-the-art bidirectional Monte Carlo to posterior inference evaluation is a valuable contribution.
- Provides a principled, quantitative tool as an alternative to heuristic assessments.
- Integration with WebPPL and Stan is a significant practical strength that broadens accessibility.
- Recommendation: accept based on novelty, practical relevance, and clear presentation.
