This paper uses the recently developed bidirectional Monte Carlo to evaluate MCMC-based post-estimation methods. The authors analyze the lower bounds of the KL-divergence between the actual post-model and the estimated distribution. This method has been integrated into WebPPL and Stan. Overall, it is a good paper. The authors use a technique, namely a bidirectional Monte Carlo, which was recently developed to analyze MCMC-based post-estimation. It is a lively problem, and the idea is interesting. I recommend accepting this paper.
