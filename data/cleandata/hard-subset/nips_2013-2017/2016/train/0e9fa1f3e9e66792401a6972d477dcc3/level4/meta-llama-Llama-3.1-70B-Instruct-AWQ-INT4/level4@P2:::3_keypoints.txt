- Proposes BREAD: extends BDMC to bound the symmetrized KL between true posterior and approximate MCMC samples using AIS/reverse annealing.  
- Clear, concise introduction and thorough related-work review, including BDMC and probabilistic programming applications.  
- Technical contributions are well-explained with a detailed analysis of the BREAD method.  
- Novel and useful contribution to probabilistic programming and MCMC reliability evaluation.  
- Experiments are well-designed and demonstrate BREAD’s effectiveness across several models.  
- Presentation and clarity issues: definition of the vector v is unclear.  
- Inconsistent labeling ("rev" vs "fwd") may cause confusion and should be standardized.  
- Missing definition of the estimator \hat{p}(y) whose expectation is taken in equations (5–7) and (9–11).  
- Choice of the annealing schedule β_t is not explained.  
- Acronym RAIS is not defined in the figure caption.  
- Figures need improved readability (larger axes and tick labels).  
- Overall positive recommendation but clarity/presentation fixes required; rated 7/10.
