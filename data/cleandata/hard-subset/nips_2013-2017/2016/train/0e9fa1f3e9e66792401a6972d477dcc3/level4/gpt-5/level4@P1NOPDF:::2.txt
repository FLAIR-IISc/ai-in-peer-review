This paper leverages the recent bidirectional Monte Carlo (BDMC) technique to evaluate MCMC-based posterior inference methods. The authors analyze lower bounds on the KL divergence between the true posterior and the approximate distribution, offering a principled way to assess the quality of MCMC approximations. The method is implemented in both WebPPL and Stan, underscoring its practical relevance.

Overall, this is a strong and timely paper. Using BDMC to analyze MCMC-based posterior inference tackles a hot and important problem, and the idea is interesting and well executed. I recommend accepting this paper.
