This work leverages the recently developed bidirectional Monte Carlo technique to assess MCMC-based methods for posterior inference. The proposed methodology centers on analyzing lower bounds of the KL-divergence between the true posterior and the approximate distribution. The practical utility of this approach is demonstrated through its integration into the widely-used probabilistic programming languages WebPPL and Stan.

Overall, this is a strong and valuable contribution. The authors apply a state-of-the-art method to the significant and highly relevant problem of analyzing posterior inference. The core idea is compelling, and the work is well-motivated. I recommend this paper for acceptance.
