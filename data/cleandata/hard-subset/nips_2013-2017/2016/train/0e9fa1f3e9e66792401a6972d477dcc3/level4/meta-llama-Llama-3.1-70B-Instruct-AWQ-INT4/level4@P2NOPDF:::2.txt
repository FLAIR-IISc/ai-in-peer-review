This paper leverages the recent bidirectional Monte Carlo technique to assess MCMC-based posterior inference methods. The authors provide a thorough analysis of the lower bounds of KL-divergence between the true posterior and the approximate distribution. Furthermore, they successfully integrate this method into WebPPL and Stan, demonstrating its practical applicability. Overall, this is a well-crafted paper that effectively utilizes recent advancements in bidirectional Monte Carlo to tackle a pressing issue in MCMC-based posterior inference. The idea is intriguing, and the approach is sound. I strongly recommend accepting this paper for its valuable contribution to the field.
