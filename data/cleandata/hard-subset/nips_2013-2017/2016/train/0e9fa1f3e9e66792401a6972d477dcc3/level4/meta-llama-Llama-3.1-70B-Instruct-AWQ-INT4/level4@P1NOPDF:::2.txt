This paper leverages the recent bidirectional Monte Carlo technique to evaluate MCMC-based posterior inference methods. The authors analyze the lower bounds of KL-divergence between the true posterior and the approximate distribution. This method is integrated into both WebPPL and Stan. Overall, this is a well-crafted paper. The authors effectively utilize recent advancements, specifically bidirectional Monte Carlo, to analyze MCMC-based posterior inference. This is a timely and intriguing problem, and the idea presented is interesting. I recommend accepting this paper.
