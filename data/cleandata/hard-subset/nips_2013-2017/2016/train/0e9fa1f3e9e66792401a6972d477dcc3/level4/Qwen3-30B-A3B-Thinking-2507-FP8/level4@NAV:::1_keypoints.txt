- Introduces bidirectional Monte Carlo (BDMC) to estimate Jensen–Shannon divergence between an MCMC sampler and the target, for debugging/evaluating MCMC in PPLs.  
- The core idea is promising and addresses a real gap because existing MCMC diagnostics are inadequate.  
- Method closely aligns with prior validation approaches (Geweke 2004; Cook et al. 2006) that test on data generated from the prior.  
- Major clarity issue in Section 3.1: q(·) is used before being defined; it should be explicitly stated as the marginal distribution of the AIS sampler after T steps.  
- The main text relies too much on the supplement; the paper must be self-contained.  
- Add a concise, step-by-step procedural guide for applying BDMC so readers can follow the workflow without reconstructing details.  
- Minor: separating unobserved variables into z and θ is unnecessary—treat them together.  
- Minor typographical error: missing closing parenthesis at line 165.
