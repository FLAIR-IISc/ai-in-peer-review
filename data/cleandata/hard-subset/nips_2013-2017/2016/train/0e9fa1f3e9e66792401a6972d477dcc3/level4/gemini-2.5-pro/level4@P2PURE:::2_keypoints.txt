- Applies bidirectional Monte Carlo to evaluate the quality of MCMC-based posterior inference methods.
- Derives lower bounds on the KL divergence between the true posterior and the MCMC-generated approximate distribution.
- Demonstrates practical utility by integrating the method into probabilistic programming frameworks WebPPL and Stan.
- Addresses the important and relevant problem of assessing posterior inference quality.
- Presents a novel, well-executed contribution and is recommended for acceptance.
