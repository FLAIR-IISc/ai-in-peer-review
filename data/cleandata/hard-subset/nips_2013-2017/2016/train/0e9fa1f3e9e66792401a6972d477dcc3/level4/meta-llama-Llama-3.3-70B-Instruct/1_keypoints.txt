- Presents a novel BDMC-based method to estimate J-S divergence between an MCMC sampler and its target, aimed at debugging/evaluating MCMC in PPLs.  
- Leveraging BDMC to find sampler convergence issues is intriguing and potentially valuable compared to existing diagnostics.  
- Related prior-work (Geweke 2004; Cook et al. 2006/2012) on testing MCMC using prior-drawn data is relevant and should be acknowledged.  
- Section 3.1 derivation is obscure and hard to follow; key steps are not clearly presented.  
- q(.) (the marginal of the AIS sampler after T steps) is introduced prematurely and not explicitly defined in the main text.  
- The supplement clarifies parts, but the main paper should be self-contained and not require the supplement for comprehension.  
- Provide a clear, step-by-step practical guide for applying the method rather than forcing readers to assemble it from dispersed parts.  
- Simplify notation by consolidating z and theta since they are always used together.  
- Fix minor errors (e.g., missing closing parenthesis on Line 165).
