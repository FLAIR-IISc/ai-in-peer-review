This paper applies the recently proposed bidirectional Monte Carlo (BDMC) framework to evaluate MCMC-based posterior inference methods. The authors derive lower bounds on the KL divergence between the true posterior and an approximate distribution and implement the approach in WebPPL and Stan. The problem addressed—assessing the quality of MCMC posterior approximations—is timely and important, and the idea is both clear and compelling. The integration into widely used probabilistic programming systems further underscores the method’s practicality. Overall, this is a solid and useful contribution. I recommend acceptance.
