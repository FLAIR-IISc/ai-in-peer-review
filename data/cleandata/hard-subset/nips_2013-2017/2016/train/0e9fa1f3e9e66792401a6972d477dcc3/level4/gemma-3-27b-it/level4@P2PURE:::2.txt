This paper leverages bidirectional Monte Carlo to evaluate Markov Chain Monte Carlo (MCMC)-based posterior inference methods. The authors analyze lower bounds on the Kullback-Leibler (KL) divergence between the true posterior and the approximate distribution produced by these methods. The technique has been implemented within both WebPPL and Stan. Overall, this is a strong paper. The authors effectively apply a recent advance – bidirectional Monte Carlo – to analyze MCMC-based posterior inference, a significant and challenging problem. The approach is interesting, and I recommend acceptance.
