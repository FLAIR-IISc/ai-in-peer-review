- Proposes a novel method using Bidirectional Monte Carlo to compute rigorous lower bounds on KL divergence for evaluating MCMC posterior approximations.
- Focuses on the challenging problem of MCMC diagnostics and offers a principled alternative to heuristic convergence checks.
- Demonstrates practical utility by integrating the evaluation method into WebPPL and Stan.
- Integration into popular probabilistic programming frameworks shows feasibility and a clear path to practitioner adoption.
- Paper is well-written, clearly presents the problem, solution, and implementation.
- Overall assessment: strong, well-executed contribution of high relevance; recommended for acceptance.
