1. Proposes using BDMC to estimate Jensen-Shannon divergence for debugging/evaluating MCMC samplers in PPLs.  
2. Core idea is compelling and addresses an important gap in principled sampler validation.  
3. Main weakness: lack of clarity—derivation (Section 3.1) is hard to follow and notation (e.g., q(.)) is introduced before definition.  
4. Paper needs a clear, step-by-step algorithmic description (e.g., algorithm box) for practitioners.  
5. Omits relevant prior work on sampler validation using prior-generated data (Geweke 2004; Cook et al. 2006) and should compare/contrast.  
6. Minor: unnecessary notational distinction between unobserved variables z and theta—can be simplified.  
7. Minor: typographical error—missing closing parenthesis at line 165.  
8. Overall: novel and important contribution but impact limited by clarity and insufficient engagement with related literature; revision could change the evaluation.
