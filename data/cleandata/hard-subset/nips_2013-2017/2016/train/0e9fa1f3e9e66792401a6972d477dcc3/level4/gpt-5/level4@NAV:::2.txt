This paper employs the recent bidirectional Monte Carlo approach to assess MCMC-based posterior inference methods. The authors derive lower bounds on the KL divergence between the true posterior and the approximate distribution. The approach is implemented in WebPPL and Stan. Overall, this is a strong paper. The authors leverage a state-of-the-art technique, namely bidirectional Monte Carlo, to examine MCMC-based posterior inference. This is a timely problem and the idea is interesting. I recommend accepting this paper.
