This paper leverages the recent bidirectional Monte Carlo method to evaluate MCMC-based posterior inference methods. The authors analyze lower bounds of KL-divergence between the true posterior and the approximate distribution. The method is successfully integrated into WebPPL and Stan. Overall, this is a well-written and engaging paper. The authors' use of recent advances, namely bidirectional Monte Carlo, to analyze MCMC-based posterior inference is timely and interesting. This is a pressing problem, and their approach is noteworthy. I recommend accepting this paper.
