- Proposes using bidirectional Monte Carlo (BDMC) to estimate J-S divergence between an MCMC sampler and the target distribution for debugging/evaluating MCMC in PPLs.  
- Reviewer likes the idea; sees BDMC as potentially useful because existing MCMC diagnostics are problematic.  
- Two relevant prior works (Geweke 2004; Cook, Gelman & Rubin 2006) that validate MCMC by running on data sampled from the prior should be cited and discussed.  
- Section 3.1 is hard to follow: q(.) is used before being defined and should be explicitly stated (e.g., the marginal of the AIS sampler after T steps).  
- Important derivation details are pushed to the supplement; the main text should be self-contained and present a clear step-by-step application of the method.  
- Minor: splitting unobserved variables into z and theta is cluttered and unnecessary since they always appear together.  
- Minor: typographical error at line 165 (missing closing parenthesis).
