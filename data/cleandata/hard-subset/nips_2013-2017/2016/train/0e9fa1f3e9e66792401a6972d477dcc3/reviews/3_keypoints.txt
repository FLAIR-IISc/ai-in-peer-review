- Proposal: use the gap between BDMC’s stochastic lower and upper bounds as a generic performance measure for MCMC transition operators.  
- Contributions: implementations of BDMC for Stan and WebPPL and a protocol for evaluating posterior inference quality.  
- Strength: method is simple, easy to implement, and potentially high-impact for probabilistic programming systems.  
- Novelty concern: technical contributions are modest; the Jensen–Shannon divergence bound observation is nice but not clearly novel.  
- Missing related work: does not cite or discuss Radford Neal’s Tempered Transitions (1996), which closely relates to BDMC and its acceptance ratio interpretation.  
- β schedule issue: authors under-discuss choice of β_t; Δβ affects bounds and comparisons and should be treated more systematically.  
- AutoBDMC unclear: the meaning and mechanism of “auto” in AutoBDMC are never unambiguously defined.  
- Suggestion: propose an iterative scheme to decrease Δβ until bounds are sufficiently tight; experiments should explore this.  
- Clarity problems: sections 3.1–3.2 feel rushed and writing quality slips, causing several ambiguities.  
- Notation ambiguity: definition of vector v ("all variables sampled in AIS before final stage") is loose and confusing given two AIS sweeps.  
- Inconsistent labels: use of "rev"/"fwd" vs "for"/"back" is inconsistent and unclear.  
- Missing estimator definition: \hat{p}(y) (the estimator inside expectations in eqs. 5–7 and 9–11) is not defined, making derivations hard to follow.  
- Equation error: equation (6) is incorrect as written (KL divergence arguments appear identical); intended terms are unclear.  
- AutoBDMC components ambiguous: unclear whether it means (a) using the bound gap to assess convergence and (b) using the fixed hyperparameter scheme from sec. 3.2.  
- Experiments lack detail: unclear how β_t is chosen in practice; code implies step = 1/options.steps but this should be stated.  
- Figure ambiguity: unclear whether "HMC / No-U-Turn steps" indicates BDMC used with that number of steps.  
- Undefined acronyms: RAIS (reverse AIS) and variants (u)c(R)AIS are not defined in captions.  
- Presentation issues: axes and tick labels in figures are too small.  
- Typos: missing closing parentheses in D_JS expression; NUTS citation misspells Hoffman as "Homan."  
- Recommendation: could be acceptable if authors fix clarity, related-work discussion, and experiment thoroughness by camera-ready.
