- Presents a novel extension of bidirectional Monte Carlo (BDMC) to estimate the symmetrized KL divergence between true posterior and approximate MCMC samples.  
- Integrates the method into two popular probabilistic programming languages, WebPPL and Stan, enhancing applicability.  
- Well-written and logically organized manuscript with helpful figures and tables.  
- Technically sound with theoretical analysis and experiments supporting the claims.  
- Experiments are well-designed and demonstrate the method's effectiveness in evaluating MCMC accuracy.  
- Addresses a timely and important problem with potential to improve reliability of MCMC inference.  
- Original contribution that could significantly impact probabilistic inference practice.  
- Assumes substantial background in probabilistic inference and MCMC, which may limit accessibility for non-experts.  
- Some technical derivations (e.g., bias of the stochastic lower bound) are hard to follow without strong mathematical background.  
- Strengths outweigh weaknesses; reviewer recommends acceptance.
