This article proposes a method for splitting a matrix into a product of two sparse matrices. This method reduces the rank of the matrix from low to high. This method is called multi-level sparse matrix factorization. One advantage of this type of factorization is that different types of regularization can be applied at different levels. Overall, the general concept of multi-level sparse matrix factorization is fundamental and clearly described.
