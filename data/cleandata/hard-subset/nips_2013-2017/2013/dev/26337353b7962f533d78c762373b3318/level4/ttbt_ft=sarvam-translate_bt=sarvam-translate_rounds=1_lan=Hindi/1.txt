This paper proposes a method for decomposing a matrix into the product of two or more sparse matrices, where the rank decreases from low to high. This method is called multi-level sparse matrix factorization. One advantage of this type of decomposition is that different levels of regularization can be applied at different levels. Overall, the general idea of multi-level sparse factorization is fundamental and has been clearly described.
