- Proposes multi-level sparse matrix factorization: decomposing a matrix into a hierarchy of >2 sparse factors with decreasing rank across levels.
- Allows different levels of regularization to be applied separately, offering flexibility in modeling.
- The idea is original and the method is clearly described.
- Tuning concern: suggest deriving data-driven tuning procedures instead of relying on user-specified hyperparameters.
- Optimization concern: ask for analysis of convergence properties, convergence speed, and computational complexity of the learning algorithm.
- Overall assessment: an interesting and novel method.
