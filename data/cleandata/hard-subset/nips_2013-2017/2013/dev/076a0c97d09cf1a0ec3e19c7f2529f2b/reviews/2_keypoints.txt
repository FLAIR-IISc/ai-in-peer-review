- Proposes an interesting nonparametric ISPD-kernel-based test for three-variable interactions with clear, exhaustive algebraic proofs.  
- Paper is well-written and presents a solid, novel statistical approach with promising bioinformatics applications.  
- Lacks experiments on non-synthetic (real genomic) data to demonstrate practical utility.  
- Needs empirical comparisons to other genomics/interaction methods (e.g., partial correlation and other state-of-the-art tests).  
- Conclusion’s suggestion of extending the test to >3 variables appears to conflict with limitations discussed in Section 4.3.  
- Behavior and scalability in large/high-dimensional spaces are not evaluated and should be addressed.  
- Minor issue: Figure 3 is not referenced in the main text.
