The paper extends the existing machine for kernel dependency tests to a three-way interaction. It is an interesting and well-executed work that attacks an important problem for which currently very limited tools exist. The paper is generally well-written.

A few minor comments:

*   Explain the V-structure more clearly on the first use.
*   Provide an indicator for V-statistics (I had heard of U-statistics, but not V-statistics).
*   The equation (1) as written is not clear. You need to define P^* more clearly. "Where" after (1) does not clarify it.

In the experiments, clarify the sample size. A good extension of kernel dependency tests to a three-way interaction.
