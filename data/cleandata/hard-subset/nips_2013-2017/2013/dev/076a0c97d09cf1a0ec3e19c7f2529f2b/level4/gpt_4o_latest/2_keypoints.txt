- Introduces a compelling nonparametric method for testing three-variable interactions using ISPD kernel embeddings.  
- Manuscript is well-written, clear, and thorough in presenting algebraic proofs.  
- Method is promising for practical applications, especially detecting complex gene interactions in bioinformatics.  
- Lacks experiments on real-world (non-synthetic) datasets and comparisons with genomics methods (e.g., partial correlation).  
- Claim about extending to interactions of more than three variables seems inconsistent with limitations discussed in Section 4.3.  
- Needs evaluation of performance in high-dimensional settings.  
- Minor issue: Figure 3 is not referenced in the main text.  
- Overall: robust and innovative approach but would benefit from more comprehensive benchmarking against state-of-the-art interaction tests.
