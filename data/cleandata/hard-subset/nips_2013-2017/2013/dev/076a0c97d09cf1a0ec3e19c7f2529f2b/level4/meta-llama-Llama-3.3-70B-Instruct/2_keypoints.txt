- Proposes a compelling nonparametric test for three-variable interactions using ISPD kernel embeddings.  
- Paper is well-structured, clearly written, and provides comprehensive algebraic foundations.  
- Method is promising for uncovering complex interaction effects in bioinformatics applications.  
- Lacks experiments on real-world gene interaction data and comparative analysis against genomics methods (e.g., partial correlation).  
- Conclusion claim about extending the test beyond three variables conflicts with points made in section 4.3.  
- Needs investigation of method performance in high-dimensional settings.  
- Minor oversight: Figure 3 is not referenced in the main text.  
- Overall: robust and innovative framework that would benefit from more extensive comparisons to state-of-the-art interaction testing methods.
