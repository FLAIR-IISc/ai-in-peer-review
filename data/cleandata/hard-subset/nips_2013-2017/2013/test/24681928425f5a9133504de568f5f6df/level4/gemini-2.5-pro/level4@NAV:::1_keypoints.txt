- Introduces a technique for inferring the structure of stochastic And-Or grammars.  
- Claims generalization of prior structure learning but is actually a specialization.  
- The specialization is a key reason for the method’s tractability.  
- The specialization remains a significant contribution and authors should avoid overstating generality.  
- The fundamental algorithm is sound and performs well against other grammar induction approaches.  
- Experimental evaluation should include additional comparisons, particularly Stolcke and Omohondro (ref. 14), as an important benchmark.  
- Overall, a well-executed paper presenting a valuable method for structure learning within a specific class of grammars.
