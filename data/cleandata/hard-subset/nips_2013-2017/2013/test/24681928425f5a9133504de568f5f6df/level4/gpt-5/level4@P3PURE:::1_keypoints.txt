- Presents a method for learning structure of stochastic And-Or grammars that makes the problem more tractable, though it is a special-case rather than a true generalization of prior structure learning work.  
- The framing and contribution are reasonable and worthwhile for this specific class of grammars.  
- The basic algorithm is sensible and shows strong empirical performance compared to other grammar induction methods.  
- Experimental evaluation is limited and would benefit from additional baselines; specifically, Stolcke and Omohondro’s method (ref. 14) appears applicable and should be included.  
- Overall assessment: solid paper presenting an effective method for a particular class of grammars.
