- Presents a method for learning stochastic And-Or grammars that identifies a special tractable case rather than a true generalization of prior structure learning work.
- The identified special case and resulting method constitute a reasonable and valuable contribution.
- The focus on this specific, tractable problem is justified and distinct from broader NLP structure-learning challenges.
- The proposed algorithm is sensible and successfully induces grammars compared to other methods.
- Experimental evaluation is weak and should be strengthened with additional comparisons to other approaches.
- Specifically recommend comparing to Stolcke and Omohondro (ref. 14), which appears applicable to both tasks.
- Overall assessment: a solid paper presenting a method for learning the structure of a particular class of grammars.
