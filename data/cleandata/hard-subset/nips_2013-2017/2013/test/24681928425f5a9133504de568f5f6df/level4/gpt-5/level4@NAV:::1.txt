This paper introduces a method for learning the structure of stochastic And-Or grammars. While the paper claims to “generalize” prior work on structure learning, the approach is in fact a special case that makes the problem tractable. This is a fair and valuable contribution on its own, and I would avoid suggesting that the problem is more general than the structure learning challenges typically addressed in NLP. The core algorithm is well-motivated and performs competitively against other grammar induction methods.

The experimental evaluation could be strengthened by including additional comparisons to related approaches. In particular, the method of Stolcke and Omohondro (reference 14) appears applicable to both tasks and would provide a useful comparison point. Overall, this is a solid paper that presents a method for learning the structure of a specific class of grammars.
