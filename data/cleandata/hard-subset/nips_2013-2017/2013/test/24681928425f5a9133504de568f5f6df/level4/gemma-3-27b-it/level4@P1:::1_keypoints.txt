- Presents a method for learning the structure of stochastic And-Or grammars, addressing a specific tractable case.  
- Claims generalization of prior structure learning work while focusing on a constrained, solvable setting.  
- Proposed algorithm is sensible and well-motivated.  
- Empirically demonstrates successful performance and competitive results against existing grammar induction methods.  
- Experimental evaluation is limited and could be strengthened with additional comparisons.  
- Specifically recommends comparing to Stolcke and Omohondro [14], which appears applicable and would be a useful benchmark.  
- Overall a solid, clearly explained, and empirically validated contribution to unsupervised grammar learning.
