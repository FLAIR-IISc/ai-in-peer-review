- Presents a method for learning the structure of stochastic And-Or grammars.  
- Claims to generalize prior structure-learning work but actually focuses on a specific, tractable special case.  
- Emphasizing the tractable special case is a strength, making the problem solvable and a valuable contribution.  
- Authors should avoid overstating the method’s generality, especially relative to broader structure-learning problems (e.g., NLP).  
- Proposed algorithm is sound and performs well against the baselines shown.  
- Experimental evaluation is incomplete and could be strengthened with additional comparisons.  
- Specifically recommend comparing to the Stolcke and Omohondro [14] method, which appears applicable.  
- Overall: a solid paper presenting an effective method for a particular class of grammars.
