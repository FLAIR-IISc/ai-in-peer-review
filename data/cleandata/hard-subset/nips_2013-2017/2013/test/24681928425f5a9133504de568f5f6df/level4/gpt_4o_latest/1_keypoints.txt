- Introduces a method for learning the structure of stochastic And-Or grammars.  
- Claim of "generalization" over prior work is overstated; method is a specific, simplified case.  
- The simplification makes the problem more tractable and is a valid, meaningful contribution.  
- The paper should avoid framing the problem as more general than typical NLP structure-learning challenges.  
- Core algorithm is well-designed and performs effectively against other grammar induction methods.  
- Experimental evaluation needs stronger comparisons—specifically include Stolcke and Omohondro (ref. 14) as a baseline.  
- Overall, a strong paper presenting a method for learning structure of a specific class of grammars.
