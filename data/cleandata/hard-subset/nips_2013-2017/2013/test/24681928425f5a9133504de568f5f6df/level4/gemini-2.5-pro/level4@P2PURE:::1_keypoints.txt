- Presents a method for learning the structure of stochastic And-Or grammars.
- Claims of generalization are misleading; the approach is actually a special case.
- The specialization makes the problem tractable and is a valuable contribution.
- Paper should be reframed as a tractable special case of structure learning in NLP rather than a generalization.
- Core algorithm is sensible and shows success against the tested grammar induction baselines.
- Experimental evaluation is limited and should include more comparisons.
- Specifically compare against the Stolcke and Omohondro [14] method, which appears applicable.
- Overall, a solid paper that effectively learns structure for a particular class of grammars.
