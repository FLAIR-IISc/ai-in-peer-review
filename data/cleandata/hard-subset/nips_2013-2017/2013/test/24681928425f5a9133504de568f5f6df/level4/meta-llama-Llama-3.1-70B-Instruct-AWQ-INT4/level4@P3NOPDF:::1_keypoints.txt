- Presents a method for learning the structure of stochastic And-Or grammars (a specific class of grammars).
- Authors claim to generalize prior work, but the approach is actually a special-case restriction that improves tractability.
- The contribution is valuable to the field.
- Proposed algorithm is sensible and empirically successful at inducing grammars compared to some baselines.
- Experimental evaluation shows effectiveness but is missing broader comparisons to relevant methods.
- Stolcke and Omohondro (ref. 14) appears applicable to the tasks and should be included as a comparison.
- Overall a solid, clearly presented paper with well-executed experiments; additional comparisons would strengthen it.
