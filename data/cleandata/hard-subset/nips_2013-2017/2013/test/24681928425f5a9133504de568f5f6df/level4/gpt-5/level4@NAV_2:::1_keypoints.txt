- Proposes a technique for inducing stochastic And-Or grammars.  
- Claims to generalize prior structure-learning work but actually targets a restricted, tractable case.  
- Positioning is reasonable; contribution is meaningful but not broadly generalizable beyond typical NLP structure-learning problems.  
- Core algorithm is sensible and achieves competitive performance versus other grammar induction methods.  
- Empirical evaluation is limited and would benefit from additional baselines.  
- Specifically recommends comparing to Bayesian model merging (Stolcke & Omohundro) as a relevant baseline.  
- Overall assessment: a solid paper presenting an effective method for structure learning within a specific class of grammars.
