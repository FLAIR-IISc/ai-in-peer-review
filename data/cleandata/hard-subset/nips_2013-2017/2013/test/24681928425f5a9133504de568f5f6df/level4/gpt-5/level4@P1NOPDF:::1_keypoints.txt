- Proposes a method for learning the structure of stochastic And-Or grammars.
- Claims to “generalize” prior work but actually targets a constrained special case, so the generalization claim is overstated.
- Focusing on this constrained case is reasonable and the contribution is valuable on its own.
- Core algorithm is sensible and effective, performing well relative to existing grammar-induction methods.
- Experimental evaluation needs broader comparisons; Stolcke and Omohundro (ref. 14) is an important missing baseline.
- Overall: a solid paper offering a clear and useful approach for learning structure of a specific class of grammars.
