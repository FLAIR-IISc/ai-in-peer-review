- Introduces a method for learning structure of stochastic And-Or grammars.
- Authors claim the method “generalizes” prior work, but it is actually a tractable special case.
- The tractable specialization is a reasonable and valuable contribution.
- Core algorithm is well-motivated and technically sound.
- Empirical results are competitive and support the paper’s claims.
- Experimental evaluation lacks a key comparison to Stolcke and Omohondro (Ref. 14), which is directly applicable.
- Omitting that benchmark weakens the paper’s relative performance analysis.
- Writing is generally clear and the technical exposition is solid.
- Addressing the missing comparison would strengthen the submission and clarify practical significance.
- Overall: a strong, focused, and publishable contribution with minor revisions needed.
