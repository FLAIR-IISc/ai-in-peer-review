This work introduces a technique for inferring the structure of stochastic And-Or grammars. The authors position this as a generalization of previous structure learning research, but it is more accurately a specialization, which is a key reason for the method's tractability. This is a significant contribution on its own merits, and I would suggest not overstating the generality of the problem in relation to typical structure learning in NLP. The fundamental algorithm is sound and proves effective when benchmarked against other grammar induction approaches.

The experimental evaluation could be strengthened by including additional comparisons to existing methods. Specifically, the approach by Stolcke and Omohondro (reference 14) appears directly applicable to the tasks in this paper and would serve as an important benchmark.

Overall, this is a well-executed paper that presents a valuable method for structure learning within a particular class of grammars.
