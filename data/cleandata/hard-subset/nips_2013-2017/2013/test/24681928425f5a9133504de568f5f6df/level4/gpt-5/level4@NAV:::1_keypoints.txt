- Introduces a method for learning the structure of stochastic And-Or grammars.  
- Claims to “generalize” prior work are overstated; the approach is actually a tractable special case.  
- The contribution is fair and valuable but should avoid broader generality claims relative to typical NLP structure learning.  
- The core algorithm is well-motivated and competitive with other grammar induction methods.  
- Experimental evaluation is limited and could be strengthened with more comparisons.  
- Specifically recommend comparing to the Stolcke and Omohondro method (ref. 14) as a relevant baseline.  
- Overall: a solid paper presenting a method for learning structure of a specific class of grammars.
