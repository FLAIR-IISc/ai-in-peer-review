- Introduces a novel method for jointly learning structure and parameters of stochastic AND-OR grammars to model recursive phenomena (e.g., natural language).  
- Proposes an unsupervised incremental structure learning algorithm that adds AND-OR fragments based on model likelihood and prior gains.  
- Evaluated on event grammar and image grammar tasks, achieving competitive results versus prior work.  
- Provides a computationally feasible framework for learning stochastic grammars with AND and OR rules, which is a significant contribution.  
- Limitation: Tractability and scalability of the learning algorithm are not evaluated; unclear if it scales to thousands of data points.  
- Limitation: No experiments on natural language benchmarks (e.g., Penn Treebank); including such experiments would substantially strengthen the paper’s impact.
