- Proposes a method for learning the structure of stochastic And–Or grammars.  
- Claims to generalize prior structure-learning work, but is actually a tractable, specialized instance.  
- The specialization is reasonable and provides a meaningful contribution, but the paper should not overstate its generality.  
- Core algorithm is well-motivated, sensible, and shows competitive performance versus existing grammar induction approaches.  
- Empirical evaluation is limited and should include broader comparisons; Stolcke and Omohundro (ref. 14) is a particularly relevant baseline.  
- Overall: a solid paper that advances structure learning for a specific class of grammars.
