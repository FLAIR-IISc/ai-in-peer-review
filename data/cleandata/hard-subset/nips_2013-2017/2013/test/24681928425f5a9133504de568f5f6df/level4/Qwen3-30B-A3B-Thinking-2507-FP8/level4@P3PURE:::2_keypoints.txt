- Proposes a method to learn both structure and parameters of stochastic AND-OR grammars via tractable, unsupervised structure learning that incrementally adds AND-OR fragments based on likelihood and prior gains.  
- Demonstrates the approach on event grammars and image grammars, reporting results competitive with prior work.  
- Reviewer finds the approach compelling with promising empirical results.  
- Primary concern 1: paper does not address computational tractability or scalability—unclear if the method scales to datasets with thousands of examples.  
- Primary concern 2: lacks evaluation on natural language (the most relevant domain); recommends experiments on standard NLP benchmarks (e.g., Penn Treebank) to validate applicability.
