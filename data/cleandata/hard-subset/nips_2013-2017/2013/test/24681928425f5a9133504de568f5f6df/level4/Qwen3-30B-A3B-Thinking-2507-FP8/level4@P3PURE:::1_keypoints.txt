- Proposes a method for learning the structure of stochastic And-Or grammars.
- Claims to "generalize" prior structure-learning work, but actually restricts to a special case that makes the problem tractable.
- The narrower focus is reasonable, though less general than structure-learning challenges in NLP.
- The core algorithm is sensible and shows competitive performance against existing grammar-induction methods.
- Experimental evaluation is generally sound but could be strengthened with more baseline comparisons.
- Specifically recommends comparing to Stolcke and Omohondro (ref. 14), which appears applicable and would be a valuable benchmark.
- Overall, a solid contribution presenting an effective method for learning structure in a specific class of grammars.
