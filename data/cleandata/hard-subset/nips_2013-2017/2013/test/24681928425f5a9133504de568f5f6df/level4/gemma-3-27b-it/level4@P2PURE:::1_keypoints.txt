- Introduces a method for learning the structure of stochastic And-Or grammars.  
- Claims to generalize prior structure learning but actually implements a restrictive special case that makes the problem tractable.  
- Suggests the work should be framed as addressing a specific instance of the broader structure learning problem.  
- Core algorithm is well-reasoned and shows strong performance against existing grammar induction methods.  
- Experimental evaluation needs additional comparisons, notably the Stolcke and Omohondro method (ref. 14) as a baseline.  
- Overall assessment: a sound contribution presenting a method for learning structure of a defined class of grammars.
