This paper presents a method for learning the structure of stochastic And-Or grammars. Although the authors suggest that this approach generalizes previous work on structure learning, it is actually a special case that renders the problem tractable. This is a reasonable point in its own right, and the paper makes a valuable contribution. Therefore, I would not argue that the problem is more general than the structure learning problem typically encountered in NLP.

The basic algorithm proposed is sensible and has demonstrated success when compared to other methods for inducing grammars. However, the experimental evaluation could be strengthened by including more comparisons to alternative methods. In particular, the method of Stolcke and Omohondro (reference 14) appears to be applicable to both tasks and would provide a valuable comparison point. Overall, this is a solid paper that presents a method for learning the structure of a particular class of grammars.
