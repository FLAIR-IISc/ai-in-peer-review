This paper presents a method for learning the structure of Stolkinde's and Omohonड्रो's grammar. The paper suggests that this structure " generalizes" previous work on learning grammar, but it is actually a special case that simplifies the problem. This is a valid point in itself, and the paper makes a good contribution, so I will not try to argue that the problem is more general than the problem of learning grammar encountered in NLP. The basic algorithm is more insightful and successful than other methods that induce grammar.

Experimental evaluation can be done better compared to other methods. In particular, Stolkinde's and Omohonड्रो's method (reference 14) is applicable to both tasks and will be a valuable point of comparison.
