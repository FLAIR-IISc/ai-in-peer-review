- Presents a method for learning the structure of stochastic And-Or grammars.
- Claims to “generalize” prior structure learning approaches, but is actually a restrictive special case that enables tractability.
- The restriction is a reasonable justification, yet the authors should avoid overstating generality relative to NLP structure learning.
- Core algorithm is sensible and shows success against existing grammar induction methods.
- Experimental evaluation is limited and should include comparisons to a broader set of approaches.
- Stolcke and Omohondro (ref. 14) appears applicable to both tasks and should be used as a comparative baseline.
- Overall, a solid contribution for learning the structure of a particular class of grammars.
