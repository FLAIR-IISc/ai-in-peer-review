- Proposes a method for learning the structure of stochastic And-Or grammars, extending prior structure-learning work.  
- Focuses on a specialized, more manageable scenario rather than general structure learning.  
- The specialization is justified; the contribution is valuable within its scope.  
- The core algorithm is sensible and performs well compared to other grammar-induction methods.  
- Experimental evaluation should include more comparisons to alternative methods.  
- Specifically recommend comparing against Stolcke and Omohondro (ref. 14), which is applicable to the tasks.  
- Overall, a solid paper presenting a viable method for a particular class of grammars.
