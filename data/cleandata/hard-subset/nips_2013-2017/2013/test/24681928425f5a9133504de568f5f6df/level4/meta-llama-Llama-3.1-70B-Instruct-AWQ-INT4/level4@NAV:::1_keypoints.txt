- Proposes a novel approach for learning the structure of stochastic And-Or grammars.  
- Claims generalization of prior structure learning work, but reviewer argues it’s actually a specialized, more tractable case.  
- Despite limited generality, the method is a valuable contribution on its own.  
- No need to debate its generality relative to broader NLP structure learning challenges.  
- Fundamental algorithm is sound and performs well compared to other grammar induction methods.  
- Experimental evaluation should be strengthened with additional comparisons.  
- Specifically recommend comparing to Stolcke and Omohondro (ref. 14), which appears applicable to both tasks.  
- Overall assessment: a solid paper presenting a method for learning the structure of a specific class of grammars.
