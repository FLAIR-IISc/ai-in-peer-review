- Presents a method for learning the structure of stochastic And-Or grammars.  
- Authors claim the method “generalizes” prior work, but it is actually a special-case restriction that makes the problem tractable.  
- The restriction is valid and the contribution is worthwhile, but not more general than typical NLP structure-learning problems.  
- Core algorithm is sensible and performs well compared to existing grammar-induction methods.  
- Experimental evaluation is limited and could be strengthened with more comprehensive comparisons.  
- Specifically, the Stolcke and Omohondro method (ref. 14) appears applicable and should be included as a benchmark.  
- Overall assessment: a solid paper presenting a method for learning structure of a specific class of grammars.
