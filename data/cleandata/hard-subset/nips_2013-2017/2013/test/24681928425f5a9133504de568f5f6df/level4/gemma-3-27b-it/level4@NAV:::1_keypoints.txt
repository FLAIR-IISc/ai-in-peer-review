- Introduces a technique for structure learning in stochastic And-Or grammars.  
- Authors claim a generalization of prior approaches, but it is more accurately a tractable special case.  
- The observation about limited generality is valid yet does not undermine the paper’s contribution.  
- The core algorithm is well-reasoned and shows effective performance compared to existing grammar induction methods.  
- Experimental evaluation should be expanded, notably by comparing to the Stolcke and Omohondro (ref. 14) approach.  
- Overall, a sound paper that details a method for learning the structure of a specific grammar class.
