This paper proposes a method for learning the structure of stochastic And-Or grammars. While the manuscript claims that it “generalizes” prior work on structure learning, the approach in fact targets a constrained special case that makes the problem tractable. This focus is reasonable and the contribution is valuable on its own, but I would not present it as more general than the structure-learning problems typically addressed in NLP.

The core algorithm is sensible and appears effective, performing well relative to existing grammar induction methods. However, the experimental evaluation would benefit from a broader set of comparisons. In particular, the method of Stolcke and Omohundro (reference 14) seems applicable to both tasks considered here and would provide an important baseline.

Overall, this is a solid paper that offers a clear and useful approach to learning the structure of a specific class of grammars.
