- Introduces a method for learning the structure of stochastic And-Or grammars.  
- The claim of "generalizing" prior work is misleading—it's actually a special case that enables tractable learning.  
- The contribution is valid and the problem scope relative to broader NLP structure learning is appropriate.  
- The core algorithm is well-motivated, logically sound, and competitive with existing grammar induction methods.  
- Experimental evaluation should include additional comparisons, especially with Stolcke and Omohondro (Ref. 14), as a meaningful benchmark.  
- Overall, a well-constructed paper presenting a notable contribution to structure learning for a specific grammar class.
