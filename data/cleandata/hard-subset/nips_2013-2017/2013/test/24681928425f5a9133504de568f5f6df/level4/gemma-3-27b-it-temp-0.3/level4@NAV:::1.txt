This paper introduces a technique for structure learning in stochastic And-Or grammars. The authors position this work as a generalization of prior structure learning approaches; however, it is more accurately described as a tractable special case. This is a valid and worthwhile observation, and the paper represents a positive contribution – the claim of broader generality is not essential to its merit. The core algorithm is sound and demonstrates effective performance relative to existing grammar induction methods.

The experimental evaluation would benefit from expanded comparisons with alternative techniques. Specifically, including results against the method proposed by Stolcke and Omohondro (reference 14) – which appears applicable to both tasks – would provide a valuable benchmark. Overall, this is a strong paper detailing a method for learning the structure of a specific grammar class.
