- Efficient fragment value computation using sufficient statistics.  
- Evaluated on event parsing and visual object tasks, showing meaningful advance over prior work.

- Unclear if method generalizes to fragments with arity >2 or is restricted to Chomsky normal form—needs clarification.

- Missing precise descriptions of experimental setup and data representation.  
- No clear presentation of the nature or examples of learned structures.  
- Insufficient comparison details to baselines, limiting assessment of empirical gains.
