This paper proposes a method for learning the structure of stochastic And-Or grammars. While the authors claim it “generalizes” prior work on structure learning, the proposed formulation is actually a special case, which is what makes the problem tractable. This is a reasonable and worthwhile contribution on its own; I would not assert that it is more general than the structure-learning problems typically addressed in NLP. The core algorithm is sensible and performs well compared to other grammar induction methods.

The experimental evaluation could be strengthened with additional baselines. In particular, the method of Stolcke and Omohondro (reference 14) appears applicable to both tasks and would serve as a valuable point of comparison. Overall, this is a solid paper presenting a method for learning the structure of a specific class of grammars.
