This paper introduces a technique for structure learning in stochastic And-Or grammars. The authors position this work as a generalization of prior structure learning approaches; however, it is more accurately described as a tractable special case. This is a valid observation, and the work constitutes a worthwhile contribution – the claim of broader generality is not essential to its merit. The core algorithm is well-reasoned and demonstrates effective performance relative to existing grammar induction methods.

The experimental analysis would benefit from expanded comparisons. Specifically, including a comparison to the approach by Stolcke and Omohondro (reference 14) – which appears applicable to both the presented tasks – would strengthen the evaluation. Overall, this is a sound paper that details a method for learning the structure of a defined grammar class.
