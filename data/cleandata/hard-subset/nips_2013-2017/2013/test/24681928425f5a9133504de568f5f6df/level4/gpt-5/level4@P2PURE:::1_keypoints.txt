- Proposes a method for learning the structure of stochastic And-Or grammars.  
- Authors' claim of “generalizing” prior work is overstated; the formulation is actually a tractable special case.  
- The restriction to a special case is a reasonable and worthwhile contribution on its own.  
- The core algorithm is sensible and shows strong empirical performance compared to other grammar induction methods.  
- Experimental evaluation is limited and should include additional baselines, notably the Stolcke and Omohondro (ref. 14) method.  
- Overall, a solid paper for learning structure of a specific class of grammars.
