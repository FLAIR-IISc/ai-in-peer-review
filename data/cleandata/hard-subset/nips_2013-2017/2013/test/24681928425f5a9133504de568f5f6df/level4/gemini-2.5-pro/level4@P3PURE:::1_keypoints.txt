- Presents a method for learning the structure of stochastic And-Or grammars.  
- The claim of "generalizing" past methods is misleading — it’s actually a restricted special case that enables tractability.  
- Focusing on this tractable subproblem is a valuable contribution and should not be overstated as broader generalization.  
- The proposed algorithm is sensible and shows strong empirical performance against other grammar induction methods.  
- Experimental evaluation could be improved by adding comparisons, notably the method of Stolcke and Omohondro [14].  
- Overall: a solid paper presenting an effective method for a particular class of grammars.
