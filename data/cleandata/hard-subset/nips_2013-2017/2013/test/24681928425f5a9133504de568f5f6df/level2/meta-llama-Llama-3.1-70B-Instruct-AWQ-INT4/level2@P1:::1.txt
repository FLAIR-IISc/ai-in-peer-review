The paper "Unsupervised Structure Learning of Stochastic And-Or Grammars" presents a unified formalization of stochastic And-Or grammars that can be applied to different types of data, including text, images, and events. The authors propose an unsupervised approach to learning the structures and parameters of such grammars, starting from a trivial initial grammar and iteratively inducing compositions and reconfigurations in a unified manner. The approach is evaluated on event grammars and image grammars, achieving comparable or better performance than previous approaches.

* A unified formalization of stochastic And-Or grammars that is agnostic to the type of data being modeled.
* An unsupervised approach to learning the structures and parameters of stochastic And-Or grammars.
* A method for inducing compositions and reconfigurations in a unified manner, which is more efficient and robust than previous approaches.
