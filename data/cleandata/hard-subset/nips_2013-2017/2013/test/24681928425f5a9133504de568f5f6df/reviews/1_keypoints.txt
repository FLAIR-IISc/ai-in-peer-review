- Presents a method for learning the structure of stochastic And-Or grammars.  
- Claims to “generalize” previous structure learning work, but is actually a special case.  
- The restriction to a special case makes the problem tractable — the reviewer sees this as reasonable.  
- The basic algorithm is sensible and performs well compared to other grammar induction methods.  
- Experimental evaluation could be improved with more comparisons to other methods.  
- Specifically recommends comparing to Stolcke and Omohondro (ref. 14) as a valuable baseline.  
- Overall: a solid contribution for learning the structure of a particular class of grammars.
