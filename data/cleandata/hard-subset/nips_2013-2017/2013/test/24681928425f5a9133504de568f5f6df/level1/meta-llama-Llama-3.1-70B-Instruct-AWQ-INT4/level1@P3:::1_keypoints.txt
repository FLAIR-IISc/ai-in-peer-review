- Clear and concise introduction highlighting challenges and limitations of existing approaches.  
- Well-organized, easy-to-follow presentation with thorough related-work review.  
- Technically sound mathematical framework with detailed derivation of posterior gain and optimization procedure.  
- Good balance of theoretical rigor and practical intuition, accessible to a broad audience.  
- Experimental evaluation on event and image grammar datasets is thorough and shows the method outperforming prior approaches in several cases.  
- Significant contribution with potential applicability across computer vision, NLP, and robotics.
