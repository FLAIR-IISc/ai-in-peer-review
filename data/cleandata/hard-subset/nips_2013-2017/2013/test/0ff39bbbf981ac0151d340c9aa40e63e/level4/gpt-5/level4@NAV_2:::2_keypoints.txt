- Proposes a two-stage pipeline combining matrix completion with latent semantic analysis for cross-lingual document representations.  
- Optimizes matrix completion via a projected gradient descent procedure.  
- Empirically outperforms monolingual and cross-lingual baselines on cross-lingual sentiment classification (multilingual Amazon reviews).  
- Learning curves show high accuracy can be achieved with relatively few unlabeled parallel pairs.  
- Experimental evaluation is comprehensive and includes comparisons to two baselines and two state-of-the-art dimensionality-reduction methods.  
- Paper is clearly written, well-structured, and provides full hyperparameter/selection details for reproducibility.  
- Minor clarity suggestion: better motivate the use of matrix completion in the Introduction.  
- Novel contribution: applying matrix completion to cross-lingual representation learning with demonstrated empirical gains and applicability to other tasks.
