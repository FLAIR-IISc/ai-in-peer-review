- Evaluation robustness is unclear and needs further clarification.  
- Reported gains over OPCA/CCA may be due to linear SVM sensitivity to feature scaling rather than the method itself.

- Recommend replacing the linear SVM with a scaling-insensitive classifier (e.g., gradient-boosted decision trees in Weka) to validate true efficacy.  
- If results persist with a scaling-insensitive model, the work shows matrix completion as a controlled non-linear inference for cross-lingual tasks (significant contribution).  
- The core idea is novel within the translingual representation learning context.  
- Clarify term-treatment: whether the unified document-term matrix uses language-agnostic normalization (lemmatization) or preserves language-specific surface forms.
