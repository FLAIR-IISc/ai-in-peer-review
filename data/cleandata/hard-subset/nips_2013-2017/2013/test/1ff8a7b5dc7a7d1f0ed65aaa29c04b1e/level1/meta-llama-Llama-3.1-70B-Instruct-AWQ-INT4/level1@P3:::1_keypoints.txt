- Proposes a novel deep recurrent neural network (DRNN) architecture that stacks a hierarchy of RNNs to better model temporal hierarchies.  
- Well-written and well-organized paper that clearly explains the DRNN architecture and its components.  
- Provides detailed description of hidden state evolution, output generation, and training setup.  
- Offers in-depth analysis showing DRNNs can capture temporal hierarchies and long-term dependencies.  
- Empirical results achieve state-of-the-art performance on character-level language modeling (Wikipedia corpus).  
- Thorough evaluation of architecture components and behavior across tasks presented.  
- Strength: comprehensive analysis and clear exposition of how DRNNs work.  
- Weakness: lacks comprehensive comparisons to other state-of-the-art architectures.  
- Weakness: limited demonstration of applicability beyond character-level language modeling.  
- Weakness: evaluation could be more comprehensive (more tasks, baselines, or ablations).  
- Originality: hierarchical RNN idea not entirely new, but implementation and analysis are original.  
- Significance: makes a meaningful contribution to deep learning by improving temporal modeling in RNNs.  
- Recommendation: Accept with minor revisions to add comparisons, broaden evaluation, and clarify applicability.  
- Rating: 8/10.
