- Paper summary: Studies stacked deep RNNs (top-only and all-layers outputs) on character-level LM, showing deeper layers capture longer time scales and achieve strong performance.
- Contribution: Empirical and analytical study of stacked RNNs at scale, not architectural novelty.
- Strength: Holding parameter count constant, deeper RNNs outperform single-layer RNNs on char LM.
- Strength: Multiple probes (layer ablation, typo perturbation, context switches) support emergence of temporal hierarchy.
- Strength: Shows plain BPTT+SGD can learn long-range structures (e.g., matching parentheses), narrowing the HF gap.
- Strength: Careful experimental setup with comparable parameter budgets and transparent training details.
- Weakness: Novelty claim overstated; stacked/“deep” RNNs have prior work (e.g., Graves 2013, stacked LSTMs).
- Weakness: Analyses conducted on a single dataset; generality is untested.
- Weakness: Missing truncated BPTT baselines despite prior evidence of their effectiveness and unclear HF cost comparisons.
- Weakness: Focuses on vanilla RNNs; lacks comparisons with LSTMs/GRUs to contextualize findings.
- Quality: Experimental methodology is sound and reproducible; analyses convincingly support time-scale claims.
- Clarity: Well written and organized, but novelty claims should be softened and prior stacked-RNN work cited.
- Originality: Limited architecturally; originality lies in large-scale empirical analysis and BPTT findings.
- Significance: Practically useful results showing depth helps at fixed parameter budgets and that plain BPTT captures long-range dependencies.
- Improvement: Reframe as analysis paper and explicitly cite prior stacked RNN/LSTM work.
- Improvement: Add truncated BPTT baseline (e.g., 30 steps) and test its effect on learning parentheses.
- Improvement: Add a second task/domain (e.g., speech) to validate generality of time-scale hierarchy.
- Improvement: Include matched-parameter comparisons with LSTM/GRU stacks.
- Accept arguments: Clear empirical evidence for depth benefits, insightful analyses, and demonstration that plain BPTT captures long-range dependencies.
