### NIPS Review

**Paper Summary:**

This paper investigates the properties of Deep Recurrent Neural Networks (DRNNs), an architecture created by stacking multiple RNN layers, where each layer receives the hidden state sequence from the layer below as its input. The central hypothesis is that this deep structure allows the network to learn a temporal hierarchy, with different layers operating on different time scales, making it well-suited for tasks with complex, multi-scale temporal dependencies.

The authors evaluate this architecture on the challenging task of character-level language modeling using a large Wikipedia corpus. They propose and compare two variants: one with an output only from the top layer (DRNN-1O) and one with outputs from all layers (DRNN-AO). Their experimental results show that both DRNN models significantly outperform a standard (shallow) RNN with a similar parameter count. Notably, their DRNNs achieve state-of-the-art performance, matching or exceeding results from more complex models (like multiplicative RNNs) trained with advanced second-order optimization methods (Hessian-free), while using only standard Stochastic Gradient Descent (SGD) with gradient clipping.

The paper's main contribution is not just the performance result, but the in-depth analysis that provides compelling evidence for the temporal hierarchy hypothesis. Through a series of well-designed experiments, the authors demonstrate that:
1.  Higher layers in the DRNN learn to operate on longer time scales, as measured by their response to perturbations.
2.  Different layers contribute differently to the final prediction, with lower layers capturing short-term statistics and higher layers handling longer-range dependencies like sentence structure and parenthesis matching.
3.  The DRNN is capable of modeling very long-term dependencies (e.g., closing parentheses over hundreds of characters), suggesting it has learned more than simple sequence memorization.

### Evaluation

**Quality: Excellent**

The paper is technically sound and presents a complete and convincing piece of research.
*   **Well-supported Claims:** The central claim that DRNNs learn a temporal hierarchy is not merely asserted but rigorously tested through multiple analytical experiments. The perturbation analysis (Fig. 3) provides direct quantitative evidence of different time scales in different layers. The qualitative analysis of generated text after ablating specific layers (Table 2) and the specific case study on parenthesis matching (Fig. 4) provide strong, complementary support.
*   **Rigorous Experiments:** The experiments are conducted on a large-scale, standard benchmark (the Wikipedia character corpus), allowing for direct comparison with prior state-of-the-art work. The authors are careful to keep the number of trainable parameters roughly constant across their models (RNN vs. DRNNs), ensuring a fair comparison of architectural choices.
*   **Honesty and Limitations:** The authors are transparent about their limitations, such as the high computational cost preventing extensive hyperparameter search and limiting them to three network instantiations. They also acknowledge that the different training procedure for the DRNN-1O (incremental layer-wise training) makes a direct comparison with the DRNN-AO slightly difficult, as performance gains could be attributed to either the architecture or the training method. This honesty strengthens the paper's credibility.

**Clarity: Excellent**

The paper is exceptionally well-written, organized, and easy to follow.
*   **Clear Motivation:** The introduction clearly articulates the conceptual gap between deep feedforward networks (hierarchical processing, no memory) and shallow RNNs (memory, no hierarchical processing at a single time step) and motivates the DRNN as a natural synthesis of the two.
*   **Precise Description:** The model architecture and training procedures are described with sufficient detail. The equations are clear, and the accompanying diagrams (Fig. 1) are helpful. A knowledgeable reader would likely be able to reproduce the work based on the information provided.
*   **Logical Flow:** The paper builds its argument logically, starting with the model definition, presenting the main performance results, and then dedicating a significant portion to the analysis that explains *why* the model is successful.

**Originality: Good**

While the core idea of stacking RNN layers is not entirely novel (as the authors themselves cite, e.g., Graves et al. [8]), the paper's contribution is original and significant in several ways:
*   **Novelty in Analysis:** To my knowledge, this is one of the first papers to provide such a thorough and direct investigation into the *emergent properties* of deep RNNs. Prior work often used stacked RNNs as a black box to improve performance. This paper's primary contribution is in opening that box and analyzing the learned temporal hierarchy, which is a novel and important insight.
*   **Significant Empirical Contribution:** The demonstration that a relatively simple DRNN trained with SGD can match the performance of more complex architectures trained with computationally expensive second-order methods was a highly significant result at the time. It questioned the necessity of complex optimizers for training deep recurrent models and made state-of-the-art sequence modeling more accessible.
*   **Clear Differentiation:** The paper does an excellent job of positioning its work relative to prior art, acknowledging architectural precedents while clearly stating its novel focus on in-depth analysis and understanding.

**Significance: Excellent**

The results and ideas presented in this paper are highly significant and have had a lasting impact on the field.
*   **Impact on Practice:** The paper provides strong evidence that depth is a crucial ingredient for powerful recurrent models, just as it is for feedforward models. This has influenced the design of nearly all modern state-of-the-art sequence models (e.g., in translation, speech recognition), where stacked LSTMs or Transformers are now standard.
*   **Impact on Research:** The analytical techniques used in the paper (e.g., measuring recovery time from perturbations) provide a blueprint for how to analyze and understand the internal dynamics of deep recurrent models. The insights about temporal hierarchies have spurred further research into how neural networks process information over time.
*   **Advancement of State-of-the-Art:** The paper demonstrably advanced the state of the art in a challenging and important domain (language modeling), providing both better results and a deeper understanding of the underlying model.

### Arguments For and Against Acceptance

**Arguments for Acceptance:**
1.  **Strong Empirical Results:** Achieves state-of-the-art performance on a major benchmark with a simpler training algorithm than competing methods.
2.  **Excellent Analysis:** The core strength of the paper is its deep dive into *why* the model works, providing convincing evidence for the emergence of a temporal hierarchy. This is what makes it a top-tier scientific contribution.
3.  **High Significance and Impact:** The findings directly influenced subsequent research and the practical application of deep learning for sequence data. The principle that "depth in time" is beneficial is a foundational concept.
4.  **Exceptional Clarity:** The paper is a model of clear scientific communication.

**Arguments against Acceptance:**
1.  **Limited Architectural Exploration:** Due to computational constraints, the study is limited to 5-layer DRNNs and a single shallow RNN. A broader exploration of how properties change with depth would have been interesting, though perhaps infeasible for a conference paper.
2.  **Confounded Training Methods:** The best-performing model (DRNN-1O) uses a specialized layer-wise training scheme, making it difficult to isolate the benefit of the architecture from the benefit of the training curriculum.

### Final Recommendation

This is a very strong paper. The minor weaknesses are far outweighed by the significant strengths. The combination of state-of-the-art empirical results with a deep, insightful analysis of the model's behavior makes this an exemplary NIPS paper. It not only presents a method that works but also provides a valuable understanding of *how* it works. I strongly recommend acceptance.
