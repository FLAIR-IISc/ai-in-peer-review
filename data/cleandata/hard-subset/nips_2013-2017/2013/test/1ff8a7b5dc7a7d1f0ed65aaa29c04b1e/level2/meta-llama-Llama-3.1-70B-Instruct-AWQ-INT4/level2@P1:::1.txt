This paper, "Training and Analyzing Deep Recurrent Neural Networks", presents a novel architecture for processing time series data, called Deep Recurrent Neural Networks (DRNNs). The paper demonstrates the effectiveness of DRNNs on character-level language modeling, achieving state-of-the-art performance for recurrent networks on a Wikipedia text corpus.
