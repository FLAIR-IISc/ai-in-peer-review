**Review of the Paper "Inverse Density as an Inverse Problem: the Fredholm Equation Approach"**

**Summary**

The paper presents a novel approach to estimating the ratio of two densities, q/p, by formulating it as an inverse problem using the Fredholm equation of the first kind. The authors propose a principled framework for constructing algorithms and analyzing them theoretically using regularization techniques and Reproducing Kernel Hilbert Spaces (RKHS). The resulting algorithms, called FIRE (Fredholm Inverse Regularized Estimator), are flexible, simple, and easy to implement. The paper also provides a detailed theoretical analysis of the algorithms, including concentration bounds and convergence rates for the Gaussian kernel.
