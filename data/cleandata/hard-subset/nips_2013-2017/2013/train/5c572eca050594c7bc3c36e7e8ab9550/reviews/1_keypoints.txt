- Proposes a new density ratio estimation method by reformulating the problem as a Fredholm integral equation.  
- Novelty compared to existing methods rests on this integral-equation reformulation.  
- Strong: paper is well written and addresses an important, interesting problem.  
- Missing theoretical detail: justify statements like ∫ k_t(x,y) f(x) dx = f(y) + O(t) with proof or reference.  
- Interesting special case q=1 suggests density estimation without bandwidth→0; discuss how bandwidth roles differ from KDE.  
- Add brief explanation of Tikhonov regularization for readers unfamiliar with it.  
- All proofs are in the supplementary material and have not been checked by the reviewer.  
- Explain why a naive plug-in approach (estimating p and q separately) is inferior to the proposed method.  
- Unclear empirical/theoretical comparison to prior methods (e.g., [12]); provide minimax-optimality discussion and distance of Corollaries 2 and 4 from optimal rates.  
- Provide references for datasets used (CPUsmall, Kin8nm, USPS).  
- Include toy experiments (e.g., 1D p and q plots of true vs estimated p/q) to illustrate method performance and quality.  
- Overall: nice paper but comparative performance and some theoretical/experimental details are unclear.
