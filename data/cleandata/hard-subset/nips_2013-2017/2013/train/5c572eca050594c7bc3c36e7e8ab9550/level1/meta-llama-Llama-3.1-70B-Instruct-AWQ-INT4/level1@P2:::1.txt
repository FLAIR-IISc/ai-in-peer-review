The authors apply regularization techniques, combined with Reproducing Kernel Hilbert Space (RKHS) norms, to develop a principled framework for constructing algorithms and analyzing them theoretically.

The paper is well-written, and the authors provide a clear and concise explanation of the problem, the proposed approach, and the theoretical analysis. The use of mathematical notation is consistent and easy to follow. The paper is well-organized, and the authors provide a good balance between theoretical analysis and experimental results.
