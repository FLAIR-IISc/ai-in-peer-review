The paper "Inverse Density as an Inverse Problem: the Fredholm Equation Approach" proposes a novel approach to density ratio estimation by formulating it as an inverse problem using the Fredholm equation of the first kind. The paper provides theoretical convergence results and experimental validation of practical performance.

The main contribution of the paper is the development of a principled framework for constructing algorithms and analyzing them theoretically. The authors show that their approach enables reliable cross-validation (CV) for parameter selection in an unsupervised setting, which is a significant advantage over existing methods. The paper also provides a detailed theoretical analysis for the case of the Gaussian kernel, including concentration bounds and convergence rates.
