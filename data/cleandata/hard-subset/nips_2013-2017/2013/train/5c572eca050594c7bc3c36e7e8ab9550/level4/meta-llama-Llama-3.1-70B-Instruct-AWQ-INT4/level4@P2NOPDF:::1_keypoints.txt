- Proposes a novel Fredholm-integral-based method for density ratio p/q estimation distinct from existing approaches.  
- Paper is well-written and addresses an important, interesting problem.  
- Missing proof or reference for the kernel approximation statement ∫ k_t(x,y) f(x) dx = f(y) + O(t); include proof or citation.  
- Should discuss the special case q = 1 (density estimation) and how bandwidth behavior here differs from standard KDE.  
- Provide a clearer discussion of the role and selection of bandwidth parameters compared to KDE.  
- Add a brief explanation of Tikhonov regularization for readers unfamiliar with it.  
- All proofs are in the supplementary material, but their correctness has not been independently verified.  
- Explain why a naive plug-in approach (estimating p and q separately) is inferior to the proposed method.  
- Lack of clear performance comparison to existing methods (e.g., [12]) and unclear how the provided upper bounds relate to minimax optimality.  
- Provide references for datasets used (CPUsmall, Kin8nm, USPS).  
- Include toy problem demonstrations (e.g., 1-D plots of true vs. estimated p/q for fixed known p and q) to illustrate estimation quality.  
- Overall, a promising approach but unclear empirical performance vs. prior algorithms and missing illustrative experiments.
