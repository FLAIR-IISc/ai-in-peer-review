- Novel reformulation: paper introduces a new Fredholm integral equation approach to density ratio estimation (p/q).  
- Writing and topic: clear, engaging writing; density ratio estimation is important and interesting.  
- Missing derivation: the claim ∫ k_t(x,y)f(x)dx = f(y) + O(t) lacks an in-paper proof, supplement proof, or reference.  
- KDE connection: method applies to density estimation (q=1); discussion needed on how bandwidth roles differ from KDE and no bandwidth→0 requirement.  
- Regularization clarity: include a brief explanation of Tikhonov regularization for readers unfamiliar with it.  
- Supplementary proofs: all proofs are relegated to the supplement and their correctness is unchecked in the paper.  
- Baseline comparison: add comparison to the naive plug-in approach (separate p and q estimation) and explain superiority.  
- Minimax/related work: unclear how performance compares to other methods (e.g., [12]) and whether provided upper bounds approach optimal minimax rates.  
- Dataset citations: provide references for datasets used (CPUsmall, Kin8nm, USPS).  
- Toy experiments: include toy 1-D examples with known p and q (plots of true vs estimated p/q) to demonstrate estimation quality.  
- Overall: innovative approach but unclear comparative performance and missing experimental/theoretical details warrant further work.
