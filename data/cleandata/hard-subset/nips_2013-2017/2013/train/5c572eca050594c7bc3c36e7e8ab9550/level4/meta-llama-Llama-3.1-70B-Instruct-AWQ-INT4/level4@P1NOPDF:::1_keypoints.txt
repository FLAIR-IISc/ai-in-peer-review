- Proposes a novel reformulation of density ratio estimation as a Fredholm integral equation.  
- Paper is well-written and addresses an important problem.  
- Omits proofs/references for statements like ∫ k_t(x,y)f(x)dx = f(y) + O(t).  
- Needs deeper discussion of bandwidth roles and the q=1 density-estimation special case versus KDE.  
- Should include a brief explanation of Tikhonov regularization for non-expert readers.  
- All proofs are in the supplementary material and were not verified by the reviewer.  
- Should explain why a naive plug-in density-estimation approach is inferior to the proposed method.  
- Lacks clear empirical or theoretical comparison to existing methods (e.g., whether rates match minimax bounds from [12]).  
- Must provide references for datasets used (CPUsmall, Kin8nm, USPS).  
- Would benefit from toy experiments with known p and q (e.g., 1D plots of true vs estimated p/q).  
- Overall: a nice contribution, but empirical performance relative to other algorithms and illustrative toy demonstrations are unclear/missing.
