The approach is based on using a kernel and a Fredholm integral equation, yielding an inverse problem. From the viewpoint of machine learning, the assumption of densities is a challenging one, as it is well-known that in any epsilon neighborhood of a probability measure P that has a ν-density, there are probability measures that have no ν-densities. Therefore, the estimation of a ratio of two densities is problematic from the viewpoint of machine learning.
