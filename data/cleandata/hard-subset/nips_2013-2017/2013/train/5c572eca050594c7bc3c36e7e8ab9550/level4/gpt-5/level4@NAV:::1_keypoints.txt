- Writing and significance: Manuscript is clearly written and addresses an important problem.  
- Missing technical justification: Needs proof or citation for claims like ∫ k_t(x,y) f(x) dx = f(y) + O(t).

- Regularization background: Add a brief overview of Tikhonov regularization for readers unfamiliar with it.  
- Proofs location: All proofs are deferred to the supplement and have not been verified by the reviewer.  
- Plug-in baseline: Discuss why estimating p and q separately then taking a ratio is inferior to the proposed method.  
- Comparison to prior work: Unclear how method compares to existing approaches (e.g., [12]) and whether provided rates are minimax-optimal.  
- Dataset citations: Include references for datasets used (CPUsmall, Kin8nm, USPS).  
- Empirical illustration: Add a simple toy example (e.g., 1D p and q) showing true p/q vs. estimate to assess quality.  
- Overall assessment: Interesting and well-written paper, but uncertain about empirical/ theoretical performance relative to competitors and lacking illustrative toy demos.
