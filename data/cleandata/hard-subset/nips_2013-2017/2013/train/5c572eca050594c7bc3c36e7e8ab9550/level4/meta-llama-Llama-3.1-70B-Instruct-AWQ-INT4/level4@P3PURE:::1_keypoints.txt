- Main contribution: reformulates density ratio estimation p/q as a Fredholm integral equation.  
- Strength: paper is well-written and tackles an important problem.  
- Missing justification: claim ∫ k_t(x,y)f(x)dx = f(y) + O(t) needs a proof or reference.  
- Bandwidth discussion needed: method applies to density estimation (q=1) and appears not to require bandwidth→0—clarify role of bandwidth vs. KDE.  
- Explain Tikhonov regularization briefly for readers unfamiliar with it.  
- Proofs are only in the supplement and were not verified by the reviewer.  
- Need rationale for why naive plug-in (estimating p and q separately) is inferior to the proposed method.  
- Comparative analysis lacking: unclear how the paper’s convergence bounds compare to minimax rates and prior work (e.g., [12]).  
- Provide references for datasets used (CPUsmall, Kin8nm, USPS).  
- Add toy experiments with known p and q (e.g., 1D plots of true vs. estimated p/q) to demonstrate empirical quality.  
- Overall: promising approach but unclear empirical/optimal performance relative to existing algorithms; more theory details and demos required.
