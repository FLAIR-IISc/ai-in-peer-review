- Proposes a novel method reformulating density ratio estimation p/q as a Fredholm integral equation.  
- Paper is well-written and tackles an important, interesting problem.  
- Missing derivation/proof or reference for the key approximation ∫ k_t(x,y) f(x) dx = f(y) + O(t).  
- Method can handle density estimation as q=1 and does not require bandwidth→0, unlike standard KDE; needs deeper discussion of bandwidth roles.  
- Include a brief explanation of Tikhonov regularization for readers unfamiliar with it.  
- Proofs are included in the supplement, but the reviewer did not verify their correctness.  
- Should explain why a naive plug-in approach (estimate p and q separately) is inferior to the proposed method.  
- Comparative performance is unclear—need to clarify relation to prior work that derives minimax bounds (e.g., [12]) and whether provided bounds are minimax-optimal.  
- Provide references for datasets used (CPUsmall, Kin8nm, USPS).  
- Add demonstrations on toy problems with known p and q, e.g., 1D plots comparing true p/q and estimated p/q.  
- Overall valuable contribution but lacks clarity on empirical performance comparisons and illustrative examples.
