- Novel reformulation of density ratio estimation as a Fredholm integral equation offering a fresh theoretical foundation.  
- Manuscript is well-written, clearly structured, and accessible.  
- Significant theoretical contribution, notably the connection to density estimation when q=1 without requiring bandwidth → 0.  
- Missing justification for the key technical claim ∫ k_t(x,y) f(x) dx = f(y) + O(t); needs proof or precise reference.  
- Insufficient explanation of how the method’s bandwidth role differs from KDE and why it avoids KDE’s bandwidth-convergence requirement.  
- Paper should briefly explain Tikhonov regularization and its relevance to solving the integral equation for the NIPS audience.  
- Lacks comparative analysis to state-of-the-art: unclear how the provided convergence rate compares to known minimax lower bounds and to naive plug-in estimators.  
- Empirical validation is weak—no toy demonstrations (e.g., 1D Gaussian mixtures) or plots comparing true vs. estimated p/q.  
- Dataset citations (CPUsmall, Kin8nm, USPS) are missing and should be provided for reproducibility.  
- All proofs are only in the supplement and have not been verified by the reviewer; main-text proof sketch or clearer pointers would help.  
- Urgent revisions requested: clarify bandwidth behavior in the q=1 case, provide comparisons to existing methods (theory/empirics), and include a simple illustrative experiment.
