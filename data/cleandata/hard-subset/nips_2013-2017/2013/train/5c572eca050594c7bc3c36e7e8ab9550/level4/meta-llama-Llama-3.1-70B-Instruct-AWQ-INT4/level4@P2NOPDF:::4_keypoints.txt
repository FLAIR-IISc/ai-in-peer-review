- Proposes two estimators for the Radon–Nikodym derivative f = p/q using equalities (i) Ep[k(x,·) f] = E_q k(x,·) and (ii) Ep[k(x,·) f] ≈ q(x).

- Replaces integral operators with empirical versions and uses {k(x,·)} as test functions so standard kernel methods can be applied.

- Provides convergence guarantees under smoothness and boundedness assumptions; rates depend strongly on the ambient dimension.

- Overall assessment: well-executed, valuable contribution with supporting experiments.
