- Presentation: paper is well-written and addresses a significant, compelling problem.  
- Missing proofs: key asymptotic claims (e.g., ∫k_t(x,y)f(x)dx = f(y) + O(t)) need formal proof or reference.

- Baseline comparison: include comparison to the naive plug-in approach (estimate p and q separately) to show advantages.  
- Minimax/related work: clarify how the method’s rates compare to existing algorithms (e.g., [12]) and whether corollaries are near minimax optimal.  
- Dataset references: provide citations/sources for CPU_small, Kin8nm, USPS datasets.
