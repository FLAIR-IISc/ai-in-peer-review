- Proposes estimating the ratio of two probability density functions using a kernel and a Fredholm integral formulation, resulting in an inverse problem.  
- Argues the assumption that both measures admit densities is problematic in ML, since arbitrarily close measures may lack densities.  
- The dominating measure nu is not specified (e.g., Lebesgue, counting), which undermines the paper’s rigor.  
- Omitting nu raises concerns about treatment of sets of nu-measure zero.  
- Manuscript fails to clearly state key assumptions such as the domain of x (Rd vs. a general topological space).  
- Authors do not state whether the density p is strictly positive for all x.
