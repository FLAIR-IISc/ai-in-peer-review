- Novel contribution: reformulates density ratio estimation p/q as a Fredholm integral equation, a significant theoretical idea.  
- Strength: paper is well-written and addresses a timely problem clearly.  
- Missing justification: the approximation ∫ k_t(x,y) f(x) dx = f(y) + O(t) needs a proof or reference.  
- Bandwidth role unclear: clarify how bandwidths here differ from KDE and why the q=1 case yields consistency without vanishing bandwidth.  
- exposition: include a brief explanation of Tikhonov regularization for readers unfamiliar with it.  
- Proofs location: all proofs are in the supplementary material and have not been independently verified by the reviewer.  
- Baseline comparison: explicitly contrast the method with a naive plug-in estimator (estimating p and q separately) and explain why that is inferior.  
- Rates comparison: clarify how the convergence rates in Corollaries 2 and 4 compare to the minimax optimal rate from [12].  
- Dataset citations: provide references for CPUsmall, Kin8nm, and USPS datasets.  
- Empirical illustration: add a toy 1D example with known p and q (plots of true vs. estimated p/q) to demonstrate estimation quality.
