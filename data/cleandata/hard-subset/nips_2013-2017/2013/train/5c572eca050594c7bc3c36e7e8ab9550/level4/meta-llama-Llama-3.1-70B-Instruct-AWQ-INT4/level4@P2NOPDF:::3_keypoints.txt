- Proposal: estimator for ratio of two PDFs using a kernel and a Fredholm integral leading to an inverse problem.  
- Conceptual concern: assuming existence of densities is strong — in any ε-neighborhood of a measure with a ν-density there exist measures without ν-densities, complicating estimation.  
- Missing detail: the dominating measure ν is not specified (e.g., Lebesgue, counting, or other).  
- Rigor issue: unclear treatment of sets of ν-measure zero and whether results hold there.  
- Ambiguous domain: authors do not state whether x lies in R^d or a more general topological space.  
- Positivity assumption: it is not specified whether the density p is positive for all x.  
- Recommendation: explicitly describe the dominating measure ν and clearly state all assumptions about the PDFs and underlying space.
