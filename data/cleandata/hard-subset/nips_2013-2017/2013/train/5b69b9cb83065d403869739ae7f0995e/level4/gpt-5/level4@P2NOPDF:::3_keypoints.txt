- Mathematical development is too sketchy and omits many nontrivial steps, hurting verifiability.
- Appendix (line 139): no justification that the second term of (11) is O(1/sqrt(m)) and beta’s role/scaling is unclear.
- Several derivations (e.g., leading to (19)) seem to assume m and n grow at the same rate; this assumption must be stated and the rectangular regimes (m/n→0 or ∞) discussed.
- Line 167: using m * τ_w^2 as E[A_{il}^2] is unjustified—require an explicit generative model, justification of the approximation, and clarification of mean contributions.
- Convergence to MAP is not rigorously shown: the limit β→∞ interchange with the KL minimization is not justified and conditions for MAP recovery should be provided.
- Proposition 1’s proof is too brief; derivation of equation (17) (and missing intermediate steps) must be included or sketched self‑containedly.
- Readability suffers where key steps are skipped and notation (especially β) is not consistently tracked.
- The algorithm appears to estimate first and second moments of marginals and may be an instance of Expectation Propagation (EP); prior EP work (e.g., Stern et al. 2009) should be cited and differences highlighted.
- There is a plausible connection to Minka’s “clutter” EP formulation that should be discussed if relevant.
- Empirical evaluation is weak: comparing only to k-means++ is insufficient—stronger baselines (GMM+EM, spectral clustering, agglomerative methods, DP‑means, modern initializations) are needed.
- Overall, sketchy proofs and possible equivalence to prior EP methods make technical correctness and novelty difficult to assess.
