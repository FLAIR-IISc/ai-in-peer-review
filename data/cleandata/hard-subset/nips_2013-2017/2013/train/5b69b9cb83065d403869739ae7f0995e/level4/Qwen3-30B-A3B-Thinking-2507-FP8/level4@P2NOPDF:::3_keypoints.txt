- Mathematical derivations lack detail; many non-trivial steps are omitted even in the appendix.  
- Appendix L139: Claim that the second term in (11) is O(1/√m) is unjustified, and the omission of β in Section 2 is not explained.  
- Asymptotic analysis (e.g., derivation of (19)) appears to tacitly assume m and n grow at the same rate; the terse treatment raises rigor concerns.  
- Appendix L167: The approximation m τ_w^2 ≈ E[A_il^2] is unjustified since A is observed data while m τ_w^2 is a likelihood variance.  
- The claimed convergence of the message-passing algorithm to the MAP as β → ∞ is unproven; interchanging limit and minimization of KL is not rigorously established.  
- Proposition 1’s proof is insufficiently detailed; derivation of equation (17) is unclear.  
- Clarity: Paper is well-structured and communicates core contributions effectively.  
- Originality: Method appears to be a direct application of Expectation-Propagation to matrix factorization (cf. Stern et al. 2009) and relates to Minka’s EP/clutter work, so novelty is limited.  
- Significance: Evaluation is weak (only compared to k-means++), and sketchy proofs plus lack of theoretical/algorithmic novelty limit the paper’s practical and scientific impact.
