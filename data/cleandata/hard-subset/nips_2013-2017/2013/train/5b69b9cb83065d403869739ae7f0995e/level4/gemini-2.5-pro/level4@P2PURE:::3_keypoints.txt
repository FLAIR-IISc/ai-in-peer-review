- Mathematical derivations are sketchy and omit non-trivial steps, making correctness hard to verify.
- Line 139 (Appendix): unclear why the second term of Eq. (11) is O(1/√m); β is omitted from the discussion in Section 2.
- Eq. (19) and related asymptotic analysis appear to assume m and n grow at the same rate; overall asymptotic arguments are terse and potentially invalid.
- Line 167: approximating E[A_{il}^2] by mτ_w^2 is unjustified because A is observed data while mτ_w^2 is a likelihood variance.
- No justification that the message-passing algorithm for finite β converges to the MAP solution as β → ∞; interchange of limit and minimization is not shown.
- Proof of Proposition 1 is insufficiently detailed; derivation of Eq. (17) is missing.
- Clarity: paper is well-structured and main points are presented clearly.
- Originality is questionable: the algorithm appears to be a direct application of Expectation Propagation to matrix factorization (see Stern et al. 2009); appendix updates look like standard EP.
- Possible (non-critical) connection to Minka’s EP "clutter problem" if the likelihood is replaced by a uniform mixture of Gaussians.
- Significance: experiments compare only to k-means++; given the extensive clustering literature and the technical/novelty concerns, practical impact is unclear.
