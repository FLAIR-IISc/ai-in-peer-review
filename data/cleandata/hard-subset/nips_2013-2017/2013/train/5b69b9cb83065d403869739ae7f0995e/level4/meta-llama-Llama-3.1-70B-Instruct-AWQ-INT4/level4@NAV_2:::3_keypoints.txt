- Proposes an AMP algorithm for Bayesian low-rank matrix reconstruction and applies it to clustering.
- Paper is well-structured and explains the problem, algorithm, and clustering application clearly.
- Mathematical derivations and analysis are sketchy, with omitted non-trivial steps.
- Asymptotic analysis (Section 2) lacks rigor and does not fully discuss implications of m and N growing at the same rate.
- Certain approximations (e.g., E[A_il^2] = m τ_w^2) require further justification.
- Connection to Expectation-Propagation (EP) and prior EP-based matrix factorization work is unclear; novelty is not well established.
- Experimental evaluation is limited to k-means++ and needs comparisons with other state-of-the-art clustering algorithms.
- Minor issues: omission of parameter β in Section 2 and an overly brief proof of Proposition 1.
- Pro-acceptance: novel application of AMP to low-rank reconstruction/clustering and promising empirical results versus k-means++.
- Con-acceptance: sketchy math, unclear relation to existing work, limited experiments, and minor technical omissions.
