- Clarity: paper is well organized and the main message is straightforward to follow.  
- Originality concern: the algorithm appears to estimate first/second posterior moments and may essentially be Expectation Propagation (EP) applied to matrix factorization; cite Stern et al. (www09).  
- Appendix Section 1 looks like standard EP update procedures; the authors should clarify differences from EP.  
- Note a potentially interesting connection to the EP “clutter” problem (Minka) if the likelihood is a uniform mixture of Gaussians—mention but not a novelty challenge.  
- Significance: empirical evaluation only against k-means++ is insufficient given extensive clustering literature.
