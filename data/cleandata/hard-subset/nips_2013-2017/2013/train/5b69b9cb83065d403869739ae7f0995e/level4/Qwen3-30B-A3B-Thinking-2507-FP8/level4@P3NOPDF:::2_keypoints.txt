- Paper proposes a BP/approximate message-passing algorithm for low-rank matrix reconstruction, framing clustering as matrix reconstruction with asymptotic approximations and an unconventional encoding.  
- Major concern: evaluation framework is unjustified—reducing clustering to matrix reconstruction is nonstandard and the method is not benchmarked on standard matrix-reconstruction datasets (e.g., Netflix), so competitiveness is unclear.  
- Major concern: novelty is misrepresented—matrix factorization for clustering is not new and the relationship to Expectation Propagation/EP is not properly acknowledged or cited.  
- Major concern: clarity and mathematical rigor are lacking—key derivations are in the supplement, asymptotic approximations lack empirical validation, notation is redundant/confusing, and scaling choices are unexplained.  
- Minor issue: VBMF-MA implementation lacks a clear primary citation and earlier VBMF references are ambiguous.  
- Minor issue: language and phrasing contain grammatical problems and the title should explicitly mention the method (e.g., via approximate message passing).  
- Minor issue: supplementary-material steps (e.g., Eq.14→15 big-O handling) are unclear and the novelty of message approximations isn’t contrasted with prior work.  
- Minor issue: notational inconsistencies (e.g., capital N vs. lowercase m, omitted β subscripts despite β-dependent terms) confuse readers.  
- Strength: the algorithm is well-motivated for the clustering-as-reconstruction task and shows clear advantages over K-means in experiments.  
- Strength: supplementary diagram (Fig.1) is helpful and the mathematics is followable with effort despite presentation issues.  
- Recommendation: paper has critical flaws in evaluation, novelty claims, and exposition that make it unsuitable for publication in current form.  
- Required revision: justify or change the evaluation—benchmark on standard datasets or explain why they’re inappropriate.  
- Required revision: correct novelty claims and explicitly cite prior work linking matrix factorization and clustering and relate to EP.  
- Required revision: simplify and clarify mathematics—move key derivations to the main text, remove redundant notation, and explain scaling/symmetry choices.  
- Required revision: fix language and citations (specify VBMF-MA reference and address grammatical issues).  
- Decision: Reject.
