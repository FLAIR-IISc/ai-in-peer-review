- Mathematical derivations are insufficiently detailed, omitting non-trivial steps even in the appendix.  
- (1) Appendix line 139: unexplained why the second term in Eq. (11) scales as O(1/√m); β appears omitted in Section 2.  
- (2) Analysis around Eq. (19) and related asymptotics seem to assume m and n grow at the same rate; the asymptotic arguments are terse and lack rigor.  
- (3) Appendix line 167: unjustified use of mτ_w^2 as the expectation of A_il^2 despite A being observed and mτ_w^2 representing likelihood variance.  
- (4) Unclear how the finite-β message-passing algorithm converges to the MAP solution; interchange of limit and KL minimization is not justified.  
- (5) Proposition 1’s proof is too brief, with the derivation of Eq. (17) left unclear.  
- Clarity: the paper is well-structured and clearly communicates its core contributions.  
- Originality: the algorithm appears to be an application of Expectation-Propagation to matrix factorization (cf. Stern et al. 2009) and relates to Minka’s clutter model, raising novelty concerns.  
- Significance: evaluation against only k-means++ is insufficient given clustering literature; sketchy proofs and apparent reliance on prior EP work weaken practical impact.
