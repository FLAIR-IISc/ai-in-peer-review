- Presents an AMP-based algorithm for low-rank matrix reconstruction in a Bayesian framework allowing structural priors (e.g., sparsity, non-negativity).  
- Main contributions: extends AMP factorization to MAP estimation and applies it to K-means by enforcing one-hot cluster assignment factors.  
- Empirical results on synthetic and real data claim the AMP-based clustering outperforms Lloyd’s K-means and K-means++.  
- Paper is well-structured, clearly written, and draws an insightful connection between low-rank factorization and clustering.  
- Major weakness: mathematical derivations and proofs are overly brief and omit crucial steps, undermining verifiability.  
- AMP derivation relies on an asymptotic analysis (m, N → ∞) that is not formally presented or justified.  
- Key proofs (e.g., Proposition 1) are sketched rather than detailed, reducing confidence in theoretical claims.  
- Originality concern: derived update equations closely resemble Expectation Propagation (EP) and the relationship to EP is not addressed.  
- Missing citation and discussion of related work, notably Stern et al. (2009), which applied a related variational message-passing approach.  
- Significance is promising but limited by concerns about rigor and novelty despite addressing an important problem.  
- Experimental evaluation is limited to K-means variants and is not comprehensive enough to demonstrate broad practical utility.  
- Stronger empirical comparisons (e.g., GMMs, spectral clustering, other modern clustering methods) are recommended.  
- Arguments for acceptance: novel and clever K-means reformulation as constrained low-rank factorization.  
- Arguments for acceptance: compelling empirical results showing lower K-means loss and higher clustering accuracy than standard K-means variants.  
- Arguments for acceptance: clear exposition that communicates core ideas well.  
- Arguments for rejection: lack of mathematical rigor and insufficiently justified assumptions.  
- Arguments for rejection: unclear novelty relative to EP and missing relevant citations (e.g., Stern et al. 2009).  
- Arguments for rejection: limited experimental comparison to only K-means baselines.  
- Requested clarifications: explicitly compare and clarify the relationship to EP and cite related prior work.  
- Requested clarifications: provide detailed AMP derivations, state asymptotic assumptions, justify the MAP limit β → ∞, detail Proposition 1 proof, justify the τ estimator, and broaden experimental baselines.
