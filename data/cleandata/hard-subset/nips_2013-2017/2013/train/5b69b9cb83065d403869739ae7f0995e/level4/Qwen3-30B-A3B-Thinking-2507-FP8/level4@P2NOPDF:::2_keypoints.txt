- Novelty: The paper introduces a BP-based algorithm for low-rank matrix reconstruction and frames clustering as a reconstruction task.  
- Positive evaluation: Mathematics is generally followable, experiments show clear advantages over K‑means in the clustering setting, and the introduction/prior-work summary is well structured.  
- Helpful supplement: Supplementary diagram (Fig. 1) is particularly useful.  
- Lack of standard benchmarks: Evaluation uses clustering (non-standard) and omits standard benchmarks like the Netflix dataset, preventing objective comparison to prior VBMF work.  
- Unvalidated asymptotic approximations: Key large-N, m approximations are relegated to the supplement, lack empirical validation, and are insufficiently explained.  
- Misstated novelty: The paper implies the clustering–matrix factorization connection is original, but this is established in prior literature and must be acknowledged.  
- Missing citation/context: Relation to Expectation Propagation is not addressed and BP’s EP connection should be clarified; VBMF-MA implementation needs a primary reference.  
- Overly complex notation: Excessive hats, tildes, subscripts, and superscripts hinder readability and should be simplified.  
- Unclear mathematical exposition: Several equations (e.g., Eqs. 5, 8, 9, 10) lack intuitive explanations and algebraic simplifications (e.g., factoring constants) are needed.  
- Unexplained modeling choices: The role of m in the noise variance breaks row–column symmetry and is not justified.  
- Language and minor errors: Multiple grammatical mistakes and awkward phrases throughout the main text and supplement need correction.  
- Title precision: The title should specify "using approximate message passing" (or similar) to reflect the method.  
- Recommendation: Reject in current form; the manuscript requires substantial revision before fair evaluation.  
- Required revisions: Use standard datasets (e.g., Netflix), empirically validate approximations and their limits, correct citation gaps and acknowledge prior clustering–reconstruction work, and simplify notation with intuitive explanations for key equations.
