- Paper proposes an AMP algorithm for Bayesian matrix factorization and adapts it to clustering, compared experimentally to k-means++.
- Mathematical development is terse and omits several nontrivial steps, including in the appendix.
- Appendix line 139: unclear why the second term of equation (11) is O(1/√m); β also appears missing in Section 2.
- Several derivations (e.g., equation (19)) seem to assume m and n scale at the same rate; asymptotic justification is brief and lacks rigor.
- Line 167: unjustified replacement of E[A_il^2] by m τ_w^2 despite A being observed data and τ_w^2 a model variance parameter.
- Unclear how the finite-β message passing algorithm converges to the MAP solution; interchanging lim_{β→∞} and argmin of KL is not justified.
- Proof of Proposition 1 is too condensed; derivation of equation (17) is not shown.
- Paper is well organized and main contributions are easy to follow (clarity positive).
- Method appears closely related to expectation propagation (EP) for matrix factorization; Appendix Section 1 resembles standard EP updates and connections to Stern et al. should be clarified.
- Possible link between proposed clustering method and Minka’s clutter model (EP) when using a uniform Gaussian mixture likelihood; novelty question remains.
- Empirical evaluation is limited to clustering and comparison only to k-means++, which is insufficient given clustering literature breadth.
- Sketchy proofs and potential equivalence to existing EP methods undermine confidence in technical correctness and contribution significance.
