- Mathematical derivations are sketchy and omit nontrivial steps, including in the appendix.
- Appendix line 139: unclear why the second term of Eq.(11) is O(1/√m); needs justification.
- β (inverse temperature/scale) appears omitted in Section 2 and throughout parts of the analysis.
- Asymptotic arguments assume m and n grow at the same rate; this is tacit and needs justification for rigor.
- Line 167: treating m τ_w^2 as E[A_{il}^2] is unjustified since A is observed and m τ_w^2 is only the likelihood variance.
- For finite β, it is unclear how the message-passing algorithm converges to the MAP solution; swapping limits/minimization is not justified.
- Proof of Proposition 1 is too terse—derivation of Eq.(17) is not shown.
- Paper may essentially be applying Expectation Propagation (EP) to matrix factorization; earlier EP work on matrix factorization (Stern et al.) should be acknowledged and compared.
- Section 1 of the appendix appears to reproduce standard EP update procedures; authors should clarify novelty.
- Potential connection to the clutter problem in EP (mixture/uniform-clutter likelihoods) is suggested but not explored.
- Empirical evaluation is limited (only compared to k-means++); broader comparisons are needed to demonstrate practical impact.
