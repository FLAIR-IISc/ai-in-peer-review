- Mathematical derivations are sketchy and omit several non-trivial steps, including in the appendix.
- Appendix line 139: unclear justification that the second term of eq. (11) is O(1/√m); β is omitted from discussion and affects this analysis.
- Asymptotic analysis (including derivation of eq. (19)) seems to assume m and n grow at the same rate; overall terse and potentially not rigorous.
- Appendix line 167: claim that m τ_w^2 represents E[A_il^2] is questionable—A is observed, so m τ_w^2 is the likelihood variance and needs verification.
- Unclear how message passing for finite β converges to MAP; interchange of limit and minimization of KL (lim_{β→∞} min_q KL = min_q lim_{β→∞} KL) is not justified.
- Proof of Proposition 1 is insufficiently detailed; derivation of eq. (17) is not clearly explained.
- Clarity: paper is well-structured and core ideas are readily understandable.
- Originality: method appears to be essentially Expectation Propagation (EP) for matrix factorization; prior work by Stern et al. covers similar approaches.
- Appendix Section 1 seems to present standard EP update procedures; authors should clarify if this is incorrect.
- Potential connection: clustering approach relates to Minka’s clutter problem (with uniform Gaussian mixture likelihood), an interesting link but not a direct originality critique.
- Significance: comparison only to k-means++ is insufficient given clustering literature; sketchy proofs and possible reapplication of EP weaken claimed contributions.
