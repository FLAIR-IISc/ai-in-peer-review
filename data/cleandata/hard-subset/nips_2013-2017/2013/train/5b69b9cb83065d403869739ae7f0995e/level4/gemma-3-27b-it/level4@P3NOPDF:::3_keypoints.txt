- Mathematical derivations are insufficiently detailed and omit many non-trivial steps, especially in the appendix.  
- Claim that the second term of Eq.(11) is O(1/√m) (App., line 139) lacks justification and the role of β is unclear.  
- Asymptotic analysis seems to assume m and n grow at similar rates without stating this, limiting generality.  
- Approximation that m τ_w^2 equals E[A_il^2] (line 167) is unjustified because observed data contains more than variance.  
- Convergence behavior for finite β and relation to MAP is not addressed; interchange of lim and min for KL is not justified.  
- Proof of Proposition 1 is too concise and derivation of Eq.(17) is unclear.  
- Paper is well-structured and the core idea linking AMP-based matrix factorization to clustering is clear.  
- Novelty is questionable: approach may be a re-implementation of Expectation Propagation and resembles Stern et al.; distinction from EP is not made clear.  
- Suggested investigation: potential connection to Minka’s “clutter” problem (EP foundational work) could clarify novelty.  
- Experimental comparison is limited to k-means++; broader benchmarking against state-of-the-art clustering methods is needed.  
- Overall recommendation: interesting idea but major concerns on rigor, clarity, and novelty; recommend rejection unless addressed.
