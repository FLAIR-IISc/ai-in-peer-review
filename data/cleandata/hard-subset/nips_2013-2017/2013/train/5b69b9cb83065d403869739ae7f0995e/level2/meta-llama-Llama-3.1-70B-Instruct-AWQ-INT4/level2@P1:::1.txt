This paper proposes an efficient approximate message passing (AMP) algorithm for low-rank matrix reconstruction and clustering. The authors formulate the problem in a Bayesian framework, which allows them to exploit structural properties of matrices in addition to low-rankedness, such as sparsity. They extend the AMP algorithm to deal with other estimations such as the maximum a posteriori (MAP) estimation and apply it to a clustering problem by reformulating it as a low-rank matrix reconstruction problem with an additional structural property.

The paper is well-written, and the authors provide a clear and concise explanation of the proposed algorithm and its properties. The numerical experiments demonstrate the efficiency of the proposed algorithm on both artificial and real data sets.
