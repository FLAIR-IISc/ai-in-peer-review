- Proposes a novel AMP algorithm for Bayesian low-rank matrix reconstruction and clustering that can incorporate structural priors like sparsity.
- Extends AMP to the fixed-rank regime and introduces a new AMP-based clustering algorithm.
- Paper is well-written with clear problem setup, notation, and reproducible details for expert readers.
- Technically sound with thorough theoretical analysis and well-supported claims.
- Numerical experiments show the method outperforms K-means and variational Bayes matrix factorization in clustering tasks.
- Authors carefully evaluate the algorithm and discuss its strengths and limitations.
- Results are significant, advancing the state of the art and potentially useful in image and signal processing applications.
- Arguments for acceptance: novel and effective algorithm, comprehensive analysis, and strong experimental validation.
- Arguments against acceptance: highly technical (may be hard for some readers), limited discussion of relations to other methods, and few real-world dataset experiments.
- Overall recommendation: accept for publication at NIPS.
