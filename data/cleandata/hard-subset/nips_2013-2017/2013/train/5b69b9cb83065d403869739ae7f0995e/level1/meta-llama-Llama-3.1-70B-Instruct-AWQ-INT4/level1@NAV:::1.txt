This paper proposes a novel approximate message passing (AMP) algorithm for low-rank matrix reconstruction and clustering. The authors formulate the problem in the Bayesian framework, which allows them to exploit structural properties of matrices in addition to low-rankedness. The proposed algorithm is derived from the belief propagation algorithm and is shown to be efficient and effective.

The paper is well-written, and the authors provide a clear explanation of the problem, the proposed algorithm, and its properties. The numerical experiments demonstrate the effectiveness of the proposed algorithm in clustering tasks, both on artificial and real data sets.
