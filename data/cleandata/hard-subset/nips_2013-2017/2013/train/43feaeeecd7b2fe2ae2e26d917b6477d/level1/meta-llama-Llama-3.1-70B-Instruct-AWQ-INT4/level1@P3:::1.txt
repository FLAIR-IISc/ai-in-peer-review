The second approach, Quadratic Poisson Graphical Model (QPGM), modifies the Poisson distribution by introducing a quadratic base measure. The third approach, Sub-Linear Poisson Graphical Model (SPGM), modifies the Poisson distribution by introducing sub-linear sufficient statistics.
