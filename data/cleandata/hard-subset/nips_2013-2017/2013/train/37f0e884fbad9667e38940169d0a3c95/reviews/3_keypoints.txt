- Proposes a novel hybrid stochastic/batch SGD with convergence using O(log(1/ε)) full gradients and O(k^2 log(1/ε)) stochastic gradients.  
- Convergence proof appears correct and novel, aside from minor mistakes listed.  
- Major concern: relevance to ML—strong convexity from regularization gives μ ≈ C·N, so the method is faster than batch GD only if N = O(C^3/L^3), an uninteresting regime.  
- The proposed convergence is high-probability only, while batch gradient descent guarantees deterministic convergence.  
- Authors need a careful discussion comparing to batch GD to justify practical advantage and ML relevance.
