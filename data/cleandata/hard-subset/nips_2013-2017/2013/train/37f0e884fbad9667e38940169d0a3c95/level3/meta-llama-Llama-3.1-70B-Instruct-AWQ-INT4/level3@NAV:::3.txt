The paper "Linear Convergence with Condition Number Independent Access of Full Gradients" proposes a novel hybrid stochastic/batch Stochastic Gradient Descent (SGD) algorithm, called Epoch Mixed Gradient Descent (EMGD). The algorithm is designed to reduce the number of full gradients required for smooth and strongly convex optimization problems by leveraging the availability of stochastic gradients.

The main idea of the paper is to divide the optimization process into a sequence of epochs, where each epoch consists of a fixed number of iterations. At each epoch, the algorithm performs a mixed gradient descent step, which combines the full gradient and stochastic gradients to update the intermediate solution. The authors provide a convergence proof for the EMGD algorithm, showing that it can achieve a linear convergence rate with a high probability.
