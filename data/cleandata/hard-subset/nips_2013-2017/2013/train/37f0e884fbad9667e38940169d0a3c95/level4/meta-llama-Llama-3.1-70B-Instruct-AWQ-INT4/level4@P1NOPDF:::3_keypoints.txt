- Paper proposes a hybrid stochastic/batch SGD algorithm with convergence to ε-accuracy using O(log(1/ε)) full gradients and O(k^2 log(1/ε)) stochastic gradients.  
- Convergence proof appears correct and novel, aside from several minor errors noted.  
- Major concern: relevance to ML — with regularizer-induced strong convexity µ = C·N, the method is faster than batch GD only if N = O(C^3/L^3), an uninteresting regime.  
- Additional concern: proposed convergence guarantee is high-probability, while batch gradient descent offers deterministic guarantees.  
- The paper must better justify and discuss any practical advantage over batch gradient descent for ML settings.
