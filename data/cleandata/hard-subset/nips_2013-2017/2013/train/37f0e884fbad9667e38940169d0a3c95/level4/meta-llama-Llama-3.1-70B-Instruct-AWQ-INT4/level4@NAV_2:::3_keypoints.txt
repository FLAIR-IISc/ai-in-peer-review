- Proposal: Epoch Mixed Gradient Descent (EMGD), a hybrid stochastic/batch SGD that uses O(log(1/ε)) full gradients and O(k^2 log(1/ε)) stochastic gradients to reach ε-accuracy.  
- Convergence proof: appears correct and novel, though contains minor mistakes.  
- Main concern: relevance to ML — with regularizer-induced strong convexity (λ ≈ C·N), EMGD is faster than batch GD only if N = O(C^3/L^3), an uninteresting regime.  
- Probabilistic vs deterministic: EMGD’s convergence holds only with high probability, whereas batch gradient descent guarantees deterministic convergence.  
- Required action: authors must clearly discuss and demonstrate practical benefits of EMGD over batch GD in ML settings.
