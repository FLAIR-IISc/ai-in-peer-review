The paper "Linear Convergence with Condition Number Independent Access of Full Gradients" proposes a novel algorithm, Epoch Mixed Gradient Descent (EMGD), for optimizing smooth and strongly convex functions. The algorithm utilizes both full and stochastic gradients to achieve a linear convergence rate, reducing the number of full gradients required from O( √ κ log 1/ǫ ) to O(log 1/ǫ ), independent of the condition number.

The authors provide a thorough analysis of the algorithm's convergence rate, demonstrating its ability to find an ǫ-optimal solution by computing O(log 1/ǫ ) full gradients and O(κ2 log 1/ǫ ) stochastic gradients. The paper also presents a detailed comparison with existing algorithms, highlighting the advantages of EMGD in terms of its ability to handle constrained optimization problems and its simpler proof of convergence.
