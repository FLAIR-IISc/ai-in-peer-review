- Introduces a hybrid SGD algorithm combining stochastic and full-batch gradients with convergence to ε-accuracy using O(log(1/ε)) full gradients and O(k^2 log(1/ε)) stochastic gradients.  
- Convergence proof appears novel and essentially correct, though with some minor mistakes.  
- Major practical concern: under common ML regularization (μ ≈ C·N), the method outperforms batch GD only when N = O(C^3 / L^3), an impractically small regime.  
- The proposed convergence rate holds only with high probability, while batch GD’s rate is deterministic; this distinction must be emphasized and discussed.  
- Authors should explicitly characterize scenarios where the algorithm has a clear advantage over batch gradient descent.
