- Paper proposes a hybrid stochastic/batch SGD that converges to an ε-accurate solution using O(log(1/ε)) full gradients and O(k^2 log(1/ε)) stochastic gradients.
- Convergence proof is mostly correct and novel, aside from a few minor errors.
- Main concern: relevance to ML—when strong convexity comes from regularization (μ ≈ C·N), the method is faster than batch GD only in an uninteresting regime (N = O(C^3/L^3)).
- Additional concern: proposed convergence guarantees are high-probability, whereas batch gradient descent gives deterministic rates.
- Authors need a thorough discussion comparing practical advantage vs. batch GD to justify ML relevance.
