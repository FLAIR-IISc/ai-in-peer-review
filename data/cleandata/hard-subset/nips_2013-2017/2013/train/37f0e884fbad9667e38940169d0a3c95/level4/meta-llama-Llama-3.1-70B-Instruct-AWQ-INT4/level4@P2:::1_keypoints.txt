- Proposes Epoch Mixed Gradient Descent (EMGD) that mixes full and stochastic gradients to get linear convergence with only O(log 1/ε) full gradients, independent of condition number κ.
- Paper is clear, well-organized, and places contributions in context with prior NIPS and other work.
- Theoretical development is technically sound with a self-contained, easy-to-follow convergence proof.
- EMGD is novel and interesting, demonstrating advantages over existing methods like SAG and SDCA.
- EMGD handles constrained optimization (unlike SAG) and has a simpler convergence proof than SAG.
- Paper lacks an experimental section; authors should add experiments to show practical performance.
- Section 3.4 should be expanded to discuss limitations, e.g., the need to fix epoch length in advance.
- Authors should discuss EMGD’s memory and parallelization trade-offs versus SAG and SDCA.
- Overall a solid theoretical contribution; with added discussion and experiments the paper would be stronger.
