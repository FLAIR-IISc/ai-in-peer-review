**Summary**

This paper proposes a novel optimization algorithm called Epoch Mixed Gradient Descent (EMGD) for smooth and strongly convex optimization problems. The algorithm combines full gradients and stochastic gradients to achieve a linear convergence rate. The authors show that EMGD can reduce the number of full gradients required from O(√κ log 1/ε) to O(log 1/ε), where κ is the condition number. The algorithm is analyzed theoretically, and its performance is compared with other optimization algorithms.
