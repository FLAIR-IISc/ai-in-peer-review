- Studies PGM convergence for trace-norm regularized risk minimization, extending linear convergence beyond strong convexity.  
- Main technical novelty: simultaneous SVD of an optimal solution and its gradient, reducing the matrix problem to a vector case.  
- Derives a local Lipschitzian error bound for the trace-norm objective (non-polyhedral epigraph).  
- Theoretical development appears sound and correct.  
- Originality moderate: overall proof strategy closely follows Luo–Tseng and prior frameworks.  
- Missing citation: the key inequality ||R(X^k)||_F ≤ κ3 ||X^k - X^{k+1}||_F was explicitly proven in Sra (NIPS 2012) and should be cited/discussed.  
- Minor technical gap: paper should justify why the chosen step-size condition (e.g., α < 1) is sufficient to obtain that inequality.  
- Clarity/structure could be improved by stating linear convergence claims up front and deferring the detailed error-bound proof.  
- Significance is mainly theoretical and asymptotic, with limited immediate practical impact given strong early performance of accelerated sublinear methods.
