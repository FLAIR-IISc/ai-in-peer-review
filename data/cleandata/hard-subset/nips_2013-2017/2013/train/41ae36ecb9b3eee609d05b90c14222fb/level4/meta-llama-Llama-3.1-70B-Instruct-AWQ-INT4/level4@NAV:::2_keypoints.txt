- Major omission: lacks a clear comparison/analogy to the vector l1-norm case (refs [20,22]); should explain why l1 techniques do not directly transfer and note that l1 follows as a corollary.  
- This omission affects perceived novelty/value for some readers, though the contribution remains valuable.

- Notation issue: calling P a “sampling” or “mask” operator is misleading when P is fixed rather than random.
