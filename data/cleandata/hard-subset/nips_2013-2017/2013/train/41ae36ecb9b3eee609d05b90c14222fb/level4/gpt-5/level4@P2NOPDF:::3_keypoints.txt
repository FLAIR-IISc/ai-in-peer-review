- Post-rebuttal: the inequality |R_k| ≤ κ |X^k − X^{k+1}| is established in Sra (NIPS 2012), Lemma 4.
- Summary: proves a local error bound and asymptotic linear convergence of proximal gradient for trace-norm regularization under Luo–Tseng–type relaxed conditions.
- Proof strategy largely follows established techniques, with a new observation that enables simultaneous diagonalization of matrices.
- Quality: technically sound overall; the appendix concern about the κ3 inequality is addressed by the rebuttal and related Sra (2012) result.
- Clarity: exposition is clear but technically dense; recommend stating main results up front and deferring technical proofs to improve readability.
- Originality: moderate; main novelty is the simultaneous diagonalization insight, while the rest builds on existing frameworks.
- Significance: mainly theoretical with limited direct algorithmic impact; asymptotic (not explicit nonasymptotic) rates temper practical significance.
- Overall assessment: solid incremental theoretical contribution; clarify the appendix inequality and reorganize presentation to strengthen the paper.
