- Proves linear convergence of proximal gradient for trace-norm regularized problems f(X)=h(A(X)) with h strongly convex on compacts and Lipschitz gradient.  
- Extends earlier error-bound-based results (Tseng, Tseng & Yun, Zhang et al.) from lasso/group lasso to trace norm.  
- Contribution is non-trivial but largely technical.  
- Presentation of proofs is mostly clear.  
- Sequence of inequalities before inequality (13) is confusing and needs clarification.  
- Authors should detail how constants κ1,...,κ4 depend on the choices of underbar{α} and bar{α}.  
- Lacks numerical examples demonstrating the claimed linear convergence (e.g., semilog plot of function value vs iterations).  
- Would benefit from discussion of implications for ADMM and possible extensions to that algorithm.  
- Suggests exploring simulations where loss is not strongly convex but proximal gradient still converges linearly, and discussing failure cases.  
- Overall provides a technical optimization contribution but needs more context, examples, and future-work discussion.
