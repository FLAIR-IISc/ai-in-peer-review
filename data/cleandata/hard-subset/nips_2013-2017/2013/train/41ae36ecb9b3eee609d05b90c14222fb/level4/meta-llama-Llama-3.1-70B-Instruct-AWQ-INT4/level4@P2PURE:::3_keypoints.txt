- Main contribution: shows proximal gradient for trace-norm regularization converges asymptotically at a linear rate under a relaxed strong-convexity condition.  
- Approach: follows Luo and Tseng’s framework to prove a local error bound, then derive linear convergence.  
- Key technical novelty: a new observation enabling simultaneous diagonalization of matrices, reducing the problem to the vector case.  
- Quality: paper appears technically sound overall.  
- Technical concern: appendix (line 085) needs justification why choosing κ3 (with α < 1) yields R(X^k) <= κ3 ||X^k - X^{k+1}||_F.  
- Clarity: readable but technical; recommend outlining main claims first and postponing the detailed local error-bound proof.  
- Originality: moderate — simultaneous diagonalization is interesting, but most steps follow existing approaches.  
- Significance: mainly theoretical with limited impact on algorithm design; significance reduced by the asymptotic nature of the result.  
- Overall assessment: incremental yet moderately interesting theoretical contribution.
