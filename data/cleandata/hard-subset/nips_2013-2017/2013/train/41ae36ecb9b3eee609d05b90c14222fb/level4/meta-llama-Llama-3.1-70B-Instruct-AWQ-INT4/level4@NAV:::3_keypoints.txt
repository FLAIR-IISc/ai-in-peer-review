- Reviewer notes a formal proof of |Rk| <= κ |Xk - X_{k+1}| exists in Suvrit Sra, "Scalable nonconvex inexact proximal splitting" (NIPS'12, Lemma 4).  
- Paper relaxes strong convexity and proves a local error bound to show asymptotic linear convergence of proximal gradient for trace-norm regularization.  
- Approach largely follows Luo & Tseng’s established framework.  
- Novel observation: simultaneous diagonalization of matrices, which is interesting and potentially independently useful.  
- Technical soundness is satisfactory overall.  
- Reviewer requests clarification in appendix (line 085) on choice of κ3 (with α < 1) and how it yields R(X^k) <= κ3 ||X^k - X^{k+1}||_F.  
- Clarity is good; suggests presenting main claims first and postponing technical proofs to emphasize linear convergence result.  
- Originality is moderate: diagonalization idea is new, but most steps are incremental.  
- Significance is primarily theoretical with limited algorithmic impact; the asymptotic nature of the result reduces practical significance.  
- Overall assessment: incremental, moderately interesting theoretical contribution.
