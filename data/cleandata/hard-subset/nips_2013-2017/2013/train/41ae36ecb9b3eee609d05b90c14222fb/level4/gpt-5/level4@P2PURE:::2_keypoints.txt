- Paper is carefully and clearly written and largely follows Tseng’s Lipschitzian error-bound technique.  
- Main part of the proof appears correct and resolves an open question about applying Tseng’s approach to the nuclear norm.  
- Missing clear comparison/analogy to the ℓ1 (vector) case and prior works [20, 22]; authors should explain relation and whether ℓ1 techniques extend directly.

- Minor issue: fix broken reference at line 337.
