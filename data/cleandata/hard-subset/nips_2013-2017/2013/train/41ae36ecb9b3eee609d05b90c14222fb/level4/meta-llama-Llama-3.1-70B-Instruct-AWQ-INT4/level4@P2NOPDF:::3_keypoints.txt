- Proof for |Rk| ≤ κ|Xk - X_{k+1}| is already established in Suvrit Sra, NIPS'12 (Lemma 4).
- Paper analyzes proximal gradient for trace-norm regularized risk minimization and shows asymptotic linear convergence via a local error bound in the Luo & Tseng framework.
- Key technical novelty: an observation that allows simultaneous diagonalization of matrices, reducing the problem to the vector case.
- The derivation of the local error bound largely follows known approaches; originality is moderate.
- The paper appears technically sound overall.
- Concern: appendix (line 085) needs justification for choosing κ3 (with α < 1) to obtain R(X^k) ≤ κ3 ||X^k - X^{k+1}||_F.
- Clarity suggestion: outline main claims up front and postpone the technical proof of the local error bound until after proving the linear rate.
- Significance is mainly theoretical with limited impact on algorithm design; the result is incremental and asymptotic, reducing practical impact.
