- Problem/contribution: The paper relaxes strong convexity for trace-norm regularized risk minimization and proves a local error bound to show asymptotic linear convergence of proximal gradient.

- Technique: Follows Luo–Tseng framework and introduces an observation enabling simultaneous diagonalization to reduce the matrix problem to a vector case.

- Quality/validity: The work appears technically sound overall.

- Specific concern: Appendix line 085 needs an explanation of why kappa3 (specifically α < 1) implies R(X^k) ≤ kappa3 ||X^k - X^{k+1}||_F.

- Clarity: Paper is mostly clear; reviewer suggests stating main claims first and postponing the technical local-error-bound proof before deriving linear rate.

- Originality: Moderate — simultaneous diagonalization observation is novel, but the rest largely follows established approaches.

- Significance: Primarily theoretical with limited algorithmic impact; asymptotic nature of the result reduces practical significance, making the contribution incremental and moderately interesting.
