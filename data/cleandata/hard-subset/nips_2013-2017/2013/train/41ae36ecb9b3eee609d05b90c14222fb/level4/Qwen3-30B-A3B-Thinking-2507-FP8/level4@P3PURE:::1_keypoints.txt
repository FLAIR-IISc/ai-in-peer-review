3. Main contribution: proves linear convergence of proximal gradient for trace-norm regularization when f(X)=h(A(X)) with A linear and h strongly convex on compacts with Lipschitz gradient.  
4. Relation to prior work: extends Tseng [20] and Zhang et al. [22] (error-bound analyses for lasso/group lasso) to the trace norm—nontrivial but largely technical.  
5. Presentation: proofs are for the most part clear.  
6. Strength: generalizes Tseng et al.’s linear-convergence result to trace-norm regularized problems.  
7. Weakness: contribution is primarily technical with limited conceptual novelty.  
8. Weakness: lacks a numerical example (e.g., semilog plot of function value vs iterations) to illustrate/validate linear convergence.  
9. Detailed comment: proof outlines for Theorem 3.1 and Lemma 3.2 closely mirror Tseng [20]; authors should explicitly cite and distinguish their technical contributions.  
10. Detailed comment: the dependence of constants κ1…κ4 on the step-size bounds \underline{α} and \overline{α} is not specified.  
11. Detailed comment: the inequality chain before (13) is confusing—the final step relies on trace-norm convexity and the subgradient condition −\bar{G}∈τ∂||X||_* (cf. Tseng p.289), not the intermediate inequalities.  
12. Overall summary: technically sound incremental extension; addressing the requested simulation and discussion of failure cases would substantially strengthen the paper.
