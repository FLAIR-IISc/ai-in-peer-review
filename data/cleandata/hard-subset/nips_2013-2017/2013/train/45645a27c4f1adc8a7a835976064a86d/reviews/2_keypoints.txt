- Proposes generalizing FAB inference and the factorized information criterion (FIC) to latent feature models using mean-field inference and accelerated feature shrinkage.  
- Produces a more automatic model-selection procedure requiring little hand tuning.  
- Demonstrates significant computational efficiency gains on synthetic and real datasets.  
- Thorough empirical evaluation against a wide range of competing methods; references are sufficient.  
- Paper is clearly written and well structured.  
- Method is a logical, solid extension of prior FAB/FIC work—combines several approximations into a novel, effective approach.  
- Major concern: unclear when/how the combined approximations (FIC, mean-field, shrinkage) may fail; authors should examine failure modes and limits of the approximations.
