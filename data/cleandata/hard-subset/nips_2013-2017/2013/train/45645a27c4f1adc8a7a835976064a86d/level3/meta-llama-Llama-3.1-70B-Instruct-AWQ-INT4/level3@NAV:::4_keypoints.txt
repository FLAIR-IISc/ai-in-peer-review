- Extends FAB inference to latent feature models (LFMs) by deriving a FIC lower bound applicable despite non–block-diagonal Hessian.
- Shows the derived FIC for LFMs has the same representation as the FIC for mixture models — notable theoretical contribution.
- Provides a thorough, well-written derivation of the FIC for LFMs.
- Empirical evaluation on synthetic and real datasets shows superior predictive performance of FAB/LFM.
- FAB/LFM achieves lower computational cost compared to fast Gibbs for IBP, variational Bayes, and ME-IBP.
- Strength: novel derivation of FIC for LFMs.
- Strength: strong empirical results demonstrating effectiveness.
- Strength: clarity and readability of the paper.
- Weakness: contribution is somewhat incremental, building on prior FAB work.
- Weakness: paper lacks a detailed discussion of limitations and future research directions.
