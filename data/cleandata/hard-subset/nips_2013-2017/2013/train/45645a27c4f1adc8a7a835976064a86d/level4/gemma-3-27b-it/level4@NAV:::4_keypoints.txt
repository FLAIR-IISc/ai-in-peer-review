- Extends factorized asymptotic Bayesian (FAB) inference to latent feature models (LFMs).
- Identifies and addresses FAB’s limitation that it requires a block-diagonal Hessian, which LFMs do not satisfy.
- Derives a novel FIC lower bound for LFMs that retains the same mathematical form as the FIC for mixture models.
- Validates the method with experiments on synthetic and real-world datasets.
- Compares against fast Gibbs IBP, variational Bayes, and MEIBP, showing superior prediction accuracy and computational efficiency.
- Paper is clearly written with a detailed, well-explained derivation.
- Contribution is incremental but considered valuable.
- Reviewer not an expert in the subfield but believes the work is of strong interest to the NIPS community.
- Overall judgment: strong paper; recommends acceptance.
