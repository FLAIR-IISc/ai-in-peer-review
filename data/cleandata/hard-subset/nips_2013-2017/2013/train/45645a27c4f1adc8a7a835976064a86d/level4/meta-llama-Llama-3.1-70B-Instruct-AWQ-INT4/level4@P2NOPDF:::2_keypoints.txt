- Proposes an efficient algorithm for model selection in latent feature models using FAB inference and the FIC.
- Generalizes FAB to latent feature models via mean field latent inference and accelerated global feature shrinkage.
- Automates model selection with minimal manual intervention.
- Demonstrates efficacy on synthetic and real-world datasets with significant computational efficiency gains.
- Presents high-quality results with thorough analysis and comprehensive comparisons to competing methods.
- Provides sufficient and relevant references.
- Paper is well-structured and clearly written.
- Originality lies in logically extending prior FAB/FIC work and combining approximations into a novel method.
- Lacks detailed analysis of the limitations and failure modes of the combined approximations (FIC, mean field, shrinkage).
- Overall a valuable contribution with potential significant impact on model selection for latent feature models.
