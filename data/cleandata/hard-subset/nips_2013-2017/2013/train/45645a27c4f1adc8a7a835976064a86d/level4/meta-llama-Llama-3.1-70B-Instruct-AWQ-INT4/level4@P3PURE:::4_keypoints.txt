- Extends factorized asymptotic Bayesian (FAB) inference to latent feature models (LFMs).
- FAB previously applied to mixture models and HMMs required block-diagonal Hessian; LFMs violate this condition.
- Authors derive a lower bound of the factorized information criterion (FIC) for LFMs and show it matches the representation for MMs.
- Empirical evaluation uses synthetic and real-world datasets.
- Compares FAB/LFM to fast Gibbs with Indian Buffet Process, variational Bayes (VB), and MEIBP.
- FAB/LFM yields better predictive performance and lower computational cost than competitors.
- Paper is well-written with thorough derivation steps.
- Contribution may be incremental, but practical efficiency makes it a valuable contribution; reviewer supports acceptance.
