- Extends factorized asymptotic Bayesian (FAB) inference to latent feature models (LFMs).
- FAB previously worked for mixture models and HMMs but required block-diagonal Hessian of the complete-data log-likelihood.
- Authors derive a valid lower bound on the factorized information criterion (FIC) for LFMs, yielding an analogous form to the FIC for MMs.
- Empirical evaluation on synthetic and real datasets compares FAB/LFM to IBP Gibbs, variational Bayes (VB), and MEIBP.
- FAB/LFM outperforms alternatives in predictive performance and is more computationally efficient.
- Paper is well-written with thorough derivation steps.
- Contribution is incremental but non-trivial and interesting.
- Reviewer supports acceptance and believes the work is relevant to the NIPS community.
