- Extends factorized asymptotic Bayesian (FAB) inference to latent feature models (LFMs).
- Addresses FAB’s prior limitation requiring block-diagonal Hessian, which LFMs do not generally satisfy.
- Derives a lower bound of the factorized information criterion (FIC) for LFMs with the same representation as for mixture models.
- Empirical evaluation on synthetic and real datasets comparing FAB/LFM to fast Gibbs with IBP, variational Bayes, and MEIBP.
- FAB/LFM outperforms alternatives in prediction accuracy and computational cost.
- Paper is well-written with thorough derivations.
- Contribution is incremental but valuable and of interest to the NIPS community.
- Reviewer recommends acceptance.
