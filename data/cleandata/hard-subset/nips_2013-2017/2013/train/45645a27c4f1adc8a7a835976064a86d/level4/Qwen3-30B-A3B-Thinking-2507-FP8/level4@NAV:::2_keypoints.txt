The authors extend FAB/FIC to latent feature models using mean-field latent inference and accelerated global-feature shrinkage for automated model selection.

The method integrates FIC, mean-field approximation, and shrinkage to reduce manual hyperparameter tuning.

Shows substantial computational efficiency and strong empirical performance on synthetic and real datasets.

Rigorous analysis with comprehensive comparisons against diverse baselines and adequate citations.

Paper is well-written, logically structured, and clearly presented.

Represents a logical and significant generalization of prior FAB/FIC work with an effective novel integration of approximations.

Recommendation: provide deeper analysis of failure modes and scenarios where the interplay of FIC, mean-field, and shrinkage may degrade performance.
