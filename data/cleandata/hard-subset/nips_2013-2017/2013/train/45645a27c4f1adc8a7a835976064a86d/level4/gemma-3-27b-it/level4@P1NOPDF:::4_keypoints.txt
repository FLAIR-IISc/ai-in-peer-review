- Extends factorized asymptotic Bayesian (FAB) inference from mixture/HMMs to latent feature models (LFMs).
- Addresses FAB’s prior limitation requiring block-diagonal Hessian by deriving a lower bound on the FIC for LFMs.
- Shows the derived lower bound for LFMs has the same form as the FIC for mixture models.
- Provides a thorough and clear derivation of the proposed method.
- Empirically evaluates on synthetic and real-world datasets.
- Compares against fast Gibbs IBP, variational Bayes (VB), and MEIBP.
- Demonstrates superior predictive performance and lower computational cost versus baselines.
- Characterizes the contribution as incremental but valuable and interesting.
- Results are convincing and likely of interest to the NIPS community.
- Reviewer recommends acceptance.
