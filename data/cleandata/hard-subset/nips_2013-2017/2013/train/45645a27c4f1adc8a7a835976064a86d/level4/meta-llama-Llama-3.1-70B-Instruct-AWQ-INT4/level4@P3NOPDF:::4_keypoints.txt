- Extends Factorized Asymptotic Bayesian (FAB) inference to latent feature models (LFMs).  
- FAB previously successful for mixture models and HMMs but required block-diagonal Hessian condition.  
- Authors derive a lower bound of the factorized information criterion (FIC) for LFMs despite the condition not holding.  
- The derived FIC lower bound for LFMs has the same representation as the FIC for mixture models.  
- Empirical evaluation on synthetic and real-world datasets.  
- Compared against fast Gibbs sampling with IBP, variational Bayes (VB), and MEIBP.  
- FAB/LFM outperforms competitors in prediction accuracy and computational efficiency.  
- Paper is well-written with thorough derivation steps.  
- Contribution is somewhat incremental but interesting and relevant to the NIPS community.  
- Reviewer supports acceptance.
