- Extends Factorized Asymptotic Bayesian (FAB) inference to latent feature models (LFMs).
- Overcomes FAB’s prior limitation requiring a block-diagonal Hessian, which LFMs do not satisfy.
- Derives a new lower bound for the factorized information criterion (FIC) applicable to LFMs.
- Shows the derived FIC lower bound has the same representation as FIC for mixture models.
- Empirically evaluates the method on synthetic and real-world datasets.
- Compares FAB/LFM to fast Gibbs (IBP), variational Bayes, and MEIBP baselines.
- Demonstrates superior predictive performance and computational efficiency for FAB/LFM.
- Paper is well-written with clear, thorough derivation steps.
- Contribution is incremental but interesting and likely of interest to the NIPS community.
- Reviewer finds the results convincing and recommends acceptance.
