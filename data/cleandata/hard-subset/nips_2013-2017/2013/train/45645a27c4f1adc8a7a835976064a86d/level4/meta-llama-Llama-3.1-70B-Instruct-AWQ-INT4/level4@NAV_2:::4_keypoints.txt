- Extends FAB inference to latent feature models (LFMs), addressing the Hessian-condition limitation and deriving a lower bound of FIC for LFMs with same representation as for mixture models.  
- Thorough, well-written derivation of the method.  
- Empirical results (synthetic and real data) show FAB/LFM outperforms fast Gibbs IBP, VB, and MEIBP in prediction and computation.  
- Contribution is incremental—an extension of existing FAB/MMs.  
- Paper assumes substantial background in Bayesian inference and LFMs.  
- Technically sound with a clear, well-structured derivation.  
- Novelty lies in applying FAB to LFMs, though built on prior work.  
- Results indicate significant potential for efficient and accurate model selection and prediction, relevant to the NIPS community.  
- Reviewer supports acceptance.
