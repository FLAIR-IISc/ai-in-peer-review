- Extends factorized asymptotic Bayesian (FAB) inference to latent feature models (LFMs).  
- Overcomes FAB’s prior limitation requiring block-diagonal Hessian of the complete likelihood.  
- Derives a lower bound of the factorized information criterion (FIC) for LFMs.  
- Shows the derived FIC lower bound has the same representation as FIC for mixture models (MMs).  
- Provides experimental comparisons on synthetic and real datasets against fast Gibbs/IBP, variational Bayes, and MEIBP.  
- Reports superior performance in prediction accuracy and computational cost for FAB/LFM.  
- Paper is well-written with thorough derivation steps.  
- Contribution viewed as incremental but interesting and useful.  
- Reviewer not an expert in the area but believes the work is of interest to the NIPS community.  
- Recommendation: support acceptance.
