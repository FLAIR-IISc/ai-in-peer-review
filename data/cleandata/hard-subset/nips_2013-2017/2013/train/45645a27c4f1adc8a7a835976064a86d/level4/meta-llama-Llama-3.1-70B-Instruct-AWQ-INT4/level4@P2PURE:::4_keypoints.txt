- Extends Factorized Asymptotic Bayesian (FAB) inference from mixture/HMMs to latent feature models (LFMs).
- Overcomes FAB’s limitation requiring block-diagonal Hessian by deriving a lower bound of the factorized information criterion (FIC) for LFMs.
- Shows the derived lower bound has the same representation as the FIC for mixture models.
- Evaluates the proposed FAB/LFM on synthetic and real-world datasets.
- Compares against fast Gibbs IBP, variational Bayes (VB), and MEIBP.
- Reports superior prediction performance and lower computational cost for FAB/LFM.
- Paper is well-written with thorough derivation steps.
- Contribution is incremental but interesting and efficient.
- Reviewer (not an expert) believes the work is relevant to the NIPS community and supports acceptance.
