- Targets inference in high-treewidth graphical models where exact inference is intractable.  
- Introduces a new notion of "tractability" requiring parameter-space conditions for rapid Gibbs convergence.  
- Paper is well-written, clearly organized, and provides thorough background and literature context.

- Experiments are well-designed to evaluate the proposed methods.
