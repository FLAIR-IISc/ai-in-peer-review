- Introduces a hierarchical Bayesian nonparametric stochastic block model to infer tree-structured clusters from network links.  
- Proposes an agglomerative, tree-constrained learning algorithm that restricts allowable partitions to those consistent with a tree.  
- Claims large runtime improvements (orders of magnitude) and comparable or better accuracy than the Infinite Relational Model (IRM) on two social network datasets.  
- Addresses an important, broadly relevant scalability problem in Bayesian nonparametric models and offers a practical hierarchical extension for network analysis.  
- Key technical insight: using a tree constraint to enable efficient marginal likelihood computation is clever and effective.  
- Empirical results are strong: modest gains in accuracy/AUC/predictive log-likelihood but substantial speedups, making the method practically attractive.  
- The connection between the greedy agglomerative algorithm and principled Bayesian posterior inference (e.g., MCMC or MAP) is unclear and needs clarification.  
- Section 4 (learning algorithm) is hard to follow and should be substantially revised to clarify whether the method approximates posterior inference or is a heuristic.  
- Manuscript has distracting spelling/grammar errors and imprecise prose that reduce clarity.  
- Terminology is vague in places (e.g., "local changes", "iterations") and needs precise definitions of algorithmic steps.  
- Datasets (Monastery networks, NIPS authorship) are under-described; brief descriptions of entities and link types are necessary for broader readership.  
- Some mathematical presentation (e.g., Equation 5) is overly complex and could be simplified with more intuitive explanation.  
- Figure 3 (Monastery results) is hard to interpret for readers unfamiliar with the dataset; better annotations or a more familiar dataset would improve clarity.  
- Overall: novel and promising approach with compelling speedups, but requires stronger theoretical grounding of the learning procedure and substantial writing/presentation revision.
