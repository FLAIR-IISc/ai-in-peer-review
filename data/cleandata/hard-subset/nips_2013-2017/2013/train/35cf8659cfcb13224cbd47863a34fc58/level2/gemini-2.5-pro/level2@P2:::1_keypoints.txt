- Core contribution: Introduces BHCD, a Bayesian nonparametric model that represents hierarchical communities as a tree and uses a fast greedy agglomerative algorithm to learn a mixture of SBMs.  
- Strength — Efficiency and scalability: Greedy agglomerative inference with cached sufficient statistics yields O(n^2 log n) time and is ~100x faster than MCMC IRM with similar or better predictive performance.  
- Strength — Clarity and organization: Paper is well-written, with clear motivation, derivations (e.g., marginal likelihood recursion), algorithm description, and illustrative figures.  
- Strength — Solid experimental validation: Qualitative and quantitative experiments (Sampson, NIPS coauthorship, full NIPS) convincingly demonstrate interpretability, predictive performance, and scalability.  
- Strength — Probabilistic foundation: Fully Bayesian model enables principled model selection via marginal likelihood and coherent prediction on held-out data.  
- Weakness — Greedy inference limitations: Greedy merging can get stuck in local optima; multiple restarts help but the paper lacks discussion or experiments comparing final hierarchy quality to long-run MCMC.  
- Weakness — Contextualizing originality: Method heavily adapts Bayesian Rose Trees; paper should more explicitly delineate novel contributions vs. adaptations from [14].  
- Weakness — Limited comparisons: Experimental baselines focus on IRM; quantitative comparisons to other relevant models (LFRM, ILA) are missing or only qualitatively referenced.  
- Weakness — Hyperparameter sensitivity: Several priors/hyperparameters are fixed without analysis; sensitivity study or discussion of prior influence is needed.  
- Weakness — Justification for BHCD-sparse: The sparse variant that forbids merges between disconnected components is presented heuristically and would benefit from a principled prior-based motivation.  
- Conclusion: Strong, practical contribution likely to be influential due to its scalability and principled Bayesian approach; recommend acceptance despite limitations.
