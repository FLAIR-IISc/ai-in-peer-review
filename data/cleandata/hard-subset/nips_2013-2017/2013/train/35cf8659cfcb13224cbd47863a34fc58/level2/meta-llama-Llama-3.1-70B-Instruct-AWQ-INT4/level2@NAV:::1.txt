This paper proposes a novel Bayesian nonparametric model, Bayesian Hierarchical Community Discovery (BHCD), for discovering hierarchical community structure in social networks. The model is a tree-structured mixture of stochastic blockmodels, which allows for efficient inference of hierarchical community structure.

The authors demonstrate the effectiveness of their approach through experiments on three datasets: Sampson's monastery network, a subset of the NIPS co-authorship network, and the full NIPS co-authorship network. The results show that BHCD is able to discover reasonable hierarchies and make predictions about two orders of magnitude faster than one of the fastest existing Bayesian nonparametric schemes, whilst attaining comparable performance.

The paper is well-written, and the authors provide a clear explanation of their model and inference algorithm. The experiments are thorough, and the results are presented in a clear and concise manner.
