- Proposes a fully Bayesian Gaussian process state-space model (GP-SSM) with a GP prior on transition dynamics, marginalizing the function and inferring the joint smoothing distribution via a tailored PMCMC sampler.
- Novel and interesting idea to use a GP prior over transition dynamics and marginalization for inference.
- Paper is well-written with a clear, detailed explanation and a thorough review of related work.
- Method handles nonparametric models and can capture complex dynamics that parametric models may miss.
- Demonstrates flexibility by outperforming/learning accurate dynamics even when parametric baselines are misspecified.
- Experiments are well-designed and show effectiveness, including a cart-and-pole benchmark relevant to real-world problems.
- Main limitation: computational cost of the PMCMC sampler may be prohibitive for large datasets or complex models despite proposed mitigations.
- Secondary limitation: hyperparameter selection can strongly affect performance and may be challenging for practitioners unfamiliar with GPs.
- Paper would benefit from additional experiments or comparisons to other approaches to further validate effectiveness.
- Reviewer scores: Quality 8/10, Clarity 9/10, Originality 8/10, Significance 8/10.
- Overall recommendation: accept with revisions to address the noted limitations.
