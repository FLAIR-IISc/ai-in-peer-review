The PMCMC sampling is used in an SSM through the GP process, only before expanding to actual parameters instead of simple filtering and smoothing. The PMCMC method has a nice and simple application. Unfortunately, the challenges of implementing the PMCMC scheme have not been properly evaluated and assessed in detail, as it could have been a truly significant contribution. A more detailed and critical evaluation of the method's strengths and weaknesses would have made the paper valuable, as it is an application of the PMCMC method. It is probably an exaggeration to say that the experimental section should have been revised to provide more evaluation than just demonstrations. The application of the PMCMC method in an SSM would have been more useful for evaluating the practical difficulties of implementing such a scheme – and also to understand how well it actually works.
