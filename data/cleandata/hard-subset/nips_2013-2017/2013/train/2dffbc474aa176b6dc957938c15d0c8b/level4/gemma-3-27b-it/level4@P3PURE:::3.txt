This paper leverages Particle Markov Chain Monte Carlo (PMCMC) sampling within a switching state-space model (SSM) framework, extending the application of Gaussian process (GP) priors to the underlying parameters rather than solely for filtering and smoothing. This represents a straightforward and sensible application of PMCMC methodology. However, a significant weakness is the lack of a detailed evaluation regarding the practical challenges encountered when implementing and tuning the PMCMC scheme. A more thorough and critical analysis of the approach’s strengths and weaknesses would have substantially increased the paper’s value, particularly given its nature as an application of established methodology. While a comprehensive revision of the experimental section to move beyond demonstration might be overly ambitious, assessing the practical difficulties of implementing PMCMC in this SSM context – and quantifying its performance under these conditions – would have made this work considerably more useful.
