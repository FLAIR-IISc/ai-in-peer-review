This work applies Particle Markov Chain Monte Carlo (PMCMC) sampling to a state-space model with a Gaussian Process prior, enabling inference over model parameters rather than being limited to filtering and smoothing. The paper presents a clear and straightforward application of the PMCMC methodology.

However, the manuscript's primary weakness is the absence of a detailed discussion and evaluation of the challenges inherent in successfully implementing the PMCMC scheme. A thorough investigation of these practical hurdles would have represented the paper's most significant contribution.

As an application-focused paper, its value would be considerably greater with a more detailed and critical assessment of the proposed approach's strengths and weaknesses. The current experimental section serves more as a demonstration of feasibility than a rigorous evaluation of performance. While a complete revision of the experiments may be a substantial undertaking, this lack of in-depth analysis is a notable shortcoming.

Ultimately, the paper would be more impactful if it focused not just on applying PMCMC to this model, but on assessing the practical difficulties encountered and providing a quantitative analysis of how effectively the scheme performs in this context.
