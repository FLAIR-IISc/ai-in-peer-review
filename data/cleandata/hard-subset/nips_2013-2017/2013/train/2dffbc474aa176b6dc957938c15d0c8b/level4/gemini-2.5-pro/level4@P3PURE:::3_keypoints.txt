- Applies Particle MCMC to a state-space model with a Gaussian Process prior to infer static parameters beyond filtering/smoothing.  
- The contribution is largely a straightforward application of existing PMCMC methodology (limited novelty).  
- Main weakness: lacks detailed discussion and evaluation of practical challenges in getting the PMCMC scheme to work effectively for this model.  
- Missing a critical assessment of the approach’s practical difficulties, strengths, and weaknesses.  
- Needs a more in-depth analysis of sampler tuning and performance (mixing, convergence, computational cost).  
- Experimental section functions as a demonstration rather than a rigorous evaluation of performance.  
- Paper’s value would be increased by assessing implementation difficulties and empirically analyzing how well the method works in practice.  
- Recommend a more thorough/critical evaluation or revision of the experiments to address these gaps.
