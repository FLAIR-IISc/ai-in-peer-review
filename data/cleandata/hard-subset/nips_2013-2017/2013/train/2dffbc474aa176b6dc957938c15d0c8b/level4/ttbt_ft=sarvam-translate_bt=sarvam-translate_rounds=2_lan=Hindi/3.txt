The PMCMC sampling is used in SSM, before it is extended to actual parameters, with the GP process, not just for general filtering and smoothly. The PMCMC method has a good and straightforward application. Unfortunately, the challenge of properly evaluating the PMCMC scheme should be addressed, and its more detailed evaluation has not been mentioned, as it would be a truly significant contribution. A more detailed and critical evaluation of the strengths and weaknesses of the approach would make the paper more valuable, considering that it is an application of the PMCMC method. It is perhaps too much to ask that the experimental section be revised to provide more evaluation than performance. The application of the PMCMC method in SSM would be more useful in assessing the practical difficulties in implementing such a scheme – and how well it actually works.
