PMCMC sampling is used in an ssm through the GP process, only before expanding it to actual parameters instead of simple filtering and smoothing. The PMCMC method has a beautiful and simple application. Unfortunately, the exact assessment of the challenges in implementing the PMCMC scheme has not been detailed and evaluated, as it could have been a truly significant contribution. A more detailed and critical evaluation of the method's strengths and weaknesses would have made the paper valuable, as it is an application of the PMCMC method. It would probably be an exaggeration to say that the experimental section should have been revised to provide more assessment than just demonstrations. The application of the PMCMC method in an ssm would have been more useful in evaluating the practical difficulties in implementing such a scheme – and also in understanding how well it actually works.
