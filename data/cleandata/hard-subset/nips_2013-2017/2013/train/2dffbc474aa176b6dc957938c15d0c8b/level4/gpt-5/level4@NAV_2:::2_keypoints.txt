- Applies particle MCMC for Bayesian inference in Gaussian process state-space models, focusing on particle Gibbs with ancestor sampling (PGAS).  
- Manuscript is clearly written and presents an interesting, original application of particle MCMC.  
- Contributes model-specific developments, notably a sparse GP-SSM construction.  
- Lacks empirical comparisons with other particle MCMC variants, especially particle marginal MH (PMMH) and particle Gibbs with backward sampling.  
- Baseline methods (PMMH and backward-sampling PG) seem straightforward to implement and should be included.  
- Requests performance curves (e.g., ACF or ESS) as functions of particle count N and time horizon T to show scaling.  
- Unclear how N should scale with T; reviewer expects PMMH to need particles growing ~quadratically with T, while particle Gibbs variants should need sublinear growth.  
- Overall assessment: solid, well-written work that would be significantly strengthened by empirical comparisons against PMMH and particle Gibbs with backward sampling.
