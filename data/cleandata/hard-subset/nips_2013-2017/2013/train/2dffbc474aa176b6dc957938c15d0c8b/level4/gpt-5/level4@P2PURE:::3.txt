The paper applies PMCMC sampling to a state-space model with a Gaussian process prior to infer model parameters, not just to perform the usual filtering and smoothing of latent states. It is a clear, straightforward application of PMCMC methodology. However, the paper does not provide a thorough discussion and evaluation of the practical challenges involved in getting the PMCMC scheme to work, which would likely have been the most significant contribution. A more detailed and critical assessment of the approach’s strengths and weaknesses would substantially improve the paper’s value, given that it is primarily an application of PMCMC. It may be unrealistic at this stage to expect a full revision of the experimental section, but it currently reads more as a demonstration than an evaluation. An application of PMCMC to SSMs would be far more useful if it assessed the practical difficulties in making such a scheme work—and how well it actually performs.
