The authors leverage PMCMC sampling within a state-space model (SSM) featuring a Gaussian Process (GP) prior, successfully extending its application beyond traditional filtering and smoothing. The straightforward application of PMCMC methodology is commendable. However, a notable omission is a thorough examination of the challenges associated with implementing the PMCMC scheme, which would have constituted a significant contribution. A more in-depth and critical assessment of the approach's strengths and weaknesses would have substantially enhanced the paper's value, given its focus on applying PMCMC methodology. While it may be unrealistic to expect a revised experimental section, a more comprehensive evaluation of the practical difficulties in implementing such a scheme and its actual performance would have been a valuable addition.
