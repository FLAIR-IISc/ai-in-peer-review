- Clear and original contribution: application of particle MCMC to Gaussian process state-space models, with sparse GP-SSM methodology being a valuable addition.  
- Major limitation: absence of comparative analysis against other established particle MCMC methods.  
- Specifically missing comparisons to particle marginal Metropolis–Hastings (PMMH) and particle Gibbs with backward sampling (e.g., Whiteley et al.).  
- Implementing these alternative methods appears feasible and would meaningfully strengthen the paper.  
- Results lack sufficient detail: should include metrics (e.g., autocorrelation function, Effective Sample Size) plotted as functions of N and T.  
- Unclear how N should scale with T; literature suggests PMMH may scale quadratically with T while particle Gibbs methods show sublinear growth.  
- Overall: well-written and practical, but would benefit considerably from comparative evaluation against PMMH and particle Gibbs with backward sampling.
