- Applies particle MCMC (ancestral sampling particle Gibbs) to Gaussian process state-space models.  
- Paper is well-structured and offers an original perspective; model-specific methods like sparse GP-SSM add value.  
- Major omission: no comparison to other particle MCMC schemes, notably PMMH and particle Gibbs with backward sampling (Whiteley et al.).  
- Such comparative experiments would be straightforward to add and are important for benchmarking.  
- Lacks graphical performance diagnostics (e.g., ACF or ESS) as functions of particle count N and time horizon T.  
- Unclear scaling: paper does not show how N must grow with T; PMMH may require quadratic growth while particle Gibbs may be sublinear.  
- Including the suggested comparisons and plots would substantially improve the paper’s clarity, impact, and understanding of scalability.
