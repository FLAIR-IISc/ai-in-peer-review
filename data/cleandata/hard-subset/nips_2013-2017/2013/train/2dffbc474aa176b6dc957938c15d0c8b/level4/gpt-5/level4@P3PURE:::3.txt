The paper applies PMCMC sampling to a state-space model with a Gaussian process prior, extending inference to model parameters rather than focusing solely on filtering and smoothing. This is a clear and straightforward application of PMCMC methodology.

However, the manuscript does not provide a sufficiently thorough evaluation of the practical challenges involved in getting the PMCMC scheme to work. Such an evaluation would likely constitute the most significant contribution of the paper. A more detailed and critical assessment of the approach’s strengths and weaknesses would substantially increase the paper’s value, particularly given that the core contribution is an application of an existing methodology.

At this stage, it may be unrealistic to expect a complete revision of the experimental section, but the current experiments function primarily as demonstrations rather than as rigorous evaluations. The work would be considerably more useful if it systematically assessed the practical difficulties of implementing PMCMC in this SSM setting and provided clear evidence of how well the method actually performs.
