- Authors apply PMCMC to SSMs with a GP prior to infer model parameters, extending beyond typical filtering/smoothing — a straightforward, useful application.  
- Paper fails to provide a detailed evaluation of the practical challenges involved in getting the PMCMC scheme to work.  
- A thorough analysis of the method’s strengths and weaknesses is missing and would significantly increase the paper’s value for assessing implementation difficulty and performance.  
- The experimental section is limited; even if not fully revised, the paper needs a deeper assessment of challenges and limitations of applying PMCMC to SSMs.
