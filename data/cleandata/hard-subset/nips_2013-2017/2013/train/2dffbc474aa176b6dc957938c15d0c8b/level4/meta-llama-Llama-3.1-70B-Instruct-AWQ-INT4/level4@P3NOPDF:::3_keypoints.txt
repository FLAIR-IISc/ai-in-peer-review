- Paper applies Particle MCMC to state-space models with a Gaussian Process prior and demonstrates feasibility.  
- The idea is intriguing but the contribution is largely a straightforward application.  
- The paper lacks a comprehensive evaluation of implementation challenges for PMCMC.  
- Experimental section reads as a demonstration rather than a rigorous empirical evaluation.  
- Missing critical assessment of practical difficulties such as convergence issues, particle degeneracy, and computational cost.  
- Insufficient comparison with existing methods to contextualize benefits and limitations.  
- With deeper analysis and further development, the work could become a more significant contribution.
