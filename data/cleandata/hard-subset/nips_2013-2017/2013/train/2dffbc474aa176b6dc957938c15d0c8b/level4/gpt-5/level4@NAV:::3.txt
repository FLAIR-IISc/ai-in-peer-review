The paper employs PMCMC sampling in a state-space model with a Gaussian process prior to target the actual parameters, rather than focusing solely on the usual filtering and smoothing. It is a clear and straightforward application of PMCMC methodology. Unfortunately, the manuscript does not provide a proper, detailed assessment of the challenges involved in getting the PMCMC scheme to work, which would have constituted the more significant contribution. A more thorough and critical evaluation of the approach’s strengths and weaknesses would have increased the paper’s value, given that it is primarily an application of PMCMC. It may be unrealistic to expect the experimental section to be fully revised to include more evaluation than demonstration. An application of PMCMC methodology to SSMs would be more useful if it assessed the practical difficulties in making such a scheme work—and how well it actually performs.
