- Paper applies Particle MCMC to a state-space model with a Gaussian Process prior, extending PMCMC to infer static parameters.  
- Approach is a straightforward application of existing methodology rather than a novel algorithmic contribution.  
- Major weakness: lacks detailed discussion and evaluation of practical implementation challenges of the PMCMC scheme.  
- Missing critical analysis of method-specific strengths and weaknesses in the GP-SSM context.  
- No thorough treatment of difficulties in getting the scheme to work or strategies to mitigate them.  
- Experimental section reads as a demonstration rather than a rigorous evaluation of performance.  
- Current results give little insight into practical utility, limitations, or real-world performance.  
- Paper would be more useful if it assessed implementation challenges and provided clear empirical evaluation of the scheme.
