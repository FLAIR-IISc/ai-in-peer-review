- Paper applies PMCMC to an SSM with a GP prior and is technically sound.
- Core idea is well-motivated but primarily a straightforward application of existing methods.
- Main weakness: lack of rigorous evaluation of practical implementation challenges.
- Missing analysis of computational cost and how demands scale with model complexity.
- No quantification of convergence behavior or frequency of PMCMC failures.
- No assessment of sensitivity to tuning parameters or robustness across data regimes.
- Fails to compare performance against alternatives (standard MCMC, variational methods).
- Lacks runtime, effective sample size, and mixing-rate comparisons, especially regarding GP priors.
- Single synthetic experiment is insufficient to establish robustness or real-world viability.
- Reviewer recommends including failure cases, empirical benchmarks, and parameter sensitivity studies.
- Experimental section reads as a proof-of-concept rather than a critical assessment for practitioners.
- Conclusion: competent technical execution but insufficient practical evaluation; major revision required for publication.
