- Application: Authors apply PMCMC sampling with a Gaussian process prior in state-space models to estimate parameters, extending beyond standard filtering/smoothing.
- Novelty: The approach is straightforward but represents a notable use of PMCMC methodology.
- Major omission: Paper lacks a thorough examination of practical challenges in implementing the PMCMC scheme.
- Evaluation gap: The manuscript fails to provide an in-depth, critical assessment of the approach's strengths and limitations.
- Experimental limitation: The experiments are demonstrative rather than a comprehensive evaluation, reducing confidence in practical performance.
- Recommendation: A detailed analysis of implementation difficulties and actual performance would significantly improve the paper's assessment of PMCMC applicability to SSMs.
