- Applies PMCMC to SSMs with a GP prior — a straightforward and potentially valuable application.  
- Main weakness: insufficient evaluation of practical implementation and tuning challenges of PMCMC.  
- Demonstrates functionality but lacks analysis of convergence difficulties and sensitivity to PMCMC parameter settings.  
- Reads more like a feasibility demonstration than a rigorous empirical evaluation.  
- Needs a critical comparison to existing SSM parameter estimation methods (strengths/weaknesses).  
- Should discuss computational cost, scalability, and performance on challenging datasets.  
- Authors should identify scenarios where PMCMC is unsuitable and articulate trade-offs.  
- A robust empirical evaluation is necessary to justify novelty and practical impact.  
- Revision should focus on making PMCMC reliably work and quantifying performance under varied conditions.  
- Specifically investigate effects of particle number, proposal distributions, and acceptance rates on accuracy/stability.  
- Provide convergence diagnostics beyond showing successful estimation.  
- Compare proposed approach to alternatives (e.g., EM, variational inference) to contextualize advantages/disadvantages.
