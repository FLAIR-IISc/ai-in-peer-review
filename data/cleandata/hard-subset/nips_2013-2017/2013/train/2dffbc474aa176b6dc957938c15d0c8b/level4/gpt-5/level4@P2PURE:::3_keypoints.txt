- Applies PMCMC to a state-space model with a Gaussian process prior to infer model parameters (beyond filtering/smoothing of latent states).
- The work is a clear, straightforward application of PMCMC methodology.
- Paper fails to thoroughly discuss or evaluate practical challenges involved in making the PMCMC scheme work.
- A detailed, critical assessment of the method’s strengths and weaknesses is missing and would substantially improve the paper.
- The experimental section reads as a demonstration rather than a rigorous evaluation.
- While a full experimental revision may be unrealistic, more substantive evaluation is needed.
- The paper would be far more useful if it assessed practical difficulties and how well PMCMC actually performs in SSMs.
