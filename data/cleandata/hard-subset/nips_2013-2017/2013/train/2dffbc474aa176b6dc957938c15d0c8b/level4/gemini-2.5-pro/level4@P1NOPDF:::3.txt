This paper presents a straightforward application of Particle MCMC (PMCMC) methodology to a state-space model (SSM) with a Gaussian Process (GP) prior. The approach extends the standard use of PMCMC for filtering and smoothing to also perform inference over the model's static parameters.

The primary weakness of the paper, however, is the lack of a detailed discussion and evaluation of the practical challenges involved in implementing this PMCMC scheme. For a paper that is fundamentally an application of existing methodology, its most significant contribution would have been a critical analysis of the method's strengths and weaknesses in this specific context. A thorough treatment of the practical difficulties in getting such a scheme to work—and a clear assessment of how well it actually performs—is unfortunately missing.

Consequently, the experimental section serves more as a demonstration of the proposed model rather than a rigorous evaluation. While it may be too late in the review process to request a complete revision of the experiments, the current results provide little insight into the practical utility and limitations of the approach. A paper applying PMCMC methodology to SSMs would have been far more useful to the community had it assessed the practical difficulties of implementation and offered a clear evaluation of the scheme's real-world performance.
