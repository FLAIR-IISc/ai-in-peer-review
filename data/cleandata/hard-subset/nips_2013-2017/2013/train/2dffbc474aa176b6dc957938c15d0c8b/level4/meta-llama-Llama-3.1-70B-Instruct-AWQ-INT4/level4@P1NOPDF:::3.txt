The authors exploit PMCMC sampling in a state-space model (SSM) with a Gaussian process (GP) prior to extend its application to actual parameters, moving beyond the typical filtering and smoothing tasks. This is a nice, straightforward application of the PMCMC methodology.

However, it is unfortunate that the paper does not provide a detailed evaluation of the challenges involved in getting the PMCMC scheme to work. A thorough analysis of the strengths and weaknesses of the approach would have significantly enhanced the value of the paper, given that it is an application of PMCMC methodology. A more critical evaluation would have been particularly useful in assessing the practical difficulties of implementing such a scheme and its actual performance.

While it may be too much to expect the experimental section to be revised to provide a more comprehensive evaluation, a more in-depth assessment of the challenges and limitations of applying PMCMC methodology to SSMs would have been more valuable than the current demonstration.
