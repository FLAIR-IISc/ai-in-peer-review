

The paper applies PMCMC sampling within a state space model (SSM) using a Gaussian process (GP) prior to estimate actual model parameters, extending beyond standard filtering and smoothing. While the application of PMCMC methodology is straightforward and well-executed, the paper lacks a proper evaluation of the practical challenges in implementing the PMCMC scheme. This omission is significant, as a detailed assessment of these challenges would have constituted the paper's key contribution. A more thorough and critical evaluation of the approach's strengths and weaknesses—particularly regarding the practical difficulties of implementation and the scheme's actual performance—would have substantially increased the paper's value, given its nature as an application of PMCMC methodology. The experimental section focuses primarily on demonstration rather than rigorous evaluation, and while revising it to include deeper assessment may be challenging, such an evaluation would have been essential for the paper to make a meaningful contribution. The application would have been more valuable if it had focused on assessing the practical difficulties of implementing PMCMC in this context and how effectively the scheme performs.
