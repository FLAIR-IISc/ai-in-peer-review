- Paper applies Particle Markov Chain Monte Carlo (PMCMC) within state-space models using a Gaussian process prior to extend parameter estimation beyond filtering and smoothing.
- The application of the PMCMC methodology is presented clearly and directly.
- Major weakness: the paper lacks a thorough evaluation of implementation and tuning challenges for the PMCMC scheme.
- The absence of a detailed analysis of these practical difficulties misses an opportunity for a substantive contribution.
- The paper would benefit from a more critical and comprehensive assessment of its method’s strengths and limitations.
- A realistic and useful improvement would be to assess practical difficulties encountered when applying PMCMC to SSMs and to quantify resulting performance.
