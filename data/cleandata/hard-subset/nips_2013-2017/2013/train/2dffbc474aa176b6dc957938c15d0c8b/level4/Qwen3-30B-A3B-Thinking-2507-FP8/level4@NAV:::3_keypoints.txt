- Presents a straightforward application of PMCMC within a state-space model using a Gaussian process prior to infer model parameters beyond filtering/smoothing.  
- Major shortcoming: lacks a thorough evaluation of practical challenges in implementing the PMCMC scheme.  
- Missing detailed analysis of implementation issues, strengths, and limitations that would have been the paper’s primary contribution.  
- Experimental section demonstrates the method but does not deeply evaluate how well the PMCMC scheme works in practice.  
- Paper would be more impactful if it focused on practical implementation challenges and empirical performance rather than merely demonstrating the approach.
