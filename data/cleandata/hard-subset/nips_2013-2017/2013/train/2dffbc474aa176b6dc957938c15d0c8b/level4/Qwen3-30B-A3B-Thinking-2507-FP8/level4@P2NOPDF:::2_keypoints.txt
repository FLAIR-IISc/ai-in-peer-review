- Clear, well-written application of particle MCMC to GP-SSMs, focusing on ancestral sampling particle Gibbs.  
- Original contribution introducing sparse GP-SSM with practical computational benefits.  
- Major omission: no comparison to established particle MCMC variants (PMMH, particle Gibbs with backward sampling).  
- Lack of benchmarks makes it unclear whether the proposed method offers tangible advantages over existing techniques.  
- Empirical results omit key diagnostics (ACF, ESS) as functions of particle count N and time horizon T.  
- Without ACF/ESS vs N and T, scaling requirements for N (linear, sublinear, etc.) to maintain efficiency are unclear.  
- Assertion that PMMH requires quadratic particle growth in T while particle Gibbs scales sublinearly is plausible but unsubstantiated by experiments.  
- Recommendation: include comparisons to PMMH and particle Gibbs with backward sampling and provide ACF/ESS plots to clarify computational/convergence scaling.  
- Overall assessment: promising and valuable contribution but currently incomplete; addressing the above would substantially strengthen the paper.
