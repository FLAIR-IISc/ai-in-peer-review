The paper employs PMCMC sampling in a state-space model with a Gaussian process prior over the transition dynamics to perform inference over the actual parameters, rather than restricting attention to standard filtering and smoothing. This is a clear and straightforward application of PMCMC methodology. However, the manuscript lacks a substantive assessment of the practical challenges involved in making the PMCMC scheme work, which would likely be the most meaningful contribution. A more detailed and critical evaluation of the approach’s strengths and weaknesses would increase the paper’s value, given that it primarily applies existing PMCMC techniques. While it may be ambitious to request a full revision of the experimental section, shifting from demonstration to more rigorous evaluation would be important. An application-focused study of PMCMC for SSMs would be more useful if it examined the practical hurdles in deploying the method—and provided evidence of how reliably and effectively it performs.
