- Introduces a Bayesian method for state and parameter estimation using a GP prior for transition dynamics and PMCMC with ancestor sampling.  
- Technically sound work with a clear and accessible presentation.  
- Novel contribution: online marginalization of transition dynamics via ancestor sampling.  
- Addresses computational efficiency through sparsity and low-rank Cholesky updates/downdates.  
- Empirical results are brief but sufficient; opportunities for improvement noted.  
- Likely to stimulate discussion on inference for non-Markovian state-space models and trade-offs of learning transitions vs specifying parametric models.  
- Suggest expanding discussion comparing a priori parametric transition models to using them as the GP mean, especially for out-of-sample prediction/forecasting.  
- Table 1 and its description are unclear about whether RMSE is computed on withheld (out-of-sample) data; recommend adding an out-of-sample/forecast RMSE column.  
- Minor issue: CPF-AS is used in Algorithm 1 before it is defined in the text.  
- Overall assessment: strong, novel paper likely to provoke interesting discussions.
