- Applies PMCMC to a state-space model with a Gaussian Process prior, enabling parameter inference beyond filtering/smoothing; clear and straightforward application.  
- Primary weakness: lacks detailed discussion and evaluation of practical challenges in implementing the PMCMC scheme.  
- Would benefit from a more detailed and critical assessment of the approach’s strengths and weaknesses as an application-focused paper.  
- Experimental section demonstrates feasibility but does not provide a rigorous performance evaluation.  
- Paper would be more impactful if it focused on practical difficulties and included quantitative analysis of scheme performance.
