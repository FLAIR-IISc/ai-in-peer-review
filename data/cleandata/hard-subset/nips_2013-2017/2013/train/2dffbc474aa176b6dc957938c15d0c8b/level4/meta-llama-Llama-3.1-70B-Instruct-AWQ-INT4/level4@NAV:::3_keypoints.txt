- Paper applies PMCMC within a state-space model with a GP prior, extending beyond traditional filtering and smoothing.
- The straightforward application of PMCMC methodology is commendable.
- The manuscript lacks a thorough examination of practical challenges in implementing the PMCMC scheme.
- A deeper, critical assessment of the method’s strengths and weaknesses is missing and would significantly improve the paper.
- A more comprehensive evaluation of implementation difficulties and actual performance (even qualitatively) is recommended.
