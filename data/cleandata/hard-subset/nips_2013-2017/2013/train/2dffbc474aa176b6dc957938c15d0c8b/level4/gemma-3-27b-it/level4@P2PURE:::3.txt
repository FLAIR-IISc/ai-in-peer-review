This paper leverages Particle Markov Chain Monte Carlo (PMCMC) sampling within a state-space model (SSM) employing a Gaussian process prior, extending parameter estimation beyond typical filtering and smoothing approaches. The application of PMCMC methodology is clear and direct. However, a significant weakness is the lack of a thorough evaluation regarding the challenges of implementing and tuning the PMCMC scheme; a detailed analysis of these difficulties would have represented a substantial contribution. A more critical and comprehensive assessment of the approach’s strengths and limitations would have elevated the paper’s value, particularly given its nature as an application of established methodology. While a substantial revision of the experimental section to move beyond demonstration might be unrealistic, a more useful contribution would have focused on assessing the practical difficulties encountered when applying PMCMC to SSMs, and quantifying the resulting performance.
