- Presents particle MCMC for inference in GP-SSMs based on ancestral-sampling particle Gibbs; clearly written and includes original, model-specific methodology (notably sparse GP-SSM).  
- Major weakness: no comparison to other particle MCMC schemes (PMMH, particle Gibbs with backward sampling), which would be straightforward and informative.  
- Results lack diagnostic visualizations (e.g., ACF, ESS) plotted against particle number N and time series length T to assess algorithm behavior.  
- Needs empirical analysis of scaling: whether N must scale linearly, sublinearly, or otherwise with T—prior work suggests PMMH may scale quadratically while particle Gibbs is sublinear.  
- Overall: promising and well-written, but impact would rise substantially with comparative experiments against PMMH and backward-sampling PG and detailed N vs. T performance plots.
