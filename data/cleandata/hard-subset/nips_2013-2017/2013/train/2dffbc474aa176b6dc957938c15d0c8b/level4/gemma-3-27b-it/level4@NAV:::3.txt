The paper leverages Particle Markov Chain Monte Carlo (PMCMC) sampling within a state-space model, employing a Gaussian Process prior to infer actual parameters – a step beyond standard filtering and smoothing techniques. This represents a clear and direct application of PMCMC. However, a significant opportunity was missed: a thorough discussion and evaluation of the practical challenges encountered when implementing the PMCMC scheme is lacking. Such an analysis would have constituted a key contribution. A more in-depth and critical assessment of the approach’s advantages and limitations would substantially enhance the paper’s value, particularly given its nature as a methodological application. While a comprehensive revision of the experimental section for more rigorous evaluation may be ambitious, focusing on the practical difficulties in implementing and assessing the performance of this PMCMC-based state-space model would have greatly increased the work’s utility.
