PMCMC sampling is exploited within a state-space model (SSM) using a Gaussian process (GP) prior, extending the method to estimate actual parameters rather than relying solely on typical filtering and smoothing techniques. This is a straightforward and well-executed application of PMCMC methodology. It’s a shame that a more thorough evaluation of the challenges involved in implementing and tuning the PMCMC scheme isn’t described and analyzed in greater detail, as that would have significantly strengthened the paper's contribution. A more detailed and critical assessment of the approach’s strengths and limitations would have increased the paper’s value, given its nature as an application of PMCMC. Revising the experimental section to provide more rigorous evaluation beyond demonstration seems ambitious. An assessment of the practical difficulties encountered when applying PMCMC to SSMs – and a quantifiable measure of its performance – would have made this work particularly useful.
