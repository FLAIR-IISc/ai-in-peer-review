- Paper applies Particle MCMC to state-space models with Gaussian Process priors to infer static parameters beyond filtering/smoothing.
- The approach is conceptually straightforward and a logical extension of PMCMC to this model class.
- Major weakness: lacks thorough evaluation of practical implementation and tuning challenges of PMCMC.
- Missing discussion/empirical analysis on tuning proposal distributions, particle counts, and other hyperparameters.
- Paper mostly demonstrates feasibility rather than providing rigorous performance evaluation.
- Lacks assessment of sampler efficiency, mixing times, and sensitivity to number of particles.
- No quantitative comparison to alternative methods or performance under different settings.
- Because it mainly applies an existing method without new practical insights, its contribution is limited.
- A deeper exploration of strengths, weaknesses, and practical difficulties would be needed for stronger impact.
