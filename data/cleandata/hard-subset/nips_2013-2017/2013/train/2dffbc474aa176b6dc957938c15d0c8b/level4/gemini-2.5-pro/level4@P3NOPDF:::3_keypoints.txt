- Presents PMCMC for joint parameter inference in GP-based state-space models as a logical extension of existing methods.  
- Approach is straightforward and applies an established methodology to a relevant model class.  
- Major weakness: lacks a thorough, critical evaluation of the proposed scheme.  
- Paper fails to analyze practical challenges, strengths, and limitations specific to PMCMC in this context.  
- Does not study sensitivity to tuning (number of particles, proposal design) or mixing difficulties in the sampler.  
- Experimental section demonstrates feasibility but does not provide rigorous performance assessment.  
- Missing analysis of computational cost and comparison with alternative inference methods.  
- Contribution is limited by superficial evaluation; needs comprehensive empirical investigation to be practically useful.
