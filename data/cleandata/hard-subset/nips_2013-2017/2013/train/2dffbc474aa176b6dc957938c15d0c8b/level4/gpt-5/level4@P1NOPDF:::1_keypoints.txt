- Introduces a Bayesian method for joint state and parameter estimation in nonlinear state-space models using a Gaussian process prior on transition dynamics.  
- Performs inference with particle MCMC augmented by ancestor sampling; online marginalization of transition dynamics is novel and well-motivated.  
- Addresses computational efficiency through sparsity and low-rank Cholesky updates/downdates, strengthening the technical contribution.  
- Empirical results are brief but adequate; the reviewer offers suggestions for improvement.  
- Work likely to stimulate discussion on inference for non-Markovian state-space models and on trade-offs between learned GP dynamics versus specifying parametric transitions.  
- Suggest expanding discussion on differences between using a fixed parametric transition model and using a comparable model as the GP mean, especially for out-of-sample/forecasting performance.  
- Table 1 and its description are unclear about whether reported RMSE is held-out or conditioned on the same data; reviewer requests an out-of-sample RMSE column for forward-time forecasts.  
- Minor issue: CPF-AS is used in Algorithm 1 before being defined; define at first use or add a forward reference.  
- Overall judgment: technically strong, clear, novel paper likely to stimulate interesting discussion.
