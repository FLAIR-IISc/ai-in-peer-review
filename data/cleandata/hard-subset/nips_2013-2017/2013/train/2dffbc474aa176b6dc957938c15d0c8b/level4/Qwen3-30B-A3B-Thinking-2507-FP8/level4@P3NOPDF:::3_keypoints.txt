- Paper applies PMCMC to SSMs with GP priors and is technically sound in implementation.
- Core contribution is weakened by inadequate evaluation of practical implementation challenges.
- Authors fail to analyze computational costs of GP-based transitions within PMCMC.
- No discussion of convergence behavior, mixing, or sensitivity to hyperparameters.
- Missing strategies for efficient sampling (proposal tuning, particle degeneracy mitigation).
- Lacks comparative analysis against simpler alternatives (standard MCMC, variational methods) on metrics like ESS, mixing, runtime.
- Experimental section reads as model validation rather than critical methodological assessment.
- No evaluation of scalability, robustness to noise, or feasibility in high-dimensional settings.
- Paper omits a balanced discussion of strengths (flexibility for non-linear dynamics) and weaknesses (computational overhead, implementation complexity).
- Fails to provide actionable implementation insights, diagnostics, or mitigation strategies.
- Recommends adding convergence diagnostics, benchmarking, and a clear "Limitations"/implementation challenges subsection.
- As is, the work is a competent application but does not advance understanding of PMCMC’s practical constraints or adoption guidance.
