- Applies PMCMC within a switching state-space model and extends Gaussian process priors to underlying parameters rather than only filtering/smoothing.  
- Represents a straightforward and sensible application of established PMCMC methodology.  
- Major weakness: lacks detailed evaluation of practical challenges in implementing and tuning the PMCMC scheme.  
- Needs a more thorough, critical analysis of the method’s strengths and weaknesses to increase the paper’s value.  
- Experimental section is mainly demonstrative; a comprehensive revision may be ambitious but is desirable.  
- Recommends assessing practical difficulties and quantifying PMCMC performance in this SSM context to make the work more useful.
