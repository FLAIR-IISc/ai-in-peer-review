- Proposes a fully Bayesian nonparametric state-space model with a Gaussian process prior over transition dynamics.  
- Introduces a PMCMC algorithm to sample from the smoothing distribution while marginalizing the transition function.  
- Leverages sparse Gaussian processes to reduce computational complexity.  
- Approach is well-motivated and a natural extension of prior GP state-space work.  
- Use of PMCMC (with ancestor sampling) is a key technical contribution enabling efficient inference.  
- Paper provides a clear and detailed algorithmic description, including marginalization steps.  
- Experiments on two nonlinear systems (including cart-pole) show accurate one-step predictions and sensible uncertainty estimates.  
- Lacks a detailed, critical evaluation of strengths and weaknesses of the method.  
- Does not sufficiently discuss practical challenges of PMCMC (hyperparameter selection, number of particles).  
- Needs more comprehensive comparisons to alternative methods and analysis of computational complexity.  
- Overall a solid, potentially useful contribution but would be strengthened by deeper evaluation and limitations discussion.
