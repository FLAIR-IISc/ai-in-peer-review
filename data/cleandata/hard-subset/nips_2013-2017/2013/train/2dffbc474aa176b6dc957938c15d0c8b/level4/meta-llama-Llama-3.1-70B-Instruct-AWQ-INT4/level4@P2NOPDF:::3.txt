This paper presents a straightforward application of Particle Markov Chain Monte Carlo (PMCMC) sampling to a state-space model (SSM) with a Gaussian process (GP) prior, extending the methodology beyond traditional filtering and smoothing. The approach is well-executed, and the idea of leveraging PMCMC in this context is promising.

However, the paper falls short in providing a thorough evaluation of the challenges associated with implementing the PMCMC scheme. A more detailed analysis of the strengths and weaknesses of this approach would have significantly enhanced the paper's value, given its focus on applying PMCMC methodology to SSMs. Specifically, the experimental section could have benefited from a more critical assessment of the practical difficulties in getting the scheme to work, as well as its actual performance.

As it stands, the paper primarily demonstrates the application of PMCMC to SSMs, rather than providing a comprehensive evaluation. A more in-depth examination of the challenges and limitations of this approach would have made the paper more impactful and useful to the reader.
