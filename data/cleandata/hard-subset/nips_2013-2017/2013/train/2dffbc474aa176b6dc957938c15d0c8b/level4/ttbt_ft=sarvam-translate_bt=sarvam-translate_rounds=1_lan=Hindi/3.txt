The PMCMC sampling is used in SSM before extending it to actual parameters with the GP process, not just for general filtering and smoothing. The PMCMC method has a good and straightforward application. Unfortunately, the PMCMC scheme's proper assessment of the challenge to be addressed and its more detailed evaluation are not mentioned, as this would have been a truly significant contribution. A more detailed and critical evaluation of the approach's strengths and weaknesses would have made the paper more valuable, considering that it is an application of the PMCMC method. It is perhaps too much to ask that the experimental section be revised to provide more assessment than performance. The application of the PMCMC method in SSM would have been more useful in assessing the practical difficulties in implementing such a scheme – and how well it actually works.
