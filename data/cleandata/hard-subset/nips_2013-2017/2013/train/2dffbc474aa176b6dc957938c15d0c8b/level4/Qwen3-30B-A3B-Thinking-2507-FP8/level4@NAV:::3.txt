

The paper presents a straightforward application of PMCMC methodology within a state space model employing a Gaussian process prior to enable inference of actual model parameters, extending beyond standard filtering and smoothing. However, the absence of a thorough evaluation of the practical challenges encountered in implementing the PMCMC scheme represents a significant shortcoming. This missing analysis would have constituted the paper's primary contribution, as a detailed and critical assessment of the approach's strengths, limitations, and practical difficulties would have substantially increased its value. While the experimental section demonstrates the methodology, it falls short of providing the deeper evaluation required to assess how effectively the PMCMC scheme functions in practice. The paper would have been more impactful by focusing on the practical challenges of implementing such a scheme and evaluating its empirical performance rather than merely demonstrating its application.
