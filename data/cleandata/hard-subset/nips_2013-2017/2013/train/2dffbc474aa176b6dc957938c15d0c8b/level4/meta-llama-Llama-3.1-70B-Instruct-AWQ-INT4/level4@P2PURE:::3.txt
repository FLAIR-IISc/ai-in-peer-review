The authors apply PMCMC sampling to a state-space model (SSM) with a Gaussian process (GP) prior, extending its use beyond traditional filtering and smoothing. This straightforward application of PMCMC methodology is nicely presented. However, the paper falls short in providing a thorough evaluation of the challenges encountered when implementing the PMCMC scheme, which would have been a significant contribution. A more detailed and critical assessment of the approach's strengths and weaknesses would have increased the paper's value, given its focus on applying PMCMC methodology. Ideally, the experimental section would have been revised to provide a more comprehensive evaluation, rather than merely demonstrating the application. A more practical assessment of the difficulties in implementing PMCMC in SSMs and its actual performance would have made the paper more useful, highlighting the challenges of getting such a scheme to work and its effectiveness in practice.
