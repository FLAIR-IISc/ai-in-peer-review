- Applies PMCMC to a state-space model with a Gaussian process prior, extending inference to model parameters rather than only filtering/smoothing.  
- The work is a clear and straightforward application of existing PMCMC methodology.  
- Manuscript does not sufficiently evaluate the practical challenges of getting the PMCMC scheme to work.  
- A more detailed and critical assessment of the approach’s strengths and weaknesses would substantially increase the paper’s value.  
- Current experiments serve mainly as demonstrations rather than rigorous evaluations.  
- It may be unrealistic to expect a complete experimental revision now, but the paper should systematically assess implementation difficulties and provide clear evidence of performance.
