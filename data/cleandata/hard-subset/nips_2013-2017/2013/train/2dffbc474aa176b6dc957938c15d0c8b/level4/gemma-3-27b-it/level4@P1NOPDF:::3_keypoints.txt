- Applies PMCMC within a state-space model using a GP prior to estimate actual parameters rather than only filtering/smoothing.  
- The application of PMCMC is straightforward and well executed.  
- Paper lacks a thorough evaluation of implementation and tuning challenges of the PMCMC scheme.  
- Missing a detailed, critical assessment of the method’s strengths and limitations.  
- Experimental section is demonstrative and needs more rigorous evaluation.  
- The work would benefit from assessing practical difficulties and providing quantifiable performance measures for PMCMC on SSMs.
