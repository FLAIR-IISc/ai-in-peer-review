- Presents a Bayesian method for state and parameter estimation in nonlinear state-space models using a GP prior for transition dynamics and PMCMC with ancestor sampling.  
- Novel contribution: online marginalization of the transition dynamics enabled by ancestor sampling.  
- Technical strengths: clear, accessible presentation and overall sound methodology.  
- Computational contributions: discusses sparsity and low-rank Cholesky updates/downdates for efficiency.  
- Empirical results are brief but adequate; however, clarification and possible extension requested.  
- Suggestion: compare using a parametric transition model a priori versus using that model as the GP mean function (e.g., out-of-sample forecasting performance).  
- Request to clarify Table 1: whether RMSE is computed on withheld (out-of-sample) data or on the conditioned training set; recommend adding an out-of-sample forecast RMSE column.  
- Notes the paper will likely stimulate discussion on inference for non-Markovian SSMs and trade-offs of learned versus parametric dynamics.  
- Minor issue: abbreviation CPF-AS appears in Algorithm 1 before being defined in the text.
