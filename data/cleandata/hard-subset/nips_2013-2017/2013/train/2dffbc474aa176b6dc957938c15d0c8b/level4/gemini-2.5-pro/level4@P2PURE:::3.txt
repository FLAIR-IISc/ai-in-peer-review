This paper applies Particle MCMC (PMCMC) to a state-space model (SSM) with a Gaussian Process prior, enabling inference over the model's parameters in addition to standard state filtering and smoothing. The work is a straightforward application of the PMCMC methodology.

The paper's main weakness is that it does not adequately describe or evaluate the practical challenges of getting the PMCMC scheme to work effectively. A detailed and critical analysis of the method's strengths and weaknesses, particularly the difficulties in its implementation, would have been the most valuable contribution. Since this is an application of an existing methodology, such an evaluation is crucial.

The experimental section currently serves as a demonstration of the approach rather than a rigorous evaluation of its performance. A more useful contribution would have focused on assessing the practical difficulties of applying PMCMC to this class of SSMs and quantifying how well the proposed scheme actually performs.
