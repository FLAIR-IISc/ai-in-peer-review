- Applies PMCMC to GP state-space models to enable parameter inference; a clear, straightforward use of established methodology.
- Fails to describe or evaluate practical challenges in making PMCMC effective in this setting.
- Lacks detailed assessment of strengths/weaknesses: computational cost, mixing/convergence behavior, sensitivity to particle count and proposal choices, GP hyperparameter handling, and dataset robustness.
- Experimental section is primarily demonstrative and not rigorously evaluated.
- Needs experiments diagnosing failure modes, quantifying efficiency (e.g., ESS per second), and testing sensitivity to key design choices.
- Should compare against strong baselines and include targeted ablations and stress tests to reveal when the method succeeds or fails.
- Currently offers little practical guidance for implementing PMCMC with GP-based SSMs in realistic scenarios.
- Recommends strengthening empirical analysis and providing clear recommendations on parameter settings, diagnostics, and trade-offs.
