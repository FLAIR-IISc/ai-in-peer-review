This paper presents a straightforward application of Particle Markov Chain Monte Carlo (PMCMC) sampling to a state-space model (SSM) with a Gaussian process (GP) prior, extending the methodology to infer actual parameters rather than just performing filtering and smoothing. 

While the application is nice, it is disappointing that the authors do not provide a more detailed evaluation of the challenges encountered in implementing the PMCMC scheme, which would have been a significant contribution. A more critical assessment of the strengths and weaknesses of the approach would have increased the paper's value, given its focus on applying PMCMC methodology.

Ideally, the experimental section would be revised to provide a more comprehensive evaluation rather than merely demonstrating the application. A more useful assessment would have focused on the practical difficulties in implementing such a scheme and its actual performance.
