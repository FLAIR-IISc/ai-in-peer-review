- Proposes applying particle MCMC to Gaussian process state-space models, focusing on ancestral-sampling Particle Gibbs.  
- Paper is clearly written, interesting, and original.  
- Development of a model-specific sparse GP-SSM is a useful contribution.  
- Major limitation: no empirical comparisons to relevant particle MCMC methods, especially PMMH and Particle Gibbs with backward sampling.  
- Comparisons to PMMH and backward-sampling Particle Gibbs would be straightforward to implement and informative.  
- Experimental results lack quantitative performance analysis (e.g., ACF or ESS) as a function of particle count N and time series length T.  
- Important open question: how N must scale with T to maintain inference quality; prior work suggests PMMH may need N scaling quadratically with T while Particle Gibbs variants may require sublinear growth.  
- Recommendation: strengthen the paper with a more thorough empirical evaluation including PMMH and Particle Gibbs with backward sampling and scaling analyses.
