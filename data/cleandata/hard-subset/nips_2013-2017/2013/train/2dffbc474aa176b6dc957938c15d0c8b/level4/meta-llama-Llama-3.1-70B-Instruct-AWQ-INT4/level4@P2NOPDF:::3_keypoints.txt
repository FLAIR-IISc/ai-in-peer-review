- Presents a straightforward, well-executed application of PMCMC to SSMs with a GP prior.  
- Leveraging PMCMC in this context is a promising methodological idea.  
- Paper lacks a thorough evaluation of implementation challenges for the PMCMC scheme.  
- Missing detailed analysis of the approach’s strengths and weaknesses.  
- Experimental section fails to critically assess practical difficulties in getting the scheme to work.  
- Experiments do not sufficiently evaluate actual performance of the method.  
- Overall, the paper demonstrates application rather than providing a comprehensive, impactful evaluation of limitations.
