The paper leverages Particle Markov Chain Monte Carlo (PMCMC) sampling within a state-space model, employing a Gaussian Process prior to infer actual parameters – a departure from standard filtering and smoothing approaches. This represents a clear and direct application of PMCMC techniques. However, the manuscript would be significantly strengthened by a more thorough discussion and evaluation of the challenges encountered in implementing and tuning the PMCMC scheme. A detailed, critical analysis of the method’s strengths and limitations would elevate the paper’s contribution, particularly given its focus on applying PMCMC. While extensive revision of the experimental section may be unrealistic, a more rigorous evaluation – beyond demonstration – of the practical difficulties and performance of the proposed PMCMC-based state-space model would substantially increase its value to the field. Assessing the practical hurdles in achieving convergence and the resulting accuracy would have been a particularly impactful contribution.
