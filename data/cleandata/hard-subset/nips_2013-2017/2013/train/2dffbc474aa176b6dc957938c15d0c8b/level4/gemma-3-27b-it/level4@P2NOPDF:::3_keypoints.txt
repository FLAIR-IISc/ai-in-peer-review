- Applies Particle Markov Chain Monte Carlo (PMCMC) to switching state-space models with a Gaussian process prior for parameters, moving beyond standard filtering/smoothing.
- The application of PMCMC is conceptually sound and presented clearly.
- Major weakness: limited discussion of practical implementation and tuning challenges for PMCMC.
- Lacks thorough evaluation of convergence behavior and sensitivity to parameter settings.
- Paper demonstrates functionality but omits a critical assessment of performance and limitations.
- Because PMCMC is an established method, the paper needs a detailed analysis of its strengths and weaknesses in this specific context.
- A focused empirical investigation of practical hurdles and quantitative efficacy under varying conditions would improve impact.
- Would benefit from deeper discussion of encountered practical difficulties and comprehensive analysis of how well PMCMC overcomes them.
