- Applies PMCMC within a state-space model with a Gaussian process prior to estimate model parameters beyond filtering/smoothing.
- The PMCMC implementation is straightforward and competently executed.
- The paper fails to properly evaluate practical challenges of implementing the PMCMC scheme.
- This omission is significant because a detailed assessment of implementation challenges would have been the paper’s main contribution.
- Lacks a thorough, critical evaluation of the method’s strengths and weaknesses, particularly regarding practical difficulties and actual performance.
- Experiments are primarily demonstrative rather than rigorously evaluative.
- Including a deeper assessment (though challenging) is essential for the work to make a meaningful contribution.
