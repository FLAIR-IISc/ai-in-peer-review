- Applies ancestral-sampling particle Gibbs to GP-SSMs with a sparse GP-SSM formulation; paper is well-written and original.  
- Major omission: no comparisons to established particle MCMC variants, especially PMMH and particle Gibbs with backward sampling (Whiteley et al.), which are straightforward to implement.  
- Lacks quantitative performance metrics — should report ACF or ESS plots as functions of particle count N and time horizon T.  
- Needs to clarify practical scaling: does N need to grow linearly or sublinearly with T for acceptable performance?  
- Should explicitly discuss theoretical scaling: PMMH often needs O(T^2) particles, while particle Gibbs variants typically require sublinear growth in N with T (per Whiteley et al.).  
- Overall assessment: solid application, but significantly strengthened by adding these method comparisons and performance analyses.
