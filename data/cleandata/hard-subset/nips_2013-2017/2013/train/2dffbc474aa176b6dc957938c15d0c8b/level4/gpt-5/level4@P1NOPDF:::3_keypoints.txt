- Applies PMCMC to a state-space model with a Gaussian process prior, extending inference to static parameters — a sensible, straightforward application of established methods.
- Paper lacks a thorough account of the practical challenges involved in making the PMCMC scheme work in this setting.
- This omission is unfortunate, since such an evaluation would likely be the most meaningful contribution.
- Needs a detailed, critical assessment of strengths and weaknesses: what works well, what fails, and under what conditions.
- Experimental section reads as a demonstration rather than a rigorous evaluation.
- Should analyze implementation and tuning difficulties, computational costs, robustness, and how well the method performs in practice.
- Even without redesigning experiments, the paper would benefit from deeper empirical analysis and discussion beyond illustrative results.
- Overall: clear and competent application but limited contribution due to lack of critical, detailed evaluation of practical performance and limitations.
