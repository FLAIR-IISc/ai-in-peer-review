- Presents a fully Bayesian approach for nonlinear nonparametric state-space models using GP-SSMs and PMCMC samplers.  
- Places a Gaussian process prior on the state transition function for flexible, data-adaptive dynamics modeling.  
- Uses PMCMC to sample the smoothing distribution and marginalize over the state transition function for direct state-trajectory inference.  
- Well-motivated problem and natural choice of GP-SSMs with PMCMC.  
- Clear, concise introduction and well-organized, easy-to-follow technical sections.  
- Technically sound presentation and implementation.  
- Main contribution is a straightforward application of existing PMCMC methodology, offering limited novelty.  
- Paper fails to adequately describe or evaluate practical challenges of implementing the PMCMC scheme.  
- Lacks a detailed, critical assessment of method strengths, weaknesses, and convergence/implementation issues.  
- Experimental section is limited to a few demonstrations and does not provide a rigorous empirical evaluation.  
- Would benefit from more information on practical implementation challenges and how they were addressed.  
- Should include more thorough empirical comparisons to state-of-the-art methods.  
- Reviewer scores: Quality 7/10, Originality 5/10, Significance 6/10.  
- Recommendation: accept with encouragement to expand practical details, limitations discussion, and rigorous experiments.
