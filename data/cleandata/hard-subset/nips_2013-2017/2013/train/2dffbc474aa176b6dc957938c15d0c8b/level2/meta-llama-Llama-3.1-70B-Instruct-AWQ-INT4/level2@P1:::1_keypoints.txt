- Fully Bayesian inference for nonlinear nonparametric state-space models using a Gaussian process prior over transition dynamics.  
- PMCMC algorithm proposed to efficiently sample the smoothing distribution while marginalizing the transition function.  
- Demonstrated effective learning on a nonlinear benchmark and a cart-and-pole system with clear experiments and results.  
- Paper is well-written, clear, and technically thorough with a good related-work discussion.  
- Strength: novel, fully Bayesian approach that can learn complex dynamics and is widely applicable.  
- Weakness: high computational cost on large datasets.  
- Weakness: sparse GP-SSM reduces complexity but trade-offs and limitations need more discussion.  
- Weakness: lacks rigorous theoretical convergence guarantees for the PMCMC sampler.
