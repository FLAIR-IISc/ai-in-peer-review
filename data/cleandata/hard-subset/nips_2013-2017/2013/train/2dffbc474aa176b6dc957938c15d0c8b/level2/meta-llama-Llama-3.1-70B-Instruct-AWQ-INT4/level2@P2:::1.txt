The paper "Bayesian Inference and Learning in Gaussian Process State-Space Models with Particle MCMC" presents a fully Bayesian approach to inference and learning in nonlinear nonparametric state-space models. The authors place a Gaussian process prior over the state transition dynamics, resulting in a flexible model able to capture complex dynamical phenomena. They use a tailored particle Markov Chain Monte Carlo (PMCMC) algorithm to efficiently sample from the smoothing distribution whilst marginalizing over the state transition function.

The main contribution of the paper is the development of a PMCMC sampler that can handle the nonparametric nature of the model. The authors demonstrate the effectiveness of their approach on two benchmark systems: a nonlinear system and a cart and pole system. The results show that the proposed method can learn accurate models of the dynamics and provide high-quality smoothing distributions.
