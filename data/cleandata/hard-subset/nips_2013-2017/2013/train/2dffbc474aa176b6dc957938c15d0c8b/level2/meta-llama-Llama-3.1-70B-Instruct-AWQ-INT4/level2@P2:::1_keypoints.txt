- Develops a tailored particle MCMC (PMCMC) sampler that marginalizes the transition function and samples from the smoothing distribution.
- Demonstrates the method on two benchmarks (a nonlinear system and cart-pole) showing accurate dynamics learning and high-quality smoothing.
- Experimental results are convincing and supported by helpful visualizations.
- Paper is well-written, clearly explaining technical details with consistent notation and definitions.
- Strong balance between technical depth and intuitive explanations, making the work accessible to a broad audience.
- Thorough literature review that situates the contribution within current research.
