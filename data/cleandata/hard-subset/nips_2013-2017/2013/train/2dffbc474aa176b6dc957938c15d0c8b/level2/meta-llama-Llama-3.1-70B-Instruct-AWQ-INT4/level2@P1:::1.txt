This paper presents a fully Bayesian approach to inference and learning in nonlinear nonparametric state-space models, using a Gaussian process prior over the state transition dynamics. The authors propose a particle Markov chain Monte Carlo (PMCMC) algorithm to efficiently sample from the smoothing distribution, while marginalizing over the state transition function. The approach is shown to be effective in learning the dynamics of complex systems, and the authors demonstrate its application to a nonlinear system benchmark and a cart and pole system.

The paper is well-written, and the authors provide a clear and concise explanation of the proposed approach. The paper also has some weaknesses. One of the main limitations is the computational cost of the proposed algorithm, which can be high for large datasets.
