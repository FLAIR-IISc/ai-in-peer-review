- Presents a Bayesian method for joint state and parameter estimation with GP priors over transition dynamics using PMCMC with ancestor sampling.  
- Technically sound with clear, accessible presentation.  
- Novelty: online marginalisation of transition dynamics via ancestor sampling.  
- Strong consideration of computational efficiencies (sparsity and low-rank Cholesky updates/downdates).  
- Empirical results are brief but sufficient; could be expanded.  
- Suggest adding discussion comparing using a parametric transition model a priori vs as the GP mean (implications for forecasting/out-of-sample).  
- Table 1 and preceding text unclear whether RMSE is in-sample or out-of-sample; recommend adding an out-of-sample/forecast RMSE column.  
- Minor issue: abbreviation CPF-AS is used before being defined.  
- Overall: strong, novel paper likely to stimulate discussion on inference for non-Markovian state-space models.
