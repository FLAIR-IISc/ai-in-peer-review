- Proposes LoSST, a novel heuristic algorithm for online tracking of time-varying graphical model structure/parameters.
- Assumes locally stationary data (i.i.d. within time windows) and updates sufficient statistics in real time.
- Provides clear, detailed description of LoSST including components and update equations.
- Situates contribution with a concise related-work overview highlighting limitations of prior methods.
- Demonstrates promising experimental results showing LoSST can track structural changes.
- Includes a real-world application to US price index volatility illustrating practical potential.
- Writing is clear, well-organized, and easy to follow.
- Related work and limitations of existing methods are thoroughly discussed.
- Experimental evaluation is primarily compared only to the PC algorithm, limiting scope.
- Lacks comparisons to other standard graphical-structure learning methods to establish generality.
- Extending LoSST to Bayesian networks may be nontrivial due to difficulties maintaining sufficient statistics and this limitation should be stated more explicitly.
- Overall contribution is significant and promising, but additional experiments would strengthen the claim.
- Quality: 8/10 — well-written with solid experiments but limited scope.
- Clarity: 9/10 — paper is organized and easy to follow.
- Originality: 8/10 — novel algorithm though builds on existing tracking ideas.
- Significance: 8/10 — meaningful contribution with practical potential but needs broader validation.
