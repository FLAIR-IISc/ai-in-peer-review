- Proposes LoSST, a novel online method for tracking time-varying graphical structure in locally stationary data.  
- Algorithm tracks sufficient statistics online and dynamically relearns graphical structure as needed.  
- Paper is well-written, clear, and provides a thorough related-work review.  
- Algorithm description is detailed and easy to follow.  
- Experimental results are promising and demonstrate LoSST’s effectiveness.  
- Authors discuss limitations and outline avenues for future work.  
- Main weakness: claim that “other standard graphical model structure learning algorithms would perform similarly” is not well justified.  
- Empirical comparison is limited — only evaluated against the PC algorithm.  
- Insufficient detail on computational complexity and scalability to large datasets.  
- Overall assessment: significant and novel contribution to graphical models.  
- Recommendation: accept with minor revisions (rating 8/10).  
- Minor revision requests: add computational complexity and scaling details.  
- Minor revision requests: provide a more thorough comparison to other structure-learning algorithms.  
- Minor revision requests: clarify and justify the claim about other algorithms’ comparable performance.
