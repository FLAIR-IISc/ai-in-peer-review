- Proposes a systematic method to enable structure learning for SEMs on locally i.i.d. (time-dependent) data; well-written and clear.
- Techniques to extend learning to time-dependent data are simple and effective, validated using the PC algorithm.
- Reviewer objects to the claim that “any other standard graphical model structure learning algorithm would perform similarly,” calling it too strong.
- Suggests rephrasing to state experiments focus on the PC algorithm and its adaptation, or provide justification for broader generalization.
- Notes conclusions mention difficulty extending the approach to Bayesian networks due to maintaining sufficient statistics; this limitation should be acknowledged earlier.
- Recommends framing the work more directly as targeting SEMs while noting potential but nontrivial extensions to other models.
- Overall assessment: promising, easy-to-implement formulation with encouraging results, though experiments are currently limited to PC algorithm comparisons.
