- Proposes a systematic method to learn SEM structure from locally i.i.d. (time-varying) data.  
- Paper is well-written and easy to follow.  
- Addresses the important, often-overlooked problem of learning from time-dependent data.  
- Proposed extensions are simple and appear effective, validated using the PC algorithm.  
- Statement that “any other standard… algorithms would perform similarly” is too strong — should be rephrased or justified.  
- Extending the method to Bayesian networks is non-trivial (sufficient-statistics issues); this limitation should be stated earlier and scope framed as SEM-specific.  
- Experimental results are promising but limited to comparisons using only the PC algorithm.  
- Reviewer has read the authors’ feedback.
