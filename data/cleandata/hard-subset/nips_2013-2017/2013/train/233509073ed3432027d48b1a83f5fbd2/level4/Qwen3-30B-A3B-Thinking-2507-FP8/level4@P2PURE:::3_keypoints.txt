- Proposes a systematic approach for SEM structure learning under a locally i.i.d. assumption where the generating model can change over time.  
- Manuscript is well-written, well-structured, and addresses the often-overlooked problem of learning from time-dependent data.  
- Proposed techniques are simple, straightforward to implement, and shown to be effective in experiments.  
- Experimental evaluation is encouraging but primarily focused on the PC method.  
- The claim that other standard structure learning algorithms would perform similarly is too strong—either rephrase to be speculative or provide justification.  
- Point out upfront that the work targets SEMs; extending to Bayesian networks is nontrivial (issues preserving sufficient statistics) and requires additional effort.  
- Recommend explicitly stating limitations and scope regarding extensions to other graphical models.  
- Reviewer confirms having reviewed the authors’ feedback.
