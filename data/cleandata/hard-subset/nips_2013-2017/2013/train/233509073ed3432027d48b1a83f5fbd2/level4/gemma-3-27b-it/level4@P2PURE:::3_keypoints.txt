- Introduces a systematic approach for structural learning in SEMs when data are only locally i.i.d. (time-varying DGPs).  
- Well-written, clearly presented, addresses the important and often-overlooked problem of learning from time-dependent data.  
- Proposed adaptations for structure learning on time-dependent datasets are simple and effective, validated via experiments with the PC algorithm.  
- Reviewer recommends softening or justifying the claim that “any other standard graphical model structure learning algorithm would perform similarly,” and instead emphasizing the PC focus or providing evidence.  
- Notes that extending the approach to Bayesian network structure learning is nontrivial due to maintaining sufficient statistics; suggests stating this limitation earlier and framing the work as SEM-focused with possible but challenging generalization.  
- Experimental results are promising; reviewer confirms they have reviewed the authors’ response.
