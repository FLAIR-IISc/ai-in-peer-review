- Introduces a systematic method for learning SEM structure from locally i.i.d. (non‑stationary) data.  
- Tackles the important, often-neglected problem of learning when the generative model changes over time.  
- Manuscript is well-written and the core ideas are easy to follow.  
- The techniques to extend structure learning to time-dependent data are simple and shown effective in experiments using the PC algorithm.  
- The claim that "any other standard graphical model structure learning algorithms would perform similarly" is too strong and needs tempering.  
- Recommend either rephrase to limit the claim to the PC experiments or provide theoretical/empirical justification for broader generalizability.  
- The limitation about applying the framework to Bayesian Networks (difficulty maintaining sufficient statistics) is important and should be disclosed earlier; frame the paper up front as a solution specifically for SEMs.  
- Overall assessment: valuable, straightforward, and implementable method with promising results; addressing the above points would strengthen the manuscript.  
- Reviewer acknowledges having read the authors' feedback.
