- Proposes a principled method for structure learning in SEMs when data are only locally i.i.d. (evolving over time).
- Paper is clearly written and tackles an important, underexplored problem (time-dependent structure learning).
- Introduces a simple, effective extension of the PC algorithm for this setting; experimental results are encouraging.
- Critique: the claim that “any other standard graphical model structure learning algorithms would perform similarly” is too strong—recommend rephrasing as a conjecture or providing justification.
- Suggestion: acknowledge earlier that extending the approach to Bayesian networks may be nontrivial (sufficient-statistics challenges), and explicitly frame the paper’s scope as focused on SEMs.
- Overall assessment: well-formulated, easy to implement, promising results, but evaluations are limited to PC-based comparisons; reviewer has read the authors’ feedback.
