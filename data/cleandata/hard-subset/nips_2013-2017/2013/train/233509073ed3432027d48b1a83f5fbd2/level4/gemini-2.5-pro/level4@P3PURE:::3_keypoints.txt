- Proposes a systematic approach for structure learning of SEMs when data are only locally i.i.d. (time-varying data-generating model).  
- Paper is well-written, clear, and addresses an important, often-neglected problem.  
- Proposed techniques are simple, effective, and demonstrated via experiments using the PC algorithm.  
- Claim that "any other standard graphical model structure learning algorithms would perform similarly" is too strong; suggest rephrasing or providing justification/empirical evidence.  
- Extending the approach to Bayesian networks is non-trivial due to maintaining sufficient statistics; recommend explicitly framing the paper’s scope as SEM-focused and noting extensions may be difficult.  
- Overall assessment: clear, implementable formulation with promising results, though experiments are limited to the PC method.
