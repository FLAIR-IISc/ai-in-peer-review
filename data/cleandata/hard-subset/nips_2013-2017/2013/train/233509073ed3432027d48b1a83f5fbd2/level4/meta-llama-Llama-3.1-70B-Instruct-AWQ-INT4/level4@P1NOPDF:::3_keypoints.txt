- Proposes a systematic method for structure learning in SEMs that handles locally i.i.d. (time-dependent) data.  
- Paper is well-written, easy to follow, and tackles the important but neglected problem of learning from time-dependent data.  
- Proposed extensions for time-dependent data are simple and effective, with experiments demonstrating performance using the PC method.  
- The claim that “other standard graphical model structure learning algorithms would perform similarly” is too strong; rephrase or provide justification/experiments.  
- Extending the approach to Bayesian networks may be nontrivial due to difficulties maintaining sufficient statistics; recommend stating this limitation and scope earlier.  
- Experimental results are promising but primarily focused on PC, so generality across methods should be clarified.  
- Reviewer is positive overall and has considered the authors’ feedback.
