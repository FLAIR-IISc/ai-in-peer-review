- Introduces a systematic approach for SEM structure learning under locally i.i.d. (time-dependent) data, addressing an important overlooked problem.  
- Paper is clearly written; proposed techniques are simple, effective, and demonstrated via extensions to the PC algorithm.  
- Claim that “any other standard graphical model structure learning algorithm would perform similarly” is too broad; recommend rephrasing or providing justification and/or focusing on PC.  
- Extending the approach to Bayesian network structure learning is challenging due to maintaining sufficient statistics; this limitation should be noted earlier and the paper’s scope focused on SEM.  
- Overall: promising, accessible, and implementable work with encouraging PC-based experiments; reviewer appreciates the authors’ clarifications.
