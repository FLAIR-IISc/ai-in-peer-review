- Proposes a systematic approach for structure learning in SEMs that handles locally i.i.d. and time-dependent data generated by different underlying models over time.  
- Paper is well-written, clear, and addresses the important but overlooked problem of learning from time-dependent data.  
- Techniques to extend learning to time-dependent data are simple and shown effective via experiments using the PC method.  
- Experimental claim that "any other standard graphical model structure learning algorithms would perform similarly" is too broad; recommend rephrasing or providing justification and/or focusing explicitly on PC.  
- Note that extending the approach to Bayesian networks is non-trivial due to difficulties in maintaining sufficient statistics; this limitation should be disclosed earlier and the paper framed more precisely as SEM-focused.  
- Overall assessment: simple, implementable formulation with promising experimental results, albeit evaluated only with the PC method.
