- Introduces a systematic approach for SEM structure learning under locally i.i.d. (time-varying) data; well-written and fills an important gap, with simple effective techniques demonstrated using the PC method.  
- Concern: the claim that "any other standard graphical model structure learning algorithms would perform similarly" is too broad; clarify that experiments compare PC on time-independent vs adapted time-dependent data or provide justification for generalization.  
- Recommendation: explicitly state early that the work targets SEMs, since extending to Bayesian networks may face additional issues (e.g., sufficient-statistics constraints), to avoid overgeneralization while noting future directions.  
- Overall: clear formulation, promising experimental results (though focused on PC), and a simple, implementable method; reviewer has read authors' prior responses.
