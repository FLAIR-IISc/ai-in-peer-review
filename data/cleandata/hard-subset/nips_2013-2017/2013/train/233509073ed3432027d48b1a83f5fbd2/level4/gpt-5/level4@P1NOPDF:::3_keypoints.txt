- Proposes a systematic approach for structure learning of SEMs when data are only locally i.i.d. (time-dependent).  
- Paper is well written, easy to follow, and tackles an important, often-overlooked problem.  
- Proposed techniques are simple and, based on PC-method experiments, appear effective for time-dependent data.  
- Experimental generality claim (“any other standard… would perform similarly”) is too strong — either rephrase to emphasize focus on PC and conjecture for others, or provide detailed justification for broader claims.  
- Scope limitation: extending the approach to Bayesian networks and other models may be nontrivial (maintaining sufficient statistics); disclose this earlier and frame the paper explicitly around SEMs.  
- Overall: straightforward formulation, easy to implement, promising experimental results despite focus on PC; reviewer has read the authors’ feedback.
