- Proposes a systematic approach to structure learning for SEMs when data are only locally i.i.d. (time-varying generative model).  
- Well written and easy to follow.  
- Addresses an important, underexplored problem: learning from time-dependent data.  
- Techniques are simple and, based on experiments with the PC algorithm, appear effective.  
- Claim that “any other standard graphical model structure learning algorithms would perform similarly” is too strong — suggest rephrasing as a conjecture or providing justification.  
- Extending the approach to Bayesian networks may be nontrivial due to difficulties maintaining sufficient statistics; recommend stating the SEM focus and limited scope up front.  
- Experiments focus only on adapting and comparing the PC method, so broader claims about other methods should be tempered.  
- Overall assessment: solid, promising contribution with clear formulation and implementable method.
