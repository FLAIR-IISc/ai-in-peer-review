- Proposes a systematic method to adapt structure learning for SEMs to locally i.i.d. (time-dependent) data.  
- Paper is well-written and easy to follow.  
- Addresses an important, often-neglected problem: learning from time-dependent data.  
- Proposed techniques are straightforward and effective, demonstrated via experiments with the PC method.  
- Experimental claim that “any other standard graphical model structure learning algorithms would perform similarly” is too broad and should be rephrased or justified.  
- Recommend clarifying that experiments focus on PC and, if asserting generality, provide reasons why other methods would behave similarly.  
- Conclusions note challenges in extending the approach to Bayesian networks (maintaining sufficient statistics); the paper should state its SEM focus up front and acknowledge nontrivial extension issues.  
- Overall assessment: simple formulation, implementable, and promising results despite being primarily a PC-method comparison; reviewer has read the authors’ feedback.
