- Addresses an important gap: proposes a method for SEM structure learning under locally i.i.d. (time-varying) data.  
- Well-motivated: standard structure-learning algorithms often ignore time-dependence.  
- Clear presentation and technically sound methodology.  
- Experimental validation uses the PC algorithm, showing the adapted method handles time-dependent data effectively.  
- Core idea is simple, promising, and yields robust results for the PC method.  
- Minor issue 1: Claim that "any other standard graphical model structure learning algorithms would perform similarly" is too strong and unsupported.  
- Suggestion for issue 1: Rephrase to limit claims to PC comparisons or provide justification/evidence for generalization to other methods (e.g., GES, MMPC).  
- Minor issue 2: Extension to Bayesian networks may be non-trivial due to sufficient-statistics challenges, which risks overstating generality.  
- Suggestion for issue 2: Explicitly frame the work as targeting SEMs (not all graphical models) while acknowledging possible but nontrivial extensions.  
- Overall assessment: well-written, meaningful contribution with compelling experiments; requested revisions are minor and reasonable.
