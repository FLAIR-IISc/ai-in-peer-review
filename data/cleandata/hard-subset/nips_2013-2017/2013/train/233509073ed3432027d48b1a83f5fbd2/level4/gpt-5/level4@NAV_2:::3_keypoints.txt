- Introduces a coherent method for structure learning in SEMs when data are only locally i.i.d. (model can switch over time).  
- Manuscript is clear, easy to follow, and tackles the important, often-overlooked problem of learning from time-dependent data.  
- Temporal-variation mechanisms are straightforward and appear effective based on experiments with the PC algorithm.  
- Experimental claim that “any other standard graphical model structure learning algorithms would perform similarly” is too strong; either temper it or provide detailed justification.  
- Extending the approach to Bayesian networks may be nontrivial due to maintaining sufficient statistics; paper should state its SEM scope earlier and caution that extensions aren’t immediate.  
- Overall assessment: well-written, easy to implement, and empirically promising, though evaluation is limited to PC.  
- Reviewer acknowledges having read the authors’ feedback.
