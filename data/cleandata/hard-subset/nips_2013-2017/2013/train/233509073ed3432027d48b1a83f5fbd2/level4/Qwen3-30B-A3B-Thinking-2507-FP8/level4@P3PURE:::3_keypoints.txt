- Presents a systematic approach for SEM structure learning with locally i.i.d. (time-dependent) data.  
- Paper is well-written, clear, and tackles an important, overlooked problem.  
- Proposed techniques are simple, implementable, and shown effective via experiments using the PC method.  
- Claim that “any other standard graphical model structure learning algorithms would perform similarly” is overly strong and should be rephrased or justified.  
- Recommend explicitly stating experiments focus on the PC method and that similarity to other methods is speculative unless justified.  
- Note limitation: extending the approach to Bayesian networks may be nontrivial due to maintaining sufficient statistics; this should be acknowledged upfront.  
- Suggest framing the contribution as specific to SEMs and noting that extensions to other models require additional work.  
- Overall assessment: promising results but mainly PC-focused; address scope clarification and overgeneralization to strengthen the paper.  
- Reviewer has read and considered the authors’ feedback.
