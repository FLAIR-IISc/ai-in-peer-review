- Introduces a systematic approach for structure learning of SEMs when data are only locally i.i.d. (time-dependent / from distinct underlying models).
- Paper is well-written, clear, and easy to follow.
- Addresses the important and often-overlooked problem of learning from time-dependent data.
- Proposed extensions are straightforward and effective, demonstrated empirically using the PC method.
- Claim that "any other standard graphical model structure learning algorithms would perform similarly" is overly strong and should be rephrased or justified.
- Recommend explicitly state experiments focus on PC and either speculate cautiously about other methods or provide detailed reasoning for broader applicability.
- Limitation: extending the approach to Bayesian networks may be nontrivial due to maintaining sufficient statistics; suggest disclosing this earlier and framing the work around SEMs.
- Overall assessment: promising, simple-to-implement formulation with encouraging experimental results, though experiments are limited to PC; reviewer acknowledges authors' responses.
