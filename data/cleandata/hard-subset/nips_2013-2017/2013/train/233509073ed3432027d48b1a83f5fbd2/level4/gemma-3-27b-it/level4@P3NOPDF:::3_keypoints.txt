- Proposes a method for SEM structure learning in non-stationary environments with local i.i.d., extending established methods (demonstrated with the PC algorithm) to time-dependent data.  
- Paper is well-written, clear, practically relevant, and straightforward to implement.  
- The claim that “any other standard graphical model structure learning algorithm would perform similarly” is overly strong.  
- Authors should either justify why similar performance is expected across algorithms (e.g., common reliance on conditional independence tests) or restrict/rephrase the claim to the PC algorithm and speculate about generalizability.  
- The noted difficulty in extending the approach to Bayesian network structure learning (maintaining sufficient statistics) is important and should be acknowledged earlier; frame the work explicitly as addressing SEMs and note nontrivial extensions to other graphical models.  
- Experimental results are encouraging but focused on PC; addressing the above suggestions would enhance clarity and impact.
