- Presents a systematic framework for structure learning in SEMs from locally i.i.d. (time-dependent, potentially non-stationary) data.  
- Tackles the important and under-studied problem of learning when the generative model changes over time.  
- Paper is well-written and easy to follow.  
- Proposed adaptation techniques are simple and appear effective in experiments.  
- Empirical validation is demonstrated using the PC algorithm.  
- Suggestion 1: The claim that "any other standard graphical model structure learning algorithms would perform similarly" is too strong; either temper it or provide justification/experiments.  
- Suggestion 2: Note earlier that extending the approach to Bayesian networks is non-trivial due to maintaining sufficient statistics; frame contributions more explicitly as for SEMs.  
- Overall summary: promising, straightforward-to-implement formulation and encouraging PC-focused results.  
- Reviewer has read the authors' feedback.
