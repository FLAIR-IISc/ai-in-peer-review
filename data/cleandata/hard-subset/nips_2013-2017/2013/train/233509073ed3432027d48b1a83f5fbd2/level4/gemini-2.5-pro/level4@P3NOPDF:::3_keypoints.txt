- Paper proposes a systematic method for SEM structure learning under locally i.i.d. (time-dependent) data, addressing changing-generating-model scenarios.
- The manuscript is well-written and easy to follow.
- Proposed extensions to structure learning are simple to implement and appear effective in experiments using the PC algorithm.
- Overall contribution is strong, straightforward to understand, and yields promising results.
- Claim that “any other standard graphical model structure learning algorithms would perform similarly” is too strong; either rephrase as a proof-of-concept or provide theoretical/empirical justification for generalization.
- Clarify scope earlier: explicitly frame the method as tailored to SEMs and note that extending to Bayesian networks/other model classes is non-trivial (e.g., due to maintaining sufficient statistics).
- Experiments focus on a single base algorithm but convincingly demonstrate the method’s value; addressing the above suggestions would improve clarity and impact.
- Reviewer acknowledges having read the authors’ response.
