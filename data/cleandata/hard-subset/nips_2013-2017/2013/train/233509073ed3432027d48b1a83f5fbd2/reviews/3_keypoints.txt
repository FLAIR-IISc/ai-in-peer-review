Paper proposes a systematic method to enable SEM structure learning with locally i.i.d. (time-varying) data.  
Well written, easy to follow, and addresses the important, under-studied problem of learning from time-dependent data.  
Techniques to handle time-dependent data are simple and experimentally effective when applied to the PC algorithm.  
The claim that "other standard graphical model structure learning algorithms would perform similarly" is too strong—rephrase or provide justification and clarify that experiments focus on PC.  
Extending the approach to Bayesian networks may be nontrivial due to difficulties maintaining sufficient statistics; the paper should state its SEM-specific scope up front and note that extensions may not be straightforward.  
Experimental results are promising but limited to a PC-method comparison.  
Reviewer acknowledges having read the authors' feedback.
