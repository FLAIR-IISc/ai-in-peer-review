- Theoretical results are sound, with a valuable dual characterization of overlapped and latent Schatten norms.  
- Duality between overlapped and latent Schatten norms is the paper’s core and novel contribution.  
- Improved theoretical guarantees advance convex regularization for tensor decomposition.  
- Experimental evaluation is superficial and lacks sufficient detail.  
- The selection process for Tucker ranks (r₁,…,r_k) in experiments is not explained.  
- Implementation and effectiveness of group regularization (e.g., l_{1,∞} norm) are unclear when tensors are low-rank across all modes.  
- The paper omits comparisons to non-convex tensor decomposition methods commonly used in practice.  
- Paper is well-organized and clearly written.  
- Underdeveloped empirical analysis limits assessment of practical utility and overall impact.
