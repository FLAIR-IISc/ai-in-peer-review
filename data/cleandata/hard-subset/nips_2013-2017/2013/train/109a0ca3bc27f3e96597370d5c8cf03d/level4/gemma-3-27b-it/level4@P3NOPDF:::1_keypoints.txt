- Paper derives theoretical MSE bounds for the latent norm in tensor factorization and shows improvements over existing analyses for the overlap norm.
- Theoretical results are supported by numerical simulations and a generalization/duality theorem linking the norms.
- Paper is generally well-written and the technical content appears sound (supplementary proofs not fully verified by reviewer).
- Primary concern: narrow, largely theoretical focus with weak connection or evaluation on core machine learning problems or applications.
- Contributions feel incremental: mainly theoretical justification for an already empirically promising method rather than a major technical advance.
- Missing lower bounds accompanying the presented upper bounds, making it hard to judge tightness and true algorithmic superiority.
- Abstract claim of proposing a “new class of structured Schatten norms” is misleading since the paper mainly analyzes existing approaches and the generalization is not used to develop new algorithms.
- Figure 1 is placed prematurely and needs Section 2 background to be understood.
- Several minor presentation and editorial issues (specific typos and phrasing errors cited).
- Overall recommendation: refine the paper to strengthen ML relevance or expand theoretical contributions to justify inclusion in NIPS.
