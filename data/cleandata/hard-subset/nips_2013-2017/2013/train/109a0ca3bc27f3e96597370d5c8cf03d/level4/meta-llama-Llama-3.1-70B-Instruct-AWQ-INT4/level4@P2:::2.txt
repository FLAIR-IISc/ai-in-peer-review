**Summary of the Main Ideas and Relation to Previous Work**

The paper "Convex Tensor Decomposition via Structured Schatten Norm Regularization" proposes a new approach for tensor decomposition using structured Schatten norms. The authors analyze the performance of the latent approach for tensor decomposition, which was empirically found to perform better than the overlapped approach in some settings. They show that the latent approach performs as well as knowing the mode with the smallest rank, and provide a novel duality result for structured Schatten norms.

The paper relates to previous work on tensor decomposition, including the use of Schatten norms for low-rank inducing regularization. The authors also connect their work to the broader studies on structured sparsity. The paper builds upon the work of [26], which analyzed the performance of the overlapped approach for tensor decomposition.
