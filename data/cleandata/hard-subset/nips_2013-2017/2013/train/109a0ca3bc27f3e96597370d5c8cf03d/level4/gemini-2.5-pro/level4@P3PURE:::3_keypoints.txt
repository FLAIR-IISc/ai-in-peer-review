- Strong theoretical contribution: provides new performance guarantees for convex tensor decomposition using the latent Schatten norm, improving on overlapped Schatten-norm results.  
- Novel dual characterization of overlapped and latent Schatten norms, of independent interest.  
- Experimental evaluation is too brief and lacks sufficient detail.  
- Clarify how Tucker ranks (r1,...,rk) were chosen for experiments.  
- Report how group regularization (e.g., l_{1,∞}) performs when the tensor is simultaneously low-rank in all modes.  
- Compare performance against established non-convex tensor decomposition methods.  
- Paper is well-organized and clearly written; theoretical arguments are easy to follow.  
- Significant contribution to a fundamental problem, but overall impact limited by the weak experimental section.
