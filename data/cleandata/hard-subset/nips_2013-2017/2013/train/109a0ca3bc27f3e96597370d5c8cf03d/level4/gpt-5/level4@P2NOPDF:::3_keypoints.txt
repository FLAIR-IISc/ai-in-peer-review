- Derives theoretical performance bounds for convex tensor decomposition with latent Schatten-norm regularization, improving on overlapped-norm results.
- Solid theoretical contributions; dual characterization connecting overlapped and latent Schatten norms is especially interesting.
- Experimental section is brief and lacks sufficient detail.
- Unclear how Tucker ranks (r1,…,rk) were selected in the experiments.
- Unclear how group regularization (e.g., l1,∞ norm) behaves when the tensor is simultaneously low-rank across all modes.
- Missing empirical comparison with non-convex tensor decomposition approaches.
- Paper is well organized and easy to follow (clarity).
- Main original contribution is the duality between overlapped and latent Schatten norms, of independent interest.
- Advances theoretical guarantees for tensor decomposition and is significant, but requires a more comprehensive experimental evaluation.
