This paper provides theoretical limits on the performance of convex tensor decomposition algorithms regularized by the latent Schatten norm. It improves upon the known result for regularization using the overlapped Schatten norm.

Quality: This paper achieves theoretical results for tensor decomposition using the latent Schatten norm. In particular, the duality characterization of the overlapped and latent shadows is interesting. However, experiments are briefly considered and not discussed in detail. For example, how were the Tucker ranks (r1,...rk) of the examples chosen? How does group regularization like l_1,\infty work when the tensor is low rank in all modes together? How does this method perform compared to non-convex approaches?

Clarity: This paper is well-organized and clear to understand.

Originality: The main original result in the paper is the duality between the overlapped and latent shadow norms. This result appears to be independently interesting.

Importance: Tensor decomposition is an important problem in many applications. Since this paper improves upon existing results for this problem, I believe it is important work.
