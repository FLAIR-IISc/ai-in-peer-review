- Paper analyzes convex norm-regularized optimization for approximately low-rank tensor factorization, focusing on the “latent” norm and comparing it to the “overlap” norm.  
- Authors derive theoretical mean-squared error bounds for the latent norm under several noise models, showing favorable performance relative to analyses of the overlap norm.  
- Theoretical results are validated with numerical simulations that support prior empirical findings.  
- The paper introduces a generalization of both norms and proves a duality theorem for the generalized norm.  
- Paper is generally well-written and appears technically sound (proofs not checked in detail).  
- Scope is narrow, highly technical, and only peripherally connected to machine learning, limiting potential NIPS impact.  
- Contributions are incremental: mainly additional theoretical support for an existing approach [25] rather than novel methodology.  
- Techniques used do not appear to be novel.  
- Missing lower bounds to complement the presented upper bounds, which weakens algorithm comparisons.  
- Abstract’s claim of proposing a “new class” of structured Schatten norms is misleading given the paper mainly analyzes existing approaches.  
- Figure 1 is introduced too early and requires later background to interpret.  
- Several minor typos/phrasing issues identified (lines 38, 40, 395).
