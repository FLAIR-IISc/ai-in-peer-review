- Establishes theoretical performance bounds for convex tensor decomposition regularized by the latent Schatten norm, improving over prior overlapped Schatten norm results.  
- Provides a noteworthy dual characterization relating the overlapped and latent Schatten norms.  
- Delivers strengthened theoretical guarantees for tensor decomposition under the latent Schatten norm.  
- Experimental evaluation is brief and under-detailed.  
- Missing experimental details: how Tucker ranks (r1,...,rk) were chosen.  
- Missing experimental analysis: behavior of group regularization (e.g., l1,∞) when the tensor is low-rank in all modes.  
- Missing baseline comparisons: no comparison against non-convex tensor decomposition approaches.  
- Paper is well structured and easy to follow.  
- Originality: the duality result is the primary novel contribution and of independent interest.  
- Significance: advances an important problem and meaningfully strengthens existing results.
