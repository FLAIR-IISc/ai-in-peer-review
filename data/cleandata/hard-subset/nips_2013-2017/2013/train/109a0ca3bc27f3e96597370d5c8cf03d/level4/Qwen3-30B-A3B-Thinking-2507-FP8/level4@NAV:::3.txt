

Quality: The paper establishes theoretical performance bounds for a convex tensor decomposition algorithm regularized by the latent Schatten norm, improving upon prior results for overlapped Schatten norm regularization. While the dual characterization of the overlapped and latent Schatten norms is a compelling contribution, the experimental section is inadequately developed. Key details are missing: how the Tucker ranks (r₁, ..., rₖ) were selected for examples, how l_{1,∞} group regularization functions when the tensor is simultaneously low-rank across all modes, and how the method compares to non-convex approaches.

Clarity: The paper is well-organized and clearly written.

Originality: The central novel contribution is the duality relation between the overlapped and latent Schatten norms, which holds independent theoretical interest.

Significance: Tensor decomposition is a fundamental problem with broad applications, and this work provides meaningful theoretical improvements. The results are significant, though the brief experimental analysis limits the demonstration of practical impact.
