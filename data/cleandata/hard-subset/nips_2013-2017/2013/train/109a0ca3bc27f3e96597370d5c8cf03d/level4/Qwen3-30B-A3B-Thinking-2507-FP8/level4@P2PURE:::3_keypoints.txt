- Theoretical contribution: establishes improved bounds for convex tensor decomposition using the latent Schatten norm versus prior overlapped Schatten results.  
- Dual characterization: provides a notable duality between the overlapped and latent Schatten norms.  
- Experimental shortcomings: experiments are brief and under-detailed; critical questions remain unanswered (how Tucker ranks r₁…rₖ were chosen, behavior of group regularization like ℓ₁,∞ when tensor is low-rank across all modes, and comparison to non-convex methods).  
- Clarity: paper is well-organized and clearly written.  
- Originality: the duality result is the central novel contribution and appears independently interesting.  
- Significance: advances theoretical guarantees for an important tensor decomposition problem, but impact is weakened by insufficient experimental evaluation.
