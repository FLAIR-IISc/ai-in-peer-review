- Provides theoretical performance bounds for a convex tensor decomposition algorithm regularized by the latent Schatten norm, improving on overlapped Schatten norm results.
- Derives the dual characterization between overlapped and latent Schatten norms, an original and independently interesting contribution.
- Theoretical results are significant for the important, widely applicable problem of tensor decomposition.
- Paper is well-organized and clearly presented.
- Experimental section is brief and underdeveloped.
- Missing details on how Tucker ranks (r1,...,rk) were chosen for experiments.
- Should evaluate group regularization (e.g., l_{1,∞}) when the tensor is low-rank in all modes.
- Needs comparison to non-convex tensor decomposition approaches.
- Overall a notable theoretical contribution but weakened by limited empirical validation.
