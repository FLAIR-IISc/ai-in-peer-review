- Provides theoretical performance bounds for a convex tensor decomposition algorithm regularized by the latent Schatten norm.  
- Improves on existing results for overlapped Schatten norm regularization.  
- Duality characterization between overlapped and latent Schatten norms is a notable, independently interesting contribution.  
- Theoretical results are technically sound and offer strong guarantees for the latent Schatten approach.  
- Experimental validation is present but limited in scope and detail.  
- Unclear how Tucker ranks (r1,…,rk) were selected for test examples.  
- Missing comparative analysis with non-convex tensor decomposition methods.  
- Further evaluation of group regularization (e.g., l1,∞) when tensors are simultaneously low-rank across modes is needed.  
- Paper is well-organized and clearly written, aiding comprehension.  
- Originality centers on the novel duality result with broader significance beyond this application.  
- Significance: advances state-of-the-art in an important, widely applicable problem, though stronger experiments would increase impact.  
- Pros: strong theory, novel duality, clear presentation, addresses an important problem.  
- Cons: limited experiments, no non-convex comparisons, insufficient exploration of related regularizers.
