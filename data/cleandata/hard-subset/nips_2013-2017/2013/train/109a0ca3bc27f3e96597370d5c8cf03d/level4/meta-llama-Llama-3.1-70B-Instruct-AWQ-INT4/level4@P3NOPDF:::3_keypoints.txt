- Provides theoretical bounds for convex tensor decomposition regularized by the latent Schatten norm, improving on overlapped-norm results.  
- Presents a dual characterization of the overlapped and latent Schatten norms, a novel and independently interesting result.  
- Experimental section is brief and underdeveloped, reducing the paper’s empirical support.  
- Missing details on how Tucker ranks (r1,...,rk) were chosen for experiments.  
- Lacks discussion/evaluation of group regularization (e.g., l1,∞) when the tensor is low-rank in all modes.  
- No comparison with non-convex tensor decomposition approaches, which would strengthen the evaluation.  
- Paper is well-organized and clear to readers familiar with the topic.  
- Overall significant contribution to tensor decomposition theory, though empirical work needs expansion to increase impact.
