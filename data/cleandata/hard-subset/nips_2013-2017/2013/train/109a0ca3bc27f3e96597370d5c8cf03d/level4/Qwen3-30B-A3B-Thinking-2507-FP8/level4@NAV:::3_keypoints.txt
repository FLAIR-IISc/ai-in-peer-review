- Establishes theoretical performance bounds for a convex tensor decomposition algorithm using the latent Schatten norm, improving on prior overlapped Schatten norm results.  
- Provides a compelling dual characterization between overlapped and latent Schatten norms.  
- Experimental section is underdeveloped and lacks important details.  
- Missing explanation of how Tucker ranks (r₁,…,r_k) were chosen in experiments.  
- Fails to clarify how the l_{1,∞} group regularization operates when the tensor is simultaneously low-rank across all modes.  
- No comparison to non-convex tensor decomposition methods.  
- Paper is well-organized and clearly written.  
- The duality relation is the central novel contribution with independent theoretical interest.  
- Results are significant for tensor decomposition theory, but limited experiments weaken demonstration of practical impact.
