- Presents theoretical performance bounds for convex tensor decomposition using latent Schatten norm, improving on overlapped Schatten norm results.
- Main theoretical contribution: derivation of results for tensor decomposition under latent Schatten norm regularization.
- Highlights dual characterization of both overlapped and latent Schatten norms.
- Duality between overlapped and latent Schatten norms is an original and independently valuable result.
- Experimental section is brief and insufficiently detailed.
- Unclear how Tucker ranks (r1,...,rk) were chosen for the examples.
- Unaddressed question: effectiveness of group regularization (l1,∞) when the tensor is low-rank across all modes.
- Lacks a comparative analysis against non-convex tensor decomposition methods.
- Manuscript is well-structured and clear, aiding comprehension.
- Work is significant given tensor decomposition’s broad applicability, but would benefit from more comprehensive experiments.
