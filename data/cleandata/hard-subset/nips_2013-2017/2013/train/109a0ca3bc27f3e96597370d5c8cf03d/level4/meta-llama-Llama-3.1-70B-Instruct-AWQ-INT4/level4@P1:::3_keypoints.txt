- Provides theoretical bounds for convex tensor decomposition using the latent Schatten norm, improving on overlapped Schatten norm results.  
- Dual characterization of the overlapped and latent Schatten norms is a notable and independent contribution.  
- Quality: strong theoretical results, but experiments are brief and lack methodological details.  
- Experiments: authors do not specify how Tucker ranks (r1,...,rk) were chosen.  
- Experiments: lack discussion of group regularization (e.g., l1,∞) when tensors are low-rank in all modes.  
- Experiments: no comparison with non-convex tensor decomposition approaches.  
- Clarity: paper is well-organized and easy to follow.  
- Originality: main novel insight is the duality between overlapped and latent Schatten norms.  
- Significance: advances an important tensor decomposition problem with meaningful theoretical improvements.  
- Against acceptance: insufficient experimental detail and missing comparisons to existing methods and real-world implications.  
- Recommendation: accept, but require more detailed experiments and broader comparisons to address concerns.
