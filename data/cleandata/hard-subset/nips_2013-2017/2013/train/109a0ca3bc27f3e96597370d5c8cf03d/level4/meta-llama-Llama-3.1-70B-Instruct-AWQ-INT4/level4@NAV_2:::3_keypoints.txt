- Provides theoretical performance bounds for a convex tensor decomposition algorithm regularized by the latent Schatten norm, improving on overlapped Schatten results.  
- Dual characterization of overlapped and latent Schatten norms is a novel and interesting contribution.  
- The paper’s theoretical results are significant for tensor decomposition and improve existing results.  
- The manuscript is well organized and clear.  
- Main originality stems from the duality result between overlapped and latent Schatten norms.  
- Experiments are brief and underdeveloped, lacking methodological detail.  
- Unclear how Tucker ranks (r1,...,rk) for examples were chosen.  
- Missing analysis of group regularization (e.g., l1,∞) behavior when the tensor is low-rank in all modes.  
- No comparison to non-convex tensor decomposition methods; empirical performance vs alternatives is absent.  
- Paper would benefit from more discussion on the implications and practical impact of the theoretical results.  
- Arguments for acceptance: novel theory, independent-interest dual characterization, and improved bounds.  
- Arguments against acceptance: insufficient experimental detail, missing comparisons, and limited discussion of implications.
