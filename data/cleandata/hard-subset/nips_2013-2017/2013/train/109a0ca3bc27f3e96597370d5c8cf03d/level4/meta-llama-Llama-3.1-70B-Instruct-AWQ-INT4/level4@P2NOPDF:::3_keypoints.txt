- Provides theoretical performance bounds for convex tensor decomposition regularized by the latent Schatten norm.  
- Improves on existing results that use the overlapped Schatten norm for regularization.  
- Presents a dual characterization between the overlapped and latent Schatten norms (main original contribution).  
- Theoretical results are intriguing and the paper is well-written and clear.  
- Experimental section is brief and underdeveloped.  
- Missing details on how Tucker ranks (r1,...,rk) were chosen in experiments.  
- Does not evaluate group regularization methods (e.g., l1,∞) when the tensor is low-rank in all modes.  
- Lacks comparison with non-convex tensor decomposition approaches, leaving performance questions unanswered.
