- Presents theoretical performance bounds for convex tensor decomposition using latent Schatten norm, improving over overlapped Schatten norm results.
- Dual characterization of overlapped and latent Schatten norms is a key, independently interesting contribution.
- Theoretical contributions to tensor decomposition via the latent Schatten norm are notable.
- Experimental section is concise and underdeveloped.
- Lacks explanation of how Tucker ranks (r1, ..., rk) were selected in examples.
- Needs discussion on the effectiveness of group regularization (e.g., l_{1,∞}) when the tensor is low-rank in all modes.
- Would benefit from comparisons with non-convex tensor decomposition approaches.
- Paper is well organized and clearly written.
- Overall significant: improves existing results and is valuable to the tensor decomposition field despite brief experiments.
