This paper derives theoretical performance bounds for a convex tensor decomposition method regularized by the latent Schatten norm, improving upon results previously known for overlapped Schatten norm regularization.

Quality: The paper presents solid theoretical results for tensor decomposition using the latent Schatten norm. The dual characterization of the overlapped and latent Schatten norms is particularly interesting. However, the experimental evaluation is too brief and lacks detail. For example: how were the Tucker ranks (r1, ..., rk) chosen in the experiments? How does group regularization such as l_1,∞ behave when the tensor is simultaneously low-rank in all modes? How does the proposed method compare to non-convex approaches?

Clarity: The paper is well organized and easy to follow.

Originality: The main original contribution is the demonstrated duality between the overlapped and latent Schatten norms, which appears to be of independent interest.

Significance: Tensor decomposition is an important problem in many applications, and this paper improves upon existing results for it. The theoretical contributions are interesting and the writing is clear. However, the experiments are presented only briefly toward the end of the paper.
