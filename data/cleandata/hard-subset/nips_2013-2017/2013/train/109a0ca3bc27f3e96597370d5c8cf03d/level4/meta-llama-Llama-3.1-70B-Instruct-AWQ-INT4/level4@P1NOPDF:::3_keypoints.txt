- Main contribution: provides theoretical bounds for convex tensor decomposition regularized by the latent Schatten norm, improving on results for overlapped Schatten norm.  
- Novelty: dual characterization of overlapped and latent Schatten norms is an interesting, original result.  
- Quality (theory): strong theoretical results; primary strength of the paper.  
- Clarity: paper is well-organized and easy to follow.  
- Experiments: experimental section is brief and underdeveloped.  
- Missing experimental details: authors do not explain how Tucker ranks (r1,...,rk) were chosen.  
- Missing analysis: need discussion of group regularization (e.g., l_{1,∞}) when tensor is low-rank in all modes.  
- Missing comparison: should compare the proposed convex method with non-convex approaches to assess relative performance.  
- Significance: relevant and potentially impactful for tensor decomposition, but greater experimental depth would increase impact.
