

Quality: The paper establishes theoretical bounds for convex tensor decomposition regularized by the latent Schatten norm, improving upon prior results for overlapped Schatten norm regularization. While the dual characterization of the overlapped and latent Schatten norms is a notable technical contribution, the experimental section lacks sufficient detail. Specifically: (1) The selection criteria for Tucker ranks (r₁, ..., rₖ) in the examples are not explained; (2) The behavior of group regularization (e.g., ℓ₁,∞) when the tensor is simultaneously low-rank across all modes remains unclear; (3) A comparative analysis against non-convex approaches is absent.  

Clarity: The paper is well-organized and clearly written.  

Originality: The core original contribution is the duality relationship between the overlapped and latent Schatten norms, which appears to hold independent interest.  

Significance: Tensor decomposition is a fundamental problem with broad applications. This work advances the theoretical understanding of convex regularization for tensor decomposition by providing improved bounds, making it a meaningful contribution to the field. The theoretical results are compelling, the paper is well-written, and the significance is clear despite the limited experimental evaluation.
