- Provides theoretical performance bounds for convex tensor decomposition using the latent Schatten norm, improving on overlapped Schatten norm results.
- Dual characterization of overlapped and latent Schatten norms is a notable, independently interesting contribution.
- Experimental evaluation is brief and lacks sufficient detail.
- Method for selecting Tucker ranks (r1,…,rk) in experiments is not explained.
- Does not discuss behavior of group-regularization methods (e.g., l1/l∞) when the tensor is simultaneously low-rank across all modes.
- Lacks comparison to non-convex tensor decomposition approaches.
- Paper is well-organized and clearly written.
- Results are significant given tensor decomposition’s broad applications, but limited experiments reduce practical impact.
