- Provides theoretical performance guarantees for convex tensor decomposition with latent Schatten norm regularization, improving bounds over overlapped Schatten norm methods.
- Theoretical contributions are solid; the dual relationship between latent and overlapped Schatten norms is a key, noteworthy finding.
- Experimental validation is underdeveloped and lacks sufficient detail.
- Authors do not explain Tucker rank (r1,...,rk) selection in experiments or how l1,∞ group regularization operates when tensors are low-rank in all modes.
- No performance benchmark against prominent non-convex methods.
- Manuscript is well-structured and clearly written.
- Originality lies mainly in the duality result, which has independent theoretical interest.
- Significance: advances theoretical understanding of an important problem, but impact is limited by the brief/inconclusive experimental section.
