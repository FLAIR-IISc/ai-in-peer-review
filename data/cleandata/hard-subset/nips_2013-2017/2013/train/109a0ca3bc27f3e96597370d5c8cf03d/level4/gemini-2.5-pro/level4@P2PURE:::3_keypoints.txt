- Provides theoretical performance bounds for a convex tensor decomposition algorithm using latent Schatten norm regularization.  
- Improves analysis over existing methods based on the overlapped Schatten norm.  
- Dual characterization of overlapped and latent Schatten norms is a particularly interesting and original contribution.  
- Theoretical results are a significant advance for convex tensor decomposition methods.  
- Paper is well-organized and clearly written.  
- Experimental evaluation is brief and lacks sufficient detail.  
- Experiments do not specify how Tucker ranks (r1,...,rk) were chosen.  
- Unclear how a group regularization like l1,∞ performs when the tensor is low-rank across all modes.  
- No performance comparison against non-convex tensor decomposition methods, limiting empirical assessment.
