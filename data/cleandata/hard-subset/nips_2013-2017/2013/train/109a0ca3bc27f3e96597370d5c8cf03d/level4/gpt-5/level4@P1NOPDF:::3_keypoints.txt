- Derives theoretical performance bounds for convex tensor decomposition regularized by the latent Schatten norm, improving prior overlapped Schatten-norm results.
- Dual characterization connecting overlapped and latent Schatten norms is a key, novel contribution.
- Theoretical results are solid and compelling.
- Paper is well organized and easy to follow.
- Experimental evaluation is brief and under-elaborated.
- Experiments lack detail on how Tucker ranks (r1,...,rk) were chosen.
- No analysis of how l1,∞ group regularization behaves when the tensor is low-rank across all modes.
- Missing comparison to non-convex tensor decomposition approaches.
- Work is significant for tensor decomposition but the weak experiments reduce practical impact.
