- Provides theoretical bounds for convex tensor decomposition using the latent Schatten norm, improving on overlapped Schatten norm results.  
- Derives the dual characterization of the overlapped and latent Schatten norms, a main original and independently interesting result.  
- Theoretical contributions are significant for tensor decomposition problems encountered in many applications.  
- Paper is well-organized and clearly written, accessible to readers familiar with the topic.  
- Experimental section is brief and lacks sufficient elaboration.  
- The process for selecting Tucker ranks (r1,...,rk) in experiments is not explained.  
- Does not discuss how group regularization (e.g., l1,∞) performs when the tensor is low-rank in all modes.  
- No comparison provided with non-convex tensor decomposition approaches.  
- Arguments for acceptance: novel theoretical results, significant duality finding, and clear presentation.  
- Arguments against acceptance: insufficient experimental detail, missing rank-selection discussion, and lack of non-convex baseline comparison.  
- Overall recommendation: accept — contributions outweigh limitations.
