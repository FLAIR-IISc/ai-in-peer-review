- Introduces and analyzes new structured Schatten norms for tensors (overlapped and latent) applied to convex tensor decomposition.
- Main theoretical contribution: a novel duality between the overlapped and latent Schatten norms.
- Rigorous analysis of the latent approach, establishing consistency and denoising performance.
- Experimental results support the effectiveness of the latent approach.
- Paper is well-organized and clearly written; technical sections are accessible.
- Background and motivation are thorough and helpful.
- Experimental methodology lacks important details (e.g., choice of Tucker ranks, group regularization implementation).
- Missing comparison against non-convex tensor decomposition approaches.
- Quality: 8/10 — significant contributions and interesting theoretical results.
- Clarity: 9/10 — strong organization and readable exposition.
- Originality: 9/10 — duality result and latent analysis are novel.
- Significance: 9/10 — results likely impactful across multiple applications.
- Arguments for acceptance: significant theoretical contributions with supporting experiments.
- Arguments against acceptance: insufficient experimental details and no non-convex baselines.
