- Presents theoretical performance bounds for a convex tensor decomposition algorithm using the latent Schatten norm, improving on results for the overlapped Schatten norm.  
- Provides a noteworthy dual characterization of both the overlapped and latent Schatten norms.  
- Experimental evaluation is brief and lacks sufficient detail.  
- Does not explain the criteria for selecting Tucker ranks (r1,…,rk) in the experiments.  
- Unclear how group-regularization methods (e.g., l1/l∞ norms) behave when the tensor is simultaneously low-rank across all modes.  
- Lacks comparison of the proposed method’s performance against non-convex approaches.  
- Paper is well-organized and readily understandable.  
- The duality result is an original contribution and the work advances an important problem, but empirical validation is under-emphasized.
