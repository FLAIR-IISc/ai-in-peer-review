- Presents theoretical bounds for convex tensor decomposition using the latent Schatten norm, improving on results with the overlapped Schatten norm.  
- Dual characterization of the overlapped and latent Schatten norms is a novel and interesting contribution.  
- Theoretical results are compelling and advance the state-of-the-art for an important tensor decomposition problem.  
- Paper is well organized and clearly written.  
- Experiments are brief and under-explained (e.g., how Tucker ranks r1,…,rk were selected).  
- Unclear how group regularization (e.g., l1/l∞) behaves when the tensor is low-rank across all modes.  
- Missing comparison of the proposed convex method to non-convex approaches.  
- Overall significant and original work, but the experimental section needs more elaboration.
