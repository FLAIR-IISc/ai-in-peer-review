- Provides theoretical error bounds for a convex tensor decomposition method regularized by a latent Schatten norm, improving prior guarantees for the overlapped Schatten norm.  
- Delivers solid theoretical analysis; the dual characterization linking overlapped and latent Schatten norms is a particularly noteworthy and novel contribution.  
- The duality result appears to be of independent interest beyond this application.  
- Experimental section is brief and under-detailed, weakening empirical support.  
- Missing experimental details: how Tucker ranks (r1,…,rk) were selected in synthetic experiments.  
- Missing evaluation of group-style regularizers (e.g., ℓ1,∞) when the tensor is low-rank across all modes.  
- Lacks empirical comparison with standard non-convex tensor decomposition baselines.  
- Manuscript is clearly written and well structured, making technical contributions easy to follow.  
- Overall significance: meaningful contribution to a central problem with strong theory and exposition, but experiments should be expanded.
