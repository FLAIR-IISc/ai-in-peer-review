- Establishes improved theoretical bounds for convex tensor decomposition using the latent Schatten norm over prior overlapped Schatten norm results.
- Provides a notable technical contribution: dual characterization between overlapped and latent Schatten norms.
- Experimental section lacks explanation of how Tucker ranks (r₁,…,r_k) were chosen.
- Unclear how group regularization (e.g., ℓ₁,∞) behaves when the tensor is simultaneously low-rank across all modes.
- Missing comparative analysis against non-convex tensor decomposition approaches.
- Paper is well-organized and clearly written.
- Core originality lies in the duality relationship, which has independent interest.
- Significant contribution: advances theoretical understanding of convex regularization for tensor decomposition despite limited experiments.
