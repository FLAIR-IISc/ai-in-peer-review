- Establishes theoretical bounds for convex tensor decomposition using the latent Schatten norm, improving on prior overlapped Schatten norm results.  
- Derives notable theoretical results, with the dual characterization of overlapped and latent Schatten norms being particularly noteworthy.  
- Experimental section is brief and under-detailed—missing how Tucker ranks (r1,…,rk) were chosen.  
- Lacks analysis of group regularization (e.g., l1,∞) behavior when the tensor is low-rank across all modes.  
- Does not compare the proposed convex method’s performance against non-convex approaches.  
- Well-structured and clearly presented.  
- Contribution is significant for tensor decomposition theory, but empirical evaluation is limited.
