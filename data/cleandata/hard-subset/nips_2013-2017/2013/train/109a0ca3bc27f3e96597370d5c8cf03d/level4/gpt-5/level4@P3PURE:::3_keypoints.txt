- Presents theoretical bounds for convex tensor decomposition using the latent Schatten norm, improving on prior overlapped Schatten results.
- Dual characterization of the overlapped and latent Schatten norms is a central, broadly useful contribution.
- Delivers solid theoretical results for tensor decomposition under the proposed regularization.
- Experimental section is too brief and lacks necessary detail and depth.
- Unclear how Tucker ranks (r1,…,rk) were selected in the experiments.
- Missing analysis of group regularization behavior (e.g., l1,∞ norm) when the tensor is low-rank in all modes.
- No comparison shown with non-convex tensor decomposition approaches in practice.
- Paper is well organized and generally easy to follow.
- Work is original in its duality result and significant for advancing theoretical understanding of tensor decomposition, but empirical evaluation needs stronger comparative analysis.
