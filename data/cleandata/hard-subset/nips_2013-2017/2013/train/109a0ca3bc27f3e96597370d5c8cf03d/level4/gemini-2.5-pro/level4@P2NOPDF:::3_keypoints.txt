- Presents theoretical analysis of convex tensor decomposition regularized by the latent Schatten norm with performance bounds improving over overlapped Schatten norm results.
- Provides a novel and elegant dual characterization of the overlapped and latent Schatten norms, a significant independent contribution.
- Experimental evaluation is brief and lacks sufficient detail to assess practical implications.
- Does not specify methodology for selecting Tucker ranks in synthetic experiments, hurting reproducibility.
- Fails to compare empirically with alternative regularizers (e.g., group regularization such as the l1,∞ norm) for tensors low-rank across all modes.
- Does not benchmark performance against established non-convex tensor decomposition algorithms.
- Paper is well-organized and clearly written; theoretical arguments are logical and easy to follow.
- Work is significant and well-motivated; theoretical novelty is valuable despite limited experimental validation.
