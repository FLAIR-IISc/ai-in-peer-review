- Provides theoretical bounds for convex tensor decomposition regularized by the latent Schatten norm, improving on overlapped Schatten norm results.
- Derives a dual characterization of the overlapped and latent Schatten norms, highlighted as a noteworthy and original contribution.
- Experimental section is brief and underdeveloped, limiting empirical validation.
- Authors do not explain how Tucker ranks (r1,...,rk) were chosen in experiments.
- Should discuss performance of group regularization (e.g., l_{1,∞}) when the tensor is low-rank in all modes.
- A comparison with non-convex tensor decomposition approaches is recommended to better assess practical performance.
- Paper is well-organized and clear, accessible to readers familiar with the topic.
- Tensor decomposition is an important application area, and the theoretical improvements are significant despite the experimental limitations.
