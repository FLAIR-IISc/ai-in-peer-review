- Establishes theoretical performance bounds for convex tensor decomposition using latent Schatten norm, improving on overlapped Schatten norm results.  
- Provides a dual characterization of overlapped and latent Schatten norms, a notable and independently interesting contribution.  
- Experimental evaluation is limited and under-detailed; the selection of Tucker ranks (r1,…,rk) is not explained.  
- Unclear how group regularization methods (e.g., l1/∞ norms) behave when the tensor is simultaneously low-rank across all modes.  
- No comparison to non-convex tensor decomposition methods is provided.  
- Paper is well-structured and clearly written.  
- Originality primarily stems from the demonstrated duality between the norms.  
- Significance is high due to broad applicability and improved theoretical results, but experiments should be strengthened.
