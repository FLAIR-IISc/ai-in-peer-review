- Derives improved theoretical performance bounds for convex tensor decomposition regularized by the latent Schatten norm.  
- Provides a dual characterization of the overlapped and latent Schatten norms, an original contribution of independent interest.  
- Theoretical contributions strengthen existing results for tensor decomposition and are clearly presented.  
- Paper is well organized and easy to follow.  
- Experimental evaluation is too brief and lacks necessary detail.  
- Missing details: how Tucker ranks (r1,...,rk) were chosen in experiments.  
- Missing analysis: behavior of group regularization (e.g., l1,∞) when tensor is low-rank in all modes.  
- Missing comparison: performance versus non-convex tensor decomposition approaches.
