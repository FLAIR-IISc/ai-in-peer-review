- Establishes improved theoretical bounds for convex tensor decomposition using the latent Schatten norm, with the dual characterization as a key technical contribution.  
- Dual characterization of overlapped and latent Schatten norms is novel and provides useful insights for optimization and low-rank analysis.  
- Experimental section is underdeveloped and lacks critical methodological details.  
- No justification is provided for the choice of Tucker ranks (r₁,…,rₖ), making practical relevance unclear.  
- The paper fails to explain how group regularization (e.g., ℓ₁,∞) enforces simultaneous low-rank structure across tensor modes.  
- Missing comparisons to state-of-the-art non-convex tensor decomposition methods, limiting assessment of practical trade-offs.  
- Paper is well-structured with coherent mathematical derivations; minor notation consistency (subscript) fixes are needed.  
- The extension to latent Schatten norm constitutes a non-trivial, significant theoretical advance beyond prior work.  
- The work fills an important theoretical gap with potential methodological impact, but its contribution is primarily theoretical.  
- Overall: meaningful and sound theoretical contribution, but insufficient empirical validation weakens practical impact and prevents a strong acceptance recommendation.
