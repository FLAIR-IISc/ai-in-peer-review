**Summary of the Main Ideas**

This paper introduces a new class of structured Schatten norms for tensors, including the overlapped and latent Schatten norms, which are used for convex-optimization-based tensor decomposition. The authors analyze the performance of the latent approach and show that it performs better than the overlapped approach, especially when the underlying tensor is low-rank in a specific mode. The paper also presents a novel duality result between the overlapped and latent Schatten norms, which generalizes previous results in structured sparsity literature.
