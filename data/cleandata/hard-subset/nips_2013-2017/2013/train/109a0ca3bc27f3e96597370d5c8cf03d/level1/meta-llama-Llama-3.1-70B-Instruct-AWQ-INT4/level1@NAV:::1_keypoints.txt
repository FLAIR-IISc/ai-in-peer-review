- Presents a novel framework for structured Schatten norms for tensors, encompassing overlapped and latent Schatten norms.  
- Provides a novel duality result for structured Schatten norms.  
- Theoretically analyzes the latent approach and shows why it can outperform the overlapped approach in some settings.  
- Offers thorough analysis of the latent approach including consistency and deterministic error bounds.  
- Paper is well-written, clearly organized, and gives a concise introduction to tensor decomposition and existing approaches.  
- Technical contributions are significant and the analysis is detailed.  
- Assumes substantial background in tensor decomposition and convex optimization, which may hinder non-expert readers.  
- Lacks sufficient numerical experiments to fully demonstrate practical effectiveness of the latent approach.  
- Recommendation: Accept with minor revisions to add more experiments and more background material for accessibility.  
- Rating: 8/10.
