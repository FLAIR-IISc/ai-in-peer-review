**Summary of the Paper**

This paper proposes a novel approach to tensor decomposition using structured Schatten norms. The authors introduce two types of norms: the overlapped Schatten norm and the latent Schatten norm. They establish a duality between these two norms and show that the latent Schatten norm is more effective for tensor decomposition, especially when the underlying tensor is low-rank in a specific mode. The authors provide theoretical guarantees for the performance of the latent approach and demonstrate its superiority over the overlapped approach through numerical experiments.
