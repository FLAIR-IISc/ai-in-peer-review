- Presents a novel framework of structured Schatten norms for tensors, encompassing overlapped and latent Schatten 1-norms.  
- Proves a new duality: the dual of the overlapped Sp/q-norm is the latent Sp*/q*-norm.  
- Provides a rigorous theoretical analysis of the latent approach, including a generic consistency result.  
- Derives a tighter error bound that leverages low-rank-ness under an incoherence assumption.  
- Empirical evaluation supports theoretical predictions and shows the latent approach often outperforms the overlapped approach.  
- Complexity comparison: overlapped complexity scales with the average (sqrt) of mode-k ranks, latent scales linearly with the minimum mode-k rank.  
- Strengths: novel framework, duality result, strong theoretical analysis, and clear organization/writing.  
- Weakness: experimental evaluation is brief and lacks important details (e.g., how Tucker ranks were chosen).  
- Weakness: no comparison with non-convex tensor decomposition methods.  
- Weakness: lacks analysis of group regularization (e.g., l_{1,∞}) when tensor is low-rank in all modes.
