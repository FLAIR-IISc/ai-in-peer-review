- Studies overlapped and latent structured Schatten norms for tensors and their use in convex tensor decomposition.  
- Provides a novel duality result between the overlapped and latent Schatten norms.  
- Main contribution: theoretical bounds for convex tensor decomposition using the latent Schatten norm.  
- Shows latent approach improves on overlapped results, especially when the tensor is low-rank in only one mode.  
- Dual characterization of the overlapped and latent Schatten norms is original and of independent interest.  
- Paper is well-organized and well-written with strong theoretical analysis.  
- Experimental evaluation is brief and provides only limited evidence for the latent approach.  
- Insufficient detail on how Tucker ranks (r1,...,rk) were chosen in experiments.  
- No comparison to non-convex tensor decomposition methods.  
- Lacks deeper analysis of group regularization behavior (e.g., l_{1,∞}) when the tensor is low-rank in all modes.  
- Recommendation: accept with minor revisions to expand experiments and analyze group regularization.  
- Rating: 8/10.
