- Presents a novel duality result between overlapped and latent Schatten norms, linked to structured sparsity literature.

- Provides theoretical analysis for the latent approach: consistency statement, deterministic bound, and Gaussian-noise bound.  
- Numerical results corroborate theoretical predictions and demonstrate latent approach effectiveness.  
- Offers a unified framework for structured Schatten norms that includes both overlapped and latent norms.
