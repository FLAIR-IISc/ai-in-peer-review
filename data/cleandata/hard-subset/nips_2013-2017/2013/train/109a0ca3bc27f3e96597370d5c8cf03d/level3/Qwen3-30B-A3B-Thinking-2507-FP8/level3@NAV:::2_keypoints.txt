- Paper studies structured Schatten norms for convex tensor decomposition, comparing overlapped vs latent approaches.
- Theoretical result: latent approach error scales with minimum mode-k rank, whereas overlapped scales with average rank.
- Introduces a novel duality between structured norms and gives recovery guarantees for noisy tensor decomposition.
- Strength: technically sound theory with clear ties to structured sparsity literature.
- Strength: empirical validation matches theoretical predictions and shows latent outperforms overlapped when tensor is low-rank in a specific mode.
