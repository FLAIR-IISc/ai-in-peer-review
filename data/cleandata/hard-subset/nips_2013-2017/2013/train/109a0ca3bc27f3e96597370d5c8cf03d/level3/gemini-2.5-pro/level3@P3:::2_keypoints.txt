- Paper studies convex low-rank tensor decomposition with "structured Schatten norms," comparing overlapped vs latent norms; main theory: latent error scales with the minimum mode-rank, overlapped with the sum.
- Provides a novel duality result linking the latent and overlapped norms.
- Strength: technically significant and well-written; theoretical analysis explains prior empirical superiority of the latent method; supported by proofs and experiments.
- Weakness: lacks clarity for broader audience, has missing technical details in key proofs, and an incomplete discussion of related work and practical issues.
- Motivation: introduction should include a concrete latent-variable model perspective to make multilinear rank intuition clearer.
- Suggest adding an illustrative graphical model figure showing latent variables mapping to observed moments and linking hidden dimensions to mode-ranks.
- Emphasize the practical advantage: method remains effective when only some mode-ranks are small.
- Critical omission: proof of Lemma 5 is missing from the supplementary material and must be provided.
- Lemma 4: provide a proof sketch or full proof in the appendix rather than only citing another paper.
- Theorem 2: explicitly justify the step from inequality (12) to (13), clarifying the singleton decomposition argument.
- Theorem 2: state the permutation ambiguity of recovered components explicitly.
- Related work: add comparisons to HOSVD and variants, cite De Lathauwer et al. (2000) and Kolda & Bader (2009) for context.
- Incoherence assumption: explain it on its own terms and avoid potentially misleading direct comparisons to assumptions in other works.
- Computational complexity: include discussion of per-iteration cost and overall complexity of the convex optimization methods.
- Terminology: clarify that "mode-rank" = "multilinear rank" and briefly note other tensor rank notions (e.g., CP rank).
- Notational fixes: avoid confusing superscript/component notation (use subscripts) and use consistent symbols for mode-k rank throughout.
- Minor edits: fix abstract typo "structures Schatten" → "structured Schatten" and the missing parenthesis in the supplement.
- Recommendation: accept conditional on addressing major technical gaps (especially Lemma 5 and Theorem 2) and improving accessibility, proofs, and context.
