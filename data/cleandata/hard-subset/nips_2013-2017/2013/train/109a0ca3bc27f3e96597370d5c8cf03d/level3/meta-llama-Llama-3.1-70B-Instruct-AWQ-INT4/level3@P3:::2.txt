This paper proposes a new class of structured Schatten norms for tensors, including the overlapped and latent Schatten norms, and studies their performance in the context of convex tensor decomposition. The authors provide a novel duality result between the overlapped and latent Schatten norms, which is closely related to the results in structured sparsity literature. They also analyze the performance of the latent approach for tensor decomposition, showing that it performs better than the overlapped approach in many cases, especially when the unknown tensor is only low-rank in one mode.

The paper is well-written and technically sound, with a clear explanation of the concepts and techniques used. The authors provide a thorough analysis of the performance of the latent approach, including a consistency statement, a deterministic bound, and a bound for Gaussian noise. They also provide numerical results to confirm their theoretical predictions.
