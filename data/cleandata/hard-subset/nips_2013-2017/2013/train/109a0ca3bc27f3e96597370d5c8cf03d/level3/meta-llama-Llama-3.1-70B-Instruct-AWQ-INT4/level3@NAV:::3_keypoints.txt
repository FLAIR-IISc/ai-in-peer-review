- Proposes a novel framework for convex tensor decomposition using structured Schatten norms.
- Provides theoretical bounds for the latent Schatten norm that improve upon overlapped Schatten norm results.
- Offers a dual characterization of overlapped and latent Schatten norms, an original result of independent interest.
- Strong theoretical analysis; paper is well-organized and clearly written.
- Thorough explanation of structured Schatten norms, their properties, and relation to structured sparsity.
- Detailed analysis of the latent approach’s performance, including consistency and deterministic bounds.
- Experimental evaluation is brief and lacks sufficient detail.
- Insufficient reporting of experimental setup details, e.g., how Tucker ranks were chosen.
- No comparison to non-convex tensor decomposition approaches to contextualize strengths/weaknesses.
- Missing analysis of group regularization behavior (e.g., l_{1,∞}) when the tensor is low-rank in all modes.
- Overall contribution is important and likely of interest to researchers in tensor decomposition.
- Recommendation: accept with minor revisions to expand experiments, add non-convex comparisons, and analyze group regularization.
- Rating: 7/10; Confidence: 8/10.
