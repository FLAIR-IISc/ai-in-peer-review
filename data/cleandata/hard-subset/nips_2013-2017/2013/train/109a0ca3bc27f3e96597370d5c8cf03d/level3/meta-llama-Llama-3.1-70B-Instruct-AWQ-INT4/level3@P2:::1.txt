The paper "Convex Tensor Decomposition via Structured Schatten Norm Regularization" studies a new class of structured Schatten norms for tensors, specifically focusing on the "latent" norm and comparing it to the "overlap" norm. The authors derive theoretical mean squared error (MSE) bounds for the latent norm under several noise models, showing a favorable comparison to overlap norm analyses. They also confirm their theoretical results with numerical simulations.

The paper is generally easy to follow and appears technically sound. The results may be useful for researchers in optimization for tensor decompositions.
