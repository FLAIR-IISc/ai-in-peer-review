- Introduces a convex tensor-decomposition framework using structured Schatten norms with improved reconstruction guarantees.  
- Provides rigorous analysis of the latent approach with consistency and denoising performance guarantees.  
- Novel duality result for structured Schatten norms that is interesting beyond tensor problems.
