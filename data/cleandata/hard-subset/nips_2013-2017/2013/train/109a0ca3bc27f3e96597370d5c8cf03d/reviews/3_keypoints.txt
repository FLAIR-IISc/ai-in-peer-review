- Provides theoretical bounds for convex tensor decomposition using the latent Schatten norm, improving on results for the overlapped Schatten norm.
- Dual characterization of overlapped and latent Schatten norms is an interesting, original result of independent interest.
- Theoretical analysis is strong and the paper is well organized and clear.
- Experimental evaluation is brief and not sufficiently detailed.
- Missing experimental detail: how Tucker ranks (r1,...,rk) were chosen.
- Missing comparison to non-convex tensor decomposition approaches.
- Missing analysis of group regularization (e.g., l_{1,∞}) behavior when the tensor is low-rank in all modes.
- Overall significance: important problem and the paper meaningfully improves existing results.
