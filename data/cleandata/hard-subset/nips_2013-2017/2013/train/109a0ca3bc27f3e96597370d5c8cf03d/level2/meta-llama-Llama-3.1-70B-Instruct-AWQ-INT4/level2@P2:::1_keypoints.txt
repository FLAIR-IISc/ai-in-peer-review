- Proposes a novel framework for structured Schatten norms for tensors encompassing overlapped and latent norms.  
- Analyzes the latent approach for tensor decomposition and provides theoretical guarantees for consistency and denoising performance.  
- Introduces a novel duality result between overlapped and latent Schatten norms, a key contribution to structured sparsity.  
- Numerical simulations corroborate the theoretical predictions and demonstrate the latent approach’s effectiveness.  
- Thorough analysis of the latent approach represents a significant improvement over the overlapped approach.  
- Presentation is clear, well-organized, and uses consistent mathematical notation.  
- Originality is high due to the new framework and theoretical results.  
- Significance is high given the potential impact on tensor decomposition methods in data analysis and ML.  
- Quality is strong: rigorous analysis and clear, concise explanations.  
- Main weakness: paper assumes substantial prior knowledge of tensor decomposition and structured sparsity, limiting accessibility.  
- Suggested improvement: include more background material to help readers without prior expertise.  
- Recommendation: accept — substantial contribution with strong theory and empirical support despite accessibility concerns.
