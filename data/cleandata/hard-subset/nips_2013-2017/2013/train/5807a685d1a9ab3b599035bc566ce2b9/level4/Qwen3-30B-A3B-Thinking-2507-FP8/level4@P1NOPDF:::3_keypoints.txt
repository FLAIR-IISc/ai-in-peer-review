- Proves a tight minimax L2 estimation lower bound under local differential privacy for discrete distributions and shows randomized response is optimal.  
- Extends to density estimation: independent per-user type perturbation meets the lower bound.  
- Result is significant and relevant to the NIPS community; reviewer recommends acceptance.  
- Exposition is overly technical and should be simplified for accessibility.  
- Notation for norms is cumbersome (uses generic "ℓ"); bounds should be stated explicitly in L2.  
- Omits discussion/comparison with L1 norms and other standard DP lower bounds; adding this would strengthen context.  
- The key insight that independent per-user perturbation suffices for optimal LDP mechanisms is not explicitly highlighted and should be emphasized.  
- Section 3 appears to extend prior FOCS 2013 work, which reduces novelty and the reviewer’s enthusiasm.  
- Overall: clean, sound results; accept with minor revisions on notation and L1/L2 context.
