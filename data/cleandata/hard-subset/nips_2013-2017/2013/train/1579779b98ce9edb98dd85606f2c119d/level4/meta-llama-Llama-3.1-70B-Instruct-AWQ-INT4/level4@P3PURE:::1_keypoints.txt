- Studies MCTS in zero-sum extensive-form perfect-information games with simultaneous moves; proves convergence to an approximate Nash equilibrium under certain conditions and supports this empirically.
- Overall positive: clear presentation and a useful sufficient condition for MCTS convergence, with the main contribution being theoretical.
- Concern about novelty: the proof technique may not be sufficiently novel.
- Suggestion to analyze and compare UCB/UCT-style algorithms, since they are widely used and have prior analyses for trees.
- Theory–experiment gap: authors prove convergence for mean propagation, but experiments show current sample value propagation also performs well—request explanation.
- Conclusion: findings are interesting but the work is not technically outstanding.
