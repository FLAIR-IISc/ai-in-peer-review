- Proves MCTS converges to an approximate Nash equilibrium in zero-sum extensive-form games with perfect information and simultaneous moves, with empirical validation.  
- Overall solid and clearly presented, but the novelty of the proof technique is questionable.  
- Does not analyze common UCB algorithms; including UCB analysis/comparison would strengthen the paper.  
- Convergence proven for mean propagation, yet experiments show propagating current sample values also performs well—needs explanation.  
- Interesting study but not technically groundbreaking.
