- Proposes MCTS convergence to an approximate Nash equilibrium in zero-sum extensive-form games with perfect information and simultaneous moves, supported by empirical analysis.
- Paper is solid and clearly presented, offering a useful sufficient condition for MCTS convergence.
- Concern about limited novelty: proof techniques may not be sufficiently novel given the paper’s primarily theoretical contribution.
- Omits discussion and comparison of UCB algorithms, which are common in prior literature and could strengthen the work.
- Theory shows mean-propagation convergence, but experiments indicate current-sample-value propagation also performs well and lack a theoretical explanation.
- Overall interesting and valuable but not especially exceptional from a technical standpoint.
