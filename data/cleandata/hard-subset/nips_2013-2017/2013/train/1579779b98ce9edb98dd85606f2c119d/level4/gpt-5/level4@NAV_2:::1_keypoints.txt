- Paper shows MCTS converges to an approximate Nash equilibrium in two-player zero-sum perfect-information simultaneous-move games, with empirical results supporting theory.
- Reviewer finds the paper solid, useful, and clearly written.
- Concern about limited novelty of the proof techniques given the theoretical focus.
- UCB-based methods are not analyzed or compared, despite their prevalence and existing tree analyses.
- Theory only treats propagation of mean estimates, while experiments show propagating current sample values also works—an explanation is needed.
- Overall interesting results but not particularly exceptional from a technical perspective.
