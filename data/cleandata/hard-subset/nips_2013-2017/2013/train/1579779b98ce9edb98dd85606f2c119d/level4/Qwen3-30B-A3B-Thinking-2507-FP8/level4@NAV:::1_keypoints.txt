- Proves MCTS converges to an approximate Nash equilibrium under specific conditions, with empirical results supporting the theory.  
- Presents a clear, useful sufficient condition and solid theoretical structure, but the novelty of the proof technique is not sufficiently justified.  
- Fails to address UCB-based algorithms despite their prevalence and prior tree analyses; the paper should include UCB methods and comparative evaluations.  
- Theory covers convergence for mean propagation, yet experiments show current-sample propagation also performs well; the authors need to explain this discrepancy.  
- Technically sound and interesting results, but the contribution does not substantially advance the field beyond existing work.
