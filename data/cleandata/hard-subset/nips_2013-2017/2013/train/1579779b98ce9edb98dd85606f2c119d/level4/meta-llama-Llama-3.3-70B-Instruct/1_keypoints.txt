- Studies MCTS in zero-sum extensive-form games with perfect information and simultaneous moves, proving convergence to an approximate Nash equilibrium under specific conditions and supporting this with experiments.
- Establishes a sufficient condition for MCTS convergence and presents a clear theoretical result, though the novelty of the proof technique is questioned.
- Omits analysis and comparison of UCB/UCT algorithms; reviewer recommends including UCB given its prevalence and existing tree analyses.
- Proof addresses only mean propagation convergence, while experiments show current sample value propagation also converges; an explanation for this is requested.
- Overall an interesting paper but not technically outstanding.
