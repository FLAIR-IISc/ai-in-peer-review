- Proves MCTS converges to an approximate Nash equilibrium in zero-sum, perfect-information, simultaneous-move games, with empirical support.  
- Provides a sufficient condition for convergence; presentation is clear but the novelty of the proof technique is questionable.  
- Does not analyze widely-used UCB algorithms; adding discussion and comparison with UCB/Trees analyses would strengthen the work.  
- Theoretical convergence is shown for mean propagation, yet experiments indicate current-sample propagation also performs well—an explanation for this mismatch is requested.  
- Overall interesting and useful results, but not a major technical breakthrough.
