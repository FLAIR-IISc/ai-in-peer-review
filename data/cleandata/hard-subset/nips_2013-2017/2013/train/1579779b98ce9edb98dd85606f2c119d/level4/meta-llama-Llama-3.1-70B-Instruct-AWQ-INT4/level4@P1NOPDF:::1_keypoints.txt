- Paper analyzes MCTS in zero-sum extensive-form games with perfect information and simultaneous moves, proving convergence to an approximate Nash equilibrium under certain conditions and supporting this with experiments.

- Establishes a useful sufficient condition for MCTS convergence; presentation is clear but the novelty of the proof technique is questioned.

- UCB algorithms, common in the literature, are not analyzed or compared; the authors should consider including UCB analyses/comparisons.

- Theoretical results cover propagation of the mean, yet experiments show propagation of the current sample value also performs well; an explanation for this discrepancy is requested.

- Overall, findings are interesting but the technical contributions are not viewed as particularly outstanding.
