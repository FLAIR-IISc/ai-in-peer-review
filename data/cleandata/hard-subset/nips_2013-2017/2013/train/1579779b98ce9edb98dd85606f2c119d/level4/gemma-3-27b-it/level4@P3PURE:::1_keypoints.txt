- Studies MCTS in zero-sum extensive-form games with perfect information and simultaneous moves; proves MCTS converges to an approximate Nash equilibrium under specified conditions and supports this with experiments.
- Paper is sound, gives a sufficient condition for MCTS convergence, and is clearly presented.
- Reviewer questions the novelty of the proof technique used.
- UCB algorithms, commonly used in MCTS, are not analyzed; adding discussion/comparison with UCB would strengthen the work.
- Theoretical result covers propagation of the mean, but experiments show propagating the current sample also works well; an explanation is needed.
- Findings are interesting but not a major technical breakthrough.
