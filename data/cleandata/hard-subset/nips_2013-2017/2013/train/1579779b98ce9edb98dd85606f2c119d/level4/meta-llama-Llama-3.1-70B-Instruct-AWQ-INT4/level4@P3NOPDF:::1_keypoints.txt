- Paper gives a sufficient condition for MCTS convergence in zero-sum simultaneous-move perfect-information games and presents clear, useful theoretical results.  
- Main contribution is theoretical, but reviewer questions the novelty of the proof technique and asks for clarification on how it differs from prior methods.  
- Recommend the authors explicitly explain what new insights their analytic approach provides compared to existing work.  
- Suggest including analysis and comparison of UCB-based algorithms (UCB for trees), since UCB is widely used and currently not treated.  
- Experiments show propagation of current sample value performs well despite no theoretical guarantees; reviewer asks for an explanation of this empirical-theory gap.  
- Paper is interesting but not highly technically outstanding; addressing the novelty, UCB comparison, and empirical observations would strengthen it.
