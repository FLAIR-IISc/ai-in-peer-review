- Studies MCTS in zero-sum extensive-form games with perfect information and simultaneous moves, proving conditions for convergence to an approximate Nash equilibrium and supporting them with experiments.
- Solid paper that establishes a sufficient condition for MCTS convergence and is clearly presented, but the proof techniques may lack novelty.
- Should include discussion and comparison with UCB-based methods (e.g., UCB for Trees), which are common and previously analyzed.
- Proof focuses on mean-propagation convergence, yet experiments show current-sample propagation also performs well; an explanation for this empirical behavior is needed.
- Overall interesting results but the technical contributions do not represent a major advancement in the field.
