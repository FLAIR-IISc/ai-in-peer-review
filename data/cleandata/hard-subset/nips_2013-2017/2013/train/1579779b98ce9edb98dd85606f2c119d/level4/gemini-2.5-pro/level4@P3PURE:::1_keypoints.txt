- Proves MCTS converges to an approximate Nash equilibrium in zero-sum, perfect-information, simultaneous-move games; supported by experiments and clearly presented, but technical novelty is limited.
- Main theoretical contribution is a sufficient condition for MCTS convergence, though the proof technique appears not very novel.
- Analysis omits UCB-based algorithms, which are widely used; adding UCB analysis and convergence comparisons would materially strengthen the paper.
- Theory only covers propagation of the mean, while experiments show propagating current sample values also performs well; authors should explain or provide intuition for this empirical gap.
