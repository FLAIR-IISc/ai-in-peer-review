- Well-written paper providing a valuable sufficient condition for MCTS convergence, though novelty of the proof technique is questionable given the paper’s theoretical focus.
- Empirical results corroborate the theoretical convergence to an approximate Nash equilibrium.
- The analysis omits UCB-style algorithms despite their widespread use; incorporating UCB (and comparisons) would strengthen the work.
- The proof addresses only convergence of mean propagation, while experiments show current sample value propagation also performs well—an explanation is needed.
- Overall technical impact is interesting but somewhat limited.
