- Proves MCTS converges to an approximate Nash equilibrium in zero-sum, perfect-information, simultaneous-move games, with experiments supporting the theory.
- Clear presentation and useful sufficient condition for convergence.
- Concern about novelty of the proof technique given the theoretical nature of the contribution.
- Omits analysis of widely-used UCB-based algorithms; including and comparing UCB would strengthen the paper.
- Theoretical convergence is shown only for mean propagation, yet experiments show sample-value propagation also performs well; authors should explain this discrepancy.
- Overall interesting and relevant results, but not technically outstanding.
