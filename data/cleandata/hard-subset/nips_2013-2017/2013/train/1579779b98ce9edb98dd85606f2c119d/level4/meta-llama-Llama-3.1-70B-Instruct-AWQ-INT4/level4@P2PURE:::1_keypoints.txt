- Studies MCTS in zero-sum extensive-form games with perfect information and simultaneous moves, proving convergence to an approximate Nash equilibrium under certain conditions; empirical results support the formal claim.

- Paper is well-written and gives a useful sufficient condition for MCTS convergence, but the novelty of the proof technique is questionable.

- UCB algorithms (commonly used) are not analyzed; the authors should consider and compare UCB variants given existing UCB-for-trees analyses.

- The proof only shows convergence for propagation of the mean, yet experiments show convergence for the current sample value too — further explanation is requested.

- Technically interesting results, but the contribution does not stand out significantly.
