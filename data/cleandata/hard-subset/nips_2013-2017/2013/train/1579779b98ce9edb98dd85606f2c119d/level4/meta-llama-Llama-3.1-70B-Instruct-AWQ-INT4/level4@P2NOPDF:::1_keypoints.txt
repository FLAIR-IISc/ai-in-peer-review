- Paper studies MCTS for zero-sum extensive-form games with perfect information and simultaneous moves, proving convergence to an approximate Nash equilibrium under certain conditions.  
- Empirical results align with and support the theoretical convergence claims.  
- Main contribution is a sufficient condition for MCTS convergence and a clear presentation, but the novelty of the proof technique is questioned.  
- The paper omits analysis of widely-used UCB algorithms; the reviewer suggests adding UCB analysis and empirical comparisons.  
- Theoretical proof covers only mean propagation, yet experiments show good convergence for current sample value propagation — an explanation for this discrepancy is requested.  
- Overall interesting and useful but not technically outstanding.
