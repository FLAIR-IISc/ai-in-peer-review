- Paper analyzes MCTS for perfect-information, simultaneous-move, zero-sum extensive-form games and proves convergence to an approximate Nash equilibrium under certain conditions, with empirical results supporting the theory.
- Manuscript is clearly presented and provides a valuable sufficient condition, but the novelty of the proof methodology is questionable.
- The paper omits analysis of widely used UCB algorithms; including and comparing UCB would significantly strengthen the work.
- Theoretical convergence is proved only for mean propagation, yet experiments show strong performance for current sample-value propagation—an explanation for this empirical gap is needed.
- Overall the findings are interesting, but the technical contribution is not especially outstanding.
