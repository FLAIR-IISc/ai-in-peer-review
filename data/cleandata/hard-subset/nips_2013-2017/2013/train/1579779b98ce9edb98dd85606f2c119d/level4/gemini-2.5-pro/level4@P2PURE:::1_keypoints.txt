- Paper proves MCTS converges to an approximate Nash equilibrium for zero-sum extensive-form games with perfect information and simultaneous moves, supported by empirical results.  
- Well-presented work offering a useful sufficient condition for MCTS convergence.  
- Concern about the novelty of the proof technique since the main contribution is theoretical.  
- Omits analysis of widely-used UCB algorithms; should include theoretical and empirical comparison to UCB-based methods.  
- Convergence proof only covers propagation of the mean value, yet experiments show propagating sample values also works—authors should provide intuition or explanation.  
- Overall technical contribution is interesting but appears limited.
