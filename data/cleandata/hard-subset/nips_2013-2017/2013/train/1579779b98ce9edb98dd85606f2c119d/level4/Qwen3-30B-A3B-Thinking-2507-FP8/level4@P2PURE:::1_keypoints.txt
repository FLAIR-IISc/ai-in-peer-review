- Establishes MCTS convergence to an approximate Nash equilibrium in zero-sum extensive-form games with perfect information and simultaneous moves, supported by experiments.  
- Proof technique lacks novelty, limiting the paper's theoretical contribution.  
- Omits analysis and comparison of widely-used UCB algorithms and their theoretical convergence.  
- Convergence proof covers only mean propagation, yet experiments show current-sample propagation also performs well; this discrepancy is unaddressed.  
- Overall technical contribution is modest due to non-novel proofs and missing algorithmic comparisons.
