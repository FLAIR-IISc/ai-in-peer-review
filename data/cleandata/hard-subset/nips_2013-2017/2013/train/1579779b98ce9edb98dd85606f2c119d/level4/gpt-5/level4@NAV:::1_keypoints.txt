- Studies MCTS in zero-sum extensive-form games with perfect information and simultaneous moves; proves MCTS converges to an approximate Nash equilibrium under certain conditions and empirical results corroborate the theory.
- Overall a solid paper with clear exposition and a useful sufficient condition for MCTS convergence.
- Concern about the novelty of the proof technique given the mainly theoretical contribution.
- UCB algorithms, commonly used in the literature, are not analyzed or compared; adding UCB analysis/comparison would strengthen the work.
- Theory covers propagation of the mean, but experiments show propagating the current sample also performs well—an explanation for this discrepancy is needed.
- Findings are interesting but, technically, the paper is not particularly outstanding.
