- Studies MCTS in zero-sum extensive-form games with perfect information and simultaneous moves; proves MCTS converges to an approximate Nash equilibrium under certain conditions and empirically validates the result.  
- Overall a good, clearly presented paper with a useful sufficient condition for convergence.  
- Concern that the proof technique may lack novelty for the paper’s main theoretical contribution.  
- Paper does not analyze widely used UCB/UCT algorithms; authors should consider and compare UCB variants given existing analyses.  
- Theoretical result only proves convergence for propagation of the mean, yet experiments show current sample-value propagation also works—needs explanation.  
- Technically interesting but not highly outstanding; contribution appears incremental.
