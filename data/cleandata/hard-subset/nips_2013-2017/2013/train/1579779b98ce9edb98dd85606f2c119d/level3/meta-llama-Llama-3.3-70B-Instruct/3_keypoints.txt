- Analyzes achievable regret trade-offs in online learning with expert advice, focusing on the absolute loss game with two experts.  
- Provides a complete characterization of the set of realizable trade-offs and shows it forms a convex polygon.  
- Gives an explicit formula for the polygon’s vertices.  
- Presents a random-walk-based method to compute the optimal strategy for a given trade-off.  
- Studies asymptotic regret rate trade-off profile and proves the expected trade-off is achievable but not tight.  
- Derives a formula for the optimal constant in the trade-off, relevant for practitioners.  
- Paper is well-written, technically sound, and clearly organized with thorough introductions and related work.  
- Technical sections are detailed and use clear notation and mathematical derivations.  
- Experimental evaluation is limited and shows the proposed approach slightly underperforms a standard baseline.  
- Main limitation: primary focus on absolute loss; extensions to linear losses and multiple experts are limited.  
- Overall significant contribution to online learning theory and useful for informing algorithm design.  
- Reviewer recommendation: accept.
