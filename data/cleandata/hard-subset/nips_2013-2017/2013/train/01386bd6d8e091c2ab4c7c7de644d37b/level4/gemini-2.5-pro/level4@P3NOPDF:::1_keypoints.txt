- Addresses limited field-of-view by statistically "stitching" sequential neural recordings to infer circuit-wide properties.
- Novel focus on inferring interactions between neurons that were never recorded simultaneously.
- Uses a latent linear dynamical system with EM to estimate coupling, stimulus fields, and noise covariances from incomplete observations.
- Core technical achievement: can recover pairwise correlations between non-simultaneously recorded neurons.
- Simulated versus real-data discrepancy: simulations succeed without population overlap but real data required overlap—authors should explain simulation assumptions (e.g., stationarity, perfect model) that enable this.
- Large difference in correlation magnitudes between simulated and experimental results—need discussion of factors such as real-data noise, model mismatch, or unmodeled biology.
- Figures and legends lack sufficient detail; figure captions and main-text walkthroughs should be expanded for clarity.
- Overall a strong, interpretable contribution likely valuable to computational and experimental neuroscience, pending clarifications above.
