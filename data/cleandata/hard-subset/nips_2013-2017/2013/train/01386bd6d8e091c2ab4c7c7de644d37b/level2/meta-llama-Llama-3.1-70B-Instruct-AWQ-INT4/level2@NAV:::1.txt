This paper presents a novel statistical method for inferring neural population dynamics from multiple partial recordings of the same neural circuit. The authors propose a latent dynamical system model that "stitches" together sequentially imaged sets of neurons into one model, allowing for the characterization of population dynamics beyond the number of simultaneously imaged neurons.

The main claim of the paper is that this method can accurately predict correlations between non-simultaneously recorded neuron pairs, even when the neurons were not imaged simultaneously. The authors support this claim through simulations and experimental data from mouse somatosensory cortex.

The paper is well-written and clearly organized, making it easy to follow the authors' reasoning and methodology. The authors provide a thorough review of related work and clearly explain the limitations of previous approaches.

The method presented in the paper is technically sound, and the authors provide a detailed description of the model and its parameters. The use of expectation maximization (EM) algorithm for parameter estimation is well-justified, and the authors provide a clear explanation of the E-step and M-step.

The results presented in the paper are impressive, with the method showing good performance in predicting correlations between non-simultaneously recorded neuron pairs. The authors also provide a thorough evaluation of the method's performance, including comparisons to a "naive" approach and a "fully observed" model.

The significance of the paper lies in its potential to expand our understanding of neural population dynamics, particularly in situations where simultaneous imaging of all neurons is not possible. The method presented in the paper could be used to analyze data from a wide range of neural systems, and could potentially lead to new insights into the neural basis of behavior and cognition.

Overall, I would rate the quality of the paper as excellent, and I believe it makes a significant contribution to the field of neural population dynamics.
