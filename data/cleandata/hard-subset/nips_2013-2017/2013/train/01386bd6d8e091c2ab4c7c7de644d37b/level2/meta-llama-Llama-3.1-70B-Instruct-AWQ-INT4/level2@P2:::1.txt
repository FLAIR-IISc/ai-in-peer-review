**Summary**

The paper proposes a statistical method for inferring neural population dynamics from multiple partial recordings of the same neural circuit. The method is based on a latent dynamical system model that can "stitch" together sequentially imaged sets of neurons into one model. The authors demonstrate the effectiveness of their method using simulated and experimental data from mouse somatosensory cortex.

The paper is technically sound, and the claims are well-supported by theoretical analysis and experimental results. The authors provide a clear and detailed description of their method, including the mathematical formulation of the latent dynamical system model and the expectation-maximization algorithm used for parameter estimation. The experimental results are also well-presented, with clear figures and tables that illustrate the performance of the method.

One potential issue with the paper is that the authors do not provide a detailed analysis of the limitations of their method. Additionally, the authors do not provide a comparison of their method with other existing methods for inferring neural population dynamics.
