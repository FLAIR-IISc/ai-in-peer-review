- Novel method to combine multiple partial recordings into a single model to infer population dynamics and interactions, including between non-simultaneously observed neurons.
- Technically sound with a well-justified linear Gaussian dynamical system and effective prediction of correlations; validated on simulated and real data.
- Well-written and clear presentation with detailed methods and effective figures/tables.
- Original contribution in focusing on non-simultaneously observed neurons and using a linear Gaussian model to merge recordings.
- Significant implications for neuroscience by enabling inference of functional connections and new analyses of neural circuits and behavior.
