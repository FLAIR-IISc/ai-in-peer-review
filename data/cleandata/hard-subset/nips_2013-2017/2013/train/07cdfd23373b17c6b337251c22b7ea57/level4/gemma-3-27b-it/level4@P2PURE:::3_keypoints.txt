- Positive overall: paper is well-written, technically sound, and recommended for acceptance.  
- Clarify the term “parsimonious” modeling with a brief definition, as it’s central to the contribution.  
- Correct the misleading claim about down-sampling zeros — Gopalan et al. suggest it does not harm accuracy.  
- The LDA comparison is inappropriate: LDA’s generative outputs are always valid documents, whereas the triangle model can produce invalid networks, so the LDA defense is unconvincing.  
- Cite Hoffman et al. alongside Amari and Sato: Sato’s algorithm is inapplicable in the presence of “local variables” as defined by Hoffman.  
- Provide more detail on how power-law networks are generated in the latent space.  
- Strengthen evaluation by comparing to Gopalan et al.’s algorithm for accelerating MMSB (in addition to or instead of MMSB batch).  
- Minor edits: change “mixture-membership” to “mixed-membership,” remove the final sentence on p8 (keep the penultimate), and cite the JMLR version of “Stochastic Variational Inference” in the bibliography.
