- Introduces a novel block-wise algorithm for fitting CLIME, replacing traditional column-wise approaches.

- Provides theoretical convergence rates for objective value and distance to optimality.
- Designs the algorithm to exploit matrix structure (sparsity, low-rank) to reduce computation.
- Implements a scalable parallel framework compatible with shared and distributed memory systems.
- Presents numerical experiments comparing runtime against main competitors on synthetic and real-world data.
- Conducts a detailed performance study showing speedup with varying block sizes and core counts.
