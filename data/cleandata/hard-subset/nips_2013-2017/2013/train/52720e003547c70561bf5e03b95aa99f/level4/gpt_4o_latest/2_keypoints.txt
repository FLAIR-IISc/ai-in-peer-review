- Proposes an inexact ADMM for the linear program in CLIME and proves O(1/T) convergence.
- Implements the algorithm on shared-memory and distributed-memory systems.
- Provides numerical comparisons with DC-QUIC, Tiger, and CLIME-Flare.
- Paper is technically sound.
- Derivation of inexact ADMM follows standard approaches (reference provided).
- CLIME subproblems decompose componentwise; the main cost is matrix-matrix multiplication.
- ADMM is well-suited to parallelization given CLIME’s structure; matrix-matrix multiplications parallelize efficiently.
- Numerical experiment setup lacks detail (e.g., achieved accuracy per solver not reported).
- Paper is well-structured and clear.
- Evaluation on both shared- and distributed-memory architectures is a notable contribution.
- Scalability benefits mainly stem from CLIME’s simple structure; the derivation itself is not particularly novel.
