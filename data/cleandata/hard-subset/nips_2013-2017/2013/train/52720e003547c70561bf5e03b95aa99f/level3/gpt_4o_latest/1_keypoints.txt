- Uses block cyclic data distribution to balance load and optimize memory usage.  
- Scales to ultra-large problems—millions of dimensions and trillions of parameters.  
- Achieves one trillion parameters in ~11 hours on 400 cores, showing near-linear speedups on shared and distributed-memory systems.  
- Empirical results demonstrate superior scalability and performance versus DC-QUIC, Tiger, and Flare on synthetic and real datasets.
