**Review of "Large Scale Distributed Sparse Precision Estimation"**

The paper "Large Scale Distributed Sparse Precision Estimation" presents a novel approach to tackle the problem of sparse precision matrix estimation in high dimensions using the CLIME estimator. The authors propose a block-wise alternative to the traditional column-wise solvable CLIME estimator and develop an inexact alternating direction method of multipliers (ADMM) algorithm for CLIME. The paper also presents a scalable distributed framework for the computations, which scales to millions of dimensions and trillions of parameters, using hundreds of cores.

**Summary of Main Ideas**

The paper starts by introducing the problem of sparse precision matrix estimation and the CLIME estimator.
