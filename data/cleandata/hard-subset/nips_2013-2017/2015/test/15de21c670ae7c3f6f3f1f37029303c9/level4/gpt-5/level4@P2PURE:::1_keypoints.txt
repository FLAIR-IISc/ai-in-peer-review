- Limited engagement with relevant literature; must compare to Monte Carlo EM and stochastic approximation methods with Markovian dynamics.  
- Many existing results/theory already encompass the authors’ claims, so contributions appear marginally novel.  
- Uniform ergodicity assumption is unnecessary — prior work handles non-uniform ergodic settings.  
- Reinitializing the chain to a fixed distribution r each iteration is unjustified; reinitializing from the previous sample is often preferable and well-studied.  
- The authors’ rationale that reinitialization simplifies analysis is insufficient as a justification.  
- Presentation issues: assumptions for Theorems 1 and 2 are unclear and should be stated explicitly.  
- Strong assumptions used for results like Theorem 6 are unnecessary; see Benveniste, Métivier, and Priouret for more general theory.  
- Overall, the manuscript shows insufficient awareness of prior work and limited novelty.
