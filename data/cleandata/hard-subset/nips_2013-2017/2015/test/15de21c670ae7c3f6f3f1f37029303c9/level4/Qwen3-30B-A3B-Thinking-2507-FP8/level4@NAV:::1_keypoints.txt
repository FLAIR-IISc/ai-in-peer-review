- Significant lack of engagement with established literature on Monte Carlo EM and stochastic approximation with Markovian dynamics.  
- Proposed algorithm is closely related to Monte Carlo EM and broader stochastic approximation methods; authors must explicitly compare to existing work.  
- A more general theoretical framework already exists that covers their results without requiring uniform ergodicity.  
- Unjustified reinitialization of the Markov chain at a fixed distribution r each iteration; prior work analyzes reinitializing from the previous sample.  
- Presentation is poor: assumptions for Theorems 1 and 2 are unclear and not explicitly stated.  
- Strong assumptions (e.g., for Theorem 6) are unnecessary given existing, more general results (e.g., Benveniste, Métivier, Priouret), making novelty marginal.  
- Overall insufficient awareness of the literature undermines the manuscript's contribution.
