

Comments on Quality:
The results provide meaningful and useful convergence rates for learning algorithms in terms of mixing times. The primary concern is that the lower bound (Theorem 7) is challenging to interpret. An expanded discussion of this theorem and a broader exploration of applicable lower bounds for the problem would be beneficial.

Comments on Clarity:
The presentation is clear.

Comments on Originality:
The problem framing (within the "fast mixing" model class) and the theoretical results are both original.

Comments on Significance:
The significance is currently unclear. While the paper addresses a gap in the literature on learning intractable models using MCMC, its broader impact requires further elaboration. The submission would benefit from additional discussion of potential impact, including future extensions and opportunities for building upon this work. The introduction of a novel class of tractable models based on good MCMC-based approximability of expectations, combined with the novel and interesting theoretical convergence rate results, warrants acceptance.
