- Quality: Provides meaningful and useful convergence rates linking learning algorithms to MCMC mixing times; main weakness is an unclear, hard-to-interpret lower bound in Theorem 7 that needs expanded discussion.
- Clarity: The paper is well-written and clearly presented.
- Originality: Contributions are original, including the "fast mixing" model class framing and the theoretical results.
- Significance: Fills a literature gap on learning intractable models with MCMC but would benefit from a clearer articulation of broader impact.
- Recommendation: Improve discussion of implications, future extensions, and how others can build on the results; overall the paper is a valuable contribution and recommended for acceptance.
