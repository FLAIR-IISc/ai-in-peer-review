- Presents theoretical analysis of learning algorithms for a novel "fast mixing" model class defined by MCMC sampler mixing times.
- Provides meaningful and useful convergence rates for learning expressed in terms of mixing times.
- Original and technically strong framing; novel and insightful perspective on model tractability via MCMC efficiency.
- Theoretical results appear original and fill a gap in learning intractable models with MCMC-based methods.
- Overall presentation is clear and well-written.
- Theorem 7 (lower bound) is hard to interpret and needs an expanded, clarifying discussion.
- Broader significance and potential applications, extensions, and follow-up opportunities are underdeveloped and should be elaborated.
- Despite these issues, the paper is strong and recommended for acceptance.
