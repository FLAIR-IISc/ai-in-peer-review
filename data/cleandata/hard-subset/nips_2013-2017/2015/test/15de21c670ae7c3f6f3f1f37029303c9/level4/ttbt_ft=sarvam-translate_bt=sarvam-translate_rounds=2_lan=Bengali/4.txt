Comments on Quality:
The results provide the rate of convergence of learning algorithms in terms of mixing time. The limits seem meaningful and useful.
The only significant complaint is that the lower limit (proof 7) is very difficult to explain. A more detailed discussion of the theorem and, in general, lower limits applicable to the problem would have been more useful.
Comments on Clarity:
The presentation is clear.
Comments on Originality:
Both the structure of the problem ("fast mixing" model class) and the theoretical results seem original.
Comments on Importance:
The reviewer finds its significance unclear. It seems that this paper fills a gap in the literature on complex model learning using MCMC, and in this sense, it seems important.
The submitted topic could be further developed through further discussion of its potential impact (including future extensions and opportunities based on it). This submitted topic presents a new class of complex models using good MCMC-based approximations of expectations. The theoretical results providing the rate of convergence for learning these models seem new and interesting, and therefore the submitted topic should be accepted.
