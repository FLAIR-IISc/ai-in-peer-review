- Provides substantive convergence rates for learning algorithms via mixing times, with meaningful and applicable bounds.
- Primary concern: limited interpretability of the lower bound (Theorem 7); needs more detailed exposition and a broader discussion of relevant lower bounds.
- Presentation is clear and well-structured.
- Problem formulation around the "fast mixing" model class and the theoretical contributions are novel.
- Significance is unclear: the broader impact is underdeveloped and requires clarification.
- Recommend adding explicit discussion of potential applications, future extensions, and opportunities for building on these results.
- Despite concerns, the paper introduces a novel tractable model class based on efficient MCMC-based expectation approximation, with interesting convergence rate guarantees, and merits acceptance.
