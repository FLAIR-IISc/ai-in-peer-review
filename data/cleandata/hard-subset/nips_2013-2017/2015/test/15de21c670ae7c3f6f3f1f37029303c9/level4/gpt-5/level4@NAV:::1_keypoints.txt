- Insufficient engagement with relevant literature, especially work on Monte Carlo EM and stochastic approximation with Markovian dynamics.  
- Proposed algorithm is closely related to existing Monte Carlo EM and stochastic approximation schemes and should be compared to prior methods.  
- A broader theoretical framework already exists that encompasses many of the authors’ results.  
- Uniform ergodicity and restarting the Markov chain from a fixed distribution r each iteration are unnecessary assumptions.  
- Warm-starting from the previous sample is preferable and has been thoroughly analyzed in prior work.  
- Exposition is unclear: assumptions for Theorems 1 and 2 are not explicitly stated and are scattered in the text.  
- Strong assumptions used for results like Theorem 6 are overly restrictive and unnecessary (see Benveniste, Métivier, Priouret).  
- Overall limited awareness of prior work leads to marginal novelty of the paper’s contributions.
