- Authors show poor awareness of existing literature; manuscript misses key related work.
- Proposed algorithm closely resembles Monte Carlo EM and stochastic approximation methods with Markovian dynamics.
- Much of the manuscript’s theory is already covered by established frameworks that handle broader settings.
- Existing theory does not require uniform ergodicity or reinitializing the Markov chain at a fixed distribution r.
- Reinitializing the chain for analysis is unjustified, and reinitializing with the previous sample has been extensively analyzed before.
- Presentation is weak: assumptions for Theorems 1 and 2 are not clearly stated in place.
- Theorem 6 relies on strong/unnecessary assumptions when more general results (e.g., Benveniste, Métiivier, Priouret) exist.
- Overall contribution appears marginally novel due to insufficient engagement with prior work.
- Manuscript should better compare/contrast with related work and clearly highlight its unique contributions.
