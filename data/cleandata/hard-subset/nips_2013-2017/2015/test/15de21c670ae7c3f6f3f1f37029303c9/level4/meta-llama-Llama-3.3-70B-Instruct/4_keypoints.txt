- Presents convergence rates linking learning algorithms to mixing times, offering meaningful and useful bounds.
- The provided lower bound (Theorem 7) is difficult to understand and needs a clearer explanation and broader discussion of applicable lower bounds.
- Overall presentation is well-organized and easy to follow.
- Novel problem formulation within the "fast mixing" model class and accompanying theoretical results appear to be original.
- Addresses a gap on using MCMC for learning intractable models and introduces a new class of tractable models based on MCMC-approximable expectations.
- Would benefit from a deeper analysis of potential impact, future extensions, and opportunities for building on the work, though the submission is noteworthy and worthy of acceptance.
