- Paper presents convergence rates for learning algorithms in terms of mixing times; bounds are meaningful and potentially very useful.  
- Primary concern: interpretability of the lower bound in Theorem 7; needs more detailed discussion and broader contextualization of lower bounds.  
- Presentation is clear and well-structured.  
- Framing via a “fast mixing” model class and the associated theoretical results appear to be original contributions.  
- Significance is somewhat unclear but the work addresses a gap about learning intractable models with MCMC, indicating notable significance.  
- The paper would benefit from a more thorough discussion of potential impact, future research directions, and avenues for follow-up work.  
- Introducing a new class of tractable models based on strong MCMC-based expectation approximation is a valuable contribution.  
- Reviewer recommends acceptance given the novelty and interest of the theoretical convergence results.
