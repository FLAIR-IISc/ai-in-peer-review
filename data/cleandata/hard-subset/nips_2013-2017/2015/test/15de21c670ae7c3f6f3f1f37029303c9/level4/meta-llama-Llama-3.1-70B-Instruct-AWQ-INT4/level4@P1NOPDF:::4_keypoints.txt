- Quality: Provides convergence rates for learning algorithms in terms of mixing times with meaningful and useful bounds.  
- Quality concern: Theorem 7 (the lower bound) is hard to interpret and needs deeper discussion and broader analysis of lower bounds.  
- Clarity: Presentation is clear and well-organized, easy to follow.  
- Originality: Framing via the "fast mixing" model class and the theoretical results are original contributions.  
- Originality/Approach: Novel use of MCMC to address learning intractable models.  
- Significance: Fills a gap in the literature on learning intractable models with MCMC, though the broader impact is not fully articulated.  
- Suggestion: Author should expand discussion of potential impact, future extensions, and opportunities for follow-up work.  
- Overall: Introduces a novel tractable model class and novel convergence results; paper is worthy of acceptance.
