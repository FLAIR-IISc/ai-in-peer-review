- Provides meaningful, useful convergence-rate bounds for learning algorithms expressed in terms of mixing times.
- Theorem 7's lower bound is hard to interpret; the paper needs a more detailed discussion and broader exploration of lower bounds.
- Paper presentation is clear and well-organized.
- The "fast mixing" model class and the theoretical results appear to be original contributions.
- Significance is not immediately obvious to the reviewer, though the work addresses a gap in learning intractable models with MCMC.
- Recommend adding a deeper discussion of potential impact, future extensions, and directions for follow-up work.
- Introducing a novel class of tractable models based on MCMC-approximability is a valuable contribution.
- Theoretical results on convergence rates are novel and make a strong case for acceptance.
