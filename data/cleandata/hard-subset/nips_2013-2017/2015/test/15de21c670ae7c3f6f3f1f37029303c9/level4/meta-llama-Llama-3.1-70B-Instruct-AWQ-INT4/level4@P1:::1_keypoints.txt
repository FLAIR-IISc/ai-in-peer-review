- Main issue: manuscript shows limited awareness of the literature; algorithm closely related to Monte Carlo EM and stochastic approximation with Markovian dynamics and needs comparison to existing work.  
- Existing theory already covers many of the authors' results and extends to more general settings (e.g., no need for uniform ergodicity).  
- Reinitializing the Markov chain at a fixed distribution r each iteration is unnecessary; reinitializing from the previous sample may be preferable and has been analyzed before.  
- Presentation is suboptimal: assumptions for Theorems 1 and 2 are unclear and should be stated explicitly within the theorems.  
- The authors use overly strong assumptions for results like Theorem 6; more general results exist (e.g., Benveniste, Métivier, Priouret).  
- The results are only marginally novel due to the lack of engagement with existing literature.  
- Manuscript strengths: well-organized and clearly written, with a good explanation of the algorithm and underlying theory.  
- Reviewer scores: Quality 6/10, Clarity 8/10, Originality 4/10, Significance 5/10.  
- Recommendation: Do not accept in current form; require significant revisions to address literature awareness and novelty before resubmission.
