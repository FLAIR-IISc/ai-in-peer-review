- Provides convergence rates for learning algorithms in terms of mixing times; the obtained bounds are meaningful and useful.  
- The lower bound in Theorem 7 is difficult to interpret and needs an expanded discussion and broader context of lower bounds for the problem.  
- Presentation is clear and effectively conveys the main ideas and technical contributions.  
- Framing the problem via a "fast mixing" model class and the associated theoretical results appear original.  
- The paper fills a gap in learning intractable models using MCMC, though the overall significance is not fully articulated.  
- The submission would benefit from a more in-depth discussion of potential impact, future extensions, and follow-up opportunities.  
- Introducing a novel class of tractable models based on MCMC-approximability and providing new convergence-rate guarantees is a notable contribution supporting acceptance.
