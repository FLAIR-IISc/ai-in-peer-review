Comments on Quality:
The results provide convergence-rate bounds for learning algorithms in terms of mixing times. These bounds appear meaningful and useful. The only substantial concern is that the lower bound (Theorem 7) is hard to interpret. A more detailed discussion of this theorem and, more generally, of the lower bounds relevant to the problem would be valuable.

Comments on Clarity:
The presentation is clear.

Comments on Originality:
Both the problem formulation (via the “fast mixing” model class) and the theoretical results appear original.

Comments on Significance:
The significance is not entirely clear to the reviewer. The work does appear to fill a gap in the literature on learning intractable models using MCMC, and in that sense it seems significant. The submission would be improved by a deeper discussion of the potential impact, including future extensions and opportunities to build on this work. The paper introduces a novel class of tractable models based on strong MCMC-based approximability of expectations. The theoretical results providing convergence rates for learning these models appear novel and interesting, and the submission should therefore be accepted.
