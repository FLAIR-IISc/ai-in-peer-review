**Summary of the Paper**

The paper "Maximum Likelihood Learning With Arbitrary Treewidth via Fast-Mixing Parameter Sets" proposes a novel approach to maximum likelihood learning in high-treewidth undirected graphical models. The authors introduce the concept of "fast-mixing parameters" and show that when parameters are constrained to this set, Markov chain Monte Carlo (MCMC) sampling can be guaranteed to quickly converge to the stationary distribution. This allows for the development of a fully-polynomial time randomized approximation scheme for maximum likelihood learning.
