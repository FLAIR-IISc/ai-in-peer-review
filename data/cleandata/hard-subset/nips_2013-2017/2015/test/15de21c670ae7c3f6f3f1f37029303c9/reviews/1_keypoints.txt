- Major lack of awareness of prior literature on related algorithms and theory.
- The proposed algorithm is closely related to Monte Carlo EM and stochastic approximation with Markovian dynamics.
- The authors should compare their method to existing work on optimizing intractable likelihoods.
- Existing theory already covers the authors’ results and more general scenarios.
- Unnecessary/incorrect assumptions: Markov chains need not be uniformly ergodic and reinitializing at a fixed distribution r is not required.
- Reinitializing with the previous sample may be preferable and has been extensively analyzed previously.
- Presentation is poor: assumptions for Theorems 1 and 2 are unclear and should be stated explicitly near the theorems.
- Strong assumptions used for results like Theorem 6 are unnecessary; cite Benveniste, Métiivier and Priouret for more general results; overall novelty is marginal.
