- Proposes a novel approach to approximate MLE in exponential families using fast-mixing MCMC combined with proximal gradient descent.  
- Provides theoretical guarantees for both convex and strongly convex cases.  
- Paper is well-motivated and authors candidly discuss limitations, pessimistic assumptions, and future work.  
- Theoretical analyses are solid but largely a nontrivial combination of existing techniques.  
- Offers insight into CD-type algorithms and includes a simple experiment supporting the theory.  
- Important clarity omissions: never explicitly states log-likelihood convexity (Hessian = covariance).  
- Missing summary of all assumptions and omission of the convexity of the parameter set Θ in the main text.  
- Practical impact is limited because proving fast-mixing for MCMC is generally hard, shifting one difficult problem to another.  
- Well-written and clear overall but contains minor typos and wording errors.  
- Originality and significance are strong; addresses a difficult problem better than prior work.  
- Recommendation: accept with revisions to fix clarity omissions and minor errors.  
- Reviewer rating: 8/10, confidence: 8/10.
