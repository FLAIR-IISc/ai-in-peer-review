- Introduces new upper and lower bounds on matrix determinants and applies them to bounding DPP likelihoods.  
- Central idea is straightforward but carefully executed, yielding valuable insights and extension opportunities.  
- Makes creative use of pseudo-inputs to bound functionals of positive definite kernels.  
- Problem is timely, of broad interest, and the approach appears general with applications beyond DPPs.  
- Empirical evaluation is thoughtfully designed and highlights conditions under which a variational approximation fails.  
- Chosen application domain aligns well with technical contributions and demonstrates practical relevance.  
- Minor: unclear why parameterizing K (rather than L) is emphasized, since no concrete solution for K is proposed.  
- Minor: request to provide a conventional supplementary document instead of an extended paper as supplement.  
- Future work suggestion: develop bounds that operate directly on the K matrix.  
- Future work suggestion: assess computational and memory scalability of K-based bounds implemented via pseudo-inputs.
