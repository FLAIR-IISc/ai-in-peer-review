- Proposes novel upper and lower bounds on the matrix determinant with applications to bounding DPP likelihoods.  
- Main idea is straightforward but the paper is well-executed, enlightening, and enables potential improvements.  
- Creative use of pseudo-inputs to bound functionals of positive definite kernels.  
- Approach is general and could apply beyond determinantal point processes.  
- Experimental evaluation is well-constructed and highlights key behaviors.  
- Experiments effectively demonstrate failures of variational approximations under certain conditions.  
- Chosen application area is interesting and highly relevant.  
- Minor: Emphasis on parameterizing K (rather than L) is unclear since the paper doesn’t really address it.  
- Minor: Recommend preparing a standard supplement instead of uploading an extended paper version as the supplement.  
- Future work: Consider bounding the determinant directly in terms of the K matrix for better interpretability.  
- Future work: Investigate the scalability of the pseudo-input method when applied to K-parameterized bounds.
