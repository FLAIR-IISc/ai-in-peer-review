- Proposes novel upper and lower bounds on the matrix determinant with applications to bounding DPP likelihoods.
- Main idea is straightforward but the paper is well-executed, enlightening, and enables future improvements.
- Introduces a creative use of pseudo-inputs to bound functionals of a positive definite kernel.
- Approach is general and likely applicable beyond determinantal point processes.
- Experimental evaluation is well-constructed and demonstrates key behaviors, including failures of a variational approximation under some conditions.
- The chosen application area is interesting and highly relevant.
- Minor: Emphasis on parameterizing K (instead of L) is unclear because the paper does not actually tackle that parameterization.
- Minor: Recommend providing a standard supplemental document rather than an extended paper as the supplement.
- Future work: Explore bounding the determinant directly in terms of the K matrix to improve interpretability.
- Future work: Investigate the scalability of the pseudo-input method.
