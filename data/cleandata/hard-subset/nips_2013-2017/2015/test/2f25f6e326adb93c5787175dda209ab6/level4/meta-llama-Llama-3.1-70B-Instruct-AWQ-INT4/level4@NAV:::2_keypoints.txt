- Presents novel upper and lower bounds for the matrix determinant with significant implications for bounding DPP likelihoods.  
- Core concept is simple yet the paper is well-crafted and insightful, laying groundwork for further advances.  
- Introduces a creative application of pseudo-inputs to bound functionals of positive definite kernels for a timely problem.  
- Method appears general and may extend beyond determinantal point processes.  
- Experiments are well-designed and highlight key aspects, notably limitations of variational approximations under certain conditions.  
- Chosen application area is engaging and highly relevant to the problem.  
- Minor: Emphasis on parameterizing K (vs L) is unclear—paper describes the issue but does not address it.  
- Minor: If accepted, authors should provide a standard supplement rather than an extended paper as the supplement.  
- Suggested future work: Explore interpretability of K parameterization and directly bounding the determinant in terms of K.  
- Suggested future work: Investigate scalability implications of using pseudo-inputs for a K-based bounding approach.
