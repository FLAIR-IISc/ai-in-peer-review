- Proposes novel upper and lower bounds on matrix determinants with applications to bounding DPP likelihoods.
- Core idea is straightforward but the paper is well-executed, insightful, and opens avenues for improvements.
- Creative application of pseudo-inputs to bound functionals of positive definite kernels.
- Timely problem likely to interest the machine learning community.
- Approach is general and has potential applications beyond determinantal point processes.
- Experimental evaluation is well-designed and demonstrates key points, including limitations of a variational approximation.
- Chosen application area is interesting and highly relevant to the problem.
- Minor concern: emphasis on parameterizing K (rather than L) is unclear since the authors do not address the parameterization choice.
- Recommendation: provide a standard supplementary document rather than an extended paper upload if accepted.
- Future work suggestion: consider bounding the determinant directly in terms of K and assess scalability when using pseudo-inputs.
