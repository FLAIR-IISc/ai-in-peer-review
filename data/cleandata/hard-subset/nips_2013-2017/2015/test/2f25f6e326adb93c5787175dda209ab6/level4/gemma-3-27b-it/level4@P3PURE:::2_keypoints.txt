- Proposes novel upper and lower bounds on the matrix determinant with applications to bounding DPP likelihoods.  
- Core idea is straightforward but well-executed and insightful, enabling potential improvements.  
- Creative use of pseudo-inputs to bound functionals of positive definite kernels.  
- Problem is timely and likely of broad interest to the research community.  
- Approach is general and could apply beyond determinantal point processes.  
- Experimental evaluation is well-designed and highlights key points, including limitations of a variational approximation.  
- Chosen application area is interesting and highly relevant to the problem.  
- Minor concern: prominence given to parameterizing K (rather than L) is unclear since the paper doesn't address the parameterization challenge.  
- Recommendation: if accepted, provide a dedicated supplementary document rather than an extended manuscript upload.  
- Suggested future work: explore bounding the determinant directly in terms of K and evaluate scalability when using pseudo-inputs.
