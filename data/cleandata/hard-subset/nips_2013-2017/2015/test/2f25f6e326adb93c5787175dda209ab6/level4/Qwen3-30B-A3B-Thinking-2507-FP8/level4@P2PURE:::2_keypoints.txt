- Proposes novel upper and lower bounds on the matrix determinant with applications to bounding DPP likelihoods.  
- Main idea is simple, well-executed, and insightful, opening avenues for future improvements.  
- Introduces a creative use of pseudo-inputs to bound functionals of positive definite kernels.  
- The problem is timely and will interest multiple researchers in the community.  
- Approach is sufficiently general and may apply beyond determinantal point processes.  
- Experimental evaluation is well-designed and demonstrates key points, including failure modes of a variational approximation under certain conditions.  
- Chosen application area is compelling and highly relevant to the work.  
- Minor comment: unclear why parameterizing the kernel as K (instead of L) is emphasized in abstract and main text.  
- Minor comment: if accepted, authors should provide a standard supplement rather than an extended paper upload.  
- Suggestion for future work: explore interpretability of the K parameterization and bounding the determinant directly in terms of K.  
- Suggestion for future work: evaluate the scalability of the pseudo-input method.
