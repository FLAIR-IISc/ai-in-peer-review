- Introduces new upper and lower bounds for the matrix determinant and uses them to bound the likelihood of determinantal point processes.  
- Presentation is clear, the idea is straightforward, and the work offers valuable insights that open avenues for future refinement.  
- Clever use of pseudo-inputs to establish bounds on functionals of positive definite kernels.  
- Addresses a pertinent problem with broad appeal in the machine learning community.  
- Methodology is generally applicable beyond the specific DPP context.  
- Experiments are well designed and effectively demonstrate limitations of a variational approximation under certain conditions.  
- Chosen application area is interesting and highly relevant to the problem.  
- Minor concern: emphasis on parameterizing kernel K (vs L) is unclear, since the paper characterizes but does not resolve that parameterization issue.  
- Minor recommendation: provide a dedicated supplementary document rather than an extended manuscript upload.  
- Suggested future work: investigate directly bounding the determinant in terms of K and analyze scalability when using the pseudo-input approach.
