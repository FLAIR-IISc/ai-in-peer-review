- Proposes novel upper and lower bounds on the matrix determinant, applied to bounding DPP likelihoods.  
- Core idea is simple but executed carefully and opens several avenues for improvement.  
- Creative use of pseudo-inputs to bound functionals of positive definite kernels.  
- Problem is timely and of broad interest; approach appears general with potential applications beyond DPPs.  
- Experimental evaluation is well designed and clarifies key behaviors, including cases where a variational approximation fails.  
- Application area chosen is interesting and highly relevant.  
- Minor concern: prominence of parameterizing K (vs L) is unclear and not addressed—either de-emphasize or clarify concrete steps taken.  
- Minor request: if accepted, provide a conventional supplementary document rather than an extended paper as the supplement.  
- Future work suggestion: investigate developing determinant bounds directly for the K matrix and analyze scalability when using the pseudo-input method.
