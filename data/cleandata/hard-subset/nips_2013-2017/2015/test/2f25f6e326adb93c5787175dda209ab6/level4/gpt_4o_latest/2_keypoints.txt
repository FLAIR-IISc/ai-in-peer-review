- Introduces novel upper and lower bounds on the matrix determinant, applied to estimating DPP likelihoods.
- Core idea is straightforward but well executed, insightful, and lays groundwork for further advances.
- Presents a novel application of pseudo-inputs to bound functionals of positive definite kernels.
- Addresses a timely problem likely to attract significant research interest.
- Approach is general and promises applicability beyond determinantal point processes.
- Experimental evaluation is well designed and highlights key insights and limitations.
- Experiments specifically reveal limitations of a variational approximation under certain conditions.
- Chosen application area is compelling and highly relevant to the problem.
- Minor comment: Emphasis on parameterizing K (vs L) is unclear, as the paper does not resolve this.
- Minor comment: If accepted, authors should provide a standard supplementary document rather than an extended paper upload.
- Suggestion for future work: Explore directly bounding the determinant in terms of the K matrix and the interpretability of K-parameterization.
- Suggestion for future work: Investigate the scalability of the pseudo-input method when applied to such determinant bounds.
