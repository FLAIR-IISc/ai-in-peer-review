- Introduces novel upper and lower bounds on matrix determinants, applied to bounding DPP likelihoods.
- Central idea is simple but well executed, illuminating, and opens avenues for refinement.
- Makes creative use of pseudo-inputs to bound functionals of positive-definite kernels.
- Approach appears general with potential uses beyond determinantal point processes.
- Experimental study is thoughtfully designed and clarifies key aspects, including cases where a variational approximation fails.
- Application choice is engaging and highly pertinent to the problem.
- Minor concern: unclear emphasis on parameterizing K (vs L) throughout the paper without resolving the issue.
- Minor suggestion: provide a conventional supplementary document rather than an extended paper as the supplement.
- Future work: investigate interpretability of the K parameterization and bounding the determinant directly in terms of K.
- Future work: analyze how directly bounding determinants would scale when using the pseudo-input method.
