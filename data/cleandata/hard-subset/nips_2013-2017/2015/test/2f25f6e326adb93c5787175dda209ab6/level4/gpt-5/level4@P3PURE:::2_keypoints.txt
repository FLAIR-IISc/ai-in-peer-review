- Proposes novel upper and lower bounds on the matrix determinant, with application to bounding DPP likelihoods.  
- Main idea is simple, well executed, and illuminating, opening avenues for further improvements.  
- Makes creative use of pseudo-inputs to bound functionals of positive definite kernels.  
- Approach is quite general and has potential applications beyond DPPs.  
- The problem is timely and should interest many in the community.  
- Experimental evaluation is well designed and highlights failures of a variational approximation under certain conditions.  
- Chosen application area is interesting and highly relevant to the study.  
- Minor comment: unclear why parameterizing K (instead of L) is emphasized when the paper does not address the issue.  
- Minor comment: recommend providing a standard supplementary document rather than an extended version of the paper as the supplement.  
- Future work suggestion: consider bounding the determinant directly in terms of K and assess scalability of the pseudo-input approach.
