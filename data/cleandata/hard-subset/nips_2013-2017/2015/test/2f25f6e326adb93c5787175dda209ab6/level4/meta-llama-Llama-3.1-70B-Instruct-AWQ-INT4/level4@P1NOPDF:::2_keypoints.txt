- Proposes novel upper and lower bounds on the matrix determinant with applications to bounding determinantal point process likelihood; concept is straightforward but well executed and promising.  
- Introduces a creative use of pseudo-inputs to bound functionals of a positive definite kernel; approach is general and likely of broad interest beyond DPPs.  
- Experimental evaluation is well constructed and effectively highlights key behaviors, including failure modes of a variational approximation under certain conditions.  
- The chosen application area is interesting and highly relevant to the problem studied.  
- Minor concern: emphasis on parameterizing K (instead of L) is unclear, since the paper does not actually address this choice in depth.  
- Minor suggestion: if accepted, provide a standard supplement rather than an extended version of the paper as the supplement.  
- Future-work suggestion: explore interpretability of the K parameterization and consider bounding the determinant directly in terms of K.  
- Future-work question: assess the scalability of the pseudo-input method when applied to bounding determinants in terms of K.
