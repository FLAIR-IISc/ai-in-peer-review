- Proposes novel upper and lower bounds on matrix determinants with applications to bounding DPP likelihoods.
- Main idea is simple, well-executed, enlightening, and enables further improvements.
- Creative use of pseudo-inputs to bound functionals of positive definite kernels; approach is general and applicable beyond DPPs.
- Paper is well-written and well-organized, with clear explanations of problem, solution, and experiments.
- Experimental evaluation is well-constructed and illustrates key behaviors, including failures of a variational approximation under certain conditions.
- Application area is interesting and relevant; related work discussion is thorough.
- Minor issues: unclear why parameterizing K (instead of L) is emphasized.
- Minor issues: recommend providing a standard supplement rather than only an extended paper version as the supplement.
- Suggestion: consider bounding the determinant directly in terms of K and assess scalability of the pseudo-input method.
- Overall assessment: significant, technically sound contribution; high scores (9/10) for quality, clarity, originality, and significance; recommendation: accept with minor revisions.
