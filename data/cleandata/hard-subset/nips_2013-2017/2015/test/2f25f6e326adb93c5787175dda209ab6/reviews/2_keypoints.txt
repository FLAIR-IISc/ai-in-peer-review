- Proposes novel upper and lower bounds on the matrix determinant with applications to bounding DPP likelihoods.
- Main idea is simple, well executed, and enlightening, paving the way for further improvements.
- Creative use of pseudo-inputs to bound functionals of positive definite kernels.
- Timely problem likely to be of interest to the community.
- Approach is general and has potential applications beyond determinantal point processes.
- Experimental evaluation is well constructed and illustrates key behaviors, including failures of a variational approximation under certain conditions.
- Chosen application area is interesting and highly relevant.
- Minor: unclear why parameterizing K (instead of L) is emphasized, since the paper does not address it in depth.
- Minor: recommend providing a standard supplement rather than only an extended paper version as the supplement.
- Future work suggestion: consider bounding the determinant directly in terms of K and assess scalability of the pseudo-input method.
