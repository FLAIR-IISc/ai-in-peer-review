- Problem studied: constrained contextual bandits with budget and time constraints.  
- Main algorithm (ALP): uses an LP relaxation replacing hard budget constraint with average remaining budget bτ/τ.  
- ALP guarantee: achieves O(1) regret (within a constant of optimum) except in certain boundary cases.  
- Unknown-reward algorithm (UCB-ALP): combines ALP with UCB estimates for context-action pairs.  
- UCB-ALP guarantees: O(log T) regret under mild condition, and O(√T) regret in boundary cases.  
- Technical quality: theoretically sound with thorough regret analysis supporting claims.  
- Clarity/reproducibility: well-written, well-organized, provides enough detail for expert reproduction.  
- Originality: novel combination of LP-based ALP with UCB for constrained contextual bandits.  
- Significance: advances state-of-the-art and improves prior approaches for this hard problem.  
- Limitation: O(log T) result depends on a mild condition; boundary cases face a lower bound of Ω(√T).  
- Dependence on Lemma 1: reviewer initially skeptical but convinced after authors’ rebuttal and clarified proof.  
- Recommendation: strongly recommend acceptance.
