- Paper studies contextual bandits against a stronger baseline that uses adaptive linear programming to forgo a percentage of suboptimal rounds (a "budget").
- The problem formulation is interesting and the proposed solutions are novel.
- Reviewer’s main concern: the analysis of boundary cases is unclear—why do these require separate treatment?
- In particular, it’s not explained why the condition ρ = q_j should be treated as a special case, especially for j > 1.
