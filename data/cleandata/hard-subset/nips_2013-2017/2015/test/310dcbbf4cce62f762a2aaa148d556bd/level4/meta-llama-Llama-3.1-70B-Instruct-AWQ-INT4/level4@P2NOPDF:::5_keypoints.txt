- Addresses budget-constrained contextual bandits with discrete contexts and actions.  
- Main contribution: computationally efficient algorithm via LP approximation with logarithmic regret.  
- Paper is well-written and clearly presented.  
- Future work suggestion: use a parametric reward model for scalability to many contexts.  
- Clarify rationale for combining UCB with ALP in Section 4.1 and explicitly connect to "optimism in the face of uncertainty" / UCRL2-style updates.  
- Include numerical experiments in an extended version to evaluate practical performance.  
- Suggests a relevant related work: http://arxiv.org/abs/1506.03374.  
- Overall recommendation: merits acceptance—interesting improvement over existing research.
