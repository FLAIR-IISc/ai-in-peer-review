Given the concise nature of this review, my primary question concerns the justification for analyzing boundary cases separately. The value ρ = q<sub>j</sub> does not appear to possess any unique properties, particularly for j > 1. This paper presents an analysis of contextual bandits with a rigorous baseline—one employing adaptive linear programming to strategically abstain from acting in a predetermined percentage of suboptimal rounds, termed a “budget” by the authors. The setup is compelling, and the proposed solutions are novel.
