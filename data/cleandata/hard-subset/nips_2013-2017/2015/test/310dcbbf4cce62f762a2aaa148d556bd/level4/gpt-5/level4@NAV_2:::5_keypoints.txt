- Studies budget-constrained contextual bandits with discrete contexts and actions.  
- Main contribution: computationally efficient algorithm using adaptive LP relaxation plus UCB-based learning, achieving logarithmic-in-T regret.  
- Paper is clearly written and easy to follow.  
- Suggest exploring parametric reward models for large context spaces in future work.  
- Request clarification in Section 4.1 on the principle behind the UCB-ALP combination—likely optimism under uncertainty similar to UCRL2.  
- Recommend adding numerical experiments in an extended version.  
- Suggest citing and explicitly comparing with http://arxiv.org/abs/1506.03374.  
- Recommendation: accept—provides an efficient algorithm with optimal (logarithmic) regret and meaningful improvement over prior work.
