- Study of a budget-constrained contextual bandit with discrete contexts/actions; main contribution is an LP-based, computationally efficient algorithm achieving O(log T) regret.  
- Paper is well written and easy to follow.  
- Future work: consider a parametric reward model to generalize across many contexts and improve sample efficiency.  
- Clarify the rationale behind the UCB-ALP algorithm in Section 4.1, explicitly connecting it to optimism-in-the-face-of-uncertainty and algorithms like UCRL2.  
- Include numerical experiments in an extended version to demonstrate practical performance and scalability.  
- Add citation to related work: http://arxiv.org/abs/1506.03374.  
- Overall recommendation: merits acceptance—provides an efficient algorithm with (near-)optimal regret and a clear improvement over existing work.
