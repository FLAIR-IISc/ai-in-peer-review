- Studies contextual bandits with budget and time constraints for finite discrete contexts, fixed costs, and a single budget constraint.
- Assumptions simplify the setting but are reasonable for practical scenarios.
- Achieves O(log T) regret via an adaptive linear programming approximation of the oracle.
- Introduces efficient algorithms based on this adaptive LP approximation.
- Paper is well-written, clear, and thoroughly documented with relevant citations and motivation.
- Strong theoretical contribution; appears to be first to attain logarithmic regret in constrained contextual bandits.
- Proofs are convincing, though not every detail was independently verified by the reviewer.
- Practical aspects are only briefly discussed in Sections 5 and 6.
- No experimental results are provided.
- Overall, a good paper that makes suitable simplifying assumptions and could benefit from deeper practical discussion.
