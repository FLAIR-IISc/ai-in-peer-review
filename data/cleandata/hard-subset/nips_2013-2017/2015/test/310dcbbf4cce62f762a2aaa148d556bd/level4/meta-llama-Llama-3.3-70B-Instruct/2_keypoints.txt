- Studies contextual bandits with budget and time constraints using finite discrete contexts, fixed costs, and a single budget—simplified but practical setting.
- Introduces adaptive linear programming approximation of the oracle to achieve O(log T) regret for the proposed algorithms.
- Paper is well-structured, clearly written, and well-motivated with relevant citations, constituting a solid theoretical contribution.
- Practical implications are only briefly discussed (Sections 5 and 6), and the paper lacks experimental results.
- The achievement of logarithmic regret in constrained contextual bandits is significant and, to the reviewer’s knowledge, unprecedented.
- Provided proofs are convincing, although the reviewer did not examine each proof in exhaustive detail.
- Overall assessment: strong paper with appropriate simplifying assumptions, but would benefit from a more in-depth discussion of practical aspects.
