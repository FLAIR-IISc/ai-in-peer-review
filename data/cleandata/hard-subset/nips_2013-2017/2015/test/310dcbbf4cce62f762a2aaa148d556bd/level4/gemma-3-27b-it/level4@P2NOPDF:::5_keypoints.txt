- Addresses constrained contextual bandits with discrete contexts/actions, using an LP approximation for computational efficiency.  
- Main contribution: algorithm achieving a logarithmic (log T) regret bound.  
- Paper is exceptionally well-written and clearly presented.  
- Suggestion: use a parametric reward model for scalability when contexts are many, to enable generalization and reduce computation.  
- Request clearer rationale for combining UCB with ALP in Section 4.1; explicitly state the optimism principle and link to UCRL2-style policy updates.  
- Recommend adding numerical experiments in an extended version to validate theory and assess empirical performance and sensitivity.  
- Suggest considering related work: http://arxiv.org/abs/1506.03374.  
- Overall assessment: significant, efficient contribution with near-optimal regret; recommends acceptance.
