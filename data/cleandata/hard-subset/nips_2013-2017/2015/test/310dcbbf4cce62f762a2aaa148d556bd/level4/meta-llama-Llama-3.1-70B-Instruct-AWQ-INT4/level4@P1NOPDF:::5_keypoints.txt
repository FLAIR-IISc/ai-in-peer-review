- Tackles a budget-constrained contextual bandit problem with discrete contexts and actions.  
- Main contribution: computational efficiency via LP approximation and a logarithmic regret bound.  
- Paper is well-written and easy to follow.  
- Future work: consider parametric reward models for better generalization with many contexts.  
- Suggest clearer explanation of UCB-ALP rationale, highlighting the optimism principle and connection to UCRL2-like updates.  
- Recommend adding numerical experiments in an extended version.  
- Relevant prior work: arXiv:1506.03374.  
- Overall assessment: merits acceptance — efficient algorithm with (mostly) optimal regret and an improvement over existing research.
