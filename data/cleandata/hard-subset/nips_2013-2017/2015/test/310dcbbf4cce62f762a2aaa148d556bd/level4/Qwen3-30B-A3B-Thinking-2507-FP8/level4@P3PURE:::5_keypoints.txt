- Addresses a well-motivated contextual bandit problem with discrete contexts and actions under a budget constraint.  
- Primary contributions: computational efficiency via LP relaxation and a logarithmic regret bound.  
- Paper is well-structured and clearly written.  
- Suggestion: consider a parametric reward model for scalability when the number of contexts is large.  
- Suggestion: clarify rationale for combining UCB with ALP, explicitly connecting it to the “optimism in the face of uncertainty” principle and UCRL2-style updates.  
- Suggestion: include numerical experiments in an extended version to validate practical efficacy.  
- Relevant comparison: arXiv:1506.03374.  
- Overall recommendation: merits acceptance—efficient algorithm with optimal regret guarantees in most settings and meaningful improvement over prior work.
