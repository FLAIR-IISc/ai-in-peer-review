- Proposes a computationally efficient algorithm for budget-constrained contextual bandits with discrete contexts/actions using a linear programming approximation.  
- Algorithm achieves a log(T) regret bound.  
- Paper is very well written and easy to follow.  
- Suggestion: consider a parametric reward model for settings with many contexts.  
- Suggestion: clarify the rationale for UCB-ALP in Section 4.1; likely follows optimism-in-the-face-of-uncertainty and relates to UCRL2 policy updates.  
- Suggestion: include numerical experiments in an extended version.  
- Suggestion: cite related work (http://arxiv.org/abs/1506.03374).  
- Overall: merits acceptance — provides an efficient, often optimal-regret improvement over existing work.
