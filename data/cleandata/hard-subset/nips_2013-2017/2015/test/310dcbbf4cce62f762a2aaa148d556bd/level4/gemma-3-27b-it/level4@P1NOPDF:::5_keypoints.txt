- Addresses a budget-constrained contextual bandit problem with discrete contexts and actions.  
- Main contribution: achieves computational efficiency via an LP approximation and a resulting O(log T) regret bound.  
- Paper is exceptionally well-written and easy to follow.  
- Suggests exploring a parametric reward model for scalability to many contexts.  
- Requests clarification in Section 4.1 on the rationale for combining UCB with ALP (UCB-ALP), noting it appears to use optimism and may relate to UCRL2 policy updates.  
- Recommends adding numerical experiments in an extended version to strengthen results.  
- Provides a relevant literature pointer: http://arxiv.org/abs/1506.03374.  
- Overall recommendation: merits acceptance as an interesting, genuine improvement offering an efficient constrained-CB algorithm with near-optimal regret.
