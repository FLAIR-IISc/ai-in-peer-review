- Addresses constrained contextual bandits with discrete contexts/actions using an LP approximation and achieves a log(T) regret bound.
- Paper is exceptionally well-written and clear.
- Suggests exploring a parametric reward model to handle a large number of contexts.
- Requests elaboration in Section 4.1 on why UCB is combined with the Accelerated Linear Program (ALP) in UCB-ALP, noting the role of optimism.
- Recommends connecting the UCB-ALP policy update to UCRL2-style updates for additional insight.
- Recommends adding numerical experiments to strengthen the paper.
- Points to arXiv:1506.03374 as a relevant reference.
- Overall recommendation: merits acceptance as an efficient, near-optimal improvement over existing approaches.
