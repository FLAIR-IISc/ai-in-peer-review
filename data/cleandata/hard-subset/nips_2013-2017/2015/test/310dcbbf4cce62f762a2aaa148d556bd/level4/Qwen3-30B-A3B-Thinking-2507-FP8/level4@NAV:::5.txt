

This paper addresses a straightforward budget-constrained contextual bandit problem with discrete contexts and actions. The primary contributions are the computational efficiency of the proposed algorithm (achieved through LP approximation) and the established log(T) regret bound. The manuscript is exceptionally well-written and highly accessible.  

Suggestions for improvement:  
1. In future work, consider adopting a parametric reward model to handle large context spaces effectively.  
2. Clarify the rationale for combining UCB with ALP in Section 4.1 (e.g., explicitly state the use of optimism in the face of uncertainty, which may align with policy updates in UCRL2).  
3. Include numerical experiments in an extended version of the paper.  

For relevant context, see: http://arxiv.org/abs/1506.03374. This paper merits acceptance, as it delivers an efficient algorithm for constrained contextual bandits with near-optimal regret in most scenarios, representing a meaningful advancement over prior work.
