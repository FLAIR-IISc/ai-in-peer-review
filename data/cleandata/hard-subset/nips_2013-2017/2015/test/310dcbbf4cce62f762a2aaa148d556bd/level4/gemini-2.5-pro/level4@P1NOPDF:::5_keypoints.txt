- Addresses budget-constrained contextual bandits with discrete contexts/actions.  
- Main contributions: a computationally efficient algorithm using a linear-programming approximation and an O(log T) regret bound.  
- Paper is well-written and easy to follow.  
- Suggestion: extend to parametric reward models to scale beyond the tabular setting with many contexts.  
- Suggestion: more clearly state that UCB-ALP is guided by “optimism in the face of uncertainty” and clarify connections to policy updates like UCRL2.  
- Suggestion: include numerical experiments to validate empirical performance (in an extended version).  
- Suggestion: consider related work http://arxiv.org/abs/1506.03374.  
- Recommendation: merits acceptance; novel, efficient, and near-optimal in many standard cases.
