- Introduces a novel setup comparing contextual bandits to a strong adaptive linear-programming baseline that can abstain from acting a fraction of rounds (“budget”).
- Core idea and proposed solutions are unique and potentially valuable.
- Central aspect of the analysis is unclear: the paper separates boundary cases but does not convincingly justify why.
- Specifically unclear why the case ρ = q_j needs special treatment, especially for j > 1.
- The mathematical presentation does not indicate any inherent singularity or unique properties when ρ = q_j.
- Reviewer requests clarification of the motivation for treating these boundary cases separately; resolving this would significantly strengthen the manuscript.
