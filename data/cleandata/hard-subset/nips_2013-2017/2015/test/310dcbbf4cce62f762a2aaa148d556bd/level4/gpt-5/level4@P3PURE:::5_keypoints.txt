- Studies budget-constrained contextual bandits with discrete contexts and actions.  
- Main contribution: computationally efficient algorithm using an LP approximation that achieves O(log T) regret.  
- Paper is well written and easy to follow.  
- Suggestion: consider parametric reward models for large numbers of contexts in future work.  
- Suggestion: clarify the rationale behind combining UCB with ALP in Section 4.1; it seems to follow optimism and may be equivalent to UCRL2 policy updates.  
- Suggestion: include numerical experiments in an extended version.  
- Suggestion: cite related work (http://arxiv.org/abs/1506.03374).  
- Overall recommendation: merits acceptance; provides an efficient algorithm with (near-)optimal regret and improves on existing work.
