- Studies contextual bandits under both budget and time constraints.
- Analysis restricted to finite/discrete contexts, fixed costs, and a single budget constraint; these simplifying assumptions are reasonable and enable analysis.
- Proposes adaptive linear-programming approximation to the oracle and achieves O(log T) regret.
- Paper is clear, well organized, cites relevant prior work, and states motivation explicitly.
- Solid theoretical contribution; appears to be the first work achieving logarithmic regret for constrained contextual bandits.
- Practical aspects are only briefly discussed and no experimental results are provided.
- Proofs are convincing overall, though the reviewer did not verify every detail.
