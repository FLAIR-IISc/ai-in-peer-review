- Addresses budget-constrained contextual bandits with discrete contexts/actions via a simple, effective framework.  
- Main contribution: computational efficiency through LP approximation and attainment of a log(T) regret bound.  
- Paper is exceptionally well-written and easy to follow.  
- Suggests future work: incorporate a parametric reward model for scalability with many contexts.  
- In Section 4.1, UCB is combined with ALP; clarify the UCB-ALP rationale and explicitly connect it to optimism/UCRL2-style updates.  
- Recommend adding numerical experiments to empirically validate theoretical results.  
- Points to related work: http://arxiv.org/abs/1506.03374 for broader literature context.  
- Overall recommendation: merits acceptance—efficient algorithm that achieves (near-)optimal regret and improves on prior work.
