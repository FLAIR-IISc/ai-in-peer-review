- Addresses contextual bandits with budget and time constraints in a simplified setting: finite discrete contexts, fixed costs, single budget constraint.
- Assumptions are simplified but reasonable for many practical applications.
- Main contribution: algorithms that achieve O(log T) regret via an adaptive linear programming approximation of an oracle policy.
- Solid theoretical contribution: well-written, clearly motivated, and properly situated in the literature.
- Significant novelty: to the reviewer’s knowledge, first work to achieve logarithmic regret for constrained contextual bandits.
- Proofs appear convincing though not verified in full detail by the reviewer.
- Primary weakness: limited treatment of practical aspects—Sections 5 and 6 are brief and there are no experimental results.
- Recommendation: strengthen the paper with a more detailed discussion of practical implications.
