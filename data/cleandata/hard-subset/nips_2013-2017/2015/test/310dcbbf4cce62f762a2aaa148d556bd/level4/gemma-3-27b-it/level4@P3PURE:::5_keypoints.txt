- Tackles budget-constrained contextual bandits with discrete contexts and actions.  
- Main contribution: computationally efficient algorithm via LP approximation achieving O(log T) regret.  
- Paper is exceptionally well-written and easy to follow.  
- Suggest exploring parametric reward models to scale to many contexts.  
- Clarify rationale for combining UCB with ALP in Section 4.1 and state the connection to optimism/UCRL2-style policy updates.  
- Recommend adding numerical experiments in an extended version.  
- Suggest referencing arXiv:1506.03374.  
- Overall recommendation: accept — provides an efficient, near-optimal algorithm and improves on existing work.
