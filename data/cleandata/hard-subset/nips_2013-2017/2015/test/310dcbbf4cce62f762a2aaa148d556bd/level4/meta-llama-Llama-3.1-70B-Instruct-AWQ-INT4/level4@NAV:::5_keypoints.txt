- Tackles a budget-constrained contextual bandit problem with discrete contexts and actions.  
- Main contribution: computationally efficient algorithm via LP approximation achieving O(log T) regret.  
- Paper is exceptionally well-written and easy to follow.  
- Suggestion: consider incorporating a parametric reward model for scalability to many contexts.  
- Suggestion: clarify the rationale for combining UCB with ALP in Section 4.1, noting its link to optimism/UCRL2-style policy updates.  
- Suggestion: include numerical experiments in an extended version.  
- Relevant prior work suggested: http://arxiv.org/abs/1506.03374.  
- Recommendation: accept — offers an efficient, often optimal-regret improvement over existing constrained CB methods.
