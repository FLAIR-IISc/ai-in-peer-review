- Paper studies contextual bandits with budget and time constraints under simplified but reasonable assumptions (finite discrete contexts, fixed costs, single budget).  
- Algorithms achieve O(log T) regret using an adaptive linear programming approximation of the oracle.  
- Paper is well-organized, clearly written, and thoroughly documented with relevant citations and explicit motivation.  
- Theoretical contribution is solid and novel in obtaining logarithmic regret for constrained contextual bandits.  
- Proofs are convincing, though not examined in exhaustive detail by the reviewer.  
- Practical implications are only briefly discussed (Sections 5 and 6).  
- Absence of experimental results is a notable limitation.  
- Overall, the paper makes valuable contributions and invites further exploration of practical aspects.
