- Paper presents an interesting setup and novel solutions for contextual bandits evaluated against a stronger baseline using adaptive linear programming to allow passing a percentage of suboptimal rounds ("budget").
- Positive novel idea: using adaptive LP to decide a budget of allowable suboptimal rounds.
- Major critique: analysis treats boundary cases differently without justification — specifically, it is unclear why the case rho = q_j for j > 1 requires special handling, as it does not seem to have unique properties.
