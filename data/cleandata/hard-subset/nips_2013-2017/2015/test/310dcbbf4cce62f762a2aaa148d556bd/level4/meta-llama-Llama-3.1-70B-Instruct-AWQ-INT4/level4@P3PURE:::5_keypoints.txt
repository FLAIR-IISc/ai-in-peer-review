- Addresses a budget-constrained contextual bandit with discrete contexts/actions.  
- Main contribution: computationally efficient algorithm via LP approximation with O(log T) regret.  
- Paper is well-written and easy to follow.  
- Suggestion: consider a parametric reward model for scalability to many contexts.  
- Suggestion: clarify the rationale of UCB-ALP in Section 4.1 and explicitly connect it to optimism/UCRL2-style updates.  
- Suggestion: include numerical experiments in an extended version.  
- Relevant related work: http://arxiv.org/abs/1506.03374.  
- Overall recommendation: merits acceptance as an efficient, near-optimal improvement over existing work.
