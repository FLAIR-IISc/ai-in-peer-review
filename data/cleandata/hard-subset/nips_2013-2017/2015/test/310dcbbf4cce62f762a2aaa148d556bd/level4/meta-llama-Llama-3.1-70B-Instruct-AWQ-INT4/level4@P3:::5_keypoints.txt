- Problem: budget-constrained contextual bandits with discrete contexts and actions.  
- Main contribution: computationally efficient algorithm using LP approximation.  
- Theoretical result: algorithm (UCB-ALP) achieves logarithmic regret in most cases.  
- Presentation: paper is well-written, clear, and easy to follow.  
- Strength: novel, efficient method for constrained contextual bandits with strong regret guarantees.  
- Strength: LP approximation is a clever idea with thorough performance analysis.  
- Suggestion: consider more complex/parametric reward models as contexts scale.  
- Suggestion: clarify rationale for combining UCB with ALP, linking to optimism/UCRL2.  
- Suggestion: include numerical experiments to illustrate theoretical results.  
- Related work: should cite "Contextual bandits with global constraints and objective" (arXiv:1506.03374).  
- Overall recommendation: merits acceptance as a significant improvement over existing work.  
- Rating: 8/10; Confidence: 9/10.
