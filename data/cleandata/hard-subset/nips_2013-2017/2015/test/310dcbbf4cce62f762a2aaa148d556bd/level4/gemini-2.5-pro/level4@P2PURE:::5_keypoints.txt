- Problem: budget-constrained contextual bandit with discrete contexts and actions.  
- Main contributions: computationally efficient algorithm using a linear-programming approximation and an O(log T) regret bound.  
- Presentation: paper is exceptionally well-written and clear.  
- Suggestion: extend framework to parametric reward models for large numbers of contexts.  
- Suggestion: in Section 4.1, more explicitly justify combining UCB with the ALP (optimism principle) and discuss possible equivalence to UCRL2.  
- Suggestion: include numerical experiments to strengthen empirical validation.  
- Related work: consider citing http://arxiv.org/abs/1506.03374.  
- Recommendation: merits acceptance—provides an efficient algorithm achieving optimal regret in most cases and improves on prior approaches.
