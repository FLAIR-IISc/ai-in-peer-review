- Addresses a budget-constrained contextual bandit with discrete contexts/actions.  
- Main contribution: computational efficiency via LP approximation and achieving log(T) regret.  
- Paper is well-written and easy to follow.  
- Suggests future work: use parametric reward models for many-context settings.  
- Section 4.1 (UCB-ALP) needs clearer explanation of its rationale and connection to optimism/UCRL2.  
- Recommends adding numerical experiments and cites http://arxiv.org/abs/1506.03374 as relevant.  
- Overall recommendation: accept — offers an efficient, often optimal algorithm improving prior work.
