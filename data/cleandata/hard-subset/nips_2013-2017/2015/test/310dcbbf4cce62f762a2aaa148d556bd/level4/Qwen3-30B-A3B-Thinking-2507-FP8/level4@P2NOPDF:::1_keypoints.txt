- Novelty unclear: reduction to JK arms (finite contexts J and actions K) aligns with existing theory and may render the contribution redundant; authors must explain why this reduction doesn’t trivialize the work.  
- Regret bound concern (Theorem 2): the O(√T + ...) term likely omits dependence on K and should scale as O(√(KT)); derivation needs verification.  
- Problem-dependent bounds lacking: current O(KJ log T) or O(√T + KJ log T) bounds are not gap-refined; need gap-dependent bounds (e.g., ∑ 1/Δ_i) for tighter characterization.  
- Proof approach suboptimal: reliance on UCB order events rather than gap concentration seems to prevent refined, gap-dependent bounds; supplementary analysis should be reworked.  
- Lower bounds missing/refinement needed: provide matching problem-dependent and problem-independent lower bounds that account for context complexity (J) instead of trivial static-context bounds.  
- Overlap with prior work: the setting is closely related to budgeted/action-constrained bandits, which undermines perceived novelty—authors must clarify advances beyond existing finite-armed constrained bandit results.  
- Overall: technically sound and well written but insufficiently novel; key deficiencies (gap-dependent bounds and refined lower bounds) must be addressed before stronger endorsement.
