- It is unclear why the condition ρ = q_j merits separate handling for j > 1.  
- The paper analyzes the contextual bandit problem against a stronger-than-usual baseline.  
- The baseline employs adaptive linear programming to strategically skip a portion of suboptimal rounds.  
- The authors term this skipping mechanism a "budget."  
- The problem formulation is interesting and the proposed solutions are unique.
