- Addresses constrained contextual bandits with discrete contexts/actions, using LP approximation for computational efficiency and achieving a log(T) regret bound as the main contribution.
- Manuscript is exceptionally well-written and clearly presented.
- Suggest exploring a parametric reward model for scalability when there are many contexts.
- Clarify motivation for combining UCB with ALP in UCB-ALP (relate to optimism in the face of uncertainty and possible equivalence to UCRL2 policy updates).
- Add numerical experiments in an extended version to strengthen empirical validation.
- Consider related work: http://arxiv.org/abs/1506.03374.
- Overall recommendation: accept — provides an efficient, near-optimal-regret algorithm and a notable improvement over existing approaches.
