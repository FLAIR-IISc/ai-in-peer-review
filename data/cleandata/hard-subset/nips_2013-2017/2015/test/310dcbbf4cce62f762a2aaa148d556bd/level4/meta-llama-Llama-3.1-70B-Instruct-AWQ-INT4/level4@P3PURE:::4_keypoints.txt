- Paper proposes an interesting setup and unique solutions for evaluating contextual bandits against a stronger baseline.  
- Baseline uses adaptive linear programming to decide whether to skip a certain percentage of suboptimal rounds, called the "budget."  
- Reviewer questions the treatment of boundary cases in the analysis.  
- Unclear why the case rho = q_j needs special handling, especially for j > 1.  
- The reviewer argues rho = q_j does not appear to have distinct properties that justify a separate analysis and requests clarification.
