- Addresses contextual bandits under budget and time constraints.
- Analysis restricted to finite discrete contexts, fixed costs, and a single budget constraint (simplified but practical).
- Proposes an adaptive linear programming approximation of the oracle.
- Algorithms achieve O(log T) regret.
- Well-written, thoroughly documented, cites relevant prior work, and provides clear motivation.
- Strong theoretical contribution; claimed first to achieve logarithmic regret in constrained contextual bandits.
- Proofs appear sound though not fully verified by the reviewer.
- Practical considerations are only briefly discussed (Sections 5–6) and no experimental results are provided; more practical discussion would improve the paper.
