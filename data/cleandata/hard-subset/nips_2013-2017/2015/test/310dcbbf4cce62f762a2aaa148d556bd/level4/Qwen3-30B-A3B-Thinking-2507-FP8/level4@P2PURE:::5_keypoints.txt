- Problem: constrained contextual bandits with discrete contexts and actions under a budget constraint.  
- Main contribution: computationally efficient algorithm via linear-program approximation with a log T regret bound.  
- Presentation: paper is exceptionally well-written and clear.  
- Suggestion: consider a parametric reward model when the context space is large.  
- Suggestion: clarify why UCB is combined with ALP in Section 4.1—appears to follow optimism and may be equivalent to UCRL2-style policy updates.  
- Suggestion: add numerical experiments in an extended version.  
- Related work: cite/compare to http://arxiv.org/abs/1506.03374.  
- Recommendation: merits acceptance — meaningful advance with near-optimal regret in most cases.
