- Paper analyzes contextual bandits against a stronger baseline that uses adaptive linear programming to pass on a fixed percentage of suboptimal rounds ("budget").

- The problem setup is interesting and the proposed algorithms/solutions are novel.

- Main concern: the analysis treats boundary cases separately but lacks sufficient justification.

- Specifically unclear why the case rho = q_j requires separate treatment, especially for j > 1, since that value doesn’t appear inherently special.
