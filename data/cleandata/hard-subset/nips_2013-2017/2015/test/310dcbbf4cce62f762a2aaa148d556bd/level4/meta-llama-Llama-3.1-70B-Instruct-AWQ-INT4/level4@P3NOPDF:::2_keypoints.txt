- Studies contextual bandits with budget and time constraints under finite discrete contexts, fixed costs, and a single budget constraint.  
- These simplifying assumptions are reasonable and enable tractable analysis.  
- Introduces algorithms using an adaptive linear programming approximation of the oracle.  
- Achieves O(log T) regret, a novel theoretical result for constrained contextual bandits.  
- Paper is well-written, clear, and thoroughly documented with relevant citations and explicit motivation.  
- Proofs are convincing, though not every detail was independently verified by the reviewer.  
- Practical aspects are only briefly discussed (Sections 5 and 6) and lack depth.  
- No experimental results are provided to support theoretical findings.  
- Recommendation: Accept with minor revisions to add experiments and expand discussion of practical implications.  
- Overall rating: 8/10.
