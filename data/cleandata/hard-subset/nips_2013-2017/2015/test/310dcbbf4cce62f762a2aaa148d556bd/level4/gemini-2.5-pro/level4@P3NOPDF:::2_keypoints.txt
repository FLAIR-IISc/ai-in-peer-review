- Tackles contextual bandits with budget and time constraints (finite discrete contexts, fixed action costs, single budget) and proposes an adaptive LP-based approximation achieving O(log T) regret.
- Novel and significant theoretical contribution: first to achieve logarithmic regret for constrained contextual bandits.
- Paper is clearly written, well-motivated, and positions itself effectively within prior work.
- Technical approach is elegant and principled; proofs appear sound and convincing.
- Assumptions (finite contexts, fixed costs, single budget) are reasonable and justified for tractability.
- Limited discussion of practical applicability, implementation challenges, and LP computational complexity/scalability.
- No experimental validation; empirical results (even synthetic) and comparisons to baselines are recommended.
- Overall assessment: strong theoretical paper with minor practical/empirical shortcomings; recommendation to accept.
