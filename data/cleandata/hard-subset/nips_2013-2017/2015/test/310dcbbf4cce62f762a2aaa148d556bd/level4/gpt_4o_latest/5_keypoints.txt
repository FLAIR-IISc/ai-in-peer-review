- Addresses a budget-constrained contextual bandit problem with discrete contexts and actions.  
- Main contribution: computational efficiency via LP approximation and a log(T) regret bound.  
- Paper is well-written and easy to understand.  
- Suggestion: incorporate a parametric model for the reward function when contexts are numerous.  
- Suggestion: clarify the integration of UCB with ALP in Section 4.1 and the reasoning behind the UCB-ALP algorithm.  
- Observation: the approach appears to follow optimism-in-the-face-of-uncertainty and may be analogous to UCRL2 policy updates—explain this connection.  
- Suggestion: include numerical experiments in an extended version.  
- Suggestion: cite/consider the related paper http://arxiv.org/abs/1506.03374.  
- Overall recommendation: accept — introduces an efficient algorithm with optimal regret in most scenarios and is a meaningful improvement.
