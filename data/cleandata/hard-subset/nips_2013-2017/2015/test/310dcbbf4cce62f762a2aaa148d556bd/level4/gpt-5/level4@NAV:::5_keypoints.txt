- Studies a budget-constrained contextual bandit problem with discrete contexts and actions.  
- Main contribution: computational efficiency via an LP approximation plus a log(T) regret guarantee.  
- Paper is clearly written and easy to follow.  
- Suggest future work: consider a parametric reward model for large numbers of contexts.  
- Suggest clarifying the rationale for combining UCB with ALP in Section 4.1 — appears to follow optimism-in-the-face-of-uncertainty, akin to UCRL2.  
- Recommend adding numerical experiments in an extended version.  
- Suggest citing/consulting arXiv:1506.03374.  
- Overall recommendation: merits acceptance — provides an efficient algorithm with near-optimal regret and improves existing work.
