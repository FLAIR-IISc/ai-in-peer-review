- Examines budget-constrained contextual bandits with discrete contexts and actions.  
- Primary contribution: computationally efficient algorithm via LP approximation achieving log(T) regret.  
- Manuscript is exceptionally well-structured and clear.  
- Suggests future work: incorporate a parametric reward model for scalability to many contexts.  
- Requests clarification in Section 4.1 on why UCB is combined with ALP—likely due to optimism in the face of uncertainty and possibly equivalent to UCRL2 policy updates.  
- Recommends adding numerical experiments in an extended version.  
- Suggests citing/considering relevance of http://arxiv.org/abs/1506.03374.  
- Recommendation: accept—provides an efficient, mostly optimal-regret algorithm and notable improvement over prior work.
