- Studies contextual bandits with budget and time constraints for finite discrete contexts, fixed costs, and a single budget limit.  
- Assumptions simplify the setting but are practically relevant.  
- Proposes algorithms using an adaptive linear programming approximation of the optimal policy.  
- Achieves O(log T) regret bounds.  
- Paper is clearly written, well-documented, and properly cites related work.  
- Strong theoretical contribution and likely first to prove logarithmic regret for constrained contextual bandits.  
- Proofs are convincing though not exhaustively verified by the reviewer.  
- Limited discussion of practical considerations (Sections 5–6) and no experimental validation.  
- Recommends further discussion of practical implications and potential extensions.
