- Studies contextual bandits with both budget and time constraints.
- Analysis limited to finite discrete contexts, fixed costs, and a single budget constraint.
- Simplifying assumptions are reasonable in practice despite the restricted setting.
- Introduces an adaptive linear programming approximation of the oracle.
- Obtains O(log T) regret for constrained contextual bandits.
- Paper is clear, well documented, and cites relevant prior work with explicit motivation.
- Theoretical contributions are significant and novel (first to achieve logarithmic regret in this setting).
- Proofs are convincing overall, though not exhaustively checked by the reviewer.
- Practical aspects are only briefly discussed in Sections 5 and 6.
- No experimental results are presented; practical discussion should be expanded.
