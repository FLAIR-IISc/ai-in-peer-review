**Summary**

The paper proposes a novel approach to graph transduction using orthonormal representations, which are shown to be PAC learnable. The authors introduce a spectral norm regularized formulation, SPORE, for graph transduction, which is derived from a new PAC bound. They also propose an efficient algorithm, Infeasible Inexact Proximal (IIP), to solve the SPORE formulation, which is shown to converge at a rate of O(1/√T). The paper also extends the analysis to the multiple graph setting and proposes an MKL-SPORE formulation.
