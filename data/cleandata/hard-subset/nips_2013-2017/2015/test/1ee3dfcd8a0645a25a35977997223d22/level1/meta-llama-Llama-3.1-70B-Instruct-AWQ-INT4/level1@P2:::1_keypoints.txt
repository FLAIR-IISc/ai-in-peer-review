- Presents SPORE, a novel spectral-norm-regularized orthonormal embedding approach for graph transduction.  
- Provides a generalization bound and labelled sample complexity estimate showing SPORE is efficiently PAC learnable.  
- Proposes IIP, an inexact proximal method to solve SPORE, with a proven convergence rate of O(1/√T).  
- Extends the IIP analysis and sample complexity results to multiple graph transduction.  
- Experimental evaluation on real and synthetic graphs shows SPORE outperforms existing methods.  
- Paper is well-written with consistent mathematical notation and clear explanations.  
- Technical analysis is thorough and the approach is sound.  
- The work is original and significant to the graph transduction field.
