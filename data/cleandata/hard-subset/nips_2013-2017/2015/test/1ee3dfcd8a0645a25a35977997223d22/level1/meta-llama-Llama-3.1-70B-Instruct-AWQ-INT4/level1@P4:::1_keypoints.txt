- Introduces a novel graph transduction approach using orthonormal representations and shows these are PAC learnable via a generalization bound linking low spectral norm + empirical error to better generalization.  
- Derives a spectral-norm-regularized formulation, SPORE, from the generalization bound.  
- Proposes IIP, an efficient inexact-proximal algorithm to solve the SPORE formulation.  
- Extends SPORE to the multi-graph setting as MKL-SPORE.  
- Paper is well-written with a clear, concise presentation of contributions.  
- Technical content is sound and thoroughly analyzed.  
- Experimental results validate the effectiveness of the approach.  
- Notation is at times overwhelming and needs clearer explanation.  
- Related work section is not comprehensive and should be expanded.  
- Experiments are limited to a few datasets and require broader evaluation.
