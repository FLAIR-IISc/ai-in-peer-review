**Summary of the Paper**

The paper proposes a novel approach to graph transduction by optimizing orthonormal graph embeddings. The authors derive a new generalization bound based on the maximum eigenvalue of the kernel matrix, which leads to a convex optimization criterion involving the spectral norm of the kernel matrix. They propose an efficient algorithm, SPORE, to solve this optimization problem using inexact proximal methods. The paper also introduces an extension of SPORE to the multiple-kernel setting, called MKL-SPORE. The authors demonstrate the effectiveness of their approach through experiments on various tasks, showing significant performance improvements over prior methods.
