**Summary**

The paper proposes a novel approach to graph transduction using orthonormal representations. The authors show that these representations are PAC learnable and introduce a spectral norm regularized formulation, SPORE, for graph transduction. They also develop an efficient first-order method, IIP, to solve for SPORE. The algorithm and analysis are extended to the multiple graphs setting.

The experimental results demonstrate the effectiveness of SPORE in graph transduction tasks, outperforming existing methods. The authors also show that combining multiple graphs using the proposed MKL-SPORE formulation improves classification accuracy.
