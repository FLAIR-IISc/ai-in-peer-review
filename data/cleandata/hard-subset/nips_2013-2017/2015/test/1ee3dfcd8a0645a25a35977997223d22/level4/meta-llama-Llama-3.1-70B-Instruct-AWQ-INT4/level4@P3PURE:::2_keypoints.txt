- Tackles learning vertex labels on graphs, extending Ando and Zhang's idea of spherical orthonormal embeddings for improved generalization.  
- Proposes a joint optimization that learns embeddings and labels, with a regularizer equal to the spectral norm of the graph kernel matrix motivated by Theorem 1's generalization bound.  
- Derives an asymptotic upper bound on the method's generalization error with favorable scaling and translates it into a labeled sample complexity bound.  
- The optimization can be formulated as an SDP, but SDPs are computationally heavy.  
- Proposes an inexact proximal method to scale optimization to graphs with thousands of vertices.  
- Experiments show the proposed method outperforms previous approaches.
