- Studies optimization of orthonormal graph embeddings to improve learning of functions on graphs.
- Reviews Laplacian-based prior work and associated generalization bound (eqn. 2).
- Derives a new generalization bound based on the maximum eigenvalue of the kernel matrix (eqn. 4).
- Introduces a novel optimization criterion motivated by the new bound (eqn. 5).
- Observes λ1(K) is non-differentiable but the overall criterion is convex as a sum of convex functions.
- Solves the resulting SEER problem using inexact proximal methods.
- Extends approach to MKL-SEER and reports significant empirical performance improvements.
- Claims strong theoretical foundations and methodological ingenuity.
- Presents a clear, complete paper that advances prior graph transduction embedding methods.
