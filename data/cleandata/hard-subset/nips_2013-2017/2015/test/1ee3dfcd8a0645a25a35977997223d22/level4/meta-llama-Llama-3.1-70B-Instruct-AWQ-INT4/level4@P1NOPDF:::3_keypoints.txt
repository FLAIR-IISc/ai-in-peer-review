- Investigates optimization of orthonormal graph embeddings and their impact on learning functions on graphs.
- Reviews prior Laplacian-based generalization bound (eqn. 2).
- Derives a new bound based on the maximum eigenvalue of the kernel matrix (eqn. 4).
- Proposes an alternative optimization criterion (eqn. 5) from that bound.
- Notes λ1(K) is non-differentiable but the resulting optimization criterion is convex (sum of convex functions).
- Solves the optimization with inexact proximal methods specifically designed for SEER.
- Experimental results show SEER and MKL-SEER yield significant performance improvements.
- Provides strong theoretical justification and several clever methodological insights.
- Paper is complete, well-explained, and a valuable advancement over prior approaches.
