- Addresses learning vertex labels on graphs, building on Ando & Zhang's unit-sphere embedding idea and providing a concrete embedding choice.

- Main contribution: a joint optimization formulation for embedding and labels with a regularizer equal to the spectral norm of the graph kernel, motivated by Theorem 1's generalization bound.

- Provides asymptotic upper bounds on generalization error and corresponding labeled sample complexity with favorable scaling versus prior work.

- Notes the optimization can be written as an SDP, but proposes an inexact proximal method to scale to graphs with thousands of vertices.

- Experimental results suggest the proposed method outperforms previous methods.
