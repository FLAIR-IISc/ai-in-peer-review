- Problem: Optimize orthonormal graph embedding and its effect on learning functions on the graph (graph transduction).
- Background: Reviews prior Laplacian-based work and corresponding generalization bound (Eq. 2).
- Theory: Introduces a new generalization bound based on the maximum eigenvalue of the kernel matrix (Eq. 4).
- Criterion: Derives an alternative optimization objective from the new bound (Eq. 5).
- Convexity: Notes λ1(K) is non-differentiable but the overall objective remains convex as a sum of convex functions.
- Optimization: Proposes and implements an inexact proximal method solver for SEER.
- Experiments: Evaluates SEER and MKL-SEER across multiple tasks and reports significant performance gains.
- Contribution: Presents a principled, challenging convex formulation justified by theory.
- Impact & presentation: Demonstrates meaningful advancement over prior methods with convincing empirical results; paper is complete and well explained.
