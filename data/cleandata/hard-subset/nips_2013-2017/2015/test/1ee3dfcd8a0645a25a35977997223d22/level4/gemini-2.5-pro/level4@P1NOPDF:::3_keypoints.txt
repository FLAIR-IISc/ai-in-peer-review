- Addresses optimization of orthonormal graph embeddings to improve learned function performance in graph transduction.
- Reviews prior Laplacian-based work and its generalization bound (Eq. 2).
- Derives a novel generalization bound based on the maximum eigenvalue of the kernel matrix (Eq. 4).
- Proposes an alternative optimization criterion driven by the new bound (Eq. 5).
- Notes non-differentiability of the λ₁(K) term but shows the overall objective is convex as a sum of convex functions.
- Introduces SEER to solve the resulting optimization problem using inexact proximal methods.
- Extends the approach to multiple kernel learning with MKL-SEER.
- Experimental results demonstrate significant performance improvements over existing methods on various tasks.
- Theoretical justification and insights (novel criterion and solution approach) are strong and well-motivated.
- Paper is complete, clearly explained, and represents a significant advancement over prior approaches.
