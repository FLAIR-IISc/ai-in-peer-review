- Studies optimization of orthonormal graph embeddings and their effect on learning functions defined on graphs.
- Reviews Laplacian-based methods and the existing generalization bound (equation 2).
- Proposes a novel generalization bound based on the maximum eigenvalue of the kernel matrix (equation 4).
- Derives an alternative optimization criterion (equation 5); although λ1(K) is non-differentiable, the overall criterion is convex as a sum of convex functions.
- Solves the resulting SEER optimization problem using inexact proximal methods.
- Empirical evaluation of SEER and MKL-SEER shows notable performance improvements across multiple problems.
- Paper offers solid theoretical foundations and clever methodological contributions.
- Presents a significant improvement over prior approaches with comprehensive theoretical justification and empirical evidence.
