- Extends Ando & Zhang: studies vertex label learning via unit-sphere orthonormal embeddings and tackles optimal embedding selection.  
- Main contribution: novel optimization that jointly learns embeddings and vertex labels using a regularizer equal to the spectral norm of the graph kernel matrix.  
- Theorem 1 motivates the regularizer by providing an upper bound on generalization error.  
- Derives an asymptotic upper bound on generalization error with favorable scaling relative to prior work, and a labeled sample complexity bound.  
- Although the optimization can be cast as an SDP, the paper proposes an inexact proximal method to scale to graphs with thousands of vertices.  
- Empirical results show the proposed method outperforms existing approaches.
