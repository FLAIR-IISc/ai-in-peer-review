- Motivation for SEER and advantages of enforcing an orthonormal embedding need clearer, more convincing articulation.
- The paper presents an elegant, original algorithm (IIP) to approximately solve the SEER problem and is a promising contribution.
- Experimental results generally support the approach but should include ablations isolating benefits of SEER and the orthonormal representation (eq. 3).
- Report the approximation accuracy of the IIP solution relative to the true SEER objective; this is currently missing.
- The multi-graph transduction extension is presented at a high level and lacks sufficient detail, motivation, intuition, and implementation specifics.
- The multi-graph experiments are hard to assess (e.g., Table 4 noisy case where one graph outperforms multiple graphs is counterintuitive).
- The paper is dense and sometimes difficult to read, over-relying on supplementary material which hurts accessibility and visibility.
- Overall: proposes SEER for orthonormal embeddings, introduces an IIP-based solver, and provides reasonably convincing empirical results.
