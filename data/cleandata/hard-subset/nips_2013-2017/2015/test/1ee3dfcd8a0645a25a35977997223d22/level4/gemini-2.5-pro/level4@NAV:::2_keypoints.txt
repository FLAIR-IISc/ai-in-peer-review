- Tackles vertex label learning on graphs by extending Ando & Zhang's spherical orthonormal graph embedding approach.
- Main contribution: joint optimization that learns graph embedding and vertex labels via a novel regularizer—the spectral norm of the graph kernel matrix.
- Theorem 1 motivates the regularizer by providing an upper bound on generalization error.
- Provides an asymptotic upper bound on generalization error with improved scaling, yielding an upper bound on labeled sample complexity.
- Original SDP formulation is computationally prohibitive for large graphs.
- Proposes an inexact proximal method to scale the approach to graphs with thousands of vertices.
- Empirical results claim the proposed method outperforms prior approaches.
