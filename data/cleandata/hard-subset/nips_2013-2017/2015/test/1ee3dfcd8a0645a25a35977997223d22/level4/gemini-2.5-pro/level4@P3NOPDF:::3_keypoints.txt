- Addresses optimizing orthonormal graph embeddings for function learning in graph transduction and introduces SEER.
- Derives a new generalization bound based on the maximum eigenvalue of the kernel matrix (Equation 4).
- Proposes an optimization criterion to minimize the kernel's maximum eigenvalue (Equation 5) despite non-differentiability.
- Notes the overall objective is a sum of convex functions, making the problem tractable.
- Solves the challenging optimization via inexact proximal methods.
- Extends the approach to multiple kernel learning (MKL-SEER).
- Provides comprehensive experiments showing consistent, significant performance improvements over prior methods.
- Strong theoretical justification linking Laplacian-based bounds to the new eigenvalue-based bound.
- Novel and clever formulation with principled handling of optimization challenges.
- Paper is complete, clearly written, and effectively bridges theory and practice.
- Overall assessment: significant, well-executed contribution with strong empirical validation; strong recommendation for acceptance.
