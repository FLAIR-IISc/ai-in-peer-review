- Focus: optimization of an orthonormal graph embedding and its impact on learning functions on graphs.
- Reviews prior Laplacian-based generalization bound (Eq. 2).
- Introduces a new bound based on the maximum eigenvalue of the kernel matrix (Eq. 4).
- Derives an alternative optimization criterion from that bound (Eq. 5).
- Notes λ1(K) is nondifferentiable but the overall objective is convex as a sum of convex functions.
- Solves the SEER problem using inexact proximal methods.
- Experiments: SEER and MKL-SEER applied to multiple tasks and show significant performance improvements.
- Theoretical justification is strong and the paper makes several insightful methodological moves.
- Tackles a difficult optimization problem and provides convincing real-world applications.
- Represents a meaningful advancement over prior approaches; the paper is complete and clearly explained.
