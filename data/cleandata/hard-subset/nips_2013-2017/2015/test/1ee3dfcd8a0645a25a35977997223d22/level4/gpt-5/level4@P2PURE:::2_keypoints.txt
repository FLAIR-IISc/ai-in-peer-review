- Paper addresses vertex label prediction on graphs, extending Ando & Zhang by proposing a concrete optimization to jointly learn an embedding and labels with a new regularizer (spectral norm of the graph kernel matrix) motivated by Theorem 1.  
- Theorem 1 provides an upper bound on generalization error that motivates the spectral-norm regularizer.  
- The paper derives asymptotic upper bounds on generalization error with improved scaling over prior work, which also yield labeled sample complexity bounds.  
- The optimization can be posed as an SDP, but the authors propose an inexact proximal method to scale to graphs with thousands of vertices.  
- Experiments show the proposed method can outperform prior approaches.
