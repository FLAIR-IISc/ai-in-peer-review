- Proposes a novel spectral regularized embedding framework (SEER) for graph transduction.  
- Justification for using an orthonormal embedding is insufficiently developed.  
- Presents a compelling, original IIP algorithm to approximate SEER solutions.  
- Experimental results generally support the approach but lack ablation isolating SEER and the orthonormal representation.  
- Needs quantitative evaluation of IIP’s approximation accuracy relative to the SEER objective.  
- Multiple-graph extension is described too abstractly; it needs more motivation, implementation details, and intuition.  
- Counter-intuitive results in multi-graph experiments (single graphs outperforming multiple in noisy case) are hard to interpret.  
- Paper readability suffers from high density and excessive reliance on supplementary material.
