- Problem: learning an optimal orthonormal embedding for a graph to improve graph-based transduction tasks.
- Prior work: reviews graph Laplacian approaches and their generalization bound (Eq. 2).
- Novel bound: introduces a new generalization bound based on the maximum eigenvalue of the kernel matrix (Eq. 4).
- Optimization criterion: derives an alternative objective motivated by the new bound (Eq. 5).
- Non-differentiability issue: λ1(K) is not differentiable, but the overall objective remains convex as a sum of convex functions.
- Algorithm: proposes SEER and a multiple-kernel variant MKL-SEER, solved via inexact proximal methods.
- Experiments: empirical evaluation shows significant performance improvements over competing methods.
- Overall assessment: strong theoretical justification, clever formulation, effective handling of optimization challenges, and convincing practical results—complete and well-explained contribution.
