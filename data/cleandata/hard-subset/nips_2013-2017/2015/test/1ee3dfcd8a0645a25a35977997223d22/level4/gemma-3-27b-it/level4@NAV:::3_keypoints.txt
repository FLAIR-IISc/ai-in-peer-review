- Studies optimization of orthonormal graph embeddings and their impact on learning functions on graphs.
- Reviews Laplacian-based related work and existing generalization bounds (eqn. 2).
- Introduces a new generalization bound based on the maximum eigenvalue of the kernel matrix (eqn. 4).
- Derives a novel convex optimization criterion motivated by that bound, despite λ₁(K) being non-differentiable (eqn. 5).
- Proposes the SEER algorithm and solves it using inexact proximal methods.
- Extends method to MKL-SEER and evaluates both empirically across multiple datasets.
- Empirical results show substantial performance gains over existing methods for graph transduction.
- Paper is theoretically sound, innovative, clearly written, and presents a notable practical improvement.
