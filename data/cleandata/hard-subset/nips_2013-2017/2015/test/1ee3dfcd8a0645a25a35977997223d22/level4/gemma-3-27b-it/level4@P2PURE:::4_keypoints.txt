- Introduces a novel spectral-regularized embedding framework for graph transduction; orthonormal embedding needs stronger justification (minor concern).  
- Proposes an interesting, elegant IIP algorithm to approximately solve SEER; original contribution and promising alternative.  
- Experimental results generally support the proposed solution.  
- Lacks experiments isolating the benefits of the SEER framework and the orthonormal representation (eq. 3).  
- Does not evaluate the approximation accuracy of the IIP solution with respect to the original SEER problem.  
- Multiple-graph extension is described at a high level and needs more detailed explanation, motivation, intuition, and implementation specifics.  
- Table 4 (noisy case) is counterintuitive: single graph outperforming multiple graphs contradicts the motivation for multi-graph methods.  
- Readability is hampered by paper density and heavy reliance on supplementary material, suggesting the paper may be overpacked.  
- Overall: solid novel framework and algorithm with reasonably convincing experiments, but requires clarifications and additional evaluations to strengthen the contribution.
