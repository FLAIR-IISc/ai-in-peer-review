- Proposes SEER with orthonormal embeddings; idea is promising but the benefits are insufficiently justified (minor concern).  
- IIP algorithm is an original, compelling approximate solver and a clear contribution.  
- Experimental evaluation is weak and needs substantial refinement to validate claims.  
- Experiments fail to isolate the contribution of SEER or the orthonormal representation from the full pipeline.  
- No quantitative analysis comparing IIP’s approximation accuracy to the exact SEER solution, risking presentation as a heuristic.  
- Multi-graph extension is described superficially with poor methodological clarity and insufficient motivation.  
- Table 4 (noisy case) shows single-graph outperforming multi-graph, contradicting the paper’s motivation and requiring explanation.  
- Manuscript readability suffers from excessive density and over-reliance on supplementary material for key technical details.  
- Key derivations and implementation details (orthonormal constraint, multi-graph algorithm) should be moved into the main text for clarity.  
- Recommended revisions: demonstrate SEER/orthonormal benefits separately, justify and fix multi-graph results, and streamline the main paper.
