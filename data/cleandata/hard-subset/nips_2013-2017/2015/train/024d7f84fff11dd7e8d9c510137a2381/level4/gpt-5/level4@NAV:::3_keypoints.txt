- Main contribution: derives the form of the gradient for both dynamic and static risk-averse MDPs.  
- Extends prior CVaR-specific results to a broader class of risk measures (modest but useful generalization).  
- Outcome is largely a straightforward extension of the risk-neutral policy gradient.  
- Presentation: paper is well structured, clearly written, and easy to follow.  
- Correctness: results appear correct to the reviewer’s knowledge.  
- Minor issue: Theorem 2.1 notation “ξ P_θ” is unclear; recommend adopting the notation from Theorem 6.6 in [26].
