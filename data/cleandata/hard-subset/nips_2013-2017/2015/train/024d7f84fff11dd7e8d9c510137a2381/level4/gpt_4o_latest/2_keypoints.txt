- Introduces methods for estimating policy gradients under coherent risk measures and proposes corresponding RL algorithms.  
- Extends prior work from specific risk measures to the broader class of coherent risk measures, offering a unified framework.  
- Demonstrates the algorithm on a toy finance example where assets have individual return distributions.  
- Paper is well-motivated with algorithms logically derived from motivation and prior research.  
- Experiment is simple and static but effectively showcases risk-averse behavior and adaptability to different risk measures.  
- Equations are technically sound and the manuscript includes comprehensive literature references.  
- Clarity is exceptional, well above average.  
- Original contribution: novel algorithms for risk-sensitive RL and a non-trivial extension to coherent risk measures.  
- Significance: makes meaningful contributions to risk-sensitive RL, providing well-defined algorithms and insights into trained policy behavior.
