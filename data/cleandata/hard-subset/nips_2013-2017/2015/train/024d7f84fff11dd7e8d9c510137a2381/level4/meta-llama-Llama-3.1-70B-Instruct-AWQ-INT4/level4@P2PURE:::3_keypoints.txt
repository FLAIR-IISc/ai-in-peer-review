- Paper analyzes risk-averse MDPs with static and dynamically consistent risk measures, generalizing prior CVaR-focused work.  
- Main contribution: derives the form of the policy gradient for both dynamic and static risk settings.  
- Contribution is minor but useful, essentially a straightforward extension of risk-neutral policy gradient results.  
- Paper is well-organized, well-written, and accessible.  
- Results appear correct based on reviewer verification.  
- Notation concern: the use of ξP_θ in Theorem 2.1 is potentially confusing; recommend adopting the notation from Theorem 6.6 of [26].  
- Typo on line 141 ("risk enevlop[e]") needs correction.  
- Clarification needed in Section 3: MDPs with Markov risk measures are generally tractable; reported intractability stems from policy parametrization, not the risk measure.  
- Overall assessment: a minor but solid and valuable extension of existing results.
