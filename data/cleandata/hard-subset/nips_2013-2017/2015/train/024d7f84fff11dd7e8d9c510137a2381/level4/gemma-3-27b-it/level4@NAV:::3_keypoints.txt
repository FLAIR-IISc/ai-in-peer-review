- Paper derives gradient formulas for both dynamic and static risk-averse MDPs using static and dynamically consistent risk measures.
- Main contribution generalizes prior CVaR-focused work, providing a modest but valuable extension.
- Results are essentially straightforward adaptations of standard policy gradient methods from risk-neutral settings.
- Paper is clearly structured, well-written, and easy to understand.
- Reviewer finds the presented results accurate.
- Suggest clarifying the notation \xi P_\theta in Theorem 2.1 (align with Theorem 6.6 of [26]).
- Minor typo at Line 141 ("risk enevlop[e]").
- Recommend explicitly stating that intractability arises from policy parametrization rather than from using Markov risk measures, since MDPs with Markov risk measures are generally tractable.
- Overall assessment: a minor but sound and significant advancement to the literature.
