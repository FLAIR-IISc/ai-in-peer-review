- Studies risk-averse MDPs with static and dynamically consistent risk measures.
- Main contribution: derives the gradient form for both dynamic and static risk settings.
- Generalizes prior CVaR-only results; extension is minor but useful.
- Results are essentially a straightforward extension of risk-neutral policy gradient.
- Paper is well-organized, well-written, easy to follow; results appear correct.
- Notation issue: the $\xi P_\theta$ in Theorem 2.1 is confusing; recommend using notation from Theorem 6.6 in [26].
- Typo: "enevlop[e]" on line 141 should be "envelope."
- Clarification suggestion: emphasize that MDPs with Markov risk measures are generally tractable and the intractability here stems from policy parametrization.
- Overall verdict: a minor but solid and important extension of existing work.
