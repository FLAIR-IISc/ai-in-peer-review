The authors analyze the setting of the risk-avoidance Markov decision process with static and dynamically consistent risk measures (mapping). The main contribution, in particular, is to show the form of the gradient for both dynamic and static settings.

This paper is a generalization of previous results, which were specifically focused on CVaR. This extension presents a small but useful contribution. The result is a simple extension of the policy gradient in risk-neutral settings.

This paper is well-organized, well-written, and easy to understand.

As far as I can tell, the results are correct.
