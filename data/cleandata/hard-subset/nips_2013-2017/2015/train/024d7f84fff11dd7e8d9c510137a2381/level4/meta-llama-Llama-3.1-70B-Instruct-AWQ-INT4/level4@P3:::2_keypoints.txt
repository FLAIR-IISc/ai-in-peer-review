- Novelty: Extends policy gradient to the entire class of coherent risk measures, a nontrivial and original contribution.  
- Motivation: Well-motivated by growing interest in risk-sensitive objectives to regularize variability in MDPs.  
- Scope: Covers both static and time-consistent dynamic risk measures, generalizing and unifying prior work.  
- Technical soundness: Equations and proofs are solid and the paper cites relevant literature thoroughly.  
- Clarity and organization: Writing is clear, logical, and free of errors; complex concepts are presented well.  
- Algorithms: Proposes clear new algorithms (policy gradient, actor-critic variants) for coherent risk optimization.  
- Experimental support: Simple experiment demonstrates risk-averse behavior and flexibility across risk types.  
- Limitation: Experiments are relatively simple and only on a static risk problem; broader benchmarking is needed.  
- Suggested additions: Provide more comprehensive experiments and further discussion of applications and limitations.  
- Overall assessment: Strong paper; recommend accept with minor revisions; rating 8/10.
