- Extends policy gradient methods to the general class of coherent risk measures, providing a unified framework beyond prior specific risk measures.
- Proposes novel RL algorithms (including actor-critic-style approaches) for risk-sensitive optimization under coherent risk measures.
- Mathematical derivations are technically sound and all relevant citations are provided.
- Paper is well-written and clarity is above average.
- Experimental validation uses a toy finance portfolio example demonstrating risk-averse behavior and flexibility across risk settings.
- Experiments are limited to a static risk problem, restricting empirical evaluation.
- Extension from prior work is non-trivial and the originality is sufficient for the venue.
- The contribution is significant given the importance and challenge of risk-sensitive RL.
