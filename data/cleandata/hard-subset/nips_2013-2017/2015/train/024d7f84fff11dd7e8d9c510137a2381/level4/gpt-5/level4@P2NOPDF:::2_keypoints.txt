- Develops policy-gradient estimators and RL algorithms for risk-sensitive objectives defined by coherent risk measures.  
- Generalizes prior methods for specific risk measures to a unified framework for all coherent risk measures.  
- Builds on policy gradient and actor-critic techniques to optimize coherent-risk objectives.  
- Demonstrates the approach on a toy financial portfolio problem with asset-specific return distributions.  
- Motivation and problem formulation are clear and follow naturally from prior work.  
- Experimental evaluation is simple and focused on a static risk setting.  
- Empirical results support risk-averse behavior and show flexibility across different risk specifications.  
- Paper is well written with clear notation and organization.  
- Derivations appear technically sound and references are relevant and complete.  
- Novelty is sufficient: a nontrivial extension of existing risk-sensitive RL methods to coherent risks.  
- Significance: meaningful contribution to risk-sensitive RL, though empirical scope is limited.
