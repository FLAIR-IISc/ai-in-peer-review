- Proposes a novel method for estimating policy gradients in risk-sensitive problems using coherent risk measures.
- Extends prior techniques limited to specific risk measures to a unified framework covering all coherent risk measures.
- Introduces RL algorithms (policy gradient, actor-critic variants) for coherent risk optimization.
- Demonstrates the approach on a toy finance example where assets have distinct return distributions.
- Motivation, problem formulation, and algorithms are clear and logically derived from prior work.
- Experiments are simple/static but indicate risk-averse behavior and flexibility across risk types.
- Paper is well-written, technically sound, and cites relevant literature; clarity is above average.
- Originality is sufficient since the extension is non-trivial.
- Significance is substantial given the importance of risk-sensitive RL and the clear algorithmic contributions.
