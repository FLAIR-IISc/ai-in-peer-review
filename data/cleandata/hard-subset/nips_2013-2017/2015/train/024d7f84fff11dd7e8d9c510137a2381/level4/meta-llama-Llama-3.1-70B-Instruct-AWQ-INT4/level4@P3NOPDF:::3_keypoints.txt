- Analyzes risk-averse MDPs with static and dynamically consistent risk measures; primary contribution is derivation of gradient forms for both settings.
- Extends prior CVaR-specific results to more general risk measures — a minor but useful contribution.
- Results are largely a straightforward extension of the risk-neutral policy gradient.
- Paper is well-organized, well-written, clear, and mathematically sound; derivations appear correct.
- Theorem 2.1 notation ("\xi P_\theta") is confusing; recommend using notation consistent with Theorem 6.6 in [26].
- Minor typo on line 141 ("risk enevlop[e]") needs correction.
- Section 3 should add context: MDPs with Markov risk measures are generally tractable; the intractability arises from policy parametrization, not the risk measure.
- Overall assessment: a minor but solid and important extension likely to interest researchers in risk-averse MDPs.
