- Paper analyzes risk-averse MDPs with static and dynamically consistent risk measures; main contribution is deriving the gradient for both settings.
- Generalizes prior CVaR-specific results; extension is minor but useful and follows straightforwardly from risk-neutral policy-gradient methods.
- Paper is well organized, clearly written, and easy to follow.
- Results appear to be correct.
- Theorem 2.1: notation ξP_θ is confusing; recommend referencing Theorem 6.6 in [26] and adopting that notation.
- Typo at Line 141: “risk enevlop[e]”.
- Clarify in Section 3 that MDPs with Markov risk measures are generally tractable; the intractability here stems from policy parametrization, not the risk measure.
- Overall assessment: a minor but solid and important extension of existing results.
