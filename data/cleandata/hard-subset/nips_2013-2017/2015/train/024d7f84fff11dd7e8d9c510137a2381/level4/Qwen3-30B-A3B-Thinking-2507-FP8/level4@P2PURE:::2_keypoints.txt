- Introduces methods to estimate policy gradients for risk-sensitive RL using coherent risk measures and proposes corresponding RL algorithms.  
- Extends existing techniques for specific risk measures to a unified framework covering the general class of coherent risk measures.  
- Demonstrates the algorithm on a finance toy example with assets having distinct return distributions.  
- Motivation is clear and problem formulation and algorithms follow logically from prior work.  
- Experiments use a static risk setting (limited complexity) but effectively show risk-averse behavior and flexibility across risk types.  
- Paper is well-structured with rigorous technical derivations and comprehensive references.  
- Exposition is exceptionally clear.  
- Original contribution: novel risk-sensitive RL algorithms and a non-trivial generalization to coherent risk measures.  
- Significant and timely contribution, presenting natural extensions of policy gradient and actor-critic methods to coherent risk optimization.
