- Presents a unified framework extending policy-gradient methods to the full class of coherent risk measures.
- Derives novel policy gradient theorems using the dual representation of coherent risk measures and the envelope theorem.
- Proposes a sampling-based policy-gradient algorithm for static coherent risks.
- Proposes an actor-critic algorithm for time-consistent dynamic coherent risks.
- Manuscript is exceptionally well-written, technically sound, and clear for its technical depth.
- Generalization to all coherent risk measures is original, significant, and advances risk-sensitive RL.
- Empirical evaluation is limited to a simple static financial trading problem.
- Experiments do not demonstrate scalability or performance in complex, dynamic benchmarks.
- Actor-critic algorithm’s need to sample multiple next-state transitions from a simulator may limit applicability.
- Overall theoretical contributions are substantial; reviewer strongly recommends acceptance.
