- Presents a unified policy-gradient approach for the full class of coherent risk measures, both static and dynamic.
- Derives a new formula for the gradient of static coherent risk.
- Proposes an algorithm to estimate the gradient of general static coherent risk using sampling plus convex programming.
- Develops a new policy gradient theorem for Markov coherent risk and an actor-critic algorithm for dynamic risk.
- Paper is well-organized and well-written, making the approach easy to follow.
- Results appear correct and the technical analysis is thorough and sound.
- Generalizes prior CVaR-specific results to a broader class of risk measures, which is a useful extension.
- Use of coherent risk measures provides a more comprehensive framework for risk-sensitive RL and has wide applicability.
- Notation issue: the symbol "\xi P_\theta" in Theorem 2.1 is confusing and should be revised to match prior work.
- Typo: "risk enevlop[e]" on line 141 should be "risk envelope".
- Authors should clarify that tractability concerns come from policy parametrization rather than inherent in Markov risk measures.
- Recommendation: accept with minor revisions to fix notation, typos, and clarify the tractability point.
