**Summary of Main Ideas**

This paper presents a unified approach to risk-sensitive reinforcement learning (RL) by extending policy gradient methods to the whole class of coherent risk measures. The authors consider both static and dynamic risk measures and provide new formulas for the gradient of static and dynamic coherent risk measures. They also develop a new policy gradient theorem for Markov coherent risk and propose an actor-critic algorithm for solving the dynamic risk problem.

**Related Work**

The paper is well-situated within the existing literature on risk-sensitive RL. The authors provide a clear overview of previous work on specific risk measures, such as variance, conditional value-at-risk (CVaR), and mean-semideviation. They also highlight the limitations of these approaches and explain how their work generalizes and extends previous results.
