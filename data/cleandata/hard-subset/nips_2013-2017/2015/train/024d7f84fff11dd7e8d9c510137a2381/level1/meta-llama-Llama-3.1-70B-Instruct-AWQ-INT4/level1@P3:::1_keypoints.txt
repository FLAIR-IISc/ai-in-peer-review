- Related work: Places work within literature on variance, CVaR, mean-semideviation and explains how it generalizes prior specific-risk approaches.
- Quality: Technically sound with well-organized presentation and proofs in supplementary material; includes a numerical illustration.
- Clarity: Well-written, clear notation, standard terminology, and logical organization.
- Originality: Novel extension of policy gradients to the full class of coherent risk measures with new theoretical results.
- Significance: Addresses important problem of risk-sensitive decision-making and offers a flexible framework relevant to real-world applications and model uncertainty.
