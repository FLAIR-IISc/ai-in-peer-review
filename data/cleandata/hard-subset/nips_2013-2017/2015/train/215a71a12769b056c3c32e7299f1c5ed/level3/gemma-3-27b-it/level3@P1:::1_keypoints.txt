- Introduces highway networks with LSTM-like gates to enable training of very deep feedforward networks.  
- Demonstrates strong empirical performance on MNIST, CIFAR-10, and CIFAR-100 using standard SGD, especially as depth increases.  
- Provides thorough analysis of learned gate outputs, revealing layer importance and dynamic routing of information.  
- Motivated by recent successes and challenges of deep networks; situates work relative to optimizers, initializations, activations, and skip connections.  
- Novelty: simple, effective adaptation of LSTM gating to feedforward architectures to mitigate vanishing gradients.  
- Empirical results: shows ability to train much deeper networks and competitive or superior benchmark performance, with insightful comparison to Fitnets.  
- Analysis strength: excellent investigation of gate biases and activity patterns (Figures 2 and 4).  
- Clarity: paper is well-written with clear explanations and balanced notation.  
- Mathematical error: Equation 5’s Jacobian claim is incorrect because sigmoid gates output values in (0,1); authors must correct and clarify backpropagation.  
- Initialization strategy: using negative transform-gate biases is effective but the choice by depth is ad-hoc and needs systematic investigation.  
- Comparative claims: unclear whether comparisons to prior work used identical experimental conditions; must state replication details.  
- Reporting inconsistency: some results report mean±std while others report only best accuracy; all experiments should report mean and std.  
- Theoretical gap: lacks rigorous theoretical analysis of how gates enable conditional identity behavior and long-range propagation.  
- Arguments for acceptance: novel effective architecture, convincing experiments, insightful gate analysis, and available code/hyperparameters.  
- Arguments against acceptance: Jacobian math error, unsatisfactory bias justification, and unclear experimental comparison setup.  
- Overall assessment: significant contribution warranting acceptance pending minor revisions to correct math, clarify comparisons, and investigate initialization.  
- Requested revisions: fix Equation 5 and explain backpropagation; provide performance vs. initial bias plot; clarify experimental replication details; report mean and std for all experiments; add brief theoretical discussion of gate implications.
