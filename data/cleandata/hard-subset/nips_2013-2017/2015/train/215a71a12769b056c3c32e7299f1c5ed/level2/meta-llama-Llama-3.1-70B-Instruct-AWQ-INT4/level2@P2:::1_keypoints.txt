- Introduces highway networks: a novel, LSTM-inspired architecture using adaptive gating to regulate inter-layer information flow.  
- Enables unimpeded information flow across many layers, allowing training of extremely deep networks.  
- Well-written paper with clear, concise explanations and a thorough introductory review of deep learning depth challenges.  
- Provides detailed mathematical formulation of the highway network architecture.  
- Experiments show highway networks can be trained directly with SGD while plain deep nets become hard to optimize.  
- Demonstrates good generalization and competitive performance on several benchmark datasets.  
- Main contribution: architecture that makes training very deep, efficient models feasible.  
- Paper is well-organized and easy to follow.
