- Proposes an architecture that carries inputs unchanged through layers using input-dependent gates.
- Enables training of much deeper networks than standard feed-forward architectures.
- Provides an in-depth analysis of how gates select inputs across different layers.
- Paper is high quality, clearly written, and original.
- Acknowledges similarities to FitNets and LSTMs but clearly explains differences and includes comparisons.
- Presents promising empirical results.
- Reviewer has no suggested improvements.
- Minor editorial note: typo "Tables 3 and 3" (p5).
- Overall: a very solid paper with a novel technique and thorough analysis.
