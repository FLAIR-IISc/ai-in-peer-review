- Proposes a generalized framework of "information highways" that add direct connections to upper layers for training very deep networks.  
- Empirical results show the method is effective and improves classification accuracy.  
- The concept is novel, generalizing prior cross-layer techniques while retaining originality.  
- Experimental validation is sound and convincingly supports the paper's contributions.  
- Analysis could be strengthened with a greater variety of details or illustrative examples.  
- Paper is well-written and its core messages are communicated clearly.  
- The work is significant for the field given the need to train increasingly deep networks for key applications.
