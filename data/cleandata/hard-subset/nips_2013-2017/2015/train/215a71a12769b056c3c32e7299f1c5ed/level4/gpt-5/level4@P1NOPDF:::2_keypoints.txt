- Proposes a gated architecture that uses input-dependent gates to carry inputs forward unchanged, enabling training of much deeper networks than standard feedforward architectures.  
- Includes insightful analysis of trained models, particularly how gates select inputs across layers.  
- Paper is high quality, clearly written, and original.  
- Acknowledges similarities to FitNets and LSTMs but clearly articulates differences and provides comparisons.  
- Results are promising.  
- Reviewer has no substantive suggestions for improvement.  
- Minor typo: p.5 refers to “Tables 3 and 3.”  
- Overall assessment: a very solid paper with a novel technique, good results, and thorough analysis.
