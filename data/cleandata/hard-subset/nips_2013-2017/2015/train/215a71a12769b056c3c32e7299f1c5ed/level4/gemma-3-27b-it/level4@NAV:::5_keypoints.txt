- Proposes a compelling, LSTM-inspired method for training very deep feedforward networks with a successful implementation.  
- Reviewer notes personal familiarity with the idea (had considered it previously).  
- Offers an alternative viewpoint: interpret very deep nets as having multi-layered “sub-layers” per layer, similar to Inception/VGG, and suggests checking if this applies to highway networks.  
- Experimental suggestion: train one deep network on both MNIST and CIFAR-10 with branched inputs to test shared representations and cross-domain parameter reuse at inference.  
- Recommends extending evaluation to larger datasets such as ImageNet.  
- Overall judgment: strong paper, simple and elegant core idea, well-executed analysis, no significant weaknesses.
