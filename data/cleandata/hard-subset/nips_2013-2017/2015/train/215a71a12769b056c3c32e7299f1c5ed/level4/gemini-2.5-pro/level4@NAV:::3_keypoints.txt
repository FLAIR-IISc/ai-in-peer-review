- Proposes adapting LSTM-style gating to train exceptionally deep neural networks.  
- Paper is well-executed, clearly written, and presents a novel idea with strong results.  
- Addresses long-standing challenges of initialization and vanishing/exploding gradients that impede deep networks.  
- Demonstrates a viable method for training networks up to ~100 layers.  
- Experiments highlight information routing and the dynamic importance of individual layers.  
- Typo noted: Section 4.1’s "most transform blocks are active on average" should likely read "not active on average."  
- Suggests adding a comparison to a simple near-identity initialization baseline and clarifying differences from highway networks.  
- Overall assessment: significant contribution and very clear manuscript.
