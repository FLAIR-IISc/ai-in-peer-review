**Quality:** The concept seems new, and the experiments are quite robust. Some variations or figures could be used in the analysis section.

The research paper describes an effective method of very deep neural network training, creating a direct connection with "information highways" or the upper network layers. The contributions are quite new and well-supported by experimental evidence.
