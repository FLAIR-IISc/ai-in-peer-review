Here is a research paper that proposes a generalization of multi-layer neural network training. The concept of the "Information Highway" is well illustrated through experiments. Furthermore, the authors demonstrate that this method can improve classification accuracy results.

**Quality:** The concept seems new, and the experiments are quite robust. Some variations or figures could be used in the analysis section.

**Clarity:** The key messages of the research paper are well presented and quite clear.

**Originality:** The proposed method is sufficiently original, although it is a generalization of previous neural network techniques.

**Importance:** The research paper highlights the significance of the field, as deeper neural network training is required to achieve better results in application areas.

The research paper describes an effective method of very deep neural network training by creating a direct connection with the "Information Highway" or the above network layers. Although this is a generalization of previous techniques like cross-layer connections, the authors show through experiments that this method is effective. The contributions are quite new and well supported by experimental evidence.
