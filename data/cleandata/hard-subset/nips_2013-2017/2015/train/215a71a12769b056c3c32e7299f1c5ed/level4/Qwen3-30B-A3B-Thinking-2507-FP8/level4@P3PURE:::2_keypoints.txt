- Proposes a novel architecture that uses input-dependent gates to "carry" inputs unchanged through layers.  
- Enables training of much deeper networks than standard feedforward architectures.  
- Provides a thorough, desirable analysis of how gates select inputs across layers.  
- Paper is high quality, clearly written, and original.  
- Acknowledges similarities to FitNets and LSTMs but clearly explains differences and includes appropriate comparisons.  
- Reports promising empirical results.  
- Reviewer suggests no specific improvements.  
- Notes a typo: "Tables 3 and 3" on page 5.  
- Overall assessment: very solid paper with a novel technique, strong results, and compelling analysis.
