- Proposes a generalization for training very deep neural networks via “information highways” (direct connections to upper layers).  
- Demonstrated empirically to improve classification accuracy.  
- Experimental evaluation is solid and effectively supports the claims.  
- The idea is novel, though it generalizes prior cross-layer connection techniques.  
- Analysis sections are weaker: they need broader details or additional illustrative material.  
- Main messages are clearly and well delivered.  
- The method is significant for the field given the importance of training deeper networks.  
- Overall, an effective, empirically supported contribution to training deep networks.
