This paper introduces a novel architecture for training very deep neural networks, utilizing input-dependent gates to allow information to propagate directly through layers without modification. 
This gating mechanism demonstrably facilitates the training of significantly deeper networks compared to traditional feedforward approaches.
A particularly strong aspect of this work is the insightful analysis of the trained network’s gating behavior across different layers – precisely the investigation one would expect and desire.
The paper is of a high standard, exhibiting both clarity of presentation and originality.
While acknowledging related work such as FitNets and LSTMs, the authors effectively delineate the distinctions and provide appropriate comparisons.
The reported results are encouraging.
I do not have any suggestions for improvement.
Please note a minor typographical issue: “Tables 3 and 3” (p5).
Overall, this is a robust and well-executed paper presenting a novel technique with compelling results and a thorough analysis.
