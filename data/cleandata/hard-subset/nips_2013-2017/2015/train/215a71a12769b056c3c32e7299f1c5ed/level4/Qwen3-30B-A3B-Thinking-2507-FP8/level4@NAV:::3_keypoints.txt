- Proposes using LSTM-like units to enable training of extremely deep neural networks.  
- Manuscript is high-quality, well-written, and presents a novel approach with compelling results.  
- Directly addresses initialization and vanishing/exploding gradient challenges, demonstrating training of networks up to 100 layers.  
- Experimental analysis of information flow and layer contributions is particularly insightful.  
- Correction suggested: Section 4.1 should state "most transform blocks are inactive on average," not active.  
- Suggestion to discuss how transform gates compare to simple identity-matrix initialization (plus minor noise) as an alternative.  
- Represents a significant contribution to the field and was a pleasure to evaluate.
