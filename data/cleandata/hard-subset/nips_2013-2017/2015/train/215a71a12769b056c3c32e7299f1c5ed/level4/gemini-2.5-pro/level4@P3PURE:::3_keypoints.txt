- Proposes Highway Networks using LSTM-style gating to facilitate training of extremely deep networks.
- High-quality, well-written, original idea with compelling empirical results.
- Addresses vanishing/exploding gradient optimization issues and enables training of up to ~100-layer networks.
- Experiments reveal dynamic information routing and provide insightful analysis of layer functional importance.
- Notes a typo in Section 4.1: "most transform blocks are active on average" should read "are not active on average."
- Recommends adding discussion/comparison to a baseline that initializes layers near the identity to show advantages of transform gates.
- Overall verdict: excellent paper and a pleasure to read.
