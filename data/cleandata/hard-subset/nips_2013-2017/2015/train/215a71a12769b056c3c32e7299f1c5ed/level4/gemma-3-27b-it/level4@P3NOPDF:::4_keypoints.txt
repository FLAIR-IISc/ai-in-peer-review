- Proposes “information highways”: direct bypass connections to improve information flow in deep neural networks.
- Novel and significant extension of skip/cross-layer connections with theoretical justification.
- Experimental results are solid and show improved classification accuracy.
- Paper is exceptionally well-written, with clear motivation, consistent notation, and reproducible experiment descriptions.
- Analysis section needs deeper investigation into why information highways are effective.
- Recommend visualizing learned highway weights and activation patterns to explain the mechanism.
- Recommend sensitivity studies on highway configurations (number, placement) to assess robustness.
- Request a more nuanced discussion of computational cost and comparison to other acceleration techniques.
- Method has high significance for training very deep models across CV, NLP, and speech.
- Overall recommendation: accept, with suggested follow-up analyses to strengthen the paper.
