- Proposes training very deep neural networks via 'information highways'—direct connective paths to upper layers.  
- Method is a generalization of prior cross-layer connection techniques.  
- Solid experimental evaluation showing improved classification accuracy.  
- Core idea is novel and represents a significant conceptual advance.  
- Claims are well-supported by results, though analysis needs more detailed illustrations and varied analytical experiments.  
- Paper is well-written with clear presentation of contributions and method mechanics.  
- Work is highly significant, offering a practical solution for training increasingly deep networks and advancing state-of-the-art performance.
