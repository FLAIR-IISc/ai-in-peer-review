- Proposes "information highways": direct connections from lower to upper layers to better train very deep networks.  
- Solid experimental results showing the method is effective and improves classification accuracy.  
- Overall work quality is strong; the core idea appears novel.  
- Method is sufficiently original and framed as a generalization of prior cross-layer connection techniques.  
- Paper's main messages are communicated clearly.  
- Contribution is significant for enabling training of increasingly deep neural networks.
