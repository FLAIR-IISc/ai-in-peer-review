- Proposes an innovative LSTM-based method for training very deep neural networks; strong contribution with clear writing and compelling results.  
- Directly addresses long-standing training challenges (vanishing/exploding gradients, sensitive initialization) and demonstrates successful training of 100-layer networks.  
- Experiments and visualizations (information routing and layer importance) are insightful and improve understanding of learned representations.  
- Suggest revising Section 4.1 wording: “most transform blocks are active on average” → “not active on average” for clarity.  
- Recommend discussing how the learned transform gates differ from simple identity (±noise) layer initialization and why gating is a more adaptive solution.  
- Overall verdict: excellent paper, significant contribution, highly recommended.
