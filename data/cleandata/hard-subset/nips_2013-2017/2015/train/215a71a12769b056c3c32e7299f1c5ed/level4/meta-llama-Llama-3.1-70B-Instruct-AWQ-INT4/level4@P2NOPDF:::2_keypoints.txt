- Proposes a novel architecture that uses input-dependent gates to propagate the input unchanged through layers.  
- Enables training of much deeper networks than standard feed-forward architectures.  
- Provides a thorough, insightful analysis of how gates select inputs across layers.  
- Analysis illuminates the inner workings of the proposed architecture.  
- Paper is high quality, clearly written, and original.  
- Acknowledges similarities to FitNets and LSTMs but distinguishes and compares them appropriately.  
- Experimental results are promising and demonstrate the technique's potential.  
- No substantive improvements suggested; only a minor typo ("Tables 3 and 3" on page 5).  
- Overall assessment: a solid paper with a novel method, strong results, and valuable analysis.
