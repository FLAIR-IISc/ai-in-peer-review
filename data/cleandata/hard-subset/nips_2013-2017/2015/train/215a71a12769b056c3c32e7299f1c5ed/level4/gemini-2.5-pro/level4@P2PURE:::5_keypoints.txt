- Proposes an LSTM-inspired gating mechanism (highway networks) enabling training of very deep feedforward networks; method is simple, elegant, and well-analyzed.  
- Reviewer previously considered a similar gating idea and congratulates the authors for the first successful implementation and analysis.  
- Suggests an alternative "macro-layer" / compositional interpretation (like Inception/VGG) and asks if highway networks can be viewed this way.  
- Recommends an experiment: train one deep network jointly on MNIST and CIFAR-10 with a shared trunk and task-specific output heads to probe feature sharing.  
- Specifically proposes checking whether parameters underutilized by MNIST are recruited for the more complex CIFAR-10 task in the multi-task setting.  
- No major criticisms overall.  
- Advises evaluating the network on larger datasets (e.g., ImageNet) to further strengthen the paper.
