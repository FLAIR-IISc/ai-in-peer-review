- Proposes LSTM-like highway network units for training very deep networks.  
- Paper is high-quality, well-written, original, and yields interesting results.  
- Addresses initialization and vanishing/exploding gradient issues, demonstrating training of networks up to 100 layers.  
- Experiments show routing of information and the significance of individual layers, giving useful insights.  
- Specific correction: Section 4.1’s phrase “most transform blocks are active on average” should read “not active on average.”  
- Suggestion: discuss identity-matrix (plus noise) initialization as an alternative and clarify how transform gates differ.  
- Overall positive recommendation and commendation for the contribution.
