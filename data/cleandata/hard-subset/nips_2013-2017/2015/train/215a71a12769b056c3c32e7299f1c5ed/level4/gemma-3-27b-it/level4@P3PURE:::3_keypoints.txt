- Proposes using LSTM units to train very deep networks; original idea with compelling results.
- High-quality, well-written contribution and overall excellent paper.
- Addresses vanishing/exploding gradient and initialization challenges to enable training of 100-layer networks.
- Experiments on information routing and layer importance are particularly insightful.
- Correction needed: Section 4.1’s claim “most transform blocks are active on average” should read “not active on average.”
- Suggestion: compare to identity-initialized layers (with small noise) as a baseline.
- Suggestion: clarify how transform gates differ from simple identity initialization and from highway networks in general.
