This paper introduces an architecture for very deep networks that allows the input to be carried unchanged through a layer via gates conditioned on the input itself.
This makes it possible to train networks much deeper than with a standard feedforward architecture.
The authors conduct a very insightful analysis of the trained models to examine how the gates choose their inputs at different layers of the network, which is exactly what I hoped to see.
The paper is high quality, clearly written, and original.
Although there is naturally some overlap with recent work (notably FitNets and LSTMs), the differences are clearly articulated and comparisons are provided where appropriate.
The results are promising.
I have no suggested improvements.

This is a very strong paper. The proposed method is novel and achieves solid results. The analysis is interesting and thorough.
