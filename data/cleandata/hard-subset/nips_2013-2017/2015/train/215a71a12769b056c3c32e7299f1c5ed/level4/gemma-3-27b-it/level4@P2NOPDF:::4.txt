This paper introduces an innovative approach to training very deep neural networks, termed “information highways,” which facilitates direct connections between layers. The core idea—allowing information to flow more freely through the network—is intuitively appealing and demonstrably effective, as evidenced by the reported classification accuracy improvements.

**Quality:** The concept presented is novel and the experimental validation is robust. While the analysis is sound, expanding it with additional details or illustrative examples would further strengthen the paper’s impact. Exploring the behavior of the information highways in different network architectures or on more diverse datasets could provide richer insights.

**Clarity:** The paper’s central message is clearly articulated and easily understood. The authors effectively communicate the motivation behind information highways and their proposed implementation. The writing is concise and avoids unnecessary jargon.

**Originality:** Although building upon existing techniques such as cross-layer connections, the proposed method represents a significant and original generalization. The authors don’t simply *add* connections, but introduce a learned gating mechanism which intelligently controls information flow, differentiating it from previous approaches.

**Significance:** This work addresses a critical challenge in modern deep learning: effectively training increasingly deep networks. As achieving state-of-the-art results in many applications necessitates deeper architectures, methods that improve training and performance in these networks are highly significant. The paper's contribution has the potential to enable the successful deployment of even more complex and powerful models.

In summary, the paper presents a compelling and effective technique for training deep neural networks. The 'information highway' approach is well-motivated, thoroughly evaluated, and has clear potential for broad impact within the field. The experimental results convincingly demonstrate the method’s benefits. While the analysis section could benefit from greater depth, the overall quality of the work is high, and I recommend acceptance.
