

The paper introduces a generalization for training deep neural networks with many stacked layers, leveraging "information highways" through direct connections to upper network layers. This approach is well-illustrated through experimentation, demonstrating improved classification accuracy. The idea is sufficiently novel as a generalization of prior techniques like cross-layer connections, and the experimental validation is solid. The contributions are novel and well-supported by evidence. The paper effectively addresses the significance of the field, as increasingly deeper networks are required for better application results, and the proposed method provides an effective solution. The main messages are clearly conveyed, though the analysis section would benefit from additional detail or illustrative examples.
