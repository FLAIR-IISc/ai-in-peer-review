- Proposes "information highways": direct connections from lower to higher layers to enable training of very deep networks.
- Experiments convincingly show these connections enable effective training of much deeper models and improve classification accuracy.
- The idea appears novel and the experimental evaluation is solid and supportive.
- Analysis sections are limited; more varied, detailed analysis or illustrations are needed to explain why and when the method works best.
- Presentation and main messages are clearly communicated and easy to follow.
- Builds on prior cross-layer connection techniques but is sufficiently original in formulation and application.
- The contribution is significant for enabling stable, effective training as networks grow deeper.
- Overall, a meaningful and practical advance with strong empirical support, though strengthened analysis would improve the paper.
