- Proposes an architecture for very deep networks that lets inputs be “carried” unchanged through layers via input-dependent gating.  
- This gating mechanism enables training of much deeper models than standard feedforward designs allow.  
- Provides an informative analysis of trained networks, showing how gates select and route information across layers.  
- Paper is high quality, clearly written, and original.  
- Clearly articulates and compares differences with related work (notably FitNets and LSTMs).  
- Experimental results are promising.  
- Reviewer has no suggested improvements.  
- Minor editorial note: references “Tables 3 and 3” (p. 5) appears incorrect.  
- Overall assessment: a very strong paper with a novel method, good results, and thorough analysis.
