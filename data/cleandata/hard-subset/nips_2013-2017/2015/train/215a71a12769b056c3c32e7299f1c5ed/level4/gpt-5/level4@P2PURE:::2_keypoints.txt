- Introduces an architecture that carries the input unchanged through layers via input-dependent gates, enabling training of much deeper networks.
- Gate mechanism’s activations depend on the input, allowing selective routing and preservation of information across layers.
- Provides insightful, thorough analysis of trained models, especially how gates select inputs across layers.
- Paper is high-quality, clearly written, and original.
- Naturally resembles FitNets and LSTMs but differences are clearly explained and appropriate comparisons are made.
- Experimental results are promising and demonstrate the method’s effectiveness.
- Reviewer has no suggested improvements.
- Minor issue: duplicated reference to “Tables 3 and 3” (p. 5).
