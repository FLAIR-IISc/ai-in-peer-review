- High-quality, clearly written, and original paper.  
- Proposes a novel architecture for very deep networks using input-dependent gates that let information be carried unchanged through a layer.  
- Mechanism enables training significantly deeper networks than standard feed-forward architectures.  
- Strong analysis of trained networks, investigating how gates select inputs across layers and providing valuable insight.  
- Well positioned relative to related work (e.g., LSTMs, FitNets), clearly explaining key differences and providing empirical comparisons.  
- Overall solid contribution: novel technique, thorough analysis, and promising results with no suggested improvements.
