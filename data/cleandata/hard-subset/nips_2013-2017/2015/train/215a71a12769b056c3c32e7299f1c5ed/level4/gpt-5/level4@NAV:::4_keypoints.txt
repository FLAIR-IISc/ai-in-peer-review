- Introduces a generalized “information highway” method for training very deep neural networks via direct connections to higher layers.  
- Experiments strongly support the information highway concept and show improved classification accuracy.  
- The core idea appears novel and is a meaningful generalization of prior cross-layer connection techniques.  
- Experimental evaluation is strong and convincingly substantiates the contributions.  
- Analysis sections are somewhat limited and would benefit from more varied details or illustrative examples.  
- Main messages are communicated clearly and the paper is easy to follow.  
- The work is significant for the field because it facilitates training increasingly deep networks needed for better application results.
