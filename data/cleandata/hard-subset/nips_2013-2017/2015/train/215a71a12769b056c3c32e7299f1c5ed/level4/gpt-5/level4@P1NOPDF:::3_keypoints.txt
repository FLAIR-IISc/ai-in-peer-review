- Proposes using LSTM-style gating (highway networks) to train very deep (100-layer) networks; original idea with strong, interesting results.
- Paper is high-quality and well-written, demonstrating a practical method to overcome vanishing/exploding gradients and initialization issues.
- Experiments on information routing and layer importance are particularly compelling.
- Minor error: Section 4.1 should read “not active on average” instead of “most transform blocks are active on average.”
- Suggestion: discuss how transform gates differ from simply initializing each layer to the identity (plus small noise).
- Overall assessment: excellent paper and a pleasure to read.
