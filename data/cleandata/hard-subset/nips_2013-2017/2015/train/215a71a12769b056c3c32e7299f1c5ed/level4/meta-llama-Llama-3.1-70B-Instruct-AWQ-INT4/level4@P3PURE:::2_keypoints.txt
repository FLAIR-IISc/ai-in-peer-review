- Proposes a novel architecture that lets the input be "carried" unchanged through layers via input-dependent gates.  
- Enables training of much deeper networks than standard feedforward architectures.  
- Provides a thorough, intriguing analysis of gate behavior and how gates select inputs across layers.  
- Paper is high quality, clearly written, and original.  
- Notes similarities to FitNets and LSTMs but clearly explains differences and includes appropriate comparisons.  
- Experimental results are promising.  
- Reviewer has no suggested improvements.  
- Minor typographical error: text references "Tables 3 and 3" on page 5.  
- Overall assessment: a very solid paper presenting a novel technique with good results and insightful analysis.
