- Proposes a novel architecture for very deep networks using input-dependent gates that carry the input unchanged through layers.  
- Enables training of much deeper networks than standard feed-forward architectures.  
- Conducts an insightful analysis of how gates select their inputs across layers, a crucial contribution.  
- Paper is high quality, clearly written, and original.  
- Related to FitNets and LSTMs but differences are clearly explained and relevant comparisons are provided.  
- Experimental results are promising.  
- Reviewer has no suggested improvements but notes a minor typo: "Tables 3 and 3" on page 5.  
- Overall assessment: a very solid paper presenting a novel technique with good results and thorough analysis.
