- Experimental results show improved classification accuracy, demonstrating the method's effectiveness.  
- The idea is innovative and the experimental framework is robust.

- The paper is clearly written and easy to comprehend.

- The approach generalizes prior methods by establishing direct connections to upper network layers.  
- The work is significant for enabling training of increasingly deeper networks across application domains.
