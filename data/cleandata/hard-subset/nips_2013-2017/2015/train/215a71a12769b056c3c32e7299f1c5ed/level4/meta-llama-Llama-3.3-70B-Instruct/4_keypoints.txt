- Experimental results show improved classification accuracy, demonstrating the method’s effectiveness.  
- Methodology builds on prior cross-layer connection techniques but introduces novel elements and is well-motivated.  
- Empirical evaluation is robust and substantiates the claimed contributions.  
- Paper quality is high overall, with an innovative concept and solid experimental framework.  
- Analysis sections are somewhat limited and would benefit from more varied details or visual illustrations.  
- Clarity is strong; primary messages are conveyed effectively and are easy to understand.  
- Originality is noteworthy despite being an extension of existing neural network techniques.  
- Work is significant given the increasing need to train deeper networks across applications.
