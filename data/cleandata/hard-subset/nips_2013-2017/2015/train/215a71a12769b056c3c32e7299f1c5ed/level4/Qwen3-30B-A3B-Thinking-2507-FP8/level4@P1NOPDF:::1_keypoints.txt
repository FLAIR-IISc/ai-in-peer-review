1. Sigmoid gate outputs never equal 0/1, so Eq.(5) gradients are inaccurate — authors must clarify the exact backpropagation used.  
2. Initialization via constant negative bias per depth is unsatisfactory; provide performance vs. initial bias (bias acts as a regularizer encouraging longer highways).  
3. The phrase "report by recent studies in similar experimental settings" is vague — explicitly state whether setups are comparable and detail differences from prior work.  
4. Reporting only max accuracies is inappropriate — report mean accuracies with standard deviations for all methods.  
5. Include a learning-theory analysis of input-dependent gate-enabled identity mapping and propagation through selective layers to strengthen the paper.
