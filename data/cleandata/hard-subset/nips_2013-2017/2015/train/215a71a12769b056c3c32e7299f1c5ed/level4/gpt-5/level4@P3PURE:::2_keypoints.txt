- Proposes an architecture with input-dependent gates that can carry inputs unchanged through layers.  
- Enables training of much deeper networks than standard feedforward architectures.  
- Provides a thorough, interesting analysis of how gates select inputs across layers.  
- Paper is high quality, clearly written, and original.  
- Acknowledges similarity to FitNets and LSTMs but clearly explains differences and includes comparisons.  
- Experimental results are promising.  
- Reviewer has no suggested improvements.  
- Minor typo noted: “Tables 3 and 3” (p. 5).  
- Overall assessment: solid paper with a novel technique, good results, and thorough analysis.
