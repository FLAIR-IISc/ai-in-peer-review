This paper proposes an architecture for very deep networks that enables input to be "carried" through a single layer without any changes, relying solely on gates.

It enables training very deep networks compared to a standard feed forward architecture.

The authors provide a very interesting analysis of the trained network to determine how the gates select their input across the different layers of the network, which is exactly what I wanted to see.

This paper is of high quality, clearly written, and original.

Although there is some similarity with other recent works (especially FitNet and LSTM), the differences are clearly stated and compared where appropriate.

Promising results have been achieved.

I have not suggested any improvements.

Note "Tables 3 and 3" (page 5).

This is a very solid paper. The proposed technique is new and achieves good results. The analysis is interesting and in-depth.
