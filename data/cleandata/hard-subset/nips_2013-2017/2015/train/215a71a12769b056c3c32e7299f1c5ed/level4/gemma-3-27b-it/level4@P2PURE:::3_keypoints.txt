- Introduces a novel method for training very deep neural networks using LSTM units.  
- Strong, well-written paper with an original core idea and compelling experimental results.  
- Directly tackles initialization and vanishing/exploding gradient issues, demonstrating training of 100-layer networks.  
- Experiments on information routing and layer importance are insightful.  
- Section 4.1 claim “most transform blocks are active on average” is likely incorrect and should read “not active on average.”  
- Recommend discussing identity-initialization baseline and explicitly how transform gates differ from and relate to highway networks.  
- Overall assessment: excellent, convincing contribution and a pleasure to review.
