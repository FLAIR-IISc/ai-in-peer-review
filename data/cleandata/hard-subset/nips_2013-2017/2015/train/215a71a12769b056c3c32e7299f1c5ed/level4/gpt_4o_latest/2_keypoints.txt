Introduces an architecture that uses input-conditioned gates to let inputs pass unchanged through very deep network layers.

Enables training of much deeper networks than standard feedforward architectures allow.

Provides a compelling analysis of how gates select inputs across layers, matching reviewer expectations.

Paper is high-quality, well-written, and demonstrates originality.

Acknowledges parallels to FitNets and LSTMs but clearly differentiates and provides relevant comparisons.

Experimental results are promising.

Reviewer has no recommendations for improvement.

Typographical/reference issue: "Tables 3 and 3" noted on p.5.

Overall assessment: strong, innovative method with good results and thorough, insightful analysis.
