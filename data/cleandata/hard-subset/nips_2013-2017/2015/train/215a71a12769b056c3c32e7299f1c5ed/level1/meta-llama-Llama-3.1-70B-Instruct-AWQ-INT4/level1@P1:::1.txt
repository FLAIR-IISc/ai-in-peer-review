The paper "Training Very Deep Networks" proposes a novel architecture called highway networks, which allows for the training of very deep neural networks without the difficulties associated with traditional deep networks. This architecture is evaluated on several benchmark datasets, including MNIST, CIFAR-10, and CIFAR-100, and is shown to outperform traditional deep networks in terms of accuracy and ease of training.
