- Proposes a novel method reconciling backpropagation with neuromorphic hardware by modeling spikes and discrete synapses as continuous probabilities.  
- Demonstrates method on a sparsely connected MNIST network achieving high accuracy and energy efficiency on the TrueNorth chip.  
- Main contribution: a learning rule and network topology enabling training compatible with neuromorphic hardware.  
- Paper is well-written, clear, and easy to follow with sufficient background for non-experts.  
- Technical details are well-presented and understandable.  
- Experiments show effectiveness on MNIST and practical energy-efficient deployment.
