This paper proposes a novel approach to reconcile the incompatibility between backpropagation and neuromorphic hardware by treating spikes and discrete synapses as continuous probabilities. The authors demonstrate the effectiveness of their approach by training a sparsely connected network on the MNIST dataset and achieving high accuracy and energy efficiency on the TrueNorth chip.

The paper is well-written and easy to follow. The authors provide a clear explanation of the problem they are trying to solve and the approach they take to solve it. The technical details are well-presented, and the authors provide sufficient background information for readers who may not be familiar with neuromorphic computing.
