- Technical quality: technically sound with clear presentation, theoretical analysis, experimental support, and sufficient detail for reproduction.  
- Clarity: well-written and well-structured with helpful figures, though some mathematical derivations may be hard for non-expert readers.  
- Originality: introduces a novel probabilistic framing of spikes and synapses that enables backprop on neuromorphic-compatible networks.  
- Significance: high potential impact for energy-efficient neuromorphic computing and deployment on diverse devices, demonstrated empirically on a common benchmark.
