Reference [15]'s "expectation backpropagation" algorithm, which utilizes expectation propagation as a backpropagation update rule, is specifically designed for binary neurons with binary weights and can accommodate constrained connectivity. This existing work undermines the novelty of the presented claim.

According to the available information, the algorithm in reference [15] achieved the best results for binary weights and neurons, as demonstrated on the MNIST dataset (http://arxiv.org/pdf/1503.03562v3.pdf) without data augmentation, similar to the approach employed in this paper.
