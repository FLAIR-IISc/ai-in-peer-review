- Experimental results on the TrueNorth chip are compelling, showing high accuracy and energy efficiency.

- Arguments for acceptance: novel combination of backprop with spiking/low-precision/constrained connectivity, strong TrueNorth results, addresses deployment challenge, clear writing.  
- Arguments against acceptance: overstated novelty claims, unclear hardware details (0.15 bits) and training overhead, unexplained ensemble results, limited discussion of generalizability.
