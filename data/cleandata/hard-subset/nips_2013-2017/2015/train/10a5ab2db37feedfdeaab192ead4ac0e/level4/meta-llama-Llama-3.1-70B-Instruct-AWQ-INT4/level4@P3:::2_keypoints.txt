- Proposes a novel probabilistic mapping from high-precision analog variables to spike-based networks with binary synapses via sampling.  
- Idea is innovative and potentially applicable to other algorithms and hardware platforms.  
- Manuscript is well-structured, clear, and concise with informative figures and tables.  
- Provides a thorough explanation and adapts backpropagation to a probabilistic neural representation.  
- Validation on MNIST and chip-measured performance figures convincingly demonstrate effectiveness.  
- Work is original and represents a clear contribution to the field.  
- Main limitation: the significance and practical usefulness of the method are not clearly highlighted or contextualized.  
- Important implementation details are missing (input-to-spike conversion, frequencies used, shared weight values).  
- Lacks clear comparison to alternative methods and discussion of practical deployment scenarios.  
- Related work is incomplete: should cite Qiao et al. (Frontiers in Neuroscience 2015) and arXiv:1506.05427.  
- Recommendation: accept with revisions to add implementation details, clarify significance, and include related-work comparisons.
