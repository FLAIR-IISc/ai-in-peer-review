5. Though experiments show strong accuracy and power-efficiency, the methodological contributions are overstated—the core approach builds on Refs. [14] and [15] without sufficient attribution.

6. The authors acknowledged these concerns in response, but the manuscript still needs revisions to integrate citations, clarify lineage, and correct novelty/claims.
