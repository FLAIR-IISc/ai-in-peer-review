- Proposes Norm-POEM, a self-normalized risk estimator for BLBF that avoids propensity overfitting in conventional unbiased estimators.  
- Demonstrates Norm-POEM consistently outperforms the conventional estimator on several multi-label classification tasks.  
- Paper is well-written, clear, and original with thorough background and method exposition.  
- Provides a rigorous theoretical analysis showing advantages of the self-normalized estimator.  
- Extensive experiments validate the proposed method’s effectiveness.  
- Strength: clear introduction to BLBF and limitations of unbiased risk estimators.  
- Strength: comprehensive discussion of related work, including CRM and counterfactual estimators.  
- Strength: detailed explanation of the estimator and its practical advantages.  
- Weakness: assumes reader familiarity with BLBF and counterfactual estimation, limiting accessibility.  
- Weakness: experimental evaluation could be broader—more datasets and comparisons to other state-of-the-art methods.  
- Weakness: lacks detailed discussion of computational efficiency and real-world deployment considerations.  
- Overall: significant contribution with potential impact for recommendation, advertising, and healthcare applications.
