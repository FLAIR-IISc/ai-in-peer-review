- Addresses bridging backpropagation-trained deep networks and neuromorphic spiking hardware via a "constrain-then-train" approach.  
- Treats continuous weights and neuron outputs as probabilities so standard backpropagation yields networks mappable to binary synapse spiking hardware.  
- Trains networks with connectivity constrained to TrueNorth architecture and samples probabilistic synapses for deployment.  
- Validated on MNIST: ensemble of 64 nets achieves 99.42% (108 μJ/image); single network 92.7% (0.268 μJ/image) on TrueNorth.  
- Novel combination of constrained connectivity, probabilistic backpropagation, spiking neurons, and low-precision synapses in one framework.  
- Outperforms prior spiking/binary-synapse works (previous bests: 99.12% and 97.88%) achieving highest MNIST accuracy on TrueNorth.  
- Strength: elegant, theoretically sound idea of treating analog variables as probabilities.  
- Strength: manuscript is very well written, clearly organized with strong figures and detailed methodology.  
- Strength: compelling experimental validation with real chip-measured power-efficiency data.  
- Strength: insightful network initialization to reduce redundancy between neurons on a core.  
- Weakness: practical deployment significance underemphasized; needs explicit application scenarios (edge, mobile, IoT, wearables).  
- Weakness: lacks clear comparison of advantages over alternative methods (refs [9]-[12]), hindering assessment of relative merits.  
- Weakness: insufficient implementation details for reproducibility (input-to-spike conversion, spike frequencies, weight-sharing, exact neuron model).  
- Weakness: missing citations to related probabilistic spiking work (Qiao et al., Frontiers 2015 and arXiv:1506.05427).  
- Quality: technically sound with convincing results, but reproducibility slightly undermined by missing details.  
- Clarity: exceptionally clear and precise, aside from the noted missing implementation specifics.  
- Originality: highly original and advances the field beyond prior subset approaches.  
- Significance: demonstrates impressive hardware efficiency but needs stronger articulation of real-world impact and deployment scenarios.  
- Recommendation: accept with minor revisions to add implementation details, comparisons to alternatives, deployment discussion, and the missing citations.
