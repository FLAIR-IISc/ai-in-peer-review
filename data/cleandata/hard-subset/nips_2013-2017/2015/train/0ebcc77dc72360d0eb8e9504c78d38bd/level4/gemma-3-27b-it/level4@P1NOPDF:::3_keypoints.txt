- Introduces Infinite-Metric GP Optimization (IMGPO), a Bayesian optimization method that avoids auxiliary optimization and d-cover sampling.
- Well-organized paper with main contributions clearly stated in the introduction.
- Section 3.2 provides a clear illustrative example that gives a useful high-level understanding of the algorithm.
- Section 3.3 effectively details the algorithm’s implementation, aiding replication and future work.
- Experimental results are promising and encouraging.
- Synthesizes ideas from both branches of Bayesian global optimization literature (with and without continuity estimation).
- Clearly written and represents a strong contribution to the NIPS community.
