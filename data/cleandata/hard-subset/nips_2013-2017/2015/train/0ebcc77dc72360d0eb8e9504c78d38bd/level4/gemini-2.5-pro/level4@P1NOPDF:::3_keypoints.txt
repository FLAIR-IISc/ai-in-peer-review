- Introduces Infinite-Metric GP Optimization (IMGPO), a novel Bayesian global optimization algorithm.  
- Does not require d-cover sampling or an auxiliary optimization procedure.  
- Manuscript is exceptionally well-written and well-organized.  
- Core contributions are clearly articulated throughout the paper.  
- Section 3.2’s illustrative example effectively builds high-level intuition for the algorithm.  
- Section 3.3 details the algorithm implementation and ensures reproducibility.  
- Experimental results are compelling and support the method’s effectiveness.  
- Significant contribution in synthesizing ideas across different Bayesian optimization approaches (those using and not using continuity estimation).  
- Overall, a clear and valuable contribution to the NIPS community.
