The paper presents a method for Bayesian global optimization that avoids the need for d-cover sampling or auxiliary optimization procedures. 

The paper is well-organized, with the main contributions clearly articulated in the introductory section. The authors provide a helpful illustrative example of the algorithm in Section 3.2, offering readers a good high-level understanding. Furthermore, Section 3.3 effectively communicates the algorithm’s implementation details, facilitating potential replication and future work. The experimental results are promising and encouraging.

The authors also rightly point out that this work synthesizes ideas from both sides of the Bayesian global optimization literature – approaches that do and do not rely on continuity estimation. The paper introduces Infinite-Metric GP Optimization (IMGPO), an algorithm for Bayesian optimization that bypasses the requirements of auxiliary optimization or d-cover sampling. Overall, the paper is clearly written and represents a strong contribution to the NIPS community.
