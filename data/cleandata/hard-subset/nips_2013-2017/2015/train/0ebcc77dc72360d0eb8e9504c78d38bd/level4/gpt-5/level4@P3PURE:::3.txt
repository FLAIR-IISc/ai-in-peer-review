This paper presents Infinite-Metric GP Optimization (IMGPO), a Bayesian global optimization method that eliminates the need for d-cover sampling and auxiliary optimization. The manuscript is clearly written and well organized: the main contributions are articulated in the primary sections, making the core ideas easy to follow.

Section 3.2 provides a helpful illustrative example that offers a strong high-level understanding of the algorithm. Section 3.3 further clarifies the method by detailing its programmatic workflow, which should facilitate future implementation and replication. The experimental results are quite exciting and support the practical promise of the approach.

The authors also situate their work within the broader literature by bringing together ideas from Bayesian global optimization methods that both do and do not rely on continuity estimation. Overall, the paper clearly describes IMGPO—an algorithm for Bayesian optimization that avoids auxiliary optimization and d-cover sampling—and represents a strong contribution to the NIPS community.
