- Proposes a new global optimization algorithm that avoids delta-cover sampling and attains exponential regret, generalizing Freitas et al. (2012).  
- Paper is well-written, easy to follow, and clearly positions the method within bound-based search literature.  
- Reviewer believes the work merits community attention based on its theoretical contributions.  
- Experiments do not evaluate multiple initial function evaluations (standard in BO), preventing meaningful statistical comparison.  
- Lacks comparison to state-of-the-art Bayesian optimization methods, including information-theoretic approaches like Entropy Search.  
- All experiments are on synthetic functions (max dim=6); no real-world benchmark or application is included.  
- Overall: interesting theory but experimental section is below NIPS standards; reviewer score = 5.
