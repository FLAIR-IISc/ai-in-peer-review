The paper presents a novel method for Bayesian global optimization that eliminates the need for d-cover sampling or auxiliary optimization. The authors have organized the paper effectively, clearly highlighting the main contributions in the main section. A well-chosen illustrative example in section 3.2 provides a high-level understanding of the algorithm, while section 3.3 offers a clear and programmatically-oriented explanation, facilitating future implementation and replication. The experimental results are also noteworthy and demonstrate the potential of the proposed approach. Furthermore, the authors successfully integrate ideas from the Bayesian global optimization literature, both those that utilize continuity estimation and those that do not. The paper introduces Infinite-Metric GP Optimization (IMGPO), a Bayesian optimization algorithm that does not require auxiliary optimization or d-cover sampling. Overall, the paper is well-written and makes a significant contribution to the NIPS community.
