

The paper presents a Bayesian global optimization method that eliminates the need for d-cover sampling or auxiliary optimization. The main contributions are clearly articulated in the primary sections. Section 3.2 provides an effective illustrative example, offering readers a clear high-level understanding of the algorithm. Section 3.3 further enables reproducibility by detailing the algorithm's implementation programmatically. Experimental results are compelling and demonstrate significant promise. The authors effectively synthesize concepts from both continuity-estimation-based and non-continuity-estimation-based Bayesian global optimization literature. The proposed method, Infinite-Metric GP Optimization (IMGPO), delivers a clear contribution to the NeurIPS community through its well-written exposition and novel approach.
