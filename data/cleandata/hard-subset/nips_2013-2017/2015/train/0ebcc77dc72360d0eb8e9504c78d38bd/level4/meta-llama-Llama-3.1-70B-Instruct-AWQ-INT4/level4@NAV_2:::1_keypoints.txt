- Proposes IMGPO, a novel GP-based global optimization algorithm that avoids delta-cover sampling and auxiliary optimization.  
- Generalizes de Freitas et al. by leveraging an unknown bound via continuity at the global optimizer.  
- Simultaneously conducts global and local search by considering all candidate bounds.  
- Theoretical analysis shows exponential convergence with regret O(λ^(N+Ngp)), λ < 1.  
- Paper is well-written and situates the algorithm within bound-based search literature.  
- Experiments on synthetic functions (≤6D) show IMGPO generally outperforms SOO, BaMSOO, GP-PI, and GP-EI.  
- Experimental methodology is limited: no experiments with multiple initial evaluations of f (standard in BO).  
- Missing comparisons to state-of-the-art BO methods (e.g., information-theoretic approaches like Entropy Search).  
- No real-world experiments; all evaluations are synthetic and low-dimensional.  
- Experimental section deemed below NIPS standards despite interesting theoretical contributions.  
- Reviewer vote: 5 — paper deserves attention but experimental improvements required for higher score.
