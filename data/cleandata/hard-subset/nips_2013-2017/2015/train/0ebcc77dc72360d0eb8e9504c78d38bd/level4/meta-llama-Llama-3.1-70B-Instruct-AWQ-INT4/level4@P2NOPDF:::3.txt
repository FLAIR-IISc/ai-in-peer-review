The paper presents a novel approach to Bayesian global optimization, eliminating the need for d-cover sampling or auxiliary optimization. The authors have organized the manuscript effectively, clearly highlighting the main contributions in the primary sections.

A notable strength of the paper is its use of illustrative examples, particularly in Section 3.2, which provides readers with a high-level understanding of the algorithm. Furthermore, Section 3.3 offers a clear and detailed explanation of the algorithm's workings, making it easier for future researchers to implement and replicate the method.

The experimental results are also noteworthy, demonstrating the potential of the proposed approach. The authors have successfully integrated ideas from various Bayesian global optimization literature, including those that utilize continuity estimation and those that do not.

The paper introduces Infinite-Metric GP Optimization (IMGPO), an innovative algorithm for Bayesian optimization that does not rely on auxiliary optimization or d-cover sampling. The manuscript is well-written, and the authors make a significant contribution to the NIPS community.
