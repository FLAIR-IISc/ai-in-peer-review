- Introduces a novel global optimization algorithm that avoids delta-cover sampling and attains exponential regret.  
- Generalizes Freitas et al. (2012) by removing reliance on an impractical sampling procedure.  
- Well-written, clear, and well-situated within bounded-based search literature.  
- Merits attention from the community based on theoretical contributions.  
- Reviewer assigned score 5 due to concerns about the experimental evaluation.  
- Experiments lack multiple random initial evaluations of f, preventing meaningful statistical comparisons.  
- No comparisons to state-of-the-art Bayesian optimization methods (e.g., information-theoretic/Entropy Search).  
- All experiments are on synthetic functions up to 6 dimensions; no real-world or practical application experiments included.  
- Conclusion: strong theoretical results but experimental section is insufficient for a top-tier BO paper.
