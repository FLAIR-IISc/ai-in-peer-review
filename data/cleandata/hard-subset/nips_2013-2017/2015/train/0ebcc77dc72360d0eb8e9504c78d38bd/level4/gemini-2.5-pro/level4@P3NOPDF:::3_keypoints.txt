- Introduces IMGPO, a novel algorithm for Bayesian global optimization.
- Eliminates the need for an auxiliary optimization subroutine to maximize the acquisition function.
- Avoids reliance on d-cover sampling.
- Synthesizes ideas from approaches that do and do not use continuity estimation.
- Paper is exceptionally well-written and well-organized.
- Section 3.2’s illustrative example provides strong intuition for the algorithm.
- Clear pseudocode in Section 3.3 improves reproducibility and ease of implementation.
- Empirical evaluation shows IMGPO performs competitively against established baselines.
- Technical contribution reduces computational overhead by sidestepping auxiliary optimization.
- Offers a more unified and potentially more robust perspective bridging continuity-based and non-continuity methods.
- Overall high-quality work with a strong recommendation for acceptance.
