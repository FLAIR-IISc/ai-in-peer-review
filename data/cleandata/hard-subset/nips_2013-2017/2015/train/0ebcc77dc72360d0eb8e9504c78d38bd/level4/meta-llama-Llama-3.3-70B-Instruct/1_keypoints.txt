- Proposes a novel global optimization algorithm that avoids delta-cover sampling and achieves exponential regret, extending Freitas et al. (2012).  
- Paper is well-structured, clear, and situates the algorithm within bounded-based search literature.  
- Reviewer considers the work worthy of attention but assigns a score of 5.  
- Major concern: experimental section is inadequate by BO standards.  
- Experiments lack multiple initial evaluations/random restarts, preventing meaningful statistical comparisons.  
- No comparisons against state-of-the-art BO methods (e.g., Entropy Search).  
- All experiments are synthetic and low-dimensional (≤6D); no real-world/wetlab or parameter-tuning tasks.  
- Conclusion: strong theoretical contribution but experimental validation is insufficient for a NIPS-level paper.
