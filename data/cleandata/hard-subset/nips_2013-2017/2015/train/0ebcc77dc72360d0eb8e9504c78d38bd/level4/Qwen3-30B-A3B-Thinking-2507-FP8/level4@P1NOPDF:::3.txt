

The paper introduces Infinite-Metric GP Optimization (IMGPO), a novel Bayesian global optimization method that avoids the need for d-cover sampling or auxiliary optimization. The authors clearly articulate the core contributions in the main body, making the technical advances accessible. Section 3.2 provides an effective illustrative example that conveys the algorithm’s intuitive mechanics at a high level. Section 3.3 further strengthens the presentation by offering clear pseudocode, enabling straightforward implementation and replication. The experimental results are compelling, demonstrating the method’s practical efficacy. The paper also thoughtfully synthesizes ideas from both continuity-estimation-based and continuity-estimation-free approaches in Bayesian global optimization, highlighting the algorithm’s position within the broader literature. Overall, the writing is clear and the contribution represents a meaningful advancement for the NIPS community.
