- Proposes Infinite-Metric GP Optimization (IMGPO), a novel Bayesian optimization method with exponential convergence that avoids auxiliary optimization and δ-cover sampling.  
- Leverages an unknown bound encoded by continuity at the global optimizer to guide search.  
- Paper is well-organized and contributions are clearly described.  
- Provides a strong theoretical foundation supporting the algorithm’s claims.  
- Section 3.2 offers a clear illustrative example that aids high-level understanding.  
- Section 3.3 describes the algorithm programmatically, facilitating implementation and replication.  
- Experimental results show IMGPO outperforms or is competitive with state-of-the-art algorithms.  
- Integrates ideas from Bayesian optimization approaches that both use and do not use continuity estimation, offering a unique perspective.  
- Writing is clear, technically sound, and demonstrates understanding of related work with proper comparisons.  
- Main weakness: potential lack of scalability to higher-dimensional problems, as acknowledged by the authors.  
- Paper would benefit from a more detailed discussion of practical applications and limitations.
