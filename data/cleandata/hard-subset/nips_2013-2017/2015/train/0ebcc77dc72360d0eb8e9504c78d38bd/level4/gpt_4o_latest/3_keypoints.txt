- Proposes a Bayesian global optimization method that removes the need for d-cover sampling and auxiliary optimization.  
- Paper structure effectively conveys the main contributions in the main section.  
- Section 3.2 provides a clear, high-level illustrative example of the algorithm.  
- Section 3.3 programmatically details the algorithm, aiding implementation and replication.  
- Experimental results are particularly compelling and strengthen the paper’s appeal.  
- Paper situates the work within Bayesian global optimization literature, covering approaches that both rely on and bypass continuity estimation.  
- Introduces Infinite-Metric GP Optimization (IMGPO), a well-written and significant contribution to the NIPS community.
