- Novel algorithm for global optimization achieving exponential regret while avoiding delta-cover sampling.  
- Generalizes Freitas et al. (2012) by removing an impractical sampling requirement.  
- Paper is well-written, clearly presented, and well-positioned within bound-based search literature.  
- Theoretical results are compelling.  
- Presents a practical algorithm that removes a significant limitation of prior work.  
- Reviewer gives a borderline score of 5 due to weak experimental rigor.  
- Experiments lack statistical comparison (appear to use single runs; no means/standard errors reported).  
- Insufficient baselines—omits prominent information-theoretic methods like ES/PES.  
- Limited experimental scope—only synthetic low-dimensional benchmarks (up to D=6), no real-world or large-scale tasks.  
- Overall: strong theory and clarity, but empirical validation must be substantially improved.
