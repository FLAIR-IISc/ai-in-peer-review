

The paper introduces a Bayesian global optimization method that eliminates the need for d-cover sampling or auxiliary optimization. The main contributions are clearly articulated in the primary sections. Section 3.2 provides a well-illustrated example of the algorithm, offering readers a clear high-level understanding. Section 3.3 further enhances reproducibility by programmatically detailing the algorithm's implementation. The experimental results are compelling. The authors also effectively synthesize ideas from both branches of the Bayesian global optimization literature—those that use continuity estimation and those that do not. The paper formally presents Infinite-Metric GP Optimization (IMGPO), an algorithm for Bayesian optimization without auxiliary optimization or d-cover sampling. It is clearly written and makes a significant contribution to the NIPS community.
