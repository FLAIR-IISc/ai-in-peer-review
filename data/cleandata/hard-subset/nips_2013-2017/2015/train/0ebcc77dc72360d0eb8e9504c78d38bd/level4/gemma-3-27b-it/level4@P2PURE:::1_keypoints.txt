- Proposes a novel global optimization algorithm that avoids delta-cover sampling and achieves exponential regret, generalizing Freitas et al. (2012).  
- Paper is well-written, clear, and situates the method within the bounded-based search literature.  
- Reviewer believes the work merits attention but assigns a score of 5 due to experimental shortcomings.  
- Experiments do not use multiple initial evaluations, so statistically sound comparisons are lacking.  
- No comparisons to state-of-the-art Bayesian optimization methods (e.g., Information-theoretic approaches like Entropy Search).  
- Experimental evaluation limited to synthetic functions up to 6 dimensions; no real-world parameter-tuning experiments.  
- Summary: strong theoretical contribution but experimental section falls short of NIPS expectations.
