This paper introduces Infinite-Metric Gaussian Process Optimization (IMGPO), a novel method for Bayesian global optimization that avoids the need for computationally expensive d-cover sampling or auxiliary optimization procedures. The paper is well-structured, with the core contributions clearly presented. Section 3.2 offers a helpful illustrative example that provides a strong intuitive understanding of the algorithm, and Section 3.3 details the implementation, facilitating replication by other researchers. The experimental results are promising. Furthermore, the authors effectively synthesize ideas from both continuity-estimation-based and non-continuity-estimation-based approaches within the Bayesian optimization literature. Overall, this is a clearly written and significant contribution to the NIPS community.
