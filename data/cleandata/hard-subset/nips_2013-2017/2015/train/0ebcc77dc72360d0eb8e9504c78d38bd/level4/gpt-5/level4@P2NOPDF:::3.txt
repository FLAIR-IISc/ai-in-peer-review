This paper introduces Infinite-Metric GP Optimization (IMGPO), a Bayesian global optimization method that avoids both d-cover sampling and auxiliary optimization. The manuscript is well organized, with the main contributions clearly presented in the core sections.

Section 3.2 provides a clear, illustrative example that builds high-level intuition for the algorithm. Section 3.3 complements this with a concise, programmatic description that should make implementation and replication straightforward. The experimental results are compelling and suggest strong practical performance.

The authors also position their work thoughtfully within the Bayesian optimization literature, integrating ideas from approaches that do and do not rely on continuity estimation. Overall, the paper is clearly written and, in my view, makes a strong and valuable contribution to the NIPS community.
