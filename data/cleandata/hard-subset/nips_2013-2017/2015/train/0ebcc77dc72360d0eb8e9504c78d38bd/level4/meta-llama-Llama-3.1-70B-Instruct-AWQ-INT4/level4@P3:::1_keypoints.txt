- New global optimization algorithm (IMGPO) avoiding delta-cover sampling and achieving exponential regret, generalizing de Freitas et al. (2012).
- Paper is well-written, easy to follow, and clearly positions the algorithm within bound-based search literature.
- Provides a thorough description of IMGPO and its relationship to previous algorithms.
- Strong theoretical results: exponential convergence rate and regret bound are well-presented.
- Experimental section is insufficient by BO standards.
- Does not evaluate methods across multiple initial evaluations, limiting statistical comparisons.
- Fails to compare against state-of-the-art BO approaches, including information-theoretic methods like Entropy Search.
- Experiments are limited to synthetic functions (≤6 dimensions) with no real-world or practical application tests.
- Conclusion: theoretically valuable and interesting algorithm but needs more comprehensive experiments and real-world validation.
- Overall rating: 5 (borderline).
