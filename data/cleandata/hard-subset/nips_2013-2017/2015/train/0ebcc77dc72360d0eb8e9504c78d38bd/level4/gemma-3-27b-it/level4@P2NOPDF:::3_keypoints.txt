- Introduces IMGPO, a novel Bayesian global optimization method that avoids costly d-cover sampling and auxiliary optimization steps.  
- Core contribution is a streamlined Bayesian optimization algorithm, clearly articulated in the paper.  
- Section 3.2 gives a compelling example that builds strong conceptual understanding of IMGPO.  
- Section 3.3 details implementation in a way that facilitates replication by other researchers.  
- Programmatic clarity is particularly valuable for adoption and reproducibility.  
- Experimental results are promising and demonstrate IMGPO’s potential.  
- The work synthesizes ideas from different strands of Bayesian optimization, including approaches with and without continuity estimation.  
- Overall, the paper is well-written and offers a practical, efficient alternative to existing Bayesian optimization techniques.
