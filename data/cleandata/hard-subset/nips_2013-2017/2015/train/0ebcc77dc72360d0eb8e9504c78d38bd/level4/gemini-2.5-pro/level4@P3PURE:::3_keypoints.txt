- Introduces Infinite-Metric GP Optimization (IMGPO), a Bayesian global optimization algorithm that avoids d-cover sampling and auxiliary optimization.
- Paper is well-organized, clearly written, and the main contributions are well articulated.
- Clarity is a key strength; Section 3.2 provides an effective illustrative example for high-level understanding.
- Section 3.3 clearly outlines the algorithm implementation, facilitating replication.
- Strong technical contribution by synthesizing ideas from branches that do and do not rely on continuity estimation.
- Experimental results are compelling and demonstrate the proposed method's promise.
- Overall, the work is well-motivated, clearly presented, and makes a significant contribution to Bayesian optimization.
