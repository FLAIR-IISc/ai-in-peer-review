This paper introduces a novel Bayesian global optimization method, Infinite-Metric GP Optimization (IMGPO), that avoids the complexities of d-cover sampling and auxiliary optimization procedures. 

The manuscript is well-structured, with a clear presentation of the core contributions in the main body of the work. 

A compelling illustrative example in Section 3.2 effectively conveys a high-level understanding of the algorithm, and Section 3.3 provides a detailed programmatic description suitable for implementation and replication.

The experimental results demonstrate promising performance. 

Notably, the authors successfully integrate concepts from both continuity-estimation-based and non-continuity-estimation-based approaches within the Bayesian global optimization literature. This work is both clearly articulated and represents a significant contribution to the NIPS conference.
