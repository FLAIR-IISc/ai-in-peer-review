This paper introduces Infinite-Metric Gaussian Process Optimization (IMGPO), a novel Bayesian global optimization method that avoids reliance on d-cover sampling or auxiliary optimization procedures. 

The paper is well-structured, with a clear presentation of its core contributions in the main body of work. 

Section 3.2 effectively illustrates the algorithm’s functionality, providing a valuable high-level understanding for readers. Furthermore, Section 3.3 offers a detailed programmatic description, facilitating potential implementation and replication of the proposed method.

The experimental results presented are compelling and demonstrate the promise of IMGPO. 

Notably, the authors highlight the paper’s strength in synthesizing concepts from diverse areas within the Bayesian global optimization literature – specifically, approaches both with and without continuity estimation.

Overall, this is a clearly written and significant contribution to the field, and will be of considerable interest to the NIPS community.
