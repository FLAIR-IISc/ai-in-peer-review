This paper presents Infinite-Metric GP Optimization (IMGPO), a method for Bayesian global optimization that does not require auxiliary optimization or d-cover sampling. A key contribution is the synthesis of ideas from two distinct lines of work in Bayesian global optimization: those that rely on continuity estimation and those that do not.

The paper is well-organized, with its main contributions clearly articulated. The authors provide an effective illustrative example in Section 3.2 that gives the reader a high-level understanding of the algorithm. Furthermore, Section 3.3 offers a clear, programmatic description of the algorithm that should facilitate its implementation and replication. The experimental results are compelling and demonstrate the effectiveness of the proposed method.

Overall, this is a well-written paper that makes a strong contribution to the field of Bayesian optimization.
