- Proposes a novel Bayesian global optimization method (IMGPO) that removes the need for d-cover sampling and auxiliary optimization.  
- Paper is well organized with main contributions clearly presented.  
- Effectively balances theoretical depth with intuitive explanations.  
- Illustrative example in Section 3.2 provides a high-level, accessible understanding of the algorithm.  
- Section 3.3 gives a programmatic explanation that facilitates implementation and replication.  
- Experimental results demonstrate the practical potential of the proposed approach.  
- Successfully integrates ideas from multiple strands of Bayesian global optimization literature.  
- Addresses both approaches that use continuity estimation and those that do not.  
- Writing is clear and accessible.  
- Contribution is substantial and valuable to the NIPS community.
