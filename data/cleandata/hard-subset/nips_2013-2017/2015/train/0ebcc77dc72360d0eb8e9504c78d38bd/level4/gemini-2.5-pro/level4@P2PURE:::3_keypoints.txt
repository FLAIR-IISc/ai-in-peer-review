- Introduces Infinite-Metric GP Optimization (IMGPO), a Bayesian global optimization method.
- IMGPO avoids the need for auxiliary optimization and d-cover sampling.
- Synthesizes ideas from two Bayesian optimization approaches: those using continuity estimation and those that do not.
- Paper is well-organized with clearly articulated main contributions.
- Section 3.2 provides an effective illustrative example for high-level understanding.
- Section 3.3 gives a clear, programmatic algorithm description that aids implementation and replication.
- Experimental results are compelling and demonstrate the method’s effectiveness.
- Overall, a well-written paper making a strong contribution to Bayesian optimization.
