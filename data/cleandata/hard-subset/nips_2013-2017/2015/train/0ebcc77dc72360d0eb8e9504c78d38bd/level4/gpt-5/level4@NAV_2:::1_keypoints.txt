- Introduces a global optimization algorithm that avoids δ-cover sampling and achieves exponential simple-regret convergence, extending de Freitas et al. (2012).  
- Manuscript is clearly written and positions the algorithm well within bound-based search methods.  
- Reviewer believes the work merits attention from the community.  
- Assigned score 5 because the experimental evaluation is insufficient.  
- Experiments do not use multiple random initializations of f, so no meaningful statistical comparisons are provided.  
- Experiments omit comparisons with state-of-the-art Bayesian optimization methods, particularly information-theoretic approaches (e.g., Entropy Search).  
- All experiments are on synthetic benchmarks (max dimension 6); no real-world tasks (e.g., wet-lab or hyperparameter tuning) are included.  
- Theory and presentation are appealing, but the experimental evaluation is below conference (NIPS) standards.
