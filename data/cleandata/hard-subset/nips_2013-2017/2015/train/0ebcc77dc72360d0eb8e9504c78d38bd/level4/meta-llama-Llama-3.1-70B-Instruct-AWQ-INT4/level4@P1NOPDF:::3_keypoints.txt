- Proposes a novel Bayesian global optimization method (IMGPO) that removes the need for d-cover sampling and auxiliary optimization.
- Paper is well-organized with main contributions clearly outlined early on.
- Section 3.2 provides a clear, high-level illustrative example of the algorithm.
- Section 3.3 offers a programmatically-oriented explanation that facilitates implementation and replication.
- Experimental results are strong and demonstrate the algorithm’s potential.
- Successfully integrates ideas from Bayesian global optimization literature, including approaches both with and without continuity estimation.
- Overall well-written and a significant contribution to the NIPS community.
