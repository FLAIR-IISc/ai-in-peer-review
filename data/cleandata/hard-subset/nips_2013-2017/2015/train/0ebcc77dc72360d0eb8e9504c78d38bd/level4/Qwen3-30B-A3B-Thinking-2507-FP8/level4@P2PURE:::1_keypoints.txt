- Introduces a novel global optimization algorithm that avoids delta-cover sampling while achieving exponential regret.  
- Generalizes and improves on Freitas et al. (2012) by removing an impractical sampling requirement.  
- Manuscript is well-written, clearly structured, and situates the algorithm within bound-based search methods.  
- Reviewer recommends strong accept (score 5) but with reservations due to experimental weaknesses.  
- Experiments do not include multiple randomized initial evaluations, preventing meaningful statistical comparisons.  
- No comparisons against state-of-the-art Bayesian optimization methods (e.g., information-theoretic/Entropy Search).  
- All experiments are on synthetic functions (max dimension 6); no real-world or practical tuning tasks are evaluated.  
- Theoretical contributions are strong, but the experimental rigor is insufficient for a NIPS-level submission.
