- Proposes a novel global optimization algorithm achieving exponential regret without delta-cover sampling, generalizing Freitas et al. (2012).  
- Paper is clearly written and well-situated in the literature; theoretical contributions are interesting and valuable.  
- Experiments lack statistical rigor: single runs without multiple random initializations prevent meaningful, robust comparisons.  
- Insufficient baselines: omits comparisons to key state-of-the-art methods, notably information-theoretic approaches like Entropy Search and Predictive Entropy Search.  
- Limited evaluation scope: only low-dimensional synthetic benchmarks (≤6D); no real-world or ML hyperparameter tuning tasks to demonstrate practicality or scalability.  
- Overall: strong theoretical core but empirical validation is weak, leading to a borderline recommendation pending substantial experimental improvements.
