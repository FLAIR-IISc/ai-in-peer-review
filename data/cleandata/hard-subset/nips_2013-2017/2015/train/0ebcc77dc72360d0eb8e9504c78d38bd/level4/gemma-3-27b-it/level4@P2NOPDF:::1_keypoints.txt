- Introduces a novel global optimization algorithm that avoids delta-cover sampling.  
- Demonstrates exponential regret, extending Freitas et al. (2012) and addressing sampling impracticality.  
- Paper is clearly written and well-structured.  
- Work has potential significant interest to the research community.  
- Overall assessment score: 5.  
- Experimental evaluation is insufficient to justify a higher score.  
- Lacks experiments varying the number of initial function evaluations (standard in BO for statistical comparisons).  
- Omits comparisons to state-of-the-art Bayesian Optimization methods, notably information-theoretic approaches like Entropy Search.  
- Experiments are limited to synthetic functions (≤6 dimensions) and lack real-world parameter-tuning or "wet lab" applications.  
- Addressing the empirical shortcomings would substantially strengthen the paper.
