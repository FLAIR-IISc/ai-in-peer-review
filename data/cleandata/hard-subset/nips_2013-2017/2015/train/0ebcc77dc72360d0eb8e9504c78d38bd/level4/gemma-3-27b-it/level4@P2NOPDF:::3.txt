This paper introduces Infinite-Metric Gaussian Process Optimization (IMGPO), a novel approach to Bayesian global optimization that avoids the computational burdens of d-cover sampling and auxiliary optimization procedures commonly found in existing methods. The core contribution – a streamlined Bayesian optimization algorithm – is clearly articulated within the main body of the paper. 

The authors effectively illustrate the algorithm’s functionality. Section 3.2 provides a compelling example that fosters a strong conceptual understanding, while Section 3.3 details the implementation aspects in a way that should facilitate replication by other researchers. This programmatic clarity is particularly valuable.

The experimental results are promising and demonstrate the potential of IMGPO. Furthermore, the authors appropriately highlight the work's contribution as a synthesis of ideas from diverse strands within the Bayesian optimization literature—specifically, approaches utilizing and foregoing continuity estimation. 

Overall, this is a well-written and significant contribution to the NIPS community. The paper presents a valuable advancement in Bayesian optimization, offering a practical and efficient alternative to existing techniques.
