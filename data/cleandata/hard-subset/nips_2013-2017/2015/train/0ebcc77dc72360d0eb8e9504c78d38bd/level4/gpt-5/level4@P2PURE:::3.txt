The paper presents Infinite-Metric GP Optimization (IMGPO), a Bayesian global optimization algorithm that eliminates both d-cover sampling and auxiliary optimization. The manuscript is well organized, with the main contributions clearly articulated in the main section. Section 3.2 offers an effective illustrative example that provides a clear high-level understanding of the approach, while Section 3.3 concisely conveys the algorithmic details in a programmatic form that supports implementation and replication. The experimental results are compelling. The authors also situate the work within the broader Bayesian global optimization literature, synthesizing ideas from approaches that do and do not rely on continuity estimation. Overall, the paper is clearly written and makes a strong contribution to the NIPS community.
