

The paper introduces Infinite-Metric GP Optimization (IMGPO), a novel Bayesian global optimization method that eliminates the need for d-cover sampling or auxiliary optimization. The main contributions are clearly articulated in the primary section. Section 3.2 provides a well-chosen illustrative example that effectively conveys the algorithm's high-level intuition. Section 3.3 offers a precise, programmatic description of the algorithm, facilitating implementation and replication. The experimental results are compelling and demonstrate significant promise. The authors also effectively synthesize key ideas from Bayesian global optimization literature, specifically addressing both approaches that utilize continuity estimation and those that do not. The paper is well-written and presents a meaningful contribution to the NIPS community.
