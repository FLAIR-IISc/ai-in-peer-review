- Main idea: leverage an unknown bound from continuity at the global optimizer, formalized in Assumption 1 (existence of x* and a finite semi-metric `).  
- Proposes IMGPO: a GP-based global optimization algorithm using hierarchical partitioning and bound-based search to select queries.  
- Theoretical result: IMGPO attains exponential convergence O(λ^{N+N_gp}) with λ < 1; provides a thorough regret bound analysis.  
- Algorithm description and connections to prior work are clear and detailed.  
- Experiments show IMGPO generally outperforms SOO, BaMSOO, GP-PI, and GP-EI; SOO is competitive on Rosenbrock2.
