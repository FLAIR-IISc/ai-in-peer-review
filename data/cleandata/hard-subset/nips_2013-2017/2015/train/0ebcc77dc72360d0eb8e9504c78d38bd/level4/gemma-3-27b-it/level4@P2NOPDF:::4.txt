This paper presents an interesting approach and appears to be well-executed. The abstract’s formulation feels somewhat redundant (line 49). A minor typographical issue exists in the function definition on line 72, with a misplaced parenthesis. 

My understanding of Gaussian Processes (GPs) and global optimization is limited; therefore, my evaluation is based on an informed, though potentially incomplete, assessment. However, the paper is exceptionally well-written and serves as a clear and didactic explanation of the proposed methods. The authors effectively utilize figures to illustrate the procedures, enhancing comprehension. 

Both the theoretical analysis and the experimental results strongly support the claims regarding the advantages of the presented approach. The results appear conclusive in demonstrating the effectiveness of the method. Overall, this is a solid contribution.
