- Introduces a new global optimization algorithm achieving exponential regret while avoiding delta-cover sampling.  
- Generalizes de Freitas et al. (2012) by replacing their impractical sampling procedure.  
- Well-written, easy to follow, and clearly placed within bound-based search literature.  
- Noteworthy theoretical contribution deserving community attention.  
- Experimental section is weak, prompting a borderline recommendation.  
- Does not use multiple random initializations, preventing meaningful statistical comparisons.  
- Fails to compare against state-of-the-art BO methods (e.g., Entropy Search).  
- Evaluation limited to low-dimensional (≤6D) synthetic functions with no real-world experiments (e.g., hyperparameter tuning).  
- Conclusion: interesting theory but insufficient experimental validation for publication at this venue.
