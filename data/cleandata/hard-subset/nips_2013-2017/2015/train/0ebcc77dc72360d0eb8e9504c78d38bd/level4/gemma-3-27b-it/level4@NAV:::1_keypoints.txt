- Proposes a novel global optimization algorithm that avoids delta-cover sampling and achieves exponential regret.  
- Extends Freitas et al. (2012) by removing reliance on an impractical sampling procedure.  
- Manuscript is clearly written and well-structured and situates the algorithm within bounded-based search literature.  
- The work has potential interest for the research community.  
- Reviewer assigns score 5 due to concerns with the experimental evaluation.  
- Experiments do not use multiple initial evaluations, preventing robust statistical comparison.  
- Results are not benchmarked against current state-of-the-art Bayesian optimization methods (e.g., Entropy Search).  
- Experimental evaluation is limited to synthetic functions up to 6 dimensions with no real-world parameter tuning tasks.  
- Overall: promising theoretical contribution but experimental validation requires significant improvement.
