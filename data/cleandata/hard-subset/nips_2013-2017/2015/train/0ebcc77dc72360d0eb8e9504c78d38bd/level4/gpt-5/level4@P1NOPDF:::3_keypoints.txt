- Proposes Infinite-Metric GP Optimization (IMGPO), a Bayesian global optimization algorithm that avoids d-cover sampling and auxiliary optimization.
- Manuscript is well organized with main contributions clearly described.
- Section 3.2 provides a clear, illustrative example that conveys a high-level understanding of the algorithm.
- Section 3.3 presents the method programmatically, facilitating implementation and replication.
- Experimental results are compelling and support the proposed approach.
- Paper effectively unifies ideas from Bayesian global optimization literature that both use and do not use continuity estimation.
- Overall, the paper is clearly written and makes a strong contribution to the NIPS community.
