This paper presents Infinite-Metric GP Optimization (IMGPO), a Bayesian global optimization algorithm that avoids both d-cover sampling and auxiliary optimization.

The manuscript is well organized; the main contributions are clearly described in the main section. Section 3.2 provides a clear, illustrative example that conveys a high-level understanding of the algorithm. Section 3.3 then lays out the method programmatically, which should facilitate future implementation and replication.

The experimental results are compelling and support the approach. In addition, the paper helpfully unifies ideas from the Bayesian global optimization literature that both use and do not use continuity estimation.

Overall, the paper is clearly written and makes a strong contribution to the NIPS community.
