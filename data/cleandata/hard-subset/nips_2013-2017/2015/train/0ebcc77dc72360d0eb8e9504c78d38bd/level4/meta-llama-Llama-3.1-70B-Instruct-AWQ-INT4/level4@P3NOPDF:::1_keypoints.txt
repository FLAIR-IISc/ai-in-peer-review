- Proposes a novel global optimization algorithm that avoids delta-cover sampling and achieves exponential regret.  
- Generalizes Freitas et al. (2012) by removing an impractical sampling requirement.  
- Paper is well-written, easy to follow, and clearly situates the method within bounded-based search literature.  
- Theoretical contributions are significant and deserving of community attention.  
- Assigned a score of 5 primarily because the experimental section is insufficient.  
- Experiments omit comparisons over multiple initial evaluations of f, preventing meaningful statistical analysis.  
- No comparison to state-of-the-art BO methods, notably information-theoretic approaches like Entropy Search.  
- All experiments are synthetic and low-dimensional (max dim = 6); no real-world (e.g., wetlab or parameter tuning) evaluations.  
- Overall: strong theory but experimental evaluation does not meet expected NIPS standards.
