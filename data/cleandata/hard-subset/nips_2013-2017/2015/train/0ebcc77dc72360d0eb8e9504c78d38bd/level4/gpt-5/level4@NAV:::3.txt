Review- This work presents a Bayesian global optimization approach that dispenses with d-cover sampling and any auxiliary optimization.
The manuscript is structured so that the core contributions are clearly articulated in the main section.
Section 3.2 offers a helpful illustrative example of the algorithm, giving readers a clear, high-level intuition.
Section 3.3 further lays out the algorithm procedurally, facilitating future implementation and replication.
The experimental results are likewise quite compelling.
The authors also note that the paper synthesizes ideas from the Bayesian global optimization literature that both do and do not rely on continuity estimation.
The work introduces Infinite-Metric GP Optimization (IMGPO), a Bayesian optimization method that avoids auxiliary optimization and d-cover sampling. The paper is clearly written and constitutes a strong contribution to the NIPS community.
