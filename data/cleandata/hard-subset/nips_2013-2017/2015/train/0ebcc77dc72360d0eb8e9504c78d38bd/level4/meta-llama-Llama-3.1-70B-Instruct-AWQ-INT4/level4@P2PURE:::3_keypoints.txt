- Proposes a novel Bayesian global optimization method eliminating the need for d-cover sampling and auxiliary optimization.  
- Paper is well-organized and clearly highlights the main contributions.  
- Section 3.2 provides a well-chosen illustrative example giving a high-level understanding of the algorithm.  
- Section 3.3 offers a clear, programmatically-oriented explanation that facilitates implementation and replication.  
- Experimental results are strong and demonstrate the proposed approach’s potential.  
- Successfully integrates ideas from Bayesian global optimization literature, both with and without continuity estimation.  
- Introduces the Infinite-Metric GP Optimization (IMGPO) algorithm.  
- Well-written and a significant contribution to the NIPS community.
