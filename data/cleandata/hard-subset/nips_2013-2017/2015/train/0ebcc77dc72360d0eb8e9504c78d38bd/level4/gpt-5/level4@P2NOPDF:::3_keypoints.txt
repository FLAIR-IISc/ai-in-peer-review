- Introduces Infinite-Metric GP Optimization (IMGPO), a Bayesian global optimization method that avoids both d-cover sampling and auxiliary optimization.  
- Manuscript is well organized with main contributions clearly presented.  
- Section 3.2 offers a clear, illustrative example that builds high-level intuition.  
- Section 3.3 provides a concise, programmatic description that facilitates implementation and replication.  
- Experimental results are compelling and indicate strong practical performance.  
- Positions the work thoughtfully within the Bayesian optimization literature, integrating approaches that do and do not rely on continuity estimation.
