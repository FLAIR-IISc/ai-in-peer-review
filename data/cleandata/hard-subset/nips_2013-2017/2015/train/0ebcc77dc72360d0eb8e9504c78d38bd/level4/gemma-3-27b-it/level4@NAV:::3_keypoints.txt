- Introduces Infinite-Metric Gaussian Process Optimization (IMGPO), a novel Bayesian global optimization method that avoids d-cover sampling and auxiliary optimization procedures.
- Paper is well-structured with a clear presentation of core contributions.
- Section 3.2 provides an effective high-level illustration of the algorithm’s functionality.
- Section 3.3 offers a detailed programmatic description that aids implementation and replication.
- Experimental results are compelling and demonstrate IMGPO’s promise.
- Strength lies in synthesizing concepts across Bayesian global optimization, combining approaches with and without continuity estimation.
- Overall, the paper is clearly written, significant, and of considerable interest to the NIPS community.
