- Introduces a new global optimization algorithm that avoids delta‑cover sampling, achieves exponential regret, and generalizes Freitas et al. (2012).
- Paper is well written, easy to follow, and clearly positions the algorithm within bound‑based search literature.
- Reviewer believes the work deserves attention and assigns a score of 5.
- Experimental methodology lacking: no multiple initial evaluations of f, so statistical comparisons are not meaningful.
- Missing comparisons to state‑of‑the‑art Bayesian optimization methods (e.g., Entropy Search / information‑theoretic approaches).
- Experiments limited to synthetic functions (≤6D); no real wet‑lab or parameter‑tuning benchmarks to show practical performance.
- Overall: solid theoretical contribution but experimental evaluation is below NIPS standards.
