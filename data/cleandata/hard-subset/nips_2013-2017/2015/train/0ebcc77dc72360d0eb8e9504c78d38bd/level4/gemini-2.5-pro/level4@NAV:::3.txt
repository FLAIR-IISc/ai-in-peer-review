This paper presents Infinite-Metric GP Optimization (IMGPO), an algorithm for Bayesian global optimization that circumvents the need for d-cover sampling or auxiliary optimization. The manuscript is well-structured, with its primary contributions clearly articulated in the main section.

The authors include an effective illustrative example in Section 3.2, which provides the reader with a strong high-level intuition for the algorithm. Following this, Section 3.3 clearly details the procedural mechanics of the method, facilitating future implementation and replication. The experimental results are compelling and show considerable promise.

Notably, the authors also bridge distinct ideas from the Bayesian global optimization literature, synthesizing concepts from approaches that do and do not use continuity estimation. This is a clearly written paper that represents a strong and valuable contribution to the NeurIPS community.
