The paper presents a novel method for Bayesian global optimization that eliminates the need for d-cover sampling or auxiliary optimization. The authors have organized the paper effectively, clearly outlining the main contributions in the primary section. 

The illustrative example in section 3.2 is particularly noteworthy, providing readers with a high-level understanding of the algorithm. Furthermore, section 3.3 offers a clear and programmatically-oriented explanation of the algorithm's inner workings, facilitating future implementation and replication. 

The experimental results are also impressive and demonstrate the algorithm's potential. Additionally, the authors successfully integrate ideas from the Bayesian global optimization literature, both those that utilize continuity estimation and those that do not. 

The paper introduces Infinite-Metric GP Optimization (IMGPO), an algorithm for Bayesian optimization that does not require auxiliary optimization or d-cover sampling. Overall, the paper is well-written and makes a significant contribution to the NIPS community.
