- Introduces IMGPO, a Bayesian optimization algorithm attaining exponential convergence without auxiliary acquisition optimization or δ-cover sampling.
- Leverages a GP prior, hierarchical partitioning, and principled UCB use to balance global and local search while implicitly handling unknown continuity bounds.
- Builds on and contrasts with GP-UCB and BaMSOO, keeping GP guidance but avoiding computational bottlenecks and unrealistic sampling assumptions.

- 1D illustrative walkthrough and precise pseudocode provide intuitive understanding and make implementation straightforward.
- Empirical results are compelling across benchmarks and avoid hand-tuned kernels, supporting robustness and theoretical claims.
- Technical development appears sound, deriving an exponential regret bound from stated assumptions.
