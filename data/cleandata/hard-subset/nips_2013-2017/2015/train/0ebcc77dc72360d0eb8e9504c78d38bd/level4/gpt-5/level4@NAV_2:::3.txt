The paper presents a Bayesian global optimization approach that removes the need for δ-cover sampling and avoids auxiliary optimization.

The manuscript is structured so that the primary contributions are clearly articulated in the main body.

Section 3.2 offers an illustrative example that effectively conveys a high-level intuition for the algorithm.

Section 3.3 provides a clear, programmatic description that supports implementation and reproducibility.

The experimental results are likewise compelling.

The authors also highlight how the work bridges strands of Bayesian optimization that do and do not rely on continuity estimation.

Overall, the paper introduces Infinite-Metric GP Optimization (IMGPO), a Bayesian optimization algorithm that forgoes auxiliary optimization and δ-cover sampling. The writing is clear, and the contribution is strong for the NIPS community.
