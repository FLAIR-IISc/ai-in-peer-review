This paper introduces Infinite-Metric GP Optimization (IMGPO), a novel algorithm for Bayesian global optimization that notably does not require d-cover sampling or an auxiliary optimization procedure. The manuscript is exceptionally well-written and organized, with the core contributions clearly articulated throughout the main sections.

The illustrative example in Section 3.2 is particularly effective, providing readers with a strong high-level intuition for the algorithm's mechanics. This is complemented by Section 3.3, which clearly details the algorithm's implementation, ensuring reproducibility. The experimental results presented are compelling and support the method's effectiveness.

A significant contribution of this work is its synthesis of ideas from different schools of thought within Bayesian global optimization, bridging approaches that do and do not use continuity estimation.

In summary, this is a clearly articulated paper that presents a strong and valuable contribution to the NIPS community.
