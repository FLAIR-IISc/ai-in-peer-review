- Novel algorithm for global optimization that avoids delta-cover sampling and achieves exponential regret, generalizing Freitas et al. (2012).  
- Paper is well-written, easy to follow, and clearly explains the algorithm’s role within bounded-based search literature.  
- Work deserves attention from the community.  
- Reviewer score: 5 (not higher) due to experimental weaknesses.  
- Experiments do not use multiple initial evaluations/replicates, preventing meaningful statistical comparisons.  
- No comparisons with state-of-the-art Bayesian optimization methods (e.g., information-theoretic approaches like Entropy Search).  
- All experiments are on synthetic functions (max 6D); no real-world parameter-tuning problems included.  
- Conclusion: strong theoretical results but experimental section below the standards expected for a NIPS paper.
