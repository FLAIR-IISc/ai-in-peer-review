- Proposes a Bayesian global optimization method that removes the need for d-cover sampling and any auxiliary optimization.  
- Introduces Infinite-Metric GP Optimization (IMGPO) as the new method achieving the above.  
- Manuscript is well-structured with core contributions clearly articulated.  
- Section 3.2 provides a clear, high-level illustrative example that aids intuition.  
- Section 3.3 presents the algorithm procedurally, facilitating implementation and replication.  
- Experimental results are compelling and support the method’s effectiveness.  
- Paper synthesizes ideas from Bayesian global optimization literature both with and without continuity estimation.  
- Overall clear writing and a strong contribution to the NIPS community.
