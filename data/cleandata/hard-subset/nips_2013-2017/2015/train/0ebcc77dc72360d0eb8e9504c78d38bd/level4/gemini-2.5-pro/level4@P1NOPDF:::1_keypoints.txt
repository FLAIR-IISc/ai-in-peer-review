- Introduces a new global optimization algorithm achieving exponential regret, generalizing de Freitas et al. (2012) by avoiding delta-cover sampling.  
- Paper is well-written, easy to follow, and clearly places the algorithm within bound-based search literature.  
- Reviewer considers the work a noteworthy contribution deserving community attention.  
- Recommends a score of 5 due to insufficient experimental rigor for a NIPS paper.  
- Experiments lack multiple random initializations for f, so no meaningful statistical comparisons are possible.  
- No comparisons to state-of-the-art Bayesian optimization methods (e.g., Entropy Search).  
- All experiments are on synthetic functions up to six dimensions; no real-world applications (e.g., hyperparameter tuning or wet-lab experiments).  
- Summary: theoretical results are interesting, but the experimental section is not sufficiently rigorous for NIPS.
