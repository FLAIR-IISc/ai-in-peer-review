This paper presents a novel approach to Bayesian global optimization that eliminates the need for d-cover sampling and auxiliary optimization. 

The manuscript is well-structured, with the primary contributions clearly outlined in the main section, facilitating easy comprehension.

A notable strength of the paper is the inclusion of an illustrative example in section 3.2, providing readers with a high-level understanding of the algorithm's functionality.

Furthermore, section 3.3 offers a clear and concise explanation of the algorithm's workings, enabling straightforward implementation and replication by future researchers.

The experimental results are also noteworthy, demonstrating the potential of the proposed method.

The authors successfully integrate concepts from the Bayesian global optimization literature, both with and without continuity estimation, thereby expanding the field's knowledge base.

The paper introduces Infinite-Metric GP Optimization (IMGPO), a Bayesian optimization algorithm that operates independently of auxiliary optimization and d-cover sampling. Overall, the manuscript is well-written and makes a valuable contribution to the NIPS community.
