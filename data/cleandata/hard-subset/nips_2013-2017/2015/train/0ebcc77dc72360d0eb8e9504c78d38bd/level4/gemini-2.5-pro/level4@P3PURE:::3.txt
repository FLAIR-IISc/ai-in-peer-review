This paper introduces Infinite-Metric GP Optimization (IMGPO), a novel algorithm for Bayesian global optimization that avoids the need for d-cover sampling or auxiliary optimization. The paper is well-organized and clearly written, with its main contributions well-articulated.

A key strength of the paper is its clarity. The authors provide an effective illustrative example in Section 3.2, which offers the reader a strong high-level understanding of the algorithm. Furthermore, Section 3.3 clearly outlines the algorithm's implementation, which should facilitate replication of the work.

The paper makes a strong technical contribution by synthesizing ideas from two distinct branches of the Bayesian global optimization literature: those that do and do not rely on continuity estimation. The experimental results are compelling and demonstrate the promise of the proposed method.

Overall, this paper presents a significant contribution to the field of Bayesian optimization. It is well-motivated, clearly presented, and supported by strong experimental results.
