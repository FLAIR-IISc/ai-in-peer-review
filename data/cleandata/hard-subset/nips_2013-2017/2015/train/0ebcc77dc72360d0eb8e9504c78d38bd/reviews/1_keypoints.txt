- Introduces a new global optimization algorithm that avoids delta-cover sampling and achieves exponential regret, generalizing Freitas et al. (2012).
- Paper is well written, easy to follow, and clearly positions the algorithm within bounded-based search literature.
- Reviewer votes 5 but lowers score due to weaknesses in the experimental section.
- Experiments do not use multiple initial evaluations of f (standard in BO), preventing meaningful statistical comparisons.
- No comparisons with state-of-the-art Bayesian optimization methods (e.g., information-theoretic approaches like Entropy Search).
- All experiments are on synthetic functions (maximum dimension 6); no real-world (wet-lab or parameter tuning) evaluations.
- Theoretical contributions are interesting, but the experimental evaluation is below the conference standard.
