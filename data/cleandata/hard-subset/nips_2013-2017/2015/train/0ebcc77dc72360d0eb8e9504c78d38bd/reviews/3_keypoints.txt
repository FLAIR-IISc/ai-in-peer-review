- Proposes a Bayesian global optimization method that avoids d-cover sampling and auxiliary optimization.
- Introduces Infinite-Metric GP Optimization (IMGPO) as the algorithmic instantiation.
- Main contributions are clearly and coherently presented in the main section.
- Section 3.2 provides a strong illustrative example that conveys high-level intuition.
- Section 3.3 presents programmatic details that facilitate implementation and replication.
- Experimental results are compelling and demonstrate the method’s effectiveness.
- Integrates ideas from Bayesian optimization literature both with and without continuity estimation.
- Well written and represents a strong contribution to the NIPS community.
