- Proposes IMGPO, a novel Bayesian optimization algorithm achieving exponential convergence without auxiliary optimization or δ-cover sampling.  
- Leverages GP prior/posterior to reduce uncertainty in the semi-metric, enabling the exponential rate.  
- Algorithm is clearly and well-organized, with explicit relation to prior methods (DIRECT, SOO).  
- Provides thorough theoretical analysis, including a proof of exponential convergence.  
- Discusses how the tightness of the GP UCB affects the regret bound.  
- Uses illustrative graphs to explain procedures and show algorithm behavior.  
- Experimental comparison to SOO, BaMSOO, GP-PI, and GP-EI demonstrates strong empirical performance.  
- Paper is well-written and didactic, accessible to non-experts.  
- Meets conference criteria for quality, clarity, originality, and significance.
