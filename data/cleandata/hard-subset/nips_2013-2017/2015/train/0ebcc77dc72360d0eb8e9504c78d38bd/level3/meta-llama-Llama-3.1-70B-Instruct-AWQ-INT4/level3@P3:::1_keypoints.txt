- IMGPO encodes an unknown bound via continuity at the global optimizer and simultaneously conducts global and local search over all candidate bounds.
- Paper is well-written and clearly positioned within the bound-based search literature.

- Practical analysis explains why avoiding reliance on a particular bound is advantageous.
