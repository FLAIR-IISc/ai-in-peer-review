The authors propose an Infinite-Metric GP Optimization (IMGPO) algorithm that leverages the existence of an unknown bound encoded in the continuity at a global optimizer. The algorithm simultaneously conducts global and local searches based on all possible candidates of the bounds, using the bound estimated by GP to reduce the number of candidates.
