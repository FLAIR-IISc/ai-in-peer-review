- Introduces Infinite-Metric GP Optimization (IMGPO), a novel Bayesian optimization method achieving exponential convergence without auxiliary optimization or δ-cover sampling.  
- Main contributions are clearly and coherently presented, making the paper easy to follow.  
- Frames the problem and limitations of existing Bayesian optimization methods (need for auxiliary optimization and δ-cover sampling).  
- IMGPO leverages an unknown bound encoded by continuity at the global optimizer.  
- Section 3.2 gives a strong illustrative example conveying high-level intuition of the algorithm.  
- Section 3.3 provides programmatic details that facilitate implementation and replication.  
- Paper clearly relates IMGPO to previous algorithms and highlights its advantages.  
- Experimental results (Section 5) are compelling; IMGPO outperforms SOO, BaMSOO, GP-PI, and GP-EI in most cases.  
- Effectively integrates ideas from Bayesian optimization literature with and without continuity estimation.  
- Paper is technically sound, well-written, and presents a thorough analysis.  
- Weakness: lacks detailed discussion of computational complexity, especially regarding number of function evaluations.  
- Weakness: provides limited insight into hyperparameter choices and their impact on performance.  
- Overall assessment: strong contribution to the community; recommended for acceptance.  
- Review rating: 9/10, Confidence: 8/10.
