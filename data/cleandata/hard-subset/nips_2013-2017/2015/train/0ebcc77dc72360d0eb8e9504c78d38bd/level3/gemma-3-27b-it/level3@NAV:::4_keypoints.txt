- Proposes IMGPO, a GP-based Bayesian optimization method achieving exponential convergence without auxiliary optimization or δ-cover sampling by using an unknown semi-metric bound and combined global/local search.  
- Paper is well-written and didactic; Figure 1 effectively illustrates the hierarchical partitioning procedure.  
- Theoretical results (including Theorem 1) convincingly demonstrate exponential convergence.  
- Experimental results on benchmark functions support the theoretical claims.  
- Empirical comparisons (SOO, BaMSOO, EI, PI) show advantages when a precise Lipschitz constant is unavailable.  
- Authors acknowledge scalability limitations and outline future research directions.  
- Abstract is somewhat redundant and should focus more on the core novelty (handling the unknown semi-metric).  
- Minor technical error: misplaced parenthesis in the function definition (line 72) needs correction.  
- Reviewer admits limited familiarity with GPs but finds the paper clear enough to judge technical soundness.  
- Strong literature citations indicate good understanding of related work.  
- Overall recommendation: accept, conditional on addressing the abstract and parenthesis issues.
