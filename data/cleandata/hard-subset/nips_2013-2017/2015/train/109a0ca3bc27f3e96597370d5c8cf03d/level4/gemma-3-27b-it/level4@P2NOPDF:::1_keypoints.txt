- Introduces a recurrent convolutional encoder-decoder for single-image novel-view rendering, demonstrated on faces and chairs.  
- Paper is well-written with a clear, detailed model description.  
- Evaluation is insufficient and relies largely on qualitative results.  
- Figures should be scaled up for improved readability.  
- Lacks quantitative evaluation and suitable error metrics to compare against state-of-the-art.  
- Significance limited by incomplete experimentation and analysis.  
- Needs evaluation on additional datasets beyond Multi-PIE and Chairs to show generalizability.  
- Should test more complex rotation trajectories (non-fixed axes, non-repeating patterns).  
- Novel core contribution but not convincingly validated against geometry-based approaches.  
- Minor grammatical correction: change “rotate” to “rotated” on line 124.
