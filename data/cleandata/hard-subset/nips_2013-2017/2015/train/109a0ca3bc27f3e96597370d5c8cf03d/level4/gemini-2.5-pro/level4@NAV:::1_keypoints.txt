- Proposes a recurrent convolutional encoder-decoder to synthesize novel object views from a single image.  
- Demonstrates model by generating rotated images of faces (Multi-PIE) and chairs.  
- Explores curriculum learning and class interpolation between chairs.  
- Assesses disentangled representations via cross-view object recognition.  
- Paper is clearly written and detailed.  
- Experimental evaluation is insufficient and relies heavily on qualitative results.  
- Figures should be enlarged for clearer visual inspection.  
- Lacks quantitative metrics and direct comparisons to state-of-the-art methods.  
- Limited datasets (only Multi-PIE and Chairs); needs broader, more diverse datasets.  
- Should test generalization to more complex rotation trajectories beyond a static axis.  
- Contributions are not fully substantiated, making comparison to geometry-based approaches difficult.  
- Minor typographical errors noted (e.g., "rotate" → "rotated").
