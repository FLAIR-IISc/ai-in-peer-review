**Summary**

The authors propose a novel recurrent convolutional encoder-decoder network for 3D view synthesis from a single image. The network is trained end-to-end on the task of rendering rotated objects starting from a single image, and is able to capture long-term dependencies along a sequence of transformations. The authors demonstrate the quality of the network's predictions for human faces on the Multi-PIE dataset and for a dataset of 3D chair models, and show its ability to disentangle latent data factors without using object class labels.
