- Proposes a self-normalized risk estimator to avoid propensity overfitting in batch learning from logged bandit feedback (BLBF).
- Introduces Norm-POEM, a new learning algorithm using the proposed estimator that improves generalization over POEM.
- Paper is well-written, with clear motivation and intuitive justification for the approach.
- Provides a thorough analysis of the propensity overfitting problem and its consequences.
- Comprehensive experimental evaluation demonstrating Norm-POEM’s effectiveness across settings.
- Strength: clearly identifies the propensity overfitting issue and addresses it with a principled estimator.
- Strength: develops a practical algorithm (Norm-POEM) and validates it empirically.
- Weakness: self-normalized estimator may incur higher computational cost than the unbiased estimator.
- Weakness: Norm-POEM may require careful hyperparameter tuning in practice.

- Con-accept: potential practical limitations due to compute cost and tuning requirements.
