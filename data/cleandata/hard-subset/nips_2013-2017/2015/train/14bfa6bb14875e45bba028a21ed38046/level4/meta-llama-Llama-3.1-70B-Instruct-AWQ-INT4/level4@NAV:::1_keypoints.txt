- Paper objective is clear and experiments provide a strong empirical foundation.
- Manuscript clarity is hindered by several unresolved issues.
- Translation invariance of the RPN is not clearly justified, especially relative to [19], and the role of Overfeat is ambiguous.
- Several hyperparameters (e.g., n=3, k=9) were manually set without sufficient justification or analysis.
- The multi-stage training procedure appears complex and potentially unstable; effects of repeated training and alternative settings were not explored.
- Authors propose a novel region proposal method that shares CNN features for object localization with classification and regression layers.
- Shared feature computation yields roughly 10× speedup over Fast R-CNN with slightly improved accuracy.
- To remove the assumption of given regions in localization training, the authors introduce an alternative multi-stage training procedure.
