- Proposes a region proposal network (RPN) that shares convolutional features with R-CNN and uses two fully connected layers to predict object bounds and objectness per position.  
- Heuristically combines RPN with Fast R-CNN to achieve state-of-the-art results on PASCAL VOC07 and VOC12.  
- Shared convolutional layers substantially reduce the runtime for generating region proposals.  
- Strength: the feature-sharing idea is ingenious and yields large runtime savings.  
- Strength: the method achieves state-of-the-art object detection performance.  
- Strength: the paper is well-written and clearly presented.  
- Limitation: RPN and Fast R-CNN are only weakly coupled via alternating optimization, with no convergence proof.  
- Limitation: RPN used independently as a proposal method does not clearly outperform selective search.  
- Overall: excellent improvement in efficiency and accuracy, but the alternating-optimization combination is somewhat heuristic.
