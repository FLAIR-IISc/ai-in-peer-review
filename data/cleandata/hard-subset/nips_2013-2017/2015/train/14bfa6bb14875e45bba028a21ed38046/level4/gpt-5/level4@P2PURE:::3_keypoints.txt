- Paper is a natural/ incremental extension of existing CNN-based object detection methods.  
- Concern: Alternating training between RPN and R-CNN appears to use only a single iteration, effectively assuming initial RPN proposals are optimal.  
- Concern: Unclear correspondence between pixel-wise sliding window step on top CNN layer and step size on the input image; pooling may cause large image-step and poor bounding-box localization.  
- Clarification requested: Definition of h_a referenced in lines 173–174 is missing.  
- Contribution summary: Proposes two-stage detector—RPN via sliding-window on top-layer feature map followed by Fast R-CNN on proposals; shared conv layers and GPU lead to near real-time (~5 fps) performance.
