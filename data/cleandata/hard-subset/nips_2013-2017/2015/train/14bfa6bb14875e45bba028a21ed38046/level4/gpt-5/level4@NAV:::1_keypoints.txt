- Objective is clear and experimental results are strong.
- Manuscript is hard to follow and contains several confusing aspects.
- Question why the RPN is translation-invariant while [19] is not, and how this relates to OverFeat.
- Many hyperparameters are chosen manually without justification (e.g., n=3, k=9).
- Training procedure appears complicated and potentially unstable; why not repeat the same training schedule and were other configurations evaluated?
- Novel region proposal method shares CNN features and includes classification (object vs. non-object) and regression (bounding boxes).
- The shared-feature RCNN variant is ~10× faster than Fast R-CNN and slightly more accurate, using a multi-stage alternating training due to region assumptions.
