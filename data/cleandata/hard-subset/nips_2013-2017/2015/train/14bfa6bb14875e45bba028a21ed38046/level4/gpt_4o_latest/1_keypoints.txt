- Objective is clear and experimental results are robust.
- Paper is somewhat difficult to follow with unclear aspects.
- Why is the RPN translation-invariant while [19] is not, and how does this compare to Overfeat?
- Many parameters are manually set without justification (e.g., n=3, k=9).
- Training procedure appears complex and unstable; authors did not report repeated runs or hyperparameter search to justify the chosen procedure.
- Introduces an innovative region proposal method that leverages shared CNN features used for object localization.
- Proposal module includes a classification layer for object/non-object and a regression layer for bounding-box prediction.
- Sharing feature computation yields ~10x speedup over Fast R-CNN with a slight accuracy improvement.
- Proposes a multi-stage training procedure to handle the object-localization CNN’s reliance on pre-defined regions.
