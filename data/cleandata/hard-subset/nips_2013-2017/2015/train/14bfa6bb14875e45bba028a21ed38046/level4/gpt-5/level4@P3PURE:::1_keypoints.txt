- Clear goal and solid experimental results demonstrating a promising method.
- Novel region proposal network (RPN) that shares CNN features with the object localization module.
- RPN includes a classification layer (object vs. non-object) and a regression layer (bounding-box prediction).
- Sharing features makes the combined system about 10× faster than Fast R-CNN and slightly more accurate.
- Because the localization CNN assumes proposals during training, the authors use a multi-stage, alternating training procedure.
- Paper is hard to follow and several explanations are confusing or insufficiently justified.
- Claim that the RPN is translation-invariant while [19] is not needs clarification, including how OverFeat compares.
- Several parameters are manually fixed (e.g., n=3, k=9) without justification or sensitivity analysis.
- Training procedure appears complex and potentially unstable; authors should justify not repeating the procedure or report exploration of alternative settings.
