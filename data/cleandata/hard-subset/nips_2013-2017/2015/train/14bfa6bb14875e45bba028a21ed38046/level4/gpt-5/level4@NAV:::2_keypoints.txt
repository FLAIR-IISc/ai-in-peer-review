- Introduces a region proposal network (RPN) that shares convolutional features with R-CNN and uses two fully connected layers to predict bounding boxes and objectness at each spatial location.  
- RPN is integrated with Fast R-CNN via an alternating-optimization scheme and attains state-of-the-art results on VOC07 and VOC12.  
- Reusing the detector’s convolutional layers makes proposal generation significantly faster.  
- Clever sharing of convolutional layers between RPN and R-CNN greatly accelerates proposal generation.  
- Achieves state-of-the-art object detection performance.  
- Paper is clear, well-structured, and supported by comprehensive experiments.  
- Integration with Fast R-CNN relies on alternating optimization without a convergence guarantee (heuristic).  
- As an independent proposal method, RPN does not clearly outperform Selective Search.  
- Overall: excellent improvement in efficiency and accuracy, with the primary concern being the heuristic RPN–Fast R-CNN integration.
