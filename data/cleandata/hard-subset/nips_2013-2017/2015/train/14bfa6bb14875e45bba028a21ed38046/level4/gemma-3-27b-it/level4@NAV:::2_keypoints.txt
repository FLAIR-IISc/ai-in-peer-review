- Proposes a region proposal network (RPN) that shares convolutional features with R-CNN and uses two fully connected layers to predict box bounds and objectness, integrated with Fast R-CNN.  
- Demonstrates state-of-the-art object detection results on VOC07 and VOC12.  
- Significantly reduces computation time for region proposal generation via feature sharing.  
- Strength: sharing conv layers between RPN and R-CNN greatly improves proposal-generation efficiency.  
- Strength: achieves top-tier detection performance.  
- Strength: paper is clearly written and well-organized.  
- Weakness: the alternating training procedure for RPN and Fast R-CNN is heuristic and lacks a formal convergence analysis.  
- Weakness: RPN, while effective, does not outperform Selective Search as an independent proposal method.  
- Overall: strong contribution that improves speed and accuracy of R-CNN–based detection; main concern is the heuristic joint-training strategy.
