- Proposes an RPN that shares convolutional features with R-CNN and uses two fully connected layers to predict bounding boxes and objectness at each position.  
- Integrates RPN with Fast R-CNN into a unified detection pipeline.  
- Achieves state-of-the-art results on PASCAL VOC07 and VOC12.  
- Sharing convolutional layers substantially speeds up region proposal generation.  
- Paper is well-written with comprehensive experimental evaluation.  
- Training relies on alternating optimization without a rigorous convergence proof.  
- As a standalone proposal method, RPN does not outperform selective search.  
- Overall: significant gains in R-CNN efficiency and accuracy, but the alternating-optimization heuristic needs further investigation.
