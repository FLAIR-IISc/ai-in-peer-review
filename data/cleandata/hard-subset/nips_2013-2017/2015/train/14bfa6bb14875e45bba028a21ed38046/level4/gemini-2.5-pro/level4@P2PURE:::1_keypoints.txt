- Proposes a novel RPN that shares convolutional features with the detection network, combining an objectness classifier and box regressor to make region proposals nearly cost-free.
- Claims the resulting detector is ~10× faster than Fast R-CNN with slightly higher accuracy; overall goal is clear and experiments appear strong.
- Presentation is sometimes hard to follow and several methodological details need clearer explanation.
- The claim that the RPN is translation-invariant requires further justification and comparison to methods like Selective Search and OverFeat.
- Key anchor hyperparameters (3 scales, 9 aspect ratios) are introduced without analysis; an ablation/sensitivity study is needed.
- The multi-stage training procedure is complex and its stability/robustness is not discussed; experiments with alternative schemes and multiple runs should be reported.
