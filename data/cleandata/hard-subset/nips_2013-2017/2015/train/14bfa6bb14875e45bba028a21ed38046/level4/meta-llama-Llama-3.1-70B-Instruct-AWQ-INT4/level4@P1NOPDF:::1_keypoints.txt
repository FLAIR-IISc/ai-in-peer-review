- Paper has a clear goal and presents solid experimental results.
- Manuscript is often challenging to follow and needs clearer exposition.
- Authors claim their RPN is translation-invariant while [19] is not; this distinction needs a detailed explanation and comparison to Overfeat.
- Several hyperparameters (e.g., n=3, k=9) are set manually without justification; their selection and impact should be analyzed.
- Training procedure appears complicated and unstable; authors should show robustness via repeated runs and exploration of alternative settings.
- Proposed RPN shares CNN features with object localization and uses separate classification and regression layers.
- Combined model yields ≈10× speedup over Fast R-CNN while maintaining slightly higher accuracy.
- To avoid assuming given regions in localization training, authors propose a multi-stage training procedure.
