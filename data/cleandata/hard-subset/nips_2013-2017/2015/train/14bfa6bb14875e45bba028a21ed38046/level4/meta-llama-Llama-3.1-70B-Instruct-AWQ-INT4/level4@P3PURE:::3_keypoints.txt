- Work is a natural extension of existing CNN-based object detection methods.
- Optimization concern: only one iteration of alternating optimization between RPN and R-CNN is reported, implying initial proposals are treated as optimal and raising convergence/stability questions.
- Implementation concern: step size of the pixel-wise sliding window on the top CNN layer is unspecified; due to pooling the top feature map is much lower resolution, so the implied image-level step may be large and cause inaccurate bounding boxes.
- Presentation issue: definition of h_a (lines 173–174) is missing, making the mathematical description unclear.
- Method summary: two-stage detector (RPN as sliding-window on top feature map + Fast R-CNN), shared convolutional layers and GPU acceleration, reported near real-time performance (~5 fps).
