- Proposes a region proposal network (RPN) that shares convolutional features with R-CNN and uses two fully connected layers to predict boundaries and objectness per position.  
- Integrates RPN with Fast R-CNN to produce state-of-the-art detection results on VOC07 and VOC12.  
- Sharing convolutional layers between proposal and detection networks significantly reduces runtime for region proposal generation.  
- Demonstrates improved efficiency and accuracy for R-CNN–based object detection.  
- Paper is well-written, clearly presented, and experimentally thorough.  
- Innovative design of shared convolutional layers is a key technical contribution.  
- Achieves top-tier empirical performance in object detection benchmarks.  
- The alternating optimization used to train RPN and Fast R-CNN lacks a formal convergence proof.  
- RPN as a standalone proposal method does not outperform selective search.  
- The combined training methodology appears heuristic, raising concerns about its theoretical grounding.
