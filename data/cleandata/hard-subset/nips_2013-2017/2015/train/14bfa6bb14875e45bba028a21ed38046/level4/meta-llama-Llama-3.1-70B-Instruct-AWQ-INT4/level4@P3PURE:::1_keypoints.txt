- Paper has a clear goal and solid experimental results.
- Claim that their RPN is translation-invariant while [19] is not is unclear and needs justification; compare with Overfeat.
- Several parameters are chosen manually (e.g., n=3, k=9) without explanation or analysis.
- Training procedure appears complex and potentially unstable; unclear if results are stable across repeated runs.
- Unclear whether alternative training/settings were tried and whether current settings are optimal.
- Proposes a novel region proposal method that shares CNN features with object localization.
- Method uses a classification layer for object/non-object and a regression layer for bounding-box prediction.
- Shared-feature RCNN achieves ~10× speedup over Fast R-CNN and slightly better accuracy.
- Introduces a multi-stage training procedure to handle the inconsistency that localization CNN training assumes given regions.
