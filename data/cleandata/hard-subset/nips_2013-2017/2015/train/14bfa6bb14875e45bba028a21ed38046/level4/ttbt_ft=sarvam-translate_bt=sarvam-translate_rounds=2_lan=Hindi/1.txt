The paper's goal is clear, and the experimental results are solid. However, the paper is not easy to understand due to some misleading sections mentioned below. 1. Why is the RPN translation-invariant, but not [19]? What about overfitting? 2. Several parameters are set manually without further analysis. Some names: n=3, k=9? 3. The training process does not seem complex and stable. Why is the same training process not applied multiple times? Did the authors try many other settings, but did they find that the current setting performs best? The authors proposed a new region proposal method that shares the CNN feature used for object localization. The region proposal method involves a classification layer to identify object vs. non-object and a regression layer to predict the object bounding box. Due to sharing the feature computation with the object localization module, the combined new RCNN is ~10 times faster and slightly more accurate than the Fast RCNN. Since the object localization CNN's training process assumes that regions are given, a multi-step alternative training process is proposed.
