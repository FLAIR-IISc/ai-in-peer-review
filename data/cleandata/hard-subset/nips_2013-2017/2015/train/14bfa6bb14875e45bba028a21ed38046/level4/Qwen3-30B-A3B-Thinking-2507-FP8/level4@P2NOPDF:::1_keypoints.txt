- Novel contribution: shares CNN features between region proposal and localization modules, yielding ~10x speedup over Fast R-CNN with slightly better accuracy and well-supported experiments.  
- Overall clarity problem: significant exposition issues hinder understanding of key technical contributions and implementation details.  
- Translation-invariance concern: paper must explicitly justify why the RPN is translation-invariant, how this differs from OverFeat ([19]), and why that distinction matters.  
- Hyperparameter justification: choices like n=3 (anchor scales) and k=9 (anchor aspect ratios) are arbitrary; require sensitivity analysis or empirical justification for reproducibility.  
- Training stability/reproducibility: multi-stage training is complex but lacks analysis of sensitivity to initialization/hyperparameters, multiple trials, ablations, or comparisons to alternative schedules.  
- Recommendation: clarify technical distinctions and justify implementation/training choices to enable reproducibility before publication.
