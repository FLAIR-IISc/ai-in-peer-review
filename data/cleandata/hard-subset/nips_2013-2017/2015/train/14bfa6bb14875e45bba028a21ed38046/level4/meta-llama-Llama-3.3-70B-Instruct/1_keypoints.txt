- Objective is clear and experimental findings are robust.
- Presentation is convoluted with several ambiguous sections needing clarification.
- RPN is claimed translation-invariant, but [19] is not; the translation-invariance of Overfeat is unclear.
- Many parameters are manually set without justification (e.g., n=3, k=9).
- Training protocol appears complex and potentially unstable; no repeated runs reported to verify consistency.
- Unclear whether many hyperparameter/settings were explored before selecting the reported configuration.
- Introduces a novel region-proposal approach that leverages shared CNN features for object localization.
- Method includes a classification layer for object vs. non-object and a regression layer for bounding-box prediction.
- Shared feature computation yields roughly a 10× speedup over Fast R-CNN with slightly improved accuracy.
- Proposes a multi-stage training protocol to address reliance on pre-defined regions in localization CNN training.
