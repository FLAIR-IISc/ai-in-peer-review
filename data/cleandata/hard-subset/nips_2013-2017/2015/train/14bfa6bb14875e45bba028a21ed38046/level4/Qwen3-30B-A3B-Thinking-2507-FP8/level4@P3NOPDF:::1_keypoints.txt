- Novel RPN that shares CNN features with detection yields ~10x speedup over Fast R-CNN while maintaining accuracy.  
- Core contribution is well-motivated and experiments show robust efficiency gains without major accuracy loss.  
- Proposed staged training procedure is a practical solution to the dependency between proposals and localization.  
- Paper suffers from poor flow and insufficient technical justification in critical sections, harming reproducibility.  
- Translation-invariance claim is unclear: must distinguish feature-map translation invariance from proposal-mechanism invariance and clarify comparison to OverFeat/[19].  
- Key hyperparameters (n=3 scales, k=9 aspect ratios) are set without justification or ablation to show sensitivity/optimality.  
- Staged training procedure appears ad-hoc and potentially unstable; lacks evidence on stability, variance across runs, and comparisons to end-to-end or alternative strategies.  
- Authors’ statement of trying many settings is unsupported by experimental evidence or ablation studies.  
- Recommendations: explicitly clarify translation-invariance, provide anchor (n,k) ablation, and report training stability or direct comparisons to other training schemes.  
- Overall: strong practical contribution but needs these clarifications to meet technical rigor and reproducibility standards.
