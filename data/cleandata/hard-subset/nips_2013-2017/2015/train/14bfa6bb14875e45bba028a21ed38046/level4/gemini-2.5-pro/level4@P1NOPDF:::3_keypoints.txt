- Paper proposes a two-stage CNN detector: an RPN operating on final conv feature maps to generate proposals, followed by a Fast R-CNN detector sharing conv layers, achieving near real-time (~5 fps).

- Concern 1: Paper seems to describe only a single iteration of alternating optimization between RPN and Fast R-CNN, implying a strong assumption that initial RPN proposals are sufficient; authors should explain why only one iteration and whether further joint-training iterations were tried.

- Concern 2: Sliding window on the final conv feature map has a large effective stride in input image space due to pooling, potentially harming bounding-box localization; clarification requested on how precise localization is achieved despite coarse feature resolution.

- Concern 3: The term h_a (lines 173–174) is undefined or unclear; authors should provide a clear definition.
