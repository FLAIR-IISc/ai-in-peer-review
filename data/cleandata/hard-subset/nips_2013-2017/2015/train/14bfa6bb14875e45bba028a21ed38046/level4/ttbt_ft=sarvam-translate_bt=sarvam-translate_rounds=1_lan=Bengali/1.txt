The goals of this paper are clear, and the experimental results are reliable. However, it is not easy to follow due to several misleading sections. 1. Why is RPN translation-invariant, but not [19]? What about overfitting? 2. Many parameters are manually determined without any analysis. For example: why n=3, k=9. 3. The training method seems complex and not stable. Why is the same training method not being applied multiple times? Did the authors try many other settings, but see that the current settings are working best? The authors proposed a new region-based approach that shares the CNN feature used for object localization. The region-based approach has a classification layer for object vs. non-object detection and a regression layer for predicting the object bounding box. Because the feature computation is shared with the object localization module, the combined new RCNN is about 10 times faster and slightly more accurate than the RCNN. Since the object localization CNN's training method assumes regions are provided, a multi-stage alternative training method is proposed.
