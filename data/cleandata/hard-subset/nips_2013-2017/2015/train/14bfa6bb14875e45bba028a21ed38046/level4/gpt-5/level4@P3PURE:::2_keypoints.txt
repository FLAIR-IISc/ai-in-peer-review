- Introduces a region proposal network (RPN) that shares convolutional features with R-CNN and uses two fully connected layers to predict object bounds and objectness at each spatial position.
- Heuristically combines the RPN with Fast R-CNN for detection.
- Achieves state-of-the-art detection results on VOC07 and VOC12.
- Significantly reduces runtime for region proposal generation by sharing convolutional layers with the detection network.
- Smart idea of feature sharing that greatly improves efficiency.
- Paper is well written with thorough experimental evaluation.
- Training of RPN and Fast R-CNN is only weakly coupled via alternating optimization and lacks a convergence proof.
- As a standalone proposal method, RPN does not appear to outperform Selective Search.
- Main concern: the alternating/heuristic combination strategy for RPN and Fast R-CNN is heuristic and lacks theoretical guarantees.
