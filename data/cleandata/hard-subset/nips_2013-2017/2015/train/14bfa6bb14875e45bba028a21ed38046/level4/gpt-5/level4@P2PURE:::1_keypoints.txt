- Clear objective and strong experimental results.
- Proposes a novel region proposal network that shares CNN features with the object localization module.
- Proposal network contains a classification layer (object vs. non-object) and a regression layer for bounding-box prediction.
- Sharing feature computation yields about 10× speedup over Fast R-CNN with slightly better accuracy.
- Uses a multi-stage alternating training procedure because localization training assumes region proposals are given.
- Paper’s claim that the RPN is translation-invariant (unlike [19]) is unclear and needs justification and comparison to OverFeat.
- Several hyperparameters are chosen manually (e.g., n=3, k=9) without analysis or justification.
- Training procedure appears complicated and potentially unstable; unclear why the schedule isn’t repeated or whether alternative settings were extensively explored.
