- Proposes a novel region proposal method that shares CNN features with object localization.
- Method comprises a classification layer for object/non-object and a regression layer for bounding-box prediction.
- Sharing feature computation yields an RCNN about 10× faster than Fast R-CNN and slightly more accurate.
- Introduces a multi-stage training procedure to address the localization CNN’s assumption of given regions.
- Paper has potential but must address the above concerns to improve clarity and impact.
