- Clear goal and solid experimental results demonstrating a promising approach.
- Core contribution: novel region proposal method that shares CNN features with localization, avoiding redundant computation.
- Architecture includes classification and regression layers and reuses localization CNN features.
- Reported benefit: ~10× speedup over Fast R-CNN with marginal accuracy loss.
- Exposition ambiguity: unclear claim that the RPN is translation-invariant while [19]/OverFeat is not—needs clarification of the distinction.
- Key hyperparameters (e.g., n=3, k=9) are chosen without justification or sensitivity analysis.
- Training procedure is complex, described as multi-stage, but the necessity and advantages over simpler iterative training are not explained.
- Lack of ablation studies on the training protocol weakens robustness claims.
- No evidence of training stability or result consistency across multiple runs; unclear if reported setup was cherry-picked.
- Implementation details are underspecified, harming reproducibility despite a compelling speed–accuracy trade-off.
