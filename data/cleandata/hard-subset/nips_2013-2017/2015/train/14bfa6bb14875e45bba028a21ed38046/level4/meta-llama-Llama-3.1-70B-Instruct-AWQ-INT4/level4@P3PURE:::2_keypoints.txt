Proposes a region proposal network (RPN) that shares convolutional features with Fast R-CNN and uses two fully connected layers to predict bounding boxes and objectness per position.

Combines RPN with Fast R-CNN heuristically to achieve state-of-the-art results on VOC07 and VOC12.

Significantly reduces region proposal runtime by sharing convolutional layers between proposal and detection networks.

Strength: sharing convolutional layers is an ingenious idea that yields substantial runtime savings.

Strength: method achieves state-of-the-art object detection performance.

Strength: paper is well-written and clearly presents the approach.

Limitation: RPN and Fast R-CNN are trained via alternating optimization, a weakly coupled, heuristic scheme with no convergence proof.

Limitation: RPN used standalone for proposals does not clearly outperform selective search.

Overall: excellent work improving efficiency and accuracy, but the heuristic alternating optimization for combining RPN and Fast R-CNN is the primary concern.
