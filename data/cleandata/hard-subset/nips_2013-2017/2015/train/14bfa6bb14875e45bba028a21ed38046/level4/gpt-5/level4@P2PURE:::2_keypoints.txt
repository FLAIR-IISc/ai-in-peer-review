- Proposes a Region Proposal Network (RPN) that shares convolutional features with R-CNN and adds two fully connected layers to predict bounding boxes and objectness at each spatial location.  
- RPN is heuristically combined with Fast R-CNN for detection and achieves state-of-the-art results on VOC07 and VOC12.  
- Sharing convolutional layers between RPN and detector substantially reduces runtime for generating region proposals.  
- Clever idea and substantial runtime reduction for proposal generation.  
- Demonstrates state-of-the-art object detection performance.  
- Paper is well written with thorough experimental evaluation.  
- Training is only weakly coupled via alternating optimization and lacks a convergence proof.  
- RPN used independently for proposals does not appear to outperform Selective Search.  
- Overall a strong contribution improving efficiency and accuracy, with the primary concern being the heuristic alternating optimization strategy for combining RPN and Fast R-CNN.
