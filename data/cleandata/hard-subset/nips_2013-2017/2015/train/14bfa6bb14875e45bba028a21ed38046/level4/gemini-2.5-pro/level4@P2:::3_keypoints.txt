- Introduces Region Proposal Network (RPN) that reuses convolutional feature maps to generate region proposals, enabling nearly zero extra cost and forming the Faster R-CNN system.  
- RPN is a fully-convolutional sliding-window that predicts objectness and bbox refinements for multiple anchors; training uses a 4-step alternating optimization to share conv layers with Fast R-CNN.  
- Empirically achieves ~5fps with VGG-16, improves mAP on PASCAL VOC over external-proposal methods, and adds only ~10ms overhead for proposals.  
- Major strength: novel and significant unification of proposal generation and detection that addresses a key computational bottleneck.  
- Major strength: excellent speed–accuracy tradeoff, making high-quality detection practical for near real-time applications.  
- Major strength: thorough experimental validation and ablation studies demonstrating benefits of layer sharing, classification+regression outputs, and two-stage design.  
- Major strength: clear, well-organized writing with implementation details that aid reproducibility.  
- Weakness: the 4-step alternating training is complex and feels engineered; unclear if a single cycle is sufficient and whether further iterations or joint training would help.  
- Weakness: operating on a coarse final feature map stride (e.g., 16px) may limit localization accuracy for small objects; more analysis of small-object performance is needed.  
- Minor point: explicitly define variables h and h_a used in bbox regression parameterization.  
- Minor point: provide more intuition for differing normalizations of N_cls and N_reg used to balance loss terms.  
- Recommendation: strongly positive — paper is a significant, well-supported advance deserving acceptance.  
- Arguments for acceptance: unifies proposal and detection, removes major computational bottleneck, achieves state-of-the-art results, and is well-validated and clearly presented.  
- Arguments for rejection: training is not fully end-to-end and the justification for stopping after a single alternation cycle is weak.
