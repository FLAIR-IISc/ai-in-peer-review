This paper presents a Region Proposal Network (RPN) that shares the conv feature with R-CNN and includes two fully connected layers that estimate object bound and objectness scores at each location. The RPN is combined with Fast R-CNN for heuristic-based object detection and achieves state-of-the-art performance on VOC07 and VOC12. The running time for generating region proposals has been significantly reduced because the RPN shares the conv layer with the detection network.

Advantages:

- The smart idea of sharing the conv layer between RPN and R-CNN has greatly reduced the run-time of object proposals.
- State-of-the-art performance on object detection.
- Well-written paper.

Disadvantages:

- The training of RPN and Fast R-CNN is weakly combined by optional optimization and there is no convergence evidence.
- RPN can be used independently as a region proposal method. However, it does not perform better than selective search.
- It is a very good piece of work, which improves both the efficiency and accuracy of R-CNN in object detection. Well-written paper and complete experimental evaluation. The only concern is how to combine RPN and Fast R-CNN for object detection. The proposed optional optimization method is a bit heuristic.
