- Introduces a region proposal network (RPN) that shares convolutional features with R-CNN and uses two fully connected layers to predict object bounds and objectness per position.  
- Integrates the RPN with Fast R-CNN for detection, achieving state-of-the-art results on VOC07 and VOC12.  
- Shared convolutional layers substantially reduce runtime for region proposal generation.  
- Clever design choice of feature sharing significantly lowers computational cost.  
- Method achieves top-tier object detection performance.  
- Paper is well-written and clearly presented.  
- Training via alternating optimization between RPN and Fast R-CNN lacks a formal convergence guarantee.  
- As a standalone region proposal method, the RPN does not clearly outperform selective search.  
- Overall a strong contribution improving both efficiency and accuracy, though the integration/training strategy feels somewhat heuristic.
