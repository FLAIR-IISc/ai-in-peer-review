- Clear goal and solid experimental results.
- Claim that their RPN is translation-invariant vs. [19] (and Overfeat) needs a detailed explanation and direct comparison highlighting the methodological differences that provide translation robustness.
- Several parameters (e.g., n=3, k=9) are set manually and appear arbitrary; require deeper analysis of the parameter space and their impact on performance.
- Training procedure is complicated and unstable; unclear if the proposed multi-stage alternative is optimal—provide more training details, ablations, and evidence of hyperparameter exploration.
- Novel region proposal method shares CNN features with object localization using a classification layer (object vs. non-object) and a regression layer (bounding boxes).
- Shared feature computation yields roughly a 10× speedup over Fast R-CNN while maintaining a slight accuracy improvement.
- Paper is promising but needs the above clarifications and analyses to strengthen clarity, contributions, and impact.
