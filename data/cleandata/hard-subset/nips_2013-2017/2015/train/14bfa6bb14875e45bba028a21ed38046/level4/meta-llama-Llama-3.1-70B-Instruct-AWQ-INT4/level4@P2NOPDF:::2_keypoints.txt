- Proposes a Region Proposal Network (RPN) that shares convolutional features with R-CNN and adds two FC layers to predict object bounds and objectness per position.  
- Integrates RPN with Fast R-CNN to form a unified detection pipeline, achieving state-of-the-art results on VOC07 and VOC12.  
- Sharing convolutional layers substantially reduces runtime for generating region proposals.  
- The idea of feature sharing between proposal and detection networks is novel and effective.  
- The method yields strong empirical detection performance and thorough experimental validation.  
- The paper is clearly written and easy to follow.  
- Major weakness: the alternating optimization used to combine RPN and Fast R-CNN is heuristic and lacks convergence guarantees.  
- RPN as an independent proposal method does not consistently outperform selective search.  
- Overall: a commendable contribution that improves efficiency and accuracy, but training integration needs a more rigorous approach.
