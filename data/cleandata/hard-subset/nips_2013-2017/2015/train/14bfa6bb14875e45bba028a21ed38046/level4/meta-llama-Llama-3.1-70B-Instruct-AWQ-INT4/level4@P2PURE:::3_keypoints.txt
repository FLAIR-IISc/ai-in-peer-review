- Optimization appears to use only a single alternating iteration between RPN and R-CNN, which may assume suboptimal initial proposals and hurt performance.
- The step size of the pixel-wise sliding window on the top CNN layer is unclear; heavy downsampling can map to large image-level steps and produce inaccurate bounding boxes.
