The goal of this paper is clear, and the experimental results are reliable. However, it is not easy to follow due to several misleading sections. 1. Why is RPN translation-invariant, but [19] not? What about overfitting? 2. Many parameters are determined manually without any analysis. For example: why n=3, k=9. 3. The training method seems complex and not stable. Why isn't the same training method applied multiple times? Did the authors try many more settings, but found that the current settings work best? The authors proposed a new region-based method that shares the CNN feature used for object localization. The region-based method has a classification layer for object vs. non-object identification and a regression layer for predicting the object bounding box. Since the feature computation is shared with the object localization module, the combined new RCNN is about 10 times faster and slightly more accurate than RCNN. Since the object localization assumes the CNN training method to provide regions, a multi-stage alternative training method has been proposed.
