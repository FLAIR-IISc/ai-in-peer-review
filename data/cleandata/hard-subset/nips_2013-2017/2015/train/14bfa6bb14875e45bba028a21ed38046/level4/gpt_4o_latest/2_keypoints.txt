- Proposes a region proposal network (RPN) that shares convolutional features with R-CNN and uses two fully connected layers to predict object boundaries and objectness per spatial position.  
- Integrates the RPN with Fast R-CNN in a heuristic/alternating manner for object detection.  
- Achieves state-of-the-art detection results on VOC07 and VOC12.  
- Sharing convolutional layers with the detector significantly reduces runtime for generating region proposals.  
- The paper is well-written and easy to follow.  
- Training relies on alternating optimization that is weakly coupled and lacks a formal convergence guarantee.  
- Although RPN can be used standalone for proposals, it does not clearly outperform selective search.  
- Experimental evaluations are comprehensive; overall the work substantially improves R-CNN efficiency and accuracy, with the main concern being the heuristic alternating optimization.
