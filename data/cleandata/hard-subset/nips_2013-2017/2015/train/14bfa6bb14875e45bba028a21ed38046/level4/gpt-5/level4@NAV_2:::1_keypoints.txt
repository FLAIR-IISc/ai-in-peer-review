- Objective is well-defined and empirical results are strong.
- Manuscript is somewhat difficult to follow with several confusing aspects.
- Why is the RPN translation-invariant while [19] is not, and how does this compare to OverFeat?
- Many hyperparameters are chosen manually without justification (e.g., n=3, k=9).
- Training pipeline appears complex and potentially unstable; why not repeat the alternating training schedule multiple times?
- Did the authors explore other training configurations and confirm the current one is best?
- Introduces a new region proposal mechanism that shares CNN features with the object localization network.
- Proposal module uses a classification head (object vs. background) and a regression head (bounding box prediction).
- Integrated RCNN system is ~10× faster than Fast R-CNN and marginally more accurate.
- Because detector training assumes fixed regions, a multi-stage alternating training procedure is proposed.
