- Proposes a Region Proposal Network (RPN) that shares convolutional features with R-CNN and uses two fully connected layers to predict bounding boxes and objectness at each spatial position.  
- Integrates RPN with Fast R-CNN to form a detection pipeline that achieves state-of-the-art results on VOC07 and VOC12.  
- Sharing convolutional layers between RPN and detection network drastically reduces region proposal runtime.  
- Innovative idea of feature sharing leads to large runtime savings for proposal generation.  
- Empirically achieves top-tier object detection performance.  
- Paper is well-written with clear methodology and thorough experimental evaluation.  
- Training of RPN and Fast R-CNN is only weakly coupled via alternating optimization, with no convergence proof.  
- RPN used independently as a proposal method does not clearly outperform selective search.  
- Primary concern: the heuristic/alternating combination of RPN and Fast R-CNN; a more rigorous integration would strengthen the work.  
- Overall: high-quality, impactful contribution that improves both efficiency and accuracy in object detection.
