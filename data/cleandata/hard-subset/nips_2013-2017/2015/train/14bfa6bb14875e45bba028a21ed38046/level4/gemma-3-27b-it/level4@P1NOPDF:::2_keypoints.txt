- Introduces an RPN that shares convolutional features with R-CNN and uses two fully connected layers to predict object bounds and objectness per position.  
- Heuristically integrates the RPN with Fast R-CNN and achieves state-of-the-art results on VOC07 and VOC12.  
- Major benefit is dramatically reduced runtime for region proposals due to shared convolutional layers.  
- Pro: Sharing convolutional layers is a clever idea that substantially reduces proposal generation time.  
- Pro: The approach attains state-of-the-art object detection performance.  
- Pro: The paper is well-written and presented clearly.  
- Con: Training RPN and Fast R-CNN uses alternating optimization, is loosely coupled, and lacks formal convergence guarantees.  
- Con: As a standalone proposal method, the RPN does not clearly outperform Selective Search.  
- Overall: Strong contribution improving both efficiency and accuracy, with the main concern being the heuristic method for combining RPN and Fast R-CNN.
