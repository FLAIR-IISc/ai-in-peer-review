- Introduces a Region Proposal Network (RPN) that shares convolutional features with the detector and uses two fully connected layers to predict boxes and objectness at each spatial position.  
- Integrates RPN with Fast R-CNN into a single pipeline, achieving state-of-the-art results on PASCAL VOC 2007 and 2012.  
- Reusing backbone features greatly reduces the computational cost of generating proposals.  
- Clever feature sharing substantially reduces runtime for proposal generation.  
- Demonstrates strong, state-of-the-art object detection performance.  
- Clear writing and a thorough experimental evaluation.  
- Integration relies on alternating optimization without convergence guarantees, making the training procedure feel ad hoc.  
- As an independent proposal generator, the RPN does not outperform Selective Search; a more principled joint optimization or convergence analysis would strengthen the work.
