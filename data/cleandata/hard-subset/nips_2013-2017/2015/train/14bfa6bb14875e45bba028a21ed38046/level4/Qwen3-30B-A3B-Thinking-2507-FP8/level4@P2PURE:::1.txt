

The paper's goal is clear and its experimental results are solid. However, the paper is difficult to follow due to several confusing aspects, as detailed below.  
1. Why is RPN translation-invariant while [19] is not? How does this relate to Overfeat?  
2. Many parameters are manually set without analysis. For instance, why n=3 and k=9?  
3. The training procedure appears complex and unstable. Why not repeat the training procedure multiple times? Did the authors explore alternative settings but find the current configuration optimal?  

The authors propose a novel region proposal method that shares the CNN feature used for object localization. This method comprises a classification layer for distinguishing objects from non-objects and a regression layer for predicting bounding boxes. By sharing feature computation with the object localization module, the combined R-CNN achieves approximately 10× speedup over Fast R-CNN while maintaining slightly higher accuracy. Due to the object localization CNN's training procedure assuming predefined regions, a multiple-stage alternative training procedure is proposed.
