- Proposed method: two-stage detector with RPNs on top-layer CNN feature map feeding Fast R-CNN; shared backbone + GPU give near real-time ~5 fps.  
- Alternating optimization unclear: paper reports only a single RPN–R-CNN update, needs clarification or ablation showing whether iterative training is required and its impact on accuracy.  
- Sliding-window spatial scaling unspecified: step/stride on the downsampled feature map (e.g., 1:16) is not defined; must state stride relative to input and justify localization robustness.  
- Undefined notation h_a: h_a is used but never defined—authors must specify its meaning, dimensionality, and whether it’s a fixed anchor height or learned.  
- Overall: idea builds on prior work but technical ambiguities undermine reproducibility and generalizability; these issues must be resolved before acceptance.
