- Paper's goal is clear and experimental results are solid.  
- Paper is hard to follow because of several confusing aspects.  
- Question: Why is RPN translation-invariant while [19] is not, and how does this relate to Overfeat?  
- Question: Many hyperparameters are manually set without analysis (e.g., why n=3 and k=9?).  
- Question: Training procedure seems complex and unstable — why not repeat experiments and were alternative settings explored?  
- Contribution: Novel region proposal method that shares CNN features with object localization (classification + bounding-box regression).  
- Result: Shared computation yields ~10× speedup over Fast R-CNN with slightly higher accuracy; a multi-stage training procedure is proposed due to region-assumption in localization CNN.
