- Proposes a Region Proposal Network (RPN) that shares convolutional features with R-CNN and uses two fully connected layers to predict bounding boxes and objectness at each spatial location.  
- Integrates the RPN with Fast R-CNN via an alternating (heuristic) optimization scheme to combine proposal generation and detection.  
- Reports state-of-the-art object detection results on VOC 2007 and VOC 2012.  
- Sharing convolutional layers between RPN and detection network significantly reduces runtime for generating region proposals.  
- Paper is well-written and includes thorough experimental evaluations.  
- Training RPN and Fast R-CNN is weakly coupled through alternating optimization, with no convergence proof provided.  
- As a standalone proposal method, RPN does not clearly outperform selective search.  
- Overall an excellent contribution improving both efficiency and accuracy; primary concern is the heuristic integration method.
