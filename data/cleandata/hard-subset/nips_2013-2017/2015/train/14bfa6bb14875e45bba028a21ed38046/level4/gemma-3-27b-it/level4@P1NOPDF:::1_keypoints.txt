- Clear goal and strong experimental results reported.
- Overall paper flow is poor and several sections are confusing.
- Claim that the RPN is translation-invariant while [19] is not is not well explained; question raised about OverFeat’s behavior.
- Key hyperparameters (e.g., n=3, k=9) chosen without justification or sensitivity analysis.
- Training procedure is complex and may be unstable; robustness not assessed via repeated runs or broader exploration of training settings.
- Proposed method: novel region proposal that shares CNN features with localization, using classification and regression layers.
- Empirical outcome: shared-feature R-CNN is ~10x faster than Fast R-CNN with a slight accuracy improvement.
- Training solution: multi-stage alternating training to handle dependence on pre-defined region proposals.
