- Proposes a region proposal network (RPN) that shares convolutional features with Fast R-CNN and uses two fully connected layers to predict object bounds and objectness per position.  
- Combines RPN and Fast R-CNN via a heuristic alternating optimization scheme.  
- Achieves state-of-the-art object detection results on VOC07 and VOC12.  
- Substantially reduces runtime for region proposal generation by sharing convolutional layers with the detection network.  
- Sharing convolutional layers is a clever idea that significantly cuts computational cost.  
- Paper is well-written, clearly presented, and includes thorough experimental evaluation.  
- RPN and Fast R-CNN training are weakly coupled through alternating optimization, with no convergence guarantees.  
- As a standalone region proposal method, RPN does not clearly outperform Selective Search.  
- Overall a strong contribution improving both efficiency and accuracy, with the primary concern being the heuristic combination/training method.
