- Objective is clear and experimental results are robust.  
- Manuscript is hard to follow with confusing sections that need clarification.  
- Authors claim their RPN is translation-invariant while [19] is not; require a detailed explanation and comparison to Overfeat.  
- Several hyperparameters (e.g., n=3, k=9) are chosen manually without justification; needs analysis or acknowledgement of further study.  
- Training procedure appears complicated and possibly unstable; recommend repeated runs to assess consistency and clarification on hyperparameter search.  
- Proposed region proposal method shares CNN features and uses classification + regression layers, yielding ~10× speedup over Fast R-CNN with slight accuracy gains.  
- To remove the assumption of given regions during training, authors introduce a multi-stage training procedure.
