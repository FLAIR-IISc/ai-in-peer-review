- Proposes a Region Proposal Network (RPN) that shares convolutional features with R-CNN and uses two fully connected layers to predict bounding boxes and objectness at each spatial position.  
- Integrates the RPN with Fast R-CNN using heuristic alternating optimization.  
- Achieves state-of-the-art detection results on PASCAL VOC 2007 and 2012.  
- Significantly reduces runtime for region proposal generation by sharing convolutional layers with the detection network.  
- Shared feature design substantially improves efficiency of proposal generation.  
- Paper is clearly written and provides thorough experimental validation.  
- Training coupling between RPN and Fast R-CNN is weak (alternating optimization) and lacks a convergence proof.  
- RPN as a standalone region proposal method does not substantially outperform selective search.  
- Overall a strong contribution improving both efficiency and accuracy, with the main concern being the heuristic RPN–Fast R-CNN integration that needs theoretical justification.
