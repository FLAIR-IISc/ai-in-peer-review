- Paper is a logical progression of CNN-based object detection but raises several concerns needing address.
- Optimization seems to use only a single alternating iteration between RPN and R-CNN, casting doubt on proposal optimality.
- Need clarification of the pixel-wise sliding-window step size on the top-layer feature map because pooling downscales features and may cause large image-level steps and inaccurate bounding boxes.
- The variable h_a (lines 173–174) is not clearly defined and needs clarification.
- Proposed method: two-stage detector—RPN applied to top-layer features plus Fast R-CNN, sharing convolutional layers and achieving ~5 FPS with GPU.
