- Proposes a Region Proposal Network (RPN) that shares convolutional features with R-CNN and uses two fully connected layers to predict box bounds and objectness at each position.
- Heuristically combines RPN with Fast R-CNN via alternating optimization for object detection.
- Achieves state-of-the-art detection performance on VOC07 and VOC12.
- Greatly reduces region proposal runtime by sharing conv layers with the detection network.
- Paper is well-written with thorough experimental evaluations.
- Alternating optimization for joint training is heuristic and lacks a convergence proof.
- RPN can be used standalone for proposals but does not clearly outperform Selective Search.
- Overall a strong contribution improving efficiency and accuracy; main concern is the heuristic combination of RPN and Fast R-CNN.
