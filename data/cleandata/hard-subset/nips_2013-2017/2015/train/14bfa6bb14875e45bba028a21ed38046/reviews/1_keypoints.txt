- Goal is clear and experimental results are solid.
- Paper is not easy to follow and contains confusing parts.
- Unclear why RPN is translation-invariant while [19] is not; question about Overfeat’s behavior.
- Many hyperparameters are set manually without justification (e.g., n=3, k=9).
- Training procedure seems complicated and unstable.
- Authors do not report repeated runs or exploration of alternative training settings to justify the chosen procedure.
- Proposes a novel region proposal method that shares CNN features with object localization (classification + regression).
- Combined RCNN is about 10× faster than Fast R-CNN and slightly more accurate.
- Proposes a multi-stage training alternative because localization CNN assumes regions are given.
