- Introduces Region Proposal Network (RPN) to remove region proposal bottleneck by sharing full-image convolutional features with Fast R-CNN.  
- Sharing features yields near cost-free region proposals and enables real-time detection.  
- Demonstrates state-of-the-art accuracy on PASCAL VOC 2007 and 2012 while achieving substantial speedups (≈5 fps with VGG-16).  
- Effectively addresses the computational cost limitation of prior object detection pipelines.  
- Elegant integration with Fast R-CNN that leverages shared computation to drastically reduce runtime.  
- Experimental results show improvements in both accuracy and speed over Selective Search and EdgeBoxes.  
- Ablation studies provide useful insights into RPN component contributions.  
- Code release enhances reproducibility and impact.  
- Related work discussion is adequate but could compare more deeply with newer efficient proposal methods.  
- Analysis of trade-offs between proposal quality (Recall-to-IoU) and detection accuracy is limited and could be expanded.  
- Motivation for chosen anchor scales and aspect ratios is not thoroughly explored.  
- Arguments for acceptance: novel, effective solution with strong empirical results, clear presentation, and released code.  
- Arguments against acceptance: limited discussion of recent related work and insufficient analysis of anchor selection.  
- Overall assessment: high-quality, foundational contribution that merits strong recommendation for acceptance.
