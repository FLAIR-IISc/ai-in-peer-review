- Recommendation: Paper merits publication after discussion and rebuttal.  
- Strength: Presents an elegant, clearly written nonconvex relaxation for global optimization of decision trees with a fixed structure.  
- Impact: Work has potential to stimulate further research on related methodologies.  
- Experimental results: Performance is marginal/disappointing and limits the paper’s rating.  
- Rating justification: Stronger empirical outcomes would justify a higher score; current assessment considered appropriate.  
- Suggested improvement: Prune the final split in each tree after global optimization and re-optimize that split and its leaf via standard methods to potentially boost performance.  
- Concern: Important to assess whether the global optimization framework increases the risk of overfitting.
