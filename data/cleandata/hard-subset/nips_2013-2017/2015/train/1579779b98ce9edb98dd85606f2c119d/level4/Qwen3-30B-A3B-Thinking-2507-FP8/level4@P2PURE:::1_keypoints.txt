- Strong paper deserving publication.
- Main contribution: elegant nonconvex relaxation for global optimization of decision trees with a fixed structure.
- Likely to stimulate further research on related methods.
- Experimental results are somewhat underwhelming.
- Core contribution remains compelling despite modest experiments.
- Stronger empirical results would likely raise the score; current assessment is appropriate.
- Suggested fix: discard each tree's final split after global optimization, then optimize that split and its leaf with standard methods to improve performance.
- Important to evaluate whether global optimization increases overfitting.
