- Overall positive recommendation for publication after discussion and rebuttal; paper warrants acceptance.  
- Proposes a nonconvex relaxation for global optimization of decision trees with a pre-specified, fixed structure.  
- Paper is very clearly written.  
- Core idea is elegant, novel, and theoretically interesting.  
- Main weakness: experimental results are disappointing and do not clearly outperform existing techniques.  
- Practical impact is diminished by weak empirical validation; stronger experiments would raise the rating.  
- Suggestion: try a hybrid approach—globally optimize structure, then re-optimize each leaf's final split and leaf values using a standard greedy criterion.  
- Suggestion: investigate and experimentally evaluate overfitting, since global optimization may be more prone to overfitting than greedy methods.
