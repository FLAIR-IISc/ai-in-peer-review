- Reviewer supports publication, remaining positive after discussion and rebuttal.
- Endorses the work for its elegant approach and potential to stimulate further research.
- Experimental results are somewhat disappointing but the current score is deemed appropriate.
- Paper clearly presents a nonconvex relaxation for global optimization of decision trees with a fixed structure.
- Theoretical contribution is strong; empirical performance is less compelling by comparison.
- Suggested improvement: refine trees after global optimization by re-optimizing final splits and leaf nodes with standard methods to boost results.
- Cautions that global optimization methods can increase overfitting and this should be considered in evaluation.
