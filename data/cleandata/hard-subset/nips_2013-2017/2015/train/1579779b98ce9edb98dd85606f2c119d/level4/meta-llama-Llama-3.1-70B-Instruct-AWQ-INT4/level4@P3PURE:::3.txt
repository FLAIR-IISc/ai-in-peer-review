The authors propose an interesting approach to searching for the best decision tree of a specific depth by formulating it as a structured prediction problem. Their method outperforms the typical greedy approach for decision tree construction in terms of test set accuracy, albeit with relatively small improvements.

The proposed method is a notable contribution, offering a fresh perspective on decision tree construction. However, the empirical gains over the standard greedy approach are modest. It would be beneficial for the authors to provide more insights into the scenarios or problem characteristics where their method yields more substantial improvements.

Additionally, the authors could further enhance the paper by discussing the computational complexity and scalability of their approach, as well as its potential applications in real-world problems. Overall, the submission is well-written, and the ideas are clearly presented, making it a valuable contribution to the field.
