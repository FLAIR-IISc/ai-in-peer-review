- Proposes a non-greedy method to optimize decision trees with fixed structure by formulating split learning as structured prediction with latent variables.  
- Derives a convex-concave upper bound on empirical loss and optimizes it with SGD.  
- Method is efficient and scalable, making training of deep trees feasible.  
- Clear, well-written presentation with a novel connection between decision tree learning and structured prediction.  
- Experimental results are underwhelming and do not significantly outperform baselines.  
- Some evidence of improved generalization and reduced overfitting, but not strong.  
- Suggestion: combine global optimization with a final greedy re-optimization (e.g., re-optimize last split) to potentially improve results.  
- Concern: potential for overfitting from global optimization is not fully evaluated; more experiments needed.  
- Overall assessment: elegant and valuable contribution likely to spur further research despite modest empirical gains.  
- Recommendation: Accept.  
- Reviewer confidence: 8/10.
