- Strength: Elegant, non-greedy global optimization formulation.

- Weakness: Experimental results are underwhelming relative to the method's promise.  
- Suggestion: Try a post-processing variant—remove the last split on each path then reoptimize that split and associated leaves with the greedy procedure.
