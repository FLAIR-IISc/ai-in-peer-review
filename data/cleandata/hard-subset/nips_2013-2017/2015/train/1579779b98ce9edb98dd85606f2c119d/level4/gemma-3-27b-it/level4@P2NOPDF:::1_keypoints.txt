- Proposes an elegant nonconvex relaxation method to globally optimize decision trees with fixed structure.  
- Reviewer maintains a positive assessment and recommends acceptance.  
- Core contribution is theoretically sound, well-articulated, and likely to stimulate further research.  
- Experimental results are disappointing: observed performance gains are modest.  
- Suggests improving performance by re-optimizing the final split and its leaf after the global optimization.  
- Warns of a trade-off: global optimization methods can be prone to overfitting.  
- Recommends stronger evaluation of generalization (more validation or regularization strategies).  
- Novelty and clarity justify publication despite weak empirical results.  
- Stronger experimental outcomes would have merited a higher score.  
- Paper is very clearly written and easy to follow.
