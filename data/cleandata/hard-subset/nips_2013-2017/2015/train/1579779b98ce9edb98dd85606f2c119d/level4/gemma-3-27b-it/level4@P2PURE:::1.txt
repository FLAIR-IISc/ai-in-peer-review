Following discussion with other reviewers and consideration of the rebuttal, I maintain my positive assessment of this paper, recommending publication due to the elegance of its approach and its potential to stimulate further research in related areas. While the experimental results are somewhat disappointing, I believe the current score accurately reflects this. Stronger empirical results would likely warrant a higher rating. The paper presents a clear and well-written nonconvex relaxation for the global optimization of decision trees with a fixed structure. Although the approach is elegant, the experimental performance is a limitation. A potential improvement would be to refine the final split and corresponding leaf node in each tree using standard optimization techniques after applying the global optimization method, which might yield enhanced results. It’s also important to acknowledge that global optimization approaches are generally susceptible to increased overfitting, and this should be evaluated.
