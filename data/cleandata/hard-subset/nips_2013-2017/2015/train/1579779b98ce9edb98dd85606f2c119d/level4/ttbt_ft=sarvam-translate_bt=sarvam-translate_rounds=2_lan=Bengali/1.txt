After reviewing and editing: discussing it with other reviewers and looking at the rebuttals, I still believe that this is a good paper that deserves publication based on the elegance of its approach and the potential for further research on the methods involved. I agree that the experimental results are somewhat disappointing. However, I would probably have valued this paper more if I had obtained stronger results, so I think this score is appropriate. This very clearly written paper proposes a non-convex relaxation for the global optimization of a structured decision tree. The approach is elegant, but the experimental results are somewhat disappointing. A simple change could be, after using this global optimization, to remove the last split of each tree and optimize it and the leaf node in the standard way, which could lead to better results. It is also important to evaluate that global optimization will generally lead to more over-fitting.
