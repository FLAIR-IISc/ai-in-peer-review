- Paper merits publication; approach is elegant and could inspire further work.
- Proposes a nonconvex relaxation for global optimization of fixed-structure decision trees.
- Presentation is clear and well-written.
- Experimental results are somewhat underwhelming despite an acceptable score.
- Suggests discarding the last split in each tree, then optimizing the last split and leaf with standard methods to potentially improve results.
- Recommends evaluating whether global optimization tends to increase overfitting.
