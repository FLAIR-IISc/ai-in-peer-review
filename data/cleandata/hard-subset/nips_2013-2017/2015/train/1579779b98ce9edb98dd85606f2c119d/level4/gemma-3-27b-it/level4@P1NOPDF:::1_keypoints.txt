- Reviewer supports publication after discussion and rebuttal and remains positive about the paper.  
- The approach is an elegant nonconvex relaxation for global optimization of decision trees with fixed structure.  
- The method has potential to stimulate further research.  
- Experimental results are somewhat disappointing / not as compelling, though the current score is considered appropriate.  
- A simple improvement suggested: after global optimization, remove the final split in each tree and re-optimize that split and its leaf using a standard method.  
- Concern that global optimization may increase overfitting and this should be evaluated.
