- Strong paper deserving publication after discussion and rebuttal.
- Proposes an elegant nonconvex relaxation technique for global optimization of decision trees with a pre-defined structure.
- Work has high potential to stimulate further research in the area.
- Experimental results are somewhat underwhelming and experimental validation is limited.
- Assigned score reflects the weaker empirical results; stronger results would merit a higher rating.
- Suggestion: refine the final split per tree by discarding it after global optimization and re-optimizing it with the corresponding leaf using standard methods to improve performance.
- Caution: global optimization approaches can increase overfitting and this should be addressed in the evaluation.
