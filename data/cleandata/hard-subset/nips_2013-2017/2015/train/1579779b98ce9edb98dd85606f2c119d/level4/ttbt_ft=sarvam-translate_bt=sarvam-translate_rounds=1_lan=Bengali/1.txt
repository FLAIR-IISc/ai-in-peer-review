After reviewing and editing: After discussing with other reviewers and seeing the rebuttals, I still believe this is a good paper worthy of publication based on the elegance of its approach and the potential for further research on the methods involved. I agree that the experimental results are somewhat disappointing. However, I would have probably valued this paper even more if I had received stronger results, so I think this score is appropriate. This very clearly written paper proposes a non-convex relaxation for the global optimization of a structured decision tree. The approach is elegant, but the experimental results are somewhat disappointing. A simple change could be, after using this global optimization, dropping the last split of each tree and optimizing it and the leaf node in the standard way, which could lead to better results. It is also important to evaluate that global optimization will generally lead to more over-fitting.
