After careful consideration of the feedback and discussion with fellow reviewers, I reaffirm my initial assessment that this paper merits publication. The proposed approach is elegant and has the potential to inspire further research in related areas, outweighing the somewhat disappointing experimental results. With more compelling results, I would likely assign an even higher rating, making the current score suitable.

The paper presents a well-written and clear proposal for a nonconvex relaxation method for globally optimizing decision trees with a fixed structure. The approach is indeed elegant, yet the experimental results fall short of expectations. One potential refinement could be to discard the last split in each tree after applying global optimization and then optimize it and the leaf node using standard methods, which may yield improved results. Furthermore, it is essential to evaluate whether global optimization generally leads to increased overfitting.
