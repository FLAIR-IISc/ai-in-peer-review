- Post-rebuttal conclusion: reviewer still views the paper as strong and deserving acceptance due to method elegance and potential to spur follow-up work.  
- Empirical results are somewhat underwhelming; stronger experiments could have increased the rating.  
- Paper is very clearly written and introduces a nonconvex relaxation for global optimization of decision trees with fixed structure.  
- The proposed approach is elegant, but the experimental gains are modest.  
- Suggested modification: after global optimization, remove each tree’s final split and re-optimize that split and its leaf node in the standard way to potentially improve performance.  
- Concern: important to evaluate whether global optimization increases overfitting.
