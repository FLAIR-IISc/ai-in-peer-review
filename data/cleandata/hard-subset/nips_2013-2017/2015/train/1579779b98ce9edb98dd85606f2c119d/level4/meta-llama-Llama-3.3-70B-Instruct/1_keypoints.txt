- Reaffirms paper warrants publication due to sophisticated methodology and research potential.
- Presents a lucid, elegant nonconvex relaxation framework for globally optimizing decision trees with a fixed structure.
- Writing is clear and the methodological contribution is strong.
- Experimental results are somewhat underwhelming, though deemed justifiable; stronger results would have increased the rating.
- Suggests a refinement: drop the final split after global optimization, then re-optimize that split and corresponding leaf using conventional methods.
- Urges evaluation of whether global optimization increases overfitting and calls for thorough assessment of this risk.
