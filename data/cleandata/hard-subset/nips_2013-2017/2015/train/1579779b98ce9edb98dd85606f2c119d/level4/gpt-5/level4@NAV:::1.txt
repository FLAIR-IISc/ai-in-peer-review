Review—Edit after feedback: After discussion with the other reviewers and consideration of the rebuttal, I still believe this is a good paper that merits publication based on the elegance of the approach and its potential to spur further research on related methods. I agree that the experimental results are somewhat disappointing. With stronger results I would likely have rated the paper even higher, so I think the current score is appropriate. The paper is very clearly written and proposes a nonconvex relaxation for global optimization of decision trees with a fixed structure. Elegant approach, but the empirical results are somewhat underwhelming. One simple change would be, after applying this global optimization, to remove the last split in each tree and reoptimize that split and the leaf node using the standard method, which might yield better results. It is also important to evaluate whether global optimization generally increases overfitting.
