- Presents a clear, elegant nonconvex relaxation for global optimization of decision trees with fixed structure.  
- Approach is well-motivated and technically sound, offering a principled solution to a hard problem.  
- Experimental results are modest and somewhat disappointing compared to the theoretical contribution.  
- Despite weak empirical gains, the conceptual novelty makes the paper compelling and likely to stimulate further research.  
- Reviewer considered the authors' rebuttal and discussions with other reviewers and still recommends acceptance.  
- Suggestion: after global optimization, remove the final split and re-optimize that split and its leaf with standard methods to potentially improve performance.  
- Recommendation to explicitly discuss increased overfitting risk because the global optimization targets training loss without regularization.
