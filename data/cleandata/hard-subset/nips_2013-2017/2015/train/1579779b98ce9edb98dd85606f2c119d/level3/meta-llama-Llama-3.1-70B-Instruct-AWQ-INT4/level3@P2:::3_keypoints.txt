- Introduces a weight-matrix norm regularizer to reduce overfitting.  
- Builds on structured prediction with latent variables while enabling efficient learning of deeper trees and handling arbitrary loss functions.  
- Presents a novel formulation linking decision tree optimization to structured prediction.  
- Experimental results show non-greedy method generally outperforms greedy tree builders on test accuracy, though gains are typically small.  
- Empirically less prone to overfitting and achieves better generalization across tree depths.
