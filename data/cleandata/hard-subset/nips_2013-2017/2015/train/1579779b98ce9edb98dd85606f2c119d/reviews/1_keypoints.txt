- Paper is elegant, clearly written, and merits publication; likely to spur further research.
- Proposes a nonconvex relaxation for global optimization of decision trees with a fixed structure.
- Experimental results are somewhat disappointing, though the score is considered appropriate.
- Suggestion: after global optimization, drop and re-optimize the last split and its leaf node using standard methods to potentially improve results.
- Important to evaluate whether the global optimization increases overfitting.
