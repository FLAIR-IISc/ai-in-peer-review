- Proposes a modified EM algorithm for high-dimensional, sparse parameter estimation in latent variable models.  
- Proves convergence to a local optimum and derives the convergence rate; provides a statistical testing framework for estimator sparsity.  
- Paper is well-organized with a comprehensive related-work summary.  
- Sections 3 and 4 are densely written and need clearer, simpler exposition for readers.  
- Claim of minimax-optimal rate √(s* log(d/n)) lacks explicit justification in the main manuscript (supplement not reviewed).  
- Sparsity estimator ^s in Eq. (3.8) depends on unknown true sparsity s*; guidance is needed on choosing ^s in practice.  
- Requirement for initial β^init satisfying ||β^init − β*||2 < R/2 is unclear; authors should explain how to obtain such an initializer.  
- Role and necessity of the truncation step in Algorithm 1 are not explained; consequences of omitting truncation should be discussed.  
- After rebuttal reviewer raised score from 6 to 7, calling it a solid statistical paper but noting readability for ML researchers and practical applicability remain concerns.
