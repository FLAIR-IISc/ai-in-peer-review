- Positive: Paper offers an interesting, thorough analysis of EM in high-dimensional settings, with convergence results and an inference procedure.
- Missing detail: Needs to state conditions and procedures for obtaining the initial solution β^{init} that satisfies the requirement referenced (line 60, p.2).
- Algorithm choice: Unclear guidance on when to use each of the two proposed algorithms in practice.
- Tuning parameters: Selection and sensitivity of \hat s and λ are not explained; need discussion of data-dependent choices and their impact on sampling properties.
- Prior work overlap: The decorrelated score test appears identical to a previous paper and should be properly cited and its novelty (or required adaptations) clarified.
- Technical conditions: The many technical assumptions used in proofs need discussion about whether they hold for the latent variable models considered.
- Novelty: The paper’s original contributions versus straightforward application of existing results (e.g., [2] and others) are not clearly stated; authors should highlight what is new and nontrivial.
- Presentation: Major technical challenges and proofs are mostly in the supplement; more of these details should be included in the main text to improve clarity.
