- Proposes a modified EM algorithm for high-dimensional sparse parameter estimation.
- Establishes conditions for convergence to the global solution and derives the convergence rate.
- Develops a statistical testing framework to verify the sparsity of the estimator.
- Paper is well-organized and related work is adequately summarized.
- Content is dense; sections 3 and 4 are hard to follow and would benefit from simplification.
- Claims the rate sqrt(s*log(d/n)) is minimax-optimal but no proof is provided in the main manuscript (reviewer did not check supplement); a clear proof is requested.
- In Eq.(3.8), the chosen s_hat depends on the unknown true sparsity s; guidance is needed on how to choose s_hat in practice.
- Unclear how to select an initial value beta^init satisfying ||beta^init - beta*||_2 < R/2; practical initialization guidance is needed.
- The truncation step in Algorithm 1 requires explanation and justification; consequences of omitting it should be discussed.
- Reviewer raised score from 6 to 7 after a satisfactory rebuttal.
- Paper is statistically solid but has limited readability and practical usefulness for real-world ML applications.
