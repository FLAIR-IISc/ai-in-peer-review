- Proposes a modified EM algorithm for sparse, high-dimensional parameters with conditions for convergence to the global solution and derived convergence rate.  
- Constructs a statistical test framework to verify the sparsity of the estimator.  
- Paper is well-organized and related work is sufficiently summarized.  
- Sections 3 and 4 are dense and hard to follow; simplifying them would improve clarity.  
- Claims the rate sqrt(s·log(d/n)) is minimax-optimal but lacks proof in the main manuscript; needs explanation or supplemental reference.  
- The choice of \hat{s} in Eq.(3.8) depends on unknown sparsity s; practical guidance for selecting \hat{s} is requested.  
- Unclear how to choose initial value β^init to satisfy ||β^init − β*||_2 < R/2; authors should clarify initialization strategy.  
- Truncation step in Algorithm 1 is not well-explained; need elaboration on its role and the consequences of omitting it.  
- After rebuttal, authors addressed concerns and the reviewer raised the score from 6 to 7.  
- Overall a solid statistical contribution, but readability and practical applicability for ML researchers and real-world use are limited.
