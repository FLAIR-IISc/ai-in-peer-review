- Proposes a modified EM algorithm for sparse, high-dimensional parameter estimation with proven convergence to the global solution, derived convergence rates, and a statistical test framework for estimator sparsity.
- Manuscript is well-structured and provides a thorough related-work summary.
- Sections 3 and 4 are highly dense and difficult to follow; additional simplification is needed to improve accessibility.
- Claim of minimax optimality √(s log(d/n)) (line 066) lacks an explicit proof in the main manuscript; the proof should be provided or clarified.
- Equation (3.8) uses an estimator that depends on the unknown sparsity level s — the paper should explain how to select s in practice.
- The paper must explain how to obtain a practical initial estimator β^init satisfying ||β^init − β*||₂ < R/2 (line 275).
- The necessity of the truncation step in Algorithm 1 is unclear; the authors should state what issues arise if truncation is omitted.
- After rebuttal the reviewer raised the score from 6 to 7; it is viewed as a strong statistical contribution but remains hard for ML researchers to digest and needs more justification of practical utility.
