- Proposes a modified EM algorithm for estimating sparse, high-dimensional parameters with global convergence guarantees and convergence rate analysis.
- Introduces a novel statistical testing framework to assess the sparsity of the estimator.
- Well-structured with comprehensive related work, but presentation is dense; sections 3 and 4 are hard to follow and need simplification.
- Claim that sqrt(s log(d/n)) is minimax-optimal lacks proof or citation and requires justification.
- The estimator hat{s} in Equation (3.8) depends on the unknown s; no practical procedure is given for selecting hat{s}.
- Algorithm requires an initialization beta_init satisfying ||beta_init - beta*||_2 < R/2, but practical methods to obtain such an initialization are not discussed.
- Purpose and necessity of the truncation step in Algorithm 1 are unclear; authors should explain effects of omitting it.
- After rebuttal the authors addressed the reviewer’s concerns satisfactorily, leading to a score increase from 6 to 7.
- Overall a solid statistical contribution, but readability for machine-learning practitioners and practical utility (sensitivity to initialization and difficulty estimating sparsity) remain questionable.
