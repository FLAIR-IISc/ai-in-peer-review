- Introduces a modified EM for high-dimensional sparse parameter estimation, with global convergence conditions, convergence rate analysis, and a statistical testing framework for sparsity.  
- Paper is well-structured with adequate related-work coverage.  
- Presentation is dense and hard to read, especially Sections 3 and 4; needs simplification for accessibility.  
- Claims minimax-optimal estimation error sqrt(s log(d/n)) but does not include a clear proof in the main text.  
- Estimator hat{s} (Eq. 3.8) depends on true sparsity s; no practical procedure given to select hat{s}.  
- Requires an initial beta^{init} satisfying ||beta^{init}-beta^*||_2 < R/2; no guidance on how to obtain such an initialization.  
- Role and necessity of the truncation step in Algorithm 1 are unclear; consequences of omitting it are not discussed.  
- After rebuttal, reviewer found responses satisfactory and raised score from 6 to 7.  
- Overall statistically rigorous, but density and uncertain practical utility for real-world applications remain concerns.
