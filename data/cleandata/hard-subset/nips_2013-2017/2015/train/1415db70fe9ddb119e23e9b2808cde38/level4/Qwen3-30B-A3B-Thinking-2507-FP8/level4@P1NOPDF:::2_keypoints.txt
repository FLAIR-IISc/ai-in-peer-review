- Proposes a modified EM algorithm for high-dimensional sparse parameter estimation.  
- Establishes convergence to the global solution under specific conditions and derives the convergence rate.  
- Introduces a statistical testing framework to assess estimator sparsity.  
- Manuscript is well-structured with a thorough related-work summary.  
- Sections 3 and 4 are technically dense and difficult to follow; simplification needed for readability.  
- Critical issue 1: Claim that rate √(s log(d/n)) is minimax-optimal is stated without derivation or clear reference—needs justification.  
- Critical issue 2: Equation (3.8) defines ŝ using unknown sparsity s and Section 3.2 requires an initial β^init with ||β^init−β*||₂ < R/2—practical selection/obtaining of these is unclear.  
- Critical issue 3: The truncation step in Algorithm 1 lacks justification; authors should explain consequences of omitting it.  
- After rebuttal, reviewer found responses satisfactory and raised the score from 6 to 7.  
- Overall: strong theoretical contribution with solid statistical foundations, but limited accessibility for practitioners and unclear practical utility.
