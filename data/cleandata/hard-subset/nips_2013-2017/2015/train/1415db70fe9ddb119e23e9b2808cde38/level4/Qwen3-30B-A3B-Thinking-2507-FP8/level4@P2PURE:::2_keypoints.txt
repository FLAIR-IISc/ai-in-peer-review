- Proposes a modified EM algorithm for sparse high-dimensional parameter estimation with conditions for global convergence and derived convergence rate, plus a statistical test framework for estimator sparsity.  
- Paper is well-organized and provides an adequate summary of related work.  
- Sections 3 and 4 are dense and hard to follow; the presentation needs simplification for readers.  
- Claim that √(s log(d/n)) is minimax-optimal (line 66) lacks an explicit proof in the main manuscript—please provide it.  
- Equation (3.8) uses an estimator ĥs that depends on unknown s—clarify how ĥs should be chosen in practice.  
- Need a practical procedure to obtain an initial β^{init} satisfying ‖β^{init} − β*‖2 < R/2 (line 275).  
- The role and necessity of the truncation step in Algorithm 1 are unclear—explain what happens if it is omitted.  
- After rebuttal, the reviewer raised the score from 6 to 7 and found the authors’ response satisfactory.  
- Overall assessment: a solid statistical paper but difficult for machine learning researchers to read and with questionable practical utility for real-world applications.
