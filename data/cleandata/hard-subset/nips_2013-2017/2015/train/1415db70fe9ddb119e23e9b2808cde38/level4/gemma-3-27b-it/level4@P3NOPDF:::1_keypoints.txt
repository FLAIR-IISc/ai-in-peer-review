- Extends EM to high-dimensional settings but the novelty and specific challenges overcome are not clearly articulated.  
- Provides theoretical guarantees but fails to address the crucial practical issue of obtaining the required initialization β^{init}; needs discussion of how to find it.  
- Proposes two algorithms but lacks guidance or comparative analysis on when to prefer one over the other.  
- Omits discussion on selection and sensitivity of tuning parameters (ŝ and λ); needs practical or automated selection strategies.  
- The decorrelated score test appears equivalent to Ning and Liu (2013); this equivalence should be explicitly cited and the paper should state whether any new theory was needed.  
- Relies on many technical conditions; the paper should discuss their plausibility for relevant latent variable models and provide examples.  
- Presentation is terse and overly dependent on supplementary material; the main paper should be more self-contained and detail key adaptations of existing techniques.  
- Overall: a theoretically sound extension but lacking practical guidance and a clear statement of novel contributions; addressing the above would improve clarity and impact.
