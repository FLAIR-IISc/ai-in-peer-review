- Proposes a modified EM algorithm for sparse high-dimensional parameters, proving convergence to the global solution and deriving convergence rates.
- Develops a statistical testing framework to assess estimator sparsity.
- Paper is well-structured with an adequate literature review.
- Content is dense; Sections 3 and 4 are difficult to follow and should be simplified for readability.
- Claim that sqrt(s*log(d/n)) is minimax-optimal lacks an explicit proof in the main manuscript (reviewer did not check supplement) and needs a clear proof.
- Eq. (3.8) uses ^s that depends on unknown s; reviewer asks how to choose ^s in practice.
- Reviewer asks how to obtain an initial beta^init satisfying ||beta^init - beta*||_2 < R/2 in practical settings.
- Reviewer is unclear on the role of the truncation step in Algorithm 1 and what happens if it is omitted.
- After rebuttal, the reviewer’s concerns were addressed and score increased from 6 to 7.
- Overall judged a solid statistics paper but somewhat hard for ML researchers to digest and with uncertain practical applicability.
