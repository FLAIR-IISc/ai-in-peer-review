

The paper introduces a modified EM algorithm tailored for sparse, high-dimensional parameter estimation. The authors establish convergence conditions to the global solution, derive the convergence rate, and develop a statistical test framework to assess estimator sparsity. The manuscript is well-structured with comprehensive related work coverage. However, sections 3 and 4 are excessively dense and difficult to follow; simplification would significantly improve readability.  

- The claim in line 066 that $\sqrt{s \log(d/n)}$ is minimax-optimal lacks proof in the main text (supplementary materials were not reviewed). Please provide a clear derivation.  
- In Eq. (3.8), $\hat{s}$ depends on $s$, which is unknown in practice. How should $\hat{s}$ be selected? Additionally, how can an initial estimate $\beta^{\text{init}}$ satisfying $\|\beta^{\text{init}} - \beta^*\|_2 < R/2$ (line 275) be practically obtained?  
- The necessity of the truncation step in Algorithm 1 is unclear. What are the consequences of omitting this step?
