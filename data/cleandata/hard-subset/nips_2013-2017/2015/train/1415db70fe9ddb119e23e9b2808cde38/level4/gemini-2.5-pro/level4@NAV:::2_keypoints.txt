- Proposes an EM variant for sparse, high-dimensional parameters with theoretical guarantees.  
- Establishes conditions for convergence to the global optimum and derives the convergence rate.  
- Develops a statistical testing framework to assess sparsity of the estimator.  
- Manuscript is well-structured and related work coverage is comprehensive.  
- Presentation is highly condensed; sections 3 and 4 are difficult to follow and should be simplified.  
- Claim that rate sqrt(s * log(d/n)) is minimax-optimal lacks an explicit proof in the main text.  
- In (3.8) the chosen ŝ depends on unknown sparsity s — request guidance on selecting ŝ in practice.  
- Need practical guidance for selecting initial β^init to satisfy ||β^init − β*||_2 < R/2.  
- Role and necessity of the truncation step in Algorithm 1 are unclear; consequences of omitting it are not explained.  
- After rebuttal, reviewer’s concerns were addressed and score raised from 6 to 7.  
- Paper is a solid statistical contribution but is dense for ML readers and its practical real-world utility remains somewhat unclear.
