- Proposes a modified EM algorithm for sparse, high-dimensional parameters.  
- Establishes conditions for convergence to a global solution and derives convergence rates.  
- Constructs a statistical testing framework to assess estimator sparsity.  
- Paper is well organized with sufficient related-work summary.  
- Sections 3 and 4 are dense and hard to follow; need simplification for broader readability.  
- Claim that sqrt(s log(d/n)) is minimax-optimal lacks a clear proof in the main manuscript.  
- Eq.(3.8): selection of hat{s} depends on unknown s; practical guidance for choosing hat{s} is not provided.  
- Requirement for an initial beta^{init} satisfying ||beta^{init}-beta^*||_2 < R/2 is impractical; authors should suggest strategies for initialization.  
- Purpose and necessity of the truncation step in Algorithm 1 are unclear; consequences of omitting it are not discussed.  
- After rebuttal, reviewer raised score from 6 to 7, calling it a solid statistical contribution.  
- Accessibility to machine learning researchers and practical utility for real-world applications remain concerns.
