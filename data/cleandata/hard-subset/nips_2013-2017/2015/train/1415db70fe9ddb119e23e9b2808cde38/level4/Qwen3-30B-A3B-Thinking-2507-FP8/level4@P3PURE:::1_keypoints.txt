- Paper studies EM in high-dimensional settings and proposes convergence and inference results, but several clarifications and strengthening are needed.

- Clarify under what conditions and how a practical initial solution β^{init} meeting the paper’s requirement can be obtained.

- Provide practical guidance on selecting between the two proposed algorithms.

- Explain how to choose tuning parameters (ŝ and λ) in practice, assess sensitivity to their choice, and offer data-driven selection methods.

- Properly cite Ning & Liu (2017) and explicitly state what novel adaptations (if any) are required to apply the decorrelated score test to the latent-variable context.

- Discuss whether the numerous technical conditions required by the theory are satisfied for the specific latent-variable models considered, not just assumed.

- More clearly articulate the novelty and nontrivial technical challenges of the work and include substantive exposition in the main paper rather than relying on supplementary material.
