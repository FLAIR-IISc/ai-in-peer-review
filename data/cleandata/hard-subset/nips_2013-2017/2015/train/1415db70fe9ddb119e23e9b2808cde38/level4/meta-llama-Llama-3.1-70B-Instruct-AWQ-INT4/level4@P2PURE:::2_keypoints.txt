- Proposes a modified EM algorithm for sparse, high-dimensional parameters with derived global convergence conditions and rates.  
- Constructs a statistical testing framework to verify estimator sparsity.  
- Paper is well-organized and related work is adequately summarized.  
- Sections 3 and 4 are dense and hard to follow; presentation should be simplified.  
- Claim that rate sqrt(s·log(d/n)) is minimax-optimal lacks a proof or clear reference in the main manuscript.  
- Choice of s-hat (^\s) in Eq.(3.8) depends on unknown true sparsity s, with no practical selection guidance.  
- No clear procedure for obtaining an initial beta^init that satisfies ||beta^init - beta*||_2 < R/2.  
- Importance and consequences of the truncation step in Algorithm 1 are not explained.  
- After rebuttal, authors addressed concerns sufficiently to raise the review score from 6 to 7.  
- Overall statistically sound but limited readability and practical applicability for real-world ML use.
