- Proposes a modified EM algorithm for sparse, high-dimensional true parameters with conditions guaranteeing convergence to the global solution and a derived convergence rate.  
- Develops a statistical testing framework to verify the sparsity of the estimator.  
- Paper is well organized and related work is adequately summarized.  
- Sections 3 and 4 are dense and should be simplified to improve readability.  
- Claim that √(s·log(d/n)) is minimax-optimal is stated but lacks a clear proof in the main manuscript.  
- Dependence of the chosen sparsity level s_hat on true s is noted, yet practical guidance is missing since s is unknown.  
- Need guidance on how to choose s_hat and how to select an initial beta^init satisfying ||beta^init − beta*||_2 ≤ R/2.  
- Truncation step in Algorithm 1 is not clearly justified; authors should explain effects of omitting it.  
- Authors’ rebuttal was satisfactory, leading to score increase from 6 to 7.  
- Despite being a solid statistical contribution, readability for ML researchers is limited.  
- Practical applicability of the proposed method remains somewhat uncertain.
