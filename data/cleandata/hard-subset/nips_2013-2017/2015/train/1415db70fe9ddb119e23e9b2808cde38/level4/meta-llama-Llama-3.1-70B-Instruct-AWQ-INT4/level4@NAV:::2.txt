This paper presents a modified EM algorithm tailored for sparse and high-dimensional true parameters, providing conditions for convergence to the global solution and deriving the rate of convergence. Additionally, the authors develop a statistical test framework to verify the sparsity of the estimator.

The paper's organization is clear, and the related work is adequately summarized. However, the content is dense, particularly in sections 3 and 4, which may hinder comprehension. Further simplification would enhance readability for the audience.

One point of clarification is required: the authors claim that sqrt(s*log(d/n)) is minimax-optimal (line 066), but the proof is not evident in the main manuscript (the supplementary material was not reviewed). A clear proof would be beneficial.

In Eq.(3.8), the dependence of ^s on s is noted, but in practical scenarios, the value of s is unknown. Guidance on choosing ^s and selecting an appropriate initial value beta^init that satisfies ||beta^init - beta*||_2  R/2 (line 275) would be helpful.

The truncation step in Algorithm 1 is not clearly justified. An explanation of the consequences of omitting this step in the EM algorithm would be appreciated.
