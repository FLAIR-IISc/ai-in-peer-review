- Presents a modified EM algorithm for sparse high-dimensional parameters with global convergence conditions, convergence rate, and a statistical testing framework; well organized with good related-work summary.
- Sections 3 and 4 are dense and hurt readability; the manuscript needs simplification for broader accessibility.
- The claim that sqrt(s*log(d/n)) is minimax-optimal (line 066) lacks a clear proof in the main text and supplementary material; a detailed proof is requested.
- Eq.(3.8) defines ^s dependent on unknown s; the choice of ^s and how to pick an initial beta^init satisfying ||beta^init - beta*||_2 < R/2 need clarification.
- The truncation step in Algorithm 1 appears crucial, but its role and the consequences of omitting it are not clearly explained.
- Reviewer was satisfied with the rebuttal, raising the score from 6 to 7.
- Although statistically sound, the paper’s complexity limits accessibility to ML researchers, and its practical applicability/real-world usefulness is questioned.
