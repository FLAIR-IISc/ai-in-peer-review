- Paper is well organized with adequate related-work summary, but is dense and sections 3–4 are hard to follow; needs simplification.
- Reviewer couldn’t find a proof that √(s log(d/n)) is minimax-optimal in the main manuscript and requests a clear demonstration (did not check supplement).
- In Eq.(3.8) the estimator ĥs depends on unknown s; reviewer asks how to choose ĥs in practice and how to obtain an initial β^{init} satisfying ||β^{init}−β^*||_2 < R/2 in real-world settings.
- Requests explanation of the truncation step in Algorithm 1 and what would happen if that step were omitted.
