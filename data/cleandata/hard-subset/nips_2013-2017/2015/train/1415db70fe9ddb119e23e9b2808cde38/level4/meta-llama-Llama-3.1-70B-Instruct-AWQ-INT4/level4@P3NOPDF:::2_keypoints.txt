- Proposes a modified EM algorithm for sparse, high-dimensional parameters, gives convergence conditions and rates, and builds a statistical test for estimator sparsity.  
- Paper is well-organized with adequate related work, but the content is dense and sections 3–4 are hard to follow; simplify for readability.  
- Claims the rate sqrt(s*log(d/n)) is minimax-optimal (line 066) but the proof is missing from the main manuscript; include the proof or point to the supplement.  
- In Eq.(3.8) the chosen ^s depends on unknown s; provide practical guidance on selecting ^s.  
- It is unclear how to pick an initial beta^init meeting ||beta^init - beta*||_2 < R/2 (line 275); discuss initialization strategies.  
- The truncation step in Algorithm 1 seems crucial but its role and the effect of omitting it are not explained; elaborate on this.  
- After rebuttal some concerns were addressed, raising the score from 6 to 7.  
- Readability for machine-learning researchers needs improvement, and the practical usefulness of the method for real-world applications is somewhat questionable.
