- Proposes a modified EM algorithm for sparse, high-dimensional parameters and proves global convergence, convergence rate, and a statistical test for sparsity.
- Paper is well-organized and reviews related work, but the exposition is very dense—Sections 3 and 4 are hard to follow and need simplification.
- Claims the rate sqrt(s·log(d/n)) is minimax-optimal (line 066) but provides no proof in the manuscript; requests a clear justification.
- Equation (3.8) uses ^s that depends on true sparsity s (unknown); asks how ^s should be chosen in practice.
- Analysis requires an initial beta^init with ||beta^init - beta*||_2 < R/2 (line 275); asks how to obtain such an initial value in practice.
- Motivation for the truncation step in Algorithm 1 is unclear; asks for explanation and implications of omitting it.
- After rebuttal, reviewer raised score from 6 to 7, calling it a solid statistics paper but noting it may be hard for ML researchers to read and its practical utility is not fully established.
