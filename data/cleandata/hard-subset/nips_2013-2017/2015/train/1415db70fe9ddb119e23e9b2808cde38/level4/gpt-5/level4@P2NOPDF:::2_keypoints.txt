- Proposes a modified EM algorithm for high-dimensional problems with a sparse true parameter, proving convergence to the global optimum and deriving convergence rates.  
- Introduces a hypothesis-testing framework to assess estimator sparsity.  
- Paper is well organized with adequate related work coverage.  
- Presentation is dense (especially Sections 3 and 4); arguments are hard to follow and need simplification and clearer guidance.  
- Claim that the rate sqrt(s log(d/n)) is minimax-optimal lacks an explicit proof or precise citation in the main text—request to provide one.  
- Eq. (3.8) uses a \hat{s} that depends on unknown s—need practical guidance on selecting \hat{s} and obtaining an initial beta^{init} satisfying ||beta^{init} − beta*||_2 < R/2.  
- Request clarification on the role of the truncation step in Algorithm 1 and the effects on convergence/statistical guarantees if omitted.  
- After rebuttal, reviewer’s concerns were satisfactorily addressed and score raised from 6 to 7, but paper remains hard to read for typical ML researchers and its practical usefulness is somewhat uncertain.
