- Proposal: modified EM algorithm for sparse high-dimensional parameters with global convergence guarantees, convergence rate, and a statistical test for estimator sparsity.  
- Organization: paper is well-organized and related work is adequately summarized.  
- Readability: content is very dense; Sections 3 and 4 are difficult to follow and need simplification.  
- Missing proof: claim that sqrt(s·log(d/n)) is minimax-optimal (line 066) lacks a clear proof in the main manuscript.  
- Choice of ^s: Equation (3.8) makes estimated sparsity ^s depend on true s, but no practical guidance is given for selecting ^s.  
- Initialization: unclear how to choose beta^init in practice to satisfy ||beta^init - beta*||_2 < R/2.  
- Truncation step: Algorithm 1’s truncation is unexplained and the effects of omitting it are not discussed.  
- Reviewer outcome: author responses were satisfactory, raising the reviewer’s score from 6 to 7.  
- Practical relevance: solid statistical contribution but limited readability for ML researchers and questionable usefulness in real-world applications.
