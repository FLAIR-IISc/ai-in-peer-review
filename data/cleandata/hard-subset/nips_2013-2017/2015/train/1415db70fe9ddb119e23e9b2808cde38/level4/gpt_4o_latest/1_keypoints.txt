- Paper claims EM convergence in high-dimensional settings and proposes an inference procedure; reviewers ask for clearer statement of main contributions and novelty.
- Authors must explain how an initial solution β^{init} satisfying the condition on p.2 (line 60) can be identified in practice and under what conditions it exists.
- Two algorithms are presented—provide practical guidance on when to choose one over the other and compare their empirical/ computational trade-offs.
- Clarify how to select the tuning parameters ŝ and λ in practice, assess sensitivity to these choices, and whether they can be chosen in a data-driven way (cross-validation, stability selection, etc.).
- The decorrelated score test is identical to Ning & Liu’s method and should be explicitly cited; state whether any novel adaptations were required for the latent-variable EM context.
- Theoretical results depend on many technical conditions—discuss whether these assumptions hold for the specific latent-variable models studied and the plausibility of each condition.
- The paper builds on [2] and Ning & Liu; more clearly delineate what is genuinely novel, why extending these tools is nontrivial, and provide more insight into the technical challenges beyond the supplementary material.
