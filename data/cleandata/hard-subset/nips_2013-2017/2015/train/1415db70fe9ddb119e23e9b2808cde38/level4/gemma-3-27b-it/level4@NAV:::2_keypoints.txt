- Introduces a modified EM algorithm for sparse high-dimensional parameter estimation with convergence guarantees and a sparsity-testing framework.  
- Paper is well-structured with comprehensive literature review, but presentation is dense and sections 3–4 need clearer, simpler exposition.  
- Claim of minimax optimality at rate sqrt(s*log(d/n)) is stated but not proved in the main manuscript; a clear proof is needed.  
- Eq.(3.8) defines ^s using the unknown true sparsity s; practical guidance for selecting ^s is required.  
- The initialization condition ||beta^init - beta*||_2 < R/2 is hard to satisfy in practice; authors should clarify how to obtain such an init.  
- The role and necessity of the truncation step in Algorithm 1 are unclear; explain consequences of omitting it.  
- After rebuttal the reviewer was satisfied and raised the score from 6 to 7.  
- Contribution is technically sound for statistical theory but could be made more accessible to machine-learning practitioners.  
- Practical applicability to real-world problems remains somewhat uncertain.
