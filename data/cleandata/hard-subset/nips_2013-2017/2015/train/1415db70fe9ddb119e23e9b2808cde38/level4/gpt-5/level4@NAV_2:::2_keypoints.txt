- Paper proposes an EM variant for high-dimensional latent variable models with sparse parameters.  
- Authors claim convergence to the global solution with geometric rates and near-optimal statistical error bounds.  
- A hypothesis testing framework is provided to test whether components are zero and assess estimator sparsity.  
- Manuscript is well structured and related work is adequately covered.  
- Presentation is very dense (especially Sections 3–4) and difficult to follow; needs streamlining.  
- The claim that the rate sqrt(s·log(d/n)) is minimax-optimal lacks a clear proof or citation in the main text.  
- Eq. (3.8)’s tuning parameter ŝ depends on unknown sparsity s — request practical guidance on selecting ŝ.  
- Need practical procedure to obtain initialization β̂_init satisfying ||β̂_init − β*||_2 ≤ R/2.  
- Question the necessity of the truncation step in Algorithm 1 and ask for theoretical or empirical failure modes if omitted.  
- After rebuttal, main concerns were addressed and score increased from 6 to 7; paper is solid statistically but remains challenging for typical ML readers and its practical utility is somewhat uncertain.
