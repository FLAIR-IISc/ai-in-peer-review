- Contribution: Proposes a sparse-parameter variant of the EM algorithm for high-dimensional sparse settings.  
- Theory: Establishes conditions for global convergence and provides convergence rates.  
- Inference: Presents a statistical testing framework to assess estimator sparsity.  
- Presentation: Paper is well structured and covers related literature adequately.  
- Readability: Exposition is dense; Sections 3 and 4 are hard to follow and need simplification.  
- Missing proof: Claim that sqrt(s*log(d/n)) is minimax-optimal lacks a clear proof in the main manuscript.  
- Practical tuning: Eq. (3.8) uses ^s which depends on unknown s — guidance on choosing ^s in practice is missing.  
- Initialization: No practical method given to obtain an initial beta^init satisfying ||beta^init - beta*||_2 < R/2.  
- Algorithmic component: Unclear why the truncation step in Algorithm 1 is essential; effect of omitting it is not discussed.  
- Rebuttal/outcome: Authors’ response addressed concerns; reviewer raised score from 6 to 7.  
- Overall assessment: Solid statistical contribution but hard to read for ML researchers and practical usefulness is somewhat questionable.
