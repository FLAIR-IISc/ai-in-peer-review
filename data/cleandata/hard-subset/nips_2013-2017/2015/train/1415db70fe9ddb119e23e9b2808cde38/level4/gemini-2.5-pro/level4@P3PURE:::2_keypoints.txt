- Proposes a modified EM for sparse, high-dimensional parameters with convergence conditions and rate, plus a statistical test for estimator sparsity.  
- Paper is well-organized with sufficient related work but very dense; Sections 3–4 are hard to follow and need simpler exposition.  
- Reviewer couldn't find a proof that the rate sqrt(s*log(d/n)) is minimax-optimal; requests a clear proof or direct reference.  
- Practical implementation concern: the tuning parameter ^s in (3.8) depends on unknown true sparsity s—needs guidance on how to choose ^s in practice.  
- Convergence requires an initial beta^init with ||beta^init - beta*||_2 < R/2; requests practical advice on obtaining such an initialization.  
- Unclear role of the truncation step in Algorithm 1; asks for theoretical or practical consequences if truncation is omitted.  
- Post-rebuttal: authors satisfactorily addressed questions, improving the score.  
- Overall: solid statistics contribution but very theoretical/dense and practical usefulness for real-world ML applications remains somewhat questionable.
