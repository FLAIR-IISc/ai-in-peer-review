- Proposes a modified EM algorithm for parameter estimation in high-dimensional, sparse settings and provides convergence-to-global-solution conditions plus convergence rate analysis and a statistical test for estimator sparsity.
- Paper is well-structured with a sufficient related-work overview.
- Presentation is dense; sections 3 and 4 are particularly hard to follow and need clearer, simpler exposition.
- Claim that sqrt(s·log(d/n)) is minimax-optimal (line 66) lacks a proof in the main manuscript.
- In Eq.(3.8), the estimator hat{s} depends on the unknown true sparsity s; guidance on selecting hat{s} in practice is missing.
- No practical guidance on choosing an initial beta^{init} that satisfies ||beta^{init} - beta^*||_2 < R/2 is provided.
- The role of the truncation step in Algorithm 1 and the effects of omitting it are not explained.
- After rebuttal the reviewer raised the score from 6 to 7, noting the paper is statistically rigorous but could be more accessible to ML researchers and its practical utility is still uncertain.
