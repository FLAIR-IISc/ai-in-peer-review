- Proposes a modified EM algorithm for high-dimensional sparse parameters with convergence conditions and rate of convergence, plus a test for estimator sparsity.
- Paper is well-organized with adequate related work, but the technical exposition is very dense and Sections 3–4 are hard to follow.
- Claim that the rate sqrt(s*log(d/n)) is minimax-optimal lacks a proof or clear citation in the main text.
- Practical implementation issue: Eq. (3.8) uses ŝ that depends on true sparsity s, which is unknown—need guidance on choosing ŝ.
- Practical implementation issue: convergence requires an initial β_init with ||β_init − β*||_2 < R/2—authors should advise how to obtain such initialization in practice.
- The role and necessity of the truncation step in Algorithm 1 are not clearly explained; consequences of omitting it should be discussed.
- After rebuttal, the reviewer’s concerns were satisfactorily addressed and score increased from 6 to 7.
- Overall assessment: a solid statistics paper but very dense for general ML readers, and its practical usefulness for real-world applications is somewhat questionable.
