- Overall: Study of high-dimensional EM with convergence and inference is important and promising, but several clarifications are required.

- Initialization: Explain how to construct and verify an initial beta_init that satisfies the paper’s required condition in practice.

- Algorithm choice: Provide practical guidance and trade-offs (computational cost, numerical stability, statistical performance) to choose between the two proposed algorithms.

- Tuning parameters: Describe how to choose s_hat and lambda in practice, their sensitivity, and provide data-dependent selection methods and theoretical/empirical sensitivity analyses.

- Decorrelated score test: Explicitly cite Ning and Liu and clarify whether applying their decorrelated score in the latent-variable/EM context requires new ingredients or is a direct application.

- Assumptions: State whether the technical assumptions hold for the considered latent-variable models, specify the regimes where they apply, and highlight any hard-to-verify or restrictive conditions.

- Novelty and contribution: More clearly articulate the genuine novel contributions and nontrivial obstacles overcome versus straightforward extensions of prior work, and move core technical ideas from the supplement into the main text.

- Citation consistency: Ensure Ning and Liu’s work is consistently and explicitly cited wherever the decorrelated score is used.
