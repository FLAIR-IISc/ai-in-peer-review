The paper proposes a novel high dimensional EM algorithm that incorporates sparsity structure into parameter estimation for high dimensional latent variable models. The authors also develop a new inferential procedure for testing hypotheses related to low dimensional components of the high dimensional parameter. The framework is general and can be applied to various latent variable models.

The main contributions of the paper are two-fold. Firstly, the authors propose a high dimensional EM algorithm that converges at a geometric rate and achieves a (near-)optimal statistical rate of convergence. Secondly, they develop a decorrelated score test for testing hypotheses related to low dimensional components of the high dimensional parameter. The test is shown to be asymptotically normal and optimal in the sense that it attains the semiparametric information bound.

The paper is well-written, and the authors provide a clear and concise introduction to the problem and their contributions. The methodology section is also well-organized, and the authors provide a detailed description of their algorithm and inferential procedure.
