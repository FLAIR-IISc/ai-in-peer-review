**Summary of Main Ideas**

This paper presents a method for training recurrent neural networks to act as near-optimal feedback controllers for a range of dynamical systems and tasks, such as swimming, flying, biped and quadruped walking with different body morphologies. The approach combines supervised learning with trajectory optimization, specifically Contact-Invariant Optimization (CIO), to generate stable and realistic behaviors. The neural network learns both optimal controls and a model of dynamics simultaneously, without requiring motion capture or task-specific features or state machines. The method includes interleaving supervised learning with trajectory optimization, injecting noise during training, training for unexpected changes in the task specification, and using the trajectory optimizer to obtain optimal feedback gains in addition to optimal actions.

**Strengths**

1. **Novel combination of techniques**: The paper combines supervised learning with trajectory optimization, which is a novel approach to generating neural network controllers.
2. **Scalability**: The method is able to scale to larger and harder control problems, including three-dimensional characters and tasks.
3. **Robustness**: The approach includes noise injection during training, which helps to prevent overfitting and improves the robustness of the controller.
4. **Real-time interactive control**: The method allows for real-time interactive control of the character, which is a significant improvement over traditional methods.

**Weaknesses**

1. **Computational cost**: The method requires significant computational resources, including cloud computing and a GPU implementation, which may be a limitation for some applications.
2. **Limited evaluation**: The paper only evaluates the method on a limited set of tasks and characters, which may not be representative of all possible applications.
3. **Comparison to other methods**: The paper only compares the method to naive neural network training on a static optimal trajectory dataset, which may not be a fair comparison.

**Originality**

2. **Extension to three-dimensional characters**: The method is able to handle three-dimensional characters and tasks, which is a significant extension of previous work.
