- Embeds feedback gains inside neural network policies to improve robustness to disturbances and regression errors.
- Softly couples trajectory optimization and policy optimization via a deviation cost penalty so the policy learns simpler, more robust approximations of optimal trajectories than MPC alone.
- Uses sophisticated parallelization for task optimization, but this is an implementation detail rather than the core contribution.
- Trained feedback controllers successfully control a diverse set of robots.
- Paper is of the highest quality—exceptionally clear, a technical tour de force with striking empirical results.
- Beautiful integration of graphics, control, and neural networks enabling controller generation for varied body morphologies and tasks; deeply inspiring.
