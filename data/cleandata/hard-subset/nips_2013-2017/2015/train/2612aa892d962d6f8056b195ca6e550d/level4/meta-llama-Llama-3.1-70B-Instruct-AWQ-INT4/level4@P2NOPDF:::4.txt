This paper proposes a novel approach to interactive control of complex characters by combining trajectory optimization with policy learning. The core idea is to jointly learn trajectories and policies, with the additional insight that decoupling the two optimization problems into alternating subproblems has two key advantages: it allows for the reuse of existing, well-documented methods, and it is effective in practice. Although I am not an expert in this domain, the paper appears to demonstrate technical sophistication and impressive results.

The authors have successfully combined advanced concepts with a clear implementation, showcasing the benefits of using sensory noise during training and learning trajectories jointly with policies. The paper is well-written, with a suitable level of detail considering the complexity of the problem and its implementation. Each section focuses on a separate aspect of the problem, and the cross-references between sections are helpful.

One aspect that could be improved is the motivation for the extra optimization step in Section 7. Given the previous sections, I expected the policy to function without the need for additional optimization. I assume that the residual trajectory cost or policy regression error necessitates re-optimization at every time step to prevent error accumulation. However, I am not entirely clear on this point, and further clarification would be beneficial.

A minor suggestion is to provide one or two references to support the statement on line 153, which mentions recent approaches. Additionally, the idea of keeping updates small in parameter space seems counterintuitive, but I understand its utility in the context of block-alternating between subproblems.

There is a typo on line 89 ("to unify"). The paper's results appear impressive, and the proposed optimization scheme and implementation seem sensible. However, as this research area is not my primary focus, I am uncertain about the novelty and significance of the work.

Overall, the paper presents a well-structured and clearly written contribution to the field, with impressive technical results. While I am not in a position to fully assess its impact, the authors' approach and findings are certainly noteworthy.
