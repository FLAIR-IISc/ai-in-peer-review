- Proposes embedding feedback gains into neural network policies to improve robustness to disturbances and regression errors.
- Softly couples trajectory optimization and policy optimization via a cost penalty for deviation.
- Enables policies to learn simpler, more robust approximations of optimal trajectories than MPC-based trajectory optimization alone.
- Employs sophisticated parallelization to accelerate optimization, though this is a supporting implementation detail.
- Demonstrates successful application of the trained feedback controllers across a diverse set of robots.
- Paper exhibits exceptional clarity, technical mastery, and strong experimental results.
- Seamlessly integrates graphics, control, and neural networks in controller synthesis.
- Produces controllers applicable to a wide range of body morphologies and tasks, making the work an inspiring contribution.
