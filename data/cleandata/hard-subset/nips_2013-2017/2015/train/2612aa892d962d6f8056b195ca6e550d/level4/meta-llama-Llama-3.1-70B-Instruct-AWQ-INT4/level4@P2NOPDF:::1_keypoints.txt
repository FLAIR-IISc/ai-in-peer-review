- Proposes embedding feedback gains into neural-network policies to improve robustness to disturbances and regression errors.  
- Softly couples trajectory optimization and policy optimization via a cost penalty for deviation.  
- Policy learns simpler, more robust approximations of optimal trajectories than MPC-only trajectory optimization.  
- Emphasizes parallelized task optimization, though parallelization is secondary to the main contribution.  
- Trained feedback controllers successfully applied across a diverse set of robots.  
- Paper exhibits exceptional quality, clarity, and technical mastery.  
- Results are aesthetically pleasing and demonstrate strong practical performance.  
- Seamlessly integrates ideas from graphics, control, and neural networks to generate controllers for multiple body morphologies and tasks.  
- Overall, an inspiring and impactful contribution.
