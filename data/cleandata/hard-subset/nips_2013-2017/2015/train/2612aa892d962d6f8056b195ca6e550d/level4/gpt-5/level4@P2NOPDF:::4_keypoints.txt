- Proposes jointly learning trajectories and control policy by alternating trajectory optimization and policy learning.  
- Decoupling lets the method reuse established trajectory solvers and works well in practice.  
- Contribution is technically strong but lacks new theoretical insights.  
- Demonstrates high technical sophistication in concept and implementation, with strong empirical results.  
- Shows clear benefits of injecting sensory noise during training and of learning trajectories jointly with the policy.  
- Writing is clear, detailed appropriately, and sections are well scoped with helpful cross-references.

- Skepticism about the desirability of keeping updates small in parameter space; requests intuition or an ablation to justify this choice.

- Minor: clarify early whether the policy is intended for open-loop, closed-loop, or MPC-style execution and how Section 7 fits that taxonomy.
