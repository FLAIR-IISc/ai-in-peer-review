- Proposes embedding feedback gains within neural network policies to enhance robustness to disturbances and reduce regression error.
- Uses a soft coupling between trajectory and policy optimization via a deviation cost.
- Enables the policy to learn simplified, robust approximations of optimal trajectories.
- Policies often outperform trajectories produced by model predictive control (MPC) alone.
- Employs sophisticated parallelization primarily as an enabling technique, not a core contribution.
- Demonstrates efficacy across a diverse set of robotic systems.
- Paper is exceptionally well-written, technically sound, and presents compelling, inspiring results integrating graphics, control, and neural networks.
