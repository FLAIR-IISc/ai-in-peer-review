The author begins by proposing a technique to incorporate feedback into neural network policies, which strengthens them for error and regression. Trajectory optimization and policy optimization are gently linked by a cost penalty for deviation, which allows the policy to learn reasonable estimates of optimal trajectories that are actually easier and more robust than their own MPC-based trajectory optimization. Tasks are optimized using sophisticated paralysis, although this is not the central purpose of the paper and is rather a way to. Finally, various types of robots are controlled using trained feedback controllers.

This paper is of the highest quality, incredibly clear, presents an outstanding example of the technique, and presents quite beautiful results in its applications. Bravo. It is a beautiful work of graphics, control, neural networks combined in the pursuit of creating controllers for various physical structures and tasks. It is inspiring.
