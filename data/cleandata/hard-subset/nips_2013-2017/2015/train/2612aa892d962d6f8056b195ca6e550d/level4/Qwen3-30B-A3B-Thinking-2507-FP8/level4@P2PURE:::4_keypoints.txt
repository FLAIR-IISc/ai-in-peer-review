- Core contribution: jointly learning trajectories and policies, using alternating subproblems to reuse existing methods and achieve practical performance.  
- Paper is clearly written and technically sound.  
- Reviewer not expert in this domain and cannot assess potential impact or novelty confidently.

- Authors show high technical sophistication in concepts, implementation, and results.  
- Claimed advantages—(i) incorporating sensory noise during training and (ii) learning trajectories jointly with the policy—are clearly demonstrated.  
- Writing is effective, with appropriate detail, distinct sections, and useful cross-references.
