- Integrates feedback gains into neural network policies to improve robustness to disturbances and regression error.
- Softly couples trajectory optimization and policy optimization via a deviation penalty in the cost.
- Enables the policy to learn simpler, more robust approximations of optimal trajectories than MPC-based trajectory optimization alone.
- Uses sophisticated parallelization for task optimization (effective engineering detail, not the core contribution).
- Demonstrates trained feedback controllers on a diverse set of robots.
- Paper is exceptionally clear, high-quality, and a tour de force of technique with strong application results.
- Successfully marries graphics, control, and neural networks across varied body morphologies and tasks.
- Overall impression: compelling and inspiring work.
