This paper presents an interesting approach, but several aspects of the submission require improvement. 

To enhance clarity, it would be beneficial to explicitly state from the outset that the method relies on full model knowledge, as expressed in Equation 6. This upfront declaration would provide readers with a clearer understanding of the proposed approach.

Upon reviewing the manuscript, it becomes apparent that Sections 4 and 6 are disproportionate in terms of their content and contribution to the overall narrative. Specifically, Section 4 fails to mention the recurrent units, which were highlighted in the abstract. This omission may lead to confusion among readers, as the abstract sets certain expectations that are not met in the subsequent sections.

Furthermore, the description of the stochastic neural network on line 405 raises questions. The training data is indeed stochastic, but the network itself appears to be deterministic. This discrepancy warrants further clarification to ensure that readers grasp the underlying concepts accurately.

In response to the rebuttal, which provided additional information, this reviewer concurs that the paper demonstrates promising results. However, to elevate the manuscript to its full potential, a thorough restructuring is recommended to address the aforementioned concerns and improve the overall flow of the narrative.
