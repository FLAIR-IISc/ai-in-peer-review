- Novel strategy embeds feedback gains in neural network policies to improve robustness to disturbances and regression errors.
- Softly couples trajectory optimization and policy optimization via a cost penalty for deviation.
- Learned policies yield simpler and often more robust approximations of optimal trajectories than MPC-based trajectory optimization.
- Uses sophisticated parallelization techniques for task optimization (secondary contribution).
- Method successfully applied to control a diverse set of robots using the trained feedback controllers.
- Paper demonstrates exceptional clarity, technical mastery, and visually impressive results.
- Seamless integration of graphics, control, and neural networks across varied body morphologies and tasks.
- Inspiring work with strong potential to advance the fields of robotics and control.
