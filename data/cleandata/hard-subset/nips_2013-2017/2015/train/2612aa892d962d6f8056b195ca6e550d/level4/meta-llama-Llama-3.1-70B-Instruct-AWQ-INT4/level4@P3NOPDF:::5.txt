This paper presents a novel approach to neural networks, but it requires some clarification and restructuring to effectively convey its contributions. 

One potential improvement is to clearly state the assumptions and requirements of the proposed method from the outset. Specifically, it would be beneficial to mention early on that full model knowledge (as presented in Equation 6) is necessary for the approach to be applicable. This would help set reader expectations and provide context for the subsequent sections.

Upon reviewing the paper, it becomes apparent that Sections 4 and 6 are disproportionate in terms of their content and depth. Section 4, in particular, seems to lack a detailed discussion of the recurrent units mentioned in the abstract. This omission may leave readers wondering about the relevance and significance of these units in the context of the proposed method.

Furthermore, the description of the neural network as "stochastic" in line 405 appears to be misleading. While the training data may be stochastic, the network itself appears to be deterministic. This inconsistency in terminology may cause confusion and warrants clarification.
