- Proposes a novel method to integrate feedback gains into neural-network policies to improve resilience to disturbances and regression errors.  
- Introduces a cost penalty for deviation that softly couples trajectory optimization with policy optimization.  
- Enables policies to learn simplified yet robust approximations of optimal trajectories.  
- Learned policies outperform trajectories generated by MPC-based trajectory optimization alone.  
- Uses advanced parallelization to accelerate optimization, though this is secondary to the main contribution.  
- Trained feedback controllers are successfully applied across a wide range of robotic systems and body morphologies.  
- Manuscript exhibits high quality, clarity, and technical sophistication.  
- Work expertly blends graphics, control theory, and neural networks and delivers impressive results.  
- Presentation is outstanding and the contribution is inspiring for the field.
