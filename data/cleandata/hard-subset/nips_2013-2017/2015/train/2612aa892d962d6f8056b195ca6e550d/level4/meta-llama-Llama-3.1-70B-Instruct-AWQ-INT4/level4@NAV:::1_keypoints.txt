- Proposes a novel method to incorporate feedback gains into neural network policies, improving resilience to disturbances and regression errors.  
- Softly couples trajectory optimization and policy optimization via a cost penalty for deviation, letting the policy approximate optimal trajectories more simply and robustly than MPC-style methods.  
- Uses sophisticated parallelization for task optimization, though this is secondary to the main contribution.  
- Demonstrates successful application to control a diverse set of robots with trained feedback controllers.  
- Paper is clear, technically sophisticated, and produces impressive results.  
- Effective integration of graphics, control, and neural networks to generate controllers for varied body morphologies and tasks.  
- Overall quality is outstanding; the work is compelling and inspiring for the field.
