- Embeds feedback gains directly in neural-network policies to achieve robust control under disturbances and regression error.
- Softly couples trajectory optimization and policy optimization with a cost penalty for deviation, producing simpler and more robust approximations than MPC.
- Optimization uses sophisticated parallelization, presented as an implementation detail rather than the main contribution.
