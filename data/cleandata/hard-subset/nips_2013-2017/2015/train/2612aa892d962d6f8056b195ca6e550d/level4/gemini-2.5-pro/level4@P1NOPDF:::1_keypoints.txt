- Proposes embedding feedback gains within neural network policies to improve robustness to disturbances and regression errors.  
- Core method implements a soft coupling between trajectory optimization and policy optimization via a cost penalty for deviation.  
- Enables policies to learn simpler, often more robust approximations of optimal trajectories compared to MPC-only trajectory outputs.  
- Uses a sophisticated parallelization framework for the optimization, presented as a practical implementation detail rather than a main contribution.  
- Demonstrates the approach by successfully controlling a diverse set of robots with the learned feedback controllers.  
- Reviewer judges the paper as top-quality: technically impressive, very clear writing, and excellent application results that effectively combine graphics, control, and neural networks.
