- Incorporates feedback gains directly into neural network policies to improve robustness to disturbances and regression errors.
- Employs a soft coupling between trajectory and policy optimization via a cost penalty for deviation, letting the policy learn effective approximations of optimal trajectories.
- Learned trajectories are simpler and more robust than those from MPC-based trajectory optimization alone.
- Optimization uses sophisticated parallelization, presented as a practical necessity rather than a core technical contribution.
- Resulting trained feedback controllers successfully command a diverse range of robots across morphologies and tasks.
- Manuscript is exceptionally clear and demonstrates masterful command of complex techniques.
- Presents impressive application results and an elegant synthesis of computer graphics, control theory, and neural networks.
- Overall assessment: inspiring, high-caliber research.
