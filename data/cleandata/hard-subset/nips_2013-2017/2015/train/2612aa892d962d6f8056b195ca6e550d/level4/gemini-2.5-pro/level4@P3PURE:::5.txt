This paper shows promising results, but its presentation and structure require significant revision.

The manuscript would be improved by stating from the outset that the method requires full knowledge of the model, as specified in Equation 6. Clarifying this key requirement earlier would help frame the paper's contributions more effectively.

The paper's organization needs to be re-evaluated. There is a noticeable disproportion between Section 4 and Section 6. Furthermore, the abstract highlights the use of recurrent units, but these are surprisingly absent from the discussion in Section 4. This omission disconnects the introduction of the model from its core components.

A clarification is requested regarding the statement on line 405. The authors describe the model as a stochastic neural network; however, the network architecture itself appears deterministic, with stochasticity arising from the training data. Please clarify the basis for this characterization.

In summary, while the results are encouraging, the paper should be substantially restructured to improve its clarity and logical flow.
