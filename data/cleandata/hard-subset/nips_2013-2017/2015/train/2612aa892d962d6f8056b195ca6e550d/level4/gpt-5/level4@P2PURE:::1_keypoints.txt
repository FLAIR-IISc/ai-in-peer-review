- Introduces embedding feedback gains into neural network policies to improve robustness to disturbances and regression errors.
- Softly couples trajectory optimization and policy optimization via a deviation penalty in the cost.
- Enables policies to learn simpler, more robust approximations of optimal trajectories than MPC-based trajectory optimization alone.
- Uses sophisticated parallelization for task optimization (implementation aid, not the main contribution).
- Demonstrates trained feedback controllers on a diverse set of robots with strong application results.
- Overall assessment: outstanding—exceptionally clear, technically comprehensive, and inspiring integration of graphics, control, and neural networks.
