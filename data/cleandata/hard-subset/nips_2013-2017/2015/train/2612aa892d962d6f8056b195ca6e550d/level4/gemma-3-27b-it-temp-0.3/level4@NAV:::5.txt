This paper presents promising results but would benefit from restructuring for clarity and completeness. Specifically, it would be helpful to explicitly state the requirement for full model knowledge (as detailed in Eq. 6) earlier in the presentation. The current balance between Sections 4 and 6 feels disproportionate, and Section 4 omits discussion of the recurrent units mentioned in the abstract. Furthermore, line 405’s claim of a “stochastic NN” requires clarification; while the training data is stochastic, the network architecture itself appears deterministic.
