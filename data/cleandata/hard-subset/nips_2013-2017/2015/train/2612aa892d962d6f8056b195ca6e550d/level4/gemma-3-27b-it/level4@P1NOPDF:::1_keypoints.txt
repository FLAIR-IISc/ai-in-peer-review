- Proposes embedding feedback gains inside neural network policies to achieve robustness to disturbances and regression errors.
- Softly couples trajectory optimization and policy optimization using a cost penalty for deviation.
- Policy learns simpler, more robust approximations of optimal trajectories than MPC-based trajectory optimization alone.
- Demonstrates that these learned feedback controllers successfully control a diverse set of robots.
- Uses sophisticated parallelization to optimize tasks, presented as an implementation detail rather than a main contribution.
- Paper quality is exceptionally high and exceptionally clear.
- Represents a significant technical achievement with compelling application results.
- Effectively merges graphics, control, and neural networks across a wide range of body morphologies and tasks.
- Overall described as beautiful, inspiring work.
