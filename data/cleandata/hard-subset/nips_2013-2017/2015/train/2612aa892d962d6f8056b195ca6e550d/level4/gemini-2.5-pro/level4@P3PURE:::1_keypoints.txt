- Embeds feedback gains within neural-network policies to improve robustness to disturbances and regression error.  
- Soft coupling of trajectory optimization and policy optimization via a penalty on deviation.  
- Policy learns effective approximations of optimal trajectories through the soft coupling.  
- Learned trajectories are often simpler and more robust than those from MPC-only trajectory optimization.  
- Heavy parallelization is used for optimization but presented as an implementation detail, not the main contribution.  
- Resulting feedback controllers successfully control a diverse set of robots.  
- Paper is exceptionally clear, technically impressive, and presents compelling, well-rendered results.  
- Work is an inspiring synthesis of graphics, control, and neural networks across varied body morphologies and tasks.
