- Proposes embedding feedback gains into neural network policies to make them robust to disturbances and regression errors.  
- Softly couples trajectory optimization and policy optimization via a cost penalty on deviation so the policy learns approximations of optimal trajectories.  
- Learned policy approximations are simpler and more robust than trajectories produced by MPC-based trajectory optimization alone.  
- Uses sophisticated parallelization techniques for task optimization (ancillary to the main contribution).  
- Successfully applies trained feedback controllers to a diverse set of robots.  
- Paper exhibits exceptional clarity, strong technical mastery, and impressive empirical results.  
- Elegantly integrates ideas from graphics, control, and neural networks to handle varied body morphologies and tasks.
