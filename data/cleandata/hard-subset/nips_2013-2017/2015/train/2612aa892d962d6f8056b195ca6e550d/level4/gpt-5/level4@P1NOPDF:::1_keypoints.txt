- Proposes embedding feedback gains in neural-network policies, improving robustness to disturbances and regression errors.  
- Softly couples trajectory optimization and policy optimization via a cost penalty for deviations, letting policies learn simpler, more robust approximations than MPC alone.  
- Uses sophisticated parallelization to optimize tasks efficiently (an effective engineering contribution though not central).  
- Demonstrates the method on a diverse set of robots controlled by the trained feedback controllers.  
- Overall assessment: exceptionally clear, high-quality, tour de force marrying graphics, control, and neural networks with compelling, inspiring results.
