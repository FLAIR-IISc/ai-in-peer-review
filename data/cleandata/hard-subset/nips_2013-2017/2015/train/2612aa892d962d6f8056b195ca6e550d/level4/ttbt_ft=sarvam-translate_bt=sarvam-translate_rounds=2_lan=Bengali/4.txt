This paper proposes combining trajectory optimization with policy learning to enable complex character interactions. The main proposal is to learn both the trajectory and the policy simultaneously, along with additional empirical insights that, if the two optimization problems are divided into sequential sub-problems, i) existing, well-documented methods can be reused, and ii) it works reasonably well in practice. Although I understand the paper well, I am not skilled enough to comment on the potential impact of this paper, as it appears to be technically sophisticated on the surface, despite lacking new theoretical insights.
