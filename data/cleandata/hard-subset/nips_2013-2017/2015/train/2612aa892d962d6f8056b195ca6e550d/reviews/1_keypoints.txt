- Proposes embedding feedback gains into neural network policies to improve robustness to disturbances and regression error.  
- Softly couples trajectory optimization and policy optimization via a cost penalty for deviation.  
- Policy learns simpler, more robust approximations of optimal trajectories than MPC trajectories alone.  
- Uses sophisticated parallelization to optimize tasks, primarily as an implementation detail.  
- Demonstrates trained feedback controllers on a diverse set of robots.  
- Paper is exceptionally clear, high-quality, and technically impressive.  
- Successfully integrates graphics, control, and neural networks across varied morphologies and tasks.  
- Results are inspiring and represent a significant methodological contribution.
