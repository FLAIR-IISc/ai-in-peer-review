- Proposes a novel time-sensitive recommendation framework combining low-rank user-item structure with a Hawkes process.  
- Provides a principled stochastic-process modeling of rating observations.  
- Presents a convex formulation and detailed technical exposition of the low-rank Hawkes process.  
- Experimental evaluation is thorough, using synthetic and real datasets, and shows superiority over existing methods.  
- Paper is well-written, clearly explained, and easy to follow.  
- The approach is original and innovative for time-sensitive recommendation.  
- Significance is high, with demonstrated effectiveness on real-world music streaming and shopping event logs.  
- Theoretical issues: Equation (8) is unclear about which parameter is maximized and ρ* may be unbounded.  
- Theorem 2’s practical relevance is weakened because its prescribed ρ is not used in experiments.
