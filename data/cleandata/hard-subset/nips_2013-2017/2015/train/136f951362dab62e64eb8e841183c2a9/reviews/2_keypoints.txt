- Proposes a point-process collaborative filtering model combining constant and self-exciting intensities via user/item latent vectors, with an optimization algorithm and empirical evaluation.  
- Experiments should include a sensible baseline: a simple ensemble of STiC [12] and SVD, given their complementary strengths.  
- Unclear how the γ function bandwidth parameter σ is set and no analysis of sensitivity to σ.  
- Missing details on selection/tuning of other key hyperparameters (e.g., latent dimension) for both the proposed method and baselines.  
- Needs more dataset characterization relevant to temporal modeling (e.g., number of recurring events per user/item pair, temporal irregularity).  
- Overall quality assessed as reasonably good but not great.  
- Clarity is lacking; paper contains language issues, typos, and grammar errors.  
- Originality: interesting and novel combination of point processes and latent factor models.  
- Significance: problem and method have practical value, but experiments and presentation require improvement.
