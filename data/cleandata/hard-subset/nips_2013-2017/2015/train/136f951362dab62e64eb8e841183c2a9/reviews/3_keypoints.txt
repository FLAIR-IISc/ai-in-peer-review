- Presents a novel, distributed algorithm for time-sensitive recommendation combining low-rank user-item structure with a Hawkes process, reporting better results than existing methods.  
- Uses a principled stochastic-process modeling of observations (ratings).  
- Theoretical concern: Eq. (8) is unclear about which parameter is maximized (is it over ρ?), and ρ* may be unbounded.  
- Theorem 2’s relevance is weakened because its prescribed ρ is not used in experiments.  
- Experimental baselines: should include SVD++ (a strong, time-aware baseline) to strengthen comparisons.  
- Clarity: paper is well written and easy to follow.  
- Originality: approach appears novel for the relatively new time-sensitive recommendation problem.  
- Significance: work is potentially impactful due to modeling choice, distributed solvability, and improved empirical performance.
