- Proposes a novel time-sensitive recommendation algorithm using low-rank user–item structure and the Hawkes process; distributed and outperforms existing methods.
- Theoretical issue: Eq. (8) is ambiguous about which parameter the maximum is taken over (presumably ρ) and whether ρ* can be unbounded.
- Theorem 2 concern: the theorem is not used to select ρ in experiments, reducing its practical relevance.
- Experimental baselines: should include SVD++ (Netflix Challenge winner) for a stronger comparison.
- Clarity: the paper is well-written and easy to follow.
- Originality: appears novel given the relative novelty of time-sensitive recommendation.
- Significance: the problem is important and the proposed approach could have substantial impact.
