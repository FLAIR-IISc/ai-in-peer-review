- Proposes a novel algorithm combining low-rank user–item matrix structure with Hawkes processes for time-sensitive recommendation.
- Algorithm is distributed and empirically improves over existing methods.
- Principled stochastic-process-based modeling of ratings.
- Theoretical issue: Eq. (8) is ambiguous about which parameter is being maximized (is it ρ?), and whether ρ* can be unbounded.
- Theorem 2’s practical relevance is unclear because it is not used to select ρ in experiments.
- Experimental baselines are incomplete: SVD++ (a strong, time-aware recommender) should be included for a more comprehensive comparison.
- Paper is well-written and easy to follow.
- The approach appears novel by combining low-rank structure with Hawkes processes for temporal recommendations.
- The problem is significant and the method’s ability to capture temporal dynamics and scale in a distributed way is valuable.
