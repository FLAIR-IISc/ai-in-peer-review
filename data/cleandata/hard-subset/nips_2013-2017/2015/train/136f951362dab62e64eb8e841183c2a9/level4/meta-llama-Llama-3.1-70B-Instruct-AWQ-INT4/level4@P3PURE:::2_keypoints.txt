- Proposes a point-process collaborative filtering model with event density = constant intensity + self-exciting intensity, using two sets of user/item latent vectors and an optimization algorithm; empirical results show advantages over common alternatives.

- Experimental comparisons should include an ensemble baseline of STiC [12] and SVD, since that ensemble could handle new-item recommendations the authors claim baselines cannot.

- The bandwidth parameter σ for the γ function in the self-exciting process is not specified; a sensitivity analysis over σ is needed.

- Paper lacks details on hyperparameter selection (e.g., latent vector dimensionality) for both the proposed method and baselines.

- Need more dataset characterization focused on temporal structure, such as counts of recurring events per user/item pair and measures of temporal irregularity.

- Presentation quality needs improvement: several typos and grammatical errors throughout the text.

- The method is an interesting and original combination of point processes and latent-factor models.

- The problem and method have good practical value, and the optimization algorithm and empirical advantages are meaningful.

- Reviewer ratings: Quality 6/10, Clarity 6/10, Originality 7/10, Significance 8/10.
