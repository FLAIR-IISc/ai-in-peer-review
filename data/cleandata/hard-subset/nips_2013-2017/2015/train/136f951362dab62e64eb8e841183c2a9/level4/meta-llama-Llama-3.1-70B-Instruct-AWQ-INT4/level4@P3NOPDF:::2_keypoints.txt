- Model: proposes a point-process collaborative filtering model where event density = constant intensity + self-exciting intensity from past events, using two sets of user/item latent vectors.  
- Methodology: derives an optimization algorithm for parameter estimation and reports empirical gains over common alternatives.  
- Missing baseline: should compare to a simple ensemble of STiC and SVD, given the authors’ claim about new-item recommendations.  
- Bandwidth σ: paper omits how the γ-function bandwidth σ is set and provides no sensitivity analysis to σ.  
- Hyperparameters: lacks details on selection of important hyperparameters (e.g., latent dimension) for both baselines and the proposed method.  
- Dataset details: insufficient description of the two real-world datasets; needs stats like recurring events per user/item pair and temporal irregularity.  
- Presentation: language quality suffers from typos and grammatical errors throughout the text.  
- Strengths: original and interesting combination of point processes with latent factor models; method has practical value.  
- Overall judgment: quality is reasonably good but not outstanding; experiments and presentation need improvement to boost significance.  
- Recommended fixes: add the ensemble baseline, report hyperparameter selection and sensitivity analyses, expand dataset characterization, and correct language errors.
