- Paper proposes a novel time-sensitive recommendation algorithm combining low-rank user–item structure with a Hawkes process; it is distributed and outperforms existing methods.
- Reviewer appreciates the principled stochastic-process modeling of observations (ratings).

- Paper is well-written and easy to follow.
- Contribution is novel given the emerging nature of time-sensitive recommendation.
- Work is significant due to potential impact, distributed solvability, and superior empirical results.
