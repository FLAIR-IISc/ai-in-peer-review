- Introduces a collaborative filtering model using point processes with baseline + self-exciting intensity from latent user/item vectors.  
- Provides an optimization algorithm and empirical results claiming improvement over common baselines.  
- Experimental evaluation should include a logical baseline: an ensemble of STiC and SVD.  
- Authors should analyze sensitivity to the bandwidth parameter σ used in the γ function.  
- Paper lacks details on hyperparameter selection (e.g., latent dimensionality) for the model and baselines, reducing reproducibility.  
- Needs more dataset statistics relevant to temporal structure (e.g., average recurring events per user/item, irregularity measures).  
- Overall quality is reasonably good but not excellent.  
- Presentation requires careful revision due to imprecise language, typos, and grammatical errors.  
- Method is original in combining point process modeling with latent-factor collaborative filtering.  
- Significance is high: modeling temporal and recurrent interactions is practically valuable; addressing experimental and presentation issues would enhance impact.
