- Proposal summary: introduces a point-process collaborative filtering model combining a constant intensity and a self-exciting intensity based on past events, with separate user/item latent-vector intensity sets.
- Method/algorithm: derives an optimization algorithm for parameter estimation and reports empirical improvements over common alternatives.
- Baseline omission: experiments should include a sensible ensemble baseline (STiC + SVD) to address the authors’ claim about recommending new items.
- Bandwidth detail missing: paper does not state how the γ bandwidth parameter σ is chosen for the self-exciting term.
- Sensitivity analysis missing: no analysis of how results vary with different σ values.
- Hyperparameter selection missing: lacks details on how important hyperparameters (e.g., latent dimension) were chosen for both proposed and baseline methods.
- Dataset description lacking: needs more dataset characteristics (e.g., counts of recurring events per user/item pair, temporal irregularity) given the temporal focus.
- Presentation quality: overall quality is reasonable but not outstanding, with typos and grammatical errors reducing clarity.
- Originality: method is an interesting and original combination of point processes and latent factor models.
- Significance: problem and method have good practical value, but experimental and presentation improvements are needed to realize potential.
