- Contribution: Proposes a novel time-sensitive recommendation algorithm combining low-rank user–item modeling with Hawkes processes, supporting distributed optimization and outperforming existing methods.  
- Quality (theory): Paper is principled but has theoretical ambiguities—Eq. (8) is unclear about which parameter is maximized (is it over ρ?) and whether ρ* can be unbounded.  
- Quality (theory): Theorem 2 is stated but apparently not used to choose ρ in experiments, reducing its practical relevance.  
- Experiments: Empirical evaluation shows improvement but baselines could be stronger; recommend including SVD++ (Netflix Prize winner) as an additional baseline.  
- Clarity: Paper is well written and easy to follow.  
- Originality: Work appears novel—time-sensitive recommendation is relatively new and the low-rank + Hawkes process combination is interesting.  
- Significance: Problem is impactful; proposed distributed algorithm and empirical gains make the approach promising.
