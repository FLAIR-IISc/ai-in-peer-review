- Proposes a novel time-aware recommendation method combining low-rank user-item modeling with a Hawkes process to capture temporal dynamics; algorithm is distributable and outperforms baselines.
- Praises the principled, stochastic process–based modeling approach.
- Theoretical concern: Equation (8) is ambiguous about which parameter is being maximized; if it is ρ, the possibility of an unbounded ρ* must be addressed.
- Practical concern: Theorem 2’s relevance is limited because it does not inform selection of ρ in experiments.
- Experimental suggestion: include SVD++ as a baseline, given its strong performance in time-sensitive settings like the Netflix challenge.
- Clarity: manuscript is clearly written and easy to follow.
- Originality: work is novel in its combination of techniques for time-sensitive recommendation.
- Significance: method has potential high impact by providing a high-performing, distributable solution for time-aware recommendation.
