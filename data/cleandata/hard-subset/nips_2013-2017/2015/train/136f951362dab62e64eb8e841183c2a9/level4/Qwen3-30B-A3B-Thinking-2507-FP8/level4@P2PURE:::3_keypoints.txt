- Introduces a novel algorithm combining low-rank user–item matrix modeling with Hawkes processes for time-sensitive recommendation; supports distributed computation and reports superior performance.

- Uses a principled stochastic process framework but has issues: Equation (8)’s maximization is unclear (which parameter, e.g., ρ?), and ρ* might be unbounded.

- Theorem 2’s role is unclear because it isn’t used in experimental parameter selection, reducing its practical significance.

- Empirical evaluation omits SVD++ (a leading, time-sensitive baseline and Netflix Prize method); including it would substantially strengthen comparisons.

- Paper is well-written and clearly structured.

- The approach is original within the underexplored area of time-sensitive recommendation.

- The problem is significant and the paper offers a meaningful contribution toward solving it.
