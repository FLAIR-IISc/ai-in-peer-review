- Proposes a novel time-sensitive recommendation algorithm leveraging low-rank user-item structure and the Hawkes process; distributed solution and empirical outperformance of existing methods.
- Reviewer appreciates the principled stochastic-process modeling of observations but raises concerns and suggestions.
- Theoretical issue: Eq. (8) is ambiguous about which parameter is maximized (is it over ρ?), whether ρ* can be unbounded, and Theorem 2 is not used in selecting ρ in experiments, reducing its relevance.
- Experimental suggestion: include SVD++ (Netflix challenge winner) as a baseline to strengthen comparisons, though current baselines are generally acceptable.
- Clarity: paper is well-written and easy to follow.
- Originality: approach appears novel given the relative novelty of time-sensitive recommendation.
- Significance: problem is impactful and the proposed distributed Hawkes + low-rank method shows promising empirical gains.
