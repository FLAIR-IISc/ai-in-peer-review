- Introduces a point-process collaborative filtering model with event intensity = base intensity + self-exciting intensity.  
- Both intensity components are parameterized by separate latent vectors for users and items.  
- Derives an optimization algorithm for parameter estimation.  
- Provides empirical studies showing advantages over common alternatives.  
- Experimental evaluation is weak: missing a simple but informative baseline ensemble (STiC + SVD).  
- Lacks details and sensitivity analysis for the γ bandwidth parameter σ used in the self-exciting process.  
- No description of how other hyperparameters (e.g., latent dimension) were chosen for the proposed model or baselines.  
- Datasets need richer characterization (e.g., recurring events per user-item pair, measures of temporal irregularity).  
- Overall quality is reasonable but would improve if experimental weaknesses are addressed.  
- Presentation needs careful proofreading to fix grammatical errors and typos.  
- Method is original in combining point process theory with latent-factor collaborative filtering.  
- Problem is significant and well-motivated, but impact depends on stronger experiments and clearer presentation.
