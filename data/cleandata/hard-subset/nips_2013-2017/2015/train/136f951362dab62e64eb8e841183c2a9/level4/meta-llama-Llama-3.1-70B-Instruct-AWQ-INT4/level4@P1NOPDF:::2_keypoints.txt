- Proposes a point-process collaborative filtering model combining a constant and a self-exciting intensity per user-item, using two sets of latent vectors and a derived optimization algorithm.  
- Empirical studies claim advantages over common alternatives.  
- Experiments should include a baseline ensemble of STiC and SVD, since that ensemble can address recommending new items.  
- Need details on the γ function for self-excitation, specifically how the bandwidth σ is set and sensitivity to σ.  
- Lack of information on how important hyperparameters (e.g., latent dimension) are chosen for both proposed method and baselines.  
- Provide dataset statistics relevant to temporal structure (e.g., number of recurring events per user-item, temporal irregularity).  
- Overall quality is reasonably good but not outstanding.  
- Clarity is lacking due to language, typos, and grammar errors; writing should be improved.  
- Method is original, combining point processes with latent factor models.  
- Problem and method have practical significance, but experimental and presentation improvements are needed to increase impact.
