- Proposes a novel point-process collaborative filtering model combining constant and self-exciting intensities with two sets of user/item latent vectors and an optimization algorithm; empirical results claim advantages over common alternatives.
- Missing a sensible baseline: should compare to an ensemble of STiC [12] and SVD since that can handle new-item recommendations.
- Lacks explanation and sensitivity analysis for the bandwidth parameter σ used in the γ function of the self-exciting process.
- Insufficient reporting of important hyperparameter choices (e.g., latent vector dimensionality) for both the proposed method and baselines.
- Needs more dataset characterization relevant to temporality (e.g., number of recurring events per user-item pair, temporal irregularity).
- Overall quality is reasonable but not outstanding; experimental studies and presentation need improvement.
- Paper clarity suffers from language, typos, and grammar errors; requires careful proofreading.
- Originality is strong: interesting and significant combination of point processes and latent factor models.
- Significance is high in practical value for recommendation systems, but impact is limited by weaker experiments and presentation.
