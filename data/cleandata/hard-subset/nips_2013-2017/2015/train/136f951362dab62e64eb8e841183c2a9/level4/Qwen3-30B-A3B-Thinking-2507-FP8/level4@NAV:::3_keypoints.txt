- Proposes a novel algorithm for time-sensitive recommendation using low-rank user-item structure and Hawkes processes.
- Method is amenable to distributed computation and empirically outperforms existing methods.
- Offers a theoretically sound stochastic-process-based model for ratings.
- Clarification needed: in Equation (8) and Theorem 2, the maximization parameter is unclear (is it over ρ?), could ρ* be unbounded, and Theorem 2 is not applied in experiments.
- Experimental baselines omit SVD++, a top-performing time-sensitive method (Netflix Challenge winner) that should be included.
- Paper is well-written and logically structured.
- The problem is novel and the methodology is a meaningful original contribution.
- The work is significant for advancing time-sensitive recommendation.
