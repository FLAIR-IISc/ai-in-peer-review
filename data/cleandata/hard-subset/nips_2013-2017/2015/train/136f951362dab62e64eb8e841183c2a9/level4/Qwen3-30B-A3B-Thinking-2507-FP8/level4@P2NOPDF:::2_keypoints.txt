- Introduces a point-process collaborative filtering model with constant + self-exciting intensities using latent user and item vectors; claims superior empirical performance.  
- Fails to include a critical baseline: an STiC+SVD ensemble that would handle new items and challenge the novelty claim.  
- Choice of the bandwidth parameter σ in the self-excitation γ function is unexplained and needs sensitivity analysis.  
- Hyperparameter selection (notably latent dimension) for both the proposed method and baselines is inadequately reported.  
- Omits key temporal dataset statistics: recurrence frequency per user-item, inter-event time variance/temporal irregularity, and event density patterns.  
- Technical approach is sound, but the empirical evaluation is incomplete and undermines the strength of the claims.  
- Writing contains grammatical errors, awkward phrasing, and repetitive method descriptions; requires careful editing and concision.  
- Originality: integration of point processes with latent factors is novel in this form but relates to prior Hawkes-based CF work.  
- Significance: addresses an important problem and is promising for recurrent temporal interactions, but impact is limited by weak experimental validation.  
- Recommended fixes: add STiC+SVD baseline, run σ sensitivity analyses and justify hyperparameters, report temporal dataset stats, and improve clarity and presentation.
