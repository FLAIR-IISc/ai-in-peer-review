- Proposal: Combines low-rank user–item modeling with Hawkes processes for time-sensitive recommendation; supports distributed implementation and shows improved performance.  
- Strength: Sound modeling—Hawkes processes capture intensity/excitation, integrated with low-rank collaborative filtering.  
- Strength: Practical—algorithm amenable to distributed optimization for large-scale tasks.  
- Strength: Empirical—outperforms several baselines on relevant datasets.  
- Theoretical clarity: Eq. (8) is unclear about which parameter is maximized—need explicit statement if maximization is over ρ and its feasible domain.  
- Boundedness concern: If ρ is optimized, clarify constraints/regularization/assumptions that prevent ρ* from becoming unbounded.  
- Theorem 2 relevance: Practical role of Theorem 2 is unclear since experiments do not appear to use it for choosing ρ; clarify its guidance for hyperparameter selection or its theoretical value.  
- Baselines: Omission of SVD++/timeSVD++ is a weakness—adding them would strengthen evaluation; if infeasible, justify or compare to published results.  
- Clarity: Paper is well written overall; main clarity gap is the definition/role of ρ in Eq. (8) and connection to Theorem 2.  
- Originality: Novel integration of Hawkes processes with low-rank matrix modeling for time-sensitive recommendation.  
- Significance: Addresses an impactful, timely problem with scalable, principled methodology likely to influence follow-up work.  
- Suggestion (theory–practice): Explicitly state optimization domain for ρ, provide conditions/short proof or citation ensuring boundedness.  
- Suggestion (theory–practice): Explain practical use of Theorem 2, align experiments with it, or add ablation/sensitivity studies comparing theorem-guided vs cross-validated ρ.  
- Suggestion (baselines): Add SVD++/timeSVD++ as baselines or provide detailed rationale and at least one comparative result.  
- Suggestion (empirics): Report sensitivity to Hawkes parameters and regularization; provide distributed solver details (communication cost, convergence, scaling).  
- Overall: Solid, well-motivated contribution with clear empirical value; main weaknesses are theoretical clarity around Eq. (8)/Theorem 2 and absence of a top time-aware baseline.
