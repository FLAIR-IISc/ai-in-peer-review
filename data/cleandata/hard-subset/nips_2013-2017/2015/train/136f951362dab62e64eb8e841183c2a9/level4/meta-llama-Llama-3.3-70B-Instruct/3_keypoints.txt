- Proposes a novel distributed method combining low-rank user-item matrices with Hawkes processes for time-sensitive recommendations that outperforms existing methods.
- Methodology is rigorous in modeling observations via stochastic processes but needs clarification on several points.
- Major theoretical concern: Equation (8) is ambiguous about which parameter is maximized; if over ρ, ρ* may be unbounded.
- Theorem 2 is not used to guide experimental selection of ρ, reducing its practical relevance.
- Experimental baselines are adequate but should include SVD++ (Netflix Prize winner) for a stronger comparison.
- Manuscript is well-structured and easy to understand.
- The work appears novel in the emerging area of time-sensitive recommendation.
- Contribution is significant: scalable, distributed algorithm capturing low-rank structure with Hawkes processes and yielding superior results.
