- Proposes a point process-based collaborative filtering model combining constant and self-exciting intensities for each user-item pair.  
- Computes two distinct intensity functions using separate user and item latent vectors.  
- Derives an optimization algorithm for parameter estimation and provides empirical validation showing advantages over common alternatives.  
- Experimental evaluation is weak: should include a simple ensemble baseline of STiC [12] plus SVD, since STiC and SVD address complementary shortcomings.  
- Paper fails to specify how the bandwidth σ for the γ function is chosen and lacks sensitivity analysis of results to σ.  
- Missing details on hyperparameter selection (e.g., latent vector dimensionality) for the proposed method and baselines.  
- Datasets are insufficiently characterized; need statistics on recurrence frequency per user-item pair and temporal irregularity given the temporal focus.  
- Quality is acceptable but not strong.  
- Presentation contains language issues, typos, and grammatical errors requiring revision.  
- Method shows originality as a fusion of point processes and latent factor models.  
- Problem and approach have practical significance.
