- Presents a novel point-process collaborative filtering model combining constant and self-exciting intensities to capture temporal and recurrent user–item interactions.  
- Uses two sets of user and item latent vectors to compute separate intensities and derives an optimization algorithm for parameter estimation.  
- Empirical results show the proposed method outperforms commonly used alternatives.  
- Recommend adding more baselines (e.g., a simple ensemble of STiC and SVD) for a more comprehensive comparison.  
- Authors should clarify how the bandwidth parameter σ in the γ function was chosen and report sensitivity of results to σ variations.  
- Need more details on hyperparameter selection for both baselines and the proposed method, including latent vector dimensionality.  
- Request more dataset characterization (e.g., number of recurring events per user/item pair and temporal irregularity) to contextualize performance.  
- Overall paper quality is satisfactory but presentation needs language refinement, typo corrections, and grammar fixes.  
- Method is original and practically significant, representing a valuable contribution.
