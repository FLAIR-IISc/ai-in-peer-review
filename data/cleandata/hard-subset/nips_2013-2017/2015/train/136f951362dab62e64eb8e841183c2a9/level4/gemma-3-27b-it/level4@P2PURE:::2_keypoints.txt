- Paper proposes a point-process collaborative filtering model combining a constant intensity and a self-exciting intensity for each user-item pair.  
- Intensities are computed via two sets of latent vectors for users and items.  
- The authors derive an optimization algorithm for parameter estimation.  
- Empirical results claim advantages over existing methods.  
- Experimental evaluation is weak and could be strengthened with additional baselines.  
- Recommend comparing to an ensemble of STiC [12] and SVD, since STiC/related methods struggle with new items that SVD can handle.  
- The paper does not describe how the gamma kernel bandwidth σ is set; sensitivity analysis to σ is needed.  
- Hyperparameter selection details (e.g., latent dimensionality) for the proposed method and baselines are missing.  
- Paper should provide more dataset characterization (e.g., counts of recurring events per user/item, temporal irregularity statistics).  
- Overall quality is reasonable but could be improved.  
- Presentation suffers from language issues, typos, and grammatical errors.  
- The approach is original (combination of point-process and latent-factor models) and practically significant, but experiments and presentation need improvement.
