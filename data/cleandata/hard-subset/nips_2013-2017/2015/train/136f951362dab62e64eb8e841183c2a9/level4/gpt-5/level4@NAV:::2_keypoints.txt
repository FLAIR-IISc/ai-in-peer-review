- Proposes a point-process collaborative filtering model combining a constant base intensity and a self-exciting component with separate user/item latent vectors, and derives an optimization algorithm with empirical gains over baselines.  
- Missing baseline: should compare to an ensemble of STiC and SVD to test claims about recommending new items.  
- Unclear how the gamma kernel bandwidth sigma is chosen and how sensitive results are to this choice.  
- No details on hyperparameter selection (e.g., latent dimensionality) for the proposed method or the baselines.  
- Datasets lack temporal-specific statistics (e.g., number of repeat events per user–item pair, irregularity of temporal patterns).  
- Quality assessment: solid work but not outstanding.  
- Clarity issues: presentation needs polishing; contains wording problems, typos, and grammatical errors.  
- Originality: attractive synthesis of point processes and latent factor models.  
- Significance: practically relevant approach with reported empirical improvements, but experiments and presentation require strengthening.
