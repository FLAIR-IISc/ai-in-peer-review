- Proposed model: point-process collaborative filtering combining constant and self-exciting intensities computed from user/item latent vectors, with an optimization algorithm and empirical advantages reported.
- Experimental weakness: empirical studies could be strengthened with stronger baselines.
- Suggested baseline: compare to a simple ensemble of STiC [12] and SVD to handle recommending new items.
- Justification gap: authors’ claim that [12,13] can’t recommend new items should be tested against the STiC+SVD ensemble.
- Kernel detail missing: Gaussian kernel bandwidth σ for the self-exciting term is not specified and needs sensitivity analysis.
- Hyperparameter reporting lacking: selection details for important hyperparameters (e.g., latent dimensionality) for the proposed method and baselines are absent.
- Dataset description insufficient: provide dataset characteristics and statistics (e.g., number of recurring events per user/item pair, temporal irregularity).
- Overall quality: reasonably good but could be improved.
- Clarity issues: presentation needs polishing for language, typos, and grammar.
- Originality: interesting and novel combination of point processes with latent factor models.
- Significance: problem and method have practical value, but experiments and presentation require refinement.
