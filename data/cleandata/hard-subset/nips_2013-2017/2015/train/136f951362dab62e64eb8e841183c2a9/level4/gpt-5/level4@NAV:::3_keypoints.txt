- Proposes a time-sensitive recommendation algorithm using low-rank user–item structure and Hawkes process dynamics, solvable in a distributed manner and outperforming existing methods.
- Quality: Principled stochastic-process modeling of ratings.
- Concern: Eq. (8) — unclear over which parameter the maximization is taken (e.g., rho) and whether the optimal rho could be unbounded.
- Concern: Theorem 2’s practical relevance is limited since it does not appear to be used to select rho in experiments.
- Experimental suggestion: Include SVD++ (a strong time-sensitive baseline) to strengthen evaluation.
- Clarity: Paper is well written and easy to follow.
- Originality: Work appears novel in the time-sensitive recommendation area.
- Significance: Method is impactful due to capturing temporal dynamics, low-rank structure, distributed solvability, and improved performance.
