- Proposes a novel distributed algorithm combining low-rank user-item matrix modeling with a Hawkes process for time-sensitive recommendation, reporting superior results.
- Theoretical ambiguity in Eq. (8): unclear over which parameter the maximum is taken; if over ρ, need to state whether ρ* can be unbounded.
- Theorem 2’s practical value is diminished because it is not used in experiments to guide selection of ρ.
- Experimental evaluation could be strengthened by adding stronger baselines, notably SVD++ (Netflix challenge winner).
- Clarity: the paper is well-written and easy to follow.
- Originality: the low-rank + Hawkes process combination is a novel contribution to time-sensitive recommendation.
- Significance: addresses an impactful problem and offers a scalable, effective solution with strong empirical performance.
