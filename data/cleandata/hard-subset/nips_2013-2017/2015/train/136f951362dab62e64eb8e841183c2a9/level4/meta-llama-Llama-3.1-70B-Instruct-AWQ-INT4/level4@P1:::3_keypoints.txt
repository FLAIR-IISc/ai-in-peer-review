- Proposes a novel algorithm for time-sensitive recommendation combining low-rank user–item structure with a Hawkes process.  
- Algorithm is distributed and empirically outperforms existing methods.  
- Theoretical concern: Eq. (8) is ambiguous about which parameter the maximum is taken over (is it ρ?).  
- Theoretical concern: possibility that ρ* could be unbounded is not addressed.  
- Theorem 2’s significance is reduced because it is not used to choose ρ in experiments.  
- Experimental evaluation omits strong baselines (e.g., SVD++); adding them would strengthen results.  
- Paper is well-written and easy to follow.  
- Work is novel and addresses a significant, relatively new problem (time-sensitive recommendation).
