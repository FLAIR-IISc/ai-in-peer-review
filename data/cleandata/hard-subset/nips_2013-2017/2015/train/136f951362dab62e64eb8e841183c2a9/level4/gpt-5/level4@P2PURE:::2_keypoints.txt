- Proposal: point process–based collaborative filtering model with event intensity as baseline plus self-exciting component parameterized by user/item latent vectors.  
- Methodology: derives an optimization algorithm for parameter estimation and reports empirical advantages over common alternatives.  
- Missing baseline: should compare to an ensemble of STiC [12] and SVD since that ensemble could recommend new items.  
- Gamma kernel concern: unclear how the bandwidth parameter sigma is chosen and no sensitivity analysis reported.  
- Hyperparameter selection: no details on selection of key hyperparameters (e.g., latent dimensionality) for the proposed method or baselines.  
- Dataset details lacking: need simple statistics such as number of recurring events per user-item pair and degree of temporal irregularity.  
- Quality: overall quality is reasonably good but not outstanding.  
- Clarity: presentation suffers from language issues, typos, and grammar problems.  
- Originality: interesting and novel combination of point processes with latent factor models.  
- Significance: problem and method have practical value, but experiments and presentation need strengthening.
