- Introduces a point process collaborative filtering model combining a constant and self-exciting intensity for each user-item pair, parameterized by user/item latent vectors.  
- Presents an optimization algorithm for parameter estimation and reports empirical performance gains over existing methods.  
- Experimental evaluation is weak and could be strengthened with additional baselines.  
- Missing comparison to a simple ensemble of STiC and SVD, which may better handle new item recommendations.  
- Unclear how the bandwidth parameter σ in the self-exciting γ function is chosen and how sensitive results are to σ.  
- Lacks details on hyperparameter selection for the proposed model and baselines (e.g., latent vector dimensionality).  
- Needs more descriptive statistics for the two real-world datasets (e.g., average recurring events per user/item pair).  
- Should report temporal-focused metrics like temporal irregularity given the model’s emphasis on temporal structure.  
- Overall quality is good but there is room for improvement.  
- Presentation requires clearer language and correction of typos/grammatical errors.  
- The approach is novel in integrating point process models with latent factor models.  
- The problem and method have practical significance, but experiments and presentation warrant improvement.
