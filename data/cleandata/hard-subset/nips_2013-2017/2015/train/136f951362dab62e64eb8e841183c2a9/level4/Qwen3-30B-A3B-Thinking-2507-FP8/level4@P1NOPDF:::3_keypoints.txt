- Addresses time-sensitive recommendation by modeling user-item interactions with a low-rank matrix plus Hawkes processes.  
- Proposed algorithm is distributed-friendly and shows superior performance over existing baselines.  
- Concern: Equation (8) maximizes over parameter ρ but it’s unclear if the optimal ρ* could become unbounded.  
- Concern: Theorem 2 (convergence guarantees) is not used in experiments, reducing its practical significance.  
- Baselines miss SVD++, a strong benchmark (Netflix Challenge winner) that should be included.  
- Clarity: Paper is well-structured and clearly written.  
- Originality: Novel combination of low-rank matrix decomposition with Hawkes processes for time-sensitive recommendation.  
- Significance: Addresses an important real-world problem and provides a technically sound contribution.
