- Proposes a point process–based collaborative filtering model combining constant and self-exciting intensities, using separate user and item latent vector sets.  
- Derives an optimization algorithm for parameter estimation.  
- Empirical results claim superiority over common alternatives, but the experimental evaluation is weak and needs strengthening.  
- Missing a more appropriate baseline: a simple ensemble of STiC and SVD (STiC can’t recommend new items, SVD can).  
- Bandwidth parameter σ for the γ function is not explained, and sensitivity to σ is not analyzed.  
- Hyperparameter selection (e.g., latent vector dimensionality) is not reported for the proposed method or baselines.  
- Real-world dataset descriptions omit key temporal characteristics (recurrence frequency per user-item pair, temporal irregularity measures) relevant to the paper’s focus.  
- Overall quality: acceptable but not outstanding.  
- Clarity: needs improvement in language, grammar, and typographical accuracy.  
- Originality: novel and interesting integration of point processes with latent factor models.  
- Significance: practically relevant, but experimental section requires substantial revision to support claims.
