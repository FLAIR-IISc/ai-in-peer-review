- Proposes a point-process collaborative filtering model combining a constant base rate and a self-exciting component parameterized by user/item latent vectors.
- Presents an optimization algorithm and empirical results showing advantages over common alternatives.
- Experimental evaluation is weak; missing sensible baseline: an ensemble of STiC and SVD (to handle new-item recommendation).
- Unclear how the γ bandwidth parameter σ is set and how sensitive results are to its choice.
- Missing details on hyperparameter selection (e.g., latent dimensionality) and validation protocol for both the proposed method and baselines.
- Lacks sensitivity analyses for key hyperparameters and model choices.
- Needs richer dataset characterization—report stats like number of recurring events per user–item pair and temporal irregularity.
- Quality: modeling and optimization are sound, but empirical study and reporting are lacking.
- Clarity: writing has language, grammar, and notation issues that hinder readability.
- Originality: interesting combination of point processes and latent factor models.
- Significance: practically valuable approach, but impact would be stronger with more comprehensive experiments and clearer reporting.
