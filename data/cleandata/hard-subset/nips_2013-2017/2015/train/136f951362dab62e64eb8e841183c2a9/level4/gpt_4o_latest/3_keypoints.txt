- Proposes a novel, distributed algorithm for time-sensitive recommendation using low-rank user-item structure and the Hawkes process, showing superior empirical performance.  
- Quality: adopts a principled stochastic-process model for ratings but has some issues to address.  
- Theoretical issue: Eq. (8) is ambiguous—unclear over which parameter the maximum is taken (is it over ρ?), and whether ρ* could be unbounded.  
- Theorem 2 concern: the theorem is not used to select ρ in experiments, reducing its practical relevance.  
- Baselines: important strong baseline SVD++ (Netflix winner) is missing and should be included to strengthen evaluation.  
- Clarity: paper is well-written and easy to follow.  
- Originality: appears novel; time-sensitive recommendation remains a relatively new research area.  
- Significance: the problem is impactful and the proposed distributed method shows promising empirical gains.
