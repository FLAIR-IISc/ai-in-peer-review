- Identifies Propensity Overfitting in the conventional counterfactual risk estimator for BLBF and proposes the Self-Normalized Risk Estimator (SNRE).
- Proposes Norm-POEM, a CRM-based learning algorithm that uses the SNRE.
- Claim: Conventional estimator can cause degeneracies in ERM due to Propensity Overfitting.
- Claim: SNRE avoids Propensity Overfitting and yields more robust risk estimates.
- Claim: Norm-POEM significantly improves generalization performance compared to conventional CRM.
- Theoretical support: SNRE is equivariant and has lower variance than the conventional estimator.
- Empirical support: Norm-POEM consistently outperforms conventional CRM on several multi-label classification benchmarks.
- Practical usefulness: Applicable to BLBF tasks common in ad placement, web search, and recommendation systems.
- Field knowledge: Paper demonstrates strong familiarity with related literature and common field knowledge.
- Novelty: SNRE is a novel estimator and Norm-POEM is a new algorithm achieving state-of-the-art results on benchmarks.
- Completeness: Experimental setup, datasets, and hyperparameter tuning are described sufficiently for reproducibility.
- Limitation: SNRE is biased (not unbiased) though strongly consistent as sample size increases.
- Limitation/assumption: Assumes the logging policy is stationary and has full support over the action space.
- Recommendation: Reviewer recommends acceptance for publication.
