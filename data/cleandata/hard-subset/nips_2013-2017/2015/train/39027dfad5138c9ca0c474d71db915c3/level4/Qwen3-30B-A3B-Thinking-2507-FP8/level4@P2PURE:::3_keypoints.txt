- Addresses batch learning from logged bandit feedback and identifies overfitting in Counterfactual Risk Minimization (CRM).
- Proposes a solution using multiplicative control variates, producing a biased, self-normalizing risk estimator.
- Empirical results show significant improvements from the modified estimator.
- Paper is high-quality, well motivated, and clearly written.
- Follow-up work on prior paper [1], not entirely novel but an important modification to the estimator.
- Lacks extended experiments and deeper analysis of optimization beyond a brief discussion (line 415).
- Reviewer requests variance measures when multiple repetitions are run (line 365).
- Unclear how experimental improvements generalize to broader real-world applicability.
- Reviewer would like additional applications and real-world case studies beyond those in [1].
