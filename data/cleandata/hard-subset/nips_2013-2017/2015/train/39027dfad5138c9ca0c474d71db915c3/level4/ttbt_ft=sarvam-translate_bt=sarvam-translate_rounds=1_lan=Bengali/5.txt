This paper proposes a batch learning method from logged bandit feedback that avoids the tendency for over-fitting.

It builds upon the 2015 work of Swaminathan and Joachim, and provides a significantly improved algorithm.

Quality: I believe the quality of the research discussed in this paper is above average. The extensions proposed in this paper have significantly improved the training of the main POEM.

Clarity: In most cases, this paper is clearly written and easy to follow.

Originality: This paper provides a new formulation of the main POEM algorithm for the BLBF problem. I believe its contributions are significantly original.

Importance: I believe the results of this paper are very interesting and definitely require further research. I am impressed that the proposed algorithm is faster than the main POEM, even though it has a more complex regularization mechanism. This algorithm may be widely used in the near future.

This is a good paper. It is clear and well understood.
