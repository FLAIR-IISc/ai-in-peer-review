- Addresses batch learning from logged bandit feedback, identifies CRM overfitting, and proposes multiplicative control variates leading to a biased estimator.
- Work is high quality and well-justified but would benefit from more extensive experiments and deeper analysis of the training objective optimization.
- Paper is clearly presented and well-written.
- Novelty: extends prior work by introducing a self-normalizing risk estimator that yields significant experimental improvements.
- Significance: learning from logged data is important, but applicability of experimental gains to real-world settings is unclear.
- Reviewer requests variance measures if multiple repetitions were run and urges broader experiments, especially on real-world applications beyond prior work.
