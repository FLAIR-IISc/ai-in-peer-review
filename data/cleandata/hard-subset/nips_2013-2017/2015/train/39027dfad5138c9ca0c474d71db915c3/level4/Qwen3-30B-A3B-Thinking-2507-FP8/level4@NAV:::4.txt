== Other comments ==  
- The setup for "batch learning from logged bandit feedback" in Section 3 (or [1]) lacks a key element: feedback should be treated as a random variable, not a deterministic function as in line 110. Rationale: In classification with unknown labels, feedback arises from a prediction attempt yielding loss Δ(y', y), where y' (the true label) may vary stochastically for fixed x. Thus, δ(x, y) is inherently random (with mean E[Δ(y', y)] over p(y'|x)), not deterministic. This necessitates an additional expectation over δ in the true risk (1), though it may not affect the risk estimator (2).  
- Lines 170–171: The ratio should be h(y|x) / h₀(y|x). Also, note the lack of linearity was previously identified in [1].  
- Line 276: Replace "argmax" with "argmin" to align with risk minimization.  
- Line 319: Use hats (̂) instead of bars for consistency with prior notation.
