- Proposes a method for batch learning from logged bandit feedback that avoids propensity overfitting and builds on Swaminathan & Joachims (2015).
- Presents a significantly improved algorithm compared to the original POEM.
- Research quality is high; proposed extensions substantially improve POEM’s training process.
- Paper is mostly clearly written and easy to follow.
- Offers a novel reformulation of POEM for the BLBF problem with significantly original contributions.
- Results are compelling; the algorithm is faster than POEM despite extra regularization and has strong adoption potential.
- Overall assessment: a good, clear, well-written paper.
