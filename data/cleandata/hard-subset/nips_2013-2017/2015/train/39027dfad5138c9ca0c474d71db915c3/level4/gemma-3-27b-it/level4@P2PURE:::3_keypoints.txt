- Addresses batch learning from logged bandit feedback, identifies overfitting in CRM, and proposes multiplicative control variates (introducing bias).
- High-quality, well-motivated work but would benefit from more extensive experiments and deeper analysis of the training objective’s optimization.
- Presentation is clear and the paper is well-written.
- The self-normalizing modification of the risk estimator (building on [1]) appears to significantly improve performance.
- Practical significance is high, but it is unclear how well the experimental gains generalize to real-world applications.
- Request to report variance measures since multiple repetitions were already performed.
- Suggestion to include more experiments and evaluations on actual real-world datasets beyond those in [1].
