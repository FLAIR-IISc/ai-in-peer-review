- Proposes a method for batch learning from logged bandit feedback that mitigates propensity overfitting and substantially improves on POEM (Swaminathan & Joachims, 2015).
- Research quality is above average, with proposed extensions yielding significant training gains over original POEM.
- Paper is for the most part clearly written and easy to follow.
- Offers a markedly new and substantially original formulation of POEM for the BLBF problem.
- Results are interesting and significant: the algorithm is faster than POEM despite added regularization and may see broad adoption.
- Overall judgment: a good, clear paper that merits further investigation.
