- Proposes a method for batch learning from logged bandit feedback that addresses propensity overfitting.  
- Extends Swaminathan & Joachims (2015) and delivers a substantially improved algorithm.  
- Research quality is above average.  
- Extensions yield substantial improvements in training efficiency for the original POEM algorithm.  
- Paper is well-written and accessible throughout.  
- Presents a substantially novel formulation of POEM for the BLBF problem, a significant original contribution.  
- Findings are compelling and warrant further investigation.  
- Proposed algorithm trains faster than original POEM despite extra regularization, suggesting potential for widespread adoption.  
- Overall assessment: a strong, clear, and well-written paper.
