- Identifies propensity overfitting in counterfactual risk estimators for BLBF as a critical problem.  
- Proposes a self-normalized estimator to mitigate propensity overfitting.  
- Introduces Norm-POEM, a new learning algorithm based on the self-normalized estimator.  
- Norm-POEM consistently outperforms the original POEM algorithm empirically.  
- Builds directly on prior work in counterfactual learning, importance sampling, and causal inference.  
- Technical quality is high with novel insight and solid theoretical justification.  
- Includes theoretical guarantees (Theorems 1 & 2) supporting the approach.  
- Empirical evaluation is comprehensive and shows surprising faster convergence for Norm-POEM.  
- Paper is generally well-written and clearly organized, with accessible notation.  
- Derivation of the variance estimator (Section 6) needs clearer, more detailed explanation.  
- Computational complexity and scalability of Norm-POEM warrant deeper analysis despite observed speed-ups.  
- Provides open-source implementation, aiding reproducibility and adoption.  
- Overall assessment: strong, significant contribution; strong recommendation for acceptance.
