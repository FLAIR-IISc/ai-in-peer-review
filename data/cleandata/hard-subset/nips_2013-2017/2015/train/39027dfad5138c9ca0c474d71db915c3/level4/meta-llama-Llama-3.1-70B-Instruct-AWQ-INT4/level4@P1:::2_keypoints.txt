- Identifies propensity overfitting in the conventional counterfactual risk estimator for BLBF.  
- Proposes a self-normalized risk estimator to mitigate propensity overfitting.  
- Develops Norm-POEM, a new learning algorithm for structured output prediction using the estimator.  
- Situates the work clearly within BLBF and off-policy RL literature and summarizes prior approaches.  
- Provides a thorough theoretical analysis: equivariance, boundedness, strong consistency, and asymptotic behavior.  
- Empirical results show Norm-POEM improves generalization and robustness relative to POEM.  
- Offers detailed comparisons of computational efficiency and hyperparameter sensitivity between methods.  
- Minor critique: notation and commentary around equation (2) are unclear/misleading about loss translation.  
- Suggests the paper would benefit from a wider discussion of implications for other BLBF/off-policy applications.  
- Overall assessment: well-written, novel, significant contribution with convincing experiments.
