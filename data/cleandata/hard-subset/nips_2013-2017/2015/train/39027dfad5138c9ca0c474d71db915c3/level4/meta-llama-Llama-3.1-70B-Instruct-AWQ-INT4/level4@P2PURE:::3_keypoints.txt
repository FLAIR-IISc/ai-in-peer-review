- Tackles batch learning from logged bandit feedback, identifies overfitting in Counterfactual Risk Minimization (CRM), and proposes multiplicative control variates yielding a biased estimator.  
- Paper is high quality and well-motivated but lacks additional experiments and deeper analysis of training objective optimization.  
- Presentation is clear and the paper is well-written.  
- Builds on prior work [1] but introduces a self-normalizing (multiplicative) risk estimator that substantially changes experimental outcomes.  
- Problem is significant and relevant, but it's unclear how experimental gains will transfer to real-world applications.  
- Reviewer requests reporting variance (in addition to repetitions) and more extensive experiments, applications beyond [1], and ideally real-world demonstrations.
