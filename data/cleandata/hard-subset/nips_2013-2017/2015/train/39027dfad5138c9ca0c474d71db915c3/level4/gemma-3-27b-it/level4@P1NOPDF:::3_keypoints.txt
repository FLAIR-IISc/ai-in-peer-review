- Addresses batch learning from logged bandit feedback, identifies CRM overfitting, and proposes multiplicative control variates as a biased estimator fix.  
- High-quality, well-motivated work but expected additional experiments and deeper analysis of training objective optimization.  
- Presentation is clear and the paper is well-written.  
- Builds on prior work; self-normalizing risk estimator modification appears to give significant experimental improvements.  
- Problem is important, but it's unclear how experimental gains will translate to real-world applications.  
- Request to report variance when multiple repetitions are performed.  
- Paper correctly identifies a CRM limitation and presents a viable solution, yet more extensive experiments and evaluations on real-world datasets are needed.
