- Introduces a method for batch learning from logged bandit feedback that addresses propensity overfitting and extends Swaminathan & Joachims (2015).  
- Proposes a substantially improved reformulation of the POEM algorithm.  
- Research quality is strong; extensions yield considerable improvements to POEM training.  
- Paper is largely lucid and well-structured, making contributions easy to follow.  
- Presents a genuinely novel and original reformulation of POEM.  
- Results are compelling: the new algorithm is computationally faster than POEM despite added regularization and has high adoption potential.  
- Overall: a strong, clearly articulated, and well-presented submission.
