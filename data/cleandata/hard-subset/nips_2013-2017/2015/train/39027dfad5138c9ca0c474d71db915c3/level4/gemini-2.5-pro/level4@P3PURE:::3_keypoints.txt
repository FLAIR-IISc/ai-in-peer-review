- Studies batch learning from logged bandit feedback, a setting relevant to ad ranking.  
- Identifies an "overfitting" problem in Counterfactual Risk Minimization (CRM).  
- Proposes multiplicative control variates, yielding a biased self-normalizing risk estimator.  
- Paper is high quality, well-motivated, and clearly written.  
- Main contribution is the CRM estimator modification, which shows significant improvements in experiments.  
- Unclear how reported improvements will generalize to more complex, real-world applications.  
- Recommend broader experiments, including additional and real-world datasets, to demonstrate generalizability.  
- Recommend deeper analysis of the optimization of the training objective (expand discussion around line 415).  
- Recommend reporting variance (e.g., standard deviations or error bars) for repeated experiments (line 365) to assess stability and significance.
