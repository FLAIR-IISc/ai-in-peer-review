- Proposes a method for batch learning from logged bandit feedback that avoids propensity overfitting and builds on Swaminathan & Joachims (2015), improving POEM.
- Research quality is above average; proposed extensions yield significant improvements in training the original POEM algorithm.
- Paper is mostly clearly written, coherent, and well-structured.
- Offers a substantially original reformulation of POEM, representing a meaningful departure from prior work.
- Results are interesting and warrant further research; the algorithm is faster than original POEM despite added regularization.
- Overall a good, engaging paper that makes a valuable contribution likely to have lasting impact.
