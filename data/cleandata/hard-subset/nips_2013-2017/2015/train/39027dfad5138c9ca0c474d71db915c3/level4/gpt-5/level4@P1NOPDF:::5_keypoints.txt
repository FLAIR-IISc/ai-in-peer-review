- Proposes a new method for batch learning from logged bandit feedback that avoids propensity overfitting.  
- Builds on Swaminathan and Joachims (2015) and delivers a substantially improved algorithm over POEM.  
- Extensions introduced lead to significant improvements in training performance.  
- Research quality is above average.  
- Paper is mostly clearly written and easy to follow.  
- Presents a substantially new formulation with significant and original contributions.  
- Results are compelling; the algorithm is faster than original POEM despite added regularization.  
- Method has strong potential for widespread adoption; overall the paper is clear, well-organized, and solid.
