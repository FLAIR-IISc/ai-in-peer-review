- Clarify delta parameter range: CRM is principled for delta in [-1,0] per [1], so NORM-POEM (eqs. 3,8) should obey this—experimental use of "delta > 0" in Table 1 is confusing and needs explanation.  
- Section 3 modeling issue: delta(x,y) is presented deterministically but should be treated as a random variable when labels/noise vary; the true risk (eq.1) should include an expectation over delta's randomness.  
- Importance-weight ratio error: lines 170–171 appear inverted; it should be h(y|x)/h0(y|x), and [1] should be credited for noting the non-linearity.  
- Objective notation: line 276 should use argmin (not argmax) since the goal is risk minimization.  
- Notational consistency: line 319 should replace bars with hats to match the rest of the paper.  
- Overall assessment (post-rebuttal): paper is a valuable follow-up to [1]; analysis of propensity overfitting is insightful, the multiplicative control variate is a compelling fix, and results show clear improvement over the POEM baseline.
