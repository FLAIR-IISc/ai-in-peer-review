- Proposes a novel method for batch learning from logged bandit feedback that effectively addresses propensity overfitting.
- Builds on Swaminathan and Joachims (2015) with significant improvements to the original algorithm.
- Extensions yield substantial improvements in training POEM, reflecting above-average research quality.
- Paper is well-written and largely easy to follow, clearly presenting complex ideas.
- Presents a significantly new formulation of POEM for the BLBF problem with substantial, original contributions.
- Results are intriguing: the proposed algorithm outperforms original POEM in speed despite extra regularization.
- Method has strong potential for widespread adoption and warrants further research.
- Overall, a high-quality, well-organized, and engaging paper with significant contributions.
