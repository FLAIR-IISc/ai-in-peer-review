- Proposes a BLBF method that avoids propensity overfitting and substantially improves on Swaminathan & Joachims (2015)/POEM.
- Quality: research is above average; extensions yield significant training improvements over original POEM.
- Clarity: paper is mostly clear and easy to follow.
- Originality: offers a substantially new formulation of POEM with significantly original contributions.
- Significance: results are interesting and merit further research; algorithm is faster than POEM despite extra regularization and could see widespread use.
- Overall: a good, clear, well-written paper.
