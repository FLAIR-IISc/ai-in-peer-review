Review- The paper presents a method for batch learning from logged bandit feedback that mitigates the propensity overfitting problem. It builds on Swaminathan and Joachims (2015) and delivers a substantially improved algorithm.
Quality: I assess the research quality as above average. The proposed extensions lead to significant gains in training over the original POEM.
Clarity: For the most part, the paper is clearly written and easy to follow.
Originality: The paper offers a markedly new formulation of the original POEM algorithm for the BLBF problem. I believe the contributions are substantially original.
Significance: The results are very interesting and merit further investigation. I am impressed that the proposed algorithm is faster than the original POEM despite incorporating additional regularization mechanisms. This approach may see broad adoption in the near future.
This is a good paper. It is clear and reads well.
