- Novel approach to batch learning from logged bandit feedback that mitigates propensity overfitting.  
- Builds on Swaminathan & Joachims (2015) and offers substantial improvements over existing algorithms.  
- Research quality is commendable and above average.  
- Proposed extensions noticeably improve training of the original POEM algorithm.  
- Paper is well-structured and mostly easy to understand.  
- Introduces a significantly novel formulation of POEM for the BLBF problem; contributions are substantial and original.  
- Results are intriguing: algorithm outperforms original POEM in speed despite added regularization.  
- Method has potential for widespread adoption and warrants further investigation.  
- Overall: a well-crafted, clear, and engaging paper that effectively conveys its message.
