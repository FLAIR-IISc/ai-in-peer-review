- Proposes a method for batch learning from logged bandit feedback that addresses propensity overfitting.  
- Builds on Swaminathan & Joachims (2015) and presents a significantly improved algorithm.  
- Extensions produce significant improvements in training over the original POEM algorithm.  
- Research quality judged above average.  
- Paper is mostly clearly written and easy to follow.  
- Offers a novel formulation of POEM for the BLBF problem; contributions are substantially original.  
- Results are interesting and warrant further research.  
- Proposed algorithm is faster than original POEM despite incorporating additional regularization.  
- Algorithm has potential for widespread use.  
- Overall assessment: strong, clear, well-written paper.
