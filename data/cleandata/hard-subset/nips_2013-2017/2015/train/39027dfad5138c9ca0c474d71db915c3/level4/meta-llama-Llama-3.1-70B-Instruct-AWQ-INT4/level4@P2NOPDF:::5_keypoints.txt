- Proposes a new method for batch learning from logged bandit feedback that addresses propensity overfitting and improves on Swaminathan & Joachims (2015).
- Research quality is above average, with substantial improvements over the original POEM algorithm.
- Paper is mostly clearly written and easy to follow, making the ideas accessible.
- Offers a novel formulation of POEM for the BLBF problem, with significant and original contributions.
- Demonstrates intriguing results: the new algorithm is faster than POEM despite adding extra regularization.
- Overall, a well-written, impactful paper whose clarity and contributions warrant further research.
