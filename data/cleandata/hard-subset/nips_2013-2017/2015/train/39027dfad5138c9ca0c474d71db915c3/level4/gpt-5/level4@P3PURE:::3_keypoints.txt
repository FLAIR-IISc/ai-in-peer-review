- Addresses batch learning from logged bandit feedback; identifies overfitting in Counterfactual Risk Minimization (CRM) and proposes multiplicative control variates yielding a biased, self-normalizing estimator.
- High quality and well motivated work.
- Reviewer expected additional experiments and more detailed analysis of training-objective optimization beyond the brief discussion around line 415.
- Presentation is clear and well written.
- As a follow-up to [1], the paper’s main novelty is making the risk estimator self-normalizing, which yields substantial experimental gains.
- Significance is good (important problem), but it’s unclear how the experimental improvements will generalize to broader real-world applications.
- If multiple repetitions are run, include variance measures (line 365).
- Request extended experiments and applications beyond those in [1], preferably including real-world cases.
