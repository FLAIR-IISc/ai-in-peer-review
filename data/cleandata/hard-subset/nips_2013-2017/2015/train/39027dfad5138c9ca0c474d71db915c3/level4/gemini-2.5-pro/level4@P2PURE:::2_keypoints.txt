- Paper improves the POEM estimator for counterfactual (bandit) multi-label learning.  
- Experimental results are strong: Norm-POEM outperforms original POEM on all datasets and rivals a CRF trained with full labels.  
- Major theoretical flaw: the mathematical interpretation of Equation (2) is incorrect.  
- Specifically, the claim that Equation (2) exhibits an "anomaly" (non‑invariance to translations of the loss) misinterprets the expectation operator and is technically invalid.  
- Example 1 is based on this flawed premise and is therefore unconvincing.  
- Reviewer urges the authors to revise the problematic commentary if the paper is accepted.  
- Practical strength: the proposed estimator is well-founded; Equation (2) is unstable when pi can be zero, and Equation (7)’s normalization (double denominator) better mitigates small pi and improves stability.
