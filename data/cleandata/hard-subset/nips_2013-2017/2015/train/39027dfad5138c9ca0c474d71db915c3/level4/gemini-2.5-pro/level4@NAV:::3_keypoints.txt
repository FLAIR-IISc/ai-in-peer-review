- Addresses batch learning from logged bandit feedback and identifies overfitting in Counterfactual Risk Minimization (CRM).  
- Proposes multiplicative control variates producing a biased, self-normalizing risk estimator as a solution.  
- Method is well-motivated, technically sound, and shows significant performance gains in experiments.  
- Builds directly on prior work [1]; primary novelty is the self-normalizing estimator.  
- Manuscript is well-written and clearly presented.  
- Evaluation scope is limited; more extensive experiments, especially on new and real-world datasets, are needed.  
- Training objective optimization requires deeper analysis beyond the brief discussion in the paper.  
- Experimental results lack reported variance despite repeated runs; measure of variance should be included.  
- Unclear how observed gains will generalize to complex, real-world applications.
