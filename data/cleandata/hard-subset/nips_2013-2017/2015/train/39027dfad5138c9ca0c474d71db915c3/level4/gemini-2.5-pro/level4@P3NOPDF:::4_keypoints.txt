- Presents a valuable follow-up to POEM, addressing batch learning from logged bandit feedback.  
- Main contributions: analysis of "propensity overfitting" and proposal of NORM-POEM, a multiplicative control variate.  
- Empirical results show clear improvement of NORM-POEM over baseline POEM.  
- Suggest generalizing problem setup to treat delta(x,y) as a random variable and include an extra expectation in the true risk formulation for greater theoretical generality.  
- Request clarification on the assumed range/rescaling of the loss delta (original CRM used delta in [-1,0]) and explanation of how experiments with "delta > 0" are handled.  
- Minor correction: importance weight ratio at lines 170–171 appears inverted; should be h(y|x)/h0(y|x).  
- Minor correction: replace argmax with argmin at line 276 since the objective is risk minimization.  
- Minor suggestion: use hats instead of bars for estimated quantities (e.g., \hat{R} vs \bar{R}) for notational consistency.  
- Overall assessment: strong, well-motivated contribution with insightful analysis; after clarifications it will be a solid conference paper.
