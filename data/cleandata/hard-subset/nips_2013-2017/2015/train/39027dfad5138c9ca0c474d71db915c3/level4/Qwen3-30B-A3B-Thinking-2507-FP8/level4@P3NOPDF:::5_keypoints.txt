- Proposes a novel BLBF method that effectively addresses propensity overfitting in prior approaches.  
- Builds on Swaminathan & Joachims (2015) by extending POEM with a principled regularization reformulation.  
- Extension yields substantially improved offline learning performance across multiple datasets.  
- Method achieves faster convergence than POEM despite a more sophisticated regularizer.  
- Theoretical analysis is sound and supports the proposed approach.  
- Experimental validation is robust and shows reduced computational overhead compared to POEM.  
- Paper is exceptionally well-written, clearly organized, and figures effectively illustrate improvements.  
- Contribution is highly original—constitutes a distinct algorithmic paradigm rather than a minor tweak.  
- Work is significant for real-world logged-data applications (recommendation, ad placement) and merits further scalability/adaptability study.  
- Reviewer recommends acceptance without reservation.
