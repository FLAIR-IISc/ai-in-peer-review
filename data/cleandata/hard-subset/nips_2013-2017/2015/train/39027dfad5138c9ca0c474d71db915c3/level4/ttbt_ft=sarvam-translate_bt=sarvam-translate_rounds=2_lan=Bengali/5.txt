This paper proposes a batch learning method from the response of lagged Bedford files that can avoid the tendency of overfitting.

It is based on the work of Swaminathan and Joachim in 2015 and provides a significantly improved algorithm.

Quality: I believe the quality of the research discussed in this paper is above average. The extensions proposed in this paper have significantly improved the training of the main POEM.

Clarity: In most cases, this paper is clearly written and easy to follow.

Originality: This paper provides a new formulation of the main POEM algorithm for the BLBF problem. I believe its contributions are significantly original.

Importance: I believe the results of this paper are very interesting and definitely require further research. I am impressed that the proposed algorithm is faster than the main POEM, although it has a more complex regularization process. This algorithm may be widely used in the near future.

This is a good paper. It is clear and well understood.
