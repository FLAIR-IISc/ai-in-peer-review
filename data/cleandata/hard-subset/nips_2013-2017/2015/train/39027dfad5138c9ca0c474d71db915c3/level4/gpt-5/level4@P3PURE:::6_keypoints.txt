- CRM corrects bias in logged-bandit learning by using propensities to achieve consistency.
- Despite consistency, CRM can overfit by exploiting example generation probabilities (propensities).
- The paper introduces a multiplicative self-normalization parameter to bound variance while preserving consistency.
- The normalized (multiplicative) counterfactual estimator mitigates this form of overfitting.
- Empirical results show the multiplicative/normalized method outperforms the non-normalized approach.
- Overall assessment: clear technique with solid empirical support; paper is good and should be accepted, though the reviewer is not completely certain.
