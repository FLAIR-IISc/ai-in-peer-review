- Proposes a new method for batch learning from logged bandit feedback that avoids propensity overfitting.
- Builds on Swaminathan & Joachims (2015) and offers a significantly improved algorithm over POEM.
- Experimental extensions yield substantial training improvements compared to original POEM.
- Research quality judged above average.
- Paper is mostly clearly written and easy to follow.
- Presents a significantly new and original formulation of the BLBF problem.
- Results are interesting and significant, warranting further research.
- Algorithm is faster than POEM despite additional regularization and may see widespread use.
- Overall assessment: a good, clear, and promising paper.
