- Proposes a method for batch learning from logged bandit feedback that mitigates propensity overfitting.
- Builds on Swaminathan & Joachims (2015) and delivers a substantially improved algorithm over POEM.
- Technical quality is above average; extensions yield meaningful training gains vs. original POEM.
- Paper is mostly clearly written and easy to follow.
- Presents a notably new formulation of POEM for the BLBF setting; contributions are genuinely original.
- Results are compelling and merit further investigation.
- Proposed algorithm is faster than the original POEM despite incorporating additional regularization.
- Overall assessment: a strong, clear, well-written paper.
