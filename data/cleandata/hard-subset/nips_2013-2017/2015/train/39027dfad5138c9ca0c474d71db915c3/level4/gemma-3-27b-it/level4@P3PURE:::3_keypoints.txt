- Paper studies batch learning from logged bandit feedback and identifies an overfitting issue in Counterfactual Risk Minimization (CRM).  
- Authors propose a multiplicative control-variates solution (self-normalizing estimator), acknowledging it introduces bias.  
- The modification appears to yield significant empirical improvements over prior work [1].  
- Work is high-quality, well-motivated, and clearly presented.  
- Reviewer expected more extensive experimentation as a follow-up to [1].  
- Reviewer requests deeper analysis of optimization of the training objective beyond the brief discussion (line 415).  
- The practical impact of the observed experimental gains on real-world applications is unclear.  
- Reviewer asks for measures of variance across repetitions to quantify results (line 365).  
- Reviewer suggests including evaluations on additional and real-world datasets beyond those in [1].  
- Overall assessment: solid, novel, and relevant work but would benefit from more experiments and deeper analysis.
