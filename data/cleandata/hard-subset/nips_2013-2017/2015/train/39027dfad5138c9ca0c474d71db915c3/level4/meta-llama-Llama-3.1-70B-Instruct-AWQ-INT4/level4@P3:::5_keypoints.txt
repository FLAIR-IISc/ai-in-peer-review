- Proposes Norm-POEM, a method for batch learning from logged bandit feedback that addresses propensity overfitting.  
- Builds on Swaminathan & Joachims (2015) and presents a substantially improved algorithm over POEM.  
- Research quality is above average with substantial improvements in training and generalization.  
- Thorough analysis of propensity overfitting and its implications for BLBF.  
- Paper is mostly clear, well-organized, and explains technical details accessibly.  
- Introduction of the self-normalized risk estimator is a novel and original contribution.  
- Norm-POEM empirically outperforms original POEM across experiments.  
- Norm-POEM is computationally efficient and faster than POEM despite added regularization.  
- Arguments for acceptance: addresses a critical BLBF issue, strong empirical gains, solid technical analysis, and clear writing.  
- Arguments against acceptance: needs additional experiments, some technical sections may be hard to follow, and limited discussion of limitations/future work.  
- Overall rating 8/10.  
- Recommendation: Accept with minor revisions.
