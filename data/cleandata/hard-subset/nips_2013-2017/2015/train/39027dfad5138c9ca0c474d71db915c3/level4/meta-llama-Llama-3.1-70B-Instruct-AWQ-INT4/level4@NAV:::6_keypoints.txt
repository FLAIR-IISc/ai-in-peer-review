- Introduces a novel approach to counterfactual risk minimization for learning from logged bandit data by correcting generation bias.
- Method is consistent but vulnerable to overfitting driven by the probability of generating logged examples.
- Proposes a multiplicative (self-normalized) parameter to bound variance while preserving consistency.
- Experimental results show the normalized counterfactual estimator outperforms the non-normalized version.
- Develops a valuable self-normalized estimator for counterfactual learning in the logged bandit setting.
- Provides targeted solutions to mitigate overfitting when hypotheses receive stronger support from logged data.
- Paper is well-written, impactful, and merits acceptance despite minor reservations.
