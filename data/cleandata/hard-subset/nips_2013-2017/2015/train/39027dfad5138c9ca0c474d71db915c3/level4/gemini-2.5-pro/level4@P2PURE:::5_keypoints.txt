- Proposes a method for batch learning from logged bandit feedback that addresses propensity overfitting.  
- Builds upon Swaminathan and Joachims (2015) and reformulates the original POEM algorithm.  
- Introduces significant, novel extensions that yield substantial improvements over POEM.  
- Research quality is high and the extensions materially improve training.  
- Paper is mostly clearly written and easy to follow.  
- Proposed algorithm is faster than original POEM despite extra regularization and has strong potential for widespread adoption.
