This research paper proposes a method for batch learning from logged bandit feedback that avoids the tendency to over-fit.

It builds on the 2015 paper by Swaminathan and Joachim and provides a significantly improved algorithm.

Quality: I found the quality of the research described in the paper to be above average. The extensions proposed in the paper bring significant improvements to the training of the original POEM.

Clarity: For the most part, the paper is clearly written and easy to understand.

Novelty: The paper significantly novelized the original POEM algorithm for the BLBF problem. I believe the contributions are quite original.

Significance: I think the results in the paper are very interesting and definitely require further research. I am impressed that the proposed algorithm is faster than the original POEM, even though it has a more regular mechanism. This algorithm could be widely used in the near future.

This is a good research paper. It is clear and good to read.
