- Proposes a novel method for batch learning from logged bandit feedback that directly addresses propensity overfitting.  
- Builds on POEM (Swaminathan & Joachims 2015) with a reformulated objective and enhanced regularization.  
- High-quality, principled solution identifying and fixing a key weakness of original POEM.  
- Reformulation yields substantial empirical performance gains.  
- Converges faster than POEM despite a more complex regularizer, implying a more favorable optimization landscape.  
- Dual benefit of improved accuracy and reduced training time with strong practical impact for logged-data domains (ads, recommendations).  
- Original and non-incremental contribution offering a new perspective on regularization in batch bandits.  
- Well-written and well-structured; methodology and experiments clearly presented and supportive of claims.  
- Overall strong paper—elegant, effective, efficient—recommended for acceptance and likely to inspire further research.
