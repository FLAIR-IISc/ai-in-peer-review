- Addresses batch learning from logged bandit feedback, identifies CRM overfitting, and proposes multiplicative control variates yielding a biased estimator.  
- Quality: high-quality and well motivated, but more experiments and deeper analysis of training objective optimization were expected.  
- Clarity: presentation is clear and the paper is well written.  
- Originality: extends prior work by using a self-normalizing (multiplicative) risk estimator, which substantially changes experimental outcomes.  
- Significance: learning-from-logged-data is relevant, but it's unclear how the experimental improvements translate to real-world applications.  
- Remarks: report variance for repeated experiments (line 365); overall solid, relevant, and novel, but would benefit from extended experiments and additional real-world cases.
