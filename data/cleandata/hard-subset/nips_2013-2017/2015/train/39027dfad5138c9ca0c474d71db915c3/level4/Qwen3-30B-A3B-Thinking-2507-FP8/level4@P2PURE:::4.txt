== Other comments ==  
- The setup for "batch learning from logged bandit feedback" (Section 3, [1]) omits that the feedback $\delta(x,y)$ should be modeled as a random variable, not a deterministic function (line 110). In classification with noisy labels, $\delta(x,y) = \Delta(y', y)$ where $y'$ is drawn from $p(y' \mid x)$ (the true labeling distribution). For fixed $x$ and predicted $y$, $\delta(x,y)$ may vary across log entries due to label noise. Thus, the true risk in (1) should include an expectation over $\delta(x,y)$ to reflect this randomness, though the estimator (2) remains unaffected.

- Line 276: Replace "argmax" with "argmin" (minimizing the risk).
