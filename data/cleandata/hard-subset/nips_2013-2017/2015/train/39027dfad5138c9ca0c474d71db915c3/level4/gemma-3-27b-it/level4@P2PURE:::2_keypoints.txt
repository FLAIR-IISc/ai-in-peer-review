- Experiments show Norm-POEM consistently outperforms POEM across datasets and approaches the performance of a CRF using full multi-label information.
- The paper addresses counterfactual learning (bandit-style single-label feedback) rather than conventional multi-label learning, though this framing is mainly motivational.
- The main contribution is a refinement of the POEM estimator from ICML 2015, and the paper is well-supported and suitable for publication pending the above concerns.
