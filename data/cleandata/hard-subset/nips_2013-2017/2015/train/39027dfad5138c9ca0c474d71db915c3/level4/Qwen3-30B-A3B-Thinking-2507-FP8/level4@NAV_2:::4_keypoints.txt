- Identifies flaw in the standard counterfactual risk estimator for BLBF and proposes a self-normalized estimator to avoid it.
- Demonstrates conventional estimators suffer from propensity overfitting in complex hypothesis spaces.
- Introduces Norm-POEM, a learning algorithm that uses the self-normalized estimator.
- Empirical results on multi-label classification show consistent improvements over existing methods.
- Clarification requested: CRM requires loss in [-1,0] but Table 1 uses delta > 0—confirm whether Norm-POEM uses the same loss rescaling and clarify interpretation for delta > 0.
- Comment: delta(x,y) is treated as deterministic in the paper but should be modeled as random under noisy labeling; Equation (1) should include an expectation over delta.
- Typo: Line 170–171 should read h(y|x)/h₀(y|x) (division, not multiplication).
- Typo: Line 276 should use argmin instead of argmax (risk is minimized).
- Notation: Line 319 should use hats (ˆ) instead of bars for consistency.
- Overall verdict: After clarifications, a solid, novel, well-motivated contribution with meaningful significance to BLBF literature.
