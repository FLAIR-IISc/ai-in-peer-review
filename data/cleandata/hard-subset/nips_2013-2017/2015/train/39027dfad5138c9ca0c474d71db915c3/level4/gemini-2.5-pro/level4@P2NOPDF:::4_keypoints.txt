- Paper presents a valuable follow-up to POEM, analyzing "propensity overfitting" and proposing a multiplicative control variate (NORM-POEM) that shows empirical improvements.  
- Experiments and problem formulation need clearer details and justification.  
- Major comment 1: Clarify the loss range `delta`—CRM motivations use `delta` in [-1,0], but experiments mention "delta > 0"; authors should state whether losses were rescaled and explain "delta > 0".  
- Major comment 2: Generalize the formulation to stochastic feedback—treat `delta(x,y)` as a random variable and define true risk with an additional expectation to reflect noisy labels/real-world randomness.  
- Minor: Lines 170–171 the importance-weight ratio appears inverted; it should likely be `h(y|x) / h_0(y|x)`, and cite [1] for the nonlinearity point.  
- Minor: Line 276 should use `argmin` (risk minimization), not `argmax`.  
- Minor: Line 319 replace "bar" notation with "hats" for notational consistency.
