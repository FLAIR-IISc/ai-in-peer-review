- Proposes a method for batch learning from logged bandit feedback that avoids propensity overfitting, building on Swaminathan and Joachims (2015) with a significantly better algorithm.
- Research quality is above average; proposed extensions lead to substantial training improvements over the original POEM.
- Paper is mostly clearly written and easy to follow.
- Presents a substantially new formulation of POEM, with contributions judged significantly original.
- Results are interesting and warrant further research; the algorithm is faster than original POEM despite extra regularization and may see widespread use.
- Overall assessment: a good, clear paper that reads well.
