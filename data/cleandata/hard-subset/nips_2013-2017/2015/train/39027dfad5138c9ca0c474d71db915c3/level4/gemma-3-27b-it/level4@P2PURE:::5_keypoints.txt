- Presents a method for batch learning from logged bandit feedback that addresses the propensity overfitting problem.  
- Builds upon and significantly improves the POEM framework of Swaminathan and Joachims (2015).  
- Proposed algorithm demonstrably yields substantial improvements over the original POEM in training.  
- Research quality is high.  
- The paper is largely clear and easy to understand.  
- Introduces a significantly novel formulation of POEM for this problem.  
- Empirical results are compelling, showing faster performance despite additional regularization.  
- Algorithm has strong potential for widespread adoption.  
- Overall assessment: a strong, clear, and well-written paper.
