- Identifies "propensity overfitting": IPS can produce degenerate risk estimates by overfitting to propensity weights instead of loss.  
- Proposes a self-normalized (multiplicative control variate) estimator to replace IPS.  
- Introduces Norm-POEM algorithm built on this estimator.  
- Norm-POEM is more robust and equivariant to loss translations; theoretically motivated.  
- Empirical results on multi-label classification show Norm-POEM significantly outperforms POEM.  
- Strengths: novel, insightful diagnosis; elegant and theoretically sound solution.  
- Manuscript is generally well-written and experiments convincingly support claims.
