- Introduces a novel approach to batch learning from logged bandit feedback (BLBF) and identifies a key failure mode in prior estimators.
- Identifies and names "propensity overfitting," where propensity-weighted estimators overfit weights and produce biased risk estimates.
- Proposes a self-normalized estimator using a multiplicative control variate to avoid propensity overfitting.
- Shows the self-normalized estimator is equivariant and has lower variance than the conventional estimator.
- Derives a new learning algorithm, Norm-POEM, that combines the self-normalized estimator with counterfactual risk minimization (CRM).
- Provides a thorough theoretical analysis and detailed derivation of the estimator and Norm-POEM.
- Offers extensive empirical evaluation demonstrating effectiveness on several multi-label classification tasks.
- Reviewer finds the paper interesting, well-written, and worth reading, recommending acceptance despite limited verification.
- Weakness: reviewer did not have time to fully verify technical details and novelty.
