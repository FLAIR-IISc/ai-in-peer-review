- Paper introduces a novel self-normalized risk estimator to address Propensity Overfitting in batch learning from logged bandit feedback (BLBF).
- Proposed estimator is more robust and avoids overfitting, leading to a new learning algorithm, Norm-POEM.
- The paper is well-organized and clearly written, with a thorough related-work review and clear exposition.
- The estimator is rigorously analyzed theoretically and validated empirically.
- Experiments are comprehensive and show Norm-POEM outperforms conventional POEM and is competitive with full-information CRF.
- Recommendation: convincing and worth publication subject to revisions.
- Notation and terminology are sometimes unclear and some definitions (e.g., hypothesis space H) are missing or delayed.
- The authors’ claim that the conventional estimator has an “anomaly” (non-invariance to loss translation) is technically incorrect and must be corrected.
- The estimator in Equation (2) is sensitive to its denominator π, which can be zero for unseen samples; handling of this issue needs discussion.
- Suggested improvements: clarify all notation, correct the technical error about the anomaly, discuss/handle zero π cases, and consider additional experiments across more scenarios.
