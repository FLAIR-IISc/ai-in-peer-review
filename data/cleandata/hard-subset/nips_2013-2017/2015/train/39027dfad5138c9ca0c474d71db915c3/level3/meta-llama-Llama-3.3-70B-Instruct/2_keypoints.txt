- Identifies propensity overfitting in the conventional unbiased risk estimator for BLBF, causing degenerate risk estimates.
- Proposes a self-normalized risk estimator (Norm-POEM) to avoid these anomalies and provide more robust risk estimates.
- Paper is well-structured, clearly written, and provides a concise introduction and related work review.
- Technical analysis of propensity overfitting and the proposed self-normalized solution is sound and thorough.
- Experimental results demonstrate Norm-POEM yields better generalization than the conventional estimator and compares favorably to a full-information CRF.
- Runtime of Norm-POEM is surprisingly faster than POEM; the cause of this speedup should be further explored.
- Notation and commentary around equation (2) are unclear and contain technically incorrect statements.
- The estimator in equation (2) is weak because its denominator can be zero if a sample was never observed in the prior.
- The modified estimator in equation (7) is a better principle but its derivation needs clearer explanation.
- The contribution is original—a novel combination of familiar techniques—and related work is adequately referenced.
- Overall assessment: strong, impactful paper that requires minor improvements in notation and derivation clarity.
