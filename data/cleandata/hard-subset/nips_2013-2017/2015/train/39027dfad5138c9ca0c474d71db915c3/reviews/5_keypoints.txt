- Proposes a method for batch learning from logged bandit feedback that avoids propensity overfitting.  
- Builds on Swaminathan & Joachims (2015) and yields a significantly better algorithm.  
- Research quality is above average with meaningful extensions to POEM.  
- Proposed extensions lead to significant improvements in training the original POEM.  
- Paper is for the most part clearly written and easy to follow.  
- Offers a significantly new formulation of POEM; contributions are original.  
- Results are interesting and warrant further research.  
- Algorithm is faster than original POEM despite added regularization and may see widespread use.
