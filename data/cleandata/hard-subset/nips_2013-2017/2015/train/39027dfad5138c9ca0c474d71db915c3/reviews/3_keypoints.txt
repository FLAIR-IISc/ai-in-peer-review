- Paper addresses batch learning from logged bandit feedback, identifies CRM overfitting, and proposes a multiplicative control-variate (self-normalizing) solution that yields a biased estimator.  
- High-quality, well-motivated work, but reviewer expected more experiments and deeper analysis of training-objective optimization.  
- Presentation is clear and the paper is well written.  
- Contribution is follow-up work on prior paper [1], with the main novelty being the self-normalizing risk estimator which improves empirical performance.  
- Significance is uncertain: problem is relevant, but unclear how experimental gains translate to real-world applications.  
- Reviewer requests variance reporting for repeated runs and more extensive experiments, especially additional and real-world applications beyond those in [1].
