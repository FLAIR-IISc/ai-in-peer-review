- Proposes a Coherent Recurrent Convolutional Network (CRCN) combining CNNs, bidirectional RNNs, and an entity-based local coherence model to generate sentences from an image stream.  
- Overall a nice/interesting piece of work.  
- Several related video-to-sentence works are missing from the literature review (e.g., "Jointly modeling deep video and compositional text...").  
- Quantitative results reported for the proposed method look quite good.  
- User study results (Table 2) show the method performs similarly to the RCN baseline, indicating only a weak advantage.  
- A statistical significance test is needed to determine whether the reported improvements are reliable.
