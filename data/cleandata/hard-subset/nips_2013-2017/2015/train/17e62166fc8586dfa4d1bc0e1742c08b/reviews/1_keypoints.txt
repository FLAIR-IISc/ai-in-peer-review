- Problem is significant and related to video-to-text/multi-sentence generation but closely mirrors prior video captioning work.  
- Important prior work (e.g., Rohrbach et al. 2014 and related multi-sentence/video description papers) is not cited.  
- Paper is well written and easy to follow; problem statement and solution are clear.  
- Proposed CRCN model is technically sound and captures discourse relationships using entity-based coherence.  
- Equation (2) appears incorrect/implausible because it suggests st depends only on ot, implying a not fully connected network.  
- Method for generating sentence sequences is reasonable but primitive and ad-hoc (retrieving sentence per image and simple concatenation).  
- Dataset description is insufficient and may breach anonymity/confidentiality (reuse of blog data from [11] and undisclosed crawling details).  
- Recommendation: acceptable as a poster paper.
