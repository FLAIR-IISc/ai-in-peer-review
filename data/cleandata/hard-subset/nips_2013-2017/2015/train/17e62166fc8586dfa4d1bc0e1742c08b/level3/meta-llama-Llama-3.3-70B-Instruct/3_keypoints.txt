- Proposes extending Generalized LASSO to non-linear measurements with precise asymptotic error analysis.
- Main contribution: shows the Generalized LASSO error under non-linear measurements equals the error under appropriately scaled noisy linear measurements in the large-dimension limit.
- Provides a simple and efficient method to compute the optimal regularization parameter.
- Paper is well-written with a clear, concise introduction and organized technical sections.
- Includes numerical examples that validate the theoretical predictions.
- Strength: delivers a precise, quantitative performance analysis for non-linear measurement settings.
- Strength: situates the work within related literature, providing clear context.
- Weakness: analysis is asymptotic, leaving finite-dimensional performance unclear.
- Weakness: relies on i.i.d. Gaussian measurement matrix assumption, which may limit practical applicability.
- Weakness: assumes the link function is unknown, which may not hold in some applications.
- Suggestion: improve clarity and organization of dense technical sections and add more intuition/motivation.
- Suggestion: expand discussion of related work and practical implications of the results.
- Recommendation: Accept with revisions.
