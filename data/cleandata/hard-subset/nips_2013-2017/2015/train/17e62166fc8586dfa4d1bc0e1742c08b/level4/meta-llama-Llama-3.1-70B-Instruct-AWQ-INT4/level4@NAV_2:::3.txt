This paper proposes an approach for generating sentence sequences from an image stream, extending both input and output dimensions to more elaborate forms compared to existing methods. The authors design a multimodal architecture called coherent recurrent convolutional network (CRCN), which integrates convolutional neural networks, bidirectional recurrent neural networks, and an entity-based local coherence model. The CRCN model is trained on a large dataset of unstructured blog posts, consisting of 20K blog posts with 140K associated images. The authors evaluate their approach using quantitative measures and user studies via Amazon Mechanical Turk, demonstrating superior performance compared to other state-of-the-art alternatives.
