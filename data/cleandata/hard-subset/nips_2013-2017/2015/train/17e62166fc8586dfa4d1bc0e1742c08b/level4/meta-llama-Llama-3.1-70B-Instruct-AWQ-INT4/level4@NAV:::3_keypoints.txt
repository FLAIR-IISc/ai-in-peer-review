Proposes a coherent recurrent convolutional network (CRCN) that combines CNNs, bidirectional RNNs, and an entity-based local coherence model for sentence generation from image streams.  
Literature review is incomplete, omitting relevant prior work (e.g., joint deep video and compositional text models bridging vision and language).  
Quantitative results are strong, but the user study (Table 2) shows performance comparable to the RCN baseline, indicating only a marginal advantage.  
A statistical significance test on the user-study results is necessary to confirm the reliability of the reported improvement.
