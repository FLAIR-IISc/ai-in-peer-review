- Problem is significant, relevant to CV and NLP, and well-stated/tackled with solid techniques.
- Paper is well-written and the proposed approach is clearly explained.
- CRCN model is technically sound for learning relationships between image and sentence sequences.
- Entity-based coherence model is a strong contribution for capturing discourse relationships.
- Omits related work on video description (e.g., Rohrbach et al. 2014), a significant oversight given relevance.
- Final sentence-generation method is primitive/ad-hoc (retrieve per-image sentences and concatenate), weaker than the modeling component.
- Possible anonymity breach: reuse of non-public dataset and identical crawling method to [11] suggests same authors, harming double-blind review.
- Potential error in Equation (2): formulation implies s_t depends only on o_t, contradicting recurrent network description and needs correction.
- Despite issues, core technical contribution is valuable; reviewer recommends acceptance as a poster.
