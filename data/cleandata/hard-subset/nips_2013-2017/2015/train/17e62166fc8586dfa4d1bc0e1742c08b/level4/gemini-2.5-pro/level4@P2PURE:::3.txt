This paper proposes a method for generating sentences from an image stream using a coherent recurrent convolutional network (CRCN). The model architecture combines convolutional neural networks, bidirectional recurrent neural networks, and an entity-based local coherence model.

Overall, this is a solid contribution, but the paper could be strengthened in the following areas:

*   The literature review is missing some relevant work on the closely related task of video-to-sentence generation. For example, the authors should consider discussing "Jointly modeling deep video and compositional text to bridge vision and language in a unified framework."

*   While the quantitative results based on automatic metrics appear strong, the user study in Table 2 shows that the proposed method performs similarly to the RCN baseline. The authors should conduct statistical significance tests to verify whether the reported improvement is reliable and not due to chance.
