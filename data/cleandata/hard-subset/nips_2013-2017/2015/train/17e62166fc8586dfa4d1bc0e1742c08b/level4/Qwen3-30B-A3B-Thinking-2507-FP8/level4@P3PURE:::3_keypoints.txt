- Proposes CRCN: integrates CNNs, bidirectional RNNs, and an entity-based local coherence model for sentence generation from image streams.  
- Approach is well-motivated and technically sound.  
- Related work omits important prior video-to-sentence efforts (e.g., "Jointly modeling deep video and compositional text..."), weakening novelty context.  
- Quantitative results look promising but user study (Table 2) shows performance comparable to the RCN baseline with only a modest advantage.  
- Requires statistical significance testing to confirm improvements are reliable and not due to random variation.  
- Overall algorithm is well-designed, but the evaluation must be substantially strengthened to support the paper's claims.
