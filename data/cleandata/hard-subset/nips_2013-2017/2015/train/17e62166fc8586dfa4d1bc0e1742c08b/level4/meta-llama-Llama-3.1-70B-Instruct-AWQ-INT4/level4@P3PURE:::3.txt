This work studies how to generate sentences from an image stream. It designs a coherent recurrent convolutional network (CRCN), which consists of convolutional neural networks, bidirectional recurrent neural networks, and an entity-based local coherence model.

Overall, it is a nice work, although it can be improved from the following aspects: 

* Several related works about video-to-sentence generation are missing, e.g., "Jointly modeling deep video and compositional text to bridge vision and language in a unified framework". Including these references would strengthen the paper's literature review.

* While the quantitative results of the proposed method look quite good, the user study in Table 2 shows that it performs similarly to one baseline, RCN. A significance test is needed to verify whether the improvement is reliable.

The algorithm for sentence generation from an image stream is nice, and the quantitative results of the algorithm look good. However, the user study only shows a weak advantage over the baselines.
