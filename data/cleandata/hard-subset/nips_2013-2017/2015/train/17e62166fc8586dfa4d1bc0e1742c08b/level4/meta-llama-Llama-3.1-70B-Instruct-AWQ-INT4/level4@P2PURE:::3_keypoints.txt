- Proposes CRCN for sentence generation from image streams, combining CNNs, bidirectional RNNs, and an entity-based local coherence model.  
- Overall well-conceived study with a nicely designed sentence-generation algorithm.  
- Quantitative results are encouraging, showing promise for the proposed method.  
- Omits discussion of related video-to-text works that jointly model deep video and compositional text; adding these references would improve context.  
- User study (Table 2) shows performance comparable to baseline RCN, indicating only a marginal advantage.  
- Recommends conducting statistical significance tests to verify the reliability of the reported improvement.
