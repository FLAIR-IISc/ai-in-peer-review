This work discusses how sentences can be generated from an image sequence. It designs a Coherent Recurrent Convolutional Network (CRCN), which is a convolutional neural network, a bidirectional recurrent neural network, and an entity-based local cohesion model.

Overall, it is a good work, although it could be improved in the following ways: * Several related works on generating sentences from video are missing, such as jointly modeling deep video and textual content to establish a link between vision and language in an integrated framework.

* Although the quantitative results of the proposed method appear quite good, user research in Table 2 shows that it performs similarly to a baseline, RCN. A significance test is needed to verify whether the improvement is reliable. An excellent algorithm for generating sentences from an image sequence. Although the quantitative results of the algorithm appear good, user research only shows weaker benefits than the baseline.
