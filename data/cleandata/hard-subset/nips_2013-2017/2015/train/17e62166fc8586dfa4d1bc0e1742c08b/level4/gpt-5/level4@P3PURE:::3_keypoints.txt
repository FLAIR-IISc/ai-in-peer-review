- Paper studies sentence generation from image streams and proposes a Coherent Recurrent Convolutional Network (CRCN) combining CNNs, bidirectional RNNs, and an entity-based local coherence model.
- Strength: problem is well motivated and the CRCN architecture is coherent and clearly presented.
- Strength: quantitative results are strong on standard metrics.
- Weakness: related work on video-to-sentence generation is incomplete; missing discussion/citation of “Jointly modeling deep video and compositional text…” and other relevant prior work.
- Weakness: user study (Table 2) shows performance similar to the RCN baseline, indicating only a weak advantage.
- Recommendation: perform statistical significance tests to verify whether observed user-study improvements over RCN are reliable.
- Overall: solid algorithm with good quantitative performance, but needs broader related-work coverage and significance analysis for user-study claims.
