This paper explores generating sentences from an image stream. It proposes a coherent recurrent convolutional network (CRCN) that integrates convolutional neural networks, bidirectional recurrent neural networks, and an entity-based local coherence model.

Overall, it is a solid contribution, though it could be improved in the following ways:
* Some relevant video-to-sentence work is missing, e.g., Jointly modeling deep video and compositional text to bridge vision and language in a unified framework.
* While the proposed method achieves strong quantitative performance, the user study in Table 2 shows results similar to the RCN baseline. A statistical significance test is needed to confirm that the improvements are reliable.

A nice algorithm for generating sentences from an image stream. The quantitative results are promising, but the user study shows only a modest advantage over the baselines.
