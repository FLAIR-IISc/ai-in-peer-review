- Proposes CRCN combining CNNs, bidirectional RNNs, and an entity-based local coherence model to generate sentences from image sequences.
- Literature review omits relevant video-to-sentence studies, including unified frameworks that jointly model deep video and compositional text.
- Quantitative results are promising but user study (Table 2) shows similar performance to the RCN baseline.
- The paper needs statistical significance testing to confirm the reported improvements.
- Overall, the algorithm is noteworthy with favorable metrics but offers only a marginal advantage over baselines in human evaluation.
