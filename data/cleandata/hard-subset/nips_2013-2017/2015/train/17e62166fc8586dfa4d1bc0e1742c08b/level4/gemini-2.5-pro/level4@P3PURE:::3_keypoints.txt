- Proposes CRCN: a method combining CNNs, bidirectional RNNs, and an entity-based local coherence model to generate sentences from image streams.  
- Novel algorithm and overall solid contribution.  
- Quantitative results appear strong.  
- Related work discussion is incomplete; several video-to-sentence papers (e.g., "Jointly Modeling Deep Video and Compositional Text...") are missing.  
- Human evaluation (Table 2) shows similar performance to the RCN baseline.  
- Authors should run statistical significance tests on user study results to confirm claimed improvements.  
- Experimental validation needs more rigor to fully substantiate superiority over baselines.
