This work addresses the problem of generating descriptive sentences from image streams using a novel architecture called a Coherent Recurrent Convolutional Network (CRCN). The CRCN integrates convolutional neural networks, bidirectional recurrent neural networks, and an entity-based local coherence model to produce more fluent and contextually relevant captions.
