This work studies how to generate sentences from image streams. It designs a consistent recurrent convolutional network (CRCN) that includes convolutional neural networks, bidirectional recurrent neural networks, and a unit-based local stability model.

Overall, it's a good work, although it could be improved in the following aspects: * Several related tasks are missing when creating sentences from video, for example, jointly modeling intensive video and creative text to connect vision and language in an integrated framework.

* The quantitative results of the proposed method seem quite good, but user studies in Table 2 show that it performs similarly to a baseline, RCN. A significance test is needed to verify whether the improvement is reliable. Good algorithm for sentence construction from image streams.
