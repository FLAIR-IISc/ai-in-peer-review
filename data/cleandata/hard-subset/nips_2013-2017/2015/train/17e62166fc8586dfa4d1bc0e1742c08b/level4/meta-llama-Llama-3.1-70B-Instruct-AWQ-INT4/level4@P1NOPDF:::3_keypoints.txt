- Proposes CRCN combining CNNs, bidirectional RNNs, and an entity-based local coherence model for sentence generation from image streams.
- Overall promising and innovative approach with strong quantitative results.
- Misses citing relevant video-to-sentence literature (e.g., "Jointly modeling deep video and compositional text..."); needs a more comprehensive related-work section.
- User study (Table 2) shows CRCN is comparable to baseline RCN, providing only a marginal advantage in human evaluation.
- Authors should run statistical significance tests to verify whether observed improvements are meaningful.
- Several areas (literature coverage, evaluation rigor) need improvement despite the method's promise.
