- Paper addresses sentence generation from image streams and proposes a Coherent Recurrent Convolutional Network (CRCN).  
- CRCN integrates convolutional neural networks, bidirectional recurrent neural networks, and an entity-based local coherence model.  
- Overall a solid contribution with a promising algorithmic approach.  
- Quantitative results are strong and promising.  
- Relevant prior video-to-sentence work is missing (e.g., jointly modeling deep video and compositional text).  
- User study (Table 2) shows only a modest advantage over the RCN baseline.  
- A statistical significance test is needed to confirm that observed improvements are reliable.
