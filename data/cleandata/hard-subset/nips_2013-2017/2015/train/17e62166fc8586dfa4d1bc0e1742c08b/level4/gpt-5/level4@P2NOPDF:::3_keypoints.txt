- Introduces CRCN for sentence generation from image streams, combining CNN visual encoding, bidirectional RNNs, and an entity-based local coherence component.  
- Approach is well-motivated and technically sound.  
- Strength: Clear problem formulation and principled architecture that explicitly targets local coherence.  
- Strength: Strong quantitative results on standard metrics, showing model effectiveness.  
- Weakness: Related work coverage is incomplete—missing comparison to prior video-to-text work (notably “Jointly Modeling Deep Video and Compositional Text…”); needs conceptual and empirical positioning vs. that approach.  
- Weakness: Human evaluation results are close to the RCN baseline; statistical significance testing (p-values/confidence intervals) and effect size discussion are needed to assess reliability and practical importance.  
- Overall: Promising architecture and quantitative performance, but human-study modest gains and expanded related work plus significance analyses would substantially improve the paper.
