- Introduces CRCN: a model combining CNNs, bidirectional RNNs, and an entity-based local coherence component for sentence generation from image streams.  
- Overall a solid contribution that could be further developed.  
- Related work section omits relevant video-to-sentence papers and unified deep video–compositional text frameworks.  
- Quantitative results look promising, but the user study (Table 2) shows performance comparable to the RCN baseline.  
- A statistical significance test is needed to verify whether observed improvements are reliable.  
- Conclusion: the method is a good approach but yields only a marginal advantage over existing baselines.
