- Proposes CRCN: combines CNNs, bidirectional RNNs, and an entity-based local coherence model for sentence generation from image streams.  
- Related work omits key video-to-sentence literature, notably "Jointly modeling deep video and compositional text..."  
- Quantitative results are strong overall, but Table 2 shows the user study yields comparable results to the RCN baseline.  
- A statistical significance test is required to confirm whether the reported improvements are reliable.  
- Overall: promising approach with strong metrics but only a marginal advantage in human evaluation.
