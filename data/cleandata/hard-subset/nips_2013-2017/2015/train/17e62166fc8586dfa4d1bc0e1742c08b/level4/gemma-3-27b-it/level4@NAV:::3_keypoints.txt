- Proposes CRCN: CNNs + bidirectional RNNs + entity-based local coherence model for sentence generation from image streams.
- Overall a promising contribution to the task.
- Manuscript lacks a comprehensive related-work review, especially papers that jointly model deep video and compositional text.
- Quantitative results look favorable but the user study (Table 2) shows performance comparable to the RCN baseline.
- A statistical significance test is needed to validate observed improvements.
- Conclusion: the algorithm has potential but shows only a marginal advantage over existing approaches in the user study.
