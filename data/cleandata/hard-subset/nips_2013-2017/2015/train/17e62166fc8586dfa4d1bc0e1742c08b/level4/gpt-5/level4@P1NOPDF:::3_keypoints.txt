- Well-motivated architecture that combines CNNs, bidirectional RNNs, and an entity-based local coherence model to maintain cross-sentence coherence.
- Integration of an entity-based local coherence model is appealing for preserving coherence across sentences.
- Strong quantitative results indicating the method is effective.
- Missing discussion of relevant video-to-sentence prior work—specifically needs to position relative to “Jointly modeling deep video and compositional text…” and related references.
- Human evaluation (Table 2) shows performance similar to the RCN baseline, suggesting only a weak observed advantage.
- A statistical significance test is needed to verify whether the reported human-study improvements are reliable.
- Overall, promising algorithm and quantitative performance, but benefits over baselines are modest and the paper needs more related-work discussion and statistical testing.
