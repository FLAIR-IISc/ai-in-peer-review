This work discusses how to generate sentences from an image stream. It designs a Coherent Recurrent Convolutional Network (CRCN), which consists of a convolutional neural network, a bidirectional recurrent neural network, and an entity-based local coherence model.

Overall, it is a good work, although it could be improved in the following ways: * Several related works on generating sentences from video are missing, such as jointly modeling deep video and compositional text to establish a link between vision and language in an integrated framework.

* Although the quantitative results of the proposed method appear quite good, user research in Table 2 shows that it works similarly to a baseline, RCN. A significance test is required to verify whether the improvement is reliable. Excellent algorithm for generating sentences from an image stream.
