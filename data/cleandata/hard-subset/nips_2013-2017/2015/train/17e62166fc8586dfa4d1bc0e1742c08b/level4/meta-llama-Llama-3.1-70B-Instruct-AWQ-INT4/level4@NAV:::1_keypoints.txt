- Task addresses retrieving sentences for image sequences and generating coherent sentence sequences; relates to video description but fails to cite relevant work (e.g., Rohrbach et al. 2014).  
- Paper is well-organized with a clear problem statement and solid technical approach.  
- CRCN neural network effectively models relationships between image and sentence sequences using entity-based coherence models.  
- Model architecture is technically sound, but sentence-sequence generation is simplistic, based on direct sentence–image associations and concatenation.  
- Data collection raises anonymity and reproducibility concerns: dataset and crawling method are undisclosed and authors appear closely affiliated with [11].  
- Equation (2) may contain an error, implying incomplete network connectivity.  
- Despite limitations, the model shows technical merit and captures discourse relationships; overall suitable for acceptance as a poster.
