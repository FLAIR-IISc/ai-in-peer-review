- Proposes CRCN for generating sentences from image streams, combining CNNs, bidirectional RNNs, and an entity-based local coherence model.
- Work is well-motivated but the literature review is limited and should include more video-to-sentence related works (e.g., "Jointly modeling deep video and compositional text...").
- Quantitative results appear promising.
- User study (Table 2) shows CRCN performs similarly to the RCN baseline.
- Authors should run statistical significance tests to verify the reliability of reported improvements.
- The user study indicates only a weak advantage over baselines, tempering the impact of the quantitative results.
