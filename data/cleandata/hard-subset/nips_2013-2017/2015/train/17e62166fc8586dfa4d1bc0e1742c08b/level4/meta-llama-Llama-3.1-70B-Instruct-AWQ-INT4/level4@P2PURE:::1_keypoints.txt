- Addresses retrieval of natural sentences for image sequences, a significant problem related to video captioning, but fails to cite relevant prior work.
- Paper is well-written and easy to follow, with a clearly stated problem and solid proposed techniques.
- Proposed CRCN model effectively captures relationships between sentence and image sequences and models discourse with entity-based coherence.
- Sentence-generation method is primitive and ad-hoc, relying on nearest-training-image sentence lookup and simple concatenation.
- Data-collection transparency is lacking: reused and newly crawled datasets are not publicly disclosed and crawling details are missing, risking anonymity and reproducibility.
- Equation (2) appears incorrect, implying st depends only on ot and suggesting an incomplete network connection.
- Reviewer recommends acceptance as a poster despite the noted issues.
