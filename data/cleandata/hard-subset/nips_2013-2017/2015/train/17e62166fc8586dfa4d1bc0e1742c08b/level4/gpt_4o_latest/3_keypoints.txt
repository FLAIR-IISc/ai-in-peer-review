Paper tackles sentence generation from image streams and proposes CRCN combining CNNs, bidirectional RNNs, and an entity-based local coherence model.  
Overall a solid paper with strong quantitative results.  
Related work on video-to-sentence generation is missing (e.g., "Jointly modeling deep video and compositional text...").  
User study (Table 2) shows comparable performance to baseline RCN and requires statistical significance testing.  
In summary: well-designed algorithm but only a marginal advantage over baselines in user evaluation.
