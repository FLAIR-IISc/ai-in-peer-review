- Proposes CRCN: combines CNNs, bidirectional RNNs, and an entity-based local coherence model for image-stream-to-sentence generation.  
- Related work misses key video-to-sentence prior efforts (e.g., Chen et al.) needed to contextualize multimodal alignment and novelty.  
- Quantitative results (CIDEr) are promising but human evaluation shows only marginal gains over RCN baseline.  
- Human-study improvements may not be statistically significant; a paired significance test (e.g., t-test) is required.  
- Description of the entity-based local coherence model is insufficiently detailed.  
- Unclear how entity tracking integrates with the recurrent component and whether entities are updated dynamically or pre-extracted.  
- Overall algorithm is well-motivated and meaningful, but missing contextualization and weak user-study evidence undermine the paper’s impact.
