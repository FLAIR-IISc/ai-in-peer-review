- Proposes the Coherent Recurrent Convolutional Network (CRCN), a technically sound architecture.
- Inclusion of an entity-based coherence model is a notable strength for modeling discourse relationships.
- Sentence-generation method (retrieving and concatenating training sentences) is simplistic and limits novel or nuanced descriptions.
