- Task and contribution: Proposes CRCN for sentence generation from image streams, combining CNNs, bidirectional RNNs, and an entity-based local coherence model.  
- Overall assessment: Strong contribution with room for improvements.  
- Related work omission: Related work misses relevant video-to-sentence papers (e.g., jointly modeling deep video and compositional text).  
- Quantitative results: Reported quantitative results are promising.  
- User study concerns: User study (Table 2) shows performance comparable to baseline RCN and needs statistical significance testing.  
- Summary: Well-designed algorithm with encouraging quantitative results but only marginal advantage over existing baselines in user study.
