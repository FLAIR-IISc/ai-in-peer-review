- Addresses retrieval and generation of natural sentences for image sequences (important problem), but fails to cite related multi-sentence video description work (e.g., Rohrbach et al. 2014).  
- Paper is well-structured and clear with a succinct problem statement.  
- Proposed CRCN model is technically sound and effectively models discourse relationships using entity-based coherence techniques.  
- Sentence-sequence generation is simplistic/ad-hoc, relying on matching sentences to similar training images and naive concatenation.  
- Dataset and data-collection details are undisclosed despite reusing/collecting data via a method from [11], raising anonymity and potential authorship overlap concerns.  
- Equation (2) may be erroneous, suggesting st is derived solely from ot and indicating possible missing connectivity in the network.  
- Overall assessment: paper could be acceptable as a poster in its current form.
