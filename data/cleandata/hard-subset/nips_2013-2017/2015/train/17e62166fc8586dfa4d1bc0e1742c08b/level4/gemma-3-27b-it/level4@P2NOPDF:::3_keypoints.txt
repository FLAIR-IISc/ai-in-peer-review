- Proposes CRCN for generating descriptive sentences from image streams.  
- CRCN combines CNNs, bidirectional RNNs, and an entity-based local coherence model for more fluent, context-aware captions.  
- Overall a strong contribution but several areas require further development.  
- Related work section is incomplete and omits recent video-to-sentence generation literature (missing citation).  
- Automated quantitative results are promising.  
- User study (Table 2) shows performance comparable to the RCN baseline, suggesting only marginal subjective gains.  
- A statistical significance test is needed to confirm whether user study improvements are reliable.  
- Addressing related-work gaps and providing rigorous statistical analysis would substantially strengthen the paper.
