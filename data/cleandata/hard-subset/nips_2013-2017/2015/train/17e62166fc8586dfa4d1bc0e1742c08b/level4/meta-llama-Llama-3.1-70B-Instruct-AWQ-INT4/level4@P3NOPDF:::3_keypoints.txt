- Paper proposes CRCN for image-stream-to-sentence generation, combining CNNs, bidirectional RNNs, and an entity-based local coherence model.  
- Work is well-motivated and contributes to vision-to-language research.  
- Literature review is weak—should include more video-to-sentence generation works (e.g., jointly modeling deep video and compositional text).  
- Quantitative results are promising, but user study (Table 2) shows CRCN performs similarly to baseline RCN.  
- Perform statistical significance testing to validate performance improvements.  
- Algorithmic contribution is notable, but the user study only shows a weak advantage; additional evaluations or deeper analyses are needed.
