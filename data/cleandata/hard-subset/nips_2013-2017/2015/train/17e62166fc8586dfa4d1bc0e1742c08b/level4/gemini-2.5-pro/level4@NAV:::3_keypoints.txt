- Introduces CRCN: a novel architecture combining CNNs, bidirectional RNNs, and an entity-based local coherence model for generating sentences from image streams.
- Overall a solid contribution.
- Related work discussion is incomplete; omits key video-to-sentence studies (e.g., "Jointly modeling deep video and compositional text...") and should be expanded.
- Automated metrics report strong quantitative results, but human evaluation (Table 2) shows performance comparable to the RCN baseline.
- A statistical significance test is necessary to confirm that observed gains are reliable.
- Conclusion: interesting algorithm with impressive quantitative results, but claims of superiority need clearer user-study advantage supported by statistical analysis.
