- Introduces CRCN: combines CNNs, bidirectional RNNs, and an entity-based local coherence component to generate sentences for an image stream.
- Overall a solid, well-conceived algorithm for generating sentence sequences from image streams.
- Quantitative evaluation shows strong, encouraging metric results for the proposed method.
- Related-work gap: misses relevant video-to-sentence literature (e.g., “Jointly modeling deep video and compositional text…”).
- Human/user study results (Table 2) are similar to the RCN baseline; statistical significance testing is needed to confirm improvements.
