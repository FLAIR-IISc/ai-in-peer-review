- Proposal: Introduces CRCN for sentence generation from image streams, combining CNNs, bidirectional RNNs, and an entity-based local coherence model.
- Strength: Solid contribution with strong quantitative results.
- Missing related work: Several relevant video-to-sentence papers (e.g., “Jointly modeling deep video and compositional text…”) are not cited.
- User study concern: Table 2 shows results similar to the RCN baseline; a statistical significance test is needed to confirm improvements.
- Summary: Good algorithm and quantitative performance, but only a weak advantage in the user study compared to baselines.
