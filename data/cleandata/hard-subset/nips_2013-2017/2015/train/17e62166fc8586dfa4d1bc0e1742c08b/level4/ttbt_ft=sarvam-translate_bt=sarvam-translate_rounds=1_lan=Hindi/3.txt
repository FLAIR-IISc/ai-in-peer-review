This work studies how to generate sentences from an image stream. It designs a consistent recurrent convolutional network (CRCN) that includes convolutional neural networks, bidirectional recurrent neural networks, and a unit-based local consistency model.

Overall, it's a good work, although it could be improved in the following aspects: * Several related works on creating sentences from video are missing, for example, modeling deep video and compositional text jointly to connect vision and language in an integrated framework.

* The quantitative results of the proposed method appear quite good, but user studies in Table 2 show that it performs similarly to a baseline, RCN. A significance test is needed to verify whether the improvement is reliable. Good algorithm for sentence construction from image streams.
