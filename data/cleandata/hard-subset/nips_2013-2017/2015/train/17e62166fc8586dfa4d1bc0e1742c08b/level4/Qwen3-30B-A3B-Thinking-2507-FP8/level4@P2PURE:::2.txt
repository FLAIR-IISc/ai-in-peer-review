

Strengths / positive aspects:  
- The paper proposes a novel and effective architecture for retrieving coherent sentence sequences to describe image sequences from blog posts.  
- It provides extensive quantitative, qualitative, and human evaluations, demonstrating clear superiority over baselines that do not incorporate coherence. An ablation study removing the coherence model [1] further validates the approach.  
- The authors commit to releasing source code and the dataset.  

Weaknesses / Questions / Unclarities:  
1. Line 94 claims [5] lacks "a mechanism for coherence between sentences." While not [5]'s focus, its method predicts a coherent intermediate semantic representation for videos by modeling the topic across multi-sentence descriptions.  
2. Figure 2b appears inconsistent with Section 3.3/Equation (2). Figure 2b suggests the fully connected layer connects to all sentences, whereas Equation (2) indicates shared parameters across sentences but only a single-sentence vector input.  
3. Meteor (http://www.cs.cmu.edu/~alavie/METEOR/) is a more appropriate automatic metric than BLEU for evaluating generated sentences, especially when only a single reference sentence is available.  
4. The use of two sequential linear functions (W{f2}, W{f1}) in Equation (2) is redundant, as two linear transformations compose to a single linear function. An ablation study demonstrating their benefit would clarify this design choice.  
5. The same fully connected layer parameters are applied to both the BRNN output (o_t) and the local coherence model (q) in Equation (2). The rationale for parameter sharing between these distinct components is unclear.  
6. Is the paragraph vector [16] fine-tuned during training, or kept fixed?  

=== post rebuttal ===  
After reviewing the authors' rebuttal, I recommend acceptance. The authors have successfully addressed all formulation, evaluation, and related work concerns raised. The paper presents a compelling new model for coherent sentence retrieval over image streams, convincingly validated through thorough experiments. To finalize, the clarification on whether the paragraph vector [16] is fine-tuned or fixed must be provided.
