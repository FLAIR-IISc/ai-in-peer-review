- Proposes new synchronous and asynchronous randomized gossip algorithms (GoSta-sync, GoSta-async) for decentralized computation of U-statistics.
- Introduces "phantom nodes" to model data propagation and averaging steps in the algorithms.
- Provides rigorous convergence analysis with explicit data- and network-dependent bounds.
- Demonstrates empirically that the proposed algorithms outperform existing methods, including U2-gossip.
