This paper introduces novel synchronous and asynchronous gossip algorithms for computing U-statistics. The authors derive theoretical convergence rates for both algorithms, demonstrating 1/t convergence for the synchronous approach and log t / t convergence for the asynchronous version.

The paper is well-structured and clearly presents the proposed algorithms alongside supporting experimental results. The topic of distributed U-statistic estimation is relevant to a range of research areas, and the presented techniques offer a valuable contribution. Specifically, the introduction of these new gossip algorithms provides a practical and theoretically sound method for estimating U-statistics with demonstrably favorable convergence properties. 

Experimental evaluation effectively showcases the performance advantages of the proposed algorithms compared to existing methods, highlighting their accuracy in practical settings. The results provide compelling evidence of the effectiveness of both the synchronous and asynchronous approaches.
