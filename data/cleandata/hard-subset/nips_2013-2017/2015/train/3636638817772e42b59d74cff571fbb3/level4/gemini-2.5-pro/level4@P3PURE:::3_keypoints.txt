- Proposes synchronous and asynchronous randomized gossip algorithms for computing U-statistics.
- Paper is well-motivated, clearly written, and provides theoretical convergence bounds.
- Empirical results on two datasets (average relative AUC error) show the method significantly outperforms the U2-gossip baseline across varied graph structures.
- Primary weakness: no comparison of run-time per iteration between the proposed methods and U2-gossip.
- Without per-iteration timing, practical utility and overall efficiency are unclear; adding these comparisons would substantially strengthen the paper.
