This paper is well-motivated and written. The results of their approach are presented on 2 datasets (evaluated on average relative AUC error). Compared to the baseline (U2-gossip), their method performs much better in various scenarios (graphs). The paper provides convergence limits for their method. - I would like to see the run-time per iteration for the proposed and U2-gossip methods. If each iteration of the proposed method is much slower than U2-gossip, the paper's usefulness will be limited. The paper proposes a sync and async random gossip algorithm for calculating U-statistics.
