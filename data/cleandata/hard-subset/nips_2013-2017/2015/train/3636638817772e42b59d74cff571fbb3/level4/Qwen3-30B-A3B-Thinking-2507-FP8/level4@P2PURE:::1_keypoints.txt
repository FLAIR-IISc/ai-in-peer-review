- Introduces novel Gossip algorithms for computing U-statistics with clear motivation and strong positioning against prior work.

- Achieves faster convergence rates than the state-of-the-art U2-Gossip (Pelckmans & Suykens, 2009).

- Demonstrates superior performance in realistic asynchronous settings.

- Offers reduced computational cost in memory and communication compared to U2-Gossip.

- Provides theoretical guarantees and analysis supporting the algorithms.

- Validates advantages through extensive empirical evaluation on two datasets.

- Paper is well-written and thoroughly argued.
