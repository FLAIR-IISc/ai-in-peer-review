The research paper is well-motivated and written. The results of their method are presented on two datasets (evaluated based on average relative AUC error). Compared to the baseline (U2-gossip), their method performs much better in various situations (graphs). The research paper provides convergent limits for their method. - I want to see the run-time for each iteration of the proposed and U2-gossip methods. If each iteration of the proposed method is much slower than U2-gossip, the utility of the research paper will be limited. The research paper proposes a sync and async randomized gossip algorithm for U-statistics computation.
