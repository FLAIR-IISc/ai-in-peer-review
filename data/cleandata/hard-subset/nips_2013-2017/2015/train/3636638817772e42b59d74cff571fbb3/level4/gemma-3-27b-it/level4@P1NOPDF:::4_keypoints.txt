- Paper addresses distributed average computation with an intuitive algorithm but offers limited novelty.
- Proofs and theoretical analysis are comprehensive and well-structured.
- Major weakness: no comparison to baseline methods.
- Recommend including simple baselines (central aggregator), randomized baselines, iterative averaging, and weighted averaging by node reliability.
- Empirical evaluation is weak due to absence of comparative analysis and demonstration of performance gains over simpler approaches.
- Practical implications of theoretical results are underexplored.
- Need discussion of performance in realistic scenarios (heterogeneous networks, varying node failure rates).
- Require sensitivity analysis and guidance on choosing algorithm parameters for different networks and problem instances.
- Overall: theoretically sound but weakened by lack of empirical baselines and limited practical discussion; addressing these would improve the contribution.
