This paper presents a new gossip algorithm for U-statistics computation. The proposed algorithms have faster convergence rates compared to state-of-the-art algorithms (from Pelcomans, Suenchens'09 to the U2-Gossip algorithm) and work in more realistic asynchronous settings. Experimental results further demonstrate the superiority of the proposed algorithms compared to existing algorithms.

The paper is well-written, the problem is well-motivated, and the authors have placed their work well in relation to existing literature. As far as I can see, the proposed algorithms are new and superior in terms of convergence and cost (memory and communication compared to U2-Gossip). Additionally, the proposed algorithms work in asynchronous settings.

In addition to theoretical certainty and analysis, the authors have conducted extensive empirical evaluations on two datasets and compared them with the baseline. This further strengthens the paper's contribution. This paper presents a new gossip algorithm for U-statistics computation, which is useful for fast convergence rates and realistic asynchronous settings. Furthermore, the algorithms have been evaluated through extensive testing and compared to state-of-the-art algorithms.
