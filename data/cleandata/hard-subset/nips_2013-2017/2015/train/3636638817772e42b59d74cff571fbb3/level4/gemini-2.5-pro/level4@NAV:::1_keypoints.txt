- Proposes novel Gossip algorithms for computing U-statistics.  
- Achieves improved convergence rates compared to U2-Gossip (Pelckmans & Suykens, 2009).  
- Designed to operate effectively in practical asynchronous settings.  
- Demonstrates superior resource efficiency (memory and communication) relative to U2-Gossip.  
- Provides theoretical guarantees and convergence analysis.  
- Empirical validation across two datasets shows advantages over established baselines.  
- Paper is well-written, strongly motivated, and situates the work clearly within related literature.  
- Overall contribution: faster, resource-efficient, asynchronously operable algorithms with comprehensive experiments.
