This paper presents a new gossip algorithm for computing U-statistics. The proposed algorithm has faster convergence rates compared to state-of-the-art algorithms (the U2-Gossip algorithm from Pelkman, Susseken's09) and works even in more realistic asynchronous settings. Experimental results further demonstrate the superiority of the proposed algorithm compared to existing algorithms.

The paper is well-written, the problem is well-motivated, and the authors have well-established their work in relation to existing literature. As far as I can see, the proposed algorithms are novel and perform better in terms of convergence and cost (memory and communication compared to U2-Gossip). Furthermore, the proposed algorithms work for asynchronous settings.

In addition to theoretical guarantees and analysis, the authors have also conducted extensive empirical evaluations on two datasets and compared the baselines. This further strengthens the paper's contribution. This paper presents a new gossip algorithm for computing U-statistics, with algorithms for real asynchronous settings along with fast convergence rates. Furthermore, the algorithm is evaluated and compared to state-of-the-art algorithms through extensive experiments.
