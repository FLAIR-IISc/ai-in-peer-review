The paper is well motivated and clearly written. The approach is evaluated on two datasets using average relative AUC error. Compared to the U2-gossip baseline, the proposed method performs substantially better across varied graph scenarios. The paper also provides convergence bounds for the method. I would like to see per-iteration run times for both the proposed method and U2-gossip; if each iteration of the proposed method is much slower than U2-gossip, the practical utility would be limited. The paper proposes synchronous and asynchronous randomized gossip algorithms for computing U-statistics.
