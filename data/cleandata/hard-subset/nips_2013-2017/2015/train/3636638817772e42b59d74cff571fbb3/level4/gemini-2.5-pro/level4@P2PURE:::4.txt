This paper addresses the problem of computing average statistics in a distributed environment. The proposed algorithm is intuitive, which may limit its novelty, though the accompanying proofs are comprehensive.

The primary weakness of the paper is its experimental evaluation, which lacks any comparison to baseline methods. Several straightforward baselines, such as those based on randomized algorithms, could have been implemented and should be considered for a more thorough comparison. While the paper tackles an interesting problem, the evaluation is weak without these baselines, making it difficult to assess the practical significance of the proposed method.
