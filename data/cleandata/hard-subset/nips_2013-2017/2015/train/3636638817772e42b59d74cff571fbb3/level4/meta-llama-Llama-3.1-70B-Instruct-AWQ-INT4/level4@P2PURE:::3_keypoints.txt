- Paper is well-motivated and well-written.  
- Presents results on two datasets evaluated using average relative AUC error.  
- Proposed method significantly outperforms baseline U2-gossip across varied scenarios and graph structures.  
- Provides convergence bounds for the proposed approach.  
- Reviewer requests per-iteration run-time comparison with U2-gossip, noting potential utility limits if iterations are substantially slower.  
- Authors introduce both synchronous and asynchronous randomized gossip algorithms for computing U-statistics (notable contribution).
