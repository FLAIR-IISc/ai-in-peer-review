This paper addresses the problem of computing average statistics within a distributed system. The proposed algorithm is conceptually straightforward, and while the mathematical proofs are thorough, the novelty of the approach feels limited. 

A key area for improvement lies in the evaluation. The paper currently lacks comparison to any baseline methods. Several readily implementable baselines, leveraging randomized algorithms for instance, would provide valuable context and strengthen the empirical validation. While the problem addressed is of interest, the absence of baseline comparisons significantly weakens the evaluation and overall impact of the work.
