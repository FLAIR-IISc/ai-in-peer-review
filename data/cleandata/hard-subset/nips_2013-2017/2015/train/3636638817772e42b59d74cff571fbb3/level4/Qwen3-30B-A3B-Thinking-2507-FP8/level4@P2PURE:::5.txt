

The paper introduces synchronous and asynchronous gossip algorithms for estimating U-statistics, deriving convergence rates of $O(1/t)$ and $O(\log t / t)$, respectively. The work is well-organized and demonstrates strong performance through theoretical results and experiments. The problem domain is of interest, and the proposed algorithms provide a valuable contribution applicable to various research communities. The experiments validate the algorithms' performance advantages over existing approaches and demonstrate their accuracy.
