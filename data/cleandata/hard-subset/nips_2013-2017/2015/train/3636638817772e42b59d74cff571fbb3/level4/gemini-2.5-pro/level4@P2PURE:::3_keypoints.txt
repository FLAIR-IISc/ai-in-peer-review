- Proposes synchronous and asynchronous randomized gossip algorithms for computing U-statistics.
- Paper is well-motivated and clearly written.
- Provides theoretical convergence bounds for the proposed methods.
- Experiments on two datasets (average relative AUC error) show the proposed algorithm significantly outperforms the U2-gossip baseline across various graph structures.
- Evaluation lacks a comparison of run-time per iteration between the proposed method and the U2-gossip baseline.
- Per-iteration computational cost is critical: a higher cost could negate practical utility despite improved convergence.
