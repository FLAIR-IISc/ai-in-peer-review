- Proposes synchronous and asynchronous randomized gossip algorithms for computing U-statistics in distributed systems.  
- Clearly motivates the work, highlighting limitations of existing gossip algorithms for U-statistics.  
- Paper is well-written with results reported on two datasets using average relative AUC error.  
- Demonstrates superiority over baseline U2-gossip across multiple scenarios and graph structures.  
- Provides convergence bounds for the proposed methods, a significant theoretical contribution.  
- Lacks run-time-per-iteration comparisons with U2-gossip, which is crucial for practical assessment.  
- Does not detail computational complexity, including memory and communication costs.  
- Evaluation is limited to two datasets; needs broader experiments on varied datasets and graph topologies.  
- Related-work discussion is thin; should more thoroughly compare existing gossip algorithms and their limitations.  
- Overall promising contribution, but addressing runtime, complexity, broader evaluation, and related work would strengthen the paper.
