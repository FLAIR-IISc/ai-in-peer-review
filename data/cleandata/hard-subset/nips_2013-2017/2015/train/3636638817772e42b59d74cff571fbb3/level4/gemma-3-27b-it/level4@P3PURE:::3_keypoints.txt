- Paper is well motivated and clearly written.  
- Experiments run on two datasets and evaluated with average relative AUC error.  
- Proposed method significantly outperforms the U2-gossip baseline across diverse graph structures.  
- Paper provides theoretical convergence bounds for the proposed method.  
- Authors propose synchronous and asynchronous randomized gossip algorithms for computing U-statistics.
