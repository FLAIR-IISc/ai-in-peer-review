- Introduces novel Gossip algorithms for computing U-statistics.  
- Achieves faster convergence rates than prior U2-Gossip (Pelckmans & Suykens, 2009).  
- Operates effectively in asynchronous network environments, increasing practical applicability.  
- Reduces computational cost in memory and communication compared to U2-Gossip.  
- Paper is clearly written, well-motivated, and situates contributions within existing literature.  
- Strong theoretical foundations and analysis supporting the algorithms.  
- Comprehensive empirical evaluation on two datasets demonstrating superior performance to baselines.  
- Overall, presents a valuable, practically relevant advancement in distributed computation of U-statistics.
