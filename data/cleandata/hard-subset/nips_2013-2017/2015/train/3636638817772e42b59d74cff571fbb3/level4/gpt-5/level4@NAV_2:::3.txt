The paper is clearly motivated and well written. Empirical results are reported on two datasets, evaluated using average relative AUC error. Compared with the U2-gossip baseline, the proposed method performs substantially better across different graph topologies. The paper also establishes convergence bounds for the method. - I would like to see per-iteration runtime comparisons for the proposed algorithms and U2-gossip. If each iteration of the proposed approach is significantly slower than U2-gossip, the practical utility would be limited. The paper introduces synchronous and asynchronous randomized gossip algorithms for computing U-statistics.
