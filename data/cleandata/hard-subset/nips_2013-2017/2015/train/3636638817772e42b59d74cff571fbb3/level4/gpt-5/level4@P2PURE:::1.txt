This paper introduces new gossip algorithms for computing U-statistics. The proposed methods achieve faster convergence than the state-of-the-art U2-Gossip algorithm of Pelckmans and Suykens (2009) and operate in more realistic asynchronous settings. The experimental results further demonstrate their superiority over existing approaches.

The paper is well written, the problem is well motivated, and the authors position their contribution clearly with respect to the existing literature. To the best of my knowledge, the algorithms are novel and superior in both convergence behavior and resource costs (memory and communication) compared with U2-Gossip. They also work in asynchronous settings.

Beyond the theoretical guarantees and analysis, the authors present extensive empirical evaluations on two datasets and compare against relevant baselines, which further strengthens the contribution.
