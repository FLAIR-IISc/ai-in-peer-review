The paper proposes synchronous and asynchronous gossip algorithms for estimating U-statistics in distributed settings. The authors derive these methods and establish convergence rates of 1/t for the synchronous variant and (log t)/t for the asynchronous variant. The exposition is clear, and the paper is well organized.

The problem addressed is timely and relevant, and the techniques should be useful to researchers working on decentralized estimation and learning. The proposed algorithms provide favorable convergence guarantees compared to existing approaches. The experimental evaluation supports the theory, demonstrating performance advantages over alternatives and illustrating accurate estimation across the tested scenarios.

Overall, this work makes a solid contribution by introducing new gossip-based estimators for U-statistics with provable rates and empirical benefits.
