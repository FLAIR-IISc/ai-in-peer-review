- Proposes novel gossip algorithms for computing U-statistics.  
- Achieves faster convergence than the state-of-the-art U2-Gossip algorithm.  
- Designed to operate in realistic asynchronous network settings.  
- Superior in both convergence rate and resource cost (memory and communication) compared to U2-Gossip.  
- Paper is well written, clearly motivated, and properly positioned within related work.  
- Provides theoretical guarantees and analysis for the proposed methods.  
- Presents extensive empirical evaluations on two datasets against relevant baselines.  
- Experimental results convincingly demonstrate the superiority of the proposed algorithms.
