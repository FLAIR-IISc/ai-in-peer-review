This paper presents a new gossip algorithm for computing U-statistics. The proposed algorithms have faster convergence rates and work in more realistic asynchronous settings compared to state-of-the-art algorithms (Pelcomans, Suencens'09 to the U2-Gossip algorithm). Experimental results further demonstrate the superiority of the proposed algorithms compared to existing algorithms.

The paper is well-written, the problem is well-motivated, and the authors have placed their work well within existing literature. As far as I can see, the proposed algorithms are new and improved in terms of convergence and cost (memory and communication compared to U2-Gossip). Furthermore, the proposed algorithms work in asynchronous settings.

In addition to theoretical certainty and analysis, the authors have conducted extensive empirical evaluations on two datasets and compared them with the baseline. This further strengthens the paper's contribution. This paper presents a new gossip algorithm for computing U-statistics, which is useful for fast convergence rates and realistic asynchronous settings. Furthermore, the algorithms have been evaluated through extensive testing and compared to state-of-the-art algorithms.
