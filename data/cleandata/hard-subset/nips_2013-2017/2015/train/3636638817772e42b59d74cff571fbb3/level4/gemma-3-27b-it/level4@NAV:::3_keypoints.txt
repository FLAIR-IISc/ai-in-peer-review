- Paper presents a well-motivated, clearly written contribution on synchronized and asynchronous randomized gossip algorithms for computing U-statistics.
- Authors provide theoretical convergence bounds for the proposed method.
- Experiments conducted on two datasets demonstrate effectiveness.
- Average relative AUC error is used as the key evaluation metric.
- Results show significant performance improvement over the U2-gossip baseline across diverse graph topologies.
- Reviewer recommends reporting per-iteration run-times for both methods to assess computational cost.
- Concern: if the proposed method has substantially slower per-iteration times than U2-gossip, its practical applicability may be limited.
