- Presents novel gossip algorithms for U-statistics with faster convergence than U2-Gossip.
- Extends algorithms to realistic asynchronous settings.
- Achieves improved convergence rates and lower memory/communication costs versus U2-Gossip.
- Provides convergence rate bounds for both synchronous and asynchronous models.
- Uses a “phantom node” reformulation in the convergence analysis (proofs are complex).
- Empirical evaluations on two real datasets show superiority over relevant baselines.
- Paper is well-written and clearly positions the work within existing literature.
- Limited baseline comparison beyond U2-Gossip could reduce broader contextualization.
