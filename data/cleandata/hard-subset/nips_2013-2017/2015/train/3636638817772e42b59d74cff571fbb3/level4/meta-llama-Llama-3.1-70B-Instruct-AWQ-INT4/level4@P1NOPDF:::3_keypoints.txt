- Well-motivated and clearly written paper.
- Experiments on two datasets with performance measured by average relative AUC error.
- Proposed method shows significantly better performance than the U2-gossip baseline across multiple graph scenarios.
- Paper provides convergence bounds for the proposed method.
- Authors introduce synchronous and asynchronous randomized gossip algorithms for computing U-statistics — a valuable contribution.
- Reviewer requests inclusion of per-iteration run-times for both the proposed method and U2-gossip.
- Concern noted: if each iteration of the proposed method is substantially slower than U2-gossip, the overall utility may be limited.
