- Paper is well motivated and clearly written.
- Proposes synchronous and asynchronous randomized gossip algorithms for computing U-statistics.
- Provides convergence bounds for the proposed methods.
- Empirical results on two datasets (measured by average relative AUC error) show the proposed method outperforms the U2-gossip baseline across varied graph scenarios.
- Reviewer requests per-iteration run-times for the proposed methods and U2-gossip, noting high per-iteration cost could limit practical utility.
