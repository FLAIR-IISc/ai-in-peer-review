- Introduces novel gossip algorithms for computing U-statistics with faster convergence than U2-Gossip (Pelckmans & Suykens, 2009).
- Algorithms operate effectively in realistic asynchronous settings, a notable practical advantage.
- Achieves improved cost-efficiency in memory and communication compared to U2-Gossip.
- Provides theoretical guarantees and convergence analysis for the proposed methods.
- Paper is well-written with clear motivation and thorough positioning within the literature.
- Extensive empirical evaluations on two datasets demonstrate superiority over relevant baselines.
