This paper addresses the problem of computing average statistics in a distributed environment. The algorithm itself is quite intuitive and may lack clear novelty, although the proofs provided are comprehensive.

One weakness of the paper is that it does not compare its results with any baseline measures. Several intuitive baselines could be generated based on randomized algorithms, and some should be considered for experimentation. The paper tackles an interesting problem, but the evaluation is weak due to the absence of baseline measures for comparison.
