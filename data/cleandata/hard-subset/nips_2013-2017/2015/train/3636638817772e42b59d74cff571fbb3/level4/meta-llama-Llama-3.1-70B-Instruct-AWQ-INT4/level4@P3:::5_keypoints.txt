- Proposes new synchronous and asynchronous gossip algorithms for decentralized estimation of U-statistics.
- Provides convergence rate analysis showing O(1/t) (synchronous) and O(log t / t) (asynchronous).
- Paper is well-organized and clearly written, facilitating understanding of techniques and proofs.
- Demonstrates empirical performance gains on real datasets, outperforming existing methods in convergence and accuracy.
- Theoretical analysis is thorough and supports the claimed rates.
- Related work review is comprehensive and highlights limitations of prior gossip methods.
- Authors discuss advantages, disadvantages, and trade-offs of their approach.
- Main weakness: assumes a relatively simple network model, leaving behavior in complex networks unclear.
- Main weakness: lacks detailed comparison to other decentralized estimation frameworks (e.g., consensus or distributed optimization).
- Recommendation: accept; paper makes a significant contribution to decentralized estimation.
- Score: 8/10, Reviewer confidence: 8/10.
