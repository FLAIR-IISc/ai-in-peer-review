The paper proposes both synchronous and asynchronous gossip algorithms for U-statistics. It develops these methods and establishes that they attain convergence rates of 1/t and (log t)/t, respectively.

The manuscript is well structured and presents results that validate the performance. The problem area is of genuine interest, making the proposed approach useful to a range of researchers. It introduces new gossip algorithms for estimating U-statistics with favorable convergence rates. The experiments demonstrate performance gains over alternative methods and highlight their accuracy.
