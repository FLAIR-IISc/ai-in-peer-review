- Proposes synchronous and asynchronous randomized gossip algorithms for computing U-statistics.  
- Paper is well-motivated and clearly written.  
- Main strength: theoretical analysis providing convergence bounds for the methods.  
- Empirical evaluation (two datasets, average relative AUC error) shows strong improvement over U2-gossip across various network topologies.  
- Experimental shortcoming: lacks per-iteration run-time comparison between proposed method and U2-gossip.  
- Practical concern: if the proposed method has substantially higher per-iteration cost than U2-gossip, its real-world applicability could be limited despite better convergence.
