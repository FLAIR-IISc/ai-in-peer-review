- Introduces novel gossip algorithms for computing U-statistics that operate in realistic asynchronous settings.
- Achieves faster convergence than the state-of-the-art U2-Gossip (Pelckmans & Suykens, 2009).
- Superior resource efficiency (memory and communication) compared to U2-Gossip.
- Paper is well written, well motivated, and clearly positions its contributions in the literature.
- Provides theoretical guarantees and extensive empirical evaluations on two datasets demonstrating superiority over relevant baselines.
