

The paper is well-motivated and clearly written. The authors present results on two datasets, evaluated using average relative AUC error, and demonstrate significantly superior performance compared to the baseline (U2-gossip) across diverse graph topologies. The work provides convergence bounds for the proposed method. I request run-time comparisons per iteration for both the proposed and U2-gossip approaches. If the proposed method exhibits substantially higher per-iteration runtime than U2-gossip, the paper's practical utility would be limited. The paper introduces synchronous and asynchronous randomized gossip algorithms for computing U-statistics.
