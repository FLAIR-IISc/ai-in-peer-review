The paper is well motivated and clearly written. The authors present results for their approach on two datasets, evaluating performance using average relative AUC error. Their method significantly outperforms the U2-gossip baseline across a variety of graph structures. Importantly, the paper provides convergence bounds for the proposed method. However, I would like to see runtime measurements per iteration for both the proposed method and U2-gossip. If each iteration of the proposed method is substantially slower than U2-gossip, the practical utility of the approach would be limited. The paper proposes synchronous and asynchronous randomized gossip algorithms for computing U-statistics.
