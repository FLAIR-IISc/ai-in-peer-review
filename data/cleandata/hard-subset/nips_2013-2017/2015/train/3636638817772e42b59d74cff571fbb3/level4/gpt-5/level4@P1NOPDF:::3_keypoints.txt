- Well motivated and clearly written manuscript.
- Results reported on two datasets using average relative AUC error.
- Proposed method substantially outperforms U2-gossip across diverse graph scenarios.
- Convergence bounds are provided for the proposed algorithms.
- Reviewer requests per-iteration runtime comparison with U2-gossip, noting higher per-iteration cost could limit practical utility.
- Paper proposes synchronous and asynchronous randomized gossip algorithms for computing U-statistics.
