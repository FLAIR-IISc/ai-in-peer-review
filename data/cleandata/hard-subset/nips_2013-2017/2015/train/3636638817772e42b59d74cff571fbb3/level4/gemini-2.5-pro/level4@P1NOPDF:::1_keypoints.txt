- Introduces novel Gossip algorithms for computing U-statistics with faster convergence than prior work.
- Algorithms improve over U2-Gossip (Pelckmans & Suykens, 2009) in convergence speed.
- Key advantage: algorithms operate in realistic asynchronous settings.
- Paper is well-written, clearly motivated, and situates contributions within existing literature.
- Proposed algorithms appear novel to the reviewer.
- Show superiority to U2-Gossip in memory and communication costs as well as convergence.
- Theoretical claims are supported by extensive empirical evaluations on two datasets.
- Experimental comparisons against established baselines convincingly demonstrate practical superiority.
- Overall assessment: strong, valuable contribution with theoretical soundness and thorough validation.
