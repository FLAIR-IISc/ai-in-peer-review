The paper is well-motivated and clearly written. The authors present results for their approach on two datasets, evaluating performance in terms of average relative AUC error. Compared to the baseline (U2-gossip), their method demonstrates significantly better performance across various scenarios (graphs). The paper also provides convergence bounds for their method.

To further strengthen the paper, I would like to see the inclusion of run-times per iteration for both the proposed method and U2-gossip. If each iteration of the proposed method is substantially slower than U2-gossip, the overall utility of the paper may be limited. The authors propose synchronous and asynchronous randomized gossip algorithms for computing U-statistics, which is a valuable contribution.
