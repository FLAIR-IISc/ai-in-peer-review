The paper is well motivated and clearly written. Results are presented on two datasets and evaluated using average relative AUC error. Compared with the U2-gossip baseline, the proposed method performs substantially better across diverse graph scenarios. The paper also provides convergence bounds for the proposed algorithms.

However, I would like to see per-iteration runtimes for both the proposed method and U2-gossip. If each iteration of the proposed method is significantly slower than U2-gossip, the practical utility of the approach would be limited.

Overall, the paper proposes synchronous and asynchronous randomized gossip algorithms for computing U-statistics.
