- Introduces synchronous and asynchronous gossip algorithms for computing U-statistics.  
- Provides theoretical convergence guarantees for both algorithms.  
- Derives convergence rates: O(1/t) for the synchronous algorithm and O((log t)/t) for the asynchronous variant.  
- Paper is well-organized and clearly presents results validating the theoretical claims.  
- Distributed estimation of U-statistics is a relevant and important problem area.  
- Proposed techniques offer a valuable contribution to researchers in this space.  
- Experimental results demonstrate performance advantages over existing approaches.  
- Experiments illustrate the accuracy of the proposed methods.
