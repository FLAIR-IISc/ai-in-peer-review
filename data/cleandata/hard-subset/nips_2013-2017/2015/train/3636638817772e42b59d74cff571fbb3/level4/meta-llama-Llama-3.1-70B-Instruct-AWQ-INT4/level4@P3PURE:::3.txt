The paper is well-motivated and well-written. The authors present results of their approach on two datasets, evaluated using the average relative AUC error. Compared to the baseline (U2-gossip), their method performs significantly better in various scenarios (graphs). The paper also provides convergence bounds for their method.

However, to fully assess the utility of the proposed approach, I would like to see the run-times per iteration for both the proposed method and U2-gossip. If each iteration of the proposed method is substantially slower than U2-gossip, the practical value of the paper may be limited. The authors propose synchronous and asynchronous randomized gossip algorithms for computing U-statistics, which is a notable contribution.
