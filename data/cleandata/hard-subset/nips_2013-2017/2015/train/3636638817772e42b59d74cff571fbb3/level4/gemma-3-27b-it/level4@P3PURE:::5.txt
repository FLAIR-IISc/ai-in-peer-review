This paper introduces synchronous and asynchronous gossip algorithms for computing U-statistics, and provides theoretical convergence guarantees for both. The authors derive convergence rates of 1/t for the synchronous algorithm and log t / t for the asynchronous variant. 

The paper is well-organized and presents results validating the derived performance characteristics. The studied problem area – distributed estimation of U-statistics – is of clear interest, and the proposed techniques offer a valuable contribution to researchers working in this space. Specifically, this work introduces novel gossip algorithms for U-statistic estimation that achieve favorable convergence rates. Experimental results demonstrate performance advantages over existing approaches and illustrate the accuracy of the proposed methods.
