

The paper is well motivated and well written. The results of the proposed approach are presented on two datasets (evaluated using average relative AUC error). Compared to the baseline (U2-gossip), the method achieves significantly better performance across varied graph scenarios. The paper provides convergence bounds for the proposed method. - I would like to see run-times per iteration for both the proposed method and U2-gossip. If each iteration of the proposed method is substantially slower than U2-gossip, the paper would have limited utility. The paper proposes synchronous and asynchronous randomized gossip algorithms for computing U-statistics.
