The paper proposes synchronous and asynchronous gossip algorithms for estimating U-statistics in distributed settings. The authors derive both methods and establish convergence rates of O(1/t) for the synchronous variant and O((log t)/t) for the asynchronous one.

The manuscript is well organized and presents experimental results that demonstrate the methods’ performance. The problem addressed is relevant to distributed learning, and the proposed techniques are likely to be useful to a broad set of researchers. The new gossip algorithms achieve favorable convergence rates, and the experiments show performance advantages over alternative approaches while illustrating strong estimation accuracy.
