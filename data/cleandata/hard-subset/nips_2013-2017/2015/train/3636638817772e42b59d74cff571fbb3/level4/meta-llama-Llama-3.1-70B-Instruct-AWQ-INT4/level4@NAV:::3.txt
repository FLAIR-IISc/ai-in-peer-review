This manuscript is well-written and effectively motivated, presenting a compelling approach to computing U-statistics through synchronized and asynchronous randomized gossip algorithms. The authors evaluate their method on two datasets, demonstrating its performance through average relative AUC error and comparing it to the baseline U2-gossip algorithm. Notably, their approach exhibits significantly improved results across diverse scenarios, including varied graph structures. Furthermore, the paper provides convergence bounds for the proposed method, lending theoretical rigor to the discussion. 

To further strengthen the manuscript, it would be beneficial to include runtime analyses for both the proposed method and the U2-gossip algorithm on a per-iteration basis. If the proposed method's iterations are substantially slower than those of U2-gossip, this could potentially limit the paper's practical applicability.
