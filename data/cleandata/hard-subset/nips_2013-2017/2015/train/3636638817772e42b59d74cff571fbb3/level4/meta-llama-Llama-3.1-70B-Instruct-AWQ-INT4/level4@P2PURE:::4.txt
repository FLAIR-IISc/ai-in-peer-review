This paper addresses the problem of computing average statistics in a distributed environment. While the proposed algorithm is intuitive, its novelty may be limited, although the accompanying proofs are comprehensive. 

A significant weakness of the paper lies in its lack of comparison to baseline measures. It would be beneficial to generate and experiment with intuitive baselines based on randomized algorithms, providing a more comprehensive evaluation of the proposed approach. Although the paper tackles an interesting problem, its evaluation is compromised by the absence of baseline comparisons, which undermines the overall assessment of its effectiveness.
