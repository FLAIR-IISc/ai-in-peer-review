This paper addresses the problem of computing average statistics in a distributed environment. The proposed algorithm is quite intuitive, which may raise concerns about its novelty, although the accompanying proofs are comprehensive.

One notable weakness of the paper is the absence of comparisons with baseline measures. Several intuitive baselines could be generated based on randomized algorithms, and it would be beneficial to consider some of these for experimentation. While the paper tackles an interesting problem, its evaluation is weakened by the lack of baseline measures, limiting the impact of the results.
