This paper presents novel synchronous and asynchronous gossip algorithms for estimating U-statistics. The authors provide a theoretical analysis of these methods, showing that they achieve convergence rates of 1/t and log(t)/t, respectively.

The paper is well-organized, and the topic of distributed U-statistic estimation is of significant interest. The proposed techniques are therefore valuable to the research community. The theoretical claims are supported by empirical results that demonstrate the algorithms' accuracy and performance advantages over competing approaches.
