- Proposes synchronous and asynchronous gossip algorithms for distributed estimation of U-statistics.  
- Proves convergence rates of O(1/t) for synchronous and O(log t / t) for asynchronous variants.  
- Technical presentation is clear with well-structured derivations supporting theoretical claims.  
- Addresses an important problem for large-scale learning systems, filling a gap in distributed U-statistic estimation.  
- Methods offer a practical solution with strong theoretical foundations.  
- Experiments demonstrate performance advantages and accuracy across multiple settings compared to existing approaches.  
- Overall a valuable contribution to distributed optimization and statistical learning research.
