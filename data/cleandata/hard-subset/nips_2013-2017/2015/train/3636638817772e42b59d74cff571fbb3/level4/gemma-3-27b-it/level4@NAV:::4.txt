This paper addresses the challenge of computing average statistics within a distributed system. The proposed algorithm is conceptually straightforward, and while the mathematical proofs are thorough, the novelty of the approach may be limited. 

A key area for improvement lies in the evaluation. The paper currently lacks comparison to any baseline methods. Several readily implementable baselines, potentially leveraging randomized algorithms, would provide valuable context for assessing the performance of the proposed solution. While the problem addressed is of interest, the strength of the evaluation is currently a concern due to the absence of these comparative analyses.
