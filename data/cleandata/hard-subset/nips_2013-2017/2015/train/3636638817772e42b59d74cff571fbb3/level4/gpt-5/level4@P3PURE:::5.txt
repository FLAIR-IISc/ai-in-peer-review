This paper proposes both synchronous and asynchronous gossip algorithms for the estimation of U-statistics, and establishes that they achieve convergence rates of 1/t and (log t)/t, respectively. The techniques are derived clearly, and the theoretical guarantees are stated explicitly.

The paper is well organized and presents empirical results that substantiate the claimed performance. The experiments demonstrate advantages over alternative approaches and illustrate the accuracy of the proposed methods.

The problem addressed is of genuine interest, and the proposed techniques should be useful to a range of researchers working on distributed estimation and related areas. Overall, the contribution consists of new gossip algorithms for U-statistic estimation with favorable convergence rates, supported by clear exposition and convincing empirical validation.
