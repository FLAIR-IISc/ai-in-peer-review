This paper introduces both synchronous and asynchronous gossip algorithms for the distributed computation of U-statistics. The authors formally derive these techniques and establish their theoretical convergence rates as 1/t and log t / t, respectively.

The manuscript is well-organized, and the presented results effectively demonstrate the algorithms' performance. The paper addresses a topic of considerable interest, making its contribution—novel gossip algorithms for U-statistic estimation with favorable convergence guarantees—of high value to the research community. The experimental evaluation validates the accuracy of the proposed methods and shows their superior performance compared to alternative approaches.
