The paper is clearly motivated and well written. The results of the approach are reported on two datasets, evaluated using average relative AUC error. Compared to the baseline (U2-gossip), the method performs substantially better across a variety of graph scenarios. The paper provides convergence bounds for the method. - I would like to see per-iteration runtimes for the proposed method and U2-gossip. If each iteration of the proposed method is significantly slower than U2-gossip, the paper’s practical utility would be limited. The paper proposes synchronous and asynchronous randomized gossip algorithms for computing U-statistics.
