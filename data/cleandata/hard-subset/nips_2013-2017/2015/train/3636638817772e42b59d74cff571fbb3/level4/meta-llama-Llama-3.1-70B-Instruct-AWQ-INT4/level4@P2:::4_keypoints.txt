- Proposes GoSta-sync and GoSta-async randomized gossip algorithms to compute U-statistics in decentralized networks.  
- Introduces "phantom nodes" to model data propagation and facilitate convergence analysis.  
- Provides rigorous convergence-rate analysis and compares performance with the U2-gossip algorithm.  
- Numerical experiments on complete, 2D grid, and Watts–Strogatz networks for AUC and within-cluster scatter.  
- Results show GoSta-sync outperforms U2-gossip across networks and yields lower inter-node estimate variance.  
- Paper is well-organized and clearly written with a thorough introduction and related-work review.  
- Major weakness: lacks comparison with baseline randomized algorithms.  
- Methodological novelty is limited—algorithms are intuitive.  
- Needs more discussion on implications and potential applications.  
- Recommendation: accept with revisions.
