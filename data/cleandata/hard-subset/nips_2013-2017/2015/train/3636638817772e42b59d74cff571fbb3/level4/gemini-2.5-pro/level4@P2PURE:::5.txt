This paper proposes synchronous and asynchronous gossip algorithms for the estimation of U-statistics. The authors provide a theoretical analysis demonstrating that these algorithms achieve favorable convergence rates of 1/t and log t / t, respectively.

The paper is well-organized and addresses a relevant area of research. The theoretical claims are supported by experimental results that illustrate the accuracy of the proposed methods and their performance advantages over existing approaches. The techniques presented should therefore be of interest to other researchers in the field.
