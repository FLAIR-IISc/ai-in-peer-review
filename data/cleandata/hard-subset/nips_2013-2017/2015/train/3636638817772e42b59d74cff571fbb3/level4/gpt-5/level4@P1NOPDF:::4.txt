This paper addresses the problem of computing average statistics in a distributed environment. The proposed algorithm is intuitive, but its novelty is not clear, although the theoretical development is thorough and the proofs are comprehensive.

A major weakness is the lack of comparison with baseline methods. I am confident that several natural baselines based on randomized algorithms could be constructed, and at least some should be included in the experiments. The paper tackles an interesting problem, but the empirical evaluation is weak and includes no baselines. Without such comparisons, it is difficult to assess the practical advantages of the proposed approach.
