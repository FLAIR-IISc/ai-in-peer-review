- Introduces novel gossip algorithms for computing U-statistics with faster convergence than U2-Gossip (Pelckmans & Suykens, 2009) and effective operation in asynchronous settings.
- Experimental results show the proposed algorithms outperform existing methods.
- Paper is well-written with clear motivation and thorough literature positioning.
- Proposed algorithms appear novel and superior in convergence rate and in memory and communication costs versus U2-Gossip.
- Algorithms are applicable to realistic asynchronous settings.
- Provides theoretical guarantees and analysis for the methods.
- Conducts extensive empirical evaluations on two datasets, comparing against baseline methods and reinforcing the contributions.
- Overall contribution: improved gossip algorithms for U-statistics with better convergence and practical applicability, validated theoretically and empirically.
