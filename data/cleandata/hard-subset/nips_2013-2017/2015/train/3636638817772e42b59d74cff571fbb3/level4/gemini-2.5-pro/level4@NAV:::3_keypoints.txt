- Introduces synchronous and asynchronous randomized gossip algorithms for computing U-statistics.  
- Strong motivation and well-written manuscript.  
- Empirical evaluation on two datasets shows superior performance over U2-gossip across varied graph topologies using average relative AUC error.  
- Theoretical contribution includes convergence bounds for the proposed algorithms.  
- Experimental evaluation lacks comparison of per-iteration run-times versus U2-gossip, which is critical because higher per-iteration cost could undermine practical utility.
