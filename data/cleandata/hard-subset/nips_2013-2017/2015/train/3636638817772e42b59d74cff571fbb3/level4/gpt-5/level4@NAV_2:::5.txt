This work introduces both synchronous and asynchronous gossip methods for estimating U-statistics. The authors develop the techniques and prove convergence rates of 1/t and (log t)/t for the two settings, respectively.

The manuscript is well organized and includes results that showcase its performance. The problem area is of clear interest, making the proposed approach useful to a range of researchers. The paper presents novel gossip algorithms for U-statistic estimation with advantageous convergence rates. Empirical evaluations demonstrate performance gains over alternative approaches and confirm its accuracy.
