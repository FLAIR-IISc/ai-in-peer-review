- Proposes synchronous and asynchronous gossip algorithms for estimating U-statistics in distributed settings.  
- Derives methods and proves convergence rates: synchronous O(1/t), asynchronous O((log t)/t).  
- Exposition is clear and the paper is well organized.  
- Addresses a timely, relevant problem useful for decentralized estimation and learning.  
- Algorithms offer favorable convergence guarantees compared to existing approaches.  
- Experiments support the theory, showing performance advantages and accurate estimation.  
- Overall a solid contribution introducing gossip-based U-statistics estimators with provable rates and empirical benefits.
