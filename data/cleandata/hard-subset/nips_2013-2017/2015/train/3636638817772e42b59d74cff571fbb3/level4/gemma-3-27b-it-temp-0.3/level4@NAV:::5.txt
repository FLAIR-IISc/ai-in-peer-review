This paper introduces novel synchronous and asynchronous gossip algorithms designed for computing U-statistics. The authors provide theoretical derivations establishing convergence rates of 1/t for the synchronous algorithm and log t / t for the asynchronous variant. 

The manuscript is clearly structured and presents compelling results validating the algorithms’ performance. The investigated problem area is relevant to current research, making the proposed techniques potentially valuable to a broad range of researchers. Specifically, this work offers new gossip-based approaches to U-statistic estimation, achieving advantageous convergence properties. Experimental evaluation demonstrates performance gains and confirms the accuracy of the proposed algorithms when compared to existing methods.
