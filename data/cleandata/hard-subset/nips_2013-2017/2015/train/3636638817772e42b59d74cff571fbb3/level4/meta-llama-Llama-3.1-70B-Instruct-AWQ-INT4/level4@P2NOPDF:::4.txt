This paper addresses the problem of computing average statistics in a distributed environment. While the proposed algorithm is intuitive, its novelty is not entirely clear. However, the accompanying proofs are comprehensive and well-presented.

A notable weakness of the paper is the lack of comparison to baseline measures. It would be beneficial to include intuitive baselines based on randomized algorithms for experimentation, providing a more comprehensive evaluation of the proposed method. Although the paper tackles an interesting problem, the evaluation is somewhat limited due to the absence of baseline comparisons.
