

The paper is well-motivated and clearly written. The authors present results on two datasets, evaluated using average relative AUC error, demonstrating that their approach significantly outperforms the baseline (U2-gossip) across varied graph scenarios. The paper also provides convergence bounds for the proposed method. However, I request run-time comparisons per iteration for both the proposed method and U2-gossip. If each iteration of the proposed method is substantially slower than U2-gossip, the practical utility of the approach would be limited. The paper introduces synchronous and asynchronous randomized gossip algorithms for computing U-statistics.
