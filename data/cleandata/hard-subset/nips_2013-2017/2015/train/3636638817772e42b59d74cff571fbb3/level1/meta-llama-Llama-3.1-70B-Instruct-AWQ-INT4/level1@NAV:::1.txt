**Summary of the Paper**

This paper proposes new synchronous and asynchronous randomized gossip algorithms, called GoSta-sync and GoSta-async, for computing U-statistics in a decentralized setting. U-statistics are a class of statistical estimators that depend on pairs of observations, and are commonly used in machine learning and data mining. The authors show that their algorithms converge to the true U-statistic at rates of O(1/t) and O(log t/t) for the synchronous and asynchronous cases, respectively, where t is the number of iterations. The convergence rates are improved compared to previous work, and the algorithms have lower memory and communication costs.
