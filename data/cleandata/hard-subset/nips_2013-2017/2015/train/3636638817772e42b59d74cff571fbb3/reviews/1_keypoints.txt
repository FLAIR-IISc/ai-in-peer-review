- Proposes novel gossip algorithms for computing U-statistics.  
- Achieves faster convergence rates than prior state-of-the-art (U2-Gossip).  
- Operates in realistic asynchronous settings.  
- Superior in memory and communication cost compared to U2-Gossip.

- Extensive empirical evaluation on two datasets.  
- Experimental results demonstrate superiority over existing baselines.  
- Paper is well written, well motivated, and properly positioned relative to prior work.
