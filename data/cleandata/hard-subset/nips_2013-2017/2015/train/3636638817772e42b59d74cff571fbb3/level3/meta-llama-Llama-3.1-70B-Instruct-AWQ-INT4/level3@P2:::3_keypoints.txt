- Proposes GoSta-sync and GoSta-async for decentralized computation of U-statistics.  
- Provides convergence bounds: GoSta-sync O(1/t) and GoSta-async O(log t / t).  
- Empirically outperforms U2-gossip across various graph scenarios.  
- Experiments on SMVguide3 and Wine Quality datasets using average relative AUC error.  
- Shows lower average error and variance compared to U2-gossip.  
- Paper is well-written, clear, and well-structured with helpful figures and tables.  
- Thorough theoretical analysis and empirical evaluation.  
- Strength: novel synchronous and asynchronous gossip algorithms.  
- Weakness: missing per-iteration run-time comparisons with U2-gossip.  
- Weakness: does not discuss computational complexity of the proposed algorithms.  
- Originality: significant contribution with new gossip methods for U-statistics.  
- Significance: addresses an important decentralized computation problem and demonstrates practical utility.  
- Recommendation: accept, requesting per-iteration run-times and a discussion of computational complexity in the final version.
