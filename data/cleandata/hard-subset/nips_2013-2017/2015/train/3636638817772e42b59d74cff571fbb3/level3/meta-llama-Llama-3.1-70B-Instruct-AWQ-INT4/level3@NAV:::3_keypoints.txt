- Proposes GoSta-sync and GoSta-async, new randomized gossip algorithms for computing U-statistics in decentralized networks.  
- Tackles a less-investigated problem in gossip algorithms with a well-motivated and well-written presentation.  
- Provides clear introduction and thorough related-work review.  
- Delivers convergence analysis for both synchronous and asynchronous settings.  
- Numerical results show the proposed methods outperform the previous U2-gossip approach.  
- Strength: Novel decentralized solution for computing U-statistics.  
- Strength: Well-organized paper with clear writing.  
- Strength: Thorough convergence-rate analysis improving over prior work.  
- Strength: Empirical results demonstrate practical interest.  
- Weakness: Missing per-iteration run-time comparisons between proposed methods and U2-gossip.  
- Concern: If iterations of the proposed methods are much slower than U2-gossip, practical utility may be limited.  
- Recommendation: Accept with minor revisions; authors should report per-iteration run-times to address practical-utility concerns.
