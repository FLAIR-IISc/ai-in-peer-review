- Motivation: Many multiclass problems have semantically similar classes and require handling top-k evaluation where cost occurs only if the ground truth is outside the top k.  
- Contribution: Proposes the top-k SVM algorithm to explicitly model this top-k cost scenario.  
- Loss formulation: Extends the standard loss to a top-k loss tailored to the problem.  
- Optimization: Derives an efficient optimization procedure for the top-k loss.  
- Scalability: Algorithm scales to very large datasets.  
- Empirical results: Experiments show clear improvements over compared methods on large-scale datasets.  
- Presentation: Paper is clearly motivated and easy to follow.
