- Proposes a top-k hinge loss for top-k classification.
- Generalizes the multiclass SVM formulation.
- Uses the sum of the k largest score components as a convex approximation to the target top-k loss.
- Presents an efficient optimization algorithm.
- Discusses relationships with previous work.
- Paper is clearly written.
- Experiments show the proposed method is effective compared to baselines.
- Did not report comparisons with [25, 8] due to unavailable implementations; reviewer requests these comparisons.
- Reviewer asks authors to make the code public upon acceptance.
- Overall: a neat formulation of top-k multiclass SVM that works well.
