This paper proposes a novel extension of the multiclass SVM, called top-k multiclass SVM, which directly optimizes for top-k performance. The authors introduce a tight convex upper bound of the top-k error, leading to a new loss function called the top-k hinge loss. They also derive an efficient optimization scheme based on an efficient projection onto the top-k simplex.

The paper is well-written, and the authors provide a clear and concise explanation of their approach. The technical contributions are significant, including the derivation of the top-k hinge loss and the efficient optimization scheme.

* The paper addresses a relevant problem in image classification, where class ambiguity is common.
