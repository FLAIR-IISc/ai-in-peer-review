- Introduces a top-k hinge loss for top-k classification that generalizes multiclass SVM by summing the k largest components.  
- The proposed loss is convex and amenable to optimization.  
- Presents an efficient optimization algorithm for the new loss.  
- Provides a thorough discussion of related work.  
- Paper is well-written with clear explanations.  
- Experimental results show the method outperforms several baseline methods.  
- Major omission: no empirical comparison to methods in [25, 8], weakening the evaluation.  
- Reviewer urges implementing or obtaining those methods for comparison before camera-ready.  
- Requests public release of code upon acceptance to aid reproducibility and adoption.  
- Overall assessment: well-formulated, effective approach and a worthwhile contribution.
