- Proposes a top-k hinge loss for top-k classification, generalizing multiclass SVM by using the sum of the k largest components as a convex approximation.
- Presents an efficient optimization algorithm for the proposed loss.
- Provides a thorough discussion of relationships to previous work.
- Paper is well-written.
- Experiments demonstrate the proposed method’s effectiveness compared to baselines.
- Suggests adding comparisons with methods in [25, 8] despite current lack of implementations.
- Recommends making the code publicly available upon acceptance.
- Overall: a neat formulation of top-k multiclass SVM that yields promising results.
