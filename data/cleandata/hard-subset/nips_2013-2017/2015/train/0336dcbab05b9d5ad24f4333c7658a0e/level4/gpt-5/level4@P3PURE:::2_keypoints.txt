- Many multiclass problems have a large number of semantically similar classes.  
- Proposes "top-k SVM" to incur loss only when the true label is outside the top k predictions.  
- Extends the loss function to a top-k loss formulation.  
- Develops an efficient optimization procedure for the proposed loss.  
- Demonstrates scalability to very large datasets.  
- Experiments show clear improvements over compared algorithms.  
- Paper is well motivated, easy to follow, and addresses a practically important problem.
