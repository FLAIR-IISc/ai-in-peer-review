This paper tackles a non-trivial problem and is clearly written. The experimental results are strong. My only suggestion is to further motivate and justify the choice of the loss function. I also noticed a typo on page 3: “globally optimal leads to” (remove “optimal”). Overall, this is a nice paper addressing an interesting problem. The core idea is to learn with respect to the top-k loss, where k predictions are produced and the loss is zero if the true class is included among these k predictions.
