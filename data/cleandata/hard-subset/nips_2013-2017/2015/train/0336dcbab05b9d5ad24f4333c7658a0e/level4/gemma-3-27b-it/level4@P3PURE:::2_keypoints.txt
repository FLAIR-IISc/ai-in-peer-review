- Addresses multiclass problems with many classes where semantic similarity between classes is common.  
- Proposes a "top-k SVM" algorithm for settings where loss is incurred only if the true label is not in the top-k predictions.  
- Extends the standard loss to a top-k loss formulation.  
- Develops an efficient optimization procedure for the top-k loss.  
- Demonstrates scalability of the algorithm to very large datasets.  
- Shows clear performance improvements over comparable existing methods in experiments.  
- Paper is clearly motivated and easy to follow.  
- Work is relevant to practical challenges like ImageNet where top-k accuracy matters.
