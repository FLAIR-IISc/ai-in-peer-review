The paper proposes a top-k hinge loss for top-k classification, generalizing the multiclass SVM formulation. The key idea is to approximate the desired top-k loss with a convex function – the sum of the k largest components. An efficient optimization algorithm is proposed, and the work appropriately discusses relationships to previous approaches. The paper is clearly written and the experimental results demonstrate the effectiveness of the proposed method compared to several baselines. 

1. The authors note the lack of comparisons to methods in [25, 8] due to implementation availability. However, a comparison to these works would be highly valuable and I encourage the authors to address this if possible.
2. To facilitate reproducibility and further research, would the authors consider making their code publicly available upon acceptance?

Overall, this is a neat and well-executed formulation of a top-k multiclass SVM that appears to perform well in practice.
