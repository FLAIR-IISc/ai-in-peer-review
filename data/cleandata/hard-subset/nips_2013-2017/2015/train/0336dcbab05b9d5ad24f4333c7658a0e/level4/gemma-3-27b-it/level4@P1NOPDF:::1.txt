The paper proposes a top-k hinge loss for top-k classification, generalizing the multiclass SVM. It employs the sum of the k largest components as a convex approximation to the target top-k loss. An efficient optimization algorithm is presented, and the work is appropriately situated within the existing literature. The paper is clearly written, and experimental results demonstrate the effectiveness of the proposed method compared to relevant baselines.

1. The authors state they did not compare against methods [25, 8] due to the unavailability of implementations. However, I am particularly interested in seeing a comparison to these approaches, if possible. 
2. Would the authors be willing to release their code upon acceptance of the paper?

This is a neat formulation of a top-k multiclass SVM that appears to perform well.
