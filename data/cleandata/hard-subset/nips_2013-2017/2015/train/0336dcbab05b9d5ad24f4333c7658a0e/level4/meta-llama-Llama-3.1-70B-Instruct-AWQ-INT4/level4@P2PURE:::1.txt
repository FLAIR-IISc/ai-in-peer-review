The paper proposes a novel top-k hinge loss for top-k classification, generalizing the multiclass SVM by utilizing the sum of the k largest components as a convex approximation to the desired top-k loss. An efficient optimization algorithm is also presented, accompanied by a discussion on its relationships with previous work. The paper is clearly written, and the experimental results demonstrate the effectiveness of the proposed method in comparison to baseline approaches.

However, there are a couple of areas that would benefit from further attention. Firstly, the authors did not provide comparisons with [25, 8] due to the unavailability of implementations. It would be highly desirable to include these comparisons in future work, as they would provide valuable insights into the proposed method's performance. Secondly, making the code publicly available upon acceptance would significantly enhance the paper's impact and facilitate further research in this area.

Overall, the paper presents a neat formulation of top-k multiclass SVM that yields promising results.
