- Proposes a top-k multiclass SVM that generalizes the Crammer and Singer multiclass SVM.  
- Introduces a top-k hinge loss: a convex upper bound on top-k error based on the sum of the k largest components.  
- Derives an efficient optimization algorithm using the Proximal Stochastic Dual Coordinate Ascent (Prox-SDCA) framework.  
- Paper is well-written with clear explanations and discussion of relationships to prior multiclass and ranking losses.  
- Experimental results are thorough and show the proposed method is effective compared to various baselines.
