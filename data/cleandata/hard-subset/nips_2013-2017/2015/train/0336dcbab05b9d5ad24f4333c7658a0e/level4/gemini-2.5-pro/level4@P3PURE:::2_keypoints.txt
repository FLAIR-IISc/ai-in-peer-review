- Addresses multiclass classification with many semantically similar classes.  
- Proposes "top-k SVM" to incur no cost if the true class is within the top-k predictions.  
- Extends the standard SVM loss to a top-k loss and provides an efficient optimization procedure.  
- Paper is clearly motivated and easy to follow.  
- Tackles an important, practical problem arising in large-scale multiclass learning.  
- Offers a principled approach to handle class similarity via the top-k objective.  
- Algorithm is scalable to large datasets.  
- Experiments demonstrate clear improvements over compared methods.
