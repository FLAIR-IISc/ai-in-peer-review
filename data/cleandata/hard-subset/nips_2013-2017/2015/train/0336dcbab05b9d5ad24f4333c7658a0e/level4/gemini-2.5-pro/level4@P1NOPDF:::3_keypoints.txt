- Presents a well-written, technically sound approach to learning under a top-k classification loss.  
- Core idea: train a model to output k predictions, with zero loss if the true class is included among them.  
- Contributions are non-trivial and supported by strong experimental results.  
- Primary suggestion: provide deeper motivation for choosing this specific loss formulation over other surrogate losses or relaxations.  
- Minor issue: typo on page 3 ("globally optimal leads to" — redundant "optimal").  
- Overall assessment: high-quality paper with an interesting method, solid execution, and convincing results.
