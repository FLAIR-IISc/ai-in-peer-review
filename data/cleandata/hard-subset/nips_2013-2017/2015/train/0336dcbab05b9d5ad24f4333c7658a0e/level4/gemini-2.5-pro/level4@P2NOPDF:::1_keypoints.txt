- Proposes a novel top-k hinge loss that generalizes multiclass SVM using the sum of the k largest scores as a tight convex bound on the top-k loss.
- Develops an efficient optimization algorithm and clearly relates the method to prior work.
- Paper is well-written and technically clear.
- Empirical results convincingly show advantages of the proposed method over selected baselines.
- The formulation is theoretically sound, intuitive, and a valuable contribution.
- Strong empirical evaluation provides convincing evidence of effectiveness.
- Concern: No empirical comparison to key prior methods [8, 25] due to unavailable implementations; such comparisons are important for situating the work.
- Request clarification on the challenges of re-implementing those baselines and suggestion to include at least subset comparisons if possible.
- Encourages releasing the implementation to promote reproducibility and facilitate future research.
- Overall assessment: solid contribution, reviewer leans toward acceptance but would be more enthusiastic with comparisons to key prior work.
