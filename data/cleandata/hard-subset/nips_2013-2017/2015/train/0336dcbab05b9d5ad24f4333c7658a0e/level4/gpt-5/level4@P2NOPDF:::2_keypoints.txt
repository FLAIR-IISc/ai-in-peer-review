- Targets multiclass tasks with large label spaces and semantic overlap among classes.  
- Introduces top-k SVM for settings where loss occurs only if the true label is not in the top k predictions.  
- Extends conventional multiclass loss to a top-k loss formulation.  
- Develops an efficient optimization procedure for the top-k objective.  
- Demonstrates strong scalability on large-scale datasets.  
- Shows consistent improvements over competitive baselines in experiments.  
- Paper is well motivated, clear, and easy to follow.  
- Directly optimizing the top-k objective better accounts for near-miss predictions and aligns training with real-world evaluation metrics.
