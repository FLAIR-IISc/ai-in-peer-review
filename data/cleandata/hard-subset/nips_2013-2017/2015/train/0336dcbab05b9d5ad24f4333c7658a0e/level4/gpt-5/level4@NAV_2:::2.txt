In many multiclass settings with numerous categories, semantic overlap among classes is common. This paper introduces a top-k SVM to directly handle evaluation regimes where loss is incurred only when the true label is absent from the top k predictions (e.g., the ImageNet challenge). After extending the loss to a top-k formulation, the authors devise an efficient optimization strategy. Experiments on large-scale datasets demonstrate the method’s scalability and show clear improvements over competing approaches.

The paper is well-motivated and easy to follow. The problem of similar classes in multiclass learning is important and frequently encountered in practice. The proposed top-k SVM effectively addresses this case, scales to very large datasets, and exhibits gains over the compared methods.
