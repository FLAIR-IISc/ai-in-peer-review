- Proposes a novel top-k hinge loss that sums the k largest hinge components as a convex surrogate, generalizing the multiclass SVM.  
- Provides an efficient optimization algorithm and clearly situates the method relative to prior work.  
- Paper is well-written and experimental results show the method is effective against selected baselines.  
- Major critique: no empirical comparison to closely related methods [25, 8]; adding such comparisons would strengthen the evaluation.  
- Suggestion: release code publicly upon acceptance to improve reproducibility and enable follow-up work.  
- Overall assessment: a solid, principled, and elegant contribution with strong empirical support.
