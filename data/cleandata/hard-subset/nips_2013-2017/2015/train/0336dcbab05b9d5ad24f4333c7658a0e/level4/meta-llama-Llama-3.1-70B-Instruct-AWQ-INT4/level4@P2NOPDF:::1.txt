The paper proposes a top-k hinge loss for top-k classification, generalizing the multiclass SVM by utilizing the sum of k largest components as a convex approximation to the desired top-k loss. This approach is accompanied by an efficient optimization algorithm, and the authors provide a thorough discussion of its relationships with previous work. The paper is well-written, and the experiment results demonstrate the effectiveness of the proposed method in comparison to baseline approaches.

There are a couple of suggestions for further improvement. Firstly, it would be beneficial to include comparisons with the methods presented in [25, 8], despite the current unavailability of implementations. Such comparisons would provide valuable insights into the performance of the proposed approach. Secondly, making the code publicly available upon acceptance would be highly appreciated, as it would enable other researchers to build upon and further develop the proposed method.

Overall, the paper presents a neat formulation of top-k multiclass SVM that yields promising results.
