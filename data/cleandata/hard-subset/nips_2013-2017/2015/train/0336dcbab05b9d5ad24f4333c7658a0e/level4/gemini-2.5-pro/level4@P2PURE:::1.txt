This paper introduces a top-k hinge loss for top-k classification, which serves as a generalization of the multiclass SVM. The formulation employs the sum of the k largest scores as a convex upper bound on the ideal top-k loss. The authors also propose an efficient optimization algorithm and provide a clear discussion of the method's relationship to prior work. The paper is well-written, and the experimental results demonstrate the effectiveness of the proposed approach compared to several baselines.

1.  The authors justify the omission of comparisons with [25, 8] by the lack of available implementations. However, given the direct relevance of these methods, the experimental evaluation would be substantially strengthened by their inclusion.
2.  To facilitate reproducibility, I would strongly encourage the authors to make their code publicly available upon acceptance.

Overall, this paper presents an elegant and effective formulation of a top-k multiclass SVM that demonstrates strong empirical performance.
