- Introduces a top-k hinge loss that generalizes multiclass SVM by using the sum of the k largest components as a convex approximation to top-k loss.  
- Proposes an efficient optimization algorithm and situates the method with connections to prior work.  
- Paper is clearly written and experimental results show the method outperforms baselines.  
- Reviewer notes missing comparisons with methods in [25, 8] due to unavailable implementations and requests these be included in the final version.  
- Reviewer asks whether the authors will make the code publicly available upon acceptance.  
- Overall assessment: well-motivated formulation that achieves strong empirical performance.
