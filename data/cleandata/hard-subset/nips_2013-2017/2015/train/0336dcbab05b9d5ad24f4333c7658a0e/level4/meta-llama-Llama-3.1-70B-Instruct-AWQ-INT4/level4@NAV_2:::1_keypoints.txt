- Proposes a top-k hinge loss for top-k classification that generalizes the multiclass SVM.  
- Uses the sum of the k largest score components as a convex approximation to the target top-k loss.  
- Presents an efficient optimization algorithm (Prox-SDCA based) for the proposed formulation.  
- Discusses relationships to prior ranking-based losses and other related work.  
- Paper is clearly written, well-organized, and includes thorough analysis of the method.  
- Experimental results demonstrate effectiveness, especially improvements in top-k accuracy versus baselines.  
- Strength: novel top-k multiclass SVM formulation.  
- Strength: efficient optimization algorithm and thorough experimental evaluation.  
- Weakness: lacks comparisons with some relevant prior work ([25, 8]) due to unavailable implementations.  
- Weakness: code is not currently public, limiting reproducibility.  
- Recommendation: Accept with minor revisions (add missing comparisons and release code upon acceptance).
