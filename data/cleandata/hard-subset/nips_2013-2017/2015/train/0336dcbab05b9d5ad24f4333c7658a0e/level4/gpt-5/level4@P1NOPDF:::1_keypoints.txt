- Proposes a top-k hinge loss for top-k classification, generalizing multiclass SVM.  
- Uses the sum of the k largest components as a convex surrogate for the top-k loss.  
- Presents an efficient optimization algorithm for the proposed loss.  
- Discusses connections to prior work.  
- Paper is clearly written.  
- Experiments show the proposed method outperforms baselines.  
- Reviewer requests comparisons with references [25, 8], which were not reported due to unavailable implementations.  
- Reviewer asks the authors to make the code public upon acceptance.  
- Overall judgment: neat and effective formulation of a top-k multiclass SVM.
