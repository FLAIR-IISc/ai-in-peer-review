- Problem: High-cardinality multiclass settings exhibit semantic overlap among categories, causing conventional losses to be suboptimal.
- Contribution: Introduces top-k SVM that incurs cost only when the true label is outside the top k predicted classes.
- Method: Extends the loss to a top-k loss and derives an efficient optimization procedure.
- Experiments: Evaluated on large-scale datasets demonstrating algorithm scalability.
- Results: Shows clear improvements over competing methods.
- Presentation: Paper is clearly motivated and easy to follow.
- Importance: Addresses a practically important problem encountered frequently in real-world applications.
