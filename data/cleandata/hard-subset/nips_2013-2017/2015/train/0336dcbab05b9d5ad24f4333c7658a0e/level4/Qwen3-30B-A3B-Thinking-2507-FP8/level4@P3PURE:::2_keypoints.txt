- Addresses an important practical challenge in large-scale multiclass classification: semantically similar classes.  
- Proposes top-k SVM tailored to settings where loss occurs only if the true class is not in the top-k predictions.  
- Extends standard SVM loss to a top-k loss formulation.  
- Derives an efficient optimization procedure for the top-k SVM.  
- Demonstrates scalability on large-scale datasets.  
- Shows clear performance improvements over competing methods.  
- Paper is well-motivated, clearly written, and experimentally convincing.
