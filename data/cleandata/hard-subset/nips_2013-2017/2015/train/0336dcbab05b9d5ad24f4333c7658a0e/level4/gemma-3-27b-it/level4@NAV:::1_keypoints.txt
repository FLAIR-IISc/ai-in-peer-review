- Introduces a top-k hinge loss that generalizes multiclass SVMs via a convex approximation using the sum of the k largest components.
- Proposes an efficient optimization algorithm for the new loss.
- Provides a good discussion of related work.
- The paper is well-written.
- Experimental results show the method is effective compared to established baselines.
- Missing comparisons to methods in [25, 8]; including them would strengthen the paper.
- Author cites implementation availability as the reason for not comparing to [25, 8].
- Requests that authors make their code publicly available upon acceptance to aid reproducibility.
- Overall assessment: a well-formulated and effective approach to top-k multiclass SVM.
