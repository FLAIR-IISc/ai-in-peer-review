- Introduces a top-k hinge loss for top-k classification that generalizes the multiclass SVM.  
- Uses the sum of the k largest components as a convex surrogate for the top-k loss.  
- Presents an efficient optimization method and discusses connections to prior work.  
- Paper is clearly written.  
- Empirical results show the method is effective compared to baselines.  
- Missing comparisons with references [25, 8] due to unavailable implementations; reviewer wants to see these comparisons.  
- Reviewer asks whether the authors will release the code upon acceptance.  
- Overall: a clean, effective formulation of top-k multiclass SVM that performs well.
