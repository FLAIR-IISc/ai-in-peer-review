- Addresses multiclass problems with many classes and semantic similarity between classes.  
- Proposes the top-k SVM algorithm to penalize only when the true label is outside the top k predictions.  
- Extends the loss function to a top-k loss formulation.  
- Develops an efficient optimization procedure for the top-k loss.  
- Demonstrates scalability on large-scale datasets.  
- Shows clear empirical improvements over comparable methods.  
- Paper is well-motivated and easy to follow.  
- Specifically targets scenarios (e.g., ImageNet) where top-k accuracy matters.
