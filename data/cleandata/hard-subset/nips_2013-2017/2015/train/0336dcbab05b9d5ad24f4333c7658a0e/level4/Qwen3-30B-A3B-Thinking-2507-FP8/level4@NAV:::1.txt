

The paper introduces a top-k hinge loss for top-k classification, generalizing multiclass SVM by employing the sum of the k largest components as a convex approximation to the target top-k loss. An efficient optimization algorithm is developed, and connections to prior work are thoroughly examined. The manuscript is well-written, and experimental results demonstrate the proposed method's effectiveness relative to baseline approaches.  
1. The authors omitted comparisons with [25, 8] due to unavailability of implementations; inclusion of these comparisons would be valuable.  
2. Will the authors release the code publicly upon acceptance?  
A concise and effective formulation of top-k multiclass SVM with strong empirical performance.
