The paper introduces a top-k hinge loss function for top-k classification, generalizing the multiclass SVM framework. It approximates the desired top-k loss by utilizing the sum of the k largest components, resulting in a convex formulation. The authors also present an efficient optimization algorithm and discuss connections to related work. The paper is well-written and the experimental results demonstrate the effectiveness of the proposed method in comparison to established baselines.
