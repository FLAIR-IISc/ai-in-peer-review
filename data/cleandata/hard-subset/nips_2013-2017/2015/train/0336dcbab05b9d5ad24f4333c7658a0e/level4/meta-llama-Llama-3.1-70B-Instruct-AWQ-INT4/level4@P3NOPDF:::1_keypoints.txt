- Proposes a novel top-k hinge loss for top-k classification that generalizes the multiclass SVM framework.  
- Uses the sum of the k largest components as a convex approximation to the top-k loss, providing a theoretically sound approach.  
- Manuscript is clearly written and includes an efficiently designed optimization algorithm.  
- Includes a thorough discussion of relationships to prior work.  
- Experimental results demonstrate effectiveness, outperforming baseline approaches.  
- Lacks empirical comparison with methods in [25] and [8]; authors cite unavailable implementations but should include these in future work.  
- Recommends releasing code upon acceptance to improve reproducibility and accessibility.  
- Overall: neat formulation with promising results and potential for meaningful impact given additional comparisons and implementation release.
