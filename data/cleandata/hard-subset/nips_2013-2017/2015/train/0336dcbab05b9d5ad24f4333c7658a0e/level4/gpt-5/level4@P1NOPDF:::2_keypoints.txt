- Addresses multiclass problems with many semantically similar categories by focusing on top-k prediction loss.
- Proposes top-k SVM that penalizes only when the true label is not in the top k predicted classes.
- Extends the standard multiclass loss to a top-k loss formulation.
- Develops an efficient optimization procedure for the proposed loss.
- Demonstrates scalability to very large datasets in experiments.
- Shows consistent performance improvements over competing methods.
- Paper is well motivated, clearly written, and easy to follow.
- Objective aligns with top-k evaluation criteria and directly handles similar-class scenarios.
