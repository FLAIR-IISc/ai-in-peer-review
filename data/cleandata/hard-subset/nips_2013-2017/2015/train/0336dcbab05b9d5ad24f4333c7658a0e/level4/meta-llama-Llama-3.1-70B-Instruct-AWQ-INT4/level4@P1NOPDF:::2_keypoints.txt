- Addresses multiclass classification with many semantically similar classes.  
- Proposes top-k SVM that incurs a cost only when the ground truth is outside the top k predictions.  
- Extends the loss function to a top-k loss formulation.  
- Derives an efficient optimization procedure for the top-k loss.  
- Demonstrates scalability to very large datasets (e.g., ImageNet-like settings).  
- Empirical results show clear improvements over compared algorithms.  
- Paper is well-motivated and clearly written.
