- Problem: In many multiclass tasks with many classes, semantic similarity causes top-k accuracy to be more relevant than top-1 accuracy.
- Contribution: Proposes a top-k SVM that explicitly targets loss only when the true label is outside the top k predictions.
- Technical: Extends the multiclass loss to a top-k loss formulation.
- Optimization: Develops an efficient optimization procedure for the proposed loss.
- Experiments: Demonstrates scalability on large-scale datasets and shows clear improvements over competing algorithms.
- Presentation: Paper is well motivated, addresses a practical issue, and is easy to follow.
