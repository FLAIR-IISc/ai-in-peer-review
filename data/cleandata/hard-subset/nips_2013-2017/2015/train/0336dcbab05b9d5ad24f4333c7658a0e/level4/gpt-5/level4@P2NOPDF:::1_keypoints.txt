- Introduces a top-k hinge loss extending multiclass SVM via a convex surrogate (sum of the k largest components) as a principled approximation to top-k error.  
- Develops an efficient optimization algorithm and clearly positions the method relative to prior work.  
- Paper is well written and experimental results show consistent improvements over strong baselines.  
- Omits comparisons with [25, 8]; reviewer requests these comparisons even via reimplementations or partial evaluations.  
- Reviewer asks authors to commit to releasing code upon acceptance to aid reproducibility and adoption.  
- Overall assessment: neat, well-motivated top-k multiclass SVM that performs well empirically.
