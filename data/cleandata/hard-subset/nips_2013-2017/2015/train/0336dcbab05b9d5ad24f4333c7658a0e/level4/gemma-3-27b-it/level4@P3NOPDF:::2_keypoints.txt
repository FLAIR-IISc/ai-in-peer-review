- Problem: multiclass classification with many semantically similar classes where only top-k containment matters.  
- Proposal: a “top-k SVM” that incurs loss only if the true label is outside the top k predictions.  
- Method: extension of standard SVM loss to a top-k loss.  
- Optimization: development of an efficient optimization procedure to scale the top-k formulation.  
- Experiments: large-scale evaluations show scalability and clear performance gains over baselines.  
- Strengths: strong motivation, clear presentation of top-k accuracy/loss, and a principled solution.  
- Core contribution: formulation of top-k SVM plus an optimization strategy that enables large-scale use.  
- Requested analyses: need finer-grained results showing which classes/datasets benefit most.  
- Clarifications needed: more details on the optimization procedure, computational cost vs baselines, and handling of ties in top-k.
