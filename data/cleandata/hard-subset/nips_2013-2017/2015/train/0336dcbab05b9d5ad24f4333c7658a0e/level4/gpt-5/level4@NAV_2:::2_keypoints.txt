- Semantic overlap among classes is common in multiclass settings with many categories.  
- The paper introduces a top-k SVM that targets evaluation regimes where loss occurs only if the true label is not in the top k predictions.  
- The authors extend the loss to a top-k formulation and devise an efficient optimization strategy.  
- Experiments on large-scale datasets show the method scales to very large data.  
- Empirical results demonstrate clear improvements over competing approaches.  
- The paper is well-motivated, easy to follow, and tackles an important, practical problem.
