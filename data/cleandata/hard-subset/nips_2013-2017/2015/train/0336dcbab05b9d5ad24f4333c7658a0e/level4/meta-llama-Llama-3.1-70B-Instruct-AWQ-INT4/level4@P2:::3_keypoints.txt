- Proposes a Top-k Multiclass SVM with a top-k hinge loss that is a tight convex upper bound on the top-k error.  
- Introduces a relaxation that penalizes errors in the top-k predictions less severely.  
- Shows the proposed loss is convex and can be optimized efficiently using Proximal Stochastic Dual Coordinate Ascent (Prox-SDCA).  
- Experimental results on five image classification datasets show consistent improvements in top-k accuracy over baselines.  
- Provides a thorough analysis of the relationship between the top-k hinge loss and ranking-based losses.  
- Paper is well-written, clear, and presents results comprehensively.  
- Suggestion: provide stronger motivation for choosing this specific loss and compare to other possible losses.  
- Minor typo on page 3: replace "globally optimal leads to" with "leads to".  
- Novel contribution: top-k hinge loss and extension of multiclass SVM to optimize top-k performance.  
- Addresses a significant problem for large-class, ambiguous multiclass tasks with practical impact.  
- Overall high-quality work; recommendation to accept pending minor revisions.
