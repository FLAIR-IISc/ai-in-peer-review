

The paper introduces a top-k hinge loss for top-k classification, generalizing the multiclass SVM by employing the sum of the k largest components as a convex approximation to the desired top-k loss. It presents an efficient optimization algorithm and discusses connections to prior work. The paper is clearly written, and experimental results demonstrate the effectiveness of the proposed method relative to baselines.  
1. The authors omitted comparisons with [25, 8] due to unavailability of implementations; I would be interested in seeing these comparisons.

A neat formulation of top-k multiclass SVM that performs well.
