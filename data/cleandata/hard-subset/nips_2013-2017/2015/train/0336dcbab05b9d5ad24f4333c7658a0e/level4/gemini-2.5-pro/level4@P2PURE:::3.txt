This is a well-written paper that presents non-trivial technical material supported by strong experimental results. The paper addresses the interesting problem of learning with a top-k loss, where the objective is to include the true class within a set of k predictions.

My only suggestion is for the authors to provide a more thorough motivation for their choice of loss function.

Additionally, a minor typo was noted on page 3: in the phrase "globally optimal leads to," the word "optimal" appears to be redundant.
