Proposes a novel top-k hinge loss that generalizes multiclass SVM for top-k classification.  
Uses the sum of the k largest components as a convex approximation to the top-k loss.  
Presents an efficient optimization algorithm.  
Provides discussion of relationships with prior work.  
Paper is clearly written.  
Experiments demonstrate effectiveness compared to baseline approaches.  
Does not compare to methods in refs [25, 8] due to unavailable implementations—such comparisons are desirable.  
Recommends making code publicly available upon acceptance to boost impact and reproducibility.  
Overall, a neat formulation of top-k multiclass SVM with promising results.
