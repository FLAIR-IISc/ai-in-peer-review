The paper proposes a top‑k hinge loss for top‑k classification tasks. It generalizes the multiclass SVM by using the sum of the k largest components as a convex surrogate for the desired top‑k loss. The authors present an efficient optimization algorithm and discuss connections to prior work. The paper is clearly written, and the experimental results demonstrate the effectiveness of the proposed method compared to baselines.

1. The authors did not report comparisons with [25, 8] due to the lack of available implementations. I would be very interested in seeing these comparisons.
2. Can the authors make the code public upon acceptance?

Overall, this is a neat and effective formulation of a top‑k multiclass SVM.
