This paper presents a substantial contribution and is clearly articulated. The empirical validation is also strong.

The primary suggestion for improvement is to provide a more thorough motivation for the selection of the loss function.

Additionally, a minor typo was noted on page 3: in the phrase "globally optimal leads to," the word "optimal" should be removed. This is a commendable paper on an interesting problem, centered on the idea of learning with a top-k loss, where zero loss is incurred if the true class is ranked among the top k predictions.
