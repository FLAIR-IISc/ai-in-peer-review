This paper presents a substantial contribution and is clearly articulated. The experimental findings are strong and support the proposed approach. 

My primary suggestion would be to provide additional justification for the selected loss function. 

I also noted a minor typographical error on page 3: “globally optimal leads to” should be revised to “globally leads to.” Overall, this is a compelling paper that addresses an interesting and relevant problem. The core idea – learning with respect to a top-k loss, where the loss is zero when the true class is within the k predicted classes – is particularly insightful.
