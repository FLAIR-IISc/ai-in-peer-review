This paper presents a non-trivial contribution and is clearly written. The experimental results demonstrate the effectiveness of the proposed approach. 

My primary suggestion is to provide additional motivation for the chosen loss function. Additionally, I noted a minor typographical error on page 3: “globally optimal leads to” should be revised to “globally leads to.” Overall, this is a strong paper that addresses an interesting problem. The core idea – learning with respect to a top-k loss, where the loss is zero if the true class is among the top-k predictions – is compelling.
