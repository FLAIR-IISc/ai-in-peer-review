The paper proposes a top-k bias loss for top-k classification. It generalizes multi-class SVMs and uses the sum of the k largest components as a convex approximation of the desired top-k loss. An efficient optimization algorithm is proposed, and the relationships with previous work are discussed. The paper is clearly written. The results of the experiment show the effectiveness of the proposed method compared to the baseline.

1. Due to the availability of implementation, the authors did not report a comparison with [25, 8]. However, I would be interested in seeing the comparison.
