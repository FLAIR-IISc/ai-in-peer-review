- Proposes a direct method to optimize top-k performance by generalizing multiclass SVM, using a convex upper bound that relaxes penalties for top-k errors.  
- Well-written paper with clear and concise explanation of the approach.  
- Experimental results show consistent improvements in top-k accuracy over multiple baselines.  
- Novel approach addressing class ambiguity in large-scale image classification.  
- Choice of the loss function needs stronger motivation and comparison to alternative losses.  
- Minor clarity/typo issues (e.g., "globally optimal leads to" on page 3).  
- Arguments for acceptance: novel idea, strong empirical results, and clear presentation.  
- Arguments against acceptance: insufficiently motivated loss choice and limited discussion of advantages/limitations relative to other methods.  
- Recommendation: accept with minor revisions to address motivation and clarity concerns.
