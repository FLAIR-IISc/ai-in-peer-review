Review- The paper introduces a top-k hinge loss for top-k classification. It extends the multiclass SVM and uses the sum of the k largest components as a convex surrogate for the desired top-k loss. An efficient optimization algorithm is presented, and connections to prior work are discussed. The paper is clearly written. Experimental results demonstrate the effectiveness of the proposed method compared to baselines.
1. The authors did not include comparisons with [25, 8] because implementations were unavailable; however, I am very interested in seeing these comparisons.
2. Can the authors make the code public upon acceptance?
A clean formulation of a top-k multiclass SVM that performs well.
