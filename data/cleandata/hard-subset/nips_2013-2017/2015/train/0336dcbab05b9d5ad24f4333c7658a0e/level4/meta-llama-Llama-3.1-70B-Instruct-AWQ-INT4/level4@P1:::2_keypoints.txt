- Proposes top-k SVM to incur cost only when the true label is outside the top-k predictions, addressing practical tasks like ImageNet.  
- Extends the loss function to a top-k hinge loss and formulates an efficient optimization procedure.  
- Uses Fenchel duality and Prox-SDCA for an efficient, well-established optimization framework.  
- Provides an efficient projection algorithm onto the top-k simplex.  
- Thorough analysis of the top-k hinge loss and its relationship to ranking-based losses.  
- Experiments on large-scale datasets show consistent top-k accuracy improvements over baselines.  
- Demonstrates scalability to millions of training examples.  
- Clearly motivated problem and easy-to-follow presentation.  
- Addresses the important practical issue of similar classes in multiclass learning.  
- Sound technical contribution with convincing empirical validation.  
- Minor weakness: limited discussion of the algorithm’s limitations and directions for future work.  
- Minor weakness: some notations and symbols are not well explained or defined.  
- Arguments for acceptance: significant contribution, scalability, clear writing, and convincing results.  
- Arguments against acceptance: needs more discussion of limitations/future work and clearer notation.
