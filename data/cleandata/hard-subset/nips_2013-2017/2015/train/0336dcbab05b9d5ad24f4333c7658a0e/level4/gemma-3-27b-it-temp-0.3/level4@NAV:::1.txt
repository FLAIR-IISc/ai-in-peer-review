This paper introduces a top-k hinge loss for top-k classification, effectively generalizing the multiclass Support Vector Machine. The authors achieve this by utilizing the sum of the k largest components as a convex approximation of the target top-k loss, and present an efficient optimization algorithm alongside a thorough discussion of related work. The manuscript is well-written and the experimental results demonstrate the proposed method’s effectiveness against established baselines. 

1. While the authors acknowledge the lack of comparison to methods in [25, 8] due to implementation availability, I believe including these comparisons would significantly strengthen the evaluation.
2. To facilitate reproducibility and further research, I recommend the authors consider making their code publicly available upon acceptance of the paper.

Overall, this is a clean and well-formulated approach to top-k multiclass SVM that performs favorably in experiments.
