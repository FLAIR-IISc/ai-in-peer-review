

Review:  
This paper introduces a top-k hinge loss for top-k classification, generalizing multiclass SVM by employing the sum of the k largest components as a convex approximation to the desired top-k loss. The authors propose an efficient optimization algorithm and discuss connections to prior work. The paper is clearly written, and experimental results demonstrate the effectiveness of the proposed method relative to baselines.  

1. The authors omit comparisons with methods in [25, 8] due to unavailability of implementations. I would appreciate seeing these comparisons in the final version.  
2. Will the authors make the code publicly available upon acceptance?  

This is a well-motivated formulation of top-k multiclass SVM that achieves strong empirical performance.
