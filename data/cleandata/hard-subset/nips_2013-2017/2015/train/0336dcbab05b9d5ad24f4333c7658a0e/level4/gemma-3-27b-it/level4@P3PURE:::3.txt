The material presented in this paper is non-trivial, and the manuscript is well-written. The experimental results are also promising. 

My primary suggestion is to provide further motivation for the choice of the proposed loss function. While the concept is clear, elaborating on the reasoning behind this specific formulation would strengthen the paper. 

I also noted a minor typographical error on page 3: “globally optimal leads to” should be revised to “globally leads to” by removing the word “optimal”.

Overall, this is a nice paper that describes an interesting problem. The core idea – learning with respect to a top-k loss, where *k* predictions are made and the loss is zero if the true class is included within those *k* predictions – is compelling.
