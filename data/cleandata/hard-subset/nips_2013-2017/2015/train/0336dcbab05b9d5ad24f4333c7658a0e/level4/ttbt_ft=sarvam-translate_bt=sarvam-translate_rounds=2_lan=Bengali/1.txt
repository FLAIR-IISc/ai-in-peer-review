Here is a proposed top-k loss approximation for the top-k classification. It generalizes the multiclass SVM and uses the sum of the k largest elements as a convex approximation for the desired top-k loss. An efficient optimization algorithm is proposed, and the relationship with previous work is discussed. The article is clearly written. Experimental results show the effectiveness of the proposed method compared to the baseline.
