- Proposes a top-k hinge loss for top-k classification that generalizes multiclass SVM using the sum of the k largest components as a convex approximation.  
- Provides an efficient optimization algorithm for the proposed loss.  
- Discusses connections to prior work.  
- Paper is clearly written.  
- Experimental results show the method outperforms baselines.  
- Reviewer notes missing comparisons with references [25, 8] and would like to see them.  
- Requests the authors commit to releasing code publicly upon acceptance.  
- Overall: neat formulation of top-k multiclass SVM that performs well.
