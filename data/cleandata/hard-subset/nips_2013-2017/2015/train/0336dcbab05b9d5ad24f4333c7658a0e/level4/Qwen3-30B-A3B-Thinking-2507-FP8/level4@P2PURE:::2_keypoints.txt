- Problem focus: multiclass classification with many classes where semantic similarity causes frequent confusions.  
- Objective: minimize cost only when the true class is not among the top-k predictions (top-k evaluation like ImageNet).  
- Method: proposes top-k SVM, extending the standard loss to a top-k loss formulation.  
- Optimization: develops an efficient optimization procedure suitable for the top-k loss.  
- Empirical results: experiments on large-scale datasets show scalability and significant improvements over competing methods.  
- Presentation: paper is clearly motivated, well-structured, and addresses an important practical problem.
