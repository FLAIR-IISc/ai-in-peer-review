- Proposes a top-k hinge loss generalizing multiclass SVM by approximating the top-k loss with the sum of the k largest components, yielding a convex formulation.
- Provides an efficient optimization algorithm and situates the method within related work.
- Paper is well-written and experiments show the proposed method outperforms established baselines.
- Reviewer notes missing empirical comparisons to methods in [25, 8]; including them would considerably strengthen the paper.
- Reviewer requests that the authors release their code upon acceptance.
- Overall assessment: a clean, effective top-k multiclass SVM formulation.
