The paper introduces a top-k hinge loss for top-k classification. It generalizes the multiclass SVM and employs the sum of the k largest components as a convex approximation to the target top-k loss. The authors propose an efficient optimization algorithm and discuss connections to prior work. The paper is clearly written, and the experimental results demonstrate the method’s effectiveness relative to baselines.

1. The authors did not report comparisons with [25, 8] due to the unavailability of implementations. However, I would be very interested in seeing these comparisons.

2. Could the authors commit to releasing the code upon acceptance?

Overall, this is a neat formulation of a top-k multiclass SVM that performs well.
