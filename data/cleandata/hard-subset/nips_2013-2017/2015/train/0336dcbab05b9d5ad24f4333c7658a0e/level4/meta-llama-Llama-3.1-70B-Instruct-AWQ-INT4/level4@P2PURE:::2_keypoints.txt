- Addresses multiclass classification where many classes are semantically similar and only top-k predictions matter (e.g., ImageNet).
- Proposes "top-k SVM" algorithm that incurs cost only if the true label is outside the top k predictions.
- Extends the loss function to incorporate a top-k loss tailored to this evaluation metric.
- Develops an efficient optimization procedure for the proposed top-k loss.
- Demonstrates scalability to extremely large datasets in experiments.
- Shows clear empirical improvements over competing methods on large-scale benchmarks.
- Paper is well-motivated, clearly written, and easy to follow.
