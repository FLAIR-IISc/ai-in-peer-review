The paper introduces a top-k hinge loss for top-k classification. It generalizes the multiclass SVM and employs the sum of the k largest components as a convex surrogate for the desired top-k loss. An efficient optimization method is provided and connections to prior work are discussed. The paper is clearly written. Empirical results demonstrate the method’s effectiveness relative to baselines.
