- Proposes a top-k multiclass SVM that directly optimizes top-k performance via a tight convex upper bound and efficient projection onto the top-k simplex for fast optimization.  
- Paper is well-written with a thorough mathematical formulation and clear optimization framework.  
- Experimental results on five image datasets show consistent improvements in top-k accuracy over various baselines.  
- Strengths: novel method, rigorous mathematical treatment, efficient optimization, and clear exposition.  
- Weakness: needs more motivation and justification for the choice of the top-k hinge loss.  
- Minor issue: typo on page 3 ("globally optimal leads to" — remove "optimal").  
- Overall: promising contribution that could be significant with minor revisions.  
- Pro-accept: novelty, thorough formulation, strong experimental validation.  
- Con-accept: minor typo and limited motivation for the loss choice.
