This paper proposes a top-k hinge loss for top-k classification. It generalizes the multiclass SVM by using the sum of the k largest components as a convex approximation to the desired top-k loss. An efficient optimization algorithm is presented, and the relationships with previous work are discussed. The paper is clearly written, and the experimental results demonstrate the effectiveness of the proposed method compared to baselines.
