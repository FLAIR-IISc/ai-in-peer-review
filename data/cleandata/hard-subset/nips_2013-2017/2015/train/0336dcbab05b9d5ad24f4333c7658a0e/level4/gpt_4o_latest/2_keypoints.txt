- Problem: Many multiclass tasks have large numbers of semantically similar classes causing standard losses to be suboptimal.  
- Contribution: Proposes top-k SVM that penalizes only when the true class is not among the top k predictions.  
- Methodology: Extends loss to a top-k formulation and derives an efficient optimization procedure.  
- Experiments: Evaluated on large-scale datasets, demonstrating scalability.  
- Results: Shows clear improvements over competing methods.  
- Clarity: Paper is well-motivated and easy to follow.  
- Practical significance: Addresses a common, practically important multiclass learning challenge.
