This paper introduces a top-k hinge loss for top-k classification, generalizing multiclass SVMs through a convex approximation utilizing the sum of the k largest components to represent the target loss. The authors present an efficient optimization algorithm and provide a good discussion of related work. The paper is well-written and the experimental results demonstrate the effectiveness of the proposed method against established baselines. 

1. While I understand the reported lack of comparison to methods in [25, 8] is due to implementation availability, I believe including these comparisons would significantly strengthen the paper.
2. To facilitate reproducibility and further research, I request the authors consider making their code publicly available upon acceptance.

Overall, this is a well-formulated and effective approach to top-k multiclass SVM.
