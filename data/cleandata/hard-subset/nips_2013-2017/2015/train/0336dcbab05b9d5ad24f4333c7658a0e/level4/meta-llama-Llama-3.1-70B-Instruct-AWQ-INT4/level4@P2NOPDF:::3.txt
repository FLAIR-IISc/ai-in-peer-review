This paper presents non-trivial material and is well-written, with notable experimental results. To further enhance the manuscript, I suggest providing additional motivation for the chosen loss function. 

A minor correction is also needed on page 3, where the phrase "globally optimal leads to" should be revised to "globally leads to" by removing the word "optimal." 

Overall, this is a well-crafted paper that tackles an intriguing problem. The authors propose an innovative approach to learning with respect to the top-k loss, where the model generates k predictions and incurs zero loss if the true class is among the top k predictions.
