- Proposes a novel top-k SVM algorithm for multiclass classification with semantically similar classes.
- Targets the top-k evaluation setting where cost is incurred only if the true label is outside the top k (e.g., ImageNet).
- Extends the traditional loss function to a top-k loss formulation.
- Develops an efficient optimization procedure for the top-k loss.
- Experiments on large-scale datasets demonstrate scalability and noticeable improvements over competing methods.
- Paper is well-motivated, easy to follow, and effectively handles classes with high similarity.
