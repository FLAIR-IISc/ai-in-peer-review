- Addresses multiclass classification with many semantically similar classes where only top-k predictions matter (e.g., ImageNet).  
- Proposes the top-k SVM algorithm that incurs loss only if the true label is outside the top k predictions.  
- Extends the traditional loss to a top-k loss and derives an efficient optimization procedure.  
- Well-motivated problem and clearly presented ideas.  
- Algorithm is efficient and scalable to large-scale datasets.  
- Experimental results show noticeable improvements over compared methods.  
- Strengths: tackles a common, challenging problem and is easy to follow.  
- Limitation: lacks detailed analyses or discussion of the algorithm’s limitations.  
- Overall: valuable contribution with potential for significant impact after minor revisions.
