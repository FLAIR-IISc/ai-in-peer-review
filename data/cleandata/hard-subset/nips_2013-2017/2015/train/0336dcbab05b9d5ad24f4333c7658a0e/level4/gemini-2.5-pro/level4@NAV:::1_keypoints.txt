- Introduces a top-k hinge loss generalizing multiclass SVM for top-k classification.  
- Uses the sum of the k largest score components as a convex surrogate for the top-k loss.  
- Proposes an efficient optimization algorithm for the new loss.  
- Clearly discusses relationships to prior work.  
- Manuscript is well-written and experiments validate the method against established baselines.  
- Missing empirical comparison to methods in [25, 8]; including them would strengthen the evaluation.  
- Requests authors to commit to releasing code and models upon acceptance for reproducibility.  
- Overall assessment: solid contribution with an elegant and effective top-k multiclass SVM formulation.
