This paper presents a non-trivial contribution and is clearly written. The experimental results demonstrate good performance. 

My primary suggestion would be to provide additional justification for the selected loss function. 

I also noted a minor typographical error on page 3: “globally optimal leads to” should be revised to “globally leads to”. Overall, this is a strong paper that addresses an interesting problem. The core idea – learning with respect to a top-k loss, where the loss is zero when the true class is within the top-k predictions – is particularly compelling.
