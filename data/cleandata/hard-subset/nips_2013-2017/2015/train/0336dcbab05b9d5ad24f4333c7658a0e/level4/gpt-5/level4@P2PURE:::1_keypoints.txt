- Proposes a top-k hinge loss that generalizes multiclass SVM by using the sum of the k largest components as a convex surrogate for the top-k loss.
- Introduces an efficient optimization algorithm and discusses connections with prior work.
- Paper is clearly written and experiments show the method outperforms baselines.
- Missing empirical comparisons with references [25, 8] due to unavailable implementations; reviewer would like to see those comparisons.
- Reviewer asks authors to commit to releasing the code upon acceptance.
- Overall assessment: neat formulation of top-k multiclass SVM with strong empirical results.
