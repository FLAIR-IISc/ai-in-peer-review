- Introduces a convex top-k hinge loss as an upper bound on top-k error and derives an efficient optimization algorithm using Fenchel duality.
- Discusses relationships between the proposed method and prior ranking-based losses.
- Paper is well-written with clear explanations and solid theoretical foundations.
- Experiments show improved top-k accuracy on several image classification datasets.
