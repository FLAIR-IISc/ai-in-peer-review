- Studies estimation of structured signals from nonlinear measurements using the Generalized LASSO and analyzes estimation error.
- Main contribution (Theorem 2.1): asymptotic equivalence of Generalized LASSO error with nonlinear measurements to that with linear measurements.
- Analysis uses a new approach based on the Convex Gaussian Min-max Theorem.
- Paper is well-written with a clear introduction and well-organized, readable technical proofs.
- Results provide a significant theoretical foundation for applying Generalized LASSO in nonlinear measurement settings.
- Limitation: assumes measurement vectors are Gaussian, which may be restrictive and needs justification.
- Issue: paper does not address how to choose the regularizer given the signal set.
- Question: the necessity and interpretation of the dependence on certain parameters is not explained.
- Missing related work: no discussion of single-index models and sufficient dimension reduction literature on linear estimation with unknown link functions.
- Missing related work: does not cite a recent paper by Yi et al. on sparse recovery with link functions that can be zero and equivalence results.
- Overall evaluation: strong, significant contribution but needs to address the above limitations and missing references (score 8/10).
