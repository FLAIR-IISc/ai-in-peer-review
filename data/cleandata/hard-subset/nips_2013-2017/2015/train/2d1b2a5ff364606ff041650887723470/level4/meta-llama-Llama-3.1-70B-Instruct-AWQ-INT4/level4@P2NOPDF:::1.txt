The reviews of other reviewers and the authors' response have not significantly altered my opinion of the paper. I must acknowledge, however, that I am not familiar with the single index model/sufficient dimension reduction literature, and thus may be unaware of relevant previous work. 

The authors derive asymptotic guarantees for the generalized square-root lasso applied to Gaussian measurements that are optionally transformed by a possibly unknown nonlinear function. These results are particularly interesting as they offer a precise characterization of the method's performance. The paper is very well-written, although it would benefit from a thorough proofread, especially in the proof section, where several typos were noted.
