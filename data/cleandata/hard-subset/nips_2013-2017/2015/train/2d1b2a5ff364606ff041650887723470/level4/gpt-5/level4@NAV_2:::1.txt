Answer to authors' response:
The authors’ rebuttal and the other reviews have not substantially altered my evaluation. I should note that I am not well-versed in the single-index model/sufficient dimension reduction literature, so I may be unaware of relevant prior work. The paper provides asymptotic guarantees for the generalized square-root LASSO with i.i.d. Gaussian measurements that are (optionally) passed through a potentially unknown nonlinear link. The findings are compelling, giving a precise, asymptotically exact characterization of the method’s performance, and the manuscript is clearly written, though it contains a number of typos, particularly in the proofs.
