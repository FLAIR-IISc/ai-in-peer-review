- Studies Generalized Lasso with non-linear measurements and Gaussian design, extending Plan & Vershynin by deriving precise asymptotic error expressions.  
- Shows predictions match many special cases (Lasso, group Lasso, nuclear norm).  
- Applies results to optimal q-bit compressed sensing quantizer design and links to Lloyd–Max quantizer.  
- Overall quality: solid, worthwhile contribution.  
- Clarity: well-written but somewhat unbalanced; lack of space omits some details.  
- Originality: builds on [17] but provides novel explicit performance-error formulas.  
- Comment: regularized and penalized formulations are equivalent with the right correspondence (p.4, l.193).  
- Comment: Figure 1 caption formatting error—use $n$=768 instead of 'n=768'.  
- Comment: typo on p.5, l.247.  
- Comment: what authors call the "proximal function" is the Moreau envelope of index τ; prox notation is misleading—suggest renaming.  
- Comment: Assumption 1 should be given a more formal interpretation using variational analysis.  
- Comment: avoid calling the generalized Lasso an "algorithm"; it is a convex program/decoder.  
- Experiments: demonstrate generalized Lasso on non-linear measurements and confirm theoretical error predictions.
