- Contribution: Extends precise asymptotic characterization of the l2-lasso (square-root lasso) to single-index models and relaxes the Gaussian prior assumption on x0.  
- Writing: The article is well-written and clear.  
- Suggestion 1: Explicitly mention in the main text the Brillinger (1982) equivalence between single-index models and linear models with scaled unknown signal and non-standard noise (currently only in Appendix A).  
- Suggestion 2: Discuss whether the practical advantage of the l2-lasso—its oracle regularization parameter not depending on unknown noise variance—actually justifies preference over the l2^2-lasso in this setting.  
- Overall: The extension is a valuable contribution to the literature on precise asymptotic behavior of lasso-type estimators.
