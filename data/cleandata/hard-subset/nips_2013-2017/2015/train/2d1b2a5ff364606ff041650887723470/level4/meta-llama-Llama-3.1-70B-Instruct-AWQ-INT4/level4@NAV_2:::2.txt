The article extends the work on precisely characterizing the statistical performance of the Generalized LASSO to non-linear measurements. The article also relaxes the usual Gaussian assumption on the prior (the distribution of x_0). It is a worthwhile contribution to the literature on precise asymptotic behavior of LASSO-type estimators.

The article is well-written. However,
1. as pointed out by Brillinger (1982), the single index model is equivalent to a linear model with scaled unknown signal and non-standard noise. The connection is desribed rigorously in Theorem 2.1, but it should also be mentioned in the main article to heuristically justify the statement in the introduction.

2. the article focuses on the Generalized LASSO. The main benefit of the Generalized LASSO over the popular `2-LASSO is that it can handle non-linear measurements. A practical question that the results could shed light on is whether this justification for the Generalized LASSO over the `2-LASSO is valid.
