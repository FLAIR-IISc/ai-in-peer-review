My assessment of this work remains largely unchanged following consideration of the other reviews and the authors’ response. I acknowledge a potential gap in my expertise – I am not deeply versed in the single index model or sufficient dimension reduction fields, and thus may be unaware of closely related prior work. The paper presents valuable asymptotic guarantees for the generalized square-root lasso when applied to Gaussian measurements, potentially subjected to an unknown nonlinear transformation. These results offer a rigorous performance analysis of the method and are presented in a clearly written manuscript, though the proof sections contain a notable number of typographical errors.
