Response to the Authors' Reaction:

Other critics' reviews and the authors' reaction have not significantly influenced my opinion on the paper. Having said that, I am not familiar with the single index model/sufficient dimension reduction literature, so I may be unaware of previous work that could be relevant. The authors have obtained infinite guarantees for the generalized square-root Lasso applied to Gaussian measures that are (optionally) transformed by a potential unknown non-linear function. The results are very interesting, as they provide an accurate description of the method's performance, and the paper is very well written (although it has quite a few typos, especially in the proof section).
