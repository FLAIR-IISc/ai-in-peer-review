- Extends precise characterization of LASSO performance to single-index models with non-linear measurements.  
- Shows Generalized LASSO with yi = g(ai^T x0) is asymptotically equivalent to linear model yi = μ ai^T x0 + σ zi with μ = E[γ g(γ)], σ^2 = E[(g(γ)-μγ)^2].  
- Provides exact asymptotic error expressions for regularized Generalized LASSO, not just bounds.  
- Generalizes and improves prior work (Brillinger 1982; Plan & Vershynin 2015) which handled only unconstrained LASSO or gave upper bounds.  
- Demonstrates practical application to q-bit compressive sensing and optimal quantizer design.  
- Technically sound: rigorous proofs and results valid for a wide class of regularizers and nonlinearities.  
- Empirically validated with illustrations supporting theoretical claims.  
- Original contribution: advances precise analysis of LASSO-type estimators beyond state-of-the-art.  
- Significant practical impact for systems with nonlinear measurements (e.g., 1-bit quantization).  
- Clear writing, good organization, and appropriate notation.
