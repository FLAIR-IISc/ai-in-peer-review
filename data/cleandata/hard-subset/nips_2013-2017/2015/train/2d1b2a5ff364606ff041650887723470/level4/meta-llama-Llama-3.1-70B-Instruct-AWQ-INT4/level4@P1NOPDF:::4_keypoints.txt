- Paper studies linear estimation with nonlinear link functions and analyzes asymptotic error of generalized lasso for arbitrary link functions (μ ≠ 0).  
- Main claim (Thm. 2.1): asymptotic estimation error is the same for linear and nonlinear link functions.  
- Theorem 2.1 assumes Gaussian measurement vectors; reviewer requests justification for this potentially restrictive assumption.  
- Unclear guidance on how to choose a regularizer based on the set K containing x0; the convex-regularizer assumption is discussed but not resolved.  
- Results reduce nonlinear problems to linear ones but still depend on μ and σ; reviewer questions whether these dependencies are necessary.  
- Paper omits relevant literature on single-index models and sufficient dimension reduction that study subspace/linear estimation under unknown links.  
- Missing citation to Yi et al. (“Optimal linear estimation under unknown link”), which proposes an algorithm for sparse recovery even when μ=0 and supports equivalence claims with theory and experiments.  
- Overall assessment: well-written and interesting results, but needs justification of assumptions and broader literature connections.
