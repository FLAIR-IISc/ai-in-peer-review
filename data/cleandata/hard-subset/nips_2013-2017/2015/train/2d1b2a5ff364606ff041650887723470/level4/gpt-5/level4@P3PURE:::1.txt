Answer to authors' response:
The other reviewers’ comments and the authors’ response have not significantly changed my opinion of the paper. That said, I am not familiar with the single-index model/sufficient dimension reduction literature, so I may be unaware of relevant prior work. The authors derive asymptotic guarantees for the generalized square-root Lasso applied to Gaussian measurements that are optionally transformed by a possibly unknown nonlinear function. The results are very interesting, as they provide a precise characterization of the method’s performance, and the paper is very well written, although there are quite a few typos, especially in the proof section.
