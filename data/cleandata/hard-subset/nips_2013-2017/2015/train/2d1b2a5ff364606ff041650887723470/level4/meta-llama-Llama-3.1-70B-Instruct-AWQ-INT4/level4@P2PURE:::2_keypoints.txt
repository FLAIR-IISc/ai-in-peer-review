- Extends precise statistical characterization of the l2-lasso to single-index models and relaxes the common Gaussian prior assumption on x_0.  
- Well-written paper that advances understanding of the precise asymptotic behavior of lasso-type estimators.  
- Recommend mentioning Brillinger (1982)’s equivalence (single-index model ↔ linear model with scaled unknown signal and non-standard noise) in the main text as a heuristic justification for the blockquote on line 140 (Appendix A already gives a rigorous account).  
- Suggest discussing the practical implication that the l2-lasso’s oracle regularization parameter is independent of noise variance σ, and whether the results justify preferring l2-lasso over l2^2-lasso in practice.
