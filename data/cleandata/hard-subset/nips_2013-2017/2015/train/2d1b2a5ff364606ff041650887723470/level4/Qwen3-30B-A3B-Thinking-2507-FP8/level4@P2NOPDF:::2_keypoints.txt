- Contribution: Extends precise asymptotic characterization of the square-root Lasso to single-index models, relaxing the Gaussian prior on x0; manuscript is well-written and technically sound.

- Point 1: The statistical equivalence of the single-index model to a linear model with scaled unknown signal and non-standard noise (Brillinger 1982) is proven in Appendix A but should be stated explicitly in the main text for heuristic clarity.

- Point 2: The paper does not clarify whether the square-root Lasso’s key practical advantage—its oracle regularization parameter being independent of noise variance σ—continues to hold in the single-index setting.

- Suggestion: Add a brief analysis or discussion on how the single-index noise structure affects regularization-parameter selection and whether square-root Lasso is preferable to standard Lasso under the relaxed assumptions.

- Recommendation: Work makes a valuable theoretical contribution and is suitable for NIPS pending these minor clarifications.
