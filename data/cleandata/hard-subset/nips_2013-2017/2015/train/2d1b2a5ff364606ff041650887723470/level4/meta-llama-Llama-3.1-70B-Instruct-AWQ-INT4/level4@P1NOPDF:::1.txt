Answer to authors' response:

The reviews of other reviewers and the authors' response have not significantly altered my opinion of the paper. That being said, I must acknowledge that I am not familiar with the single index model/sufficient dimension reduction literature, and thus I may be unaware of relevant prior work. The authors derive asymptotic guarantees for the generalized square-root lasso applied to Gaussian measurements that are optionally transformed by a possibly unknown nonlinear function. These results are particularly interesting, as they provide a precise characterization of the method's performance. The paper is very well written, although there are quite a few typos, especially in the proof section.
