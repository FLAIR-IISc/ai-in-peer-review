The other reviews and the authors' response have not significantly affected my opinion of the paper. That said, I am not familiar with the literature on single index models and sufficient dimension reduction, so it is possible I am unaware of some relevant previous work.

The authors derive asymptotic guarantees for the generalized square-root lasso applied to Gaussian measurements that are optionally transformed by a possibly unknown nonlinear function. The results are very interesting, as they provide a precise characterization of the method's performance. The paper is very well-written, although there are a number of typographical errors, especially in the proof section.
