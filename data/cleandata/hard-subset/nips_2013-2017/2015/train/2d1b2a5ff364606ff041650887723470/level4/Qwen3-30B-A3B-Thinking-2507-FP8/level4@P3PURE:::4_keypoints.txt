- Paper extends Plan and Vershynin to analyze asymptotic error of generalized lasso for arbitrary link functions with μ ≠ 0, showing error matches the linear case (Theorem 2.1).  
- Theorem 2.1 assumes Gaussian measurement vectors; reviewer requests clarification on necessity and restrictiveness of this assumption.  
- Open question 1: How should the regularizer be chosen given the set Ω that contains the true signal x0; paper discusses convex regularizers but not selection relative to Ω.  
- Open question 2: Although linear/nonlinear equivalence is shown, the result depends on μ and σ; authors should justify why these dependencies are necessary.  
- Missing literature: single-index models and sufficient dimension reduction work on linear/subspace estimation under unknown links and key contributions are not cited.  
- Missing literature: recent paper by Yi et al. on optimal linear estimation under unknown link functions (sparse recovery with μ = 0) is closely related and should be discussed.  
- Overall: Results are interesting but addressing the above theoretical clarifications and omitted references would substantially strengthen the paper.
