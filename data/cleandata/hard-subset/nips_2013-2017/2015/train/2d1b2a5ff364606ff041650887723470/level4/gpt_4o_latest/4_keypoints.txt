- Studies linear estimation with a nonlinear link, analyzing generalized lasso asymptotics for arbitrary link functions with μ ≠ 0, building on Plan & Vershynin.
- Theorem 2.1 claims the asymptotic estimation error is identical for linear and nonlinear link functions.
- Theorem 2.1 assumes Gaussian measurement vectors, which may be a restrictive assumption needing justification.
- The paper does not address how to choose the regularizer based on the constraint set K containing x0, beyond assuming convexity.
- The results depend on μ and σ (which capture estimation difficulty); the necessity of these dependencies should be discussed.
- Missing citations to the single-index model and sufficient dimension reduction literature on subspace estimation under unknown link functions.
- Missing citation to Yi et al.’s recent work (algorithm for sparse recovery even when μ = 0) that also establishes equivalence between linear and nonlinear high-dimensional estimation.
- Overall assessment: paper is well-written and presents intriguing results.
