Answer to authors' response:
The other reviews and the authors’ response have not materially changed my evaluation of the paper. That said, I am not well versed in the single-index model/sufficient dimension reduction literature, so I may be unaware of relevant prior work. The authors establish asymptotic guarantees for the generalized square-root lasso applied to Gaussian measurements that are optionally transformed by a possibly unknown nonlinear function. The results are very interesting, providing a precise characterization of the method’s performance, and the paper is generally well written, although it contains numerous typos, especially in the proof section.
