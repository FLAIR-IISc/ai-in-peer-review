Answer to authors' response:
Upon considering the reviews from other evaluators and the authors' response, my overall assessment of the manuscript remains largely unchanged. It is possible that my limited familiarity with the single index model and sufficient dimension reduction literature may have led to an incomplete consideration of relevant prior work. Nevertheless, the authors' derivation of asymptotic guarantees for the generalized square-root lasso in the context of Gaussian measurements, which may be subject to an unknown nonlinear transformation, is noteworthy. The results provide a detailed analysis of the method's performance, and the manuscript is well-written, despite containing several typographical errors, particularly in the proof section.
