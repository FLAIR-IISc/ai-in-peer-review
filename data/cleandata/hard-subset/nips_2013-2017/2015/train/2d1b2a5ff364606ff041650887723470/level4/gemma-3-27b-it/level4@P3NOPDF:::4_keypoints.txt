- Main contribution: analyzes generalized lasso for arbitrary nonlinear link functions with non-zero mean shift and shows asymptotic estimation error is identical for linear and nonlinear links (Theorem 2.1).  
- Theorem 2.1 relies on Gaussian measurement vectors; the reviewer requests justification of this normality assumption and discussion of relaxing it or alternative distributions.  
- Ask for practical guidance on selecting the regularization parameter for convex regularizers given the constraint set K containing x0.  
- Question the necessity of the error’s dependence on μ and σ—whether this reflects a fundamental statistical difference for nonlinear links or is an artifact of the analysis.  
- Literature review is incomplete: missing connections to single-index models and sufficient dimension reduction literature.  
- Missing citation/discussion of Yi et al. (“Optimal linear estimation under unknown linear estimation”), which shows related equivalence results and an algorithm for the μ=0 case.  
- Overall assessment: paper is well-written with noteworthy results; addressing the above points would improve impact and completeness.
