My evaluation of the manuscript is not substantially altered after reading the other reviewers' comments and the authors' response. I should note, however, that my expertise does not lie in the single index model and sufficient dimension reduction literature; consequently, I may not be fully aware of all relevant prior work. 

The authors establish asymptotic guarantees for the generalized square-root lasso under a setting where Gaussian measurements are processed by a potentially unknown nonlinear function. These results are quite compelling, providing a sharp characterization of the method's performance. The paper is clearly articulated, though it contains a fair number of typographical errors that are particularly frequent in the proofs.
