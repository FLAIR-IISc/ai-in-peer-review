The reviews of other reviewers and the authors’ response have not significantly altered my opinion of this paper. I admit, however, to a lack of familiarity with the single index model and sufficient dimension reduction literature, and therefore may be unaware of potentially relevant prior work. The authors derive asymptotic guarantees for the generalized square-root lasso applied to Gaussian measurements, which are optionally transformed by a potentially unknown nonlinear function. These results are very interesting, providing a precise characterization of the method’s performance. The paper is well-written, though it contains a number of typos, particularly within the proof sections.
