Following the reviews and the authors’ response, my overall assessment of the paper remains largely unchanged. I acknowledge a potential limitation in my own expertise; I am not deeply familiar with the single index model or sufficient dimension reduction literature, and thus may be unaware of related prior work. The paper presents asymptotic guarantees for the generalized square-root lasso when applied to Gaussian measurements, potentially subjected to an unknown nonlinear transformation. These results are compelling, offering a precise performance characterization of the method, and the paper is generally very well written – though it contains a noticeable number of typographical errors, particularly within the proof sections.
