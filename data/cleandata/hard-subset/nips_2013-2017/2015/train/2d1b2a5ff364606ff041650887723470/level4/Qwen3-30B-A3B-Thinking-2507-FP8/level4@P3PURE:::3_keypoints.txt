- Studies Generalized Lasso under non-linear measurements with Gaussian design, treating non-linear observations as noisy linear ones and extending Plan & Vershynin asymptotically.  
- Derives novel, precise, explicit asymptotic expressions for estimation error.  
- Validated across key special cases (Lasso, group Lasso, nuclear norm).  
- Application to q-bit compressed sensing quantizer design and connection to Lloyd–Max quantizer is insightful.  
- Quality: well-executed work.  
- Clarity: generally well-written but treatment is somewhat unbalanced and limited space omits some details.  
- Originality: extends Plan & Vershynin by providing explicit error-performance expressions.
