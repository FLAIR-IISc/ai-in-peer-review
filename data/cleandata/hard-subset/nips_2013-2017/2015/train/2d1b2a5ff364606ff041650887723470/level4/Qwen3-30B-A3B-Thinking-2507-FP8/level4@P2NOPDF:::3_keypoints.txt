- Paper derives explicit asymptotic estimation-error expressions for the Generalized Lasso under non-linear measurements with Gaussian designs, extending Plan & Vershynin.  
- Results are validated across special cases (Lasso, group Lasso, nuclear norm minimization) and applied to q-bit compressed sensing quantizer design.  
- Links optimal quantizer design for q-bit compressed sensing to the Lloyd–Max quantizer.  
- Overall quality: well-executed contribution with strong theoretical and practical implications.  
- Clarity: generally well-written but technical content is unevenly treated; key details on special cases and the quantizer are underdeveloped due to space.  
- Originality: novel extension providing explicit asymptotic error characterization for non-linear measurements.
