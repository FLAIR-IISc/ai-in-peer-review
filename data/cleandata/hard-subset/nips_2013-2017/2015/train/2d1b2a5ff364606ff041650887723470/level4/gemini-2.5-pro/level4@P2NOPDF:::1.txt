Answer to authors' response:

My assessment of the paper remains largely unchanged after considering the authors' response and the comments from the other reviewers.

I should note that I am not an expert in the literature on single index models or sufficient dimension reduction. Consequently, my evaluation may not fully account for all relevant prior work in this specific area.

The authors derive asymptotic guarantees for the generalized square-root lasso when applied to Gaussian measurements, which are permitted to be transformed by a potentially unknown nonlinear function. The results are of significant interest, as they provide a precise characterization of the method's performance under these conditions. The paper is very well-written and clearly presented. However, I noticed a number of typographical errors, particularly in the proof section, which should be carefully corrected before publication.
