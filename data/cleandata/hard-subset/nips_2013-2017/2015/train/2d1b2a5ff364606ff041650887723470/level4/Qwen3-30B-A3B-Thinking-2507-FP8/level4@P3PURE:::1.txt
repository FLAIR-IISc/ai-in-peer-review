

Answer to authors' response:  
The reviews from other reviewers and the authors' response have not significantly altered my assessment of the paper. That said, I am not familiar with the single-index model/sufficient dimension reduction literature, and I may be unaware of relevant prior work. The authors derive asymptotic guarantees for the generalized square-root lasso applied to Gaussian measurements that are optionally transformed by a possibly unknown nonlinear function. The results are highly interesting, providing a precise characterization of the method's performance, and the paper is very well written, though it contains several typos, especially in the proof section.
