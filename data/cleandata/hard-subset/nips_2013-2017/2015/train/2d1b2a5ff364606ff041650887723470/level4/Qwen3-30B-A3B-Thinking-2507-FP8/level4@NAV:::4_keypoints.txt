- Paper studies linear estimation with a nonlinear link, extending Plan & Vershynin to generalized lasso for arbitrary link functions with μ ≠ 0.  
- Main result (Theorem 2.1): asymptotic estimation error is identical for linear and nonlinear links.  
- Theorem 2.1 assumes Gaussian measurement vectors — reviewer asks the authors to justify the necessity and potential restrictiveness of normality.  
- The paper lacks guidance on how to choose a regularizer given the constraint set K containing x0, beyond noting convex regularizers.  
- The reviewer requests discussion of why the equivalence result necessarily depends on the link-characterizing parameters μ and σ.  
- Missing citations to single-index models and sufficient dimension reduction literature on estimation under unknown link functions.  
- Missing citation to Yi et al., "Optimal linear estimation under unknown link functions," which proves equivalence for sparse recovery (including μ = 0) and offers theoretical and empirical support.
