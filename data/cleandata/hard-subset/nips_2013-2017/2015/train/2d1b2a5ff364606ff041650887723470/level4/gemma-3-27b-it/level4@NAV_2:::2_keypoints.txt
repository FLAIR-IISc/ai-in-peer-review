- Extends precise asymptotic characterization of the l2-lasso to single-index models; worthwhile contribution.  
- Well-written and clearly presented with thorough motivation linking to Brillinger and Plan & Vershynin.  
- Theoretical results are rigorous and supported by illustrative figures.  
- Extensive related-work section demonstrating strong understanding of the field.  
- Suggest explicitly stating equivalence between single-index models and scaled linear models with non-standard noise in the main text (e.g., near line 140) for intuition.  
- Recommend discussing whether results support the l2-lasso’s practical advantage that optimal regularization is independent of noise variance.  
- Novelty: first precise asymptotic guarantees for Generalized LASSO with non-linear measurements.  
- Significance: potential impact on estimation with non-linear measurement devices (e.g., quantized compressive sensing).  
- Technical soundness: rigorous analysis built on a strong theoretical foundation.  
- Clarity: paper is well-organized and makes complex details accessible.  
- Concern: connection to literature on benefits of the l2-lasso could be explored further.  
- Concern: practical implications and computational complexity of applying derived error formulas are not fully discussed.  
- Overall: high-quality paper with minor suggestions; recommendation for acceptance.
