Answer to authors' response:
My opinion of the paper remains largely unchanged after reading the other reviews and the authors' response. I should note, however, that I am not an expert in the literature on single index models or sufficient dimension reduction, and therefore may be unaware of some relevant prior work.

The authors derive asymptotic guarantees for the generalized square-root lasso when applied to Gaussian measurements transformed by a possibly unknown nonlinear function. The results are compelling, as they provide a precise characterization of the method's performance. The paper is very well-written, although it contains numerous typographical errors, especially in the proof section.
