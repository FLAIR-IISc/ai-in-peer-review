Answer to authors' response:
The other reviews and the authors’ response have not materially changed my assessment. I am not well versed in the single-index model/sufficient dimension reduction literature, so I may be unaware of relevant prior work in those areas.

The paper derives asymptotic guarantees for the generalized LASSO (square-root LASSO) applied to Gaussian measurements that are optionally transformed by a possibly unknown nonlinear link function. The results are very interesting, providing a precise characterization of the estimator’s performance. The paper is generally well written, though there are quite a few typos, especially in the proofs.
