- Studies asymptotic estimation error of generalized LASSO with nonlinear link functions and shows asymptotic error equals the linear case under certain conditions.
- Extends Plan & Vershynin to arbitrary link functions with non-zero mean and derives an asymptotically exact error expression.
- Paper is well-written, clear, and provides detailed proofs and thorough analysis.
- Assumes Gaussian measurement vectors; this may be too restrictive and requires justification for necessity.
- Does not provide practical guidance on choosing a regularizer based on the set K containing x0.
- Omits literature review on single index models and sufficient dimension reduction, which are relevant.
- Fails to cite a recent paper by Yi et al. on sparse recovery for zero-mean link functions and should discuss its relevance.
- Strengths: precise analysis, exact error derivation, clear presentation.
- Weaknesses: restrictive Gaussian assumption, lack of regularizer selection guidance, literature omissions, missing Yi et al. citation.
- Recommendation: accept after addressing the above issues (justify Gaussianity, add regularizer guidance, expand literature review, cite/discuss Yi et al.).
