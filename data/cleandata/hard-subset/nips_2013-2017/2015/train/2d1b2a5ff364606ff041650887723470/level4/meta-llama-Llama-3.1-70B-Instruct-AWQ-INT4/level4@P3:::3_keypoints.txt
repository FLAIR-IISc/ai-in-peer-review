- Studies Generalized Lasso with non-linear measurements and Gaussian design, extending Plan & Vershynin to give asymptotic explicit error expressions.  
- Provides novel, precise performance error formulas not previously available.  
- Empirical validation across special cases (Lasso, group Lasso, nuclear norm) confirms accuracy of predictions.  
- Insightful application to optimal q‑bit quantizer design and its connection to the Lloyd‑Max quantizer.  
- Paper is well written and mostly clear, but space constraints leave out necessary details, making the presentation somewhat unbalanced.  
- Recommend adding explanations/references for readers lacking a strong convex analysis or variational analysis background.  
- Terminology: should not call the generalized Lasso an “algorithm”; it is a convex program/decoder.  
- Experiments support theory but need more detail on experimental settings and parameter choices.  
- Minor correction: state equivalence of regularized and penalized versions with appropriate correspondence (p.4, l.193).  
- Minor correction: Figure 1 caption formatting ('n=768' → '$n$=768').  
- Minor correction: fix typo on p.5, l.247.  
- Notation suggestion: the “proximal function” is the Moreau envelope of index τ; change notation (prox usually denotes proximal operator).  
- Suggest formal variational‑analysis interpretation of Assumption 1 for clarity.  
- Overall: solid, novel contribution that would benefit from minor corrections and additional clarifications.
