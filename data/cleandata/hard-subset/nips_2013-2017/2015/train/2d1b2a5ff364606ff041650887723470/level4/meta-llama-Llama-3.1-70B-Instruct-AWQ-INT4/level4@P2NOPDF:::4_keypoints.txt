- Paper studies linear estimation with nonlinear link functions, building on Plan and Vershynin, using generalized lasso for arbitrary links with μ ≠ 0.  
- Theorem 2.1 claims the asymptotic estimation error is identical for linear and nonlinear link functions.  
- Theorem 2.1 assumes Gaussian measurement vectors; the necessity and restrictiveness of this Gaussianity should be clarified.  
- The choice of regularizer tied to the set κ containing x0 is not fully justified; discussion beyond assuming convex regularizers is needed.  
- The results hinge on μ and σ (which measure hardness of estimation from g); authors should discuss whether these dependencies are necessary.  
- Relevant literature is missing, notably work on single index models and sufficient dimension reduction for estimation under unknown link functions.  
- Missing citation to a recent Yi et al. paper that gives an algorithm for sparse recovery even when μ = 0 and establishes equivalence under conditions.  
- Overall impression: well-written paper with interesting and significant results.
