

Answer to authors' response:  
The reviews from other reviewers and the authors' response have not substantially altered my assessment of the paper. That said, I am not deeply familiar with the single-index model and sufficient dimension reduction literature, so I may be unaware of relevant prior work. The authors establish asymptotic guarantees for the generalized square-root lasso when applied to Gaussian measurements transformed by an unknown nonlinear function (with optional transformation). The results are highly significant, offering a precise characterization of the method's performance, and the paper is exceptionally well written—despite a number of typos, particularly in the proof section.
