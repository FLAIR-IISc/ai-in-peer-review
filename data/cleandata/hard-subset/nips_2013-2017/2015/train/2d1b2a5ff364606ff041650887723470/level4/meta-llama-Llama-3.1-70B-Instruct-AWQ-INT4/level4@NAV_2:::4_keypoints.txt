- Main contribution: Theorem 2.1 shows asymptotic equivalence of estimation error for linear and nonlinear measurements using generalized LASSO (for arbitrary link functions with μ ≠ 0).
- Assumption concern: The result requires the linear measurement vectors to be Gaussian; the reviewer asks why this potentially strict assumption is necessary.
- Regularizer choice: The paper does not address how to choose a convex regularizer based on the set K containing x0.
- Role of μ and σ: Although nonlinear and linear measurements are shown equivalent, the dependence on μ and σ (which capture the difficulty from g) needs discussion on whether these dependencies are necessary.
- Missing literature: Key references on single-index models and sufficient dimension reduction are not cited.
- Missing related work: The reviewer points to a recent paper by Yi et al. on sparse recovery with link functions having zero μ that is highly relevant and should be discussed.
- Positive: Paper is well-written with interesting results but should address the questions and add relevant references to strengthen connections to existing work.
