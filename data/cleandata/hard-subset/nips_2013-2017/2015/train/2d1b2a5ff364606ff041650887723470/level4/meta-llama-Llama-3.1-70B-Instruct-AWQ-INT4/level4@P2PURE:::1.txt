The reviews of other reviewers and the authors' response have not significantly altered my opinion of the paper. Although I must acknowledge that my unfamiliarity with the single index model and sufficient dimension reduction literature may mean that I am unaware of relevant prior work. The authors derive asymptotic guarantees for the generalized square-root lasso applied to Gaussian measurements that are optionally transformed by a possibly unknown nonlinear function. These results are very interesting, as they provide a precise characterization of the method's performance. The paper is well-written, despite containing a notable number of typos, particularly in the proof section.
