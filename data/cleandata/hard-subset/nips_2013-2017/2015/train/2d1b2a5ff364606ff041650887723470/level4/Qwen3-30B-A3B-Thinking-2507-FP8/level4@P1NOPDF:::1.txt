

The authors' response and other reviewers' comments have not altered my assessment of this paper. While I acknowledge my limited familiarity with the single index model and sufficient dimension reduction literature—meaning I may have overlooked relevant prior work—the paper's technical contributions remain compelling. The authors establish asymptotic guarantees for the generalized square-root lasso applied to Gaussian measurements optionally transformed via an unknown nonlinear function. Their results provide a precise characterization of the method's performance, which is both novel and valuable. The paper is well-written and clearly structured, though it contains several typos, particularly in the proof sections. I recommend acceptance pending minor corrections to these errors.
