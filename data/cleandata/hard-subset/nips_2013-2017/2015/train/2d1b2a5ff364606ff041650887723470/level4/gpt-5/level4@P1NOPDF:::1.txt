Answer to authors' response:
The other reviews and the authors’ response have not significantly changed my assessment of the paper. I should note that I am not well-versed in the single-index model or sufficient dimension reduction literatures, so I may be unaware of relevant prior work.

The authors derive asymptotic guarantees for the generalized square-root Lasso applied to Gaussian measurements that are (optionally) transformed by a possibly unknown nonlinear function. The results are very interesting, as they provide a precise characterization of the method’s performance. The paper is generally well written, though there are quite a few typos, especially in the proof section.
