- Studies generalized Lasso with non-linear measurements and Gaussian design, extending Plan and Vershynin to an asymptotic regime with precise explicit error expressions.  
- Demonstrates accuracy of predictions through many special cases (Lasso, group Lasso, nuclear norm) and numerical simulations.  
- Novel and insightful application to designing optimal quantizers for q-bit compressed sensing and its relation to the Lloyd–Max quantizer.  
- Paper is well-written, clear, well-organized, and uses consistent notation.  
- Strong theoretical analysis supports the results.  
- High originality: significant contributions beyond [17] via explicit error formulas.  
- Request to clarify the claim that regularized and penalized versions are equivalent (p.4, l.193).  
- Typographical error to fix: misspelling of "When" (p.5, l.247).  
- Suggest providing a more formal interpretation of Assumption 1 using variational analysis.  
- Advise not to call the generalized Lasso an "algorithm"; describe it as a convex program or decoder.  
- Overall: high-quality paper with minor revisions likely to be high-impact; rating 9/10.
