- Studies Generalized Lasso with non-linear measurements under Gaussian design, using the heuristic that non-linear observations approximate noisy linear ones.  
- Extends Plan and Vershynin [17] to an asymptotic regime and derives precise, explicit expressions for estimation error.  
- Validates theoretical predictions through special cases (Lasso, group Lasso, nuclear norm).  
- Provides an insightful discussion on designing optimal quantizers for q-bit compressed sensing and its connection to the Lloyd–Max quantizer.  
- Quality: a commendable and well-executed piece of research.  
- Clarity: generally well-written but presentation is slightly unbalanced and some details are omitted due to space.  
- Originality: builds on prior work but contributes novel, significant explicit error expressions.  
- Comment: regularized and penalized formulations are equivalent under the appropriate correspondence (p.4).
