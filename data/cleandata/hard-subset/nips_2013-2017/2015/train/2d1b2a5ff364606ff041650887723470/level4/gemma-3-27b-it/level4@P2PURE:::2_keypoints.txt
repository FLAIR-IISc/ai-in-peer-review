- Extends precise statistical performance analysis of the l2-lasso to single-index models and relaxes the Gaussian prior assumption on x0.  
- Valuable contribution to literature on precise asymptotic behavior of lasso-type estimators.  
- Paper is well-written.  
- Suggest briefly noting Brillinger (1982) equivalence (single-index model ≈ linear model with scaled unknown signal and non-standard noise) in the main text to heuristically justify the blockquote on line 140; Appendix A already gives the rigorous connection.  
- Recommend using the results to investigate whether the l2-lasso’s tuning-parameter independence from noise variance yields practical advantages over the l2^2-lasso.  
- Overall, a worthwhile contribution.
