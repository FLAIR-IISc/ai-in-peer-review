- Main contribution: provides precise asymptotic explicit expressions for estimation error of the Generalized Lasso with non-linear measurements, extending Plan & Vershynin [17].  
- Validated predictions across special cases (Lasso, group Lasso, nuclear norm) and via experiments.  
- Novel application: design of optimal quantizers for q-bit compressive sensing and connection to the Lloyd–Max quantizer.  
- Quality assessment: significant and valuable contribution to compressive sensing theory.  
- Clarity: well-written but uneven; some sections feel rushed and notation is occasionally inconsistent.  
- Typographical/format issues: minor typos and caption formatting need correction (e.g., Fig.1 n=768, Page 5 typos).  
- Terminology: avoid calling the generalized Lasso an “algorithm”; it is a convex program/decoder.  
- Proximal notation: the function called “proximal” should be identified as the Moreau envelope; change notation to avoid confusion with proximal operator.  
- Assumption 1: recommend a more formal interpretation using variational analysis to clarify mathematical structure.  
- Experiments: request more experimental results, robustness checks, and detailed description of experimental setup and parameters.
