

The reviews from other reviewers and the authors' response have not altered my assessment of the paper. That said, I acknowledge my limited familiarity with the single index model and sufficient dimension reduction literature, so I may be unaware of pertinent prior work. The authors establish asymptotic guarantees for the generalized square-root lasso applied to Gaussian measurements that are optionally transformed via a possibly unknown nonlinear function. The results are highly significant, offering a precise characterization of the method's performance, and the paper is well-written (though numerous typographical errors persist, particularly in the proof section).
