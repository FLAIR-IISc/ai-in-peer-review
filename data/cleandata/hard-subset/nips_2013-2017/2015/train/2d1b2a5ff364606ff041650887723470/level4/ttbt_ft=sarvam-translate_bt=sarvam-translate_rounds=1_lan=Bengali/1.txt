Authors' Response to the Response:

Other reviewers' reviews and authors' responses did not significantly influence my opinion of the article. However, I am not familiar with the literature on single index models/adequate reduction, so I may not be aware of prior work that could be relevant. The authors provide an infinite guarantee for the generalized square root Lasso applied to Gaussian measurements that are transformed (optionally) by an unknown non-linear function. The results are very interesting, as they provide a specific characterization of the method's performance, and the article is very well written (although there are some errors, especially in the proof section).
