- Contribution: Extends precise statistical characterization of the l2-lasso to single-index models, relaxing the Gaussian prior assumption on x0 — a worthwhile advancement.

- Clarity: The article is well-written.

- Suggestion (theory-to-text): The equivalence of single-index models to linear models with scaled unknown signals and non-standard noise (Brillinger 1982) is rigorously in Appendix A but should be mentioned in the main text to support the blockquote on line 140.

- Suggestion (practical relevance): Since the l2-lasso (square-root lasso) has an oracle tuning parameter independent of noise variance, the paper should investigate whether this theoretical advantage yields practical benefits over the l2^2-lasso when noise variance is unknown.

- Overall assessment: Provides a valuable extension to the literature on precise asymptotic behavior of lasso-type estimators.
