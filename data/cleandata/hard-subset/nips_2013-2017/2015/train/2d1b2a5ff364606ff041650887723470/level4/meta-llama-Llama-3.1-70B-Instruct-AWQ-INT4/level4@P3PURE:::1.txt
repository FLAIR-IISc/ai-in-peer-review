After considering the reviews of other reviewers and the authors' response, my opinion of the paper remains largely unchanged. However, I must acknowledge that I am not familiar with the single index model/sufficient dimension reduction literature, so it is possible that I am unaware of relevant prior work. The authors derive asymptotic guarantees for the generalized square-root lasso applied to Gaussian measurements that are optionally transformed by a possibly unknown nonlinear function. These results are very interesting, as they provide a precise characterization of the performance of the method. The paper is also well-written, although there are several typos, particularly in the proof section.
