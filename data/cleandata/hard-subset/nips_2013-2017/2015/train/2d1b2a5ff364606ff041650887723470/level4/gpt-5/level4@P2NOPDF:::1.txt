Response to authors’ response:
The other reviews and the authors’ rebuttal have not materially changed my assessment. I am not well versed in the single-index model and sufficient dimension reduction literature, so I may be unaware of relevant prior work. The paper derives asymptotic guarantees for the generalized square-root Lasso applied to Gaussian measurements that may be transformed by an unknown nonlinear function. The results are compelling, offering a precise characterization of the method’s performance. The manuscript is clearly written overall, though there are numerous typos, particularly in the proof section.
