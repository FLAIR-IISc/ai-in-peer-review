- Provides precise asymptotic characterization of the l2-lasso (square-root lasso) applied to single-index models.
- Extends precise asymptotic analysis beyond standard linear models to a non-linear single-index setting.
- Relaxes the common Gaussian prior assumption on the signal, broadening theoretical applicability.
- Paper is well-written and technically sound with significant contributions to high-dimensional statistics.
- Recommend moving the Brillinger (1982) connection (single-index ↔ scaled linear model with non-standard noise) from Appendix A into the main text for intuition.
- Suggest discussing practical implications: compare l2-lasso’s oracle regularizer (independent of noise variance σ) to cross-validated l2^2-lasso in practice.
- The developed asymptotic framework is well-suited to investigate whether the theoretical advantage of l2-lasso yields practical performance gains.
- Overall strong theoretical work; addressing the clarity and practical-comparison points would increase impact.
