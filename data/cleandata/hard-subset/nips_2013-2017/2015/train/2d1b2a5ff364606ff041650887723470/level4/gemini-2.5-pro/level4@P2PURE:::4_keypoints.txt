- Paper analyzes asymptotic error of generalized LASSO for arbitrary nonlinear link functions (μ ≠ 0); Theorem 2.1 claims identical asymptotic error for linear and nonlinear links.
- Theorem 2.1 relies on Gaussian measurement vectors, which may be restrictive; the authors should justify why Gaussianity is necessary.
- The paper uses a convex regularizer tied to a set K containing x0 but gives no practical guidance on how to choose the regularizer from prior knowledge of K.
- Although the error form is identical, it depends on μ and σ of the link function; the authors should clarify why this dependence is necessary and how it encodes problem difficulty.
- Missing literature: work on Single Index Models and Sufficient Dimension Reduction, which studies estimation under unknown link functions, is not cited.
- Missing reference: Yi et al., "Optimal linear estimation under unknown nonlinear measurement," is highly relevant (handles μ = 0 and shows equivalence results) and should be discussed.
