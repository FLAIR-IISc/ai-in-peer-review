- Valuable contribution: Extends precise asymptotic analysis of the square-root Lasso to single-index models, removing the Gaussian prior assumption on the signal vector x0.  
- Well-written manuscript overall.  
- Clarification 1: The single-index model’s equivalence to a linear model with a scaled unknown signal and non-standard noise (Brillinger 1982) is proved in Appendix A but should be briefly noted in the main text to support the claim near line 140.  
- Clarification 2: The paper should explicitly address whether the square-root Lasso’s key advantage—an oracle tuning parameter independent of the unknown noise variance σ—still holds in the single-index setting.
