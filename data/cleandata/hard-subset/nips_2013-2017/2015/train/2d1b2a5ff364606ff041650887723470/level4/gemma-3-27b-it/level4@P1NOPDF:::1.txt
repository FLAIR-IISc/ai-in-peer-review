Answer to authors’ response:

The feedback from other reviewers and the authors’ response have not substantially changed my overall assessment of the paper. I admit, however, that my familiarity with the single index model/sufficient dimension reduction literature is limited, and I may therefore be unaware of potentially relevant prior work. This paper derives asymptotic guarantees for the generalized square-root lasso applied to Gaussian measurements – measurements that are optionally transformed by a potentially unknown nonlinear function. These results are very interesting, as they offer a precise characterization of the method’s performance, and the paper is generally very well written, though it contains a number of typos, particularly within the proof sections.
