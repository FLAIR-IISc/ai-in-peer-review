- Contribution: Extends precise asymptotic characterizations of the ℓ2-Lasso (square-root Lasso) to single-index models and relaxes the Gaussian prior assumption on x0 — a worthwhile contribution.  
- Presentation: The article is well written.  
- Point 1: The single-index model is equivalent to a linear model with a scaled unknown signal and non-standard noise (Brillinger 1982); this connection is rigorously shown in Appendix A but should be mentioned in the main text to justify the blockquote on line 140.  
- Point 2: The paper focuses on the ℓ2-Lasso; it should address whether the practical advantage (oracle regularization not depending on noise variance σ) justifies preferring ℓ2-Lasso over ℓ2^2-Lasso and whether the results clarify this.
