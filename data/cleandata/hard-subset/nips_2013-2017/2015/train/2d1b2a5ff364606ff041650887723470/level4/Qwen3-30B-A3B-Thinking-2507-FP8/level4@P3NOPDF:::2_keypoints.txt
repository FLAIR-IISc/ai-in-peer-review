- Extends precise asymptotic characterization of the l2-lasso (square root lasso) to single-index models and relaxes the Gaussian prior assumption on the signal vector.  
- Manuscript is well-written and clearly structured.  
- The rigorous equivalence between single-index models and linear models with scaled signals/non-standard noise (proved in Appendix A) should be stated explicitly in the main text (e.g., Section 2).  
- The paper currently claims reduction to a linear model in the main text but confines the formal justification to the appendix; integrate this key insight earlier to improve narrative flow.  
- The manuscript does not address whether the square root lasso’s practical advantage—regularization parameter λ being independent of unknown noise variance σ—persists in single-index models.  
- Authors should discuss/analyze whether heteroscedasticity or noise structure induced by the link function reintroduces σ-dependence of the optimal λ, and thus whether square root lasso remains preferable to l2²-lasso in practice.  
- Core theoretical contribution is strong and the paper is suitable for publication pending the above clarifications.
