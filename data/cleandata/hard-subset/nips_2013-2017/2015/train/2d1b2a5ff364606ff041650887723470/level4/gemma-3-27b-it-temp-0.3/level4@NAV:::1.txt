My assessment of this work remains largely unchanged after considering the other reviews and the authors’ response. I readily admit a lack of deep familiarity with the single index model and sufficient dimension reduction fields, and therefore may be overlooking potentially relevant prior work. The paper presents valuable asymptotic guarantees for the generalized square-root lasso when applied to Gaussian measurements, potentially subject to an unknown nonlinear transformation. These results offer a rigorous performance analysis of the method and are presented in a clear and well-structured manuscript, though the text contains a noticeable number of typographical errors, particularly within the proofs.
