- Studies the Generalized Lasso for non-linear measurements with Gaussian design and derives explicit asymptotic expressions for estimation error.  
- Builds on Plan & Vershynin [17], extending their framework to obtain precise, novel error formulas.  
- Theoretical predictions are validated empirically for standard Lasso, group Lasso, and nuclear norm minimization.  
- Applies results to q-bit compressed sensing, deriving optimal quantizer design and connecting to the Lloyd–Max quantizer.  
- Quality: strong, rigorous analysis and significant contribution to non-linear inverse problems.  
- Clarity: generally well-written but uneven depth; some theoretical derivations need more detailed explanations.  
- Originality: substantial novel contributions beyond prior work, notably explicit error expressions rather than order-wise bounds.
