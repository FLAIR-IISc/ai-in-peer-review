- Main result: Shows asymptotic estimation error of generalized Lasso is identical for linear and nonlinear link functions when μ ≠ 0.  
- Quality: Paper is well-written and presents interesting results.  
- Limitation: Theorem 2.1 assumes Gaussian measurement vectors; this may be overly restrictive and needs justification.  
- Question 1: No practical guidance is given for choosing the convex regularizer based on the set Κ containing the true signal x₀.  
- Question 2: The error bounds depend on μ and σ; authors should clarify why this dependence is necessary given the claimed equivalence.  
- Missing related work: Paper should discuss Single Index Models (SIM) and Sufficient Dimension Reduction (SDR) literature.  
- Missing citation: Should compare to Yi et al.'s recent paper on optimal linear estimation under unknown nonlinear observations, which handles μ = 0 and establishes related equivalence with algorithms and experiments.
