- Paper studies linear estimation with a nonlinear link, building on Plan and Vershynin, and shows (Thm 2.1) the generalized Lasso’s asymptotic error matches the linear case even for arbitrary links with μ ≠ 0.  
- Theorem 2.1’s Gaussian measurement-vector assumption may be restrictive; authors should justify necessity and discuss extensions to more general designs.  
- Paper does not explain how to choose a convex regularizer given prior knowledge that x0 ∈ K — guidance on selecting the regularizer from K is missing.  
- Although nonlinear and linear cases are shown equivalent asymptotically, the results still depend on μ and σ; the reviewer asks whether these dependencies are necessary.  
- Missing literature: work on single-index models and sufficient dimension reduction for linear vector/subspace estimation under unknown links is not reviewed.  
- Missing reference: recent paper by Yi et al. on optimal linear estimation with μ = 0 and equivalence between linear and nonlinear links (theory + empirical results).  
- Overall assessment: well-written paper with interesting results.
