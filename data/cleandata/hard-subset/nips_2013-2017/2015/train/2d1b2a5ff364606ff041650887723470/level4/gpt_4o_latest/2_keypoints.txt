1. Extends statistical analysis of the l2-lasso to single-index models and relaxes the Gaussian prior assumption on x0 — a meaningful contribution to precise asymptotic understanding of lasso-type estimators.  
2. Manuscript is well-written.  
3. Appendix A rigorously shows the single-index model is equivalent to a linear model with a scaled unknown signal and non-standard noise (Brillinger 1982); this connection should be briefly noted in the main text to justify the claim at line 140.  
4. The paper studies the l2-lasso (square-root lasso); it should discuss whether the practical advantage that its oracle regularization parameter is independent of noise variance σ implies a real-world preference over the l2^2-lasso.  
5. Overall, the paper extends l2-lasso analysis to single-index models and makes a valuable contribution to the literature.
