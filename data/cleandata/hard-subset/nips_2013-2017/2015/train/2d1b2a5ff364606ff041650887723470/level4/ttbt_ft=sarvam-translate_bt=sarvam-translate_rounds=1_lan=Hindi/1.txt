Response to the Authors' Feedback:

The reviews of other critics and the authors' feedback have not significantly influenced my opinion of the paper. That being said, I am not familiar with the Single Index Model/adequate dimension reduction literature, so I may be unaware of previous work that could be relevant. The authors have obtained asymptotic guarantees for the generalized square-root Lasso applied to Gaussian measures that are (optionally) transformed by a potential unknown non-linear function. The results are very interesting, as they provide an accurate description of the performance of the method, and the paper is very well written (although it has quite a few typos, especially in the proof section).
