- Analyzes asymptotic error of generalized lasso with arbitrary nonlinear link functions (μ ≠ 0) and claims equivalence to the linear case (Theorem 2.1).  
- Theorem 2.1 assumes Gaussian measurement vectors; authors should justify this distributional requirement and its restrictiveness.  
- Does not specify how to choose the convex regularizer based on the set κ containing the true signal x₀, limiting practical guidance.  
- Unclear why the asymptotic error depends on μ and σ—authors should explain whether these dependencies are inherent to the nonlinear setting.  
- Omits discussion of the single-index model and sufficient dimension reduction literature on estimation under unknown link functions.  
- Fails to compare to Yi et al. (ICML 2015), which proves a related equivalence for sparse recovery (including cases with μ = 0) with theory and experiments.  
- Technical arguments seem sound, but the presentation needs deeper engagement with related work and practical considerations to strengthen the contribution.
