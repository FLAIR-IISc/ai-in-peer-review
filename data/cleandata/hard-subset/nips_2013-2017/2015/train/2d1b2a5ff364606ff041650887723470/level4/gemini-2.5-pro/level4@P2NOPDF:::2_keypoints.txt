- Extends precise statistical characterization of l2-lasso to single-index models, relaxing Gaussian prior assumptions on the signal.  
- Valuable contribution to literature on precise asymptotic behavior of lasso-type estimators.  
- Paper is well-written and technical arguments appear sound.  
- Suggestion 1: Move the Brillinger (1982) view (single-index as equivalent linear model with scaled signal and non-standard noise) earlier into the main text to give intuition and justify the statement on line 140.  
- Suggestion 2: Add discussion on the practical motivation for l2-lasso (oracle regularizer independent of noise variance) and whether the asymptotic results support or refine that justification.
