- Contribution: Extends sharp asymptotic characterizations of the l2-LASSO to the single-index model and relaxes the Gaussian prior assumption on x0, making a valuable contribution to LASSO literature.
- Writing: The paper is clearly written.
- Suggestion 1: Note the Brillinger (1982) equivalence that recasts the single-index model as a linear model with a scaled signal and nonstandard noise in the main text (not only Appendix A) to support the heuristic around line 140.
- Suggestion 2: Clarify whether the usual practical advantage of the square-root (l2-) LASSO—its oracle regularization not depending on noise variance—actually holds in this single-index setting.
- Overall assessment: Advances precise performance characterizations of l2-LASSO in a meaningful and worthwhile way.
