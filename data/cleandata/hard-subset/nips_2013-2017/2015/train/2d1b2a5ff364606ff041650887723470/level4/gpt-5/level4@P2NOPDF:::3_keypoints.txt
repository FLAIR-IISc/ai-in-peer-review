- Paper studies the Generalized Lasso under non-linear measurements with Gaussian design and derives precise asymptotic estimation-error formulas validated on Lasso, group Lasso, and nuclear-norm cases; includes q-bit quantizer design with a Lloyd–Max connection.

- Quality: strong, well-executed contribution.

- Clarity: clearly written but somewhat unbalanced; some important details omitted due to space.

- Originality: builds on Plan & Vershynin but advances the state of the art by providing new explicit asymptotic error formulas.
