Review- Answer to authors' response:
The other reviewers’ comments and the authors’ response have not materially changed my assessment of the paper. However, I am not well acquainted with the single-index model/sufficient dimension reduction literature, so I may be unaware of relevant prior work. The authors establish asymptotic guarantees for the generalized square-root lasso applied to Gaussian measurements that are (optionally) transformed by a possibly unknown nonlinear function. The results are quite interesting, as they offer a precise characterization of the method’s performance, and the paper is clearly written (though there are quite a few typos, especially in the proof section).
