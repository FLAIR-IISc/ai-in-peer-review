Comments on the Authors' Response

Having reviewed the authors' response and the other reviews, my overall assessment of the paper remains largely unchanged. I should note, however, that I am not an expert in the literature on single index models and sufficient dimension reduction, and thus may be unaware of some relevant prior work. The authors derive asymptotic guarantees for the generalized square-root lasso applied to Gaussian measurements that are transformed by a potentially unknown nonlinear function. The results are compelling, as they provide a precise characterization of the method's performance. The paper is very well-written, although it contains a number of typographical errors, particularly within the proofs.
