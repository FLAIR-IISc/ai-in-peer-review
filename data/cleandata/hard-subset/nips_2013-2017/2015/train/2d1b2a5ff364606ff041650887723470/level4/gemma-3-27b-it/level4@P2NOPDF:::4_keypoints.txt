1. Studies linear estimation with a nonlinear link, extending Plan & Vershynin, and proves asymptotic estimation error for generalized lasso is identical for linear and nonlinear links (Theorem 2.1).  
2. Theorem 2.1 relies on a Gaussian assumption for the measurement vector; the necessity of this distributional assumption is not justified.  
3. No practical guidance is given for selecting a convex regularizer based on properties of the parameter set K that contains the true vector x0.  
4. The asymptotic error formula depends on µ and σ (link-function parameters); it is unclear whether this dependence is inherent or an artifact of the analysis.  
5. The paper omits discussion of substantial related literature on single-index models and sufficient dimension reduction that address estimation under unknown links.  
6. Fails to cite/reconcile recent work by Yi et al. on optimal linear estimation with unknown link (including µ = 0) that shows similar equivalence and provides algorithms and empirical validation.  
7. The manuscript is well-written and presents a noteworthy contribution via the equivalence result between linear and nonlinear estimation errors.  
8. Strengthening suggestions: justify Gaussian assumption, provide regularizer-selection guidance, interpret µ/σ dependence, and acknowledge related prior work.
