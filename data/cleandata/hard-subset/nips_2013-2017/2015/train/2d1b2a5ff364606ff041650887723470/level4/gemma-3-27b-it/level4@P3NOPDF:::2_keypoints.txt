- Extends theoretical understanding of the l2-lasso to single-index models and relaxes Gaussian input assumptions — a worthwhile contribution.  
- Paper is well-written and presents results clearly.  
- Appendix A rigorously demonstrates equivalence between single-index models and linear models with scaled signals/non-standard noise (Brillinger 1982); mention this in the main text to support the blockquote on line 140 and improve intuition.  
- Focuses on the l2-lasso (square-root lasso), whose optimal regularization parameter is theoretically independent of the noise variance σ.  
- Suggest investigating whether this variance-independence yields practical benefits over the l2^2-lasso in single-index settings, especially when σ is estimated.  
- Recommend discussing finite-sample limitations of the theoretical advantage and whether l2-lasso consistently outperforms in practice.  
- Overall: solid theoretical paper; addressing these points would enhance practical relevance and impact.
