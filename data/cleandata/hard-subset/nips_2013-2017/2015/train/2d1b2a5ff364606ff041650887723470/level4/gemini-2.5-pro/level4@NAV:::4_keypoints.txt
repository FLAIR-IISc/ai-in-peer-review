- Paper analyzes asymptotic error of generalized LASSO under arbitrary nonlinear link g and shows (Theorem 2.1) the error matches the linear case when μ ≠ 0.  
- Theorem 2.1 crucially assumes Gaussian measurement vectors; authors should justify why normality is essential and discuss applicability beyond Gaussian designs.  
- Paper uses a convex regularizer but gives no guidance on choosing the regularizer based on the constraint set χ containing x₀; clarification on regularizer selection is needed.  
- Although the result claims equivalence to the linear model, the final error depends on μ and σ (properties of g); authors should explain why this dependence remains necessary.  
- Manuscript lacks discussion of related literature on Single Index Models (SIM) and Sufficient Dimension Reduction (SDR), which are directly relevant.  
- Missing citation/discussion of Yi et al.’s recent work on optimal linear estimation under unknown nonlinear measurements, which handles μ = 0 and shows similar equivalences with algorithms and empirical support.  
- Overall assessment: well-written manuscript with significant and interesting results, but needs clarifications and stronger positioning within related work.
