- Extends precise asymptotic characterization of the l2-lasso (square-root lasso) to single-index models.  
- Relaxes the standard Gaussian assumption on the prior (distribution of x_0).  
- Overall a valuable contribution to literature on sharp asymptotics of lasso-type estimators.  
- Well-written manuscript.  
- Need to flag the Brillinger (1982) observation in the main text: single-index models are equivalent to linear models with a scaled unknown signal and non-standard noise (currently only in Appendix A) to justify the statement near line 140.  
- Practical question: the paper should address whether the main practical advantage of l2-lasso (oracle tuning not depending on noise variance) actually justifies preferring it over the l2^2-lasso, and whether the results inform this comparison.
