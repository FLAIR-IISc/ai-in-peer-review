My overall assessment of this paper remains largely unchanged following the author response and consideration of other reviews. I acknowledge a potential gap in my expertise; I am not deeply familiar with the single index model or sufficient dimension reduction literature, and therefore may be unaware of relevant prior work.

This paper presents valuable asymptotic guarantees for the generalized square-root lasso when applied to data drawn from Gaussian distributions, potentially subjected to an unknown nonlinear transformation. The core contribution – a precise characterization of the method’s performance under these conditions – is highly interesting. The paper is generally very well written and clearly presented. However, numerous typographical errors, particularly within the proof sections, detract from the polished quality of the presentation and require attention.
