- Extends precise asymptotic characterization of the l2 (square-root) lasso to single-index models and relaxes the Gaussian assumption on x0; valuable contribution and well written.

- Point 1: Single-index model is equivalent to a linear model with a scaled unknown signal and nonstandard noise (Brillinger 1982); include a brief heuristic explanation in the main text to justify the blockquote on line 140.

- Appendix A develops that equivalence rigorously, but a main-text heuristic is needed for reader guidance.

- Point 2: The paper should address whether the l2-lasso’s practical advantage—oracle regularization not depending on noise variance σ—still justifies preferring it over the l2^2-lasso in the single-index setting.
