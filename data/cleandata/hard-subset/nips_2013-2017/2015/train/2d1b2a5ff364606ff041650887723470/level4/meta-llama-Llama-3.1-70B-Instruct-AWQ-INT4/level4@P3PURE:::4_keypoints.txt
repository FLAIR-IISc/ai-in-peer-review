- Studies linear estimation with nonlinear link functions, building on Plan and Vershynin.
- Theorem 2.1: generalized lasso asymptotic error for arbitrary link functions (μ ≠ 0) matches the linear case.
- Theorem 2.1 assumes Gaussian measurement vectors; this may be restrictive and needs justification.
- No clear prescription for choosing the regularizer given the set K containing x0; convex regularizer assumption does not resolve selection.
- Although nonlinear and linear measurements are shown equivalent, the dependence on μ and σ (hardness parameters) raises questions about necessity.
- Missing literature review on single-index models and sufficient dimension reduction.
- Missing citation and discussion of Yi et al.’s recent paper (optimal linear estimation under unknown link functions), which handles sparse recovery even when μ = 0 and shows equivalence with empirical/theoretical support.
- Overall: well-written, interesting, and a valuable contribution.
