Authors' Response:

Other reviewers' reviews and the authors' response did not significantly influence my opinion of the article. However, I am not familiar with the literature on single index models/adequate reduction, so I may not be aware of previous work that could be relevant. The authors provide an infinite guarantee for the generalized square root Lasso, applied to Gaussian estimates transformed by an unknown non-linear function (optionally). The results are very interesting, as they provide a specific characteristic of the method's performance, and the article is very well written (although there are some flaws, especially in the proof section).
