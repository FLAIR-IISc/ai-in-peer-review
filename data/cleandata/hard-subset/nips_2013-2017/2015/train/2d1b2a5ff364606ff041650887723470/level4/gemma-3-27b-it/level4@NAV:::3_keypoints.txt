- Studies Generalized Lasso with non-linear measurements and Gaussian design, extending Plan & Vershynin to an asymptotic regime with explicit error formulas.
- Provides precise, explicit asymptotic expressions for estimation error.
- Validates theoretical predictions empirically across special cases (Lasso, group Lasso, nuclear norm).
- Applies results to optimal quantizer design in q-Bit Compressed Sensing and connects to the Lloyd–Max quantizer.
- Overall a strong, original contribution that significantly advances explicit performance estimation.
