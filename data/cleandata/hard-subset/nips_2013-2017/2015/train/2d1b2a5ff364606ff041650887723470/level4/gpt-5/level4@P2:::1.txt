Response to authors’ rebuttal: The other reviews and the authors’ response have not materially changed my assessment. I am not deeply familiar with the single-index model/sufficient-dimension-reduction literature, so I may be unaware of relevant prior work.

The paper derives asymptotic guarantees for the generalized square-root LASSO under Gaussian designs when the responses are obtained by passing linear measurements through a (possibly unknown) nonlinear link function. The results are interesting and provide a precise, asymptotically exact characterization of the estimator’s performance. The manuscript is generally clear and well organized, though it contains numerous typographical and minor grammatical errors, especially in the proof sections.
