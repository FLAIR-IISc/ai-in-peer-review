- Studies Generalized Lasso with non-linear measurements and Gaussian design, using the heuristic that non-linear observations can be treated as noisy linear observations.  
- Builds on Plan & Vershynin [17] and extends to an asymptotic regime, providing novel precise explicit expressions for estimation error.  
- Validated predictions on special cases (Lasso, group Lasso, nuclear norm) and applied to q-bit CS and Lloyd–Max quantizer design.  
- Overall work quality is high and the paper is well-written, though treatment is somewhat unbalanced due to space constraints.
