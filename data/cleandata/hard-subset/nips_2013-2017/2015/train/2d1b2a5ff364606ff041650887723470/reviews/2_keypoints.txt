- Contribution: Extends precise asymptotic characterization of the l2‑lasso (square‑root lasso) to single‑index models and relaxes the usual Gaussian prior assumption on x0.  
- Overall: The paper is well written.  
- Main-text clarity: The equivalence between single‑index and a linear model with scaled unknown signal and non‑standard noise (Brillinger 1982) is only in Appendix A and should be mentioned in the main text to justify the blockquote on line 140.  
- Practical relevance: Since the l2‑lasso’s oracle regularization parameter does not depend on noise variance, the paper should address whether its results support preferring l2‑lasso over the l2^2‑lasso in practice.
