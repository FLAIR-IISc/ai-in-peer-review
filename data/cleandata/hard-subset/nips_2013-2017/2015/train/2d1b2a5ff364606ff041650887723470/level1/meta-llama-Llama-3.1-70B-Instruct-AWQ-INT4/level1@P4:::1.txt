**Summary of Main Ideas and Relation to Previous Work**

The paper "LASSO with Non-linear Measurements is Equivalent to One With Linear Measurements" investigates the problem of estimating an unknown signal vector x0 from non-linear measurements yi = gi(aTi x0), where gi is a non-linear link function. The authors propose using the Generalized LASSO algorithm, which is commonly used for linear measurements, and show that it can be effective for non-linear measurements as well. The main contribution of the paper is a precise evaluation of the asymptotic behavior of the estimation error of the Generalized LASSO under non-linear measurements.

The paper builds upon previous work by Brillinger [5] and Plan and Vershynin [17], who studied the asymptotic behavior of the estimation error of the Least Squares (LS) solution and the constrained Generalized LASSO, respectively. The authors extend these results to the regularized Generalized LASSO and provide a precise error formula that captures the role of the link function g, the structure of x0, the regularizer parameter λ, and the number of measurements m.
