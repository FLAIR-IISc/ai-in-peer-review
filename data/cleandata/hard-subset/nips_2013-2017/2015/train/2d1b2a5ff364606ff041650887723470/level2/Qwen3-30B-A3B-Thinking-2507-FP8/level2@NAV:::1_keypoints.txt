- Establishes asymptotic equivalence between Generalized LASSO with non-linear measurements yi = g(ai^T x0) and a linear model yi = μ ai^T x0 + σ zi with μ = E[γg(γ)], σ^2 = E[(g(γ)-μγ)^2].  
- Uses the Convex Gaussian Min-max Theorem (CGMT) to obtain asymptotically exact error expressions rather than loose bounds.  
- Derives explicit estimation-error formulas for key structured cases: sparse, group-sparse, and low-rank recovery.  
- Numerical experiments show excellent agreement between theoretical predictions and empirical results.  
- Extends Brillinger’s classical results to regularized LASSO and proves the Lloyd–Max quantizer is optimal for 1-bit measurements.  
- Theoretical results are rigorous, well-proven, and the paper is clearly written and well-organized.  
- Minor limitation: assumes the signal has unit norm, which may need adjustment in some applications.  
- Could benefit from more discussion of practical implementation considerations for real-world use.  
- Overall assessment: strong, novel theoretical contribution to LASSO under non-linear measurements; recommend acceptance.
