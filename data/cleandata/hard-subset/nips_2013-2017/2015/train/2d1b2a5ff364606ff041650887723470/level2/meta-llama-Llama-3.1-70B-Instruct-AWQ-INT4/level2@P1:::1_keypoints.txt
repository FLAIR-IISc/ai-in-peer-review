- Main contribution: Derives explicit, asymptotically precise expressions for Generalized LASSO performance with non-linear measurements.  
- Equivalence result: Shows non-linear measurement LASSO is asymptotically equivalent to a linear model with a link-function-dependent scaling and noise variance.  
- Theoretical novelty: Extends Brillinger and Plan & Vershynin’s work to non-linear measurements, not a trivial extension.  
- Connections: Relates results to AMP and CGMT frameworks, unifying different lines of work.  
- Empirical validation: Provides examples and simulations demonstrating accuracy of theoretical predictions.  
- Presentation: Well-written, main text readable with proofs relegated to the appendix.  
- Strength: Offers precise understanding of a common practical scenario in signal processing and ML.  
- Limitation: Assumes measurement matrix with i.i.d. Gaussian entries, which may limit applicability.  
- Limitation: Allows a general non-linear link function but lacks specific-form results, potentially restricting certain applications.  
- Significance: High impact for signal processing, machine learning, and statistics.  
- Originality: Rated highly original and a significant advancement.  
- Reviewer recommendation: Recommend acceptance for publication.  
- Ratings: Quality 9/10, Clarity 9/10, Originality 9/10, Significance 9/10.
