The paper "LASSO with Non-linear Measurements is Equivalent to One With Linear Measurements" studies the problem of estimating an unknown signal vector from non-linear measurements. The authors consider the Generalized LASSO algorithm, which is a popular method for estimating structured signals from linear measurements. They show that, under certain conditions, the Generalized LASSO can be used with non-linear measurements, and that the estimation performance is equivalent to that of a linear measurement model with appropriately scaled noisy measurements.
