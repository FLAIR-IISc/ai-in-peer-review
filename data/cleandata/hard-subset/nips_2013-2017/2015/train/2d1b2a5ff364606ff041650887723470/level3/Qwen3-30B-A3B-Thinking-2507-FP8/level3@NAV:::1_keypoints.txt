- Studies Generalized LASSO for estimating structured signals from nonlinear measurements yi = g(ai^T x0) with unknown g.  
- Main result: asymptotic equivalence to scaled linear measurements with μ = E[γg(γ)] and σ² = E[(g(γ)-μγ)²].  
- Provides a precise asymptotic error formula (Theorem 2.2) in the m/n = δ regime.  
- Demonstrates applicability to sparse, group-sparse, and low-rank recovery problems.  
- Extends prior single-index model work to give the first asymptotically exact characterization for Generalized LASSO.  
- Proofs are rigorous and build on recent advances in convex optimization and high-dimensional statistics.  
- Connects theory to practice, e.g., showing Lloyd–Max quantizer minimizes estimation error in q-bit compressive sensing.  
- Paper is well-structured and clearly written with thorough related-work discussion.  
- Contains numerous typos and inconsistent notation in the proofs, reducing readability.  
- Reviewer unfamiliar with some single-index literature and may have missed related prior work, but results appear novel for NIPS.  
- Limited discussion of practical implementation challenges.  
- Overall assessment: original, significant, well-executed contribution and strong candidate for acceptance.
