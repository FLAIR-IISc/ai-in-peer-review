- Main contribution: Extends precise asymptotic characterization of the l2-lasso to single-index models and relaxes Gaussian prior assumptions on x0.  
- Core result: Provides a precise asymptotic evaluation of the estimation error of the Generalized LASSO with non-linear measurements.  
- Writing and organization: Paper is well written, clear, rigorous, and easy to follow.  
- Technical quality: Theoretical analysis and experiments are sound and support the claims.  
- Literature placement: Thorough discussion of related work and clear explanation of differences from prior contributions.  
- Suggestion: Mention equivalence between single-index and scaled linear model with non-standard noise (Brillinger 1982) in main text to justify a quoted statement.  
- Practical relevance: Results can guide choice of regularization parameter and compare algorithm performance.  
- Missing discussion: Does not address whether findings favor l2-lasso over l2^2-lasso in practice, despite oracle regularization implications.  
- Limitation: Assumes a specific prior on x0, which may not hold in all applications.  
- Reproducibility: Presentation provides enough detail for expert readers to reproduce results.  
- Originality: High—novel combination of familiar techniques applied to a new problem.  
- Recommendation: Overall recommendation to accept due to significant, well-supported contribution.
