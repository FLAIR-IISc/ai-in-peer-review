- Extends Plan & Vershynin [17] with explicit asymptotic error expressions for Generalized LASSO under non-linear measurements.  
- Shows asymptotic equivalence between non-linear measurements and scaled, noisy linear measurements, with precise scaling factor and noise variance.  
- Validates theoretical results with experiments and applies framework to sparse, group-sparse, low-rank recovery, and q-bit quantizer design.  
- Connects optimal quantizer design in q-bit compressed sensing to the Lloyd–Max quantizer.  
- Original and significant contribution addressing a gap in asymptotic error characterization.  
- Theoretical generalization to a broader class of regularizers and measurement models is strong and versatile.  
- Empirical validation supports the accuracy and practical relevance of derived expressions.  
- Paper is generally well written and organized with clear explanations.  
- Some sections lack sufficient detail (derivations and application implications, notably low-rank recovery) due to space constraints.  
- Notation issues: awkward Moreau envelope notation and insufficient explanation of equivalence between regularized and penalized formulations.  
- Assumption 1 needs a formal interpretation (e.g., via variational analysis) for broader accessibility.  
- Minor presentation errors (typo in Figure 1 caption: 'n=768' should be '$n$=768').  
- Suggestions: expand derivation details, revise notation, add formal interpretation of Assumption 1, and fix typos.  
- Recommendation: accept with minor revisions to address the noted issues.
