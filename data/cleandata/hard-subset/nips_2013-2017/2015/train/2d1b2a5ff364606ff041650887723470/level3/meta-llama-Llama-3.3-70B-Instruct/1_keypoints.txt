- Significant contribution to signal processing and estimation, particularly for non-linear measurements.
- Derives asymptotic guarantees for the generalized square-root LASSO with Gaussian measurements and optional non-linear transformation.
- Provides a precise, explicit characterization of the estimator's performance.
- Shows asymptotic equivalence to a linear-measurement model with scaling factor μ and noise variance σ^2.
- Applies the theory to q-bit compressive sensing and derives optimal quantization thresholds/levels.
- Well-motivated approach with careful presentation and strong connections to prior work (Brillinger; Plan & Vershynin).
- Paper is well-written and organized, enabling reproducibility for expert readers.
- Contains several typos, especially in the proof section.
- Technically sound with supporting theoretical analysis and experiments, but some results may be incremental.
- Recommendation for acceptance, though additional experimental validation would strengthen the paper.
