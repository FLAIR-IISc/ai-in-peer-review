- Paper extends Plan and Vershynin to non-zero mean and analyzes asymptotic estimation error for generalized LASSO with arbitrary nonlinear links.  
- Main claim: asymptotic error is identical for linear and nonlinear link functions; provides precise limit of squared error.  
- Theoretical analysis is thorough, technically sound, and supported by numerical experiments.  
- Paper is well-written and results are clearly explained.  
- Novel and significant contribution with practical implications for nonlinear measurement devices.  
- Assumes Gaussian measurement vectors, limiting generality to non-Gaussian settings.  
- Lacks clear practical guidance on choosing the regularizer given the constraint set K containing x0.  
- Needs more detailed discussion of dependence on link parameters µ and σ (link difficulty).  
- Should expand related work discussion on single-index models and sufficient dimension reduction.  
- Must cite and compare to recent work by Yi et al. on optimal linear estimation under unknown links and sparse recovery when µ = 0.  
- Recommendation: accept pending revisions to address the above concerns.  
- Quality: 8/10; Clarity: 9/10; Originality: 9/10; Significance: 9/10.
