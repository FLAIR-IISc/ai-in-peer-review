- Problem: estimates structured signals from non-linear measurements in compressed sensing/sparse recovery.
- Method: analyzes the Generalized LASSO (regularized convex program) applied to non-linear measurements.
- Main theoretical contribution: derives precise asymptotic error formulas for Generalized LASSO with non-linear measurements.
- Key finding: asymptotic performance with non-linear measurements is equivalent to that with linear measurements after scaling and noise adjustment depending on the link function.
- Practical tool: provides a computable convex program giving the precise error formula.
- Empirical validation: simulations demonstrate the accuracy of the predicted error formulas across scenarios.
- Novelty: generalizes prior work (Plan & Vershynin) from constrained to regularized Generalized LASSO and yields more precise error expressions.
- Writing and organization: paper is well-written, clearly organized, and balances theory and simulations.
