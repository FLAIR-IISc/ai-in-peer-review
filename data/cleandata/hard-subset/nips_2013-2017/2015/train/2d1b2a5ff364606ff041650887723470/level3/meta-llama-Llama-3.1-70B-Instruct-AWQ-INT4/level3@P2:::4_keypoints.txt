- Paper studies asymptotic estimation error of generalized LASSO for linear estimation with arbitrary nonlinear link functions, extending Plan & Vershynin to µ ≠ 0.  
- Main claim (Theorem 2.1): asymptotic error is identical for linear and nonlinear link functions.  
- Paper is well-written with a clear introduction, organized technical sections, and illustrative examples/simulations.  
- Gaussian assumption on measurement vectors is insufficiently motivated; authors should explain necessity and implications.  
- Paper focuses on convex regularizers but fails to address how to choose the regularizer given the constraint set K containing x0.  
- Dependence of the theorem on µ and σ (link difficulty) is not well-explained; need more insight into these parameters’ roles.  
- Missing citations and discussion of relevant literature on single-index models and sufficient dimension reduction.  
- Missing citation to recent work by Yi et al. on optimal linear estimation under unknown link functions (addresses sparse recovery and µ = 0 case).  
- Contribution is original in extending results to µ ≠ 0 and offers new insights into nonlinear-to-linear equivalence.  
- Problem addressed is significant with potential applications in statistics and signal processing.  
- Arguments for acceptance: novel extension of prior work, clear exposition, and relevant simulations.  
- Arguments against acceptance: weak justification of Gaussian assumption, lack of regularizer selection guidance, and missing pertinent citations.
