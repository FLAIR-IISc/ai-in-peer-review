- Main contribution: precise asymptotic characterization of l2-LASSO (square-root LASSO) extended to single-index models and non-Gaussian priors.
- Core result: analysis of Generalized LASSO with non-linear measurements showing asymptotic equivalence to linear measurements up to a link-dependent scaling and noise variance.
- Provides an asymptotically exact expression for the estimation error formulated as a convex program that can be computed numerically.
- Includes multiple examples and simulations that validate the theoretical predictions and demonstrate good performance under non-linearities.
