This paper studies the asymptotic estimation error for linear estimation with arbitrary nonlinear link using the generalized LASSO. The main claim, as stated in Theorem 2.1, is that the asymptotic error is identical for linear and nonlinear link functions. This result extends the work of Plan and Vershynin to the case where the link function is not known to be linear, i.e., µ ≠ 0.

The paper assumes Gaussian measurement vectors, which may be seen as a limitation. However, the authors argue that this assumption is necessary to derive the precise error formulae. It would be interesting to investigate whether similar results can be obtained under different assumptions on the measurement vectors.
