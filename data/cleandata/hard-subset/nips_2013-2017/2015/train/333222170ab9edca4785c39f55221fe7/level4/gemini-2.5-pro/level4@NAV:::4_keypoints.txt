- Introduces "on the job learning" where a model can query an annotator between receiving an example and making a prediction.  
- Algorithm for structured prediction is well-explained, but the face-recognition implementation is ambiguous about what exactly is queried (entire instance or something else).  
- In non-structured settings information won’t propagate between positions, calling into question the rationale for the proposed utility function.  
- The threshold-based baseline uses two seemingly arbitrary parameters; the paper needs justification and sensitivity analysis for these values.  
- Experimental evaluation lacks a comparison to uncertainty sampling, a common active-learning baseline.  
- Authors strongly separate their setting from active online learning, but active learning could be adapted to this problem (e.g., update model after query responses), and literature on query-set selection should be considered.  
- Overall the framework is intriguing but several key details are unclear and its applicability appears limited.
