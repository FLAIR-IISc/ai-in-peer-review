- Framework is novel and clear for structured prediction but its application to face recognition is unclear, especially whether the queried instance is the only point of application.  
- In non-structured settings the model does not propagate information between adjacent examples, calling into question the relevance of the proposed utility function.  
- The threshold baseline uses two seemingly arbitrary parameters; the authors must explain their origin and report sensitivity analyses.  
- Add an uncertainty-sampling baseline (as in active learning) for a meaningful comparison.  
- The distinction from active online learning is overstated since active learning methods could be adapted (e.g., update on responses then predict), leveraging existing query-selection theory.  
- Overall the framework is promising but several methodological details are unclear and its applicability appears limited.
