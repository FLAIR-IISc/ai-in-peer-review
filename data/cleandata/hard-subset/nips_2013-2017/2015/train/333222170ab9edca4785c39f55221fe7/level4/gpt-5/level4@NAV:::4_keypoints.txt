- Introduces "on the job learning" where annotator queries occur between receiving an example and making a prediction.
- Algorithm is straightforward for structured prediction, but unclear how it was applied to face recognition — was the entire instance queried?
- For non-structured tasks the model won't propagate information across examples, so the utility function appears ill-specified.
- The threshold baseline has two seemingly arbitrary parameters — no explanation of how they were chosen or sensitivity to them.
- Add an uncertainty-sampling (active learning) baseline for comparison.
- Active learning could be adapted by treating the active set as a sequence and updating the model as responses arrive; established theory could guide query selection.
- Framework seems novel but some details are unclear and its applicability appears limited.
