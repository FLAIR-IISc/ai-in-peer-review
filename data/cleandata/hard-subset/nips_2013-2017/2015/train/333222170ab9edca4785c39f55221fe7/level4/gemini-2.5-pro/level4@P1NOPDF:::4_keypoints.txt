- Introduces "on-the-job learning" where the model may query an annotator after seeing input but before final prediction.
- Implementation for face recognition is unclear — did the model query whole instances, and how were queries handled in a non-structured setting?
- Utility function validity is questionable since non-structured tasks do not propagate information between adjacent examples as assumed.
- Thresholding baseline uses two seemingly arbitrary parameters; the paper must justify their choice and analyze sensitivity.
- Experiments lack an uncertainty sampling baseline, a standard active-learning comparison.
- The setting overlaps with active learning: sequences could be treated as active sets and the model updated with query responses before final prediction.
- Overall contribution weakened by unclear application details and apparently narrow applicability.
