- Algorithm is clear for structured prediction, but the face recognition application is poorly explained—unclear whether the instance itself was queried.  
- In non-structured settings the model doesn't propagate information between adjacent examples, casting doubt on the utility function's applicability.  
- The threshold baseline uses two seemingly arbitrary parameters; the paper should state their origin and show sensitivity analyses.  
- Add an uncertainty-sampling baseline (as in active learning) to better contextualize performance.  
- The authors' claim of distinction from active online learning is overstated—active learning could be adapted by treating the active set as a sequence and updating on responses.  
- Overall the framework appears novel but some methodological details are unclear and its applicability seems limited.
