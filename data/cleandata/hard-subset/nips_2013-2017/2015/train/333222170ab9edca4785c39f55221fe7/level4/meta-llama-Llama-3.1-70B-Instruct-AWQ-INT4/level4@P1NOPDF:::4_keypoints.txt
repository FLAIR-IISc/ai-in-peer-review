- "On the job learning" is clear for structured prediction but its application to face recognition is not well motivated.  
- In the non-structured setting the model does not propagate information between adjacent examples, casting doubt on the utility function's relevance.  
- The threshold baseline uses two seemingly arbitrary parameters; authors should justify their origin and test sensitivity.  
- Include an uncertainty-sampling baseline (as in active learning) for comparison.  
- Active learning could be adapted to this domain (update on responses, predict at end) and provides theoretical tools, even if it ignores temporal aspects.  
- The framework appears novel but several methodological details are unclear.  
- The overall applicability of the proposed approach seems fairly limited.
