- Introduces on-the-job learning (model may query an annotator before final prediction); application to structured prediction is well-explained.
- Face recognition experiment is unclear: how were queries made (entire instance or parts)? method’s applicability to non‑structured settings is questionable.
- Unclear how information would be propagated between adjacent examples in non‑structured tasks, undermining the proposed utility function.
- Threshold-based baseline depends on two seemingly arbitrary parameters; authors must justify choices and provide a sensitivity analysis.
- Missing comparison to a standard uncertainty sampling baseline (common in active learning).
- Claimed separation from active online learning is not clearly justified; active learning frameworks could be adapted and bring theoretical grounding.
- Overall contribution is weakened by lack of clarity for non‑structured tasks and insufficiently rigorous experimental baselines and discussion.
