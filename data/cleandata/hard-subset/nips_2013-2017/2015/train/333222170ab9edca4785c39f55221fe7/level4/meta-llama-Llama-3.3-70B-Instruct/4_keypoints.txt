- Introduces "on the job learning": annotators are queried between example presentation and prediction.
- Algorithm is well-explained for structured prediction (sequence queries) but face-recognition application is unclear—uncertain whether the instance itself was queried.
- In non-structured settings the model does not propagate information between adjacent examples, raising doubts about the utility function's relevance.
- Threshold baseline uses two parameters with unclear origins and arbitrary values; need the source and sensitivity analysis.
- Missing baseline: include uncertainty sampling (active learning) for comparison.
- Authors distinguish from active online learning, but active learning can be adapted by treating the sequence as the active set and updating the model upon responses.
- Framework seems novel but some details are unclear and its applicability appears limited.
