- Proposed "on-the-job learning" setup is well-explained for structured prediction but its application to the face recognition task is unclear (was the query just the instance itself?).  
- In non-structured settings the model cannot propagate information across adjacent examples, calling into question the utility function’s validity.  
- The threshold baseline uses two seemingly arbitrary parameters — their origin and result sensitivity are not reported.  
- Add a baseline based on uncertainty sampling (active learning) for a stronger comparison.  
- The authors’ distinction from active online learning may be overstated; active learning could be adapted by treating the active set as a sequence and updating on responses.  
- Overall the framework is novel but several details are unclear and its applicability appears limited.
