- Proposes a method to build high-accuracy classifiers from scratch with no labeled data.  
- Well-motivated challenge of noisy crowd-sourced labels; novel focus on timing and latency optimization.  
- Strong evaluation by comparing classifier performance to human-level accuracy; Table 2 is informative.  
- LENSE beats a 3-vote baseline but loses to a 5-vote baseline, revealing a trade-off between efficiency and consensus-based denoising.  
- Example in Line 154 is abstract and unclear; needs a concrete query with realistic crowd responses and timings.  
- Inconsistent baseline terminology: “entropic” vs. “threshold” in Tables 2 and 3—should be unified.  
- Related work (Section 5) is placed too late; should appear earlier to establish context and novelty.  
- Overall idea is promising but explanations and paper structure need clearer examples and standard organization to strengthen impact.
