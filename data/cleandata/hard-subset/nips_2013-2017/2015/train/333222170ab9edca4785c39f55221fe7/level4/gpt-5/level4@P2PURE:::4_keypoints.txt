- Unclear how the method was applied to face recognition: is the query just the instance, and how does the utility function work in a non-structured setting?
- In non-structured tasks the model would not propagate information between adjacent examples, so the proposed utility formulation may not make sense.
- The threshold baseline uses two seemingly arbitrary parameters with no explanation of how they were chosen or sensitivity analysis.
- An uncertainty-sampling (active learning) baseline is missing and would be informative for comparison.
- The claimed distinction from active online learning is weak — active learning could be adapted by treating the active set as a sequence and updating on responses.
- Overall, the framework is novel but several details are unclear and its applicability appears fairly restricted.
