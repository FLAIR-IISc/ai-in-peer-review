- Method is clear for structured prediction but unclear for face recognition/non-structured tasks: was the whole instance queried, and without propagation between examples the utility function may lose meaning.
- Threshold baseline uses two seemingly arbitrary parameters; authors must report their origin and sensitivity and consider adding an uncertainty-sampling (active learning) baseline.
- Claimed distinction from active online learning is overstated: active learning could be adapted here and its existing theory could guide query selection, though it wouldn’t capture the temporal aspect.
- Framework is novel but several details are unclear and its applicability appears fairly restricted.
