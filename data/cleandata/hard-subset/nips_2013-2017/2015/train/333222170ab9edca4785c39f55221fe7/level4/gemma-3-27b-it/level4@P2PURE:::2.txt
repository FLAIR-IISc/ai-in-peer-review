This paper makes a valuable contribution by evaluating classifier accuracy against human performance. The results in Table 2 are particularly insightful, demonstrating that the LENSE system surpasses a 3-vote baseline but falls short of a 5-vote system, highlighting a crucial comparison between the benefits of increased consensus in crowd responses and the efficiency of the LENSE approach. The illustrative example beginning at Line 154 is rather abstract; clarifying it with a concrete query example—accompanied by realistic crowd response data and timing information—would significantly improve readability. The inconsistent labeling of the threshold baseline as both “entropic” and “threshold” in Tables 2 and 3 creates confusion. I also question the placement of the related work in Section 5. Despite these presentation issues, the paper addresses a compelling problem, though the explanations could benefit from greater clarity.
