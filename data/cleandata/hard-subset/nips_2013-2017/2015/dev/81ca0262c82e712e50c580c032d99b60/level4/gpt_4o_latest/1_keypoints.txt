- Paper introduces a novel path-integral control method that learns uncontrolled dynamics with a GP while using a known control matrix G, and allows noise and controls in distinct subspaces.  
- Experiments on three simulated robotics tasks show favorable performance against state-of-the-art RL and control baselines.  
- Derivations appear mostly correct and combination of learned stochastic forward model with path integral control is a novel, significant contribution.  
- Major concern: task-generalization derivation (Eq.13→Eq.14) is unclear and may be incorrect—relation between reward exponents and value-function exponents needs clarification.  
- Eq.14 interpretation issue: ψ is only the exponent of the final cost, so its use across time steps is questionable.  
- Task-generalization seems limited to target-defined tasks; extension to other task types may require a task-similarity kernel.  
- Experimental section lacks reproducibility details (specific dynamics, reward functions, trajectory lengths); these should be added to the appendix.  
- Metric concern: reported score ψ (exponent of value) may be optimistic or robot-computed; recommend reporting average or cumulative (discounted) reward instead.  
- GP-related clarity issues: regression targets differ between Eq.(2) and line 208 (reference controls omitted), and GP hyperparameter selection is not explained.  
- Ambiguous phrasing: "posterior distribution can be obtained by constraining the joint distribution" — likely means conditioning and should be clarified.  
- Algorithm 1 is confusing: it seems to optimize an open-loop control sequence while paper discusses learning a feedback controller; how noise is handled is unclear.  
- Figures 1 and 2 should include error bars for interpretability; clarify whether ψ in Figure 1 is exponent of final costs or another quantity.  
- Rebuttal still leaves typographical/notation errors (e.g., Ψ vs Φ, missing Ψ_{t+Δt}) that need correction.  
- Minor but important issues: grammatical errors, empty sections, formatting problems (margins, Table 1, scaled brackets in Eq.(8)), and several notation inconsistencies (bold vs italic x_t in Eq.13).  
- Request clarification on how ~G is derived from G, and fixes for specific textual issues (Table/line references, PILCO/policy improvement wording, actuator count typo).
