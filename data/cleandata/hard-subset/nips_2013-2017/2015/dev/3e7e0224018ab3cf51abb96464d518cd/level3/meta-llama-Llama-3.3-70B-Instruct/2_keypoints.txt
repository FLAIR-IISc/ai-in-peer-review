- Main contribution: provides a bound on the price of anarchy for coarse correlated equilibria via reduction to several full-information games.
- Method: novel combination of familiar techniques to extend PoA results to Bayesian games.
- Technical soundness: most of the paper appears correct.
- Readability: paper is difficult to read due to unclear setup and notation.
- Specific clarity issue: definition of empirical distributions of values in population P_i is not clearly explained.
- Critical flaw: Lemma 10 is unclear—its hypothesis and the convergence of a subsequence of D^T are not adequately explained, casting doubt on the proof.
- Generality and originality: lacks generality and significant originality; limited scope reduces impact.
- Community fit: subject may not be of strong interest to the NIPS community.
- Recommendation: currently recommend rejection; suggest major revisions to fix Lemma 10 and improve clarity and generality for reconsideration.
