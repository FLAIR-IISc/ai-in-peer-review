- Overall positive: paper is interesting and results are compelling.  
- Relevance concern: testing monotonicity on a discrete cube is questionable; request a real-world example showing scientific relevance.  
- Literature critique: characterization of statistics literature as focused on large-sample regimes is misleading; many tests use exact null simulation and there are finite-sample results (e.g., Dümbgen & Walther 2008); references are too sparse.  
- Finite-sample claim challenged: the test relies on existence of N0 so type I error < α for N > N0, which resembles asymptotic behavior and is not verifiable in practice.  
- Summary concern: problem may not be common in practice and the paper is too dismissive of relevant statistical prior work.
