- Proposes a bidirectional tree RNN: upward pass followed by a downward pass to enrich node representations with larger-context information.  
- Application yields state-of-the-art performance on SemEval 2012 Task 2.  
- Extension is simple, elegant, and effective.  
- High-level idea resembles prior work ([18]) but the downward computation differs and yields superior performance.  
- Method is well-motivated, clearly presented, and the paper is generally well-written.  
- Experimental results are strong but some setup details are unclear.  
- Unclear whether the [18] baseline in Table 2 used dropout, extra training data, or hybrid vectors.  
- Ensemble result should not be presented in a table comparing individual models.  
- Phrase "widely used mix of both" in Section 4.4 needs clarification.  
- Overall assessment: well-executed paper based on a simple yet elegant idea; rating 9/10.
