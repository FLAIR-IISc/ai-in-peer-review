- Introduces a bidirectional extension of recursive neural networks that adds a downward pass to propagate larger-context information to all tree nodes.  
- Method is simple, elegant, and effective.  
- Related to prior work [18] at a high level, but differs in downward-pass computation and appears to outperform it.  
- Achieves state-of-the-art performance on SemEval 2012 Task 2.  
- Paper is well-motivated, clearly presented, and well-written.  
- Experimental concern: unclear whether the reported result for the [18] bidirectional RNN baseline in Table 2 used dropout and additional training data.  
- Experimental concern: unclear whether hybrid vectors were used for that baseline.  
- Presentation concern: ensemble result should not be compared alongside individual models in the same table.  
- Request for clarification: explain the phrase "widely used mix of both" in Section 4.4.  
- Overall: a well-executed paper based on a simple, elegant idea.
