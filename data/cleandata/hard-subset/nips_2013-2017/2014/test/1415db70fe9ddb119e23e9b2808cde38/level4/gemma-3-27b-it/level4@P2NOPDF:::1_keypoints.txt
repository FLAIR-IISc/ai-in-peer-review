- Introduces BPRNNs: adds a novel backward pass for phrase-level predictions and embeddings informing word embeddings and labels.
- Well-written, well-motivated, and clearly described model.
- Builds on Irsoy & Cardie (2008) but the citation is incomplete.
- Hybrid word vectors are a key contribution and may be the main distinction from prior work.
- Experimental comparisons may be unfair: unclear if RNN and B-RNN baselines used hybrid word vectors.
- Need clarification on model selection: how “best” models were tuned and the single best model identified.
- Baselines lack ensemble methods; this omission should be acknowledged and discussed as a factor.
- The term “belief propagation” is potentially misleading; “forward-backward” could be more descriptive.
- Section 3.5 (Training) lacks detail; likely uses BPTS (cite Goller 1996) and needs discussion of unfolding/deep gradients and training challenges/optimizations.
- Section 3.6 is unclear and must explicitly differentiate the proposed model from Irsoy & Cardie.
- Section 4.1 needs more accessible explanation of SemEval 2013 Task 2, specify which subtask was addressed, and clarify parser constraint roles.
- Sections 4.3 and 4.4 could be combined for conciseness.
- Overall: interesting and valuable variant; addressing experimental fairness, training details, and task description would improve impact.
