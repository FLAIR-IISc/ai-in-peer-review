- Paper proposes a bidirectional extension to tree-structured RNNs with an upward pass followed by a downward pass to enrich node representations.  
- Approach is straightforward, elegant, and effective.  
- Achieves state-of-the-art results on SemEval 2012 Task 2.  
- Conceptually similar to prior work [18], but performs different computations during the downward pass and appears to outperform [18].  
- Paper is well-motivated and clearly written.  
- Reviewer has experimental-setup concerns: did the [18] baseline in Table 2 use dropout and additional training data, and were hybrid vectors used?  
- Ensemble result should not be included in a table comparing individual models.  
- Clarification needed for the phrase “widely used mix of both” in Section 4.4.  
- Overall assessment: a well-executed paper built on a simple and elegant idea.
