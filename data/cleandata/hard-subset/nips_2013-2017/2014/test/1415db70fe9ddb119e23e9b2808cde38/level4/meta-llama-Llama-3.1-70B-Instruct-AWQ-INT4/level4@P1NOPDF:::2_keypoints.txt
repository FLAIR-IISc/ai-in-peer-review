- Introduces a bidirectional tree RNN (upward pass then downward pass) to augment node representations with larger-context information.
- Achieves state-of-the-art performance on SemEval 2012 Task 2.
- Extension is simple, elegant, and effective.
- Downward-pass computation differs from prior work [18] and appears to yield superior performance.
- Method is well-motivated, clearly presented, and the paper is well-written.
- Experimental concern: unclear whether the [18] baseline result in Table 2 used dropout and additional training data.
- Experimental concern: unclear whether hybrid vectors were used for the alternative bidirectional model.
- Concern that the ensemble result should not be shown in a table comparing individual models.
- Request for clarification of the phrase “widely used mix of both” in Section 4.4.
- Overall assessment: a well-executed paper built on a simple, elegant idea.
