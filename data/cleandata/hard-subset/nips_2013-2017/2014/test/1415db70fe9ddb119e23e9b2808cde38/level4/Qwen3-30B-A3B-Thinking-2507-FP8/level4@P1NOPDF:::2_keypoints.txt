- Proposes a bidirectional recursive neural network (upward then downward tree passes) to enrich node representations and achieves state-of-the-art on SemEval 2012 Task 2.  
- Extension is simple, elegant, effective and differs substantially from prior bidirectional work [18] in the downward-pass computation.  
- Reported performance clearly surpasses the prior work.  
- Method is well-motivated, clearly presented, and the paper is generally well-written.  
- Experimental setup concerns: unclear if results for the alternative model from [18] (Table 2) used dropout, additional training data, or hybrid word vectors.  
- Ensemble result should not be shown in a table comparing individual model performances.  
- Wording issue: the phrase "widely used mix of both" in Section 4.4 is unclear and needs specification.  
- Overall assessment: a well-executed paper presenting a simple yet effective idea.
