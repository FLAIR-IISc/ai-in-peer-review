- Introduces a bidirectional recursive neural network that adds a downward pass to propagate larger-context information to all tree nodes.  
- Achieves state-of-the-art performance on SemEval 2012 Task 2.  
- Extension is simple, elegant, effective, and well motivated; paper is clearly written.  
- Approach is similar in high-level idea to prior work [18], but uses a different downward computation and yields superior performance.  
- Experimental results look excellent but the experimental setup raises concerns.  
- Unclear whether the [18] baseline in Table 2 was trained with dropout, additional training data, or hybrid vectors.  
- The ensemble result should not be presented alongside individual-model comparisons in the same table.  
- Ambiguity in Section 4.4: unclear what "widely used mix of both" refers to.
