- Proposes a method to bound the Average Causal Effect (ACE) by identifying witnesses/instruments and solving an LP with relaxations and a fast algorithm to handle faithfulness violations.
- Addresses the important problem of causal estimation from observational data.
- Novelly combines instrumental variable ideas, causal discovery, and optimization under the "Witness Protection Program" framing.
- Explicitly relaxes faithfulness assumptions to provide robustness to real-world data imperfections.
- Develops practical relaxations and a faster, message-passing-like algorithm to mitigate computational cost.
- Provides thorough experiments on synthetic and real data and compares favorably to naive estimators.
- Generally well-written and logically structured despite complex content.
- Methodology is complex and requires strong background in causal inference, optimization, and graphical models.
- Performance depends on choosing relaxation parameters (w, y, x, β, β̄) with insufficient practical guidance.
- Scalability remains a concern; even relaxed LPs may be costly in very high dimensions and scaling analysis is abstract.
- Notation and some transitions are dense or confusing (e.g., ζyx.w vs κyx.w) and could be clarified.
- Core LP formulation builds on prior work (Ramsahai [16]); interpretation and relaxations are novel but fundamental approach is not entirely new.
- Technically sound with correct derivations and supportive experimental results.
- Originality lies in the combination of techniques and relaxation strategy, with significant focus on robustness to faithfulness violations.
- Potentially significant contribution if scalability and usability (parameter selection) are improved.
- Pro: addresses a hard problem, theoretically sound, principled faithfulness handling, promising experiments, well-researched.
- Con: complex methodology, unclear parameter selection, computational scalability issues, incremental novelty in core LP.
- Overall assessment: strong paper with potential impact; recommended accept with minor revisions to improve clarity, parameter guidance, and scalability analysis.
