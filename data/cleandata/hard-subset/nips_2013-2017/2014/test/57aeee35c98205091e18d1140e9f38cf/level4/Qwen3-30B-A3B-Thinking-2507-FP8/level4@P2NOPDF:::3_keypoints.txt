- Introduces WPP, an algorithm to compute ACE bounds allowing controlled violations of faithfulness via relaxation parameters and linear programming.  
- The problem is timely and the approach is a principled combination of established mathematical tools.  
- Core contribution is conceptually interesting and likely to stimulate further research.  
- Practical utility is questionable because WPP provides a distribution over bounds without guidance on the plausibility of relaxation scenarios.  
- Many relaxation settings produce overly wide, practically uninformative ACE intervals (e.g., influenza result ~(-0.23, 0.64)).  
- WPP returns intervals with no point estimate, risking misinterpretation as confidence intervals around a single estimate.  
- Experimental evaluation is severely deficient and does not convincingly demonstrate practical benefit.  
- The proposed "bias" metric is ill-defined—comparing WPP intervals to competitors’ point estimates—making comparisons meaningless.  
- Paper fails to show that WPP improves on standard faithfulness-based approaches in real-world data.  
- Method appears technically sound and sufficiently detailed in the supplement, but main text lacks clarity on some key points.  
- Selecting relaxation parameters is a crucial practical challenge deferred to future work, hindering usability assessment.  
- Explanations of subtle parameter roles (e.g., how relaxation parameters define admissible sets Z for witness W) are insufficient, complicating implementation.  
- Algorithmic presentation has abrupt abstraction shifts and is hard to follow in places.  
- Claims that WPP “keeps inference more honest” or “provides a compromise” are misleading given wide bounds that do not aid interpretation.  
- Contribution is significant conceptually for future work but not robust or ready for routine practitioner use.  
- Numerous minor issues, typos, unclear phrasings, and missing citations need correction and clarification.  
- Overall recommendation: Reject due to poor experimental evaluation, unclear practical relevance, and unresolved interpretational challenges.
