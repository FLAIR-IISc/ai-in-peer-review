- Proposes Witness Protection Program (WPP) to bound ACE by relaxing faithfulness and using Entner's Rule 1 to identify instruments.  
- Introduces linear programming formulation to derive ACE bounds under constrained causal paths.  
- Adds a Bayesian learning approach to account for uncertainty in bound estimation.  
- Paper is well-structured and clearly written with a thorough methodological exposition.  
- Use of Entner's Rule 1 and relaxation of faithfulness are novel and significant contributions.  
- Method potentially widely applicable across fields needing ACE estimation.  
- Experimental validation is limited to synthetic data and one real-world example, raising generalization concerns.  
- Choice and motivation of relaxation parameters are weak and sensitivity to them is unclear.  
- Lacks detailed empirical comparison to alternative methods (e.g., IV approaches, regression discontinuity).  
- Some notation and terminology may be unfamiliar to non-experts, affecting clarity.  
- Originality is mixed since some ideas/techniques are not entirely new.  
- Reviewer recommends acceptance but suggests addressing experimental breadth, parameter justification, and comparative evaluations.
