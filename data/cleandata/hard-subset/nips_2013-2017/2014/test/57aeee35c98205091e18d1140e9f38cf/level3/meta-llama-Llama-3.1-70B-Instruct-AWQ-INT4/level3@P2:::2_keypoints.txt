- Proposes a novel approach to estimate causal effects using observational conditional independencies to identify "weak" paths in an unknown causal graph.  
- Relaxes the standard faithfulness assumption to allow varying degrees of path cancellations that produce conditional independencies without eliminating confounding paths.  
- Produces a posterior distribution over bounds on the average causal effect using a linear programming formulation combined with Bayesian inference.  
- Builds on prior work (instrumental variables and Entner et al.) and modifies Entner et al.'s results to generate candidate instrumental variables.  
- Introduces a linear programming method to obtain bounds on the average causal effect and a Bayesian learning approach to handle uncertainty.  
- Strength: can provide narrow and accurate bounds on the average causal effect even without an optimized algorithm.  
- Strength: novel combination of linear programming and Bayesian inference showing promise for observational causal estimation.  
- Weakness: applying Entner's Rule 1 in the inverse direction is not clearly justified and needs explicit assumption/discussion (e.g., Fig. 2 structure).  
- Weakness: no generic criteria for selecting relaxation parameters, which strongly influence the resulting bounds.  
- Weakness: experimental implementation and algorithm details are poorly described; parameter definitions and table references are unclear or missing.  
- Weakness: typos and incorrect/missing table references reduce clarity.  
- Originality is limited by prior work, though the combination of three ideas has some novel aspects that are not fully validated.  
- Significance is promising but limited by sparse experiments and insufficient theoretical and empirical validation of generality and robustness.  
- Recommendations: justify inverse-rule validity and Fig. 2, develop parameter-selection criteria, improve algorithm/experiment details, run additional experiments, and strengthen theoretical/empirical validation.  
- Overall assessment: rating 6/10 with confidence 8/10.
