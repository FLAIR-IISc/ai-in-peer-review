The WPP is a significant contribution to the field of causal inference, as it provides a more robust and flexible approach to estimating causal effects in the presence of confounding variables.

The authors also provide a thorough discussion of the related work and the limitations of their approach. The use of Bayesian inference also provides a more robust and flexible approach to estimating causal effects, as it can handle uncertainty and non-linear relationships between variables.
