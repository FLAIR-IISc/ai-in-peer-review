- Paper proposes a Newton-like method for minimizing a smooth convex cost plus multiple decomposable norms, with two main contributions: an active subspace selection procedure and a convergence proof for solving the quadratic approximation on the changing active subspace.  
- Empirical results show about 10× speedup over state-of-the-art on two important problems, and appendix experiments split speedup between quadratic approximation and active-subspace selection.  
- Major concern: critical material in the appendix makes the submission more suitable for a journal than an 8-page conference review.  
- Checked proofs are correct and well written where inspected, aside from a few minor typos.  
- Paper is overall well written and organized, but references should be numbered in order.
