- Proposes a novel denoising method using local sensor communication and game-theoretic best-response dynamics.  
- Provides theoretical guarantees for denoising effectiveness, including synchronous and random-order asynchronous updates.  
- Experimental results demonstrate successful denoising and learning a target function from few label queries.  
- Clear, well-organized presentation with enough detail for expert reproduction.  
- Strengths: innovative approach, thorough theoretical analysis, and convincing experiments.  
- Vulnerable to "collisions" where distinct image patches share the same content, hurting performance.  
- Analysis of the denoising dynamics is not particularly compelling and appears obvious.  
- Lacks comparison to state-of-the-art results on certain datasets (e.g., CIFAR-10).  
- Technically sound and claims are well-supported by theory and experiments.  
- High originality and significant advance over prior work.  
- Would benefit from more discussion of practical applications and limitations.
