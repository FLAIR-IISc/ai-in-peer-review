- Presents a novel neural approach for representing symbolic expressions and finding identities; limited expression scope but potentially impactful for symbolic libraries.
- Primary concern: paper emphasizes finding lower-complexity expressions but the algorithm does not enforce this constraint.
- Authors should discuss methods to limit the search space to lower-complexity solutions (would guide future work).
- Section 2 restrictions are attributed to the scheduler but lack formal analysis or empirical justification; unclear if scheduler is the sole limiter.
- Paper structure is unclear; stages of the method are not well explained and should be broken down and clarified.
- Technical concern: unclear constraints on vector/matrix sizes (e.g., RBM-1 expression seems invalid for k=1, n=1); needs clarification.
- Typos to fix: "show for" (p.332), "has contains" (p.148), "is is" (p.289), "by be" (p.338).
- Overall: interesting approach; addressing the above issues would strengthen the paper.
