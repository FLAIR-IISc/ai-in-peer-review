- Presents an interesting approach using distributed, recursive neural representations to classify algebraic identities.  
- Problem definition seems overly restricted; authors should clarify scope and limitations.  
- Methodology needs clearer explanation, especially the role and form of the weight tensor W3 (l×l×l) and whether a neural tensor network or a simple linear network is used.  
- Important to justify architecture choice since prior work (e.g., Bowman) shows tensor networks can outperform RNNs on similar tasks.  
- In Section 7.2, unclear whether the RNN is still used for classification during evaluation of different search strategies.  
- Proposal generation in the search algorithm is not well-explained and requires additional clarification.  
- Numerous typos and labeling errors throughout (specific suggested corrections provided), including Figure 3 label which should be RNN not TNN.  
- Overall promising work but needs clearer explanations and error corrections to improve quality and impact.
