- Proposes two methods for searching for simpler symbolic expression trees: an n-gram method and an RNN-based embedding method.
- N-gram method computes n-grams over sequences of subexpressions.
- RNN method embeds expression trees in a continuous space to predict which rule will combine given subtrees.
- Empirically, the n-gram approach outperforms the RNN on two of three tasks (Figure 3).
- Quality of the work is rated high.
- Clarity is an issue; the paper is somewhat hard to follow, especially Section 7.2.
- Originality is rated high.
- Significance is rated low: the approach is unlikely to be adopted by efficiency-focused researchers, the problem feels contrived, and practical significance is unclear.
