- Novel application of DNNs to identify mathematical identities; concept is interesting.  
- Work appears preliminary and may not meet NIPS publication standards.  
- Method’s performance at discovering identities is unimpressive even with a highly restricted input set.  
- Expression set is so limited that a simple mathematical approach might be more effective than using DNNs.  
- Approach seems overly complex for the problem; scalability and generalization to broader expressions are unclear.  
- Overall interesting but falls slightly short of the publication threshold.
