- Proposes two methods to find simpler symbolic expression trees: an n-gram–guided search and an RNN-based recursive embedding to predict production rules.
- N-gram method outperforms the RNN on two of three evaluated tasks (Figure 3).
- Overall paper quality is rated high.
- Exposition is sometimes hard to follow, particularly Section 7.2.
- Originality is rated high.
- Significance is rated low: practical impact is unclear and the problem setting seems somewhat contrived.
- Doubt that practitioners seeking computational efficiency would adopt these methods; overall significance is uncertain.
