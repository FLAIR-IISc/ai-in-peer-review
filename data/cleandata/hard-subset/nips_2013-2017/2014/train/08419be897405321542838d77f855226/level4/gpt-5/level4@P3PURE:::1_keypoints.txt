- Applies deep neural networks to the novel task of discovering mathematical identities — an interesting idea.
- Paper feels preliminary and does not meet the bar for a NIPS publication.
- Method does not demonstrate strong performance in discovering identities.
- Evaluation is limited to a very narrow set of inputs and expressions.
- The expressions come from a restricted class that could likely be handled by direct mathematical rules.
- Approach feels like using a sledgehammer to crack a nut given the problem simplicity.
- Unclear that the method would scale or be sensible when extended to a broader range of expressions.
- Overall: interesting direction but currently slightly below the threshold for publication.
