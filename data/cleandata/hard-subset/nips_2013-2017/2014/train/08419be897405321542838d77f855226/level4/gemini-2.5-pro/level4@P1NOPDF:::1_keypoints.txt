- Applies DNNs to discovering mathematical identities — novel and intriguing direction but preliminary.
- Experimental performance is not particularly impressive, even on highly constrained input expressions.
- The domain of expressions explored is very limited; simpler symbolic or rule-based methods would likely be more effective.
- Using a DNN here feels like overkill (“sledgehammer to crack a nut”).
- It is unclear how the proposed method would scale to more complex problems.
- Modest initial results do not convincingly justify choosing a deep learning approach over alternatives.
- Overall interesting but slightly below the bar for acceptance at NIPS.
