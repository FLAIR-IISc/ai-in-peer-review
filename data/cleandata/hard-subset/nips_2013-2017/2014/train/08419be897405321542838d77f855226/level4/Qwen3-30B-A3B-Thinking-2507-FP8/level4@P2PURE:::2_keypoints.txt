- Proposes two methods for searching simpler symbolic expression trees: an n-gram model over sub-expression sequences and an RNN that generates continuous representations to predict subtree-combination rules.  
- Experimental result: the n-gram approach outperforms the RNN on two of three tasks (Figure 3).  
- Overall quality: High.  
- Clarity concern: paper is somewhat difficult to follow, especially Section 7.2.  
- Originality: High.  
- Significance concern: Low — problem formulation appears contrived and unlikely to attract users seeking efficient computation; the practical significance is unclear.
