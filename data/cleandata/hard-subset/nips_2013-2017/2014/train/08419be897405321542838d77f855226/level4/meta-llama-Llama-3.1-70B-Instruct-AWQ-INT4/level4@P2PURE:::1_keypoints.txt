- Approach may be overly complex for the task and could likely be handled by mathematical rules.
- Use of DNNs appears disproportionate to the problem’s complexity.
- Unclear whether the method can be scaled to broader expressions or justified given modest initial results.
- Overall interesting but falls slightly short of the publication threshold.
