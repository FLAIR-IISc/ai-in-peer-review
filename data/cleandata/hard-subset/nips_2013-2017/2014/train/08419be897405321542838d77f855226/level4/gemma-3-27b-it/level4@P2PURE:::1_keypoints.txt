- Paper applies deep neural networks to the novel task of discovering mathematical identities — an interesting idea.
- Work appears preliminary and may not meet NeurIPS publication standards.
- Method’s performance in discovering identities is not particularly compelling.
- Input expressions come from a restricted set, limiting the significance of results.
- Given the restricted expressions, a direct rule-based approach might be more efficient (sledgehammer-to-crack-a-nut concern).
- Authors’ claim of extensibility is unclear; scalability and justification for broader application are not demonstrated.
- Reviewer had prepared a more detailed review but cannot locate it.
- Overall: intriguing contribution but currently slightly below the conference’s acceptance threshold.
