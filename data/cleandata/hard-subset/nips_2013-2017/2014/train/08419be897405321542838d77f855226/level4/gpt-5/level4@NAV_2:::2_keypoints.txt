- Paper proposes two methods for simplifying symbolic expression trees: an n-gram model over sequences of sub-expressions and a recursive neural network that learns continuous tree representations to predict combination rules.  
- According to Figure 3, the n-gram approach outperforms the RNN on two of three tasks.  
- Overall quality of the work is rated high.  
- Presentation is somewhat difficult to follow, especially Section 7.2.  
- Originality of the approaches is rated high.  
- Significance is rated low: the formulation feels contrived, it seems unlikely to be adopted for efficiency-focused computation, and practical impact is unclear.
