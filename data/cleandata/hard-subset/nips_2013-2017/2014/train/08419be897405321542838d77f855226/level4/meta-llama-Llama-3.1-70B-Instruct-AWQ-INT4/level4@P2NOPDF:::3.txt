This paper presents an interesting approach to discovering more efficient algebraic identities, leveraging distributed and recursive neural representations to classify these identities successfully.

However, the problem definition appears to be somewhat restricted, and further clarification would be beneficial. 

Additionally, the explanation of the "weight tensor W3 (of size lxlxl, so that the output is also size l)" is unclear. It would be helpful to explicitly state whether a neural tensor network is used instead of a simple linear network. Notably, Bowman's work demonstrated the superiority of tensor networks over RNNs in such tasks.

The section on Efficient Identity Discovery (7.2) lacks clarity regarding the use of RNNs for classification while evaluating different search strategies. The proposal generation process in the search is also not clearly explained and warrants further elaboration.
