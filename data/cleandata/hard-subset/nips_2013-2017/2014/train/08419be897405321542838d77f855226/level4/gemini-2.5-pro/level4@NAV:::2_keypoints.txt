- Proposes two methods for concise symbolic expression trees: an n-gram approach over sub-expression sequences and an RNN that learns continuous tree embeddings to predict subtree-combination rules.
- Empirical result (Fig. 3): the n-gram method outperforms the RNN on two of three evaluated tasks.
- Overall quality judged high.
- Clarity concerns: manuscript is difficult to follow in parts, with Section 7.2 especially hard to comprehend.
- Originality judged high.
- Significance judged low: practical utility for computational-efficiency optimization is questionable and the problem formulation appears artificial.
- Broader impact is unclear — methods for finding more efficient symbolic expressions are presented but their wider importance is not well established.
