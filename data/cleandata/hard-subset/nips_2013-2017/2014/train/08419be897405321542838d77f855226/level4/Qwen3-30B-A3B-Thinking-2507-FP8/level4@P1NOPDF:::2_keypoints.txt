- Proposes two methods: n-grams from sub-expression sequences and an RNN that embeds expression trees to predict subtree-combination rules.  
- N-gram method outperforms the RNN on two of three benchmark tasks (Figure 3).  
- Quality: technically sound with adequate experimental design.  
- Clarity: generally clear but Section 7.2 is hard to follow; RNN training objective and n-gram sequence construction need clearer exposition.  
- Originality: high—novel use of n-grams for symbolic expression search and embedding-based RNN approach.  
- Significance: low—the problem seems contrived and lacks practical relevance; methods do not convincingly address real-world computational optimization needs.
