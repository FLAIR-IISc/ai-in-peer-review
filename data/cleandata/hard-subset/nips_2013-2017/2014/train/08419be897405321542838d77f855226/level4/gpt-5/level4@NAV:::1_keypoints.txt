- Paper applies DNNs to a novel domain (discovering mathematical identities).
- Ideas are original but the work feels preliminary.
- Method does not exhibit strong performance at finding identities, even on limited inputs.
- The expression class considered is highly constrained; a straightforward mathematical procedure might be simpler and more effective.
- The approach seems like using an overly powerful tool for a small task.
- Unclear that the method would scale to broader classes of expressions or that scaling is warranted given middling results.
- Reviewer finds the paper interesting but judges it below the threshold for publication.
