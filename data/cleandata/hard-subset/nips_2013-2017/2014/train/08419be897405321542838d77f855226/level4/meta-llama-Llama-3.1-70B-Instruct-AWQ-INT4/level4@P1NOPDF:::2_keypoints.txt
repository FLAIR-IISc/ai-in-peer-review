- Proposes two methods to search for simpler symbolic expression trees: an n-gram model over sub-expression sequences and a recursive neural network (RNN) embedding approach.
- Uses those representations to predict the most likely rule for combining subtrees.
- Empirical result (Fig. 3): the n-gram method outperforms the RNN on two of three tasks.
- Quality: reviewer rates the work as high quality.
- Clarity: paper is somewhat hard to follow; Section 7.2 needs clearer explanation and elaboration.
- Originality: reviewer considers the work highly original.
- Significance: rated low—problem appears contrived, adoption unlikely, and practical impact is unclear.
