- Paper applies DNNs to discovering mathematical identities; the core idea is interesting but preliminary.  
- Primary concern: the proposed method’s performance is weak and the results are not compelling.  
- Evaluation used a narrow distribution of expressions, where targeted rule-based approaches could be more effective.  
- Using a DNN feels disproportionate to the task complexity (“sledgehammer to crack a nut”).  
- It is unclear whether the methodology would practically scale to broader or more complex expressions.  
- Modest initial results do not convincingly show that larger models or scaling would yield significant improvements.  
- Recognizes potential interest in the research, but the current work falls slightly below NeurIPS publication threshold.
