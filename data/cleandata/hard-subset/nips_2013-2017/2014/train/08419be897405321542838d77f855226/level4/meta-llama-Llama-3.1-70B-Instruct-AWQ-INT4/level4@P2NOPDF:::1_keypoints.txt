- Reviewer lost a longer version; this review is brief as a result.
- Paper applies DNNs to the novel task of discovering mathematical identities.
- Ideas are innovative but the work is preliminary.
- Proposed method does not show impressive performance, even on a limited input set.
- Test expressions come from a narrow range, raising concerns about generality.
- Likely that simple mathematical rules could solve the tested cases, making DNN use unnecessary ("sledgehammer to crack a nut").
- Unclear whether the approach can be scaled or made meaningful beyond the small tests.
- Overall interesting but judged slightly below the publication threshold.
