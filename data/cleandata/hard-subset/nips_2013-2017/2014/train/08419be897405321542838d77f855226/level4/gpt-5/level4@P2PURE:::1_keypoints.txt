- Applies deep neural networks to discovering mathematical identities; direction is interesting but preliminary.  
- Method does not demonstrate strong performance at uncovering identities, even on a limited input set.  
- Expression set is highly restricted; a simple hand-crafted procedure could likely solve it, making the DNN approach disproportionately heavy.  
- Unclear if the method will scale or generalize to a broader range of expressions, and scaling may not be warranted given modest results.  
- Overall assessment: interesting work but slightly below the threshold for acceptance.
