- Novel application: paper applies DNNs to finding mathematical identities, which is an innovative direction.  
- Preliminary work: overall the study appears early-stage and not yet mature for a top-tier venue.  
- Weak performance: method does not demonstrate impressive results in discovering identities, even on limited inputs.  
- Narrow expression set: experiments use a restricted set of expressions, limiting practical relevance.  
- Simpler alternatives: developing explicit mathematical rules might be more effective than DNNs for this task.  
- Scalability concerns: unclear whether the approach can be scaled to broader or more complex expression spaces.  
- Marginal justification: mediocre initial results make it questionable to extend or sensibly apply the method.  
- Improvement needed: authors should provide stronger empirical evidence and demonstration of generalization to complex expressions.
