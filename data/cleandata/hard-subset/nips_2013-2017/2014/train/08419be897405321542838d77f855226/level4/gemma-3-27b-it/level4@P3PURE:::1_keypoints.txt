- Applies DNNs to the novel problem of discovering mathematical identities.
- Core ideas are interesting but the work feels preliminary.
- Demonstrated performance is not particularly compelling.
- Input expressions are from a constrained set, limiting the significance.
- Many identities could likely be derived more straightforwardly by manual reasoning.
- The approach seems disproportionate to the task given the narrow scope.
- Unclear whether the method generalizes or scales effectively to broader classes of expressions.
- Modest initial results make it questionable whether scaling is justified.
- Overall assessment: interesting exploration that currently falls slightly below the publication threshold.
