- Paper applies deep neural networks to the novel task of discovering mathematical identities.
- Ideas are considered innovative but the work feels preliminary.
- Method's performance on identifying identities is not especially compelling despite highly constrained inputs.
- Dataset/expressions are narrow enough that a mathematical rule might have solved the task more simply (“sledgehammer to crack a nut”).
- Unclear whether the approach will generalize or scale to broader classes of expressions.
- Given modest results, it is questionable whether extending the approach is worthwhile.
- Overall interesting but judged slightly below the publication bar for NeurIPS.
