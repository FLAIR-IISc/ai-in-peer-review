- Novel application of deep neural networks to discovering mathematical identities — interesting direction.
- Primary concern: the proposed method does not demonstrate compelling performance.
- Results are unimpressive, especially given the highly constrained set of input expressions used for evaluation.
- The restricted expression class could be handled more effectively by simpler symbolic or rule-based approaches.
- Applying deep learning here appears unnecessarily complex — a "sledgehammer to crack a nut."
- The paper does not provide a clear path or convincing evidence for scaling to more general classes of expressions.
- Modest performance on the simplified task raises doubt about efficacy on more complex problems.
- Limited experimental scope and questionable justification for the method's complexity undermine the contribution.
- Overall, the work is promising but feels too preliminary for publication at NIPS.
