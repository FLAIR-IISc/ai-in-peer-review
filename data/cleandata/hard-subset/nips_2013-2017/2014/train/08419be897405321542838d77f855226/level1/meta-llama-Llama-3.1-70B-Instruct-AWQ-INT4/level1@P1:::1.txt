The paper "Learning to Discover Efficient Mathematical Identities" proposes a novel framework for discovering efficient mathematical identities using machine learning techniques. The authors introduce an attribute grammar framework for representing symbolic expressions and develop two learning approaches, an n-gram model and a recursive neural network (RNN), to guide the search for efficient identities.

The paper is well-motivated, and the problem of discovering efficient mathematical identities is an important one. The authors provide a clear and concise overview of the related work in the areas of theorem proving, program induction, and probabilistic programming.
