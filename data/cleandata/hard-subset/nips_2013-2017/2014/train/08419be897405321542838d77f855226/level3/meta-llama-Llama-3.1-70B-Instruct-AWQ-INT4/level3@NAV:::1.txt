**Summary**

The paper "Learning to Discover Efficient Mathematical Identities" proposes a novel approach to discovering mathematical identities using deep neural networks (DNNs) and attribute grammars. The authors introduce a framework that represents symbolic expressions as a sequence of grammar rules and uses machine learning techniques to learn a search strategy for the grammar trees. The approach is demonstrated on a set of mathematical expressions, including polynomial expressions and elementary symmetric polynomials.
