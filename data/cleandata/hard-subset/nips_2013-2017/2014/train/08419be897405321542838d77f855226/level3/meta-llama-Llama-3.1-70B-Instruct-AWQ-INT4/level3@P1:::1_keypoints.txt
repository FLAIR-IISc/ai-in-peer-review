- Proposes a novel DNN-based method (attribute grammar + n-gram and RNN models) to discover efficient mathematical identities.  
- Paper is well-written with a clear explanation of approach and related work.  
- Technically sound implementation and methodology.  
- Application of DNNs to finding mathematical identities is original.  
- Reviewer questions whether the contribution is significant enough for NIPS given modest results.  
- Method's performance at discovering identities is not particularly impressive.  
- Experiments use a very limited set of input expressions.  
- Approach may be overly complex for the narrow problem; hand-derived rules might suffice.  
- Unclear whether the method scales to more general expressions or if scaling is justified.  
- Reviewer finds the work interesting but believes it falls slightly below acceptance threshold.  
- Recommendation: reject, but encourages continued exploration and addressing raised concerns.
