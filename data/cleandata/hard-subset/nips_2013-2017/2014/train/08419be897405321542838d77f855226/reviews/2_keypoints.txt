- Proposes two methods for finding simpler symbolic expression trees: n-gram models over sub-expression sequences and a recursive neural network producing continuous subtree representations to predict combination rules.
- N-gram method outperforms the RNN on two of three tasks (per Figure 3).
- Overall quality of the paper is rated high.
- Clarity is limited; the paper is somewhat hard to follow, notably Section 7.2.
- Originality is rated high.
- Significance is rated low: the problem appears contrived and unlikely to be adopted for efficient computations.
- Practical usefulness of the proposed search methods for producing computationally efficient symbolic expressions is unclear.
