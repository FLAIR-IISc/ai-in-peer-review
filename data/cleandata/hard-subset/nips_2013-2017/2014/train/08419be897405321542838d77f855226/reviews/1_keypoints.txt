- Applying DNNs to finding mathematical identities is a novel idea.  
- The work feels preliminary and may not meet NIPS publication standards.  
- The method's performance at discovering identities is not particularly impressive.  
- Experiments used a very limited set of input expressions.  
- Given the limited domain, hand-derived mathematical rules might have been simpler (“sledgehammer to crack a nut”).  
- It's unclear the approach would scale to more general expressions or that scaling is justified given the modest results.  
- Reviewer finds the paper interesting but slightly below the threshold for acceptance.
