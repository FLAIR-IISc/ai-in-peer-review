1. Paper proposes a fully collapsed Gibbs (CG) sampler for the Beta-Negative Binomial Process (BNBP).
2. Lemma 1 and the derived sampler are novel and interesting.
3. Major concern: the experimental evaluation is insufficient.
4. Authors' motivation centers on sampler advantages: simple implementation, fast convergence, good mixing, and strong predictive performance.
5. The work focuses on sampling algorithm development, not on new statistical modeling.
6. Therefore experiments should compare the proposed sampler to other BNBP sampling algorithms, not to HDP-LDA.
7. The paper provides the CG sampler but lacks experimental results comparing it with other BNBP samplers.
