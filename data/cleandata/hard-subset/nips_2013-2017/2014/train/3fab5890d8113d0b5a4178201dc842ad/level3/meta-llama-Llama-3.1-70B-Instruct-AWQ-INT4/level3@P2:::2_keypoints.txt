- Proposes a novel fully collapsed Gibbs (CG) sampler for the Beta-Negative Binomial Process (BNBP) topic model.  
- Derives the exchangeable partition probability function (EPPF) for the BNBP, which is novel.  
- The CG sampler is distinct from existing samplers and intuitively interpretable.  
- The sampler is simple to implement and reportedly has fast convergence and good mixing.  
- Paper is well-written, well-organized, and provides a clear introduction to BNBP and applications.  
- Technical derivations and theoretical claims are sound and well-supported.  
- Experimental evaluation is insufficient and lacks comparisons to other BNBP samplers.  
- Authors compare only to HDP-LDA (a different model), making performance assessment relative to other BNBP samplers unclear.  
- Work emphasizes sampling algorithm development rather than new statistical modeling.  
- Major concern: absence of empirical comparisons against existing BNBP sampling algorithms.  
- Recommendation: revise to include experiments comparing the proposed sampler to other BNBP samplers.  
- Suggest removing or de-emphasizing the HDP-LDA comparison and focusing evaluation on BNBP baselines.
