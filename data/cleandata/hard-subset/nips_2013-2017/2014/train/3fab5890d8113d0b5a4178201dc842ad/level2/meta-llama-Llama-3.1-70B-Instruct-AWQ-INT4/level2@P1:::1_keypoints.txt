- Introduces an EPPF for the beta-negative binomial process (BNBP) applied to mixed-membership modeling.  
- Derives a novel nonparametric Bayesian topic model based on the BNBP, distinct from existing models.  
- Presents a general method to obtain an EPPF for mixed-membership models using integer-valued stochastic processes.  
- Provides a fully collapsed Gibbs sampler that converges quickly, mixes well, and is straightforward to implement.  
- Experimental results show state-of-the-art predictive performance compared to HDP and LDA when topic counts are comparable.  
- Thorough experiments on three corpora with clear comparisons to baselines.  
- Paper is well-written, well-organized, and easy to follow.  
- Significant potential impact by offering a new tool for mixed-membership/topic modeling.  
- Assumes reader familiarity with BNBP and EPPF, which may limit accessibility.  
- Needs a clearer, more detailed explanation of the relationship between BNBP and the derived EPPF.  
- Experimental section would benefit from more details about the corpora and chosen hyperparameters.  
- Recommendation: accept for NIPS, with suggested additions on dataset/hyperparameter details and expanded BNBP–EPPF explanation.
