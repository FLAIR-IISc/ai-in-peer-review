- Proposes a fully collapsed Gibbs (CG) sampler for the beta-negative binomial process (BNBP).  
- Lemma 1 and the resulting sampler are novel and interesting.  
- Main concern: experimental evaluation is insufficient.  
- Paper’s motivation focuses on sampler advantages (simple implementation, fast convergence, good mixing, strong predictive performance).  
- Experiments compare against HDP-LDA, which is not an appropriate baseline for demonstrating improvements in BNBP inference.  
- Recommendation: include comparisons and experimental results against other existing BNBP sampling algorithms.
