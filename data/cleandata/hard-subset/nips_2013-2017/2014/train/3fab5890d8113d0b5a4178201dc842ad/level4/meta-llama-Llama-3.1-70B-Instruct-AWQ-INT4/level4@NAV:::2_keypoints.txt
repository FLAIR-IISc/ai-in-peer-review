- Introduces a fully collapsed Gibbs (CG) sampler for the BNBP with a novel lemma and sampler; theoretical contribution is strong.  
- Experimental design raises concerns about the validity of comparisons.  
- Main motivations for the CG sampler are simplicity, fast convergence, good mixing, and strong predictive performance.  
- Because the paper focuses on the sampling algorithm, evaluations should compare against other BNBP sampling algorithms.  
- Current experiments compare to HDP-LDA, which is inappropriate; include comparisons to existing BNBP samplers to better establish efficacy.
