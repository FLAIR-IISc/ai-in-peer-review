- Paper derives the exchangeable partition probability function (EPPF) for the beta negative binomial process (BNBP) and develops a collapsed Gibbs sampler for a BNBP topic model.
- The manuscript is well-written, reasonably clear, and technically sound.
- The primary result (BNBP EPPF for mixed-membership/count-mixture models) is interesting and potentially useful for related models.
- The BNBP topic model demonstrates the EPPF but shows only marginal improvements over HDP-LDA; the main takeaway is that BNBP and HDP-LDA have distinct characteristics.
- Reviewer recommends experiments comparing the collapsed sampler to the sliced sampler [10] and truncated sampler [8], and studying mixing trade-offs versus uncollapsed representations (which favor parallelization).
- Overall assessment: interesting paper that meets the publication threshold, with only minor comments about the experimental section.
