- Introduces the exchangeable partition probability function (EPPF) for the beta negative binomial process (BNBP) and develops a collapsed Gibbs sampler for the BNBP topic model.  
- Paper is well-written, clear, and technically sound.  
- Main contribution: deriving the BNBP's EPPF for mixed-membership modeling via "count-mixture" models, offering useful tools for related EPPF derivations.  
- Empirical demonstration (BNBP topic model) shows utility but only marginal improvement over HDP-LDA; highlights that the two models have distinct characteristics.  
- Recommends additional experiments comparing the collapsed sampler to the sliced sampler [10] and the truncated sampler [8].  
- Suggests investigating mixing tradeoffs of the collapsed sampler and considering the parallelization advantages of uncollapsed representations.  
- Overall assessment: interesting paper that meets publication criteria; experimental section could be strengthened with the suggested comparisons.
