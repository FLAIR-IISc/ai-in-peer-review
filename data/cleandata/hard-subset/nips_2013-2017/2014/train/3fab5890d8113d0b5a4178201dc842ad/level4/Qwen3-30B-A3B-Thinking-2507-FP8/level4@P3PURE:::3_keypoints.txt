- Presents the EPPF for the beta negative binomial process (BNBP) and develops a collapsed Gibbs sampler for the BNBP topic model using this EPPF.  
- Paper is well-written, clear, and technically sound.  
- Primary contribution: derivation of the BNBP's EPPF via "count-mixture" models is theoretically interesting and provides tools for deriving EPPFs for related models.  
- BNBP topic model is a reasonable illustration of the EPPF's utility, but empirical improvements over HDP-LDA are marginal.  
- Strongest takeaway: BNBP topic model and HDP-LDA exhibit distinct characteristics rather than clear superiority.  
- Suggests a more compelling comparison against the sliced sampler [10] and truncated sampler [8] for BNBP inference.  
- Notes that uncollapsed representations support parallelization and recommends examining mixing properties relative to the presented collapsed sampler.  
- Overall assessment: an interesting contribution that meets the publication threshold; minor experimental comments were provided.
