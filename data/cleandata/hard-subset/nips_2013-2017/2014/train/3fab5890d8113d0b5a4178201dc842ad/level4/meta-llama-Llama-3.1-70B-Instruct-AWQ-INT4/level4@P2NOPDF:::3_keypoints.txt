- Derives the exchangeable partition probability function (EPPF) for the beta negative binomial process (BNBP) and develops a collapsed Gibbs sampler for the BNBP topic model.  
- Paper is well-written, reasonably clear, and technically sound.  
- The EPPF derivation is an interesting primary result and may help derive EPPFs for related models.  
- BNBP topic model shows only marginal improvements over HDP-LDA, limiting strong empirical conclusions.  
- Recommends comparing the collapsed sampler with the sliced sampler [10] and truncated sampler [8] to assess parallelization advantages and mixing tradeoffs.  
- Overall an interesting paper that passes the publication threshold.  
- Only a few minor comments on the experimental section.
