- Novel contribution: introduces a fully collapsed Gibbs sampler for the BNBP with Lemma 1, presented as a novel and compelling result.  
- Primary concern: experimental evaluation is insufficient.  
- Motivation targets algorithmic properties (simplicity, rapid convergence, good mixing, strong predictive performance), so empirical validation should compare different BNBP sampling algorithms directly.  
- Comparing only against HDP-LDA is inadequate; the paper must benchmark the proposed sampler against existing BNBP samplers to demonstrate its advantages.
