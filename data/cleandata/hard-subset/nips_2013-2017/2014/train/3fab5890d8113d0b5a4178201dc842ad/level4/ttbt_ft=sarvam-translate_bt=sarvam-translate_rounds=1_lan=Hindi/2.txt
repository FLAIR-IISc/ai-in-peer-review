This paper proposes a fully collapsed Gibbs (CG) sampler for BNP.

The theorem and derived sampler are novel and interesting.

However, my concern is regarding the experiments.

Your motivation is to create a CG sampler for BNP because its implementation is simple, convergence is fast, mixing is good, and it performs cutting-edge prediction.

That is, this work focuses on the sampling algorithm, not on statistical modeling.

Therefore, you need to compare other sampling algorithms with the proposed algorithm for BNP, not the HDP-LDA.

A fully collapsed Gibbs sampler for BNP was provided. Experimental results of other sampling algorithms for BNP are needed.
