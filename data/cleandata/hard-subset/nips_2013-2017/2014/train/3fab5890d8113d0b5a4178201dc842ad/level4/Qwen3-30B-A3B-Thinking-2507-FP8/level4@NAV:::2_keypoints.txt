- Introduces a fully collapsed Gibbs sampler for the Beta-Negative Binomial Process (BNBP).  
- Lemma 1 and the derived sampler show novelty and technical interest.  
- Experimental evaluation is inadequate and requires significant revision.  
- Paper emphasizes algorithmic benefits (simple implementation, fast convergence, good mixing, strong predictive performance) over statistical modeling.  
- Comparisons are inappropriate: must compare against other BNBP-specific samplers rather than HDP-LDA.  
- Experimental results for existing BNBP sampling methods are essential to validate the proposed approach.
