- Proposes a fully collapsed Gibbs (CG) sampler for the BNBP and derives a novel Lemma 1 — an interesting methodological contribution.
- Major concern: experimental evaluation is insufficient to support claims about simplicity, fast convergence, good mixing, and state-of-the-art predictive performance.
- Because the paper’s focus is on the sampling algorithm (not modeling), it must be compared against other existing BNBP sampling algorithms.
- Current experiments only compare to HDP-LDA, which does not directly address the sampler-focused motivation.
- Recommendation: include experimental comparisons with other BNBP samplers to demonstrate the proposed algorithm’s advantages.
