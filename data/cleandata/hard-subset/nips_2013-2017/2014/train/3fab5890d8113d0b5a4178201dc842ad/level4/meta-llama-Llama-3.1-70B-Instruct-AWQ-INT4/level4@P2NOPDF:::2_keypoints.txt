- Paper proposes a novel fully collapsed Gibbs sampler for the Beta-Negative Binomial Process (BNBP), supported by Lemma 1 and a derived sampler.  
- Main concern is the experimental evaluation rather than the theoretical contribution.  
- Motivation for the CG sampler is simple implementation, fast convergence, good mixing, and strong predictive performance.  
- Because the focus is on sampling algorithms, comparisons should be against other BNBP samplers, not HDP-LDA.  
- The paper lacks experimental results comparing the proposed CG sampler to existing BNBP sampling algorithms.  
- Including such BNBP-focused comparisons would substantially strengthen and clarify the paper’s contributions.
