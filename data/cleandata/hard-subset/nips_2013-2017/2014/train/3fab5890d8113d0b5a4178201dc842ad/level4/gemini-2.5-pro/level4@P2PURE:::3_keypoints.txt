- Derives the exchangeable partition probability function (EPPF) for the beta negative binomial process (BNBP) and builds a collapsed Gibbs sampler for the BNBP topic model.
- Paper is well-written, clear, and technically sound.
- Primary contribution: significant derivation of the BNBP EPPF for mixed-membership/count-mixture models with a methodology useful for related models.
- Demonstration on a BNBP topic model is reasonable, but experiments against HDP-LDA show only marginal improvements; results mainly show the models have distinct characteristics rather than a clear performance advantage.
- Important missing comparison: contrast the proposed collapsed sampler with existing uncollapsed BNBP inference methods (sliced sampler [10], truncated sampler [8]) to assess mixing efficiency and parallelization trade-offs.
- Overall assessment: interesting and valuable work that meets the threshold for publication; remaining comments are minor and focus on the experimental section.
