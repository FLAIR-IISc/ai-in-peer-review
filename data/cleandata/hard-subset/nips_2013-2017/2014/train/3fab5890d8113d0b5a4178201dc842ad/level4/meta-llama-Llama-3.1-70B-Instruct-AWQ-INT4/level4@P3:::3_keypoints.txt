- Novel derivation of the BNBP's EPPF for mixed-membership modeling via count-mixture models — significant and interesting contribution.  
- Derives a collapsed Gibbs sampler for the BNBP topic model using the EPPF.  
- Technical presentation and derivations are sound and sufficiently detailed.  
- Paper is well-written with a thorough introduction and contextualized related work.  
- BNBP topic model reasonably demonstrates utility, but improvements over HDP-LDA are only marginal.  
- Main concrete conclusion is that BNBP and HDP-LDA have distinct characteristics, not clear superiority.  
- Recommend experiments comparing the collapsed sampler to the sliced [10] and truncated [8] samplers to assess mixing and parallelization tradeoffs.  
- Evaluation should include more comparisons to other methods and robustness checks across parameter settings.  
- Clarity could be improved: make the introduction more concise and add more headings/subheadings in technical sections.  
- Paper is potentially significant for topic modeling and nonparametric Bayesian methods and overall meets the publication threshold with minor experimental fixes.
