

This paper introduces a fully collapsed Gibbs (CG) sampler for the Beta-Negative Binomial Process (BNBP). Lemma 1 and the derived sampler demonstrate novelty and technical interest. However, the experimental evaluation requires significant revision. The paper’s motivation centers on the CG sampler’s simple implementation, fast convergence, good mixing, and state-of-the-art predictive performance—emphasizing algorithmic contribution over statistical modeling. Consequently, the comparison must be with other sampling algorithms specifically designed for the BNBP, not HDP-LDA. The experimental results for existing BNBP sampling methods are essential to validate the proposed approach.
