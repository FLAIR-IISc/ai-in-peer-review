Paper derives the EPPF for the Beta Negative Binomial Process (BNBP) and builds a collapsed Gibbs sampler for the BNBP topic model.

Manuscript is well-written, clear, and technically sound.

Primary contribution—the EPPF derivation via a "count-mixture" representation—is interesting and valuable.

Approach could serve as a framework for deriving EPPFs for related models.

BNBP topic model is a reasonable application to demonstrate the EPPF and prediction rule.

Experimental comparison to HDP-LDA yields only marginal improvements; models exhibit distinct characteristics.

Reviewer recommends direct comparison of the proposed collapsed Gibbs sampler to uncollapsed methods (sliced sampler [10], truncated sampler [8]) for the BNBP.

Suggest examining trade-offs in mixing efficiency versus parallelization benefits between collapsed and uncollapsed representations.

Overall contribution meets the threshold for publication; suggested changes are minor and mainly concern strengthening the experimental evaluation.
