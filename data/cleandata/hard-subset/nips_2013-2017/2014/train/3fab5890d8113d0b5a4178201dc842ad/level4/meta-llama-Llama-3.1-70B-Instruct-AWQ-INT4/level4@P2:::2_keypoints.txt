- Novel derivation of the exchangeable partition probability function (EPPF) for the BNBP.  
- Proposal of a fully collapsed Gibbs sampler for the BNBP.  
- Application to topic modeling with a BNBP topic model that is distinct from existing models.  
- Model and sampler have straightforward implementation.  
- Paper is well-organized and explains the BNBP and its application clearly.  
- Experimental results report state-of-the-art predictive performance and competitiveness with HDP.  
- Main contribution is theoretical (EPPF) and the new sampler rather than broad empirical comparisons.  
- Experiments compare to HDP-LDA instead of other BNBP sampling algorithms.  
- Missing comparison to alternative BNBP samplers (e.g., blocked Gibbs).  
- Lacks experimental evidence on convergence speed, mixing, and computational complexity of the proposed sampler.  
- Authors should discuss limitations and potential future work.  
- For acceptance: novelty of EPPF and sampler, distinct/easy-to-implement model, strong predictive results.  
- Against acceptance: insufficient empirical comparison with other BNBP samplers and limited discussion of sampler limitations.
