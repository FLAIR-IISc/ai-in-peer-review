- Introduces the BNBP EPPF and uses it to construct a collapsed Gibbs sampler for a BNBP topic model.  
- Manuscript is well written, reasonably clear, and technically sound.  
- Main contribution—deriving the BNBP’s EPPF for mixed-membership via count-mixture models—is interesting and provides useful tools for related models.  
- The BNBP topic model is a reasonable demonstration, but improvements over HDP-LDA are marginal; the two models show distinct characteristics rather than clear superiority.  
- Recommend additional experiments: compare the collapsed sampler with the sliced sampler and the truncated sampler for BNBP, and evaluate mixing vs. parallelization trade-offs of uncollapsed representations.  
- Overall an interesting paper that clears the publication threshold; only minor comments on the experimental section.
