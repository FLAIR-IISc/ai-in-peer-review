- Derives an EPPF for the beta–negative binomial process (BNBP) and constructs a fully collapsed Gibbs sampler for a BNBP-based topic model.  
- Paper is well-written, fairly clear, and technically solid.  
- Main contribution: derivation of the BNBP EPPF for mixed-membership modeling via a count-mixture construction—interesting and potentially useful for related models.  
- Experiments show the BNBP topic model demonstrates the EPPF’s utility, but improvements over HDP-LDA are modest; the models exhibit different characteristics rather than clear superiority.  
- Recommends a more informative experimental comparison against the slice-based sampler [10] and truncated sampler [8] for BNBP inference.  
- Suggests examining mixing trade-offs with uncollapsed formulations, which are naturally advantageous for parallelization, relative to the presented collapsed sampler.  
- Overall assessment: interesting submission that meets the acceptance threshold; only minor experimental comments.
