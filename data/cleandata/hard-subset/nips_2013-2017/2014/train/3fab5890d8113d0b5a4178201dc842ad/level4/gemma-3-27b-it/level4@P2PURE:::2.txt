This paper presents a fully collapsed Gibbs (CG) sampler for the Bayesian Nonparametric Mixture Model with Indian Process Prior (BNBP). The lemma presented and the resulting sampler are novel and represent an interesting contribution. 

My primary concern lies with the experimental evaluation. The authors motivate the development of this CG sampler for the BNBP based on its potential for simple implementation, fast convergence, good mixing properties, and state-of-the-art predictive performance. Given this focus on the sampling algorithm itself, rather than statistical modeling advancements, the appropriate benchmark should be other sampling algorithms designed for the BNBP – not HDP-LDA. To adequately demonstrate the benefits of the proposed sampler, a comparative analysis against existing BNBP sampling methods is required; the experimental results of these alternative algorithms are currently missing.
