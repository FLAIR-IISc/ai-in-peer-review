- Paper proposes a fully collapsed Gibbs (CG) sampler for the beta-negative binomial process (BNBP).
- Lemma 1 and the resulting sampler are novel and interesting.
- Main concern: the experimental evaluation is insufficient.
- Paper’s motivation emphasizes simple implementation, fast convergence, good mixing, and state-of-the-art predictive performance.
- Contribution focuses on the sampling algorithm rather than new statistical modeling.
- Experiments should compare the proposed method to other BNBP sampling algorithms instead of HDP-LDA.
- Currently missing: experimental results for alternative BNBP inference methods.
- Reviewer requests additional comparisons to validate claimed advantages of the CG sampler.
