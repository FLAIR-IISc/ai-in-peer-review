- Presents the EPPF for the beta negative binomial process (BNBP) and uses it to derive a collapsed Gibbs sampler for a BNBP topic model.
- Paper is well-written, reasonably clear, and technically sound.
- Primary contribution—the BNBP EPPF derived via “count-mixture” models—is interesting and potentially useful for related models.
- BNBP topic model is a reasonable illustration, but improvements over HDP-LDA are only marginal; mainly shows they have distinct characteristics.
- Recommends experiments comparing the collapsed sampler to the sliced sampler [10] and the truncated sampler [8] for BNBP inference.
- Notes uncollapsed representations have natural advantages for parallelization; suggests evaluating mixing trade-offs versus the collapsed sampler.
- Overall assessment: interesting paper that meets the publication threshold, with only minor experimental comments.
