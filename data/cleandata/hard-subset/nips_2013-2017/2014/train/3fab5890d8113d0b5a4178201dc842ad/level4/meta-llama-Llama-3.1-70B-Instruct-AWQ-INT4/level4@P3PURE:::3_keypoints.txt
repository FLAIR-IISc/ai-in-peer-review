- Presents the EPPF for the beta negative binomial process (BNBP) and develops a collapsed Gibbs sampler for a BNBP topic model.
- Well-written, reasonably clear, and technically sound.
- Primary contribution: derivation of the BNBP's EPPF for mixed-membership modeling via "count-mixture" models; interesting and useful for related models.
- BNBP topic model is a reasonable illustration but shows only marginal improvements over HDP-LDA.
- Secure conclusion: BNBP topic model and HDP-LDA have distinct characteristics.
- Recommends comparing the collapsed sampler to the sliced sampler [10] and truncated sampler [8] for BNBP inference.
- Notes uncollapsed representations have advantages for parallelization; suggests studying mixing trade-offs between collapsed and uncollapsed samplers.
- Overall assessment: interesting paper that meets the publication threshold, with only minor experimental-section comments.
