- Proposes a novel Bayesian nonparametric mixed-membership model for grouped data based on the Beta-Negative Binomial Process (BNBP).
- Main theoretical contribution: derives an exchangeable partition probability function (EPPF) for the BNBP and establishes prediction rules.
- Connects BNBP to EPPF theory, helping unify EPPF analysis and Bayesian nonparametrics and potentially inspiring further work.
- Introduces randomization of group sizes (instead of treating them as fixed), enabling derivation of certain marginal distributions.
- Reviewer regards the paper as making important theoretical contributions.
- Practical utility is questioned: the proposed inference algorithm is more complex than HDP both theoretically and computationally.
- Empirical improvements over HDP are small and may not justify the added complexity.
- The paper lacks an intuitive explanation or justification for why the proposed framework should outperform HDP in practice.
- Reviewer requests the authors to elaborate on expected practical performance gains and is overall inclined to recommend acceptance.
