- Proposes a fully collapsed Gibbs (CG) sampler for the Bayesian Nonparametric Beta Process (BNBP); Lemma 1 and the derived sampler are novel and interesting contributions.

- Primary concern: the experimental evaluation is inadequate.

- Paper's motivation: CG sampler aims for simple implementation, fast convergence, good mixing, and state-of-the-art predictive performance.

- Because the focus is on the sampling algorithm, comparisons to other sampling algorithms for the BNBP are necessary.

- Experiments instead compare the proposed sampler to HDP-LDA, which is not directly relevant to the stated goals.

- Needed: experimental results comparing the proposed fully collapsed Gibbs sampler to existing BNBP sampling algorithms for a comprehensive evaluation.
