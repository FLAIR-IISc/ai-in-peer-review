- Derives the exchangeable partition probability function (EPPF) for the beta negative binomial process (BNBP) and develops a collapsed Gibbs sampler for a BNBP-based topic model.
- Paper is well-written, clear, and technically sound.
- Main contribution—the BNBP EPPF derivation via a "count-mixture" framework—is novel and useful for related models.
- BNBP topic model is a reasonable application but shows only marginal improvements over HDP-LDA.
- Experiments demonstrate BNBP and HDP-LDA have distinct behaviors but do not establish a clear advantage for the new model.
- Recommends comparing the collapsed sampler to existing uncollapsed methods (e.g., sliced sampler [10], truncated sampler [8]) to evaluate mixing speed, efficiency, and parallelization trade-offs.
- Overall assessment: interesting paper that meets the threshold for publication; experimental section could be improved as suggested.
