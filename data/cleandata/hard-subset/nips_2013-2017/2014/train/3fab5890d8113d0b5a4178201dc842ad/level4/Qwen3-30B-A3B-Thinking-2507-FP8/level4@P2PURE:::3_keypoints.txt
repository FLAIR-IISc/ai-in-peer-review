- Introduces the EPPF for the beta negative binomial process (BNBP) and develops a collapsed Gibbs sampler for the BNBP topic model.  
- Paper is well-written, clear, and technically sound.  
- Primary contribution — derivation of the BNBP EPPF via "count-mixture" models — is novel and useful for related models.  
- Empirical improvements over HDP-LDA are marginal.  
- More defensible conclusion is that BNBP topic model and HDP-LDA exhibit distinct characteristics rather than clear superiority.  
- Recommends comparing the proposed collapsed sampler to the sliced sampler [10] and the truncated sampler [8] for BNBP inference.  
- Notes uncollapsed representations facilitate parallelization; should examine mixing trade-offs relative to the collapsed sampler.  
- Overall the paper is interesting and meets the publication threshold; minor experimental-section comments remain.
