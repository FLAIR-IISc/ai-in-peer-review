- Introduces a novel fully collapsed Gibbs sampler for the Beta-Negative Binomial Process (BNBP) via Lemma 1 and its derived sampler.  
- Experimental section is problematic and raises concerns.  
- Motivation is that the CG sampler should offer simple implementation, fast convergence, better mixing, and improved predictive performance.  
- Because the paper focuses on the sampling algorithm, it should benchmark against existing BNBP sampling algorithms.  
- Comparing only to HDP-LDA is insufficient; experiments should include other BNBP samplers to properly evaluate efficacy.
