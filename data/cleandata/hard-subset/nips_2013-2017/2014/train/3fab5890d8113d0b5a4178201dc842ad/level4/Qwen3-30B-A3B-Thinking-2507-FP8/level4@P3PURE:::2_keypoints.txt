- Proposes a fully collapsed Gibbs (CG) sampler for the Bayesian nonparametric block model (BNBP).  
- Lemma 1 and the derived sampler are novel and interesting.  
- Primary concern: experimental evaluation is insufficient.  
- Authors' stated motivation: simple implementation, fast convergence, good mixing, and state-of-the-art predictive performance; emphasis is on the sampling algorithm rather than modeling.  
- Therefore, comparisons should be against other samplers developed for the BNBP, not against HDP-LDA.  
- The paper must include experimental results comparing the proposed CG sampler to existing BNBP samplers.
