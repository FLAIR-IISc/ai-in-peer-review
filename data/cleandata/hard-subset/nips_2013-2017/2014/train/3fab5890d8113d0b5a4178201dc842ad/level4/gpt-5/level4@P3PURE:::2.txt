This paper proposes a fully collapsed Gibbs (CG) sampler for the Beta-Negative Binomial Process (BNBP). Lemma 1 and the resulting sampler are novel and interesting.

My main concern is the experimental evaluation. The paper motivates the CG sampler for the BNBP on the grounds of simple implementation, fast convergence, good mixing, and state-of-the-art predictive performance. In other words, the contribution is primarily about the sampling algorithm rather than the statistical model. Therefore, the experiments should compare the proposed sampler against other sampling algorithms for the BNBP, rather than against HDP-LDA. The paper provides a fully collapsed Gibbs sampler for the BNBP, but experimental results comparing it to other BNBP inference methods are needed.
