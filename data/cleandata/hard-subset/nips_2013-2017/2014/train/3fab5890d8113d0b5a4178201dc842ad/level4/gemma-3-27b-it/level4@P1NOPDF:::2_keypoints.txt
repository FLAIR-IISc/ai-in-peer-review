- Lemma 1 and the resulting fully collapsed Gibbs (CG) sampler for the BNBP are novel and interesting contributions.
- Primary concern: experimental evaluation is insufficient to support claims about the sampler.
- Paper frames contributions as algorithmic (simple implementation, fast convergence, good mixing, strong predictive performance), not as modeling advances.
- Therefore comparisons should be against other existing sampling algorithms for the BNBP, not against HDP-LDA.
- Empirical results for alternative BNBP samplers are crucial to establish the proposed sampler’s merits.
- Providing a comparative analysis with other BNBP samplers would significantly strengthen the paper’s evaluation and claims.
