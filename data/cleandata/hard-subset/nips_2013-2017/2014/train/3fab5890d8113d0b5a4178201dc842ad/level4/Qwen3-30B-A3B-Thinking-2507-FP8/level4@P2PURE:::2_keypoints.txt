- Proposes a fully collapsed Gibbs (CG) sampler for the Beta-Negative-Binomial Process (BNBP).  
- Lemma 1 and the derived sampler are novel and interesting.  
- Main concern: experimental evaluation is insufficient.  
- Authors claim benefits (simple implementation, fast convergence, good mixing, strong predictive performance) and focus on the sampling algorithm rather than modeling.  
- Must compare the proposed CG sampler against other established BNBP sampling algorithms, not against HDP-LDA (a different model).  
- Current experiments compare to HDP-LDA, which is inappropriate; experiments should include comparisons to existing BNBP samplers.
