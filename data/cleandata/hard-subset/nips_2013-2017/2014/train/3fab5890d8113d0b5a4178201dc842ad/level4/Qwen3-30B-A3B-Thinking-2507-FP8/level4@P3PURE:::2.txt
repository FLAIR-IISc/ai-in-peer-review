

This paper proposes a fully collapsed Gibbs (CG) sampler for the Bayesian nonparametric block model (BNBP). Lemma 1 and the derived sampler are novel and interesting. However, my primary concern lies with the experimental evaluation. The authors' motivation for developing this CG sampler centers on its simple implementation, fast convergence, good mixing properties, and state-of-the-art predictive performance—emphasizing that this work focuses on the sampling algorithm, not statistical modeling. Consequently, the comparison must be with other sampling algorithms specifically designed for the BNBP, not with HDP-LDA. A fully collapsed Gibbs sampler for the BNBP is provided, but the experimental results comparing the proposed sampler with other existing BNBP samplers are required.
