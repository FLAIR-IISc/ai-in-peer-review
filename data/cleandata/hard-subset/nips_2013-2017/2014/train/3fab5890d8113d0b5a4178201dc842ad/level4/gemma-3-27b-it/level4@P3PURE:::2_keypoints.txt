- Proposes a fully collapsed Gibbs (CG) sampler for the Bayesian Nonparametric Mixture Model (BNBP).  
- Lemma 1 and the derived sampler are novel and an interesting contribution.  
- Primary concern: experimental evaluation is inadequate.  
- Authors motivate CG sampler by claimed simplicity, fast convergence, good mixing, and state-of-the-art predictive performance.  
- Framing treats the work as a sampling-methods contribution rather than a statistical-modeling advance.  
- Empirical comparisons should be against other BNBP sampling algorithms (not HDP-LDA).  
- Inclusion of experiments comparing performance to alternative BNBP samplers is crucial for proper assessment.
