

This paper proposes a fully collapsed Gibbs (CG) sampler for the Beta-Negative-Binomial Process (BNBP). Lemma 1 and the derived sampler are novel and interesting. However, my primary concern lies with the experimental evaluation. The authors' motivation for developing this CG sampler centers on its simple implementation, fast convergence, good mixing properties, and state-of-the-art predictive performance, indicating that the work focuses on the sampling algorithm rather than statistical modeling. Consequently, the authors must compare the proposed CG sampler against other established sampling algorithms for the BNBP itself, not against HDP-LDA (as HDP-LDA is a distinct model). The paper provides a fully collapsed Gibbs sampler for the BNBP, but experimental results comparing this sampler to other existing BNBP sampling algorithms are required.
