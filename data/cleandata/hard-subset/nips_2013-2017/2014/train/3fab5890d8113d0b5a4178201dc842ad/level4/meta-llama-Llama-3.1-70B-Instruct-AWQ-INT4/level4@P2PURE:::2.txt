This paper proposes a fully collapsed Gibbs (CG) sampler for the Bayesian Nonparametric Beta Process (BNBP), introducing a novel Lemma 1 and its derived sampler, which are both interesting and original contributions. However, I have concerns regarding the experimental evaluation. The primary motivation behind developing the CG sampler for the BNBP is its simplicity, fast convergence, good mixing properties, and state-of-the-art predictive performance, emphasizing the focus on the sampling algorithm rather than statistical modeling. Consequently, a comprehensive evaluation should compare the proposed algorithm to other sampling algorithms designed for the BNBP, rather than the Hierarchical Dirichlet Process Latent Dirichlet Allocation (HDP-LDA). While the paper successfully provides a fully collapsed Gibbs sampler for the BNBP, experimental results comparing it to other BNBP sampling algorithms are necessary to substantiate its effectiveness.
