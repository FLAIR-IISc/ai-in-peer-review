This paper introduces a fully collapsed Gibbs (CG) sampler for the BNBP.
Lemma 1 and the resulting sampler are novel and compelling.
However, my primary concern is the experimental evaluation.
Your motivation is to develop a CG sampler for the BNBP due to its simple implementation, rapid convergence, strong mixing, and state-of-the-art predictive performance.
That is, the emphasis here is on the sampling algorithm, not on statistical modeling.
Therefore, you should compare the proposed method against other BNBP sampling algorithms, rather than HDP-LDA.
A fully collapsed Gibbs sampler for the BNBP is presented. Experimental results comparing it to alternative BNBP samplers are needed.
