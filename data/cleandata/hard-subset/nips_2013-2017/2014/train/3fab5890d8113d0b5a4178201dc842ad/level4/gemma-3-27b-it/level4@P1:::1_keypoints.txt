- Derives an Exchangeable Partition Probability Function (EPPF) and prediction rules for the BNBP, bridging BNP and EPPF literature.  
- Randomizing group sizes enables derivation of key marginal distributions otherwise hard to obtain.  
- Significant theoretical contribution toward unifying EPPF analysis and Bayesian nonparametrics.  
- Clear, well-organized presentation of technical material.  
- Inference algorithm is more complex than HDP in both theory and implementation.  
- Empirical performance gains over HDP are modest and may not justify added complexity.  
- Lacks an intuitive or strong theoretical explanation for when/why the framework should outperform HDP.  
- Practical utility and concrete scenarios of advantage over HDP remain unclear.  
- Reviewer encourages authors to elaborate practical advantages and identify settings with substantial performance gains.  
- Overall recommendation: lean toward acceptance despite practical concerns.
