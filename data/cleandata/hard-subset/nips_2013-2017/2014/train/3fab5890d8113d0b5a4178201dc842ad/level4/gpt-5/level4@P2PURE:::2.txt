This paper proposes a fully collapsed Gibbs (CG) sampler for the BNBP. Lemma 1 and the resulting sampler are novel and interesting. 

My main concern is the experimental evaluation. Your stated motivation is to construct a CG sampler for the BNBP because of its simple implementation, fast convergence, good mixing, and state-of-the-art predictive performance. In other words, the contribution is an inference/sampling algorithm rather than a new statistical model. 

Therefore, the experiments should compare the proposed sampler against other sampling algorithms for the BNBP, not against HDP-LDA. While you provide a fully collapsed Gibbs sampler for the BNBP, experimental results for other BNBP sampling algorithms are needed.
