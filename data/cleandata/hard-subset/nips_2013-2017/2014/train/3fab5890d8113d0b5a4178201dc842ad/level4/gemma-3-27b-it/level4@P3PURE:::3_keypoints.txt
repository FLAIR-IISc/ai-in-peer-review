- Well-written, clear, and technically sound paper.  
- Primary contribution: derivation of the beta negative binomial process (BNBP) EPPF for mixed-membership modeling via “count-mixture” models.  
- The EPPF result is interesting and potentially useful for deriving EPPFs for related models.  
- Demonstrated utility via a collapsed Gibbs sampler for a BNBP topic model.  
- Empirical improvements over HDP-LDA are marginal; main firm conclusion is that BNBP and HDP-LDA have distinct characteristics.  
- Experimental section should more compellingly compare the collapsed sampler to the sliced sampler [10] and the truncated sampler [8] for BNBP inference.  
- Note: uncollapsed representations offer advantages for parallelization; examining mixing tradeoffs between collapsed and uncollapsed samplers would be valuable.  
- Overall assessment: interesting paper that meets the publication threshold, with suggested experimental expansions.
