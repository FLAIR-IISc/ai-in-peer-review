- Proposes a fully collapsed Gibbs (CG) sampler for the Bayesian Nonparametric Beta Process (BNBP).  
- Introduces a novel Lemma 1 and a derived sampler, both flagged as interesting and original contributions.  
- Primary motivation is algorithmic: simplicity, fast convergence, good mixing, and state-of-the-art predictive performance.  
- Emphasizes focus on sampling algorithm rather than statistical modeling.  
- Major concern: experimental evaluation is insufficient.  
- Argues comparisons should be made against other BNBP sampling algorithms, not HDP-LDA.  
- Calls for experiments comparing the proposed CG sampler to existing BNBP samplers to substantiate effectiveness.
