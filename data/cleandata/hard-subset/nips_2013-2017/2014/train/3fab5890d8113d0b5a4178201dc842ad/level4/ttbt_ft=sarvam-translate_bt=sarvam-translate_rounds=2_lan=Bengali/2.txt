This paper proposes a fully collapsed Gibbs (CG) sampler for BNNP.

The proof and the resulting sampler are novel and interesting.

However, my concern is the tests.

Your objective is to create a CG sampler for BNNP because it has easy implementation, fast convergence, good mixing, and improved predictive performance.

That is, this work focuses on the sampling algorithm rather than the statistical modeling.

Therefore, you need to compare the proposed algorithm for BNNP with other sampling algorithms, not with HDP-LDA.

A fully collapsed Gibbs sampler for BNNP has been provided. Experimental results of other sampling algorithms for BNNP are needed.
