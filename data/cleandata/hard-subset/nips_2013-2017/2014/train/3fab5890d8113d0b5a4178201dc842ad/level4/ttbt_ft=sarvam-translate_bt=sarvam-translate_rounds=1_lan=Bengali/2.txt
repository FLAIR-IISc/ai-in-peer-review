This paper proposes a fully collapsed Gibbs (CG) sampler for BNNP.

The theorem 1 and the resulting sampler are new and interesting.

However, my concern is the experiments.

Your objective is to create a CG sampler for BNNP because it has simple implementation, fast convergence, good mixing, and advanced predictive performance.

That is, this work focuses on sampling algorithms rather than statistical modeling.

Therefore, you need to compare the proposed algorithm for BNNP with other sampling algorithms, not with HDP-LDA.

A fully collapsed Gibbs sampler for BNNP is provided. Experimental results of other sampling algorithms for BNNP are needed.
