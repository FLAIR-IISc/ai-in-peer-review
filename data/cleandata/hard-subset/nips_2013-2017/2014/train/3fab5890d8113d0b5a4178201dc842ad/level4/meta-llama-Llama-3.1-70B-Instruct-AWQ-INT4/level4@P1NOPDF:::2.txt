This paper proposes a fully collapsed Gibbs (CG) sampler for the Bayesian Nonparametric Bayesian Mixture (BNBP) model. The authors derive a novel Lemma 1 and develop a corresponding sampler, which is an interesting contribution.

However, my main concern lies in the experimental evaluation. The authors' motivation for constructing a CG sampler for the BNBP is its potential for simple implementation, fast convergence, good mixing properties, and state-of-the-art predictive performance. Given this focus on the sampling algorithm rather than statistical modeling, I believe it is essential to compare the proposed algorithm with other existing sampling algorithms for the BNBP.

Instead, the experiments compare the proposed sampler with the Hierarchical Dirichlet Process Latent Dirichlet Allocation (HDP-LDA) model. While this comparison may provide some insight, it does not directly address the authors' primary motivation for developing the CG sampler. To demonstrate the effectiveness of the proposed algorithm, experimental results comparing it with other BNBP sampling algorithms are necessary.
