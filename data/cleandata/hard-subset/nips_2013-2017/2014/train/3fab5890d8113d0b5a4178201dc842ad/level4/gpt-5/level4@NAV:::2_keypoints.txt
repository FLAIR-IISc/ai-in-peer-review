- Introduces a fully collapsed Gibbs (CG) sampler for the BNBP.  
- Lemma 1 and the resulting sampler are novel and compelling.  
- Primary concern: the experimental evaluation is inadequate.  
- Paper’s motivation focuses on sampler properties (simple implementation, rapid convergence, strong mixing, state-of-the-art predictive performance), i.e., algorithmic contribution rather than modeling.  
- Evaluation should compare against other BNBP sampling algorithms rather than HDP-LDA.  
- Experimental results directly comparing to alternative BNBP samplers are required.
