This paper proposes a fully collapsed Gibbs (CG) sampler for the Bayesian Nonparametric Mixture Model (BNBP). Lemma 1 and the derived sampler are novel and represent an interesting contribution. 

However, my primary concern lies with the experimental evaluation. The authors motivate the development of this CG sampler for the BNBP based on its anticipated simplicity, fast convergence, good mixing properties, and state-of-the-art predictive performance. This framing positions the work as a contribution to sampling methodology, rather than statistical modeling. Consequently, the empirical validation should focus on comparing the proposed CG sampler against *other* sampling algorithms designed for the BNBP, rather than HDP-LDA. A fully collapsed Gibbs sampler for the BNBP has been presented; the inclusion of experimental results demonstrating performance relative to alternative BNBP sampling methods is crucial for a comprehensive assessment of this work.
