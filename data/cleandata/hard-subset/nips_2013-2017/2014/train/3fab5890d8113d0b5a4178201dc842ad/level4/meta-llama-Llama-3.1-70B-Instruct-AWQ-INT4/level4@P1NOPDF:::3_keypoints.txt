- Derives the exchangeable partition probability function (EPPF) for the beta negative binomial process (BNBP) and builds a collapsed Gibbs sampler for a BNBP topic model.  
- The paper is well-written, reasonably clear, and technically sound.  
- The primary EPPF derivation is interesting and offers useful tools for deriving EPPFs for related models.  
- The BNBP topic model demonstrates utility but yields only marginal improvements over HDP-LDA.  
- The clear takeaway is that BNBP and HDP-LDA have distinct characteristics rather than large performance differences.  
- Recommend experimentally comparing the collapsed sampler to the sliced sampler [10] and the truncated sampler [8] for BNBP inference.  
- Note that uncollapsed representations naturally favor parallelization; suggest examining mixing vs. parallelization trade-offs for the collapsed sampler.  
- Overall assessment: interesting work that passes the publication threshold, with only minor experimental-section comments.
