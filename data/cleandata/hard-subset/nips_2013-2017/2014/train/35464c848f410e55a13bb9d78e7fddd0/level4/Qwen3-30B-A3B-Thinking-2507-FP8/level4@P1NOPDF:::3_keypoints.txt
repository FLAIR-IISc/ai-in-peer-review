- Proposal: Introduces a data-dependent "Fredholm kernel" that integrates unlabeled data in a principled way.  
- Theory: Establishes several theoretical properties of the Fredholm kernel.  
- Empirics: Demonstrates competitive performance against state-of-the-art methods.  
- Presentation: Manuscript is well-written and clearly organized.  
- Hyperparameter concern: No systematic approach given for selecting the kernel width; ask if cross-validation under standard assumptions (e.g., smoothness) could determine it.  
- Failure modes: Suggests including toy examples showing scenarios where the method underperforms to characterize its limitations.  
- Recommendation: Paper is compelling with strong theoretical and empirical support and is recommended for acceptance.
