- Proposes a data-dependent "Fredholm kernel" that naturally incorporates unlabeled data.  
- Novelty lies in using unlabeled data within the kernel.  
- Establishes several theoretical properties of the proposed kernel.  
- Experimental results show favorable performance versus state-of-the-art methods.  
- Paper is well written and well organized.  
- Asks whether there is a systematic way to select the Fredholm kernel width (e.g., via cross-validation under certain assumptions).  
- Suggests adding toy experiments demonstrating failure cases to clarify effective settings.  
- Reviewer finds the paper interesting, very clear, and recommends acceptance.
