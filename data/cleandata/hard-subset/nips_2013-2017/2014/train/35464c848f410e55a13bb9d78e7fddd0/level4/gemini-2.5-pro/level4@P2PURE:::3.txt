The authors propose a data-dependent kernel, termed the Fredholm kernel. The primary novelty lies in its principled approach to incorporating unlabeled data. The paper establishes several theoretical properties of this kernel and provides experimental verification that it compares favorably with state-of-the-art methods.

The paper is well-written and organized.

Detailed comments:
1. The paper would benefit from a discussion on how to select the kernel width for the Fredholm kernel. Is there a systematic approach? For example, can cross-validation be used for this purpose, perhaps under specific assumptions?
2. To better characterize the method, it would be helpful to include toy experiments illustrating its limitations or potential failure cases. This would provide valuable insight into the settings where the proposed method is most effective.

The paper is interesting and very clearly written. I recommend acceptance.
