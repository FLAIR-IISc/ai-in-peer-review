- Proposes a data-dependent "Fredholm" kernel that naturally leverages unlabeled data.  
- Novelty: kernel effectively utilizes unlabeled datasets.  
- Demonstrates several theoretical properties of the Fredholm kernel.  
- Empirically competitive with existing state-of-the-art methods.  
- Paper is well-written and well-organized.

- Overall: interesting, clearly communicated work; reviewer votes for acceptance.
