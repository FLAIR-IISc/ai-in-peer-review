This approach fits naturally into the kernel framework and can be interpreted as constructing new data-dependent kernels, which they call Fredholm kernels. They demonstrate that methods based on Fredholm learning show competitive performance in the standard semi-supervised learning setting.

The authors relate their ideas to previous work at NIPS and elsewhere, including kernel methods and semi-supervised learning techniques.
