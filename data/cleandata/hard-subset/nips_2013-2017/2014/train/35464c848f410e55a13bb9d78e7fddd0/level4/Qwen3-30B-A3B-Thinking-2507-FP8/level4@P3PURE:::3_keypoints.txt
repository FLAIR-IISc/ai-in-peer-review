- Proposes a data-dependent "Fredholm kernel" that naturally incorporates unlabeled data.  
- Establishes several theoretical properties of the proposed kernel.  
- Experimental results show the method compares favorably with state-of-the-art approaches.  
- Paper is well-written and well-organized.  
- Does not provide a systematic approach for selecting the kernel width; unclear under what assumptions cross-validation is valid.  
- Suggests adding toy experiments illustrating failure scenarios to clarify when the method is effective.  
- Reviewer recommends acceptance.
