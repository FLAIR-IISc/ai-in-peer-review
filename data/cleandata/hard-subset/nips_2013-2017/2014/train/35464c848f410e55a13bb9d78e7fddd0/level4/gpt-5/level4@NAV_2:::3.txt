The paper introduces a data-dependent kernel, called the Fredholm kernel, whose key novelty is the natural incorporation of unlabeled data. The authors establish several theoretical properties of this kernel and empirically demonstrate that it performs competitively with existing state-of-the-art methods.

The manuscript is clearly written and well organized.

Detailed comments:
1. Is there a principled procedure for selecting the kernel width for the Fredholm kernel? For instance, under certain assumptions, can this be chosen via cross-validation?
2. It would be helpful to include simple toy cases where the proposed method does not work well. I would like to understand the settings in which the approach is most effective.

The paper is interesting and very clearly presented. I vote for acceptance.
