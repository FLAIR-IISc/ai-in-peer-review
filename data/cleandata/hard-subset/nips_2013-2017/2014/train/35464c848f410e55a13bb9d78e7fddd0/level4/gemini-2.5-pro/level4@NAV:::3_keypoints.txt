- Introduces a data-dependent "Fredholm kernel" with a principled use of unlabeled data.  
- Authors derive several theoretical properties of the Fredholm kernel.  
- Empirical results show competitive performance with state-of-the-art methods.  
- Manuscript is well-structured and clearly written.  
- Request for a systematic methodology to select the Fredholm kernel width, e.g., conditions under which cross-validation is effective.  
- Suggestion to add an experiment showing scenarios where the method underperforms to clarify its limitations.  
- Overall assessment: compelling paper; recommend acceptance.
