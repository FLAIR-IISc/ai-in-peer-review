- Proposes a data-dependent "Fredholm kernel" that naturally incorporates unlabeled data.  
- Novelty lies in integrating an unlabeled dataset into the kernel design.  
- The paper establishes several theoretical properties of the proposed kernel.  
- Empirical results show the method compares favorably with state-of-the-art approaches.  
- The manuscript is well-written and well-organized.  
- Raises concern about lack of a systematic method for selecting kernel width (e.g., when/how cross-validation applies).  
- Recommends adding toy/illustrative experiments to demonstrate scenarios where the method may fail.  
- Overall assessment: interesting, clearly written, and recommended for acceptance.
