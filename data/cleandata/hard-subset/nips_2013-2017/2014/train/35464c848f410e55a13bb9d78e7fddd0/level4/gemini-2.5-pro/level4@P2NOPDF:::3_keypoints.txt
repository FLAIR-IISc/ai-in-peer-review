- Introduces Fredholm kernels, a novel class of data-dependent kernels that leverage unlabeled data.
- Main novelty: natural integration of an unlabeled dataset into the kernel construction.
- Provides theoretical analysis of the kernel’s properties.
- Experimental results demonstrate strong performance compared to state-of-the-art.
- Paper is well-written, clearly organized, and easy to follow.
- Practical concern: unclear how to select the Fredholm kernel width; need guidance on hyperparameter tuning (e.g., cross-validation).
- Suggestion: include experiments on limitations/failure modes (synthetic/toy datasets) to better characterize when the method fails.
- Recommendation: accept.
