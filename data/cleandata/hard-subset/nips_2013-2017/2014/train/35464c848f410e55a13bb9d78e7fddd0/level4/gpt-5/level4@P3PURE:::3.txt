Summary:
The authors propose a data-dependent kernel, termed Fredholm kernels, whose key novelty is the natural incorporation of unlabeled data. They establish several theoretical properties of the kernel and present experiments showing that it compares favorably with existing state-of-the-art methods. The paper is clearly written and well organized.

Detailed comments:
1) Is there a systematic approach to selecting the kernel width for the Fredholm kernel? For example, under certain assumptions, can the kernel width be reliably chosen via cross-validation?
2) It would be helpful to include toy experiments that illustrate cases where the proposed method may not work, in order to better understand the settings where it is most effective.

Overall assessment:
The paper is interesting and clearly written. I recommend acceptance.
