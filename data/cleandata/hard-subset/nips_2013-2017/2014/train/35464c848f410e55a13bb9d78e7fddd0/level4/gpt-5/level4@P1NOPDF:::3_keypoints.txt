- Proposes a data-dependent "Fredholm kernel" with the key novelty of naturally incorporating unlabeled data.
- Establishes several theoretical properties of the proposed kernel.
- Provides experiments showing favorable performance compared to existing state-of-the-art methods.
- Paper is well written and well organized.
- Question about whether there is a systematic approach to selecting the Fredholm kernel width (e.g., via cross-validation under some assumptions).
- Suggestion to include toy experiments illustrating cases where the method may fail to clarify effective settings.
- Overall assessment: interesting, clearly written, recommended for acceptance.
