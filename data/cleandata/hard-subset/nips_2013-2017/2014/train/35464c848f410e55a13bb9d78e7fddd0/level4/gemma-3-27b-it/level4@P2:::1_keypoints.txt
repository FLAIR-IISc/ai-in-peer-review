- Minor copyedits: Fix typos—Line 82 “will a”→“will be a”, Line 83 missing conjunction, Section 4 “Fredhom”→“Fredholm”, Line 247 “porportional”→“proportional”, Line 283 “That is, or…”→“That is, for…”, and rewrite awkward sentence at Line 352.  
- Originality: Method is original, builds meaningfully on prior work, and is distinct from recent NIPS (2013–2017) contributions.  
- Significance: Results are significant and impactful—effective use of unlabeled data, strong empirical gains, and valuable theoretical insights for semi-supervised learning.  
- Overall assessment: Well-written, technically sound, and a valuable contribution; addressing Equation 3 and parameter selection details would strengthen the manuscript.  
- Arguments for acceptance: Novel, theoretically grounded approach; strong experimental results; clear presentation; potential for high impact.
