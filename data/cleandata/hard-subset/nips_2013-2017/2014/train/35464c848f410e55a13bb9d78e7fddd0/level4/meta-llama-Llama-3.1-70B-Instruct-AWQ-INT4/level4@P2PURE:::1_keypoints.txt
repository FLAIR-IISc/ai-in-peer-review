- Proposes a semi-supervised learning framework using Fredholm kernels, leading to a discretized Fredholm integral equation.  
- High technical quality with solid theoretical analysis and convincing experimental results.  
- Introduces a new data-dependent kernel that incorporates unlabeled data; resulting classifier differs from the classical representer-theorem solution but is still elegant and computationally simple.  
- Concern: Equation 3’s validity when the associated kernel is not PSD is unclear—request a detailed derivation/proof.  
- Concern: Experimental protocol unclear—how "optimal" parameters were chosen for all methods and the definition of "optimal" are not explained.  
- Generally well-written and organized, with main ideas clearly explained.  
- Minor clarity/typo issues throughout (e.g., Lines 82, 83, Section 4 title, 247, 283, 352) that need correction.  
- Originality: Novel contribution though related to a recent NIPS paper; differences from prior work are clearly explained.  
- Significance: Results are important and significant; method validated across multiple datasets with a solid theoretical foundation and convincing experiments.
