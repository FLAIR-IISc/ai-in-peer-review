- Proposes Fredholm kernels: a data-dependent kernel method that naturally leverages unlabeled data.  
- Demonstrates several theoretical properties of the proposed kernel.  
- Empirically shows competitive performance against existing state-of-the-art methods.  
- Paper is well-written and well-organized.  
- Raises question about a systematic approach for selecting the Fredholm kernel width (e.g., cross-validation under assumptions).  
- Suggests adding toy experiments showing scenarios where the method may not perform well to clarify limitations.  
- Reviewer recommends acceptance.
