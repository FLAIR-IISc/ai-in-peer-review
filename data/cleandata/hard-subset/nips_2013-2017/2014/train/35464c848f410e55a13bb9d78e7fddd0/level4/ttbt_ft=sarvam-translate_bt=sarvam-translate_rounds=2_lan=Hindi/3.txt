The authors proposed a data-dependent kernel called the Fredholm kernel. The kernel's novelty lies in its natural use for unlabeled datasets. The authors demonstrated several theoretical properties of the kernel and experimentally verified that the kernel compares favorably with existing state-of-the-art methods.

This paper is well-written and organized.

Detailed comments:

1. Is there a systematic approach to selecting the kernel width for the Fredholm kernel? For example, under certain assumptions, can you select the kernel width by cross-validating?

2. It would be good to add some test experiments to show that the proposed method may not work. I just want to know in what setting the proposed method is most effective.

This paper is interesting and very clearly written. I vote for acceptance.
