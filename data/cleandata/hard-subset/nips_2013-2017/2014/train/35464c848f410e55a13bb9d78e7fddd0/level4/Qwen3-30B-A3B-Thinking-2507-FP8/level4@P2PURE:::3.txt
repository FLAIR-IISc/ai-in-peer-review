

The authors propose a data-dependent kernel named Fredholm kernels, whose novelty lies in naturally incorporating unlabeled datasets. The authors establish several theoretical properties of the kernel and experimentally demonstrate that it compares favorably against existing state-of-the-art methods. The paper is well written and well organized.  

Detailed comments:  
1. Is there a systematic method for selecting the kernel width for Fredholm kernels? For instance, under specific assumptions, could kernel width be determined via cross-validation?  
2. It would be beneficial to include toy experiments illustrating scenarios where the proposed method may not perform well. This would clarify the settings in which the method is most effective.  

The paper is interesting and clearly written. I recommend acceptance.
