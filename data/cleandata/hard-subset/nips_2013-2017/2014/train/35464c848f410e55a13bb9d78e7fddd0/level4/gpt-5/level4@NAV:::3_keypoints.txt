- Proposes a data-dependent "Fredholm" kernel that naturally leverages unlabeled data.  
- Novelty lies in the use of unlabeled data within the kernel design.  
- Establishes several theoretical properties of the proposed kernel.  
- Empirical results show competitive or superior performance vs. state-of-the-art methods.  
- Paper is well written and well organized.  
- Question: lacks a systematic approach for selecting the Fredholm kernel width (e.g., via cross-validation under certain assumptions).  
- Suggestion: include toy experiments illustrating scenarios where the method may fail or underperform to clarify its effective settings.  
- Overall recommendation: interesting paper, clear presentation, vote for acceptance.
