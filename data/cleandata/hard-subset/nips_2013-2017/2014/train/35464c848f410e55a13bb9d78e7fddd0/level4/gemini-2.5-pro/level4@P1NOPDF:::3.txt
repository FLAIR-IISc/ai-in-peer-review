This paper introduces Fredholm kernels, a novel data-dependent kernel designed to naturally incorporate unlabeled data. The authors establish several key theoretical properties for this kernel and provide empirical validation demonstrating that their approach compares favorably with state-of-the-art methods.

The manuscript is well-written and clearly organized.

**Detailed Comments:**

1.  The paper would benefit from a discussion on how to systematically select the kernel width for the Fredholm kernel. For example, it would be helpful to know if cross-validation is a viable strategy for this purpose and what assumptions, if any, this might require.

2.  To better understand the limitations and strengths of the proposed method, it would be beneficial to include a toy experiment. Specifically, an example illustrating a scenario where the Fredholm kernel might not perform well would be very insightful. This would help to clarify the settings in which the proposed method is most effective.

Overall, this is an interesting and clearly presented paper. I recommend acceptance.
