The authors propose a data-dependent kernel termed Fredholm kernels. The novelty lies in leveraging unlabeled data in a natural way. The authors establish several theoretical properties of the kernel and empirically demonstrate that it compares favorably with state-of-the-art methods.

The paper is well written and well organized.

Detailed comments:
1. Is there a systematic approach to selecting the kernel width for the Fredholm kernel? For example, under certain assumptions, can the kernel width be chosen via cross-validation?
2. It would be helpful to include some toy experiments that illustrate cases where the proposed method may not perform well. I would like to understand the settings in which the method is most effective.

The paper is interesting and very clearly written. I vote for acceptance.
