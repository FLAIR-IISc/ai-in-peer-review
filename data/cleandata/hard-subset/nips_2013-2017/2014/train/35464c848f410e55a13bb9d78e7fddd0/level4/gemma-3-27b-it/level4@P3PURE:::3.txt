The authors propose a data-dependent kernel, termed Fredholm kernels, with the key novelty being its natural incorporation of unlabeled data. They demonstrate several theoretical properties of this kernel and experimentally verify its performance, showing favorable comparisons with existing state-of-the-art methods.

The paper is well written and organized.

Detailed comments:

1. Is there a systematic approach for selecting the kernel width for the Fredholm kernel? For example, can a kernel width be selected via cross-validation under certain assumptions?
2. It would be beneficial to include some toy experiments illustrating scenarios where the proposed method might *not* perform well. Understanding in which settings the method is most effective would strengthen the paper.

The paper is interesting and very clearly written. I vote to accept.
