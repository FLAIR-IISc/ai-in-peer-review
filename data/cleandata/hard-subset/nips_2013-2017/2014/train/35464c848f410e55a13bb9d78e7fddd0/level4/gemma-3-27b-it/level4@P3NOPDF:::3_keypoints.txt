- Introduces a novel data-dependent Fredholm kernel that effectively leverages unlabeled data.  
- Naturally incorporates the unlabeled dataset into the kernel construction.  
- Provides compelling theoretical analysis demonstrating several desirable kernel properties.  
- Experimental results are competitive with, and often exceed, state-of-the-art methods.  
- Paper is exceptionally well-written and clearly organized.  
- Overall assessment: strong contribution with significant potential impact; recommend acceptance.  
- Practical concern: kernel width selection needs discussion—provide strategies (grid search/CV) and, if possible, theoretical justification or rules for tuning.  
- Suggestion: include analysis/toy experiments on limitations and failure modes to show datasets or settings where the method helps least or assumptions break.
