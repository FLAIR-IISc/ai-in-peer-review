

The authors introduce a data-dependent kernel termed Fredholm kernels, with novelty centered on naturally incorporating unlabeled datasets. They establish several theoretical properties of the kernel and empirically demonstrate its competitive performance against state-of-the-art methods.  
The paper is well-written and logically structured.  
Detailed comments:  
1. Is there a systematic methodology for selecting the kernel width in Fredholm kernels? For instance, could kernel width be determined via cross-validation under specific assumptions?  
2. Including toy experiments illustrating scenarios where the proposed method may underperform would be beneficial. This would clarify the method’s effective application domains.  
The paper presents an interesting contribution with clear exposition. I recommend acceptance.
