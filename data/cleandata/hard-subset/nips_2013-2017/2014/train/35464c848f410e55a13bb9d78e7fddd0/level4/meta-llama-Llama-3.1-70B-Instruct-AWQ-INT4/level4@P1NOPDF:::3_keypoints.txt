- Proposes a data-dependent "Fredholm" kernel that naturally leverages unlabeled data.  
- Novelty claims focus on the kernel's ability to incorporate unlabeled data.  
- Several theoretical properties of the Fredholm kernel are demonstrated.  
- Experiments show the kernel compares favorably with current state-of-the-art methods.  
- Paper is well-written and well-organized, making arguments easy to follow.  
- Question: lacks a systematic approach for selecting the Fredholm kernel width (e.g., role of cross-validation).  
- Suggestion: add toy experiments showing scenarios where the method fails or underperforms to clarify limitations.  
- Overall assessment: interesting, clearly written work; reviewer votes for acceptance.
