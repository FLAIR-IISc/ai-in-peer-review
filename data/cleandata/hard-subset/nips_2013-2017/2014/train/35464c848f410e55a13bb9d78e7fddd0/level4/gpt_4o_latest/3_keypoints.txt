- Introduces a data-dependent "Fredholm" kernel that naturally leverages unlabeled data.  
- Demonstrates several theoretical properties of the proposed kernel.  
- Experimental results show competitive performance against state-of-the-art methods.  
- Paper is well-written and well-structured.  
- Asks whether there is a systematic method to select the Fredholm kernel width (e.g., via cross-validation under certain assumptions).  
- Recommends adding toy experiments showing failure scenarios to clarify where the method is most effective.  
- Overall recommendation: accept.
