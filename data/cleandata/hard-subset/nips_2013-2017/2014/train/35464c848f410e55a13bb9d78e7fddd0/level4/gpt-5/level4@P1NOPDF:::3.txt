The authors propose a data-dependent kernel, termed Fredholm kernels. The key novelty is the natural incorporation of unlabeled data. The authors establish several theoretical properties of the kernel and provide experiments showing that it compares favorably with existing state-of-the-art methods. The paper is well written and well organized.

Detailed comments:
1. Is there a systematic approach to selecting the kernel width for the Fredholm kernel? For example, under some assumptions, can the kernel width be selected via cross-validation?
2. It would be helpful to include some toy experiments that illustrate cases where the proposed method may not work well. This would clarify the settings in which the method is most effective.

Overall, the paper is interesting and clearly written. I recommend acceptance.
