- Novel framework: introduces a semi-supervised learning formulation using discretized Fredholm integral equations to incorporate unlabeled data.  
- Technical quality: strong theoretical support and convincing experiments.  
- Data-dependent kernel: method yields a data-dependent kernel integrating unlabeled information, producing an elegant and computationally straightforward classifier that differs from the standard representer-theorem solution.  
- Equation 3 concern: reviewer asks whether Equation 3 remains valid when the associated kernel is not PSD and requests a derivation/proof.  
- Experimental details question: unclear how the single "optimal" parameter for all benchmarks was chosen and what "optimal" precisely means.  
- Clarity: paper is well-written and organized, but contains minor typos and a few sentences needing revision.  
- Originality: builds on recent work (e.g., [9]) but the core contribution is novel and distinctions are clearly articulated.  
- Significance: results are significant, supported by extensive evaluation across diverse datasets and solid theoretical foundations.  
- Overall: well-crafted paper with minor technical/editorial issues that are easily addressable.
