The authors propose a data-dependent kernel, termed Fredholm kernels. The novelty of this kernel lies in its natural incorporation of unlabeled data. The authors demonstrate several theoretical properties of the kernel and experimentally verify that it performs favorably compared to existing state-of-the-art methods.

The paper is well-written and organized.

Detailed comments:

1. Is there a systematic approach to selecting the kernel width for the Fredholm kernel? For instance, can a kernel width be selected via cross-validation under certain assumptions?

2. It would be beneficial to include some toy experiments illustrating scenarios where the proposed method might *not* perform well. I am interested in understanding in which settings this method is most effective. 

The paper is interesting and very clearly written. I vote to accept.
