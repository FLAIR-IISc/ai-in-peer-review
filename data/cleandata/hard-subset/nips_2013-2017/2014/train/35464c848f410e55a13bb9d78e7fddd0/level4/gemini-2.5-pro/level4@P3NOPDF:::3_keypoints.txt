- Introduces the Fredholm kernel, a novel data-dependent kernel that leverages unlabeled data for semi-supervised learning.  
- Provides thorough theoretical analysis establishing key properties of the kernel.  
- Demonstrates strong empirical performance against state-of-the-art baselines across multiple datasets.  
- Paper is clearly written and well organized.  
- Request for clarification on hyperparameter selection (e.g., kernel width) and whether systematic tuning (e.g., cross-validation) is justified.  
- Suggestion to analyze limitations and failure modes, possibly via a toy experiment showing scenarios where the kernel underperforms.  
- Overall recommendation: high-quality paper, recommend acceptance.
