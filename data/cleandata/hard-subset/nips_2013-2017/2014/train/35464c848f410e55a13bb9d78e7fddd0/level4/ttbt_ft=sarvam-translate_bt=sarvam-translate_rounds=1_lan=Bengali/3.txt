The authors have proposed a data-dependent kernel called the Fredholm kernel. The novelty of this kernel lies in its use of unlabeled datasets in a natural way. The authors have demonstrated several theoretical properties of the kernel and experimentally proven that the kernel is comparatively better than current state-of-the-art methods.

The research paper is well-written and organized.

Detailed comments:

1. Is there a systematic method for selecting the kernel width for the Fredholm kernel? For example, under certain assumptions, can you select the kernel width through cross-validation?

2. It would be good to add some small tests to show situations where the proposed method might not work. I just want to know under what circumstances the proposed method is most effective.

The research paper is interesting and very clearly written. I vote in favor of accepting it.
