- Proposes a data-dependent "Fredholm kernel" that naturally incorporates unlabeled data.  
- Establishes several theoretical properties of the proposed kernel.  
- Experiments show the method compares favorably with state-of-the-art approaches.  
- Paper is clearly written and well organized.  
- Raises question about systematic selection of kernel width (e.g., reliable cross-validation under assumptions).  
- Suggests adding toy experiments to illustrate scenarios where the method may fail.  
- Overall recommendation: interesting work and recommended for acceptance.
