The authors propose a data-dependent kernel, termed the Fredholm kernel. The novelty of this approach lies in its ability to naturally incorporate an unlabeled dataset. The authors establish several theoretical properties of the kernel and provide experimental verification showing that it compares favorably with state-of-the-art methods.

The paper is well-written and organized.

Detailed Comments:
1.  Is there a systematic approach for selecting the kernel width for the Fredholm kernel? For instance, under what assumptions, if any, could a parameter like the kernel width be selected via cross-validation?

2.  The paper would be strengthened by the inclusion of illustrative or "toy" experiments that demonstrate scenarios where the proposed method might not work. This would provide valuable insight into the settings where the method is most effective.

The paper is interesting and very clearly written. I recommend acceptance.
