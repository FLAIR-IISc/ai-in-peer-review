

The authors propose a data-dependent kernel termed the Fredholm kernel, whose novelty lies in naturally incorporating unlabeled data. The paper establishes several theoretical properties of the kernel and experimentally demonstrates that it compares favorably with state-of-the-art methods. The paper is well-written and well-organized.  

Detailed comments:  
1.  The paper does not specify a systematic approach for selecting the kernel width for the Fredholm kernel. Under what assumptions (if any) can kernel width be selected via cross-validation?  
2.  It would be valuable to include toy experiments illustrating scenarios where the proposed method may fail. This would clarify the specific settings in which the method is most effective.  

The paper presents an interesting contribution with clear exposition. I recommend acceptance.
