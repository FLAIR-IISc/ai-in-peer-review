The authors propose a data-dependent kernel, termed the Fredholm kernel. Its novelty lies in the natural use of unlabeled data. The authors establish several theoretical properties of the kernel and provide experimental evidence that it performs favorably compared to current state-of-the-art methods. The paper is well written and well organized.

Detailed comments:
1. Is there a systematic approach to selecting the kernel width for the Fredholm kernel? For example, under certain assumptions, can it be chosen via cross-validation?
2. It would be helpful to include toy experiments that illustrate cases where the proposed method may not work, to clarify the settings in which it is most effective.

The paper is interesting and very clearly written. I vote for acceptance.
