This paper introduces Fredholm kernels, a data-dependent kernel method with the key innovation of effectively leveraging unlabeled data. The authors provide both theoretical justification for the kernel's properties and empirical evidence demonstrating competitive performance against existing state-of-the-art techniques. 

The manuscript is well-structured and clearly presented.

Specific comments for the authors:

1. The paper would benefit from a discussion of systematic kernel width selection for the Fredholm kernel. Could a method, such as cross-validation under specific assumptions, be employed to determine an appropriate kernel width?
2. Including some small-scale experiments illustrating scenarios where the proposed method *might* underperform would strengthen the work. Understanding the contexts in which the method is most effective would be valuable.

Overall, this is an interesting and well-written contribution. I recommend acceptance.
