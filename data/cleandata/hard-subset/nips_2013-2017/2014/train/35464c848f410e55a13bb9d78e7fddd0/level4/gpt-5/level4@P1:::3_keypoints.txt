- Proposes a data-dependent "Fredholm kernel" that naturally incorporates unlabeled data.  
- Presents several theoretical properties of the kernel.  
- Empirical results show the method compares favorably with state-of-the-art.  
- Paper is well written and well organized.  
- Asks for a systematic approach to selecting the kernel width (e.g., cross-validation under assumptions).  
- Suggests adding toy experiments showing failure cases to clarify when the method may not work.  
- Overall recommendation: accept.
