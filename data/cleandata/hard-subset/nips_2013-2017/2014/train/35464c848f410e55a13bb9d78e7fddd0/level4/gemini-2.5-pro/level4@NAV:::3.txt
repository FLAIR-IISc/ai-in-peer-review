This work introduces a data-dependent kernel, termed the Fredholm kernel. The primary contribution lies in its principled use of unlabeled data. The authors establish several of the kernel's theoretical properties and provide empirical evidence demonstrating that its performance is competitive with state-of-the-art approaches.
The manuscript is well-structured and lucidly written.

Detailed comments:
1. Could the authors elaborate on a systematic methodology for selecting the kernel width for the Fredholm kernel? For instance, under what assumptions, if any, could this hyperparameter be effectively tuned using a technique like cross-validation?
2. The paper would be strengthened by the inclusion of an illustrative experiment highlighting scenarios where the proposed method might underperform. Such an analysis would help to better characterize the specific settings in which the kernel is most impactful.

This is a compelling and clearly presented paper. I recommend acceptance.
