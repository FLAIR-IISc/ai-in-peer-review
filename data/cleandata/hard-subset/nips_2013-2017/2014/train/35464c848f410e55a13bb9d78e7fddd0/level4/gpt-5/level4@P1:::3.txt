The authors propose a data-dependent kernel, termed the Fredholm kernel, that incorporates unlabeled data in a natural way. They present several theoretical properties of the kernel and empirically show that it compares favorably with state-of-the-art methods. The paper is well written and well organized.

Detailed comments:
1. Is there a systematic approach to selecting the kernel width for the Fredholm kernel? For example, under some assumptions, can it be selected via cross-validation?
2. It would be helpful to add toy experiments that illustrate cases where the proposed method may not work. This would clarify the settings in which the method is most effective.

Overall, the paper is interesting and clearly written. I recommend acceptance.
