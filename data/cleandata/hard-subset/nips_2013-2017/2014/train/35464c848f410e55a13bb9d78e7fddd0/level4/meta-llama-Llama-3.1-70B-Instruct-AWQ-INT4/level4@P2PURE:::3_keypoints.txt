- Proposes a data-dependent "Fredholm" kernel that naturally leverages unlabeled data.  
- Proves several theoretical properties of the Fredholm kernel.  
- Experimental results show competitiveness with state-of-the-art methods.  
- Paper is well-written and clearly organized.  
- Suggests adding a systematic method for selecting the Fredholm kernel width (e.g., investigate cross-validation under assumptions).  
- Recommends including toy experiments that illustrate scenarios where the method fails or performs poorly.  
- Overall: interesting and clearly written, merits consideration for acceptance.
