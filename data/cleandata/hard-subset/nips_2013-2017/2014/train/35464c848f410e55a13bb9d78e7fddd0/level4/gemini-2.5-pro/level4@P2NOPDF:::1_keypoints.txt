- Proposes a novel semi-supervised learning framework using Fredholm kernels, yielding a discretized Fredholm integral equation.  
- Strong technical quality with solid theoretical analysis and convincing experimental results.  
- Introduces a new data-dependent kernel that effectively leverages unlabeled data; resulting classifier is elegant and computationally efficient though different from classical representer-theorem solutions.  
- Question: Unclear whether Equation 3 holds when the associated kernel is non-PSD; request a proof/derivation for the non-PSD case.  
- Question: Experimental setup needs clarification on how the "optimal" parameters were chosen (was a validation set used, and what was the optimality criterion?).  
- Generally clear and well-organized, but contains several typos/grammar issues that should be fixed (specific line corrections noted).  
- Original and novel contribution distinct from a recent NIPS paper, well-positioned relative to prior work.  
- Significant results demonstrated across diverse datasets, showing broad applicability.  
- Overall recommendation: accept, contingent on addressing the minor points raised.
