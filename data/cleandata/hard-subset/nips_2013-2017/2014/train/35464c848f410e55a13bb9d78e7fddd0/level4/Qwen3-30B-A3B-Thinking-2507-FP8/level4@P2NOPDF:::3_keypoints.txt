- Introduces Fredholm kernels, a novel data-dependent kernel that leverages unlabeled data in a principled way without extra assumptions.  
- Naturally integrates unlabeled data, distinguishing it from prior semi-supervised kernel approaches.  
- Provides rigorous theoretical analysis and establishes key properties of the kernel.  
- Shows competitive empirical performance against state-of-the-art methods on multiple benchmarks.  
- Paper is exceptionally well-written, clear, and logically structured, making it broadly accessible.  
- Missing a systematic procedure for selecting kernel width; recommend discussing cross-validation or practical guidance.  
- Suggest adding a small toy experiment demonstrating scenarios where Fredholm kernels may underperform to clarify limitations.  
- Overall strong, well-motivated contribution with a recommendation for acceptance.
