The authors propose a data-dependent kernel, termed the Fredholm kernel, which innovatively leverages unlabeled datasets in a natural manner. The paper demonstrates several theoretical properties of this kernel and experimentally verifies its competitiveness with existing state-of-the-art methods.

The paper is well-written and organized, making it easy to follow.

Detailed comments:

1. The authors should consider providing a systematic approach for selecting the kernel width for the Fredholm kernel. For instance, under certain assumptions, can cross-validation be employed to determine an optimal kernel width?
2. Including toy experiments to illustrate scenarios where the proposed method may not perform well would be beneficial. This would help elucidate the settings in which the proposed method is most effective.

Overall, the paper is interesting and clearly written, warranting consideration for acceptance.
