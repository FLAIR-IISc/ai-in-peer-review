- Proposal of data-dependent "Fredholm kernels" that naturally incorporate unlabeled data.  
- The authors establish several theoretical properties of the proposed kernel.  
- Experiments show the method compares favorably against existing state-of-the-art methods.  
- Paper is well written and well organized.  
- Reviewer asks for a systematic method to select the kernel width (e.g., can cross-validation under specific assumptions determine it?).  
- Reviewer suggests adding toy experiments demonstrating scenarios where the method may fail to clarify its effective settings.  
- Overall recommendation: accept.
