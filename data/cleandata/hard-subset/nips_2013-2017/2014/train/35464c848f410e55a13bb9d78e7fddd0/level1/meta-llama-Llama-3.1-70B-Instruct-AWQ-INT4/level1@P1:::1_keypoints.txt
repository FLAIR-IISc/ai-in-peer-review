- Introduces a Fredholm kernel that can effectively leverage unlabeled data under the "noise assumption."  
- Provides theoretical analysis showing properties and benefits of Fredholm kernels.  
- Presents experimental results demonstrating improved performance in semi-supervised settings.  
- Paper is well-written, well-organized, and clearly relates to prior work.  
- The framework rests on a solid mathematical foundation and sound theoretical results.  
- Experimental evidence is convincing and supports the proposed approach.  
- Assumes reader familiarity with Fredholm integral equations; more background would help non-experts.  
- Lacks sufficient discussion of limitations and potential real-world application constraints.  
- Some experiments need more detail about datasets and experimental setup.
