- Authors propose a data-dependent "Fredholm" kernel that naturally incorporates unlabeled data.  
- Novelty: uses unlabeled dataset in a natural way.  
- Paper presents several theoretical properties of the kernel.  
- Experiments show the kernel compares favorably with state-of-the-art methods.  
- Paper is well written and well organized.  
- Question: Is there a systematic way to select the Fredholm kernel width (e.g., via cross-validation under some assumptions)?  
- Suggestion: add toy experiments showing failure cases to clarify the settings where the method is most effective.  
- Overall assessment: interesting, clearly written, recommended for acceptance.
