- Proposes a novel supervised and semi-supervised learning framework using a regularized Fredholm integral equation.  
- Introduces Fredholm kernels as data-dependent kernels within the kernel framework.  
- Demonstrates competitive performance in standard semi-supervised learning experiments.  
- Paper is well-written with clear presentation of ideas.  
- Provides both theoretical and experimental evidence that Fredholm kernels leverage unlabeled data under a noise assumption.  
- Offers an interesting "soft-thresholding PCA" interpretation of the Fredholm kernel.  
- Needs discussion of Nystrom/kernel PCA integral-equation approximations and clarification of similarities/differences.  
- Should cite operator-equation approaches in SVM/kernel literature and explain how Eq. 2 relates or differs.  
- A representer theorem is missing — either prove it for Eq. 2 or give a precise reference and show Eq. 2 as a special case.  
- Lacks comparison with other data-dependent kernels (e.g., Fisher kernels, probability product kernels).  
- Contains typos to fix (e.g., "Not that" line 165, "achived" line 221, "chossing" line 341, "Table ??" line 672).  
- Overall assessment: strong paper with significant contribution; rating 8/10, accept with minor revisions, confidence 8/10.
