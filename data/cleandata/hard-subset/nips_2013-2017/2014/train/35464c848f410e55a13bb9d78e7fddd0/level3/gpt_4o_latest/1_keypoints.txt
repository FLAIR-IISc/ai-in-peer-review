- Originality: Novel kernel-based framework that generalizes traditional methods and clearly differentiates from related work.
- Significance: Addresses effective use of unlabeled data in noisy environments with meaningful improvements on synthetic and real datasets.

- Weakness — Minor clarity issues: Some typos and unclear sentences (e.g., explanation of the noise assumption) need polishing.

- Arguments against acceptance: Missing proof for Equation 3 in the main paper and inadequate parameter selection details.
