The paper "Learning with Fredholm Kernels" presents a novel framework for supervised and semi-supervised learning based on reformulating the learning problem as a regularized Fredholm integral equation. This approach fits naturally into the kernel framework and can be interpreted as constructing new data-dependent kernels, which the authors call Fredholm kernels.

The main contribution of the paper is the proposal of a new framework for learning based on solving a regularized Fredholm equation. The framework naturally combines labeled and unlabeled data and can be expressed as a kernel method with a non-standard data-dependent kernel. The authors also discuss the "noise assumption" in semi-supervised learning and provide theoretical and empirical evidence that Fredholm kernels can effectively utilize unlabeled data under this assumption.
