- Proposes a novel Method-of-Moments approach for learning Mixture of HMMs by leveraging spectral properties of the global transition matrix to resolve permutation ambiguity.  
- Enables efficient parameter estimation for MHMMs.  
- Technically sound with theoretical analysis and experimental support.  
- Provides a complete algorithm with sufficient details for reproducibility.  
- Paper is well-written, well-organized, and uses consistent notation.  
- Combines existing techniques in a creative and original way for MHMM learning.  
- Empirical results on synthetic and real data are comparable to or better than EM.  
- Offers insights into spectral properties of the global transition matrix that may have broader use.  
- Limitation: assumes the same number of hidden states for each HMM.  
- Limitation: lacks a thorough analysis of computational complexity.  
- Recommendation: accept for publication.
