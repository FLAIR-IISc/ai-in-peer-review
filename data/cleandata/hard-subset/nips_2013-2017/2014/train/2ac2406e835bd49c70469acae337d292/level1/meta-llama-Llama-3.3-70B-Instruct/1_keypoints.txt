- Proposes learning Mixture of HMMs via Method of Moments by representing an MHMM as a single HMM with a block-diagonal transition matrix.  
- Leverages existing MoM algorithms for HMMs to learn MHMM parameters.  
- The block-diagonal representation introduces a permutation ambiguity.  
- Resolves permutation ambiguity by exploiting spectral properties of the global transition matrix.  
- Paper is well-written with clear, detailed, and well-organized technical sections.  
- Experimental results show effectiveness in accuracy and computational efficiency on synthetic and real data.  
- Novel and efficient approach that addresses limitations of traditional EM for MHMMs.  
- Provides a thorough spectral analysis of the global transition matrix.  
- Assumes equal number of hidden states in each component HMM, which may be unrealistic.  
- Lacks comprehensive comparisons with other MHMM learning methods (e.g., variational inference, Gibbs sampling).  
- Does not discuss potential limitations such as sensitivity to initialization or hyperparameter choices.  
- Recommendation: accept with minor revisions to address the noted limitations.
