- Proposes learning Mixture of HMMs (MHMM) via Method of Moments by representing MHMM as a single HMM with a block-diagonal transition matrix.
- Leverages existing MoM algorithms for HMMs to learn the MHMM representation.
- Identifies a permutation ambiguity introduced by this representation and resolves it using spectral properties of the global transition matrix.
- Theoretical analysis and experimental results support the main claims.
- Algorithm is well-motivated, clearly presented, and easy to follow.
- Experimental results show improved accuracy and computational efficiency compared to EM.
- Paper is well-written with a thorough related-work review.
- Novel contribution: using spectral properties to resolve permutation ambiguity.
- Experiments are well-designed and results are clearly presented.
- Limitation: assumes each component HMM has the same number of hidden states.
- Limitation: lacks detailed analysis of computational complexity and scalability.
- Limitation/suggestion: would benefit from additional experiments testing robustness to varied data types and noise.
