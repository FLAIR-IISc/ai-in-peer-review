- Presents a spectral algorithm for learning MHMMs by formulating the mixture as a single larger HMM so standard spectral methods apply.  
- Introduces a stabilization-based solution for the parameter permutation problem, assuming each component HMM has a unique stationary distribution.  
- Analysis of estimation noise and the role of eigenvalues (Eq. 7) is an insightful theoretical contribution.  
- Presentation suffers from unclear notation and insufficient discussion of related work (e.g., spectral initialization of EM by Chaganty & Liang, 2013).  
- Experimental evaluation is basic and the results are poorly presented, reducing clarity and impact.  
- Corollary 1 uses terms (e.g., 1_J^T) that are not clearly defined and needs explicit clarification.  
- The structure of lim_{e→∞} Ā^e should be stated explicitly (JK x JK block-diagonal with identical columns within each J x J block).  
- All vectors and matrices should be defined with their dimensions when first introduced to improve readability.  
- Figure 4 is hard to interpret and should be replaced with a clear table for direct comparison.  
- Reported EM baseline performance seems unusually poor; authors should run EM multiple times and report the best run (likelihood/accuracy) as the standard baseline.  
- Recommend adopting standard notation from prior work (e.g., Hsu et al. 2009: use T for transitions and n, m for observation/hidden state counts) to align with community conventions.
