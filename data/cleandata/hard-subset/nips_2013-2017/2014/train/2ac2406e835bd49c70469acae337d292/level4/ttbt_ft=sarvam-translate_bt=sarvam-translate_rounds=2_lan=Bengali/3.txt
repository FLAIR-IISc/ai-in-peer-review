The authors have presented a spectral method for learning the mixture of HMMs.

The algorithm will be a slightly different version of the Hsu, Kakade, and Zhang algorithm, with the only difference being that the recovered transition matrix will be an intentional arrangement of the original matrix, which will mix different components. The authors have proposed a de-perturbation algorithm that seems to work reasonably well.

Occasionally, some minor errors are noticed, such as:

"For each e, identify the eigenvalues that have all the energy."

Read the proof more carefully.

To explain this work better, it may be relevant to discuss the following papers:

Chaganty and Liang. Spectral experts for estimating mixtures of linear regressions. In
International Conference on Machine Learning (ICML), 2013.
