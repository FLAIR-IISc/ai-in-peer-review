- Proposes a spectral, method-of-moments algorithm for learning mixtures of HMMs (MHMM) by treating MHMM as a special case of HMM.  
- Introduces a stabilization-based technique to resolve parameter permutation, assuming each HMM transition matrix has a single eigenvalue equal to 1.  
- Leverages existing spectral HMM and mixture-model literature (Anandkumar et al. 2012, 2013) as foundational work.  
- Provides analysis for the estimation-noise case that uses eigenvalue behavior in the algorithm.  
- Presents experimental validation on synthetic and real datasets showing effectiveness.  
- Strength: novel, intuitive stabilization step for undoing parameter permutation.  
- Strength: theoretical support and analysis under estimation noise.  
- Weakness: notation and presentation are unclear; vectors/matrices and dimensions are not always defined on first use.  
- Weakness: lacks comprehensive empirical comparison with EM and other existing MHMM learning methods.  
- Weakness: experimental results presentation could be clearer and more concise (tables preferred over graphs).  
- Quality: technically sound with claims supported by theory and experiments, but needs more algorithmic detail (complexity and convergence).  
- Clarity: generally well-organized but specific parts (Corollary 1, limit expressions, spectral properties) need clearer exposition.  
- Originality: stabilization step is novel, but overall approach builds on prior spectral HMM/mixture work and needs clearer positioning of contributions.  
- Significance: addresses an important ML problem with potential practical impact, but implications and broader impact are under-discussed.  
- Acceptance arguments: novel stabilization, noise-aware eigenvalue analysis, and supporting experiments.  
- Rejection arguments: presentation/notation issues, insufficient comparative evaluation, and unclear experimental reporting.  
- Recommendation: accept conditional on revisions to clarify notation, strengthen comparisons and contributions discussion, and improve experimental presentation.
