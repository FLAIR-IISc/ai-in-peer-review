- Proposes a spectral method for learning mixtures of Hidden Markov Models (HMMs).
- Uses a variant of the Hsu–Kakade–Zhang spectral learning algorithm as the core approach.
- Identifies the main technical challenge: direct spectral recovery yields correct transition matrices but with component labels arbitrarily permuted.
- Main contribution is a novel de-permutation algorithm to resolve component-label ambiguity, showing strong empirical performance.
- Lacks sufficient contextualization with related spectral mixture-model work (e.g., Chaganty & Liang on mixtures of linear regressions).
- Manuscript needs careful proofreading to fix minor typographical errors (example sentence quoted).
