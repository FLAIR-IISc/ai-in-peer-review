- Introduces a spectral method for learning mixtures of HMMs, as a variant of the Hsu–Kakade–Zhang algorithm.  
- Key difference/issue: the recovered transition matrix is an arbitrary permutation of the true matrix, mixing the components.  
- Authors propose a de-permutation algorithm to correct this, which appears to work reasonably well in practice.  
- Paper has occasional minor typos that require careful proofreading (e.g., "For each e, identify of eigenvalues that contain all of the energy").  
- Recommend situating the work within related literature (e.g., Chaganty & Liang, ICML 2013) to clarify contributions and relevance, especially regarding de-permuting.
