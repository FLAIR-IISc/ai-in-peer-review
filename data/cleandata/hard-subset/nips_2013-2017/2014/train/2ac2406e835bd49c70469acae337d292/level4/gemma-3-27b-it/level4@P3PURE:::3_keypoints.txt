- Presents a spectral method for learning a mixture of HMMs, building on Hsu, Kakade & Zhang’s algorithm.  
- Algorithm is a subtle but important variant of the standard spectral approach.  
- The recovered transition matrix is an arbitrary permutation of the true matrix, causing component mixing.  
- Authors propose a depermutation algorithm to resolve component mixing; it appears to work reasonably well in practice.  
- Paper needs more thorough proofreading (several minor typos noted, e.g., “For each e, identify of eigenvalues that contain all of the energy.”).  
- Paper should better situate itself in related work (e.g., Chaganty & Liang, “Spectral experts for estimating mixtures of linear regressions,” ICML 2013).
