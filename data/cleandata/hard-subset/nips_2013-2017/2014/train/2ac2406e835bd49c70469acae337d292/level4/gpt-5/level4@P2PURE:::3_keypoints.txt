- Presents a spectral method for learning a mixture of HMMs and focuses on how to de-permute the recovered components.
- Without the de-permutation step, the algorithm is essentially a trivial variant of the Hsu, Kakade, and Zhang spectral algorithm.
- As-is, the recovered transition matrix can be an arbitrary permutation of the true one, causing component mixing.
- The proposed de-permutation procedure appears to work reasonably well in practice.
- The manuscript contains several small typos and needs careful proofreading (example: “For each e, identify of eigenvalues that contain all of the energy.”).
- The paper should better situate itself in related work, e.g., cite and relate to Chaganty and Liang (ICML 2013) on spectral methods for mixtures.
