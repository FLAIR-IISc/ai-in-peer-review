- Summary: Proposes spectral learning for a single HMM and a depermutation step to recover block-diagonal structure for mixtures of HMMs.  
- Major 1: Paper is well-written but experimental setup descriptions lack sufficient detail.  
- Major 2: Method estimates full HMM parameters before recovering sparsity, undermining the scalability/sparsity motivation of using mixture HMMs; applicability conditions are unclear.  
- Major 3: No guarantee the spectral method yields (near) sparse transition matrices; robustness to small noise is questionable.  
- Major 4: Experiments are unrealistic—testing depermutation on noisy matrices rather than learning from observation data drawn from a mixture HMM; should train from generated observation data.  
- Major 5: Section 4.2 omits critical mixture HMM parameters (transition/observation matrices); these should be reported (e.g., in an appendix).  
- Major 6: Table 1 shows EM initialized by spectral sometimes worse than spectral alone, which contradicts expectations and needs explanation.  
- Minor 1: Typo: subscript of Λ in Section 3.2.1 should be K, not 2.  
- Minor 2: Figure text and numbers are too small to read.  
- Overall concern: Strong theory but method may not generalize to real-world datasets due to the practical limitations above.
