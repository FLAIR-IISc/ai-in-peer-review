The authors propose a spectral method for learning a mixture of Hidden Markov Models (HMMs). While the core algorithm appears as a straightforward extension of the Hsu, Kakade & Zhang approach, the recovered transition matrix is subject to an arbitrary permutation of the true component order, leading to a mixing of components. To address this, the authors introduce a depermutation algorithm that appears to perform effectively. 

The manuscript contains several minor typographical errors; for example, “For each e, identify of eigenvalues that contain all of the energy.” Careful proofreading is recommended. 

Furthermore, the paper would benefit from a more thorough discussion of related work, particularly in relation to approaches like that of Chaganty and Liang ("Spectral experts for estimating mixtures of linear regressions," ICML 2013). The authors correctly identify the central challenge of resolving the permutation ambiguity when applying a spectral method to this problem.
