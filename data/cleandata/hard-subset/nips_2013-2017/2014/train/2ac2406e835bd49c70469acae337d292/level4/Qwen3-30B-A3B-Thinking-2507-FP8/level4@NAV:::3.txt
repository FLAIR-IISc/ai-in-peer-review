

The authors present a spectral method for learning a mixture of HMMs. The algorithm constitutes a trivial variant of the standard Hsu, Kakade & Zhang approach, except that the recovered transition matrix is an arbitrary permutation of the true one, thereby mixing the components. The authors propose a depermutation algorithm that appears to function reasonably well.  

Minor typographical errors are present; for example:  
"For each e, identify of eigenvalues that contain all of the energy."  
Please proofread more carefully.  

The paper would benefit from stronger contextualization, particularly by relating to relevant prior work such as:  
Chaganty and Liang. Spectral experts for estimating mixtures of linear regressions. In *International Conference on Machine Learning (ICML)*, 2013.  

The authors address the key question of how to "de-permute" the results.
