

Review:  
The authors present a spectral method for learning a mixture of HMMs. The algorithm constitutes a trivial variant of the standard Hsu, Kakade & Zhang approach, except that the recovered transition matrix is an arbitrary permutation of the true one, thereby mixing the various components. The authors propose a depermutation algorithm that appears to work reasonably well.  

The paper contains several minor grammatical errors and typos; for instance:  
"For each e, identify of eigenvalues that contain all of the energy."  
This should read: "For each e, identify eigenvalues that contain all of the energy." Please proofread more carefully.  

The paper would benefit from stronger contextualization. Specifically, it should relate to prior work on spectral methods for mixture models, such as Chaganty and Liang's "Spectral Experts for Estimating Mixtures of Linear Regressions" (ICML 2013). The core challenge of "how to de-permute" the recovered transition matrix deserves clearer exposition.
