- Presents a spectral method for learning a mixture of HMMs.  
- Algorithm is a trivial variant of the Hsu, Kakade & Zhang approach.  
- Recovered transition matrix is an arbitrary permutation of the true one, causing component mixing.  
- Authors propose a de-permutation algorithm that appears to work reasonably well.  
- Minor typographical errors (e.g., "For each e, identify of eigenvalues that contain all of the energy."); needs proofreading.  
- Paper needs stronger contextualization with related work (e.g., Chaganty & Liang 2013).
