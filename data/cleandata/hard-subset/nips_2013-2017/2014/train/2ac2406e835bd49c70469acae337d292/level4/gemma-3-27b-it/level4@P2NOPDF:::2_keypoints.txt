- Proposes applying an existing spectral HMM algorithm to mixtures of HMMs by reformulating MHMMs as a special HMM case.  
- Introduces a stabilization technique to resolve parameter permutation by assuming each HMM transition matrix has a single eigenvalue equal to one.  
- Notation is unclear and inconsistent; vectors and matrices (and their dimensions) should be defined on first use.  
- Corollary 1 needs explicit definition of 1J^T and a clearer explanation of lim_{e->∞} \bar{A}^e as a JK×JK block-diagonal matrix with identical columns within each J×J block.  
- Related work section is thin; must discuss relevant prior work such as Chaganty and Liang (2013) on spectral initialization for EM.  
- Experimental presentation is insufficient; Figure 4 is hard to interpret and should be replaced with a table.  
- EM results in Table 1 are surprisingly poor; report best (maximum) EM accuracy (not average) and investigate why EM underperforms.  
- Minor notation consistency issues: consider Hsu et al. (2009) conventions (use T for transition matrices and n,m for observations/hidden states).  
- The permutation-mitigation approach is interesting and intuitive.  
- Provides a non-trivial analysis of estimation noise and a meaningful use of eigenvalues (Eq. (7)) in the algorithm.  
- Overall: promising core idea but requires clearer notation, stronger related work grounding, and substantially improved experimental reporting for publication readiness.
