- Presents a spectral method for learning a mixture of HMMs.
- Algorithm is a trivial variant of Hsu, Kakade & Zhang; recovered transition matrix can be an arbitrary permutation that mixes components.
- Authors propose a de-permutation algorithm that appears to work reasonably well.
- Paper contains minor grammatical errors and typos (example given) and needs careful proofreading.
- Paper should better contextualize with prior spectral mixture work (e.g., Chaganty & Liang, ICML 2013).
- The core challenge of how to de-permute the recovered transition matrix needs clearer exposition.
