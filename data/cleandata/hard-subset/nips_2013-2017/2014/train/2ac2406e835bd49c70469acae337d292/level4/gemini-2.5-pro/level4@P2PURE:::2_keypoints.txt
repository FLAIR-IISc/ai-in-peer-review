- Presents a spectral algorithm for learning MHMMs by casting an MHMM as a larger HMM to apply Anandkumar et al. (2012).
- Proposes a novel stabilization-based method to resolve parameter permutation, assuming each component HMM transition matrix has a single eigenvalue of 1.
- Stabilization scheme is intuitive and interesting; analysis under estimation noise and novel use of eigenvalues (Eq. 7) are valuable contributions.
- Notation is occasionally unclear; vectors and matrices should have dimensions defined on first introduction.
- Corollary 1 is confusing; explicitly define 1_J^T and clarify the block-diagonal structure and identical columns within each JxJ block.
- Literature review is incomplete; prior work on initializing EM with spectral estimates (e.g., Chaganty & Liang, 2013) is missing.
- Experimental evaluation is preliminary; Figure 4 is hard to read and should be replaced with a table.
- EM results in Table 1 are surprisingly low; report the best EM performance across multiple runs (not just the average) for fair comparison.
- Minor notation suggestion: adopt established notation from Hsu et al. (2009) (use T for transitions, n/m for observation/hidden states).
