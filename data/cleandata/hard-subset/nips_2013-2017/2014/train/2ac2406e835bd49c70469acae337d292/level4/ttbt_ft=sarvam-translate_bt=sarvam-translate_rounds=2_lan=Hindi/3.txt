The authors present a spectral method for learning a mixture of HMMs.

This algorithm will be a slight modification of the standard algorithm by Hsu, Kakade, and Zhang, except that the obtained transition matrix will be an arbitrary sequence of real matrices, which will add up the different components. The authors propose a de-perturbation algorithm that seems to work reasonably well.

Sometimes some typing errors also appear. For example:

"For each e, identify the eigenvalue that contains all the energy."

Please proofread more carefully.

This paper could do a better job of putting this work in context, perhaps by linking it to a paper like this:

Chaganty and Liang. Spectral Experts for Estimating Mixtures of Linear Regressions. At the International Conference on Machine Learning (ICML), 2013.

The authors present a spectral method for learning a mixture of HMMs, which addresses the important question of what to "de-perturb" the results.
