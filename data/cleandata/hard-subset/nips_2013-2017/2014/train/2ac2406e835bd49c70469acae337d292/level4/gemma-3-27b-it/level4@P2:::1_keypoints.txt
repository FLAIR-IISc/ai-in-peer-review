- Novelty: applies spectral MoM to MHMMs using equivalence to a single HMM with block-diagonal transition and spectral permutation resolution.  
- Theoretical soundness: provides clear theoretical justification and well-articulated lemmas on block-diagonal equivalence and spectral properties.  
- Computational efficiency: MoM approach avoids iterative forward-backward EM and promises better scalability on large datasets.  
- Clear presentation: paper is generally well-written and logically structured.  
- Practical applicability concern: method assumes accurate full HMM estimation before block recovery, which may fail in noisy or limited-data real-world settings.  
- Sparsity concern: learned transition matrix may be dense despite an underlying sparse block structure, causing incorrect depermutation.  
- Limited experimental validation: synthetic experiments do not convincingly demonstrate robustness to realistic noise or learning-from-observations scenarios.  
- Insufficient experimental detail: missing transition/observation matrix parameters and other setup details hurt reproducibility and interpretability.  
- Unexpected EM behavior: EM initialized with spectral estimates sometimes performs worse than spectral alone, which is counterintuitive and unexplained.  
- Minor technical issues: correction needed in proof (Λ subscript should be K, not 2) and figure text is too small.  
- Arguments for acceptance: novel, theoretically sound method with potential computational advantages and insightful spectral analysis.  
- Arguments against acceptance: practical applicability is questionable under noise, experiments are insufficient, parameters are missing, and EM behavior is concerning.  
- Overall assessment: interesting idea with solid theory but limited practical validation and unresolved limitations.  
- Recommendation: Weak reject, with openness to reconsideration after major revision addressing the raised points.  
- Suggested author actions: address sparsity concerns, run realistic/noisy experiments, provide full experimental parameters, and clarify failure modes and EM behavior.
