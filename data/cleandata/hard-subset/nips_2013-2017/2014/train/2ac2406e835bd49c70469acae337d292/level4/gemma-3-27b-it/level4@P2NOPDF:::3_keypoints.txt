- Introduces a spectral method for learning mixtures of HMMs, building on Hsu, Kakade & Zhang.
- Identifies critical challenge: recovered transition matrix is arbitrarily permuted, shuffling mixture components.
- Proposes a depermutation algorithm to resolve the permutation ambiguity.
- Empirical results suggest the depermutation algorithm functions effectively.
- Paper needs more rigorous editing—contains typos and grammatical errors; proofreading recommended.
- Needs stronger connection to related work (e.g., Chaganty & Liang 2013) and discussion of prior spectral methods addressing permutation issues.
- Main contribution: practical solution to the permutation problem, though novelty and positioning would be clearer with improved related-work coverage and polish.
