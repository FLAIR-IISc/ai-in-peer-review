- Proposes a spectral method for learning a mixture of HMMs, as a variant of the Hsu, Kakade, and Zhang algorithm.  
- The recovered transition matrix is an arbitrary permutation of the true one, mixing the components.  
- Introduces a de-permutation algorithm to correct this permutation; it appears to work reasonably well.  
- De-permutation algorithm is a main contribution and is well-explained with supporting experiments.  
- Paper needs more thorough proofreading to fix typos and grammatical errors (example sentence given).  
- Should provide more context and comparisons to related spectral methods (e.g., Chaganty & Liang 2013).  
- Organization and presentation are generally good but some sections (e.g., introduction) should be expanded or reorganized for better flow.  
- Overall a solid contribution that could be stronger after revisions; reviewer rating: 7.
