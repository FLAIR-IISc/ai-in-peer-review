The authors present a spectral method for learning a mixture of HMMs.

This algorithm will be a slight variation of the standard algorithm by Hsu, Kakade, and Zhang, except that the obtained transition matrix will be an arbitrary permutation of the real matrix, which will combine the different components. The authors propose a de-perturbation algorithm that seems to work reasonably well.

Sometimes some typing errors also appear. For example:

"For each e, identify the eigenvalues that contain all the energy."

Please proofread more carefully.

This paper could do a better job of putting this work in context, perhaps by relating it to papers like:

Chaganty and Liang. Spectral Experts for Estimating Mixtures of Linear Regressions. At the International Conference on Machine Learning (ICML), 2013.

The authors present a spectral method for learning a mixture of HMMs, addressing the important question of who to "de-perturb" the results.
