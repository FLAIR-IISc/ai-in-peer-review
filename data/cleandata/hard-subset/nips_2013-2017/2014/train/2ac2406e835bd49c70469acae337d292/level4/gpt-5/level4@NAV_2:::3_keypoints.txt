- Proposes a spectral method-of-moments approach for learning mixtures of HMMs.  
- Without addressing permutation, the approach is a straightforward adaptation of Hsu–Kakade–Zhang but yields an arbitrarily permuted global transition matrix that interleaves states from different components.  
- Introduces a de-permutation procedure based on the spectral structure of the global transition matrix, which appears to work reasonably well in practice.  
- Contains occasional minor numerical/typographical issues (example sentence given); needs careful proofreading.  
- Could better situate the work in related literature (e.g., discuss Chaganty & Liang, ICML 2013).  
- Overall: presents a spectral method for mixtures of HMMs and tackles the central problem of de-permuting estimated parameters.
