- Presents a spectral method for learning mixtures of HMMs.  
- Algorithm is essentially a trivial variant of Hsu, Kakade, and Zhang’s method, with recovered transition matrices appearing as arbitrary permutations of the true components.  
- Authors propose a de-permutation algorithm that appears to work reasonably well in practice.  
- Paper contains typos and grammatical errors (example: the phrase "For each e, identify of eigenvalues that contain all of the energy" is incorrect).  
- Needs stronger contextualization in the literature—e.g., relate to Chaganty and Liang (ICML 2013) and other spectral methods for mixtures—to clarify contributions and novelty.
