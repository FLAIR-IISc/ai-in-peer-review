- Summary: Applies spectral method-of-moments to learn a single HMM, then resolves permutation ambiguity to recover block-diagonal MHMM structure.
- Major 1: Paper is clearly written but the experimental section is underdeveloped and needs more detail and analysis.
- Major 2: Method requires accurately estimating a full HMM before depermuting, which may be impractical; need guidance on regimes where it works well or fails.
- Major 3: Recovered transition matrices will not be exactly sparse—no mechanism is presented to encourage/guarantee approximate block-diagonality and spectral estimators may yield dense approximations.
- Major 4: Experiments perturb transition matrices directly; should instead sample observation sequences from MHMMs and evaluate the full learning pipeline under observational noise and limited data.
- Major 5: Section 4.2 lacks detail on transition/emission parameters and mixing proportions—exact settings should be provided (e.g., in an appendix).
- Major 6: Table 1 shows EM sometimes worsening spectral initialization; need explanation (local minima, overfitting, model mismatch, regularization differences).
- Minor 1: Typo in Section 3.2.1: subscript of Λ in the proof appears to be K rather than 2.
- Minor 2: Figures are hard to read; enlarge text and numerical labels.
- Overall concern: Interesting theoretical ideas but practical robustness on real data remains questionable.
