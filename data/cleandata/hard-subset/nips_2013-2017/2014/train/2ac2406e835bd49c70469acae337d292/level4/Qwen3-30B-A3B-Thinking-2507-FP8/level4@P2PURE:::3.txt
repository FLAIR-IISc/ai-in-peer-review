

The authors present a spectral method for learning a mixture of HMMs. The proposed algorithm constitutes a trivial variant of the standard Hsu, Kakade & Zhang approach, except that the recovered transition matrix is an arbitrary permutation of the true one, thereby mixing the components. The authors introduce a depermutation algorithm that appears to work reasonably well. The paper contains a few minor typos; for example: "For each e, identify of eigenvalues that contain all of the energy." Please proofread more carefully. The paper could better contextualize this work, for instance by relating to Chaganty and Liang's "Spectral experts for estimating mixtures of linear regressions" (ICML 2013). The authors address the key question of how to "de-permute" the results.
