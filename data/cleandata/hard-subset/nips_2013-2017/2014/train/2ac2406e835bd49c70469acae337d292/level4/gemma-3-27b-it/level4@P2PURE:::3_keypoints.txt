- Proposes a spectral method for learning mixtures of HMMs, largely a straightforward extension of Hsu, Kakade & Zhang.
- Recovered transition matrix is subject to arbitrary permutation of component order, causing component mixing.
- Introduces a depermutation algorithm that appears to work effectively in practice.
- Manuscript has several minor typographical errors and needs careful proofreading.
- Lacks sufficient discussion of related work, notably Chaganty & Liang (ICML 2013) on spectral methods for mixtures.
- Correctly identifies the central challenge as resolving the permutation ambiguity in spectral approaches.
