- Proposes a spectral method for learning mixtures of HMMs, building on Hsu, Kakade, and Zhang (2012).  
- Core algorithm is a straightforward adaptation of standard spectral approaches.  
- Recovered transition matrix suffers an arbitrary permutation of component labels, mixing components.  
- Authors introduce a depermutation procedure that appears effective in practice.  
- Depermutation is described briefly without rigorous justification or performance guarantees.  
- Unclear how depermutation uniquely identifies the correct permutation, especially under noise or high dimensionality.  
- Manuscript contains grammatical errors and inconsistent notation that undermine presentation quality.  
- Fails to adequately situate work within spectral mixture literature; should discuss links to Chaganty & Liang (2013) and related methods.  
- Lacks quantitative evaluation of depermutation (e.g., permutation error rates, label recovery accuracy) and ablation studies.  
- Contribution is mainly algorithmic without sufficient theoretical grounding or comparative analysis; recommend major revision.
