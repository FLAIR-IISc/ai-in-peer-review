- Presents a spectral method variant for learning mixtures of HMMs, building on the Hsu–Kakade–Zhang algorithm.  
- The recovered transition matrix can be an arbitrary permutation mixing components; the authors propose a de-permutation algorithm that appears to work reasonably well.  
- Paper contains minor errors/typos (e.g., "For each e, identify of eigenvalues that contain all of the energy.") and needs proofreading to improve clarity.  
- Lacks sufficient contextualization and comparison to related work (e.g., Chaganty & Liang 2013) to clarify novelty and contributions.  
- Overall, a notable contribution addressing the key problem of de-permuting spectral estimates for mixtures of HMMs.
