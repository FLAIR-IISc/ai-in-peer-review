- Introduces a spectral algorithm for MHMMs by framing an MHMM as a special-case HMM and leveraging Anandkumar et al. (2012).
- Main contribution: a stabilization technique to resolve parameter permutation, assuming each HMM transition matrix has a single eigenvalue = 1.
- Notation is unclear and inconsistent; vectors and matrices should be defined with dimensions on first use.
- Related-work review is incomplete; prior work using spectral estimates to initialize EM (e.g., Chaganty & Liang, 2013) should be discussed.
- Experimental evaluation is limited and Figure 4 is hard to interpret; present results in tables instead of plots.
- EM’s poor reported performance is surprising; recommend reporting best EM accuracy (not average) and ensuring sufficient iterations.
- Corollary 1 needs clarification: define 1_J^T and explain lim_{e->∞} \bar{A}^e as a JK×JK block-diagonal matrix with identical columns in each J×J block.
- The paper provides an analysis that accounts for estimation noise and an insightful application of eigenvalues (Equation (7)).
- Suggest adopting consistent notation (e.g., Hsu et al. (2009): T for transition matrices, n and m for observations and hidden states).
- Overall: method is interesting and intuitively motivated but requires clearer notation, stronger related work context, and improved experimental presentation.
