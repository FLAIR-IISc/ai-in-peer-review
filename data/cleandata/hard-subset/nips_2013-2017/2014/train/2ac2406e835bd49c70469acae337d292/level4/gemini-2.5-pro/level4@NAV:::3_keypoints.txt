- Introduces a spectral approach for learning mixtures of Hidden Markov Models.
- Method is a direct adaptation of the Hsu, Kakade & Zhang spectral algorithm.
- Identifies the key challenge that the standard spectral method recovers the transition matrix up to an unknown permutation, which mixes distinct components.
- Proposes a de-permutation algorithm to resolve this ambiguity; empirical results suggest it is effective.
- Manuscript needs more careful proofreading—contains several minor typographical errors (example: "For each e, identify of eigenvalues that contain all of the energy.").
- Contribution would be stronger with better placement in the literature; recommend discussing related spectral methods for mixture models (e.g., Chaganty & Liang, ICML 2013).
