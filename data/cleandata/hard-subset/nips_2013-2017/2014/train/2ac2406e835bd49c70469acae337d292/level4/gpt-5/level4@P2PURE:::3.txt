The authors present a spectral method for learning a mixture of HMMs. Absent the de-permutation step, the algorithm is essentially a trivial variant of the standard Hsu, Kakade, and Zhang algorithm; in particular, the recovered transition matrix can be an arbitrary permutation of the true one, thereby mixing the components. The paper proposes a de-permutation procedure to address this, which seems to work reasonably well.

There appear to be a number of small typos. For example: “For each e, identify of eigenvalues that contain all of the energy.” Please proofread more carefully.

The paper could do a better job of situating this work in context, for instance by relating it to:
Chaganty and Liang. Spectral experts for estimating mixtures of linear regressions. In International Conference on Machine Learning (ICML), 2013.
