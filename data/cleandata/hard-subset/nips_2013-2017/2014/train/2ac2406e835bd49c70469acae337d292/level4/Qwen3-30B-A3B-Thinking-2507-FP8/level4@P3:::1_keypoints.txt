- Proposes a spectral Method of Moments approach for Mixture of HMMs (MHMMs) by viewing an MHMM as a single HMM with block-diagonal transition matrix.
- Key technical contribution: resolves permutation ambiguity in MoM-based learning via spectral properties of the global transition matrix to recover individual HMM parameters.
- Builds on prior spectral learning work for latent variable models and is the first theoretically grounded extension to MHMMs.
- Claims substantial computational advantage over EM, avoiding sequence-specific forward-backward passes.
- Strength: paper is very clear and well-written, with elegant presentation of the block-diagonal insight.
- Strength: original solution to permutation ambiguity with solid theoretical guarantees (Lemmas 1–3).
- Strength: experiments show large speedups and utility as EM initialization on real data.
- Weakness: experimental validation is insufficiently realistic—depermutation tests use permuted matrices rather than MHMM-generated data and an artificial Dirichlet noise model.
- Weakness: synthetic experiments omit critical MHMM parameter details (transition, emission specs), preventing assessment of task difficulty.
- Weakness: real-data experiment is narrow (only two classes) and lacks strong comparisons to establish practical benefit.
- Weakness: method requires estimating a full MK×MK HMM before depermuting, contradicting MHMM sparsity motivation and likely infeasible for large MK.
- Weakness: paper gives no concrete guidance on when the approach is appropriate (e.g., limits on K and M) versus infeasible.
- Weakness: no theoretical or empirical guarantee that the learned transition matrix will be sparse or close to block-diagonal in practice.
- Weakness: unclear practical impact over EM—experiments do not show consistent improvement and spectral alone often underperforms EM with spectral initialization.
- Recommendation: borderline acceptance—novel and theoretically strong but needs more realistic experiments and clearer discussion of limitations.
- Suggested revisions: add realistic MHMM data experiments with full parameter details, quantify accuracy vs. computational trade-offs, and clarify applicability constraints.
