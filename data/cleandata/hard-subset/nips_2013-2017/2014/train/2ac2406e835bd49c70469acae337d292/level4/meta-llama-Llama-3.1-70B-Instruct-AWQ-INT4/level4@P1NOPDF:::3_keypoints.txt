- Proposes a spectral method for learning mixtures of HMMs, a variant of the Hsu–Kakade–Zhang algorithm.  
- The recovered transition matrix can be an arbitrary permutation of the true one, mixing components.  
- Introduces a de-permutation algorithm that appears to perform reasonably well.  
- Paper contains occasional typos and requires thorough proofreading.  
- Needs stronger contextualization and related-work discussion (e.g., Chaganty & Liang 2013) to clarify significance and novelty, particularly regarding de-permuting.
