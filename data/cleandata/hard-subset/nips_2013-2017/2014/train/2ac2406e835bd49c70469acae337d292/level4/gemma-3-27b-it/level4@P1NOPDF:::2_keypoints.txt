- Proposes a spectral algorithm for learning mixtures of HMMs by formulating an MHMM as a special-case HMM and applying an existing spectral method (Anandkumar et al., 2012).  
- Introduces a stabilization-based solution for the parameter permutation problem that assumes each HMM’s transition matrix has a single eigenvalue equal to 1.  
- Notational issues need fixing (unclear definitions, inconsistent symbols).  
- Related work review is insufficient; should cite and discuss works such as Chaganty and Liang (2013) on initializing EM with spectral estimates.  
- Experiments are basic and presentation is weak; recommend replacing Figure 4 with tables for clarity.  
- Reported poor EM performance is surprising; should report best-case EM accuracy (not just averages) and allow more iterations.  
- The stabilization scheme to address permutation is interesting and intuitive.  
- Provides analysis of algorithm performance under estimation noise and an intriguing use of eigenvalues (Equation (7)).  
- Corollary 1 is unclear—define 1J^T and clarify the structure of lim_{e->inf} \bar{A}^e as a JK x JK block-diagonal matrix with identical columns in each JxJ block.  
- Require defining every vector/matrix and its dimensions at first introduction to improve clarity.  
- Minor suggestion: align notation with Hsu et al. (2009) (use T for transition matrices, and n,m for observation/hidden state counts) to aid readability.
