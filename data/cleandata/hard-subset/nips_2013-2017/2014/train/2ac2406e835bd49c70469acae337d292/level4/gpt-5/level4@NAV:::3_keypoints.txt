- Presents a spectral approach for learning mixtures of HMMs.
- Algorithm is essentially a straightforward variant of the Hsu, Kakade, and Zhang method.
- The recovered transition matrix is an arbitrary permutation of the true one, which mixes the components.
- Authors propose a de-permutation procedure that appears to work reasonably well.
- Occasional minor typos; example: “For each e, identify of eigenvalues that contain all of the energy.” — please proofread.
- Paper could better situate the work in context, e.g., relate to Chaganty and Liang (ICML 2013).
- Addresses the key question of how to “de-permute” spectral results for mixtures of HMMs.
