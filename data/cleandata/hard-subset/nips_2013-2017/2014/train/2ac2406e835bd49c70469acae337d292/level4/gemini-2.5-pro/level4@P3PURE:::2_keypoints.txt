- Presents a spectral algorithm for learning mixtures of HMMs by casting an MHMM as a large HMM and applying Anandkumar et al. (2012).
- Main contribution is a stabilization step to resolve parameter permutation ambiguity, assuming each component HMM transition matrix has a single eigenvalue 1.
- Provides analysis under estimation noise and an interesting use of eigenvalues (Eq. 7).
- Related-work discussion is insufficient; prior work on initializing EM with spectral estimates (e.g., Chaganty & Liang, 2013) is missing.
- Experimental evaluation is limited and lacks clarity on EM baseline performance.
- Notational clarity is poor throughout the paper.
- Corollary 1 terms are undefined; explicitly define 1_J^T and the structure of lim_{e->inf} \bar{A}^e (JK x JK block-diagonal with identical columns within each JxJ block).
- Require dimensions for vectors and matrices at first introduction to improve readability.
- Replace Figure 4 with a table for easier interpretation.
- EM baseline in Table 1 is suspiciously poor; report best result over multiple runs (with sufficient iterations) rather than the average for fair comparison.
- Suggest adopting standard notation from Hsu et al. (2009) (e.g., T for transitions, n/m for observation/hidden state counts).
