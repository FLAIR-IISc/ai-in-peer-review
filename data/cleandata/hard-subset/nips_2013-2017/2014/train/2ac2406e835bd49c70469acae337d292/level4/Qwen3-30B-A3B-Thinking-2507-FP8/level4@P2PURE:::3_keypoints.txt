- Proposes a spectral method for learning mixtures of HMMs.  
- Algorithm is essentially a trivial variant of Hsu, Kakade & Zhang, with the recovered transition matrix being an arbitrary permutation that mixes components.  
- Introduces a de-permutation algorithm that appears to work reasonably well.  
- Paper contains minor typos and needs better proofreading (e.g., "For each e, identify of eigenvalues that contain all of the energy.").  
- Lacks contextualization; should relate to prior work such as Chaganty and Liang (ICML 2013) on spectral methods for mixtures.  
- Addresses the important question of how to de-permute spectral recovery results.
