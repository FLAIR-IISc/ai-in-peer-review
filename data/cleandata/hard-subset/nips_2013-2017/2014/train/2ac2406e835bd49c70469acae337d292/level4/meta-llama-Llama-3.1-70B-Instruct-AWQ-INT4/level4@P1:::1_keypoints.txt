- Proposes a method-of-moments/spectral algorithm to learn mixtures of HMMs by treating an MHMM as a single HMM.  
- Introduces a procedure to resolve permutation ambiguity and recover block-diagonal transition structure.  
- Paper is well-written, clear, and well-organized.  
- Novel use of spectral learning to handle permutation ambiguity is a key contribution.  
- Relies on accurately estimating full HMM parameters before reversing permutations.  
- Potential computational expense for large problems or limited training data is a concern.  
- Experimental results demonstrate effectiveness in some cases but are limited.  
- Need for more evaluation of robustness to noise and generalization to complex problems.  
- Scores well on clarity and originality.  
- Weaker on significance and overall quality due to limited experiments and discussion.  
- Authors should provide more discussion of limitations and future work.  
- Recommendation: accept with minor revisions (Rating 7/10, Confidence 8/10).
