- Proposes a spectral algorithm for learning a mixture of HMMs by casting MHMM as a special-case HMM and applying Anandkumar et al. (2012) off the shelf.
- Introduces a novel stabilization step to resolve parameter permutation, assuming each component transition matrix has a single eigenvalue equal to 1.
- Analyzes robustness to estimation noise and introduces an intriguing use of eigenvalues (Equation (7)) in the algorithm.
- Presentation issues: inconsistent notation and missing definitions; vectors/matrices and their dimensions should be defined when first introduced.
- Related-work gap: should cite prior work such as Chaganty and Liang (2013) on initializing EM with spectral estimates and align notation with Hsu et al. (2009).
- Experimental issues: experiments are basic and results presentation is weak (Figure 4 hard to read); recommend tables instead of graphs.
- EM results unclear: need to explain why EM performs poorly in Table 1 and report best EM accuracy across runs (not just the average).
- Specific clarifications requested: define 1J^T and elaborate the structure of lim_{e→∞} bar{A}^e; consider using standard HMM notation (T, n, m).
- Overall: interesting method combining an existing spectral estimator with a new stabilization step to undo parameter permutation.
