- Presents a spectral method for learning mixtures of HMMs, based on the Hsu–Kakade–Zhang framework.  
- Key challenge: recovered transition matrix is an arbitrary permutation of the true one, scrambling component identities.  
- Main contribution: a novel de-permutation algorithm to resolve the permutation ambiguity, with strong experimental performance.  
- Recommendation: place the work in broader context by relating to other spectral methods for mixture models (e.g., Chaganty & Liang, ICML 2013).  
- Editorial: manuscript needs careful proofreading to fix typos and grammatical errors (example sentence cited).
