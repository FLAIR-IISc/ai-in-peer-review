- Proposes a spectral method for learning mixtures of HMMs, adapting the Hsu–Kakade–Zhang algorithm.  
- The adapted algorithm recovers a transition matrix that may be an arbitrary permutation of the true one, mixing components.  
- Introduces a de-permutation algorithm to resolve component mixing, which performs reasonably well in practice.  
- Manuscript contains occasional minor errors/typos (example sentence given) and needs proofreading.  
- Paper needs better contextualization with related work (e.g., Chaganty & Liang 2013) and clearer positioning of contributions.
