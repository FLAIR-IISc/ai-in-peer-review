- Proposes a spectral-method variant (building on Hsu, Kakade & Zhang) for learning mixtures of HMMs.
- Identifies key technical challenge: naive spectral application yields arbitrarily permuted transition matrices that mix components.
- Introduces a de-permutation algorithm to fix the permutation issue; method appears to work reasonably well in experiments.
- Experimental evaluation suggests the proposed approach is effective.
- Presentation needs careful proofreading to fix typographical/grammatical errors that hurt clarity (example sentence cited).
- Paper lacks sufficient contextualization with related spectral methods for mixtures; should discuss connections to prior work (e.g., Chaganty & Liang 2013).
