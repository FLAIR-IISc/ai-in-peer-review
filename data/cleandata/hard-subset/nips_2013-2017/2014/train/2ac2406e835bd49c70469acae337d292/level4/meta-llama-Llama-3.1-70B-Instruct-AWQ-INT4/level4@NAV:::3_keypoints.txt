- Introduce a spectral variant of the Hsu–Kakade–Zhang algorithm for learning mixtures of HMMs.  
- The recovered transition matrix is only identified up to an arbitrary permutation that mixes components.  
- Authors propose a de-permutation algorithm that appears to produce satisfactory empirical results.  
- Paper contains minor typos (e.g., "For each e, identify of eigenvalues...") and needs thorough proofreading.  
- The manuscript lacks sufficient contextualization and discussion of related literature.  
- Recommend citing/comparing to Chaganty & Liang (ICML 2013) on spectral experts for mixtures of linear regressions.  
- A clearer connection to the broader literature would strengthen the contribution and impact.
