The authors introduce a spectral method for learning a mixture of Hidden Markov Models (HMMs). While the core algorithm closely resembles the established Hsu, Kakade & Zhang approach, a key distinction arises in the recovery of the transition matrix, which can be an arbitrary permutation of the ground truth, effectively mixing the mixture components. To address this, the authors propose a depermutation algorithm that appears to perform effectively. 

The manuscript contains minor typographical errors that warrant careful review; for example, the phrase "identify of eigenvalues" should be checked. 

Furthermore, the paper would benefit from a more thorough discussion of related work, particularly in connection to approaches like that of Chaganty and Liang (ICML 2013) on spectral methods for estimating mixtures of linear regressions. The authors appropriately highlight the challenge of depermuting the results as a central contribution of their work.
