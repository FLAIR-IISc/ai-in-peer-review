- Proposes a spectral method adapting Hsu, Kakade, and Zhang to learn mixtures of HMMs.  
- Recovered transition matrix is a permuted version of the true matrix, mixing components.  
- Introduces a de-permutation algorithm that appears reasonably effective at resolving this.  
- Paper contains occasional minor typos and proofreading is needed (example given).  
- Should discuss related work (e.g., Chaganty & Liang, ICML 2013) to improve context and relevance.  
- Core contribution: a spectral approach for mixtures of HMMs with a focus on resolving the de-permutation problem.
