- Presents a spectral method for learning a mixture of HMMs.  
- Algorithm is largely a trivial variant of Hsu, Kakade & Zhang; recovered transition matrix can be an arbitrary permutation mixing components.  
- Authors propose a de-permutation algorithm that appears to work reasonably well.  
- Occasional typos/proofreading issues (e.g., "For each e, identify of eigenvalues..."); needs careful proofreading.  
- Paper should better situate itself in related work (e.g., Chaganty & Liang, ICML 2013).  
- Addresses the key question of how to de-permute/align spectral outputs to true components.
