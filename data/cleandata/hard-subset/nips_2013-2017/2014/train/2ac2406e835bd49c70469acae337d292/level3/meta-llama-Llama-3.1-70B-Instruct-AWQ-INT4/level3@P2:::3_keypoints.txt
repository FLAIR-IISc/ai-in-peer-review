- Proposes a novel spectral method for learning mixtures of HMMs using method-of-moments and a global transition matrix.  
- Leverages spectral properties to resolve permutation ambiguity in component estimation.  
- Extends existing spectral HMM algorithms and offers a computationally efficient alternative to EM.  
- Well-motivated problem statement with clear explanation of approach.  
- Technical content is sound with thorough analysis of algorithm properties.  
- Provides a de-permutation algorithm with analysis showing it works reasonably well.  
- Experimental results show strong accuracy and computational efficiency.  
- Good comparison to EM and other spectral learning methods.  
- Lacks a more thorough discussion of related work (e.g., Chaganty & Liang, ICML 2013).  
- Needs more detailed analysis of computational complexity and scalability to large datasets.  
- Paper has occasional typos and proofreading issues that should be fixed.  
- Recommendation: accept conditional on addressing related-work coverage, complexity/scalability analysis, and proofreading.
