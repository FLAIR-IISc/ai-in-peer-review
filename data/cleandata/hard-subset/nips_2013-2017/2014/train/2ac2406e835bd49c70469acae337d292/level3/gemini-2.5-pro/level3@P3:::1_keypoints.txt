- Proposes a novel method-of-moments approach for learning MHMMs by treating an MHMM with K components and M states each as a single HMM with MK states and a block-diagonal transition matrix.
- Algorithm: use spectral HMM learning to estimate the global MK-state model, then resolve the permutation ambiguity to recover individual component HMMs.
- Main technical contribution: de-permutation via spectral analysis—identify K dominant eigenvalues and cluster columns of a low-rank reconstruction to recover block structure.
- Extends spectral learning methods from single HMMs to the more complex MHMM setting, offering an alternative to EM.
- Strength: tackles an important, practical problem and offers a novel, theoretically elegant alternative to EM that is less initialization-sensitive.
- Strength: solid theoretical foundation—Lemma 1 equivalence and a well-motivated spectral de-permutation argument in the noiseless case.
- Strength: clear, well-written exposition and logical presentation of methods and proofs.
- Weakness: practicality concerns—requires estimating an HMM with MK states, which is computationally and statistically demanding and limits applicability to small K.
- Weakness: robustness concerns—the method relies on a preserved spectral gap under finite-sample noise, but no guarantees or strong justification are provided.
- Weakness: experimental evaluation is inadequate and possibly flawed—noise experiments add noise to the true matrix instead of generating sequences and bypass the key estimation step.
- Weakness: experiments lack necessary details (MHMM parameters, etc.), hindering reproducibility.
- Weakness: unexpected results where EM initialized by the spectral method sometimes worsens performance, requiring explanation or diagnosis.
- Minor: typo in Lemma 2 eigenvalue subscript (should be K, not 2).
- Minor: figures are hard to read due to very small font sizes.
- Overall: promising and theoretically appealing work but currently lacks rigorous empirical validation and realistic experiments required for a top-tier venue.
