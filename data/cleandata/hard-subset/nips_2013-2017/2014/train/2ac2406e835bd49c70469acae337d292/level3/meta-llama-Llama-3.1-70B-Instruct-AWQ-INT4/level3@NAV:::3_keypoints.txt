- Proposes a spectral Method-of-Moments algorithm for learning mixtures of HMMs by representing an MHMM as a single HMM with a block-diagonal transition matrix.  
- Uses an existing MoM algorithm to recover parameters up to a permutation ambiguity.  
- Introduces a de-permutation algorithm that leverages spectral properties of the global transition matrix to recover individual HMM parameters.  
- Technically sound with solid theoretical analysis and supporting experimental results.  
- Occasional typos and proofreading issues need fixing.  
- Well-organized and clear; experiment details are reproducible, but the introduction could better situate the work in related literature.  
- Novel combination of known techniques; overall approach is useful but largely a trivial variant of Hsu, Kakade, and Zhang.  
- The de-permutation algorithm is a significant contribution, though its novelty could be emphasized more.  
- Addresses the important problem of permutation ambiguity in MHMM learning and offers useful spectral insights.  
- Experimental results demonstrate effectiveness and practical potential.  
- Lacks a detailed comparison to closely related work (e.g., Chaganty and Liang).  
- Recommendation: accept with revisions to improve novelty framing, related-work comparison, and proofreading.
