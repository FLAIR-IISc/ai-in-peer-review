- Proposes Low-Rank Time-Frequency Synthesis (LRTFS), a novel signal synthesis model with low-rank time-frequency structure.  
- Extends the Gaussian Composite Model (GCM) to represent signals as sums of layers with individual time resolutions and latent low-rank structure.  
- Presents two expectation-maximization (EM) algorithms for parameter estimation in the LRTFS model.  
- Demonstrates effectiveness on audio tasks, specifically music decomposition and speech enhancement.
