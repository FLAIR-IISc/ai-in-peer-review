**Summary of the Paper**

This paper proposes a novel signal synthesis model with low-rank time-frequency structure, called Low-Rank Time-Frequency Synthesis (LRTFS). The model is an extension of the Gaussian Composite Model (GCM) and allows for the representation of signals as a sum of layers with their own time resolution and latent low-rank structure. The authors describe two expectation-maximization algorithms for estimation in the new model and demonstrate its effectiveness in audio signal processing applications, including music decomposition and speech enhancement.
