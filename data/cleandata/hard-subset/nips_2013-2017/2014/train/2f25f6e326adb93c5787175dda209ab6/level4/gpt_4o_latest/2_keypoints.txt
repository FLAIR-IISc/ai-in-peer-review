- Proposes novel shift from modeling TF analysis coefficients to modeling time-domain signals via TF synthesis coefficients.  
- This perspective enables multi-resolution representations not possible with traditional NMF.  
- Reviewer finds the contribution intriguing and is impressed by the multi-resolution extension.  
- Asks whether, in single-resolution mode, the method yields comparable W and H matrices to IS-NMF.  
- Asks whether the proposed algorithm matches the efficiency and robustness of IS-NMF’s multiplicative update algorithm.
