- Novel extension of NMF that models the time-domain signal directly rather than TF analysis coefficients.
- Introduces a generative model of TF synthesis coefficients to enable modeling in the synthesis domain.
- Enables multi-resolution representations that traditional NMF cannot attain.
- Multi-resolution extension is interesting and has significant potential.
- Authors present promising results on multi-resolution signal decompositions.
- Reviewer requests evaluation in the single-resolution case against IS-NMF.
- Wants to know if the proposed method yields similar W and H matrices to IS-NMF.
- Asks whether the algorithm matches the speed and stability of multiplicative updates for IS-NMF.
- Key conceptual contribution: shifting from TF analysis modeling to time-domain modeling to enable new representations.
- Overall assessment: intriguing idea; further developments and evaluations are awaited.
