- Proposes a flexible time-frequency factorization framework applicable to arbitrary transforms and directly linked to the time-domain signal.
- Moves beyond STFT-based formulations by not relying on analysis coefficients of STFT transforms.
- Theoretical development is interesting and has solid foundations.
- Paper is well-written and accessible to researchers in the area.
- Originality is above average.
- Significance of the contribution is not fully conveyed.
- Primary weakness: results and evaluation are limited.
- Supplemental material is not particularly convincing.
- Lacks empirical comparisons to the prior methods it builds upon, which would strengthen the paper.
