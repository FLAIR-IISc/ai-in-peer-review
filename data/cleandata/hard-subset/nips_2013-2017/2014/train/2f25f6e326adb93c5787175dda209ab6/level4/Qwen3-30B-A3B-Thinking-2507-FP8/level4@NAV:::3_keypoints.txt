- Moves beyond STFT-based formulations to a more general, versatile framework.  
- Manuscript is accessible to experts and well written.  
- Demonstrates strong technical quality and robust theoretical development.  
- Originality is above average.  
- Significance of the contribution is not sufficiently articulated.
