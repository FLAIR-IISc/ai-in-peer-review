- Proposes LRTFS: a novel low-rank time-frequency synthesis model linking time-frequency synthesis and NMF, applicable to arbitrary TF transforms and the time domain.
- Paper is well-written, clear, and accessible to researchers.
- Mathematical derivations are sound and EM estimation algorithms are well-described.
- LRTFS framework is original and flexible for time-frequency factorizations.
- Paper does not clearly communicate the broader significance or practical implications of LRTFS.
- Experimental results and supplemental material are underwhelming and not fully convincing.
- Evaluation lacks comprehensive comparisons with existing methods.
- Demonstrated on two experiments (music hybrid decomposition and speech enhancement) but more extensive studies needed.
- Theoretical contribution is significant despite empirical weaknesses.
- Recommendation: accept with minor revisions to clarify significance and add more comparisons.
- Rating: 7/10.
