- Presents a thoughtful, flexible interpretation of time/frequency factorizations applicable to arbitrary transforms and tied to the time-domain signal rather than STFT analysis coefficients.  
- Well-written and easy to follow for readers familiar with the area, indicating good overall quality.  
- Originality is above average.  
- Significance of the contribution is not communicated as effectively as it could be.  
- Results and evaluation are disappointing; supplemental material is unconvincing.  
- Paper lacks comparisons to the methods it builds upon, which would have strengthened it.  
- Theoretical development is substantial with solid foundations, making the work an interesting contribution despite evaluation shortcomings.
