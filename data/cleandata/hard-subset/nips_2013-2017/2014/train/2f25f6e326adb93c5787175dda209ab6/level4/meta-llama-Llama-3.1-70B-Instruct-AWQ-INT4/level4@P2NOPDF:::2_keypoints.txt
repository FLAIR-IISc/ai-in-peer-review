- Proposes a novel NMF extension that models the time-domain signal via a generative model of TF synthesis coefficients instead of conventional TF analysis coefficients.  
- Enables multi-resolution representations that traditional NMF could not achieve.  
- Applying NMF to synthesis (rather than analysis) coefficients is a valuable and original contribution.  
- Reviewer finds the paper interesting and commends the exploration of multi-resolution extensions.  
- Requests evaluation in single-resolution settings versus IS-NMF: whether the learned W and H are similar and whether the proposed algorithm matches IS-NMF in speed and stability (multiplicative updates).
