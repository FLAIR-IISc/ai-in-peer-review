- Proposed framework models the time-domain signal via a generative model of time-frequency (TF) synthesis coefficients, unlike regular NMF which models TF analysis coefficients.

- This modeling choice enables multi-resolution representations that traditional NMF cannot achieve.

- The reviewer finds the paper interesting and the multi-resolution extension particularly appealing.

- The authors present promising results on multi-resolution signal decompositions.

- Reviewer requests evaluation in the single-resolution case compared to IS-NMF, to see if the proposed algorithm yields similar W and H matrices.

- Reviewer asks whether the proposed method matches IS-NMF in speed and stability (e.g., relative to IS-NMF's multiplicative update algorithm).

- Emphasis that modeling the time-domain signal is a significant departure from traditional NMF and opens new possibilities for multi-resolution modeling.
