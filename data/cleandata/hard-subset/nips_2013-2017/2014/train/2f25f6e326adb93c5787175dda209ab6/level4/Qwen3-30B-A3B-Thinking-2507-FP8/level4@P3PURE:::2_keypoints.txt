- Introduces a novel generative framework that shifts NMF’s perspective from time-frequency analysis coefficients to the time-domain signal via a generative model of TF synthesis coefficients.
- This reorientation enables multi-resolution signal representations that were not feasible in traditional NMF.
- The approach is conceptually interesting, with the multi-resolution extension particularly compelling.
- Evaluation focuses mainly on multi-resolution decompositions; single-resolution performance relative to IS-NMF is not shown.
- Unclear whether the method recovers similar factor matrices W and H in the single-resolution case compared to IS-NMF.
- Unclear whether the proposed algorithm matches the computational efficiency and numerical stability of standard multiplicative updates for IS-NMF.
- Addressing the single-resolution comparison and efficiency/stability concerns would strengthen the assessment of the method’s practical utility.
