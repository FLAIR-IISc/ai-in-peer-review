This is an interesting paper. In particular, I personally really like the idea of multi-resolution extensions.

While the authors present some results on multi-resolution signal decomposition, I am also curious to know the performance of the proposed method in the single-resolution case compared to IS-NMF. Does it give the same W and H? Is the current algorithm as fast and stable as the qualitative update algorithm for IS-NMF?
