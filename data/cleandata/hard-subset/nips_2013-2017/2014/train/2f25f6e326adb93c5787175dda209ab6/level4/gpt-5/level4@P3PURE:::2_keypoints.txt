- Proposes modeling the time-domain signal via a generative model of TF synthesis coefficients instead of the usual TF analysis-coefficient NMF, enabling multi-resolution representations.  
- Multi-resolution extension is a strong contribution; results on multi-resolution signal decompositions are compelling.  
- Reviewer requests a comparison of single-resolution performance to IS-NMF.  
- Reviewer asks whether the method recovers similar basis (W) and activations (H) as IS-NMF in the single-resolution case.  
- Reviewer asks whether the proposed algorithm matches the speed and stability of IS-NMF’s multiplicative-update algorithm.
