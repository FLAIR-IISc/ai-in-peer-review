- Proposes modeling time-domain signals via a generative model of TF synthesis coefficients instead of conventional NMF on TF analysis coefficients.  
- This shift enables multi-resolution representations that standard NMF cannot feasibly handle.  
- The approach is interesting, well motivated, and the multi-resolution extension is particularly appealing.  
- Reviewer requests evaluation in the single-resolution setting to compare with IS-NMF.  
- Question: Do the learned factors W and H resemble those obtained by IS-NMF under comparable conditions?  
- Question: How do convergence speed and numerical stability compare with standard IS-NMF multiplicative updates?  
- Addressing these comparisons would clarify the relationship to established methods and strengthen the empirical claims.
