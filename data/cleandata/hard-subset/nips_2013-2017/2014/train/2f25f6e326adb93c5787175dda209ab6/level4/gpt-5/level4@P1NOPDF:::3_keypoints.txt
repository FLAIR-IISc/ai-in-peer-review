- Proposes a flexible time/frequency factorization framework applicable to arbitrary transforms and directly tied to the time-domain signal.
- Moves beyond STFT-based formulations to offer a more general theoretical interpretation.
- Paper is clearly written, accessible, and of good overall quality.
- Originality is above average, though the significance is not fully articulated.
- Experimental results and evaluation are the weakest aspect of the paper.
- Supplemental material is unconvincing and lacks rigorous empirical support.
- Fails to compare the proposed approach to the methods it builds on, weakening empirical claims.
- Despite limited experiments, the strong theoretical contribution makes the paper interesting and not fatally flawed.
