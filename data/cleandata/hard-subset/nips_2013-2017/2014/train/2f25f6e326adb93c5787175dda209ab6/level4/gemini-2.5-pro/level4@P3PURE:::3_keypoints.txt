- Introduces a flexible time-frequency factorization framework applicable to arbitrary transforms and directly tied to the time-domain signal.  
- Avoids reliance on STFT analysis-coefficient formulations.  
- Paper is well-written, clear, and accessible to domain readers.  
- The approach is original and the theoretical development is solid.  
- Primary weakness: evaluation and results are weak and lack comparisons to the methods the paper builds upon.  
- Supplemental-material results are not particularly convincing.  
- Broader significance and impact are not fully communicated due to limited empirical comparison.  
- Despite evaluation shortcomings, the strong theory makes the contribution interesting and valuable, though a more comprehensive empirical evaluation would strengthen the paper.
