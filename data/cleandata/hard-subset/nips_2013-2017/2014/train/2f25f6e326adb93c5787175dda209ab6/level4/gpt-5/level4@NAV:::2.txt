Whereas the standard NMF formulation models the TF analysis coefficients of a signal, the proposed approach models the time-domain waveform directly by specifying a generative model for TF synthesis coefficients. This makes multi-resolution representations attainable, which were not feasible in the conventional NMF setting.

This is an interesting paper. In particular, I am very fond of the multi-resolution extension.

Although the authors present results on multi-resolution signal decompositions, I am also interested in the method’s behavior in the single-resolution regime compared with IS-NMF. Does it recover similar W and H? Is the current algorithm as fast and as stable as the multiplicative update scheme for IS-NMF?
