- Proposes a flexible time-frequency factorization framework usable with arbitrary transforms and directly tied to the time-domain signal.  
- Offers a generative model for the raw signal, addressing limitations of STFT-based prior work.  
- Enables multi-resolution representations not possible with traditional NMF.  
- Theoretical development is strong, with a clear presentation and EM algorithms.  
- Paper is technically solid, accessible, and of above-average originality.  
- Experimental evaluation is limited and not fully convincing.  
- Lacks comparisons to established methods it builds upon.  
- Supplemental material is not particularly compelling.  
- Significance and broader applicability are not fully demonstrated or emphasized.  
- More extensive experiments and comparisons would substantially strengthen the contribution.
