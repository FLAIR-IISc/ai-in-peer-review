- Introduces an NMF extension that models time-domain signals via a generative model of TF synthesis coefficients rather than TF analysis coefficients.
- Novel formulation enables multi-resolution representations that standard NMF cannot provide.
- The multi-resolution extension is highlighted as a particularly valuable contribution.
- Paper lacks evaluation in the single-resolution case and should include it.
- Needs direct comparison to Itakura-Saito NMF (IS-NMF) to contextualize performance.
- Questions whether the proposed method yields similar basis (W) and activation (H) matrices to IS-NMF in single-resolution.
- Requests comparison of computational speed and numerical stability versus the standard multiplicative update algorithm for IS-NMF.
