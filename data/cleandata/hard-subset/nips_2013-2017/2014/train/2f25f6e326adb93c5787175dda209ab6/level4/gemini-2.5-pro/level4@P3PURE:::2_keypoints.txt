- Proposes a novel NMF framework that models time-domain signals via TF synthesis coefficients instead of standard TF analysis coefficients.
- Main advantage: enables multi-resolution representations that standard NMF cannot provide.
- Multi-resolution extension is a particularly interesting and notable contribution.
- Paper shows results for multi-resolution signal decompositions.
- Lacks evaluation in the single-resolution case and a direct comparison with standard Itakura-Saito NMF (IS-NMF).
- Unclear whether the method produces comparable factor matrices (W and H) to IS-NMF.
- Needs analysis of computational speed and stability relative to the multiplicative-update algorithm for IS-NMF.
