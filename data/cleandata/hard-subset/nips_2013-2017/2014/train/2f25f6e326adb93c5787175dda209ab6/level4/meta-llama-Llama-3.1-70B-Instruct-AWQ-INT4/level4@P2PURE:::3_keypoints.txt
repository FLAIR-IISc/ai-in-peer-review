- Proposes a thoughtful, flexible interpretation of time/frequency factorizations applicable to arbitrary transforms and directly tied to time-domain signals rather than STFT coefficients.  
- Paper is well-written and accessible to readers familiar with the area.  
- Originality is above average, though the significance of the work could be communicated more clearly.  
- Presentation and evaluation of experimental results are disappointing; supplemental material is unconvincing.  
- Missing comparisons to the methods it builds upon; such comparisons would have strengthened the paper.  
- Strong theoretical contributions that make the paper interesting and compensate for limited empirical validation; shortcomings are not fatal.
