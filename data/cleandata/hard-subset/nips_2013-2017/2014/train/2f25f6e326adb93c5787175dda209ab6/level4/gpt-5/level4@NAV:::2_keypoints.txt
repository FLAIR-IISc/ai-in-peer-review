- Proposed method models the time-domain waveform via a generative model for TF synthesis coefficients, unlike standard NMF which models TF analysis coefficients.
- This formulation enables multi-resolution representations that conventional NMF could not achieve.
- Reviewer finds the paper interesting and particularly likes the multi-resolution extension.
- Reviewer requests comparison in the single-resolution regime to IS-NMF: does the method recover similar W and H?
- Reviewer asks whether the current algorithm matches IS-NMF’s multiplicative-update scheme in speed and stability.
