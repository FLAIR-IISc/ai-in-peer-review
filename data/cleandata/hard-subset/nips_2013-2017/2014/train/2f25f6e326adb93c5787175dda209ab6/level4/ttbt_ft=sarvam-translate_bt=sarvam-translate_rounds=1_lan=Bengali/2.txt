While the general NMF framework considers a generative model of the TF analysis coefficients of a signal, the proposed framework considers a generative model of the time-domain signal of the TF synthesis coefficients, describing a generative model. This opens the door to our multi-resolution representation, which was not possible in the traditional NMF setting.

This is an interesting paper. In particular, I personally really like the idea of the multi-resolution extension.

Although the authors have presented some results on multi-resolution signal decomposition, I am interested in knowing the performance of the proposed method in the single-resolution case compared to IS-NMF. Does it give similar W and H? Is the current algorithm as fast and stable as IS-NMF's multiplicative update algorithm?
