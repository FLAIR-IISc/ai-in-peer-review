- Paper departs from standard NMF by modeling the time-domain signal via a generative model of TF synthesis coefficients, enabling multi-resolution representations unavailable in traditional NMF.

- Reviewer finds the paper interesting and particularly likes the multi-resolution extension.

- Reviewer requests evaluation in the single-resolution setting to permit direct comparison with IS-NMF.

- Ask how the proposed method performs in the single-resolution case compared with IS-NMF.

- Ask whether the learned factors W and H resemble those obtained by IS-NMF.

- Ask whether the proposed algorithm is as fast and numerically stable as the standard multiplicative update algorithm for IS-NMF.

- Notes that multi-resolution results are promising but addressing these questions is needed to clarify benefits and trade-offs relative to established baselines.
