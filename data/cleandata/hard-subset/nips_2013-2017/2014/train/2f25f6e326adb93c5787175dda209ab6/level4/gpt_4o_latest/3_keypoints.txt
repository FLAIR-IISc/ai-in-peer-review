- Introduces an adaptable framework for time/frequency factorizations applicable to arbitrary transforms while preserving a direct link to the time-domain signal.  
- Offers an insightful interpretation that moves beyond STFT-analysis-coefficient formulations.  
- Paper is well-written, accessible to experts, and of high quality.  
- Originality is above average, though the paper does not fully convey the significance of the contribution.  
- Results and evaluation are underwhelming and constitute the weakest aspect.  
- Supplemental material is not particularly compelling.  
- Lacks empirical comparisons to the methods it builds upon.  
- Despite limited results, the theoretical development is strong and makes the paper intriguing and valuable.
