- Introduces a synthesis-based time–frequency factorization framework applicable to arbitrary transforms and tied directly to the time-domain signal.
- Moves beyond STFT-centered formulations.
- Manuscript is clear, accessible, and technically solid.
- Originality is above average but practical significance is not fully conveyed.
- Experimental section is disappointing and the supplemental material is unpersuasive.
- Paper lacks empirical comparisons against the methods it builds upon.
- The theoretical development is interesting and strong enough to carry the paper.
- Stronger empirical validation and comparative baselines would notably strengthen the work.
- Overall: a good, well-written paper with solid theory; absence of more results is not fatal.
