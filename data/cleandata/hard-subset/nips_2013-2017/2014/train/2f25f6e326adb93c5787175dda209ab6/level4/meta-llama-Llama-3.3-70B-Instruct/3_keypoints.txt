- Proposes a flexible time/frequency factorization framework that does not rely on STFT analysis coefficients and applies to any transform.  
- Establishes a direct connection between the proposed factorization and the time-domain signal.  
- Writing quality and clarity are high, making the paper accessible to specialists.  
- Originality is above average, offering novel theoretical contributions.  
- The significance of the work could be communicated more effectively.  
- Presentation of results and evaluation is weak; supplementary materials are not fully persuasive.  
- Lacks comparative experiments against prior methods, which would have strengthened the paper.  
- Despite limited empirical validation, the strong theoretical foundation makes the manuscript compelling.
