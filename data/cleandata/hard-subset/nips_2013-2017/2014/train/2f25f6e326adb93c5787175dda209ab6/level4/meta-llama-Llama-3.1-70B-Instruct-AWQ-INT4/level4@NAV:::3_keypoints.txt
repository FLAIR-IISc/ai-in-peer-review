- Proposes a flexible time/frequency factorization framework that generalizes beyond STFT and links directly to the time-domain signal.  
- Avoids reliance on analysis coefficients of STFT transforms, making the approach applicable to arbitrary transforms.  
- Manuscript is well-written, accessible to experts, and demonstrates above-average originality.  
- Theoretical development is substantial and engaging.  
- Significance and practical impact are not conveyed as effectively as they could be.  
- Results and evaluation are weak; supplementary material is unpersuasive.  
- Lacks empirical comparisons with prior methods it builds upon, which would have strengthened the paper.
