- Framework departs from standard NMF by using a generative model of the time-domain signal, modeling TF synthesis coefficients instead of TF analysis coefficients.
- This change of perspective enables exploration of multi-resolution representations that traditional NMF cannot access.
- The multi-resolution extension is an intriguing and compelling idea.
- Authors provide results demonstrating the method’s potential on multi-resolution signal decompositions.
- The paper lacks evaluation in the single-resolution case and comparison with Iterative Shrinkage NMF (IS-NMF).
- Important open questions: does the method recover similar W and H matrices as IS-NMF, and is its algorithm as fast and stable as IS-NMF’s multiplicative updates?
