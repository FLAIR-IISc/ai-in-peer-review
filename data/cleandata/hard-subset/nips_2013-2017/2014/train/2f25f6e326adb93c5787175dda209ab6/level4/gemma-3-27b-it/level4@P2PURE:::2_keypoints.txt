- Proposes shifting the generative model from TF analysis coefficients to the time-domain via a generative model of TF synthesis coefficients.  
- This modeling shift enables multi-resolution representations not possible with traditional NMF.  
- Reviewer finds the multi-resolution extension particularly appealing.  
- Paper demonstrates results on multi-resolution signal decomposition.  
- Reviewer requests evaluation of the method in a single-resolution scenario.  
- Reviewer asks for a comparison to Iterative Shrinkage-NMF (IS-NMF), specifically whether it yields comparable W and H matrices.  
- Reviewer asks about algorithmic speed and stability compared to the standard multiplicative update algorithm used for IS-NMF.
