- Proposes a generative model of the time-domain signal via time-frequency (TF) synthesis coefficients instead of modeling TF analysis coefficients as in traditional NMF.
- This modeling shift enables multi-resolution representations that standard NMF cannot readily provide.
- The multi-resolution extension is a particularly appealing and novel aspect of the work.
- The paper demonstrates results on multi-resolution signal decompositions.
- Reviewer requests evaluation in a single-resolution scenario and comparison to Iterative Shrinkage-NMF (IS-NMF).
- Questions whether the resulting W and H matrices resemble those from IS-NMF.
- Asks whether the proposed algorithm matches IS-NMF’s multiplicative-update algorithm in speed and stability.
