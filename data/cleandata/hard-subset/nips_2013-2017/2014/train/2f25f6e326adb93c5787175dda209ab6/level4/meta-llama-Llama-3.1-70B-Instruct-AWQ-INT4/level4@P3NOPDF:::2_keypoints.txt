- Proposes a novel NMF extension that models the time-domain signal via a generative model of TF synthesis coefficients instead of TF analysis coefficients.  
- This shift enables multi-resolution representations that were not feasible in traditional NMF.  
- Authors present promising results for multi-resolution signal decompositions.  
- Reviewer requests evaluation in the single-resolution case and comparison to existing IS-NMF.  
- Specifically asks if the method yields similar factor matrices (W, H) to IS-NMF and whether it matches IS-NMF’s speed and stability (multiplicative updates).  
- Modeling the time-domain signal directly is a significant and valuable departure from traditional NMF.  
- With additional comparisons and evaluations, the approach could have significant impact in signal processing.
