Whereas standard NMF posits a generative model over the time–frequency (TF) analysis coefficients of a signal, the proposed approach models the time-domain waveform directly by specifying a generative model for the TF synthesis coefficients. This shift enables multi-resolution representations that are not achievable in the conventional NMF framework.
