- Proposes a flexible interpretation of time/frequency factorizations applicable to arbitrary transforms and tied to the time-domain signal.  
- Offers a more generalizable framework compared to existing STFT-coefficient–based formulations.  
- Paper is well-written and clear for readers familiar with the area.  
- Originality is above average.  
- The significance of the contribution is not communicated as effectively as it could be.  
- Presentation and empirical evaluation are disappointing.  
- Supplemental material is unconvincing.  
- Lacks quantitative or qualitative comparisons with the methods it builds upon.  
- The omission of comparisons is not fatal but weakens the paper.  
- Theoretical development is substantial, interesting, and a valuable contribution.  
- More comprehensive results and comparisons would substantially strengthen the paper.
