- Proposes a thoughtful reinterpretation of time/frequency factorizations tied directly to the time-domain signal rather than STFT analysis coefficients.  
- Introduces a flexible framework applicable to arbitrary transforms.  
- Paper is clearly written and of good quality for readers in the area.  
- Originality is above average.  
- Significance of the contribution is not fully conveyed in the paper.  
- Results and evaluation are disappointing and the supplementary material is unconvincing.  
- Lacks empirical comparisons to the existing methods it builds upon.  
- Theoretical development is strong and makes the work interesting despite limited experiments.
