- Proposes a flexible framework for time/frequency factorizations applicable to arbitrary transforms and directly tied to the time-domain signal.  
- Contrasts with existing STFT-based formulations, offering greater flexibility.  
- Paper is well-written and clear for readers familiar with the field.  
- Demonstrates above-average originality.  
- Broader significance and practical impact are not fully communicated.  
- Primary weakness is experimental evaluation: supplementary results are unconvincing.  
- Fails to compare the proposed method against the prior work it builds on.  
- Theoretical development is strong and makes the contribution interesting despite weak experiments.  
- Would be substantially improved by more comprehensive empirical results and direct comparisons.
