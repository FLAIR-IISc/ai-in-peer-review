- Introduces a generalized time/frequency factorization framework applicable to arbitrary transforms directly coupled to the time-domain signal.
- Contrasts with established STFT-based methods by not relying on analysis coefficients.
- Paper is well-articulated, high quality, and accessible to domain experts.
- Originality is commendable, though the paper could better communicate its overall significance.
- Major weakness: experimental results and evaluation are insufficient.
- Supplementary material is not fully convincing.
- Omits comparative analysis against the methods it builds upon.
- Theoretical contribution is substantial and makes the paper interesting despite empirical gaps.
- A more thorough set of results and comparisons would have significantly enhanced the paper.
