- Enables multi-resolution signal representations that standard NMF cannot achieve.  
- The multi-resolution extension is interesting and compelling.  
- Requests clarification on the single-resolution case: how do the proposed factor matrices (W and H) compare to IS-NMF — do they align?

- Requests assessment of numerical stability/robustness: is the algorithm as stable and robust as IS-NMF?
