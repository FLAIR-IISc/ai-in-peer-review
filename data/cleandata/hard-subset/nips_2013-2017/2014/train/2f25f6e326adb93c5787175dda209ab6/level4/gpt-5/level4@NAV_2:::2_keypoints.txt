- Proposed method models the time-domain waveform via a generative prior on TF synthesis coefficients rather than TF analysis coefficients, enabling multi-resolution representations not possible with conventional NMF.  
- Reviewer finds the paper compelling and particularly appreciates the multi-resolution extension.  
- Requests evaluation in the single-resolution regime to compare performance with IS-NMF.  
- Asks whether the learned W (bases) and H (activations) resemble those from IS-NMF.  
- Asks whether the proposed algorithm matches IS-NMF multiplicative updates in speed and stability.
