- Proposed method models the time-domain signal via a generative model of time-frequency synthesis coefficients rather than modeling time-frequency analysis coefficients as in conventional NMF.  
- This modeling distinction enables multi-resolution representations that traditional NMF frameworks cannot attain.  
- The multi-resolution extension is an intriguing and particularly compelling contribution.  
- Reviewer requests empirical evaluation in the single-resolution case versus IS-NMF to assess comparability of learned W and H matrices.  
- Reviewer asks whether the proposed algorithm matches the speed and stability of IS-NMF’s multiplicative update algorithm.
