- Proposes a generative model for the time-domain signal via TF synthesis coefficients, unlike conventional NMF which models TF analysis coefficients.  
- Key advantage: enables multi-resolution representations missing in traditional NMF.  
- The multi-resolution extension is novel and a strong contribution.  
- Evaluation focuses on multi-resolution decompositions; analysis lacks single-resolution experiments.  
- Recommends direct comparison to a standard baseline (e.g., IS-NMF) to assess W and H equivalence and to compare convergence speed and stability with standard multiplicative updates.
