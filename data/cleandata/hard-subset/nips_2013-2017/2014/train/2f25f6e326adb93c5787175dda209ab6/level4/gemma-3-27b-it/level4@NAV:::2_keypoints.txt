- Novel NMF framework that models the time-domain signal via a generative model of time-frequency (TF) synthesis coefficients instead of modeling TF analysis coefficients.
- This approach enables multi-resolution representations that standard NMF cannot inherently provide.
- The work is compelling, and the potential for multi-resolution analysis is particularly promising.
- Reviewer asks for a performance comparison against Iterative Shrinkage-NMF (IS-NMF) in a single-resolution setting, specifically whether the resulting W and H matrices show similar characteristics.
- Reviewer requests an evaluation of the algorithm’s speed and stability relative to the multiplicative-update rules used for IS-NMF.
- Core innovation reiterated: shifting generative modeling from TF analysis coefficients to the time-domain via TF synthesis coefficients to achieve multi-resolution analysis.
