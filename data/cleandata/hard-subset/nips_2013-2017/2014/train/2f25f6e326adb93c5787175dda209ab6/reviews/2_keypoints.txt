- Proposed framework models the time-domain signal via generative TF synthesis coefficients rather than TF analysis coefficients used in regular NMF.  
- This approach enables multi-resolution representations that traditional NMF cannot provide.  
- Reviewer finds the multi-resolution extension interesting and appealing.  
- Authors present some multi-resolution decomposition results, but more evaluation is desired.  
- Reviewer requests comparison in the single-resolution case with IS-NMF (do W and H match?).  
- Reviewer asks whether the proposed algorithm matches IS-NMF’s multiplicative updates in speed and stability.
