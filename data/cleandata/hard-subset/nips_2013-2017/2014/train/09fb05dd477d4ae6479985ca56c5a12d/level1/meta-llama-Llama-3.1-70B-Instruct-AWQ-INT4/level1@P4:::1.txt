This paper proposes Large Scale Detection through Adaptation (LSDA), an algorithm that transforms an image classifier into an object detector. The main idea is to learn the difference between the two tasks and transfer this knowledge to classifiers for categories without bounding box annotated data. The authors relate their work to previous research on domain adaptation, multiple instance learning, and object detection.
