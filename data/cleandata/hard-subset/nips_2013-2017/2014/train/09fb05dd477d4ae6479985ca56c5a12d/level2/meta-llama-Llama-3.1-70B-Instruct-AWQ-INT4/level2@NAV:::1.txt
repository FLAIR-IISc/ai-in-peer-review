This paper presents a novel approach to object detection, Large Scale Detection through Adaptation (LSDA), which enables the transformation of image classifiers into object detectors without requiring bounding box annotations for all categories. The authors propose a multi-stage algorithm that uses corresponding classification and detection data to learn the change from a classification CNN network to a detection CNN network and applies that difference to future classifiers for which there is no available detection data.

The paper is well-written and clearly explains the problem, the proposed approach, and the experimental results. The authors provide a thorough analysis of the related work and demonstrate the effectiveness of their approach through quantitative results on the ILSVRC2013 detection dataset. The paper also includes a detailed error analysis and discusses the limitations of the approach.

* The paper presents a novel and effective approach to object detection that can be applied to a large number of categories without requiring bounding box annotations.
* The authors provide a thorough analysis of the related work and demonstrate the effectiveness of their approach through quantitative results on a challenging dataset.
