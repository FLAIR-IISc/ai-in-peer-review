- Presents a framework to transfer classification-trained deep networks to object detection, achieving 4th place on ImageNet LSVRC-2013.
- Manuscript is well-written and methodology is straightforward to understand.
- Proposed CNN architecture is novel and practical to implement.
- Overall contribution is strong.
- Central adaptation method lacks rigorous, principled justification.
- No clear motivation or interpretation for modeling adapted detection weights as Wj^c + W{avg}; meaning of the offset is unexplained.
- Unclear why the kNN variant outperforms due to the above conceptual gaps.
- Experimental comparisons are unfair: evaluation uses a small validation subset rather than the standard test set.
- Reported performance improvements on the small subset lack statistical significance and weaken the claims.
