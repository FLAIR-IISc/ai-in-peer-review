- Proposes a framework to adapt deep neural networks trained for image classification to object detection.
- Evaluated on ImageNet LSVRC-2013 and achieved 4th place in the object detection challenge.
- Paper is clearly presented and the proposed CNN structure is simple and easy to implement.
- Overall paper quality is good.
- Adaptation method requires stronger, more principled justification and clearer motivation.
- It is unclear why adapted detection weights are formed as W_j^c + W_avg; the physical meaning of this "offset" is not explained.
- No clear explanation for why the kNN variant is superior to alternatives.
- Comparison with prior work may be unfair because the authors use part of the validation set rather than the test set.
- The small size of the val2 set means reported performance gains lack statistical justification.
- Because of differing experimental settings and insufficient methodological explanation, the paper’s contributions are not fully validated.
