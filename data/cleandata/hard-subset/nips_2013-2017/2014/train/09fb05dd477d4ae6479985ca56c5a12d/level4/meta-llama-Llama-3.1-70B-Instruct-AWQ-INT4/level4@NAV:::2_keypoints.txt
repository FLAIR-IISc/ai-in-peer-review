- Introduces a framework to adapt image-classification CNNs to object detection and achieved 4th place on ImageNet LSVRC-2013 detection challenge.
- Paper is well-presented, easy to follow, and proposes an interesting, implementable CNN structure.
- Overall quality is good, but the primary contribution—the adaptation method—needs stronger justification and clearer motivation.
- Section 3.2 lacks explanation for summing W_j^c and W_avg for adapted detection weights and the physical meaning of the "offset parameter".
- The superiority of the kNN variant is not explained, making its advantage unclear.
- Comparisons are compromised by evaluating on only a small subset of the validation set rather than the test set, leading to potentially unfair results.
- The small size of the val2 set makes reported performance gains statistically insignificant despite the authors' footnote about RCNN.
