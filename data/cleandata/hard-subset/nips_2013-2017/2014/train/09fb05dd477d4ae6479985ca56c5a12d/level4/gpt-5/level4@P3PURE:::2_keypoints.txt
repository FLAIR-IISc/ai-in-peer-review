- Proposes a framework to adapt image-classification CNNs for object detection.
- Achieves 4th place on the ImageNet LSVRC-2013 object detection challenge.
- Paper is clearly presented and generally easy to follow.
- Proposed CNN architecture is interesting and simple to implement.
- Overall quality of the paper is good.
- Main concern: adaptation method lacks strong, principled motivation.
- Unclear why detection weights are defined as Wj^c + Wavg and what the offset parameter physically means.
- Unclear rationale for why the kNN variant should yield better performance.
- Empirical comparison fairness concern: authors use part of the validation set instead of the test set.
- Small val2 set and differing evaluation settings mean reported performance gains are not statistically justified.
