- Proposes a framework to adapt classification-trained deep networks to object detection.  
- Evaluated on ImageNet LSVRC-2013 and ranked 4th in the detection challenge.  
- Paper is well-organized, easy to follow, and presents a simple, effective CNN structure.  
- Overall manuscript quality is satisfactory.  
- Major concern: the adaptation method lacks comprehensive justification and clear motivations.  
- Specific ambiguity: why weights are formed by summing W_j^c and W_avg, and the physical meaning of the "offset parameter" are not explained.  
- The claimed superiority of the kNN variant is not well-justified.  
- Comparison fairness issue: experiments use only part of the validation set (val2) rather than the test set, making results potentially unfair and statistically insignificant due to small sample size.
