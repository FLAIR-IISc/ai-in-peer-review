- Proposal: Framework to adapt image-classification DNNs to object detection; simple, easy-to-implement CNN structure.
- Evaluation: Tested on ImageNet LSVRC-2013, ranked 4th in the object detection challenge.
- Presentation: Paper is well-presented and generally easy to follow; overall good quality.
- Main concern 1: Adaptation method lacks rigorous justification and principled motivation.
- Main concern 1 details: Unclear why adapted weights use W_j^c + W_{avg} and the physical meaning of the 'offset parameter' is not explained; unclear why the kNN variant is superior.
- Main concern 2: Comparison fairness: authors used part of the validation set (val2) rather than the test set, making comparisons to prior work potentially unfair.
- Main concern 2 details: Small size of val2 makes reported performance gains statistically insignificant; RCNN reportedly has similar performance on val2.
- Recommendation: Provide a more detailed, principled explanation of the adaptation method and perform fair comparisons on the test set.
