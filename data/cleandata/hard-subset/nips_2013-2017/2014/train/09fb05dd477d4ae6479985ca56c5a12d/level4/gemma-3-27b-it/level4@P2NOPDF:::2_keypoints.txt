- Presents a framework to adapt image-classification CNNs for object detection, achieving strong results (4th in ImageNet LSVRC-2013 detection).  
- Paper is well-written and largely easy to understand.  
- Proposed CNN architecture is simple and easy to implement.  
- Overall contribution is solid and comparable to leading methods.  
- Primary concern 1: Lack of clear justification for the adaptation method, especially the summation weighting of W_j^c and W_avg and the role of the offset parameter.  
- Primary concern 1 (cont.): The intuition and principled explanation for why the kNN variant improves performance are not provided.  
- Primary concern 2: Comparative evaluation may be unfair—authors used part of the validation set rather than the standardized test set for comparisons.  
- Primary concern 2 (cont.): Small size of val2 limits statistical significance; results should be reported on the standard test set for rigorous comparison.  
- Recommendation: Address methodological justification and evaluate on a consistent, standardized test set to strengthen the paper.
