- Proposes a framework to adapt image-classification DNNs for object detection tasks.
- Evaluation on ImageNet LSVRC-2013 yields 4th place in the object detection challenge.
- Paper is well-organized and generally easy to understand.
- Introduces a CNN structure that is interesting and straightforward to implement.
- Overall paper quality is good.
- Primary contribution (adaptation method) lacks strong, systematic justification and motivation.
- Unclear why adapted weights are defined as Wj^c + W_avg and no physical interpretation of the offset parameter is provided.
- Not explained why the kNN variant yields better performance.
- Comparison with other methods is problematic because part of the validation set (not the test set) was used, making comparisons unfair.
- Small val2 set size makes reported performance gains statistically unreliable despite the authors' footnote about RCNN.
- Summary: method is competitive with state-of-the-art but comparisons use different settings and the adaptation technique needs clearer explanation.
