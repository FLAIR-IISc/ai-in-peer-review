- Proposes a framework to adapt image-classification CNNs into object detectors.  
- Reports 4th place on the ImageNet LSVRC-2013 detection challenge.  
- Presentation is clear and the proposed CNN architecture is interesting and straightforward to implement.  
- Major concern: the adaptation method lacks principled justification—unclear why detection weights are written as W_j^c + W_avg, the “offset” W_avg is unexplained, and the kNN variant’s advantage is unclear.  
- Major concern: experimental comparisons use a portion of the validation set rather than the test set, harming fairness and statistical support given the small split.  
- Overall: method attains near–state-of-the-art performance but evaluations are under different settings and the adaptation mechanism needs clearer explanation.
