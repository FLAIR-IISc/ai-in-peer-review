- Presents a framework to adapt image-classification CNNs for object detection, achieving 4th place on ImageNet LSVRC-2013.  
- Paper is clearly written, easy to understand, and the proposed CNN structure is interesting and straightforward to implement.  
- Overall contribution is solid and competitive.  
- Primary concern: adaptation method lacks principled justification—no clear rationale for computing detection weights as W_j^c + W_avg.  
- The physical interpretation of the “offset parameter” and the reason kNN variant performs better are unexplained.  
- Secondary concern: comparisons may be unfair because experiments use part of the validation set instead of the standard test set.  
- Small val subset prevents statistically significant conclusions about reported performance gains.  
- Recommendation: provide deeper explanation of adaptation method and revisit comparative evaluations for fairness.
