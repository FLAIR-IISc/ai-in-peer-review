- Framework adapts pre-trained image-classification DNNs to object detection, achieving a strong 4th place on ImageNet LSVRC-2013.  
- Paper is well-written, accessible, and presents an interesting, straightforward CNN architecture — overall a solid contribution.  
- Core adaptation method requires stronger justification; key design choices and underlying rationale are not thoroughly explained.  
- The summation of weights W_j^c and W_avg in Sec. 3.2 is unexplained and its role as an “offset parameter” (and the advantage of the kNN variant) is unclear.  
- Comparative evaluation may be flawed: authors use a small validation subset (val2) rather than the standard test set, limiting statistical significance despite a footnote about RCNN results.  
- Overall promising and competitive work, but needs deeper methodological explanation and fairer comparative evaluation to be stronger.
