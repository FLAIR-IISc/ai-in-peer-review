- Proposes a framework to adapt image-classification DNNs for object detection.
- Evaluated on ImageNet LSVRC-2013, achieving 4th place in the detection challenge.
- Paper is clearly presented, easy to follow; CNN structure is interesting and simple to implement.
- Main concern: the adaptation method lacks principled justification and clearer motivation.
- Specific issue: unclear rationale and physical meaning for weighting with W_j^c + W_avg (the “offset parameter”) and why the kNN variant improves performance.
- Experimental concern: comparisons use a portion of the validation set instead of the standard test set, yielding an unfair comparison.
- Authors note RCNN shows similar performance on val2, but val2 is too small for statistical significance.
- Overall: competitive approach, but needs stronger methodological justification and fairer experimental comparisons.
