- Proposes a framework to adapt image-classification DNNs to object detection, ranking 4th on ImageNet LSVRC-2013 detection challenge.  
- Paper is well-presented, easy to follow, with a simple-to-implement CNN; overall quality is good.  
- Main contribution (adaptation method) lacks rigorous justification and clear underlying motivation.  
- Unclear why adapted detection weights are W_j^c + W_avg (Section 3.2) and what the 'offset parameter' physically represents.  
- No explanation for why the kNN variant is superior; needs clearer rationale.  
- Comparison with other methods is questionable because the authors use a small validation set (val2) instead of the test set.  
- Performance gains on val2 may be statistically insignificant; authors should use the test set or provide more detailed validation analysis.  
- Overall result is comparable to state-of-the-art but requires fair comparisons and stronger methodological explanation to be fully convincing.
