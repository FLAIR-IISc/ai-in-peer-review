- Presents a framework to adapt image-classification CNNs for object detection.  
- Evaluated on ImageNet LSVRC-2013 and ranks 4th in the detection challenge.  
- Paper is clearly written; proposed CNN architecture is interesting and straightforward to implement.  
- Overall paper quality is good.  
- Central adaptation approach lacks principled motivation and stronger theoretical justification.  
- It is unclear why adapted detection weights are defined as W_j^c + W_avg and what the “offset” parameter means.  
- No clear explanation for why the kNN variant yields better performance.  
- Comparisons use part of the validation set instead of the test set, making them less fair.  
- The val2 set is small, so reported performance gains are not statistically well justified despite the RCNN note.  
- Overall: method is competitive but needs clearer adaptation rationale and fairer evaluation settings.
