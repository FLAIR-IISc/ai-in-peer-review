- Presents a framework to adapt pre-trained image-classification CNNs to object detection, achieving 4th place on ImageNet LSVRC-2013.  
- Paper is well-written and the proposed CNN structure is interesting and straightforward to implement.  
- Overall a solid contribution with competitive results.  
- Primary concern 1: insufficient justification for key design choices in the adaptation method.  
- Unclear why the authors sum W_j^c and W_avg for the adapted detection task and what the resulting “offset parameter” represents.  
- Unclear why the kNN variant improves performance; lacks principled explanation.  
- Primary concern 2: fairness of comparative evaluation — authors used part of the validation set for hyperparameter tuning/model selection instead of a held-out test set.  
- The small validation subset undermines the statistical significance of observed gains; a dedicated held-out test set is needed for rigorous comparison.  
- Addressing the methodological justifications and evaluation protocol would significantly strengthen the paper.
