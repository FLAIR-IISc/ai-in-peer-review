- Proposes a framework to adapt image-classification DNNs for object detection, achieving 4th place on ImageNet LSVRC-2013 detection.
- Paper is clearly presented, easy to follow, with an interesting and straightforward CNN structure; overall good quality.
- Main contribution (adaptation method) lacks rigorous justification and systematic motivation.
- Unclear why adapted detection weights are Wj^c + W_avg; the physical meaning of the “offset parameter” is not explained and the kNN variant’s superiority is not justified.
- Comparisons are unfair because the authors use part of the validation set instead of the test set, making results not directly comparable.
- Small validation subset (val2) makes reported gains statistically insignificant and the RCNN footnote does not sufficiently address this.
