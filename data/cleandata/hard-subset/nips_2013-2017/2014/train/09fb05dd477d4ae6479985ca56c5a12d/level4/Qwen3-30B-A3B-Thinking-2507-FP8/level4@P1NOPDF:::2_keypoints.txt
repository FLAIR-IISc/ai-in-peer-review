- Proposes a framework to adapt pretrained image-classification DNNs to object detection; method ranked 4th in ImageNet LSVRC-2013 detection.
- Paper is well-structured, accessible, and uses a straightforward, easy-to-implement CNN architecture; overall high quality.
- Core adaptation method lacks theoretical justification and rigorous motivation.
- The formulation W_detect = W_j^c + W_avg (Section 3.2) is unexplained; the physical interpretation of the offset parameter is missing.
- The reason the kNN variant outperforms other approaches is unclear and not justified.
- Comparison to competing methods is unfair because evaluation is done on a validation subset rather than the official test set.
- The small val subset makes reported gains statistically insignificant; authors should provide full test-set results and address the protocol discrepancy.
