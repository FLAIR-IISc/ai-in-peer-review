- Proposes a framework to adapt image-classification DNNs for object detection.  
- Evaluation on ImageNet LSVRC-2013 ranks 4th in the object detection challenge.  
- Paper is clearly written and the CNN architecture is interesting and easy to implement.  
- Overall paper quality is good.  
- Main concern: the adaptation method lacks principled motivation and stronger justification.  
- Unclear why adapted detection weights are Wj^c + Wavg and what the “offset parameter” means; explanation of why the k-NN variant works is missing.  
- Comparison concern: authors evaluate on part of the validation set instead of the test set, making comparisons unfair.  
- Small validation subset (val2) undermines statistical justification for reported performance gains.  
- Summary: method is competitive with state-of-the-art, but comparisons use different settings and the adaptation mechanism needs clearer explanation.
