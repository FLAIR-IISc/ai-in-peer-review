- Proposed framework adapts image-classification pretrained DNNs to object detection and achieved 4th place on ImageNet LSVRC-2013 detection.
- Paper is clearly presented, easy to follow; proposed CNN structure is interesting and simple to implement.
- Overall paper quality is good.
- Main adaptation method (claimed primary contribution) lacks robust justification and principled motivation.
- Specific technical concern: the rationale for weighting with W_j^c + W_avg and the meaning of the “offset parameter” are not explained, leaving the kNN variant’s improvement unexplained.
- Evaluation/comparison may be flawed because experiments use part of the validation set instead of the held-out test set, and the small validation subset makes observed gains statistically insignificant.
- Recommendation: clarify the evaluation setup and provide a more thorough explanation of the adaptation method’s underlying principles.
