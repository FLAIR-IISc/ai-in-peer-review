- Proposes a framework to adapt image-classification CNNs for object detection.  
- Evaluated on ImageNet LSVRC-2013 and ranks 4th in the detection challenge.  
- Paper is well-written and the proposed CNN architecture is interesting and straightforward to implement.  
- Overall quality of the paper is good.  
- Major concern: the central adaptation method lacks principled justification and theoretical/intuition for design choices.  
- Specific concern: no explanation for formulating adapted weights as Wj^c + W_{avg} or for the ‘offset parameter’, leaving the kNN variant’s superior performance unexplained.  
- Major concern: empirical comparisons may be unfair because evaluation uses a small validation subset instead of the standard full test set, raising questions about statistical significance.
