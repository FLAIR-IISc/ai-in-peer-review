- Proposes LSDA to adapt image-classification CNNs to object detection, achieving 4th place on ImageNet LSVRC-2013 detection.
- Paper is well-structured, clearly written, and uses a simple, practical CNN architecture that’s easy to implement.
- Main methodological concern: adaptation approach lacks sufficient theoretical justification and principled motivation.
- Specific formulation issue: expressing detection weights as Wj^c + W_avg (Section 3.2) is not adequately explained; the offset parameter’s physical meaning is unclear and why the kNN variant helps is not justified.
- Experimental concern: comparisons are unfair because the authors evaluated on part of the validation set rather than the official test set.
- Small validation subset prevents statistical validation of reported gains, complicating direct comparisons with other methods.
- Recommendation: provide a more rigorous theoretical foundation for the adaptation method and perform fairer comparisons using the same evaluation protocol as prior work.
