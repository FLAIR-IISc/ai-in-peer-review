- Proposes a framework to adapt classification DNNs to object detection; ranks 4th on ImageNet LSVRC-2013.
- Paper is clearly presented; proposed CNN structure is interesting and easy to implement; overall quality is good.
- Main contribution (the adaptation method) lacks principled justification and clear motivation.
- It’s unclear why adapted detection weights are formed as W_j^c + W_avg, the physical meaning of the "offset" is not explained, and the advantage of the kNN variant is unexplained.
- Comparisons are unfair: authors used part of the validation set rather than the test set, and the small validation subset makes reported gains not statistically justified.
- Overall: method achieves comparable state-of-the-art performance but comparisons use different settings and the adaptation method needs clearer explanation.
