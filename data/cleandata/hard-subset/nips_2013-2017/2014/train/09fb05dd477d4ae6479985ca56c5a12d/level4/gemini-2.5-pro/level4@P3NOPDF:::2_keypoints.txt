- Adapts pre-trained image-classification DNNs to object detection and achieves 4th place on ILSVRC-2013 detection, showing strong empirical performance.  
- Paper is clearly written and well-organized, making the approach accessible.  
- Proposed CNN adaptation is simple and practical, aiding reproducibility and implementation.  
- Lacks principled/theoretical justification for the core adaptation, particularly the summation of class-specific weights with an average offset (W_avg) and its intended role.  
- Experimental comparison may be unfair: results are reported on a small val2 subset instead of the standard test set, which could make performance gains statistically insignificant and comparisons (e.g., to R-CNN) inconclusive.  
- Recommends deeper theoretical motivation and evaluation on the standard test set or more rigorous statistical analysis to strengthen the paper.
