- Proposes a framework to adapt image-classification DNNs for object detection.  
- Evaluation on ImageNet LSVRC-2013 yields 4th place in the object detection challenge.  
- Paper is clearly presented and generally accessible with a straightforward-to-implement CNN structure.  
- Overall paper quality is good.  
- Core adaptation method lacks sufficient theoretical justification and rigorous motivation.  
- Section 3.2 does not explain why adapted detection weights sum W_j^c and W_avg or the physical meaning of the "offset parameter," leaving the kNN variant’s superiority unclear.  
- Comparisons use only a portion of the validation set (not the test set), producing potentially unfair comparisons.  
- The small validation subset prevents statistically significant validation of gains and leads to inconsistent comparison settings.
