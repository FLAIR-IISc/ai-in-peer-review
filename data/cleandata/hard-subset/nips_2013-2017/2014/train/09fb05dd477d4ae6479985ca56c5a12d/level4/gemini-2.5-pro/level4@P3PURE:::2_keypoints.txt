- Proposes a framework to adapt image-classification CNNs for object detection, evaluated on ImageNet LSVRC-2013.  
- Method achieved 4th place in the ImageNet object detection challenge.  
- Paper is clearly presented and the proposed CNN architecture is interesting and simple to implement.  
- Overall work quality is good.  
- Major concern: adaptation method lacks principled justification and motivation for design choices.  
- Specific unclear point: why adapted detection weights are formulated as Wj^c + W{avg} and the intuition for this "offset parameter" is not explained.  
- Unclear why the kNN variant outperforms the proposed method.  
- Major concern: comparisons are not on a level playing field—authors evaluate on a validation subset while competitors use the test set.  
- The `val2` subset is small, so reported gains may not be statistically significant.  
- Recommendation: strengthen the paper by providing clearer explanations for the adaptation method and conducting evaluations under standard settings for fair comparison.
