Proposed framework to adapt image-classification-trained deep nets for object detection.  
Evaluation on ImageNet LSVRC-2013 shows the method ranks 4th in the detection challenge.  
Paper is clearly presented, easy to follow; CNN architecture is interesting and simple to implement; overall quality is good.  
Main contribution (adaptation method) lacks principled justification and detailed motivation.  
Unclear why adapted detection weights are Wj^c + W_avg in Section 3.2 and what the physical meaning of the "offset" parameter is.  
No clear explanation for why the kNN variant outperforms other variants.  
Comparisons are unfair because the authors used part of the validation set instead of the test set.  
Claim that RCNN has similar performance on val2 is not statistically justified due to val2’s small size.  
Overall: method achieves comparable performance to state-of-the-art, but comparisons use different settings and the adaptation method needs better explanation.
