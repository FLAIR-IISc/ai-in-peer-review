- Proposes a CNN incorporating attention-based top-down feedback.  
- Shows state-of-the-art performance on CIFAR datasets.  
- Paper is novel and interesting in model design and vision application.  
- Forward-looking approach combining bottom-up and top-down signals for training.  
- Evaluates the model across time and uses gated signals to constrain representations.  
- Representation quality hinges on the attention policy; authors should suggest or demonstrate failure cases.  
- Experiments include visualizations that help interpret the model.  
- Method is more complex than feedforward CNNs; main concern is lack of ILSVRC results to show scalability.  
- Recommends experiments on larger datasets for practical insights; model is worth further study.
