- Model: convnet with feedback connections that gate activation of some filters to implement visual attention.  
- Training: the gating/attention policy is learned with reinforcement learning on top of the convnet.  
- Novelty: no single component is highly novel, but the combined approach is well-chosen and effectively executed.  
- Results: achieves state-of-the-art performance on CIFAR.  
- Significance: provides empirical support that feedback/attention is a promising direction for vision models.  
- Presentation: paper quality and clarity are good.  
- Recommendation: authors should scale the approach to larger problems/datasets (e.g., ImageNet).
