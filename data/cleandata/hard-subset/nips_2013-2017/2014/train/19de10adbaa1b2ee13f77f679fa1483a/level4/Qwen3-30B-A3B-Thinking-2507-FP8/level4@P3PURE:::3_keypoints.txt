- Proposes a CNN with attention-based top-down feedback that achieves state-of-the-art results on CIFAR datasets.  
- Demonstrates meaningful novelty by integrating bottom-up and top-down signals during training.  
- Evaluation across temporal steps provides useful insight into model dynamics.  
- Use of gated signals to constrain representations is a particularly strong design choice.  
- Effective visualizations make the experimental section engaging and interpretable.  
- Model performance critically depends on the learned attention policy, so identifying failure cases is important.  
- Main concern: increased complexity versus standard feedforward CNNs—scalability is unclear.  
- Lacks results on ILSVRC or higher-resolution, larger-scale datasets to demonstrate practical scalability.  
- Integration of attention-based feedback with CNNs is promising; larger-scale experiments would strengthen the contribution.
