- Proposes a CNN with feedback connections that enable visual attention via gated filter activation.  
- Training uses reinforcement learning on top of a CNN to learn the feedback policy.  
- Individual components aren’t highly novel, but the integrated approach is well-executed and promising.  
- Demonstrates state-of-the-art results on CIFAR, supporting the benefit of feedback and attention in vision.  
- Paper is well-written with clear explanations and high overall quality.  
- Combining CNNs with RL-driven feedback attention is a compelling research direction.  
- Suggested future work: validate and scale the method on more complex datasets (e.g., ImageNet).
