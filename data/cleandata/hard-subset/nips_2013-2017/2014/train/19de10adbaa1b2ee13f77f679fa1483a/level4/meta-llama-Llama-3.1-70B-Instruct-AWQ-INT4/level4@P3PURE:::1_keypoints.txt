- Proposes a convnet with feedback connections that gate filter activations to learn visual attention.  
- Feedback mechanism implemented via gating certain filters.  
- Model trained with reinforcement learning on top of the convnet.  
- Individual components are not highly novel, but the combined approach is promising.  
- Demonstrates state-of-the-art results on CIFAR, confirming the effectiveness of feedback and attention.  
- Paper quality and clarity are good.  
- Suggestion: evaluate on larger, more challenging datasets (e.g., ImageNet) to further validate capabilities.
