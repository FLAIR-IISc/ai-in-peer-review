- Proposes a convolutional network with feedback connections that gate certain filter activations to learn visual attention.  
- Feedback gating is learned via reinforcement learning applied on top of the convnet.  
- Individual components are not particularly novel, but the overall combined approach is strong and promising.  
- Demonstrates state-of-the-art results on CIFAR, reinforcing that feedback/attention is a productive direction.  
- Paper quality and clarity are good.  
- Suggests scaling experiments to larger benchmarks (e.g., ImageNet) if not already done.
