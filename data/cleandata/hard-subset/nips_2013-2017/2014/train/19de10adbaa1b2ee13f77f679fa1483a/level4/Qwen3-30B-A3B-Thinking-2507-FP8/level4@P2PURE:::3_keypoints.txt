- Proposed CNN adds top-down attention-based feedback and reports state-of-the-art results on CIFAR.  
- Novel, engaging model design that integrates bottom-up and top-down signals during training.  
- Temporal evaluation with gated signals to constrain representations is especially compelling.  
- Representation quality depends critically on the learned attention policy; authors should identify or demonstrate failure cases.  
- Experiments are well-executed and include helpful visualizations.  
- The method is more complex than standard feedforward CNNs and needs stronger validation.  
- Major concern: no ILSVRC results, so scalability to larger, higher-resolution datasets is unproven.  
- Overall judgment: attention-based feedback is promising and merits further study; larger-scale experiments would significantly strengthen the contribution.
