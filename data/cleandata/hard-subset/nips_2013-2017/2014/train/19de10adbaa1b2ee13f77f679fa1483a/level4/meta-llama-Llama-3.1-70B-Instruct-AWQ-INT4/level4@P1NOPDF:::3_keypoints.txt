- Proposes a CNN with attention-based top-down feedback, achieving state-of-the-art on CIFAR datasets.
- Novel and engaging model design that combines bottom-up and top-down signals.
- Forward-thinking approach; notable use of gated signals to constrain representations over time.
- Evaluation includes temporal analysis and useful visualizations aiding understanding.
- Learned representation quality depends heavily on the attention policy.
- Reviewer asks for demonstration or discussion of failure cases where the attention policy fails.
- Experiments are well-designed but limited to smaller datasets.
- Primary concern: scalability—results on ILSVRC (large-scale images) are needed to validate practicality.
- Method is more complex than feedforward CNNs; larger-dataset insights would strengthen the paper.
