- Proposes a CNN with attention-based top-down feedback, achieving state-of-the-art results on CIFAR datasets.
- Paper is interesting, well-written, and presents a novel model design for computer vision.
- Integrates bottom-up and top-down signals for CNN training, which is a forward-looking approach.
- Evaluation over time and use of a gated signal to constrain learned representations are appreciated.
- Experiments are insightful and visualizations help understand model behavior.
- Lacks discussion of model limitations and potential failure cases tied to the attention policy.
- Primary concern is scalability: complexity vs. traditional CNNs and reliance on results only on CIFAR.
- Recommends testing on larger datasets (e.g., ILSVRC) to demonstrate practical utility and scalability.
