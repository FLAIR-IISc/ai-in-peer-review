- Proposes a CNN with attention-based top-down feedback that achieves state-of-the-art results on CIFAR and innovatively combines bottom-up and top-down signals.  
- Model design and write-up are engaging and demonstrate clear novelty in applying feedback to vision CNNs.  
- Evaluates the model across time and uses gated signals to constrain representations, which is an interesting methodological choice.  
- The quality of learned representations is highly dependent on the attention policy; analysis of failure modes or policy sensitivity is missing.  
- Experiments are well-designed with useful visualizations, but the method's increased complexity raises concerns about scalability.  
- Key shortcoming: lack of results on ILSVRC/ImageNet to demonstrate scalability to larger images/datasets; additional large-scale experiments would strengthen the paper.
