- Proposes a CNN with attention-based top-down feedback that achieves state-of-the-art results on CIFAR.
- Paper is engaging and presents a novel, forward-thinking integration of bottom-up and top-down signals in CNN training.
- Evaluation over time and the use of gated signals to constrain representations are notable strengths.
- Learned representation quality depends heavily on the attention policy; missing demonstration of failure cases or where the algorithm breaks.
- Experiments use visualization effectively to provide deeper understanding of the model.
- Method is more complex than traditional feedforward CNNs, raising concerns about scalability.
- Primary concern: lacks results on ILSVRC or larger-image datasets to demonstrate scalability and practical applicability.
- Overall the combination of attention-based feedback and CNNs is intriguing and warrants further investigation with larger-scale experiments.
