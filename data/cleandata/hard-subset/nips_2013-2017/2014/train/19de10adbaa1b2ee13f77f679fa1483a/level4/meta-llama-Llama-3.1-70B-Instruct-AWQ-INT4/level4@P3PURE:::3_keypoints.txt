- Proposes a CNN with attention-based top-down feedback, achieving state-of-the-art on CIFAR.
- The paper is interesting and novel in model design and vision application.
- Advances are forward-looking by combining bottom-up and top-down training signals.
- Notable evaluation across time using gated signals to constrain representations.
- Representation quality depends on the attention policy; the paper should explore or illustrate failure cases.
- Experiments are well-designed and include helpful visualizations.
- Primary concern: added complexity vs. feedforward CNNs—needs ILSVRC results to show scalability to larger datasets/images.
- Overall the model is promising and worthy of further study; more practical insights from large-scale experiments are preferred.
