Review- A convolutional network with feedback connections to learn visual attention. The feedback is implemented by gating the activations of certain filters. The model is trained with reinforcement learning on top of a convnet. Although no component is particularly novel, the overall approach is, in my view, very strong and the right direction. As expected, they back this up with state-of-the-art results on CIFAR. This meaningfully reinforces that feedback/attention is the way forward. Quality and clarity are good. A compelling vision method with strong results: convnets with RL-driven feedback attention. If possible, the authors should scale to larger benchmarks like ImageNet if they have not already.
