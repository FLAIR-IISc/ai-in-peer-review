- Presents a ConvNet with feedback connections to learn visual attention.
- Feedback is implemented by gating the activations of specific filters.
- The model is trained within a reinforcement learning framework built on the ConvNet.
- Individual components (ConvNets, feedback, RL) are not novel, but their synthesis is effective and compelling.
- The approach is significant and represents a promising direction for the field.
- Demonstrates state-of-the-art results on CIFAR, convincingly showing the method's strength.
- Provides strong empirical validation that integrating feedback and attention advances vision models.
- Writing and presentation are high quality and clear.
- Suggestion: scale and evaluate the model on larger datasets such as ImageNet.
