- Novel CNN architecture integrating top-down attention-based feedback, achieving state-of-the-art results on CIFAR.
- Manuscript is engaging and demonstrates innovation by combining bottom-up and top-down signals in CNN training.
- Evaluation over time and use of gated signals to constrain representations are particularly noteworthy.
- Learned representation quality heavily depends on the attention policy; the paper should explore failure modes and limitations.
- Experimental design is compelling and uses visualizations to deepen model understanding.
- Main concern: increased complexity versus feedforward CNNs and lack of ILSVRC results to demonstrate scalability to larger datasets/images.
- The attention-feedback synergy is intriguing and warrants further investigation with experiments on larger datasets for practical insights.
