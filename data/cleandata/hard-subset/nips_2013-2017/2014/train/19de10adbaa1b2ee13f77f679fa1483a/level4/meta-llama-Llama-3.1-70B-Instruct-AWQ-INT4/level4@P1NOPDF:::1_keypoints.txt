- Proposes a convolutional network with feedback connections to learn visual attention.
- Feedback is implemented by gating the activation of certain filters.
- The model is trained using reinforcement learning on top of a convnet.
- No single component is highly novel, but the combined approach is a promising direction.
- Demonstrates state-of-the-art results on the CIFAR dataset.
- Results indicate that incorporating feedback and attention mechanisms is a viable research path.
- Submission quality and clarity are good.

- Suggests evaluating the method on more challenging datasets (e.g., ImageNet).
