- Proposes a CNN with feedback connections that gate selected filter activations, trained using reinforcement learning.  
- Individual components (convolutions, RL, attention) are not novel, but their combination and application are compelling.  
- Achieves state-of-the-art performance on CIFAR, demonstrating effectiveness of the approach.  
- Provides further evidence that feedback and attention mechanisms can improve visual recognition.  
- Paper is well-written and generally clear in presentation.  
- The architecture is well-motivated and the experiments are convincingly executed.  
- Recommended future work: evaluate scalability on larger, more complex datasets such as ImageNet.
