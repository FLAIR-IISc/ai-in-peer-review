- Introduces a CNN with attention-based top-down feedback, achieving state-of-the-art results on CIFAR datasets.
- Manuscript is engaging and presents an innovative architecture combining bottom-up and top-down signals.
- Evaluation over time and the use of gated signals to constrain representations are notable strengths.
- Quality of learned representations appears highly dependent on the attention policy.
- Authors should analyze or demonstrate failure cases to better characterize algorithm limitations.
- Experimental presentation (including visualizations) is intriguing and aids understanding.
- Primary concern: method complexity vs. traditional feedforward CNNs—scalability to larger datasets is unclear.
- Inclusion of ILSVRC (or other large-scale) results would significantly strengthen claims about scalability and practicality.
- Overall, the attention-feedback synergy is compelling and warrants further investigation on larger datasets.
