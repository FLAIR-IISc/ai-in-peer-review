- Proposes a novel CNN with top-down attention-based feedback achieving state-of-the-art on CIFAR-10/100.  
- Integrates bottom-up and top-down signals during training, a forward-looking design for vision tasks.  
- Includes temporal evaluation showing representation evolution over time.  
- Uses gated signals to constrain and shape feature learning.  
- Lacks concrete failure-case examples; attention policy-dependent representation quality needs deeper analysis.  
- Experimental section is well-executed with helpful visualizations clarifying model behavior.  
- Major concern: no evaluation on larger-scale benchmarks (e.g., ILSVRC-2012 / ImageNet) to demonstrate scalability.  
- Without ImageNet/higher-resolution results, applicability to real-world, challenging vision problems remains uncertain.  
- Recommendation: prioritize including ImageNet experiments and more failure analyses to strengthen claims.  
- Conclusion: core novelty and technical contribution are valuable but currently limited by evaluation scope.
