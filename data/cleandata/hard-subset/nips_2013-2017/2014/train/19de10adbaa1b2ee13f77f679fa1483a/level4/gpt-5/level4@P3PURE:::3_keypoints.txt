- Proposes a CNN with attention-based top-down feedback, achieving state-of-the-art on CIFAR.
- Novel and engaging model design that integrates bottom-up and top-down signals.
- Forward-looking use of temporal evaluation and gated signals to constrain learned representations.
- Reviewer appreciates interpretability aided by helpful visualizations in experiments.
- Requests analysis of failure cases where the attention policy misguides or feedback harms performance.
- Method is more complex than traditional feedforward CNNs.
- Main concern: no ILSVRC (large-scale, high-resolution) results to demonstrate scalability.
- Overall: combination is compelling and worth further study, but needs practical large-scale experiments to be more convincing.
