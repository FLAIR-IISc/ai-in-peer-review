- Proposes a convolutional neural network with feedback connections to learn visual attention via reinforcement learning.
- Implements the feedback mechanism by gating the activation of specific convolutional filters.
- Individual components are not particularly novel, but the combined approach is well-motivated and promising.
- Demonstrates effectiveness by achieving state-of-the-art results on the CIFAR dataset.
- Results provide strong evidence that incorporating feedback and attention mechanisms is a fruitful research direction.
- Paper quality and clarity are commendable.
- The approach is well-executed and yields impressive empirical results.
- Suggests future work applying the method to more challenging datasets such as ImageNet.
