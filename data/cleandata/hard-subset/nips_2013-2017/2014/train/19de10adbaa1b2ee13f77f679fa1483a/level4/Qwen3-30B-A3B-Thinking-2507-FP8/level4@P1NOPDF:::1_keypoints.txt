- Proposes a CNN with feedback connections implementing visual attention via filter-level gating of activations.
- Integrates reinforcement learning on top of a standard CNN to optimize the attention mechanism.
- Individual components (CNN backbone, RL training, gating) are not novel, but their integration is compelling and well-executed.
- Demonstrates state-of-the-art performance on CIFAR-10 and CIFAR-100, strongly supporting feedback-driven attention.
- Technical presentation is clear, well-structured, and convincingly validates the core hypothesis.
- Methodology is sound and provides strong empirical evidence that RL-driven feedback enhances visual attention.
- The work effectively bridges reinforcement learning and attention modeling in CNNs and is a solid contribution.
- Recommendation: extend experiments to larger-scale benchmarks like ImageNet to assess broader applicability.
