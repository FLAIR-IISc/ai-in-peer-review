- Proposes a CNN with feedback connections that learn visual attention by gating specific filters via reinforcement learning.  
- Individual components are not novel, but their synthesis into a cohesive attention system is well-conceived and promising.  
- Achieves state-of-the-art results on CIFAR, offering strong empirical evidence that integrating feedback and attention advances deep vision models.  
- Paper quality and clarity are good.  
- Recommendation: evaluate scalability on larger, more complex datasets (e.g., ImageNet).
