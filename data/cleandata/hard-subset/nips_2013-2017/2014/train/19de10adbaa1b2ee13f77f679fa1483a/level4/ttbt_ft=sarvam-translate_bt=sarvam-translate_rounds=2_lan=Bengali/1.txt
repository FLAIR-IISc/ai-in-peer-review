A convent with feedback reinforcement to learn attention. Feedback is provided by activating specific filters. The model is learned using reinforcement learning on top of the convent. None of this is particularly new, but in my opinion, the method adopted is really good and it's the right path. And as expected, they have proven this with cutting-edge results in CIFAR. This is important to ensure that feedback/attention is the right path. The quality and clarity are good. A very good method for attention with good results: RL-driven feedback convent with attention. If possible, the authors should delve into deeper problems like ImageNet, if they haven't already worked on it.
