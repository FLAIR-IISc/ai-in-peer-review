- Proposes augmenting a CNN with selective attention and a training method for attention gating parameters to improve classification under limited resources and varying tasks.  
- Tackles an important computer vision problem—adaptive reconfiguration of deep processing—but the method remains a black box offering little explanation of why it works.  
- Classification accuracy is strong, showing recurrent computation can improve recognition beyond standard feed-forward baselines.  
- Representation analysis is weak and does not clarify the roles of learned parameters (mean and variance of the theta distribution).  
- The paper moves beyond fixed-parameter feed-forward networks, presenting a promising direction despite limited interpretability.  
- Exposition is generally clear, but several equations contain typos or need better explanation.  
- Major: The connection to dynamical systems/recurrent networks should be explored more; the method seems closer to recurrent parameter traversal than to reinforcement learning.  
- Major: The cat-image analysis is uninformative—why do all layer-1 activations increase initially, does global scaling affect the objective, and is the output invariant to it?  
- Major: Recommend focused analysis on a small image region, tracking local gating evolution, and evaluating cases where the model corrects Maxout misclassifications.  
- Major: Need ablations or constraints to disentangle effects of learned theta mean and variance.  
- Minor: Algorithm 1 unclear—define h_M and clarify whether F[i] and Θ[i] are stored per image or overwritten.  
- Minor: Equation 11 lacks summation index; clarify whether Equation 8 should be indexed per image (j).  
- Minor: After Equation 11, define symbols x and d.  
- Minor/theoretical: Question necessity of explicit theta regularization given the prior, and the theoretical motivation/implementation of the final gradient-based optimization step (how gradients are computed and whether this adjusts posterior hyperparameters).  
- Overall: Interesting and promising approach, but needs stronger analytical and methodological clarification to explain why it works.
