The paper proposes a convolutional network augmented with feedback connections to learn visual attention. Feedback is implemented by gating the activations of selected filters, and the overall model is trained with reinforcement learning on top of a convnet. While no individual component is especially novel, the integration is strong and, in my view, the right direction for the field. As expected, the method achieves state-of-the-art results on CIFAR, which is significant in reinforcing that feedback/attention is a promising next step. The paper’s quality and clarity are good. Overall, this is a compelling approach to vision with solid results: convnets with RL-driven feedback attention. If possible, the authors should extend the experiments to larger-scale problems such as ImageNet, if they are not already doing so.
