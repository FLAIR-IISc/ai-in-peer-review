A monastery with a feedback connection for learning visual attention. Feedback is obtained by activating certain filters. The model is learned at the top of the monastery with reinforcement learning. No part is particularly new, but in my opinion, the approach adopted is really good and the right way to proceed. And as expected, they prove it with cutting-edge results on CIFAR. It is important to confirm that feedback/attention is the right way to proceed. The quality and clarity are good. A very good approach to vision with good results: RL-powered feedback attention monastery. If possible, the authors should delve deeper into major problems like ImageNet if they are not already.
