- Proposes a convolutional network augmented with feedback connections to implement internal selective attention.  
- Feedback gates activations of selected filters to apply attention.  
- Attention mechanism is trained via reinforcement learning on top of a pretrained convnet.  
- Individual components are not highly novel, but the overall formulation is compelling and directionally promising.  
- Achieves state-of-the-art results on CIFAR, supporting the value of feedback/attention.  
- Paper quality and clarity are strong.  
- Recommendation: extend evaluation to larger benchmarks such as ImageNet.
