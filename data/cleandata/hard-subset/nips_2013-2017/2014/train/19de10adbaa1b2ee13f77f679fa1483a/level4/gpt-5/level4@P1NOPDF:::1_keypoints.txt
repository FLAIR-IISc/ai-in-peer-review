- Proposes a convolutional network with feedback connections that implement visual attention by gating selected filter activations.  
- Trains the attention policy with reinforcement learning on top of the convnet.  
- Individual components are not highly novel, but the overall approach is well conceived.  
- Argues this direction is promising for advancing vision models.  
- Provides strong empirical evidence with state-of-the-art results on CIFAR.  
- Paper is well written and of good quality.  
- Recommends scaling experiments to larger datasets (e.g., ImageNet) to demonstrate scalability and broader impact.
