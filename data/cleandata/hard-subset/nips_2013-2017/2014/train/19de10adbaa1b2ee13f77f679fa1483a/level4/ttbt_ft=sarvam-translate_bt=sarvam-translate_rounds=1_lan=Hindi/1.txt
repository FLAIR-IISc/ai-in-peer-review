A convent with a feedback connection for learning visual attention. Feedback is achieved by gating the activation of certain filters. The model is learned on top of the convent with reinforcement learning. No part is particularly new, but in my opinion, the approach adopted is really good and the right way to move forward. And as expected, they prove it with cutting-edge results on CIFAR. It is important to confirm that feedback/attention is the right way to move forward. The quality and clarity are good. A very good approach to vision with good results: RL-powered feedback attention convent. If possible, the authors should delve deeper into larger problems like ImageNet if they are not already.
