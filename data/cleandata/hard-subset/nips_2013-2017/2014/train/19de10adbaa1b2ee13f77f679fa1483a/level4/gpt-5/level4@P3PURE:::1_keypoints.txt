- Proposes a convolutional network with feedback connections to learn visual attention.  
- Feedback operates by gating activations of selected filters.  
- The attention policy is trained with reinforcement learning on top of the convnet.  
- Individual components aren’t novel, but their integration is strong and directionally important for vision.  
- Shows state-of-the-art results on CIFAR, supporting the feedback/attention claim.  
- Paper quality and clarity are good.  
- Overall, a very good approach: convnets with RL-driven feedback attention.  
- Suggests evaluating on larger-scale problems (e.g., ImageNet) and considering deeper models.
