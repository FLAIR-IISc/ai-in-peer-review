- Proposes a CNN that integrates top-down processing via attention-based feedback.  
- Reports state-of-the-art performance on CIFAR datasets.  
- Novel contribution in model design and applying combined bottom-up and top-down signals to CNN training.  
- Evaluation includes temporal analysis and a gated signal to constrain representations; visualizations improve interpretability.  
- Notes dependence of learned representations on the attention policy and requests demonstration or discussion of failure cases.  
- Main concern: lacks experiments on ILSVRC/larger high-resolution datasets to demonstrate scalability.  
- Overall assessment: approach is compelling and merits further study; would benefit from practical insights from larger-scale experiments.
