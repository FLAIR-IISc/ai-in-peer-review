- Proposes a convolutional network with feedback connections that gate specific filter activations to implement visual attention.  
- Uses reinforcement learning to train the feedback/attention mechanism on top of the convolutional network.  
- Individual components are not novel, but the overall integration and approach is promising and well-executed.  
- Achieves state-of-the-art results on CIFAR, supporting the value of feedback and attention mechanisms.  
- Paper quality and clarity are strong.  
- Suggestion: extend experiments to larger-scale datasets (e.g., ImageNet) for further validation.
