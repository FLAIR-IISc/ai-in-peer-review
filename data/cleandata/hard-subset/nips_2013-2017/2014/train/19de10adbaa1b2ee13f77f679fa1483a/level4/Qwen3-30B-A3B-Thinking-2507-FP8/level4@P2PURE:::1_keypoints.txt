- Introduces a CNN with feedback connections implemented via filter gating for learning visual attention.  
- Trains the feedback mechanism using reinforcement learning on top of a standard CNN architecture.  
- Individual components are not novel, but the combined approach is strong and well-executed.  
- Approach aligns with a promising direction for attention mechanisms in vision.  
- Demonstrates state-of-the-art results on CIFAR, validating feedback-driven attention.  
- Presentation and clarity of the paper are excellent.  
- Recommends extending evaluation to larger-scale benchmarks (e.g., ImageNet) to assess generalizability.
