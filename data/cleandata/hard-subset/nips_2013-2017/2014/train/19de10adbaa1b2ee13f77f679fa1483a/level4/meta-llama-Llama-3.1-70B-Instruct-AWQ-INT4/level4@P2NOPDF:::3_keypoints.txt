- Proposes a CNN with attention-based top-down feedback that integrates bottom-up and top-down signals.  
- Claims state-of-the-art performance on CIFAR datasets.  
- Paper is engaging and shows innovation in model design for vision tasks.  
- Evaluation over time and use of gated signals to constrain representations are notable strengths.  
- Experiments include useful visualization techniques that aid model understanding.  
- Major concern: increased complexity compared to feedforward CNNs without evidence of scalability.  
- Lacks results on ILSVRC/larger-image datasets to demonstrate practical scalability.  
- Need to investigate failure cases, since performance depends heavily on the attention policy.  
- Overall, an intriguing model deserving further investigation and experiments on larger datasets.
