This paper presents a convolutional neural network (CNN) incorporating feedback connections to facilitate visual attention through gated filter activation. The model is trained using reinforcement learning on top of a CNN, although individual components may not be particularly novel, the overall approach is well-executed and promising. The authors demonstrate state-of-the-art results on the CIFAR dataset, providing substantial evidence for the efficacy of feedback and attention mechanisms in vision tasks. The paper is well-written, with clear explanations and good overall quality. The proposed approach, combining CNNs with reinforcement learning-driven feedback attention, is a compelling direction for vision research. A potential future direction could be to apply this method to more complex and larger-scale problems, such as ImageNet, to further validate its effectiveness.
