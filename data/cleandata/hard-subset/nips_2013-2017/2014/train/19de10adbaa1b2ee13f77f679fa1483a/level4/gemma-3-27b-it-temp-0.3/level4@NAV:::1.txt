This work presents a convolutional network incorporating feedback connections to facilitate visual attention, implemented through gated filter activations. The model is trained using a reinforcement learning framework built upon a convolutional base. While the individual components are well-established, the integration represents a strong and promising direction for research. The authors demonstrate the effectiveness of this approach with state-of-the-art performance on the CIFAR dataset, providing compelling evidence supporting the potential of feedback and attention mechanisms in visual processing. The manuscript is well-written and clear. Overall, this is a valuable contribution demonstrating a robust approach to vision – specifically, leveraging reinforcement learning to drive feedback attention within convolutional networks. Future work extending this methodology to larger, more complex datasets such as ImageNet would be highly impactful.
