- Proposes a CNN with attention-based feedback for top-down processing.  
- Achieves state-of-the-art results on CIFAR datasets.  
- Novel model design effectively integrates bottom-up and top-down signals during training.  
- Evaluation across temporal dimensions with gated signals to constrain representations is noteworthy.  
- Representation quality depends on the learned attention policy; paper lacks specific failure cases or failure analysis.  
- Experimental section is well-executed with insightful visualizations elucidating model behavior.  
- Method introduces greater complexity than standard feedforward CNNs.  
- Primary concern: no results on ILSVRC or larger, higher-resolution datasets to demonstrate scalability.  
- Integration of attention-based feedback with CNNs is intriguing and warrants further large-scale investigation.
