- Proposes a CNN with top-down, attention-based feedback and reports state-of-the-art results on CIFAR.  
- Model design is novel and the application to computer vision is interesting.  
- Forward-looking contribution by integrating bottom-up and top-down signals during training.  
- Strong evaluation of model behavior over time and effective use of gated signals to constrain learned representations.  
- Representation quality is heavily dependent on the attention policy; authors should discuss or demonstrate failure modes.  
- Experiments are compelling and visualizations help interpret the model.  
- Primary concern: lack of results on ILSVRC/high-resolution datasets to demonstrate scalability.  
- Recommendation: run additional experiments on larger datasets to validate scalability and provide practical insights.
