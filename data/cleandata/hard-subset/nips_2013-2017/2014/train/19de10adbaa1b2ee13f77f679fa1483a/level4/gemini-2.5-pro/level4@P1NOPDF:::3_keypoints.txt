- Proposes a CNN with top-down processing via an attention-based feedback mechanism.  
- Claims state-of-the-art performance on CIFAR datasets.  
- Paper is well-written and demonstrates novelty in model design and CV application.  
- Integrates bottom-up and top-down signals, a forward-looking contribution.  
- Evaluation over time and use of a gating mechanism to constrain learned representations are strengths.  
- Quality of representations depends on the attention policy; authors should show or discuss failure cases.  
- Experiments are insightful and visualizations help explain model behavior.  
- Main concern: no evaluation on more challenging, large-scale datasets.  
- Scalability is a critical question given higher model complexity than feedforward CNNs.  
- Recommends including results on datasets like ILSVRC to validate effectiveness on larger images and more classes.  
- Overall: compelling model worthy of further study but impact would increase with large-scale experiments.
