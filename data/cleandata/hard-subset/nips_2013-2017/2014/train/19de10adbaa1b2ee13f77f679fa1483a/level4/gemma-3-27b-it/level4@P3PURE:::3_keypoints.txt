- Proposes a CNN with attention-based top-down feedback connections.  
- Demonstrates state-of-the-art performance on CIFAR datasets.  
- Novelty in both model design and application to vision tasks.  
- Combining bottom-up and top-down signals is a forward-looking approach.  
- Evaluation includes model dynamics over time and use of gated signals to constrain representations.  
- Quality of learned representations depends heavily on the attention policy.  
- Reviewer requests discussion or demonstration of potential failure cases.  
- Experiments are well-executed and include helpful visualizations.  
- Primary concern: increased complexity vs feedforward CNNs and lack of ILSVRC (large-scale, high-resolution) results to demonstrate scalability.  
- Overall: compelling model worthy of further investigation; more experiments on larger datasets would provide valuable practical insights.
