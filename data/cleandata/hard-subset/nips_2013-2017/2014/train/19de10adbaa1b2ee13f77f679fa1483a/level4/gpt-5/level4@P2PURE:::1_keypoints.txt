- Proposes a convolutional network augmented with feedback connections to learn visual attention.
- Feedback implemented by gating activations of selected filters.
- Overall model trained with reinforcement learning on top of a convnet.
- Individual components are not highly novel, but their integration is strong and promising.
- Achieves state-of-the-art results on CIFAR, supporting feedback/attention approaches.
- Paper quality and clarity are good.
- Recommendation: extend experiments to larger-scale datasets like ImageNet.
