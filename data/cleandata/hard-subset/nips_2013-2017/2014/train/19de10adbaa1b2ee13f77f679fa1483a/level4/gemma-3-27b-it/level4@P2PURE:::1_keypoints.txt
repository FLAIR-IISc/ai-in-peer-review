- Proposes a convolutional neural network with feedback connections that learn visual attention by gating selected filter activations.
- Trains the feedback/attention mechanism using reinforcement learning applied on top of the convolutional network.
- No single component is novel, but the overall combination is strong and represents a promising research direction.
- Demonstrates state-of-the-art results on CIFAR, supporting the value of feedback and attention mechanisms.
- Paper is well-written and clear.
- Recommends exploring scalability to larger, more challenging datasets (e.g., ImageNet).
