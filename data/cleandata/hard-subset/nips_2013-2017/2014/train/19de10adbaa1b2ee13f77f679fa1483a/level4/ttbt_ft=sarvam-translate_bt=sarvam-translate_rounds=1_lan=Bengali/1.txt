A convent with feedback connections for learning visual attention. Feedback is provided by activating certain filters. The model is learned using reinforcement learning on top of the convent. No part of it is particularly new, but in my opinion, the method adopted is really very good and this is the right path. And as expected, they have proven this with state-of-the-art results in CIFAR. This is significant to ensure that feedback/attention is the right path. The quality and clarity are good. A very good method for visual attention with good results: RL-driven feedback convent with attention. If possible, the authors should delve into the deeper issues like ImageNet, if they have not already worked on it.
