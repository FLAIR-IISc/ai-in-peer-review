- Introduces a CNN with attention-based top-down feedback and reports state-of-the-art results on CIFAR datasets.  
- Novel and compelling contribution that advances model design by explicitly incorporating top-down signals.  
- Temporal evaluation and gated signals are strong aspects that help constrain learned representations.  
- The quality of representations depends on the attention policy; the paper should discuss potential failure modes and limitations.  
- Experimental analysis is thorough and includes useful visualizations illustrating model behavior.  
- Increased complexity compared to feedforward CNNs raises concerns about scalability.  
- Primary concern: need evaluation on larger, higher-resolution datasets (e.g., ImageNet/ILSVRC) to demonstrate practical applicability.  
- Overall conclusion: promising and impactful approach; experiments on larger datasets would strengthen the claims.
