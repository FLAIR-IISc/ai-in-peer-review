- Proposes a CNN with feedback connections that enable visual attention by gating specific filter activations.
- Feedback mechanism is implemented via gating of filter activations.
- Model training uses reinforcement learning on top of the CNN framework.
- No single component is groundbreaking, but the combined approach is well-conceived and promising.
- Demonstrates state-of-the-art performance on CIFAR, supporting the value of feedback and attention mechanisms.
- Paper quality and clarity are strong.
- Combining CNNs with RL-driven feedback attention is a notable contribution to vision research.
- Suggested future work: apply the method to more complex datasets (e.g., ImageNet).
