- Introduces a CNN with feedback connections to learn a visual attention mechanism.  
- Feedback implemented by gating activations of specific convolutional filters and trained with reinforcement learning.  
- Individual components are not novel, but their synthesis is compelling and promising.  
- Demonstrates effectiveness by achieving state-of-the-art results on CIFAR, providing empirical support for feedback and attention in vision.  
- Paper quality and clarity are high; the approach is well executed and yields impressive results.  
- Suggests future work to evaluate performance and scalability on larger datasets such as ImageNet.
