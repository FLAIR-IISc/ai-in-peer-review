- Proposes a novel CNN architecture that adds top-down processing via an attention-based feedback mechanism.  
- Claims state-of-the-art performance on CIFAR datasets.  
- Novelty: combines bottom-up and top-down signals for CNN training, a forward-looking approach.  
- Strength: evaluates model behavior over time and uses a gated signal to constrain learned representations.  
- Weakness: learned representation quality depends heavily on the attention policy; needs analysis of potential failure modes.  
- Experiments are solid with insightful visualizations that clarify model behavior.  
- Major concern: scalability — method is more complex than feedforward CNNs but only evaluated on CIFAR.  
- Recommendation: demonstrate practical viability via experiments on large-scale, high-resolution datasets (e.g., ILSVRC).
