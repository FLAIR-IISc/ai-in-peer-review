- Introduces a convolutional neural network with feedback connections to learn visual attention.  
- Feedback is implemented by gating activations of selected filters.  
- Trains the feedback mechanism using reinforcement learning on top of the convolutional network.  
- No single component is highly novel, but the combined approach is strong and promising.  
- Achieves state-of-the-art results on CIFAR, supporting the value of feedback and attention in vision.  
- Writing and presentation are clear and of good quality.  
- Overall a well-executed method that yields compelling results.  
- Authors should explore scalability to larger, more complex datasets (e.g., ImageNet).
