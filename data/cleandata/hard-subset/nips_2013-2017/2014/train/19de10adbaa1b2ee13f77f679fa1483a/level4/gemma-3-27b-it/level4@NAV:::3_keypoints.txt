- Introduces a CNN architecture integrating top-down processing via attention-based feedback.  
- Reports state-of-the-art performance on CIFAR datasets.  
- Novel and compelling contribution to model design and computer vision application.  
- Explicitly models both bottom-up and top-down signaling, a forward-looking choice.  
- Evaluation of model dynamics over time and use of gated signals is especially insightful.  
- Reliance on the learned attention policy suggests the need to explore potential failure modes.  
- Experimental analysis is well-executed and includes useful visualizations.  
- Primary concern: increased complexity — needs scalability proof on larger, harder datasets (e.g., ILSVRC).  
- Overall assessment: promising approach deserving further investigation and larger-scale experiments.
