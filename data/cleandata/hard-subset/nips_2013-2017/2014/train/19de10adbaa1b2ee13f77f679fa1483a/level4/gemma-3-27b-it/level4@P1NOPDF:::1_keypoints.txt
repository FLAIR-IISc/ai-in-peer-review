- Proposes a convolutional network with feedback connections that gate specific filter activations to learn visual attention.
- Feedback mechanism is implemented by gating activations and trained using reinforcement learning on top of a CNN.
- Individual components aren’t novel, but the overall combination is strong and a promising research direction.
- Demonstrates state-of-the-art results on CIFAR, supporting the effectiveness of feedback and attention mechanisms in vision.
- Paper is well-written and clear; the approach is well executed.
- Primary recommendation: evaluate on larger, more challenging datasets (e.g., ImageNet) to test scalability and generalizability.
