- Novel architecture combining bottom-up CNNs with top-down, attention-driven feedback, offering a forward-looking alternative to purely feedforward models.  
- Temporal dynamics analysis showing a gated mechanism that modulates and refines internal representations across timesteps.  
- Paper is well-written and includes attention visualizations that clarify and qualitatively support the model's behavior.  
- Major concern about scalability: no evaluation on large-scale, high-resolution benchmarks (e.g., ImageNet), leaving computational/memory scaling and practical utility unclear.  
- Need for analysis of failure modes: lack of examples or discussion showing when and how the attention mechanism misidentifies regions, causing errors.  
- Overall promising results on CIFAR but requires large-scale validation to confirm broader impact and practicality.
