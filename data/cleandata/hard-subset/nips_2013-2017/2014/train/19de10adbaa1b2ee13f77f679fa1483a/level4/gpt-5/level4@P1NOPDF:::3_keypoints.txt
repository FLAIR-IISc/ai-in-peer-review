- Proposes a CNN that incorporates top-down processing via attention-based feedback, reporting state-of-the-art results on CIFAR.
- Clear novelty in model design and application: jointly leverages bottom-up and top-down signals for training.
- Positive on temporal evaluation and use of gated signals to constrain representations.
- Raises concern that learned representation quality depends critically on the attention policy; requests discussion or demos of failure modes and sensitivity to suboptimal policies.
- Experiments are interesting and visualizations aid understanding.
- Main weakness: lacks results on ILSVRC or other large, high-resolution datasets to demonstrate scalability.
- Overall assessment: compelling model that merits further study; practical insights from larger-scale experiments would be especially valuable.
