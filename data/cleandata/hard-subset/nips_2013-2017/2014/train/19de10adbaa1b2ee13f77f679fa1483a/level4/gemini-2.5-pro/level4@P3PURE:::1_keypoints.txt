- Proposes a CNN that learns visual attention via feedback connections implemented by gating specific filter activations and trained with reinforcement learning.  
- Individual components are not novel, but their synthesis is compelling and well-motivated.  
- Achieves state-of-the-art results on the CIFAR dataset, demonstrating strong empirical effectiveness.  
- Results provide significant validation for feedback and attention as promising directions for future vision models.  
- Paper quality and clarity of presentation are good.  
- Overall, a very good approach combining CNNs with RL-driven feedback for attention that delivers impressive results.  
- Recommended next step: evaluate scalability on larger datasets (e.g., ImageNet).
