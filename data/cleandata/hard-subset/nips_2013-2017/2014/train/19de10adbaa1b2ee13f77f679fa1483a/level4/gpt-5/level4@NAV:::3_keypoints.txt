- Presents a CNN with attention-based top-down feedback and achieves state-of-the-art results on CIFAR.  
- Paper is engaging and novel in model design and vision application, combining bottom-up and top-down signals.  
- Evaluation across time and use of a gated signal to constrain representations are appreciated.  
- Needs analysis or demonstration of failure modes since learned representations depend on the attention policy.  
- Experiments include helpful visualizations that aid understanding.  
- Main concern: increased complexity vs. feedforward CNN — lacks results on ILSVRC to demonstrate scalability to larger datasets/images.  
- Overall: intriguing model worthy of further study; more practical insights from larger-scale experiments are preferred.
