- Introduces a CNN architecture that incorporates top-down processing via attention-based feedback.  
- Demonstrates state-of-the-art performance on CIFAR datasets.  
- Novel contribution in both model design and application to vision tasks.  
- Integrates bottom-up and top-down signals for training CNNs.  
- Evaluates model dynamics over time and uses gated signals to constrain learned representations.  
- Representation quality depends heavily on the attention policy; authors should provide failure cases or discuss scenarios where the algorithm might fail.  
- Experiments are well-executed and include visualizations that enhance interpretability.  
- Method is more complex than traditional feedforward CNNs; scalability to larger, higher-resolution datasets (e.g., ILSVRC) is not demonstrated.  
- Primary suggestion: evaluate on larger datasets to show scalability and provide more practical insights.
