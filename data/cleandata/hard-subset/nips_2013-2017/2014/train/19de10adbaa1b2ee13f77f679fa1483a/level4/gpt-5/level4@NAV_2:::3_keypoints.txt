- Proposes a CNN architecture with top-down processing via attention-driven feedback connections.  
- Method achieves state-of-the-art performance on CIFAR datasets.  
- Novel and forward-looking integration of bottom-up and top-down signals within a CNN.  
- Evaluation across time and use of gated signals to constrain representations are strong points.  
- Learned representation quality depends heavily on the attention policy; request to illustrate or discuss failure scenarios.  
- Experiments include visualizations that improve interpretability.  
- Method is more complex than conventional feedforward CNNs, raising concerns about practicality.  
- Main concern: lacks results on ILSVRC / larger, higher-resolution datasets to demonstrate scalability.
