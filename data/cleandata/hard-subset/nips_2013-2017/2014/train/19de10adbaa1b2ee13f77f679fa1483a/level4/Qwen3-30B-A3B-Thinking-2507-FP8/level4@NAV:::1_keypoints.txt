- Proposes a CNN with feedback connections implemented via filter gating for visual attention.
- Trains the model by integrating reinforcement learning with the base convolutional architecture.
- Individual components are not highly novel, but the overall integration is well-conceived.
- Achieves state-of-the-art performance on CIFAR-10, supporting feedback-driven attention.
- Paper is well-written, technically sound, and clearly presented.
- Recommends extending evaluation to larger-scale benchmarks (e.g., ImageNet) to strengthen the contribution.
