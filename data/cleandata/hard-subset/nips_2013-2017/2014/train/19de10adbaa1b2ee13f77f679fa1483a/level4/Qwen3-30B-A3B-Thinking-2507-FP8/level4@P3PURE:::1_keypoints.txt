- Proposes a CNN augmented with feedback connections that gate specific filter activations to model visual attention.
- Uses reinforcement learning to train the feedback attention mechanism on top of a standard CNN.
- No single component is highly novel, but the integration of RL-driven feedback for attention is well-motivated.
- Demonstrates state-of-the-art performance on CIFAR-10, providing strong empirical validation.
- Presentation and technical clarity are excellent.
- Results effectively support the core thesis that feedback-based attention is promising for vision tasks.
- Recommends extending evaluation to larger-scale benchmarks such as ImageNet.
