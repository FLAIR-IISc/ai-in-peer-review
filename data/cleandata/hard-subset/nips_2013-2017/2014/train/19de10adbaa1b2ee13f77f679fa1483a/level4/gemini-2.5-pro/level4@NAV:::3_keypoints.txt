- Novel CNN architecture that integrates attention-based feedback for top-down processing.  
- Demonstrates state-of-the-art performance on CIFAR datasets.  
- Well-written paper with a novel contribution in both model architecture and computer vision application.  
- Conceptual framework combining bottom-up and top-down signals is a promising research direction.  
- Temporal evaluation and use of a gated signal to constrain learned representations are particularly compelling.  
- Recommend discussing potential failure modes, since representation quality critically depends on the attention policy.  
- Experimental validation is thorough and includes insightful visualizations that clarify model behavior.  
- Primary concern is scalability due to increased complexity compared to standard feedforward networks.  
- Suggest evaluating on a large-scale benchmark (e.g., ILSVRC) to validate effectiveness on higher-resolution, larger datasets.  
- Overall, the attention-based feedback + CNN approach is interesting and warrants further investigation, contingent on larger-scale empirical evidence.
