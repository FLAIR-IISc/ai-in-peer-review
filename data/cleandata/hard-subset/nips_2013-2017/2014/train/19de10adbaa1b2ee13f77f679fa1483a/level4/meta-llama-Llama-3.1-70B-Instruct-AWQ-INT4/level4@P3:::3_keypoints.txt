- Proposes dasNet, a novel CNN architecture with internal selective attention via top-down feedback.  
- Uses reinforcement learning to train an attention policy that modulates feature maps.  
- Demonstrates state-of-the-art performance on CIFAR-10 and CIFAR-100.  
- Paper is well-organized, clearly written, and places the work properly within related literature.  
- Technical description and training algorithm are clear and reproducible.  
- Experiments are well-designed and results are presented clearly.  
- Novelty: effectively combines CNN strengths with flexible top-down processing and attention-based feedback.  
- Concern about scalability—no experiments on larger, more challenging datasets like ILSVRC.  
- Lacks analysis of failure cases or behavior when the attention policy is suboptimal.  
- Recommendation: accept with minor revisions to address scalability and failure-mode analysis.  
- Rating: 8/10; Confidence: 8/10.
