- Proposes a convolutional neural network with feedback connections that learns visual attention via reinforcement learning.  
- Key innovation: gated activations on certain filters to selectively focus on relevant features.  
- Individual components are not highly novel, but the combined approach is well-justified and effective.  
- Demonstrates state-of-the-art performance on the CIFAR dataset.  
- Result is significant evidence supporting the use of feedback and attention mechanisms in computer vision.  
- Paper is well-written with clear explanations and good overall quality.  
- Combining convnets with RL-driven feedback attention is a promising direction for vision research.  
- Suggested next step: evaluate the method on more challenging datasets such as ImageNet.
