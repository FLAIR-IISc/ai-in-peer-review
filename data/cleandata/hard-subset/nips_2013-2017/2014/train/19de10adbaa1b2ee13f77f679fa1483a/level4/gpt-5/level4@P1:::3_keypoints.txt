- Proposes a CNN with attention-based top-down feedback using learned gates to iteratively modulate filters over time, refining representations; achieves state-of-the-art on unaugmented CIFAR-10/100.  
- Paper is engaging and presents a novel, forward-looking combination of bottom-up and top-down signals in CNNs.  
- Temporal evaluation and gated signals are valuable for constraining and analyzing learned representations.  
- Key concern: model quality depends critically on the learned attention policy; authors should show or discuss failure cases.  
- Experiments are interesting and visualizations help interpret model behavior.  
- Major limitation: increased complexity versus feedforward CNNs and no ILSVRC/larger-scale results to demonstrate scalability to higher-resolution images.  
- Overall the approach is compelling and merits further study; practical insights from larger datasets are preferred.
