- Introduces dasNet: a CNN with feedback connections implementing iterative visual attention.  
- Uses reinforcement learning to learn an attention policy on top of a pre-trained CNN.  
- Key idea: iteratively focus internal attention on extracted features to improve classification.  
- Well-motivated discussion of CNN limitations and benefits of feedback.  
- Architecture and attention mechanism are clearly and thoroughly described.  
- Experimental results: achieves state-of-the-art on unaugmented CIFAR-10 and CIFAR-100.  
- Presentation quality is high; experiments and results are well-presented.  
- Novelty lies in the combined use of CNN, feedback, and RL rather than in individual components.  
- Significance: approach offers a new perspective and could generalize to many vision tasks.  
- Limitations: individual components not highly novel and evaluation limited to small datasets.  
- Missing analysis of computational complexity of the proposed method.  
- Recommendations: scale to larger datasets (e.g., ImageNet), analyze computational costs, and provide more RL implementation/detail.
