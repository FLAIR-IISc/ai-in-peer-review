**Summary of the Main Ideas**

The paper proposes a parallel randomized block coordinate method, called Parallel Direction Method of Multipliers (PDMM), to solve optimization problems with multi-block linear constraints. PDMM is an extension of the Alternating Direction Method of Multipliers (ADMM) and is designed to handle problems with multiple blocks. The method updates the blocks in parallel and uses a backward step on the dual update to ensure convergence. The authors establish the global convergence and iteration complexity of PDMM with constant step size. They also show that PDMM can be used to solve overlapping block problems and provide experimental results on robust principal component analysis and overlapping group lasso.
