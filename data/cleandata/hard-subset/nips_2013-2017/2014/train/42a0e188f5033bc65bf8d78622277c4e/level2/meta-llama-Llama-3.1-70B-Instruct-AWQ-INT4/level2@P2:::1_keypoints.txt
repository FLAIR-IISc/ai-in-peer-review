- Proposes PDMM, a novel parallel randomized block coordinate method for optimization with multi-block linear constraints.  
- Establishes global convergence and iteration complexity for PDMM with constant step size.  
- Demonstrates PDMM outperforms state-of-the-art methods on robust PCA and overlapping group lasso.  
- Provides a unified framework analyzing exact and inexact PDMM variants.  
- Clearly discusses connections between PDMM and related methods (ADMM, PJADMM).  
- Paper is well-written, well-organized, with clear notation and explanations.  
- Technical development and convergence analysis are sound.  
- Experimental results are impressive and validate the method’s effectiveness.  
- Minor omission: lacks implementation details (choice of step size, number of blocks updated).  
- Minor omission: limited discussion of PDMM’s limitations and future research directions.  
- Recommendation: accept for publication pending minor revisions.
