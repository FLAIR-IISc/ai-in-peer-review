The paper "Parallel Direction Method of Multipliers" proposes a novel parallel randomized block coordinate method for solving optimization problems with multi-block linear constraints. The authors establish the global convergence and iteration complexity of the proposed method, called Parallel Direction Method of Multipliers (PDMM), with constant step size. The method is shown to be a generalization of the Alternating Direction Method of Multipliers (ADMM) and its variants, and it can solve a wider range of problems, including those with overlapping blocks.

The paper is well-written, and the authors provide a clear and concise explanation of the proposed method and its theoretical analysis. The experimental results demonstrate the effectiveness of PDMM in solving robust principal component analysis and overlapping group lasso problems.
