- Paper proposes PDMM for block-separable minimization with linear constraints: randomly updates K primal blocks per iteration, applies a backward step for the dual, allows parallel primal updates, shows sublinear convergence and generalizes several ADMM variants.  
- Paper is well written and reviewer is inclined to accept.  
- Clarify algorithm (eqs. (5),(6)): unclear whether all coordinates of y^{t+1} and \hat{y}^{t+1} or only a single dual block i are updated, and how block i is selected.  
- Typo in Theorem 2 proof: “\sum_{i=1}^I” appears to be missing after “{”.  
- More discussion required for Theorem 2: verify consistency with known convergence rates of special-case ADMM variants (via choices of K and J) and address how to optimally split the matrix A into blocks.  
- Suggest empirical comparison on tensor completion (2012) multi-block problem to identify which ADMM variant performs best.  
- Minor typos: missing space after “min” in (9); remove extra space before “Section” on line 217.  
- Overall positive recommendation but requests expanded discussion after Theorem 2.
