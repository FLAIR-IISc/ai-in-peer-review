- Contribution: Proposes PDMM — a randomized K-block primal update ADMM variant with a backward dual step, parallelizable, with proven sublinear convergence that generalizes several ADMM variants.
- Overall assessment: Paper is well-structured and clear; reviewer inclined to accept pending clarifications and extra discussion.
- Major point 1: Algorithm description (Eqs. (5),(6)) is ambiguous about whether all coordinates of y^{t+1} and \hat{y}^{t+1} are updated or only a single block i, and how i is selected — needs clarification.
- Major point 2: Proof of Theorem 2 appears to be missing a summation term (∑_{i=1}^I) in an expression — needs correction.
- Major point 3: Theorem 2 needs more discussion on how its convergence rate reduces to known rates for special ADMM variants (given choices of K and J) and guidance on optimal splitting of matrix A into blocks.
- Major point 4: Request for comparison/discussion with tensor completion (2012) which has multiple-block structure, and indications of which ADMM variant might be optimal for that problem.
- Minor points: Formatting fixes — add space after "min" in Eq. (9) and remove extra space before "Section" on line 217.
- Expectation: Reviewer expects additional discussion following Theorem 2 to improve completeness and clarity before final acceptance.
