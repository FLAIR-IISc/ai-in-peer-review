- Proposes PDMM, a parallel randomized block coordinate method with backward dual refinement for block-separable convex problems under linear constraints.  
- PDMM randomly selects K primal blocks to update in parallel and proves a sublinear convergence rate.  
- Method generalizes several ADMM variants and relates to sADMM and PJADMM.  
- Paper is well written, clearly organized, and easy to follow with a clear algorithm description.  
- Theoretical analysis is rigorous and well presented.  
- Experimental results convincingly demonstrate effectiveness on real problems (e.g., robust PCA, overlapping group lasso).  
- High originality: PDMM is a novel method not previously proposed.  
- High significance: parallelizability makes PDMM suitable for large-scale optimization.
