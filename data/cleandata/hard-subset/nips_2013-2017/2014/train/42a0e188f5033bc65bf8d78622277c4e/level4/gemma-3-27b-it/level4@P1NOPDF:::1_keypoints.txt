- Addresses minimizing block-separable functions with linear constraints and proposes PDMM, a multi-block ADMM variant.
- PDMM randomly selects K primal blocks per iteration, uses a backward step for dual refinement, and supports parallel primal updates.
- The authors prove a sublinear convergence rate and show PDMM generalizes several existing ADMM variants.
