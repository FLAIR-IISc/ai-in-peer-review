- Addresses block-separable convex minimization with linear constraints and proposes Parallel Direction Method of Multipliers (PDMM).
- PDMM randomly selects K primal blocks for parallel updates and uses a backward step to refine dual variables for efficient parallel implementation.
- Authors prove global convergence and O(1/T) iteration complexity for PDMM with constant step size and show it generalizes several ADMM variants.
- Paper is well-structured, clearly written, and makes a compelling theoretical contribution extending ADMM to multi-block settings.
