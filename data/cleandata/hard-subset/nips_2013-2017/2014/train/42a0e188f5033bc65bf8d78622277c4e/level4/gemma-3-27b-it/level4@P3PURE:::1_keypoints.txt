- Paper studies minimizing block-separable functions with linear constraints and proposes PDMM, an ADMM variant that randomly updates K primal blocks and uses a backward step for the dual.  
- Primal updates in PDMM can be implemented in parallel; authors prove a sublinear convergence rate and show PDMM generalizes several ADMM variants.  
- Overall evaluation: paper is well-written, easy to follow, and recommended for acceptance.
