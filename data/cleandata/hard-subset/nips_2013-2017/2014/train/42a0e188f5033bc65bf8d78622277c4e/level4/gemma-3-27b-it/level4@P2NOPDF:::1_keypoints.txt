- Proposes PDMM, an ADMM variant for block-separable problems that updates a random subset of K primal blocks, uses a backward dual step, allows parallel primal updates, proves sublinear convergence, and generalizes several ADMM variants.

- Paper is exceptionally well-written and the reviewer recommends acceptance.

- Clarify algorithm (Eqs. 5–6): unclear whether all coordinates of y^{t+1} and ŷ^{t+1} are updated or only those for the selected block i, and the block-selection procedure is not specified.

- Typographical error in Theorem 2: the summation symbol “∑_{i=1}^{I}” appears to have a missing brace.

- Expand discussion of Theorem 2: show whether convergence rates of existing ADMM variants match Theorem 2 under appropriate parameter choices (K, J) and provide theoretical guidance on optimal block decomposition of A.

- Provide comparative analysis/insights of PDMM versus other ADMM variants on tensor completion problems with multiple blocks, and indicate which variant performs best.

- Minor edits: add a space after “min” in Eq. (9) and remove extraneous space before “Section” on line 217.

- Reiteration: positive overall assessment; expanding the Theorem 2 discussion would significantly strengthen the paper.
