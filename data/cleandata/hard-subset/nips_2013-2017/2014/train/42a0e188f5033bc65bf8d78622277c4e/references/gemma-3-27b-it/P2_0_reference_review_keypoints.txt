- Strength: Tackles the important problem of parallelizing ADMM for large-scale optimization.
- Strength: Novel combination of backward dual step, sparsity consideration, and flexible random block selection.
- Strength: Provides global convergence proof and iteration complexity guarantees.
- Strength: Empirical validation on robust PCA and overlapping group lasso shows favorable performance and studies effect of number of blocks K.
