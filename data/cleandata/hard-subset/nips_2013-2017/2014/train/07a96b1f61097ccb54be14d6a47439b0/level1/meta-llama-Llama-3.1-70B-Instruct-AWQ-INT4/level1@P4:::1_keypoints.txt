- Main theoretical result: if the dictionary is low-rank, LRR is immune to the coherence parameter that grows with cluster number.

- Theoretical analysis is sound and well-motivated.  
- Experiments on synthetic matrices and real motion sequences demonstrate the method's effectiveness.
