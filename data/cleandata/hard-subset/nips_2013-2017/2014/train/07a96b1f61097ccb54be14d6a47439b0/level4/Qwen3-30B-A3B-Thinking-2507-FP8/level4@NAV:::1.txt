

This paper tackles the challenge of robustly estimating a low-dimensional subspace from inherently coherent contaminated observations, where prior RPCA methods exhibit degraded performance as data coherence increases—a well-established theoretical limitation. The authors overcome this bottleneck through a novel approach. Given the prevalence of such coherence patterns in real-world data, addressing this limitation holds significant practical value. The proposed method is both theoretically rigorous and empirically validated with strong performance.  

I would be interested to learn whether the method can also address subspace clustering/segmentation problems. The authors might consider commenting on this potential extension.  

This work resolves a critical challenge in robust PCA. The paper demonstrates excellent technical quality and should be accepted.
