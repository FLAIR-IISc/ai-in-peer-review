- Addresses robust low-dimensional subspace estimation under coherent observation models where standard RPCA degrades with increasing coherence.  
- Proposes a novel and effective method that circumvents RPCA’s theoretical limitations under coherence.  
- Problem is highly significant given the prevalence of coherent structures in real-world data.  
- Method is theoretically sound.  
- Empirically validated with strong practical performance.  
- Reviewer asks authors to comment on possible extension to subspace clustering/segmentation.  
- Overall assessment: high-quality work; recommend acceptance.
