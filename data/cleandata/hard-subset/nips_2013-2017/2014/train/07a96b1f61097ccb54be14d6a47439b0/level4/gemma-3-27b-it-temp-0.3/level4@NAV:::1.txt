This paper tackles the challenging problem of robust low-rank subspace recovery from data corrupted by outliers, specifically addressing scenarios where the observations exhibit inherent coherence. A key limitation of existing Robust PCA (RPCA) techniques is their performance degradation with increasing data coherence; this work effectively bypasses this theoretical bottleneck through a novel approach. Given the prevalence of clustered data structures in real-world applications, resolving this issue represents a substantial contribution. The method is rigorously grounded in theory and demonstrably effective in practice. 

I have a question regarding potential extensions of this work: Could the proposed framework be adapted to address subspace clustering or segmentation problems? The authors’ insights on this possibility would be valuable.

Overall, this paper presents a significant advancement in robust PCA. The quality of the work is excellent and I recommend acceptance.
