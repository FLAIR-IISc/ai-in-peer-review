- Theoretically shows that, under conditions on dictionary A, LRR can remove dependence on μ, yielding stronger recovery guarantees for exact and noisy low-rank + sparse decomposition.  
- Empirical simulations and real-data experiments support the theoretical claims and suggest applicability to other domains (e.g., text).  
- Related to prior subspace-clustering work but provides a more general, less prescriptive assumption about clustering structure.
