This paper addresses the important problem of robustly estimating a low-dimensional subspace from contaminated observations, particularly when those observations are inherently coherent. A standard theoretical limitation of prior Robust PCA (RPCA) methods is performance degradation with increasing data coherence. However, this paper cleverly circumvents this bottleneck. Given the prevalence of cluster structure in real-world data, addressing this issue is highly significant. The proposed method is both theoretically sound and demonstrates strong practical performance. 

I have a question for the authors: Could the proposed method also be applied to the subspace clustering/segmentation problem? Any comments on this potential connection would be appreciated.

Overall, this paper solves an important problem in robust PCA and is of very high quality. I recommend acceptance.
