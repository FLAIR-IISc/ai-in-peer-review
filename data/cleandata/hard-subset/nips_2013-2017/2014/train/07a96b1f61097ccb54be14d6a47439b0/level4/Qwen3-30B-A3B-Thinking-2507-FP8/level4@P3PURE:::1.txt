

This paper addresses robust low-dimensional subspace estimation under coherent observation models, where standard RPCA methods suffer from performance degradation as data coherence increases. The authors circumvent this theoretical limitation in a novel and effective manner. Given the prevalence of such coherent structures in real-world data, solving this problem is highly significant. The proposed method is theoretically sound and empirically validated through strong practical performance.  

The authors might consider addressing whether the approach extends to subspace clustering/segmentation problems. Could they provide comments on this potential application?  

This paper tackles an important challenge in robust PCA. The quality is high, and it should be accepted.
