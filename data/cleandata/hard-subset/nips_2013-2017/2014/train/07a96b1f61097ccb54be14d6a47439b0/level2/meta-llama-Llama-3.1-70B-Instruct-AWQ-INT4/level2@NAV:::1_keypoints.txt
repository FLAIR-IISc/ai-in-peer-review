- Argues RPCA is insufficient for coherent data common in many applications.
- Claims LRR can avoid coherence parameters that grow with the number of clusters.
- Claims the dictionary matrix A should be low-rank and contain information about the ground-truth matrix.
- Claims the proposed algorithm outperforms RPCA in extensive experiments.
- Provides theoretical analysis proving LRR can avoid coherence parameters under certain conditions.
- Demonstrates effectiveness with experiments on synthetic and real data.
