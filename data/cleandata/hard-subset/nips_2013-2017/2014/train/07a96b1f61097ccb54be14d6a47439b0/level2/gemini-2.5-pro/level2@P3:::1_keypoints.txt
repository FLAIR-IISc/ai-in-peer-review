- Addresses recovering a low-rank matrix corrupted by gross sparse errors and highlights RPCA’s degradation when the low-rank matrix is coherent.  
- Identifies and formalizes the "µ2-phenomenon": µ2 coherence increases with number of clusters (union-of-subspaces structure), degrading RPCA recovery.  
- Proposes using Low-Rank Representation (LRR) with dictionary A and shows (Theorem 1) that if A is low-rank and its column space contains that of L0, recovery is independent of µ2.  
- Provides a practical two-stage unsupervised algorithm: RPCA for initial L0 estimate, build low-rank dictionary from it, then apply LRR for refined recovery.  
- Theoretical contributions are formalized as theorems and supported by experiments on synthetic and real data.  
- Synthetic experiments show a larger successful recovery region for the proposed method compared to RPCA.  
- Real-world experiments (corrupted motion sequences) demonstrate significant practical gains, improving SSC error rate by ~40% relative.  
- Paper is well-written, organized, and includes helpful notation, figures, and clear illustrations of the µ2-phenomenon.  
- Novel contributions: linking coherence to clustering, proposing LRR with a learned low-rank dictionary to mitigate coherence, and proving µ2-immunity under dictionary conditions.  
- Significance: addresses a key RPCA limitation, yields a simple and computationally efficient method likely useful in practice and impactful for related research.  
- Weakness: practical algorithm depends on quality of initial RPCA estimate (heuristic two-step refinement).  
- Weakness: theoretical analysis relies on the strong condition U0 ⊂ UA, which holds only approximately in unsupervised settings.  
- Suggested improvements: analyze robustness to imperfect initial RPCA estimates and characterize the basin of attraction for the refinement.  
- Suggested improvements: study sensitivity to the rank-estimation heuristic (σi > 10−3σ1) used to build the dictionary.  
- Suggested improvements: discuss stability of LRR solutions when U0 is only approximately contained in UA (connect theory to practice).  
- Overall: strengths outweigh weaknesses; the paper offers novel theory, a practical algorithm, and compelling empirical validation.
