- Novel EM algorithm for learning the full DPP kernel via eigendecomposition.  
- Thorough background on DPPs and prior work.  
- Well-written and well-organized with clear mathematical notation.  
- Detailed computational complexity analysis and comparison to projected gradient ascent.  
- Demonstrated effectiveness on a product recommendation task with promising results.  
- Application of EM to DPP learning is novel though EM itself is standard.  
- Assumes substantial prior DPP knowledge, reducing accessibility for some readers.  
- Lacks a detailed convergence analysis of the proposed optimization algorithm.  
- Experimental results are limited; needs evaluation on larger or more challenging datasets.  
- Potentially significant impact on recommendation, summarization, and sensor placement.  
- Arguments for acceptance: novelty, clarity, and solid complexity analysis.  
- Arguments against acceptance: accessibility issues, missing convergence proofs, and limited experiments.  
- Overall recommendation: accept with minor revisions to address stated weaknesses.
