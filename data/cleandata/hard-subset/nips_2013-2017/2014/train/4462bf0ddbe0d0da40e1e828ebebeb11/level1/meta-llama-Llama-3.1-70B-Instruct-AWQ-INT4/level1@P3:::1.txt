This paper proposes a novel algorithm for learning the full kernel matrix of a determinantal point process (DPP) using expectation-maximization (EM). The authors change the kernel parameterization from matrix entries to eigenvalues and eigenvectors, which allows them to develop an effective optimization procedure. The proposed EM algorithm is compared to a naive approach of maximizing likelihood by projected gradient ascent on the entries of the kernel matrix, and it is shown to be more robust and faster.

The paper is well-written, and the authors provide a clear and concise introduction to DPPs and their applications. The proposed EM algorithm is well-motivated, and the authors provide a detailed derivation of the algorithm. The experimental results are also well-presented, and the authors provide a thorough analysis of the performance of the proposed algorithm.

* The proposed EM algorithm is novel and well-motivated.

* The experimental results are well-presented, and the authors provide a thorough analysis of the performance of the proposed algorithm.
