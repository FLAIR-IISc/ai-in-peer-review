- Proposes a novel EM algorithm to learn the full DPP kernel by reparameterizing via eigenvalues and eigenvectors.  
- Eigen-based parameterization enables an effective optimization procedure and EM derivation.  
- EM is shown to be more robust and faster than naive projected gradient ascent on kernel entries.  
- Paper is well-written with a clear, concise introduction to DPPs and their applications.  
- Provides a detailed and rigorous derivation of the proposed EM algorithm.  
- Experimental results are well-presented and include thorough performance analysis.  
- Strength: novel, well-motivated EM method.  
- Strength: clear exposition and detailed algorithm derivation.  
- Strength: strong experimental presentation and analysis.  
- Weakness: assumes reader familiarity with DPPs, reducing accessibility for some readers.  
- Weakness: does not provide a thorough computational complexity analysis.  
- Weakness: experiments are limited to a single dataset, so generalization is unclear.  
- Recommendation: accept with minor revisions to add complexity analysis and additional datasets.  
- Rating: 8/10; Confidence: 8/10.
