- Proposes an EM algorithm for learning DPPs combining eigendecomposition, Stiefel manifold optimization, DPP-as-mixtures representation, and Jensen’s log-likelihood lower bound.

- Experiments show favorable behavior versus a simple K-Ascent baseline, but evaluation is limited and lacks comparisons to other subset-selection methods, leaving the benefit of DPPs unclear.

- Method appears well-founded and its effectiveness believable.

- Writing and presentation are unclear and sometimes sloppy, with notation undefined or introduced after use and a convoluted structure.

- Paper is highly original; reviewer is unaware of similar prior work.

- Contribution is a solid advance and useful for the subset-selection literature.
