- Proposes an EM algorithm for learning DPPs combining eigendecomposition, Stiefel-manifold optimization, mixture-of-elementary-DPPs representation, and a Jensen lower bound.  
- Experiments show the proposed method outperforms a simpler "K-Ascent" baseline.  
- Method is well-founded and believable in its correctness.  
- Evaluation is limited: experiments are sparse and do not compare to alternative subset-selection methods.  
- Experimental analysis lacks error bars and statistical significance testing.  
- Writing and presentation are unclear and sometimes sloppy, making the paper hard to follow.  
- Notation issues: some symbols are used before being defined (e.g., marginal kernel K, V).  
- Specific wording and explanations need clarification (e.g., “minimizes the change to Frobenius norm”, meaning of “weight”).  
- Section on p.4 over-relies on reference [26] instead of outlining the simpler approach directly.  
- Rationale for choices in equation (30) and the initialization (p.7) is unclear and needs justification.  
- Paper appears original and not similar to existing work.  
- The method is a significant and useful contribution to the subset selection literature.
