- Proposes an EM algorithm for learning DPPs, combining eigendecomposition, Stiefel manifold optimization, mixture representation, and a Jensen’s-inequality lower bound.  
- Integrates several nontrivial components into a coherent method.  
- Experiments show favorable performance compared to a simpler “K-Ascent” baseline.  
- Method appears well-founded and plausibly correct.  
- Empirical evaluation is limited and lacks comparisons to non-DPP subset-selection baselines cited in the paper.  
- Writing and exposition are unclear and occasionally sloppy, making the paper hard to follow.  
- Notation is sometimes undefined or introduced out of order; definitions appear after use.  
- Presentation traces the derivation and alternatives too early; should state the final method clearly first.  
- Work is original; reviewer is not aware of closely related approaches.  
- The method is a meaningful contribution to subset selection literature.
