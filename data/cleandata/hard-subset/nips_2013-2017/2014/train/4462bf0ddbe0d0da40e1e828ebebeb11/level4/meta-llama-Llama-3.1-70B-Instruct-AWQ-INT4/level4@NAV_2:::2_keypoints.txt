- Presents an EM algorithm for learning Determinantal Point Processes (DPPs).
- Method combines eigendecomposition of the kernel, Stiefel-manifold optimization, DPP-as-mixtures representation, and Jensen-based log-likelihood lower bound.
- Experiments show the proposed method performs favorably compared to a simpler "K‑Ascent" baseline.
- Method appears well-founded and plausibly correct.
- Evaluation is limited: experiments are few and do not compare against alternative subset‑selection methods.
- Recommendation to strengthen evaluation by including comparisons with other methods mentioned in the introduction.
- Writing and presentation are unclear and sometimes sloppy; notation is often undefined or introduced after use.
- Paper is unnecessarily convoluted—should present the main method clearly and postpone side discussions.
- Originality: the approach appears novel.
- Significance: a solid advance and useful contribution to subset‑selection literature.
- Specific clarity issues: marginal kernel K not defined (p.2, l.81); V not defined (p.2, l.100); unclear phrasing about Frobenius-norm minimization (p.3, l.159).
- Additional specifics: p.4 section is hard to follow and should outline the simpler approach rather than only referring to [26]; "weight" is undefined (p.4, l.189); rationale for choice in equation (30) and why empirical covariance/init was not used is unclear (p.7, l.324).
