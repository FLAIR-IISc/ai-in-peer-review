- Proposes an EM algorithm for learning DPPs that combines eigendecomposition of the kernel, optimization on the Stiefel manifold, representation as mixtures of elementary DPPs, and Jensen’s-inequality lower-bounding of the log-likelihood.
- Experimental results show the proposed approach behaves favorably compared to a simpler "K-Ascent" scheme.
- Method is well-founded and plausibly effective, but the empirical evaluation is limited.
- Experiments lack comparisons with alternative subset-selection methods, so the specific contribution of using DPPs is unclear.
- Writing and presentation are unclear and sometimes sloppy, making the paper hard to follow.
- Notation and definitions are often missing or appear after use, disrupting readability.
- Paper structure intertwines derivation and side remarks instead of presenting the method clearly followed by a discussion.
- Paper appears original; reviewer is not aware of similar prior work.
- The method is a solid advance and a useful contribution to subset selection literature.
