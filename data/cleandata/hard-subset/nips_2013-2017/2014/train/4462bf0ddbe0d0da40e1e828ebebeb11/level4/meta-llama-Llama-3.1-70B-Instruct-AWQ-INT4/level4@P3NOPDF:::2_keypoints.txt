- Proposal: Introduces an EM algorithm for learning DPPs combining eigendecomposition, Stiefel-manifold optimization, mixture representation of DPPs, and Jensen-based lower-bounding of log-likelihood.  
- Experimental result: Shows the proposed approach outperforms a simpler "K-Ascent" scheme on presented experiments.  
- Quality: Method appears well-founded and believable, but empirical evaluation is limited.  
- Evaluation gap: Experiments fail to compare against alternative subset-selection methods discussed in the introduction, leaving the practical benefit of DPPs unclear.  
- Clarity: Paper is not clearly written—notation is sometimes undefined, definitions are delayed, and the narrative mixes development with side remarks.  
- Presentation suggestion: Reorganize to present the core method clearly first and defer discussion/side remarks to later.  
- Originality: Work appears original; no similar prior work is known to the reviewer.  
- Significance: Represents a solid advance and a useful contribution to subset-selection literature.
