- Proposes an EM algorithm for learning DPPs combining eigendecomposition, Stiefel-manifold optimisation, DPP-as-mixtures representation, and a Jensen lower bound; experimentally outperforms K‑Ascent.
- Quality: Method is well-founded and believable, but evaluation is limited and lacks comparisons to other subset-selection methods.
- Clarity: Writing is unclear and sloppy, with inconsistent notation, definitions appearing after use, and a convoluted structure mixing method development and side remarks.
- Originality: Appears original; reviewer not aware of similar work.
- Significance: Represents a solid advance and a useful contribution to subset selection literature.
- Issue: Marginal kernel K is used before being defined (p.2, l.81).
- Issue: Variable V is not defined before use (p.2, l.100).
- Issue: Phrase "minimises the change to Frobenius norm" is unclear; should state "chooses the closest (in Frobenius norm) PSD matrix to Q" (p.3, l.159).
- Issue: Section on p.4 is hard to follow; should outline the simpler approach directly instead of only referring to [26].
- Issue: Term "weight" is not clearly defined (p.4, l.189).
- Issue: Rationale for equation (30) is unclear; explain why m_{ij} is not used and why empirical covariance would not serve as an initialization (p.7, l.324).
