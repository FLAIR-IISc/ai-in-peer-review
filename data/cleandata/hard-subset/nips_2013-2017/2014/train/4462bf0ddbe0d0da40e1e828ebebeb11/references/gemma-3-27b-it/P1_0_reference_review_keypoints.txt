- Summary: Proposes an EM algorithm to learn the full DPP kernel L via its eigendecomposition, avoiding problematic PSD projection in gradient ascent and offering runtime benefits.
- Relation to prior work: Positions contribution against scalar-weight, ensemble, and parametric L methods, and notes connections to k-DPPs and previous learning approaches.
- Novelty: EM formulation over eigenvalues/eigenvectors is a clever, genuinely novel approach to DPP learning.
- Theoretical soundness: Derivation using Jensen’s inequality and closed-form eigenvalue updates appears correct and well-explained.
- Practical advantages: EM avoids degeneracy of gradient ascent and can be faster; runtime analysis is insightful.
- Empirical validation: Experiments on a baby registry dataset show improved robustness and efficiency versus a K-Ascent baseline.
- Clarity & reproducibility: Paper is well-written, mathematically clear, and authors provide code/data.
- Implementation complexity: EM (notably Stiefel manifold updates) is non-trivial to implement and needs more practical guidance.
- Sensitivity to initialization: Performance depends significantly on initialization (Wishart vs moment-matching), requiring further exploration.
- Limited evaluation domain: Experiments are confined to a single baby registry dataset, so generalizability is unclear.
- Step size selection: The choice and impact of the coordinate-ascent step size η are not fully detailed.
- k-DPP marginalization: The use of k-DPP marginalization for eigenvalue updates is mentioned but not sufficiently elaborated.
- Overall assessment: Strong, significant contribution with theoretical and empirical merit, but with some practical concerns.
- Arguments for acceptance: Addresses an important problem, offers a novel sound method, shows empirical benefits, and provides code for reproducibility.
- Arguments against acceptance: Complex implementation, limited datasets, and sensitivity to initialization.
- Recommendation: Accept with minor revisions.
- Requested revisions: Add implementation details for Stiefel updates, investigate alternative initializations, test on more datasets, clarify step-size selection, and elaborate k-DPP marginalization.
- Scores: Quality 9/10, Clarity 8/10, Originality 9/10, Significance 8/10.
