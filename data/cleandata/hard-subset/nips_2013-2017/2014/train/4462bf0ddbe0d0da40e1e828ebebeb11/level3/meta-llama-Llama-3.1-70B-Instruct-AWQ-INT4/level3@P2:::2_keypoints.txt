- Proposes a novel EM algorithm for learning full DPP kernels using eigendecomposition, Stiefel-manifold optimization, mixtures of elementary DPPs, and a Jensen lower bound.  
- Changes kernel parameterization from matrix entries to eigenvalues/eigenvectors to avoid projection and handle non-convexity.  
- EM derivation is well-founded, original, and clearly presented in places.  
- Experimental results show EM outperforms a simple K-Ascent baseline.  
- Experiments are limited in scope and lack comparisons to other subset-selection methods, leaving practical benefits unclear.  
- Paper write-up is sloppy and sometimes hard to follow, with interleaved derivations and side remarks that obscure the main thread.  
- Notation problems: key symbols (e.g., marginal kernel K and V) are used before being defined.  
- Language and phrasing are unclear in places (e.g., misuse of “minimises the change to Frobenius norm”).  
- External references (e.g., [26]) are not adequately summarized; simpler approaches should be outlined directly.  
- Ambiguous terms need clarification (e.g., what “weight” means on p.4 line 189).  
- Questionable rationale in equation (30); authors should explain choices like not using m_{ij} and not initializing with empirical covariance.  
- Overall: technically solid and original with significant potential, but needs much clearer exposition and substantially stronger experimental comparisons.
