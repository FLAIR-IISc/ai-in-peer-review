This paper proposes some new and sufficient conditions that ensure whether a causal question can be inferred from the data in the presence of missing observations. It extends previous work that explicitly models the process of creating missing data in causal diagrams. Although the paper is clearly written and builds on previous contributions through this method, additional insights, according to this reviewer, are a valuable addition to the state of the art in analyzing causal processes in the presence of missing data. It is a dedicated paper, clearly written.
