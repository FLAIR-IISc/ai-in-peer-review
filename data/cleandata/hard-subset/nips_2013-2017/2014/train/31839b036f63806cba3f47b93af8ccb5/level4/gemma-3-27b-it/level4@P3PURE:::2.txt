This paper presents novel necessary and sufficient conditions for determining whether a causal query is identifiable given data with missing observations. It extends prior work, which explicitly models the missing data mechanism, by incorporating this mechanism directly into the causal diagram. The paper is clearly written and, while building upon previous contributions in this area, the additional insights provided represent a valuable contribution to the state of the art in the analysis of causal mechanisms under missing data. This is a focused and well-written work.
