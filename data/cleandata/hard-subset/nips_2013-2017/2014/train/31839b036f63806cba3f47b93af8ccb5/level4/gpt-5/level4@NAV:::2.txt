This paper introduces novel necessary and sufficient criteria to determine whether a causal query can be identified from data in the presence of missing observations. It extends prior work that explicitly represents the missing data–generating mechanism within the causal diagram. The paper is clearly written and, while it builds on earlier contributions using this approach, the additional insights offered constitute, in this reviewer’s opinion, a valuable contribution to the state of the art in the analysis of causal mechanisms under missing data. Focused paper, clearly written.
