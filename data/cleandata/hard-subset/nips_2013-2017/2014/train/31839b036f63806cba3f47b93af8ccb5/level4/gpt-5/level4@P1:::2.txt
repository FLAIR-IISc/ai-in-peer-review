This paper proposes novel necessary and sufficient conditions for determining whether a causal query can be inferred from data with missing observations. It extends prior work that explicitly represents the missing data–generating mechanism within the causal diagram. The paper is clearly written and, while it builds on previous contributions using this approach, the additional insights constitute, in my view, a valuable contribution to the state of the art in analyzing causal mechanisms under missingness. Overall, it is a focused and well-presented paper.
