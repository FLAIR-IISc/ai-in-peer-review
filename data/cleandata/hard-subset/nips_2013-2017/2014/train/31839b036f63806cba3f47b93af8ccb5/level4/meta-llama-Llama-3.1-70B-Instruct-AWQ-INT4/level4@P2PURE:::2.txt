This paper proposes novel necessary and sufficient conditions for inferring causal queries from data in the presence of missing observations, extending prior work that explicitly models the missing data generating mechanism within the causal diagram. The paper is well-written, and while it builds upon previous contributions using this approach, the additional insights provided are, in this reviewer's opinion, a valuable contribution to the state of the art in analyzing causal mechanisms with missing data. Overall, the paper is focused and clearly written.
