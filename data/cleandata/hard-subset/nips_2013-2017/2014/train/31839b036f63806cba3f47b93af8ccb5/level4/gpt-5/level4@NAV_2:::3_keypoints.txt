Main comments
1) Question realism of assuming known censoring/missingness mechanism in ML applications; suggest moving more Figure 1 details into main text.  
2) Framework assumes full population roster is known (individual existence known); explicitly state this limitation (excludes population-size estimation settings).  
3) Inconsistent use of “m-graph” regarding inclusion of V* nodes across figures; clarify whether V* nodes are omitted only for brevity.  
4) m-graphs forbid R variables as parents of V or U despite treating R as mechanisms; justify this restriction or state it is a convenience assumption.  
5) The term “neighbor” is used without definition; provide a formal definition.  
6) Recoverability result assumes positivity; clarify if Garcia et al. impose positivity too, since their claim may need stronger conditions.  
7) Statement that “ML yields consistent estimates under MAR” is too vague; clarify why ML can or cannot be applied to the broader missingness processes studied and state scope/limitations.
