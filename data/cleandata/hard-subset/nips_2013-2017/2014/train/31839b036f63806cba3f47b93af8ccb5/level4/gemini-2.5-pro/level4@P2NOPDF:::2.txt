This paper presents novel necessary and sufficient conditions for determining whether a causal query can be identified from data in the presence of missing observations. The proposed approach extends a line of research that explicitly incorporates the missing-data generating mechanism into the causal diagram.

The manuscript is exceptionally well-written, and its core arguments are presented with clarity. While the work builds upon previous contributions that use this graphical modeling strategy, the additional insights it provides are significant. In my view, this focused paper constitutes a valuable contribution to the state of the art in the analysis of causal mechanisms with incomplete data.
