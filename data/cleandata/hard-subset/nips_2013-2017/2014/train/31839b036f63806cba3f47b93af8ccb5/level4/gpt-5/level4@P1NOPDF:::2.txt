This paper proposes novel necessary and sufficient conditions for determining when a causal query can be inferred from data in the presence of missing observations. It extends prior work that explicitly incorporates the missing-data generating mechanism into the causal diagram. The paper is clearly written; although it builds on earlier contributions using this approach, the additional insights offered here represent, in my view, a valuable contribution to the state of the art in analyzing causal mechanisms with missing data. A focused, well-presented paper.
