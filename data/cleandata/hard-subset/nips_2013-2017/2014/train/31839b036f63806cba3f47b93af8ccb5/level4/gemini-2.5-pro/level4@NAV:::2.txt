This paper establishes novel necessary and sufficient conditions for determining the identifiability of a causal query from data subject to missingness. The work builds upon prior research that explicitly incorporates the missing data generation process into the causal graph. The manuscript is well-articulated, and while it extends previous contributions using this methodology, this reviewer finds that its additional insights constitute a significant advance in the state-of-the-art for the analysis of causal systems with incomplete data. A focused and well-written contribution.
