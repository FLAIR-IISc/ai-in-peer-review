This paper proposes essential and sufficient conditions for a causal question to be inferred from data in the presence of missing observations. It expands on previous work that explicitly models the mechanism generating missing data in a causal graph. The paper is clearly written, and while it builds upon previous contributions using this approach, providing additional insights, it is, in this reviewer's opinion, a valuable contribution to the state of the art in analyzing causal mechanisms in the presence of missing data. The focused paper is clearly written.
