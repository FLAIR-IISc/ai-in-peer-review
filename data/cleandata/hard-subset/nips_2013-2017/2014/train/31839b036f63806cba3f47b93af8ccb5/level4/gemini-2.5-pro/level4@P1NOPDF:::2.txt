This paper establishes novel necessary and sufficient conditions for the identifiability of causal queries from data with missing observations. The approach extends previous work that explicitly incorporates the missing-data generating mechanism into the causal diagram.

The paper is very clearly written. While it builds upon previous contributions using this graphical modeling approach, the additional insights it provides constitute, in this reviewer's opinion, a valuable contribution to the state of the art in causal analysis with missing data. This is a focused paper that makes a clear and significant contribution to the field.
