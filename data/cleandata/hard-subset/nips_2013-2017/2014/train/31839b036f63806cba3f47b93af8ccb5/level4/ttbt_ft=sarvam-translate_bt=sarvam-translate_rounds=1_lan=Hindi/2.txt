This paper proposes novel necessary and sufficient conditions to ensure whether a causal question can be inferred from the data in the presence of missing observations. It expands on previous work that explicitly models the mechanism generating the missing data in a causal graph. The paper is clearly written and, while it builds on previous contributions using this approach, the additional insights provided are, in this reviewer's opinion, a valuable contribution to the state of the art in analyzing causal mechanisms in the presence of missing data. The focused paper is clearly written.
