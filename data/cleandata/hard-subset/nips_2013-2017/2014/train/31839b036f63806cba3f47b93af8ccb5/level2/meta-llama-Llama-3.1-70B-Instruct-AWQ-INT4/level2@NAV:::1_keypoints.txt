- Introduces new graphical criteria for recoverability and non-recoverability of joint and conditional distributions.  
- Provides necessary and sufficient graphical conditions for recovering causal effects in attrition problems.  

- Paper is technically sound with clear, concise presentation.  
- Presents novel, original results that extend existing literature on missing-data graphical models.  
- Well-organized and easy-to-follow exposition.  
- Addresses an important, practically relevant problem in ML and statistics.  

- Focus is narrow; results may not generalize beyond the specific recovery problem studied.
