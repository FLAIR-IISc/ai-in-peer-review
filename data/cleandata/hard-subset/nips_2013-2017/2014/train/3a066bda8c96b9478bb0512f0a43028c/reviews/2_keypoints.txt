- Proposes SVM modification that weights each example (0–1) in the loss, extended to latent/unobserved per-example weights.  
- Well described and experiments (semantic segmentation, object detection) show benefits over equal-weight training.  
- Major novelty concern: method appears equivalent to existing "example-dependent costs" (e.g., SVMlight); novelty over prior work is unclear.  
- Latent-weight extension is interesting but straightforward; optimization is non-convex, no theoretical analysis provided.  
- Heavy regularization is used, suggesting learned weights stay close to initial values — authors should comment on this.  
- Incorrect claim: saying positivity in [0,1] makes it non-standard regression is wrong (e.g., logistic regression is standard and bounded to [0,1]).  
- Minor typo in abstract: "continues" should be "continuous."
