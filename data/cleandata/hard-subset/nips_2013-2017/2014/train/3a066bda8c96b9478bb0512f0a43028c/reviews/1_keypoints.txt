- Proposes expectation-loss SVM (e-SVM) that handles continuous attributes of positive instances via soft weighting.
- Applies e-SVM to learn visual segmentation classifiers under weak supervision.
- Validated on semantic segmentation and object detection with promising results.
- Addresses an important problem in visual learning under weak supervision.
- Presentation is clear and well-motivated.
- Experiments are interesting and well-executed.
- Technical content is an extension of multiple instance learning by replacing binary positives with soft weights.
- Method applies in both observed and latent settings and represents a valid NIPS contribution.
