- Proposes modifying SVM loss to incorporate per-example weights in [0,1].  
- Extends method to latent (unobserved) per-example weights.  
- Method is well-described and yields empirical improvements in semantic segmentation and object detection.  
- Core idea closely related to established example-dependent cost techniques (e.g., SVMlight).  
- Latent-weight extension is interesting but conceptually straightforward.  
- Formulation becomes non-convex and the paper lacks theoretical analysis.  
- Strong regularization keeps weights near initial values; authors should discuss its role and impact.  
- Minor: incorrect claim that bounded [0,1] makes it “not a standard regression problem” (logistic regression contradicts this).  
- Minor: spelling error in abstract—“continues” should be “continuous”.  
- Overall: useful SVM variant but the fundamental concept of example-dependent costs is already known.
