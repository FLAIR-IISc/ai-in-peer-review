- Proposed method: semantic labeling via candidate segments, feature extraction, and regression on IoU; object detection uses weak bounding-box labels with latent spatial extent and weighted (transductive) SVMs, achieving competitive semantic labeling and state-of-the-art VOC2007 detection.

- Originality limited: algorithmic contributions modest and several relevant prior works on weighted SVMs and transductive SVMs are not cited.

- Missing citations: fails to acknowledge key related work (e.g., Suykens 2002, X. Yang 2005, Vapnik 1998, Lapin et al. 2014).

- Possible novelty: introduction of a sum constraint on weights might be new but needs literature verification.

- Significance: outperforming recent CNN results with a conventional approach is an important and interesting finding.

- Quality concerns: major omission of prior work; framework is engineering-heavy but not beyond established practice.

- Methodological comparison needed: justify sum-constrained weighted SVM for unit-interval regression vs. (regularized) logistic regression.

- Hyperparameter details missing: provide selection rationale and values for regularization coefficients λW and λR.

- Empirical strength: primary contribution is strong experimental results.

- Clarity issues: title/abstract misrepresent contributions and make incorrect claims about informativeness of regression targets; excessive focus on minor algorithmic variants and insufficient detail on feature engineering.

- Minor editorial comments: several typos and phrasing/abbreviation issues (lines 19, 94, 98, 350) that should be corrected.

- Recommendation: paper merits publication after revisions addressing prior work, methodological comparisons, clarity, and minor fixes, possibly better suited to a computer vision venue.
