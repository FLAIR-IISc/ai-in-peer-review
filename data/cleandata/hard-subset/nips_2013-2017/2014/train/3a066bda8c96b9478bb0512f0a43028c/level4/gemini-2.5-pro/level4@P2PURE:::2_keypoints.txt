- Proposes SVM modification that assigns continuous per-example weights in [0,1] and incorporates them into the loss; extends to treat weights as latent variables.  
- Method is clearly described and experiments on semantic segmentation and object detection show benefits over an equally-weighted SVM.  
- Core idea closely resembles established "example-dependent costs" (e.g., SVMlight); authors must clarify novelty and relation to prior work.  
- Latent-weight extension uses alternating optimization and yields a non-convex objective, but no theoretical analysis of the optimization is provided.  
- Strong regularization on the weights may prevent meaningful deviation from initialization; authors should discuss its impact.  
- Request comment on convergence properties of the proposed algorithm.  
- Minor: incorrect claim that bounded [0,1] outputs are "not a standard regression problem" (logistic regression produces [0,1]).  
- Minor typo in abstract: "continues" should be "continuous".
