- Introduces expectation loss SVM (e-svm) to handle continuous attributes of positive instances.
- Applies e-svm to learning visual segmentation classifiers under weak supervision.
- Validated empirically on semantic segmentation and object detection with promising results.
- Addresses a significant and relevant problem in visual learning.
- Strong motivation, clear presentation, and well-executed, interesting experiments.
- Technical contribution is an extension of multiple instance learning replacing binary positive weighting with soft weighting.
- Method is applicable in both observed and latent settings.
- Overall a solid, valuable contribution suitable for the conference.
