- Proposes modifying SVM loss with per-example weights in [0,1], and extends to latent (unobserved) per-example weights.
- Method is well-described and experiments show potential gains on semantic segmentation and object detection.
- Unclear novelty: approach resembles existing example-dependent costs (e.g., SVMlight) used widely in ML.
- Latent-weight extension is straightforward but makes the problem non-convex and lacks theoretical analysis.
- Strong regularization is used, implying learned weights stay near initial values; authors should justify/clarify this.
- Incorrect claim: logistic regression already produces outputs bounded in [0,1], so the paper’s statement about regression is misleading.
- Minor typo in abstract: "continues" should be "continuous".
- Overall contribution is weakened by prior work on example-dependent costs.
