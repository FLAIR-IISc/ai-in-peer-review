- Proposes SVM modification with per-example weights in [0,1] and extension to latent (unobserved) per-example weights.  
- Method is clearly described and experiments on semantic segmentation and object detection show potential benefits over equal weighting.  
- Novelty concern: approach appears similar to well-known example-dependent cost schemes (e.g., SVMlight) and authors do not differentiate their contribution.  
- Latent-weight extension is interesting but conceptually straightforward and yields a non-convex optimization problem.  
- No theoretical analysis provided for the non-convex formulation.  
- Strong regularization seems to keep per-example weights close to initialization; authors should comment on this effect.  
- Minor: L050 inaccurately claims bounded outputs aren’t a standard regression problem — logistic regression outputs in [0,1].  
- Minor: Abstract typo — “continues” should be “continuous”.  
- Summary: introduces a weighted SVM variant with promising results, but originality is unclear given existing example-dependent cost methods.
