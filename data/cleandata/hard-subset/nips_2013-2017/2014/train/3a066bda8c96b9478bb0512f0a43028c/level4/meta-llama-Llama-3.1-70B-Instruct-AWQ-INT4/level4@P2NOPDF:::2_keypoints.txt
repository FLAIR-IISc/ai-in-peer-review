- Proposes an SVM variant that incorporates a per-example weight in [0,1] into the loss and extends to treat unobserved weights as latent variables.  
- Method is well-described and experimentally shows advantages for semantic segmentation and object detection versus equal-weight baselines.  
- Unclear distinction from existing "example-dependent costs" — such costs are established and tools like SVMlight already support them.  
- Extension to unobserved weights is interesting but straightforward, yields a non-convex problem, and lacks theoretical analysis.  
- Strong regularization is used, implying learned weights remain close to initial values; authors should comment on this.  
- Incorrect minor claim that bounded [0,1] makes the problem non-standard regression (logistic regression is also bounded).  
- Typo in abstract: "continues" should be "continuous."
