- Framework: regress IoU for candidate segments for semantic labeling and treat precise object extent as a latent variable for bounding-box-only detection.  
- Methods: use a weighted SVM for regression and a weighted transductive SVM (latent e-SVM) for detection.  
- Results: performance matches state-of-the-art for semantic labeling and achieves best-reported results on VOC2007 detection.  
- Originality: limited algorithmic novelty; key prior work on weighted SVMs and transductive SVMs is not cited.  
- Novelty caveat: imposing a sum constraint on weights may be novel but not substantiated.  
- Baseline omission: should compare with regularized logistic regression for regression-on-[0,1] targets.  
- Hyperparameters: paper lacks details on how regularization coefficients (lambda_W, lambda_R) were chosen.  
- Engineering: object detection approach requires substantial engineering but is within norms.  
- Strength: principal contribution is strong empirical performance.  
- Clarity issues: title and abstract do not clearly state core contribution and misleadingly downplay regression targets.  
- Presentation balance: too much space on minor algorithmic variants and too little on crucial feature-engineering details.  
- Minor errors: several typos and notation/abbreviation issues listed; reviewer raised quality score pending revisions and recommends publication after addressing citations and clarity.
