- Proposes Expectation-Loss SVM (e-svm) to handle continuous attributes of positive instances in weakly supervised learning.
- Applied to visual segmentation classifiers; validated on semantic segmentation and object detection with promising results.
- Addresses an important problem in visual learning under weak supervision with strong motivation.
- Presentation is clear and the experiments are interesting and well-executed.
- Technical contribution: simple but meaningful extension of MIL by replacing binary positive-instance weighting with soft weighting.
- Method is applicable in both observed and latent settings, strengthening its practical value.
