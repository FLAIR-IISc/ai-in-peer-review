- Proposes modifying SVM loss with per-example weights in [0,1] and extending to latent per-example weights.
- Method is clearly described and experimental results show improvements on semantic segmentation and object detection.
- Novelty is questioned since example-dependent costs are already well-established (e.g., SVMlight).
- Latent-weight extension is interesting but appears straightforward and introduces non-convexity.
- Paper lacks theoretical analysis addressing the non-convexity from latent weights.
- Authors use strong regularization that keeps weights close to initial values; reviewer asks for justification and discussion of its impact.
- Incorrect claim that bounded [0,1] outputs are not standard regression (logistic regression contradicts this).
- Minor typo in abstract: “continues” should be “continuous.”
