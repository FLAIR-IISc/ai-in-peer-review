- Introduces expectation loss SVM (e-SVM) that accommodates continuous attributes of positive instances.  
- Applies e-SVM to train visual segmentation classifiers under weak supervision.  
- Demonstrates effectiveness with promising results in semantic segmentation and object detection.  
- Tackles an important weak-supervision challenge with a clear, well-motivated framework.  
- Experiments are interesting and skillfully executed.  
- Technical contribution extends multiple instance learning by replacing binary weights with soft weighting of positives (incremental but useful).  
- Applicable in both observed and latent settings, making it a valuable contribution to the community.
