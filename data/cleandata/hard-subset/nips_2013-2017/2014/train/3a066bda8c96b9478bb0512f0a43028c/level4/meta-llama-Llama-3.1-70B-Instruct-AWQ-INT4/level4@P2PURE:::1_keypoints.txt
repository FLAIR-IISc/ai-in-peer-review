- Proposes Expectation Loss SVM (e-SVM) to handle continuous attributes of positive instances.  
- Applies e-SVM to learn visual segmentation classifiers under weak supervision.  
- Validated on semantic segmentation and object detection, showing promising results.  
- Addresses an important problem in weakly supervised visual learning with clear, well-motivated presentation.  
- Experiments are interesting, well-executed, and demonstrate the method’s efficacy.  
- Technical contribution: extends standard multiple instance learning by replacing binary positive weighting with soft (expectation) weighting.  
- Method is applicable in both observed and latent settings, representing a valid conference contribution.
