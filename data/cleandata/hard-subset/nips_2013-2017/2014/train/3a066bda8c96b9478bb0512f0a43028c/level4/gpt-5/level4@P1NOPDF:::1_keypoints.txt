- Proposes expectation-loss SVM (e-SVM) to handle continuous attributes of positive instances in learning.
- Replaces binary positive-instance selection in MIL with soft weighting (expectations).
- Formulation covers both observed and latent positive-instance settings.
- Applied to weakly supervised visual segmentation and object detection.
- Experimental validation shows promising results on semantic segmentation and object detection tasks.
- Addresses an important problem in weakly supervised visual learning with strong motivation.
- Presentation is clear and the experiments are well executed and interesting.
- Technical contribution is mainly an extension of standard MIL (soft expectations vs binary), but the formulation is sound.
- Represents a valid and relevant contribution for the conference.
