- Introduces per-example weights in [0,1] for SVM loss and extends to latent (unobserved) per-example weights.  
- Method is clearly presented and experiments show benefits for semantic segmentation and object detection versus uniform weighting.  
- Reviewer questions novelty: approach appears similar to established example-dependent costs (e.g., SVMlight).  
- Latent-weight extension is interesting but straightforward, yields a non-convex problem, and lacks theoretical analysis.  
- Strong regularization is used to keep learned weights near initial values; reviewer asks for comment on this choice.  
- Minor inaccuracy: claim that outputs in [0,1] are nonstandard is incorrect (e.g., logistic regression).  
- Minor typo: abstract “continues” should be “continuous.”
