- Proposes SVM modification for weak supervision by assigning a per-example weight in [0,1], and models unobserved weights as latent variables.  
- Method is clearly described and experiments show potential gains in semantic segmentation and object detection.  
- Unclear novelty: resembles existing example-dependent cost approaches (e.g., SVMlight) and distinction isn't made clear.  
- Latent-weight extension is interesting but straightforward and yields a non-convex optimization problem.  
- Paper lacks theoretical analysis of the non-convex formulation and the impact of latent weights.  
- Strong regularization likely keeps weights near initial values; authors should provide insight or analysis.  
- Incorrect claim: bounding to [0,1] does not make it non-regression — logistic regression is an example of a bounded [0,1] model.  
- Minor error: spelling in abstract ("continues" should be "continuous").
