- Introduces an expectation-loss SVM (E-SVM) that handles continuous attributes of positive instances.  
- Applies E-SVM to train visual segmentation classifiers under weak supervision.  
- Methodology is rigorously validated on both semantic segmentation and object detection with promising results.  
- Effectively addresses a significant challenge in visual learning under weak supervision with clear motivation and presentation.  
- Experiments are interesting and well-executed.  
- Technical contribution: extends multiple instance learning by replacing binary positives with soft weighting, applicable in both observed and latent settings.
