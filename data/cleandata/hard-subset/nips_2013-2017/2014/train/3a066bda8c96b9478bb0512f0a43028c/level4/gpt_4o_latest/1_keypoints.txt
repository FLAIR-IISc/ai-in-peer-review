- Proposes an expectation loss SVM (e-SVM) that handles continuous attributes of positive instances.  
- Applies e-SVM to train visual segmentation classifiers under weak supervision.  
- Evaluated on semantic segmentation and object detection, showing promising results.  
- Addresses the important problem of visual learning with weak supervision; approach is well-motivated and clearly articulated.  
- Experiments are compelling and effectively executed.  
- Technical contribution extends multiple instance learning by replacing binary labels with soft weighting of positives and works in both observed and latent settings.  
- Overall, a meaningful and relevant contribution to the conference with clear presentation and useful results.
