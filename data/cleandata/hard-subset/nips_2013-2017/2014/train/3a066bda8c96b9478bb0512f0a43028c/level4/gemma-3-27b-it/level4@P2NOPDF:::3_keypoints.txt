- Paper proposes regression-based system for semantic labeling and object detection, predicting IoU for segments and using latent bounding-box variables for detection.
- Achieves state-of-the-art object detection on VOC 2007 and competitive semantic labeling results.
- Algorithmic novelty is limited and several relevant prior works are not cited.
- Weighted SVMs and related literature (e.g., weighted SVM, SVM+) are omitted from related work.
- The authors’ sum constraint on weights may be novel but is not thoroughly investigated.
- The proposed “latent e-SVM” appears to be a weighted variant of existing transductive/latent SVM approaches.
- Surpassing recent CNN results with conventional methods is a significant and interesting outcome.
- Paper quality is undermined by missing references and insufficient justification for method choices.
- Need comparison to regularized logistic regression to justify using sum-constrained weighted SVM for unit-interval regression.
- Clarification required on selection of regularization coefficients λ_W and λ_R.
- Paper devotes excessive space to minor algorithmic variants and insufficient detail to feature engineering.
- Title and abstract do not clearly state core contributions; one abstract claim about regression vs. binary labels is inaccurate.
- Several minor writing and terminology errors are present (typos, undefined abbreviations).
- Overall: strong empirical results but limited novelty; suitable for publication with revisions and better related-work discussion.
