- Proposes an SVM modification that assigns per-example weights in [0,1] and incorporates them into the loss.
- Method is clearly described and experimentally beneficial for semantic segmentation and object detection versus equal-weighting.
- Unclear novelty: lacks comparison/clarification relative to existing "example-dependent costs" (e.g., SVMlight).
- Extension to latent (unobserved) per-example weights is interesting but leads to a non-convex problem.
- No theoretical analysis of the resulting non-convex optimization is provided.
- Strong regularization on weights used in experiments suggests weights are heavily constrained; authors should justify/inspect this.
- Incorrect claim that bounded [0,1] makes the problem non-standard regression — logistic regression is bounded to [0,1].
- Minor typo in abstract: "continues" should be "continuous."
