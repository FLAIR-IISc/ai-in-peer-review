- Proposes several new methods for video classification, most notably a two-stream architecture and a multi-task learning framework.  
- Methodology, implementation, and experiments are described in sufficient detail; paper is clear and well-written.  
- Empirical results are strong, substantially improving over [13] and competitive with state-of-the-art.  
- The two-stream architecture and incorporation of optical flow are novel and a particularly interesting contribution.  
- Demonstrates that the optical flow stream provides significant performance gains of broad interest.  
- Work builds upon the framework of [13] but offers notable advances.  
- Paper’s focus feels divided across multiple contributions rather than unified by a single central theme.  
- Suggest revising abstract/introduction to emphasize the two-stream/optical-flow narrative.  
- Suggest adding two relevant citations (CVPR 2011 and BMVC 2010) to related work.  
- Recommendation: Accept to NIPS 2014 conditional on addressing the suggested revisions.
