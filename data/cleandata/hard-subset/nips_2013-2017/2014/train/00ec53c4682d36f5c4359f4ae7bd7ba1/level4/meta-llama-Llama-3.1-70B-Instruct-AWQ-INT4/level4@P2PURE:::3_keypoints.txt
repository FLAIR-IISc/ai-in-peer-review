- Methodology, implementation, and procedures are clearly described and well-written.  
- Demonstrates promising empirical results supporting the proposed methods.  
- Builds on prior work [13] and introduces a two-stream framework that leverages optical flow for significant gains.  
- The incorporation of optical flow is a key finding likely of broad interest.  
- Paper feels fragmented across multiple contributions rather than centered on a single theme.  
- Results show impressive improvements over [13] and are competitive with the state-of-the-art.  
- Suggests refocusing the abstract and introduction to emphasize the two-stream/optical-flow contribution.  
- Recommends adding citations: CVPR 2011 “Learning Hierarchical Spatio-temporal Features for Action Recognition with Independent Subspace Analysis” and BMVC 2010 “Evaluation of local spatio-temporal features for action recognition.”  
- Overall offers significant practical contributions; recommend acceptance pending addressing the suggested improvements.
