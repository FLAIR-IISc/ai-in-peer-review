- Proposes a two-stream neural architecture: a spatial ConvNet on single frames and a temporal ConvNet on short sequences of dense optical flow for action classification, matching SOTA on two standard datasets.
- Method outperforms other ConvNet-based action recognition approaches.
- Strong empirical evaluation and impressive results despite applying standard ConvNet pipelines to a new input modality.
- Systematic exploration of ways to train ConvNets on optical flow, clearly showing benefits of optical flow input.
- Minor: Unclear why the spatial network substantially outperforms the similar-architecture models from [13].
- Minor: Would be useful to compare different frame-sampling schemes for the spatial stream.
- Overall: Solid empirical contribution of interest to researchers in video classification with ConvNets.
