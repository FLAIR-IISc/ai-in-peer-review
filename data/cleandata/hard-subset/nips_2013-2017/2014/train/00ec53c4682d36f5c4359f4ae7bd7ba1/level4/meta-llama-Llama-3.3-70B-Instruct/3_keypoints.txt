- Proposes novel video classification approaches including a two-stream formulation and multi-task learning.  
- Methodology, implementation, and procedures are thoroughly described and the paper is clear and well-written.  
- Demonstrates novelty by building upon and improving the framework in [13].  
- Two-stream framework is particularly compelling; optical flow component provides significant performance gains.  
- Paper is well-organized and easy to follow but appears to span multiple contributions rather than a single unified theme.  
- Results show substantial improvements over [13] and are competitive with the state-of-the-art.  
- Suggestion: concentrate abstract and introduction around the two-stream/optical-flow theme for clearer focus.  
- Suggestion: add citations to key prior works (CVPR 2011 "Learning Hierarchical Spatio-temporal Features..." and BMVC 2010 "Evaluation of local spatio-temporal features...").  
- Offers substantial practical contributions and ideas that complement core deep learning concepts.  
- Recommendation: accept to NIPS 2014, contingent on addressing the suggested improvements.
