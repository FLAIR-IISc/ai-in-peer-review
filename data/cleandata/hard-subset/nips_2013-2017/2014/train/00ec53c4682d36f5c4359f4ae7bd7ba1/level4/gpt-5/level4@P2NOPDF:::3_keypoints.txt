- Introduces new video-classification methods: a two-stream architecture and a multi-task learning setup.  
- Methodology, implementation, and experimental procedures are described in commendable detail; manuscript is clear and well written.  
- Empirical results are convincing and show substantial improvements.  
- Contributions appear novel, building on prior work [13].  
- Two-stream design (appearance + optical flow) is particularly compelling and drives most performance gains.  
- Reported gains over [13] are impressive and competitive with state of the art.  
- Presentation can feel fragmented across multiple contributions rather than focused on a single theme.  
- Suggest tightening abstract/introduction to focus on the two-stream architecture and role of optical flow.  
- Suggest adding two missing citations: CVPR 2011 “Learning Hierarchical Spatio-temporal Features…” and BMVC 2010 “Evaluation of local spatio-temporal features for action recognition.”  
- Overall: significant practical contributions; recommend acceptance at NIPS 2014 contingent on addressing the above suggestions.
