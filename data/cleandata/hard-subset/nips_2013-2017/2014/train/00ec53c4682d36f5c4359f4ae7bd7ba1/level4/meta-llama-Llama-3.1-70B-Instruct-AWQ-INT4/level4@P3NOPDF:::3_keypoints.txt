- Proposes novel video-classification methods: a two-stream formulation and multi-task learning.  
- Methodology, implementations, and procedures are described clearly and in good detail.  
- Shows strong empirical results, improving upon [13] and competitive with state-of-the-art.  
- Two-stream framework, particularly incorporating optical flow, is a major and noteworthy contribution.  
- Builds on the framework established in [13] and extends it meaningfully.  
- Paper appears divided across multiple contributions rather than focused on a single theme.  
- Suggest tightening the abstract/introduction to emphasize the two-stream/optical-flow contribution.  
- Recommend adding citations to related work (CVPR 2011: hierarchical spatio-temporal features; BMVC 2010: evaluation of local spatio-temporal features) and other DL/CV literature.  
- Offers significant practical contributions that complement core deep-learning concepts.  
- Reviewer recommends acceptance to NIPS 2014 contingent on addressing the suggested improvements.
