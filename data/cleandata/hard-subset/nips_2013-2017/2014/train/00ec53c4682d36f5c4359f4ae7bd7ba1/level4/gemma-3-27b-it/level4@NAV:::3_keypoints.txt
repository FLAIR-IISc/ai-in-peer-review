- Proposes novel deep learning methods for video classification using a two-stream formulation and multi-task learning.  
- Methodology, implementation, and experiments are thoroughly described; writing is clear and well organized.  
- Builds on [13] and convincingly shows substantial benefits from adding an optical flow component to the two-stream architecture.  
- Multiple contributions feel somewhat disjointed and would benefit from a more unified central theme.  
- Results significantly improve over [13] and are competitive with current state-of-the-art methods.  
- Recommendation to revise abstract/introduction to emphasize the two-stream/optical-flow contribution as the primary focus.  
- Suggests adding citations to CVPR 2011 “Learning Hierarchical Spatio-temporal Features…” and BMVC 2010 “Evaluation of local spatio-temporal features for action recognition.”  
- Overall assessment: significant practical contributions and compelling ideas; recommend acceptance contingent on addressing the suggestions.
