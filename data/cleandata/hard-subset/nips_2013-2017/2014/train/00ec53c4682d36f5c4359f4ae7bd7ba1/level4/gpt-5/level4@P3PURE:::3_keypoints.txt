- Proposes new video classification methods: a two-stream formulation plus multi-task learning, with detailed methodology and implementation.
- Paper is clear, well written, and presents strong empirical results.
- Methods are novel; the two-stream architecture is especially compelling because incorporating optical flow yields significant performance gains.
- The paper’s presentation feels split across multiple contributions rather than tightly centered on a single theme.
- Results show impressive improvements over [13] and are competitive with the state of the art.
- Suggestion: refocus the abstract and introduction to emphasize the two-stream design and optical-flow contribution.
- Suggestion: add missing citations — “Learning Hierarchical Spatio-temporal Features for Action Recognition with Independent Subspace Analysis” (CVPR 2011) and “Evaluation of local spatio-temporal features for action recognition” (BMVC 2010).
- Recommendation: accept to NIPS 2014, conditional on addressing the suggested improvements.
