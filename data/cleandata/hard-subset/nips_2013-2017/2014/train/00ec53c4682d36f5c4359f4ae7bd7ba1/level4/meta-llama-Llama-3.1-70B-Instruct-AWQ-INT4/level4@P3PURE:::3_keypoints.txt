- Proposes new methods for video classification, notably a two-stream formulation and multi-task learning.  
- Methodology, implementation, and procedures are described in good detail; paper is clear and well-written.  
- Two-stream framework—especially the optical flow component—adds significant improvements and is of broad interest.  
- Methods are novel, building upon the framework of [13].  
- Results show impressive improvements over [13] and are competitive with state-of-the-art.  
- Paper feels fragmented across multiple contributions rather than presenting a single cohesive theme.  
- Suggest focusing the abstract and introduction on the two-stream/optical-flow contribution to better reflect the main contributions.  
- Paper lacks citations to relevant prior works (CVPR 2011 ISA paper and BMVC 2010 local spatio-temporal features evaluation).  
- Reviewer recommends acceptance at NIPS 2014 conditional on addressing the suggested improvements.
