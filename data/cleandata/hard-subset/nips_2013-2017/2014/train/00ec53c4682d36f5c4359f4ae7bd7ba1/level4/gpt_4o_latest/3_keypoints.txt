- Introduces novel video classification methods: a two-stream formulation and multi-task learning.  
- Methodology, implementation details, and procedures are described comprehensively.  
- Well-written and clear, with strong empirical results demonstrating the methods' merit.  
- Two-stream approach, particularly the incorporation of optical flow, is compelling and yields significant improvements.  
- Builds on framework of [13] but shows impressive gains and is competitive with state-of-the-art.  
- Paper feels divided across multiple contributions rather than a single cohesive theme.  
- Suggest focusing the abstract and introduction more on the two-stream/optical-flow significance.  
- Recommend adding missing citations: CVPR 2011 "Learning Hierarchical Spatio-temporal Features..." and BMVC 2010 "Evaluation of local spatio-temporal features for action recognition."  
- Overall recommendation: accept at NIPS 2014 provided suggested improvements are incorporated.
