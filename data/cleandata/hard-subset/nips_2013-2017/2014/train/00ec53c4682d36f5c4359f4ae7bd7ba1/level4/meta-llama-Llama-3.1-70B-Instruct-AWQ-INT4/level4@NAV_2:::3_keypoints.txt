- Proposes a novel deep-learning approach to video classification using a two-stream formulation and multi-task learning.  
- Clear, well-written paper with detailed descriptions of methodology, implementation, and procedures.  
- Methods are presented fairly and produce convincing, effective results.  
- Significant novelty, particularly the two-stream framework highlighting the importance of optical flow.  
- Results show substantial improvements over [13] and are competitive with state-of-the-art methods.  
- Paper feels fragmented across multiple contributions rather than a single unified theme.  
- Missing relevant citations in deep learning (e.g., CVPR 2011 "Learning Hierarchical Spatio-temporal Features...") and computer vision (e.g., BMVC 2010 "Evaluation of local spatio-temporal features...").  
- Suggest focusing the abstract and introduction on the two-stream formulation and incorporation of optical flow.  
- Suggest adding the missing deep learning and computer vision citations.  
- Recommendation: conditional acceptance to NIPS 2014, pending the authors address the suggested improvements.
