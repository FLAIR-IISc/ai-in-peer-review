- Proposes new deep-learning methods for video classification: a two-stream architecture and a multi-task learning setup.  
- Methodology, implementation details, and experimental procedures are described thoroughly.  
- Writing is clear and the empirical evaluation convincingly demonstrates strong performance.  
- Contributions appear novel, building on [13] but introducing notable advances.  
- The two-stream design (incorporating optical flow) is particularly compelling and yields substantial gains.  
- Results are of broad interest to the community and competitive with the state of the art.  
- Paper feels somewhat fragmented across multiple contributions rather than centered on a single unifying theme.  
- Reported results show impressive improvements over [13].  
- Suggestion: focus abstract and introduction more on the central two-stream/optical-flow theme.  
- Suggestion: add missing citations—CVPR 2011 (Learning Hierarchical Spatio-temporal Features...) and BMVC 2010 (Evaluation of local spatio-temporal features...).  
- Overall recommendation: accept to NIPS 2014, conditional on addressing the above suggestions.
