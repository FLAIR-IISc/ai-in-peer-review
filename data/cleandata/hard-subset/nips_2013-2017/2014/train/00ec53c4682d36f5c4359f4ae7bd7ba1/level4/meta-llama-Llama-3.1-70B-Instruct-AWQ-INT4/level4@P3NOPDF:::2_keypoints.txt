- Presents a novel neural architecture that combines a frame-based CNN and an optical-flow-sequence CNN for action classification.
- Achieves state-of-the-art performance on two standard video classification datasets.
- Method significantly outperforms other ConvNet-based action recognition approaches.
- Thorough experiments showing benefits of using dense optical flow as ConvNet input.
- Authors explored several training variants for the optical-flow ConvNet, with clear empirical gains.
- Unclear why the spatial (frame) network outperforms the similar model in [13]; needs analysis of design and training differences.
- Suggestion to compare different frame sampling schemes for the spatial stream to identify optimal selection strategies.
- Overall a well-crafted empirical paper with impressive results and useful exploration of model aspects.
