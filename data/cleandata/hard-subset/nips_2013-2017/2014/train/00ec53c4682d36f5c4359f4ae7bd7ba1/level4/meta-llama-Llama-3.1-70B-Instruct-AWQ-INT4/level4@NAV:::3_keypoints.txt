- Introduces novel video classification methods: a two-stream formulation and multi-task learning approach.  
- Paper is clear, well-written, and provides thorough methodology, implementation, and procedures.  
- Methods show promising results with impressive gains over [13] and competitive performance with state-of-the-art.  
- Novelty is a key strength, building upon and improving the framework in [13].  
- Two-stream framework with optical flow yields notable improvements and will attract researcher interest.  
- Paper reads like multiple contributions rather than a single cohesive theme.  
- Suggest refining the abstract and introduction to focus on the core theme (two-stream + optical flow).  
- Recommend adding relevant citations: CVPR 2011 (hierarchical spatio-temporal features with ISA) and BMVC 2010 (evaluation of local spatio-temporal features).  
- Offers substantial practical contributions and interesting extensions of deep learning concepts.  
- Recommendation: accept for NIPS 2014, pending incorporation of suggested improvements.
