- Introduces novel deep learning approaches for video classification: a two-stream architecture and a multi-task learning framework.  
- Methodology, implementation, and experiments are thoroughly described and clearly presented.  
- Writing is precise and the proposed methods are convincingly validated with strong empirical results.  
- Two-stream formulation is particularly valuable; integrating optical flow substantially improves performance and is of broad interest.  
- Paper is well-structured and achieves substantial gains over [13], with competitive performance against state-of-the-art methods.  
- Narrative is fragmented across multiple contributions rather than unified under a single coherent theme.  
- Recommend refocusing the abstract and introduction to emphasize optical flow integration as the core contribution.  
- Critical missing citations to add: "Learning Hierarchical Spatio-temporal Features for Action Recognition with Independent Subspace Analysis" (CVPR 2011) and "Evaluation of local spatio-temporal features for action recognition" (BMVC 2010).  
- Overall recommendation: accept at NIPS 2014, contingent on addressing the suggested revisions.
