- Introduces novel video classification methods: a two-stream formulation plus a multi-task learning framework.  
- Methodology, implementation, and experiments are described with sufficient clarity and precision.  
- Paper is well-structured and clearly written overall.  
- Two-stream formulation (incorporating optical flow) is the key insightful contribution and yields significant performance gains.  
- Achieves impressive improvements over [13] and is competitive with state-of-the-art methods.  
- Contributions are novel, though they build upon the framework of [13].  
- Presentation feels occasionally fragmented, integrating multiple contributions without a unified central theme.  
- Revision needed: abstract and introduction should focus more cohesively on the two-stream/optical-flow core contribution.  
- Revision needed: add omitted prior work, specifically CVPR 2011 "Learning Hierarchical Spatio-temporal Features…" and BMVC 2010 "Evaluation of Local Spatio-temporal Features…".  
- Recommendation: accept at NIPS 2014, subject to addressing the suggested revisions.
