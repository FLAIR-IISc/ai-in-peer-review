- Proposes two novel deep learning methods for video classification: a two-stream architecture and a multi-task learning approach.  
- Methodology, implementation, and experimental procedures are described in sufficient detail.  
- Paper is well-written and clearly presented.  
- Methods are novel, building on [13], with the two-stream architecture a particularly interesting contribution.  
- Optical flow stream provides significant performance improvements — a key result.  
- Contributions feel somewhat divided rather than unified under a single theme.  
- Results show impressive improvements over [13] and are competitive with the state-of-the-art.  
- Suggest revision: refocus abstract/introduction to emphasize the two-stream architecture and optical flow.  
- Missing relevant citations: Le et al. (CVPR 2011) and Wang et al. (BMVC 2010).  
- Recommendation: accept to NIPS 2014 conditional on addressing the suggested improvements.
