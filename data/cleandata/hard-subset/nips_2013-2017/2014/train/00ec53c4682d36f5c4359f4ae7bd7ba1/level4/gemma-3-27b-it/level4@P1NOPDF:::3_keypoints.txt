- Proposes several new deep-learning methods for video classification, notably a two-stream formulation and multi-task learning.  
- Methodology, implementation details, and experimental procedures are described thoroughly.  
- Paper is clear, well-written, and convincingly demonstrates effectiveness through strong experimental results.  
- Approaches are novel and build on [13]; the two-stream network is a particularly interesting contribution.  
- Incorporation of optical flow into the two-stream network yields significant improvement and will interest researchers.  
- Paper feels somewhat dispersed across multiple contributions rather than focused on a single unifying theme.  
- Results show impressive improvements over [13] and are competitive with the current state of the art.  
- Suggestion: refocus abstract/introduction to emphasize the two-stream network and optical flow as the primary contribution.  
- Suggestion: add missing citations — CVPR 2011 “Learning Hierarchical Spatio-temporal Features…” (ISA) and BMVC 2010 “Evaluation of local spatio-temporal features for action recognition.”  
- Overall recommendation: accept to NIPS 2014, contingent on addressing the suggested improvements.
