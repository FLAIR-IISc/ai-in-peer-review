- Proposes novel video classification methods using a two-stream network and multi-task learning.
- Methodology, implementation, and experimental procedures are described thoroughly.
- Paper is clearly written and demonstrates effectiveness via strong experimental results.
- Two-stream approach with optical flow is a particularly compelling and novel contribution.
- Results show significant improvement over [13] and are competitive with state-of-the-art.
- Paper is well-organized but feels fragmented across multiple contributions rather than a single unifying theme.
- Recommend focusing the abstract and introduction on the two-stream network and optical flow integration.
- Missing relevant citations that should be included: “Learning Hierarchical Spatio-temporal Features for Action Recognition with Independent Subspace Analysis” (CVPR 2011) and “Evaluation of local spatio-temporal features for action recognition” (BMVC 2010).
- Overall recommendation: accept to NIPS 2014, contingent on addressing the suggested improvements.
