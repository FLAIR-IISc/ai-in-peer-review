- Proposes novel deep learning methods for video classification: a two-stream architecture plus multi-task learning.
- Methodology, implementation, and experiments are described in sufficient detail; paper is well-written.
- Two-stream architecture—particularly the optical flow stream—is a notable and interesting contribution.
- Demonstrates that the optical flow stream yields significant performance improvements.
- Presents multiple distinct contributions, which slightly dilute the central message.
- Results show impressive improvements over [13] and are competitive with the state-of-the-art.
- Suggest revising abstract/introduction to emphasize the two-stream/optical-flow focus.
- Suggest adding omitted relevant citations: Le et al. (CVPR 2011) and Wang et al. (BMVC 2010).
- Recommendation: accept to NIPS 2014, conditional on addressing the suggested improvements.
