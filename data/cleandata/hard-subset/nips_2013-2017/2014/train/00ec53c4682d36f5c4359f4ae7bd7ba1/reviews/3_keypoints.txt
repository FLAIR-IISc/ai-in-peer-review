- Proposes new methods for video classification: two-stream formulation and multi-task learning.  
- Methodology, implementation, and procedures are described in good detail.  
- Paper is clear, well written, and presents a fair case for the methods with strong results.  
- Methods are novel and complement existing deep learning frameworks.  
- Two-stream framework is particularly interesting: incorporating optical flow yields significant improvements.  
- The paper feels split across multiple contributions rather than having a single cohesive theme.  
- Results show impressive improvements over [13] and are competitive with state-of-the-art.  
- Recommendation to refocus abstract/introduction on the two-stream/optical-flow significance.  
- Missing citations to add: CVPR 2011 "Learning Hierarchical Spatio-temporal Features for Action Recognition with Independent Subspace Analysis" and BMVC 2010 "Evaluation of local spatio-temporal features for action recognition."  
- Overall recommendation: accept at NIPS 2014 conditional on addressing suggested improvements.
