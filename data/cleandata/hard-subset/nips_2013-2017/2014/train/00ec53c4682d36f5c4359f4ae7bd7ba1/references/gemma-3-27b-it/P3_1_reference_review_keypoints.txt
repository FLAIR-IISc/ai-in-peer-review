- Overall assessment: Significant, well-executed contribution that convincingly decouples spatial and temporal information; recommended for acceptance.
- Core idea: Proposes a two-stream architecture with a spatial CNN for frames and a temporal CNN for stacked optical flow, fused for classification.
- Pretraining: Spatial stream leverages ImageNet pre-training to improve performance on video tasks.
- Temporal stream variants: Explores optical flow stacking, trajectory stacking, bidirectional flow, and mean flow subtraction.
- Fusion: Combines stream outputs via averaging and SVM; fusion methods are simple but effective.
- Multi-task learning: Introduces multi-task training to mitigate limited video data and improve learning.
- Empirical results: Achieves strong, state-of-the-art or competitive results on UCF101 and HMDB51 benchmarks.
- Ablation studies: Thorough experiments evaluate input configurations and fusion strategies, providing clear component insights.
- Neuroscience link: Draws an appropriate analogy to ventral/dorsal visual streams as biological motivation.
- Optical flow dependency: Performance is limited by optical flow quality; robustness to inaccurate flow and alternative motion representations are not explored.
- Computational cost: Lacks detailed analysis of computational expense, especially for optical flow computation and comparison to other methods.
- Fusion limitations: Only simple fusion techniques are studied; more sophisticated learned or attention-based fusion could help.
- Scalability concerns: Evaluation is confined to smaller benchmarks; scalability to larger datasets like Sports-1M is not demonstrated.
- For acceptance: Novel architecture, strong empirical evidence, thorough ablations, clear writing, and practical multi-task approach.
- Against acceptance: Dependence on optical flow accuracy, potential high computational cost, and limited fusion sophistication.
- Specific suggestion — Section 3.1: Clarify rationale for 224x224 input size (computational constraint or other reason).
- Specific suggestion — Section 4: Make the multi-task loss explicit by providing the combined loss equation.
- Specific suggestion — Table 4: Specify exact versions and parameters of state-of-the-art methods used for comparison.
- Future work suggestion: Explore learned motion representations and more advanced fusion techniques.
