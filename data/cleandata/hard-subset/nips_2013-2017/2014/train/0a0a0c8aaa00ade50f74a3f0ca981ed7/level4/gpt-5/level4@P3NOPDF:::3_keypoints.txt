- Integrates three biological constraints—Dale’s law, continuous non-binary rate dynamics, and sparse memory representations—within one framework.
- Optimizes a cost with three terms: fixed-point enforcement, stability margins, and weight regularization to prevent saturation.

- Inhibitory neurons act as optimized auxiliary variables that stabilize dynamics and balance excitation.
