- Introduces signal aggregate constraints (SACs) to incorporate domain knowledge into blind source separation/energy disaggregation.
- Proposes an additive factorial HMM (AFHMM) with SACs for single-mixture energy disaggregation.
- Paper is well-structured and ideas are clearly presented.
- Provides a comprehensive related-work review and situates SACs among prior AFHMM constraints.
- Derives a convex quadratic program for a relaxed AFHMM with SACs, enabling efficient computation.
- Clarifies relation to posterior regularization, strengthening theoretical connection.
- Experimental results on real Household Electricity Survey (HES) data show significant improvement over original AFHMM and recent state-of-the-art.
- Thorough evaluation includes discussion of computational complexity and scalability.
- Excessive and unusually long acronyms harm readability.
- Manuscript is hard to read in places; long paragraphs and poor organization reduce clarity.
- Authors place the constraint on the prior rather than directly on the posterior; this should be explicitly clarified.
- Overall contribution is significant; recommendation: accept with minor revisions (rating 8/10).
