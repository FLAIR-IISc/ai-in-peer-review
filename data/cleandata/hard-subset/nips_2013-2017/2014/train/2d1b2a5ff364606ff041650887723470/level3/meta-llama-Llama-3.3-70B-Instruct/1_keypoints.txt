- Proposes a novel blind source separation method: AFHMM augmented with Signal Aggregate Constraints (SACs).  
- Develops a convex quadratic program for approximate inference in the AFHMM+SACs framework.  
- Demonstrates strong empirical performance on toy simulations and real domestic energy disaggregation data.  
- Outperforms baseline AFMM and AFAMAP methods in experiments.  
- Paper is technically sound with a robust simulation section.  
- Writing and structure are clear and well-organized.  
- Lacks theoretical analysis (e.g., formal proof of convexity and scaling arguments).  
- Minor issues/clarifications needed (possible notation error, discussion of signal-to-noise ratios).  
- Novel and original contribution with significant improvements over state-of-the-art for energy disaggregation.  
- Recommendation: Accept with minor revisions to address theoretical gaps and clarifications.
