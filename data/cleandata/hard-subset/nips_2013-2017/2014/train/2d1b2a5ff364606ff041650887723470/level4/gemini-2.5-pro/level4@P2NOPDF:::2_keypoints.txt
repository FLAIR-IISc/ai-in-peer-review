- Proposes an additive factorial HMM (AFHMM) for aggregate electricity usage with a "signal aggregate constraint."
- Technical quality is good; the model appears novel and sound.
- Paper is very difficult to read; poor clarity obscures contributions.
- Excessive, often long acronyms disrupt the logical flow; reduce acronym use and spell out terms.
- Unclear motivation for constraining the prior rather than the posterior; explicitly state this is an a priori constraint based on prior knowledge and explain its effect on the posterior.
- Core contribution is valuable but needs thorough revision of clarity and exposition for the NIPS audience.
