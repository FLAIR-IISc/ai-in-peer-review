An additive factorial hidden Markov model has been developed to model electricity usage. This model incorporates the so-called signal aggregate constraint.

The paper is of good quality, but it is really difficult to read. There are many abbreviations, and some of them are indeed log (even 11 characters!). It seems that the authors wanted to impose a constraint on the density of the background, and therefore they imposed a constraint on the previous density. They should have stated that they have a previous constraint, and therefore a background constraint.
