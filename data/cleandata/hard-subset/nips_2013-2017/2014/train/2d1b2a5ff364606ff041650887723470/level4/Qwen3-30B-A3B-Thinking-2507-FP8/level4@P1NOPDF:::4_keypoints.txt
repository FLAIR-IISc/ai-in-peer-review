- Paper extends AFHMM by adding constraints that enforce the sum of chain outputs to match a known aggregate, and proposes a convex relaxation for MAP inference applied to large real-world energy-disaggregation datasets.  
- The core idea and optimization are straightforward but compelling due to application at scale and practical relevance.  
- Clarify whether SACAFHMM achieves the same integer-solution benefits as AFAMAP’s per-chain state constraints, or whether SACAFHMM attains lower error despite non-integer solutions.  
- AFAMAP and SACAFHMM appear orthogonal; SACAFHMM constraints could likely be incorporated into the AFAMAP optimization—exploring this combination may improve performance.  
- The paper conflates model names and algorithms (e.g., treating AFAMAP as a model rather than an algorithm for AFHMM); terminology and distinctions should be clarified throughout.  
- Overall assessment: sound work with convincing real-world results, but clearer technical distinctions and precise terminology would strengthen the contribution.
