This paper presents an additive factorial hidden Markov model designed to model electricity usage, which incorporates a signal aggregate constraint.

While the technical quality of the work is high, the paper's readability is a significant concern. The manuscript's clarity is hampered by an overabundance of acronyms, some of which are exceptionally long, making the text difficult for the reader to follow.

Methodologically, the motivation for placing the constraint could be made more explicit. It appears the authors seek to constrain the posterior density and achieve this by placing a constraint on the prior density. It would be beneficial to clearly state that an *a priori* constraint is being introduced, which consequently ensures the posterior is also constrained.
