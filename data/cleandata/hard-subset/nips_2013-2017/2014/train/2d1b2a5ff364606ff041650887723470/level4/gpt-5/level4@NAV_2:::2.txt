The paper introduces an additive factorial hidden Markov model for modeling electricity consumption, augmented with “signal aggregate constraints” that encourage each latent source’s total signal to match a specified target.

The technical quality appears good, but the paper is difficult to read. There is heavy use of acronyms, some of which are quite long (e.g., 11 characters), which hampers readability. Conceptually, the approach aims to constrain the posterior distribution, but implements this via a prior-like regularization. The authors should clearly state that the constraint is imposed a priori, which consequently induces the desired constraint on the posterior.
