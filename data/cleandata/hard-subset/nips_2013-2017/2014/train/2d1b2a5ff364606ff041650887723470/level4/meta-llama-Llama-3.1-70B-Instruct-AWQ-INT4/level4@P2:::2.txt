This paper introduces an additive factorial hidden Markov model (AFHMM) that incorporates signal aggregate constraints (SACs) to address the problem of blind source separation (BSS) in energy disaggregation. The authors' approach is to constrain the posterior density by introducing a prior constraint on the aggregate values of the source signals.
