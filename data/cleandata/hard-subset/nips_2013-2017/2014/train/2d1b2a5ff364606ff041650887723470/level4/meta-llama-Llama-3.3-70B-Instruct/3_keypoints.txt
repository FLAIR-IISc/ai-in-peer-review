- Introduces AFHMM with signal aggregate constraints (SAC) and a convex quadratic relaxation for the model.  
- Method yields promising results on synthetic and real-world data.  
- Paper is well-structured and grounded in solid mathematical principles.  
- Motivation for addressing the problem is clearly articulated.  
- Evaluation shows significant improvements in performance metrics (NDE and SAE).  
- Comparison may be unfair: baselines (AFHMM without SAC, AFAMAP) use less data than SACAFHMM; should explore other methods that can incorporate SAC.  
- Figure 1 is unclear: AFAMAP appears to outperform SACAFHMM at distances >10^4, indicating poorly chosen constraints can drastically degrade performance.  
- Suggest extending the algorithm to handle more complex constraints (mutual exclusivity, limited resource usage, time-limited device operation).  
- Future work should broaden the model to accommodate a wider range of constraint types.
