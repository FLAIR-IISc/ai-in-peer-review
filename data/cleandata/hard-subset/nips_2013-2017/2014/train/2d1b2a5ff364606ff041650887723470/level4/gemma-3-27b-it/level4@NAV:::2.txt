The paper presents an additive factorial hidden Markov model for electricity usage modeling, incorporating a signal aggregate constraint. 

The technical quality of the work is strong; however, the presentation significantly hinders readability. The manuscript relies heavily on acronyms, including several excessively long ones (up to 11 letters). Furthermore, the authors appear to be imposing a constraint on the prior density with the intention of influencing the posterior, and this should be explicitly stated as an *a priori* constraint leading to a constrained posterior distribution.
