- Proposes an additive factorial HMM for electricity usage with a signal aggregate constraint.
- Technical contribution appears sound.
- Paper suffers from poor readability due to excessive, very long acronyms that hinder comprehension.
- Unclear motivation for the primary constraint: authors seem to intend to constrain the posterior but instead impose a constraint on the prior.
- Recommend explicitly state the a priori nature of the constraint and clearly explain how this choice propagates to constrain the posterior.
