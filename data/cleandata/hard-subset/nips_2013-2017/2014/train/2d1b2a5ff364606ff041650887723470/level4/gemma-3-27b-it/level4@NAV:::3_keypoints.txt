- Clear writing and solid mathematical foundation.  
- Well-articulated and justified problem motivation.  
- Evaluation shows notable performance gains in NDE and SAE.  
- Comparative evaluation may be unfair because SAC-AFHMM requires significantly more data than AFHMM and AFAMAP.  
- Need benchmarks against alternative methods that also leverage SAC, even if less efficient.  
- Figure 1 is unclear and may indicate AFAMAP outperforms SAC-AFHMM for large distances (>1e4); current discussion is potentially misleading.  
- Discussion should be more nuanced about cases where poorly defined constraints degrade performance.  
- Algorithm’s ability to handle more complex real-world constraints (mutual exclusivity, limits on simultaneously active devices, time-based usage limits) is unexplored and should be investigated.  
- Authors acknowledge scope for future work to extend the model to a wider range of constraints.
