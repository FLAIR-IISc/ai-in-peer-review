This paper develops an additive factorial hidden Markov model to model electricity usage, incorporating what the authors term a signal aggregate constraint.

The technical quality of the paper is good; however, it is very difficult to read. The clarity is significantly hindered by an overabundance of acronyms, some of which are excessively long (e.g., up to 11 letters).

A key point regarding the framing of the model also requires clarification. The authors seem to motivate the need for a constraint on the posterior density, but the constraint is ultimately implemented on the prior density. The paper would be stronger and less confusing if the authors explicitly stated that they are imposing an a priori constraint, which, in turn, constrains the posterior.
