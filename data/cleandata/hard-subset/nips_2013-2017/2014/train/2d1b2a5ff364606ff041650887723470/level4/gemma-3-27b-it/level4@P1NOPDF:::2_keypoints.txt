- Proposes an additive factorial hidden Markov model for electricity usage with a signal-aggregate constraint.  
- Paper is a solid contribution but the manuscript is difficult to read.  
- Excessive use of acronyms, some excessively long (up to eleven letters).  
- Conceptual issue: authors appear to aim to constrain the posterior but instead impose the constraint on the prior; they must explicitly state this is an a priori constraint and clarify how it influences the posterior.  
- Review text is incomplete—the reviewer requested the full remainder of the review to continue polishing.
