- Proposes an additive factorial HMM to model electricity usage, incorporating a signal aggregate constraint.  
- Overall paper quality is good.  
- Paper is challenging to read due to excessive use of acronyms (some very long), which harms clarity.  
- Authors appear to impose a posterior constraint by constraining the prior; they should explicitly state this a priori constraint and how it constrains the posterior.  
- Need clearer separation between technical model details and the underlying assumptions.  
- Provide more context and explicit explanations for the numerous acronyms to improve accessibility.  
- Readability and clarity should be improved through revisions.
