- Clearly written manuscript with a solid mathematical foundation.  
- Well-articulated and compelling motivation for the problem.  
- Formulation of inference as a convex quadratic problem for a relaxed AFHMM.  
- Strong empirical performance on synthetic and real-world datasets, improving NDE and SAE.  
- Experimental comparison may be unfair: baselines do not leverage aggregate signal constraints (SAC), so gains could stem from extra data access.  
- Figure 1 may contradict claims (AFAMAP appears to outperform SACAFHMM for distances > 10^4); the paper does not address whether poorly chosen constraints can hurt performance.  
- Paper should explore or discuss extending the framework to richer constraint types (logical/temporal, mutual exclusion, cardinality, duration) to strengthen contributions and outline future work.
