- Proposes SACAFHMM: AFHMM extended with sum-consistency constraints and a convex-relaxed MAP inference, applied to energy disaggregation on two real datasets.  
- Strength: simple, sensible optimization combined with large-scale real-world experiments; results are promising and valuable.  
- Clarify whether SACAFHMM produces solutions closer to integer values or whether performance gains arise from handling non-integral solutions.  
- Suggest exploring incorporation of SACAFHMM constraints into AFAMAP (single-chain-active) to test for synergistic improvements.  
- Notes AFAMAP and SACAFHMM are orthogonal strategies; constraints from one could be added to the other.  
- Critique: paper conflates models and optimization procedures, making comparisons (e.g., “AFHMM” vs “AFAMAP”) imprecise.  
- Request confirmation that references to “AFHMM” comparisons actually mean the alternating Viterbi optimization method.  
- Advise improving presentation to clarify interplay between methods and distinction between model vs algorithm.  
- Recommendation: accept with the suggested clarifying revisions.
