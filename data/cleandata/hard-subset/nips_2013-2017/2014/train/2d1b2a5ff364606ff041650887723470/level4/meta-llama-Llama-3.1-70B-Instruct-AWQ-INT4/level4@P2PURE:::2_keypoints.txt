- Proposes an additive factorial hidden Markov model (AFHMM) to model electricity usage while incorporating the signal-aggregate constraint.  
- Overall paper quality is good.  
- Readability is harmed by excessive use of acronyms, some extremely long (up to 11 letters).  
- Methodological concern: the authors impose the constraint on the prior (not directly on the posterior); they should explicitly state this a priori constraint and how it influences the posterior.
