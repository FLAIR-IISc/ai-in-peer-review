- Novel method: formulates blind source separation as an additive factorial HMM (AFHMM) with signal aggregate constraints (SACs) to incorporate domain knowledge.  
- Motivating example: energy disaggregation—uses expected total appliance usage to improve component separation.  
- Technical soundness: method is technically sound and well-presented.  
- Empirical performance: outperforms regular AFHMM and AFAMAP; improvements are statistically significant (t-tests).  
- Optimization speed: runtime comparable to AFAMAP.  
- Theory presentation: theoretical sections are clear, but convexity of the final procedure (eq.6) is not fully proven and should be shown.  
- Scalability: missing theoretical scaling analysis with respect to data length and number of hidden states.
