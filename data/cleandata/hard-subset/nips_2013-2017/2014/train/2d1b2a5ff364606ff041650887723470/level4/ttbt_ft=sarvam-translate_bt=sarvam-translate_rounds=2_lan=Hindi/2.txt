A weighted factorial hidden Markov model has been developed to model electricity usage. This model includes a so-called signal aggregate constraint.

The paper's quality is good, but it's actually difficult to read. It contains many abbreviations, and some of them are actually logs (even 11 characters!). It seems that the author is looking for a constraint on the posterior density, and therefore they put a constraint on the prior density. They should have stated that they have a prior constraint, and therefore the posterior is constrained.
