The authors present an additive factorial hidden Markov model for electricity usage, incorporating the signal aggregate constraint. 

The paper's technical quality is satisfactory; however, its readability is compromised. The excessive use of acronyms, including some lengthy ones (up to 11 letters), hinders comprehension. 

Upon closer examination, it appears the authors aim to impose a constraint on the posterior density by applying it to the prior density instead. For clarity, it would be beneficial to explicitly state that an a priori constraint is being employed, which subsequently influences the posterior density.
