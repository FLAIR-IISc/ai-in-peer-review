An additive factorial hidden Markov model is proposed to model electricity consumption. The model includes the so-called signal aggregate constraint.

The paper is of good quality, but it is quite difficult to read. It uses too many acronyms, some of which are very long (up to 11 letters). It appears the authors intend to place a constraint on the posterior density, yet they impose it on a prior density. They should state that the constraint is imposed a priori and, consequently, that the posterior is constrained.
