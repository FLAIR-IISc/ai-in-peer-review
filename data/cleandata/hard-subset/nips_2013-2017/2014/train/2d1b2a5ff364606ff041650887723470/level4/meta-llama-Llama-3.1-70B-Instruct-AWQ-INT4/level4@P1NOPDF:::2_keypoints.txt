- Proposal: an additive factorial hidden Markov model for electricity usage that incorporates a signal aggregate constraint.
- Strength: overall paper quality is good.
- Readability issue: paper is difficult to read due to an excessive number of acronyms, some very long.
- Technical concern: authors appear to intend a posterior constraint but actually impose the constraint on the prior.
- Suggestion: explicitly state the a priori constraint and clarify how it consequently constrains the posterior.
