Introduces a constrained AFHMM that forces the sum of latent outputs to match a known aggregate signal and uses a convex relaxation of the MAP problem for inference, validated on two real-world energy disaggregation datasets (100 UK homes low-frequency; 10 homes higher-frequency without per-appliance monitoring).

Core conceptual contribution is incremental and the optimization approach is relatively standard, but the paper is compelling due to successful application to large-scale, practical datasets.
