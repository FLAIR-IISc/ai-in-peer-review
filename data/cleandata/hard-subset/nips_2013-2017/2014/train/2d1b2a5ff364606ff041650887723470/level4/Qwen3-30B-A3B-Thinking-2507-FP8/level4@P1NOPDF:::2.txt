

The paper proposes an additive factorial hidden Markov model for electricity usage prediction, incorporating a signal aggregate constraint. While the technical approach is sound and the paper's quality is good, readability suffers significantly due to excessive acronyms. Several acronyms are unwieldy (up to 11 characters), which hinders comprehension. More critically, the authors impose a constraint on the prior density but frame it as a constraint on the posterior density. This misalignment requires explicit clarification: the constraint is inherently a priori, and the posterior constraint is a consequence, not the starting point. The authors should explicitly state that they impose an a priori constraint on the prior density, leading to the constrained posterior behavior they observe. This distinction is essential for methodological clarity and should be addressed in the revision.
