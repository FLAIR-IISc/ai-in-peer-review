- Well-written paper with a strong mathematical foundation.
- Clear and compelling problem motivation.
- Empirical evaluation shows performance gains measured by NDE and SAE.
- Baseline comparisons may be unfair because competing methods use less data than SAC-AFHMM.
- Need for alternative, less computationally demanding ways to incorporate SAC.
- Figure 1 is hard to interpret and suggests AFAMAP may outperform SAC-AFHMM at large distances, implying poorly specified constraints can degrade performance and claims of consistent gains may be misleading.
- The work should explore more complex constraint types (e.g., mutually exclusive appliances, limits on number of active devices, time-duration constraints) and clarify whether the algorithm is extensible to such constraints.
