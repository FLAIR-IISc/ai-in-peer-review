- Well-written paper with a strong mathematical foundation.  
- Problem motivation is compelling.  
- Evaluation shows significant improvements in NDE and SAE metrics.  
- Comparison may be unfair because SACAFHMM uses more data than AFHMM and AFAMAP; alternative SAC-incorporating approaches with similar computational efficiency should be considered.  
- Figure 1 is unclear: AFAMAP outperforms SACAFHMM for distances > 10^4, contradicting claims of consistent superiority and suggesting poor constraints can greatly worsen performance.  
- The paper does not evaluate more complex, realistic constraint types (e.g., mutually exclusive devices, limits on number of active items, duration limits).  
- It’s unclear whether and how the framework can be extended to handle diverse real-world constraint categories; future work should investigate this.
