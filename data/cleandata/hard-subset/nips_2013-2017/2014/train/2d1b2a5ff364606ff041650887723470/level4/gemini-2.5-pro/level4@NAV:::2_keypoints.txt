- Introduces an additive factorial HMM for modeling electricity usage that incorporates a signal aggregate constraint.  
- Technical quality of the work is high.  
- Readability is poor due to an overabundance of (often very long) acronyms that impede clarity.  
- Methodological motivation for where the constraint is placed is unclear; it appears the authors constrain the prior to obtain a constrained posterior and should explicitly state this a priori constraint and its effect on the posterior.
