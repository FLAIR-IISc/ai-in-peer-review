This paper develops an additive factorial hidden Markov model to model electricity usage, incorporating a so-called “signal aggregate constraint.” The technical quality appears good, and the problem is important; however, the paper is difficult to read.

There are too many acronyms, several of which are excessively long (even up to 11 letters), which makes the exposition cumbersome. Please reduce the number of acronyms, define them clearly at first use, and consider replacing especially long acronyms with more descriptive terms.

In addition, the treatment of the constraint is confusing. It appears that the authors intend to place a constraint on the posterior density, yet they implement it by constraining the prior. If the intent is to impose an a priori constraint, please state this explicitly and make clear that, as a consequence, the posterior is constrained. Clarifying the target and role of the constraint would improve readability and interpretability.
