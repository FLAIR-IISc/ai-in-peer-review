- Clearly written manuscript with a solid mathematical foundation.
- Well-motivated problem.
- Evaluation shows notable gains in NDE and SAE.
- Comparisons may be unfair because competing methods (AFHMM, AFAMAP) use less data than SACAFHMM; ask if other SAC-capable but less efficient approaches exist.
- Figure 1 is unclear: AFAMAP seems to outperform SACAFHMM for distances >10^4, suggesting poorly chosen constraints can hurt performance and the claim that results are always better may be misleading.
- The paper only uses simple SAC types; it should address whether the algorithm can incorporate richer constraints (e.g., mutually exclusive devices, cardinality limits, duration limits).
- Overall: well written with strong motivation; future work should extend the model to broader classes of constraints.
