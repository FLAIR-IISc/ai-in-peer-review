- Introduces an Additive Factorial HMM (AFHMM) for energy disaggregation (NILM) that explicitly enforces a Signal Aggregate Constraint (SAC) so inferred appliance draws sum to mains.

- Presents a probabilistic framework and inference algorithm to learn model parameters and perform disaggregation.

- Directly embedding SAC in the model is technically sound and more principled than post-processing enforcement.

- Factorial HMM and additive structure are a natural, well-motivated fit for modeling multiple independent appliances.

- The core technical idea is strong and represents a clear advance for probabilistic modeling in this domain.

- Major clarity and readability issues significantly reduce accessibility and impact.

- Ambiguity in constraint formulation: paper implies a posterior constraint but actually constrains the prior/transition; authors should explicitly state they impose an a priori structural constraint to ensure the posterior property.

- Excessive, often long acronyms disrupt reading; reduce acronyms, spell out terms, and consider a notation/acronym table.

- Prose is dense and convoluted; needs simplification, improved narrative flow, and clearer logical progression.

- Overall promising contribution but requires substantial revision—especially clarifying prior/posterior logic and improving exposition—before being conference-ready.
