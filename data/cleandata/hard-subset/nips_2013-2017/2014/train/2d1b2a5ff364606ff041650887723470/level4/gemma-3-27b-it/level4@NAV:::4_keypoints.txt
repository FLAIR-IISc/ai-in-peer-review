- Proposes an extension to additive factorial HMMs that enforces constraints so chain outputs sum to a known aggregate.  
- Develops a convex relaxation for MAP inference and applies it to energy disaggregation.  
- Validated on large real-world datasets: low-frequency data from 100 UK homes and higher-frequency data from 10 homes without appliance monitoring.  
- Approach is conceptually simple and uses straightforward optimization, yet has impactful practical results.  
- Suggestion: clarify whether the single-chain-state constraint increases integer solutions or if SACAFHMM’s gains come from accurate non-integer solutions.  
- Suggestion: explore integrating SACAFHMM constraints directly into the AFAMAP optimization, as the methods appear complementary.  
- Issue: paper blurs distinction between model (AFHMM) and algorithm (AFAMAP); AFAMAP is an algorithm for MAP in AFHMM and should be clearly distinguished.  
- Overall assessment: a sound, reasonable contribution combining algorithmic and applied work with promising real-world performance.
