- Well-written paper grounded in solid mathematical principles.  
- Clear and compelling problem motivation.  
- Demonstrates significant improvement in NDE and SAE metrics on synthetic and real datasets.  
- Comparison fairness concern: AFHMM-SAC is compared to methods that use less data, which may bias results.  
- Question whether alternative, less computationally demanding methods could also leverage SAC.  
- Figure 1 is unclear and suggests AFAMAP outperforms AFHMM-SAC for distances > 10^4, implying poorly specified constraints can severely degrade performance.  
- The negative impact of mis-specified constraints is not adequately discussed, contradicting claims of consistent improvement.  
- Expected exploration of richer constraint types (mutually exclusive appliances, limits on number of active devices, time‑bounded usage); need clarification if the algorithm can be extended to these.
