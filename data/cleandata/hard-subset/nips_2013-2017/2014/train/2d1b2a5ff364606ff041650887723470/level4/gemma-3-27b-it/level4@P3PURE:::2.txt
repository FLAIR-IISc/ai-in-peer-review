An additive factorial hidden Markov model is developed to model electricity usage, incorporating a signal aggregate constraint.

The technical quality of this paper is good, but its readability is significantly hampered. The authors employ an excessive number of acronyms, and some are unduly long (even eleven letters!). A core conceptual issue arises in the imposition of the constraint; it appears the authors seek to constrain the posterior density by placing a constraint on the prior density. This approach should be explicitly framed as an *a priori* constraint, leading to the desired posterior constraint.
