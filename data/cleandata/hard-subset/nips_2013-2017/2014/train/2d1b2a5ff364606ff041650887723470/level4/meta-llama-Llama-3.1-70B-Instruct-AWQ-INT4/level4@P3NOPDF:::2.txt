This paper proposes an additive factorial hidden Markov model to model electricity usage, incorporating the signal aggregate constraint. The overall quality of the paper is good; however, it is challenging to read due to the excessive use of acronyms, some of which are quite lengthy (up to 11 letters). This detracts from the clarity of the presentation and may hinder comprehension for readers unfamiliar with the specific terminology.

Upon closer examination, it appears that the authors are seeking to impose a constraint on the posterior density, which they achieve by placing a constraint on the prior density. To improve the transparency of their approach, the authors should explicitly state that they have an a priori constraint, which in turn constrains the posterior. This clarification would enhance the reader's understanding of the underlying methodology and its rationale.

The paper would benefit from a more balanced presentation, with a clearer distinction between the technical aspects of the model and the underlying assumptions. Additionally, providing more context and explanations for the numerous acronyms would make the paper more accessible to a broader audience. Overall, while the paper demonstrates good quality, its readability and clarity could be improved with some revisions to address these concerns.
