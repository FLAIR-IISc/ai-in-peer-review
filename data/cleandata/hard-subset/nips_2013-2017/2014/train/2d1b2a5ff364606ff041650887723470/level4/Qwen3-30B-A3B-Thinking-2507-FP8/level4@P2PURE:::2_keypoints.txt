- Proposes an additive factorial HMM for electricity usage that incorporates a signal aggregate constraint.  
- Technical quality of the paper is strong.  
- Readability is significantly impaired.  
- Manuscript uses an excessive number of acronyms, some very long (e.g., 11 letters).  
- Authors appear to constrain the prior to indirectly constrain the posterior; they should explicitly state this is an a priori constraint so it’s clear the posterior inherits it.
