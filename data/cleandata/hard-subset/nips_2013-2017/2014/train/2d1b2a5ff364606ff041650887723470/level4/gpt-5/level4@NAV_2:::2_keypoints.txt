- Introduces an additive factorial hidden Markov model for electricity consumption, augmented with "signal aggregate constraints" to make each latent source's total signal match a specified target.
- Technical quality appears good.
- Paper is difficult to read due to heavy use of acronyms, some very long.
- Conceptually the method aims to constrain the posterior but is implemented via a prior-like regularization.
- Authors should explicitly state the constraint is imposed a priori, which then induces the desired posterior constraint.
