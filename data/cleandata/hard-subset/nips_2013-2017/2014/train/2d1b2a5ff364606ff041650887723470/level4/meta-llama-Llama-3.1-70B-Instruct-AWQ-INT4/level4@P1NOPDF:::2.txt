The authors propose an additive factorial hidden Markov model to capture the patterns in electricity usage, incorporating a signal aggregate constraint. The overall quality of the paper is good; however, it is challenging to read due to an excessive number of acronyms, some of which are lengthy (even up to 11 letters). 

It appears that the authors are seeking to impose a constraint on the posterior density, but instead, they place a constraint on the prior density. For clarity, it would be beneficial if the authors explicitly stated that they have an a priori constraint, which in turn, constrains the posterior.
