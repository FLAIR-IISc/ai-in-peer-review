The authors propose an additive factorial hidden Markov model to model electricity usage, incorporating the signal aggregate constraint. The overall quality of the paper is good; however, the writing style makes it challenging to follow. 

One major obstacle is the excessive use of acronyms, some of which are lengthy (up to 11 letters). This not only disrupts the flow of the text but also hinders comprehension. The authors would benefit from using more descriptive variable names or explaining these acronyms in greater detail to facilitate understanding.

Furthermore, it appears that the authors are seeking to impose a constraint on the posterior density, but instead, they place this constraint on the prior density. To clarify this approach, it would be beneficial for the authors to explicitly state that they have an a priori constraint, which consequently constrains the posterior density. This minor rephrasing would significantly enhance the readability and clarity of the paper.
