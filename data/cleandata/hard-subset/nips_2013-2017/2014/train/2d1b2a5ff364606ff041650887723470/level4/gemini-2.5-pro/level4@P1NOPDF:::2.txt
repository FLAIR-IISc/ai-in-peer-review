This paper proposes an additive factorial hidden Markov model to model electricity usage. A key feature of the model is the incorporation of the signal aggregate constraint.

While the technical contribution of the paper is sound, its overall quality is severely undermined by poor readability. The paper is challenging to follow due to an excessive number of acronyms, some of which are exceptionally long (extending up to 11 letters), which hinders comprehension.

Furthermore, there is a lack of clarity regarding the motivation for the primary constraint. It appears the authors' goal is to constrain the posterior density, but they achieve this by placing a constraint on the prior density. The paper would be much improved if the authors explicitly stated that they are imposing an a priori constraint and then clearly explained how this choice propagates to constrain the posterior distribution. This would greatly clarify the motivation and mechanics of their proposed model.
