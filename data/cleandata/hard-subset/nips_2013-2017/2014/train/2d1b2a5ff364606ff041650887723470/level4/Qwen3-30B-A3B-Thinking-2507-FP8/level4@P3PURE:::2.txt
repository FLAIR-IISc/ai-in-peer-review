

The paper introduces an additive factorial hidden Markov model for electricity usage modeling, incorporating a signal aggregate constraint. The quality of the paper is generally good. However, the readability is significantly hindered by an excessive number of acronyms, some of which are notably long (e.g., one exceeding 11 letters). The authors appear to impose a constraint on the prior density to indirectly constrain the posterior density; they should explicitly state that they have an a priori constraint, which consequently necessitates a constrained posterior.
