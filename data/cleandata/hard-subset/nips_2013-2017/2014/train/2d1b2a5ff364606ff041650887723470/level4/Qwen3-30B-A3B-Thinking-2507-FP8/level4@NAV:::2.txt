

An additive factorial hidden Markov model is developed to model electricity usage, incorporating a signal aggregate constraint. The paper's technical quality is sound; however, readability is significantly hindered by an excessive number of acronyms, some unjustifiably long (e.g., 11 letters). The authors appear to impose a constraint on the prior density to indirectly constrain the posterior density, rather than directly addressing a posterior constraint. They should explicitly state that their constraint is a priori, thereby necessitating the posterior constraint.
