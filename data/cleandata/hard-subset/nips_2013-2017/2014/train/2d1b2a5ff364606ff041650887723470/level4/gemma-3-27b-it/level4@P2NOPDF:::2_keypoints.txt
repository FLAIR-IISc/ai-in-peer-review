- Introduces an additive factorial hidden Markov model (AFHMM) for electricity usage with a signal aggregate constraint.  
- Technical quality of the work appears sound.  
- Presentation significantly hinders comprehension.  
- Excessive, often unwieldy acronyms (some >11 letters) impose a substantial cognitive load.  
- The constraint is presented confusingly—authors should clarify it is imposed a priori and thus indirectly constrains the posterior (not framed as a direct prior constraint).  
- Recommend using full terms on first mention, adding an acronym table, and removing unnecessary acronyms.  
- AFHMM and the signal-aggregate constraint are reasonable and potentially valuable contributions but are obscured by presentation issues; fixing acronym overload and clarifying prior vs. posterior placement is crucial.
