- Proposes signal aggregate constraints (SACs) to incorporate domain knowledge into blind source separation.
- Integrates SACs into an additive factorial HMM (AFHMM) for the energy disaggregation problem.
- Employs a convex quadratic program for efficient approximate inference.
- Results on a real-world dataset significantly improve the original AFHMM and outperform a recent state-of-the-art.
- Provides a thorough derivation and clear explanation of the method and its advantages.
- Paper is generally well-written but dense with acronyms, some of which are long and confusing (e.g., AFHMM+SAC).
- The motivation behind the proposed method needs clearer explanation.
- Authors should explicitly state that SACs serve as prior constraints on the posterior density.
- Minor presentation and clarity improvements would make the paper more accessible to non-experts.
- Overall contribution is significant and results are promising for blind source separation.
- Recommendation: Accept with minor revisions; rating 8/10.
