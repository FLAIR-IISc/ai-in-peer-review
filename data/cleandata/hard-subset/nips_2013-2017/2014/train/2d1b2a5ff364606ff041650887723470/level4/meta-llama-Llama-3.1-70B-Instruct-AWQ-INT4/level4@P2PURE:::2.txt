The authors propose an additive factorial hidden Markov model to capture the complexities of electricity usage, incorporating the signal aggregate constraint. The overall quality of the paper is good, but its readability is compromised due to an excessive use of acronyms, some of which are overly long (up to 11 letters). 

Upon closer examination, it appears that the authors are seeking to impose a constraint on the posterior density, which they achieve by placing a constraint on the prior density instead. For clarity, the authors should explicitly state that they have an a priori constraint, which consequently influences the posterior density.
