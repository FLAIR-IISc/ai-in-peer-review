- Clear writing and solid mathematical grounding.
- Well-motivated problem.
- Empirical results show substantial improvements in NDE and SAE.
- Comparison fairness concern: reported gains compare against baselines that use less information; need baselines that can also incorporate SAC to isolate SAC's benefit.
- Figure 1 concern: AFAMAP outperforms SAC-AFHMM at large distances, suggesting noisy/poor constraints can degrade performance; require discussion of failure modes and sensitivity.
- Constraint expressiveness: request support for richer constraints (mutual exclusivity, global cardinality, temporal/duration) and discussion of extensions (linear inequalities, integer vars, soft penalties) and computational trade-offs.
- Clarify how SAC is obtained in practice, including data sources and noise models, and robustness to misspecified/inconsistent aggregates.
- Provide ablation studies to separate effects of convex relaxation from inclusion of SAC.
- Provide sensitivity analyses showing performance as constraints are perturbed or partially incorrect.
- Overall: well written and promising, but needs fairer comparisons, robustness analysis, and extension/complexity discussion for broader constraint classes.
