

An additive factorial hidden Markov model is proposed for modeling electricity usage, incorporating a signal aggregate constraint. The technical quality of the paper is strong, though its readability is significantly impaired. The manuscript employs an excessive number of acronyms, some of which are excessively long (e.g., 11 letters). Critically, the authors appear to impose a constraint on the prior density to indirectly constrain the posterior density; they should explicitly state that this is an a priori constraint, thereby clarifying that the posterior inherits this constraint.
