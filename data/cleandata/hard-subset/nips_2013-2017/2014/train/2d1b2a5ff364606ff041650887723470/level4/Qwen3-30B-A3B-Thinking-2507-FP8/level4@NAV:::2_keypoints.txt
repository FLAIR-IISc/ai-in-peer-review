- Proposes an additive factorial hidden Markov model for electricity usage with a signal-aggregate constraint.  
- Technical quality of the work is sound.  
- Readability is significantly reduced by excessive and sometimes unnecessarily long acronyms (e.g., 11-letter acronyms).  
- Authors impose a constraint on the prior density to indirectly constrain the posterior rather than constraining the posterior directly.  
- The paper should explicitly state the constraint is a priori, clarifying why a posterior constraint is then required.
