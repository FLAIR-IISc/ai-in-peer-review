- Proposes an additive factorial hidden Markov model for electricity-usage prediction with a signal-aggregate constraint.  
- Technical approach is sound and overall paper quality is good.  
- Readability is harmed by excessive and unwieldy acronyms (some up to 11 characters).  
- Critical methodological issue: the authors impose a constraint on the prior density but present it as a posterior constraint.  
- Must explicitly state the constraint is a priori, with the constrained posterior as a consequence, not the starting point.  
- Revision should clarify this distinction for methodological correctness and clarity.
