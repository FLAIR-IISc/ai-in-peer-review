An additive factorial hidden Markov model has been developed to model electricity usage. This model incorporates the so-called signal aggregate constraint.

The paper's quality is good, but it is really difficult to read. There are many abbreviations here, and some of them are indeed log (even 11 characters!). It seems that the authors wanted to impose a constraint on the posterior density, and therefore they imposed a constraint on the prior density. They should have stated that they have a prior constraint, and therefore a posterior constraint.
