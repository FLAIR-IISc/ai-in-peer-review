This paper develops an additive factorial hidden Markov model for modeling electricity usage, incorporating what the authors term the "signal aggregate constraint."

While the technical quality of the paper is good, it is difficult to read. The presentation is hindered by an overabundance of acronyms, some of which are excessively long. Furthermore, the motivation for the constraint is not clearly articulated. It appears the authors' goal is to constrain the posterior density, which they achieve by placing a constraint on the prior. The authors should state explicitly that they are introducing an *a priori* constraint and that the posterior is consequently constrained.
