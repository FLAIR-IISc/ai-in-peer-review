Paper proposes an additive factorial HMM for electricity consumption with a "signal aggregate" constraint.  
Technical quality of the work appears solid.  
Manuscript is difficult to read due to excessive use of acronyms.  
Many acronyms are uncommon or very long—reduce them, prefer descriptive terms, and define any abbreviations clearly at first use.  
Conceptual inconsistency: the paper appears to aim for a posterior constraint but implements the constraint through the prior.  
Authors should explicitly state they impose an a priori constraint so the posterior is constrained as a consequence, which would improve methodological clarity.
