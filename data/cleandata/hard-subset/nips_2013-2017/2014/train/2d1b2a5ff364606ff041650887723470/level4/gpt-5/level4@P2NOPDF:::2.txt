This paper develops an additive factorial hidden Markov model for electricity consumption, incorporating a “signal aggregate” constraint. The technical quality appears solid, but the manuscript is difficult to read. It relies on too many acronyms—many of them uncommon and some extremely long—which makes the exposition hard to follow. Please reduce the number of acronyms, prefer descriptive terms where possible, and define any necessary abbreviations clearly at first use.

Conceptually, the paper seems to aim for a constraint on the posterior distribution, yet the constraint is implemented via the prior. If this is the intended modeling approach, the authors should state explicitly that they impose an a priori constraint, and therefore the posterior is inherently constrained as a consequence. Making this point clear would improve the coherence of the methodological narrative.
