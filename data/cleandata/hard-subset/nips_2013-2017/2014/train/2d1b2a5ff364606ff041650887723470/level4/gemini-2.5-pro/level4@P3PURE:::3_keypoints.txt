- Paper is well-written and grounded on a solid mathematical foundation.
- Motivation for the problem is clear and compelling.
- Evaluation shows significant improvements in NDE and SAE metrics.
- Comparison may be unfair: SACAFHMM uses aggregate information that AFHMM and AFAMAP do not; compare to methods that can incorporate aggregate constraints.
- Figure 1 is unclear and suggests AFAMAP may outperform SACAFHMM at large distances, raising concern that noisy/inaccurate constraints can degrade performance contrary to claims.
- Paper should discuss extending the framework to other constraint types (mutual exclusivity, cardinality limits, temporal constraints) and the feasibility of such extensions.
