- Proposes an additive factorial hidden Markov model (AFHMM) for electricity usage with an integrated signal aggregate constraint.  
- Paper quality is satisfactory but readability suffers from excessive and sometimes overly long acronyms.  
- Authors appear to intend imposing a constraint on the posterior but instead apply the constraint to the prior.  
- Recommend explicitly stating that an a priori constraint is introduced, which then constrains the posterior density.
