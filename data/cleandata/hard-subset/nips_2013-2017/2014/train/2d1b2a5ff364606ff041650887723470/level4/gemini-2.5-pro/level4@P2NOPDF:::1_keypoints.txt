- Introduces AFHMM-SAC: integrates Signal Aggregate Constraints into AFHMM for blind source separation, demonstrated on energy disaggregation.
- Leverages domain knowledge of expected aggregate source values to improve separation quality.
- Converts the log-posterior objective into a convex optimization problem to enable efficient, reliable inference.
- Validated on synthetic and real-world domestic energy datasets.
- AFHMM-SAC outperforms standard AFHMM and AFAMAP; improvements are statistically significant and optimization speed is comparable to AFAMAP.
