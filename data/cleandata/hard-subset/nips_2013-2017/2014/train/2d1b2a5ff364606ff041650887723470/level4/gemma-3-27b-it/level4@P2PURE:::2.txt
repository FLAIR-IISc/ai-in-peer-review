An additive factorial hidden Markov model is developed to model electricity usage, incorporating a signal aggregate constraint. 

The paper presents a strong technical contribution, but its readability is significantly hampered. The excessive use of acronyms—some quite lengthy (up to 11 letters)—impedes comprehension. Furthermore, the authors appear to impose a constraint on the prior density in an attempt to influence the posterior; they should explicitly state this as an *a priori* constraint and clarify its effect on the posterior distribution.
