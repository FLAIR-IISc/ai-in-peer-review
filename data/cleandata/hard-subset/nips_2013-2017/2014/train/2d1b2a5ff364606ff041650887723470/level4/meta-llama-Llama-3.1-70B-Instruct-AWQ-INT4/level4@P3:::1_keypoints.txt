- Proposes AFHMM+SAC: incorporating signal aggregate constraints into additive factorial HMMs for blind source separation.  
- Evaluated on a toy dataset and real domestic energy readings, showing practical applicability.  
- AFHMM+SAC outperforms regular AFHMM and AFAMAP in experiments.  
- Improvements are statistically significant (t-tests).  
- Optimization speed of AFHMM+SAC is comparable to AFAMAP.  
- Theoretical presentation is clear, but convexity of the final procedure (eq. 6) lacks a formal proof.  
- Paper would benefit from theoretical scaling arguments for data length and number of hidden states.  
- Minor notation inconsistency on page 4 (line 202) needs correction.  
- Clarify choice of low noise variance in the toy example and typical SNRs in real data.  
- Comment on SNR between mains readings and aggregate appliance output in the energy application.  
- Well-written, well-structured, and effectively motivated with clear explanations.  
- Novel and original approach with significant results; reviewer recommends acceptance with minor revisions.
