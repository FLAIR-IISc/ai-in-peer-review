An additive factorial hidden Markov model is developed to model electricity usage, and the model incorporates the so-called signal aggregate constraint. The quality of the paper is good, but it is very hard to read: there are too many acronyms, and some are unusually long (up to 11 letters).

It appears the authors intend to impose a constraint on the posterior density but instead place the constraint on the prior density. They should explicitly state that they are introducing an a priori constraint and, consequently, that the posterior is constrained.
