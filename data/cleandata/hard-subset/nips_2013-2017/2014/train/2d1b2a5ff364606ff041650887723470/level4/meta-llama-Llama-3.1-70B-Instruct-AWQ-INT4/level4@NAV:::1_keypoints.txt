- Presents a novel blind source separation method: AFHMM with signal aggregate constraints (SACs).  
- Motivated by leveraging domain knowledge to improve time-series component separation.  
- Demonstrates concept using energy disaggregation (appliance-level usage from mains).  
- Converts MAP objective into a convex optimization problem for inference.  
- Validated on a toy dataset and real domestic energy readings.  
- Technically sound with a strong simulation section.  
- Outperforms AFMM and AFAMAP, improvements significant by t-tests.  
- Optimization runtime comparable to AFAMAP.  
- Clarification needed on choice of low noise variance in the toy example and typical SNR in real data.  
- Needs a formal proof that the final procedure (equation (6)) is convex.  
- Would benefit from theoretical scaling analysis w.r.t. data length and number of hidden states, and comparison with AFAMAP.  
- Paper is well-written, well-structured, and literature is thoroughly covered.  
- Possible notation/subscript error on page 4 (line 202) before equation (6).  
- Work appears novel and original; reviewer not expert enough to judge broader significance.  
- Authors addressed reviewer questions satisfactorily; recommended to incorporate clarifications in next version.
