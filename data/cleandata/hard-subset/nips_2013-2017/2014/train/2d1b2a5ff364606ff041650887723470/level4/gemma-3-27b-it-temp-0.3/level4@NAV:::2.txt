This paper introduces an additive factorial hidden Markov model for electricity usage modeling, incorporating a signal aggregate constraint. The technical quality of the work is strong; however, the manuscript presents significant readability challenges. Excessive use of acronyms, including some that are quite lengthy (up to 11 letters), hinders comprehension. Furthermore, the approach to constraining the solution appears conceptually reversed – the authors effectively impose a constraint on the prior distribution to achieve a desired effect on the posterior. Clarification is needed to explicitly state that the constraint is applied *a priori*, and consequently influences the posterior density.
