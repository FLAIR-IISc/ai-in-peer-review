- Introduces an additive factorial hidden Markov model for electricity usage modeling with a signal aggregate constraint.
- Technical quality of the work is strong.
- Presentation significantly hinders readability.
- Manuscript relies heavily on acronyms, including some very long ones (up to 11 letters).
- Authors appear to impose a constraint on the prior density to influence the posterior; this should be explicitly stated as an a priori constraint producing a constrained posterior.
