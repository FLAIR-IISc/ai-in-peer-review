- Introduces an additive factorial HMM for electricity usage modeling with a "signal aggregate constraint."
- Technical contribution is sound and potentially valuable for energy analytics.
- Major readability issues from excessive acronyms, including overly long and non-intuitive ones, which impede comprehension.
- Conceptual error: paper describes the constraint as imposed on the posterior but it is actually derived from an a priori constraint on the prior.
- Authors do not explicitly state that the constraint is a priori on the prior, creating critical theoretical ambiguity.
- Methodological details are buried in jargon and unclear notation, making it hard to assess novelty or practical utility.
- The specific form of the signal aggregate constraint is not well motivated or connected to observable electricity usage or physical limits.
- Recommended fixes: drastically reduce acronym use, explicitly reframe the constraint as a prior condition, and provide concrete justification linking the constraint to domain knowledge.
- As currently presented the paper’s promise is obscured; addressing exposition and the logical gap is essential for impact.
