- Introduces an additive factorial HMM (AFHMM) for electricity usage modeling with a novel "signal aggregate constraint."
- Core idea and technical approach are promising and generally sound.
- Major readability issues due to excessive, complex notation and overly long/obscure acronyms.
- Authors should expand and simplify acronyms and reduce notational clutter for clarity.
- The signal aggregate constraint is interesting but its application is ambiguously described.
- The paper appears to impose the constraint on the prior, not directly on the posterior; this distinction must be clarified.
- The motivation for using a factorial HMM structure is insufficiently explained (assumptions and computational benefits unclear).
- Limited comparison to existing load forecasting methods and related factor/HMM approaches weakens contextualization.
- Experimental results show promise on real data but lack depth in analysis and interpretation.
- Recommend adding statistical significance testing and visualizations of learned latent factors to improve insight.
- Overall recommendation: reject in current form; substantial revision for clarity, constraint explanation, comparisons, and analyses required.
