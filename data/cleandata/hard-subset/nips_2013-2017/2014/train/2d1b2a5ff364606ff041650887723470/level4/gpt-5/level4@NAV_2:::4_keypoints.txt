- Extends additive factorial HMM by adding constraints that force each chain’s total output to match a prespecified aggregate, with a convex relaxation for MAP inference evaluated on real energy-disaggregation datasets (100 low-frequency homes; ~10 high-rate homes with incomplete instrumentation).

- Core ideas are simple and optimization setup direct, but the contribution is persuasive and convincingly demonstrated on sizable, realistic data.

- Question about integrality: does the SAC-augmented AFHMM improve integrality like AFAMAP’s “at-most-one-chain-changes” constraint, or do performance gains occur even when solutions remain fractional? A discussion is needed.

- Suggestion to explore combining approaches: AFAMAP and SAC-augmented AFHMM appear orthogonal, so incorporating SAC terms into AFAMAP could yield improvements and should be investigated for the final version.

- Clarify terminology and distinctions between models and inference: AFAMAP is an approximate MAP algorithm for AFHMM, but “AFHMM” in experiments seems to refer to alternating chainwise Viterbi—this conflation should be corrected and clarified.

- Overall assessment: modest methodological extension with a solid optimization and compelling real-world results; the work is solid.
