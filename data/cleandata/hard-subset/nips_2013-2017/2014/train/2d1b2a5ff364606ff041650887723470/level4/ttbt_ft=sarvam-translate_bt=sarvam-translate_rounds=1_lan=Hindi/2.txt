An additive factorial hidden Markov model has been developed to model electricity usage. This model includes a so-called signal aggregate constraint.

The paper's quality is good, but it is actually difficult to read. It contains many abbreviations, and some of them are actually logs (even 11 characters!). It seems that the author is looking for an obstacle on the posterior density and therefore they put an obstacle on the prior density. They should have stated that they have a prior obstacle and therefore the posterior is constrained.
