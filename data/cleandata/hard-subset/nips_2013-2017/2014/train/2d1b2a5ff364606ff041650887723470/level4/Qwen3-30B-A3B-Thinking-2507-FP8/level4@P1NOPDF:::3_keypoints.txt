1. Well-written paper with strong mathematical foundation.  
2. Clear and compelling problem motivation.  
3. Evaluation shows significant improvements in NDE and SAE.  
4. Comparison may be unfair because baselines use less data than SAC-AFHMM.  
5. Recommend comparing to other methods that can incorporate SAC but are more computationally efficient.  
6. Figure 1 shows AFAMAP outperforming SAC-AFHMM for distances >10^4, contradicting the claim that SAC always helps.  
7. Need discussion on whether poorly chosen constraints can degrade performance.  
8. Paper focuses only on signal-aggregation constraints; unclear if framework generalizes to physical, count, or temporal constraints.  
9. Future-work mention of other constraints lacks concrete technical pathways or extensions.
