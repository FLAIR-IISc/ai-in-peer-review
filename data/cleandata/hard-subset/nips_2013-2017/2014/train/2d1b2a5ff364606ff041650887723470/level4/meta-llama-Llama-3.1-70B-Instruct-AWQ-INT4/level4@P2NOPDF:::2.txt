This paper proposes an additive factorial hidden Markov model to capture the patterns of electricity usage. A key feature of the model is the incorporation of the signal aggregate constraint.

The overall quality of the paper is good, but its readability is hindered by an excessive use of acronyms, some of which are lengthy (up to 11 letters). This detracts from the clarity of the presentation.

Upon closer examination, it appears that the authors are seeking to impose a constraint on the posterior density. However, they achieve this by placing a constraint on the prior density instead. To improve the transparency of their approach, the authors should explicitly state that they have an a priori constraint, which subsequently influences the posterior density. This clarification would enhance the understanding of their methodology.
