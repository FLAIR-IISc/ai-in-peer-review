- Paper proposes an additive factorial HMM for electricity usage with a “signal aggregate constraint”; technical quality good and problem important but paper is hard to read.
- Excessive use of acronyms (some very long); reduce their number, define at first use, and consider replacing long acronyms with descriptive terms.
- Treatment of the constraint is confusing: it reads like a posterior constraint but is implemented by constraining the prior.
- If the constraint is intended a priori, state this explicitly and explain that it consequently constrains the posterior.
- Clarify the target and role of the constraint to improve readability and interpretability.
