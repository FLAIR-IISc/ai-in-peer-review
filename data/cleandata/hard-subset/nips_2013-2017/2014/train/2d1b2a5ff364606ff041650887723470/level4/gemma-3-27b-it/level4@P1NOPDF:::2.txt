An additive factorial hidden Markov model is developed to model electricity usage, incorporating a signal aggregate constraint.

The paper presents a solid contribution, but is unfortunately difficult to read. The authors introduce numerous acronyms, some of which are excessively long – even eleven letters! A key conceptual issue arises in the approach to constraining the model. It appears the authors seek a constraint on the posterior density, yet implement this by imposing a constraint on the prior density. They should explicitly state that they are applying an *a priori* constraint, and therefore influencing the posterior distribution.
