- Introduces AFHMM with signal aggregate constraints (SAC) and a convex quadratic relaxation, showing promising results on synthetic and real datasets.  
- Well-written manuscript with a strong mathematical foundation.  
- Clear and effective motivation for the problem.  
- Evaluation reports significant performance gains in key metrics (NDE and SAE).  
- Comparisons are unfair: baselines (AFHMM without SAC and AFAMAP) use less data than SACAFHMM.  
- Questions whether alternative methods could incorporate SAC with lower computational cost.  
- Figure 1 is ambiguous: AFAMAP outperforms SACAFHMM for distances >10^4, contradicting claims of consistent superiority and suggesting constraints may harm performance.  
- Paper lacks exploration of richer constraint types (e.g., mutually exclusive devices, cardinality limits, duration limits); future work should generalize constraints.
