- Introduces AFHMM with Signal Aggregate Constraints (SACs) to tackle blind source separation in energy disaggregation.
- SACs constrain the posterior via a prior on aggregate source values to encourage totals close to expected values.
- Paper is generally well-structured and readable, though heavy acronym use harms readability.
- Suggestion: define acronyms briefly when first introduced.
- Clear derivation of AFHMM and incorporation of SACs, including a convex quadratic program for approximate inference.
- Real-world experiments show large gains: 45% reduction in normalized disaggregation error vs original AFHMM.
- Empirical improvement of ~29% compared to a recent state-of-the-art method.
- Results are well-presented and the paper is well-written overall.
- Minor issues: insufficient detail on experimental setup and evaluation metrics.
- Minor issue: could provide a more detailed explanation of AFHMM and how SACs are incorporated.
- Paper is original and makes a significant contribution to energy disaggregation.
- Recommendation: accept with minor revisions to address clarity and experimental details.
