- Introduces AFHMM with signal aggregate constraints (SAC) and a relaxed convex quadratic formulation, showing promising results on synthetic and real datasets.  
- Paper is well-structured and grounded in solid mathematical principles.  
- Problem motivation is clear and well-articulated.  
- Evaluation reports significant improvement on metrics such as NDE and SAE.  
- Comparisons to AFHMM without SAC and AFAMAP may be biased because those methods use less data; recommend testing alternative SAC methods that trade off computational efficiency.  
- Figure 1 suggests AFAMAP may outperform SACAFHMM at distances >10^4, indicating poorly chosen constraints can hurt performance and requiring clarification.  
- Recommend exploring incorporation of more complex constraints (e.g., mutual exclusivity, time limits) and extending the algorithm to handle various constraint types.
