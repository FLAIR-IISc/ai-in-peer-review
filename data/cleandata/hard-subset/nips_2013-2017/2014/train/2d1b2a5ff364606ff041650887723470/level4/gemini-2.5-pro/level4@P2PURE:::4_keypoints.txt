- Contribution: Extends AFHMM with sum-to-aggregate constraints and a convex MAP-relaxation, evaluated on two real-world energy disaggregation datasets (100 low-frequency UK homes; 10 high-frequency homes without appliance monitoring).

- Strength: Methods and optimization are straightforward but compelling, with a strong applied focus and successful scaling to large, real-world data.

- Clarity issue: The paper conflates models and inference algorithms (e.g., comparing “AFHMM” vs “AFAMAP”); AFAMAP is an inference algorithm for AFHMM and this should be stated explicitly.

- Orthogonality concern: AFAMAP and SACAFHMM ideas appear orthogonal — sum-to-aggregate constraints could likely be integrated into AFAMAP; the authors should explore this combination.

- Relaxation comparison needed: The paper should analyze relaxation properties more deeply (e.g., AFAMAP’s one-chain-change constraint promotes integrality); clarify whether SACAFHMM yields similar integrality or lower error despite non-integral solutions and discuss trade-offs.
