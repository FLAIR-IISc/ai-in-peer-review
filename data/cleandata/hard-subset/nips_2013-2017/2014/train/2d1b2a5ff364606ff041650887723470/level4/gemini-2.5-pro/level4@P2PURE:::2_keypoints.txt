- Proposes an additive factorial hidden Markov model for electricity usage with a "signal aggregate constraint."
- Technical quality of the work is good.
- Presentation is hard to read because of an overabundance of acronyms, some excessively long.
- Motivation for the signal aggregate constraint is not clearly articulated.
- It appears the authors constrain the posterior indirectly by placing a constraint on the prior.
- Authors should explicitly state the constraint is a priori and that the posterior is consequently constrained.
