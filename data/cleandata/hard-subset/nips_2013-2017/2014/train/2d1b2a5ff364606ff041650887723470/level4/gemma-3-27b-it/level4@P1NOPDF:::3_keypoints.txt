- Well-written paper with a strong mathematical foundation.  
- Clear and compelling motivation for the problem.  
- Evaluation shows significant improvements in NDE and SAE.  

- Baseline comparisons may be unfair because competing methods use less data than SACAFHMM.  
- Need to consider other existing methods that incorporate SAC (even if computationally heavier) for stronger evaluation.  
- Figure 1 is hard to interpret and may show AFAMAP outperforming SACAFHMM for large distances, suggesting poorly specified constraints can harm performance; discussion should be more nuanced.  
- Expected to see and evaluate more complex constraint types (mutual exclusivity, cardinality limits, time-based limits); feasibility of extending the algorithm to such constraints should be explored.
