- Introduces signal aggregate constraints (SACs) in AFHMMs to force each unknown source’s total signal toward a specified value using domain knowledge.
- Claim 1: Adding SACs to AFHMM improves energy disaggregation performance.
- Claim 2: AFHMM+SAC significantly outperforms a recent state-of-the-art method (AFAMAP).
