- Proposes an additive factorial hidden Markov model for electricity usage that incorporates a signal aggregate constraint.  
- Overall paper quality is good but the manuscript is very hard to read.  
- Excessive use of acronyms, some unusually long (≈11 letters), which harms readability.  
- Authors appear to place the constraint on the prior rather than directly on the posterior and should explicitly state it is an a priori constraint, thereby constraining the posterior.
