- Proposes AFHMM with Signal Aggregate Constraints (SACs) for blind source separation, illustrated on energy disaggregation.  
- Transforms the posterior log objective into a convex optimization problem.  
- Validated on a toy dataset and real domestic energy data.  
- Strong simulation results: outperforms AFMM and AFAMAP, with improvements significant by t-tests.  
- Optimization speed is comparable to AFAMAP.  
- Question about toy experiment: very low noise variance—need clarification if this is realistic or chosen to make separation easy; also request SNR discussion for mains vs aggregate appliance signals.  
- Theoretical presentation is good but needs a formal proof that the procedure in equation (6) is convex.  
- Recommend providing theoretical scaling/complexity analysis (data length, number of hidden states) and tractability comparison to AFAMAP for larger datasets.  
- Minor notation issue: possible extra commas in subscripts before p on page 4 (line 202).  
- Novel and original work, though reviewer not expert enough to assess full significance to HMM community and energy application.  
- Authors addressed reviewer queries satisfactorily in feedback; suggest incorporating those clarifications into the paper.
