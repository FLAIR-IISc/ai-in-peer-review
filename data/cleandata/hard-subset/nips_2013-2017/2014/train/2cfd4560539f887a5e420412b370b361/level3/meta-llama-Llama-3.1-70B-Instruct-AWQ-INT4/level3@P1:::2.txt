This paper proposes a novel deep recursive neural network (deep RNN) architecture, which is constructed by stacking multiple recursive layers on top of each other. The authors apply this architecture to the task of fine-grained sentiment classification using binary parse trees as the structure. The paper is well-written, and the results are impressive, achieving state-of-the-art performance on a challenging sentiment classification dataset.

The proposed changes to the traditional recursive neural network architecture are clever and well-motivated. The authors provide a clear explanation of the benefits of untying leaf-node and nonterminal-node parameters, using rectified linear units (ReLUs) in the recursive setting, and leveraging large unsupervised word vectors while keeping smaller hidden nonterminal representations. The deep architecture introduces inter-layer and inter-node connections rather than simply stacking layers, which allows for a more nuanced representation of the input data.

The experimental results are thorough and well-presented. The authors compare their deep RNN model to several baselines, including shallow recursive nets, multiplicative RNN variants, and paragraph vectors. The results show that the deep RNN model outperforms all the baselines, achieving a new state-of-the-art performance on the task.

However, there are a few minor issues with the paper. The authors do not provide an ablation study or comparison to evaluate the effectiveness of the parent inputs coming only from the last hidden layer (i.e., dropping the V matrix connection). Additionally, Table 1(a) improperly reports results on the final test set instead of the development set, implying tuning on the test set. Finally, the paper contains a few typos and a repeated/malformed sentence that needs fixing.

Overall, the paper is well-written, and the results are impressive. The proposed deep RNN architecture is a significant contribution to the field of natural language processing, and the authors provide a thorough evaluation of its effectiveness.

Quality: The paper is technically sound, and the claims are well-supported by theoretical analysis and experimental results. The paper is a complete piece of work, and the authors are careful and honest about evaluating both the strengths and weaknesses of the work.
