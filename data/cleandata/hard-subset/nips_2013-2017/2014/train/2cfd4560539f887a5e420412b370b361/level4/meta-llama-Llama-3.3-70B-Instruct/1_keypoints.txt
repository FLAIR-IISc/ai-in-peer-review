- Introduces a novel "deep recursive neural network" architecture by stacking multiple recursive layers for fine-grained sentiment classification.  
- The paper is well-structured, clear, and easy to follow.  
- Technical treatment is robust and well-founded.  
- The core idea of stacking recursive networks is noteworthy.  
- Provides sufficient experimental data to evaluate the approach.  
- Concept is straightforward and logical but lacks deeper intuition for why increased depth improves performance.  
- Needs clearer rationale for selecting the specific network architecture used.  
- Overall, the paper offers a meaningful contribution with clear context and reasonable experimental support.
