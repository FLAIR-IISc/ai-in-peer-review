- Introduces a deep recursive neural network (DRNN) by stacking recursive layers and evaluates it on fine-grained sentiment classification.  
- The paper is well-written and clearly structured.  
- The technical approach is sound; stacking recursive layers is well-motivated and effectively implemented.  
- Experimental evaluation is comprehensive, using appropriate baselines and metrics.  
- The core idea is straightforward but promising for modeling compositionality in language.  
- The authors should better justify why deeper architectures specifically benefit fine-grained sentiment tasks.  
- The paper needs clearer rationale for architectural design choices (layer structure, activation functions) relative to alternatives.  
- Overall, a novel and well-supported contribution to neural architectures for linguistic compositionality.
