- Proposes a novel deep recursive neural network by stacking multiple recursive layers, evaluated on fine-grained sentiment classification.  
- Paper is well-written and enjoyable to read.  
- Technically sound; the central idea of stacking recursive networks is compelling.  
- Provides a reasonable set of experiments to evaluate different aspects of the method.  
- Concept is straightforward and logical.  
- Lacks discussion on why a deeper network is intuitively advantageous for the target task.  
- Lacks justification for the choice of the specific network architecture.  
- Overall presents an intriguing idea with clear background and sufficient experimental evidence.
