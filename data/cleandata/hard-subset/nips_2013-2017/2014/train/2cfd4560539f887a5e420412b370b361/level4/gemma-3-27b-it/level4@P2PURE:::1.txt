Paper 1180: Deep Recursive Neural Networks for Compositionality in Language

This paper introduces a deep recursive neural network (deep RNN) architecture, constructed by stacking multiple recursive layers, and evaluates it on the task of fine-grained sentiment classification.

Clarity
- The paper is generally well-written and easy to follow.

Quality
- The paper appears technically sound.
- The core idea – a deep RNN constructed by stacking layers of individual recursive networks – is compelling. 
- The authors provide a sufficient amount of experimental results to evaluate various aspects of their method.

Originality
- The proposed idea is straightforward and well-reasoned.
- The paper could benefit from further discussion regarding:
 + the intuition behind why a deeper network is particularly well-suited for this task; and
 + the rationale for choosing this specific network architecture.

Significance
- The paper presents an interesting idea, a clear background description, and reasonable experimental validation.
