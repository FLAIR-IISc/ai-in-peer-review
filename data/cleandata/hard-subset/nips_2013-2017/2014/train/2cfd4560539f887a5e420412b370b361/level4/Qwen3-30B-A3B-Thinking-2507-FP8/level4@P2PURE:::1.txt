

Paper 1180: Deep Recursive Neural Networks for Compositionality in Language  
This paper introduces a deep recursive neural network (DRNN) architecture, constructed by stacking multiple recursive layers, and evaluates it on fine-grained sentiment classification.  

Clarity  
- The paper is well-written and clearly presented.  

Quality  
- The technical approach is sound.  
- The core idea—constructing a deep recursive neural network through stacking multiple recursive layers—is compelling.  
- The experimental evaluation provides sufficient results to assess key aspects of the method.  

Originality  
- The proposed approach is straightforward and well-motivated.  
- The authors should expand discussion on:  
  + the intuitive rationale for deeper networks being beneficial for this task,  
  + the justification for the chosen network architecture.  

Significance  
- The paper presents an interesting idea, a clear background, and reasonable experimental support.
