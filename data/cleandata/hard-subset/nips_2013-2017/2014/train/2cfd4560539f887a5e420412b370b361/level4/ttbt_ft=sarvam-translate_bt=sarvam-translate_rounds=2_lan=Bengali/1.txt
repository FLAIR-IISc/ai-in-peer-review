For Paper No. 1180: Deep Recurrent Neural Networks for Language Processing

This paper presents a new architecture - Deep Recurrent Neural Networks (DRNNs) - that is built by stacking multiple recurrent layers. The authors have evaluated the proposed model on fine-grained sentiment classification tasks.

- In general, this paper is well-written and enjoyable to read.

- The core concept is beautiful, such as Deep Recurrent Neural Networks, which are built by stacking multiple layers of separate recurrent networks.
- The authors have provided a reasonable amount of experimental results to evaluate various aspects of the method.
