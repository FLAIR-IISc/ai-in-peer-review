- Paper is generally well-written, clear, and easy to follow.  
- Proposed deep recursive neural network (stacking recursive layers) is technically sound and conceptually strong.  
- Experiments are reasonable and results convincingly evaluate the architecture on fine-grained sentiment classification.  
- Contribution is simple yet effective: depth extension of recursive networks offers a natural way to improve compositional representations.  
- Paper lacks deeper intuition on why increased depth helps capture fine-grained sentiment.  
- Needs more justification for the specific network architecture and exploration/comparison with alternative designs.  
- Work is well-motivated and significant, demonstrating potential of deep recursive networks for compositional reasoning and insights on depth effects in NLP.
