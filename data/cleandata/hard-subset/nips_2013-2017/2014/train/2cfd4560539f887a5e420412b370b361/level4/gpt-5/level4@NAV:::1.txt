Review - Paper 1180: Deep Recursive Neural Networks for Compositionality in Language
This work presents a novel architecture, a deep recursive neural network (deep RNN), created by stacking multiple recursive layers. The authors evaluate the model on fine-grained sentiment classification.

Clarity
- Overall, the paper is clearly written and easy to read.

Quality
- The technical content appears sound.
- The core idea is appealing: a deep recursive neural network formed by stacking multiple individual recursive nets.
- The authors provide a reasonable set of experiments to assess various aspects of the approach.

Originality
- The proposed concept is simple and sensible.
- It would be beneficial to add more discussion on
 + why, intuitively, increased depth helps for the target task
 + why the specific network architecture was chosen

Significance
- The paper presents an interesting idea, clear background, and reasonable experimental support.
