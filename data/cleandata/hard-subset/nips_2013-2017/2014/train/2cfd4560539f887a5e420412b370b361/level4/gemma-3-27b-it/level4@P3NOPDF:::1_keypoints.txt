- Introduces Deep Recursive Neural Network (Deep RNN) by stacking multiple recursive layers for compositional language modeling.  
- Evaluated on fine-grained sentiment classification, showing empirical improvements.  
- Paper is well-written, clear, and uses consistent notation.  
- Technical approach is sound and a logical extension of recursive neural networks.  
- Experiments are thorough with valuable ablation studies and appropriate baselines.  
- Contribution is a simple but effective extension: applying depth to recursive models.  
- Lacks deeper theoretical motivation for why increased depth aids compositional semantics.  
- Needs more justification for specific architectural choices and relation to models like tree-LSTMs.  
- Suggests adding error analysis to reveal failure modes and guide future work.  
- Recommend discussing computational complexity of deeper models versus shallow ones.  
- Suggest addressing overfitting risks and describing regularization strategies used.  
- Overall assessment: solid, promising contribution with strong results; recommend acceptance.
