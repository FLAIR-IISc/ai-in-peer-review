Review for Paper 1180: Deep Recursive Neural Networks for Compositionality in Language

This work presents a novel deep recursive neural network (deep RNN) architecture, which is formed by stacking several recursive layers on top of one another. The efficacy of this proposed model is demonstrated through its application to fine-grained sentiment classification.

**Clarity**
- The manuscript is exceptionally well-written, clear, and straightforward to follow.

**Quality**
- From a technical standpoint, the work appears robust and correct.
- The central contribution—the deep recursive neural network built from stacked recursive units—is an elegant and compelling concept.
- The experimental evaluation is thorough, providing sufficient results to validate the different components of the proposed method.

**Originality**
- The core concept is both intuitive and novel in its application.
- The paper could be strengthened by a more detailed discussion on a few points:
  + The intuition behind why greater depth is beneficial for this specific task.
  + The rationale for selecting this particular network configuration over potential alternatives.

**Significance**
- Overall, this paper presents a valuable contribution, featuring an interesting core idea, a well-articulated background, and solid experimental validation.
