

Review for Paper 1180: Deep Recursive Neural Networks for Compositionality in Language  
This paper proposes a novel architecture—deep recursive neural network (deep RNN)—achieved by stacking multiple recursive layers, and evaluates it on fine-grained sentiment classification.  

Clarity  
- The paper is clearly written and easy to follow.  

Quality  
- The work is methodologically sound.  
- The core concept—constructing a deep RNN through layered recursive networks—is well-motivated and logically presented.  
- The experimental evaluation provides sufficient results to assess the method’s performance across relevant dimensions.  

Originality  
- The proposed approach is straightforward and logically consistent.  
- The authors should strengthen the discussion by addressing:  
  + The intuitive rationale for deeper architectures improving compositionality in language tasks.  
  + Justification for the specific architectural choices made.  

Significance  
- The paper delivers a compelling idea, a well-structured background, and robust experimental validation.
