- Introduces a novel deep recursive neural architecture achieving state-of-the-art results on a challenging sentiment classification dataset.  
- Results are impressive and the paper’s exposition is exceptionally clear.  
- Proposes several well-motivated, experimentally validated modifications to prior RNNs: untying leaf and non-terminal nodes.  
- Employs rectified linear units (ReLU) within the recursive framework.  
- Uses large unsupervised word vectors combined with smaller hidden non-terminal node dimensions.  
- Implements a deep architecture with inter-layer and tree-node connections rather than simple layer replacement.  
- Missing ablation/comparison: omitting the matrix V child-to-parent connection would have been an informative comparison.  
- Reporting concern: Table 1(a) results appear tuned on the test set and should be reported on the development set.  
- Minor typo: “comprise a class of architecture” should be “comprises.”
