- Introduces a novel deep recursive neural network that achieves state-of-the-art performance on a challenging sentiment dataset.  
- Results are impressive and the paper’s exposition is notably clear.  
- Proposes untied weights for leaf and nonterminal nodes.  
- Uses rectified linear units (ReLUs) within the recursive framework.  
- Leverages large unsupervised word vectors with smaller hidden representations for nonterminal nodes.  
- Presents a genuinely deep architecture with connections between layers and tree nodes; suggests an important comparison removing the V-mediated connection (ablation).  
- Concern: Table 1(a) appears to report results on the final test set rather than the development set, implying possible overfitting/tuning on the test set.  
- Minor grammar issue: “comprise a class of architecture” should be corrected (e.g., “comprise a class of architectures”).
