- Introduces a deep recursive neural network formed by stacking multiple recursive layers, evaluated on fine-grained sentiment classification.
- Paper is clearly written and pleasant to read.
- Work appears technically sound.
- Main idea—stacking recursive nets to form a deeper model—is appealing.
- Provides a reasonable amount of experimental evidence evaluating various aspects of the method.
- Idea is simple and sensible but would benefit from more discussion of why depth helps and why this specific architecture was chosen over alternatives.
- Significance: interesting contribution with clear background and reasonable experimental support.
