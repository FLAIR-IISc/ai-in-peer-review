- Results are remarkable and the paper’s presentation is exceptionally clear.
- Innovative and effective modification: decoupling leaf nodes from nonterminal nodes.
- Innovative and effective modification: employing rectified linear units (ReLUs) in the recursive framework.
- Innovative and effective modification: using large unsupervised word vectors while keeping hidden nonterminal nodes smaller.
