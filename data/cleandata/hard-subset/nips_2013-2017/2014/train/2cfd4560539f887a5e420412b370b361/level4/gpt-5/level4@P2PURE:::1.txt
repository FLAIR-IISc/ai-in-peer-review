Review for paper 1180: Deep Recursive Neural Networks for Compositionality in Language

This paper introduces a new architecture—a deep recursive neural network (deep RNN)—constructed by stacking multiple recursive layers. The authors evaluate the model on fine-grained sentiment classification.

Clarity
- Overall, the paper is well written and pleasant to read.

Quality
- The paper appears technically sound.
- The central idea is appealing: a deep recursive neural network formed by stacking multiple individual recursive nets.
- The authors provide a reasonable amount of experimental results to assess various aspects of the method.

Originality
- The proposed idea is simple and reasonable.
- It would benefit from additional discussion on:
  + the intuitive reasons why a deeper network is advantageous for the target task;
  + the rationale for the specific architectural choices.

Significance
- The paper presents an interesting idea, a clear background description, and reasonable experimental support.
