- Novel deep recursive neural architecture achieving state-of-the-art results on a challenging sentiment benchmark.  
- Strong empirical performance; manuscript is exceptionally well-written and easy to follow.  
- Architectural modifications to RNN frameworks are insightful, well-justified, and empirically validated.  
- Key contribution: separate parameterizations for leaf and non-terminal nodes.  
- Key contribution: application of Rectified Linear Units (ReLUs) within the recursive model.  
- Key contribution: use of large, pre-trained word embeddings while keeping smaller hidden representations for non-terminal nodes.  
- Key contribution: deep architecture with connections between different layers at each tree node; recommend ablation removing matrix V to test its effect.  
- Major concern: Table 1(a) appears to report model selection/tuning on the final test set — should report those results on the development set instead.  
- Minor issue: manuscript needs final proofreading to correct a few typographical errors.
