- Novel deep recursive neural network that achieves significant state-of-the-art results on a sentiment dataset.  
- Paper is exceptionally well-written and easy to follow.  
- Introduces disentangled parameter matrices for leaf vs. non-terminal nodes.  
- Incorporates ReLUs within the recursive framework.  
- Leverages large pre-trained word vectors with smaller hidden representations for non-terminal nodes.  
- Proposes a genuinely deep recursive architecture with inter-layer and inter-node connections enabling complex feature interactions.  
- Suggests an additional comparison: feed each parent only the last hidden layer outputs of children (remove direct V-mediated connection) to isolate inter-layer benefits.  
- Critical presentation issue: Table 1(a) reports test-set performance during development instead of using a held-out development set, risking overfitting.  
- Minor typographical error: revise the phrase “comprise a class of architecture.”
