Paper For paper 1180: Deep Recursive Neural Networks for Compositionality in Language
This submission proposes a deep recursive neural network (deep RNN) constructed by stacking multiple recursive layers. The authors evaluate the model on fine-grained sentiment classification.

- The authors present a reasonable set of experiments to assess various facets of the method.
