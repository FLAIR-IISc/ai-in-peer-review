Review for Paper 1180: Deep Recursive Neural Networks for Compositionality in Language

Summary
This paper introduces a new architecture—deep recursive neural networks (deep RNNs)—constructed by stacking multiple recursive layers. The authors evaluate the proposed model on the task of fine-grained sentiment classification.

Clarity
- The paper is generally well written and pleasant to read.

Quality
- The work appears technically sound.
- The central idea—building a deep recursive neural network by stacking multiple layers of individual recursive nets—is well motivated.
- The authors provide a reasonable set of experiments that evaluate various aspects of the method.

Originality
- The proposed idea is simple and reasonable.
- It would strengthen the paper to include more discussion on:
  - Why a deeper network is intuitively beneficial for the target task.
  - Why the specific network architecture was chosen over alternatives.

Significance
- The paper presents an interesting idea, offers clear background, and includes reasonable empirical support.
