Paper 1180: Deep Recursive Neural Networks for Compositionality in Language

This paper introduces a novel architecture – the deep recursive neural network (deep RNN) – constructed by stacking multiple recursive layers. The authors evaluate the proposed model on the task of fine-grained sentiment classification.

Clarity
In general, this paper is well-written and easy to follow.

Quality
The paper appears technically sound. The core idea – a deep recursive neural network built by stacking layers of individual recursive networks – is appealing. The authors provide a substantial amount of experimental results to evaluate different aspects of their method. 

Originality
The proposed idea is simple and reasonable. However, it would benefit from further discussion regarding:

 + The intuition behind why a deeper network is particularly well-suited for this task.
 + The rationale for choosing this specific network architecture.

Significance
The paper presents an interesting idea, provides a clear background description, and is supported by reasonable experimental results.
