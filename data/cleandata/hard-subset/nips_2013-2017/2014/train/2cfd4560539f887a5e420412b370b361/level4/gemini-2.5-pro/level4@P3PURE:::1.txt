### Review for Paper 1180: Deep Recursive Neural Networks for Compositionality in Language

**Summary**

This paper introduces a novel architecture, the Deep Recursive Neural Network (Deep RNN), which is constructed by stacking multiple recursive layers. The authors evaluate the proposed model on the task of fine-grained sentiment classification.

**Clarity**

In general, the paper is well-written and pleasant to read.

**Quality & Significance**

The paper appears to be technically sound. The central idea of creating a deep recursive neural network by stacking individual recursive layers is elegant and well-motivated. The authors provide a reasonable amount of experimental results to evaluate various aspects of the proposed method. Overall, the paper presents an interesting idea with clear background description and solid experimental support.

**Originality & Suggestions for Improvement**

The proposed idea is simple and reasonable. However, the paper could be strengthened by adding more discussion on the following points:

*   An intuitive explanation for why a deeper network is beneficial for the target task of sentiment analysis. What kind of complex compositional phenomena does the added depth help to capture?
*   A clearer motivation for the selected network architecture. Why was this particular deep structure chosen over other possible configurations?
