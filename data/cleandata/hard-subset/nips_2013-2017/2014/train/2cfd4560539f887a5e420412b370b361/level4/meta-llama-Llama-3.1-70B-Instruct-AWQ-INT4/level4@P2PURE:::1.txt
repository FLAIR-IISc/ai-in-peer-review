Paper For paper 1180: Deep Recursive Neural Networks for Compositionality in Language

This paper proposes a novel architecture, the deep recursive neural network (deep RNN), constructed by stacking multiple recursive layers, and evaluates its performance on fine-grained sentiment classification tasks.

Clarity: 
The paper is generally well-written and enjoyable to read.

Quality: 
The paper appears to be technically sound. 
The primary contribution, the deep recursive neural network, is a promising concept, achieved by stacking multiple layers of individual recursive nets. 
The authors provide a reasonable amount of experimental results to assess various aspects of the proposed method.

Originality: 
The proposed idea is straightforward and reasonable. 
However, it would be beneficial to include more discussions on the following aspects: 
+ An intuitive explanation of why a deeper network is more suitable for the target task. 
+ A justification for the selection of the chosen network architecture.

Significance: 
The paper presents an interesting idea, provides a clear background description, and offers reasonable experimental support. 
Overall, the paper effectively communicates its contributions, provides a thorough background, and demonstrates the efficacy of the proposed approach through experiments.
