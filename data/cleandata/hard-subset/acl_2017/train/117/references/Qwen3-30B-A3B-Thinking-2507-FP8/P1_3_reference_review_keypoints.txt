- Problem: KBQA relation detection differs from general relation extraction due to thousands of relations, zero-shot test relations, and chain relation requirements.  
- Proposed method: HR-BiLSTM performs hierarchical matching between questions and relations at word- and relation-levels with residual learning.  
- Contribution 1: A novel HR-BiLSTM model addressing KB-specific relation detection challenges.  
- Contribution 2: Demonstration that improved relation detection yields 2–3% end-to-end KBQA gains.  
- Contribution 3: An entity re-ranking step that leverages relation detection scores to improve entity disambiguation.  
- Strength: Strong motivation and clear identification of the three core KBQA relation challenges.  
- Strength: Clear experimental improvements with statistical significance on SimpleQuestions and WebQSP.  
- Strength: Thorough ablation studies validating hierarchical matching, residual learning, and representation choices.  
- Strength: Explicit demonstration that intermediate task gains translate to end-task KBQA improvements.  
- Strength: Well-structured paper with clear technical descriptions and illustrative figures.  
- Weakness: Lacks specific performance analysis on zero-shot (unseen) relations; no seen vs. unseen breakdown.  
- Weakness: No computational complexity or scalability analysis relative to simpler baselines.  
- Weakness: Insufficient analysis of failure cases and examples where the model fails.  
- Weakness: Entity re-ranking formula (weighted combination) is used without justification or comparison to alternatives.  
- Weakness: Missing comparisons to more recent end-to-end KBQA approaches (e.g., Neural Symbolic Machines).  
- Question: Please report model performance specifically on the 14% unseen relations in SimpleQuestions.  
- Question: How was the α weight in entity re-ranking chosen and how sensitive is performance to it?  
- Question: Provide computational complexity details (training/inference time) and scaling behavior with relation vocabulary size.  
- Question: Share examples of failure cases, especially where entity re-ranking mis-disambiguates.  
- Overall recommendation: Accept with minor revisions to address zero-shot analysis, complexity, failure cases, and re-ranking justification.
