- Proposes HR-BiLSTM: a hierarchical BiLSTM with residual learning for relation detection that combines segmented word-level and unsegmented relation-as-token representations.  
- Enables entity re-ranking after entity linking to improve KBQA performance.  
- Reports state-of-the-art accuracy on SimpleQuestions (single-relation) and WebQSP (multi-relation).  

- Improved relation detection model (HR-BiLSTM) that performs hierarchical question-relation matching.  
- Entity re-ranking module that reduces entity name ambiguity and boosts end-to-end KBQA accuracy.  
- Empirical state-of-the-art results on two standard QA benchmarks.  

- HR-BiLSTM outperforms previous methods on KB relation detection tasks.
