- Addresses relation detection for KBQA with large and potentially unseen relation sets.
- Proposes HR-BiLSTM that combines word-level and relation-level representations.
- Incorporates residual learning to facilitate deeper representation learning.
- Integrates improved relation detection into a two-step KBQA system with entity re-ranking after entity linking.
- Demonstrates state-of-the-art performance on SimpleQuestions and WebQSP.
- Strongest contribution: using relation detection to re-rank entity candidates, improving end-to-end KBQA accuracy.
- HR-BiLSTM is a solid model for combining relation abstractions; residuals are a sensible enhancement.
- Evaluation is limited to SimpleQuestions and WebQSP, so generalizability to other KBs is unclear.
- Description of the KBQA system and the entity linking component is too cursory; more detail is needed.
- Organization of Section 3 needs improvement to clarify idea flow.
- Question: which entity linker was used and were there modifications?
- Question: how sensitive is re-ranking performance to the hyperparameter α?
- Question: are there plans to evaluate the method on additional KBQA datasets or KBs?
