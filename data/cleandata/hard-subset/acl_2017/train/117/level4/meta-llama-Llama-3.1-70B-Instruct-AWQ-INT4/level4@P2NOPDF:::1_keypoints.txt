- Tackles mapping natural language to KB relations under partial argument information and many target relations—timely and relevant.
- Proposes a novel dual-representation: segmented word-level relation tokens plus unsegmented single-token relation representation.
- Effectively re-ranks entities after entity linking, an important QA system component.
- Experimental results show improvement over the state of the art.
- Evaluation limited to a small dataset, raising generalizability and overfitting concerns.
- Paper organization could be improved (move Section 3.1 to related work and renumber to streamline presentation).
