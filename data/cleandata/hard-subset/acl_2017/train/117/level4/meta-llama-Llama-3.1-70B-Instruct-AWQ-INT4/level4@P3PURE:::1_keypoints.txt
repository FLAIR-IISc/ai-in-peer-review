- Tackles mapping natural language to KB relations for QA, addressing challenges of partial argument info and large relation spaces.
- Proposes combining word-level (segmented) and relation-as-single-token representations of input text.
- Key contribution: ability to re-rank entities after the entity linking step in QA.
- Experimental results show improvement over the state of the art.
- Major weakness: evaluation is limited to a small dataset, raising generalizability concerns.
- Suggested reorganization: move Section 3.1 to related work and promote Section 3.2 to the main method section for clearer presentation.
