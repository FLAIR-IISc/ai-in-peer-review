- Tackles a relevant QA problem: mapping natural language to KB relations with partial argument information and many possible target relations.  
- Proposes combining two input-text representations: a word-level segmented representation and a single-token relation representation.  
- Uses both segmented relation names/input and unsegmented single-token relation embeddings for improved modeling.  
- Key contribution: enables re-ranking of entities after the Entity Linking step.  
- Empirical results show improvement over the state-of-the-art.  
- Evaluated on a limited dataset, which may not generalize to broader scenarios.  
- Suggests reorganizing the paper: move section 3.1 to related work and make section 3.2 the new section 3 for clearer presentation.
