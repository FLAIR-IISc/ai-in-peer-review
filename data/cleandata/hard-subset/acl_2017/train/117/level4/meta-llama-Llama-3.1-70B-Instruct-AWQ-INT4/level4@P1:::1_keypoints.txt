- Proposes a hierarchical recurrent neural network with residual learning for KB relation detection.
- Presents a simple KBQA system that integrates entity linking and the relation detector, achieving state-of-the-art results on single- and multi-relation benchmarks.
- Addresses a relevant NLP problem: mapping natural language to KB relations in QA.
- Combines word-level and relation-level text representations to improve relation detection.
- Introduces entity re-ranking after entity linking as a notable KBQA contribution.
- Shows empirical improvements over prior work on SimpleQuestions and WebQSP.
- Evaluation is limited to a small set of datasets, which may not generalize across the KBQA domain.
- Lacks detailed analysis of model performance across different question and relation types.
- Clarify how the model handles out-of-vocabulary words and unseen relations.
- Provide more details on the entity linking component and its integration with relation detection.
- Compare performance with other state-of-the-art KBQA systems on additional datasets.
- Paper is well-written and the model is clearly explained, but sections like 3.1 need more background/context.
- Results would benefit from more in-depth analysis and discussion.
