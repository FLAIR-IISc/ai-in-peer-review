- Addresses a relevant QA problem: learning mappings from natural language to KB relations with partial argument information and many candidate relations.
- Proposes a novel dual representation: word-level segmentation of relation names and input text plus a tokenized, non-segmented relation representation.
- Primary contribution is an entity re-ranking method applied after the Entity Linking step.
- Empirical results show notable improvement over the current state of the art.
- Major weakness: evaluation is limited to a restricted dataset.
