- Addresses mapping between natural language and KB relations for QA with partial argument info and many target relations.  
- Proposes combining two input representations: word-level segmented relations/text and relation-as-single-token unsegmented representation.  
- Main contribution in QA is enabling re-ranking of entities after the entity linking step.  
- Experimental results show improvement over state-of-the-art.  
- Major weakness: evaluated on a limited dataset; needs testing on larger, more diverse datasets.  
- Organizational suggestion: move Section 3.1 to related work and reorganize/split Section 3 for better flow.  
- Overall: interesting and promising approach but requires further extensive evaluation.
