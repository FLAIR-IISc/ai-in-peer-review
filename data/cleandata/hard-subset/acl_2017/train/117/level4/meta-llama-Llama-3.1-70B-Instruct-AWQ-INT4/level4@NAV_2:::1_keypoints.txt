- Proposed hierarchical recurrent model with residual bidirectional LSTMs to detect KB relations by matching questions and relation names across abstraction levels.
- Claimed contributions: hierarchical matching with residual learning; simple KBQA system integrating entity linking and relation detector achieving state-of-the-art.
- Reviewer: novel combination of word-level and relation-level representations for relation detection.
- Reviewer: effective entity re-ranking after entity linking that boosts KBQA performance.
- Reviewer: reports state-of-the-art results on SimpleQuestions and WebQSP.
- Strength: addresses the important problem of mapping natural language to KB relations.
- Strength: new method combining two representations shows improvement over prior work.
- Strength: experimental results demonstrate the approach’s effectiveness in KBQA tasks.
- Strength: entity re-ranking is a significant practical contribution.
- Weakness: evaluation on limited dataset(s), raising concerns about generalizability.
- Weakness: lacks detailed analysis and component-level ablation of the proposed method.
- Question: how will the approach extend to more complex KBQA with multiple relations or entities?
- Question: can authors provide more insight into the effectiveness of the residual learning component?
- Question: how do authors plan to address limited training data for KBQA tasks and its impact on performance?
