- Addresses the important problem of mapping natural language to KB relations in QA with partial information and many candidate relations.  
- Proposes a novel method combining word-level tokenized representations and relation-level atomic representations.  
- Demonstrates the model’s ability to effectively re-rank candidate entities after initial entity linking.  
- Shows clear improvement over state-of-the-art on the evaluated dataset.  
- Evaluation is limited to a single dataset, raising concerns about generalizability.  
- Suggests reorganizing the paper: move Section 3.1 to Related Work and make Section 3.2 the main model section.  
- Recommends splitting the main model section into clearer subsections to improve clarity and structure.
