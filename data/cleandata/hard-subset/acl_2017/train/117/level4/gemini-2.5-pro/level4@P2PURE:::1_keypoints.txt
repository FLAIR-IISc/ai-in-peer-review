- Addresses the relevant, challenging problem of mapping natural language to KB relations in a QA setting with partial argument info and many candidate relations.
- Combines word-level segmentation of input and relation names with a relation-level model treating relations as atomic tokens.
- Key contribution: model can re-rank candidate entities after initial entity linking, improving QA performance.
- Experimental results show improvement over state-of-the-art methods.
- Weakness: evaluation is limited to a single, somewhat small dataset, raising concerns about generalizability.
- Suggestion: restructure paper by moving Section 3.1 to Related Work, make Section 3 focused on the proposed method, and add clearer subsections for clarity.
