- Addresses mapping natural language questions to KB relations in a QA setting with partial argument information and a very large set of target relations.  
- Proposes a novel method combining two representations: segmented word-level representations for input text and relation names, plus unsegmented token-level representations for relations.  
- Key contribution: ability to re-rank candidate entities after entity linking, improving the QA pipeline.  
- Experimental results show clear improvement over existing state-of-the-art methods.  
- Weakness: evaluation is limited to a single, small dataset, raising concerns about generalizability.  
- Suggestion: paper structure could be improved—move Section 3.1 to Related Work and reorganize Section 3 to focus on the proposed method with clearer subdivisions.
