- Tackles mapping from natural language to KB relations for QA under partial-argument information and large candidate relation spaces.  
- Proposes a novel fusion of two input representations: a word-level view (segmented) and a single-token relation view (unsegmented).  
- Primary QA-pipeline contribution is the ability to re-rank entities after the Entity Linking stage.  
- Empirical results show improvements over the state of the art.  
- Evaluation is limited to a small/limited dataset.  
- Suggests reorganizing the paper: move Section 3.1 to Related Work and make Section 3.2 the main Methods section for clearer structure.
