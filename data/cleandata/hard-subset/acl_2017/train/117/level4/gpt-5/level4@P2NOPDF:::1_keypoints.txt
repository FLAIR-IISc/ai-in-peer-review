- Tackles the relevant problem of mapping natural language to KB relations for QA when one argument is partially specified and the relation space is large.
- Proposes a dual representation combining (1) word-level segmentation of relation names and input text and (2) a relation-as-single-token representation with unsegmented text.
- Main QA contribution is improved entity reranking following entity linking.
- Empirical results show gains over the state of the art.
- Weakness: evaluation is limited to a small dataset, restricting conclusion generality.
- Suggestion: move Section 3.1 to Related Work and reorganize sections for a clearer methodology presentation.
