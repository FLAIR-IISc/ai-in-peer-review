- Tackles the relevant problem of mapping natural language to KB relations in QA when one argument is partially specified and target relations are numerous.
- Introduces a method combining two input representations: (i) word-level segmentation of relation names and text, and (ii) treating each relation as a single token.
- Key QA-pipeline contribution is the ability to re-rank entities after the entity linking step.
- Experimental results show improvements over the state of the art.
- Weakness: evaluated on a limited dataset.
- Suggest reorganizing the paper by moving Section 3.1 to Related Work and making current Section 3.2 the main Section 3 for clearer structure.
