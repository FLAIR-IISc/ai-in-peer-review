- Tackles an important problem: aligning natural-language questions to KB relations under partial argument info and a large relation space.  
- Uses two complementary input representations: a word-level segmented view and a relation-as-atom token view.  
- Entity re-ranking after initial entity linking is the most impactful addition in the QA pipeline.  
- Reported results show improvements over prior state-of-the-art baselines.  
- Weakness: empirical evaluation is limited to a small set of datasets.
