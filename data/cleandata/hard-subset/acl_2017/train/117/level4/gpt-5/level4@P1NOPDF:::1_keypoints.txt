- Addresses an important problem: mapping natural language to KB relations for QA with partially known arguments and many possible target relations.  
- Introduces a method combining two complementary input representations: (i) word-level segmentation of relation names and input text.  
- Also uses a second representation treating relations as single tokens without segmenting relation names or input text.  
- Contributes a capability to re-rank candidate entities after the entity linking step.  
- Empirical results show improvements over the state of the art.  
- Weakness: evaluated on a limited dataset.
