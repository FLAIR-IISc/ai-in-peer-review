- Tackles relevant problem: mapping natural language to KB relations for QA with partial argument info and large relation set.  
- Proposes combining two input representations: (i) word-level segmentation of relation names and input text, and (ii) treating each relation as a single token.  
- Main pipeline contribution: ability to re-rank entities after the Entity Linking step.  
- Empirical results: shows improvements over state-of-the-art.  
- Limitation: evaluation is restricted to a small dataset.
