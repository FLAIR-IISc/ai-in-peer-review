- Addresses mapping between natural language and KB relations for QA when one argument is partially known and relation space is very large.
- Proposes a novel approach combining two input representations: segmented word-level and unsegmented relation-as-single-token.
- Introduces entity re-ranking after the Entity Linking step as a key QA contribution.
- Experimental results show improvements over the current state of the art.
- Evaluation is limited to a small dataset.
