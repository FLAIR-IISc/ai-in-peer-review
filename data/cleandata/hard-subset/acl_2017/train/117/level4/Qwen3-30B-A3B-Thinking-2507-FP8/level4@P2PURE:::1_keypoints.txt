- Addresses a relevant problem: learning mappings between natural language and KB relations for QA with partial argument info and many target relations.  
- Proposes a novel dual representation combining word-level segmentation of relation names/text and an unsegmented token-level representation treating relations as single tokens.  
- Main QA contribution is improving post-Entity Linking entity re-ranking.  
- Empirical results show improvements over the state of the art.  
- Weakness: evaluation is limited to a single dataset.
