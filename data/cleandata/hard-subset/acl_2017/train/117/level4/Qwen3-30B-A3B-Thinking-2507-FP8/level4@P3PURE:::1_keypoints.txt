- Addresses a relevant problem: mapping natural language to KB relations for QA when partial argument info is available and the relation space is large.  
- Proposes a novel method combining two input representations: (a) word-level segmented relation/name and input text, and (b) single-token unsegmented relation/name and input text.  
- Main QA contribution is improved entity re-ranking after the Entity Linking step.  
- Experiments show statistically significant improvements over the state of the art.  
- Weakness: evaluated on only one dataset, limiting generalizability.  
- Presentation suggestion: move Section 3.1 into Related Work and renumber 3.2 as Section 3 for clearer structure.
