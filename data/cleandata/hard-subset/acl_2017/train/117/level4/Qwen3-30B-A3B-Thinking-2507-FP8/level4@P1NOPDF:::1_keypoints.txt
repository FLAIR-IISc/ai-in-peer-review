- Addresses a timely, relevant problem: mapping natural language to KB relations in QA where partial argument info and large relation spaces are common.

- Core contribution: novel method combining two complementary text representations — word-level segmentation of relation names/input and single-token atomic relation encoding.

- Practical impact: enables effective entity re-ranking after Entity Linking, improving downstream QA performance.

- Empirical claim: shows consistent improvements over the state-of-the-art, validating the approach.

- Weakness: evaluation limited to a single, relatively small dataset, raising concerns about generalizability and robustness.

- Organization suggestion: move Section 3.1 (baseline) to Related Work and make Section 3 the proposed method.

- Clarity suggestion: split the method section into clearer subsections (e.g., dual-representation framework and re-ranking mechanism) to improve logical flow and readability.
