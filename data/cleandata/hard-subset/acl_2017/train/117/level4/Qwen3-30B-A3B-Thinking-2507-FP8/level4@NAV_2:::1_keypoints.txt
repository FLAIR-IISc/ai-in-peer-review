- Addresses the important KBQA challenge of mapping questions to relations under partial argument information and a very large relation space.  
- Proposes a novel dual representation for relation detection: word-level (segmented names and question text) and relation-level (unsplit relation tokens).  
- Introduces an entity re-ranking mechanism that uses relation detection to resolve ambiguities in initial entity linking, improving the KBQA pipeline.  
- Experimental results show consistent improvements over prior state-of-the-art on SimpleQuestions and WebQSP, with the hierarchical residual BiLSTM achieving the best accuracy.  
- Evaluation is limited to two benchmarks (SimpleQuestions and WebQSP), raising concerns about generalizability to other KBQA scenarios and question types.  
- Suggests relocating Section 3.1 (relation representation granularities) to Related Work to provide context and let Section 3.2 focus on the core contribution.
