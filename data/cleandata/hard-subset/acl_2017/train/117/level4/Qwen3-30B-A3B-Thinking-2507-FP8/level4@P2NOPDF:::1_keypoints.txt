- Tackles a timely, important problem: mapping natural language to KB relations for QA under partial arguments and large relation spaces.
- Proposes a novel dual-representation method combining word-level (segmented relation names and input) and token-level (relations as single tokens) representations.
- Practical advance: enables entity re-ranking during disambiguation after initial Entity Linking.
- Empirical results consistently improve over state-of-the-art baselines on the evaluated task.
- Major weakness: evaluation is limited to a single dataset, undermining generalizability and robustness claims.
- Presentation suggestion: reorganize to move the dual-representation description to Related Work, make the main methodology a standalone section, and subdivide it into core technical proposal and QA application for clearer flow.
