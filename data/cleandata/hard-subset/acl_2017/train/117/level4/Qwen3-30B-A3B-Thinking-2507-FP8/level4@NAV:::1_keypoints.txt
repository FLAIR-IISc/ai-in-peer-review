- Tackles mapping from natural language to knowledge-base relations for QA in settings with partial argument information and a very large relation set.  
- Proposes a novel method that fuses two text representations: a word-level (segmented) representation and a single-token (unsegmented) representation.  
- Key QA contribution: ability to re-rank entities after the Entity Linking phase.  
- Empirical results show consistent improvements over state-of-the-art methods.  
- Weakness: evaluation is limited to a small dataset.  
- Organizational suggestion: move Section 3.1 to Related Work and make Section 3.2 the main method section for better logical flow.
