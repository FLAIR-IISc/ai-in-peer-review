- Addresses learning correspondence between natural language and KB relations for QA with partial argument info and many candidate relations.  
- Proposes combining two input representations: word-level segmentation of relation names and text, and a second representation treating relations as single tokens without segmentation.  
- Key QA contribution: enables re-ranking of entities after the Entity Linking step.  
- Experimental results show improvement over the current state of the art.  
- Limitation: evaluation is performed on a restricted dataset.
