- Addresses the important, challenging problem of mapping natural language questions to KB relations within QA.
- Core contribution: a novel dual representation combining word-level segmentation and relation-as-atomic-token representations.
- Dual representation captures fine-grained semantic information and broader relational context.
- Introduces re-ranking of entities after the entity linking step as a notable pipeline contribution.
- Experimental results show improvements over existing state-of-the-art methods.
- Evaluation is limited to a single dataset, leaving generalizability unclear.
- Recommends testing on more diverse datasets/knowledge bases to strengthen claims.
- Suggests reorganizing the paper: move Section 3.1 to Related Work and renumber sections for clearer flow.
- Advocates decomposing the proposed approach into logically distinct subsections for clarity.
- Overall assessment: promising and potentially effective method that merits attention pending broader evaluation and structural revisions.
