- Tackles mapping natural language to KB relations in QA with partial argument info and many candidate relations — a realistic, important problem.
- Proposes a novel method that integrates two textual relation representations: word-level segmented relations and unsegmented single-token relations.
- Integration approach is especially useful for re-ranking candidate entities after entity linking, an important QA pipeline step.
- Experimental results show improvements over existing state-of-the-art methods.
- Evaluation is limited to a single dataset; wider evaluation across diverse datasets is needed to support generalizability claims.
