- Addresses mapping between natural language and KB relations for QA under partial argument information and very large relation sets (relevant, challenging problem).  
- Proposes a novel method combining two input text representations.  
- First representation: word-level with segmentation of relation names and input text.  
- Second representation: relations treated as single tokens, no segmentation.  
- Key contribution: ability to re-rank entities after the entity linking step in QA.  
- Experiments show improvement over state-of-the-art methods.  
- Concern: evaluation conducted on a relatively limited dataset.
