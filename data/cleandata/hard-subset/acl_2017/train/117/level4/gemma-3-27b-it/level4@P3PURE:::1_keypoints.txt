- Tackles the relevant, challenging problem of mapping natural language to KB relations in QA.  
- Difficulty amplified by partial argument information and a very large set of possible target relations.  
- Proposes a novel dual-representation: segmented word-level representation (for relation names and input) plus unsegmented relation-as-token representation.  
- Main QA contribution is enabling re-ranking of entities after the entity linking step.  
- Empirical results show improvements over state-of-the-art methods.  
- Limitation: evaluation on a limited dataset, reducing generalizability.  
- Presentation suggestion: move Section 3.1 to related work and make Section 3.2 the primary presentation of the proposed method for clearer exposition.
