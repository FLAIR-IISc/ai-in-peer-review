This paper tackles the important problem of learning to map natural language to knowledge base relations, specifically within a question answering setting characterized by partial argument information and a large number of potential relations. The proposed method introduces a novel combination of input text representations: one at the word level, incorporating segmentation of both target relation names and the input text itself, and another treating relations as single tokens without such segmentation. A key contribution within the QA context appears to be the ability to re-rank entities following the entity linking stage. The experimental results demonstrate performance improvements over existing state-of-the-art approaches.

A limitation of this work is its evaluation on a relatively limited dataset. 

Regarding the paper’s structure, I suggest relocating Section 3.1 to the related work section. This would allow Section 3.2 to be restructured as the main proposal (now simply Section 3), and enable a more logical and detailed presentation of the proposed method within that section.
