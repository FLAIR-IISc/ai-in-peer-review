- Problem addressed: learning correspondence between natural language and KB relations in QA with incomplete argument information and a large relation space.  
- Main contribution: novel method integrating two textual representations—word-level segmented relation/name and input text, plus atomic-token relation representation.  
- QA-specific advantage: method can refine entity rankings after the entity linking stage.  
- Empirical result: demonstrates performance gains over existing state-of-the-art methods.  
- Limitation: evaluation is conducted on a relatively constrained dataset.
