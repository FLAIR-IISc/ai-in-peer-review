- Morphology-aware word encoder: composes characters into word embeddings via weighted prefix RNN states, learning morphemes implicitly.
- Hierarchical character decoder: word-level decoder conditions a character-level HGRU with state resets and an unfolding scheme for efficient batching.
- Empirical claim: matches strong character baselines with fewer parameters/epochs and shows robustness to OOVs/misspellings.

- Novel, linguistically motivated encoder with PCA/energy visualizations showing better stem/affix separation than ordinary RNNs.
- Practical decoding engineering: settable-state HGRU and unfolding enable single-pass batching and favorable training-time/parameter trade-offs.
- Competitive BLEU quickly: outperforms BPE after one epoch (En–Fr, En–Cs) and approaches SOTA character models with smaller models.
- Demonstrated robustness to OOVs/misspellings via qualitative translation examples.

- Evaluation fairness concerns: comparisons use different setups/hardware, baselines not uniformly converged, no significance tests, limited metrics beyond BLEU.
- Insufficient ablations: paper doesn’t isolate gains from encoder vs decoder vs batching; normalization choice for weights not experimentally justified.
- Decoder/mechanics under-specified: HGRU equations and unfolding introduced tersely; memory/runtime implications of Ty×T unfolding and delimiter supervision unclear.
- Limited empirical scope: experiments only on En–Fr and En–Cs, leaving generalization to agglutinative languages and non-Latin scripts uncertain.

- What delimiter supervision is used for target words, and how robust is training to tokenization errors or languages without whitespace?
- Please provide ablations: (a) encoder with uniform/softmax weights, (b) HGRU replaced by standard GRU with resets, (c) removal of unfolding.
- Report peak GPU memory and tokens/sec versus Lee et al. (2016) on identical hardware; any scaling issues with long-sequence unfolding?
- Do results hold on agglutinative languages (e.g., Turkish/Finnish) and non-Latin scripts (e.g., Chinese/Japanese with large character vocabularies)?
