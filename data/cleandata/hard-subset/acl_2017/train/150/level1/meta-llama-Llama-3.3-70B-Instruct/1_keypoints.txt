- Novel architecture: introduces a morphology-learning design with dual recurrent networks and a hierarchical character-level decoder.
- Efficient training: demonstrates the ability to train a deep character-level NMT model efficiently, outperforming word-level baselines and matching state-of-the-art character models.
- Learning morphology: shows the model can learn morphological patterns and correctly translate misspelled and nonce words.
- Addresses OOV issue: uses character-level modeling to mitigate out-of-vocabulary problems inherent to word-level systems.
- Efficient training (strength): claims higher performance than word-level models with effective training of deep character-level networks.
- Morphology generalization (strength): translates misspellings and nonce words, indicating robust morphological learning.
