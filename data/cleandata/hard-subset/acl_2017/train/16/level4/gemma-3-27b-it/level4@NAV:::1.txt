**Strengths:**

This work explores the valuable, yet often overlooked, information contained within arguments to enhance event detection performance. The proposed framework is notable for its clarity and simplicity. Leveraging a supervised attention mechanism – a technique successfully applied in fields like machine translation – the system demonstrably and significantly outperforms baseline methods.

**Weaknesses:**

The current implementation calculates the attention vector as a simple summation of individual component attention vectors. Exploring more sophisticated attention aggregation methods may yield improvements. Regarding the supervised attention mechanism, the two proposed strategies are relatively direct; investigating more complex approaches could potentially lead to enhanced results.

**General Discussion:**

Despite opportunities for further refinement, this paper presents an effective framework with strong performance, supported by a robust experimental evaluation. Overall, this work is suitable for acceptance.
