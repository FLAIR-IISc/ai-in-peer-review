- Strengths:
This paper leverages information from arguments – a resource often overlooked but potentially crucial – to improve event detection performance. The proposed framework is clear and straightforward. Utilizing a supervised attention mechanism, a powerful technique successfully applied in areas like machine translation, the system demonstrably outperforms strong baselines.

- Weaknesses:
The attention vector is currently computed as a simple summation of two attention vectors, one from each component. Exploring more sophisticated aggregation methods could potentially yield further improvements. Regarding the supervised attention mechanism, the two proposed strategies are relatively simple. Investigating more complex strategies might lead to enhanced performance.

- General Discussion:
Despite areas for potential refinement, this paper presents an effective framework with strong empirical results. The experiments are well-executed and provide solid support for the approach. I believe this work is suitable for acceptance.
