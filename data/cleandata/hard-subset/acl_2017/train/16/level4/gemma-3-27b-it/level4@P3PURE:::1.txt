This paper explores the incorporation of argument information – often overlooked but potentially valuable – to enhance event detection performance. The proposed framework is clear and conceptually simple. Leveraging a supervised attention mechanism, a technique successfully applied in areas like machine translation, the authors demonstrate significant performance gains over baseline systems.

However, the method of combining attention vectors – a simple summation of the two component vectors – feels somewhat limited. Exploring more sophisticated combination strategies might yield further improvements. Similarly, while the two proposed strategies for the supervised attention mechanism are straightforward and functional, investigating more complex approaches could potentially lead to better results.

Overall, despite these areas for improvement, this paper presents an effective framework and achieves strong empirical results with a solid experimental setup. I believe this work is suitable for acceptance.
