- Strengths:
This paper leverages argument information – often overlooked but potentially valuable – to enhance event detection performance. The proposed framework is clear and straightforward. By incorporating a supervised attention mechanism, a well-established and effective technique from areas like machine translation, the system achieves significant performance gains over the baseline.

- Weaknesses:
The attention vector is computed as a simple sum of two component attention vectors. Exploring more sophisticated combination methods might yield further improvements. While two strategies are presented for the supervised attention mechanism, both are relatively simple; more complex strategies could be investigated for potentially better results.

- General Discussion:
Despite areas for potential refinement, this paper presents an effective framework with strong performance, supported by a solid experimental evaluation. This work is suitable for acceptance.
