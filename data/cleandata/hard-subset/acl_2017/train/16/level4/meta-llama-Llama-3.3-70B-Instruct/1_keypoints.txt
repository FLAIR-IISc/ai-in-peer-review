- Leverages often-overlooked argument information to improve event detection with a straightforward, well-structured framework.
- Incorporates a supervised attention mechanism (borrowed from MT) that yields significant improvement over the baseline.
- Attention vector is computed as a simple summation of two part-wise attention vectors, which may be suboptimal.
- The two proposed supervised attention strategies are straightforward and could be enhanced with more complex alternatives for potentially better results.
- Overall, the framework is effective with robust experimental results and represents a solid, publishable contribution despite areas for refinement.
