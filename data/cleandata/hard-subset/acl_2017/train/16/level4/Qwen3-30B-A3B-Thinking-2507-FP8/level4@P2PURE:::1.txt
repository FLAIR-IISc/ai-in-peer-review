

- Strengths:  
This paper proposes leveraging underutilized argument information—typically overlooked yet potentially valuable—to enhance event detection performance. The framework is clear and straightforward. By incorporating a supervised attention mechanism, a technique widely adopted in tasks such as machine translation, the authors achieve significant performance gains over the baseline.  

- Weaknesses:  
The attention vector is computed as a simple summation of two component attention vectors. A more sophisticated approach for computing this vector may yield improved results. Regarding the supervised attention mechanism, the two proposed strategies are relatively straightforward. More complex strategies could potentially deliver better performance and merit exploration.  

- General Discussion:  
Despite these limitations, the paper presents an effective framework with strong empirical results. The experimental design is well-executed and robust. The work is recommended for acceptance.
