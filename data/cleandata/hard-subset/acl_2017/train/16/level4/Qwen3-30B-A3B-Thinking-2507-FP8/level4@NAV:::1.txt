

Strengths:
This work effectively leverages argument information—often overlooked yet inherently significant—to enhance event detection performance. The proposed framework is well-structured and straightforward. By incorporating a supervised attention mechanism (a technique widely adopted in tasks like machine translation), the system substantially outperforms the baseline.

Weaknesses:
The attention vector is derived merely as the summation of two constituent attention vectors. A more sophisticated methodology for computing this vector could yield improved results. Additionally, while two strategies for the supervised attention mechanism are introduced, both are relatively simple. Exploring more complex alternatives that might deliver superior performance would be worthwhile.

General Discussion:
Despite minor areas for refinement, the paper presents a highly effective framework with strong empirical results. The experimental evaluation is thorough and convincing. The work merits acceptance.
