- Leverages often-overlooked argument information to improve event detection.  
- Presents a clear, straightforward framework.  
- Employs a supervised attention mechanism and achieves significant improvements over the baseline.  
- Attention vector is formed by simply summing two component attention vectors; a more principled combination is needed.  
- Proposed supervised attention strategies are simple; more sophisticated approaches could yield better performance.  
- Experiments are solid and overall the framework is effective; paper is worth considering for acceptance.
