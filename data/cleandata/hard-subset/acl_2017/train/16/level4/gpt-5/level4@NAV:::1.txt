Review-
- Strengths:
This paper leverages argument-level information—often neglected yet highly relevant—to enhance event detection performance. The framework is simple and clear. By incorporating a supervised attention mechanism, a widely used method in tasks such as machine translation, the system achieves substantial improvements over the baseline.

- Weaknesses:
The attention vector is formed by simply summing the two part-specific attention vectors; a more suitable aggregation method might be preferable. For the supervised attention mechanism, two strategies are introduced, both quite straightforward. More sophisticated approaches might perform better and are worth exploring.

- General Discussion:
Although there is room for improvement, the paper proposes an effective framework with strong performance. The experiments are solid. It can be considered for acceptance.
