- Strengths:
This paper leverages argument information—often overlooked yet crucial—to improve event detection. The framework is clear and straightforward. Using a supervised attention mechanism, a widely adopted technique in tasks such as machine translation, the system achieves a significant improvement over the baseline.

- Weaknesses:
The attention vector is computed by simply summing the two attention vectors from each component; a more principled approach might be more appropriate. For the supervised attention mechanism, the paper proposes two strategies, both of which are quite straightforward. More sophisticated strategies could potentially perform better and are worth exploring.

- General Discussion:
Despite some areas for improvement, the paper presents an effective framework with strong performance. The experiments are solid. It can be considered for acceptance.
