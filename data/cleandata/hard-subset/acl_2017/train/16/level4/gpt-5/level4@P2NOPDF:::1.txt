- Strengths:
This paper leverages argument information—often overlooked yet important—to improve event detection. The framework is clear and straightforward. By incorporating supervised attention, a technique widely used in tasks such as machine translation, the system achieves substantial gains over the baseline.

- Weaknesses:
The final attention vector is formed by simply summing the two part-specific attention vectors; a more principled aggregation method could be more effective. The two proposed strategies for supervised attention are rather basic; exploring more sophisticated supervision schemes may yield further improvements.

- General Discussion:
Despite areas for refinement, the paper presents an effective framework with strong performance. The experimental evaluation is solid. It is suitable for acceptance.
