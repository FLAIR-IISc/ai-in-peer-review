- Explicitly leverages argument information to improve event detection, addressing an often-overlooked but informative signal.
- Presents a clear, straightforward overall framework.
- Introduces supervised attention (from NMT literature) that yields substantial improvements over the baseline.
- Final attention is formed by simply summing two attention vectors; a more principled fusion/interaction could be preferable.
- The two proposed attention supervision strategies are fairly basic; richer or more adaptive strategies could yield further gains.
- Empirically effective framework with strong experimental results.
- Experiments are solid and the paper is suitable for acceptance.
