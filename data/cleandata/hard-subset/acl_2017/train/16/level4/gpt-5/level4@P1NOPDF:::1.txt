- Strengths:
This paper leverages argument information—often overlooked despite its importance—to improve event detection. The framework is clear and simple. With the use of a supervised attention mechanism, a widely applied method in tasks such as machine translation, the system significantly outperforms the baseline.

- Weaknesses:
The overall attention vector is computed by simply summing the two attention vectors from each component. A more principled method for combining them may be more appropriate. For the supervised attention mechanism, two strategies are proposed, both quite straightforward. Exploring more sophisticated strategies could yield better results and should be considered.

- General Discussion:
Although there is room for improvement, this paper presents an effective framework with good performance. The experiments are solid. It can be considered for acceptance.
