- Strengths:
This paper explores the use of argument information, often overlooked but crucial, to enhance event detection performance. The proposed framework is clear and straightforward. By leveraging a supervised attention mechanism, a technique commonly employed in tasks such as machine translation, the system achieves significant performance gains over the baseline.

- Weaknesses:
The attention vector calculation, which simply sums the attention vectors of each part, may not be the most effective approach. Alternative methods for calculating the attention vector could be explored. Regarding the supervised attention mechanism, the two proposed strategies, while straightforward, may not be optimal. More sophisticated strategies could potentially yield better results and are worth investigating.

- General Discussion:
Despite areas for improvement, this paper presents an effective framework and achieves good performance. The experimental setup is solid, and the results are convincing. Overall, the paper makes a compelling contribution, and its acceptance is warranted.
