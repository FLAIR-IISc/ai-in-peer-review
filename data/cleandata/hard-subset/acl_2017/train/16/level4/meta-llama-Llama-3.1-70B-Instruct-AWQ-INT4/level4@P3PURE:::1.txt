- Strengths:
This paper proposes a novel approach to leveraging information from arguments, which is often overlooked but crucial, to enhance event detection performance. The framework is clear and straightforward, and the incorporation of a supervised attention mechanism, a technique widely used in tasks such as machine translation, yields significant improvements over the baseline.

- Weaknesses:
One potential limitation lies in the calculation of the attention vector, which is simply the summation of two attention vectors for each part. A more sophisticated approach to computing the attention vector may be warranted. Additionally, the two strategies proposed for the supervised attention mechanism are straightforward, and exploring more complex strategies could potentially lead to better results.

- General Discussion:
Despite areas for improvement, this paper presents an effective framework and achieves good performance. The experimental setup is solid, and the overall contribution is noteworthy. Considering the strengths and weaknesses, this paper is a strong candidate for acceptance.
