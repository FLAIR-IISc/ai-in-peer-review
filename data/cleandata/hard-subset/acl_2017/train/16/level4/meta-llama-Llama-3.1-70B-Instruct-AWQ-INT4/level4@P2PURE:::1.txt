- Strengths:
This paper explores the utilization of argument information, a previously underutilized yet valuable resource, to enhance event detection performance. The proposed framework is clear and straightforward, leveraging a supervised attention mechanism - a technique successfully applied in various tasks, including machine translation - to achieve significant performance gains over the baseline.

- Weaknesses:
The calculation of the attention vector, which is currently a simple summation of the attention vectors from each part, may benefit from a more sophisticated approach. Regarding the supervised attention mechanism, the two proposed strategies are somewhat straightforward and may not be optimal. Exploring more complex strategies could potentially yield better results.

- General Discussion:
Despite areas for improvement, the paper presents an effective framework with promising performance. The experimental design is solid, and overall, the work is worthy of consideration for acceptance.
