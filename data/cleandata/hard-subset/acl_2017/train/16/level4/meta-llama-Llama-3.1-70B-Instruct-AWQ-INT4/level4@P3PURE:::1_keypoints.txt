- Proposes a novel approach leveraging argument information to improve event detection, addressing an often-overlooked but important signal.  
- Presents a clear, straightforward framework that incorporates a supervised attention mechanism adapted from machine translation.  
- The supervised attention yields significant improvements over the baseline.  
- Attention vector is computed by simply summing two attention vectors per part, which may be overly simplistic.  
- The two proposed supervised-attention strategies are straightforward; more complex strategies might yield further gains.  
- Experimental setup is solid and demonstrates good performance.  
- Contribution is noteworthy, and the paper is a strong candidate for acceptance despite areas for improvement.
