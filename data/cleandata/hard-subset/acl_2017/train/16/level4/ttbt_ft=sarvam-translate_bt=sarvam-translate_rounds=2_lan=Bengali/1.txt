**Strength:**

This paper attempts to use information derived from reasoning, which is often overlooked but is quite important for improving event detection performance. The framework is clear and simple. With the help of the Attention Mechanism in Supervision (ATMS), an important method used in many tasks such as machine translation, their system performance significantly exceeds the baseline.

**Weaknesses:**

The Attention Vector is the sum of two Attention Vectors from each section. Perhaps the Attention Vector could be calculated in a more suitable way. For the Attention Mechanism in Supervision, two techniques have been proposed. Both techniques are quite simple. Some complex techniques might work better and should be tried.

**General Discussion:**

Although there are areas where improvements can be made, this paper proposes a reasonably effective framework and the performance is good. The test is reliable. It can be considered acceptable.
