**Strengths:**

This paper improves event detection performance by leveraging information from event arguments—an often-overlooked yet valuable source of information. The proposed framework is clear and straightforward. It successfully incorporates a supervised attention mechanism, a powerful technique that has proven effective in other tasks such as machine translation. As a result, the proposed system significantly outperforms the baseline.

**Weaknesses:**

The final attention vector is calculated as a simple summation of two component vectors. A more sophisticated method for combining these vectors, rather than a simple sum, might yield better results. Similarly, the two proposed strategies for the supervised attention mechanism are relatively straightforward. Exploring more complex supervision strategies could potentially lead to further performance gains.

**General Discussion:**

While there are areas for potential improvement as noted above, this paper presents a novel and effective framework. The experimental evaluation is solid, and the results demonstrate a clear performance improvement. Overall, this is a valuable contribution, and I recommend the paper for acceptance.
