### Strengths
This paper's primary strength lies in its novel approach to event detection, which leverages information from event arguments—a crucial but often-overlooked source of evidence. The proposed framework is conceptually simple and clearly presented. By incorporating a supervised attention mechanism, a technique successfully applied in fields like machine translation, the system achieves significant performance improvements over the baseline.

### Weaknesses
The method for combining attention vectors, a simple summation of the component vectors, may be suboptimal. The authors could explore more sophisticated approaches for this step. Furthermore, the two proposed strategies for the supervised attention mechanism are relatively straightforward. The paper could be strengthened by investigating more complex supervision strategies, which might yield further performance gains.

### General Discussion
While there are areas for potential improvement as noted above, this paper proposes a novel and effective framework with strong performance. The experimental evaluation is solid and the results are compelling. Given its contributions, the paper is a good candidate for acceptance.
