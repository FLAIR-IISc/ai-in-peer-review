- Strengths:
This paper proposes a method to improve event detection performance by incorporating information from event arguments, a source of information that is often neglected yet valuable. The proposed framework is clear and straightforward. By employing a supervised attention mechanism, a technique that has proven effective in other tasks such as machine translation, the system achieves significant performance gains over the baseline.

- Weaknesses:
The joint attention vector is calculated via a simple summation of two component vectors; more sophisticated approaches for combining these vectors could be explored. Furthermore, the two proposed strategies for the supervised attention mechanism are relatively straightforward. Investigating more complex supervision strategies might yield further improvements.

- General Discussion:
While there are areas for potential improvement, this paper presents an effective framework that achieves strong performance. The experiments are solid and well-executed. On balance, the paper is a valuable contribution and warrants acceptance.
