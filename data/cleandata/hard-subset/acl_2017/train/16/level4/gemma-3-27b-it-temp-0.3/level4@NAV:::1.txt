## Review:

**Strengths:**
This work explores the valuable, yet often overlooked, information contained within argument structure to enhance event detection performance. The proposed framework is presented with clarity and simplicity. Leveraging a supervised attention mechanism – a well-established and successful technique from fields like machine translation – the system demonstrably and significantly outperforms baseline methods.

**Weaknesses:**
The current implementation combines attention vectors via a simple summation. Exploring alternative, potentially more sophisticated, methods for attention vector calculation could be beneficial. Regarding the supervised attention mechanism, the two proposed strategies are relatively direct. Investigating more complex strategies may yield further performance improvements.

**General Discussion:**
Despite areas for potential refinement, this paper presents an effective framework achieving strong performance. The experimental evaluation is robust and well-executed. Overall, this work is suitable for acceptance.
