- Evaluation of multiple fine-grained domain adaptation methods on the dataset.

- Clear, well-written paper that explains task and experiments effectively.
- Thorough, well-designed experiments that test and compare all hypotheses.
- Dataset and annotations are a useful resource for future research.

- Task alignment with tagging/chunking/parsing is questionable; it fits information extraction better.

- Baseline results are basic and could be strengthened with more advanced NLP baselines.
- Domain adaptation findings largely corroborate known properties of user-generated text without novel insights.

- Clarify annotation guidelines, especially ambiguous labels like "sombody."
- Commit to fixing formatting issues to comply with submission guidelines.
- Explain potential applications and impact of the dataset/task for NLP and information extraction.
