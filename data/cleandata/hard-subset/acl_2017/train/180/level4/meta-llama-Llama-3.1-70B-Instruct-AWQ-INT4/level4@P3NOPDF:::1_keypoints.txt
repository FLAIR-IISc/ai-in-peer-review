- New dataset of product annotations from online cybercrime forums is a valuable contribution.  
- Paper is well-written and clearly organized.  
- Experiments are thorough, with hypotheses tested and compared.  
- Formatting concern: authors altered font size/line spacing in the abstract to exceed standard length—violates submission guidelines.  
- Scope mismatch: work appears more aligned with information extraction/IR/NLP than tagging/chunking/parsing.  
- Annotation clarity issue: labels like "somebody" (Figure 1) are hard to interpret and need more context/explanation.  
- Baselines are underwhelming given current NLP resources; stronger baselines were feasible.  
- Domain adaptation results are well-executed but unsurprising and provide no new insights beyond expected forum-specific style differences.
