- Presents a new dataset of products from online cybercrime forums; paper is well-written, clearly organized, and experiments are thorough.  
- Authors altered abstract formatting (font size/line spacing) to fit content into the 8-page limit, raising formatting/ethics concerns.  
- Paper’s topical fit is questionable: it appears to focus on information extraction rather than tagging/chunking/parsing.  
- Annotation scheme has unclear labels (e.g., "somebody" in Figure 1) that need better justification or explanation.  
- Baseline models are underwhelming given available NLP tools; stronger baselines were feasible with little extra effort.  
- Domain adaptation experiments are thorough but offer unsurprising insights about forum-specific linguistic variation and limited new contribution.
