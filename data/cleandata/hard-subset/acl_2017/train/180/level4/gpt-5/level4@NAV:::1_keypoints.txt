1. Authors manipulated abstract formatting (font size/line spacing) to extend the abstract and fit within the 8-page limit.  
2. The task is mischaracterized; it should be framed as information extraction rather than tagging, chunking, or parsing.  
3. Some annotation choices are questionable — e.g., labels like "sombody" do not clearly appear product-related.  
4. Baselines are rudimentary; stronger, readily available NLP baselines should have been included.  
5. Domain adaptation experiments largely reiterate known differences in user-generated content and do not provide novel insights.
