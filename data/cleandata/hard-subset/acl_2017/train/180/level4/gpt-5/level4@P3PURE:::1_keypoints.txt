- Introduces a new dataset of product annotations from online cybercrime forums; paper is clear, well written, and experiments are generally solid with hypotheses tested.
- Formatting compliance issue: abstract appears to use reduced font size and altered line spacing to fit the 8-page limit.
- Topical fit concern: the work is better characterized as information extraction than tagging/chunking/parsing.
- Annotation justification lacking: reasons for some annotations (e.g., labeling “somebody” as a product) are unclear.
- Baselines and results are basic; stronger, readily available NLP baselines should have been included.
- Domain adaptation experiments confirm expected differences in user-generated content and do not provide new insights.
