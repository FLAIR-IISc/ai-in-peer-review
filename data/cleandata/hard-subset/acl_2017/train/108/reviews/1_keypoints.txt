- Paper is generally well-written with minor issues.  
- Tackles a useful problem (overlapping entity detection).  
- Proposes a multigraph-based model, a variant of MH.  
- Empirical results are solid and improve state-of-the-art on benchmarks.  
- Section 3 needs to state specific issues with MH that motivate the new model.  
- Section 4: unclear why separators are introduced and what they add beyond T/I/O.  
- Section 5.1 fails to demonstrate why the new model is superior.  
- Section 5.2 is too abstract; needs concrete examples of spurious structures.  
- Overall: improvement shown empirically, but it's unclear why and how the new model works better.
