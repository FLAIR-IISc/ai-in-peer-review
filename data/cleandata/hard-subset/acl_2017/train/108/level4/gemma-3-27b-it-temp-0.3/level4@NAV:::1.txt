## Review:

This paper is generally well-written and addresses a valuable problem. The proposed multigraph-based model represents a logical extension of the existing MH approach, and the empirical results presented are robust. However, several areas require further clarification.

Specifically, the paper would benefit from a more detailed discussion of its limitations. 

1. In Section 3, while the previous model (MH) is described, the paper should explicitly articulate the shortcomings of MH that motivate the development of this new model.
2. Section 4 introduces separators, but the rationale behind their inclusion is unclear. The paper needs to explain what additional information these separators contribute beyond the Type, Instance, and Object relationships already considered.
3. The analysis in Section 5.1 does not convincingly demonstrate the superiority of the proposed model.
4. The discussion in Section 5.2 is overly abstract, making it difficult to understand *why* the new model outperforms MH. Concrete examples of the “spurious structures” referenced would greatly enhance understanding.

Overall, the paper introduces a novel model for overlapping entity detection that achieves performance gains over the state-of-the-art (MH) on benchmark datasets. However, the underlying mechanisms driving these improvements remain unclear and require further elaboration.
