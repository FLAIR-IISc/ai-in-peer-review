- Writing is mostly clear, with a few areas needing clarification to improve coherence.
- The problem (detecting overlapping entities) is useful and relevant.
- The paper proposes a multigraph-based model as a variant of the Multihop (MH) model.
- Empirical results are solid and improve on MH across benchmark datasets.
- Section 3 should explicitly describe shortcomings of MH that motivate the new model.
- Section 4: the use of separators is not justified — unclear what they add beyond T/I/O labeling.
- Section 5.1 lacks useful insight into why the new model outperforms existing approaches and should be rephrased/expanded.
- Section 5.2 is too abstract; the authors should give concrete examples of spurious structures their model handles better than MH.
- Overall, it's unclear why and how the new model achieves better results; the paper needs a more detailed explanation of its strengths and weaknesses.
