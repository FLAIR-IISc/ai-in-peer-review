- Well-written paper addressing a useful problem with a multigraph-based model built on the Maximum Homogeneity (MH) framework.  
- Solid empirical results demonstrating the model's effectiveness.  
- Need to explicitly state the limitations of the MH model that the new approach addresses (motivation unclear).  
- Purpose and benefits of introducing separators are unclear—explain what they add beyond the T/I/O framework.  
- Comparative analysis should provide deeper insight into why the new model outperforms existing approaches.  
- Discussion of improvements is overly abstract and needs concrete examples of spurious structures the new model detects better than MH.  
- Novel model improves overlapping-entity detection over MH on benchmarks but requires clearer explanation of the mechanisms behind its superior performance.
