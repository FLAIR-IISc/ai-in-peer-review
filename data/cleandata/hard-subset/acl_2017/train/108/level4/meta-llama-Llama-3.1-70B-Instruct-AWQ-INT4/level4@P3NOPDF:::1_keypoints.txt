- Paper proposes a well-motivated variant of the Multigraph-based (MH) model to detect overlapping entities, with solid empirical results.
- Writing is clear and concise, and the approach is well-integrated into existing literature.
- Empirical results convincingly demonstrate improvement over prior methods on benchmark datasets.
- Section 3: authors should explicitly state limitations of MH that motivate the new model.
- Section 4: the use of separators is not well-motivated—unclear what extra information they provide beyond T/I/O.
- Section 5.1: lacks concrete insight or examples explaining why the new model outperforms state-of-the-art.
- Section 5.2: discussion is too abstract; needs specific examples of spurious structures the new model handles better than MH.
- Overall: paper shows empirical gains but does not clearly explain why or how the new model achieves those gains; more concrete explanations and examples are needed.
