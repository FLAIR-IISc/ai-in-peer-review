- Claims empirical improvement over previous models on standard datasets.
- Provides a theoretical analysis of the model’s strengths and weaknesses.

- Tackles an interesting problem, especially crossing entities.
- Technique appears to work and the paper is well-written.
- Includes some discussion of efficiency relative to a prior system.

- Problem motivation is weak; the importance of detecting nested entities is not clearly justified.
- Important implementation details are missing, notably the decision criterion for including edges in the multigraph.
- Reported empirical improvements are slight and the effect size is unclear.
- No comparison to commercial systems or discussion of real-world impact.
- Lacks error analysis and discussion of how the system handles errors.
