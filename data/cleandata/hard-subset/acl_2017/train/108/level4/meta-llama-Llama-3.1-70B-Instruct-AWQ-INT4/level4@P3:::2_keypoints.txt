- Proposes a multigraph-based model for recognizing overlapping entities and shows it outperforms prior models on standard datasets, with analysis of differences.

Contributions
- Novel multigraph-based model for overlapping entity recognition.
- Empirical results showing the model outperforms previous models on standard datasets.
- Analysis comparing the new model to prior models and discussion of prior models' limitations.

Strengths
- Addresses an important, underexplored problem with clear motivation.
- Presents a novel and effective modeling solution.
- Provides a thorough comparative analysis clarifying strengths and limitations.

Weaknesses
- Motivation for why overlapping entity recognition is important could be expanded.
- Some model details are unclear, notably the decision criterion for including edges in the multigraph.
- Empirical evaluation is limited; lacks comparison to commercial systems and effect-size discussion.

Questions for the authors
- Can you provide more motivation/examples for the importance of overlapping entity recognition?
- Please elaborate on the decision criterion for including an edge in the multigraph.
- How would this approach extend to other structured prediction problems with complex structures?

Minor remarks
- First mention of “multigraph” should include a brief definition.
- Clarify notation/terminology (e.g., use of “state” vs “node”).
- Improve formatting and punctuation consistency.
