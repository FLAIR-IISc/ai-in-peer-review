- Strength: Well-written paper addressing a useful problem; proposed multigraph-based variant of the Matrix-Tree (MH) model shows solid empirical results.
- Weakness: Section 3 should state specific limitations of MH that motivated the new model.
- Weakness: Section 4’s introduction of separators is unclear—need to explain what extra information they convey beyond T/I/O.
- Weakness: Section 5.1 fails to explain why the new model is superior to existing approaches.
- Weakness: Section 5.2 is overly abstract; provide concrete examples of spurious structures to clarify why the model outperforms MH.
- General: Paper improves SOTA on overlapping-entity detection across benchmarks but it remains unclear why and how those improvements arise.
