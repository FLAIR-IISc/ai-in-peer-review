- Strength: Paper is well-written with only minor issues.  
- Strength: Addresses a meaningful problem (overlapping entity detection).  
- Strength: Proposes a multigraph-based model, a variant of MH.  
- Strength: Empirical results are robust and show promise.  
- Weakness: Section 3 should explicitly state MH’s limitations that motivated the new model.  
- Weakness: Section 4 needs to clarify the rationale for separators and what extra information they add beyond T/I/O.  
- Weakness: Section 5.1 fails to explain why the proposed model outperforms MH.  
- Weakness: Section 5.2 is overly abstract and should include concrete examples of spurious structures.  
- General: Model improves on MH across benchmarks but the mechanisms behind the improvement are unclear and need better articulation.
