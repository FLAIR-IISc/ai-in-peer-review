- Strength: Well written paper with an important problem, proposing a multigraph-based model (variant of MH) and showing solid empirical results.  
- Weakness: Section 3 should explicitly state MH’s limitations that motivate the new model.  
- Weakness: Section 4’s rationale for introducing separators is unclear — what do they add beyond T/I/O?  
- Weakness: Section 5.1 does not provide convincing evidence or explanations for the new model’s superiority.  
- Weakness: Section 5.2 is too abstract; the paper should give concrete examples of spurious structures.  
- General: The paper improves over MH on benchmarks for overlapping-entity detection but fails to explain clearly why and how the improvement occurs.
