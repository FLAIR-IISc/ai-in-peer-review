- Manuscript is clearly written and well-structured.  
- Addresses a significant and relevant problem.  
- Proposed multigraph-based model is a logical extension of the MH approach.  
- Empirical evaluation is robust and results are compelling.  
- Section 3 needs a clearer motivation: specify MH’s limitations that the new model addresses.  
- Section 4 must justify separators: explain what extra information they provide beyond T/I/O tags.  
- Section 5.1 analysis fails to identify sources of the new model’s superior performance.  
- Section 5.2 is too abstract and should give concrete examples of the “spurious structures” avoided.  
- Overall, the model improves over MH on benchmarks but the paper does not convincingly explain why and how the architectural changes produce the empirical gains.
