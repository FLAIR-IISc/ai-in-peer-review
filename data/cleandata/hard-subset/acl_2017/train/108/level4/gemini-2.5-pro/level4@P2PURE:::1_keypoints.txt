- Strength: Well-written and tackles the important problem of overlapping entity detection.
- Strength: Proposes a multigraph-based variant of MH claimed as novel, with strong empirical improvements over baselines.
- Weakness: Section 3 fails to articulate specific limitations of the prior MH model, leaving the motivation unclear.
- Weakness: Section 4 does not justify the introduction of separators or explain what information they add beyond standard T/I/O tags.
- Weakness: Section 5.1 lacks sufficient analysis to identify the sources of the proposed model’s superior performance.
- Weakness: Section 5.2 is overly abstract and needs concrete examples of the “spurious structures” the new model handles but MH does not.
- General: Empirical results are convincing, but the paper does not convincingly explain why/how the architectural changes lead to performance gains.
