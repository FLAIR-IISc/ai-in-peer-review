- Paper proposes a novel multigraph-based variant of the MH model for overlapping entity detection and reports solid empirical gains.  
- Writing is clear and the work tackles the important practical problem of overlapping entity recognition.  
- The multigraph model is an interesting and meaningful extension of the previous MH approach.  
- Empirical evaluation is strong, showing consistent improvements over competitive baselines on benchmarks.  
- Main weakness: unclear motivation—paper does not clearly state the specific limitations of MH that the new model addresses.  
- Rationale for introducing "separators" is unclear; need to explain what extra information they capture beyond T/I/O tags.  
- Analysis in Section 5.1 is insufficient and does not explain why the new model outperforms MH.  
- Discussion in Section 5.2 is too abstract and should include concrete examples of "spurious structures" the new model handles or avoids.
