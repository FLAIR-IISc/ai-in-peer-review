- Paper is generally well-written but needs clarification in a few areas.
- The problem is valuable and the proposed multigraph-based model is a reasonable variant of the Markov Hierarchy (MH) model.
- Empirical results are solid and demonstrate performance improvements over MH.
- Section 3 should explicitly state the limitations of MH that motivate the new model.
- Section 4 needs a clear rationale for introducing separators and what extra information they add beyond T/I/O tags.
- Section 5.1 fails to convincingly explain why the new model performs better.
- Section 5.2 is overly abstract and should give concrete examples of “spurious structures” and specific incorrect groupings MH makes.
- Overall: the paper shows that the new model outperforms MH but does not adequately explain why or how these improvements arise.
