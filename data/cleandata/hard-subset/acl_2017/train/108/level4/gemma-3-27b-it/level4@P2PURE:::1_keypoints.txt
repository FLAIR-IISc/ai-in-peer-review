- Paper is generally well-written with only minor clarity issues.  
- Addresses a valuable problem and proposes a worthwhile multigraph-based extension of the Markovian Hierarchical (MH) model.  
- Empirical results are robust and convincing across benchmarks.  
- Section 3 lacks an explicit discussion of MH’s limitations that motivate the new model.  
- Rationale for introducing separators in Section 4 is unclear; need to explain what they add beyond Type/Inclusion/Overlap features.  
- Section 5.1 does not sufficiently justify why the new model achieves performance gains.  
- Section 5.2 is overly abstract; reasons for improvements over MH remain unclear.  
- Authors should provide concrete examples of spurious structures the model identifies to improve understanding.  
- Overall: model shows improvements but paper lacks a clear, compelling explanation of how and why those gains are achieved; more motivation and detailed behavioral analysis required.
