This paper presents a novel multigraph-based model for overlapping entity detection, building upon the existing Markov Hierarchy (MH) approach. The paper is generally well-written and addresses an important problem. The empirical results demonstrate a solid improvement over the state-of-the-art. However, the justification for the improvements and certain design choices require further clarification.

Regarding strengths, the writing is clear overall, with only minor areas needing attention (detailed below). The task of overlapping entity detection is valuable, and the proposed approach shows promise. The experimental validation is convincing.

The paper would benefit from several key revisions. First, in Section 3, while the description of the MH model is adequate, the motivation for the proposed multigraph model is not sufficiently articulated. The authors should explicitly highlight the limitations of MH that their approach addresses. What specific shortcomings of MH motivate the introduction of the multigraph structure?

Second, the rationale for introducing separators in Section 4 is unclear. The paper needs to explain what additional information these separators contribute beyond the standard Type, Instance, and Ontology (T/I/O) representations. What specific benefit do they provide in modeling entity relationships and improving detection accuracy?

Third, Section 5.1, which attempts to analyze the model’s superiority, lacks compelling evidence. The presented information does not adequately explain *why* the new model outperforms MH. More specific analysis and quantifiable results are needed to support the claim of improved performance.

Finally, the discussion in Section 5.2 is overly abstract. The claim that the new model avoids “spurious structures” is not sufficiently grounded. Concrete examples illustrating these spurious structures in MH and how the proposed model mitigates them would significantly strengthen this argument. Providing such examples would offer valuable insight into the model’s improved performance and the benefits of the multigraph representation.

In summary, the paper demonstrates a positive contribution to the field of overlapping entity detection. The experimental results are encouraging, showing an improvement over the baseline MH model. However, the paper lacks a clear explanation of *how* and *why* the proposed model achieves these gains. Addressing the points raised above – specifically by clarifying the motivations for design choices, providing concrete examples, and offering more detailed analysis – would substantially enhance the paper’s clarity and impact.
