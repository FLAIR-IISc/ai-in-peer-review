- Paper is well-written and tackles a valuable problem with a logical multigraph-based extension of Markov homology (MH).  
- Empirical results are robust and show improved performance over MH on standard benchmarks.  
- The paper needs clearer motivation: explicitly state MH’s limitations that motivated the new model (Section 3).  
- Introduction of separators (Section 4) lacks justification; clarify what novel information they provide beyond T/I/O distinctions.  
- Results in Section 5.1 do not convincingly demonstrate the new model’s advantages; provide stronger empirical evidence or analysis.  
- Discussion in Section 5.2 is overly abstract; include concrete examples of spurious structures found by the new model but missed by MH.  
- Overall, the mechanism behind the performance gains is unclear; explain why and how the model achieves better results.
