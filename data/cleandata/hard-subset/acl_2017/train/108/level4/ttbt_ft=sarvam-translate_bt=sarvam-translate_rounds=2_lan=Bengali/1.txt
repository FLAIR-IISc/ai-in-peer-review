**Strengths:** The paper is well-written, but there are some flaws in a few places described below. The paper is quite useful in discussing the problem it addresses. The proposed method, a multi-graph-based model, is a variation of MH. Its empirical results are quite strong.

**Weaknesses:** Clarification is needed in a few places.

1.  In section 3, along with the description of the previous model MH, the problems with MH should be mentioned that prompted you to propose a new model.
2.  In section 4, I don't understand why a Separator is used. What additional information does it add beyond T/I/O?
3.  Why doesn't the new model provide the necessary information on why it is better in paragraph 5.1?
4.  The discussion in paragraph 5.2 is so abstract that I don't get any idea why the new model is better than MH. Can you give an example of a spurious structure?
    - General Discussion: This paper presents a new model for identifying overlapping entities in text. The new model improves upon the previous state-of-the-art model MH in tests conducted on several benchmark datasets. However, it is not clear why and how the new model works better.
