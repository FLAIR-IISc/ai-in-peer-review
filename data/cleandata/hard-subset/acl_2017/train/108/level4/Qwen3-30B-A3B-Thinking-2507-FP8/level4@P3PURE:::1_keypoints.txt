- Strength: Well-written paper with minor grammatical issues.
- Strength: Addresses a significant problem and proposes a meaningful multigraph-based variant of MH.
- Strength: Empirical results are robust and well-supported.

- Weakness: Section 3 must explicitly identify MH model limitations that motivate the proposed approach.
- Weakness: Section 4 lacks justification for introducing separators and how they add information beyond T/I/O.
- Weakness: Section 5.1 does not provide insights into why the new model outperforms MH.
- Weakness: Section 5.2 is overly abstract and should include concrete examples of spurious structures the new model resolves.

- General: Presents a novel model that improves overlapping-entity detection over MH on benchmarks but insufficiently explains the mechanism and reasons for its superior performance.
