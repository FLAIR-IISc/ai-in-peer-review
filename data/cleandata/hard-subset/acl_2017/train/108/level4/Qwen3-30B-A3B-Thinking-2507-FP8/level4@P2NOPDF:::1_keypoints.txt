- Well-written, clearly structured paper with minor issues.  
- Detecting overlapping entities is a relevant, well-motivated problem.  
- Proposed multigraph model extends the MH framework and shows robust empirical improvements.  
- Section 3 fails to clearly state MH’s specific limitations that motivate the new model.  
- Section 4 does not justify the introduction of separators or explain what they add over T/I/O representation.  
- Section 5.1 reports superior metrics but does not explain why the new model outperforms MH.  
- Section 5.2 is overly abstract and lacks concrete examples of spurious structures the new model resolves.  
- Paper achieves state-of-the-art results but lacks mechanistic clarity linking design choices to performance gains; needs illustrative examples and stronger explanation.
