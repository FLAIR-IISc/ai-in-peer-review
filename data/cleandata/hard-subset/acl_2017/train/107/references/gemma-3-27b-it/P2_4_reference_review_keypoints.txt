- Overall: Solid, well-executed study combining annotation and representation projection with practical improvements; recommend accept with minor revisions.
- Contribution: Heuristic data selection for annotation projection that improves projection-labeled data quality under noisy alignment.
- Contribution: Representation projection via pre-trained embeddings and linear mapping to transfer English NER to other languages.
- Contribution: Co-decoding schemes (notably rank-based) to combine annotation- and representation-projection systems for better performance.
- Strength: Practical focus addressing noisy projection-labeled data with extensive experiments on in-house and CoNLL datasets.
- Strength: Clear methodology and implementation details (CRFs, MEMMs, neural nets, feature engineering) that aid reproducibility.
- Strength: Consistent empirical improvements, often outperforming prior state-of-the-art on CoNLL, especially for languages with weak alignments.
- Strength: Well-structured and readable presentation.
- Weakness: Limited novelty—primarily optimization and engineering of existing techniques rather than new concepts.
- Weakness: Heavy dependence on external resources (pre-trained embeddings, translation dictionaries) without thorough analysis of their impact or biases.
- Weakness: Insufficient comparison/discussion relative to fully supervised baselines and reasons for remaining performance gap.
- Weakness: Rank-based co-decoding prioritization of annotation projection feels arbitrary and lacks theoretical justification or ablation on weighting schemes.
- Question: Please discuss potential biases from pre-trained embeddings and translation dictionaries and their effects on low-resource languages.
- Question: Clarify how alignment accuracy relates to the relative effectiveness of annotation vs. representation projection approaches.
- Question: Were attempts made to dynamically adjust co-decoding prioritization per sentence or language, and is that computationally feasible?
- Additional comment: Include error analysis to identify common failure modes and guide future work.
- Additional comment: Add a table summarizing dataset characteristics (size, entity types, domain).
