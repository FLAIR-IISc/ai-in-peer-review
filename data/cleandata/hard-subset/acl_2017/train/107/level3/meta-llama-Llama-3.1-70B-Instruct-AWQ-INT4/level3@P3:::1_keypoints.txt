- Proposes two weakly supervised cross-lingual NER approaches: annotation projection with heuristic data selection and representation projection via embedding mapping, plus two co-decoding schemes.

- Contribution: heuristic data selection scheme to pick high-quality projection-labeled NER data from noisy comparable corpora.

- Contribution: representation projection approach that maps target-language embeddings to a source language for direct NER model transfer.

- Contribution: two co-decoding schemes to combine outputs of the projection-based systems.

- Strength: novel data selection method effectively filters noisy projected labels, improving training data quality.

- Strength: representation projection is an innovative transfer mechanism and achieves competitive performance.

- Strength: co-decoding schemes are well-designed and improve combined-system performance.

- Weakness: overall approach is incremental; paper lacks deeper discussion of how it addresses prior methods’ limitations.

- Weakness: quality-score thresholding (Table 2) is unclear—uncertain whether thresholds were tuned on a dev set, hurting reproducibility.

- Weakness: insufficient information about the in-house dataset content/types, limiting assessment of generalizability.

- Weakness: description of co-decoding schemes is unclear and hard to follow; needs more implementation detail.

- Weakness: many method variants presented without clarity on whether combining more than two weakly supervised systems was tried or useful.

- Question: how were the thresholds for Table 2 determined and tuned?

- Question: what are the content and types of texts in the in-house dataset?

- Question: can the authors provide more implementation details of the co-decoding schemes?

- Question: have the authors tried combining more than two weakly supervised systems, and what were the results?

- Question: how do the authors plan to extend and evaluate the approaches on other languages and datasets?

- Additional comment: paper is well-written with clear results, but some figures/tables need improved clarity; overall a meaningful contribution with adoption potential.
