- Paper proposes weakly supervised NER via English projection, but the overall approach is not novel and the methods are incremental improvements.  
- Data selection contribution: quality-score formula is straightforward, but threshold determination for Table 2 is unclear.  
- No explanation whether thresholds were tuned on a development set, making reproducibility unclear.  
- Data selection is shown to be important, but guidance for tuning parameters for new datasets or language pairs is lacking.  
- Combination of outputs from the two systems is claimed as a contribution, but the description is unclear and hard to follow.  
- Many method variants are presented; it’s unclear whether combining more than two weakly supervised systems was tried or makes sense.  
- More information is needed about the content/types of texts in the "in-house" dataset.
