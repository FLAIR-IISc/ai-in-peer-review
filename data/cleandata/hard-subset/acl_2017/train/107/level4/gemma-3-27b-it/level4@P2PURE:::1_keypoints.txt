- Approach uses cross-lingual projection for weakly supervised NER but is largely incremental and lacks the novelty expected for ACL.
- Main contribution is a simple data-selection quality score, yet the threshold-setting methodology (Table 2) is opaque.
- The paper does not state whether thresholds were tuned on a development set.
- Evaluation shows data selection has a large impact but gives little guidance on tuning parameters for new datasets or language pairs.
- The proposed combination of the two systems is a contribution, but its description is unclear.
- It is unclear whether the authors tried or would benefit from combining more than two weakly supervised systems.
- The “in-house” dataset lacks sufficient descriptive detail to interpret the reported results.
