- Paper presents cross-lingual NER using CRF, MEMM, neural methods, two ensemble combination methods (probability- and ranking-based), a training-instance selection scheme, and a Mikolov-variant projection; overall clear and well-written with interesting combined-annotation results.  
- Missing motivation, algorithmic detail, and parameter settings for the proposed CBOW variation; include results for original CBOW and Skip-gram for comparison.  
- Recommend considering exponential decay for surrounding embeddings (cite Iacobacci et al. 2016) given the use of a decay factor.  
- Need a more detailed empirical comparison between original Mikolov cross-lingual projections and the paper’s frequency-weighted projections to demonstrate clear superiority.  
- Unclear whether improvements from the data selection scheme are statistically significant; report statistical significance tests.  
- Structural/flow suggestions: move Section 4.4 text to the start of Section 4.2 and relocate Table 2’s evaluation to the evaluation section.  
- Paper lacks a dedicated related work section; split the introduction into background/motivation and related work.  
- Evaluation section is too brief given state-of-the-art claims; provide deeper discussion and analysis of results.  
- Suggested reference to Iacobacci et al. (2016) for embedding decay strategies.
