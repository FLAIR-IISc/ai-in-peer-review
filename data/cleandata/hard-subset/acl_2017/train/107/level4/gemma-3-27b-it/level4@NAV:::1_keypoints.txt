- Limited novelty: overall strategy (cross-lingual projection for weakly supervised NER) is incremental and not sufficiently innovative for ACL.
- Positive contribution: proposed data selection scheme with a simple, direct quality-scoring formula.
- Missing detail: threshold determination in Table 2 is under-specified; unclear whether a development set was used for tuning.
- Limited practical guidance: results show importance of data selection but offer little advice on parameter tuning for new datasets or language pairs.
- Unclear system combination: explanation of how the two systems are combined is insufficient and needs elaboration.
- Exploration scope: paper examines many variants per method but does not address combining more than two weakly supervised systems.
- Dataset transparency: “in-house” dataset lacks characterization (text types, domains), limiting understanding of evaluation scope.
