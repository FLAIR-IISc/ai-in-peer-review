- Methods are incremental improvements on prior work and lack strong novelty for an ACL paper.
- Data selection scheme is a key contribution; the quality score formula is straightforward and commendable.
- The procedure for choosing thresholds in Table 2 is unclear, and it’s unspecified whether a development set was used.
- Evaluation shows data selection is important, but the paper gives no guidance on tuning parameters for new datasets or language pairs.
- The process for combining outputs from the two systems is unclear and needs further clarification.
- The paper tests many variants, but it does not report whether ensembles of more than two weakly supervised systems were considered or their potential trade-offs.
- The “in-house” dataset is insufficiently described; more detail on text types is needed to judge generalizability.
