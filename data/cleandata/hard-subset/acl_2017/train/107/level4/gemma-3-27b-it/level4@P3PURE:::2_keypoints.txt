- Paper proposes a cross-lingual NER system combining CRF, MEMM, and neural NER methods, plus probability- and ranking-based output fusion and a training-instance selection method.  
- Cross-lingual projection is performed using a variant of Mikolov’s embedding projection approach.  
- Paper is easy to follow, well-structured, clearly written, and combined-annotation results are particularly interesting.  
- Reviewer questions the motivation for the proposed CBOW variation and notes missing details and parameters.  
- Request to report results with the original CBOW and/or skip-gram models for direct comparison.  
- Suggests considering an exponential decay for surrounding embeddings (cites Iacobacci et al., 2016).  
- Requests a direct comparison between the frequency-weighted projections and Mikolov’s original cross-lingual projection to demonstrate superiority.  
- Asks for statistical significance testing to support claims that the proposed data selection scheme yields significant improvements.  
- Recommends moving Section 4.4 to the start of Section 4.2 and relocating Table 2’s evaluation to the main evaluation section for better flow.  
- Notes the absence of a dedicated related work section and recommends splitting the introduction into Introduction and Related Work.  
- Evaluation section is brief (1.5 pages); reviewer requests a more detailed discussion and analysis of results.  
- Provides a suggested reference: Iacobacci, Pilehvar, & Navigli (2016).
