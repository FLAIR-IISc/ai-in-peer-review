- Core approach is incremental: cross-lingual transfer from English is established and the paper’s novelty is limited for ACL-level contribution.
- Data selection scheme is a key contribution; the quality-scoring formula is simple and transparent.
- Threshold selection (Table 2) is opaque—paper omits whether a development set was used, harming reproducibility.
- No guidance on how to adapt/tune parameters for new datasets or language pairs, limiting practical applicability.
- Combination of the two NER systems is insufficiently described; the integration mechanism needs clearer elaboration.
- Many variants are evaluated, but it’s unclear whether combining more than two weakly supervised systems yields diminishing returns; results for larger combinations are missing.
- The “in-house” dataset is poorly described; details on its composition and sources are needed to judge generalizability.
