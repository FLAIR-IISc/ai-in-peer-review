- Methods rely on projection from English and are not novel; expected more substantial innovation for a competitive conference paper.
- Primary contribution is a heuristic data selection scheme for annotation projection.
- Quality score formula is straightforward and positive.
- Method for selecting thresholds (q and n) is under-detailed; unclear how thresholds were chosen.
- Unclear whether threshold tuning was done on a development set or how to tune for new datasets/language pairs.
- Exploration of co-decoding schemes is a secondary contribution, but descriptions are insufficiently clear.
- Many variants of each method are evaluated, but no clarity on whether combining more than two weakly supervised systems was considered.
- Lack of information about the composition of the “in-house” dataset limits assessment of result generalizability.
