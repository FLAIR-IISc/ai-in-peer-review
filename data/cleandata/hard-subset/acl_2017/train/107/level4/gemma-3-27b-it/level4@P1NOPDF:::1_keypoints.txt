- Presents weakly supervised NER methods using projection from English; approaches are incremental improvements rather than highly novel for ACL.
- Data selection scheme is a key contribution; the quality score formula is simple and effective.
- Unclear how thresholds in Table 2 were chosen—no indication whether selection was performed on a development set.
- Evaluation shows data selection matters, but the paper gives insufficient guidance on tuning thresholds/parameters for new datasets or language pairs.
- Combining outputs from the two systems is another contribution, but the description of this process is confusing and needs clarification.
- Many method variants are explored; the paper does not address whether combining more than two weakly supervised systems was investigated.
- Experiments lack detail about the “in-house” dataset—no description of text genres or types.
- Overall, the work is useful but not sufficiently groundbreaking for an ACL publication.
