- Proposes weakly supervised cross-lingual NER projection methods that are incremental improvements but not sufficiently novel for ACL.
- Data selection scheme uses a simple quality-score formula, but the threshold-setting method in Table 2 is unclear.
- Unclear whether thresholds were chosen on a development set, raising concerns about robustness.
- No guidance on how to tune parameters for new datasets or language pairs.
- Description of the process for combining outputs from the two systems is unclear and hard to reproduce.
- Paper presents multiple variants but does not report whether combining more than two weakly supervised systems was explored.
- Evaluation shows data selection is important, but lack of tuning details limits practical applicability.
- More information is needed about the types of texts in the "in-house" dataset to assess domain relevance.
