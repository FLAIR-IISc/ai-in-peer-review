- Approach improves existing English→target-language weakly supervised NER methods but lacks strong novelty for an ACL paper.  
- Data selection scheme uses a simple quality-score formula, but threshold choices (Table 2) are unexplained.  
- Unclear whether thresholds were tuned on a development set, making parameter tuning for new languages/datasets unclear.  
- Evaluation clearly shows data selection matters, but methodological clarity on selection is missing.  
- Description of the system-output combination is unclear and needs a more detailed, readable explanation.  
- Paper presents multiple method variants but does not report whether combining more than two weakly supervised systems was explored or beneficial.  
- The "in-house" dataset is insufficiently described—types of texts and domain context are not provided.
