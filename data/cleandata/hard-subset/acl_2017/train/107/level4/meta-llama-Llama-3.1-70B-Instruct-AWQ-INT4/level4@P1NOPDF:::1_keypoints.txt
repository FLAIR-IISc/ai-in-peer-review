- Overall approach (projecting English to other languages for weakly supervised NER) is not novel; methods offer incremental improvements but not strong ACL-level innovation.
- Proposed data selection scheme uses a straightforward quality-score formula and the experiments show data selection matters.
- Determination of thresholds in Table 2 is unclear; authors only say "different thresholds were tried" with no procedure described.
- Unclear whether thresholds/parameters were tuned on a development set and no guidance on tuning for new datasets or language pairs.
- Description of how the two systems' outputs are combined is unclear and hard to follow.
- Paper presents multiple method variants but does not clarify whether combining more than two weakly supervised systems was tried or what the outcomes were.
- Insufficient information about the "in-house" dataset (types of texts) — more dataset context is needed to interpret results.
