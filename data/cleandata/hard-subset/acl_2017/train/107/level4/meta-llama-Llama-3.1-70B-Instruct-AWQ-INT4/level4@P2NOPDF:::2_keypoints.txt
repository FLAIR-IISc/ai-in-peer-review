- Proposes a cross-lingual NER model combining CRF, Maximum Entropy Markov, and neural NER methods with two output-combination strategies (probability- and ranking-based) and a training-instance selection method from comparable corpora.  
- Uses a variant of Mikolov’s cross-lingual projection (frequency-weighted) for projection.  
- Paper is well-structured, easy to follow, and written in correct English; combined-annotation results are interesting and promising.  
- Motivation for the proposed CBOW variation is unclear; authors should detail the choice and report CBOW parameters and results.  
- Suggest exploring exponential decay (Iacobacci et al. 2016) as an alternative to the current decay factor for surrounding embeddings.  
- Recommend comparing original Mikolov cross-lingual projections with the proposed frequency-weighted projections to demonstrate advantages.  
- Claim of significant improvement from the data selection scheme should be supported by statistical significance testing.  
- Structural edits suggested: integrate Section 4.4 into the start of Section 4.2 and move Table 2 evaluation to the evaluation section.  
- A separate related work section is missing; the introduction should be split to include related work for clearer context.  
- The evaluation section is short and should be expanded with deeper discussion and analysis, particularly given the state-of-the-art results.  
- Suggested reference to consider: Iacobacci et al. (2016) on embeddings and exponential decay.
