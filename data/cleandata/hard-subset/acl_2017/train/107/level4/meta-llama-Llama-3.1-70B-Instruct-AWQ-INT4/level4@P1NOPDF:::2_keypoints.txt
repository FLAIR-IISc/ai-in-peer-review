- Paper proposes a cross-lingual NER model combining CRF, MEMM, and neural approaches with probability- and ranking-based output combination methods.  
- Introduces a method for selecting best training instances from cross-lingual comparable corpora and a variant of Mikolov's cross-lingual projection.  
- Overall manuscript is well-structured, easy to follow, and written in good English; combined annotation results are notable.  
- Insufficient motivation and detail for the proposed CBOW variation; authors should report CBOW/skip-gram baselines to justify the change.  
- Use of a decay factor for surrounding embeddings should be compared to exponential decay (as in Iacobacci et al. 2016) for clarity.  
- Need a direct comparison between original Mikolov projections and the proposed frequency-weighted projections to demonstrate superiority.  
- Unclear whether reported improvements are statistically significant; request for statistical significance testing.  
- Structural edit suggestion: move Section 4.4 to the start of Section 4.2 and relocate Table 2 evaluation to the evaluation section.  
- Missing a dedicated related work section; recommend splitting the introduction into background and related work sections.  
- Evaluation section is brief and needs more in-depth discussion and analysis despite achieving state-of-the-art results.  
- Suggested reference: Iacobacci et al. (2016) for embeddings and decay comparison context.
