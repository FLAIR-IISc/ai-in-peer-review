- Presents weakly supervised NER methods projecting from English; methods improve existing techniques but lack strong novelty for an ACL paper.
- Proposes a simple data-selection quality score, but the threshold-setting procedure (Table 2) is unclear and may not indicate whether a dev set was used.
- Lack of clear guidance on how to tune thresholds for new datasets or language pairs despite data selection’s importance.
- Combination of the two system outputs is described unclearly and is hard to follow; needs clearer explanation.
- Many variants are evaluated, raising the question of whether combining more than two weakly supervised systems was tried and what the results were.
- Insufficient detail about the contents and text types in the “in-house” dataset, limiting understanding of context and method limitations.
