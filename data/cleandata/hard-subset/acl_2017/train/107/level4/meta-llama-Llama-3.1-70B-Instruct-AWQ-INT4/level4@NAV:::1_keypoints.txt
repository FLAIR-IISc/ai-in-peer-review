- Approach uses multiple weakly supervised, English-to-other-language projection methods for NER but is largely incremental and not sufficiently novel for an ACL paper.
- Main contribution is a simple data-selection quality score, but how the thresholds in Table 2 were chosen is unclear (no mention of a development set).
- Evaluation shows data selection matters, yet the paper gives no guidance on tuning parameters for new datasets or language pairs.
- Description of how the outputs of the two systems are combined is unclear and needs clarification.
- Authors present many method variants; it’s unclear whether combining more than two weakly supervised systems was tried or beneficial.
- The paper lacks details about the composition and text types in the "in-house" dataset.
