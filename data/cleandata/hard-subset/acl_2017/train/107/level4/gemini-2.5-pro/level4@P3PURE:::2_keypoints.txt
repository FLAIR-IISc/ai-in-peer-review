- Introduces a cross-lingual NER model combining CRF, MEMM, and neural methods with two novel combination techniques, a data selection scheme, and a frequency-weighted embedding projection variant.
- Paper is well-structured, clearly written, and the combined-system results are particularly interesting.
- Insufficient motivation and detail for the proposed CBOW variant; parameters and comparisons to original CBOW/skip-gram are missing—include baseline results and consider exponential decay.
- No direct empirical comparison between Mikolov et al.'s original cross-lingual projection and the proposed frequency-weighted projection.
- Effectiveness claims for the data selection scheme lack reported statistical significance tests.
- Structural issues: content of Section 4.4 should be moved to the beginning of Section 4.2, and analysis of Table 2 should be relocated from model description to evaluation.
- Missing a dedicated Related Work section to better contextualize contributions.
- Evaluation section is too short and needs more extensive discussion and in-depth analysis given the state-of-the-art claims.
- Suggested reference to consider: Iacobacci et al. (2016) on embeddings and exponential decay approaches.
