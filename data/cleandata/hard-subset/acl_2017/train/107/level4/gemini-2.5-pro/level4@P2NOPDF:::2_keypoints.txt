- Presents cross-lingual NER using an ensemble of CRF, MEMM, and neural models with two novel ensemble methods, a data selection strategy, and a modified cross-lingual projection technique.  
- Paper is generally well-structured and clearly written, with promising results.  
- Motivation and technical details for the proposed CBOW variant are insufficient; architecture, hyperparameters, and an ablation/comparison with original CBOW and skip-gram are missing.  
- Recommend citing/comparing the context decay factor to the exponential decay used in Iacobacci et al. (2016).  
- The frequency-weighted projection method is not empirically compared against the original Mikolov et al. projection.  
- Claims of “significant” improvements lack reported statistical significance testing.  
- Paper organization could be improved (move Section 4.4 earlier; relocate Table 2 discussion to main evaluation).  
- Needs a dedicated Related Work section for better literature context and positioning.  
- Evaluation is too brief; expand discussion, provide error analysis, and deeper investigation of component/ensemble behavior.  
- Suggested reference: Iacobacci et al. (2016).
