- Methods are incremental extensions of an established English-to-target-language projection framework; novelty may be insufficient for a premier venue.  
- Data-selection quality scoring is simple and positive, but threshold selection in Table 2 is ambiguous.  
- It is unclear whether a development set was used for tuning thresholds, which hinders applicability to new datasets/languages.  
- The procedure for combining the two system outputs is described unclearly and is difficult to follow.  
- The paper does not discuss ensembling more than two weakly supervised variants, an obvious missing experiment.  
- The "in-house" dataset lacks crucial reproducibility details (domain and text types).
