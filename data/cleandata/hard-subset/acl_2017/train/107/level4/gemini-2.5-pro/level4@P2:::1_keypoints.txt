- Task: weakly supervised cross-lingual NER to build target-language NER without annotated target data.  
- Methods: Annotation Projection transfers English NER tags to aligned target text.  
- Methods: Representation Projection maps target embeddings into English space to apply an English NER model.  
- Combination: Co-decoding schemes combine the two systems’ outputs for improved performance.  
- Contribution: Combining the two approaches yields state-of-the-art results on CoNLL benchmarks.  
- Contribution: Heuristic data selection for annotation projection effectively filters noisy projected training data.  
- Contribution: Thorough implementation and evaluation of representation projection for NER and its comparison with annotation projection.
