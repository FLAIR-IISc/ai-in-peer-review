- Paper presents weakly supervised NER via projecting English annotations; methods are incremental and may lack sufficient novelty for top-tier venues.  
- Data-selection quality score is simple and sensible, but the paper omits how selection thresholds (Table 2) were chosen or whether tuned on a dev set, hurting reproducibility.  
- Evaluation shows data selection is important, yet no guidance is given on tuning parameters for new datasets or languages.  
- The proposed method for combining the two systems is insufficiently described and unclear to readers.  
- It is unclear whether combining more than two weakly supervised systems was tried or could yield further gains.  
- The paper lacks details about the in-house dataset (domain/genre), which limits contextual interpretation of results.
