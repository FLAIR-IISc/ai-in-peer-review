- Approach is largely incremental and not novel enough for a premier venue like ACL.
- Data selection scheme (quality-score formula) is a key contribution but methodology lacks crucial details.
- Unclear how filtering thresholds (Table 2) were chosen; no mention of a development set for tuning, harming reproducibility.
