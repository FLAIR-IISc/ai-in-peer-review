- Contribution: proposes weakly supervised NER methods via English-to-target projection, but overall approach lacks sufficient novelty for ACL.
- Data-selection scheme: quality score formula is simple and positive, yet its application is poorly explained.
- Threshold tuning: paper doesn’t specify how thresholds in Table 2 were chosen or whether a development set was used, harming reproducibility.
- Empirical result: evaluation shows data selection is important, but guidance for tuning on new datasets/languages is missing.
- Combination strategy: description of how the two system outputs are combined is unclear and hard to understand from the text.
- System variants: many method variants are presented, but authors don’t explore whether combining more than two weakly supervised systems would help.
- Dataset details: more information about the in-house dataset (domain/genre) is needed to contextualize results.
