- Presents weakly supervised English→other-language NER projection methods but is largely incremental and may lack ACL-level novelty.
- Proposes a straightforward data selection quality score as a key contribution.
- Selection thresholds for the data selection scheme are unspecified (only “different thresholds were tried”), with no clarity on whether a development set was used.
- Lack of threshold-tuning details harms reproducibility and applicability to new datasets/language pairs.
- Introduces a method to combine outputs of two systems, but the combination strategy is described unclearly.
- Authors present many method variants; it’s unclear if combining more than two systems was explored.
- The in-house dataset is insufficiently described (domain and text types), weakening interpretability of results.
