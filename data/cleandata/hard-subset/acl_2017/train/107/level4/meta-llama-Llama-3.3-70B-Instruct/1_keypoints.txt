- Proposes multiple weakly supervised English-to-other-language NER projection methods; overall framework not novel and methods are incremental, possibly insufficient for a top-tier venue.
- Data selection method is notable; quality score formula is simple and a strength.
- Threshold choices reported in Table 2 are unclear and lack detail on tuning procedures (e.g., whether a development set was used), hurting reproducibility.
- Unclear how to optimize thresholds for new datasets or language pairs.
- Combining outputs of the two systems is a key contribution but the explanation of the combination method is obscure and hard to follow.
- Paper tests several variants but does not clarify whether combining more than two weakly supervised systems was explored, leaving scalability and robustness questions.
- Insufficient information about the "in-house" dataset composition and text types, limiting assessment of applicability.
