- Approach relies on projecting English to target languages for weakly supervised NERs; methods are incremental and not sufficiently novel for ACL-level contribution.
- Data selection scheme is a useful contribution and the quality-score formula is simple, but the threshold selection procedure (Table 2) is unclear.
- Paper does not state whether thresholds were chosen using a development set, leaving practitioners unsure how to tune parameters for new datasets or language pairs.
- Combination of the two system outputs is proposed but the description of how they are combined is insufficiently clear.
- Unclear whether authors experimented with combining more than two weakly supervised systems or the potential benefits of larger combinations.
- The paper should provide more details about the types of texts included in the "in-house" dataset to improve clarity and completeness.
