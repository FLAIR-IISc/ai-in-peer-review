- Paper presents weakly supervised NER methods using projection from English but mainly offers incremental improvements over prior work rather than novel methodology.  
- Reviewer expected greater methodological innovation for an ACL submission.  
- Data selection scheme is a key contribution; quality score formula is simple and acceptable.  
- Paper fails to specify how thresholds in Table 2 were chosen (e.g., whether tuned on a development set), harming reproducibility.  
- Evaluation shows data selection is critical, but the procedure for parameter tuning on new datasets or language pairs is unclear.  
- Combination of outputs from the two proposed systems is presented but the integration mechanism is insufficiently described.  
- The paper introduces multiple variants per method but does not explore or justify combining more than two weakly supervised systems or related extensions.  
- A brief characterization of the text types in the in-house dataset is missing and would strengthen evaluation context.
