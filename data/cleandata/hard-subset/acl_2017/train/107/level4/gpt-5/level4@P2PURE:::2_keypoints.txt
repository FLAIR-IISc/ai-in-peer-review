- Paper proposes a cross-lingual NER approach using CRFs, MEMMs, neural methods, two ensembling strategies (probability- and ranking-based), a data selection method for comparable corpora, and a frequency-weighted variant of Mikolov-style cross-lingual projection.  
- Paper is clear, well structured, written in solid English, and reports interesting combined-annotation results.

- Show empirical comparisons between Mikolov’s original cross-lingual projections and the proposed frequency-weighted projection to demonstrate improvement.  
- Report statistical significance testing for claims that the data selection scheme yields significant improvements.
