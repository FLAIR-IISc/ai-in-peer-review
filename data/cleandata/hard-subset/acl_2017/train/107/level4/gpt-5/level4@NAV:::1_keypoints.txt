- Paper proposes weakly supervised NER via projection from English, but the overall paradigm is not new and techniques are mainly refinements, so novelty for ACL is limited.
- Data selection procedure is a key contribution; the quality-score formula is simple and acceptable, but selection thresholds (Table 2) are unexplained.
- It is unclear whether threshold tuning was performed on a development set and how to set parameters for new datasets or language pairs.
- Results show data selection substantially affects performance, but practical guidance on choosing thresholds is missing.
- The method for combining outputs of the two systems is another contribution, but its description is insufficiently clear.
- The paper explores several variants per method; it should address whether combining more than two weakly supervised systems was attempted.
- The manuscript lacks detail about the content and text types in the “in-house” dataset.
