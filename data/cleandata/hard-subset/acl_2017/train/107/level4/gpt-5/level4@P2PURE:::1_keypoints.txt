- Claims of weakly supervised NER via English projection are incremental; paradigm not novel and lacks substantive ACL-level novelty.
- Techniques are solid but appear as small improvements over prior work.
- Data selection contribution: quality-score formula is simple, but thresholds in Table 2 lack justification or explanation.
- Unclear whether thresholds were tuned on a development set and no guidance on tuning for new datasets/language pairs.
- Combination of outputs from the two systems is described insufficiently and hard to understand.
- Paper presents several method variants but does not explore combining more than two weakly supervised systems.
- Missing details about the composition and text types in the “in-house” dataset.
