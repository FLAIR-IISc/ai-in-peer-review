- Overall approach (English-to-target projection for weakly supervised NER) lacks substantial novelty for an ACL paper.
- Data selection scheme: scoring formula is straightforward, but how thresholds in Table 2 were chosen is unclear.
- No mention whether threshold tuning was done on a development set; tuning procedure is unspecified.
- Results show data selection is important, yet it’s unclear how to tune parameters for a new dataset or language pair.
- Combination of the two system outputs is described unclearly and hard to understand.
- Many method variants are proposed; it’s unclear whether combining more than two weakly supervised systems was attempted.
- More information is needed about the types of texts in the “in-house” dataset.
