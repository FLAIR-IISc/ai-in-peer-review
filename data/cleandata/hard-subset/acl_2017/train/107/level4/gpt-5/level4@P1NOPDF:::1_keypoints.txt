- Overall approach is incremental and lacks substantial novelty for an ACL paper.  
- Data selection scheme: quality-score formula is simple, but the threshold-setting procedure (Table 2) is unclear—were thresholds tuned on a dev set?  
- Evaluation indicates data selection is crucial, yet it’s unclear how to tune these parameters for a new dataset or language pair.  
- Combination of outputs from the two systems is described unclearly and appears non-reproducible—need a precise, explicit procedure.  
- Many method variants are introduced; did the authors try combining more than two weakly supervised systems?  
- “In-house” dataset is insufficiently described—provide domains and text types.
