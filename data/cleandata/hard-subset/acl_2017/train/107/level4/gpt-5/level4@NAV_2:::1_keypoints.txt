- Methods rely on English-to-target projection and appear as incremental enhancements, lacking substantial methodological novelty for a top-tier venue.

- Data selection scoring is simple but threshold determination (Table 2) is unspecified; unclear whether thresholds were tuned on a development set.

- Results show data selection is crucial, yet the paper does not explain how to tune parameters for new datasets or language pairs.

- Description of the procedure for combining outputs from the two systems is insufficiently clear and hard to reproduce.

- The paper presents multiple variants but does not report experiments on combining more than two weakly supervised systems.

- The “in-house” dataset lacks detail about the text domains it contains.
