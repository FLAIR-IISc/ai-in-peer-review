- Proposes a straightforward rule-based method for temporal expression recognition; useful as a preprocessing/analysis tool.  
- Reports experimental results that outperform state-of-the-art rule-based systems and a machine-learning baseline.  
- Concern that rules were designed using the same corpora as evaluation, raising risk of overfitting to those datasets/domains.  
- Paper lacks statistics or discussion on overlap of temporal expression types across the evaluated corpora.  
- Showing substantial overlap would support the claim that rules capture general patterns rather than corpus-specific idiosyncrasies.  
- Recommendation: evaluate on a held-out corpus from a different domain to demonstrate robustness and generalizability.
