- Presents a straightforward rule-based method for recognizing time expressions, useful as an analysis tool.
- Experimental results claim the method outperforms state-of-the-art rule-based approaches and a machine-learning baseline.
- Work is strong overall but there are concerns about the method’s generality.
- Rules were crafted from the same corpora used for evaluation, raising the risk of overfitting.
- The domains of the evaluated corpora may have biased the rule design.
- No statistical analysis or discussion of overlap in time expressions across the corpora is provided.
- Recommend evaluating on a new, independent corpus to demonstrate broader effectiveness.
