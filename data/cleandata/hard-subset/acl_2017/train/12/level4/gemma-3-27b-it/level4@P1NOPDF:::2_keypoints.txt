- Proposes a simple rule-based method for time expression recognition.  
- Simplicity is a strength, making the method a useful analytical/fundamental tool.  
- Experiments show the method outperforms state-of-the-art rule-based and a machine-learning baseline.  
- Major concern about generality: rules were developed from the same corpora used for evaluation, risking overfitting.  
- The domains represented in the corpora may have biased rule design.  
- Paper lacks statistical analysis/discussion of overlap of time expressions across corpora to support generality.  
- Recommended evaluation on a novel, independent corpus to demonstrate broader effectiveness.
