- Proposes a simple rule-based method for time expression recognition.
- Simplicity is a strength, making it useful for downstream applications needing accurate time identification.
- Experimental results are competitive, outperforming existing rule-based systems and one ML-based method.
- Major concern: rules were developed from the same corpora used for evaluation, raising overfitting/generalizability issues.
- The rule design was likely influenced by the domains present in the evaluation corpora.
- The paper lacks statistical analysis or discussion of overlap of time expressions across the corpora, which could support generality.
- Recommended additional evaluation on a held-out, independent corpus to better demonstrate robustness and generalizability.
