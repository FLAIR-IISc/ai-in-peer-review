- Introduces a rule-based method for time expression recognition from text.
- Method’s main strength is its simplicity, making it useful as an analytical/foundational tool.
- Experimental results show superior performance over existing rule-based and machine-learning approaches.
- Major concern about generalizability because rules were developed with close reference to the evaluation corpora.
- Possible overfitting: corpus-specific characteristics may have influenced rule design.
- Paper would benefit from statistical analysis of overlap in time expressions across the corpora to justify wider applicability.
- Stronger evidence would come from evaluation on a corpus independent of the rule-creation process.
