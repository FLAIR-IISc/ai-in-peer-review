- Simple, rule-based method provides a valuable analytical tool for time expression recognition.  
- Time expression recognition is a fundamental process useful for many applications.  
- Experimental results show the method outperforms state-of-the-art rule-based and a machine-learning-based method.  
- Concern about generality: rules were designed from the evaluation corpora and may be overfitted.  
- Domain characteristics of the corpora may have influenced rule design.  
- Paper lacks statistical analysis or discussion of overlap of time expressions across corpora.  
- Evaluation on a corpus distinct from the one used to inform rule design is needed to demonstrate broad effectiveness.
