- Proposes a rule-based method for time expression recognition.
- Key strength: method's simplicity makes it a useful analytical/foundational tool.
- Experimental claim: outperforms state-of-the-art rule-based systems and a machine-learning approach.
- Major concern: potential lack of generality because rules were developed with direct reference to the evaluation corpora.
- Risk of overfitting since rules appear tailored to those corpora.
- Corpora characteristics likely influenced rule design, raising bias concerns.
- Paper lacks statistical analysis/discussion of overlap of time expressions across corpora.
- Evaluation on an independent corpus is needed to better demonstrate generalizability.
