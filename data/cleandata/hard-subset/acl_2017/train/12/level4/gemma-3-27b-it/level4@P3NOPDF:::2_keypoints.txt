- Presents a rule-based method for time expression recognition from text.
- Strength: simple and interpretable, valuable for downstream applications.
- Experimental results: outperforms state-of-the-art rule-based systems and an ML baseline on accuracy.
- Primary concern: rules may be overfitted because they were developed from the evaluation corpora.
- Domain characteristics of the corpora may have influenced rule design, reducing generality.
- Missing analysis of overlap in time expressions across corpora; such analysis would support claimed generality.
- Recommendation: evaluate on a completely independent corpus not used in rule design to demonstrate generalization.
- Summary: promising and strong-performing approach, but limited by potential overfitting and lack of independent evaluation.
