- Introduces a straightforward rule-based method for recognizing time expressions in text.
- Method is a useful analytical tool since time expression recognition is fundamental for many applications.
- Experimental results claim the method outperforms state-of-the-art rule-based and machine learning approaches.
- Major concern about generalizability because rules were crafted from the same corpora used for evaluation.
- Risk of overfitting and possible bias from the specific domains of the evaluation corpora.
- No statistical analysis or discussion assessing overlap of time expressions across the observed corpora.
- Recommendation to evaluate on a new, distinct corpus to demonstrate broad effectiveness and generalizability.
