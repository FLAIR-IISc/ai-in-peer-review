- Proposes a rule-based method to extract time expressions by detecting time tokens (dictionary/regex/POS), expanding around them with heuristics, and merging segments; shows significant gains on three datasets.  
- Strength: paper is well-written and rules are empirically motivated and justified by evaluation results.  
- Weakness: underspecifications in the paper impede reproducibility.  
- Section 4.1: unclear what "5 seasons" refers to — authors should provide concrete examples (e.g., Ramadan, Holiday Season).  
- Section 5.1: inconsistent description — text says "two benchmark datasets" but evaluation uses three.  
- Section 5.2: should include an example of an expression without a time token to illustrate limits/capabilities.  
- Given near-ceiling performance (93% expressions contain time tokens; system achieves 92% recall), clarify how the system can be further improved.  
- Request to release the full rule set or software to facilitate reproducibility and further development.
