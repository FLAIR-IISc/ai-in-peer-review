- Proposes a novel, straightforward rule-based method for identifying time expressions in text.  
- Method serves well as an analytical tool for a fundamental NLP task.  
- Experimental results claim the method outperforms existing rule-based and machine learning approaches.  
- Concern about generality: rules were developed from the same corpora used for evaluation, risking overfitting.  
- Corpus domains may have biased rule design, limiting broader applicability.  
- Paper lacks statistical analysis or discussion of overlap of time expressions across the corpora.  
- Evaluating on a separate, distinct corpus would strengthen claims of generality and effectiveness.
