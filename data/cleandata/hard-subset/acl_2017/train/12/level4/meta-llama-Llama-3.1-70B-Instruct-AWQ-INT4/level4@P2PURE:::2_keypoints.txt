- Proposes a simple rule-based method for time expression recognition, useful as an analysis tool and a fundamental component for applications.
- Experimental results show the method outperforms state-of-the-art rule-based and machine learning approaches.
- Primary concern: rules may overfit because they were designed using the same corpora used for evaluation.
- Domain bias: the specific domains of the corpora may have influenced rule design, limiting applicability to other domains.
- Paper lacks statistical analysis or discussion of overlap of time expressions across the corpora; significant overlap would support generality.
- Suggests evaluating on a new, distinct corpus not used in rule design to better demonstrate broader effectiveness.
