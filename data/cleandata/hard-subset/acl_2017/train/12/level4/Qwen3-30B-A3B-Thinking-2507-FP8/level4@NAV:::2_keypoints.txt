- Introduces a simple rule-based approach for time expression recognition, useful as a foundational component for time-related applications.  
- Reported experimental results claim superior performance over state-of-the-art rule-based and machine-learning methods.  
- Major concern about generality: rules were developed from the same corpora used for evaluation, raising overfitting risk.  
- Domain-specific characteristics of the evaluation corpora likely influenced rule formulation.  
- Paper lacks statistical analysis or discussion of temporal expression overlap across corpora, which would inform generalizability.  
- Recommendation: validate on an independent corpus separate from the rule-development data to demonstrate broader applicability.
