- Proposes a simple rule-based method for recognizing time expressions, useful as an analysis tool.  
- Claims strong experimental performance, outperforming state-of-the-art rule-based and machine learning methods.  
- Concern about generality: rules were developed from the same corpora used for evaluation, risking overfitting.  
- Domain contents of the corpora may have biased rule design.  
- Paper lacks statistical analysis or discussion of overlaps in time expressions across the observed corpora.  
- Demonstrating substantial overlaps would have supported the rules' generality.  
- Experiments should include a new, independent corpus distinct from the rule-design process to validate broader effectiveness.
