- Proposes a rule-based method for time expression recognition, useful as a straightforward analysis/preprocessing tool.  
- Experimental results claim superior performance over state-of-the-art rule-based and machine learning methods.  
- Major concern: rules were derived from the same corpora used for evaluation, creating a risk of overfitting.  
- The domains of the evaluation corpora may have influenced rule design, limiting generality.  
- Paper lacks statistical analysis or discussion of overlap of time expressions across evaluation corpora.  
- If substantial overlap exists, it would bolster generalizability claims; absence of such analysis weakens them.  
- Recommended fix: evaluate on a distinct corpus not used in rule design to rigorously demonstrate broad applicability.
