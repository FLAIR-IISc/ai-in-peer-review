- Presents a straightforward rule-based method for identifying time expressions, useful across various applications.  
- Experimental results claim the method outperforms existing rule-based and machine-learning approaches.  
- Major concern about generality: rules were derived from the same corpora used for evaluation, risking overfitting.  
- The specific domains of the corpora may have biased rule design, limiting applicability to other domains.  
- Lacks statistical analysis or discussion of overlap in time expressions across the corpora, which could support rule generality.  
- Recommends evaluation on a novel corpus not used in rule design to demonstrate broader effectiveness.
