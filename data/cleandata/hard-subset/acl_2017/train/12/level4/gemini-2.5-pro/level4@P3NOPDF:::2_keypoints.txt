- Presents a rule-based, interpretable system for temporal expression recognition.
- Method is simple and straightforward to implement, aiding foundational NLP tasks.
- Strong empirical performance: outperforms other rule-based and ML methods on chosen corpora.
- Major concern: rules were developed from the same corpora used for evaluation, risking overfitting.
- Corpus-specific overfitting: rules may be tuned to the distribution and style of the evaluation data.
- Domain-specific bias: unclear transferability to other domains (social media, legal, scientific, etc.).
- Paper lacks statistical analysis of temporal expression overlap across corpora to support generality.
- Recommended evaluation on a held-out corpus not used in rule design to demonstrate robustness.
- Conclusion: promising, interpretable system but claims of broad effectiveness are weakened by evaluation methodology.
