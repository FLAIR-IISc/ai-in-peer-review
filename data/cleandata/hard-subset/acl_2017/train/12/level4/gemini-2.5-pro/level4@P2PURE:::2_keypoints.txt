- Proposes a simple rule-based method for temporal expression recognition; simplicity is a notable advantage for a foundational NLP task.  
- Experimental results claim the system outperforms state-of-the-art rule-based and machine-learning methods.  
- Major concern: rules were crafted from the same corpora used for evaluation, raising risk of overfitting to dataset-specific characteristics.  
- Paper lacks statistical analysis or discussion of overlap in temporal expressions across development corpora to support claims of generality.  
- Recommendation: evaluate on a held-out, completely unseen corpus to convincingly demonstrate broad generalizability.
