- Proposes a simple rule-based method for temporal expression recognition, useful as an analysis tool.  
- Experimental results claim the method outperforms state-of-the-art rule-based and machine learning systems.  
- Main concern: rules were designed from the same corpora used for evaluation, raising overfitting and domain-bias risks.  
- Paper lacks statistics/discussion on overlap of temporal expressions across the corpora to justify rule generality.  
- Stronger evidence would require evaluation on an independent corpus not used during rule design.
