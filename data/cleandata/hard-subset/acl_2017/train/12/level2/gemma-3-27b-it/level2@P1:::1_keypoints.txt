- Proposes SynTime, a type-based, heuristic system for time expression recognition grounded in dataset analysis.

- Contribution: Analyzes time expressions across four datasets, showing brevity, limited vocabulary, and consistent syntactic patterns.

- Contribution: Introduces a syntactic type-based approach (time token, modifier, numeral) as a lightweight alternative to semantic methods.

- Contribution: Provides empirical evaluation on TimeBank, WikiWars, and Tweets, showing SynTime outperforms prior methods, notably on tweets.

- Strength: Strong empirical results with consistently high recall across datasets.

- Strength: Lightweight and efficient, suitable for real-time use due to simple heuristics and type-based design.

- Strength: Well-motivated design tied to data analysis and theoretical grounding (Zipf’s principle).

- Strength: Clear, concise presentation of methodology, types, and rules.

- Strength: Practical and effective expansion strategy via adding keywords from training data.

- Weakness: Depends on accurate POS tagging; impact of POS errors is not thoroughly analyzed.

- Weakness: Limited error analysis; common failure modes and challenging expression types are not deeply explored.

- Weakness: Comparisons omit more recent (e.g., deep learning) baselines, limiting current relevance of evaluation.

- Weakness: Focuses only on recognition; limited discussion on integrating normalization with SynTime.

- Question: How sensitive is performance to POS tagging errors and different taggers?

- Question: What examples and common characteristics exist for time expressions SynTime misidentifies?

- Question: Have you evaluated SynTime against recent deep learning–based time expression systems?

- Question: How could SynTime’s type-based structure support time expression normalization?

- Additional: SynTime workflow figure is clear and helpful.

- Additional: References are comprehensive and terminology is consistent.

- Additional: GitHub link to SUTime is noted and appreciated.

- Overall: Well-written, insightful, and effective approach; recommendation for acceptance pending deeper POS-dependency and baseline analyses.
