- Proposes a simple rule-based method for recognizing time expressions from text.  
- Simplicity is a strong advantage for analysis and basic application needs.  
- Experiments show the method outperforms state-of-the-art rule-based and machine-learning methods.  
- Major concern about generality: rules were designed using the same corpora as evaluation, risking overfitting.  
- Evaluation corpora domains may have biased rule design.  
- No statistics or discussion provided on overlap of time expressions across corpora to justify generality.  
- Recommendation: test on a new, distinct corpus (separate from rule design) to demonstrate wider effectiveness.
