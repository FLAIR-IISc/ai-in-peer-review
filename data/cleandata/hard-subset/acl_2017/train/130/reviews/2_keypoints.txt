- Targets MCI detection from speech transcripts across three datasets.  
- Proposes a graph-based method leveraging word co-occurrence to encode features.  
- Encodes diverse graph-derived features (lexical, syntactic, etc.).  
- Evaluated using 5-fold cross-validation and multiple classifiers; model performance varies by dataset.  
- Uses appropriate datasets and addresses a well-defined clinical problem.  

- Writing quality is poor; manuscript needs grammar and spelling revision.  
- ML problem and instance definition are unclear; dataset/tables describe studies not transcript-level data and reported counts are inconsistent.  
- Unclear motivation for the proposed graph enrichment versus simpler word-node similarity approaches.  
- Does not leverage domain-specific biomedical NLP tools.  
- Class distribution is unclear, making accuracy a questionable metric; F1 (and class balance) should be reported.  
- Results are reported with unjustified precision on very small datasets and lack statistical significance testing.
