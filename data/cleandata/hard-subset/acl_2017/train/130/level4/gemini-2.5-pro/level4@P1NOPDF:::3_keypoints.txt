- Paper proposes an interesting, reasonable application of existing methods to detect MCI from speech and provides a thorough problem/dataset description.  
- Manuscript needs substantial editing for clarity, readability, and concise abstract.  
- Multi-view learning approach is not described; methodology lacks crucial details.  
- "Linguistic features" are not well-defined; section too brief for reproducibility.  
- Training data/source for word embeddings is unspecified and likely too small if trained on the MCI datasets.  
- Rationale for removing disfluencies (filled pauses, false starts, repetitions) is missing and questionable given their diagnostic value.  
- Use of raw term frequency instead of tf-idf for Bag-of-Words models is unexplained and needs justification.  
- Experimental design lacks statistical significance testing to support conclusions.  
- Related work section is superficial and should detail previous methods and comparisons.  
- Numerous citation and citation-format issues (missing links/citations for DementiaBank, Picnic picture, WML subtest; conference citation format).  
- Several unclear or ambiguous phrasings and statistics require rewording and clarification (e.g., "15% a year," "the best scenario").  
- Preprocessing description is incomplete/incorrect (e.g., lemmatization as "last step"; omitted normalization steps).  
- Dataset descriptions are confusing: unclear what "manually segmented" means, why segmentation differs across datasets, and undefined acronyms (e.g., ABCD).  
- Table 1 caption claims additional statistics but only shows demographics; paragraph 323–332 is confusing and needs rewriting.  
- Transcription norms and transcription decisions (e.g., manual corrections) lack citation and justification.  
- Several specific textual/grammar issues and missing punctuation throughout the manuscript.  
- Ambiguous references to features and parameters (e.g., "the shortest path," "the threshold parameter") need explicit definitions.  
- Section 4.3 lacks details on the multi-view learning scheme and classifier setup.  
- Recommend expanding methodological details and moving extensive feature descriptions to supplementary material if space is limited.
