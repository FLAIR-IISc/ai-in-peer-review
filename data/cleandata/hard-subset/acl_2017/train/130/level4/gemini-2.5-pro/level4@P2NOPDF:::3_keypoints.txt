- Interesting and valuable application of established methods to MCI detection from speech; problem and clinical context well motivated.  
- Manuscript suffers from poor clarity and needs substantial editing for readability.  
- Key methodological details are missing, hindering reproducibility.  
- Multi-view learning implementation is not described.  
- Composition and definition of the "linguistic features" are vague and underdeveloped.  
- Source corpus for training word embeddings is not specified (trained on small MCI data or larger external corpus?).  
- Removal of disfluencies (filled pauses, false starts, repetitions) is unjustified and likely discards informative signals for MCI.  
- Choice of term frequency over TF-IDF for BoW is not justified.  
- Related work section is superficial and lacks specific comparisons to prior methods and results.  
- Experimental results lack statistical significance testing.  
- Abstract is overly long and contains details better placed in the main text; needs condensation.  
- Multiple grammatical errors and awkward phrasings throughout the manuscript.  
- Several claims and figures (e.g., "15% a year") are ambiguous and need clarification.  
- Some terms and acronyms (e.g., PLN, ABCD) are undefined.  
- Dataset descriptions are confusing; datasets should be explicitly named and segmented procedures explained.  
- Table 1 caption misrepresents content (claims "additional statistics" but shows only demographics).  
- Transcription norms and preprocessing choices (manual corrections, lemmatization) lack citations and justification.  
- Preprocessing pipeline is incompletely described; some common steps are omitted or mischaracterized.  
- The rationale for manually correcting grammatical errors in transcripts is unclear and counterintuitive for MCI signals.  
- Several sentences and paragraphs need rewriting for clarity and grammar.  
- Specific feature descriptions (e.g., "shortest path," threshold parameter) are ambiguous and need precise definitions.  
- The Classification Algorithms section omits crucial implementation details, making experiments irreproducible.
