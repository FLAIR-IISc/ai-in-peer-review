- Contribution: Applies established representations and methods to MCI detection from spoken narratives; provides thorough context and datasets.
- Overall quality: Methodology and experimental setup are generally sound but omit several important details.
- Writing: Manuscript needs substantial editing for clarity and readability; abstract should be condensed.
- Multi-view learning: Specifics of the multi-view learning scheme are omitted and must be described.
- Linguistic features: Composition and definitions of the "linguistic features" set are unclear and under-described.
- Word embeddings: Unclear whether embeddings were trained on the small in-domain MCI datasets or on a larger external corpus.
- Disfluency handling: Authors remove filled pauses/repetitions without justification, despite these being potentially important MCI indicators.
- BoW/tf-idf: No rationale given for not using standard tf-idf weighting in Bag-of-Words models.
- Statistical analysis: Experimental conclusions lack statistical significance testing to support claimed differences.
- Related work: Discussion is superficial and does not detail methods or compare prior studies to current work.
- Dataset description: Identification and description of the three datasets are confusing; manual segmentation procedures and the acronym "ABCD" are undefined.
- Table 1: Caption claims additional statistics but only demographic info is provided.
- Transcription and preprocessing: Transcription norms, preprocessing steps (e.g., lemmatization, other normalizations), and justification for choices are not adequately documented.
- Feature definitions: Some feature descriptions are ambiguous (e.g., which feature "shortest path" refers to; meaning of "the best scenario").
- Threshold parameter: The meaning of the "threshold parameter" (e.g., cosine distance threshold for embeddings) is not defined.
- Section-specific missing info: Numerous small missing items—citations/links for DementiaBank and Picnic picture, definition of PLN, explanation of WML subtest, and several rephrasing/grammar fixes.
- Linguistic features section length: Section 4.2.2 needs significant expansion or should be moved to supplementary material per page limits.
- Embedding training source: Section 4.2.1 must clarify source data used to train word embeddings.
- Justification for preprocessing choices: No justification for removing disfluencies or for other preprocessing decisions that affect predictive signals.
- Experimental clarity: Descriptions in Lines 323–332 and 641–645 (dataset segmentation and other procedures) should be rewritten for clarity.
