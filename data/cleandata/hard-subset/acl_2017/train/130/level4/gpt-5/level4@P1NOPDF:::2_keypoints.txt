- Addresses MCI detection from speech transcripts across three datasets using an appropriate problem setup.
- Proposes a graph-based method leveraging word co-occurrence with features from lexical and syntactic graph characteristics.
- Evaluates models with 5-fold cross-validation and multiple classifiers, reporting performance variations across datasets.
- Manuscript has poor English and needs substantial copyediting for grammar, spelling, and style.
- Core ML task and instance definition are unclear; transcript-level dataset counts and relevant data descriptions are missing or inconsistent.
- Excessive and partly irrelevant dataset description; some reported counts do not add up (e.g., participant totals vs. tables).
- Motivation for the proposed graph enrichment is not well justified; alternative word-node approaches (e.g., vector similarity) are not discussed.
- No use of domain-specific biomedical tools or resources despite biomedical datasets.
- Class distribution is not reported, making accuracy a potentially inappropriate metric; F1 (and class balance) should be reported.
- Results are reported with excessive precision on small datasets and lack statistical tests to confirm significance of improvements.
