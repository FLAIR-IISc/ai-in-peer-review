- Presents a new application (MCI detection from speech narratives) using largely existing representations and techniques; problem, datasets, and domain are well motivated and experiments appear sound.
- Manuscript needs substantial editing to improve clarity and readability.
- Multi-view learning setup is not described anywhere.
- The precise set of “linguistic features” is not specified and Section 4.2.2 is underspecified.
- Unclear which corpora were used to train word embeddings; training on the three small datasets seems unlikely.
- No justification for removing disfluencies (filled pauses, false starts, repetitions), which could be salient MCI indicators.
- No explanation for not using standard tf-idf weighting in the BoW experiments.
- No statistical significance testing reported to support experimental conclusions.
- Related work section is superficial and treats core prior studies without sufficient methodological comparison.
- Abstract is too long and needs shortening.
- Multiple sentences/phrases need rewording for grammar and clarity (examples: Lines 22–23, 24–25, 29–30, 34–38, 73, 115–117, 147–149, 233–235, 323–332, 641–645).
- Several missing citations/links (DementiaBank, Picnic picture of the Western Aphasia Battery, transcription conventions).
- Line 70 (“15% a year”) and other quantitative phrasings are unclear and need precision.
- Several acronyms and terms are undefined (ABCD, PLN, WML subtest).
- Dataset descriptions are confusing and inconsistent (manual segmentation, which datasets were segmented, reference to three datasets while enumerating two).
- Table 1 caption promises additional dataset statistics that are absent.
- Need clarification whether word embeddings were trained on MCI datasets or external corpora (Lines 290–299, Section 4.2.1).
- Explanation needed for why BoW would be adversely affected by disfluencies (Lines 115–117).
- Ambiguity about “shortest path” and whether it refers to Feature 6 (Line 560).
- Ambiguous reference to “threshold parameter” — likely cosine-distance threshold for embeddings but not specified (Line 662).
- Several minor editorial/formatting errors and typos (e.g., missing period at Line 735, “consisted of” → “consist of”).
- Section 4.3 (Classification Algorithms) omits the specific multi-view learning scheme and lacks significance testing details.
- Recommend verifying conference rules regarding extra pages / Supplementary Material for expanded feature/method descriptions.
