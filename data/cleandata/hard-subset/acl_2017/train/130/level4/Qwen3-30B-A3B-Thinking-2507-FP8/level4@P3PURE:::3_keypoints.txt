- Paper presents an interesting, novel application of existing features/methods for MCI detection but needs substantial editing for clarity and completeness.  
- Missing technical details: multi-view learning approach is not explained and the set of "linguistic features" is unclear.  
- Word-embedding training data appear insufficient or unspecified; need to state whether embeddings were trained on MCI datasets or external corpora.  
- Questionable preprocessing: disfluencies (filled pauses, false starts, repetitions) are removed without justification despite their potential diagnostic value.  
- Grammatical corrections to transcripts are unclear and may remove clinically relevant error signals; provide examples of disfluencies and correction rationale.  
- Methodological gaps: omission of tf-idf weighting for BoW classifiers is unexplained.  
- Methodological gaps: statistical significance tests for experimental results are missing.  
- Related work is superficial: Section 4.2.1 lacks details on prior methods and explicit comparisons.  
- Abstract needs concise revision for clarity.  
- Numerous typographical and grammar issues throughout (e.g., "impacting in parsing results," "as it a lightweight," missing periods/commas).  
- Dataset descriptions: explicitly name all three datasets (e.g., Cinderella, [dataset name], DementiaBank) and include richer dataset statistics in Table 1.  
- Define acronyms and terms used (e.g., WML, ABCD, PLN) and clarify "the best scenario."  
- Provide citations/links for external resources referenced (e.g., DementiaBank, Picnic picture of the WAB) and add other missing citations.  
- Clarify segmentation/transcription practices: state if transcripts were manually segmented, cite transcription norms, and explain omitted normalization steps beyond lemmatization.  
- Explain why lemmatization is not the final preprocessing step and list any normalization omitted from the manuscript.  
- Clarify whether word embeddings were trained on the MCI datasets or external corpora and specify which dataset generated embeddings.  
- Fix tense consistency and wording issues (e.g., "consisted of" → "consists of"; "manually segmented of" → "manually segmented (e.g., into sentences)").  
- Justify disfluency removal concretely (or retain disfluencies) and address how disfluencies affect BoW representations.  
- Clarify specific feature definitions and references (e.g., whether "shortest path" refers to Feature 6) and define threshold parameters (e.g., for cosine distance).  
- Expand methodological subsections: substantially detail Section 4.2.2 and the multi-view learning scheme, and ensure compliance with supplementary material guidelines.  
- Improve citation formatting and in-text presentation (e.g., "Aluisio et al. (2016) used...").  
- Add statistical testing details and report whether reported differences are significant (Section 4.3).
