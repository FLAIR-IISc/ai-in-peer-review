- Paper proposes an interesting MCI detection application using mostly existing representations and methods; problem context, datasets, and approach are generally reasonable and informative.
- Manuscript requires substantial editing to improve overall readability and conciseness (abstract too long).
- Multi-view learning approach is not adequately explained and key conceptual details are missing.
- The set of "linguistic features" is not described in sufficient detail and needs expansion.
- It is unclear which datasets were used to train word embeddings; the described corpora may be too small for reliable embeddings.
- The rationale for removing disfluencies (filled pauses, false starts, repetitions) is not provided even though they may be informative MCI indicators.
- The paper uses plain term frequency for BoW but gives no justification for not using tf-idf, the commonly used weighting scheme.
- No statistical significance tests are reported to support experimental comparisons and claimed differences.
- Related work section is superficial; it should detail prior methods and explicitly compare them to this work.
- Numerous phrasing, grammar, and clarity issues throughout the text require revision (many specific line-by-line suggestions provided).
- Dataset descriptions need clarification: explicitly name datasets (e.g., DementiaBank, Cinderella), provide segmentation rationale, and add more statistics in Table 1 beyond demographics.
- Missing citations/links for resources and terms (e.g., DementiaBank, Picnic picture/Western Aphasia Battery, transcription norms).
- Preprocessing steps are unclear: clarify whether transcripts were manually corrected, the role/sequence of lemmatization and other normalizations, and provide transcription norms citation.
- Justify segmentation choices and explain why some datasets were manually segmented while others were not; define acronyms like ABCD and PLN.
- Feature descriptions need more detail (e.g., define "shortest path" Feature 6, expand Section 4.2.2 on linguistic features, define threshold parameter for embedding distance).
- Clarify which dataset(s) generated the word embeddings and whether external corpora were used.
- In Section 4.3, reconsider the presentation of the multi-view learning scheme (either explain it clearly or omit unnecessary detail) and report statistical significance for classifier comparisons.
- Re-examine and justify all preprocessing and feature-engineering decisions (removing disfluencies, omission of tf-idf, choice of embeddings), linking them to MCI detection rationale.
