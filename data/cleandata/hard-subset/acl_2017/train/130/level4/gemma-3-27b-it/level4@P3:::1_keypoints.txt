- Novel approach: Proposes using complex network features enriched with word embeddings to detect MCI from speech transcripts.  
- Robustness to disfluency: Network-based representations may be less sensitive to parsing errors from disfluent patient speech.  
- Portuguese contribution: Evaluates method on three Portuguese datasets, addressing a resource gap for that language.  
- Ensemble benefit: Combining features and classifiers (ensembles/multi-view learning) improves performance on some datasets.  
- Competitive performance: Complex network features achieve competitive, sometimes superior, results versus traditional linguistic features.  
- Dataset breadth: Experiments include DementiaBank (English) and two Portuguese datasets (Cinderella, Arizona-Battery), offering varied narrative tasks.  
- Clear methodology: Modeling process and incorporation of embeddings are described clearly enough for replication.  
- Sample size concern: Portuguese datasets are small (20 MCI, 20 control each), limiting generalizability and statistical power.  
- Embedding details missing: Unclear whether embeddings are pre-trained or trained on the datasets; origin and training parameters need clarification.  
- Feature selection justification weak: No solid rationale or systematic exploration for choosing specific network metrics (e.g., PageRank, Betweenness).  
- Intro/related work unfocused: Background includes tangential topics (e.g., sentiment, pedestrian detection) and should be streamlined.  
- Small effect sizes: Reported accuracy differences are often minor; practical implications and effect sizes need clearer discussion.  
- Disfluency modeling query: Unclear if authors explored explicitly modeling disfluencies within the network representation.  
- Power analysis missing: No evidence of a priori power analysis to justify sample sizes given observed effects.  
- Minor manuscript errors: Multiple typos, formatting/DOI duplications, and a repeated transcript line need correction.  
- Presentation improvement suggestion: Add a transcribed sentence with its network visualization to illustrate the method concretely.
