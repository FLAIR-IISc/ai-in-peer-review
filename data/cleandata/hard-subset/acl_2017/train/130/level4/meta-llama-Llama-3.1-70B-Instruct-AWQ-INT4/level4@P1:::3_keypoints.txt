- Novel application of mostly existing representations/features to detect MCI in speech narratives; datasets and domain are well described, proposed solution reasonable but missing some detail.  
- Contribution: novel use of complex network features for MCI detection in speech narratives.  
- Contribution: enrichment of complex network features with word embeddings to improve performance.  
- Contribution: evaluation on three datasets including DementiaBank and two Portuguese datasets.  
- Strength: thorough description of the problem, datasets, and domain.  
- Strength: proposed approach is novel and has potential to improve MCI detection.  
- Strength: evaluation across three datasets provides a comprehensive assessment.  
- Weakness: paper needs substantial editing; key details are missing and some sentences are unclear.  
- Weakness: related work section is superficial and lacks detailed comparison to prior methods.  
- Weakness: experiments lack statistical significance tests to support conclusions.  
- Weakness: unclear rationale for removing disfluencies and the role of disfluencies in MCI.
