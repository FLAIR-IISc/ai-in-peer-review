- Summary: Proposes enriching complex networks with word embeddings to detect MCI from speech transcripts and evaluates on three datasets (DementiaBank + two Portuguese) versus linguistic and bag-of-words baselines.  
- Contribution: Novel complex-networks + embeddings approach that outperforms traditional linguistic features in some cases.  
- Contribution: Demonstrates effectiveness across three datasets, including a Portuguese dataset.  
- Contribution: Provides a comparative evaluation against linguistic features and bag-of-words representations.  
- Strength: Novel and valuable methodological contribution to MCI detection from speech.  
- Strength: Thorough multi-dataset evaluation, including underrepresented Portuguese data.  
- Strength: Well-organized paper with clear explanations.  
- Weakness: Needs substantial editing; some key concepts are missing or unclear.  
- Weakness: Insufficient detail on the multi-view learning approach and unclear specification of linguistic features.  
- Weakness: No statistical significance tests reported to support experimental claims.  
- Weakness: Related work coverage is superficial with missing or unclear citations.  
- Question: Provide more details on the multi-view learning approach and comparisons to alternatives.

- Question: Supply statistical significance tests to substantiate experimental conclusions.
