- Proposes a novel method using complex network features to detect MCI from speech narratives.  
- Models speech transcripts as complex networks and enriches them with word embeddings for short neuropsychological texts.  
- Reports that complex network features outperform traditional linguistic features across all datasets.  

- Novel application of complex network features to MCI detection.  
- Thorough description of problem, datasets, and domain.  
- Empirical results indicate complex networks are effective and outperform linguistic baselines.  
- Paper is well-organized and easy to follow.

- No statistical significance testing reported for reported result differences.
