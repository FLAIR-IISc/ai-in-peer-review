- Paper applies existing representations and methods to MCI detection from speech but lacks essential experimental and methodological details.
- Writing needs substantial editing for readability and clarity throughout the manuscript.
- Multi-view learning approach is not adequately explained; details of the scheme are missing.
- The set of "linguistic features" is unclear and needs expanded description.
- It is not specified which datasets were used to train or generate the word embeddings.
- The removal of disfluencies (filled pauses, false starts, repetitions) is unjustified and may discard diagnostically important signals.
- No explanation is given for not using tf-idf weighting for BoW classifiers.
- Statistical significance tests are missing to support experimental conclusions.
- Related work section is superficial and lacks detailed comparisons to prior methods.
- Abstract is too long and should be shortened.
- Numerous sentences need rephrasing for clarity (examples: Lines 22-23, 29-30, 34-38, 73, 641-645).
- Manual correction of grammatical errors in transcripts is unclear and needs justification and examples of disfluencies.
- Line- and term-specific clarifications required (e.g., "15% a year," "the best scenario," missing citations).
- Provide citations/links for external resources: DementiaBank, Picnic picture (WAB), transcription norms.
- Explain or define acronyms and tests: PLN, WML, ABCD, and explicitly name datasets rather than using ordinal references.
- Clarify preprocessing and normalization steps (order and omitted common steps); correct wording about lemmatization.
- Specify whether word embeddings were trained on MCI datasets or external corpora.
- Clarify what "manually segmented DementiaBank and Cinderella" means and why other datasets were not segmented similarly.
- Table 1 caption should include more dataset statistics (e.g., class distribution, durations).
- Justify removal of filled pauses, false starts, repetitions from transcripts given their relevance to MCI.
- Provide a citation or link describing transcription norms and transcription team composition.
- Define technical terms used in features (e.g., "shortest path" in feature 6, "threshold parameter" for cosine distance).
- Expand Section 4.2.2 to better describe linguistic features; check conference rules about supplementary material if needed.
- Correct typos and grammatical issues (e.g., "consisted of" → "consist of," missing periods).
- Add statistical significance testing and clearer experimental detail in Section 4.3 to substantiate claims.
