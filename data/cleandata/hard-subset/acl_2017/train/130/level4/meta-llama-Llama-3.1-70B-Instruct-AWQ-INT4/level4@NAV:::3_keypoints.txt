- Positive: Novel application of existing representations and methods to detect MCI in speech; problem, datasets, and domain are well described and the experiments seem reasonable.  
- Major: Paper requires significant editing to improve readability and clarity.  
- Method clarity: The specifics of the multi-view learning approach are not explained.  
- Feature clarity: The definition and composition of the "linguistic features" set are unclear and need expansion.  
- Embeddings: It is unclear which corpora were used to train the word embeddings; using only the three datasets described appears insufficient.  
- Disfluencies: Removal of filled pauses, false starts, and repetitions is unjustified—these may be important MCI indicators.  
- BoW baseline: The paper did not use tf-idf weighting for BoW classifications and gives no justification for this choice.  
- Statistics: No statistical significance tests are reported to support experimental conclusions.  
- Related work: The related work section is superficial and needs more detailed comparison with prior methods.  
- Abstract: The abstract is too long and needs shortening.  
- Language edits: Numerous sentences require rephrasing for clarity (e.g., lines 22–23, 24–25, 29–30, 34–38, 70, 73, 115–117, 147–149, 641–645).  
- Citations missing: Provide citations/links for DementiaBank, the Picnic picture/WAB, transcription norms, and the WML subtest.  
- Acronyms: Definitions for acronyms like PLN and ABCD are missing.  
- Dataset labeling: Datasets are referred to generically (first/second/third); explicitly name them and clarify which were used for which steps.  
- Segmentation: The claim of manual segmentation for DementiaBank and Cinderella is unclear—explain "segmented" and why others were not auto-segmented.  
- Table 1: Demographic table lacks the additional dataset statistics promised in the text.  
- Preprocessing: Claims about preprocessing order (e.g., lemmatization) are inaccurate or incomplete and need correction (lines 233–235).  
- Embedding origin: Clarify whether embeddings were trained on the MCI datasets or external corpora (lines 290–299, Section 4.2.1).  
- Feature definition: The "shortest path" feature (feature 6) is poorly defined and needs clarification (line 560).  
- Linguistic features section: Section 4.2.2 needs substantial expansion and clearer definitions.  
- Threshold parameter: The cosine-distance threshold parameter for embeddings is not clearly defined (line 662).  
- Transcription norms: Provide citation or link for the transcription norms used (lines 340–440).  
- Removal rationale: Explain rationale for removing disfluencies and justify how this affects parsing and downstream features (lines 375–388, 115–117).  
- Conference formatting: Fix citation format and follow conference citation guidelines (e.g., line 185).  
- Supplementary material: Check conference rules before offloading important method details to supplementary pages.  
- Classification details: Section 4.3 omits details of the multi-view learning scheme and classifier settings.  
- Minor errors: Fix small typos/grammar (e.g., "consisted of" → "consist of" at line 322; missing period at line 735).
