- Overall assessment: very strong paper—well-structured, clear, and sound.

- Experiments: solid empirical evaluation and qualitative analysis (Table 1) consistent with expectations.
- Weakness: comparisons to related/methodologically similar approaches are limited and should be expanded.
- Request clarification: explicitly explain differences between Tian et al. (2014) and the current mixture/prototype-based approach.
- Missing citations: Neelakantan et al. (2014), Li & Jurafsky (2015), and Liu et al. (2015) should be discussed.
- Suggestion: include results from the above prior approaches in Tables 3 and 4 to strengthen comparisons.
- Question: account for why w2gm loses performance relative to w2g in the SWCS analysis.
