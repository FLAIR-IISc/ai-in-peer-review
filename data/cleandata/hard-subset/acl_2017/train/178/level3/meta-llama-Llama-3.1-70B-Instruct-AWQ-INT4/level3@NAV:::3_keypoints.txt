- Proposes a method to jointly learn concept, phrase, and word embeddings from unlabeled text using representative phrases as distant supervision.
- Method aims to capture semantic relationships between concepts, phrases, and words and is evaluated on similarity and relatedness tasks.

- Introduces a novel joint embedding approach that uses ontology-linked phrases for distant supervision.
- Provides a new evaluation dataset of similarity and relatedness for real-world entities identified by Wikipedia page and text phrase.
- Shows experimental results indicating the method captures some semantic properties of ontology concepts comparably to prior, more annotation-heavy methods.

- Model is simple and balances atomic vs. compositional treatment of phrases/concepts sensibly.
- Paper includes substantial work: model development, a new evaluation dataset, and multiple analyses.
- Method scales to a much larger vocabulary without extra expense.

- Evaluation is limited to intrinsic similarity/relatedness tasks and lacks extrinsic task validation.
- Human judgments were collected on phrases, so results should be treated as phrase-similarity rather than concept-similarity.
- Medical evaluation datasets are limited or inappropriate.
- Hyperparameters were heavily tuned on the same datasets used for evaluation, weakening validity.
- Claim of outperforming prior work is weakened because the method still relies on large manual ontologies and prior clinical annotations.

- How will the authors address evaluation dataset limitations and add extrinsic task evaluations?
- Please provide details on hyperparameter tuning and sensitivity to different settings.
- How can the method be extended to more abstract, general-domain data (e.g., BabelNet-like coverage)?

- Several minor typos and formatting issues throughout the paper.
- Notation in Equation 1 is confusing and needs clarification.
- Matrix dimensions reported in Line 220 are likely incorrect and should be fixed.
- Phrase-completion wording in Line 250 is confusing and should be clarified.
- Reference to Pedersen et al. (2007) is missing from the references.
