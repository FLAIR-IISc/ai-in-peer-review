- Method: extend word embeddings to phrases/concepts by assigning fixed identifiers to equivalent phrase/word/concept groups, replacing occurrences in the corpus to create a tagged corpus, and training on combined tagged+original corpora.  
- Data source: concept/phrase sets derived from an ontology; experiments focused on biomedical corpora and ontologies.  
- Evaluation resource: authors introduce a novel test dataset for real-world entity word similarity/relatedness, which may be the paper's most significant contribution and needs more discussion.  
- Impact: paper is well-written and intuitive but may lack substantial novelty and is limited by evaluation confined to the biomedical domain.  
- Technical issue: Eq. 8 (MAP) should use a practical cutoff instead of ranking the entire vocabulary; it does not define a probability and should remove probability references.
