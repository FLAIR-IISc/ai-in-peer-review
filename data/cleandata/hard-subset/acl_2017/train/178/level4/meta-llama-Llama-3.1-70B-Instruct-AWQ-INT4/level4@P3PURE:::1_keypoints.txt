- Proposes extending word embeddings to phrases/concepts by assigning fixed identifiers to equivalent phrases/words/concepts and replacing/augmenting the corpus for training.  
- Concept/phrase sets are derived from a biomedical ontology, so the method and evaluation are domain-focused.  
- Introduces a new test dataset for word similarity/relatedness of real-world entities — a novel and potentially major contribution.  
- Paper is well-written, but the technique is straightforward and not a substantial methodological advance.  
- Contribution scope is limited by evaluation confined to the biomedical domain.  
- The paper should include a more in-depth discussion and analysis of the generated test resource.  
- Technical issue: Equation 8 incorrectly frames the MAP calculation as a probability and should be revised to remove that probability interpretation.  
- Minor edits needed (e.g., correct "most concepts has" to "most concepts have").
