- Contribution: Introduces a new general-domain concept similarity/relatedness dataset collected via Mechanical Turk.
- Contribution: Explores trade-offs between atomic and compositional phrase/concept representations via β and η hyperparameters.
- Strength: Intuitive, well-motivated approach that leverages ontological knowledge to fill a gap between word- and concept-level representations.
- Strength: Comprehensive experiments across multiple corpora, ontologies, baselines, and a newly created dataset.
- Strength: Clear presentation of the model with helpful diagrams illustrating embedding matrices.
- Strength: Provides a mechanism (β, η) to balance compositional and atomic representations.
- Weakness: Evaluation relies primarily on intrinsic similarity/relatedness tasks with no extrinsic downstream evaluations.
