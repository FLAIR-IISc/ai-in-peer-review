- Extends word embeddings to multi-word phrases/concepts by assigning ontology-derived unique IDs, replacing occurrences in corpus, appending tagged corpus, and training embeddings on the combined text.

- Experiments are conducted in the biomedical domain using relevant corpora and ontologies.

- Authors created a new test dataset for real-world entity similarity/relatedness, which may be the paper’s most significant contribution and needs more discussion.

- Paper is well-written; the proposed technique is intuitive and straightforward but offers modest technical novelty.

- Evaluation is limited in scope by being confined to the biomedical domain.

- Technical issue: Equation 8’s MAP formulation is incorrectly presented as a probability distribution (does not sum to one); recommend using a natural ranking cut-off and removing the probability claim.

- Minor editorial correction: Line 556 should read "most concepts have" instead of "most concepts has."
