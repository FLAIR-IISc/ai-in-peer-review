- Outperforms ALIGN on a supervised entity linking task, indicating improved joint text-and-knowledge representations.
- Provides a direct, closely related-method comparison using highly similar input data.
- Includes a useful analysis of the smoothing parameter, addressing popularity effects in entity linking.
- ALIGN comparison may be confounded by different context window size, embedding dimensionality, and unclear definition of N(e_j)/graph adjacency, making fairness of the comparison uncertain.
- The term “mention” is ambiguous (surface-form type vs. document occurrence); the model appears to learn only mention-sense embeddings.
- The effect of sense disambiguation order is unclear due to lack of comparisons with other unsupervised entity linking methods.
