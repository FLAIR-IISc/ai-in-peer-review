Strengths:
- Outperforms ALIGN on supervised entity linking, suggesting better joint text–knowledge representations.
- Provides a direct comparison with ALIGN using very similar input data, strengthening experimental validity.
- Analyzes the smoothing parameter, addressing entity popularity bias.

Weaknesses:
- ALIGN comparison is weakened by hyperparameter differences (e.g., window size 10 vs 5, vector dim 500 vs 200), making it less conclusive.
- Definition of entity neighborhood N(e_j) is unclear (outlinks vs incoming vs undirected), which could substantially affect results and comparisons to ALIGN.
- Terminology is ambiguous regarding "mention" vs mention instance and "mention embedding" vs mention-sense embeddings, reducing clarity.
- The significance of the proposed sense disambiguation order is unclear without comparisons to other unsupervised ordering strategies.
