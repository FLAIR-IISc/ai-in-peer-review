Questions to authors
1) Problem statement: Is the primary goal EL, NIL detection, or KG completion? What is the precise task formulation and system components (mention detection, candidate generation, candidate ranking)?
2) Novelty: What is conceptually and technically new versus Yamada et al. (joint word–entity embeddings), Ganea & Hofmann 2017, Le & Titov 2018, BLINK 2020, GENRE 2021? Please provide a comparative table of model differences and capabilities.
3) Mention detection: Do you use Aho–Corasick/double-array tries in production? How do you handle overlapping mentions, redirects/aliases, and morphology? Any comparison to neural span detectors?
4) Candidate ranking: What representation learning objective(s) are used (skip-gram, joint text–KG, translational models)? How do you model context (local vs global coherence; graph-based reranking)? Is there a cross-encoder stage?
5) Datasets and metrics: Which benchmarks are used (AIDA-CoNLL, TAC-KBP, WNED)? Do you evaluate NIL detection? Which metrics (micro/macro accuracy, in-KB recall, NDCG)? Please include strong recent baselines.
6) Efficiency: Provide indexing and inference complexity, latency, and memory usage for mention detection and ranking. Compare to neural baselines.
7) Analysis: Include ablations (without joint training; without coherence; without trie-based detection), error analysis by entity frequency/ambiguity, and domain transfer.
8) Reproducibility: Will you release code, trained models, and mention dictionaries? What are licensing constraints?
