Questions to Authors
- What is the precise task (e.g., named entity linking/disambiguation, word sense disambiguation, or KG completion), and what is the formal problem definition?
- What are the explicit novel contributions relative to prior EL systems (Cucerzan 2007; Hoffart et al. 2011; Kulkarni et al. 2009; Pershina et al. 2015) and joint text–KG embedding methods (Wang & Li 2016; Toutanova et al. 2015; Han et al. 2016)? Please enumerate and justify.
- How are Aho–Corasick and the double-array trie used in the system? What are the dictionary size, average/95th percentile latency, and memory footprint for candidate generation?
- How are embeddings trained (objectives, corpora, KG sources, negative sampling, hyperparameters), and how are they integrated into the disambiguation scorer?
- Do you perform collective inference (e.g., PPR or graph ranking)? If so, specify the graph construction, edge weights, and convergence criteria.
- Which datasets are used (e.g., AIDA-CoNLL, TAC-KBP, MSNBC, AQUAINT)? What are the evaluation metrics (accuracy, micro/macro F1, nDCG), and are results statistically significant compared to strong baselines?
- Provide ablations isolating the effect of each component (string matching, embedding type, collective inference). What are the main failure cases?
- Please provide a corrected, deduplicated, and fully formatted bibliography with accurate author lists, titles, venues, and years.
