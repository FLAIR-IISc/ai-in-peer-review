There is no accessible abstract, problem statement, methodology, experiments, results, or conclusions. Based on the cited works, the intended topic seems to be named entity disambiguation/linking and/or joint embedding of text and knowledge graphs, with background on string matching and dictionary data structures. However, with the core content missing, I cannot reliably infer the actual technical contributions.

- Contributions as I see them: none can be substantiated from the provided material. At best, the authors attempted to compile related work spanning entity linking, multi-prototype embeddings, and joint text–KG models, but even the bibliography is severely degraded.

Strengths
- Breadth of intended related work: The references (despite being corrupted) attempt to cover classical EL (Hoffart et al. 2011; Pershina et al. 2015; Kulkarni et al. 2009; Shen et al. 2015), word embeddings and multi-sense embeddings (Mikolov et al. 2013a/b; Reisinger & Mooney 2010; Huang et al. 2012; Neelakantan et al. 2014; Tian et al. 2014; Li & Jurafsky 2015; Mancini et al. 2016), and joint text–KG models (Wang et al. 2014; Weston et al. 2013; Toutanova et al. 2015; Han et al. 2016; Wang & Li 2016; Wu et al. 2016). If the actual paper had been present, this breadth could have supported a well-situated contribution.

- Inclusion of classical indexing/string matching references (Aho–Corasick 1975; Aoe 1989) suggests attention to efficient data structures, which might have complemented an EL/KB completion pipeline had the methodology been available.

  - Duplicates of the same work with minor typos: multiple Aho & Corasick 1975 entries with variant corrupted titles; multiple Aoe 1989 entries; multiple Han et al. 2016 entries; etc.
  - Placeholder artifacts and LaTeX escapes: “Han et al\.,? 2016,” “\Q2017\E,” stray backslashes and punctuation, “...,? ...., ....,” etc.
  - Incorrect or incomplete metadata: venues missing or wrong (“Introduction to information retrieval” attributed to Schütze alone; the standard citation is Manning, Raghavan, and Schütze, Cambridge Univ.
