- Contribution 1: Improves joint text-knowledge representations, outperforming ALIGN in supervised entity linking.

- Contribution 2: Provides a direct comparison with a closely related approach for more accurate evaluation.

- Contribution 3: Analyzes a smoothing parameter to give insight into the impact of entity popularity on linking.

- Strength 1: Demonstrates effective joint text-knowledge representations yielding better entity linking performance.

- Strength 2: Delivers a comprehensive evaluation including comparisons and smoothing analysis.

- Strength 3: Introduces a novel and effective language model–based sense disambiguation method.

- Weakness 1: ALIGN comparison is incomplete and confounded by different hyperparameters, weakening the claim.

- Weakness 2: Definition of N(e_j) is unclear and adjacency directionality (directed vs undirected) is unspecified.
