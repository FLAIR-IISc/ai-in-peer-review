- Introduces a novel document clustering framework using Wasserstein distance on empirical measures over word embeddings, avoiding fixed-length vectors.
- Main contributions: a computationally efficient nonparametric clustering tool with state-of-the-art performance and a method to quantify embedding benefits over bag-of-words.
- Strength: Innovative use of Wasserstein distance yielding superior results, especially on short-text datasets.
- Strength: Thorough empirical evaluation against many baselines across diverse datasets and metrics.
- Strength: Clear, well-organized presentation with sensitivity analyses and runtime comparisons.
- Strength: High impact potential for clustering and embedding evaluation across varied corpora.
- Weakness: Insufficient information on software availability and implementation details hindering reproducibility.
- Weakness: Minor English errors and typos that reduce readability.
- Question: Is the implementation publicly available or will it be released to enable reproducibility?
- Question: Please provide details on computational resources used, especially for large-scale experiments.
