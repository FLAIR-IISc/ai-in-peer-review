- Contribution: Demonstrates that LSTMs can leverage context to improve causal relation classification beyond lexical lookup or rule-based systems.

- Contribution: Highlights limitations of prior syntactic/lexicon-heavy approaches and motivates more flexible, context-aware methods.

- Strength: Sound LSTM-based methodology with thoughtful tuning (padding strategies, optimizer experiments).

- Strength: Statistically significant improvements over baseline, notably on ambiguous connectives like "since" and "after."

- Strength: Clear, well-organized presentation and explanations.

- Strength: Comprehensive and relevant related work coverage.
