- Proposal of two evaluation metric classes for reading comprehension: prerequisite skills and readability, applied to six RC datasets.
- Main contributions: adoption of these metrics, revision of prerequisite-skill taxonomy, and annotation of six RC datasets.
- Strength: thorough analysis of six RC datasets using the two proposed metric classes.
- Strength: improved and more comprehensive classification of prerequisite skills for RC.
- Strength: produced and publicly released annotations for six existing RC datasets.
- Weakness: limited dataset coverage—only six datasets, may not generalize to all RC datasets.
- Weakness: annotations are subjective and may vary across annotators.
- Weakness: no evaluation of RC system performance using the proposed metrics, limiting practical validation.
- Question: how will the authors extend the analysis to additional RC datasets?
- Question: please provide more details on the annotation process and measures taken to ensure annotation consistency.
- Question: how do the authors plan to apply the proposed metrics to evaluate RC system performance?
- Additional comment: valuable contribution, but should expand dataset scope and include system evaluations to increase practical impact.
