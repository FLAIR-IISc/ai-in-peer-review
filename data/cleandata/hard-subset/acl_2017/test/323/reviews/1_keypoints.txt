- Paper extends the entity grid model with a convolutional neural network to learn entity-transition sequences and better generalize over longer sequences.  
- Well-written and fluent manuscript.  
- Uses a hybrid approach (neural network on an existing model) rather than a fully neural system.  
- Needs expanded comparison to the neural coherence model of Li and Hovy.  
- The Li and Hovy model scored unexpectedly low; this raises concerns about experimental setup or corpus-dependence.  
- Recommend deeper investigation into why the Li and Hovy model performed poorly and testing on corpora where it succeeds.  
- Authors should document the efforts and difficulties encountered when running the Li and Hovy system.  
- Suggest reproducing Li and Hovy’s reported results on their original datasets as a sanity check.  
- Numerous missing definite articles throughout the paper—proofreading for article usage is needed.  
- Rephrase the recurring expression "limits the model to do X" to clearer alternatives like "limits the model's capacity to do X" or "stops the model from doing X."  
- Final recommendation adjusted to 4 after author response; objective difficulties running others' code should not penalize the authors.
