- Proposes an end-to-end neural local coherence model using CNNs over entity grids to learn distributed representations and capture long-range entity transitions.  
- Demonstrates state-of-the-art performance on discrimination, insertion, and summary coherence rating tasks with notable absolute gains.  
- Main contribution: successful adaptation of CNN architecture to entity grids, improving coherence assessment over non-neural baselines.  
- Secondary contribution: end-to-end training that removes need for hand-engineered features and learns task-specific representations.  
- Positive practical contribution: authors released code, aiding reproducibility.  
- Strength: consistent and substantial empirical improvements across all three evaluation tasks.  
- Strength: CNN filters model entity transitions of varying lengths, addressing limitations of traditional grids for longer dependencies.  
- Strength: end-to-end learning simplifies modeling and likely yields better task-tuned features.  
- Weakness: insufficient justification and analysis of hyperparameter choices (filter sizes, dropout, etc.).  
- Weakness: comparisons omit recent neural coherence models (RNNs, transformers); Li & Hovy (2014) discussion is inadequate.  
- Weakness: ablation study is limited; contribution of individual Extended Grid-CNN components and specific entity features is unclear.  
- Weakness: datasets are relatively small (especially for summary coherence), raising concerns about generalizability.  
- Question: please provide detailed rationale and impact analysis for chosen hyperparameters.  
- Question: how does the model compare empirically to recent RNN- or transformer-based coherence models?  
- Question: can you include a more thorough ablation isolating each component and different entity-specific features?  
- Question: have you evaluated the model on larger, more diverse datasets, and what were the results?  
- Additional comment: related-work discussion should better cover recent neural coherence advances.  
- Additional comment: figures are helpful but need clearer explanations of model components.  
- Additional comment: overall writing is good but some sentences should be rephrased for clarity.
