- Contribution: Novel neural local coherence architecture that models long-range entity transitions and entity-specific features while maintaining generalization.

- Contribution: Pairwise ranking training method for end-to-end learning of task-specific high-level coherence features.

- Contribution: Empirical state-of-the-art results, outperforming existing models on three coherence assessment tasks.
