- Proposes a neural local coherence model over entity-grid: embedded grid cells, 1D convolution along entity columns, max-pooling, linear layer, trained with pairwise ranking.
- Contributions: neuralized entity-grid with CNNs for longer transitions, end-to-end pairwise ranking with pretraining, and empirical gains on WSJ discrimination/insertion and DUC’03.
- Strength: clear, well-motivated architecture (role embeddings, convolution along transitions, wide conv + max-pool).
- Strength: addresses sparsity of discrete grids, enabling longer transition windows (k up to 5–8).
- Strength: statistically significant empirical improvements on standard coherence tasks; pretraining helps DUC’03.
- Strength: careful experimental protocol (splits, multiple runs, hyperparameter search, significance testing) and code release.
- Strength: simple, efficient, and compatible with existing preprocessing pipelines.
- Weakness: incomplete baseline comparisons—Li & Hovy (2014) excluded and discourse-relation systems not compared on WSJ.
- Weakness: limited ablations and analysis on transition window k, number of filters, individual feature contributions, pooling choices, and learned filters.
- Weakness: dependence on coreference/syntax without robustness evaluation or surface-only variant comparisons.
- Weakness: insertion task mismatch—model not trained specifically for insertion-style reordering.
- Weakness: clarity issues in convolution/pooling notation and confusing Table 3 / insertion metric presentation.
- Question: please report ablations on transition window k and individual entity-specific feature contributions and saturation point for k.
- Question: clarify convolution shape—is it strictly 1D along sentences per entity column, and did you try 2D convolutions for inter-entity interactions?
- Question: how are entities obtained (coref resolver vs noun matching), which resolver/settings, and sensitivity to coreference quality?
- Question: can you train with insertion-style pairs and report the impact, and please clarify/fix the insertion metric/Table 3?
- Question: provide a more thorough diagnostic comparison to Li & Hovy (2014) across variants and hyperparameters.
- Additional suggestion: evaluate against harder/realistic negative permutations (local swaps, reversals, discourse-aware reorders).
- Additional suggestion: include qualitative analysis of error cases and high-activation transition patterns.
- Additional suggestion: report runtime and model parameter counts for practitioner use.
