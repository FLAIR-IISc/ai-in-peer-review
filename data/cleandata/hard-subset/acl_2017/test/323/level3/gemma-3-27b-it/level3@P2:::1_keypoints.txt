- Replaces discrete entity-grid features with learned distributed representations and CNNs to model longer-range entity transitions.
- Demonstrates state-of-the-art results on discrimination, insertion, and summary coherence rating (notably +4% discrimination, +2.5% insertion).
- Provides rigorous evaluation using three coherence tasks, pairwise ranking training, and comparisons to multiple baselines including graph-based and entity-grid variants.
- Paper is clearly written with helpful diagrams, thorough experimental details, and a discussion of limitations and future work.
- Public code release supports reproducibility and further research.

- Limited comparison to prior fully neural coherence work (e.g., Li & Hovy 2014) and a vague explanation for their reported poor performance.
- Lacks detailed analysis of reproduction attempts for Li & Hovy (missing hyperparameters, implementation details, and sanity checks on original datasets).
- Minor English/presentation issues (incorrect/missing definite articles) that reduce polish.
