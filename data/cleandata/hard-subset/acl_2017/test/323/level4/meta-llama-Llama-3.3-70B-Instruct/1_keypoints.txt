- Well-structured, clearly written paper with a valid methodology that builds on prior work and uses a neural model to address specific limitations.
- Needs a more extensive comparison with Li and Hovy’s neural coherence model.
- Surprising low performance of Li and Hovy’s model on the task raises concerns about possible experimental errors or corpus-dependent effects.
- A deeper analysis of why Li and Hovy’s model scores low (experiment setup, corpus differences) would strengthen the paper.
- Readability is good but numerous missing definite articles indicate the paper needs proofreading focused on article usage.
- Repeated phrase “limits the model to do X” should be rephrased (e.g., “limits the model’s capacity to do X” or “prevents the model from doing X”).
