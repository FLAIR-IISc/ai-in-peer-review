- Proposes a novel CNN-based model built on the entity grid representation, with clear justification of design choices.
- Methodology and underlying mechanics are well explained, especially the rationale for using CNNs.
- Extensive evaluation across tasks shows the proposed method outperforms prior approaches.
- Failure to replicate Li/Hovy (2014) results indicates a possible implementation or modeling flaw that needs investigation.
- The model is largely a straightforward neural adaptation of the entity grid rather than a radically new representation.
- Unclear why 100-dimensional vectors are needed to represent a four-dimensional grid entry (and slightly more for extended grids).
- Directly optimizing for coherence ranking improves the model, but extending transition chains from k=3 to k=6 may offer little benefit given short WSJ articles.
- Writing is repetitive and could be more concise; entity grid introduction is repeated and sections overlap.
- Section 6 is redundant in its first two-thirds and should be deleted or merged into section 2, with the remainder moved as a subsection.
- Consider renaming section 2 to "Related Work" and integrating repetitive content for clarity.
- Overall, a robust implementation but the paper would benefit from more innovative representations or novel tasks for a full paper.
- Minor edits needed: proofreading for grammar, correct figure reference (Figure 2 → Figure 1), change "Firstly/Secondly" to "First, Second", rephrase "limits the model to" for clarity, and consider removing the final paragraph of section 1.
