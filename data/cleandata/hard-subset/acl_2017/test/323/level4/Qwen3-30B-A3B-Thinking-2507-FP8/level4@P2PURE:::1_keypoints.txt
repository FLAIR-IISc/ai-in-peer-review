- Proposes an extension to the entity grid that uses a CNN to learn sequences of entity transitions, improving coherence modeling and generalization over longer entity sequences versus direct transition probabilities.

- Paper is well written and takes a sensible incremental approach by using a neural network to fix a specific limitation instead of proposing an entirely neural system.

- Evaluation should be expanded to include the neural coherence model by Li and Hovy to strengthen comparisons.

- The reported very low performance of the Li & Hovy model on the task raises concerns about possible experimental errors, corpus dependence, or relative suitability of the proposed model; this needs investigation.

- Recommend a deeper analysis of why Li & Hovy underperforms (experimental setup, dataset differences, or model mismatch) to bolster the paper's claims.

- Paper is generally fluent but has multiple missing definite articles; a focused proofread is recommended.

- Repeated awkward phrasing ("...limits the model to do X...") should be revised (e.g., "limits the model's capacity to..." or "prevents the model from...").

- Final recommendation adjusted to 4; authors should document attempts and difficulties in running Li & Hovy's system and, if not already done, try to reproduce Li & Hovy's results on their original datasets as a sanity check.
