- Proposes a CNN over entity-grid for modeling text coherence; approach is well-motivated and clearly described with a strong "Why it works" section.  
- Provides extensive evaluation across multiple tasks and reports outperforming prior methods.  
- Fails to reproduce a key result from Li & Hovy (2014), indicating a possible implementation or modeling error that must be addressed.  
- The method is largely a straightforward "neuralization" of the classic entity grid, making the contribution feel incremental.  
- Unclear justification for using 100-dimensional embeddings for grid entries that originally have only ~4 features; needs explanation of what extra information is captured.  
- Questionable benefit of extending transition chains from k=3 to k=6 given many WSJ articles are short; practical impact should be clarified.  
- Paper is repetitive—introductory material on entity grids and Related Work overlap; recommend merging Sections 2 and 6 for conciseness.  
- Writing and structure could be tightened; remove non-essential roadmap paragraph to save space.  
- Proofreading by a native English speaker is needed to fix numerous missing articles and language issues.  
- Minor editorial fixes: correct "Figure 2"→"Figure 1", use "First/Second" instead of "Firstly/Secondly", and rephrase "limits the model to".  
- Would be stronger with more novel contributions (e.g., new grid features or applications to new tasks) rather than incremental improvements.
