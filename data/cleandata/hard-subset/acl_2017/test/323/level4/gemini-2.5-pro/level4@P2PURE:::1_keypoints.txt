- Proposes an extension to the entity grid model using a CNN to learn coherent entity-transition sequences for better long-range generalization.  
- Paper is well-written and thoughtfully integrates a neural component rather than proposing a fully neural architecture.  
- Approach addresses a specific limitation of the original model and is conceptually sound.  
- Major weakness: insufficient comparison to the Li and Hovy neural coherence model despite surprising low performance reported for that baseline.  
- Raises questions about whether the Li & Hovy result reflects experimental setup errors, corpus-specific issues, or other factors.  
- Recommends documenting attempts and difficulties running the Li & Hovy system and performing a sanity check by reproducing their original results.  
- Suggests careful proofreading for missing definite articles and fixing awkward phrasing like “limits the model to do X.”  
- Reviewer increased recommendation to 4 after author response and agrees authors shouldn’t be penalized for external software difficulties.
