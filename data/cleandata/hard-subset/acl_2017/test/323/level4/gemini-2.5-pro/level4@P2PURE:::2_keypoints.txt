- Proposes a CNN applied to the entity grid for text coherence; approach is well-motivated and clearly described.
- Insightful discussion of design choices, especially the rationale for using a CNN and the "Why it works" section.
- Extensive evaluation across tasks showing the model outperforms previous methods.
- Failure to reproduce Li & Hovy (2014) results is concerning and may indicate an implementation or modeling error.
- Novelty is incremental—a straightforward neural adaptation of the existing entity-grid framework.
- Unclear justification for projecting low-dimensional grid entries into 100-d vectors; needs explanation of benefits.
- Performance difference between transition chain lengths k=3 and k=6 may be marginal, particularly for short WSJ articles.
- Writing is repetitive and could be more concise; the entity grid introduction appears multiple times.
- Section 6 largely repeats material from Section 2; merge novel content into Section 2 and rename it "Related Work."
- Impact would be greater with more innovation in representation (new features) or new application tasks.
- Minor proofreading issues: missing articles before "WSJ corpus" and "Brown... toolkit."
- Typo: "Figure 2" should be "Figure 1" on page 1, bottom left column.
- Use "First, Second" instead of "Firstly/Secondly" for style consistency.
