- Proposes a CNN for text coherence based on the entity grid, showing improvements on standard tasks.
- Well-motivated and clearly described, with a strong "Why it works" discussion.
- Extensive evaluation across multiple coherence tasks; generally outperforms previous methods.
- Failed to reproduce Li and Hovy (2014) results; needs thorough investigation and explanation.
- Conceptual novelty is limited—largely a straightforward neural adaptation of the entity grid.
- Using 100-dimensional embeddings for low-dimensional grid entries is not justified; ablation/analysis needed.
- Extending transition chains from k=3 to k=6 lacks clear practical motivation given short WSJ articles.
- Generally well-written but could be more concise.
- Significant repetition between Sections 2 and 6; recommend merging and removing redundancy.
- Needs proofreading for missing articles and general English usage.
- Figure numbering error: "Figure 2" should be "Figure 1."
- Use "First, Second" instead of "Firstly/Secondly" for academic style.
- Rephrase "limits the model to" to clearer wording like "prevents the model from considering..."
- Consider removing the standard final introduction paragraph outlining paper structure for conciseness.
