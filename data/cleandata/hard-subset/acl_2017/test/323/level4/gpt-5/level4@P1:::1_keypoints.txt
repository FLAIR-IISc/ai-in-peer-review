- Introduces a CNN over entity transitions that replaces direct transition-probability estimation, improving generalization over longer entity sequences.  
- Builds on the classic entity grid, applying a targeted neural module rather than proposing a fully end-to-end neural system.  
- Paper is clear, well-written, and the design choice to augment (not replace) the entity grid is well motivated.  
- Comparison to Li and Hovy (2014) is weak: their model’s unexpectedly low score needs investigation for possible experimental errors or corpus dependence.  
- Authors should document the difficulties running third-party software and reproduce Li & Hovy’s reported results on their original datasets as a sanity check.  
- Stylistic issues: many missing definite articles and awkward phrasing (“limits the model to do X”); recommend careful proofreading and rephrasing.  
- Final recommendation adjusted to 4 after author response; attempts to evaluate Li & Hovy’s system should be explicitly reported in the paper.
