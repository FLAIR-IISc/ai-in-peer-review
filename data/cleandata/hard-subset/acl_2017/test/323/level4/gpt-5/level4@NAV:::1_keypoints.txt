- Extends the entity grid with a convolutional neural network to learn entity-transition patterns, improving generalization over longer entity sequences.
- Clear, well-written paper that uses a neural component to fix a specific limitation rather than proposing a fully neural solution.
- Should broaden empirical comparison to the neural coherence model of Li and Hovy.
- Authors report a very low score for Li and Hovy’s model, which raises concerns about possible experimental setup errors.
- Need to investigate whether Li and Hovy’s poor performance is corpus-dependent and test the proposed model on corpora/tasks where their model performs well.
- Attempts and difficulties in evaluating Li and Hovy’s system should be documented in the paper; such objective difficulties shouldn’t be penalized.
- Recommend reproducing Li and Hovy’s results on their original datasets as a sanity check.
- Manuscript has frequent missing definite articles and awkward phrasing (e.g., “limits the model to do X”); recommend a proofreading pass and clearer alternatives.
- Final recommendation updated to 4 after the author response.
