- Extends the entity-grid model with a CNN to learn longer entity-transition patterns, generalizing beyond original direct transition probabilities.  
- Paper is well organized and clearly written.  
- Sensible, well-motivated hybrid design that integrates a neural component to address a specific limitation rather than proposing a fully neural solution.  
- Evaluation needs a more comprehensive comparison with Li and Hovy’s neural coherence model.  
- Surprising low score for Li and Hovy’s model raises questions about (i) possible experimental setup issues, (ii) corpus or task differences, and (iii) how the proposed model performs on datasets where Li and Hovy’s approach succeeds.  
- A deeper investigation of these factors would substantially strengthen the paper’s claims.  
- Writing issues: missing definite articles in multiple places; recommend careful proofreading focused on article usage.  
- Non-idiomatic repeated phrase “…limits the model to do X…” should be revised to “…limits the model’s capacity to do X” or “…prevents the model from doing X.”  
- Final recommendation: 4 (after author response).  
- Paper should document concrete steps taken to evaluate Li and Hovy’s system and specific problems encountered, and, if feasible, reproduce Li and Hovy’s published results on their original datasets as a sanity check.
