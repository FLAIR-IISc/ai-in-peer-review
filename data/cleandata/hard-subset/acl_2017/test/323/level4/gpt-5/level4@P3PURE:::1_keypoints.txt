- Proposes a CNN-based extension of the entity grid model to learn entity-transition sequences, improving generalization over longer entity sequences versus original transition-probability estimates.  
- Well motivated and clearly written; takes a pragmatic approach by adding a neural component to a specific limitation rather than proposing a fully neural model.  
- Needs a deeper comparison with Li and Hovy’s neural coherence model: the latter’s surprisingly low scores raise concerns about experimental setup, corpus dependence, and how the proposed model would fare where Li & Hovy succeeds.  
- Writing issues: many missing definite articles and awkward phrasing (e.g., “limits the model to do X”); recommend focused proofreading and rephrasing.  
- Recommendation moved to 4 after author response; authors should document efforts/problems running Li & Hovy’s code and ideally reproduce Li & Hovy’s original results as a sanity check.
