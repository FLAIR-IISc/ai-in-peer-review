- Proposal: CNN over the entity-grid to model textual coherence, well motivated and outperforming prior methods.

- Strength: Clear motivation and rationale for using CNNs on entity grids.

- Strength: Broad evaluation showing consistent gains over previous approaches.

- Strength: Useful consolidation of the entity-grid paradigm with modern neural modeling.

- Concern: Limited novelty—mostly a straightforward “neuralization” of the entity grid without richer representational innovations or new tasks.

- Concern: Embedding dimensionality (100) is unjustified and likely overparameterized; ablation over embedding sizes required.

- Concern: Transition window size k (up to 6) lacks sensitivity analysis, especially by document length; unclear benefit over smaller k.

- Concern: Reproducibility problem with Li & Hovy (2014)—needs precise preprocessing, dataset split verification, and side-by-side diagnostics.

- Suggestion: Provide ablations and diagnostics for embedding size, filter widths/heights, number of filters, depth, pooling, loss function, extended vs basic grids, parameter counts, and training time.

- Suggestion: Clarify what patterns the CNN captures beyond transition counts; include qualitative/interpretable analyses of learned filters or top-activating transitions.

- Suggestion: Include stronger non-neural baselines (e.g., regularized linear/MLP on transition features) and ensure fair hyperparameter tuning.

- Suggestion: Add representation-oriented contributions (lexicalized/semantic roles, coreference confidence, discourse markers) or new evaluations to justify full-paper scope.

- Clarity: Consolidate related work into Section 2, remove redundant exposition, streamline entity-grid introductions, and prune formulaic concluding remarks.

- Minor style: Proofread for missing articles and small errors (figure numbering, “Firstly/Secondly” usage, wording tweaks).

- Question: Why 100-dimensional grid-entry embeddings and what trade-offs were observed across sizes?

- Question: How sensitive are results to k and how does document length distribution affect optimal k?

- Question: Can you provide interpretability analyses of learned filters or embedding spaces?

- Question: What exactly differs from Li & Hovy (2014), and will you release code, preprocessing scripts, and detailed settings?

- Overall: Solid, well-executed application with strong empirical results but modest technical novelty and key design choices needing ablation and reproducibility investigation.
