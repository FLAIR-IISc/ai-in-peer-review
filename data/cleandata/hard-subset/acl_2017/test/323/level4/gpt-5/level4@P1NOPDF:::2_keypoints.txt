- Proposes a CNN-based text coherence model built on the entity grid; approach is well motivated and clearly described, with useful “Why it works” discussion.  
- Evaluation covers several tasks and shows consistent gains over prior methods.  
- Cannot reproduce a previously reported result (Li and Hovy 2014); investigate a possible implementation or modeling error.  
- Model feels like a straightforward “neuralization” of the entity grid and lacks sufficient novelty for a full paper.  
- Question the need for 100-dimensional embeddings for grid entries with only four (or slightly more) states; clarify benefits vs unnecessary capacity.  
- Skeptical that increasing transition chain length from k=3 to k=6 meaningfully improves performance given many short WSJ articles.  
- Paper is longer than necessary and repeats material; most of Section 6 should be merged into Section 2 (retitle as “Related Work”) and/or shortened.  
- Remove the boilerplate final paragraph of Section 1.  
- Minor proofreading and editorial fixes needed: missing articles (e.g., “the WSJ corpus”, “the Brown ... toolkit”), Page 1 bottom left “Figure 2” → “Figure 1”, use “First/Second” instead of “Firstly/Secondly”, rephrase “limits the model to.”
