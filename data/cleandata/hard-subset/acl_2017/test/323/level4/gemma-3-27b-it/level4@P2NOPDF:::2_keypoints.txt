- Introduces a CNN-based approach to text coherence on the entity grid; motivation and design choices (including “Why it Works”) are clear and well-argued.
- Evaluation is comprehensive and shows gains over prior state-of-the-art, but failure to reproduce Li and Hovy (2014) is concerning and needs investigation.
- Model is a straightforward neural application to the entity grid; authors should justify using 100-dimensional vectors for each grid entry.
- Varying transition chain length from k=3 to k=6 may have limited impact given the short length of many WSJ articles.
- Paper is repetitive and could be more concise; section 6 largely duplicates section 2 and should be merged/trimmed.
- Overall solid implementation but lacks a more substantial contribution (e.g., novel representation learning, additional features, or new tasks).
- Language needs polishing by a native English speaker; multiple missing articles noted.
- Figure labeling error: “Figure 2” should be “Figure 1” (page 1, bottom left).
- Stylistic suggestions: replace “Firstly/Secondly” with “First, Second.”
- Rephrase “limits the model to” to “prevents the model from considering…” for clarity.
- The standard concluding paragraph in section 1 is unnecessary and can be removed.
