- Proposes a CNN extension to the entity grid model to learn entity transition sequences and better generalize to longer sequences than the original transition-probability approach.
- Paper is well-written, clear, and takes a pragmatic approach by extending existing work with a targeted neural solution rather than an all-neural overhaul.
- Lacks a comprehensive comparison to existing neural coherence models, especially the Li and Hovy model.
- The unexpectedly low performance of Li and Hovy in the authors’ experiments raises concerns about experimental setup or corpus-dependence and needs investigation.
- Recommend evaluating the proposed model on datasets where Li and Hovy performs better to provide stronger comparative insight.
- Advises a deeper analysis of factors causing performance differences between models.
- Paper requires careful proofreading for frequent definite-article omissions and awkward phrasing like “limits the model to do X.”
- Post-rebuttal: final recommendation raised to 4, but the paper should explicitly document difficulties running others’ software.
- Suggests attempting to reproduce Li and Hovy’s original results on their datasets as a validation step if feasible.
