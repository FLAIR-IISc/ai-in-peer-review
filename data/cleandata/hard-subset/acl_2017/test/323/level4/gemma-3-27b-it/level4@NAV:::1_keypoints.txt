- Proposes extending the entity grid model with a convolutional neural network to model entity transitions and capture coherence.  
- CNN-based approach improves generalization to longer entity sequences versus direct transition probability estimation.  
- Well-written and valuable contribution that smartly augments an existing method with a targeted neural component rather than full neuralization.  
- Paper addresses a specific step’s limitation rather than replacing the entire pipeline, which is a sensible design choice.  
- Lacks a comprehensive comparison with the neural coherence model of Li and Hovy; such comparison would strengthen claims.  
- Li and Hovy’s surprisingly low performance on this task raises concerns about possible experimental errors or corpus sensitivity.  
- Authors should test whether their model’s gains hold on datasets where Li and Hovy’s model performs well.  
- Writing is generally clear but contains missing definite articles at several lines (e.g., 92, 132, 174, 488, 490, 547, 674, 764) and awkward phrasing (“…limits the model to do X…”).  
- Suggest rephrasing to “limits the model’s capacity to do X” or “prevents the model from doing X” for clarity.  
- Final recommendation adjusted to 4 after author response; replication challenges for external software should be discussed in the paper.  
- Recommend documenting efforts and difficulties in reproducing Li and Hovy’s system and attempting to reproduce their results on original datasets as validation.
