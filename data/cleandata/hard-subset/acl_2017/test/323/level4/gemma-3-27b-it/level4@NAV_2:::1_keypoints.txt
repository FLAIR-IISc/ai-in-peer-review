Proposes a CNN extension to the entity grid to learn entity-transition sequences, improving generalization over longer sequences compared to original transition-probability estimates.

Well-written, sensible incremental advance that judiciously leverages neural networks rather than adopting a fully neural approach.

Insufficient comparison to existing neural coherence models, particularly Li and Hovy (2014).

Unexpectedly low performance reported for the Li and Hovy model suggests possible experimental setup issues or corpus sensitivity and needs deeper investigation.

Recommend evaluating the proposed model and baselines on Li and Hovy’s original datasets as a sanity check.

Manuscript contains numerous missing definite articles and requires thorough proofreading for article usage.

Repeated awkward phrasing (“limits the model to do X”) should be revised to clearer alternatives (e.g., “limits the model’s capacity to X” or “prevents the model from doing X”).

Questions for authors: please elaborate on difficulties reproducing Li and Hovy (2014) and confirm whether you tried their original datasets.
