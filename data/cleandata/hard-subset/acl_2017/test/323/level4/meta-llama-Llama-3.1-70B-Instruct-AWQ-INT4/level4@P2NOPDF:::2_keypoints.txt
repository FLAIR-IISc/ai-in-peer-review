- Proposes a CNN-based model built on the entity grid with clear motivation and a helpful "Why it works" discussion.
- Evaluation is extensive and shows improvements over prior methods on several tasks.
- Failure to reproduce Li/Hovy (2014) results raises possible implementation or modeling errors that need addressing.
- Model is a relatively simple neuralization of the entity grid, using 100-d vectors per grid entry; the necessity and benefit of this design are unclear.
- Optimizing directly for coherence ranking is sensible, but differences between transition chains for k=3 versus k=6 may be marginal given short WSJ articles.
- Writing is verbose and repetitive (entity grid intro repeated); much of section 6 is redundant with section 2.
- Recommend merging relevant parts of section 6 into section 2 and renaming section 2 to "Related Work."
- Overall a solid implementation but lacks more innovative representations, features, or new tasks expected for a full paper.
- Minor proofreading needed for English and grammar.
- Specific corrections: change bottom-left of page 1 from "Figure 2" to "Figure 1"; use "First"/"Second" instead of "Firstly"/"Secondly"; rephrase "limits the model to" to "prevents the model from considering ..."; remove the standard final paragraph in section 1.
