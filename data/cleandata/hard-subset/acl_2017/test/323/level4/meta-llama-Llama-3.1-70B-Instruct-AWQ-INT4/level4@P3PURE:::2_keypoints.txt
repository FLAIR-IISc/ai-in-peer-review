- Proposes a CNN-based approach to model text coherence using the entity grid representation.  
- Clear, well-motivated description with a strong discussion of design intuitions (e.g., choice of CNN, "Why it works").  
- Extensive evaluation across tasks showing the proposed method outperforms prior approaches.  
- Failure to reproduce Li/Hovy (2014) raises concerns about potential implementation or modeling errors.  
- Model is largely a straightforward "neuralization" of the entity grid and lacks substantial novel contributions.  
- Unclear justification for using 100-dimensional vectors to represent a 4-dimensional (or slightly extended) grid entry.  
- Need more analysis on the impact of transition chain length (k=3 vs k=6), especially given short WSJ articles.  
- Writing is repetitive and could be more concise; entity grid introduction is duplicated in multiple places.  
- Section 6 duplicates Section 2 content; recommend integrating/reducing and renaming remaining content to "Related Work."  
- Overall solid implementation but a full paper should include more innovations in representations, features, or tasks.  
- Minor proofreading issues (missing articles) and wording/style suggestions.  
- Specific corrections: change "Figure 2" to "Figure 1" (p.1 bottom-left), use "First/Second" instead of "Firstly/Secondly," rephrase "limits the model to," and consider removing the final paragraph of Section 1.
