- Proposes an extension of the entity grid model using a convolutional neural network to learn entity transition sequences, improving generalization over longer sequences.

- Paper is well-written and appropriately builds on prior work by using a neural approach to address identified issues.

- The authors should expand comparisons to the neural coherence model of Li and Hovy; current comparison is insufficient.

- The surprisingly low performance of the Li and Hovy model on the task raises concerns about possible experimental errors, corpus-dependent behavior, or other confounding factors.

- Recommend deeper investigation into why Li and Hovy underperformed, including testing the proposed model on corpora/tasks where Li and Hovy succeeds.

- Document the efforts and difficulties encountered when attempting to run the Li and Hovy system in the paper.

- Suggest reproducing Li and Hovy’s reported results on their original datasets as a sanity check if not already done.

- Minor language issues: missing definite articles in several lines and awkward phrasing ("limits the model to do X"); recommend careful proofreading and rephrasing.

- Reviewer adjusted final recommendation to 4 and agrees implementation difficulties with others' software should not be held against the authors.
