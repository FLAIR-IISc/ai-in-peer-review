- Proposes CNN-based extension of the entity grid to learn sequences of entity transitions for coherence, improving generalization over longer entity sequences versus direct transition probability estimation.

- Approach is valid, builds on existing work, and the paper is well-written.

- Comparison to Li & Hovy’s neural coherence model is insufficient and should be expanded.

- The unexpectedly low score for Li & Hovy’s model raises concerns about possible experimental setup errors, corpus-dependent performance, or differences in task suitability.

- A deeper investigation (including testing the competing model on corpora/tasks where it performs well) would strengthen the paper.

- Document efforts and problems encountered when running Li & Hovy’s system in the paper.

- Proofread for missing definite articles (lines 92, 132, 174, 488, 490, 547, 674, 764) and rephrase recurring “...limits the model to do X...” for clarity.

- Final recommendation adjusted to 4 after author response; authors should try to reproduce Li & Hovy’s original results as a sanity check unless already done.
