- Proposes a CNN-based coherence model using the entity grid with clear intuition and a "Why it works" explanation.
- Well-motivated approach with extensive evaluation showing improvement over previous methods.
- Failure to reproduce Li and Hovy (2014) results suggests a potential implementation or modeling error that must be addressed.
- Model is a simple "neuralization" of the entity grid; unclear why 100-d vectors are needed for essentially 4-d entries—authors should justify this representation.
- Authors should consider directly optimizing for coherence ranking, which may yield a better model.
- Unclear benefit of using transition chains up to k=6 versus k=3 given many WSJ articles are very short.
- Writing is verbose and repetitive (entity grid intro and related work repeated); consolidate related work into section 2 and remove redundancies.
- For a full paper, more novel representations (other features) or tasks would be expected beyond the current implementation.
- Minor edits needed: proofreading for missing articles, correct "Figure 2" → "Figure 1", use "First"/"Second" instead of "Firstly"/"Secondly", rephrase "limits the model to", and remove the unnecessary final paragraph in section 1.
