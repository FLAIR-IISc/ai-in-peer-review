- Exposition needs strengthening: Figure 1’s ambiguous NOM/ACC example requires deeper discussion to clarify the task’s complexities.

- Inter-sentential zero handling: Explain how inter-sentential zero pronouns are represented (the paper implies no explicit marking), and discuss implications for modeling zero anaphora.

- Error analysis: Table 3 shows Multi-Seq gains mainly on NOM; provide deeper comparative analysis (context specificity, divergence frequency, why NOM benefits most).  
- Model-output comparison: Analyze how often and why the two models’ outputs diverge and whether improvements are context-dependent.
