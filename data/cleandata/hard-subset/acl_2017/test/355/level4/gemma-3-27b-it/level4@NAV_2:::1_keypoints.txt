- Paper applies Grid-type Recurrent Neural Networks to Japanese predicate-argument structure (PAS) analysis.  
- Approach eschews explicit syntactic structure yet outperforms state-of-the-art systems that use syntax.  
- Authors provide a clear, detailed description of implementation and experimental results.  
- Special attention to dropped arguments (zero pronouns); the multi-sequence model that considers all predicates achieves best performance on these cases.  
- Overall manuscript is detailed and clearly written.  
- Reviewer has only minor comments focused on English fluency and typos.  
- Suggest illustrating the PRED-with-context point using the Fig. 6 example where the accusative marker is included in the PRED string.  
- Reviewer did not understand the use of boldface in Table 2 and asks for clarification.  
- Specific suggested wording/typo corrections across the paper (p.1, p.2, p.3 Fig.4, p.4, p.7, p.8) to improve phrasing and grammar.
