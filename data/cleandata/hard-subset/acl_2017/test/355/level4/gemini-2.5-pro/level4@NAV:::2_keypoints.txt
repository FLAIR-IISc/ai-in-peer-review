- Adapts Zhou & Xu (2015) English SRL model to Japanese and proposes new SRL models.  
- Adds a Grid-RNN to capture interactions among arguments from multiple predicates.  
- Evaluation on a standard Japanese SRL benchmark achieves significant state-of-the-art improvement.  
- Grid-RNN gives a modest additional boost over the single-sequential model, mainly improving zero-argument detection.  
- Manuscript is well-organized, clearly written, and strongly motivated.  
- Primary contribution is extension from single-sequential to multi-sequential architecture, so novelty may be limited.  
- Modeling predicate interactions has less impact here than reported by Ouchi et al. (2015).  
- Fails to discuss or compare with Shibata et al. (2016), a relevant prior neural extension that should be included.
