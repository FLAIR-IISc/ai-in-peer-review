- Proposes document vectors learned with a skip-gram style objective plus a global context vector regularizer and dropout.  
- Individual components aren’t novel, but their combination in this form is likely novel.  
- Section 3 provides a detailed and appreciated analysis of model behavior.  
- Empirical evaluation is relatively weak and limited to sentiment analysis and k-way classification.  
- Too much space is devoted to t-SNE projections instead of deeper empirical analysis.  
- Concerns about using weak/“soft” baselines, echoing other reviewers.  
- Recommendation to accept: method is interesting, well-composed, efficient, and likely useful to others.
