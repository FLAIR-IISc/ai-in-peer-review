- Proposes document vector learning using a skip-gram-style objective with a global context vector regularizer and multiple dropout variants.  
- Individual components are not novel, but their combination is novel and sensible.  
- Section 3 gives a detailed and appreciated examination of model behavior.  
- Main weakness: empirical evaluation is modest.  
- Evaluation tasks (sentiment analysis and k-way classification) are not the most compelling choices.  
- Allocating significant space to t-SNE projections is wasteful versus deeper analysis.  
- Baselines are somewhat weak/soft, as other reviewers noted.  
- Recommendation: accept—the algorithm is interesting, well-crafted, efficient, and likely useful to others.
