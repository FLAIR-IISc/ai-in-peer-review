- Proposes document vector learning via skip-gram–style training with a global context vector regularizer and multiple dropout variants.
- Individual components are not novel, but their specific combination appears new.
- Section 3 provides a detailed and appreciated analysis of model behavior.
- Empirical evaluation is weak — sentiment analysis and k-way classification are not the most informative settings.
- Excessive space devoted to t-SNE projections (two-thirds of a page) that could be used for deeper analysis.
- Baselines are weak/“soft,” aligning with other reviewers’ concerns.
- Overall recommendation: accept — method is interesting, well-composed, computationally efficient, and likely useful to readers.
