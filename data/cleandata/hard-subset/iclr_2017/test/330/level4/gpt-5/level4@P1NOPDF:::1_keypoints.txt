- Method: Learn document vectors with a skip-gram–style objective augmented by a global context vector regularizer and several dropout variants.  
- Novelty: Individual components are not new, but their particular combination appears novel.  
- Analysis: Detailed analysis of model behavior (Section 3) is appreciated.  
- Main weakness: Empirical evaluation is limited, focusing only on sentiment analysis and k-way classification.  
- Recommendation for experiments: More challenging and informative tasks are needed to better showcase the method’s strengths.  
- Space allocation critique: Spending ~2/3 page on t-SNE projections is a poor use of space versus deeper analysis.  
- Baselines: Compared baselines are relatively soft/weak.  
- Overall verdict: Reviewer leans toward acceptance — algorithm is interesting, well-composed, efficient, and likely useful to readers.
