- Proposes a skip-gram–style document embedding with a global context vector and stochastic masking/dropout.  
- Individual components are not new, but their particular integration appears novel.  
- Section 3 gives a thorough examination of model behavior.  
- Main weakness is a relatively limited empirical evaluation.  
- Sentiment analysis and flat k-way classification are not the most informative benchmarks; stronger tasks are needed.  
- About two-thirds of a page is devoted to t-SNE visualizations that could be replaced by deeper analysis.  
- Baselines are somewhat soft.  
- Recommendation: accept — the method is interesting, well composed, highly efficient, and likely useful to the community.
