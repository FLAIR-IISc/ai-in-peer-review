- Proposes document vector method by augmenting skip-gram with a regularizer: a global context vector with dropout; combination is novel though components are not new.
- Section 3 provides a detailed and strong analysis of the model's behavior.
- Primary weakness is limited empirical evaluation (only sentiment analysis and k-way classification).
- Too much space (two-thirds of a page) devoted to t-SNE projections instead of more substantive analysis.
- Baselines are weak, a concern shared with other reviewers.
- Reviewer recommends acceptance: algorithm is interesting, well-presented, very efficient, and likely useful to the community.
