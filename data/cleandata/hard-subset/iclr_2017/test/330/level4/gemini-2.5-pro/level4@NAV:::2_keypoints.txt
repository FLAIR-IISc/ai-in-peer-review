- Proposes document embeddings as a weighted average of word vectors with a data-driven regularizer that downweights common words and upweights informative/infrequent ones; reports lower error on sentiment and classification tasks.  
- Technical novelty is limited; method mainly combines existing techniques and is notable for simplicity and computational efficiency.  
- Claiming this is the best approach requires stronger evidence across a broader range of tasks beyond the two evaluated.  
- Clarify the RNN-LM baseline's training objective: was it trained as a language model or fine-tuned to minimize classification error?  
- Clarify how the document vector was extracted from the RNN-LM: final hidden state or average of all hidden states?  
- Missing a contemporary strong baseline: bidirectional LSTM with concatenated final hidden states; comparing against this would strengthen the evaluation.
