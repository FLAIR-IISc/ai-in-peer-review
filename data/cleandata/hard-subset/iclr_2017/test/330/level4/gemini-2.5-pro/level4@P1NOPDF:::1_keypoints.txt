- Proposes a method to compute document vectors with a skip-gram style objective.  
- Model uses a global context vector for regularization combined with several forms of dropout.  
- Individual components are not novel, but their particular combination appears novel.  
- Section 3 provides a detailed and valuable analysis of the model's behavior.  
- Main weakness is limited empirical evaluation: sentiment and k-way classification are not very challenging or insightful benchmarks.  
- Nearly two-thirds of a page spent on t-SNE projections that could be better used for deeper analysis.  
- Baselines are relatively weak, a concern echoed by other reviewers.  
- Reviewer leans toward acceptance despite evaluation issues.  
- Paper is interesting, well-presented, highly efficient, and likely useful to other researchers.
