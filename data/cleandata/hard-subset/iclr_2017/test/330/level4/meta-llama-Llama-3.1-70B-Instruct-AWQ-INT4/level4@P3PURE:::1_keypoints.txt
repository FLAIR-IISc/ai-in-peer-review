- Proposes document vectors via a skip-gram style learner with a global context regularizer and varied dropout; the specific combination seems novel.  
- Section 3 provides a detailed and noteworthy analysis of model behavior.  
- Primary drawback is a relatively weak empirical evaluation.  
- Chosen tasks (sentiment analysis and k-way classification) are not very compelling; other tasks could offer deeper insight.  
- Spending two-thirds of a page on t-SNE projections is unnecessary and crowds out more useful analysis.  
- Agrees with other reviewers that the baselines are weak/soft.  
- Overall recommendation: accept — the algorithm is interesting, well-composed, efficient, and likely valuable to readers.
