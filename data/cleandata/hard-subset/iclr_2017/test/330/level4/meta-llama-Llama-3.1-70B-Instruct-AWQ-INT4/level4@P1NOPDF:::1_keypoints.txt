- Proposes document vector method using skip-gram style learning with a global context vector regularizer and varied dropout rates; combination is novel.
- Individual components of the method are not new.
- Section 3 provides detailed, valuable analysis of model behavior.
- Empirical evaluation is relatively weak.
- Chosen tasks (sentiment analysis and k-way classification) are not the most compelling; more challenging tasks should be explored.
- Two-thirds of a page spent on t-SNE projections is unnecessary and could be better used for additional analysis or experiments.
- Use of soft baselines and reduced evaluation is disappointing to other reviewers.
- Overall recommendation: accept — algorithm is interesting, well-composed, efficient, and likely useful to readers.
