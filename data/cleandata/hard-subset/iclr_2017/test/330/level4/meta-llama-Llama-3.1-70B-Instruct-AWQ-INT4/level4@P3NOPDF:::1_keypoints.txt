- Proposes a novel document embedding method using a skip-gram style objective with a global context vector regularizer and multiple dropout techniques.
- Individual components are not new, but their specific combination appears original.
- Section 3 provides a detailed, insightful analysis of model behavior.
- Main drawback is a relatively limited empirical evaluation.
- Chosen tasks (sentiment analysis and k-way classification) are not the most compelling or challenging.
- Excessive space devoted to t-SNE projections (two-thirds of a page) that could be used for further analysis or results.
- Agrees with other reviewers about relying on weak/soft baselines.
- Despite weaknesses, the algorithm is intriguing, well-composed, and efficient.
- Reviewer recommends acceptance; strengths outweigh weaknesses and the paper likely contributes useful ideas.
