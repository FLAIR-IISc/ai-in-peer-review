- Proposes a novel document embedding method combining a skip-gram style learner, a global context vector regularizer, and various dropout techniques.  
- Individual components are not new, but their combination appears to be a unique contribution.  
- Section 3 provides a detailed and appreciated analysis of model behavior.  
- Primary drawback is a relatively limited empirical evaluation.  
- Evaluation tasks (sentiment analysis and k-way classification) are not the most compelling; more interesting tasks should be explored.  
- Excessive space devoted to t-SNE projections (two-thirds of a page) is unnecessary and could be replaced by deeper analysis.  
- Uses weak/soft baselines, which other reviewers also criticized.  
- Overall recommendation: paper warrants acceptance—algorithm is interesting, well-composed, efficient, and likely useful to readers.
