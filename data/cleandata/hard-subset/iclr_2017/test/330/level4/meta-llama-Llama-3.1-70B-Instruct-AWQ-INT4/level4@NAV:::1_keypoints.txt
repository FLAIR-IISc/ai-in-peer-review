- Proposes a novel document embedding method combining skip-gram learning, a global context vector regularizer, and variable dropout rates.
- Individual components are not novel, but their combined application is a unique contribution.
- Section 3 provides an in-depth and noteworthy analysis of model behavior.
- Empirical evaluation is limited, primarily using sentiment analysis and k-way classification tasks.
- More diverse and challenging downstream tasks are needed to better demonstrate effectiveness.
- Extensive use of t-SNE projections is overused and could be replaced by deeper analyses.
- Reviewer and others express disappointment about reliance on soft baselines.
- Algorithm is well-composed, efficient, and offers potentially useful ideas for the community.
- Reviewer recommends acceptance despite evaluation limitations.
