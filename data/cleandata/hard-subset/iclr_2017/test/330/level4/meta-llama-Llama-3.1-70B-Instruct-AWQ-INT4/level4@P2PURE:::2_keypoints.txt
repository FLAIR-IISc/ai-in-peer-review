- Proposes averaging word embeddings with a data-dependent regularizer that upweights informative/rare words and downweights common words.  
- Evaluated on sentiment analysis and document classification, reporting lower error rates than baseline embedding methods.  
- Lacks substantial technical novelty; techniques are mostly existing and the main merit is simplicity and efficiency.  
- Requests broader evaluation on more diverse tasks to validate general efficacy.  
- Unclear whether the RNN-LM is trained as a language model or directly to minimize classification error.  
- Unclear whether the document representation uses the final hidden state or the average of all hidden states.  
- Recommends comparison to a bidirectional LSTM that concatenates final hidden states on classification and sentiment tasks.
