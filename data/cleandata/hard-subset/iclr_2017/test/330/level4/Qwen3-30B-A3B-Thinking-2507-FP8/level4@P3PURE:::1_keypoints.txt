- Proposes document vector learning using a skip-gram style objective augmented with a global context vector regularizer and multiple dropout variants.  
- Individual components aren’t novel, but their specific combination is a meaningful contribution.  
- Section 3 provides a detailed and appreciated behavioral analysis of the method.  
- Empirical evaluation is narrow, limited to sentiment analysis and k-way classification tasks.  
- Excessive space (two-thirds of a page) devoted to t-SNE visualizations rather than deeper performance analysis.  
- Comparison to soft baselines is inadequate, aligning with other reviewers’ concerns.  
- Method is clearly presented and computationally efficient.  
- Despite limited empirical breadth, the approach is practical and valuable, justifying acceptance.
