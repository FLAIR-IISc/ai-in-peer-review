- Proposes learning document vectors with a skip-gram style objective augmented by a global context vector regularizer and dropout variants.
- Individual components are not novel, but their specific combination is a meaningful contribution.
- Section 3 provides a thorough analysis of model behavior.
- Empirical evaluation is underdeveloped and too narrow, focusing mainly on sentiment analysis and k-way classification.
- Excessive space devoted to t-SNE visualizations reduces room for deeper analysis or additional experiments.
- Needs stronger soft baselines to better contextualize empirical gains.
- Algorithm is interesting, well-motivated, and computationally efficient.
- Reviewer recommends acceptance despite empirical limitations.
