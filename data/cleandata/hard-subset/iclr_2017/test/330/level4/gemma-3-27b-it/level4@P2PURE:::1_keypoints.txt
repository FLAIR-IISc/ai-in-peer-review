- Proposes learning document vectors with a skip-gram model augmented by a global context regularizer and dropout.
- Individual components are not novel, but their combination is a unique contribution.
- Section 3 offers a thorough and valuable analysis of model behavior.
- Main weakness is a limited empirical evaluation.
- Chosen tasks (sentiment analysis and k-way classification) are uninspired and narrow.
- Overuse of t-SNE visualizations; space would be better spent on deeper analysis.
- Evaluation lacks strong baseline comparisons, as noted by other reviewers.
- Recommendation: accept — method is interesting, well-designed, computationally efficient, and likely useful to researchers.
