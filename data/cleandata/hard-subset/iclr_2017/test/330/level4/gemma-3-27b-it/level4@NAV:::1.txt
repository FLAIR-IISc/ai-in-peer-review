This paper introduces a document vector representation learning approach based on a skip-gram model, enhanced with a global context vector regularizer and dropout techniques. Although each individual component is established, the specific combination presented here appears novel. I particularly valued the thorough behavioral analysis provided in Section 3.

A primary limitation of this work lies in the scope of the empirical evaluation. The chosen tasks – sentiment analysis and k-way classification – may not fully demonstrate the potential of the method, and the extensive use of space for t-SNE visualizations could have been better utilized for additional analysis. 

Despite a somewhat limited evaluation, and acknowledging concerns raised by other reviewers regarding baseline comparisons, I recommend acceptance. The algorithm is interesting and well-constructed, computationally efficient, and offers potentially useful ideas for the wider research community.
