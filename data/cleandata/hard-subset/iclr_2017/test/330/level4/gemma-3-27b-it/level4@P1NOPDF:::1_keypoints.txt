- Proposes document vector learning via a skip-gram approach with a global context regularizer and dropout.
- Individual components are not novel, but their combination appears to be.
- Section 3 provides a detailed and appreciated analysis of model behavior.
- Main weakness is a limited empirical evaluation.
- Chosen tasks (sentiment analysis and k-way classification) are uninspired; more challenging benchmarks are needed.
- t-SNE visualizations are excessive (about two-thirds of a page) and should be replaced with additional analysis.
- Evaluation lacks strong soft baseline comparisons, echoing other reviewers’ concerns.
- Reviewer recommends acceptance despite the reduced evaluation scope.
- Algorithm is interesting, well-designed, computationally efficient, and likely useful to other researchers.
