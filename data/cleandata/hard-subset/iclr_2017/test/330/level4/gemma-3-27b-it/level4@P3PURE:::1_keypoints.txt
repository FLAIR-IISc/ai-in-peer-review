- Proposes learning document vectors via skip-gram with a novel regularizer: a global context vector plus dropout.  
- Combination of known components is a worthwhile contribution.  
- Section 3 provides a particularly detailed and appreciated analysis of model behavior.  
- Method is well-designed and computationally efficient.  
- Limited empirical evaluation is the primary weakness.  
- Evaluation tasks (sentiment analysis and k-way classification) are uninspired; stronger, more challenging benchmarks needed.  
- Excessive t-SNE projections waste space that could be used for more analysis or experiments.  
- Needs stronger soft baseline comparisons, as other reviewers noted.  
- Despite weaknesses, reviewer recommends acceptance and believes the ideas will be valuable to researchers.
