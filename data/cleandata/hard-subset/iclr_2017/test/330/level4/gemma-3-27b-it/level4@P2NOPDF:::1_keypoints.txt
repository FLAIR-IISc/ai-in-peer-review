- Proposes document embeddings via skip-gram enhanced with a global context vector regularizer and dropout.  
- Combination of established techniques is novel.  
- Section 3 provides a thorough and valuable behavioral analysis.  
- Main weakness: empirical evaluation is limited in scope.  
- Evaluation tasks (sentiment analysis, k-way classification) are conventional and may not showcase the method’s full potential.  
- Excessive t-SNE visualizations occupy space that could be used for deeper analysis or additional experiments.  
- Lacks comparison to stronger, more recent baselines.  
- Despite evaluation limits, reviewer recommends acceptance due to an interesting, well-designed, and efficient algorithm.  
- Proposed ideas are practical, performant, and likely useful to other researchers.
