- Proposes a novel document vector representation method using skip-gram with a global context vector regularizer and dropout.  
- The individual components are not novel, but their unique combination is a notable contribution.  
- Section 3 provides a commendable, in-depth examination of model behavior.  
- Major limitation: empirical evaluation is insufficiently comprehensive.  
- Benchmark choices (sentiment analysis and k-way classification) are not the most compelling.  
- Reliance on extensive t-SNE projections; should be replaced with deeper analytical evaluation.  
- Concurs with other reviewers that baselines used are weak/soft.  
- Despite evaluation concerns, recommends acceptance because the algorithm is intriguing, well-structured, efficient, and likely valuable to readers.
