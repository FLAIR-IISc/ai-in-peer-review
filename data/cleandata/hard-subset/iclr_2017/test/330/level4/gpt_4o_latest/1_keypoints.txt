- Proposes document vector learning via a skip-gram style approach with a global context vector regularizer and various dropout mechanisms.
- Individual components are not novel, but their specific combination is innovative.
- Section 3 provides a detailed, insightful, and well-executed analysis of model behavior.
- Main limitation is a relatively weak empirical evaluation.
- Sentiment analysis and k-way classification are not the most compelling tasks to demonstrate the method's utility.
- Spending 2/3 of a page on t-SNE projections is a missed opportunity for deeper analysis or stronger evaluation.
- Reviewer concurs with others that the paper uses soft/insufficient baselines.
- Despite reservations, paper merits acceptance because the algorithm is interesting, efficient, and likely valuable to other researchers.
