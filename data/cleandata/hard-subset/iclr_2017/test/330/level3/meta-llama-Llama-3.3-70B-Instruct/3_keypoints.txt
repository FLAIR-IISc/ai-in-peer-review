- Claims the model is computationally efficient and outperforms several recent methods by a significant margin.  
- Provides theoretical motivation linking the GCN to the Weisfeiler-Lehman algorithm and demonstrates effectiveness on multiple network datasets.  
- Decision: accept the paper.
