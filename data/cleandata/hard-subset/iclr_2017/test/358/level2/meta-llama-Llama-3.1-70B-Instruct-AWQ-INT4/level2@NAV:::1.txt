**Summary of the Paper's Claims**

The paper proposes a novel approach to learning representations of datasets, rather than individual datapoints, using a neural network-based model called the Neural Statistician. The model is designed to learn summary statistics of datasets in an unsupervised fashion, allowing for efficient learning from new datasets for both unsupervised and supervised tasks. The authors claim that their approach can be used for various tasks, including clustering datasets, transferring generative models to new datasets, selecting representative samples of datasets, and classifying previously unseen classes.

**Decision**

2. **Technical Soundness**: The paper presents a well-motivated and technically sound approach, with a clear explanation of the model architecture, training procedure, and experimental results.
