**Summary of the Paper**

The paper introduces a novel approach to learning representations of datasets, rather than individual datapoints, using a neural network called the Neural Statistician. The model is based on a variational autoencoder (VAE) that learns to compute summary statistics of datasets in an unsupervised manner. The authors demonstrate the effectiveness of their approach on various tasks, including clustering, transferring generative models to new datasets, selecting representative samples, and classifying previously unseen classes.
