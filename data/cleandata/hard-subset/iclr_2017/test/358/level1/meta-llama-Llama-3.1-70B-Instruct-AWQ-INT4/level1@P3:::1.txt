**Summary of Contributions**

The paper proposes a novel extension of the variational autoencoder (VAE) model, called the Neural Statistician, which can learn to compute summary statistics of datasets in an unsupervised fashion. The model is designed to work with datasets, rather than individual datapoints, and can be used for various tasks such as clustering datasets, transferring generative models to new datasets, selecting representative samples of datasets, and classifying previously unseen classes. The authors demonstrate the effectiveness of their approach on several experiments, including clustering synthetic 1-D datasets, spatial MNIST, OMNIGLOT, and YouTube Faces.
