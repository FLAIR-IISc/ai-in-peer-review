- Paper proposes a hierarchical VAE with a top-layer abstract context unit that summarizes a dataset.
- Model demonstrates “learning to learn,” inferring distributions from few examples.
- Solid contribution to one-/few-shot learning: conceptually simple, elegant, and effective.
- Approach is simpler than recent one-shot methods and is grounded in unsupervised representation learning.
- Paper is clearly written and enjoyable to read.
- Title is overly grandiose and “statistician” is a misleading term for the method.
- Experiments are well chosen and few-shot results are strong given the method’s simplicity.
- Spatial MNIST is an interesting and potentially useful toy benchmark.
- Figure 4 inputs look overly dense—method should perhaps recognize distributions with fewer samples.
- Red points in Figure 4 do not appear to correspond to the meaningful points claimed in the text.
- Reviewer asks whether the authors will release the code.
