- Primary contribution: introduces dataset-level latent variables in a VAE to move beyond simple VAE graphical structures and enrich latent-variable modeling.

- Terminology issue: “statistician” and “statistic network” are not well justified; recommend using standard terms like “approximate posterior” for clarity.

- Experimental quality: experiments are solid and reviewer appreciated the response about one-shot generation, but language in the paper is ambiguous (esp. end of p.6).

- Clarification needed on generation procedure: confirm whether per-data-point latents z are sampled from the generative model conditioned on the context or taken from the per-data-point approximate posterior.

- Evaluation critique: generated samples are qualitative; recommend reporting principled quantitative metrics such as log p(x | c) (with c from the inferred posterior) or log p(x) on held-out data.

- Comparative suggestion: include quantitative comparisons to a vanilla VAE (without the context latent) to demonstrate the benefit of the dataset-level latent variable.
