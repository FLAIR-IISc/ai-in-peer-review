- Main contribution: introduces dataset-level (context) latent variables for VAEs, extending beyond simple VAE graphical models to richer latent structures.  
- Praise: idea is clearly motivated and well described; experiments are nicely executed.  
- Terminology critique: avoid coining “statistician”/“statistic network”; use the precise term “approximate posterior” instead.  
- One-shot generation procedure needs clearer language—current description (end of p.6) is ambiguous.  
- Author’s intended Figure 5 procedure: compute approximate posterior over the context given an input set, then generate using samples from that posterior.  
- Clarification requested (a): are data-point-specific latents z sampled from the generative model or from the approximate posterior?  
- Evaluation suggestion (b): report principled quantitative metrics such as log p(x | c) (with c from the approximate posterior) or log p(x) on held-out data to compare against a vanilla VAE.
