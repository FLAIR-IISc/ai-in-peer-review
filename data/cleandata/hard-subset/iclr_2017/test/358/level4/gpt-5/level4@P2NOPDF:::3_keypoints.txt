- Proposes a VAE variant with dataset-level latent “context” variables to capture shared structure across examples, pushing beyond simple VAE graphical structures.  
- Paper is well motivated and clearly presented; main contribution is exposing richer latent-variable models to the community.  
- Terminology concern: avoid coining “statistician”/“statistic network”; use standard terms like “approximate posterior over the dataset-level latent variable” or “inference network.”  
- “One-shot generation” wording is unclear and Figure 5’s procedure needs clarification on how generation is performed from the input set.  
- Clarify whether per-datapoint latents z are sampled from the generative model p(z|c) or taken from the approximate posterior q(z|x,c).  
- Experiments lack quantitative likelihood-based evaluation; recommend reporting log p(x|c), log p(x), or ELBOs for held-out datasets and comparing to a vanilla VAE.  
- Overall: strong and timely contribution; clearer terminology and quantitative one-shot evaluations would significantly strengthen the paper.
