- Paper proposes predicting from sets via a hierarchical VAE with a top-level context summarizing a dataset.
- Model demonstrates “learn to learn” behavior, inferring distributions from few examples.
- Welcome addition to one-/few-shot learning literature; conceptually simple and elegant.
- Appears to perform well and is simpler than other recent one-shot methods.
- Grounded in unsupervised representation learning and clearly written.
- Title is overly grandiose and the "statistician" framing is misleading.
- Experiments are well chosen and few-shot results are solid given method simplicity.
- Spatial MNIST dataset is interesting and could be a useful toy benchmark.
- Concern that input examples in Figure 4 look overly dense; method might recognize distributions with fewer samples.
- Minor nitpick: red points in Figure 4 don't seem to match the claimed meaningful points.
- Asks whether the authors will release the code.
