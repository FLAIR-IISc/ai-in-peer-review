- Introduces a VAE variant that models dataset-level latent variables; idea is clearly motivated and well described.
- Major contribution: extends VAEs beyond simple graphical models to more complex dataset-level structures.
- Critique: unclear rationale for calling the model a "statistician"; summarizing a dataset via an approximate posterior is not the only approach.
- Recommendation: avoid new terms like "statistic network" and instead use the more accurate phrase "approximate posterior".
- Experiments are well conducted and the authors' response about one-shot generation was appreciated.
- Request clarification of the "one-shot generation" wording (especially end of page 6) and the procedure shown in Figure 5.
- Question (a): Are the data point–dependent vectors z generated by the forward model or sampled from the approximate posterior?
- Suggestion (b): Quantify sample quality by reporting log p(x|c) or log p(x) on held-out data rather than relying on subjective visual quality.
- Expectation: the model’s log-probability performance versus a vanilla VAE would likely be impressive; no clear reason to omit this analysis.
