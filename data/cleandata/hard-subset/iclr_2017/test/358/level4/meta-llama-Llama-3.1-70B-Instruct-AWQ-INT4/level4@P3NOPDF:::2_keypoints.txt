- Reviewer submitted late due to OpenReview technical difficulties.
- Paper proposes a hierarchical VAE with a top-layer context unit to learn to predict distributions from sets.
- Method demonstrates few-shot "learning to learn" capability and strong empirical performance.
- Approach is conceptually simple, elegant, relies on unsupervised representation learning, and is a useful contribution to one/few-shot literature.
- Title overstates links to statistical inference; "statistician" claim is tenuous unless interpreted very broadly.
- Experimental design is appropriate and results appear robust given method simplicity.
- Spatial MNIST dataset is a useful toy benchmark contribution.
- Figure 4 concerns: inputs seem dense (should need fewer samples) and red points do not appear to match claimed meaningful points.
- Authors should release code to facilitate reproducibility and follow-up research.
