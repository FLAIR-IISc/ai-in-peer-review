- Proposes a hierarchical VAE with a top-layer abstract context unit to summarize datasets and predict distributions from sets.  
- Method enables “learning to learn” and few-shot distribution estimation from small numbers of examples.  
- Conceptually simple, elegant, unsupervised approach that is simpler than many recent one-shot learning methods.  
- Experimental performance appears satisfactory and few-shot results seem robust given method simplicity.  
- Paper is clearly written and enjoyable to read.  
- Title is overly grandiose and the use of “statistician” is misleading or loosely applied.  
- Experiments are well-chosen; spatial MNIST is an interesting toy benchmark.  
- Inputs in Figure 4 look very dense, raising questions about why so many samples are required to recognize the distribution.  
- Red points in Figure 4 do not seem to correspond to meaningful points as claimed.  
- Authors should release code to facilitate further research and experimentation.
