- Introduces a VAE variant that models dataset-level latent variables; idea is clearly motivated and well described.
- Main contribution: moves beyond simple VAE graphical models by introducing dataset-level structure to deep learning.
- Decision to accept based on a novel, well-motivated extension and experiments demonstrating effectiveness across tasks.
- Paper is well-written; the "statistic network" / "neural statistician" is well-motivated and related work is thoroughly discussed.
- Minor critique: the term "statistician" is ambiguous; recommend avoiding new nomenclature and using "approximate posterior" instead.
- Experimental clarity issue: language around the experiment (end of page 6) and the generation process in Figure 5 needs clarification.
- Question: Are data-point-dependent latent vectors z generated from the forward model or sampled from the approximate posterior in Figure 5?
- Suggestion: quantify sample quality using log p(x|c) or log p(x) on held-out datasets and compare to a vanilla VAE.
- Request more architectural details about the networks used in experiments.
- Request more visualizations, e.g., learned summary statistics and generated samples.
- Ask for more discussion of limitations and potential future work, including requiring many datasets for training.
- Question about extensions: plans to apply the approach to other tasks like image classification or NLP?
