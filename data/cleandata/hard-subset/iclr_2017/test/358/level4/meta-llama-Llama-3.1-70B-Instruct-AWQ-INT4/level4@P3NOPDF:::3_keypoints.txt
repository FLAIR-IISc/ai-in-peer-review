- Introduces a VAE variant with dataset-level latent variables; clear, well-motivated idea.  
- Main contribution: extends VAEs to more complex dataset-level graphical structures.  
- Proposes a "statistician" (or "statistic network") to model datasets, but term seems unnecessary and nonstandard.  
- Recommend using established terminology like "approximate posterior" instead of coining new names.  
- Experiments are well-presented and the one-shot generation response is appreciated.  
- Clarification needed on the generation process: are data point–dependent z vectors drawn from the forward model or the approximate posterior?  
- Claim of "high quality" samples is subjective; authors should report quantitative metrics such as log p(x|c) (c from approximate posterior) or log p(x) on held-out data.  
- Suggest comparing log-probability performance to a vanilla VAE without the context latent to strengthen the evaluation.
