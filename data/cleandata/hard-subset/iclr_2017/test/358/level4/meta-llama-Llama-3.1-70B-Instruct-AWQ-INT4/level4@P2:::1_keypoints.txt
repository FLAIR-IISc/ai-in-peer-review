- Proposes a hierarchical generative model ("Neural Statistician") with dataset-level and example-level latent variables using a double variational bound.  
- Emphasizes modeling unordered sets of datasets and learning dataset summary statistics without supervision.  
- Highlights hierarchical modeling as an important, under-explored problem in deep learning.  
- Recommends accepting the paper due to its novel and well-motivated approach.  
- Demonstrates strong empirical evaluation across tasks: clustering, transfer to new datasets, representative sample selection, and few-shot classification.

- Experimental results are impressive and show versatility of the proposed method.  
- Raises interesting future directions (e.g., hierarchical forecasting and practical applications).
