- Paper proposes a well-motivated VAE variant with dataset-level latent variables; main contribution is extending VAEs beyond simple graphical models.  
- The term "statistician" (and "statistic network") is misleading; prefer clearer terminology such as "approximate posterior" over summary statistics.  
- Experimental results are compelling, but some language (notably end of page 6) needs clarification.  
- Figure 5 workflow needs clarification: are per-data latent vectors z generated by the forward model or sampled from the approximate posterior?  
- Evaluation is qualitative; the paper should report quantitative metrics (e.g., log p(x|c) or log p(x) for held-out data) to rigorously assess one-shot generation.  
- Compare log-probability performance against a standard VAE without the context latent to demonstrate the advantage of the dataset-level latent variable.
