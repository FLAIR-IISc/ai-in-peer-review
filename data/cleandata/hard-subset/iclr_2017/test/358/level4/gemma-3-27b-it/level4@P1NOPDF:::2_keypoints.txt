- Paper proposes learning-to-predict from sets using a hierarchical VAE with a top-layer context unit that summarizes a dataset.
- Model demonstrates "learning to learn," acquiring distributions from few examples.
- Contribution is valuable for one- and few-shot learning.
- Method is conceptually simple, elegant, and appears effective.
- Simpler than recent one-shot work and leverages unsupervised representation learning.
- Paper is clearly written and enjoyable to read.
- Title seems overly ambitious; link to statistical learning is tenuous without a broad interpretation.
- Experimental setup is well-considered and few-shot results are robust given method simplicity.
- Use of spatial MNIST is interesting and could become a useful toy benchmark.
- Concern that input density in Figure 4 is high; model might be expected to recover distributions with fewer samples.
- Minor figure issue: red points in Figure 4 do not clearly match the "meaningful points" described.
- Asks whether the authors will release their code.
