- Introduces dataset-level latent variables in VAEs; motivation and concept are clear and well-explained.  
- Significant contribution: departs from standard simple VAE graphical models to explore more complex architectures.  
- Terminology concern: calling the summary network a “statistician” or “statistic network” is unnecessarily restrictive.  
- Recommendation: avoid new terms and use the precise term “approximate posterior” throughout.  
- Experiments are encouraging and the authors responded to prior one-shot generation questions, but the one-shot procedure description (esp. page 6) needs clarification.  
- Clarification requested (a): are data point-dependent latents z generated by the forward model or sampled directly from the approximate posterior?  
- Clarification/request (b): generated samples lack quantitative support — request reporting log p(x|c) or log p(x) on held-out data to rigorously evaluate one-shot generation.  
- Criticism: omission of standard VAE evaluation metrics is unjustified; comparisons to a standard VAE without dataset-level context are necessary.
