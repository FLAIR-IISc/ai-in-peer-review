- Introduces a VAE variant with dataset-level latent variables; primary contribution is moving beyond simple VAE graphical models to more complex structures.

- The term "statistician" (and "statistic network") is poorly justified; paper should use the clearer term "approximate posterior" instead of new terminology.

- Experiments are well-executed, but the description of “one-shot generation” (end of p.6) needs clearer language.

- Clarify whether data-point-dependent vectors z are produced by the forward model or sampled directly from the approximate posterior.

- Visual sample quality is promising but unsupported quantitatively; recommend reporting log p(x|c) or log p(x) on held-out data.

- Expectation that the model will show strong log-probability performance versus a standard VAE, so including such comparisons is crucial.
