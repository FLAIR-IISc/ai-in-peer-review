- Presents a novel VAE extension with dataset-level latent variables; motivation is sound and approach well articulated.
- Main strength is departing from the simple VAE graphical model and introducing a more sophisticated architecture.
- Terminology critique: calling the component a “statistician” is restrictive; prefer established terms like “approximate posterior” and consider alternatives (e.g., MLE) for dataset summarization.
- Experimental results are encouraging and authors addressed a prior one-shot generation query.
- One-shot generation description needs refinement (especially end of p.6); Figure 5 interpreted as computing posterior over context c, sampling c, then generating data via the forward model.
- Clarification requested (a): are data-point latent vectors z generated by the forward model or directly sampled from the approximate posterior?
- Clarification/requested analysis (b): include quantitative log-probability metrics (log p(x|c) or log p(x) on held-out data) to rigorously evaluate one-shot generation and compare to standard VAE.
