- Positive: The paper proposes a well-motivated VAE variant that models dataset-level latent variables and advances beyond simple VAE graphical structures.  
- Main contribution: Introduces richer graphical model structures for deep learning by modeling dataset-level (context) latents.  
- Terminology concern: The term “statistician” (and “statistic network”) is unclear and unnecessary; prefer the precise term “approximate posterior.”  
- Methodological note: Learning an approximate posterior over summary statistics is not the only approach (e.g., MLE could be used), so avoid coinage of new terms.  
- Experiments: Well-executed and response on “one-shot generation” helpful, but description/language (end of p.6) needs clarification.  
- Clarification requested (a): Are data point-dependent vectors z generated by the forward model or sampled directly from the approximate posterior?  
- Evaluation requested (b): Provide quantitative log-probability evaluations (log p(x|c) or log p(x) on held-out data) to compare against a vanilla VAE rather than only qualitative samples.
