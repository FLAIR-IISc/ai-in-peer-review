- Introduces a VAE variant with dataset-level latent variables; main contribution is moving beyond traditional VAE graphical structures to more complex models.  
- The term “statistician” is unclear; avoid coined terms like “statistic network” and use the precise term “approximate posterior,” noting other summarization methods (e.g., maximum likelihood) exist.  
- Experiments are well-executed and the authors’ response on one-shot generation is appreciated, but the manuscript’s language (especially end of page 6) needs clarification.  
- Clarify Figure 5’s procedure: are data point–dependent vectors z generated by the forward model or sampled directly from the approximate posterior?  
- Generated samples are qualitatively good, but the paper lacks quantitative evaluation; should report log p(x|c) or log p(x) on held-out data and compare to a vanilla VAE.
