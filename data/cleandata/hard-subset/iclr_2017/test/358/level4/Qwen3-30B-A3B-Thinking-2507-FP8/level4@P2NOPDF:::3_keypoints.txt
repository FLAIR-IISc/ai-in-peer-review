- Novel contribution: introduces dataset-level latent variables in a VAE, extending per-sample latent structure and addressing dataset-wide dependencies.  
- Well-motivated core idea and clear articulation.  
- Terminology issue: "statistic network" is misleading; use "approximate posterior network" or standard probabilistic terminology.  
- Experimental weakness: evaluation is qualitative (visually plausible samples) but lacks quantitative rigor.  
- Missing metrics: authors should report log p(x | c) (with c ~ q(c|X)) or log p(x) on held-out datasets to quantify modeling of dataset-level structure.  
- One-shot generation claim unclear: clarify whether c is sampled from q(c|X) or prior p(c) in Figure 5 and in generation experiments.  
- Define "one-shot" precisely (e.g., whether all samples are generated from a single c) and support it with the requested log-probability metrics.  
- Overall: promising conceptual advance but needs terminological fixes and rigorous quantitative evaluation to validate claims.
