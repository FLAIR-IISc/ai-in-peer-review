- Presents a VAE variant modeling dataset-level latent variables with clear motivation and description.  
- Main contribution: extends standard VAE graphical model to more complex dataset-level architectures.  
- Term "statistician" is misleading; terminology should be precise (use "approximate posterior" instead of "statistic network").  
- Experiments are well-executed and the authors' response on one-shot generation is appreciated.  
- Clarification needed for Figure 5: are data-dependent vectors z generated by the forward model or sampled from the approximate posterior?  
- Claim of high sample quality lacks quantitative validation.  
- Recommend reporting log p(x|c) (with c from the approximate posterior) or log p(x) on held-out data to properly evaluate one-shot generation.  
- Suggest comparing these likelihood metrics against a vanilla VAE (no context latents) to demonstrate statistical advantage.
