- Introduces a VAE variant with dataset-level latent variables; clear motivation and main contribution is extending VAE graphical structure.  
- The term "statistician" (and "statistic network") is unclear and misleading; recommend using "approximate posterior" instead.  
- Experiments are well-executed and the authors' response on one-shot generation is appreciated, but the description at the end of page 6 needs clarification.  
- Clarify whether data-point-dependent context vectors z are generated by the forward model or drawn from the approximate posterior.  
- The one-shot generation claim lacks quantitative evaluation; report log p(x | c) (with c sampled from the approximate posterior) or log p(x) on held-out datasets.  
- Compare log-probability performance against a vanilla VAE (without context latent) — this metric is essential and likely informative.
