- Introduces a VAE variant with dataset-level latent variables; clear motivation and main contribution is richer graphical-model structure beyond traditional VAEs.  
- The term "statistician" is misleading; avoid "statistic network" and consistently call that component the "approximate posterior."  
- Experiments are well-executed and the authors’ response on one-shot generation was appreciated.  
- Clarification requested for Fig. 5: confirm pipeline—compute approximate posterior over the context vector for an input set, sample from it, and feed those samples into the forward model.  
- Question (a): are the data-dependent vectors z generated by the forward model or sampled directly from the approximate posterior?  
- Question (b): sample-quality claims require quantitative evaluation—report log p(x|c) or log p(x) on held-out data to compare to a vanilla VAE and demonstrate performance gains.
