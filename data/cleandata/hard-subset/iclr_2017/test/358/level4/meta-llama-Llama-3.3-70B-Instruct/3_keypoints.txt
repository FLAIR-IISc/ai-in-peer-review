- Proposes a well-motivated VAE variant with dataset-level latent variables, clearly described and extending standard VAE graphical models.  
- Main contribution is introducing more complex dataset-level structure to the VAE framework.  
- Terminology critique: "statistician" is unclear and misleading; avoid coining "statistic network" and use accurate terms like "approximate posterior."  
- Experiments are well-conducted and the authors’ response on "one-shot generation" is appreciated.  
- Request clarification of language at the end of page 6 and explicit description of the procedure used for Figure 5 (compute approximate posterior over context c, then generate samples from the forward model).  
- Clarify whether data-point-dependent vectors z are generated by the forward model or sampled from the approximate posterior.  
- Quantification needed: report log p(x|c) (with c sampled from the approximate posterior) or log p(x) on held-out data to evaluate one-shot generation.  
- Recommend comparing log-probability performance against a standard VAE without the context latent to demonstrate empirical gains.
