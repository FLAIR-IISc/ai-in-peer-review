- Proposes a VAE variant with dataset-level latent variables; motivation is clear and model well-described.  
- Key contribution: explores richer graphical models than standard VAEs, adding expressivity.  
- The term "statistician" is unclear; recommend using standard terminology like "approximate posterior" instead of coining "statistic network."  
- Experiments are well-executed and authors addressed a prior question about one-shot generation.  
- One-shot generation (Figure 5) needs clearer description: compute approximate posterior over context c from a set, then generate conditioned on draws from that posterior.  
- Clarify whether instance-specific latent z are sampled from the prior or inferred via the approximate posterior during generation.  
- Request quantitative evaluation (e.g., log p(x|c) or marginal log p(x)) to rigorously assess one-shot generation rather than qualitative inspection.  
- Reviewer expects the model would improve log-likelihood over a vanilla VAE and questions why such quantitative analysis was omitted.
