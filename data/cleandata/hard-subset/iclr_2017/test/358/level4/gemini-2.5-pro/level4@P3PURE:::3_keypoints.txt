- Introduces a VAE variant with dataset-level latent variables; core idea is well-motivated and clearly described.
- Primary contribution: moves beyond simple VAE graphical models to more complex dataset-level structures.
- Terminology concern: using "statistician"/"statistic network" is unjustified; prefer the standard term "approximate posterior" or justify new wording.
- Experiments are well-executed, but the "one-shot generation" evaluation and the wording at the end of page 6 need clarification.
- Clarify Figure 5 procedure: when generating conditioned on c, are datapoint latent vectors z sampled from their approximate posterior or from the prior?
- Request quantitative evaluation for one-shot generation (e.g., log p(x|c) with c from the approximate posterior or marginal log p(x)) rather than only qualitative samples.
- Recommend comparing log-likelihoods to a vanilla VAE (without context) to substantiate performance claims.
