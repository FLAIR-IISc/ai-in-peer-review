- Main contribution: proposes a VAE variant with dataset-level latent variables, moving beyond the simple graphical structure of traditional VAEs; idea is well-motivated and clearly described.  
- Terminology concern: calling the method a "statistician" and using "statistic network" is misleading; the paper should prefer the more accurate term "approximate posterior."  
- Experiments: generally nice; reviewer appreciated the authors' response about one-shot generation.  
- Clarification needed: language around one-shot generation (end of p.6) and the procedure in Figure 5 should be clarified.  
- Specific question (a): are the data-point-dependent vectors z generated from the forward model or taken from the approximate posterior?  
- Specific suggestion (b): quantify one-shot generation by reporting log p(x|c) or log p(x) on held-out datasets and compare to a vanilla VAE — reviewer expects this comparison and sees no reason it was omitted.
