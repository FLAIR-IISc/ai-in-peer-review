- Reviewer had technical issues with OpenReview causing a late review.
- Paper proposes a hierarchical VAE with a top-layer context unit that summarizes a dataset to predict from sets of points.
- Method effectively "learns to learn," acquiring the ability to infer distributions from small numbers of examples (few-shot learning).
- Approach is conceptually simple, elegant, unsupervised, and simpler than many recent one-shot methods.
- Empirical performance is good; experiments are well chosen and few-shot results are solid.
- Paper is clearly written and enjoyable to read.
- Paper title/name is overly grandiose; calling it "statistician"-like is misleading.
- Spatial MNIST dataset is a useful toy benchmark, but Figure 4 inputs look denser than expected and the model might be expected to recognize distributions with fewer samples.
- Nitpick: red points in Figure 4 do not appear to correspond to the meaningful points claimed in the text.
- Reviewer asks whether the authors will release the code.
