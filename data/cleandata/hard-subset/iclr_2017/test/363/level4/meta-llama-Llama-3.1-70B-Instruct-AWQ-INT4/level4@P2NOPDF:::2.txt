This paper proposes a compare-aggregate framework that leverages word-level matching followed by aggregation using convolutional neural networks. The authors conduct a comprehensive evaluation, comparing six distinct comparison functions across four datasets and providing extensive experimental results, which are benchmarked against various published baselines.

The paper is well-written overall, effectively conveying the proposed methodology and experimental findings. A few specific comments are provided below to further enhance the manuscript.

Firstly, a minor typographical error was identified on page 4, line 5, where "including a some" should be corrected to "including some."

Regarding the methodology, it would be beneficial to provide insight into the benefits of incorporating the preprocessing and attention steps. To facilitate a deeper understanding of their contribution, the authors may consider including results obtained without these steps, enabling a direct comparison and highlighting their impact on the overall framework.

Additionally, Figure 2 is challenging to interpret, particularly when printed in hard copy. Enhancing the figure's quality would significantly improve its readability and facilitate a clearer understanding of the presented information.
