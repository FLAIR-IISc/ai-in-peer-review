This paper presents a compare-aggregate framework, leveraging word-level matching and convolutional neural network-based aggregation. A comprehensive evaluation is conducted, comparing six distinct comparison functions across four diverse datasets, with experimental results benchmarked against multiple established baselines.

The overall writing quality of the paper is commendable.

Some specific suggestions for improvement are as follows:

* On page 4, line 5, a minor correction is needed: "including a some" should be revised to "including some".
* To further clarify the contribution of the preprocessing and attention step, it would be beneficial to provide supplementary results that demonstrate the impact of omitting this step.
* Figure 2 is somewhat difficult to decipher, particularly in its printed form. Enhancing the figure's quality would significantly improve its readability.
