- Proposes a compare-aggregate model for semantic sequence comparison tasks (QA, entailment).
- Architecture: LSTM with attention, element-wise comparison, then CNN for aggregation.
- Main contribution: empirical comparison of matching methods, showing element-wise subtraction and multiplication perform best across four datasets.
- Limitation: work is incremental and offers limited novelty.
- Suggested improvement: lacks deeper qualitative analysis of how comparison functions behave across diverse sentence types.
