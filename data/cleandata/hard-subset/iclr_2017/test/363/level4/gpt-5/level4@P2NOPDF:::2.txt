This paper proposes a compare-aggregate framework that performs token-level matching followed by aggregation using convolutional neural networks. It systematically evaluates six comparison functions and assesses performance on four datasets. The authors provide extensive experimental results and compare against a range of published baselines. Overall, the paper is clearly written.
