This paper introduces a compare–aggregate architecture that conducts word-level alignment followed by aggregation via convolutional neural networks. The authors benchmark six distinct comparison functions and evaluate them on four datasets. The study provides extensive experimental results and compares them against a broad set of published baselines.

Overall, the manuscript is clearly written.
