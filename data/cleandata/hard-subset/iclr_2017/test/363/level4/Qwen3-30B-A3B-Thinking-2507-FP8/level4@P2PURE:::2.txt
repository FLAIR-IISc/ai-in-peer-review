

This paper introduces a compare-aggregate framework that performs word-level matching followed by aggregation using convolutional neural networks. It systematically compares six distinct comparison functions and evaluates their performance across four benchmark datasets, reporting extensive experimental results that are rigorously compared against multiple published baselines.  
The paper is well written overall.
