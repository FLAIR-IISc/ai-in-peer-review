- Introduces a compare-aggregate model for semantic sequence comparison tasks (e.g., QA, entailment).  
- Core framework: attentive LSTMs → element-wise comparison (subtraction/multiplication) → CNN aggregation.  
- Primary contribution: systematic evaluation of multiple comparison operations for sequence matching.  
- Empirical finding: element-wise subtraction and multiplication consistently outperform alternatives across four datasets.  
- Results are solid but the contribution is incremental rather than a major conceptual advance.  
- Recommendation: add qualitative analysis of how subtraction, multiplication, and other functions behave across diverse sentence structures.
