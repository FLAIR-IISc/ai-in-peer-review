- Proposes a compare-aggregate model for semantic comparison tasks (e.g., QA, textual entailment).
- Uses attentive LSTM outputs followed by element-wise comparison and a CNN-based aggregation.
- Main contribution is the comparison module evaluating multiple matching strategies.
- Element-wise subtraction and multiplication consistently outperform other strategies across four datasets.
- Overall contribution is incremental with limited novelty.
- Recommends a qualitative analysis of subtraction, multiplication, and other comparison functions across diverse sentence structures to strengthen the paper.
