

This paper introduces a compare-aggregate framework that performs word-level matching followed by aggregation via convolutional neural networks. The authors evaluate six distinct comparison functions across four benchmark datasets and present extensive experimental results, comparing their approach against multiple published baselines. The paper is generally well-written and clearly structured.

Minor revisions are recommended to enhance precision and impact:

*   The preprocessing and attention steps are described but their specific contribution is unclear. Please provide results demonstrating the performance impact of removing these components to clarify their necessity.
*   Figure 2 is difficult to interpret in printed form due to low resolution and overlapping elements. Please enhance the figure’s resolution and labeling for better readability in all formats.
