This paper introduces a compare-aggregate framework for performing word-level matching, followed by aggregation using convolutional neural networks. The authors compare six different comparison functions and evaluate their performance across four datasets. Extensive experimental results are presented and benchmarked against a variety of published baselines.

The paper is generally well-written.

A few specific comments:
* Page 4, line 5: “including a some” should be “including some”.
* It would be helpful to understand the benefit of the preprocessing and attention steps; could the authors provide results without these components?
* Figure 2 is difficult to read, particularly in printed form. Please improve its clarity and resolution.
