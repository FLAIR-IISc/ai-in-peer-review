This paper proposes a compare-aggregate framework that performs word-level matching followed by aggregation using convolutional neural networks. The authors compare six different comparison functions and evaluate them on four datasets. Extensive experimental results are reported and compared against various published baselines.

The paper is generally well-written.

* Page 4, line 5: “including a some” should read “including some.”
* What is the benefit of the preprocessing and attention step? Could you provide results without it to demonstrate its impact?
* Figure 2 is difficult to read, especially when printed. Please improve its clarity and resolution.
