This paper introduces a compare-aggregate framework utilizing word-level matching and subsequent aggregation via convolutional neural networks. The authors systematically compare six distinct comparison functions, assessing their performance across four datasets. The work is supported by comprehensive experimental results and a thorough comparison to existing published baseline methods. 

The paper is generally well-written and clearly presented.

Specific comments for consideration:
* Page 4, line 5: Please correct “including a some” to “including some”.
* The motivation and impact of the preprocessing and attention mechanisms would be strengthened by presenting results achieved without their inclusion.
* Figure 2’s visual clarity could be improved, particularly for readers using printed copies; please consider enhancing the figure’s resolution or presentation.
