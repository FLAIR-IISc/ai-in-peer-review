- Presents a general soft-attention alignment model for paired-text NLU (align tokens, compare aligned parts, CNN-aggregate to output).  
- Provides detailed empirical analysis of the comparison function component.  
- Recommends acceptance: timely, straightforward, reasonable solution with strong empirical performance.  
- Limitation: model (except SNLI) ignores word order, which may limit performance; suggest noting this in intro/discussion.  
- Attention mechanism resembles Luong et al. (2015); recommend citing a more relevant reference.  
- Concern about NTN overfitting due to many parameters; ask if a reduced output-dimension variant (mapping l→m) was tried.  
- SubMultNN comparison is similar to Mou et al. (2016) sentence-level matching; recommend acknowledging this connection.  
- Question the use of identical preprocessing parameters for question and answer in Eq. (1); allowing different weighting schemes might improve results.
