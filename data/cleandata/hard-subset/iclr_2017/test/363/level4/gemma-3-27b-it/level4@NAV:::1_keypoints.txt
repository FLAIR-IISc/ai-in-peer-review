- Introduces a compare-aggregate model for semantic text sequence comparison tasks (question answering, textual entailment).  
- Architecture: attentive LSTM outputs undergo element-wise comparison, followed by CNN-based aggregation.  
- Main contribution: comparative analysis of matching methods across four datasets, showing element-wise subtraction and multiplication generally improve performance.  
- Limitation: represents an incremental advance with limited novelty.  
- Suggested improvement: include qualitative analysis of subtraction, multiplication, and alternative comparison functions on diverse sentence structures.
