- Proposes a compare-aggregate model for semantic comparison tasks (e.g., QA, textual entailment).  
- Architecture: element-wise comparison of attentive LSTM outputs followed by CNN-based aggregation.  
- Primary contribution: systematic exploration of different sequence-matching methods.  
- Experiments show simple element-wise subtraction and multiplication generally perform best across four datasets.  
- Main weakness: incremental contribution with limited novelty.  
- Suggested improvement: include qualitative evaluation to show how different comparison functions behave on various sentence-pair types.
