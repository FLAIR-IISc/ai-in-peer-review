- Systematically evaluates the compare-aggregate framework across four diverse NLP tasks (MovieQA, InsuranceQA, WikiQA, SNLI).  
- Identifies a gap: prior work tested on limited tasks or didn't systematically evaluate word-level comparison functions.  
- Demonstrates general applicability of the compare-aggregate framework for sequence matching problems.
