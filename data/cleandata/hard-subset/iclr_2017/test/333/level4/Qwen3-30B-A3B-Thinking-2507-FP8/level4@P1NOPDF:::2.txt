

This paper offers a well-motivated analysis of the conditions enabling natural-looking texture generation, with results that are both surprising and thoroughly supported. The core contributions are clearly articulated and the methodological approach is sound. While the evaluation methodology requires further development—as noted by other reviewers—this limitation does not significantly undermine the paper's core contributions. The proposed framework represents a valuable step forward in the field, and the authors' direction for future work is promising. Given the strength of the analysis and the novelty of the insights, I recommend acceptance.
