- Proposes a simple baseline for parametric texture synthesis using multi-scale random filters that sometimes rival VGG-based models.  
- Authors claim texture synthesis may not require deep hierarchical representations or learned feature maps.  
- Reviewer finds the conclusions need more validation, especially regarding the role of hierarchical representations.  
- Both VGG-based and single-layer models produce suboptimal samples and are significantly worse than non-parametric methods.  
- VGG-based model appears to outperform the single-layer model on the inpainting task (Figure 7).  
- Unclear whether a true hierarchical model would handle multi-scale processing more efficiently than using many filters of varying sizes.
