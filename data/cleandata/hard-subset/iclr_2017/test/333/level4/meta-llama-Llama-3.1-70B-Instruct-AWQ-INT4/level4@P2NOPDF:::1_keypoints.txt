- Proposes a simple multi-scale random-filter baseline that can rival VGG-based parametric texture synthesis, arguing deep hierarchical or learned features may not be necessary.
- The paper is interesting and offers valuable insights into texture synthesis.
- The paper's conclusion that deep hierarchical representations are unnecessary requires further validation.
- Generated samples from both the VGG-based and single-layer models are imperfect and much worse than non-parametric methods.
- The VGG-based model appears to outperform the single-layer model in the inpainting task (Fig. 7).
- Suggests exploring hierarchical models (instead of many filter sizes) for more efficient multi-scale handling.
