- Proposes a simple baseline combining multi-scale random filters that can sometimes rival VGG-based texture synthesis, questioning the necessity of deep hierarchical learned features.  
- Reviewer finds the work interesting but says the conclusions about deep hierarchical representations require further validation.  
- Both the VGG-based and single-layer models produce imperfect samples that are significantly inferior to non-parametric methods.  
- VGG-based model performs better in the inpainting task (Figure 7).  
- Suggestion that a hierarchical model might more efficiently handle multi-scale texture synthesis than using many filters of varying sizes.
