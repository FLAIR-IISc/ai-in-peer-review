This paper provides an interesting analysis of the conditions that enable the generation of natural-looking textures. The results are quite surprising, and the analysis is thorough.

I do think the evaluation methods require further refinement, but as other reviewers have mentioned, this could be an interesting line of work moving forward. While this limitation does not detract significantly from the current paper, it is an area that warrants additional attention in future research. Nevertheless, I believe this paper should be accepted, as it presents a compelling contribution to the field.
