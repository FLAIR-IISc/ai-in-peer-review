This paper presents an intriguing analysis of the conditions that facilitate the generation of natural-looking textures. The findings are quite surprising, and the analysis is thorough and well-executed. 

I do think that the evaluation methods could be improved upon, but as other reviewers have noted, this work has the potential to lead to interesting future research directions. While further refinement in evaluation methods would be beneficial, it does not detract significantly from the current paper's contributions. Overall, I believe this paper should be accepted.
