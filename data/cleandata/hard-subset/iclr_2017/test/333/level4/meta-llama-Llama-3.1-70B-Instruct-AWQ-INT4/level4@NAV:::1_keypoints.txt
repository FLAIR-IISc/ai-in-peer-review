- Proposes a simple, robust baseline for parametric texture synthesis.  
- Shows competitive results versus a VGG-based multi-layer, pre-trained filter model in some experiments.  
- Uses multi-scale and random filters to achieve these results.  
- Concludes that texture synthesis may not require deep hierarchical representations or learned feature maps.  
- Work is insightful and thought-provoking.  
- Needs further validation, especially regarding the role of deep hierarchical representations.  
- Generated samples from both VGG and single-layer models are imperfect and worse than non-parametric methods.  
- VGG-based model appears to outperform inpainting (Figure 7).  
- Suggests exploring hierarchical models rather than many filter sizes to handle multi-scale textures more efficiently.
