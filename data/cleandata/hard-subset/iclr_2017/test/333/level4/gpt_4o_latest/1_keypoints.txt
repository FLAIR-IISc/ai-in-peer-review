- Introduces a simple yet effective baseline for parametric texture synthesis.  
- Baseline uses multi-scale random filters and sometimes matches VGG-based multi-layer pre-trained results.  
- Authors claim texture synthesis may not require deep hierarchical representations or learned feature maps.  
- The work is intriguing and thought-provoking.  
- Conclusions need stronger validation, especially about the necessity of deep hierarchical representations.  
- Outputs from both VGG-based and single-layer models remain imperfect and markedly worse than non-parametric methods.  
- VGG-based model appears to outperform the single-layer baseline on inpainting (Figure 7).  
- Suggestion/question: could a hierarchical model be a more efficient way to capture multi-scale features than many varied-size filters?
