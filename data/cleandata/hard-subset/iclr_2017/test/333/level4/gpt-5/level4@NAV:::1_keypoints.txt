- Proposes a simple yet strong baseline for parametric texture synthesis using multi-scale random filters.  
- Experiments show the baseline sometimes rivals a VGG-based, multi-layer pre-trained model.  
- Authors claim texture synthesis may not require deep hierarchical representations or learned feature maps.  
- The work is interesting and insightful but the conclusions need more validation.  
- Generated samples from both VGG and single-layer models are imperfect and substantially worse than non-parametric methods.  
- VGG-based model appears to perform better on the inpainting task (Figure 7).  
- Questions whether a true hierarchical model (vs. many filter sizes) would handle multi-scale structure more efficiently.
