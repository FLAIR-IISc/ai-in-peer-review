- Introduces a simple, effective baseline for parametric texture synthesis using multi-scale random filters.
- Multi-scale random filter textures are competitive with a more complex VGG-based multi-layer pre-trained filter model.
- Authors argue deep hierarchical representations or learned feature maps may not be essential for texture synthesis.
- Contribution is valuable and thought-provoking.
- Claim that deep hierarchical representations are unnecessary requires further investigation.
- Both the VGG model and the single-layer baseline produce lower-quality samples than non-parametric methods.
- VGG-based approach performs better in the inpainting task shown in Figure 7.
- A genuinely hierarchical model (not just multi-scale filters) might be more efficient for handling multi-scale textures.
