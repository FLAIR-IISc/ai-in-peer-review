- Proposes a novel generative model using a gradual denoising process similar to diffusion models but with far fewer steps, yielding much better computational efficiency.
- Inference chain jumps directly to q(z(0)|x) then proceeds in the same forward direction as the generative model, acting as a perturbation that pulls the generative chain toward data (akin to ladder networks).
- The model does not provide a tractable variational bound on the log likelihood.
- Visual sample quality is impressive, especially one-shot inpainting results that are uncommon in existing frameworks.
