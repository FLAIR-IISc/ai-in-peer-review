- Paper applies nonlinear RNNs to model primate RGC spiking and outperforms LN and GLM baselines.  
- Introduces a GLM-RNN hybrid that helps dissect temporal and spatial nonlinearities.  
- RNNs capture complex transient responses poorly modeled by traditional approaches.  
- RNNs achieve high predictive accuracy with relatively small amounts of experimental data.  
- Decision: Accept.  
- Reason 1 for acceptance: Tackles an important, well-defined problem (predictive modeling of sensory neurons) with well-motivated RNNs.  
- Reason 2 for acceptance: Results are scientifically rigorous, show significant improvements, and are validated across multiple datasets.  
- Novelty/Impact: Multitask RNNs for RGCs are a novel advance with implications for neuroscience and retinal prosthetics.  
- Scientific rigor: Methods are well-documented, results robust, and approach works with modest training data.  
- Interpretability: Authors attempt to interpret RNNs (spatio-temporal analyses and hybrid model) but models remain largely black-box.  
- Clarify terminology: The term “movie” and the stated frame rate/monitor refresh association is confusing and should be clarified.  
- Stimulus choice: Using unrelated image sequences may limit ecological validity; testing on dynamic natural scenes (real movies) is recommended.  
- Improve interpretability: More efforts (e.g., visualizing learned temporal dynamics or feature importance) would increase impact.  
- Generalization: Discuss or provide preliminary results on applying the method to higher sensory areas and potential challenges.  
- Q1: Provide details on how the “naturalistic movie stimulus” was constructed and why unrelated image sequences were chosen.  
- Q2: How does RNN performance compare to simpler models (e.g., GLM-RNN) on dynamic stimuli?  
- Q3: Elaborate on specific challenges or limitations encountered when interpreting the RNN’s internal dynamics.  
- Q4: How sensitive are results to hyperparameters and architecture, and could a simpler architecture achieve comparable performance?
