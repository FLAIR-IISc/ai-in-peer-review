- Paper is well-written and demonstrates RNNs can effectively predict retinal neuron firing rates.  
- Main scientific contribution is validation that RNNs work; performance gains are modest for this stimulus-locked task and offer no new biological insight.  
- Work introduces neural network modeling to a domain that has not widely adopted it, with significant potential impact (parallel to McIntosh et al.).  
- Recommend testing the framework with LFP and other neuronal inputs and at shorter discretization time scales.  
- Expect follow-up studies to build on this proof-of-concept and produce more compelling results.  
- Minor: clarify the purpose of the 0.833 ms bins in Section 3.2.  
- Minor: use consistent terminology—use "epoch" throughout.  
- Minor: consider using a log scale for the x-axis in Figure 4 to improve clarity.
