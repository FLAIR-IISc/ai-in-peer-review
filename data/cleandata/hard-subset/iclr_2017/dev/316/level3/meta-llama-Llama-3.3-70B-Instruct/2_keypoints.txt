- Proposes PATE: multiple teacher models train a student via noisy voting to guarantee training-data privacy.  
- Claims strong privacy guarantees while maintaining high utility.  
- Tackles preventing memorization of sensitive training data by models.  
- Well-motivated, building on knowledge aggregation and differential privacy.  
- Provides rigorous theoretical analysis using the moments accountant to bound privacy loss.  
- Empirical results on MNIST and SVHN show state-of-the-art privacy/utility trade-offs.  
- Reviewer’s recommendation: Accept.
