- Claims strong differential privacy guarantees while maintaining high utility, demonstrated on MNIST and SVHN.  
- Framework is general, algorithm-independent, and applicable to non-convex models (deep nets).  
- Uses semi-supervised learning and improved privacy analysis as key contributions.  
- Achieves state-of-the-art privacy/utility trade-offs.

- Acceptance reason 2: Empirical validation shows strong privacy guarantees with competitive accuracy.
