- Paper is high quality with clear, thorough writing and a well-structured narrative.
- Lacks theoretical guarantees on the learning performance of the proposed approach.
- Recommend additional experiments with different architectures or justification for the chosen architecture to validate generality.
- Reported epsilon values are not privately releasable under the authors' technique, preventing meaningful comparison with prior epsilon-delta results.
- Method may not generalize to other natural data types; authors should run experiments on additional datasets and cite them.
- Related work omission: include discussion of differentially-private semi-supervised learning (e.g., Jagannathan et al., 2013).
- Ensure quantitative comparisons in Section C are a fair comparison to GAN results.
- Clarify last paragraph of Section 3.1 to state that training data is the same with respect to the same teacher on the neighboring database, not all teachers.
- Formalize the claimed trade-off between task complexity and available data in Section 4.1.
- Revise discussion and caption of Figure 3 to remove contradictory implications about the reported gap.
