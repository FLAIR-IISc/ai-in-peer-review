- Paper is strong: engaging, well-written, comprehensive, and advances DP deep learning.  
- Lacks theoretical guarantees on learning performance; recommend experiments with different architectures or justification of chosen architecture and generalizability.  
- Reported epsilon values are not privately released; must address this to allow meaningful comparisons with prior work.  
- Method may not generalize to other natural data types; add experiments on additional datasets and explicitly cite datasets used.  
- Related-work coverage is good but should include DP semi-supervised learning literature (e.g., Jagannathan et al., 2013).  
- Ensure Section C’s quantitative comparisons are apples-to-apples with the GAN results.  
- Clarify wording in Section 3.1: “same training data” should mean same teacher on neighboring databases, not across teachers.  
- Formalize or better explain the trade-off limiting the number n of teachers in Section 4.1, especially under i.i.d. assumptions.  
- Revise Figure 3 text and caption to remove contradiction about whether a larger gap is better and the claim that the gap remains under 20%.
