**Summary of the Paper**

The paper proposes an LSTM-based meta-learner model to learn the optimization algorithm used to train another learner neural network classifier in the few-shot learning regime. The meta-learner model is designed to capture both short-term knowledge within a task and long-term knowledge common among all tasks. The model is trained to converge a learner classifier to a good solution quickly on each task, and it also learns a task-common initialization for the learner classifier. The authors demonstrate that their meta-learning model is competitive with deep metric-learning techniques for few-shot learning.
