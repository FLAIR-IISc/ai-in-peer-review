**Summary of the Paper's Claims**

The paper proposes an LSTM-based meta-learner model to learn the exact optimization algorithm used to train another learner neural network classifier in the few-shot regime. The model is designed to learn a beneficial common initialization and an update rule that allows for quick convergence of training. The authors claim that their approach outperforms natural baselines and is competitive with state-of-the-art metric learning methods for few-shot learning.
