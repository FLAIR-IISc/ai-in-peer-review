- Proposes a novel meta-learning approach by reinterpreting SGD updates as a gated recurrent model with trainable parameters.  
- Idea is original and important for transfer learning research.  
- Paper is clearly structured but could improve clarity in places.  
- Strength: interesting and feasible methodological approach.  
- Strength: results are competitive and properly compared to state-of-the-art.  
- Strength: provides useful practical recommendations for systems.  
- Limitation: analogy aligns more closely with GRUs than with LSTMs.  
- Limitation: description of data separation in meta-sets is hard to follow and would benefit from a visual.  
- Limitation: experimental evaluation is incomplete, notably lacking analysis of the impact of parameters 'it' and 'ft'.  
- Limitation: Figure 2 adds little value.  
- Minor correction: typo in Section 3.2 — "it" should be "its".  
- Major positive: authors plan to release the experimental code, which will be a valuable contribution.
