- Paper structure is well organized but some sections need clearer exposition.
- Strength: proposed meta-learning approach is intriguing and viable.
- Strength: results are competitive and comparisons to state-of-the-art are adequate.
- Strength: provides useful practical recommendations for systems.

- Weakness: experimental evaluation is incomplete — omits analysis of the impact of the method’s parameters.
