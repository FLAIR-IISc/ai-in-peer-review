- Novel, important idea: reinterpret SGD updates as a gated recurrent model with trainable parameters for meta-learning.  
- Clear paper structure but some explanations need improved clarity.  
- Strength: approach is interesting and practically feasible.  
- Strength: results are competitive and compared properly to state-of-the-art.  
- Strength: authors provide useful recommendations for practical systems.  
- Weakness: analogy should be to GRUs rather than LSTMs.  
- Weakness: description of data separation in meta-sets is convoluted and would benefit from visualization.  
- Weakness: experimental evaluation is incomplete — need to study effects of inner-loop (it) and outer-loop (ft) parameters.  
- Weakness: Figure 2 adds little value and could be removed or replaced.  
- Minor correction: typo in Sec. 3.2 — "it" should be "its".  
- Positive note: releasing the evaluation code would greatly help reproducibility and follow-up work.
