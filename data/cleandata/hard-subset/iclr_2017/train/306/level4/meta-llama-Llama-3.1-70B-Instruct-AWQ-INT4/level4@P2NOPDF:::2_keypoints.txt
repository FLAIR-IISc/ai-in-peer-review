- Novel approach: reinterprets SGD update as a gated recurrent model with trainable parameters, original and impactful for transfer learning.  
- Overall paper structure is clear but some sections need improved clarity.  
- Strength: the meta-learning approach is interesting and feasible.  
- Strength: experimental results are competitive with state-of-the-art and comparisons are appropriate.  
- Strength: provides useful recommendations for practical systems.  
- Weakness: the SGD–RNN analogy would be more accurate using GRUs rather than LSTMs.  
- Weakness: description of data separation in meta-sets is unclear and would benefit from visualization.  
- Weakness: experimental evaluation is incomplete—lacks analysis of the effect of meta-learning algorithm parameters.  
- Weakness: Figure 2 offers little insight and could be removed or replaced.  
- Minor error: typo in Section 3.2—“it” should be “its”.  
- Positive: authors plan to release code, which will aid reproducibility and further research.
