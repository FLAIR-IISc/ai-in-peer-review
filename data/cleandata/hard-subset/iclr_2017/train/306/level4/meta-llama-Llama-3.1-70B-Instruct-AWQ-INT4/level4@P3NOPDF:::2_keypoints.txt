- Proposes novel meta-learning approach interpreting SGD update as a gated recurrent model with trainable parameters.  
- Idea is original and important for transfer learning research.  
- Paper is clearly structured but parts could be clearer.  
- Approach is interesting, feasible, and yields competitive results against state-of-the-art.  
- Provides proper comparisons and useful recommendations for practical systems.  
- Analogy is closer to GRUs than LSTMs and should be clarified.  
- Description of data separation in meta sets is hard to follow and would benefit from visualization.  
- Experimental evaluation leaves open questions about the effects of inner-loop (it) and outer-loop (ft) parameters.  
- Figure 2 adds little value and could be removed or replaced.  
- Minor typo: "each coordinate has it" should be "each coordinate has its" in Section 3.2.  
- Authors’ plan to release code is a major positive and will aid reproducibility and extension.
