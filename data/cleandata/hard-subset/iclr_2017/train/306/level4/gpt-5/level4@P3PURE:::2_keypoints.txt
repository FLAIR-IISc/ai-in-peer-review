- Proposes an original meta-learning method that interprets SGD updates as a gated recurrent model with trainable parameters, relevant for transfer learning.  
- Paper is generally well organized, though some explanations need clarification.  
- Pros: interesting and feasible approach to meta-learning.  
- Pros: Competitive results with appropriate comparisons to state-of-the-art methods.  
- Pros: Offers useful recommendations for practical systems.  
- Con: The analogy to LSTMs is misleading—model appears closer to GRUs; paper should clarify and justify this framing.  
- Con: Description of data separation into meta sets is hard to follow and would benefit from a clear visualization.  
- Con: Experimental evaluation is incomplete—should analyze the effect of gating parameters i_t and f_t.  
- Con: Figure 2 currently adds little value.  
- Remark: Minor typo in Section 3.2 (“it” → “its”).  
- Remark: Releasing the evaluation code would be a major plus.
