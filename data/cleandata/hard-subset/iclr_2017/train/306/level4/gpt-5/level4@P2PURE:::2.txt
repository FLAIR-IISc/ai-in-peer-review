This paper proposes a novel approach to meta-learning by interpreting the SGD update rule as a gated recurrent model with trainable parameters. The idea is original and important for transfer learning research. The paper is well structured, though some explanations could be clearer.

Pros:
- An interesting and feasible approach to meta-learning
- Competitive results and proper comparisons to the state of the art
- Good recommendations for practical systems

Cons:
- The analogy seems closer to GRUs than to LSTMs
- The description of how data are separated across the meta sets is hard to follow and would benefit from a visualization
- The experimental evaluation is only partially satisfactory; in particular, the effect of the parameters of i_t and f_t would be of interest
- Figure 2 adds little value

Remarks:
- Small typo in Section 3.2: "This means each coordinate has it" -> "its"

The authors state: "We plan on releasing the code used in our evaluation experiments." This would certainly be a major plus.
