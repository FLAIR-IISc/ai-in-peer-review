This work introduces a novel meta-learning approach by viewing the SGD update as a gated recurrent model with learnable parameters. The idea is original and significant for transfer learning research. The paper is well-structured, though clarity could be improved in some places.

Pros:
- A compelling and practicable approach to meta-learning
- Competitive results with appropriate comparisons to the state of the art
- Useful recommendations for practical systems

Cons:
- The correspondence seems closer to GRUs than to LSTMs
- The explanation of the data split into meta sets is hard to follow and would benefit from a visual aid
- The experimental evaluation is only partially satisfying; in particular, the effect of the parameters of i_t and f_t would be of interest
- Fig. 2 adds limited value

Remarks:
- Minor typo in 3.2: "This means each coordinate has it" -> its
> We plan on releasing the code used in our evaluation experiments.
This would certainly be a major plus.
