- Proposes a novel meta-learning method that interprets the SGD update rule as a gated recurrent model with trainable parameters.  
- The idea is original and important for transfer learning research.  
- Paper is well structured, though some explanations could be clearer.  
- Pros: an interesting and feasible approach to meta-learning.  
- Pros: competitive results with proper comparisons to the state of the art.  
- Pros: provides good recommendations for practical systems.  
- Cons: the analogy aligns more closely with GRUs than with LSTMs.  
- Cons: the description of how data are separated across meta sets is hard to follow and needs a visualization.  
- Cons: experimental evaluation is only partially satisfactory; effects of parameters i_t and f_t should be investigated.  
- Cons: Figure 2 provides little added value.  
- Remark: small typo in Section 3.2 ("it" → "its").  
- Authors’ plan to release evaluation code would be a major plus.
