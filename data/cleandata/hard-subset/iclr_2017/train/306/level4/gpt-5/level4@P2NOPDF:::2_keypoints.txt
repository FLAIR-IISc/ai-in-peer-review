- Proposes a novel meta-learning method interpreting the SGD update as a gated recurrent model with learnable parameters, relevant to transfer learning.
- Paper is generally well organized but some explanations require greater clarity.
- Reports competitive results with appropriate comparisons to state-of-the-art methods.
- Offers useful recommendations for practical deployment.
- Recurrent analogy appears more aligned with GRUs than LSTMs; clarify this distinction and design choices.
- Description of how data are split into meta sets is difficult to follow; include a clear schematic or diagram.
- Empirical evaluation feels incomplete; include sensitivity analysis for key parameters (e.g., i_t and f_t).
- Figure 2 adds limited value in its current form.
- Minor typo in Section 3.2: “it” should be “its.”
- Releasing the experimental code would significantly enhance reproducibility and impact.
