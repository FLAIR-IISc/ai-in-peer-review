- Proposes a novel meta-learning method that models the SGD update as a gated recurrent model with trainable parameters; original and relevant to transfer learning.
- Paper is generally well structured, though some explanations could be clearer.
- Interesting and practical approach to meta-learning.
- Shows competitive results with appropriate comparisons to state-of-the-art baselines.
- Provides useful recommendations for real-world systems.
- The SGD-as-RNN analogy seems closer to GRUs than LSTMs.
- Description of how data are partitioned into meta-sets is hard to follow; a visual illustration would help.
- Experimental evaluation is only partially satisfying; specifically, the effects of parameters i_t and f_t should be studied.
- Figure 2 adds little value.
- Minor typo in Section 3.2: “it” should be “its.”
- Releasing the code used in experiments would be a significant plus.
