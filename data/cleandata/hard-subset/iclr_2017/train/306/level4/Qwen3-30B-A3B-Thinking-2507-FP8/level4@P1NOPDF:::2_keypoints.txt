- Novel contribution: frames SGD update as a gated recurrent model with trainable parameters, offering an original meta-learning approach for transfer learning.
- Paper is generally well-structured, though some sections need clearer exposition.
- Method is practical and competitive with state-of-the-art, backed by comprehensive comparisons.
- Provides valuable, actionable recommendations for real-world system implementation.
- Analogy to recurrent networks is more accurate for GRUs than LSTMs and should be clarified.
- Meta-set data partitioning is described unclearly and would benefit from a visual illustration.
- Experimental evaluation lacks depth, especially analysis of key hyperparameters (learning rate η, fine-tuning steps f_t).
- Figure 2 provides limited insight and should be omitted or substantially improved.
- Typo in Section 3.2: "each coordinate has it" should be "each coordinate has its."
- Authors’ plan to release evaluation code would significantly aid reproducibility and adoption.
