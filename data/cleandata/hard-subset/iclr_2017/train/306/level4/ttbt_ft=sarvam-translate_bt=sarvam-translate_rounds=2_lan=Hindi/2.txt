This paper describes a new approach to meta-learning by explaining the SDG update rule as a gated recurrent model with trainable parameters. This idea is fundamental and important for research related to transfer learning. The structure of the paper is clear, but clarity could be improved in some areas.

Pros:
- An interesting and viable approach to meta-learning
- Competitive results and a fair comparison with cutting-edge approaches
- Good recommendations for practical systems

Cons:
- It will be closer to GRU than analogy LSTM
- It is difficult to understand and not observable in the details of data separation in the meta set
- Experimental evaluation is only partially satisfactory, especially the impact of its and FT's parameters will be of interest
- Figure 2 is not very important
