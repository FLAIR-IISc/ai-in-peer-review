This paper describes a new approach to meta-learning by interpreting the SDG update rule as a gated recurrent model with trainable parameters. This idea is fundamental and important for research related to transfer learning. The paper's structure is clear, but clarity could be improved in some areas.

Pros:
- An interesting and viable approach to meta-learning
- Competitive results and a fair comparison to state-of-the-art approaches
- Good recommendations for practical systems

Cons:
- It will be closer to GRU than analogy LSTM
- The details of data separation in the meta set are difficult to understand and cannot be visualized
- Experimental evaluation is only partially satisfactory, especially the impact of its and FT's parameters will be of interest
- Figure 2 is not very significant
