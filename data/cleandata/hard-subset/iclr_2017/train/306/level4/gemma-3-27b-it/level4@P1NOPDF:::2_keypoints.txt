- Novel approach: interprets SGD updates as a gated recurrent model with trainable parameters—original and potentially impactful for transfer learning.  
- Well-structured paper but clarity could be improved in parts.  
- Competitive results with thorough comparison to state-of-the-art methods.  
- Provides useful recommendations for practical system implementation.  
- The SGD-as-RNN analogy aligns more closely with GRUs than with LSTMs.  
- Description of data separation within meta-sets is hard to follow and would benefit from a visualization.  
- Experimental evaluation is only partially convincing; key parameters (it and ft) should be investigated.  
- Figure 2 offers limited insight.  
- Minor typo in §3.2: “it” should read “its.”  
- Authors’ plan to release code would be a significant benefit to the community.
