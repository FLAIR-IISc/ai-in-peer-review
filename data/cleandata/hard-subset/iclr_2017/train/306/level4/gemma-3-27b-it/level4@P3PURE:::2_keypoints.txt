- Frames SGD update as a gated recurrent model with trainable parameters — original and significant for transfer learning.
- Paper is well-structured but some parts lack clarity.
- Presents an interesting and feasible meta-learning approach.
- Shows competitive results with proper comparisons to state-of-the-art methods.
- Gives useful recommendations for practical system implementations.
- The RNN analogy would be more accurate if framed as a GRU rather than an LSTM.
- Description of data separation within meta-sets is hard to follow and would benefit from visualization.
- Experimental evaluation is only partially convincing; the impact of the parameters it and ft should be investigated.
- Figure 2 offers limited insight.
- Minor typo in Section 3.2: “it” should be “its”.
- Authors’ plan to release code is highly encouraged and would be a substantial benefit.
