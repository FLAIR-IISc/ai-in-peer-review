- Novel meta-learning framework that models SGD updates as a gated recurrent model; significant, original, and impactful for transfer learning.
- Paper is well-organized but some sections need clearer exposition.
- Approach is compelling and practically viable.
- Results are competitive with thorough comparisons to state-of-the-art.
- Authors provide useful implementation recommendations for real-world systems.
- Architectural analogy is misleading: model resembles GRUs more than LSTMs.
- Explanation of data partitioning into meta-sets is unclear and would benefit from a figure.
- Experimental evaluation is incomplete; effects of the 'it' and 'ft' parameters require further study.
- Figure 2 adds limited insight and could be improved.
- Minor typo in Sec. 3.2 ("it" should be "its"); authors plan to release code, which would be valuable.
