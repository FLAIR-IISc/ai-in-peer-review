- Reframes SGD as a trainable, gated recurrent optimizer—an original and promising meta-learning idea.  
- Paper is well-structured and shows competitive empirical results against state-of-the-art.  
- Provides practical implementation recommendations (e.g., coordinate-wise preprocessing).  
- Experimental data partitioning (meta/train/test sets) is unclear and needs clearer explanation or a visual diagram.  
- Experimental analysis is incomplete; needs deeper investigation of the learned optimizer’s behavior and ablation studies on input gate (i_t) and forget gate (f_t).  
- The analogy to LSTM is imprecise; architecture appears closer to a GRU and should be clarified or justified.  
- Figure 2 is not very informative; recommend showing optimization trajectories versus standard optimizers on non-trivial loss surfaces.  
- Authors should release code to ensure reproducibility and facilitate follow-up work.  
- Minor typo in Section 3.2: change "it" to "its".  
- Overall: creative, well-executed contribution that would be stronger with clearer experiments and deeper internal analysis.
