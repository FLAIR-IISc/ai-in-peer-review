The paper recasts SGD as a gated recurrent model with learnable parameters — an original and significant contribution to transfer learning.

Well-organized manuscript but several sections need clearer exposition.

Presents a compelling and viable new direction for meta-learning.

Demonstrates competitive performance and is thoroughly benchmarked against state-of-the-art methods.

Provides valuable practical implementation insights.

Model analogy seems closer to a GRU than the referenced LSTM and should be corrected or better justified.

Explanation of how data is partitioned into meta-sets is hard to follow and would benefit from a diagram.

Experimental evaluation is incomplete; specifically analyze the impact of the `it` and `ft` parameters.

Figure 2 offers limited informational value and should be revised or replaced.

Minor typo in Section 3.2: "it" should be "its"; authors’ plan to release code is highly commendable and will enhance impact.
