- Novel framing: proposes treating the SGD update rule as a gated recurrent model with trainable parameters, an original meta-learning idea with strong transfer-learning potential.
- Presentation: paper is generally well-structured but some sections lack clarity and need improvement.
- Practicality: approach is practical and promising for real-world systems.
- Experiments: competitive results benchmarked appropriately against state-of-the-art methods.
- Implementation advice: authors give useful recommendations for applying the method in practice.
- LSTM analogy: comparison to LSTMs is inaccurate — the model more closely resembles a GRU.
- Data partitioning: description of meta-set partitioning is hard to follow and would benefit from a diagram or visualization.
- Limited evaluation: experimental analysis is somewhat limited; an ablation study on input gate (i_t) and forget gate (f_t) effects is needed.
- Figure 2: current Figure 2 offers limited insight and could be omitted or replaced with more informative content.
- Typo: minor typo in Section 3.2 — "it" should be "its".
- Code release: authors’ plan to release evaluation code is commendable and valuable for the community.
