- Novel framing: casts the SGD update rule as a gated recurrent model with trainable parameters, an original and significant idea for meta-learning/transfer learning.
- Practical and competitive: method is interesting, practical, achieves competitive results with thorough comparisons to state-of-the-art.
- Implementation guidance: paper offers valuable insights and recommendations for implementing the proposed system.

- LSTM analogy imprecise: described architecture more closely resembles a GRU than an LSTM.
- Unclear data partitioning: description of meta-set partitioning is difficult to follow and needs a diagram/visualization.
- Incomplete evaluation: experiments lack comprehensiveness—missing ablation/analysis of the influence of parameters i_t and f_t.
- Uninformative figure: Figure 2 provides limited insight and should be replaced with a more informative visualization (e.g., for data partitioning).

- Typo: "This means each coordinate has it" should be "its" in Section 3.2.
- Code release: authors’ intention to release evaluation code is commendable and would be a major community contribution.
