This paper describes a new approach to meta-learning, explaining the SDG update rule as a gated recurrent model with parameters for training. This concept is fundamental and important for research related to transfer learning. The paper has a clear structure, but clarity could be improved in some areas.

Pros:
- An attractive and realistic approach to meta-learning
- Competitive results and fair comparison with state-of-the-art methods
- Good recommendation for practical systems

Cons:
- This analogy will be closer to GRU than LSTML
- It is difficult to follow the description of data separation in the meta-set, and it can be imagined
- Experimental evaluation is partially satisfactory, especially its parameters and its impact will be of interest
- Figure 2 has little value
