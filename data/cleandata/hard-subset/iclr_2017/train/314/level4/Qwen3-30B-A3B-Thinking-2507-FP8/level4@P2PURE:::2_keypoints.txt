- Method is essentially a special case of universal value function approaches and should cite and position against that literature.  
- Primary technical contribution is largely the distillation/integration of existing techniques for 3D navigation and this should be stated more clearly in the abstract/introduction.  
- Paper lacks analysis of failure modes, especially how the method generalizes to novel goals.  
- On-policy training risks catastrophic forgetting if goals from the past are not revisited repeatedly.  
- Empirical claims would be stronger with results on additional domains (e.g., Atari using ROMs as intrinsic variables).  
- Overall, the formulation shows clear empirical advantages and provides experimental insights useful for future agent design.
