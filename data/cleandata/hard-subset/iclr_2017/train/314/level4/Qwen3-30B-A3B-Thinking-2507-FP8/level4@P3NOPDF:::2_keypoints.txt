- Core contribution is unclear; should explicitly present the work as integration and distillation of prior techniques rather than a novel method.  
- Method is a special case of universal value function frameworks and should be cited and framed accordingly.  
- Empirical results show a clear performance advantage in 3D navigation, supporting the utility of the proposed auxiliary intrinsic variables.  
- The paper lacks a thorough analysis of limitations and failure modes, particularly when goal distributions shift and generalization to novel goals breaks down.  
- As an on-policy algorithm, the risk of catastrophic forgetting is not discussed and no mitigation strategies (e.g., experience replay, periodic retraining) are proposed.  
- Experimental scope is limited to 3D navigation and does not demonstrate generality across domains.  
- A complementary experiment in a different domain (e.g., Atari using game-specific ROMs as intrinsic state representations) would strengthen claims of broader applicability.  
- Overall: useful empirical contribution but needs clearer upfront framing, critical evaluation of boundaries, and broader experimental validation to claim wider significance.
