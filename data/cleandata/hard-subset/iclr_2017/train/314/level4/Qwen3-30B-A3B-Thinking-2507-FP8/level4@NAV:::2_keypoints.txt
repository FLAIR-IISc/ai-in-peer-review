- Introduces an on-policy deep RL method that uses auxiliary intrinsic variables.  
- Method is effectively a specific instance of the universal value function framework and should cite that literature.  
- Main technical contribution reads as distillation of established techniques for 3D navigation; contributions need clearer articulation in abstract and introduction.  
- Analysis lacks examination of failure modes, especially limits in generalizing to novel goal distributions.  
- As an on-policy approach, the method risks catastrophic forgetting without sufficient retraining on previously encountered goals.  
- Evaluation would be strengthened by results on additional domains (e.g., Atari using ROMs as intrinsic variables).  
- Empirically, the formulation shows clear advantages and offers useful experimental insights for future agent design.
