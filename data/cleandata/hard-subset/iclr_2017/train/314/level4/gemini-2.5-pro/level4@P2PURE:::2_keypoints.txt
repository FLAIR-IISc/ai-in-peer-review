- Method is a special case of universal value function approaches and the relevant literature is cited.  
- Primary technical contribution is mainly a synthesis of existing ideas applied to 3D navigation; this should be stated explicitly in the abstract/introduction.  
- Analysis lacks investigation of failure modes, e.g., when the model fails to generalize to changing goals.  
- Conceptual concern about catastrophic forgetting: as an on-policy method, the agent may struggle without continual training on past goals.  
- Empirical contribution would be stronger with evaluations on additional domains (e.g., Atari using ROM state as intrinsic variables).  
- Overall, the paper gives a clear empirical demonstration with valuable experimental insights useful for future agent development.
