- Proposes an on-policy deep RL method augmented with auxiliary intrinsic variables for complex 3D navigation.  
- Method is effectively a special case of universal value functions; relevant prior work is cited.  
- Main technical contribution is the synthesis of several existing ideas and should be stated more explicitly in the abstract/introduction.  
- Paper lacks analysis of failure modes—unclear when the approach fails to generalize to changing goals.  
- Conceptual concern: as an on-policy method, the agent is vulnerable to catastrophic forgetting unless it retrains on distant-past goals.  
- Evaluation is narrow; testing on additional domains (e.g., Atari with RAM as intrinsic variables) would better demonstrate generality.  
- Overall, shows a clear empirical advantage and offers valuable experimental insights for future agent design.
