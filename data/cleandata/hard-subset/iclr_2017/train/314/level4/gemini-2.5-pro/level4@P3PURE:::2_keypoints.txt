- Proposes an on-policy deep RL method using auxiliary intrinsic variables; is a specific instance of a universal value function approach and cites relevant prior work.  
- Main technical contribution is the distillation and integration of several existing ideas to tackle complex 3D navigation tasks; this should be stated more explicitly in the abstract/introduction.  
- Paper lacks analysis of failure modes, e.g., when the model struggles to generalize to changing goals.  
- Conceptual concern: as an on-policy method, the agent may be prone to catastrophic forgetting without repeated training on distant past goals.  
- Results would be more convincing if validated on additional domains (e.g., Atari using RAM as the intrinsic variable).  
- Overall, the method shows a clear empirical advantage and provides useful experimental insights for future agent development.
