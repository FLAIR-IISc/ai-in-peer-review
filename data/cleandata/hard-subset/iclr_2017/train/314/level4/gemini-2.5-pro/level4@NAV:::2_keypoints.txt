- Introduces an on-policy deep RL algorithm augmented with auxiliary intrinsic variables for goal-conditioned control.  
- Approach can be framed as an instance of a universal value function and prior work is appropriately cited.  
- Main contribution is primarily a synthesis and effective application of existing concepts to 3D navigation tasks.  
- Paper should more explicitly state this synthesis/contribution in the abstract and introduction.  
- Evaluation lacks analysis of failure modes, e.g., conditions under which the model fails to generalize to changing goals.  
- Conceptual concern: on-policy training may cause catastrophic forgetting unless the agent revisits earlier goals systematically.  
- Claims of generality need broader validation across domains; testing on standard benchmarks (e.g., Atari using ROM states as intrinsic variables) is recommended.  
- Overall, the work shows clear empirical benefits and provides valuable experimental insights for future goal-conditioned agents.
