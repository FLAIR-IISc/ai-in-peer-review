- Proposes an on-policy deep RL method using auxiliary intrinsic variables, framed as a specific instance of universal value function-based approaches with proper citations.
- Synthesizes existing concepts to tackle 3D navigation, though the novel contributions should be stated more explicitly in the introduction and abstract.
- Recommends analyzing limitations and failure modes, particularly poor generalization to changing goals.
- Notes on-policy formulation is prone to catastrophic forgetting unless the agent periodically retrains on earlier goals.
- Suggests extending experiments to other domains (e.g., Atari with ROM-derived intrinsic variables) to evaluate broader applicability and robustness.
- Empirically demonstrates clear advantages and offers useful experimental insights that motivate further research.
