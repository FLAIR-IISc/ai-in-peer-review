- Frames sensorimotor control as supervised prediction of future intrinsic measurements.  
- Trained in ViZDoom to predict sequences of health, ammunition, and frags weighted by input goal triplets; goal weights guide exploration and test-time actions maximize long-term goal achievement.  
- Strong empirical results: DFP consistently outperforms A3C and DQN and matches or exceeds related DSR, and the approach won the 2016 Visual Doom AI Competition.  
- Experimental section is rigorous with direct comparisons and an ablation study validating model components.  
- Builds on established RL ideas (intrinsic motivation, auxiliary variables, forward modeling); current revision cites relevant prior work though not exhaustively.  
- Presentation nitpicks: Doom is pseudo-3D rather than true 3D; symbol “P” may be misread as probability; dual use of “j” in eq. (6) is confusing; tables should state units.  
- Recommendation: accept—methodologically sound, well-validated, and a milestone for the visual Doom benchmark.
