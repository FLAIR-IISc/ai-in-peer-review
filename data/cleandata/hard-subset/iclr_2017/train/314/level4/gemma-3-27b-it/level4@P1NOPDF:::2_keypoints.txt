- Proposes an on-policy deep RL method that adds auxiliary intrinsic variables for 3D navigation tasks.
- Method is essentially a specific case of the universal value function approach; prior work is appropriately cited.
- Main technical contribution is distilling and integrating several existing ideas to effectively tackle 3D navigation.
- Contributions should be stated more explicitly and focused in the abstract and introduction.
- Missing discussion of failure modes and conditions under which the model struggles to generalize to novel goals.
- Conceptual concern: as an on-policy method, catastrophic forgetting may occur if earlier goals are not revisited during training.
- Empirical claims would be stronger with results on additional domains (e.g., Atari) and exploration of ROM states as intrinsic variables.
- Overall assessment: shows clear empirical advantage and provides useful experimental insights for future agents.
