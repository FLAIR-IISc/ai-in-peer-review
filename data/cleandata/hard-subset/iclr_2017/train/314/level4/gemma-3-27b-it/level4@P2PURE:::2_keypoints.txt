- Introduces an on-policy deep RL method that incorporates auxiliary intrinsic variables.  
- Method can be framed as a specific instance of universal value function-based approaches, and prior work is appropriately cited.  
- Main technical contribution is consolidation of existing ideas to tackle 3D navigation tasks; this consolidation should be emphasized more in the abstract/introduction.  
- Paper lacks discussion of method limitations and failure modes.  
- Does not address generalization to changing goals or risk of catastrophic forgetting with on-policy training when previously encountered goals are not revisited.  
- Results on additional domains (e.g., Atari using ROM data as intrinsic variables) would strengthen the paper.  
- Empirically demonstrates clear benefits from the proposed formulation and provides valuable experimental insights for future RL agents.
