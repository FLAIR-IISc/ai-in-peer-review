- Proposes an on-policy deep RL method that incorporates auxiliary intrinsic variables for 3D navigation.  
- Method is a specific instance of a universal value function approach and correctly cites related prior work.  
- Claimed technical contribution is distilling existing ideas to effectively solve 3D navigation; this should be made more explicit in the abstract/introduction.  
- Reviewer asks for analysis of failure modes—clarify under what conditions the model fails to generalize to novel goals.  
- Conceptual concern: the on-policy nature may cause catastrophic forgetting if the agent does not continually retrain on distant past goals.  
- Recommends additional evaluation domains (e.g., Atari using ROM states as intrinsic variables) to strengthen empirical claims.  
- Overall assessment: demonstrates clear empirical advantage and provides experimental insights valuable for future agent development.
