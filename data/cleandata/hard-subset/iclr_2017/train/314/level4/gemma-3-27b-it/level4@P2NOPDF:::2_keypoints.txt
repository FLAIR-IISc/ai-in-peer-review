- Introduces a novel on-policy deep RL method using auxiliary intrinsic variables to boost performance.  
- Builds on universal value function frameworks and properly cites relevant prior work.  
- Core contribution is primarily a distillation/integration of established techniques tailored to 3D navigation.  
- Authors should more explicitly highlight this integration-focused contribution in the abstract and introduction.  
- Empirical results are promising and show clear empirical advantage in the target domain.  
- Paper lacks thorough analysis of limitations and failure modes; more investigation is needed.  
- Needs exploration of conditions where the agent fails to generalize to novel or dynamically changing goals.  
- Raises concern about catastrophic forgetting inherent to on-policy methods and no mitigation or continual-learning evaluation is provided.  
- Recommends testing beyond 3D navigation (e.g., Atari with game-state intrinsic variables) to assess generalizability.  
- Overall, the work is compelling, valuable to the community, and a worthwhile contribution despite areas for improvement.
