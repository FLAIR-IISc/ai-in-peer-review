- Proposes an on-policy method that predicts future intrinsic state variables.  
- Evaluated in vizDoom, forecasting sequences of (health, ammunition, frags) weighted by input "goal" triplets.  
- Goal-weighting serves as an exploration control mechanism; actions chosen to maximize long-term goal achievement.  
- Strong empirical results, including winning the 2016 vizDoom competition.  
- Thorough experimental validation with comparisons to A3C, DQN, and DSR, showing superior or comparable performance.  
- Ablation study demonstrates the contribution of individual model components.  
- Builds on prior work in intrinsic motivation, auxiliary tasks, and forward modeling; recent revision cites relevant prior work adequately.  
- Presentation note: environment is effectively 2D despite 3D rendering.  
- Notation concern: symbol “P” may be misinterpreted as a probability.  
- Notation concern: dual use of index “j” could cause confusion.  
- Tables should include explicit units for each measured quantity.  
- Overall recommendation: accept — technically sound, well-validated on a challenging benchmark, and reproducible.
