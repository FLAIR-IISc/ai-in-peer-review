- Introduces a novel on-policy deep RL method that incorporates auxiliary intrinsic variables.  
- Method can be framed as a specific instance of universal value function approaches; related work is cited.  
- Paper largely consolidates existing techniques for 3D navigation; the primary contribution is this distillation of ideas.  
- The distillation/primary contribution should be stated more explicitly in the abstract and introduction.  
- Analysis of limitations is weak; the paper should investigate failure modes and generalization to novel goals.  
- On-policy training raises risk of catastrophic forgetting for goals that are infrequently revisited and this should be addressed.  
- Evaluation should be extended to additional domains (e.g., Atari, using ROM data as intrinsic variables) to demonstrate broader applicability.  
- Despite issues, the proposed formulation shows clear empirical benefits and offers useful experimental insights for future RL agents.
