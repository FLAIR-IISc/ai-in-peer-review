- Proposes an on-policy deep RL method that incorporates auxiliary intrinsic variables.  
- Method is a special case of universal value function approaches; relevant citations are provided.  
- Main technical contribution is distilling existing ideas successfully for 3D navigation tasks.  
- Paper’s contributions should be more clearly stated in the abstract and introduction.  
- Needs discussion of failure modes, especially when the model struggles to generalize to changing goals.  
- Conceptual concern: on-policy training may cause catastrophic forgetting if past goals aren’t revisited.  
- Recommend evaluating generality in other domains (e.g., Atari, using ROM as intrinsic variables).  
- Empirically demonstrates clear advantage and provides useful experimental insights for future agents.
