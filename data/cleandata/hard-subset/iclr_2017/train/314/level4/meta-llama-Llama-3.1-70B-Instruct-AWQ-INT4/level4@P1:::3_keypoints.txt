- Novel method leverages cotemporal structure of a high-dimensional sensory stream and a lower-dimensional measurement stream to supervise sensorimotor control learning from raw input.  
- Enables learning in complex 3D-like environments without extraneous supervision.  
- Strong empirical performance, including winning the Visual Doom AI Competition Full Deathmatch track.  
- Experimental design is sound, with comparisons to state-of-the-art deep RL methods and an ablation study.  
- Ablation study shows the model’s added complexity is justified and contributes to effectiveness.  
- Approach is well-motivated and clearly explained in the paper.  
- Results outperform state-of-the-art deep reinforcement learning methods in several scenarios.  
- Limitation: experiments are restricted to a single environment (Doom).  
- Suggested improvement: evaluate generalizability by testing in additional environments and domains (e.g., robotics, autonomous vehicles).  
- Minor form issues: Doom described as 3D though it is 2D with fake 3D rendering.  
- Minor notation issues: use of "P" in (2) may mislead (not a probability); double use of "j" in (6) may be confusing.  
- Presentation suggestion: repeat measurement units in results tables, especially Table 1 with heterogeneous units.  
- Overall recommendation: clear accept and recommendation for publication.
