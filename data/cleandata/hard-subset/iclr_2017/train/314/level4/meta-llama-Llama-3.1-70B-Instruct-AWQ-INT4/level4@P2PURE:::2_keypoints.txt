- Introduces an on-policy deep RL method with auxiliary intrinsic variables; is effectively a special case of universal value function approaches and cites relevant work correctly.
- Main technical contribution is primarily a distilled integration of existing ideas applied to 3D navigation; this should be made clearer in the abstract/introduction.
- Paper would benefit from analysis of failure modes, especially where the model fails to generalize to changing goals.
- Raises conceptual concerns about catastrophic forgetting inherent in on-policy methods that do not repeatedly train on distant past goals.
- Recommends additional experiments in other domains (e.g., Atari, using ROM as intrinsic variables) to demonstrate broader generalizability.
- Empirically shows clear advantages from the proposed formulations, offering useful experimental insights for future agent development.
