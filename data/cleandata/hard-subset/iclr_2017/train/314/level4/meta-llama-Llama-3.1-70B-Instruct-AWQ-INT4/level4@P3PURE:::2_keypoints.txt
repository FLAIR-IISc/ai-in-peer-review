- Proposes an on-policy deep RL method that incorporates additional auxiliary intrinsic variables.  
- The method can be viewed as a special case of universal value function approaches and cites relevant prior work.  
- The main technical contribution seems to be distilling and integrating existing ideas for 3D navigation rather than novel theory.  
- Contributions should be articulated more explicitly in the abstract and introduction.  
- Evaluation is limited and should include a more comprehensive analysis of failure modes.  
- Needs investigation into when and why the model fails to generalize to changing goals.  
- Raises conceptual concerns about catastrophic forgetting in on-policy methods if past goals are not revisited.  
- Recommend validating applicability/robustness in additional domains (e.g., Atari using ROM as intrinsic variables).  
- Overall, shows clear empirical advantage and offers experimental insights useful for future agent development.
