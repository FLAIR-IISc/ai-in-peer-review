- Proposes an on-policy deep RL method that incorporates auxiliary intrinsic variables (a special case of UVFA).  
- Relevant prior work is appropriately cited.  
- Primary technical contribution is not clearly stated in the abstract and introduction.  
- Main novelty appears to be distilling/integrating existing ideas to effectively solve 3D navigation.  
- Missing discussion of failure modes and when the method fails to generalize to changing goals.  
- On-policy nature risks catastrophic forgetting if goals from the distant past are not revisited.  
- Would benefit from additional evaluations in other domains (e.g., Atari using ROM as intrinsic variables) to assess applicability and robustness.  
- Empirical results show a clear advantage and provide useful experimental insights; the integration effort is commendable.
