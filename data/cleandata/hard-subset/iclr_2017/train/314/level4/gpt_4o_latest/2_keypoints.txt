- Proposes an on-policy deep RL approach that augments policies with auxiliary intrinsic variables for 3D navigation.
- The method is effectively a specific instance of the universal value function (UVF) framework and cites related prior work appropriately.
- Main technical contribution is the synthesis/distillation of several existing ideas for 3D navigation, but this should be made more explicit in the abstract and introduction.
- The paper does not explore failure modes or specify conditions under which the model fails to generalize to dynamic or changing goals.
- Conceptual concern: as an on-policy method, it may suffer catastrophic forgetting unless the agent periodically revisits previously seen goals.
- Would be strengthened by additional domain evaluations (e.g., Atari using ROMs as intrinsic variables) to demonstrate broader applicability.
- Overall, the paper offers clear empirical evidence and experimental insights that could inform future agent development.
