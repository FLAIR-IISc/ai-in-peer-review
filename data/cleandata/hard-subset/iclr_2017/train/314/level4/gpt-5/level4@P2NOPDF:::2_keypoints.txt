- Method is effectively a special case of UVFA; main novelty is careful integration/distillation of existing ideas and this should be stated more clearly in the abstract/introduction.

- Missing analysis of failure modes and generalization; on-policy training may cause catastrophic forgetting unless past goals are revisited, so empirical or discussion of this limitation is needed.

- Evaluation is narrow (only 3D navigation); showing results on other domains (e.g., Atari using ROM IDs or metadata as intrinsic variables) would strengthen claims of generality.

- Overall, the paper presents clear empirical evidence and useful experimental insights for designing future agents.
