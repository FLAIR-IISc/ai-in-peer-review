- Proposes an on-policy deep RL method augmented with auxiliary intrinsic variables for 3D navigation.  
- Method is a specific instance of universal value function approaches; appropriate prior work is cited.  
- Main technical contribution appears to be distilling/integrating existing ideas for 3D navigation; contributions should be stated more clearly in abstract/introduction.  
- Requests analysis of failure modes and conditions under which the model struggles to generalize to changing goals.  
- Conceptual concern: as an on-policy method, it may suffer catastrophic forgetting if not repeatedly trained on distant past goals.  
- Suggests additional experiments on other domains (e.g., Atari, using ROM as intrinsic variables) to demonstrate generality.  
- Overall evaluation: demonstrates clear empirical advantages and offers valuable experimental insights for future agents.
