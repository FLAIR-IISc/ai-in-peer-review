- Method is essentially a special case of universal value function approaches; prior work is cited.  
- Main claimed contribution is distilling/integrating existing ideas for 3D navigation; contributions should be stated more explicitly in abstract/introduction.  
- Need analysis of failure modes—when and why the model fails to generalize to changing goals.  
- On-policy formulation raises concern about catastrophic forgetting if goals from the distant past aren’t retrained.  
- Evaluation is limited to 3D navigation; stronger paper would include additional domains (e.g., Atari using ROM as intrinsic variables).  
- Overall: empirical advantages and useful insights are shown, but clarity on contributions, failure analyses, and broader evaluation are needed to solidify the work.
