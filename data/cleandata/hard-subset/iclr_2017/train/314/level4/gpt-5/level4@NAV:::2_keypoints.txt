- Proposes an on-policy deep RL approach augmented with auxiliary intrinsic variables.  
- Method is essentially a specific instance of the universal value function framework; pertinent prior work is cited.  
- Main technical contribution appears to be consolidating existing ideas for 3D navigation; contributions should be stated more explicitly in abstract/introduction.  
- Reviewer requests analysis of failure cases and conditions under which the model fails to generalize to changing goals.  
- Conceptual concern: on-policy training risks catastrophic forgetting unless the agent continually trains on distant-past goals.  
- Suggests additional experiments on other domains (e.g., Atari, using ROM as intrinsic variables).  
- Overall assessment: clear empirical advantages and experimental insights that could be valuable for future agents.
