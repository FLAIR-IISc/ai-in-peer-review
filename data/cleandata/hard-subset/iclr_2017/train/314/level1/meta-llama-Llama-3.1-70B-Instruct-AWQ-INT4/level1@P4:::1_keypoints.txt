- Proposal: Reduce sensorimotor control to supervised learning by using cotemporal structure between high-dimensional sensory and low-dimensional measurement streams as supervision.  
- Empirical setup: Evaluated in 3D Doom simulations, showing improved performance versus state-of-the-art deep RL and generalization across environments and goals.  
- Decision: Accept.
