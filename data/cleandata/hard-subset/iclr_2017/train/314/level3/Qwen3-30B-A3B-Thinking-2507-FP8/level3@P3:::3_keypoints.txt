- Introduces Direct Future Prediction (DFP): predict future multidimensional measurements (health, ammo, frags) instead of optimizing a scalar reward.  
- DFP enables training without a fixed goal and allows dynamic goal specification at test time by selecting actions that maximize predicted measurements.  
- Achieved strong empirical results, winning the 2016 VizDoom Full Deathmatch and outperforming A3C, DQN, and DSR on challenging 3D tasks.  
- Decision: Accept — significant empirical progress, scientifically rigorous, and reproducible.  
- Tackles sensorimotor control from raw input in complex environments and acquiring general skills that pursue dynamic goals.  
- Well motivated and well situated in literature, connecting to supervised learning, Monte Carlo methods, intrinsic motivation, auxiliary tasks, and forward modeling.  
- Empirical evaluation is thorough with four Doom scenarios, appropriate baselines, ablation studies, and generalization tests.  
- Ablation study shows benefits of vectorial feedback and multi-temporal prediction.  
- Real-world validation: competition win by >50% margin supports practical effectiveness.
