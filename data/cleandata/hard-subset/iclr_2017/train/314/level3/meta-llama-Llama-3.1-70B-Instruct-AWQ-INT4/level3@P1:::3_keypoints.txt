- Introduces Direct Future Prediction (DFP) to predict future measurements from raw sensory input for sensorimotor control and dynamic goals.
- Trained without fixed goals at training time and can pursue changing goals at test time.
- Demonstrated in immersive Doom-like simulations, outperforming or matching state-of-the-art deep RL methods on challenging tasks.
- Models show generalization across environments and goals.
- Recommendation: accept.
- Strong empirical results with extensive experiments supporting the approach.
- Clear, well-motivated method situated among intrinsic motivation, auxiliary tasks, and forward modeling literature.
- Effective use of intrinsic measurements to guide exploration and learning.
- Clear and thorough presentation with detailed analysis of results.
- Minor factual issue: Doom described as 3D though it is 2D with fake 3D — correct for accuracy.
- Notational issues: "P" in equation (2) is misleading and "j" is double-used in equation (6); clarify notation.
- Presentation suggestion: repeat units in results tables for heterogeneous measurements to improve readability.
- Suggestion to evaluate on more diverse environments beyond Doom to strengthen generalizability claims.

- Question about selection of temporal offsets for prediction and their effect on performance.
- Question about experience memory and sampling procedure used for training.
