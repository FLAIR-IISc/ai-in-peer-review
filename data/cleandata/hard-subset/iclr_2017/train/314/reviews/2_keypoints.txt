- Presents an on-policy deep RL method with auxiliary intrinsic variables for 3D navigation.
- Method is essentially a special case of universal value function approaches and cites appropriate prior work.
- Main technical contribution appears to be distilling and integrating existing ideas rather than a novel algorithmic advance; contributions should be clarified in the abstract/intro.
- Would like to see analysis of failure modes and conditions where the model struggles to generalize to changing goals.
- Notes conceptual issue: on-policy training can cause catastrophic forgetting if goals from the distant past are not revisited.
- Suggests expanding experiments to other domains (e.g., Atari using ROM as intrinsic variables) to demonstrate broader applicability.
- Overall, shows clear empirical advantage and provides useful experimental insights for future agents.
