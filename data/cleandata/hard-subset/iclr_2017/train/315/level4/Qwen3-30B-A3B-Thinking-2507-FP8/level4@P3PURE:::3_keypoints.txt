- Paper is well-motivated and targets batch size effects in deep learning optimization.
- Core methodology is sound and empirical results are compelling.
- Reviewer recommends investigating adding rescaled Gaussian noise to gradients in the large-batch regime.
- Hypothesis: this noise injection could reproduce benefits of small-batch training and bridge the LB–SB performance gap.
- This modification may offer a practical way to obtain SB advantages without the computational cost of small batches and thus warrants exploration.
