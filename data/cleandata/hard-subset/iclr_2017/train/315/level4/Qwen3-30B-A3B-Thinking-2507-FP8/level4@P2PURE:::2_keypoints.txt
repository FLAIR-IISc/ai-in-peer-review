- Empirical evidence for two claims: (1) smaller-batch SGD converges to flatter minima; (2) flatter minima generalize better.  
- Limited novelty overall, but offers valuable empirical insights into deep network generalization.  
- Theoretical significance: results help identify valid assumptions for new theoretical frameworks.  
- Practical significance: suggests adjusting mini-batch sizes as a heuristic to improve generalization.
