This paper provides a valuable contribution to the community by investigating the limitations of large-batch gradient descent. The paper is well-motivated, and the authors provide a clear explanation of the issues surrounding large-batch training. The authors support their claims with theoretical analysis and experimental results, demonstrating the effectiveness of their proposed solution.
