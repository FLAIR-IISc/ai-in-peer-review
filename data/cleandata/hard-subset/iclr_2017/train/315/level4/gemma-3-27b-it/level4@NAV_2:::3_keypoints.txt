- Empirical experiments show a correlation among batch size, minimizer sharpness, and generalization gap.
- Authors test mitigation strategies (data augmentation, conservative training, robust optimization) but results are preliminary and not fully effective.
- Recommendation: Reject with encouragement to revise and resubmit.

- Concern: demonstrated correlation between sharpness and generalization does not establish causation; alternative explanations not ruled out.
- Concern: heavy reliance on empirical evidence without strong theoretical foundation.

- Suggested improvement: more comprehensive testing of remedies, including systematic hyperparameter searches and sharpness analysis.
- Suggested improvement: explore other loss-flattening strategies such as sharpness-aware minimization.
- Suggested improvement: investigate effects of network architecture and initialization on observed phenomena.
