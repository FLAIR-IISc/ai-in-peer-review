- Paper is engaging and straightforward to follow.  
- Proposes a concise, clever extension to Neural Programming Interpreters by incorporating recursion.  
- Recursion in the NPI framework significantly improves generalization from few execution traces.  
- Modest extension substantially increases the practicality of the method.  
- Maintains consistent notation with the original DeepMind paper, aiding readability for non-experts.  
- Generalization proofs are somewhat imprecise; enumeration of execution paths is feasible for discrete numerical examples but likely intractable for continuous input spaces (e.g., 3D car).  
- Asks whether the authors plan to release the source code.
