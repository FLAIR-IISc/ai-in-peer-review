This paper significantly improves upon the original NPI work, showing that the model generalizes better when trained on repetitive patterns. The authors demonstrate better sample complexity and generalization results for the addition and bubble sort programs, and add two new and more interesting functions – topological sort and QuickSort (added based on reviewer feedback). Furthermore, they actually prove that the algorithms learned by the model generalize perfectly, which, to my knowledge, is the first time this has been done in neural program induction.
