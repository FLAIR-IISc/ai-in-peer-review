- Paper presents an engaging, accessible contribution.  
- Introduces a modest but effective extension to NPIs by incorporating recursion, improving generalization from limited execution traces.  
- Shows how a minimal modification can substantially improve the practicality of a machine learning approach.  
- Consistent notation with the original DeepMind paper is commendable and aids comprehension and cross-referencing.  
- Main concern: theoretical claims’ generality is unclear, especially applicability to continuous input domains (e.g., 3D car scenario).  
- Requests elaboration or a proposed pathway for proving generalization in continuous cases, noting a rigorous proof may be intractable.  
- Asks whether the authors will release source code to support reproducibility.
