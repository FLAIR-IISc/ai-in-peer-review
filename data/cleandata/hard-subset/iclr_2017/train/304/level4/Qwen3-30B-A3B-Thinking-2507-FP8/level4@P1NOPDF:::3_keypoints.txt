- Advances the NPI framework by showing training on recursively structured traces substantially improves generalization.
- Demonstrates superior sample complexity and generalization performance on addition and bubblesort tasks.
- Introduces two novel, more challenging benchmarks: topological sort and quicksort (quicksort added after reviewer suggestions).
