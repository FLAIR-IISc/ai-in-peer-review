- Substantial improvement over the original NPI work by changing training methodology.  
- Training on recursively structured traces leads to substantially better generalization.  
- Achieves superior sample complexity and generalization on addition and bubblesort tasks.  
- Adds two more challenging tasks — topological sort and quicksort — in response to reviewer suggestions.  
- Provides a formal proof that the learned algorithms generalize perfectly, the first such proof in neural program induction.
