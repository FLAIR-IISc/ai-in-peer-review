- Improves on Neural Programmer-Interpreter by training on recursive program traces.  
- Recursive training yields substantially better generalization.  
- Empirical gains: improved sample complexity and generalization on addition and bubblesort.  
- Introduces two harder tasks (topological sort and quicksort) that strengthen empirical results.  
- Major contribution: formal proof that the learned algorithms generalize perfectly—first such proof in neural program induction.
