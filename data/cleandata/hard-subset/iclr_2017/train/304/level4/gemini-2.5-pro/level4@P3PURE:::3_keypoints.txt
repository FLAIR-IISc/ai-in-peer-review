- Significant improvement over the original Neural Programmer-Interpreter (NPI) framework.
- Training on recursively structured execution traces yields substantially better generalization.
- Demonstrates superior sample complexity and generalization on the original addition and bubblesort tasks.
- Adds two new, more challenging tasks (topological sort and quicksort) in response to reviewer feedback.
- Provides a formal proof that the learned algorithms generalize perfectly — first such proof in neural program induction.
