- Introduces recursion into Neural Programming Interpreters, a clever and effective method that improves generalization from limited execution data.
- A focused yet substantial extension that significantly broadens the practical applicability of NPI-style models.
- Consistent use of notation with the foundational DeepMind paper, which aids comprehension and cross-referencing.
- Primary concern: proofs of generality are limited to discrete examples and it is unclear how they extend to continuous input spaces (e.g., 3D car), where enumerating execution paths may be intractable.
- Suggestion: include a more detailed discussion on generalization in continuous domains.
- Practical request: make the source code publicly available for community use.
