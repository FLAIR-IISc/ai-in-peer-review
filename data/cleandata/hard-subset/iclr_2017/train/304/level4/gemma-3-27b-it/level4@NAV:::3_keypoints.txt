- Substantial advancement over prior Neural Program Induction work, showing markedly improved generalization.  
- Improved generalization achieved by training on recursively structured execution traces.  
- Strong empirical results for sample complexity and generalization on addition and bubblesort.  
- Scope expanded with two novel, harder tasks—topological sort and quicksort—added in response to feedback.  
- Provides a formal proof of perfect generalization for the learned algorithms, a novel contribution to the field.
