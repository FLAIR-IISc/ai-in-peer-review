- Proposes a concise, effective extension to NPIs by incorporating recursion, which improves generalization, especially with limited execution data.
- The addition is a non-trivial, practical enhancement that increases the applicability of the NPI framework.
- Notation is consistent with the original DeepMind NPI paper, easing understanding and cross-reference.
- Proofs and generalization arguments appear sound for discrete examples where execution paths can be exhaustively enumerated.
- Main concern: unclear applicability of the generalization analysis to continuous input spaces (e.g., 3D car example) and potential intractability of formal guarantees there.
- Suggestion: authors should clarify limitations or provide discussion/extension for continuous-domain settings.
- Request: release source code to facilitate reproducibility and further research.
