- Significant improvement over original Neural Program Induction (NPI) work.  
- Training models on recursive-form data yields substantially better generalization.  
- Demonstrated improved sample complexity and generalization on addition and bubble sort.  
- Introduced two more challenging tasks—topological sort and quicksort—based on review feedback.  
- Provided a formal proof of perfect generalization for the learned algorithms.  
- Claimed to be the first such formal generalization result in neural program induction.
