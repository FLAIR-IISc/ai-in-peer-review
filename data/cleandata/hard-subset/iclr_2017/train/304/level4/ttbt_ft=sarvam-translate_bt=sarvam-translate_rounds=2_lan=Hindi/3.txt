This paper significantly improves upon the original NPI work, demonstrating that the model generalizes much better when trained on recurrent traces. The authors have shown improved sample complexity and generalization results for the addition and bubble sort programs, and have added two new and more interesting functions – topological sort and quicksort (added based on reviewer discussion). Furthermore, they actually prove that the algorithms learned by the model are fully generalized, which, to my knowledge, is the first time this has been done in neural program induction.
