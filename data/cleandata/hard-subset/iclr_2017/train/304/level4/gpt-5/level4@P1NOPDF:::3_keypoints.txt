- Substantially improves the original Neural Programmer-Interpreter (NPI) by training on recursive execution traces.
- Training on recursive traces yields markedly stronger generalization.
- Demonstrates improved sample complexity and generalization on addition and bubblesort.
- Introduces two more challenging tasks—topological sort and quicksort (quicksort added after reviewer discussion).
- Provides formal proofs that the learned algorithms generalize perfectly, likely the first such result in neural program induction.
