This paper substantially improves upon the original NPI work, showing that the model generalizes far better when trained on recursive traces. The authors demonstrate improved sample complexity and generalization for addition and bubblesort programs, and introduce two new, more interesting tasks—topological sort and quicksort (with quicksort added based on reviewer discussion). Furthermore, they prove that the algorithms learned by the model generalize perfectly, which, to my knowledge, is the first time this has been shown in neural program induction.
