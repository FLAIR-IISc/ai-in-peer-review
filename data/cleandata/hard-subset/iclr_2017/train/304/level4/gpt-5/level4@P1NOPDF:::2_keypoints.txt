- Paper is interesting and easy to read.
- Proposal: add recursion to Neural Programmer-Interpreters (NPI), substantially improving capability and generalization from fewer execution traces.
- Strong example of a modest, nontrivial change making an ML method more practical.
- Consistent notation with the original DeepMind paper makes it easier for non-experts to follow.
- Main critique: generalization proofs are vague and may not extend beyond discrete/numerical examples.
- Enumerating execution paths works for the paper’s numerical cases but seems intractable for continuous input spaces (e.g., 3D car).
- Reviewer asks whether the authors will release the source code.
