This paper substantially improves on the original NPI work, demonstrating that the model generalizes much better when trained on recursive traces. The authors report improved sample complexity and generalization on addition and bubblesort, and they introduce two new, more interesting tasks—topological sort and quicksort, with the latter added following reviewer discussion. Moreover, they prove that the algorithms learned by the model generalize perfectly, which, to the best of my knowledge, is the first such result in neural program induction.
