- Paper is engaging and accessible, offering a concise and effective enhancement to Neural Programming Interpreters.
- Adding recursion improves generalization from few execution traces, markedly increasing practical utility.
- Maintains consistent notation with the original DeepMind paper, aiding readability and cross-referencing for non-experts.
- Generalization proof is ambiguous—unclear how the method extends to continuous input spaces (e.g., 3D car example); continuous-case generalization may be intractable and needs clarification.
- Suggestion to release source code to support dissemination and further development.
