- Significant improvement over original Neural Programmer-Interpreter (NPI), showing much better generalization when trained on recursive program traces.
- Demonstrates improved sample complexity and generalization for addition and bubblesort tasks.
- Introduces two new, more challenging tasks—topological sort and quicksort—added in response to reviewer discussion.
- Provides a formal proof that the learned algorithms generalize perfectly.
- Claims a milestone: first known formal generalization guarantee in neural program induction.
