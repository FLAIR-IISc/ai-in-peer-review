- Paper is interesting and fairly easy to read.
- Proposes a small but impactful extension: incorporate recursion into Neural Programming Interpreters (NPIs).
- Recursion significantly improves NPI generalization from fewer execution traces.
- Approach is well-motivated and the benefits are clearly explained.
- Notation is consistent with the original DeepMind paper, which helped understanding.
- Critique: generalization proofs are vague; enumerating execution paths may work for discrete cases but is unclear/intractable for continuous input spaces.
- Reviewer asks whether the authors will release the source code.
