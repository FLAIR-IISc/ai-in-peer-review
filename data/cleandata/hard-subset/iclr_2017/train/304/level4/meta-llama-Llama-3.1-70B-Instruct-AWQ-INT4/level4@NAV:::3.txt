This paper constitutes a substantial enhancement of the original NPI work, demonstrating that the model exhibits significantly improved generalization capabilities when trained on recursive traces. The authors achieve superior sample complexity and generalization results for addition and bubblesort programs, while also introducing two novel and more complex tasks - topological sort and quicksort, which were incorporated in response to reviewer feedback. Notably, the authors provide a rigorous proof that the algorithms learned by the model generalize with perfect accuracy, a milestone that, to the best of my knowledge, marks the first such achievement in the field of neural program induction.
