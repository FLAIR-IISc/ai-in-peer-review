- Proposes a small extension to Neural Programming Interpreters that adds recursion, improving generalization from fewer execution traces.  
- Approach is clear and easy to read, with notation consistent with the original DeepMind NPI paper.  
- Demonstrates practical impact of a compact, non-trivial modification making NPIs more useful.  
- Main critique: generalization proofs need more rigor and clarity.  
- Unclear how their generalization analysis extends to continuous input spaces (e.g., 3D car example); proving generalization there may be intractable.  
- Suggests authors address the continuous-case limitation in future work.  
- Asks whether the authors will release the source code.
