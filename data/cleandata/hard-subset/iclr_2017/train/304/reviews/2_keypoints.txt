- Paper is interesting and easy to read.
- Introduces a small but effective extension (recursion) to Neural Programming Interpreters (NPI).
- Allowing recursion improves generalization from fewer execution traces.
- Demonstrates how a small non-trivial change can make the method more practical.
- Reuses DeepMind paper notation, which aids readability for non-experts.
- Critique: generalization proofs are vague; handling continuous input spaces (e.g., 3D car) appears intractable.
- Asks whether the authors will release the source code.
