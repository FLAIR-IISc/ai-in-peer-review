**Summary of the Paper**

This paper proposes a novel approach to visual servoing using learned visual features and predictive dynamics models. The authors introduce a multi-scale, multi-channel, locally-connected variant of the bilinear model to predict visual feature dynamics. They also propose a weighted objective that incorporates long-term policy values and demonstrate its effectiveness in learning an effective visual servo. The paper presents a fitted-value approach to evaluate the proposed method and shows significant improvement over conventional approaches based on image pixels or hand-designed keypoints.
