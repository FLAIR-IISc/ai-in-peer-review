- Combines pre-trained VGG features, a bilinear feature-dynamics model, and fitted Q-iteration to learn visual servoing quickly for new targets with minimal data.  
- Claims effective policies from just 20 training trajectories, outperforming pixel/keypoint baselines and achieving >100x sample-efficiency vs. model-free RL.  
- Argues pre-trained features alone are insufficient and that learning feature weights via Q-iteration yields task-relevant, sample-efficient policies.  
- Well-motivated positioning between hand-designed features and data-hungry deep learning; literature review is comprehensive.  
- Empirical validation is rigorous: CitySim3D benchmark, clear metrics, many baselines (pixel, keypoint, end-to-end CNN, IBVS/PBVS), ablations, generalization tests, and runtime comparisons.  
- Results show VGG conv4_3 features perform best; conv5_3 performs worse (hypothesized due to excessive spatial invariance).  
- Theoretical grounding is solid: weighted servoing objective maps to a linear Q-function approximator and bilinear dynamics are well-motivated.  
- Novelty lies in the specific combination (bilinear dynamics + sample-efficient Q-learning for feature weighting) and demonstrated sample efficiency.  
- Suggest clarifying how learned feature weights contribute to robustness against viewpoint, lighting changes, and occlusion, with qualitative analysis.  
- Recommend more thorough discussion of limitations, especially sim-to-real transfer and failure cases under extreme occlusion.  
- Request analysis/visualization of learned weights (which channels/spatial locations are emphasized) to explain why the method works.  
- Ask for clarification of the downsampling operator between VGG layers (max-pool/avg-pool/other) and its effect on scale information.  
- Request evidence supporting the claim that conv5_3 is “too spatially invariant” (e.g., visual comparisons of conv4_3 vs conv5_3 feature maps).  
- Ask for precise measurement details behind the “two orders of magnitude” sample-efficiency claim (exact trajectory/sample counts and TRPO comparison).  
- Inquire about plans and expected challenges for real-world validation and transfer from CitySim3D to physical systems.
