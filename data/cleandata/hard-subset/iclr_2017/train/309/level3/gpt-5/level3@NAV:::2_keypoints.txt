- Proposal: UNREAL extends A3C with domain-independent auxiliary tasks (pixel-control, feature-control, reward prediction, value replay) sharing a CNN+LSTM representation.  
- Empirical result: UNREAL substantially improves data efficiency and final performance on Atari and DeepMind Lab/Labyrinth.  
- Decision: Accept — method is simple, well-motivated, and yields large, robust gains across benchmarks.  
- Motivation: Leverages abundant pseudo-rewards to shape representation while keeping the base policy on-policy and unbiased.  
- Evidence: Strong empirical results (large mean/median gains on Atari, ~10× data-efficiency on Labyrinth) with ablations and hyperparameter robustness.  
- Integration: Sound design combining parallel on-policy A3C with off-policy replay for auxiliary tasks and shared features without complex algorithmic changes.
