- Introduces UNREAL: A3C policy augmented with off-policy auxiliary tasks shaping a shared CNN+LSTM representation.  
- Demonstrates large improvements in data efficiency and final performance on Atari and a new 3D environment, Labyrinth.  
- Decision: Clear accept.  
- Key strength: Novel hybrid on-policy (A3C) / off-policy (auxiliaries) architecture that improves representation learning.  
- Strong empirical support: extensive experiments showing ~10× speedup, state-of-the-art on 57 Atari games, and robustness to hyperparameters.  
- Auxiliary tasks are general and intuitive: pixel control, feature control, reward prediction, and value replay.  
- Labyrinth environment is a valuable new, visually rich benchmark; ablations on Labyrinth show additive benefit of components.  
- Request to quantify computational cost: report wall-clock training time vs A3C and per-component overhead (e.g., deconv pixel control).  
- Feature control needs stronger validation: current results limited and possibly unstable due to non-stationary control targets.  
- Hyperparameter choices: justify fixing λ_VR and λ_RP = 1.0 or report tuning studies; treat them as tunable like λ_PC.  
- Request Atari ablation studies to confirm auxiliary-task contributions generalize across domains.  
- Training duration: learning curves still increasing—report longer-run saturation and peak agent performance.  
- Clarify that auxiliary control tasks learn separate policies/value functions rather than a single policy optimizing summed rewards.  
- Clarify off-policy training: auxiliary tasks use n-step returns from off-policy data, not standard 1-step Q-learning.  
- Figure/text corrections: reference Figure 2; cite Figure 1(d) in Sec 3.3; fix confusing Figure 3 references (use explicit panel labels).  
- Figure legend fixes: clarify what percentages (e.g., "UNREAL 87%") denote and explain "Clip" for Dueling DQN.  
- Correct Figure 5 inconsistency: text says three Labyrinth levels but plot shows two.  
- Equation corrections: place λ_C inside summation over c ∈ C; use r_{t+k}^{(c)} for auxiliary returns; define L_PC as sum of L_Q^(c).  
- Clarify policy loss sign: present A3C policy objective consistently (maximization vs negative minimization).  
- Typos/grammar: fix subject-verb agreement in Sec 3.2, add missing comma in Sec 4.2, and specify location/access of supplementary materials.
