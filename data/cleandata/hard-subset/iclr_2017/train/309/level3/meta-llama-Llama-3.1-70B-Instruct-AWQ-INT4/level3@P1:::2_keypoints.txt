- Introduces UNREAL: domain-independent auxiliary tasks (control, reward prediction, value replay) sharing CNN+LSTM features to improve deep RL feature learning.  
- Demonstrates improved data efficiency over A3C on Atari and Labyrinth.  
- Paper is well-written and results are convincing; recommend acceptance.  
- Novel, well-motivated approach to improving feature learning in deep RL.  
- Strong empirical improvements in data efficiency compared to A3C across benchmarks.  
- Clear explanation of limitations of prior work and how auxiliary tasks address them.
