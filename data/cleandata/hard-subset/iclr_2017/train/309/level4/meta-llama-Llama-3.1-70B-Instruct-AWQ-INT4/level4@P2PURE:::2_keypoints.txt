- Paper proposes auxiliary domain-independent tasks (feature-control, reward prediction, value replay) sharing a CNN+LSTM to improve feature learning in deep RL and shows improved data efficiency on Atari and Labyrinth vs A3C.
- Paper is well-written, ideas are sound, and reviewer strongly favors acceptance.
- Request to report extra computational cost: impact on training speed and which auxiliary components are most costly.
- Clarify in abstract/introduction that separate policies are learned for each auxiliary task (not a single policy optimizing all pseudo-rewards).
- Feature-control idea is intriguing but preliminary Fig.5 result is weak; reviewer worries about stability and convergence as the task changes during learning.
- Suggest continuing training for best agents to see longer-term performance trends.
- Question why auxiliary-task weight parameters (lambda_*) were fixed to 1 instead of treated/validated as hyperparameters.
- Note auxiliary tasks are trained off-policy with multi-step empirical rewards, not “true” Q-learning — this should be mentioned.
- Minor wording: rephrase the sentence implying L_pi maximizes reward since L_pi is a loss to be minimized.
- Equation 1: lambda_c should be included inside the sum.
- Text below equation 1 should read rt+k^(c) instead of rt^(c).
- Figure 2 is not referenced in the text; Figure 1(d) should be referenced in section 3.3.
- Grammar: change “the features discovered in this manner is shared” to “are shared.”
- Equation 2 references L_PC but this loss is not defined or explicitly included in the equation.
- Explain what “Clip” means for dueling networks in the legend of Figure 3.
- Request additional ablation experiments on Atari to check if individual contributions match Labyrinth patterns.
- Clarify legend of Figure 3 that mentions percentages for Labyrinth — the meaning is unclear.
- In section 4.1.2, correct references: “Figure 3 (right)” → “the top left plot of the figure”; “Figure 3 Top” → “Figure 3 Top Right.”
- Figure 5 caption likely misstates levels shown — left and middle plots appear to show only two levels, not three.
- Section 4.2: missing comma after “methods” in the sentence describing left/right halves of the figure.
- Appendix: state where supplementary materials are located; currently unclear.
- Ask for explicit value/confirmation of lambda_PC (likely 1).
