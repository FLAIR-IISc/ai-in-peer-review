- Proposes extending deep RL with auxiliary control and reward-prediction tasks to improve feature learning.  
- Augments the main policy objective with domain-independent auxiliary tasks used only to shape features.  
- Introduces the UNREAL architecture combining two state-of-the-art deep RL approaches.  
- Demonstrates state-of-the-art results and improved data efficiency on Atari and Labyrinth.  

- Recommendation: accept.  
- Main reasons: well-motivated approach and convincing empirical results on Atari and Labyrinth.  

- Paper is well-written, ideas are sound, and explanations are clear.  
- Use of auxiliary control and reward prediction is novel and effective for feature learning.  
- UNREAL is a significant contribution with demonstrated performance improvements.
