- Paper proposes augmenting RL policy objective with domain-agnostic auxiliary tasks (control, immediate reward prediction, value-function replay) using a shared CNN+LSTM representation.  
- Experiments on Atari and Labyrinth show clear benefits, notably much better data efficiency than A3C.  
- Paper is well written, ideas are sound, results convincing; recommendation: accept.  

High-level concerns:
- Please discuss additional computational cost of optimizing auxiliary tasks: training speed lost and which components are most expensive.  
- Clarify in abstract/intro that separate policies are learned for each auxiliary task (single-policy interpretation is misleading).  
- Feature-control idea not convincingly validated empirically; Fig.5 shows only slight initial benefit and evolving representations may cause stability/convergence issues.  
- Since performance is still improving, run best agents longer to show eventual performance.  
- Why aren’t auxiliary-task weights (λ_*) treated as hyperparameters; were experiments run to justify using 1?  
- Note auxiliary tasks are not trained with “true” Q-learning because they are off-policy with multi-step empirical returns.  

Minor issues / corrections:
- The policy gradient loss expression is presented as a loss to be minimized, wording should reflect that.  
- In Eq.1, λ_c should be inside the summation.  
- Just below Eq.1, r_t^(c) should be r_{t+k}^(c).  
- Figure 2 is not referenced in text; Figure 1(d) should be referenced in Section 3.3.  
- Grammar: “the features discovered in this manner is shared” → “are shared.”  
- Eq.2 references L_PC, but that term is not defined or explicitly in Eq.2.  
- Explain what “Clip” means for dueling networks in Figure 3 legend.  
- Request more ablations on Atari to compare individual-task contributions with Labyrinth.  
- In Figure 3 legend, the percentages refer to Labyrinth; this should be explicitly stated in the text.  
- Multiple figure/text reference errors: Section 4.1.2 and surrounding figure references (top/left/right) need correction.  
- Figure 5 caption/text inconsistencies about “top 5 hyperparameter settings on three Labyrinth levels” appear to refer to only two levels.  
- Section 4.2 sentence needs punctuation: add comma after “methods.”  
- Appendix: indicate where supplementary details can be found.  
- What is the value of λ_PC? (Reviewer assumes 1.)
