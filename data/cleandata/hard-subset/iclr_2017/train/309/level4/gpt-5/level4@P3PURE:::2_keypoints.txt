- Paper proposes using domain-independent auxiliary tasks (auxiliary control, reward prediction, value replay) with a shared CNN+LSTM encoder to improve representation learning in deep RL.
- Experiments on Atari and Labyrinth show clear benefits, notably improved data efficiency compared to A3C.
- Overall assessment: well written, sound ideas, convincing empirical results, recommended for acceptance.
- Request quantification of computational overhead: report wall-clock slowdown and which auxiliary components dominate cost.
- Clarify policy multiplicity early: auxiliary control tasks use separate policies (not a single policy optimizing all pseudo-rewards).
- Feature-control validation is weak: Fig. 5 shows only slight initial benefit and evolving feature targets may cause stability/convergence issues—needs analysis/experiments.
- Training duration question: why not train top agents longer to show ultimate performance?
- Auxiliary-task loss weights (λ_*) should be treated/validated as hyperparameters rather than fixed to 1.
- Q-learning detail: explicitly state auxiliary tasks are not using “true” Q-learning due to off-policy, multi-step empirical updates.
- Minor correction: policy gradient loss phrasing — the stated expression is a loss to be minimized.
- Eq. 1 fixes: λ_c should be inside the summation; r_t^(c) should be r_{t+k}^(c).
- Referencing and labeling issues: Figure 2 not referenced; Figure 1(d) should be cited in §3.3; multiple Figure 3/5 caption and text mismatches need correction.
- Grammar/typo fixes: “the features discovered in this manner is shared” → “are shared”; define L_PC which is referred to around Eq. 2.
- Clarify “Clip” in the legend for dueling networks in Figure 3.
- Request additional ablations on Atari to compare individual contribution patterns with Labyrinth.
- Explicitly state that legend percentages in Figure 3 refer to Labyrinth.
- Fix textual figure references in §4.1.2 and §4.2 (several incorrect top/left/right mentions and missing commas).
- Appendix/supplementary materials: indicate where these materials can be accessed.
- Ask for explicit value of λ_PC (assumed to be 1).
