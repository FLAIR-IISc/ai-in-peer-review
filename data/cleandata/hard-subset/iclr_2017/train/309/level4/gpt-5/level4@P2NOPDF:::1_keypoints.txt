- Introduces UNREAL: an A3C-style agent augmented with unsupervised auxiliary tasks.
- Proposes multiple auxiliary control tasks and auxiliary reward-prediction tasks.
- Evaluated on DeepMind Lab (Labyrinth) and Atari.
- UNREAL consistently outperforms the A3C baseline and learns faster.
- Strong contribution to the conference.
- Improvements not especially surprising—well-chosen auxiliary tasks are expected to improve representations and speed training; paper is mainly a proof-of-concept.
- Paper is clearly written and accessible to deep RL readers.
