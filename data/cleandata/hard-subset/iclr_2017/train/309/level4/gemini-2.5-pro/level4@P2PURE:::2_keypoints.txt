- Paper proposes auxiliary, domain-independent tasks (pixel change, reward prediction, value replay) to improve shared representation learning in deep RL.
- Experiments on Atari and Labyrinth show substantial data-efficiency gains over baseline A3C.
- Paper is well-written, ideas are sound, results convincing; reviewer recommends acceptance.
- Request comment on additional computational cost and which auxiliary components are most expensive.
- Clarify that a separate policy is learned for each auxiliary control task rather than a single composite-policy.
- Empirical support for the feature-control task is weak and potentially unstable because features change during learning; request discussion of stability/convergence.
- Suggest running longer training to see how far best agents improve, since performance was still increasing.
- Ask whether the auxiliary-task weights (λ coefficients) were treated as hyperparameters and whether tuning was performed.
- Note that auxiliary tasks are trained off-policy with multi-step returns; this should be explicitly stated.
- Minor: policy-gradient loss L_pi is described inconsistently (maximize expected reward vs a defined loss to minimize).
- Minor: In Equation 1, λ_c should be inside the summation.
- Minor: Just below Equation 1, r_t^(c) should read r_{t+k}^(c).
- Minor: Figure 2 is not referenced; Figure 1(d) should be referenced in Section 3.3.
- Minor: Grammar—"the features discovered in this manner is shared" should be "are shared."
- Minor: Loss term L_PC is referenced in text but not explicitly defined in the equation.
- Minor: Explain meaning of "Clip" in legend for Figure 3 (dueling networks context).
- Minor: Ablation studies on Labyrinth are informative; request similar ablations for Atari to confirm patterns.
- Minor: Legend of Figure 3 percentages correspond to Labyrinth experiments and should be stated explicitly.
- Minor: Incorrect figure references in Section 4.1.2 (Figure 3 (right) vs top-left/top-right inconsistencies).
- Minor: Figure 5 caption claims three Labyrinth levels but appears to show two; preceding text may need correction.
- Minor: Missing punctuation after "methods." in Section 4.2 sentence.
- Minor: Appendix refers to supplementary materials but their location is unclear.
- Minor: Value for λ_PC is not specified (assumed to be 1); please state explicitly.
