- Strong paper: introduces auxiliary, domain-independent tasks (reward prediction, value replay, novel feature control) to shape shared features; shows improved data efficiency vs A3C on Atari and Labyrinth; recommend acceptance.

Major comments:
- Computational cost: discuss training overhead (wall‑clock time/frames per second) and which auxiliary components are most expensive.
- Multiple policies clarity: clarify that separate policies are learned for different control tasks; current wording may imply a single composite policy.
- Feature control validation: empirical support is weak—only marginal, early-stage benefit shown; non‑stationarity of features may cause stability/convergence issues.
- Asymptotic performance: run longer training for best agents to assess steady‑state/asymptotic performance.
- Auxiliary task weights: clarify whether λ_* were tuned or fixed (e.g., at 1) and provide justification or ablation studies.
- Off‑policy training: explicitly state that auxiliary tasks use an off‑policy, multi‑step Q‑learning variant rather than standard Q‑learning.

Minor comments and corrections:
- Policy gradient wording: text says it maximizes expected reward but L_π is written as a loss to be minimized.
- Equation 1: λ_c should be inside the summation.
- Just below Eq.1: r_t^(c) should be r_{t+k}^(c).
- Figure 2 is not referenced; Figure 1(d) should be referenced in Section 3.3.
- Grammar: "the features discovered in this manner is shared" → "are shared."
- Equation 2: loss L_PC is referenced in text but not explicitly defined in the equation.
- Clarify "Clip" in Figure 3 legend, especially regarding dueling networks.
- Request an Atari-domain ablation analogous to the Labyrinth ablation to compare auxiliary task contributions.
- Clarify that percentages in Figure 3 legend refer to Labyrinth performance.
- Incorrect subplot references in Section 4.1.2 (e.g., "Figure 3 (right)" and "Figure 3 Top" appear incorrect).
- Figure 5 caption claims three Labyrinth levels but plots only two; caption/text need correction.
- Punctuation missing in Section 4.2 sentence describing left/right halves of the figure.
- Appendix refers to supplementary materials that are not available.
- What value was used for λ_PC? (Reviewer assumes 1.)
