- Proposes auxiliary tasks (control, immediate reward prediction, value function replay) with a shared CNN+LSTM to improve feature learning in deep RL, showing data-efficiency gains over A3C on Atari and Labyrinth.
- Paper is well-written, ideas are sound, results convincing — recommendation: accept.
- Request to quantify computational overhead of auxiliary-task optimization (impact on training speed and which components are most expensive).
- Clarify abstract/introduction to explicitly state the agent learns separate policies for each task (was ambiguous until equation (1)).
- Empirical validation of the “feature control” auxiliary task appears weak; initial benefits are subtle and evolving features raise stability/convergence concerns.
- Recommend longer training runs for top agents because performance appears to keep improving.
- Treat auxiliary task weights (λ*) as tunable hyperparameters; ask whether experiments justify using value 1.
- Acknowledge that auxiliary tasks are not trained with standard Q-Learning but off-policy with multi-step empirical rewards (as discussed on OpenReview).
- Clarify that L_pi is a loss to be minimized (not just “adjust the policy to maximise…”).
- In equation (1), include λ_c within the summation.
- Just below equation (1), r_t^(c) should be written as r_{t+k}^{(c)}.
- Figure 2 is not referenced in the text; Figure 1(d) should be referenced in section 3.3.
- Grammar fix: “the features discovered in this manner is shared” → “are shared.”
- The text around equation (2) refers to loss term L_PC which is not defined and does not appear in equation (2).
- Clarify what “Clip” means in the context of dueling networks in the legend of Figure 3.
- More ablation studies on Atari are needed to check if individual task contribution patterns from Labyrinth generalize.
- State explicitly in the text that the percentages in the legend of Figure 3 refer to Labyrinth.
- Correct figure references in section 4.1.2: “Figure 3 (right)” actually refers to the top-left plot; “Figure 3 Top” should be “Figure 3 Top Right.”
- Fix Figure 5/text mismatch: the caption/text refers to three Labyrinth levels but the left and middle plots represent only two levels; adjust wording accordingly.
- In section 4.2, add punctuation after “methods” (“...for all three methods, the right half shows…”).
- Appendix claims supplementary materials are included but they are not provided — supply or remove the statement.
- Specify the value assigned to λ_PC (presumably 1) in the paper.
