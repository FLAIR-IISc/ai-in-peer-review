- Introduces auxiliary tasks (feature control, immediate reward prediction, value function replay) with separate policies to shape learned features in deep RL.  
- Evaluated on Atari and Labyrinth, showing improved data efficiency over A3C.  
- Recommendation: Accept — significant, well-written contribution with strong experimental support.  
- Strength: Well-motivated core idea and effective implementation; diverse environments strengthen claims; improvements in data efficiency and robustness notable.  
- Request to quantify computational cost and trade-off between performance gains and increased training time; identify most expensive components.

- Recommend sensitivity analysis/hyperparameter optimization for auxiliary task weights (λ*); justify choice of fixed values (e.g., 1).  
- Acknowledge that auxiliary tasks use off-policy Q-learning with multi-step empirical rewards (as discussed in OpenReview).
