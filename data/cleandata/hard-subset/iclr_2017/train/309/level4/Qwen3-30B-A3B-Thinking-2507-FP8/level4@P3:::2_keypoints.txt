- Introduces UNREAL: an RL agent that improves feature learning by optimizing multiple auxiliary tasks (pixel control, feature control, reward prediction) alongside the main reward objective.  
- Key innovation: learns separate policies for each pseudo-reward while sharing a common representation to shape the feature extractor.  
- Empirical results: strong gains on Atari (880% human-normalized) and Labyrinth (87% with 10× faster learning), improving final performance and learning speed over A3C.  
- Decision: Accept.  
- Key reasons for acceptance: well-motivated contribution, novel auxiliary-task approach, rigorous and statistically significant experiments.  
- Addresses a core limitation—data inefficiency—by shaping representations via auxiliary tasks rather than input reconstruction.  
- Ablation studies show individual contributions of each auxiliary task; results are consistent across benchmarks.  
- Demonstrates improved robustness to hyperparameters, enhancing practical applicability.  
- Suggestion 1: Clarify how multiple policies are learned (simultaneously vs. sequentially) since the abstract is ambiguous.  
- Suggestion 2: Report computational cost of auxiliary tasks—training time increase and most costly components.  
- Suggestion 3: Provide more empirical validation for feature control beyond preliminary Figure 5 to show consistency across environments.  
- Suggestion 4: Analyze whether longer training yields further gains given the claim that performance keeps improving.  
- Suggestion 5: Justify the fixed auxiliary-task weights (λ = 1) and discuss sensitivity or tuning of these weights.  
- Question 1: Are the separate policies trained in parallel or sequentially, and how is representation sharing implemented?  
- Question 2: Under what conditions does feature control outperform baseline/pixel control, and which environments benefit most?  
- Question 3: How do the shared features specifically improve main-task performance beyond general representation learning?  
- Question 4: Where exactly are the supplementary implementation details and ablation results located?  
- Overall appraisal: well-written, significant contribution with convincing results; minor clarifications and extra analyses would further strengthen the paper.
