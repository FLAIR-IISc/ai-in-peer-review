- Proposes UNREAL: augmenting A3C with auxiliary tasks (pixel control, feature control, reward prediction) to improve representation learning, data efficiency, speed, and robustness.  
- Empirical gains: >2× human-normalized performance on 13 Labyrinth levels (87% vs 54%) and strong improvement on 57 Atari games (880% mean human-normalized).  
- Decision: Accept — strong, well-motivated, well-executed contribution addressing sparse rewards and representation learning.  
- Clear motivation: identifies sparse rewards as a representation-learning problem and distinguishes UNREAL from options/Horde/UVFA/successor representations by using auxiliaries solely for representation learning.  
- Rigorous evaluation: thorough experiments and ablations across Labyrinth and Atari, showing faster learning (10× in Labyrinth) and robustness to hyperparameters.  
- Scientific rigor and clarity: reproducible methodology, ablation evidence (pixel control > reward prediction > reconstruction), and hyperparameter robustness analyses.  
- Core strength: focuses on explaining why auxiliary tasks improve representations rather than acting as reward-maximizers.  
- Suggested improvement: add theoretical justification for why auxiliary tasks yield better representations (e.g., causal links to state changes).  
- Suggested improvement: quantify computational overhead (training time and memory) of auxiliary tasks.  
- Suggested improvement: discuss generalization of findings to other domains (robotics, NLP).  
- Question for authors: clarify how skewed sampling for reward prediction (equal zero/non-zero rewards) affects state distribution and possible representation bias.  
- Question for authors: explain why feature control is slightly worse than pixel control and whether specific network features could improve it.  
- Question for authors: clarify exact normalization method used for the reported 880% mean and 250% median human-normalized Atari scores.  
- Question for authors: provide specific data-efficiency numbers for Atari comparable to the 10× Labyrinth speedup.
