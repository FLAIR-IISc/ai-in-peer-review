- Summary: Proposes UNREAL, an RL agent using auxiliary pixel/feature control and reward prediction to learn richer representations and improve data efficiency and robustness in Labyrinth and Atari.  
- Decision: Accept with minor revisions — substantial contribution with strong results and clear methodology.  
- Strong empirical results: Significant gains over A3C with faster convergence and impressive human-normalized scores and robustness.  
- Novel combination of techniques: Effectively combines auxiliary tasks, A3C, and replay buffers rather than introducing entirely new concepts.  
- Clear motivation & related work: Well-positioned within literature, addressing perceptual sparsity and representation learning.  
- Ablation studies: Demonstrates individual contributions of pixel control, feature control, and reward prediction.  
- Detailed implementation: Appendix provides thorough details aiding reproducibility.  
- Clarity of loss function: Equation (2) is complex; suggest summarizing typical λVR, λPC, λRP values in a table.  
- Hyperparameter sensitivity: Request more detail on which hyperparameters were most sensitive and tuning strategies used.  
- Visualizations: Recommend larger figures, clearer labels, and finer hyperparameter-space representations.  
- Computational cost: Ask for discussion of training-time and computational overhead compared to A3C.  
- Connection to intrinsic motivation: Recommend explicitly relating auxiliary tasks to intrinsic motivation literature.  
- Q — Reward prediction skewing: What exact skewing method was used and were alternatives tested?  
- Q — Feature control testing: Why was feature control tested on only one level and were there limitations to broader testing?  
- Q — Deconvolutional networks: Why choose deconv nets for pixel/feature control versus simpler alternatives and what benefits did they provide?  
- Q — Scalability: How will UNREAL scale to more complex environments or larger state spaces; any anticipated bottlenecks?  
- Q — Negative results: Were other auxiliary tasks tried that failed, and if so, which and why?  
- Overall: High-quality paper with significant contribution; addressing the minor suggestions would strengthen impact.
