- Core idea: agents bootstrap communication through interaction instead of relying on passive data.  
- Well-motivated solution addressing interactive language learning for AI.  
- Experimental setup is clearly described and reproducible.  
- Results show convergence to optimal policies and emergence of meaningful communication tokens.  
- Extensions: experiments varying environments to shape semantics and grounding agent language in human language.
