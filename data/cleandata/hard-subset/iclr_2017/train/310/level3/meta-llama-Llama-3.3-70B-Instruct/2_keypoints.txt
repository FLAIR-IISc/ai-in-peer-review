- Introduces Deep Variational Bayes Filters (DVBF), combining stochastic gradient variational Bayes with a reparametrized transition model for efficient inference and latent dynamics learning.  
- Tackles learning latent state-space models from high-dimensional, non‑Markovian sequence data (e.g., images) with intractable inference.  
- Paper is well-structured with a clear methodological explanation and advantages over prior approaches.  
- Thorough related-work review highlighting limitations of existing methods.  
- Experiments on dynamic pendulum, bouncing ball, and two bouncing balls demonstrate DVBF outperforms Deep Kalman Filters in latent-state recovery and stable long-term predictions.
