Thank you for reading an interesting article.

Pros:
- This paper discusses a very important problem in understanding communication between two agents. With the increased application of reinforcement learning, this method brings us back to a fundamental question: is the machine's problem-solving method like that of humans?
- The task is simple enough to make post-learning analysis intuitive.
- It was interesting to see how informed agents use multiple symbols to send messages, while uninformed agents rely on only two symbols.

Cons:
- If the two images sent come from different categories, the task effectively becomes image classification. The symbols used effectively classify the image, which the second agent learns to determine which of the images it is. Considering all aspects, this method becomes a transfer learning problem that can potentially train much faster than a reinforcement learning algorithm.
