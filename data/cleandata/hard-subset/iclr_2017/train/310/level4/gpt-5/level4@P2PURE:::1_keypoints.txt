- Addresses an important problem: understanding communication between two agents and whether machine problem-solving resembles human problem-solving.  
- Uses a simple task that makes post-learning analysis intuitive.  
- Empirical finding: informed agents used multiple symbols to communicate, while agnostic agents relied on only two symbols.  
- Major critique: when images are from different categories the task reduces to image classification/transfer learning (symbols map to classes), which could be learned faster than with RL.
