Thank you for an interesting read.

Pros
- The paper tackles an important problem: understanding communication between two agents. As reinforcement learning is applied more broadly, this work returns to a fundamental question: does machine problem-solving resemble that of humans?
- The task is simple enough to make the post-learning analysis intuitive.
- It was interesting to see that informed agents used multiple symbols to transmit messages, whereas agnostic agents relied on only two symbols.

Cons
- If the two images sent are from different categories, the task effectively reduces to image classification. The symbols are essentially the image class, which the second agent learns to assign to one of the images. In this sense, the approach reduces to a transfer learning problem that could likely be trained much faster than a reinforcement learning algorithm.
