- Proposes a referential game: sender receives binary target t and both agents observe two images; sender sends a symbol so receiver can recover t.  
- Agents are neural networks using pretrained image features and are trained with REINFORCE.  
- Agents converge to optimal policies and the learned communication symbols reflect meaningful concepts.  
- A variant grounded in different image classes shows the agents learn even more meaningful concepts.  
- A multi-game setup alternates the referential game with supervised image classification, which further aligns symbols with meaningful/human concepts.  
- Contribution is novel and useful as an alternative to seq2seq approaches, emphasizing functional aspects of language; reviewer recommends acceptance.
