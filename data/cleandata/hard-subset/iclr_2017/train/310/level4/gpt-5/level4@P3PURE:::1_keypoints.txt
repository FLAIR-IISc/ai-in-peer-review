- Tackles the important problem of understanding how two agents communicate and whether machine problem-solving resembles human reasoning.
- Uses a simple task that makes post-learning analysis intuitive and interpretable.
- Finds that informed agents employ multiple symbols while agnostic agents rely on only two symbols.
- Criticism: When images are from different categories the task effectively becomes image classification/transfer learning (symbols correspond to classes), which could be solved faster with non-RL methods.
