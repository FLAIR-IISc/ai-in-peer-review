Thank you for an interesting read.

Pros
- The paper addresses an important problem: understanding communication between two agents. As reinforcement learning is applied to more domains, this work revisits a fundamental question: Is machine problem-solving similar to that of humans?
- The task is simple enough to make post-learning analysis intuitive.
- It is interesting that informed agents used multiple symbols to transmit messages, whereas agnostic agents relied on only two symbols.

Cons
- When the two transmitted images come from different categories, the task effectively reduces to image classification. The symbols essentially correspond to image classes, which the second agent learns to assign to one of the images. In this sense, the approach amounts to a transfer learning problem that could likely be trained much faster than a reinforcement learning algorithm.
