Thank you for an interesting read.

Pros
- The paper addresses a crucial problem: understanding communication between two agents. As applications of reinforcement learning expand, this work returns to a fundamental question: Is the problem-solving approach of machines similar to that of humans?
- The task is simple enough to make post-learning analysis intuitive.
- It is interesting to observe that informed agents use multiple symbols to transmit messages, whereas agnostic agents rely on only two symbols.

Cons
- The task effectively reduces to image classification when the two images presented are from different categories. The symbols used are essentially the image classes, which the second agent learns to assign to one of the images. In this light, the approach amounts to a transfer learning problem that could likely be trained much faster than a reinforcement learning algorithm.
