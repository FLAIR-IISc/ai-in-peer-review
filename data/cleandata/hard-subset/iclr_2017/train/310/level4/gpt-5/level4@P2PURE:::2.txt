Training natural language systems by placing multiple agents in an interactive referential communication game is a strong idea. As the authors note, there is some prior work on using multi-agent games to teach communication, though apparently not much; this certainly seems like a direction worth pursuing. Moreover, the strategy of alternating between these games and supervised learning—as implemented in Section 5 and suggested in Section 6—seems particularly fruitful.
