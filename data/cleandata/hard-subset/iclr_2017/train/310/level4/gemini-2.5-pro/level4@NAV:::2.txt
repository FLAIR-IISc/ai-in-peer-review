The concept of training natural language systems through multi-agent, interactive referential communication games is compelling. As the authors note, there is precedent for using multi-agent games to teach communication, and this certainly appears to be a valuable research avenue. The proposed method of alternating between these games and supervised learning, as executed in Section 5 and suggested for future work in Section 6, seems especially effective.
