- Core idea of training NL systems via multi-agent interactive referential communication games is compelling and underexplored.
- Alternating between these games and supervised learning (Section 5; suggested in Section 6) is a particularly fruitful approach.
- Presentation of the model is unclear; Figure 1 appears to omit network connections and the Section 3 description is hard to follow.
- Figure 1 needs refinement and at minimum a detailed caption to clarify the architecture and connections.
- The framework in Section 2 is excellent, effective, and useful for various applications.
- Results are reasonable and likely to inspire interesting future variations.
- Caveat: reviewer is confident about understanding the paper but not sufficiently familiar with closely related literature to judge novelty or placement.
