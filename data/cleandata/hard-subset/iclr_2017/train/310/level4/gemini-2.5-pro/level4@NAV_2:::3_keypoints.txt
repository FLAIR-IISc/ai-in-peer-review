- Agents converge to optimal policies and develop a symbolic code that captures semantic concepts.
- Grounding the game on object classes (vs. image instances) enhances semantic structure of the emergent language.
- A multi-task setup alternating the referential game with supervised image classification grounds symbols in natural language, improving interpretability.
- Novel and promising paradigm: learns communication from first principles then aligns it with human language.
- Valuable alternative to supervised seq2seq models by framing language as multi-agent coordination rather than statistical learning.
