- Referential game setup: sender and receiver see an image pair; sender gets binary target t, sends one symbol, receiver picks target; shared reward on success.  
- Agents are neural nets processing pre-trained image features and trained end-to-end with REINFORCE.  
- Agents converge to optimal policies and the learned protocol captures meaningful visual concepts.  
- Class-grounded variant produces even more interpretable concepts.  
- Multi-tasking (alternating referential game with supervised classification) grounds symbols in human semantics, yielding more semantically meaningful codes.  
- Framework presents a novel, functional approach to studying language emergence (contrast to seq2seq statistical modeling).  
- Experiments include insightful analyses and visualizations of what tokens represent.
