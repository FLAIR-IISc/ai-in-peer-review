The paper's core idea of training natural language systems through multi-agent, interactive referential communication games is compelling. As the authors note, while there is some prior work on using multi-agent games for communication, this direction appears underexplored and is certainly worth pursuing. Furthermore, the proposed approach of alternating between these games and supervised learning, as demonstrated in the experiment in Section 5 and suggested in Section 6, seems particularly fruitful.
