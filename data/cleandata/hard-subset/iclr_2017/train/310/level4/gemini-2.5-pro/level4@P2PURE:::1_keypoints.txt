- Addresses understanding of inter-agent communication and whether machines develop human-like problem-solving strategies.
- Task simplicity enables intuitive post-learning analysis of agent behaviors.
- Key finding: "informed" agents use multiple symbols while "agnostic" agents rely on only two symbols.
- Criticism: with images from different categories the task reduces to image classification (symbols as class labels), making the setup akin to transfer learning that could be solved more efficiently than with reinforcement learning.
