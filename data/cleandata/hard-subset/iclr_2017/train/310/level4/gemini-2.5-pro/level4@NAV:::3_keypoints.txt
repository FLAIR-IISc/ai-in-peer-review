- Presents a referential game where sender (seeing binary target t) sends a symbol and receiver predicts t; both see image pairs and agents are neural nets trained with REINFORCE on pre-trained image features.  
- Agents converge to optimal policies and the emergent symbolic protocol encodes meaningful concepts.  
- Grounding the game in specific image classes produces more distinct learned concepts.  
- A multi-game setup alternating referential play with supervised classification further grounds representations, yielding more semantically meaningful communication.  
- Contributions: task formulation, framework, and insightful analysis of emergent communication as a promising alternative to sequence-to-sequence models.
