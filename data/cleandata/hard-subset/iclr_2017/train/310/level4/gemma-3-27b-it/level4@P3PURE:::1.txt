Thank you for an interesting read.

**Pros**

- This paper tackles a crucial problem: understanding communication between two agents. As the applications of reinforcement learning continue to expand, this work brings us back to a fundamental question – is the problem-solving approach of machines analogous to that of humans?
- The task is simple enough to allow for intuitive post-learning analysis.
- It was interesting to observe that informed agents utilized multiple symbols to transmit messages, whereas agnostic agents relied on only two symbols.

**Cons**

- The task effectively reduces to image classification when the two transmitted images are from different categories. The symbols used are, in effect, image classes that the receiving agent learns to assign to either image. Consequently, this approach largely resembles a transfer learning problem, which could potentially be trained more efficiently than with a reinforcement learning algorithm.
