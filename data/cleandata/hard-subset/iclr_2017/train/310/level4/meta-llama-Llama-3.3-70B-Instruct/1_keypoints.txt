- Focuses on a critical question: how machines communicate and whether they approach problem-solving like humans, especially in RL contexts.
- Uses a simple task that enables intuitive post-learning analysis.
- Finds that informed agents use multiple symbols while agnostic agents rely on only two.
- Limitation: when sent images are from different classes, the task reduces to image classification/transfer learning (symbols ≈ image class), making RL an inefficient choice.
