Thank you for reading this interesting article.

Pros:
- This paper tackles a very important problem of understanding communication between 2 agents. As more and more applications of reinforcement learning are being explored, this approach brings us back to a fundamental question: is the problem-solving approach of machines similar to that of humans?
- The task is quite simple to facilitate post-learning analysis.
- It was interesting to see how knowledgeable agents used multiple symbols to transmit messages, while ignorant agents relied on only 2 symbols.

Cons:
- If the 2 images sent are from different categories, the task effectively becomes limited to image classification. The symbols used are effectively image classes that the other agent learns to assign to any of the images. In any case, this approach is limited to a transfer learning problem that can probably be trained much faster than a reinforcement learning algorithm.
