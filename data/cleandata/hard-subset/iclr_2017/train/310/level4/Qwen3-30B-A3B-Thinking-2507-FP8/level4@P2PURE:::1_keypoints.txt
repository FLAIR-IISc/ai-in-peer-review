- Addresses a fundamental challenge in understanding inter-agent communication and whether machine problem-solving mirrors human cognition.  
- Simple task design enables intuitive post-hoc analysis.  
- Observes that informed agents use multiple symbols while agnostic agents rely on only two symbols.  
- Limitation: when the two images belong to distinct categories, the task reduces to image classification/transfer learning (symbols act as class labels) and could be trained far faster than the proposed RL approach.
