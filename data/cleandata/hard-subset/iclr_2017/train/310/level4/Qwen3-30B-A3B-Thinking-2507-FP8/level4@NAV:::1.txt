

Thank you for this engaging contribution.  
Pros  
- The paper addresses a fundamental challenge in understanding communication dynamics between agents. As reinforcement learning applications expand, this work revisits a core question: do machine problem-solving strategies mirror human cognition?  
- The task design enables intuitive post-training analysis due to its simplicity.  
- It is insightful to observe informed agents leveraging multiple symbols for message transmission, whereas agnostic agents restrict communication to exactly two symbols.  
Cons  
- The task effectively reduces to image classification when the two transmitted images belong to distinct categories. The symbols function as learned image class identifiers, making the approach equivalent to a transfer learning problem that could likely be trained significantly more efficiently than the presented reinforcement learning framework.
