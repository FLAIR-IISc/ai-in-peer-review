Thank you for reading this interesting article.

Pros:
- This paper tackles a very important problem of understanding communication between 2 agents. With the exploration of more and more applications of reinforcement learning, this approach brings us back to a fundamental question: is the problem-solving approach of machines similar to that of humans?
- The task is quite simple to facilitate post-learning analysis.
- It was interesting to see how knowledgeable agents used multiple symbols to transmit messages, while ignorant agents relied on only 2 symbols.

Cons:
- If the 2 images sent are from different categories, the task effectively becomes limited to image classification. The symbols used are effectively image classes that other agents learn to assign to any image. In any case, this approach is limited to a transfer learning problem that can probably be trained much faster than a reinforcement learning algorithm.
