- Tackles the important problem of understanding inter-agent communication in RL, raising whether machines solve problems similarly to humans.
- Uses a simple task that makes post-learning analysis intuitive and clarifies agent behavior.
- Finds informed agents use multiple symbols to communicate while agnostic agents rely on only two symbols.
- Critique: the task can reduce to image classification/transfer learning when images are from different categories, so it might be solvable faster than with RL.
