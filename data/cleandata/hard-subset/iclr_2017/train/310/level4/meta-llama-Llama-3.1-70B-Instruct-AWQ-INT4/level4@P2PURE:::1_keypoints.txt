- Tackles understanding communication between two agents, probing whether machines use human-like problem-solving strategies in RL settings.
- The task is simple, enabling intuitive post-learning analysis.
- Observed behavior: informed agents used multiple symbols, while agnostic agents relied on only two symbols.
- Limitation: when images are from different categories the problem reduces to image classification (symbols ≈ image class), making it analogous to transfer learning that could be trained faster than RL.
