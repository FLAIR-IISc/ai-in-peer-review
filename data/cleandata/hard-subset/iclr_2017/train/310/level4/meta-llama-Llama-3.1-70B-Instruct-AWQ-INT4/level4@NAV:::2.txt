The idea of training natural language systems through multi-agent interactive referential communication games is an attractive approach. The authors acknowledge that this concept has been explored in prior research, albeit to a limited extent, and it appears to be a promising direction for further investigation. The proposed method of combining these games with supervised learning, as demonstrated in Section 5 and suggested in Section 6, seems particularly effective.
