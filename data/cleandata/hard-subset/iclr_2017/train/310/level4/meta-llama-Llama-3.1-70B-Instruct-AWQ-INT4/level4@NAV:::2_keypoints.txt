- Training NL systems via multi-agent interactive referential communication games is an attractive and promising approach.  
- Authors acknowledge prior, limited exploration of this concept and position their work as extending it.  
- Combining these games with supervised learning (Sections 5 and 6) appears particularly effective.  
- Figure 1 omits some network connections, creating confusion—diagram refinement and an explanatory caption are needed.  
- The customized architecture and the convoluted description in Section 3 hinder clarity and require simplification.  
- The framework in Section 2 is well-designed and likely useful across applications.  
- Results are reasonable and the work is likely to inspire interesting future variations.  
- Reviewer’s limited familiarity with the closest related literature may affect the assessment of the paper’s contribution.
