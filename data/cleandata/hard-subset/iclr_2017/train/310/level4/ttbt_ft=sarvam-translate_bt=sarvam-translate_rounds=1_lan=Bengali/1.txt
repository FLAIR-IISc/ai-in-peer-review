Thank you for reading an interesting article.

Pros:
- This paper discusses a very important problem in understanding communication between two agents. With the increased application of reinforcement learning, this method brings us back to a fundamental question: is the machine's problem-solving method similar to that of humans?
- The task is simple enough to make post-learning analysis intuitive.
- It was interesting to see how informed agents used multiple symbols to send messages, while uninformed agents relied on only two symbols.

Cons:
- If the two images sent come from different categories, the task effectively boils down to image classification. The symbols used effectively classify the image, which the second agent learns to determine any one of the images. Considering all aspects, this method boils down to a transfer learning problem that can potentially be trained much faster than a reinforcement learning algorithm.
