- Introduces a framework where tabula rasa agents develop meaningful communication via referential coordination games, capturing high-level semantic properties and grounded in human language through supervised learning.

- Recommendation: accept with minor revisions; grounding approach is a promising path toward human-interpretable AI communication.

- Contribution 1: Addresses how agents can communicate meaningfully with humans, grounded in Lewis' signaling game and distinguished from prior multi-agent work.

- Contribution 3: Grounding method (referential game + supervised classification) produces partially human-interpretable protocols (68% accuracy) and demonstrates metonymic generalization.

- Feedback 1: Add discussion of limitations and scalability to more complex language tasks (relationships, actions, abstract concepts).
