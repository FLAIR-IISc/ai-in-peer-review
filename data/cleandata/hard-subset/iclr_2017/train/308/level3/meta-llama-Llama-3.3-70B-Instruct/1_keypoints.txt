- Proposes Group Sparse Autoencoders (GSA) and Group Sparse CNNs (GSCNNs) that impose inter- and intra-group sparse constraints for dictionary learning and sparse coding.  
- Embeds GSA into CNNs to learn more meaningful representations from dictionaries.  
- Claims improved question classification by utilizing answer information and encoding group-sparse constraints.  
- Decision: Accept — novel and well-motivated approach to question classification.
