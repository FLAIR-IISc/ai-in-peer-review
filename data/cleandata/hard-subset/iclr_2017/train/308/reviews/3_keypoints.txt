- Paper studies why GAN training is difficult, analyzes effects of asymmetric divergences, identifies instability sources, and proposes a smoothing-based alternative.
- Strong theoretical contribution asking good questions and providing insightful answers.
- Interesting use of analysis and differential topology concepts.
- Proposes practical avenues to reduce instability in GAN training.
