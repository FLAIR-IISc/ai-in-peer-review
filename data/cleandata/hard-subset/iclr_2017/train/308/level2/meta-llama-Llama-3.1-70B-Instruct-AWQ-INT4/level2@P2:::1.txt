**Summary of the Paper**

The paper aims to provide a theoretical understanding of the training dynamics of Generative Adversarial Networks (GANs). The authors identify the sources of instability in GAN training, such as the perfect discriminator and the vanishing gradients, and propose a new direction to address these issues. They introduce a new tool to analyze the problems and provide bounds on the Wasserstein metric, which can be used to evaluate generative models.
