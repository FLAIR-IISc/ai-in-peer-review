- Paper claims a theoretical understanding of GAN training dynamics, explaining instability and mode dropping.
- Introduces a new analytical framework including a novel metric for measuring distribution distance.
- Proposes a new GAN training algorithm based on the introduced metric, claimed to be more stable and effective.
- Recommendation: accept the paper.
- Clear and concise problem statement and proposed approach.
- Theoretical framework is well-motivated and offers a new perspective.
- Analysis is thorough, rigorous, and results are clearly explained.
- The proposed algorithm shows promise at addressing instability and mode dropping.
- Needs more experimental results to validate algorithm effectiveness.
- Lacks practical implementation details for the new algorithm.
- Assumes substantial GAN background; could use more context for non-expert readers.
- Requests for authors: provide additional experiments demonstrating effectiveness.
- Requests clarification on how the new algorithm explicitly addresses mode dropping.
- Requests practical implementation details for reproducing the algorithm.
- Requests empirical comparison against existing GAN training methods.
