- Clarifies GAN training dynamics, identifying gradient vanishing in standard GANs and gradient instability in variants, and proposes a perturbation-based mitigation.
- Perturbation technique is intriguing and analogous to dropout—can be interpreted as Bernoulli sampling; authors should elaborate this connection.
- Theoretical analysis is compelling and insightful.
- Lacks empirical validation; recommend comparative experiments (e.g., like Figures 2–3) showing perturbed GAN performance versus baselines.
