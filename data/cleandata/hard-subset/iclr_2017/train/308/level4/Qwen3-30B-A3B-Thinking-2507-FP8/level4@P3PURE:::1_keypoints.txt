- Strong submission addressing two critical theoretical challenges in GANs (convergence and stability).
- Provides the first rigorous theoretical analysis of GAN convergence and stability.
- Significant contribution given early GANs' practical instability.
- Stands out from empirical work by offering deep theoretical insights into GAN architecture.

- Minor concerns about the proof of Theorem 2.5, but these do not undermine the contributions.
- Overall an excellent theoretical work that merits acceptance.
