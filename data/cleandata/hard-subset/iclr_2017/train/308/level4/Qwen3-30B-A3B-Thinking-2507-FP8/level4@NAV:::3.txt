

REVIEW SUMMARY  
This paper tackles critical challenges in training generative adversarial networks (GANs), examining the implications of asymmetric divergence functions and root causes of training instability. It subsequently introduces a smoothing-based alternative approach.  

PROS  
Strong theoretical foundation with well-posed questions and insightful resolutions.  
Elegantly applies concepts from analysis and differential topology.  
Proposes actionable pathways to mitigate GAN training instability.  

CONS  
Slightly verbose and highly technical; certain arguments and consequences require deeper elaboration (suitable for future work).
