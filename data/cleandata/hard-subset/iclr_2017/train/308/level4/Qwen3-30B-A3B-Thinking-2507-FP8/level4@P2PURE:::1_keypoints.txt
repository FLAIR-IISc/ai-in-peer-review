- Strong submission addressing two critical aspects of GANs (convergence and stability).
- Provides the first rigorous theoretical analysis of GAN convergence and stability.
- Offers deep insights into GAN architecture not present in most prior work.
- Stability analysis is especially significant given early GANs' instability and poor empirical performance.
- Theorems 2.4–2.6 are novel and introduce interesting mathematical techniques.
- Minor concern about the proof of Theorem 2.5, but it does not undermine the paper’s contributions.
- Theoretical depth and relevance make this work a valuable addition to the literature.
- Recommendation: accept.
