- Paper is high quality and merits acceptance; reviewer reaffirms initial positive assessment.
- Provides a robust examination of generative adversarial networks (GANs), an important new method.
- Offers theoretical analysis of GAN convergence and addresses stability issues, both vital topics.
- One of the pioneering theoretical works on GANs, providing deep theoretical understanding that sets it apart.
- Discussion of GAN stability is particularly significant given early GAN architectures' instability and practical limitations.
- Theorems 2.4–2.6 introduce novel concepts and use interesting mathematical techniques.
- Reviewer has minor technical questions about the proof of Theorem 2.5, but these do not detract from the paper’s merit.
