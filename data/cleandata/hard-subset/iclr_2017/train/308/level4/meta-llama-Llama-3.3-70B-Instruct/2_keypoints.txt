- Significant advancement in understanding GAN training dynamics and causes of vanishing/unstable gradients.
- Authors propose a novel perturbation technique to mitigate gradient instability in GANs.
- Perturbation method is intriguing and resembles dropout; could be modeled as Bernoulli-derived perturbations.
- Reviewer recommends exploring the theoretical/algorithmic connection to dropout more explicitly.
- Reviewer requests empirical validation of the perturbation technique to substantiate claims.
- Suggests adding experiments analogous to Figures 2 and 3 to compare performance of the perturbed GAN.
