- Paper significantly clarifies the training procedure of GANs.
- Provides fresh insights into GAN training dynamics and explains gradient vanishing in original GAN and instability in variants.
- Proposes a perturbation method to mitigate gradient vanishing and instability.
- Work has potential to inspire more principled research in GAN training.
- Reviewer notes the perturbation is conceptually related to dropout (Bernoulli interpretation) and requests the authors elaborate on this connection.
- Reviewer asks for empirical validation: include experiments like Figures 2 and 3 for the perturbed GAN to strengthen the paper.
