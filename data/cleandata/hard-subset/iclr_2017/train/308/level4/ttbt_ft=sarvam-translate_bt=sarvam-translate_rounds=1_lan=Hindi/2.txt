This research paper makes valuable contributions to providing a clearer understanding of the Generative Adversarial Network (GAN) training process.

With new insights into the dynamics of GAN and its training methods, the authors explain why gradients are either disappearing or unstable in the original GAN. More importantly, they also provide a method to avoid such difficulties by introducing perturbation. I believe this paper will inspire more theoretical research in this direction.

I am very interested in the perturbation technique to avoid gradient instability and dropout. In fact, it is quite related to the dropout technique, where the perturbation can be viewed as a Bernoulli distribution. It would be great if this relationship could be discussed.
