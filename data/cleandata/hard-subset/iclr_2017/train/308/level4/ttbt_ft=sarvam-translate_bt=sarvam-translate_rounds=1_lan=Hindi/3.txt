Summary:

This paper addresses critical questions about the difficulties in training Generative Adversarial Networks. It discusses the consequences of using an asymmetric divergence function and the sources of instability in training GANs. It then proposes an alternative using a smoother approach.

Pros:

*   Theory, good questions, good answers.
*   Interesting use of concepts from form analysis and differential topology.
*   Proposes ways to avoid instability in GANs.

Cons:

*   A bit long, technical. Some parts and results still need further development (which is perfectly fine for future work).
