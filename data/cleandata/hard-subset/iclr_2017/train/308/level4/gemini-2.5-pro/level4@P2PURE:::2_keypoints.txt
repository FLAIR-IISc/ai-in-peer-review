- Valuable contribution: provides clearer understanding of GAN training dynamics and potential to inspire more principled research.
- Explains why original GAN formulation is prone to vanishing gradients and why GAN variants can suffer from gradient instability.
- Proposes a perturbation method to overcome vanishing gradients and gradient instability.
- Perturbation method is reminiscent of dropout and can be viewed as noise from a Bernoulli distribution; paper should discuss this connection.
- Theoretical analysis is valuable but would be more compelling with empirical validation.
- Suggests adding experiments for the perturbed GAN analogous to Figures 2 and 3 for direct comparison.
