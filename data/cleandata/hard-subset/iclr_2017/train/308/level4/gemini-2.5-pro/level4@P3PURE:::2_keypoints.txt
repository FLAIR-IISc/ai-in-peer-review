- Paper provides a valuable, clearer analysis of GAN training dynamics, explaining why original GANs suffer vanishing gradients and variants can have unstable gradients, and proposes a perturbation method to mitigate these issues.  
- The proposed perturbation technique is interesting and appears related to dropout (sampling from a Bernoulli distribution); the authors should discuss this connection.  
- The paper lacks empirical validation for the perturbed GAN; add experiments comparable to Figures 2 and 3 for direct comparison.
