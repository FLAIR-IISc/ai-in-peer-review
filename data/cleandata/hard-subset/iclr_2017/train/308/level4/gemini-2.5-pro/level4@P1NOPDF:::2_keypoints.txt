- Paper clarifies GAN training dynamics and explains why generator gradients vanish or become unstable in many formulations.
- Authors propose introducing a perturbation to generator samples to avoid gradient vanishing/instability.
- Reviewer finds the perturbation technique promising and notes a conceptual similarity to dropout (noise as Bernoulli); recommends discussing this connection.
- Requests more thorough empirical validation, specifically experiments for the perturbed GAN analogous to Figures 2 and 3 for direct comparison.
- Reviewer believes the work will inspire more principled research in GAN training.
