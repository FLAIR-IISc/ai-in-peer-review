- Strong, timely theoretical analysis of GANs focusing on fundamentals.  
- Rigorous investigation of convergence criteria and training stability.  
- Addresses two critical challenges highly relevant to the research community.  
- One of the first deep theoretical treatments in a field dominated by empirical work.  
- Emphasis on stability is important given early GANs’ unstable training dynamics.  
- Core contributions are the novel and significant Theorems 2.4–2.6.  
- Mathematical techniques are elegant and may interest optimization and game-theory researchers.  
- Minor technical question about the proof of Theorem 2.5 that does not undermine the work.  
- Overall excellent, valuable, and potentially influential paper with a strong recommendation for acceptance.
