This is a very good paper, and I hope it will be accepted. I am keeping my original assessment.

This is a powerful presentation related to one of the most important and recently introduced methods of neural networks – the Generative Adversarial Network (GAN). The authors have theoretically analyzed the convergence of GANs and discussed the stability of GANs. Both of these topics are extremely important. To my knowledge, this is one of the first theoretical papers on GANs, and unlike most presentations in this field, this paper actually provides deep theoretical insights into this architecture. The issues of GAN stability are very important because the first proposed versions of the GAN architecture were very unstable and did not work well in practice. The theorems 2.4-2.6 are new and introduce interesting mathematical techniques. I have some technical questions about the proof of theorem 2.5, but these are very minor.
