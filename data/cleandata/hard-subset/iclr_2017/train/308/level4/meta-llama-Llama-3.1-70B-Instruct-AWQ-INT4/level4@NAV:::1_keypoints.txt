- High-quality paper that warrants acceptance; reviewer reaffirms initial positive assessment.

- Provides theoretical analysis of GAN convergence and discusses GAN stability.
- One of the pioneering theoretical papers on GANs, offering deeper insights than many other submissions.
- Emphasizes the importance of stability due to early GAN instability and poor practical performance.
- Theorems 2.4–2.6 introduce novel concepts and employ interesting mathematical techniques.
- Minor technical questions about the proof of Theorem 2.5, but these do not detract from the paper’s overall quality.
