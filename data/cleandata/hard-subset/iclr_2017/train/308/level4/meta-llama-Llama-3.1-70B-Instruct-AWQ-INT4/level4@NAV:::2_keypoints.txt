- Significant contribution: paper clarifies GAN training dynamics and provides novel theoretical insights.  
- Identifies root causes of vanishing gradients in the original GAN and unstable gradients in a variant.  
- Proposes a perturbation-based approach to mitigate vanishing/unstable gradient issues.  
- Perturbation technique resembles dropout and can be interpreted as a Bernoulli perturbation; needs deeper discussion.  
- Theoretical analysis should be complemented with empirical validation.  
- Recommend adding experiments for the perturbed GAN analogous to Figures 2 and 3 to substantiate claims.  
- Work is likely to stimulate further research on GAN training.
