- Strong submission providing valuable insights into generative adversarial networks (GANs).  
- Theoretical analysis of convergence and stability is particularly noteworthy and practically important.  
- One of the first theoretical papers to deeply analyze GANs, offering rare depth and understanding.  
- Discussion on stability issues addresses a critical practical problem in early GANs.  
- Theorems 2.4–2.6 are novel and introduce interesting mathematical techniques.  
- Minor technical questions remain regarding the proof of Theorem 2.5.  
- Overall, the paper makes a substantial contribution and demonstrates high technical sophistication; reviewer hopes for acceptance.
