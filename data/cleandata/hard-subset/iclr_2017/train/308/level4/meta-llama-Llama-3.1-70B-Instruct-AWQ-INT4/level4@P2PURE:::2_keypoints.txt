- Paper provides a clearer understanding of GAN training dynamics.  
- Offers new insights into causes of vanishing gradients in the original GAN and unstable gradients in GAN variants.  
- Proposes perturbations as a solution to mitigate gradient vanishing and instability.  
- Reviewer believes the work will inspire more principled research in this direction.  
- Reviewer is particularly interested in the perturbation trick and notes its resemblance to dropout (perturbation as a Bernoulli distribution); requests a deeper discussion of this connection.  
- Reviewer requests empirical evidence: add experiments analogous to Figures 2 and 3 for the perturbed GAN to demonstrate and compare its effectiveness.
