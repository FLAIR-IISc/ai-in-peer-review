- Paper provides a clearer understanding of GAN training dynamics.
- New insights explain vanishing gradients in original GANs and unstable gradients in variants.
- Authors propose perturbations to avoid gradient vanishment and instability.
- Contribution likely to inspire more principled research in GAN training.
- Reviewer is particularly interested in the perturbation trick and its resemblance to dropout/Bernoulli perturbations; suggests discussing this connection.
- Reviewer requests additional empirical studies (e.g., more experiments like Figures 2 and 3) comparing the perturbed GAN to the original GAN and variants.
