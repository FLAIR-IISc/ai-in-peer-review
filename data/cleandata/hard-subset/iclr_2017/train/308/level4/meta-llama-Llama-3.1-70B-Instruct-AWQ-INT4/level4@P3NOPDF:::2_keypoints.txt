- Paper provides clearer understanding of GAN training dynamics and explains causes of vanishing gradients in original GAN and instability in variants.
- Introduces a perturbation trick to mitigate gradient vanishing/instability, presented as a significant contribution.
- Reviewer is especially interested in the perturbation method and notes its resemblance to dropout (Bernoulli perturbation); recommends discussing this connection.
- Requests empirical validation: add experiments comparing perturbed GAN to unperturbed GAN (e.g., similar to Figures 2 and 3) to justify effectiveness.
- Overall positive assessment: paper is promising and could inspire principled research, but needs additional empirical evidence to increase impact.
