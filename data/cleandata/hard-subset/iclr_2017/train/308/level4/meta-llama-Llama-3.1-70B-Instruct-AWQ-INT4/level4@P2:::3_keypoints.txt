- Paper studies difficulties in training GANs, effects of using an asymmetric divergence, sources of instability, and proposes a smoothing-based alternative.
- Theoretical foundation for understanding GAN training challenges is strong and insightful.
- Use of analysis and differential topology brings a fresh, notable perspective.
- Proposed smoothing approach to avoid GAN instability is a significant contribution.
- Paper is very technical and lengthy, which may hinder accessibility.
- Section 2.1 is overly long; proofs could be moved to the appendix.
- Some ideas and consequences are preliminary and need further development.
- Reviewer leans toward acceptance but requests revisions to address concerns.

- Main weakness: presentation length/technicality and incomplete development of some results.
