- Strong submission offering valuable insights into generative adversarial networks (GANs).  
- Provides theoretical analysis of GAN convergence and discussion of stability as crucial contributions.  
- One of the first theoretical papers on GANs, distinguishing it from typical empirical submissions.  
- Addresses highly important stability issues given earlier GAN instability and poor practical performance.  
- Theorems 2.4–2.6 are novel and introduce interesting mathematical techniques.  
- Minor technical questions exist about the proof of Theorem 2.5, but they do not undermine the work.  
- Overall a valuable contribution and recommended for acceptance.
