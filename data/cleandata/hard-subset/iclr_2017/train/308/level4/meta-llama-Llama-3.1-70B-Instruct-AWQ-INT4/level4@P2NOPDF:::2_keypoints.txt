- Paper provides valuable insights into GAN training dynamics and variants.  
- Authors identify causes of vanishing gradients in original GANs and unstable gradients in variants.  
- Proposed solution: introduce perturbations to overcome gradient vanishing/instability.  
- Work is likely to inspire more principled follow-up research.  
- Perturbation trick is interesting and resembles dropout/Bernoulli; a discussion of this connection is recommended.  
- Empirical validation is needed; suggest adding experiments (like Figures 2 and 3) for the perturbed GAN to enable comparison.
