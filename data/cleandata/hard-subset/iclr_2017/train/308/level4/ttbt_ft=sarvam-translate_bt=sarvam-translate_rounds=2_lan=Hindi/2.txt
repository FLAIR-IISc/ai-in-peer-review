This research paper makes a valuable contribution to providing a clear understanding of the Generative Adversarial Network (GAN) training process.

With new insights into the dynamics of GANs and training methods, the authors explain why gradients are either disappearing or becoming unstable in basic GANs. More importantly, they also provide a way to avoid such difficulties by introducing noise. I believe this paper will inspire more theoretical research in this direction.
