This is a very good paper, and I hope it will be accepted. I am keeping my original assessment.

This is a strong presentation about one of the most important and recently introduced methods in neural networks – the Generative Adversarial Network. The authors theoretically analyze the convergence of GANs and discuss the stability of GANs. Both are very important. To my knowledge, this is one of the first theoretical papers about GANs, and this paper, unlike most presentations in this field, actually provides deep theoretical insights into this architecture. Stability issues are extremely important in relation to GANs because the first proposed versions of the GAN architecture were very unstable and did not work well in practice. The theorems 2.4-2.6 are novel and present mathematical techniques that are interesting. I have some technical questions about the proof of theorem 2.5, but these are very minor.
