Summary
This paper discusses important questions related to the difficulty of training generative adversarial networks. It explores the consequences of using an asymmetric divergence function and the sources of instability in GAN training. It then proposes an alternative using a smoother approach.

Pros
Theory, good questions, nice answers.
Interesting use of form analysis and differential topology concepts.
Suggested ways to avoid instability in GANs.

Cons
A bit too long, technical. Some parts and consequences still need further development (which is perfectly fine for future work).
