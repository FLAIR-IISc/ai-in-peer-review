- Significant contribution to understanding GAN training dynamics.  
- Novel insights explaining the origins of vanishing gradients in original GANs and instability in modified variants.  
- Proposes a perturbation-based method to mitigate gradient vanishing and instability.  
- Expected to stimulate further, more theoretically grounded research.  
- Perturbation technique resembles dropout/Bernoulli; a discussion of this connection is recommended.  
- Theoretical analysis is compelling but lacks supporting empirical evidence.  
- Recommend additional experiments (similar to Figures 2 and 3) to demonstrate the perturbated GAN’s efficacy and provide comparative analysis.
