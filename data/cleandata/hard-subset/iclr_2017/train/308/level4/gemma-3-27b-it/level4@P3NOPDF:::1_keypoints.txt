- Strong submission addressing theoretical convergence and stability of GANs, an important and timely problem.  
- One of the first rigorous theoretical treatments of GAN dynamics, moving beyond predominantly empirical work.  
- Directly tackles GAN instability issues that affect practical performance and hyperparameter sensitivity.  
- Core contributions are Theorems 2.4–2.6, which present novel results and interesting mathematical techniques.  
- Approach is insightful and potentially broadly applicable to GAN analysis.  
- Minor technical questions remain regarding the proof of Theorem 2.5 that require clarification.  
- Limited empirical validation; simulations or empirical ties would strengthen the theoretical findings.  
- Generally well-written with clear mathematical notation.  
- Overall: significant contribution with high impact potential; strong recommendation for acceptance.
