- Strong submission addressing a critical, recently introduced topic in neural networks (GANs).  
- Provides theoretical analysis of GAN convergence and stability, which are highly important research areas.  
- Among the first papers to offer a rigorous theoretical treatment of GANs.  
- Stands out by providing genuinely deep theoretical insight into the architecture.  
- Highlights the practical significance of stability issues given difficulties with early GAN implementations.  
- Theorems 2.4–2.6 are novel and use interesting mathematical techniques.  
- Reviewer has a few minor technical questions about the proof of Theorem 2.5.  
- Reviewer maintains a positive evaluation and hopes the paper will be accepted.
