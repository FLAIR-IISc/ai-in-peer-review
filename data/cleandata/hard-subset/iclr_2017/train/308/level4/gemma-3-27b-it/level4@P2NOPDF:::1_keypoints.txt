- Strong paper addressing the critical and rapidly developing area of GANs.  
- Provides valuable theoretical analysis of GAN convergence and stability, addressing practical training challenges.  
- One of the first rigorous theoretical treatments of GANs, offering rare genuine architectural insight.  
- Research is important given historical instability in early GAN implementations.  
- Theorems 2.4–2.6 are particularly noteworthy, introducing interesting mathematical techniques for analyzing GAN behavior.  
- Minor technical questions about the proof of Theorem 2.5, but they do not significantly detract from the work.  
- Overall positive evaluation and recommendation for acceptance.
