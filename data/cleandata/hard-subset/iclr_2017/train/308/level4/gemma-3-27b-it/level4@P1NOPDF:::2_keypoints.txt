- Paper provides a valuable contribution to understanding GAN training dynamics.
- Offers new insights into why the original GAN suffers vanishing gradients and why variants show instability.
- Proposes a perturbation-based method to mitigate gradient vanishing and instability.
- Reviewer believes the work will inspire more principled follow-up research.
- Reviewer notes the perturbation trick resembles dropout/Bernoulli sampling and asks for a more detailed discussion of this connection.
- Reviewer requests empirical evidence—experiments (e.g., like Figures 2 and 3) demonstrating the perturbed GAN’s performance to strengthen the paper.
