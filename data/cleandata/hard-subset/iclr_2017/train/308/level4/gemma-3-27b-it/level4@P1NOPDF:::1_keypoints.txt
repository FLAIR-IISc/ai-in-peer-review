- Overall recommendation: very strong paper, recommends acceptance and hopes evaluation is maintained.  
- Addresses crucial, recent method (GANs) with a strong theoretical focus on convergence and stability.  
- One of the first papers to provide genuine theoretical insight into GANs rather than only empirical results.  
- Theorems 2.4–2.6 are novel and the mathematical techniques are interesting.  
- Minor technical questions remain about the proof of Theorem 2.5.  
- Major strength: rigorous theoretical foundations that clarify conditions for GAN training success and potential pitfalls.  
- Suggestion: paper needs a more detailed discussion of practical implications (linking theorem conditions to hyperparameters, architectures, datasets, and stabilization strategies).  
- Suggestion: compare and position the theoretical framework against other recent GAN analyses to clarify contributions and limitations.  
- Overall impact: significant advance with mathematical rigor and substantial potential impact.
