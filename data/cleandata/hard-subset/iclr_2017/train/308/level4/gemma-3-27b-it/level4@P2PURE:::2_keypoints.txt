- Paper provides valuable insights into GAN training dynamics and variants, explaining causes of vanishing gradients and instability.  
- Introduces a perturbation method to mitigate gradient vanishing and instability.  
- Reviewer believes the work will stimulate further principled research in GAN training.  
- Reviewer requests discussion of the perturbation’s resemblance to dropout/Bernoulli sampling and its connection.  
- Reviewer asks for empirical evidence (e.g., experiments like Figures 2 and 3) showing the effectiveness of the perturbed GAN.
