Review- SUMMARY
This paper investigates key challenges in training generative adversarial networks. It examines the implications of employing an asymmetric divergence and identifies drivers of training instability. It then suggests an alternative based on a smoothing strategy.

PROS
- Solid theoretical grounding, well-posed questions, and convincing answers.
- Engaging use of tools from analysis and differential topology.
- Offers practical directions to mitigate GAN instability.

CONS
- Somewhat lengthy and technical; certain sections and implications would benefit from further development (appropriate for future work).
