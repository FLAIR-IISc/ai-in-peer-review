- Paper provides clearer understanding of GAN training dynamics and why gradients vanish or become unstable in variants.
- Authors reveal causes of gradient vanishing in original GANs and instability in certain GAN variants.
- Key contribution: proposing perturbations to the training process to avoid gradient vanishing and instability.
- Reviewer finds the perturbation trick promising and likely to inspire more principled research.
- Reviewer suggests explicitly discussing the connection between the perturbation trick and dropout/Bernoulli perturbations.
- Reviewer requests empirical validation: add experiments analogous to Figures 2 and 3 for the perturbed GAN for direct comparison.
