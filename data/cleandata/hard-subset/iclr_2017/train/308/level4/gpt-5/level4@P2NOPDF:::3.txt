SUMMARY
This paper tackles key challenges in training generative adversarial networks (GANs). It analyzes the consequences of using asymmetric divergences and identifies sources of training instability. The authors then propose a smoothing-based alternative to improve stability.

PROS
- Strong theoretical framing with well-posed questions and convincing answers.
- Interesting and appropriate use of tools from analysis and differential topology.
- Offers practical directions to mitigate instability in GAN training.

CONS
- Somewhat long and highly technical. Certain sections and implications would benefit from further development (which is reasonable to leave for future work).
