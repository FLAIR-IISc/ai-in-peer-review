- Paper provides a clearer understanding of GAN training procedures and dynamics.
- Analyzes why gradients vanish in the original GAN and become unstable in a variant.
- Proposes introducing perturbations to avoid gradient vanishing and instability.
- Reviewer views the work as likely to inspire more principled research in this area.
- Reviewer is particularly interested in the perturbation technique and its relation to dropout (perturbation as a Bernoulli distribution).
- Requests the paper explicitly discuss the connection to dropout.
- Asks for empirical evaluation supporting the perturbation approach.
- Requests experiments analogous to Figs. 2 and 3 for the perturbed GAN to enable direct comparison.
