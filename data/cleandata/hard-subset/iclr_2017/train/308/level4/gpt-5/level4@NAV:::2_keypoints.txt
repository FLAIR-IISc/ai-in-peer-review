- Paper makes a meaningful contribution by clarifying the training procedure of GANs.  
- Provides new insights into why gradients vanish in the original GAN and become unstable in some variants.  
- Proposes a perturbation-based method to mitigate gradient vanishing and instability.  
- Expectation that this work will motivate more principled research on GAN training dynamics.  
- Reviewer requests discussion of the connection between the perturbation technique and dropout/Bernoulli perturbations.  
- Reviewer asks for empirical evidence: include experiments analogous to Figures 2 and 3 for the perturbed GAN for comparison.
