**Summary**

This paper introduces the Gated Graph Transformer Neural Network (GGT-NN) model, which extends the Gated Graph Sequence Neural Network (GGS-NN) by allowing graph-structured intermediate representations and outputs. The GGT-NN model can learn to construct and modify graphs based on textual input and can be used for various tasks, such as question-answering and rule discovery. The model is evaluated on the bAbI tasks and two additional tasks: a cellular automaton and a Turing machine. The results show that the GGT-NN model can successfully learn to solve these tasks and can generalize to larger inputs.
