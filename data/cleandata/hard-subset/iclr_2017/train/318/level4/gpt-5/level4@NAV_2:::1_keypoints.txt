- Central contribution: a suite of differentiable graph transformations that enable learning graph-to-graph mappings/classification via gradient descent.  
- Framework naturally fits tasks like cellular automata evolution represented as graph sequences (graph growth, neighbor links, dedicated 0/1 nodes for cell values).  
- Proposed architecture can learn such graph sequences, but the Rule 30 experiment indicates the task is far from fully solved.  
- Method is combined with prior GGS-NN ideas to produce textual outputs in addition to graphs, using graphs as intermediate representations.  
- The combined model surpasses state-of-the-art results on the bAbI tasks.
