- Introduces a family of differentiable graph transformations enabling end-to-end graph-to-graph classification via gradient descent.  
- Maps the framework to learning a cellular automaton as a sequence of evolving graphs (graph growth, neighbor edges, special nodes for binary 0/1).  
- Claims the architecture can in principle learn the evolving graph sequence, but the Rule 30 experiment remained far from solved.  
- Combines the method with Gated Graph Sequence Neural Networks to produce textual outputs using graphs as intermediate representations.  
- This extension outperforms prior state of the art on the bAbI tasks.
