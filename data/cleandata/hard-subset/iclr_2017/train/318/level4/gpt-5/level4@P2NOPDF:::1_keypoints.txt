- Introduces differentiable graph transformations enabling end-to-end graph-to-graph prediction via gradient descent.
- Framework models cellular automata as evolving graph sequences: node set grows, edges connect neighboring cells, special nodes encode binary values.
- Proposed architecture is aimed at learning such evolving graph sequences, but experiments on Rule 30 remain far from solved.
- Combines this approach with prior GGS-NN work to produce textual outputs, treating graphs as intermediate representations.
- Achieves state-of-the-art results on the bAbI tasks in the graph-to-text setting.
