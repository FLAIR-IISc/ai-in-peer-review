- Introduces differentiable graph transformations for learning graph-to-graph mappings via gradient descent.
- Applies the framework to learning a cellular automaton modeled as a sequence of expanding graphs (nodes = cell values 0/1, edges = neighbors).
- Proposed architecture is designed to learn graph sequences but experiments show it fails to solve Rule 30.
- Combines the method with ideas from GGS-NNs to produce textual output instead of graphs.
- Uses graphs as intermediate representations in a hybrid model.
- The hybrid model achieves state-of-the-art performance on the bAbI tasks.
