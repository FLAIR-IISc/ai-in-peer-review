- Introduces differentiable graph transformations to learn graph-to-graph mappings via gradient descent.
- Applies this framework to cellular automata by representing evolution as a sequence of expanding graphs.
- Graph representation: nodes = cells, edges = neighbor links, cell states = pointers to special 0/1 nodes.
- Proposed architecture is intended to learn the graph sequence but fails to fully solve the Rule 30 task in experiments.
- Combines the core idea with Gated Graph Sequence Neural Networks to use graphs as intermediate representations for text generation.
- The resulting hybrid architecture achieves state-of-the-art performance on the bAbI question-answering tasks.
