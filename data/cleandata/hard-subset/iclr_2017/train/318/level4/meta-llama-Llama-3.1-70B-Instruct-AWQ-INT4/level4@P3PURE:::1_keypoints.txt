- Introduces differential graph transformations that allow learning graph-to-graph classification with gradient descent.  
- Frames the task as learning a cellular automaton represented by a sequence of expanding graphs.  
- Graph construction includes nodes pointing to neighbors and special nodes encoding binary values (0/1).  
- Proposed architecture can learn graph sequences, but the Rule 30 experiment was far from fully solved.  
- Combines ideas from GGS-NN to produce textual output from graphs as intermediate representations, achieving state-of-the-art on bAbI tasks.
