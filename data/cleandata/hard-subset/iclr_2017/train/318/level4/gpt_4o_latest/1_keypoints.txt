- Introduces differential graph transformations to enable end-to-end learning of graph-to-graph mappings via gradient descent.  
- Framework naturally fits learning cellular automata as sequences of graphs.  
- In the CA setting, node sets expand each iteration with edges linking neighbors and special 0/1 nodes encoding values.  
- Proposed architecture can represent and learn these graph sequences.  
- Empirically, the method does not fully solve the Rule 30 task — performance remains far from complete.  
- Integrates ideas from GGS-NN to produce textual outputs while using graphs as intermediate representations.  
- Achieves state-of-the-art results on the bAbI tasks.
