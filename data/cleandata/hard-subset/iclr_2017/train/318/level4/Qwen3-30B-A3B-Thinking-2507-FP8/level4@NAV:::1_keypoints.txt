- Introduces differential graph transformations enabling gradient-based learning for graph-to-graph classification.  
- Models cellular automata as sequences of evolving graphs with expanding node sets, adjacency relations, and binary (0/1) node states.  
- Proposed architecture successfully learns such graph sequences, but the Rule 30 task remains challenging in experiments.  
- Extends GGS-NN by using graphs as intermediate representations to generate textual outputs.
