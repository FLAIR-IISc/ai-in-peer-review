**Summary of the paper's claims and contributions**

This paper introduces the Gated Graph Transformer Neural Network (GGT-NN) model, which is an extension of the Gated Graph Sequence Neural Network (GGS-NN) model. The GGT-NN model allows graph-structured data to be used as an intermediate representation, enabling the model to learn to construct and modify graphs in sophisticated ways based on textual input. The model can produce a variety of outputs, including graph-structured outputs. The paper demonstrates the effectiveness of the GGT-NN model on several tasks, including the bAbI tasks, a simple cellular automaton, and a family of Turing machines.
