**Summary of the Paper**

The paper proposes a novel neural network architecture called the Gated Graph Transformer Neural Network (GGT-NN) that can learn to construct and modify graphs based on textual input. The GGT-NN model extends the Gated Graph Sequence Neural Network (GGS-NN) by allowing graph-structured intermediate representations and outputs. The model uses a set of differentiable graph transformations to process the input and produce a graph-structured output. The authors demonstrate the effectiveness of the GGT-NN model on several tasks, including the bAbI tasks, a simple cellular automaton, and a family of Turing machines.
