- Introduces differential graph transformations to enable learning graph→graph tasks with gradient descent.  
- Frames this as learning a cellular automaton represented by a sequence of growing graphs with neighbor pointers and special 0/1 value nodes.  
- Proposed architecture can learn to generate the graph sequence, but the Rule 30 experiment remained far from solved.  
- Combines ideas from prior GGS-NN work to produce textual output via graphs as intermediate representations.  
- Using graphs as intermediates, the model outperforms state-of-the-art on the bAbI tasks.
