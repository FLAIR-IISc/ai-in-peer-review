- Introduces a nonparametric neural network that dynamically grows/shrinks by adding zero-valued units and using a sparse regularizer to prune weights; approach is a continuous relaxation of discrete model selection and shows promising results.  
- Computational-complexity concern: decomposing fan-in weights into parallel/orthogonal components and converting to radial-angular coordinates likely adds substantial overhead; authors should quantify extra operations and provide empirical runtime comparisons to baselines.  
- Performance concern on convex datasets: the model produces smaller architectures but underperforms parametric networks; authors should analyze whether this is due to structural limitations, the regularization scheme, or dataset-specific factors.
