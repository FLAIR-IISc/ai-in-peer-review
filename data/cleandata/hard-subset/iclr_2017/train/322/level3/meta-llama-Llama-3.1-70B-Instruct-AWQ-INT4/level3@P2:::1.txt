**Summary of the paper's contributions**

This paper introduces a nonparametric neural network framework that automatically adapts and chooses the size of a neural network during a single training run. The authors propose a novel approach to dynamically add and remove units from the network, allowing it to learn the optimal model size for a given task. The framework is based on a sparse regularizer that eliminates irrelevant weights, and the authors provide theoretical guarantees for the soundness of their approach. The paper presents promising empirical results on several benchmark datasets, demonstrating the effectiveness of their method.
