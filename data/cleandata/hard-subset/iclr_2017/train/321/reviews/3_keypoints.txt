- Positive: Reviewer likes the paper's setting but has several criticisms and questions.  
- Failure modes: Concerns that the approach may fail as behavior richness increases, limiting the diversity of skills discovered.  
- Discretization: Sec. 5.3 implies a grid—need clarification whether state/space is discretized, why, and what happens if it is continuous.  
- Generality/embodiment: Question whether the method scales to more complex morphologies (e.g., 5-link swimmer vs 2-link) to assess generality.  
- Hand-design of rewards: Disagrees with authors’ claim of minimal domain knowledge—intrinsic rewards appear hand-crafted and tailored to specific control tasks.
