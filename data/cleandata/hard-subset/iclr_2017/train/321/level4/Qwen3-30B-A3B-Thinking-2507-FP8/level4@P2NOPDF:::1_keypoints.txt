- Strong empirical contribution to hierarchical control with robust results on benchmarks previous work failed.  
- Term "intrinsic motivation" is misleading here; pre-training on task-relevant rewards is task-specific and should be rephrased to avoid confusion.  
- Mutual Information regularization helps in simple settings but fails in complex mazes; the paper needs deeper analysis of this discrepancy.  
- The factorization S_agent vs S_rest is under-specified; S_rest must be clearly defined (e.g., function of environment state or action history) for replicability.  
- Analysis of agent switching behavior is insufficient—need correlation with task phases, failure modes during transitions, and timing effects on performance.  
- Overall: promising empirical results but the paper’s impact would improve by clarifying terminology, explaining the MI anomaly, defining S_rest, and expanding behavioral analysis.
