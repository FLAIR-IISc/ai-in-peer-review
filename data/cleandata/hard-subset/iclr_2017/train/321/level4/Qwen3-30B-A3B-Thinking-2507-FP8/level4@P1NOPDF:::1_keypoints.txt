- Overall: Interesting hierarchical-control method with strong empirical results, but the experimental analysis is limited.  
- Terminology: Calling the pretraining “intrinsic” motivation is misleading—pretraining for locomotion/grasping is task-specific and overlaps with Heess et al.’s approaches.  
- Mutual Information regularizer: Effective in simple settings but fails on complex mazes; authors should analyze why it doesn’t generalize.  
- Factorization clarity: The formulation of the residual policy (S_rest) is insufficiently described—exact details are needed for reproducibility.  
- Switching dynamics: A deeper study of subpolicy switching (triggers, timing, failure modes, and impact on performance) is required.
