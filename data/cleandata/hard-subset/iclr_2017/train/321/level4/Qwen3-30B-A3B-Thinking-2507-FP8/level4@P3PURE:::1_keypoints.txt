- Strong empirical contribution: completes benchmarks prior methods failed, but overall analysis is insufficient.

- Terminology issue: calling the pre-training “intrinsic motivation” is misleading given its task-oriented, reward-driven nature and similarity to Heess et al.

- Mutual-information regularizer: works in simple settings but provides little benefit in complex mazes (1–3), with no analysis offered for this failure.

- State-factorization underspecified: S_agent is referenced but S_rest is not clearly defined (needed for replicability; check if omitted or only in supplement).

- Policy/switching analysis missing: the paper lacks exploration of switching behavior, failure modes, and how switching timing affects performance.
