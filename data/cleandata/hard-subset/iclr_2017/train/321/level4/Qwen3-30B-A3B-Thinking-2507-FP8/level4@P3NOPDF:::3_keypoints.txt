- No systematic failure analysis as behavioral complexity increases; need metrics (skill coverage, success rates) to show when/why the method fails or plateaus.
- Section 5.3’s discretization is unclear: clarify whether and how the grid/state is discretized, why discretization is needed, and implications for continuous-state settings.
- Scalability to higher-degree-of-freedom embodiments is untested; evaluate on more complex systems (e.g., 5-link swimmer or humanoid) to assess exploration and dimensionality issues.

- Empirical results are promising but insufficient to support broad generalization claims; substantial revisions and additional experiments are required before acceptance.
