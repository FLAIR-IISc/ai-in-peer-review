- Summary: Interesting hierarchical-control contribution with strong empirical results reaching new benchmarks, but the experimental analysis is insufficient.

- Terminology: Calling the pre-training "intrinsic motivation" is inappropriate—speed/grasp rewards are task-specific and align with downstream objectives (as in Heess et al.), not true intrinsic motivation.

- Mutual information: MI regularization works in simple settings but fails on complex mazes (1–3); the paper does not offer interpretation or analysis for this failure.

- Factorization details: The factorization between S_agent and S_rest is unclear; S_rest must be explicitly defined (Duan et al. only detail S_agent) for replicability.

- Policy analysis: The paper lacks analysis of switching behavior and other policy diagnostics (failure modes, temporal dynamics of switching), which would strengthen the work.
