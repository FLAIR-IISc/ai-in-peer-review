- Unclear failure modes: how does the method cope as behavioral richness/diversity of discoverable skills increases?

- State-space discretization: is the state space discretized in Sec. 5.3, why was that choice made, and what happens if it is not discretized?

- Scalability to complex embodiments: can the method handle more complex bodies (e.g., a 5-link swimmer vs. 2-link), and does it generalize to different morphologies?

- Claim about intrinsic rewards: the paper’s intrinsic rewards appear hand-crafted and possibly tailored, challenging the claim of minimal domain knowledge compared to Heess et al. (2016).

- Request for stronger evidence: clarifications and empirical results addressing the above concerns are needed to strengthen the paper.
