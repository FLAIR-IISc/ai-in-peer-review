- Term “intrinsic motivation” is misapplied: pretraining rewards (speed, grasping) align with downstream tasks and differ from established intrinsic motivation usage; similar to Heess et al.
- Mutual Information regularization is elegant and helps generally but fails in complex mazes 1–3; authors should interpret and analyze why it fails there.
- Factorization between S_agent and S_rest needs clear, precise definition (S_rest unspecified), as this affects replicability.
- Require analysis of agent switching behavior and broader policy analysis (failure modes, effect of switching time on performance).
