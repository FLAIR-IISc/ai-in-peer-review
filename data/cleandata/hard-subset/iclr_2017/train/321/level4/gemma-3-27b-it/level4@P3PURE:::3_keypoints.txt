- Concern about failure modes: as achievable behavior richness grows, the method may struggle to discover a diverse set of skills.  
- Clarification needed on state representation: is the environment discretized in Sec. 5.3, why was discretization chosen, and what would change with a continuous state space?  
- Scalability question: unclear if the approach generalizes to more complex embodiments (e.g., 5-link swimmer vs. 2-link), which is needed to assess method generality.  
- Disagreement with authors' claim of minimal domain knowledge: the proposed intrinsic rewards appear hand-crafted and task-specific, not clearly less domain-dependent than prior work.
