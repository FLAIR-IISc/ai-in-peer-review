- Overall: Setting is compelling but the reviewer raises several criticisms and questions.
- Limitation concern: Approach may struggle to discover diverse skills as behavior complexity and richness increase.
- Section 5.3 question: Is the state space discretized (grid variable)? Rationale for discretization and consequences if omitted are unclear.
- Scalability concern: Unclear how the method generalizes to more complex embodiments (e.g., 5-link swimmer vs. 2-link); needs evaluation for generality.
- Intrinsic reward critique: The paper’s intrinsic rewards are hand-crafted and appear specific to limited control tasks, contradicting the claim of minimal domain knowledge.
