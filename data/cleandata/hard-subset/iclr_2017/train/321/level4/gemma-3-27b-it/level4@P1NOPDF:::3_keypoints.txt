- Reviewer appreciates the paper's setting but raises several criticisms and questions.

- As behavior richness increases, the approach may fail or struggle to discover and manage diverse skills (unclear failure modes).

- In Section 5.3, it’s unclear whether the state space is discretized; the reviewer asks why discretization is used and what happens if it is not.

- Scalability to more complex embodiments (e.g., a 5-link swimmer vs. 2-link) is questioned; empirical assessment is needed to judge generality.

- The claim of using only minimal domain knowledge is disputed: the intrinsic rewards appear hand-crafted and task-specific, similar to goal-based pretraining.
