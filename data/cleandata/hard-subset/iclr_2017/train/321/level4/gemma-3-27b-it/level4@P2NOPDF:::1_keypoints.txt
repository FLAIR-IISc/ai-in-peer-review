- Positive: interesting contribution to hierarchical RL with compelling experimental results on challenging benchmarks, but the analysis needs strengthening.
- Terminology concern: calling the pre-training objectives “intrinsic motivation” is misleading since they reward task-directed behaviors similar to Heess et al., not true curiosity/novelty.
- Mutual information regularization: elegant and generally helpful, but it fails to improve performance in complex maze environments (1–3); the reviewer asks for analysis of why.
- Replicability: Sagent is clearly defined, but Srest is insufficiently specified—more implementation details are needed for full reproducibility.
- Policy analysis: request for deeper study of policy switching (frequency, context, failure modes, and impact of switching timing) to explain how the hierarchy yields robustness and adaptability.
