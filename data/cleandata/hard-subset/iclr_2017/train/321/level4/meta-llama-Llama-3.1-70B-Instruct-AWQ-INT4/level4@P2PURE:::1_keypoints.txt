- Strong hierarchical-control idea with robust experiments that solve benchmarks others could not, but the experimental analysis is insufficiently comprehensive.
- The term "intrinsic motivation" is misleading here: pretraining by rewarding speed/grasp is highly task-specific and not the usual RL notion of intrinsic motivation.
- Mutual Information regularization is elegant and generally helpful but fails on more complex mazes (1–3); the cause of this limitation needs further interpretation.
- For reproducibility, the paper must clearly specify the state factorization between S_agent and S_rest (S_rest is not clearly defined).
- The paper needs deeper analysis of switching behavior, detailed policy examination (including failure modes), and study of how switching timing affects performance.
