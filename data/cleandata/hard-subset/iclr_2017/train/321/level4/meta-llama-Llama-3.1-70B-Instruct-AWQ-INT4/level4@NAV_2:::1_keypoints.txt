- Proposes a novel framework combining intrinsic motivation and hierarchical methods to train policies for sparse-reward, long-horizon tasks.  
- Uses a single proxy reward to pre-train diverse skills via Stochastic Neural Networks (SNNs) with an information-theoretic regularizer.  
- Leverages pre-trained skills by training a separate high-level policy per downstream task to accelerate learning.  
- Claims sample-efficient skill discovery and uniform performance boosts across a wide range of downstream tasks.  
- Experiments demonstrate effective skill learning and strong performance on challenging, sparse-reward, long-horizon tasks.  
- Recommendation: accept based on novelty, motivation, and strong experimental results.
