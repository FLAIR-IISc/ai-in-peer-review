- Strong experimental results showing benchmarks previous work could not solve, but the experimental analysis needs improvement.  
- Use of the term "intrinsic" motivation is misleading; the pre-training (rewarding speed/grasp) is highly task-specific and similar to prior work, raising novelty concerns.  
- Mutual Information regularization is elegant and often effective, but it appears to fail on the more complex mazes (1–3); deeper interpretation/analysis is needed.  
- Replicability issue: the factorization between S_agent and S_rest must be clearly specified—S_rest is not described in the paper.  
- Recommend analyzing agent switching behavior, policy failure modes, and the impact of switching time on performance for a fuller understanding.
