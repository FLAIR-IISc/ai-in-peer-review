- Strong experimental contribution: hierarchical control experiments are compelling and solve benchmarks previous work could not, but the experimental analysis is underdeveloped.
- Terminology concern: labeling the pre-training as "intrinsic" motivation is misleading since tasks are closely tied to the target tasks and resemble Heess et al.'s pre-training.
- Mutual Information regularizer: it's elegant and generally helpful, but shows limited effectiveness in more complex mazes (1–3) and needs deeper interpretation.
- Reproducibility gap: explicitly define the factorization between Sagent and Srest (Srest is not specified) to enable replication.
- Behavioral analysis missing: provide a deeper study of agent switching behavior, policy details, failure modes, and the impact of switching timing on performance.
