- Strong contribution to hierarchical control with impressive empirical results on benchmarks, but the accompanying analysis is weak.

- The term "intrinsic motivation" is misapplied: pre-training objectives are task-specific and aligned with downstream tasks, not task-agnostic exploration; similar to Heess et al.

- Mutual information regularization is elegant and generally effective but does not help in complex maze environments; the paper lacks interpretation for this negative result.

- Reproducibility concern: the state-space factorization (S_agent vs S_rest) needs a precise definition—S_rest is not clearly specified.

- Experimental analysis should probe learned policies more deeply (e.g., high-level controller switching behavior), report common failure modes, and study the impact of switching frequency on performance.
