- Suggestion: explore adding diversity objectives to the baseline or develop end-to-end hierarchical methods (e.g., inspired by Hierarchical REPS or Option-Critic).

- Limitation 1: Skill-differentiation mechanisms (concatenation/bilinear) only modulate the first hidden layer, which may limit distinct sub-policy capacity and may not generalize beyond locomotion tasks.  
- Limitation 2: Decoupled training keeps low-level sub-policies fixed and uses fixed sub-policy durations, placing heavy burden on unsupervised pre-training and limiting adaptability; connection to end-to-end methods should be discussed.  
- Limitation 3: Intrinsic rewards appear domain-specific due to the decoupled training paradigm, reducing general unsupervised applicability.
