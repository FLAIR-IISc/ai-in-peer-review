1. Scalability/diversity: The paper lacks discussion of limitations and failure modes as environment, state, and action spaces grow, raising concerns about discovering sufficiently diverse skills.  
2. State discretization: Clarify whether the state space is discretized (grid X), justify that design choice, and explain adaptation/challenges for continuous state spaces.  
3. Generalization to complex embodiments: Test on more complex morphologies (e.g., 5-link swimmer) to validate claims of generality beyond simple agents.  
4. Hand-crafted intrinsic rewards: The location-based intrinsic rewards appear domain-specific and comparable to pre-specified goals; authors should justify the "minimal domain knowledge" claim or temper it.
