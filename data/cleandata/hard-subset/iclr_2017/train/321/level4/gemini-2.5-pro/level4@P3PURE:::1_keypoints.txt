- Strength: Interesting hierarchical-control approach with strong experimental results solving benchmarks that prior methods could not, though the experimental analysis is weak.

- Misuse of term: Calling the pre-training objectives "intrinsic motivation" is inappropriate—these are task-relevant (locomotion/grasping) and similar to Heess et al., not truly task-agnostic intrinsic rewards.

- MI regularizer: Mutual information regularization helps in many cases but not in complex mazes (1–3); authors should analyze why it fails there.

- Replicability: The factorization into S_agent and S_rest is underspecified—S_rest needs a clear, explicit definition.

- Policy analysis: The paper lacks in-depth analysis of learned policies—e.g., high-level controller switching behavior, common failure modes, and the impact of switching time on performance.
