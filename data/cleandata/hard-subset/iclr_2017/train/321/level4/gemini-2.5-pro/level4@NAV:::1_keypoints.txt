- Strong contribution to hierarchical control with convincing empirical results on hard benchmarks, though the accompanying analysis could be deeper.
- The term "intrinsic motivation" is misaligned with common RL usage: pre-training rewards (e.g., locomotion speed, grasp success) are task-specific and resemble Heess et al.'s pre-training.
- Mutual information regularization is elegant and often effective, but it fails to help in complex maze environments (1–3); the paper should hypothesize or explain this discrepancy.
- The state-space factorization is imprecise: S_agent is described but S_rest lacks an explicit definition—adding this would improve clarity and reproducibility.
- The paper needs more in-depth analysis of emergent behavior, including high-level policy switching patterns, common failure modes, and sensitivity to the high-level action timescale.
