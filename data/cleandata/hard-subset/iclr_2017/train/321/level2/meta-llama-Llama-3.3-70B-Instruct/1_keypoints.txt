- Proposes using Stochastic Neural Networks to learn diverse skills with minimal supervision and a hierarchical controller for sparse-reward tasks.
- Reviewer recommends acceptance, citing good motivation and thorough analysis.
- Framework is clearly and concisely explained.
- Authors demonstrate deep understanding of challenges and limitations of current approaches.
- Experiments show the approach learns diverse skills and improves exploration in downstream tasks.
- Paper is well-organized and easy to follow.
- Lacks detailed hyperparameter reporting (e.g., number of skills, batch size, max path length) for reproducibility.
- Needs more comparisons with state-of-the-art baselines.
- Should discuss potential limitations and failure modes (e.g., robot instability when switching skills).
- Suggests adding hyperparameter details, more baselines, and discussion of limitations/future work.
- Asks how hyperparameters were chosen and tuned.
- Asks how authors will address instability when switching skills and proposed solutions/future directions.
- Asks how the approach could be extended to more complex tasks/environments and anticipated challenges.
