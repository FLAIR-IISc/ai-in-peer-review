**Summary**

This paper proposes a framework for hierarchical reinforcement learning using Stochastic Neural Networks (SNNs) to learn a diverse set of skills in a pre-training environment. The skills are then used as basic building blocks for solving tasks with sparse rewards. The authors demonstrate the effectiveness of their approach on several tasks, including maze navigation and object collection, and show that it outperforms state-of-the-art intrinsic motivation methods.
