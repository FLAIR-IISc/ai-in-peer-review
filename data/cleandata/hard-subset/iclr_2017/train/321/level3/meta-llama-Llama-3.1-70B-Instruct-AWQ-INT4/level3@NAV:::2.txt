**Summary of the Paper's Contribution**

This paper proposes a hierarchical reinforcement learning framework that combines stochastic neural networks (SNNs) with an information-theoretic regularizer to learn a diverse set of skills in a pre-training environment. The learned skills are then used in a hierarchical architecture to solve challenging tasks with sparse rewards. The authors demonstrate the effectiveness of their approach on several tasks, including locomotion and manipulation, and show that it outperforms state-of-the-art methods in some cases.
