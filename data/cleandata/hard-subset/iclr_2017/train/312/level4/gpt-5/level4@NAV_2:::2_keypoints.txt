- Introduces an actor–critic RL approach for neural architecture search treating architectures as variable-length sequences.  
- Architectural decisions are actions and validation performance of the trained model is used as the reward.  
- Uses an autoregressive two-layer LSTM serving as both controller and critic.  
- Evaluated on two distinct tasks and compared against several human-designed architectures.  
- Novel and thoroughly explained method with necessary refinements.  
- Benchmarks effectively demonstrate the method’s capabilities and show generated designs differ from human-devised ones.  
- Writing is clear and accessible, with related work well covered and contrasted.  
- Suggests adding detailed measurements of training time and studying compute/resources vs. final model quality.  
- Recommends investigating how human-bootstrapped starting points behave and evolve under the framework.  
- Overall assessment: an excellent and engaging paper.
