- Proposes using an RNN trained with reinforcement learning to generate task-specific neural architectures.
- Experimental results on two datasets are promising and suggest the method is sound.
- Novel and interesting approach: RNN generator combined with RL training.
- Generated architectures resemble human-designed models, indicating compatibility with human expertise.
- Training is very time-consuming even with substantial computational resources.
- Experiments fail to demonstrate generalization; need evaluation on additional, related but distinct datasets, especially for sequential models.
- Overall: strong paper but needs more experiments to convincingly show advantages over human-designed models.
