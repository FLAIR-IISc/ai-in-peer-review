- Proposes an RNN controller that generates neural network descriptions, trained with reinforcement learning.
- Experiments on two datasets show the method produces solid architectures.
- Generated architectures resemble human-designed models, suggesting compatibility with human expertise.
- Training time is long even with substantial computing resources.
- Experiments do not demonstrate generality; need evaluation on other similar/different datasets, particularly for sequential models.
- Overall promising work but requires more experiments to demonstrate advantage over human-designed models.
