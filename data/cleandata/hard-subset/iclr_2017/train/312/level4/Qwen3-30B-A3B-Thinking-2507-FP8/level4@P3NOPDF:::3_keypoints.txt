- Weakness: limited generalizability—experiments confined to two datasets with no evaluation on additional/sequential data (e.g., time-series or text).  
- Recommendation: provide computational efficiency analyses and comparisons to address the resource bottleneck.  
- Recommendation: expand experiments to multiple and sequential datasets to demonstrate transferability and robustness.
