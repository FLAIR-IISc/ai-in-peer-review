- Proposes a novel neural architecture search method using an RNN controller that generates network descriptions.  
- Controller is trained with reinforcement learning to maximize expected validation accuracy of generated architectures.  
- Demonstrates effectiveness on CIFAR-10 and Penn Treebank, achieving state-of-the-art results.  
- Reviewer recommends acceptance based on strong empirical performance and potential to drive a new research direction.  
- Paper gives a thorough explanation of the method, including controller architecture and training procedure.  
- Provides detailed analysis with comparisons to state-of-the-art models and ablation studies.  
- Writing is clear and concise, making the paper easy to follow.
