- Novel idea: uses RNNs to generate network architectures and reinforcement learning to train the RNNs — promising approach.
- Generated architectures resemble human-designed models, indicating compatibility with human expertise.
- Training time is lengthy even with substantial computing resources, raising efficiency and scalability concerns.
- Experimental evidence for generality is insufficient; need results on additional, related datasets (especially for sequential models).
- Overall: paper is promising but requires more experiments to demonstrate advantages over human-designed architectures.
