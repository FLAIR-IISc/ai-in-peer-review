- Uses an auto-regressive two-layer LSTM as the controller and critic.  
- Treats architectures as sequences and formulates architecture search as an RL problem.  
- Evaluated on CIFAR-10 and Penn Treebank with results rivaling or surpassing human-designed architectures.
