- Novel approach: RNNs generate network descriptions and RL trains the RNN — an intriguing idea.
- Experiments on two datasets demonstrate the method's viability.
- Strength: Innovative combination of RNNs and RL for architecture generation.
- Strength: Generated architectures resemble human-designed networks.
- Limitation: Training is computationally intensive, leading to prolonged runtimes.
- Limitation: Experiments lack generality — no evaluation on other similar/distinct datasets, especially for sequential models.
- Overall: Well-written paper with potential but requires further experiments to demonstrate advantages and broader applicability.
