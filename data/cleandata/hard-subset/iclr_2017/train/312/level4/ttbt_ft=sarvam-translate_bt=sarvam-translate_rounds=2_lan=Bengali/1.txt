This research paper discusses an important aspect of our field: automatic architecture search. Although this technique is currently computationally very complex, these difficulties will likely improve in the future as technology advances.

This paper covers both standard vision and text tasks and works with many benchmark datasets, demonstrating that searching outside standard RNA and CNN search spaces can be very beneficial. While one would always want this technique to be applied to more datasets, it is already sufficient that this technique is not only competitive with human architectural concepts but can even surpass them. It indicates a method for creating architectures for specific datasets without manual work at every stage.

This is a beautiful research paper written on an interesting topic, with strong results. I recommend accepting it.
