- Paper extends prior rate-distortion optimization to deep encoders/decoders and to adaptive entropy coding, and relates the approach to VAEs.
- Novelty is limited because the core rate-distortion approach was previously published.
- The switch from a perceptual metric to MSE may be a step backward compared to prior work.
- Results visibly improve over JPEG 2000 and appear strong compared to other learned codecs.
- Paper is very well written.
- Equation 10 seems incorrect: the partition function should depend on g_s(y; θ), implying the method is not equivalent to a VAE for non‑Euclidean metrics.
- Why optimize MSE instead of a perceptual metric, and why evaluate only with PSNR given the authors’ backgrounds?
- Unclear contribution of adaptive entropy coding versus deeper encoder/decoder capacity — an ablation (no adaptation) is needed.
- More details on the adaptive entropy coder and its effects are required; reviewer may raise the score if provided.
