**Summary of the Paper's Contribution**

This paper presents an end-to-end optimized image compression method using deep neural networks. The authors propose a framework that jointly optimizes the analysis and synthesis transforms, as well as the entropy code, for rate-distortion performance. The method uses a generalized divisive normalization (GDN) joint nonlinearity, inspired by models of biological neurons, to capture the features and attributes of images. The authors demonstrate that their method outperforms standard image compression methods, such as JPEG and JPEG 2000, in terms of rate-distortion performance and visual quality.
