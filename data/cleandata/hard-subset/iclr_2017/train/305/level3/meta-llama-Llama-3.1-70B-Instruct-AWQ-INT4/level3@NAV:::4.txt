**Summary of the Paper's Claims and Contributions**

The paper proposes an end-to-end learning method for image compression, which uses rate-distortion optimization with a clever relaxation to learn efficient compression from a natural image database. The method consists of a nonlinear analysis transformation, a uniform quantizer, and a nonlinear synthesis transformation, all of which are optimized jointly for rate-distortion performance. The authors demonstrate that their method generally exhibits better rate-distortion performance than standard JPEG and JPEG 2000 compression methods, and more importantly, achieves a dramatic improvement in visual quality for all images at all bit rates.
