- Novel end-to-end trainable image compression system that often outperforms JPEG and JPEG 2000 in rate-distortion.
- Main technical contribution: differentiable rate approximation via continuous relaxation (additive noise) enabling joint SGD optimization of analysis/synthesis transforms.
- Demonstrates significant perceptual quality improvements supported by MS-SSIM and objective metrics.
- Thorough empirical comparison using actual bitrates across diverse images, plus ablation studies and analysis of the relaxation (Figure 4).
- Situates work within prior nonlinear/biologically inspired transforms and contrasts similarities/differences with variational autoencoders.
