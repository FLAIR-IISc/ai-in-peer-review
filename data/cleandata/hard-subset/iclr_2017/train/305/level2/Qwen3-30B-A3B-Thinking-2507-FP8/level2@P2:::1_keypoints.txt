- Presents an end-to-end optimized image compression method using nonlinear transforms with a GDN nonlinearity inspired by biological vision.  
- Jointly optimizes analysis transform, quantizer (via continuous relaxation), and synthesis transform using stochastic gradient descent.  
- Continuous relaxation of quantization effectively enables gradient-based optimization.  
- Demonstrates superior rate-distortion performance and visual quality versus JPEG and JPEG 2000 across multiple images and bit rates.  
- Consistent improvements reported in MS-SSIM and PSNR, with qualitative visual examples.  
- Provides sufficient implementation details (including entropy coding and training methodology) to support reproducibility.  
- Well-situated in related work, with clear discussion of transform coding, generative models, and biological inspiration.  
- Nicely distinguishes the framework from variational autoencoders, addressing potential confusion.
