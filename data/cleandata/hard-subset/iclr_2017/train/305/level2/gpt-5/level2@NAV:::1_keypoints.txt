- Proposes end-to-end learned image compression using cascaded conv transforms with GDN/IGDN and relaxed quantization for SGD training.  
- Claims joint rate–distortion optimization via additive uniform noise links the method to VAEs and enables practical training.  
- Reports better rate–distortion than JPEG/JPEG2000 (especially at low rates) and markedly improved perceptual quality (MS-SSIM and visual artifacts).  
- Evaluates with a practical entropy coder (CABAC) to report actual bitrates.  
- Decision: Accept.  
- Main reason: Novel, well-motivated end-to-end RD learning with GDN/IGDN and principled quantization relaxation.  
- Empirical support is convincing with real bitrates and perceptual/visual gains.  
- Problem/approach: Replaces hand-designed pipelines with learned nonlinear transforms optimized for RD; relaxation is justified and empirically validated.  
- Literature placement: Prior work (VAEs, Toderici, Gregor) is accurately positioned; differences in objectives and evaluation are clear; GDN biological rationale cited.  
- Evidence: Results on Kodak and other sets with PSNR, MS-SSIM, visual comparisons, and CABAC-reported bitrates strengthen claims.  
- Usefulness: Simple encoder/decoder at inference and adequate architectural/training detail for reproduction in principle.  
- Limitations acknowledged: separate models per λ, independence assumptions in entropy model, and simple context modeling.  
- Suggest exploring a single λ-conditioned model to cover the RD curve.  
- Suggest improving entropy models (learned hyperpriors/context) to further reduce rate.  
- Recommend additional perceptual evaluations (user studies, more metrics) and fairness checks for color subsampling.  
- Request reporting of complexity metrics (FLOPs, latency, model size) and ablation studies (GDN vs ReLU, stages).  
- Ask for clearer reproducibility via code or detailed configs.  
- Questions for authors: Can a single λ-conditioned model match per-λ models?  
- Questions for authors: How sensitive are results to the latent independence assumption and have spatial/context priors been tried?  
- Questions for authors: What are encoding/decoding runtimes and memory footprints on standard CPUs/GPUs?  
- Questions for authors: How does performance compare to modern baselines (HEVC intra/BPG) and with 4:4:4 settings?  
- Questions for authors: Do results generalize to unseen distributions (high-res, RAW camera data)?
