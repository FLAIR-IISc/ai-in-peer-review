- End-to-end deep-learning image compression/decompression that outperforms JPEG-2000 on bit-rate vs. quality.
- Main contribution: a differentiable "rate" function enabling effective training across rate–distortion trade-offs.
- Differentiable approximations likely useful beyond image compression for other tasks needing similar functions.
- Authors provided a thorough response to the reviewer’s initial query.
- Reviewer contends a sufficiently complex network under fixed range and quantization constraints would learn max-entropy codes within the range and reach the upper bound.
- Reviewer finds authors’ second argument persuasive: enforcing that constraint requires a specific compressor output structure and would force a more complex network to replicate computations currently handled by a separate variable-rate encoder storing quantization values.
