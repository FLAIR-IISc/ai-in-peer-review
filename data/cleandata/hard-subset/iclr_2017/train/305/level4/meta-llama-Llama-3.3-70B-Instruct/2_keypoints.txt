- Novelty is limited because the core rate-distortion optimization has been published previously.  
- Potential regression by switching from optimizing a perceptual metric to using MSE.

- Manuscript is exceptionally well-written.

- Contribution of adaptive entropy coding versus deeper encoder/decoder architectures is unclear; requests ablation without adaptation.
