- Extends rate-distortion optimization to deep encoders/decoders and introduces adaptive entropy coding.  
- Discusses relationship between the proposed method and variational autoencoders.  
- Novelty is limited because the core rate-distortion approach was previously published; no clear major innovation identified.  
- Uses MSE optimization (PSNR) instead of perceptual metrics used in prior work, which may be a backward step.  
- Empirical results show clear improvement over JPEG 2000; possibly unmatched among learned schemes.  
- Paper is exceptionally well written.  
- Equation 10 appears incorrect: the partition function should depend on g_s(y; θ), so the method is not equivalent to a VAE when non-Euclidean metrics are used.  
- Reviewer questions the rationale for optimizing MSE and exclusive reliance on PSNR for evaluation.  
- The relative contribution of adaptive entropy coding versus deeper encoders/decoders is unclear; an ablation (no adaptation) and more details on the adaptive coder’s effects are requested.
