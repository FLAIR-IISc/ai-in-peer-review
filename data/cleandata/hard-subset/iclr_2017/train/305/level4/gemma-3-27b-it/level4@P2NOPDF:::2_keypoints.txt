- Applies rate-distortion optimization to deep neural networks with adaptive entropy coding and draws connections to VAEs.  
- Novelty is incremental; unclear if there is a significant new algorithmic contribution.  
- Prior work optimized perceptual metrics; this paper reverses to MSE without adequate justification.  
- Experimental results show clear, substantial improvement over JPEG 2000—possibly unmatched by other learned methods.  
- Paper is exceptionally well-written and clearly presented.  
- Possible error in Equation 10: partition function seems independent of g_s(y; θ), challenging the claimed VAE equivalence for non-Euclidean metrics—needs correction/clarification.  
- Evaluation relies solely on PSNR; lacks perceptual-quality metrics and broader justification for MSE objective.  
- Must disentangle gains from adaptive entropy coding versus increased encoder/decoder capacity; include ablation without adaptive coding.  
- Provide detailed implementation and performance impact of the adaptive coder; reviewer may raise score if clarified and additional experiments are added.
