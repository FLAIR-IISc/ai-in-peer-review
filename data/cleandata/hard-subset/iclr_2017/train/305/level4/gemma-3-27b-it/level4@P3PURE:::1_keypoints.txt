Highly convincing, well-written paper on image compression with deep neural networks.  
Effective integration of rate-distortion theory into the objective function.  
Robust evaluation by comparing against JPEG2000 rather than only JPEG.  
Likely to have a significant impact on the field.  
Recommend adding results on standard benchmark images (Lena, Barbara, Baboon) instead of Gibbons.  
Needs clearer positioning and comparison against state-of-the-art classical methods, clarifying relative strengths and weaknesses.
