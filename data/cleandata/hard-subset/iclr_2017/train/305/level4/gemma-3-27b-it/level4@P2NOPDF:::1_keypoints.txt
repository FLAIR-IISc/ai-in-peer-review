- Presents a compelling deep neural network approach to image compression, arguably the most convincing work on this topic the reviewer has seen.
- Writing is excellent; integration of rate–distortion theory into the objective is well-executed and natural.
- Key strength: comparison to JPEG2000 as a strong, relevant baseline, improving over prior work that focused on JPEG.
- Anticipated substantial impact on the field.
- Recommendation: report performance metrics on standard test images (Lena, Barbara, Baboon).
- Recommendation: provide a more thorough comparison to state-of-the-art classical compression methods (including the method mentioned in reviewer queries).
- Concern: unclear how the proposed method compares to the very best existing methods overall; a direct, comprehensive comparison is needed to establish its positioning and benefits.
