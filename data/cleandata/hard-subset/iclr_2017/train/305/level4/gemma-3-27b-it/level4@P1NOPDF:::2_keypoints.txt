- Paper extends rate-distortion optimization to deep encoders/decoders and introduces adaptive entropy coding, and links the approach to variational autoencoders.  
- Novelty is limited because the underlying rate-distortion framework was previously published.  
- Method uses MSE (PSNR) rather than perceptual metrics, which may be a backward step compared to prior perceptual-optimized work.  
- Despite metric choice, results clearly improve over JPEG 2000 and appear unmatched by other learned schemes.  
- Paper is exceptionally well written and clear.  
- Possible error in Equation 10: the partition function should depend on g_s(y; θ), affecting equivalence to VAEs with non-Euclidean metrics.  
- Reviewer requests rationale for optimizing MSE/PSNR instead of perceptual metrics given authors’ expertise.  
- Need to quantify the separate contributions of adaptive entropy coding versus deeper encoder/decoder architectures.  
- Reviewer asks for experiments without adaptive coding (as in prior work) and more details on the adaptive coder; would reconsider score if provided.
