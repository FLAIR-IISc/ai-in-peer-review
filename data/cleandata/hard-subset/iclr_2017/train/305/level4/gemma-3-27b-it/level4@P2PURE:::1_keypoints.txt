- Exceptionally strong and compelling work on image compression with deep neural networks.  
- Paper is very well-written with seamless integration of rate-distortion theory into the objective.  
- Provides a strong, relevant baseline comparison to JPEG2000.  
- Demonstrates significant improvement over prior work that often compared only to JPEG.  
- Anticipated to have substantial impact on the field.  
- Evaluation should include standard benchmark images (Lena, Barbara, Baboon) instead of Gibbons.  
- Need clear comparisons to state-of-the-art classical methods (including the specific method noted in reviewer questions).  
- Current manuscript does not clearly position neural methods relative to the best traditional approaches; clarify this for complete assessment.
