- Extends rate-distortion optimization to deep neural encoders/decoders with adaptive entropy coding and connects to VAEs.  
- Novelty is moderate: useful combination but core theoretical foundations are not new.  
- Shift from prior perceptual optimization to MSE optimization is surprising given authors’ background.  
- Empirical results are strong, outperforming JPEG 2000 and unmatched by other learned codecs to the reviewer’s knowledge.  
- Potential error in Equation 10: partition function seems independent of g_s(y;θ), undermining VAE equivalence for non-Euclidean metrics.  
- Need justification for optimizing MSE and evaluating solely with PSNR; perceptual metrics and discussion are missing.  
- Contribution of adaptive entropy coding versus deeper network architectures is unclear and needs disentangling.  
- Request experimental ablation comparing adaptive coding on/off and more details on adaptive coder implementation and its RD impact.  
- Paper is exceptionally well written and clearly presented.  
- Recommendation: accept pending corrections to Eq.10, justification for MSE/PSNR choices, and deeper analysis of adaptive coding.
