- Extends rate-distortion optimization to deep encoders/decoders and uses adaptive entropy coding, with connections to VAEs.  
- Overall novelty is incremental since core methodology was previously published.  
- Moving from perceptual metrics to MSE may be a regression compared to prior work.  
- Demonstrated substantial performance gains over JPEG 2000 and possibly the best among learned compression methods.  
- Manuscript is exceptionally well-written and clear.  
- Potential error in Equation 10: partition function likely depends on g_s(y; θ), which would break the VAE equivalence for non-Euclidean metrics.  
- Rationale for prioritizing MSE and exclusive reliance on PSNR for evaluation is unclear and surprising given the authors’ expertise.  
- Paper needs experiments disentangling the effects of adaptive entropy coding from deeper network architectures (ablation without adaptive coding).  
- More detailed analysis of the adaptive coder and its impact on performance is required to strengthen the paper.
