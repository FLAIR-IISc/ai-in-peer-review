This paper presents a highly compelling approach to image compression using deep neural networks – arguably the most convincing I’ve encountered in the field to date. The writing is exceptionally clear, and the integration of rate-distortion theory into the objective function is seamless and well-justified. A significant strength is the comparison to a relevant and strong baseline (JPEG2000), a welcome improvement over prior work often limited to JPEG comparisons. I anticipate this work will have a substantial impact on the field. 

To further strengthen the manuscript, I recommend including results on standard test images such as Lena, Barbara, and Baboon. Furthermore, a clearer positioning of the proposed method relative to state-of-the-art classical compression techniques – including those like the one I referenced in my questions – is crucial. Currently, it remains unclear how neural network-based methods compare to the best performing traditional approaches, and explicitly demonstrating this would significantly enhance the paper’s contribution.
