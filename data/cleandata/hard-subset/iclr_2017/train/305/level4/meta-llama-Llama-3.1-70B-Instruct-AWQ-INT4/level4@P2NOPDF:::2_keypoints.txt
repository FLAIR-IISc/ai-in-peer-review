- Extends rate-distortion optimization to deep encoders/decoders, upgrades to adaptive entropy coding, and explores links to variational autoencoders.  
- Novelty is limited because the rate-distortion optimization approach has been published before.  
- The paper regresses to optimizing MSE instead of a perceptual metric used in prior work, which is a step backward.  
- Results show a noticeable improvement over JPEG 2000 and may be the best learned encoding performance known.  
- The paper is well-written and clearly presented.  
- Equation 10 appears incorrect: the partition function likely depends on g_s(y; θ), calling into question VAE equivalence for non-Euclidean metrics.  
- Authors should justify choosing MSE and evaluating only with PSNR rather than perceptual metrics.  
- Provide an ablation separating the impact of adaptive entropy coding from deeper encoders/decoders (performance without adaptation).  
- Give more implementation/details on the adaptive coder and quantify its effects; this could raise the paper’s score.
