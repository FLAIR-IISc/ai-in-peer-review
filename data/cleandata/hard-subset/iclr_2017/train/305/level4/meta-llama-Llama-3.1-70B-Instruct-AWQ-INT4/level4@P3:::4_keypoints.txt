- Proposes an end-to-end learning method for image compression that optimizes rate-distortion performance via a clever relaxation.  
- Recommends acceptance: method is interesting, results impressive, analysis thorough, and presentation clear.  
- Well-motivated problem with clear explanation of background and challenges.  
- Novel approach using a relaxation; authors analyze its relationship to variational autoencoders and other compression techniques.  
- Experimental results outperform standard methods (JPEG, JPEG 2000).  
- Provides detailed evaluation across various images and bit rates.  
- Suggests adding an explicit discussion of the method’s limitations and future improvement directions.
