- Proposes extending rate-distortion optimization to deep encoders/decoders and adding adaptive entropy coding; discusses relation to VAEs.  
- Novelty is limited since it mainly builds on previously published rate-distortion work.  
- Regression to optimizing MSE (PSNR) instead of a perceptual metric used in prior work is possibly a step backward.  
- Results show notable improvement over JPEG 2000 and strong learned compression performance.  
- Paper is well-written and clearly presented.  
- Equation 10 appears incorrect: the partition function lacks dependence on g_s(y; θ), raising doubts about VAE equivalence for non-Euclidean metrics.  
- Reviewer requests motivation for using MSE/PSNR rather than perceptual metrics given authors’ background.  
- Reviewer asks for ablation separating the impact of adaptive entropy coding from deeper encoder/decoder architectures.  
- Reviewer requests more details on the adaptive coder and its quantitative effect; would consider a higher score if provided.  
- Overall recommendation: address the noted concerns and clarify the distinct contributions.
