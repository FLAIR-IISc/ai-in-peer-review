- Well-written, thorough end-to-end learning approach for image compression.
- Proposes an optimization method for rate-distortion using a clever relaxation to learn efficient compression from natural images.
- Tackles optimization within a nonlinear transform coding framework; approach is well-motivated and builds on prior work.
- Method is clearly explained.
- Comprehensive analysis with experiments and comparisons to JPEG and JPEG 2000.
- Demonstrates generally better rate–distortion performance and significant visual-quality gains measured by MS-SSIM.
- Reviewer recommends acceptance: method is interesting, results thorough, analysis well-done.
