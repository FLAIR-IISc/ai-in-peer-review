- Seamless incorporation of rate–distortion theory into the objective function.  
- Strength: evaluation uses a reasonable baseline (JPEG2000), improving over prior JPEG-only comparisons.  
- Reviewer expects the work to have significant impact in the field.

- Essential to clearly position neural network methods relative to the best previous techniques.
