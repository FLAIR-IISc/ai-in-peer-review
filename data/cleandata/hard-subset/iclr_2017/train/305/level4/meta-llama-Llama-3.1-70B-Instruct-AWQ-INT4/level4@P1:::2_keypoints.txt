- Extends rate-distortion optimization to deep encoders/decoders and adaptive entropy coding, and discusses relation to VAEs.
- Novelty is limited because the core approach was previously published.
- Results clearly outperform JPEG 2000 and represent a notable empirical improvement.
- Paper is very well written.
- Equation 10 appears incorrect; partition function likely depends on gs(y; θ), challenging VAE equivalence for non-Euclidean metrics.
- Surprising choice to optimize for MSE/PSNR rather than perceptual metrics used in prior work.
- Need to isolate the contribution of adaptive entropy coding versus deeper encoders/decoders with ablation experiments.
- More detail required on the adaptive entropy coder and its quantitative effects.
- Decision: Accept.
- Reasons for decision: well-executed extension, impressive results despite limited novelty.
- Requests for authors: clarify VAE relationship for non-Euclidean metrics, justify MSE choice, detail adaptive coder, and add experiments isolating contributions.
- Specific questions: provide more detail on the adaptive coder and its effects; how will the MSE/perceptual-metric limitation be addressed; can additional experiments isolate adaptive coding vs deeper networks?
