- Paper presents a highly compelling deep neural network approach to image compression.  
- Excellent writing and seamless integration of rate–distortion theory into the framework.  
- Comparison to JPEG2000 is a notable improvement over prior studies that only used JPEG.  
- Reviewer expects the paper to have significant impact in the field.  
- Suggest adding results on standard test images (Lena, Barbara, Baboon).  
- Recommend comparing to state-of-the-art classical (non-neural) compression methods.  
- Current version lacks clear positioning between neural-network methods and the best classical approaches.
