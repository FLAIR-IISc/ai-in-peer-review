This paper presents a novel approach to image compression using a deep neural network. The authors propose a nonlinear transform coding method that jointly optimizes the analysis and synthesis transforms for rate-distortion performance. The paper is well-written, and the results show a visible improvement over JPEG 2000, which is a significant achievement.

The specific question tackled by the paper is how to optimize a nonlinear transform coding method for image compression. The approach is well-motivated, building upon previous work on rate-distortion optimization and variational autoencoders. The paper provides a clear and detailed explanation of the method, including the architecture of the neural network, the optimization procedure, and the evaluation metrics.

The paper supports its claims with extensive experimental results, including comparisons with JPEG and JPEG 2000 on various image datasets. The results demonstrate the effectiveness of the proposed method in achieving better rate-distortion performance and improved visual quality. The authors also provide a thorough analysis of the results, discussing the advantages and limitations of their approach.

I accept this paper for its significant contribution to the field of image compression and its potential impact on the development of more efficient compression methods.
