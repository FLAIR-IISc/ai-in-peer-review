- Highly convincing paper on image compression with deep neural networks; reviewer finds it the best so far.
- Paper is very well written.
- Incorporation of rate-distortion theory into the objective is seamless and appropriate.
- Inclusion of a reasonable baseline (JPEG2000) is welcome compared to prior works using only JPEG.
- Reviewer expects the paper to have significant impact.
- Requests inclusion of results on standard test images (Lena, Barbara, Baboon).
- Asks for comparisons with state-of-the-art classical methods to clearly position neural approaches relative to best previous methods.
- Notes current submission does not make the relative positioning between neural and classical methods sufficiently clear.
