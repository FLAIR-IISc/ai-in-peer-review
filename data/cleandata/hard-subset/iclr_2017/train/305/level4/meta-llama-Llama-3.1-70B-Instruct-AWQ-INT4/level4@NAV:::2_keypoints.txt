- Extends an existing rate–distortion optimization method to deep encoders/decoders, adds adaptive entropy coding, and explores connections to VAEs.  
- Novelty is questionable because the underlying rate–distortion technique was previously published; request clarification on new contributions.  
- Using MSE is potentially a step back compared to prior work that optimized perceptual metrics.  
- Empirical results show a noticeable improvement over JPEG 2000 and may be the best among learned methods known to the reviewer.  
- Paper is exceptionally well-written.  
- Equation 10 appears incorrect: the partition function should depend on g_s(y; θ), so the method may not be equivalent to a VAE for non‑Euclidean metrics.  
- Request justification for optimizing MSE instead of a perceptual metric and for evaluating only with PSNR given the authors’ backgrounds.  
- Ask for an ablation separating the contribution of adaptive entropy coding from deeper encoder/decoder architectures, including performance without adaptation.  
- Request more details on the adaptive coder and its effects; reviewer may raise score if these concerns are addressed.
