This is the most convincing paper on image compression with deep neural networks that I have read so far. The paper is very well written, with a seamless integration of rate-distortion theory into the framework. A reasonable baseline comparison with JPEG2000 is also provided, which is an improvement over previous papers that only considered JPEG. I expect this paper to have a significant impact.

To further strengthen the paper, I would like to see additional results on standard test images such as Lena, Barbara, and Baboon, along with comparisons to state-of-the-art methods that employ classical techniques. It is essential to clearly position the neural network-based approach relative to the best previous methods, as the submitted version does not provide a clear understanding of how these two categories of methods compare.
