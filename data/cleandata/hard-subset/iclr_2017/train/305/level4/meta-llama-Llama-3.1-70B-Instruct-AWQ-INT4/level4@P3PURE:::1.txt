This paper presents the most convincing work on image compression using deep neural networks that I have encountered to date. The manuscript is well-written, and the incorporation of rate-distortion theory into the objective function fits seamlessly within the proposed framework. A notable strength of the paper is its comparison to a reasonable baseline, namely JPEG2000, which is a more meaningful benchmark than the JPEG comparisons often found in previous works.

I expect this paper to have a significant impact in the field. To further strengthen the manuscript, I suggest including results on standard test images such as Lena, Barbara, and Baboon, in addition to comparisons with state-of-the-art methods that employ more classical approaches. It is essential to clearly elucidate how neural network-based methods compare to the best previous techniques. Upon reviewing the submitted version, I still lack a clear understanding of how these two categories of methods are positioned relative to each other.
