This paper presents the most compelling work on image compression using deep neural networks that I have encountered to date. The authors have done an excellent job in writing the paper, seamlessly integrating the rate-distortion theory into the framework. Furthermore, the comparison to a suitable baseline (JPEG2000) is a notable improvement over previous studies that only considered JPEG.

I anticipate that this paper will have a significant impact in the field. To further strengthen the manuscript, I suggest including results on standard test images such as Lena, Barbara, and Baboon, in addition to comparing the proposed approach to state-of-the-art methods that employ classical techniques. This would provide a clearer understanding of how neural network-based methods compare to the best previous approaches. Currently, the submitted version does not provide a clear positioning of these two categories of methods.
