- Highly convincing paper on image compression with deep networks; best the reviewer has read so far.  
- Very well written with seamless integration of rate-distortion theory.  
- Includes a reasonable baseline comparison with JPEG2000 (better than prior work that used only JPEG).  
- Reviewer expects the paper to have significant impact.  
- Requests additional results on standard test images (Lena, Barbara, Baboon).  
- Asks for comparisons to state-of-the-art classical methods and clearer positioning of neural approaches relative to the best previous methods.
