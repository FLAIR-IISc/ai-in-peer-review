This paper extends an approach to rate-distortion optimization to deep encoders and decoders, and from a simple entropy encoding scheme to adaptive entropy coding. In addition, the paper discusses the approach's relationship to variational autoencoders.

Given that the approach to rate-distortion optimization has already been published, the novelty of this submission is arguably not very high. However, the results are a visible improvement over JPEG 2000, and the reviewer is not aware of any other learned encoding that has achieved this level of performance. The paper is very well written.

The reviewer has some questions and concerns. Equation 10 appears to be incorrect, and the partition function should likely depend on gs(y; θ). This would imply that the approach is not equivalent to a VAE for non-Euclidean metrics.

The reviewer is also curious about the decision to optimize for mean squared error (MSE) rather than a perceptual metric, as was done in previous work. Given the authors' backgrounds, it is surprising that even the evaluation was only performed in terms of PSNR.

Furthermore, the reviewer would like to know the contribution of adaptive entropy coding versus the effect of deeper encoders and decoders. This seems like an important piece of information, and it would be interesting to see the performance without adaptation, as in the previous paper. More detail on the adaptive coder and its effects should be provided.

**Decision:** Accept

**Reasons for decision:** The paper presents a well-written and well-executed extension of previous work, with impressive results that outperform JPEG 2000. While the novelty of the submission may be limited, the reviewer believes that the contributions are still significant and warrant publication.

**Additional feedback:**

* The authors should clarify the relationship between their approach and VAEs, particularly with respect to non-Euclidean metrics.
* The authors should provide more detail on the decision to optimize for MSE rather than a perceptual metric.
* The authors should provide more information on the adaptive entropy coder and its effects on the results.
* The authors should consider including additional experiments to isolate the contribution of adaptive entropy coding versus deeper encoders and decoders.

**Questions for the authors:**

* Can the authors provide more detail on the adaptive entropy coder and its effects on the results?
* How do the authors plan to address the limitation of optimizing for MSE rather than a perceptual metric?
* Can the authors provide additional experiments to isolate the contribution of adaptive entropy coding versus deeper encoders and decoders?
