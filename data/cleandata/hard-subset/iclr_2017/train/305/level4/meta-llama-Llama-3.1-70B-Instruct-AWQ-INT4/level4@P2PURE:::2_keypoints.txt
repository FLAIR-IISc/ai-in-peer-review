- Extends rate–distortion optimization to deep encoders/decoders, upgrades to adaptive entropy coding, and examines links to variational autoencoders (VAEs).  
- Novelty is limited because the rate–distortion approach was previously published (please correct me if I missed new insights).  
- Regressing to MSE optimization is a step back from prior work that used a perceptual metric.  
- Results show noticeable improvement over JPEG 2000 and best performance among learned methods.  
- Paper is well written.  
- Equation 10 appears incorrect: the partition function should depend on g_s(y; θ), implying the method may not be equivalent to a VAE for non‑Euclidean metrics.  
- The authors should justify optimizing for MSE instead of a perceptual metric and explain why evaluation is only in PSNR given their backgrounds.  
- The relative contribution of adaptive entropy coding versus deeper encoders/decoders is unclear; an ablation without adaptive coding is needed.  
- Provide more details on the adaptive coder and its quantitative effects; addressing this could increase the paper’s score.
