This paper presents the most convincing work on image compression using deep neural networks that I have come across so far. The authors have written the paper in a clear and concise manner, seamlessly integrating rate-distortion theory into the framework. Furthermore, the comparison to a robust baseline, such as JPEG2000, is a significant improvement over previous papers that only considered JPEG.

I anticipate that this paper will have a substantial impact in the field. To further strengthen the manuscript, I recommend including results on standard images like Lena, Barbara, and Baboon, in addition to comparing the proposed method to state-of-the-art classical approaches. Providing a clear comparison between neural network-based methods and traditional techniques is essential to understand the positioning of both categories.

Including such comparisons will enable a more comprehensive evaluation of the proposed method's performance and its potential to advance the state-of-the-art in image compression. By doing so, the authors can effectively demonstrate the superiority of their approach and its potential to make a meaningful impact in the field.
