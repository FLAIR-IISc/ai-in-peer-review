This is the most convincing paper on image compression using deep neural networks that I have read so far. The paper is very well written, and the incorporation of rate-distortion theory into the objective function fits seamlessly into the framework. The comparison to a reasonable baseline, such as JPEG2000, is also a welcome change from previous papers that only considered JPEG. I expect this paper to have a significant impact.

I would like to see the inclusion of results on standard test images such as Lena, Barbara, and Baboon, in addition to comparisons with state-of-the-art references that employ more classical methods. It is essential to clearly establish how neural networks compare to the best previous methods. From the submitted version, it is still unclear how these two categories of methods are positioned relative to each other.
