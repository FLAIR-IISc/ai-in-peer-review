- Paper is the most compelling work on image compression with deep nets the reviewer has seen.  
- Exceptionally well-written manuscript.  
- Elegant and seamless integration of rate–distortion theory into the objective.  
- Proper benchmarking against a strong baseline (JPEG2000), an improvement over prior work that only used JPEG.  
- Reviewer expects the paper to have substantial impact on the field.  
- Reviewer requests inclusion of results on standard test images (Lena, Barbara, Baboon).  
- Reviewer asks for explicit comparisons and positioning relative to the strongest classical (non-NN) compression methods to clarify how the approach stacks up.
