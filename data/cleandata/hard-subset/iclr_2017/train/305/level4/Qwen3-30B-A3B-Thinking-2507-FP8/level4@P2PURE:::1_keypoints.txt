- Reviewer considers this the most compelling paper on image compression using deep neural networks they have encountered.
- Manuscript is exceptionally well-written.
- Integration of rate–distortion theory into the objective function is seamless.
- Comparison to a more appropriate baseline (JPEG2000 rather than JPEG-only) is a significant strength.
- Reviewer anticipates the paper will have substantial impact.
- Requests inclusion of results on standard test images (Lena, Barbara, Baboon).
- Requests addition of state-of-the-art references for classical methods.
- Urges explicit positioning of the neural-network approach relative to the best previous methods; current submission does not clearly establish comparative standing.
