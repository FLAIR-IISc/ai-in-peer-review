

This is the most compelling paper on deep neural network-based image compression I have encountered to date. The manuscript is exceptionally well-written, and the integration of rate-distortion theory into the objective function is seamlessly executed within the proposed framework. The inclusion of JPEG2000 as a baseline—rather than relying solely on JPEG, as in prior work—provides a more rigorous and relevant comparison. I anticipate this paper will generate significant impact in the field.  

To strengthen the contribution, please include quantitative results on standard test images (Lena, Barbara, Baboon) and explicitly reference state-of-the-art classical methods (such as the ones discussed in my queries). Crucially, the paper must clearly articulate how the proposed neural network approach compares to the best-performing prior methods, as the current submission does not adequately position the two method categories.
