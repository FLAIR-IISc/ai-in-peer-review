- Extends rate–distortion optimization to deep encoders/decoders and adaptive entropy coding, with claimed VAE connections, but novelty is limited given prior RD work—clarify any overlooked innovation.  
- Regresses to using MSE as the distortion metric instead of perceptual metrics, which is a surprising step back given the authors' background—justify this choice.  
- Evaluation is limited to PSNR; include perceptual metrics (e.g., LPIPS, VMAF) to validate real-world utility.  
- Results clearly improve over JPEG 2000 and may be the first learned codec to reach this performance level—positive contribution.  
- Equation 10 appears incorrect: the partition function should depend on g_s(y; θ), not g_s(y); correct/clarify since this affects the claimed VAE reduction under non‑Euclidean metrics.  
- Unclear whether gains come from adaptive entropy coding or deeper networks—the paper lacks ablations comparing adaptive vs non‑adaptive coding and varying network depth.  
- Required fixes: (1) correct Eq.10 and clarify VAE relationship, (2) justify MSE and report perceptual metrics, (3) provide ablation studies isolating adaptive coding’s contribution.
