- Extends rate-distortion optimization to deep encoders/decoders and moves from basic entropy encoding to adaptive entropy coding; also examines relation to VAEs.  
- Core rate-distortion optimization method appears previously published; paper’s novelty is limited.  
- In some respects regressive: prior work optimized perceptual metrics, but this paper optimizes MSE.  
- Demonstrates clear empirical improvement over JPEG 2000 and may be the best-performing learned compression method to date.  
- Paper is exceptionally well written.  
- Equation 10 seems incorrect: the partition function should depend on g_s(y; theta), so the method is not equivalent to a VAE for non-Euclidean metrics.  
- Why optimize MSE/PSNR instead of perceptual metrics as in prior work? evaluation choice is surprising given authors’ expertise.  
- Unclear contribution split between adaptive entropy coding and deeper encoder/decoder networks; request results without adaptation and more analysis of the adaptive coder’s effect before increasing the score.
