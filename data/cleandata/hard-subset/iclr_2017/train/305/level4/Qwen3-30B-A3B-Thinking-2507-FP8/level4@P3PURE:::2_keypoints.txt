- Extends rate-distortion optimization to deep encoders/decoders and moves from basic entropy encoding to adaptive entropy coding; relates work to VAEs.  
- Novelty seems limited because the core rate-distortion optimization has been previously published; please clarify any overlooked innovation.  
- The paper regresses from prior perceptual-metric optimization to using MSE as the distortion measure; rationale for this choice is unclear.  
- Results show a significant improvement over JPEG 2000 and may be unmatched among learned image codecs to date.  
- Paper is well-written.  
- Equation 10 appears incorrect: the partition function should depend on g_s(y; θ), which would invalidate the claimed VAE equivalence under non-Euclidean metrics.  
- Evaluation is restricted to PSNR without any perceptual metrics, which is notable given the shift away from perceptual objectives.  
- It is unclear how much of the performance gain comes from adaptive entropy coding versus deeper network architectures.  
- Authors should provide ablation/performance comparisons without adaptive coding (as in prior work) to isolate the adaptive coder’s impact.  
- A more detailed analysis of the adaptive coder’s contribution is essential; reviewer may raise the score if this clarification is provided.
