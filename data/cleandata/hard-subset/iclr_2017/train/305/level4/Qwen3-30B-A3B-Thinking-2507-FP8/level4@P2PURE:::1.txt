

This is the most compelling paper on image compression using deep neural networks that I have encountered. The manuscript is exceptionally well-written, and the integration of rate-distortion theory into the objective function is seamless. The comparison to a more appropriate baseline (JPEG2000, rather than the JPEG-only comparisons in prior work) is a significant strength. I anticipate this paper will have a substantial impact.  
I request the inclusion of results on standard test images (Lena, Barbara, Baboon) along with state-of-the-art references for classical methods, as discussed in my queries. It is crucial to explicitly position the neural network approach relative to the best previous methods; the current submission does not clearly establish this comparative standing.
