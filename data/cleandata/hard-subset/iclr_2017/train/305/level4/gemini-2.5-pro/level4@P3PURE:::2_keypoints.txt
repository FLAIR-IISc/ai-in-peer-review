1. Extends rate-distortion optimization to deep encoders/decoders and adds adaptive entropy coding.  
2. Discusses relationship between the approach and variational autoencoders.  
3. Paper is well-written with strong empirical results.  
4. Model visibly outperforms JPEG 2000 and may be the best learned image compression to date.  
5. Novelty is limited because the core rate-distortion optimization has been published previously.  
6. Optimizing for MSE (PSNR) rather than a perceptual metric is surprising and may be a step backward.  
7. Equation 10 appears incorrect—partition function likely depends on g_s(y; theta), which could invalidate the VAE equivalence for non-Euclidean metrics.  
8. Request rationale for choosing MSE/PSNR over perceptual metrics.  
9. Ask for an ablation study to separate the effects of deeper architectures versus adaptive entropy coding and for more details on the adaptive coder; reviewer may raise score if provided.
