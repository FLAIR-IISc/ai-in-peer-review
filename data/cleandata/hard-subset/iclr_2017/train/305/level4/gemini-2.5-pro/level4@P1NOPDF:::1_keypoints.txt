- Highly convincing and exceptionally well-written paper on image compression with deep neural networks.  
- Elegant, natural integration of rate-distortion theory into the objective function.  
- Key strength: comparison against a strong baseline (JPEG2000) rather than the weaker JPEG.  
- Reviewer expects the paper to have significant impact on the field.  
- Recommendation: include results on standard benchmark images (e.g., Lena, Barbara, Baboon).  
- Recommendation: compare against state-of-the-art classical compression algorithms referenced by the reviewer.  
- Need to clarify how neural network–based approaches are positioned relative to the best traditional methods.  
- Clarifying the performance gap between neural and classical methods would substantially improve the paper's contribution.
