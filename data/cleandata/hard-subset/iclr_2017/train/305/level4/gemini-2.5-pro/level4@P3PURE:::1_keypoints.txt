- Paper is highly convincing on image compression using deep neural networks.
- Exceptionally well-written manuscript.
- Elegant integration of rate-distortion theory into the objective function.
- Strong empirical choice of baseline (comparison against JPEG2000) improves over prior work that only used JPEG.
- Reviewer expects the paper to have significant impact on the field.
- Major recommendation: expand the experimental evaluation.
- Recommendation: include results on standard benchmark images (e.g., Lena, Barbara, Baboon).
- Recommendation: provide comparisons against state-of-the-art classical compression methods.
- Concern: current submission does not clearly establish performance relative to the best traditional methods; such comparisons are crucial.
