This paper presents the most convincing approach to image compression with deep neural networks that I have read to date. The manuscript is very well-written, and the integration of rate-distortion theory into the objective function is seamless and effective. The authors are commended for comparing against a strong and relevant baseline (JPEG2000), which is a significant improvement over previous work that only considered JPEG. I expect this paper will have a substantial impact on the field.

To further strengthen the evaluation, I recommend including results on standard benchmark images such as Lena, Barbara, and Baboon. Additionally, a comparison with state-of-the-art classical methods, like the one mentioned in my questions, is necessary. It is crucial to clearly establish how neural network-based approaches compare to the best traditional methods. Based on the current submission, the relative performance and positioning of these two categories of methods remain unclear.
