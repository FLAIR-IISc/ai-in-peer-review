- Reviewer finds this the most compelling work on image compression with deep networks they've seen and expects significant impact.  
- Manuscript is exceptionally well-written.  
- Use of rate-distortion theory in the objective is elegant and well-integrated.  
- Evaluation is strengthened by using JPEG2000 as a baseline rather than only JPEG.  
- Recommends adding results on standard benchmark images (e.g., Lena, Barbara, Baboon).  
- Requests inclusion of references to state-of-the-art classical methods for comparison.  
- Asks the authors to explicitly articulate how neural-network approaches compare to leading traditional algorithms, since the current version lacks clear relative positioning.
