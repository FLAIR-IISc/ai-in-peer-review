- Presents a convincing, well-executed deep neural network approach to image compression and constitutes a significant contribution to the field.
- Manuscript is exceptionally well-written and clear.
- Principled integration of rate–distortion theory into the network objective provides a solid theoretical foundation missing from prior work.
- Empirical evaluation is strengthened by using JPEG2000 as a competitive, realistic baseline instead of the simpler JPEG.
- Experimental evaluation should include results on standard benchmark images (e.g., Lena, Barbara, Baboon) for completeness.
- Must compare against state-of-the-art classical codecs (potentially outperforming JPEG2000) to clarify how the proposed learned method stacks up against the best traditional compression techniques.
