- Paper is the most compelling work on image compression using deep neural networks the reviewer has seen.
- Exceptionally well-written and the incorporation of rate–distortion theory into the objective integrates seamlessly.
- Evaluation uses a strong baseline (JPEG2000 rather than just JPEG), which strengthens the comparison.
- Reviewer expects the paper will have significant impact.
- Strong recommendation to include results on standard benchmark images (Lena, Barbara, Baboon).
- Recommend comparisons to state-of-the-art classical methods to contextualize performance.
- Current manuscript lacks a clear positioning of neural-network-based methods relative to the best traditional approaches.
