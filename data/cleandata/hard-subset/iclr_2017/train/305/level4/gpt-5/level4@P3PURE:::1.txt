This is the most convincing paper on image compression with deep neural networks that I have read so far. The paper is very well written, and the use of rate–distortion theory in the objective integrates smoothly into the framework. The comparison against a reasonable baseline (JPEG2000, rather than only JPEG as in prior work) is appropriate. I would expect this paper to have strong impact.

Please include results on the standard test images Lena, Barbara, and Baboon (not Gibbons), along with state-of-the-art references to more classical methods, including the one I mentioned in my questions. It is important to clearly state how neural-network-based approaches compare to the best previous classical methods. In the submitted version, the relative positioning of these two categories remains unclear.

With these additions, the paper would be even stronger.
