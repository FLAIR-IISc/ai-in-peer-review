- Paper extends prior rate–distortion optimization to deep encoders/decoders and replaces simple entropy coding with adaptive entropy coding; discusses VAE connections.
- Novelty appears limited because the underlying rate–distortion approach has already been published.
- In some respects the paper is a step backward by optimizing MSE rather than the perceptual metric used in prior work.
- Results show clear improvement over JPEG 2000 and strong empirical performance relative to other learned codecs.
- Paper is very well written.
- Equation 10 seems incorrect: the partition function should depend on g_s(y; θ), so the approach may not be equivalent to a VAE for non‑Euclidean distortions.
- Why optimize MSE and evaluate only with PSNR instead of using perceptual metrics as in earlier work?
- Unclear contribution of adaptive entropy coding versus deeper encoders/decoders; request results without adaptation and more details on the adaptive coder and its impact.
