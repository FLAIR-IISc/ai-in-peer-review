This is the most convincing paper on deep-neural-network-based image compression I have read to date. It is very well written, and the use of rate–distortion theory in the objective function fits naturally within the framework. The comparison to a reasonable baseline—JPEG2000, rather than just JPEG as in many previous papers—is appropriate. I expect this work to have strong impact.

Please also include results on the standard test images Lena, Barbara, and Baboon (not “Gibbons”), along with state-of-the-art references for more classical (non-neural) methods such as the one I mentioned in my questions. It is important to state clearly how neural methods compare to the best prior approaches. In the current submission, I still do not have a clear sense of how these two categories of methods are positioned relative to each other.
