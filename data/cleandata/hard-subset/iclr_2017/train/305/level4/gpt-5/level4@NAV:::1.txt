This is the strongest paper on deep neural network–based image compression that I have encountered to date. The manuscript is very well written, and the incorporation of rate–distortion theory into the objective integrates seamlessly with the overall framework. The comparison against a reasonable baseline (JPEG2000, unlike prior work that often only considers JPEG) is appropriate. I expect this work to have substantial impact.

Please include results on Lena/Barbara/Baboon (not Gibbons), along with state-of-the-art references for more classical methods such as the one I mentioned in my questions. It is important to clearly articulate how neural networks compare to the best prior methods. From the submitted version, I still cannot tell how the two categories of methods are positioned relative to each other.
