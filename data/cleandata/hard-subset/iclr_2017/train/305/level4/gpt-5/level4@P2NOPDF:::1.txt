This is the strongest deep-learning paper on image compression I have seen to date. The manuscript is clearly written, and the integration of rate–distortion theory into the objective is natural and well motivated. The choice of baseline is appropriate—comparing against JPEG2000 rather than only JPEG provides a more meaningful evaluation. I expect this work to have significant impact.

Please include results on standard test images—Lena, Barbara, and Baboon (not Gibbons)—along with citations and comparisons to state-of-the-art classical methods such as those referenced in my questions. It is important to state clearly how neural network–based approaches compare with the best traditional techniques. In the current submission, the relative positioning of these two categories of methods is still unclear.
