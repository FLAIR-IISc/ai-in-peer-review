This is the most convincing paper on deep neural network–based image compression I have read to date. It is very well written, and the incorporation of rate–distortion theory into the objective is natural and well integrated into the framework. The comparisons use a reasonable baseline—JPEG2000—rather than only JPEG, as in many prior works. I expect this paper to have strong impact.

Please also include results on the standard test images Lena, Barbara, and Baboon (not Gibbons), along with state-of-the-art references for more classical methods such as the one I mentioned in my questions. It is important to clearly state how neural networks compare to the best previous methods. From the submitted version, it remains unclear how these two categories of methods are positioned relative to each other.
