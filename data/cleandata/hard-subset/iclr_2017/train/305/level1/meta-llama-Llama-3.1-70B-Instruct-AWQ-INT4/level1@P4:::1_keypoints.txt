- Proposes end-to-end optimized image compression using nonlinear analysis transform, uniform quantizer, and nonlinear synthesis transform.  
- Transforms built from three successive stages of convolutional linear filters and nonlinear activations inspired by biological neurons.  
- Optimizes entire model for rate–distortion using a variant of stochastic gradient descent.  
- Introduces a continuous proxy for the quantizer’s discontinuous loss, interpretable as a generative-model log-likelihood.  
- Demonstrates better rate–distortion performance and substantially improved visual quality versus JPEG and JPEG 2000 across bitrates.
