- Summary: Proposes amortised MAP inference for image super-resolution using an affine-projected CNN and three approximations (AffGAN, AffDG, AffLL), linking AffGAN to variational inference and showing competitive results.  
- Decision: Reject — interesting theory and promising AffGAN results, but empirical validation is incomplete and not convincingly superior to existing GAN-based SR.  

Supporting arguments:
- Limited comparative analysis: comparisons are primarily to MSE/MAE and lack strong GAN baselines and perceptual metrics.  
- Inconsistent performance: AffGAN shows wins on some textures but other methods fail without adequate analysis; ImageNet results exhibit unresolved high-frequency noise.  
- Theory vs. practical impact: the MAP/variational theory is not clearly connected to why affine projection yields observed perceptual improvements.  
- Implementation & reproducibility: complex implementation with many hyperparameters and insufficient provided details for replication.  

Additional feedback:
- More rigorous evaluation: compare against SOTA GAN SR (e.g., ESRGAN, Real-ESRGAN) with PSNR/SSIM/LPIPS/NIQE and user studies.  
- Ablation studies: quantify the effect of affine projection alone on performance.  
- Address high-frequency noise: investigate fixes (e.g., spectral normalization or alternative GAN training strategies).  
- Expand on failure modes: analyze why DAE and density-guided approaches underperform.  
- Clarify theoretical link to performance: explain how affine projection improves posterior exploration and perceptual quality.  
- Reproducibility: release code or detailed hyperparameter settings.  

Questions for the authors:
- Can you provide detailed comparisons of AffGAN to SOTA GAN-based SR methods including LPIPS and NIQE?  
- What modifications did you try to mitigate the ImageNet high-frequency noise, and why did they fail?  
- How exactly does the affine projection facilitate better posterior exploration compared to standard GAN training?  
- What is the computational cost and trade-off of adding the affine projection layer?  
- Can you expand on the stochastic AffGAN–variational inference connection and its potential benefits?
