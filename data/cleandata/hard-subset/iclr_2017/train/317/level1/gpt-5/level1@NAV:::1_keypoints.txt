- Proposes amortised MAP inference for single-image SR and introduces an affine projection layer to guarantee LR-HR consistency.  
- Recasts amortised MAP as matching the generator’s marginal output distribution to the HR data distribution via cross-entropy minimisation.  
- Proposes three optimisation routes: AffGAN (with instance-noise stabilization), AffDG (denoiser-guided using score estimates), and AffLL (density-guided with a continuous PixelCNN-like prior).  
- Provides theoretical links to KL minimisation and a stochastic variant connecting to amortised variational inference.  
- Empirically shows AffGAN produces sharper, more plausible images while enforcing LR consistency.  
- Decision: Reject.  
- Primary reason 1: Insufficient empirical validation against strong baselines and lack of standardized perceptual evaluation.  
- Primary reason 2: Limited evidence that amortised MAP improves SR under realistic degradations and benchmarks.  
- Evaluation relies mainly on qualitative results, PSNR/SSIM, and a 2D toy cross-entropy estimate; missing LPIPS, FID, DISTS, human study, and standard perceptual benchmarks.  
- No direct comparisons with SRGAN/ESRGAN or regression+perceptual-loss methods on standard SR datasets (Set5/14, BSD100, DIV2K).  
- Degradation model A is a learned Gaussian-stride convolution pseudo-inverse; generalization to common bicubic or real/unknown degradations is not demonstrated.  
- AffDG and AffLL underperform relative to AffGAN and are deprioritised, weakening the general “amortised MAP” claim.  
- AffGAN produces artifacts (high-frequency noise) and has at least one clear semantic failure (snake→water), indicating weaker priors or failure modes.  
- Suggested improvements: add LPIPS/FID/KID/DISTS and a small human study, and compare to SRGAN/ESRGAN and perceptual-loss baselines on standard datasets with bicubic downsampling.  
- Suggested robustness tests: mismatched/unknown A, blind SR, and multiple scales (2×/3×/4×).  
- Recommend ablations on the affine projection (fixed vs trainable A+), instance-noise schedule, and discriminator capacity to quantify stability gains.  
- Suggest strengthening AffDG/LL with modern score/density models (score-based diffusion, autoregressive/flow priors) and curricula.  
- Request reporting of computational cost, training dynamics, and release of code.  
- Questions for authors: sensitivity to choice and inaccuracies of A and A+; ability to handle unknown or spatially varying degradations (blind SR).  
- Questions for authors: please report LPIPS/FID on DIV2K with bicubic downsampling and comparisons to modern baselines.  
- Questions for authors: effect of instance-noise magnitude/annealing on stability and perceptual metrics?  
- Questions for authors: can you quantify H[qθ] or output diversity to support the KL vs cross-entropy discussion?  
- Questions for authors: could modern score/density estimators fix AffDG/LL blurriness and instability?
