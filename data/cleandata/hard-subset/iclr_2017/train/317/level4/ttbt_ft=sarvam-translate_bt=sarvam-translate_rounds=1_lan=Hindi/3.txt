This paper presents an improved MAP estimation method for SR problems. By learning a neural network that learns to project into an affine subspace of SR solutions that are consistent with the LR method, this method enables the finding of optimal solutions using various methods: GANs, noise-assisted, and density-assisted optimization.

The results have been well-demonstrated on several datasets.

Overall, I liked the paper, although I think the writing could be improved a bit and the presentation should be clarified. It was sometimes difficult to understand, and clarifying it further would help given the subject matter, as it is quite complex. Also, I would like to see some more analysis of the resulting network – what kind of features do they learn?
