- Introduces a projection step to ensure consistent HR–LR dependencies, experimentally validated to improve results.
- Proposes and evaluates three distinct methods for solving the cross-entropy problem in Eq. 9.
- Paper is excellent, well-written, and well-presented.
- Experimental results are adequate, including thoughtfully chosen toy examples and real-world applications.

- Expects the work to have significant impact on future super-resolution research.
