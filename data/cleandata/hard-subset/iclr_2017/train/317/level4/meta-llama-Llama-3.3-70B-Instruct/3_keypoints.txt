- Proposes amortised MAP estimation for super-resolution by training a network to project onto an affine subspace of solutions consistent with the low-resolution input.  
- Leverages multiple optimisation strategies (GANs, noise-assisted, density-assisted methods) to obtain valid SR solutions.  
- Experimental results are well-demonstrated across multiple datasets.  
- Reviewer finds the paper interesting and relevant.  
- Writing and presentation need significant improvement for clearer exposition.  
- Some sections are difficult to follow due to the topic’s complexity; enhanced clarity would help understanding.  
- Requests deeper analysis of the learned networks (e.g., what features they acquire) to better assess the method’s efficacy.
