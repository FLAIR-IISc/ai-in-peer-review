

The paper introduces an amortized MAP estimation approach for super-resolution (SR) problems. It proposes a neural network that projects inputs onto an affine subspace of SR solutions consistent with the low-resolution observation, enabling the discovery of proper solutions through multiple optimization strategies: GANs, noise-assisted, and density-assisted optimization. Results are effectively demonstrated across several datasets. Overall, I find the paper compelling, though the writing requires significant polishing to enhance clarity. The presentation occasionally becomes difficult to follow, particularly given the complexity of the subject matter; improving the clarity of the exposition would substantially benefit readers. Additionally, I would appreciate more detailed analysis of the learned networks—specifically, what features they encode.
