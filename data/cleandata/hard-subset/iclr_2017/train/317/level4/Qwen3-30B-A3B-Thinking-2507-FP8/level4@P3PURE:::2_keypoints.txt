- Proposes framing super-resolution as amortized MAP estimation for improved HR reconstruction.
- Introduces a projection step to maintain consistent high-resolution to low-resolution dependencies.
- Empirically validates the projection-based approach, showing superior performance on tasks.
- Presents and evaluates three distinct methods to address the cross-entropy problem in Equation 9.
- Paper is exceptionally well-written, clearly presented, and requires minor proofreading.
- Experiments are comprehensive, using well-chosen toy examples and real-world applications.
- Contributions in Sections 3.2–3.4 represent novel advances for super-resolution.
- Notes some GAN training aspects may overlap with prior work, but overall approach and findings are significant.
- Observes GAN-based training produces the most visually appealing results and will likely inspire future research.
