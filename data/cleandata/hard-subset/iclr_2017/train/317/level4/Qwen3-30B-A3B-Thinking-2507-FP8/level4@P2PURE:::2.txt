

Apologies for the delayed review.  
This paper proposes treating super-resolution as amortized MAP estimation, introducing a projection step to maintain consistent high-resolution/low-resolution dependencies, which is experimentally verified to achieve superior results. Additionally, three distinct methods for solving the cross-entropy problem in Equation 9 are proposed and evaluated.  
Summary: This is a strong paper, well-written and clearly presented. The experimental results are comprehensive, featuring well-chosen toy examples and real-world applications. The contributions to super-resolution (Sections 3.2, 3.3, 3.4) are novel; however, aspects specific to GAN training may have appeared in prior variants (see discussion). The paper will likely influence future super-resolution work, particularly the finding that GAN-based training yields the most visually appealing results, suggesting promising avenues for further research.  
The manuscript should undergo one final proofreading to address minor typographical errors.
