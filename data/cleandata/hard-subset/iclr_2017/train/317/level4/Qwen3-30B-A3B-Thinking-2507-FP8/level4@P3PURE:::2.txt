

This paper proposes framing super-resolution as amortized MAP estimation, introducing a projection step to maintain consistent HR-LR dependencies and empirically validating its efficacy for superior performance. The authors further present and evaluate three distinct approaches to solve the cross-entropy problem formulated in Equation 9.  

Summary: This is an excellent paper, exceptionally well-written and clearly presented. The experimental results are comprehensive, supported by well-chosen toy examples and real-world applications. The contributions to super-resolution (specifically Sections 3.2, 3.3, and 3.4) represent novel advances for the field. While certain aspects of GAN training described in the paper may have appeared in prior work (see also the discussion), the overall approach and findings are significant. The observation that GAN-based model training yields the most visually appealing results will likely inspire future research in super-resolution.  

The manuscript would benefit from one final proofreading pass to address minor typographical errors.
