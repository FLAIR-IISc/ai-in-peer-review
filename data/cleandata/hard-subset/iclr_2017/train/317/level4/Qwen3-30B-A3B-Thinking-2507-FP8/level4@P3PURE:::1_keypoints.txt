1) Unclear novelty relative to state-of-the-art GAN-based SR (e.g., SRGAN); paper should explicitly state how the amortized MAP formulation advances prior GAN approaches.  
2) Affine projection layer is claimed to remove need for paired {HR,LR} data, but its training requires such pairs—clarify whether this is a true reduction in supervision or just a reparameterization.  
3) Missing evaluations on standard SR benchmarks (Set5, Set14, BSD100); include these for fair comparison to prior work.  
4) Experiments only on 128×128 images; demonstrate performance on larger and arbitrary input sizes to validate scalability.  
5) Lack of exploration of latent noise in GAN latent space; report whether a noise vector was tried and its effect on output distribution and performance.  
6) Overall: strong theoretical basis and methodology, but needs clarifications and additional experiments to substantiate novelty and practical claims.
