- Proposes a novel SR framework: amortized MAP inference with a pre-trained affine projection layer and three cross-entropy minimization methods — strong contribution.  
- Needs clarification on how this formulation improves over the latest SOTA, specifically SRGAN (Photo-Realistic Single Image Super-Resolution Using a Generative Adversarial Network).  
- Affine projection claims to remove paired {HR,LR} training, but its own training requires pairs — does this merely transfer the paired-data requirement rather than eliminate it?  
- Lacks evaluations on standard SR benchmarks (Set5, Set14, BSD100) for fair comparison with prior work.  
- Experiments are limited to 128×128 images; need tests on larger/arbitrary resolutions to demonstrate scalability.  
- Did the authors explore using a latent noise vector in the GAN to better model output distributions?  
- Overall: solid theoretical grounding and novelty, but answering these questions and adding experiments would substantially strengthen the paper.
