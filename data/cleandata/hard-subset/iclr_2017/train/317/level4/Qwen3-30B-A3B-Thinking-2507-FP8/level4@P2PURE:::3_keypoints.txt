- Introduces an amortized MAP estimation approach for super-resolution (SR).
- Proposes a neural network that projects inputs onto an affine subspace of SR solutions consistent with the low-resolution observation.
- Enables solution discovery through multiple optimization strategies: GAN-based, noise-assisted, and density-assisted optimization.
- Demonstrates effective results across several datasets.
- Overall paper is compelling.
- Writing requires significant polishing; exposition is sometimes difficult to follow given the complexity.
- Reviewer requests more detailed analysis of the learned networks, specifically what features they encode.
