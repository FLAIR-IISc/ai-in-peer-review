This paper proposes treating Super-Resolution as amortized MAP estimation, introducing a projection step to maintain consistent HR-LR dependencies, which is experimentally validated to improve performance. Additionally, three distinct approaches for solving the cross-entropy problem in Equation 9 are proposed and evaluated.  
Summary: Excellent paper, exceptionally well-written and presented. Experimental results are comprehensive, featuring well-chosen simplified examples and real-world applications. The contributions to Super-Resolution (Sections 3.2–3.4) are novel; however, aspects specific to GAN training may have appeared in prior variants (as noted in the discussion). This work will be highly relevant to future Super-Resolution research, particularly the finding that GAN-based training yields visually superior results, suggesting promising avenues for further exploration.
