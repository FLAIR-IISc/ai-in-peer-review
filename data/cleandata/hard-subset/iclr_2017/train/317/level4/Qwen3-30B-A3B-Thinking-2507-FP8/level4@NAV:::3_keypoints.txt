- Introduces an amortized MAP estimation approach for super-resolution problems.  
- Trains a neural network to project onto an affine subspace of SR solutions consistent with the low-resolution observation model.  
- Enables efficient solution recovery using diverse optimization strategies (GANs, noise-assisted, density-assisted).  
- Empirical results are convincing and demonstrated across multiple datasets.  
- Overall assessment: paper is strong.  
- Writing requires significant polishing; presentation is sometimes difficult to follow due to technical complexity.  
- Recommends a deeper analysis of the features learned by the networks.
