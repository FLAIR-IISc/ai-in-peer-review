- Introduces an amortized MAP inference framework with an affine projection layer for SR and three methods to minimize cross-entropy; well-motivated and theoretically solid.  
- Unclear specific contribution relative to state-of-the-art SRGAN—needs explicit comparison.  
- Affine projection allows training without paired HR–LR at the framework level, but the layer itself requires paired training, so the paired-data requirement may only be relocated.  
- Results omit standard SR benchmarks (Set5, Set14, BSD100), hindering fair comparisons with prior work.  
- Experiments are limited to 128×128 images, raising questions about scalability to larger, arbitrary-sized inputs.  
- Should clarify whether and how a noise vector (as in standard GANs) was considered to model output variability.  
- Overall: novel and theoretically rigorous, but necessity of approach and experimental validation need clarification and additional experiments.
