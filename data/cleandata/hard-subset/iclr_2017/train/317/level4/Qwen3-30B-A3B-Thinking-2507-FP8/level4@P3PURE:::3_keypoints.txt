- Proposes an amortized MAP estimation method for super-resolution using a neural network to project onto the affine subspace of solutions consistent with the low-resolution model.
- Enables finding diverse plausible SR solutions via multiple optimization strategies (GANs, noise-assisted, density-assisted).
- Empirical results are convincingly demonstrated across several datasets.
- Technical contribution is promising and relevant to SR research.
- Manuscript requires significant polishing to improve clarity and readability.
- Exposition is sometimes hard to follow given methodological complexity; a more structured presentation is needed.
- Paper lacks deeper analysis of learned network features to explain what characteristics the model exploits for SR performance.
