

The paper introduces an amortized MAP estimation approach for super-resolution (SR) problems. By training a neural network to project onto an affine subspace of SR solutions consistent with the low-resolution (LR) observation model, the method enables efficient solution recovery through diverse optimization strategies, including GANs, noise-assisted, and density-assisted techniques. Results are convincingly demonstrated across multiple datasets. Overall, I find the paper strong, though the writing requires significant polishing to enhance clarity. The presentation occasionally becomes difficult to follow given the technical complexity, and improving this would greatly benefit readers. Additionally, a deeper analysis of the features learned by the networks would be valuable.
