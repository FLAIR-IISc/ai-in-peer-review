- Proposes an amortized MAP estimation approach for single-image super-resolution.  
- Trains a network to map low-resolution inputs into an affine subspace of solutions consistent with traditional SR methods.  
- Core idea is promising and enables diverse optimization techniques (GAN-based, noise-assisted, density-assisted).  
- Experimental results are strong and demonstrated across multiple datasets.  
- Overall a strong contribution to the field.  
- Manuscript needs significant improvements in clarity and polish; several sections are difficult to follow.  
- Additional analysis of learned network representations (investigation of features learned) is recommended.
