- Novelty: Proposes amortized MAP inference for single-image SR with an affine projection layer and three cross-entropy minimization methods—overall an innovative approach.

- Clarify advantage: Unclear what the new formulation adds over existing GAN-based SR methods (e.g., SRGAN); authors should clearly articulate benefits over prior GAN approaches.

- Affine projection training: Though the affine projection lets model training avoid paired HR/LR, its own training requires paired images—clarify whether this simply replicates existing HR/LR training procedures.

- Benchmarking: Request results on standard SR datasets (Set5, Set14, BSD100) to enable fair comparison with prior work.

- Scalability: Results are shown only on 128×128 images; need evidence the framework handles larger or arbitrary-sized inputs common in SR tasks.

- Latent noise: Did not explore/use a noise/latent vector input to the generator; adding one could improve diversity and realism and should be investigated.

- Overall assessment: Strong, theoretically solid and promising work that warrants further clarification and additional experiments to demonstrate necessity and practical advantages.
