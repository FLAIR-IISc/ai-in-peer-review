- Proposes an amortized MAP estimation method for super-resolution by learning a neural network that projects to an affine subspace of HR solutions consistent with an LR input.  
- Framework allows discovering plausible SR solutions using GAN-based, noise-assisted, and density-assisted optimization strategies.  
- Results are convincingly demonstrated across several datasets.  
- Paper is promising but requires significant polishing and clearer presentation; portions are difficult to follow.  
- Lacks deeper analysis of the learned network representations—needs investigation into what features the network encodes.
