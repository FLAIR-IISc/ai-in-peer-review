- The amortized MAP + GAN formulation yields compelling results but the specific advantages over recent GAN-based SR methods (e.g., SRGAN) are unclear and need clarification.

- The affine projection lets the model be trained without paired HR–LR data overall, but the affine projection layer itself still requires paired examples—does this merely transfer the paired-data requirement to that layer?

- Results on ImageNet are shown, but the paper should also report performance on standard SR benchmarks (Set5, Set14, BSD100) for fair comparison.

- Experiments are limited to 128×128 images; the authors should demonstrate the framework’s effectiveness on larger or arbitrary-sized inputs.

- Typical GANs use a noise vector to model a latent distribution—did the authors explore adding a noise/latent vector to their framework?
