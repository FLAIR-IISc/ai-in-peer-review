- Introduces amortized MAP inference for SR with an affine projection layer and three cross-entropy minimization methods; overall a strong contribution.
- Need clearer articulation of specific advantages and novel contributions relative to prior GAN-based SR methods (e.g., SRGAN) and concrete improvements achieved.
- Clarify whether affine projection layer training requires HR–LR pairs and whether this is effectively a transfer-learning step prior to unsupervised SR training.
- Recommend reporting quantitative results on standard SR benchmarks (Set5, Set14, BSD100) for fair comparison with prior work.
- Current experiments use 128×128 images only; evaluate scalability to larger, arbitrary-size images and discuss computational limitations.
- Ask whether a noise latent vector was considered to increase diversity and realism of generated HR images.
- Overall: theoretically sound and promising, but needs clarification on the affine projection procedure and a more thorough experimental evaluation.
