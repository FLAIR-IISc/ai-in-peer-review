- Technical novelty: Introduces an affine projection step to enforce LR–HR consistency.
- Optimization methods: Explores GAN-, denoising-, and density-based approaches to minimize the cross-entropy objective.

- Theoretical link: Establishes a connection between AffGAN and amortized variational inference.
- Illustrative experiments: 2D Swiss-roll example clearly demonstrates the benefits of MAP inference.
- Empirical evaluation: Comprehensive tests on texture and real-world datasets with informative ablation studies (e.g., AffGAN vs SoftGAN).
