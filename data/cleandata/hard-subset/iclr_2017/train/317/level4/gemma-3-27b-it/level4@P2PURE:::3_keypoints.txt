- Introduces an amortized MAP estimation method for single-image super-resolution that learns a network projecting SR solutions onto an affine subspace to enforce LR consistency.  
- The projection framework permits use of varied optimization strategies (GAN-based, noise-assisted, density-assisted) to recover plausible high-resolution outputs.  
- Demonstrates compelling empirical results across multiple datasets.  
- Overall the approach is promising and conceptually interesting.  
- Writing and presentation need significant polishing; parts of the paper are hard to follow.  
- Greater clarity and exposition are needed given the topic’s complexity.  
- Requests additional analysis of the learned network representations, specifically what features the network captures.
