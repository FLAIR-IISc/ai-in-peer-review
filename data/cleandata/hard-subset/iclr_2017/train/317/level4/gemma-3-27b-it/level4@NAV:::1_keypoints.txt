- Novel amortized MAP inference integrated with a GAN yields compelling SR results; authors should explicitly state advantages over existing GAN-based SR methods.  
- The affine projection enforces LR consistency and enables unpaired training, but its own training requires {HR,LR} pairs—clarify if this simply reuses standard paired training.  
- Recommend reporting results on standard SR benchmarks (Set5, Set14, BSD100) for direct comparison with prior work.  
- Current experiments limited to 128×128 images; evaluate on larger, arbitrarily sized images to reflect practical SR use.  
- Clarify whether a noise vector/latent space (as in traditional GANs) was included or explored and how it affects the learned distribution.  
- Overall: novel and theoretically sound with strong exploration, but needs clarification of components and more extensive experiments.
