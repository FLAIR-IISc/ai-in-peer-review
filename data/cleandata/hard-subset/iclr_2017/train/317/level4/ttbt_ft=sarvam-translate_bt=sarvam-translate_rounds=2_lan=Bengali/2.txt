This paper argues for considering super-resolution as amortized MAP. A projection step is proposed to maintain consistent HR-LR dependence, and it has yielded better results experimentally. Consequently, three different methods have been proposed and tested to solve the cross-entropy problem in equation 9.

Summary: Very good paper, very well written and presented. The experimental results are sufficient, and the paper presents well-chosen examples and real-world applications. In my opinion, the contributions in the field of super-resolution are new (3.2, 3.3, 3.4), and some specific parts for training the GAN may appear in different forms elsewhere (see also the discussion). I believe this paper will be relevant for future work related to super-resolution. The finding that GAN-based model training yields the most attractive results suggests further work in this area.

The manuscript should be proof-read once more; there were some minor typos that could be corrected.
