This paper presents an amortized MAP estimation method for SR problems. By learning a neural network that learns to project into an affinity subspace of SR solutions, which is consistent with the LR method, this method enables finding the correct solution using various methods: such as GAN, noise-assisted, and density-assisted optimization.

The results are nicely illustrated in several datasets.

Overall, I liked the paper, although I think the writing quality could be improved and the presentation should be clearer. It was sometimes difficult to follow, and clarifying the content would be helpful because it is quite complex. Also, I would like to see some more analysis of the networks' results – what kind of features they learn.
