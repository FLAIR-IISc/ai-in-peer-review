This paper argues for viewing super-resolution as a motor-driven MAP estimation. A projection step is proposed to maintain consistent HR-LR dependencies and has been experimentally verified to achieve better results. Subsequently, three different methods are proposed and tested to solve the resulting cross-entropy problem in equation 9.

Summary: Very good paper, very well written and presented. The experimental results are sufficient, the paper presents well-chosen toy examples and real-world applications. In my understanding, the contributions in the field of super-resolution are new (3.2, 3.3, 3.4), the specific part for training GANs may appear elsewhere in various forms (see also discussion). I believe this paper will be relevant for future work on super-resolution, the finding that GAN-based model training gives the most attractive results, suggests further work in this domain.

The manuscript should be proof-read once again, there were some very minor typos that could have been fixed.
