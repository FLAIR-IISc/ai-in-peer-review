- Proposes an amortized MAP estimation method for super-resolution.
- Trains a neural network to project onto an affine subspace of SR solutions consistent with the low-resolution input.
- Framework supports producing high-quality solutions via GAN-based, noise-assisted, and density-assisted optimization strategies.
- Empirical results demonstrate efficacy across several datasets.
- Reviewer considers the work a valuable contribution.
- Writing and presentation are unclear at times and need substantial improvement for readability.
- Recommends deeper analysis of the learned networks, e.g., what features the projection network captures.
