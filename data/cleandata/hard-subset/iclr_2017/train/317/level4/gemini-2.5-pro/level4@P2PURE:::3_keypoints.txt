- Proposes an amortized MAP estimation method for super-resolution using a network that projects inputs onto an affine subspace of high-resolution solutions consistent with the low-resolution image.  
- Projection enables finding high-quality solutions via GANs, noise-assisted methods, and density-assisted optimization.  
- Empirical results are demonstrated effectively across several datasets.  
- Overall assessment: promising contribution.  
- Primary weakness: presentation and writing need significant polishing for clarity.  
- Paper is sometimes hard to follow due to the complexity of the topic; clearer exposition is needed.  
- Lacks analysis of the learned networks; should analyze what features the networks learn to represent the solution subspace.
