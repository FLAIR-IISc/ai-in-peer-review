Proposes an amortized MAP estimation method for super-resolution (SR) problems.

Core approach: neural network trained to project solutions onto an affine subspace of high-resolution images consistent with a given low-resolution input.

Framework enables generation via GAN-based, noise-assisted, and density-assisted optimization techniques.

Effectiveness is well-demonstrated on several datasets.

Overall assessment: strong paper with a valuable contribution.

Primary weakness: writing and presentation quality; manuscript needs polishing for clarity and flow.

Paper is sometimes difficult to follow given the topic's complexity; clearer explanations would improve impact.

Recommendation: include deeper analysis of the learned projection networks (e.g., what features the projection captures) to provide additional insights.
