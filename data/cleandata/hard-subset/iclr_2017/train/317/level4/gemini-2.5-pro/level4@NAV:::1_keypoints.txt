1. Novel amortized MAP inference shows perceptually strong GAN results but its advantages over SOTA GAN-based SR (e.g., SRGAN) are unclear.  
2. Affine projection enforces LR consistency but requires paired {HR,LR} data for pre-training, so paired-data requirement may not be eliminated.  
3. Need quantitative results on standard SR benchmarks (Set5, Set14, BSD100) for fair comparison.  
4. Experiments are limited to 128×128 images; authors should address scalability and performance on higher resolutions.  
5. Question whether a random noise latent vector was used or tested in the generator, as in conventional GANs.  
6. Overall: a novel, theoretically solid framework offering valuable insights, but comparative advantages and more experiments are needed.
