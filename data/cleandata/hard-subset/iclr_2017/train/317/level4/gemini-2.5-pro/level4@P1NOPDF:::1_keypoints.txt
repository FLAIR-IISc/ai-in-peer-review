- Novelty: Presents an amortized MAP-inference framework for single-image SR with an affine projection layer, a novel departure from prior SR methods.
- Comparison to SRGAN: Unclear what specific advantages this formulation provides over recent GAN-based SR methods like SRGAN; authors should clarify distinct contributions.
- Paired data requirement: Affine projection layer must be pre-trained on {HR, LR} pairs, so paired-data need may be shifted rather than eliminated—please clarify.
- Benchmark evaluation: Lacks quantitative and qualitative results on standard SR benchmarks (Set5, Set14, BSD100) for fair comparison.
- Resolution and scalability: Experiments limited to 128×128 images; authors should discuss performance, scalability, and computational feasibility on larger, arbitrary-resolution images.
- GAN noise input: Model appears to omit a random noise vector for the generator; clarify if this was deliberate and whether including noise was tested and how it affected results.
- Overall assessment: Strong, theoretically supported, and novel approach with promising results, but needs clearer comparisons and more extensive experimental validation.
