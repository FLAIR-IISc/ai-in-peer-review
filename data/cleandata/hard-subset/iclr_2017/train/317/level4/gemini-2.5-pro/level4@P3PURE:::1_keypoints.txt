1. Novel amortized MAP+GAN approach is promising but authors should clarify concrete advantages over recent GAN-based SR methods (e.g., Ledig et al.'s SRGAN).  
2. Claim that paired {HR,LR} data aren't required is misleading because the affine projection layer must be pre-trained on paired data — the paired-data requirement appears shifted, not eliminated.  
3. Experimental validation should include standard SR benchmarks (Set5, Set14, BSD100) for fair, direct comparison with prior work.  
4. Results are limited to 128×128 images; need evaluation on larger/higher-resolution images to demonstrate real-world applicability.  
5. Unclear whether a latent noise vector was used in the generator — authors should report experiments with/without latent noise to assess output diversity.  
6. Overall: interesting, theoretically solid and well-explored framework, but requires clearer positioning versus SOTA and more extensive empirical validation.
