- Proposes an amortized MAP estimation method for super-resolution using a neural network projecting onto an affine subspace of solutions consistent with the LR image.  
- Method enables finding proper SR solutions via GANs, noise-assisted, and density-assisted optimization.  
- Empirical results across several datasets convincingly demonstrate the approach’s effectiveness.  
- Paper is well-motivated and presents valuable ideas for SR.  
- Writing and organization need polishing for clarity; narrative can be hard to follow given the topic’s complexity.  
- Recommend providing more intuitive explanations of key concepts and reorganizing sections to improve readability.  
- Request deeper analysis of learned networks—what features they capture and how those features drive performance.  
- Minor fixes: include more detailed dataset and experimental setup descriptions and add additional visualizations/illustrations.  
- Overall: solid contribution but would benefit substantially from clearer writing and more analytical insight.
