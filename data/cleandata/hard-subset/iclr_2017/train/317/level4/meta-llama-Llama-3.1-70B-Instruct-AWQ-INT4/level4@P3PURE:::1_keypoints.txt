- Proposes a novel amortized MAP inference framework with a pre-learned affine projection for SR; well-written with solid theoretical foundation.
- Unclear what additional insights or improvements this formulation provides over prior GAN-based SR methods (e.g., SRGAN).
- Affine projection lets the main model be trained without HR-LR pairs, but the projection layer itself requires such pairs—may simply shift the training requirement.
- Recommends reporting results on standard SR benchmarks (Set5, Set14, BSD100) for fair comparison.
- Experiments are limited to 128×128 images; unclear if the method scales to larger/arbitrary-size images.
- Asks whether a noise/latent vector was used in the GAN setup to model a distribution.
- Overall: novel and promising, but some components’ necessity/effectiveness are questioned and more experiments are needed.
