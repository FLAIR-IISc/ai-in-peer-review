The paper presents an amortised MAP estimation method for super-resolution (SR) problems. By learning a neural network that projects to an affine subspace of SR solutions consistent with the low-resolution (LR) input, the method enables finding proper solutions using a variety of methods: Generative Adversarial Networks (GANs), noise-assisted, and density-assisted optimisation.
