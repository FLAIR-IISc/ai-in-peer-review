The paper proposes an amortized MAP estimation method for super-resolution (SR) problems, which learns a neural network to project inputs onto an affine subspace of SR solutions that are consistent with low-resolution (LR) observations. This approach enables finding proper solutions using various methods, including GANs, noise-assisted, and density-assisted optimization. The results are nicely demonstrated on several datasets.

Overall, I find the paper well-motivated and appreciate its contributions. However, I feel that the writing can be significantly improved for clarity and polish. At times, the text was difficult to follow, and given the complexity of the subject matter, clearer presentation would be beneficial. Furthermore, I would appreciate more analysis of the resulting networks, such as the types of features they learn.
