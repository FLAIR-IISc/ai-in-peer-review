- Proposes an innovative super-resolution approach formulated as amortized MAP estimation.  
- Introduces a projection step to enforce consistent HR–LR dependencies and shows it improves results.  
- Presents and experimentally evaluates three methods to solve the cross-entropy problem in Eq. 9.  
- Paper is well-written and well-presented.  
- Experiments are sufficient and include well-chosen toy examples and real-world applications.  
- Contributions in Sections 3.2–3.4 are novel and likely impactful for future SR work.  
- GAN-based training produces the most visually appealing results, suggesting further research in that direction.  
- Notes some GAN training aspects have been explored in other variants, but the overall contribution remains significant.  
- Recommends a thorough proofreading to correct a few typos.
