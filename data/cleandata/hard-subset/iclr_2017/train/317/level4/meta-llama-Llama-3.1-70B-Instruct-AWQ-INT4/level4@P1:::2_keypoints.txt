- Proposes approaching Super-Resolution as amortised MAP estimation.
- Introduces a projection step to enforce consistent HR-LR dependencies, experimentally improving results.
- Proposes and tests three methods to solve the cross-entropy problem in Eq.9.
- Experimental evaluation includes well-chosen toy examples and real-world applications.
- Results show the GAN-based training produces the most visually appealing outputs.
- Contributions (sections 3.2–3.4) are novel for super-resolution, though some GAN-training aspects resemble prior variants.
- Paper is very well written and presented, with minor typos to fix.
