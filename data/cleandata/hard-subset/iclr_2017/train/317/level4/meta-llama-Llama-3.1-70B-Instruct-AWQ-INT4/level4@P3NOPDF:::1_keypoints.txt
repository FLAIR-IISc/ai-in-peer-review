- Novelty: Proposes an innovative amortized MAP inference framework with an affine projection layer for SR and three methods to minimize cross-entropy, contributing solid theoretical analysis.

- Comparison to prior GAN SR: Needs deeper discussion and empirical comparison to existing GAN-based SR (e.g., SRGAN) to clarify new insights and advantages.

- Affine projection training: Clarify how the affine projection layer is trained, whether it still requires HR-LR pairs, and if training is merely shifted to that layer.

- Standard benchmarks: Include results on conventional SR test sets (Set5, Set14, BSD100) to enable fair comparison with prior work.

- Scalability / image size: Demonstrate performance on larger, arbitrary-sized images beyond the reported 128×128 to show practical scalability.

- Latent noise: Consider incorporating and evaluating a noise/latent vector (as in typical GANs) and discuss its potential impact on learned distributions and results.

- Overall: Promising and novel work that requires additional experiments and clarifications to strengthen its impact.
