- Proposes an amortized MAP estimation method for super-resolution (SR) problems.  
- Learns a neural network that projects to an affine subspace of SR solutions consistent with the low-resolution (LR) input.  
- Framework enables finding solutions using GANs, noise-assisted, and density-assisted optimization techniques.  
- Results are demonstrated on several datasets and appear strong.  
- Reviewer generally appreciates the paper.  
- Writing and presentation need significant improvement; explanations are often unclear and difficult to follow.  
- Requests more analysis of the learned networks, specifically the types of features they capture.
