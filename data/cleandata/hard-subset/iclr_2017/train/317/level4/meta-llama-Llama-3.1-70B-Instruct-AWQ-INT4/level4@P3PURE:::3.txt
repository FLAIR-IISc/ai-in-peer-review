This paper presents an amortized MAP estimation method for super-resolution (SR) problems. By learning a neural network that projects to an affine subspace of SR solutions consistent with the low-resolution (LR) input, the method enables finding proper solutions using a variety of techniques, including GANs, noise-assisted, and density-assisted optimization. The results are nicely demonstrated on several datasets.

Overall, I appreciate the paper, although I feel that the writing can be significantly improved. The presentation should be clarified, as it was difficult to follow at times. Given the complexity of the subject matter, clearer explanations would be beneficial. Furthermore, I would like to see more analysis of the resulting networks, specifically what kind of features they learn.
