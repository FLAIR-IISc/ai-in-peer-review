**Summary of the Paper**

The paper proposes an amortised MAP estimation method for image super-resolution (SR) problems. The authors introduce a novel neural network architecture that projects the model output to the affine subspace of valid SR solutions, ensuring consistency with the low-resolution input. They then present three methods to approximate the MAP estimate: Generative Adversarial Networks (GANs), denoiser-guided SR, and density-guided SR. The experiments demonstrate the effectiveness of the proposed methods on various datasets.
