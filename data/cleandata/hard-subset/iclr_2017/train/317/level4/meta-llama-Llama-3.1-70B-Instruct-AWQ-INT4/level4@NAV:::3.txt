The paper proposes an amortized MAP estimation approach for super-resolution (SR) problems, leveraging a neural network that projects onto an affine subspace of SR solutions consistent with low-resolution (LR) methods. This enables the discovery of proper solutions through various optimization techniques, including GANs, noise-assisted, and density-assisted methods.

The method's efficacy is effectively demonstrated across multiple datasets. Overall, the paper is well-received, although the writing could benefit from refinement and the presentation could be improved for clarity. At times, the narrative was challenging to follow, and given the complexity of the subject matter, enhanced clarity would be advantageous. Furthermore, a more in-depth analysis of the resulting networks would be valuable, specifically in terms of the features they learn.
