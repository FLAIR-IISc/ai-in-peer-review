- Proposes an amortized MAP estimation method for super-resolution that trains a network to project inputs onto an affine subspace of solutions consistent with low-resolution observations.  
- Method supports finding suitable SR solutions via multiple optimization strategies (GANs, noise-assisted, density-assisted).  
- Empirical results are convincingly demonstrated across several datasets.  
- Paper is well-motivated and makes valuable contributions.  
- Writing and presentation need significant improvement for clarity and polish; parts are hard to follow.  
- More analysis is needed of the learned networks, e.g., the types of features or representations they capture.
