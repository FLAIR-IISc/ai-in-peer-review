- Proposes an amortized MAP estimation for super-resolution using a neural network that projects onto an affine subspace of SR solutions consistent with the LR observation.  
- Framework supports discovering suitable solutions via multiple optimization techniques (GANs, noise-assisted, density-assisted).  
- Method is empirically effective and demonstrated across multiple datasets.  
- Overall reception of the paper is positive.  
- Writing and presentation need refinement—narrative is sometimes hard to follow given the topic's complexity.  
- Calls for deeper analysis of the learned networks, particularly the features they capture.
