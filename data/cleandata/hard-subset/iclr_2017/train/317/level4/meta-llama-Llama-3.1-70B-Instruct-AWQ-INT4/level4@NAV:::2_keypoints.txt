- Novel formulation: Presents super-resolution as amortized MAP estimation.  
- Projection step: Proposes a projection to preserve HR–LR dependencies, experimentally shown to improve results.  
- Cross-entropy solutions: Introduces and evaluates three methods to address the cross-entropy issue in Equation 9.  
- Writing and presentation: Paper is excellent, well-written, and effectively presented.  
- Experimental evaluation: Comprehensive experiments with well-chosen toy examples and real-world applications.  
- Key contributions: Sections 3.2–3.4 contain innovative contributions to super-resolution.  
- GAN overlap: Aspects specific to GAN training may have been explored previously elsewhere.  
- GAN visual quality: GAN-based training yields the most visually appealing results and merits further investigation.  
- Impact: Reviewer expects the paper to have a lasting impact on future super-resolution research.  
- Minor edits: Recommend proofreading to fix a few minor typos before publication.
