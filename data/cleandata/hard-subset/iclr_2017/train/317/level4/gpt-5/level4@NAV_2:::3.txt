The paper proposes an amortized MAP approach to super-resolution. It trains a network that enforces consistency by projecting outputs onto the affine subspace of valid HR solutions for a given LR input, enabling proper solutions via multiple strategies: GAN-based, denoiser-guided, and density-guided optimization.

Results are clearly demonstrated across several datasets.

I like the paper overall, but the writing could be substantially polished and the presentation made clearer. Some parts were hard to follow; given the technical complexity, improving clarity would help. I would also welcome deeper analysis of the learned models—e.g., what kinds of features or representations do the networks learn?
