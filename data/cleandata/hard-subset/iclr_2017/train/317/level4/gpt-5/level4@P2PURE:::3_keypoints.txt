- Proposes an amortised MAP estimation framework for super-resolution (SR).
- Trains a neural network that projects onto an affine subspace of SR solutions consistent with low-resolution observations.
- Enables recovery using multiple strategies: GAN-based, noise-assisted, and density-assisted optimisation.
- Empirical results are clearly demonstrated across several datasets.
- Reviewer overall likes the paper.
- Writing and presentation need significant polishing; exposition is sometimes hard to follow.
- Calls for more analysis of the learned networks, e.g., what features they capture.
