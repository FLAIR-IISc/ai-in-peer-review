- Proposes framing super-resolution as amortized MAP estimation.  
- Introduces a projection step to enforce HR–LR consistency.  
- Experimentally shows the projection yields improved results across tasks.  
- Proposes and evaluates three methods for solving the resulting cross-entropy problem (Eq. 9).  
- Paper is well written, clearly presented, and convincingly argued.  
- Experiments are sufficient and well chosen, covering toy examples and real-world applications.  
- Core contributions in Sections 3.2–3.4 appear novel.  
- Some GAN-specific components resemble prior work, but their integration with amortized MAP is original and valuable.  
- Empirical finding: GAN-based training produces the most visually appealing outputs and is a promising direction.  
- Minor: manuscript has a few typos and should be proofread.
