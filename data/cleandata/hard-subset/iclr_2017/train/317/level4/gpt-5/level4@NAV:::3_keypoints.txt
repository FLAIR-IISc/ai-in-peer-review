- Proposes an amortised MAP estimation approach for super-resolution problems.  
- Trains a neural network to project onto the affine subspace of SR solutions consistent with the LR image.  
- Enables obtaining proper SR solutions via multiple procedures: GANs, noise-assisted, and density-assisted optimization.  
- Empirical results are demonstrated convincingly on several datasets.  
- Reviewer likes the paper overall.  
- Writing and presentation need polishing and clearer exposition; parts are hard to follow.  
- Requests more analysis of the learned networks, e.g., what kinds of features they capture.
