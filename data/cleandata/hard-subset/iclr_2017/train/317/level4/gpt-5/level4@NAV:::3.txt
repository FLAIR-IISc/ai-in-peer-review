The paper proposes an amortised MAP estimation approach for SR problems. By training a neural network to project onto an affine subspace of SR solutions that are consistent with the LR method, the approach enables obtaining proper solutions using a variety of procedures: GANs, noise-assisted, and density-assisted optimisation. Results are nicely demonstrated on several datasets. I like the paper overall, though I feel the writing could be polished quite a bit and the presentation made clearer. It was hard to follow at times, and considering the subject matter is quite complicated, making it clearer would help. Also, I would love to see more analysis of the resulting networks—what kinds of features do they learn?
