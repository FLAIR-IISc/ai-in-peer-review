- Reframes single-image SR as amortised MAP; under linear degradation, training reduces to minimising cross-entropy H[qθ, pY].

- Introduces an affine projection layer using A+ to enforce exact HR→LR consistency (Ay = x) for all outputs.

- Proposes three optimisation strategies for Eq.9: AffGAN (projected GAN with instance noise), AffDG (denoiser-guided via score estimates), and AffLL (likelihood-guided with tractable density).

- Experiments on Swiss-roll, textures, CelebA, and ImageNet show AffGAN yields the most visually compelling results; the projection improves LR-consistency and can improve MSE/SSIM.

- Provides theoretical connections to KL minimisation and amortised variational inference, and rationale for instance noise.

- Decision: Accept — reasons: strong reframing, simple effective projection guaranteeing consistency, and convincing theoretical and empirical support.

- Problem targeted: avoid MSE-induced blur via amortised MAP without paired HR-LR supervision while enforcing exact consistency.

- Motivation/placement: clearly articulates MSE/PSNR limitations and situates work relative to GANs, denoising score estimation, and likelihood models; novelty lies in MAP framing plus projection.

- Empirical support: toy data shows cross-entropy near ground-truth MAP; projection yields zero LR-consistency error and can aid MSE training; GAN gives superior perceptual quality.

- Request: clarify how A and A+ are chosen in practice and report sensitivity to kernel mismatch and numerical errors in A+.

- Request: provide ablations for with/without projection and with/without instance noise, and report effects on stability and final quality.

- Request: compare directly to SRGAN/ESRGAN-style baselines under identical degradations and add perceptual metrics or a user study.

- Request: discuss robustness to unknown/realistic degradations and potential extension to blind SR.

- Request: examine whether stronger score/density models (modern diffusion/flow) can close the gap for AffDG/AffLL relative to GANs.

- Question: how sensitive is AffGAN to the accuracy of A+ and to the assumed downsampling kernel?

- Question: was mode collapse observed without instance noise, and how does the annealing schedule affect diversity/entropy H[qθ]?
