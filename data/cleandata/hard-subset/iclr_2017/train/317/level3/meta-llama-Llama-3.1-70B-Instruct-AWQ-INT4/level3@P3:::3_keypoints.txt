- Proposal: amortized MAP inference for image SR using a CNN that projects outputs onto an affine subspace to guarantee LR-consistency; three solvers proposed (GAN, denoiser-guided, density-guided).  
- Novelty: well-motivated and a novel CNN architecture with potential to improve SR performance.  
- Empirical results: perceptually superior SR demonstrated across datasets (2D Swiss-roll, grass textures, CelebA, ImageNet).  
- Writing/presentation: needs substantial polishing—paper is sometimes hard to follow and requires clearer exposition.  
- Intuition/visuals: more intuitive explanations and additional visualizations are needed to illustrate concepts.  
- Network analysis: lacks deeper analysis of learned features; requests feature visualizations and ablation studies.  
- Limitations/future work: paper should discuss limitations of the proposed methods and possible future directions.  
- Recommendation: accept conditionally, provided the authors address the noted issues.
