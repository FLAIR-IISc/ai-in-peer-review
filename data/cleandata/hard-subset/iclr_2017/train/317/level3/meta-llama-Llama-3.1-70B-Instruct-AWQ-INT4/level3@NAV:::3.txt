**Summary of the Paper's Contributions**

The paper proposes an amortized MAP estimation method for image super-resolution (SR) using a neural network that projects onto an affine subspace of SR solutions consistent with the low-resolution (LR) input. The authors introduce three methods to solve the optimization problem: GAN-based, noise-assisted, and density-assisted optimization. The empirical results demonstrate the effectiveness of the proposed methods across several datasets.
