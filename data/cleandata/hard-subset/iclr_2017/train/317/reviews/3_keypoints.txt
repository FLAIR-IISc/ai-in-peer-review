- Proposes an amortized MAP estimation method for super-resolution (SR).
- Trains a neural network to project onto an affine subspace of SR solutions consistent with the low-resolution (LR) input.
- Supports finding solutions via multiple strategies: GAN-based, noise-assisted, and density-assisted optimization.
- Empirical results are well demonstrated across several datasets.
- Reviewer overall likes the paper.
- Writing and presentation need substantial polishing and clearer exposition.
- Paper is sometimes hard to follow given the complex subject matter.
- Requests more analysis of the learned networks, e.g., what features they learn.
