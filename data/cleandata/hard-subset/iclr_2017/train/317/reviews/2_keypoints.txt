- Paper reframes super-resolution as amortised MAP estimation.
- Introduces a projection step to maintain consistent HR-LR dependencies, showing improved results experimentally.
- Proposes and evaluates three methods to solve the cross-entropy problem in Eq.9.
- Overall assessment: very good paper, well written and presented.
- Experiments are sufficient with well-chosen toy examples and real-world applications.
- Contributions (secs. 3.2–3.4) appear novel for super-resolution, though some GAN training aspects may be similar to prior variants.
- Findings indicate GAN-based training yields the most visually appealing results and will be relevant for future SR work.
- Manuscript needs another proofread to fix a few minor typos.
