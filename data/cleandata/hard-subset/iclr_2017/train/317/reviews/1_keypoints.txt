1) Novel amortized MAP inference combined with GAN yields plausible SR results, but clarity needed on what it adds over recent GAN-based SOTA.  
2) Affine projection lets training without paired {HR,LR}, yet training the projection itself uses pairs — is this just transferring the need for pairs?  
3) Request for evaluations on standard SR benchmarks (Set5, Set14, BSD100) for fair comparison with prior work.  
4) Results are limited to 128×128 images — can the framework handle larger/arbitrary image sizes typical in SR?  
5) Question whether a GAN noise latent vector was used/tried to better model distributions.  
6) Overall positive: novel framework and solid theory, but more experiments and justification of necessity are required.
