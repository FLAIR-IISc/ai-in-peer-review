**Summary**

The paper proposes a testbed for evaluating end-to-end dialog systems in goal-oriented applications, specifically in the context of restaurant reservation. The authors design a set of five tasks that test various abilities of dialog systems, such as dialog management, querying knowledge bases, and interpreting the output of queries. They also evaluate their proposed approach on two additional datasets extracted from real interactions with users. The results show that an end-to-end dialog system based on Memory Networks can reach promising performance, yet still has room for improvement.
