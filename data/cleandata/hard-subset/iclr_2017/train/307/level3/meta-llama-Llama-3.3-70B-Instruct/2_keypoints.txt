- Comprehensive study on transferability of adversarial examples, highlighting chatbot limitations and need for realistic dialog validation.  
- Proposes a novel ensemble-based method to generate more transferable adversarial examples.  
- Method outperforms previous work and is supported by thorough experimental evaluation.  
- Investigates geometric properties of different models to understand transferability.  
- Reviewer recommends acceptance for tackling an important question and having a well-motivated approach.  
- Extensive experiments on large models and datasets back the paper's claims.  
- Demonstrates high success rate in attacking a black-box system (Clarifai.com).  
- Suggests deeper analysis of model geometric properties related to transferability.  
- Recommends exploring application to other domains (e.g., NLP) to show broader effectiveness.  
- Asks for clarification on how ensemble weights were chosen in the proposed approach.  
- Requests plans and anticipated challenges for extending the method to other domains like NLP.  
- Requests more examples of targeted adversarial examples and their results on Clarifai.com.  
- Concludes the paper is a significant contribution with potential to improve robustness and security, pending revisions.
