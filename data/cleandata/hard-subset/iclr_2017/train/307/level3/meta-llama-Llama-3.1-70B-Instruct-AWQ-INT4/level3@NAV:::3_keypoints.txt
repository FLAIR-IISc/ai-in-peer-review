- Introduces a new simulated restaurant-domain dataset for end-to-end goal-oriented dialog using a KB and rule-based NL generation.
- Proposes five goal-oriented tasks covering dialog management, KB querying, and interpreting query outputs.
- Evaluates models (including Memory Networks) thoroughly on the proposed tasks and two real-user datasets to validate simulation realism.
- Decision: accept — primary reasons are the novel dataset and comprehensive model evaluations showing promising results.
- Paper is well-motivated, clearly explained, and the tasks are relevant and well-designed for goal-oriented dialog research.
- Improvement: provide more details on the rule-based natural language generation (specific rules and procedures).
- Improvement: include deeper analysis explaining why certain models perform better on specific tasks.
- Improvement: discuss dataset and task limitations and how to extend them to better reflect real-world dialog complexity.
- Question: please detail the specific rules used for the rule-based NL generation.
- Question: how will you address and extend the dataset/tasks to capture real-world dialog complexities?
