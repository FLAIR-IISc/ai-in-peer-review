- Proposes a comprehensive testbed for evaluating end-to-end goal-oriented dialog systems, focused on restaurant reservation.  
- Introduces five tasks that decompose dialog into dialog management (Tasks 1–2), KB querying and interpretation (Tasks 3–4), and full dialog execution (Task 5).  
- Testbed is lightweight, reproducible, and grounded in a restaurant knowledge base.  
- Memory Networks achieve promising per-response accuracy but per-dialog accuracy remains low.  
- Validates findings on two real-world datasets (DSTC2 and a concierge dataset), showing synthetic tasks mirror real-world performance patterns.  
- Recommendation: accept — fills a gap in standardized evaluation for end-to-end goal-oriented dialog systems.  
- Experimental setup is thorough, considers OOV challenges, and compares multiple baselines (rule-based, IR, supervised embeddings, MemNets).  
- Well-motivated by limitations of slot-filling systems and the question of generalizing end-to-end methods to goal-oriented settings.  
- Testbed tasks go beyond simple language modeling and target specific capabilities required for goal-oriented dialog.  
- Strong empirical support using appropriate metrics (per-response and per-dialog accuracy) and extensive comparisons.  
- Shows MemNets can learn non-trivial operations (issuing API calls, handling OOV entities) but struggle to interpret KB results and display options.  
- Requests deeper analysis of why Task 3 (displaying options) is particularly challenging and concrete error patterns.  
- Asks for clearer comparison and relationship between the proposed testbed and existing datasets (especially DSTC).  
- Recommends discussion of adapting the testbed to other goal-oriented domains beyond restaurants.  
- Suggests expanding explanation and concrete examples of match type features for handling OOV entities.  
- Calls for more detailed analysis of differences between the synthetic tasks and the concierge dataset to pinpoint variability affecting performance.  
- Requests clarification on why per-dialog accuracy is low despite reasonable per-response accuracy and how small errors compound.  
- Notes public release of testbed and datasets is a valuable community resource that will accelerate research and highlight future challenges.
