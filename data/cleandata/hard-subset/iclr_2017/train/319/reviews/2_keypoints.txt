Proposed a modified knowledge-distillation framework that minimizes differences of summed statistics across feature maps and empirically outperforms FitNet-style distillation.

Evaluated the method on multiple computer-vision datasets.

Paper is generally well-written.

Method appears limited to convolutional architectures.

“Attention” terminology is misleading — the method simply distills summed (squared or lp-norm) activations, not attention maps.

Gradient-based attention-transfer section feels out-of-place and is neither directly compared with nor combined with the main attention-based transfer, adding little value.

Unclear how the induced 2-norm in Eq.(2) is computed (largest singular value of Q ∈ R^{H×W} is expensive); authors may have meant the Frobenius norm.

Overall, method performs well but the paper has organizational issues and unclear notation.
