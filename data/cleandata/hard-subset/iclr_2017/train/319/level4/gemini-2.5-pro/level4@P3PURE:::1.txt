This paper introduces a novel method for knowledge transfer between neural networks. The central idea of transferring knowledge through attention maps, as opposed to direct feature activations, is a promising and intuitive contribution.

However, the experimental results show only marginal improvements over knowledge distillation alone. The evaluation on ImageNet, in particular, would benefit from more extensive experiments to substantiate the claims of the proposed method.

I would be willing to reconsider my evaluation should the authors provide a more thorough experimental analysis in Section 4.2.2.
