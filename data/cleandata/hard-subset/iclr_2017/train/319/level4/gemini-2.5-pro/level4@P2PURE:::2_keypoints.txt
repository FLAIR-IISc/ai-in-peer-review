- Evaluated on a variety of computer vision datasets.  
- Paper is generally well-written and easy to follow.  
- Applicability appears limited to convolutional architectures; extension to other network types is not discussed.  
- Use of the term "attention" is misleading — the method distills summary statistics of activation maps, not a conventional attention mechanism.  
- Gradient-based attention transfer section feels disconnected and is neither compared to nor combined with the activation-based transfer.  
- Equation (2) is ambiguous about the induced 2-norm (largest singular value) versus a computationally tractable alternative like the Frobenius norm.  
- Strong empirical performance but presentation weakened by organizational issues, misleading terminology, and unclear notation.
