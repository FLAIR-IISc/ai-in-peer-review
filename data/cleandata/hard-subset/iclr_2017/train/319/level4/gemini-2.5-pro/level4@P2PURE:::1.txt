This paper proposes a novel method for knowledge transfer based on distilling attention maps. The core idea of transferring knowledge via attention rather than feature activations is promising. However, the experimental results do not demonstrate a significant improvement over knowledge distillation alone. Furthermore, the evaluation on ImageNet is limited and would require more extensive experiments to be convincing. I would be willing to reconsider my score if the authors expand upon the experimental results in Section 4.2.2.
