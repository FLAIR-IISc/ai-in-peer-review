- Evaluated on a variety of computer vision datasets, demonstrating strong empirical performance.
- Paper is generally well-written.
- Method appears limited to convolutional architectures.
- Use of the term "attention" is misleading; method distills spatial summary statistics, not conventional attention.
- Gradient-based attention transfer is presented separately, not compared or integrated, so its value is unclear.
- Computation of the induced 2-norm in Eq. (2) is unclear and potentially expensive; authors may have meant the Frobenius norm.
- Overall: strong empirical results but suffers from organizational issues and unclear notation.
