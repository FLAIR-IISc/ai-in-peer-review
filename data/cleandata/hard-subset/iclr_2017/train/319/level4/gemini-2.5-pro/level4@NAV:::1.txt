This work introduces a novel method for knowledge transfer. The core concept of transferring knowledge via attention maps, as opposed to conventional activations, is promising.

However, the experimental results indicate only a marginal improvement over baseline knowledge distillation. Furthermore, the evaluation on the ImageNet dataset would be significantly strengthened by more extensive experimentation.

I am willing to reconsider my evaluation pending a more thorough expansion of the experiments presented in Section 4.2.2.
