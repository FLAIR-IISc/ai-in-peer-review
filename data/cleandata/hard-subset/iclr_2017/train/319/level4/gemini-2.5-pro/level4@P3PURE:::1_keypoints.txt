- Proposes a novel knowledge-transfer method that uses attention maps instead of direct feature activations.  
- The attention-map approach is promising and intuitively appealing.  
- Experimental results show only marginal improvements over standard knowledge distillation.  
- ImageNet evaluation is insufficient and needs more extensive experiments to support the method's claims.  
- Reviewer would reconsider the evaluation if the authors provide a more thorough experimental analysis in Section 4.2.2.
