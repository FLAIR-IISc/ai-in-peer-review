- Proposes a novel, intuitive method transferring knowledge via attention maps rather than intermediate feature activations.  
- Core idea is promising.  
- Primary weakness: experimental evaluation is insufficient, with only marginal gains over standard knowledge distillation.  
- ImageNet experiments (Section 4.2.2) particularly fail to demonstrate a clear advantage.  
- Reviewer requests a more comprehensive large-scale evaluation on ImageNet to validate effectiveness.  
- Reviewer willing to reconsider the score if the authors provide extended results that clearly outperform existing baselines.
