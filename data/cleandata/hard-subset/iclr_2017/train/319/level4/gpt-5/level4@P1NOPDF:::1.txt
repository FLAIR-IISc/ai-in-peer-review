This paper proposes a new approach to knowledge transfer that uses attention maps rather than activations. I find the central idea appealing: transferring where a model focuses may be more robust and informative than transferring raw activations.

However, the empirical results do not show a clear or substantial improvement over standard knowledge distillation alone. As presented, the gains are limited, which weakens the case for the proposed method.

In particular, the ImageNet section would benefit from a more thorough and comprehensive set of experiments. The current evaluation in Section 4.2.2 is too brief to support strong conclusions. I encourage the authors to extend this section with additional experiments and analysis to better demonstrate the method’s advantages.

I would be willing to update my score if the authors substantially expand Section 4.2.2 with more complete ImageNet results.
