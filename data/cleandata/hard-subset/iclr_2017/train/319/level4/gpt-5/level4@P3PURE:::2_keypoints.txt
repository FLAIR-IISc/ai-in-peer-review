- Evaluated on multiple computer vision datasets.
- Paper is generally well written.
- Method appears limited to convolutional architectures.
- "Attention" terminology is misleading — the method actually matches sums of squared activations or other summed l_p statistics over feature maps.
- Gradient-based attention transfer component feels out of place, is neither compared to nor used jointly with the attention-based transfer, and adds little value.
- Unclear how the induced 2-norm in Eq. (2) is computed; the induced 2-norm is the largest singular value (expensive) and the authors may have meant the Frobenius norm.
- Overall: the distillation method works well in practice but the paper has organizational issues and unclear notation.
