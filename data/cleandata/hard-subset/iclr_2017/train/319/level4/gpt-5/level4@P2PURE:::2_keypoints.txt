- Evaluated on multiple computer vision datasets.
- Paper is generally well written.
- Method appears limited to convolutional architectures.
- The term “attention” is misleading; method actually matches summed squared values or other summed lp statistics of feature-map activations.
- Gradient-based attention transfer component feels out of place, not compared or jointly used with the main method, and adds little.
- Unclear notation in Eq. (2): computing an induced 2-norm (largest singular value) of Q∈R^{H×W} inside the loss seems expensive — authors may have meant Frobenius norm.
- Overall: method performs well empirically but the paper has organizational issues and unclear notation.
