This paper proposes a novel approach to knowledge transfer, emphasizing the transfer of attention maps rather than activations. The concept is appealing and intuitively well-motivated. However, the empirical results show only modest improvements over standard knowledge distillation. In particular, the ImageNet experiments are limited and do not sufficiently demonstrate the method’s advantages. I would be willing to reconsider my score if the authors substantially expand Section 4.2.2 with more comprehensive experiments and analysis.
