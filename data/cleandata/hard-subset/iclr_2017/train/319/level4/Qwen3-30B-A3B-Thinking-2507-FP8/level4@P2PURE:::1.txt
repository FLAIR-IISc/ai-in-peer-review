

The paper introduces a novel approach to knowledge transfer by leveraging attention maps rather than activations. While the concept of transferring attention maps is compelling, the experimental results demonstrate only marginal improvements over knowledge distillation (KD) alone, particularly in the ImageNet experiments. Additional experiments on ImageNet are necessary to substantiate the claimed benefits and establish the method's effectiveness in larger-scale settings. I would be willing to revise my score upward if the authors expand Section 4.2.2 with these additional ImageNet results.
