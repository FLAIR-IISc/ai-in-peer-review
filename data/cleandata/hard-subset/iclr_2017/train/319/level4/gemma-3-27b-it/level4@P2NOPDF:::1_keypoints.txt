- Introduces a novel, compelling idea: transferring attention maps instead of activations for knowledge distillation, offering targeted and interpretable transfer.
- Empirical results do not show a substantial or consistent advantage over standard knowledge distillation.
- ImageNet results are particularly weak/limited and raise concerns about the method’s effectiveness on that benchmark.
- Recommend expanding Section 4.2.2 with more architectures, hyperparameter settings, and alternative training regimes on ImageNet.
- Paper feels incomplete and the potential of attention-map transfer is underexplored due to modest experimental validation.
- A more comprehensive analysis and stronger ImageNet experiments would significantly strengthen the contribution.
- Reviewer is willing to reconsider the evaluation if the authors substantially extend Section 4.2.2 with additional experiments and analyses.
