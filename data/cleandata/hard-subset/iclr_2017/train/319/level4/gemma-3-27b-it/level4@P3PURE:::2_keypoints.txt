- Paper proposes a modified knowledge distillation that matches summed statistics across feature maps and empirically outperforms a FitNet-style baseline.
- Evaluation is strong: tested on a variety of computer vision datasets.
- Writing quality is generally good.
- Method seems limited to convolutional architectures.
- Calling the method “attention” is misleading — it matches summed activation statistics (e.g., Lp norms), not attention maps or attention-based transfer.
- The gradient-based “attention” transfer feels disconnected and is neither compared to nor combined with the main statistic-based transfer.
- Eq.(2) is unclear: computing the induced 2-norm (largest singular value) of an H×W matrix in the loss seems expensive; authors may have meant the Frobenius norm.
- Overall: method performs well but the paper has organizational issues and unclear notation.
