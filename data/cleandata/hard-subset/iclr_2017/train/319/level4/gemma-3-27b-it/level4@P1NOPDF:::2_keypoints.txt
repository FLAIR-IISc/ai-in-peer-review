- Evaluated the proposed methods on a variety of computer vision datasets.  
- Paper is generally well-written.  
- Method appears limited to convolutional architectures.  
- Use of “attention” terminology is misleading—method actually distills summed statistics (e.g., summed squared values or Lp norms) of activations within a feature map.  
- Gradient-based attention transfer feels disconnected from the core contribution and isn’t directly compared to or combined with the attention-style transfer.  
- Computation of the induced 2-norm in Eq. (2) is unclear and potentially expensive (largest singular value); authors may have intended the Frobenius norm.  
- Results are promising in practice but the paper suffers from organizational issues and unclear notation that weaken the contribution.
