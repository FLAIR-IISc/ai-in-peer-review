This paper introduces a novel approach to knowledge transfer, specifically focusing on the transfer of attention maps rather than direct activation values. This is a promising direction. However, the experimental results currently presented do not demonstrate a substantial performance gain over standard knowledge distillation techniques. I recommend the authors conduct a more comprehensive evaluation, particularly expanding the experiments within the ImageNet dataset. A strengthened and broadened analysis in section 4.2.2 would likely warrant a re-evaluation of the paper’s score.
