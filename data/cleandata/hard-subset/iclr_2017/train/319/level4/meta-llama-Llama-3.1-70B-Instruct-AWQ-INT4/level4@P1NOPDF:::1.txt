The paper proposes a novel approach to transferring knowledge, which I find intriguing. In particular, I appreciate the idea of transferring attention maps instead of activations, as it offers a fresh perspective on knowledge distillation. However, upon examining the experimental results, I was not convinced that the proposed method yields significant improvements over knowledge distillation alone. Specifically, the ImageNet section does not demonstrate substantial gains, which leads me to believe that additional experiments are necessary to fully validate the effectiveness of this approach. I would consider revising my score if the authors were to expand on Section 4.2.2, providing more comprehensive results to support their claims.
