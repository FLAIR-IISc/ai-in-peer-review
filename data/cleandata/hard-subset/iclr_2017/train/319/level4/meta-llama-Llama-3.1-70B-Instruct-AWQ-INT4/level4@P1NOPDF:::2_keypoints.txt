- Evaluated on multiple computer vision datasets demonstrating empirical performance.
- Paper is generally well-written.
- Method appears limited to convolutional architectures.
- Use of the term "attention" is misleading; the method actually distills summed statistics of feature-map activations (e.g., summed squared or Lp norms).
- Gradient-based "attention transfer" seems out of place, is neither directly compared to nor jointly used with the attention-style method, and adds limited value.
- The induced 2-norm in Eq.(2) is unclear and potentially expensive to compute; authors may have meant the Frobenius norm.
- Overall the distillation method is effective, but the paper suffers from organizational issues and unclear notation.
