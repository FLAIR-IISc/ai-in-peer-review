- Proposes a modified knowledge distillation minimizing differences in summed statistics across feature maps; empirically outperforms FitNet-style baseline.
- Evaluated on a variety of computer vision datasets.
- Paper is generally well-written.
- Method appears limited to convolutional architectures.
- Use of the term "attention" is misleading — it distills summed squared or Lp statistics of activations, not attention mechanisms.
- Inclusion of gradient-based attention transfer is unnecessary and not directly compared or integrated with the main method.
- Computation of the induced 2-norm in Eq.(2) is unclear and potentially computationally expensive; authors may have intended the Frobenius norm.
- Overall effective in practice but suffers from organizational issues and unclear notation.
