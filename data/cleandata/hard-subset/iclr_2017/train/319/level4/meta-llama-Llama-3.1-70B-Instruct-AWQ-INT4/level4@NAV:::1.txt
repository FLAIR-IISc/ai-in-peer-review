The paper introduces a novel approach to knowledge transfer, which is an intriguing concept. 
One aspect that stands out is the strategy of transferring attention maps rather than activation values, a choice that holds merit. 
However, the experimental results fail to demonstrate a significant enhancement over knowledge distillation alone, suggesting that additional experiments, particularly in the ImageNet section, are necessary to substantiate the proposed method's efficacy. 
I would be willing to reconsider my evaluation if the authors were to expand on the discussion in Section 4.2.2, providing more comprehensive insights and analyses.
