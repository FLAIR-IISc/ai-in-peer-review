This paper proposes a novel approach to knowledge transfer, which is a promising direction in the field of deep learning. The idea of transferring attention maps instead of activations is particularly appealing, as it has the potential to capture more nuanced and contextual information.

However, upon examining the experimental results, I was somewhat underwhelmed by the performance gains achieved by the proposed method compared to knowledge distillation alone. Specifically, the results in the ImageNet section do not demonstrate a significant improvement, which raises concerns about the efficacy of the approach.

To strengthen the paper, I would recommend conducting additional experiments in the ImageNet section to more comprehensively evaluate the proposed method. This could involve exploring different architectures, hyperparameters, or training protocols to better understand the conditions under which the method excels.

If the authors are able to provide more compelling results in the ImageNet section, I would be willing to reconsider my assessment of the paper. In particular, I would like to see an extension of Section 4.2.2, which could provide more insight into the strengths and limitations of the proposed approach.
