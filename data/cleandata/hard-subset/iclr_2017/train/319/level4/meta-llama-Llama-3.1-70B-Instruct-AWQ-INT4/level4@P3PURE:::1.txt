The paper proposes a novel approach to knowledge transfer, which is a promising direction in the field of deep learning. I appreciate the idea of transferring attention maps instead of activations, as it provides a more nuanced and context-dependent way of capturing knowledge.

However, upon examining the experimental results, I found that the performance gains achieved by the proposed method are not substantial compared to knowledge distillation alone. Specifically, the results in the ImageNet section do not demonstrate a significant improvement, which raises concerns about the effectiveness of the approach. To strengthen the paper, I believe additional experiments are necessary to thoroughly evaluate the proposed method's performance on the ImageNet dataset.

I would consider revising my assessment if the authors expand on Section 4.2.2, providing more comprehensive results and analysis to substantiate their claims.
