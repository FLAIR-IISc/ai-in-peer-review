- Proposes a modified knowledge distillation that minimizes discrepancy in the sum of statistics across feature maps and empirically outperforms a FitNet-style baseline.
- Comprehensive evaluation on a variety of computer vision datasets.
- Paper is generally well-written and clearly presented.
- Method appears specific to convolutional architectures, limiting broader applicability.
- "Attention" terminology is misleading — the method distills sums of activation statistics, not true attention mechanisms.
- Gradient-based attention transfer is unnecessary and disjointed, and is neither compared to nor integrated with the main method.
- Computation of the induced 2-norm in Eq.(2) is unclear and potentially expensive; authors may have intended the Frobenius norm.
- Promising practical results, but organizational issues and unclear notation detract from overall impact.
