The paper proposes a novel approach to knowledge transfer, which is a commendable contribution to the field. I find the idea of transferring attention maps instead of activations to be particularly intriguing. However, upon examining the experimental results, I noticed that the improvements achieved by the proposed method over knowledge distillation alone are not substantial. Specifically, the ImageNet section could benefit from more comprehensive experiments to solidify the claims made. I would consider revising my assessment if the authors provide additional results in Section 4.2.2, which would help to strengthen the paper's conclusions.
