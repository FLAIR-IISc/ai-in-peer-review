The paper proposes a novel approach to knowledge transfer, which is a valuable contribution to the field. I appreciate the idea of transferring attention maps instead of activations, as it offers a fresh perspective on knowledge distillation. However, upon examining the experimental results, I found that the improvement over traditional knowledge distillation is not substantial, particularly in the ImageNet section. To strengthen the paper, I recommend conducting additional experiments to demonstrate the effectiveness of the proposed method. If the authors can provide more comprehensive results in Section 4.2.2, I would be willing to reconsider my assessment.
