- Proposes attention transfer: student networks mimic teacher attention maps to improve knowledge transfer.
- Introduces two spatial attention map types: activation-based and gradient-based.
- Claims attention transfer significantly improves student performance, especially with knowledge distillation.

- Extensive experiments on CIFAR, ImageNet, and fine-grained tasks demonstrate effectiveness.
- Clear, well-structured introduction linking attention to vision and NLP applications.

- Results analysis is thorough and discusses limitations and future directions.
