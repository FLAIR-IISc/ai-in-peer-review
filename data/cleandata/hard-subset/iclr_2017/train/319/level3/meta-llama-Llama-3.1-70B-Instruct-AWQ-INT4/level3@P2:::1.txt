**Summary of the Paper**

This paper proposes a new knowledge transfer method for convolutional neural networks (CNNs), which leverages attention maps to improve the performance of a student network by mimicking the attention patterns of a powerful teacher network. The authors define two types of spatial attention maps, activation-based and gradient-based, and propose several novel methods for transferring attention from the teacher to the student network. The experimental results show consistent improvements across various datasets and CNN architectures, including residual and non-residual networks.
